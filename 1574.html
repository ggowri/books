"What?!"

Guy's eyes widened in shock as he stared at the powerful dark energy swirling around Osborne.

His complexion darkened considerably. "You're actually a practitioner of elemental power!"

A wave of tension washed over him.

Among the various clans in Celestara, practitioners who focused on elemental power were quite rare. This was largely because enhancing divine power was significantly easier than developing elemental essence.

Most individuals, once they became Junior Deities, shifted their focus to boosting their divine power, often neglecting elemental power or treating it as a secondary pursuit.

Only a handful of practitioners ever managed to elevate their elemental power to high levels.

But everyone knew that the most elite warriors were typically those who had focused on elemental power.

Just look at Lord Malevolence!

Or the seven top Deity Kings from the Human Community who once besieged him—God of Night, God of Light, God of Space, God of Time, God of Death, Goddess of Life, and Goddess of Fate.

Each of them had honed a specific elemental power to its absolute peak!

Their strength far surpassed that of ordinary Deity Kings.

Even so, the number of individuals who truly excelled in elemental power remained low.

It was a tough road—much harder than advancing divine power. For most people lacking exceptional talent, focusing on elemental power at the expense of divine power was a losing game.

Previously, Osborne's Thunder Power had already left Guy astonished.

He had thought he could withstand it.

But now, seeing Osborne reveal his dark power, which was even stronger than Thunder Power, Guy felt a clear sense of threat.

This realization left him shaken.

"How much power are you really hiding? Your dark power is incredibly strong! If you're allowed to grow, you could become a massive threat to all our clans!"

With that thought, a murderous intent flickered in Guy's eyes.

Osborne smiled calmly. "You'll never know how much I'm hiding because I don't need to go all out to deal with you! Right now, I can take you down!"

With a swift motion, Osborne unleashed a wave of dark energy that shot straight toward Guy.

Guy's expression hardened in an instant as he threw a punch to intercept it.

The dark energy shattered, but Guy was pushed back a couple of steps, his face darkening with anger.

Guy's demeanor turned icy. "Arrogant! "I admit you're strong now, but do you really think you can defeat me that easily? You're underestimating me! I'll show you just how powerful I really am!"

With that, Guy charged at Osborne with terrifying speed, a whirlwind of fury.

Osborne smirked. "Let's see what you've got."

He fired off another blast of dark energy, which spiraled toward Guy.

"Get lost!" Guy roared, unleashing a surge of divine power that nearly doubled his earlier strength.

It was clear he was now going all out.

Osborne's casually thrown dark energy was effortlessly shattered before Guy, who closed the distance in the blink of an eye.

A glimmer of triumph flashed in his eyes as he threw a powerful punch aimed right at Osborne's chest. "Die!"

Osborne chuckled coldly. "You've got some skill, but it's not that impressive! If I were using only Thunder Power, it might be a challenge to beat you. But too bad for you, I'm using dark power now! Get lost!"

As he spoke, a torrent of dark energy erupted around him, enveloping his fist. Then, with a mighty swing, Osborne unleashed a punch that radiated sheer force.

The clash of their attacks created a deafening sound, shaking the entire cavern to its core.

Rocks crumbled and fell from above, shattered into gravel by the turbulence generated by their battle.

Even the divine power barrier surrounding them trembled violently, on the verge of giving way.

In the midst of this chaos, a figure was sent hurtling backward, blood pouring from his mouth.

He crashed into the stone wall of the cave, leaving behind a massive dent.

That figure was Guy.

Osborne remained planted in place like an immovable mountain, while Guy had been blown away.

In that brief instant, Guy felt the sharp snap of his arm as Osborne's powerful fist, wreathed in dark energy, struck him squarely in the chest, caving it in.

With that single blow, Guy was defeated.

Despite his status as a Deity Overlord, he was still only at the early stage, and in the face of a serious Osborne, his strength was simply inadequate.

Osborne couldn't help but smirk as he appeared before Guy in the blink of an eye.

Guy spat out another mouthful of blood, his face a mask of shock and fear as he stared at Osborne.

He had lost.

Osborne's dark power was overwhelming. One punch had shattered Guy's defenses, crushed his Malevolence Amulet, and left his Divine Body severely injured.

Osborne was a force to be reckoned with!

"What... what do you want?" Guy managed to stammer, regret and terror etched across his features.

Osborne offered a calm smile. "Don't worry. I'm not going to kill you. Instead, I'll transform you into one of my Blood Clan descendants and make you my loyal subordinate!"

With that, Osborne sprang into action.
