Normally, his injuries would have been severe enough to require several days of healing to recover.

However, the medicines left by Ricardo were of exceptional quality. Their potent healing properties far surpassed anything Marcus had used before.

In just a few hours, Marcus's injuries were completely healed.

He even felt that his martial skills were on the verge of a breakthrough.

"It seems that extreme combat plays a significant role in advancing martial skills. Pushing yourself to the limit can unlock untapped potential.

People often unleash extraordinary power when facing death. I need to seize this opportunity!" Marcus thought to himself.

Marcus resolved this internally and began intense training with the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire. At the same time, he found a bottle of pills in Ricardo's spatial ring.

A refreshing herbal aroma wafted from the bottle. According to Timeless Warrior's Grimoire, the pills were known as Essence Rejuvenation Pills.

Consuming them would significantly boost a warrior's martial cultivation.

There were over a dozen pills in the bottle. Without hesitation, Marcus swallowed three of them.

A surge of potent energy coursed through his body, nourishing his muscles and strengthening his bones and organs.

Marcus channeled the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire's techniques with fervor, refining the medicinal energy that surged through his meridians.

Under the forceful infusion of the medicine's power, Marcus felt his martial skills swelling rapidly.

Soon, he had reached the very peak of Ancient Martial Stage Two!

Letting out a fierce roar, he unleashed every ounce of potential in his body and made a full-force push for Ancient Martial Stage Three.

Finally, amidst a surging resonance within him, Marcus broke through the peak of Stage Two and stepped into Ancient Martial Stage Three!

After the breakthrough, Marcus's strength underwent a tremendous transformation.

Compared to Stage Two, his power had more than doubled.

Every aspect—physical strength, agility, speed—had improved in all dimensions, elevating him to a new level.

If he were to face Ricardo now, he still might not win, but he definitely wouldn't be as humiliated as before.

At the same time, Marcus unlocked a new ability after the breakthrough.

It was telepathy!

The same telepathic ability Ricardo had used to track him!

Now, even with his eyes shut, Marcus could perceive everything in the room with perfect clarity.

It was as if a virtual world had been constructed in his mind.

He could even faintly sense things beyond the room's walls.

For example, in the next room, a young couple was enthusiastically engaged in their intimate activities.

The man was gaunt and frail, while the woman was robust, weighing at least 250 pounds. She had the poor guy rolling his eyes.

Beyond that, Marcus could observe bits and pieces of what was happening in other rooms within a radius of several dozen meters. Everything was within his awareness.

However, Marcus knew that his telepathic ability was still in its infancy, having only just broken through.

Reaching Ricardo's level of mastery would require extensive practice and refinement.

Marcus wasn't in a rush. This breakthrough had been completely unexpected, and gaining telepathic abilities filled him with excitement.

This newfound power gave him another life-saving tool for facing formidable opponents.

Marcus gradually ended his meditation as Josie stirred from her slumber on the bed, letting out a soft hum.

"Honey... hold me..."

Seeing Marcus sitting at the edge of the bed, Josie opened her arms with a playful, coquettish look.

Marcus leaned over, pressing Josie beneath him as he kissed her deeply.

Though she had just woken up, Josie's body remained incredibly sensitive.

Feeling the powerful masculine energy radiating from Marcus, Josie was swept up in emotion again, her breath coming in soft, alluring gasps.

Just as Marcus was about to continue, Leia's voice came faintly from outside the door. "Mommy, where did you go? Are you in your room?"

The sound snapped Josie out of her daze. She suddenly remembered that Leia had spent the night alone in the living room.

Guilt washed over her as she hurriedly threw on her nightgown and opened the door.

Leia, rubbing her sleepy eyes, walked in and looked up with her innocent little face. "Mommy, did you sleep with Marcus last night?"

Marcus and Josie exchanged a glance, feeling suddenly awkward.

Josie quickly explained, "No, no! Mommy just came to Marcus' room. I was with you the whole time before that."

As she spoke, Josie felt a pang of guilt.

But there was no way she could tell her daughter what had really happened last night.

"Marcus, did you bully my mommy? Her skin is all red..." Leia said innocently.
