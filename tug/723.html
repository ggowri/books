"No, no. That's not what I meant. I just feel awkward about taking so much money from you."

Natalie took a deep breath and continued, "To be honest, I was pleased that you transferred 20 billion dollars to me. It wasn't about the sum of money, but the love for me that the gesture represented.

"But I don't want your money. I'm eager to demonstrate that I'm with you not for financial reasons but because I genuinely have deep feelings for you."

Meanwhile, Natalie pulled out her cell phone and intended to transfer the 20 billion dollars back to Marcus.

He stopped her and said, "You don't have to regard the 20-billion-dollar transfer as a gift anymore."

Marcus smiled, "Regard it as the startup capital for your business."

Natalie's beautiful eyes became bright, and she asked, "How do you know I've always planned to start a business?"

Before Natalie could get a word in, he waved his hand and said, "I'm just joking. To be honest, I think it's rather meaningless for you to keep on being the manager of that jewelry store, so I want you to venture into your own business within this industry.

It will cost about 10 billion dollars to acquire these eight stores along with all of their business operations. The remaining 10 billion dollars will be used as working capital and the normal operating expenses of the eight stores. What do you think? Are you confident about developing this brand?"

But because my family was so poor that I couldn't scrape together the startup funds, I had no choice but to tuck that dream away in my heart."

Marcus promptly nodded at her and stated, "Well, I'll instruct someone to acquire those eight stores and purchase the exclusive authorization of this brand in Uswad. Starting from now, you'll be the exclusive agent of this brand in Uswad!"

Only the influential figures in Uswad were capable of achieving such a feat.

Previously, when he was in the process of acquiring the four major emerging industry groups in Kingston, which had equity valued at 350 billion dollars, it was completed in just over an hour.

After taking a deep breath, Natalie asked, "Marcus, I really want to know what your current identity is."

Marcus smiled nonchalantly, "I'm still just the same old me. What other identity could I possibly have?"

Natalie bit her lip. Realizing that he didn't want to reveal too much, she didn't ask further.

"Natalie, what a coincidence! Why are you here, too?"

The woman was tall and gorgeous. Though not as luxurious as his clothes, hers were pretty exquisite and decent.

"Hadley, when did you return to Solomia?"

At the sight of Hadley Meyer, Natalie was obviously stunned.

"I'm on annual leave, so I went back to Solomia to visit my parents and brought my boyfriend back with me," Hadley smiled.

Hadley said, "It's not that exaggerated. Our basic salary is the same, and I just have a few days of annual leave."
