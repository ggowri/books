"Pitiful Uswadian people, die!"

This grab, which appeared ordinary, contained his most powerful ninja technique.

It seemed like a simple move, but it was infused with mental power!

However, even so, Marcus didn't show any intention of dodging.

He simply allowed the ninja's massive hand to close around his throat.

This only deepened the cold smile on the ninja's face, as he thought Marcus had given up any hope of survival.

A sense of dread flooded his mind.

His body seemed to be locked in place by an invisible force.

Energy poured out of him, causing his body to tremble violently and his complexion to turn pale.

His eyes were filled with disbelief, and a voice of despair escaped his lips.

Marcus' Devouring Power was too terrifying. In less than two minutes, the ninja stopped fighting.

His eyes grew dull, losing all their original sharpness.

His skin withered rapidly, shrinking like that of a dried corpse. Eventually, he became nothing more than a mummified body.

Marcus casually tossed the corpse to the ground, then collected the Spatial Rings from all the ninjas.

The reason he allowed the ninja to grip his throat was that he had been waiting for just this moment to trap the ninja and then use his Devouring Power to absorb the energy within him, boosting his own strength.

Once that was done, Marcus ignited a flame in the palm of his hand, quickly covering the bodies of the ninjas and reducing them to ash.

After handling everything, he strode back into Bayside Villa.

No one knew it had been there, nor could anyone identify who it was.

Had Marcus been present, he would have been astonished.

The "cage" implied confinement and imprisonment.

This world, after all, was meant to be free and open, but that person referred to it as a cage.

The words were deeply thought-provoking.

They made people feel that the world they lived in might be merely a cage built by someone else.

Such a rapid pace of advancement was unheard of in the entire world of Ancient Martial Arts in Uswad. From ancient times to the present, no one has ever matched it. He was a true anomaly.

After breaking through, Marcus' overall strength surged even further.

His combat ability was now much stronger than before.

He spent the remaining time practicing the Shadow Step, the Heavenly Circle Diagram, and other techniques.

After days of continuous training, Marcus felt that his Wizard level was on the verge of breaking through to Stage Five.

Once he reached Stage Five, his power would undergo another leap.
