The Timeless Warrior's Grimoire alone was much better than this man's.

Endless flames rose up around him. In an instant, he shot several Hand Strikes towards Marcus.

The magic light contained in each Hand Strike was extremely strong, and there was also a terrifying Fire Attribute Power fused in it.

Wherever it passed, it seemed that everything would be melted.

In just a moment, Marcus' body was wrapped in the endless flames. If it were someone else, they would definitely be burned to ashes by the flames.

Facing this terrifying flame, Marcus was unmoved, and his body was covered by a faint light.

This light was like an invisible armor that completely protected Marcus' body.

Even though the flames were extremely fierce, they still couldn't hurt Marcus at all.

The flames he just released were equivalent to the strongest power of his attack.

The Fire Attribute Power lingering on his body was obviously stronger than before, containing an extremely terrifying destructive power.

He smashed out another terrifying Fist Ray.

This fist also seemed to have no power at all, but it immediately completely enveloped the man's body.
