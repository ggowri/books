Chad snapped out of it and quickly followed him.

Marcus led Chad to the Aston Martin parked in the alley, pressed the unlock button, and as the headlights flashed on, Chad's eyes nearly popped out of his head.

"Marcus, when did you get so rich? Wait, this has to be a rental, right? Or are you driving this for someone else?"

To him, Marcus had always been the poorest kid in their class back in school.

"Five or six million... only?" Chad swallowed hard.

Chad raised an eyebrow. "Sure, but can you at least let me drive a car like this?"

"Is that all you want? You'd be satisfied with just a five or six million car? If you really want it, I'll give it to you," Marcus said, tossing him the car keys.

He and Marcus had both dreamed of owning a supercar when they were younger, but reality had crushed those dreams as they got older.

"Hop in. Let's grab a drink somewhere. You drive," Marcus said, noticing the gleam of admiration in Chad's eyes.

"Now this is what I'm talking about! The engine sounds amazing!" Chad grinned, his face lighting up. He looked more alive than ever.

"Stop admiring it and drive already," Marcus reminded him.

With the improved traffic, the roads weren't as packed as usual, and Chad, who had some experience with a professional driving service, quickly adapted to the Aston Martin.
