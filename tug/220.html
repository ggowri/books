While they were talking, several people had come to Marcus.

Marcus recognized Baron next to Blake at a glance.

Baron smiled and nodded to Marcus, then continued to say to Blake, "Even my master has to respect Mr. Fitzgerald. His strength is beyond our imagination. I don't want the Axe Gang to have a conflict with Mr. Fitzgerald. If there is any misunderstanding, just solve it together. Mr. Brooks, if you feel that this is not acceptable, then vent your anger on me!"

Although Baron knew that Marcus was very powerful, he only learned about Marcus' power through his master's description and had never witnessed it.

Therefore, he was already loyal to dissuade Blake when Blake was determined to deal with Marcus.

However, this sentence failed to calm the anger of Blake but provoked him. "Baron, do you mean I need to be respectful to this person like your master does?

"You are a man who has been in the underground world for more than 20 years. How can you still be so timid? I overestimated you!"

As he spoke, Blake wanted to talk to Marcus, but Baron stopped him again. "You must not do that. Listen to me, Mr. Fitzgerald is indeed very strong. As the saying goes, when two tigers fight, one will be injured. Since we are all gangsters, why don't we sit down and talk calmly?"

"Stop talking. If you continue, just get out of the Axe Gang right now. I don't need someone like you!"

Blake became even angrier.

He couldn't understand why a young man in his early 20s could be praised so much by Baron!

Even the other masters cast puzzled looks at Baron.

In their impression, Baron was decisive in his actions, was ruthless in killing, and was a very cruel person.

Why was Baron scared when he met this guy who looked to be in his early 20s?

"Mr. Brooks, what is the relationship between this guy and Baron? Could he be Baron's illegitimate child? Otherwise, why would Baron protect him so much?"

A master joked.

The others burst into laughter.

Blake looked at Baron and said, "Baron, didn't you say this guy was very powerful? Today I will let you see how your so-called powerful person is trampled under my feet!"

As he said that, Blake looked at Marcus and said, "You are so brave to beat up my nephew. However, have you considered the consequences before coming?"

Marcus looked at Blake and said, "Stop talking nonsense. I was the one who beat up your nephew. If you want revenge, just do it."

As soon as he said this, the other members of the Axe Gang became angry, and some even tried to pull out their guns.

After all, the Axe Gang was a well-known gang in this area.

They had never been provoked like this before.

Blake waved his hands at these people, signaling them to put down their guns, and then said to Marcus, "This is the first time I have seen such an arrogant young man like you. I admire your courage. Don't worry, I won't let them shoot. That is not what a man should do. I will beat you by myself and let you know the price of being arrogant!"

As he spoke, veins bulged all over Blake's body and he punched Marcus in the face.

The power of this punch was extremely terrifying. Not only was his physical strength great, but it also contained Numinous Energy.

It could be seen that Blake had been practicing Ancient Martial Art for many years.

It was just that he hadn't found the right method, so he hadn't been able to get started.

The power of this punch was almost enough to compete with a person at Stage One of Ancient Martial Art.

Unfortunately, he met Marcus.

In Marcus' eyes, Blake's attack was powerless.

He punched out casually, not even using one out of ten of his strength.

Blake shook and went backward.

Blake looked gloomy and looked at Marcus deeply, saying, "I didn't expect that you are also a warrior. No wonder you dared to force your way into the Axe Gang!"

He instantly realized that Marcus was not as simple as he thought.

As he spoke, he took another step forward, roared, and used his unique Tiger Fist to smash Marcus' heart.

This punch was like a tiger and was extremely powerful. Its power alone was several times stronger than the previous punch. It reached Marcus' body in the blink of an eye!
