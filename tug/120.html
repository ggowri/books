"Celestial Reversal!"

At the very moment the destructive force reached Marcus, he roared and swiftly unleashed Celestial Reversal!

In an instant, Ricardo's attack was reflected back at him.

His face twisted in horror as he desperately tried to dodge, but the attack was too close and completely caught him off guard, swallowing him whole!

"Impossible! How is this possible? How can you reflect my attack?"

Ricardo's voice was filled with hopelessness as he screamed.

Marcus smirked coldly and said, "You don't deserve to know. Just die peacefully."

As soon as the words left his mouth, a deafening explosion echoed.

Ricardo's body was torn apart by the destructive force, reduced to fragments scattered across the ground.

Celestial Reversal was an ability Marcus had gained after breaking through Stage Two of the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire.

Over time, Marcus had honed the skill of Celestial Reversal to perfection through rigorous training.

When fully activated, Celestial Reversal could reflect attacks with several times their original power.

Although Ricardo was at the peak of Ancient Martial Stage Three, his attack became his own death knell under the power of Celestial Reversal.

Watching Ricardo die on the spot, Marcus collapsed to the ground, his body completely drained of energy.

He had already sustained significant injuries during the fight.

Fully deploying Celestial Reversal had taken a serious toll on his energy reserves.

At that moment, he lay on the ground, gasping for air, his forehead drenched in cold sweat, his face pale.

Quickly retrieving a medicinal herb from his Spatial Ring, he swallowed it, and his face regained a faint trace of color.

"That battle was way too close. If it weren't for my magic crystal and Celestial Reversal, I would've never stood a chance against Ricardo!"

Marcus thought to himself, his heart still trembling uncontrollably.

He was well aware that he still had a significant gap to close to match an Ancient Martial Stage Three expert.

Even with his dual training in magic and martial arts, it wasn't enough to bridge the enormous gap in levels.

This world was full of hidden experts, including those from Ancient Martial Art Sects and Wizard Guilds.

There were countless other masters from various professions.

For instance, orcs, ghost cultivators, beastmasters, physical enhancers, array masters, metahumans, and even cyborgs with surgical implants.

These individuals often possessed abilities beyond the imagination of ordinary people.

In every field, there were beings of extraordinary power.

Although Marcus had defeated Ricardo, he dared not let it go to his head.

This battle had only deepened his awareness of his own insignificance.

He knew he needed to improve his strength as quickly as possible to secure his survival when faced with future crises.

At the same time, Ricardo's attack confirmed something for Marcus—his second brother was now fully aware of him and was attempting to eliminate him through Ricardo's hands.

After resting for a while, Marcus got up and retrieved Ricardo's Spatial Ring.

As the head of the Brown family in Kingston, Ricardo was undoubtedly incredibly wealthy.

Marcus was eager to see what treasures might be hidden in Ricardo's Spatial Ring.

Returning to Sunshine Estates, Josie was still resting as she had locked her door earlier.

Taking advantage of the night, Marcus climbed to the fifth floor by hand and entered the room through the window.

Josie didn't notice that Marcus had been out earlier.

Marcus didn't disturb her.

He opened Ricardo's Spatial Ring and quickly inventoried its contents.

Besides hundreds of billions in cash, Ricardo's personal belongings were extraordinarily plentiful.

They included martial manuals, medicinal herbs, and several Ancient Martial Art artifacts.

Among the items was a notebook.

Curious, Marcus opened it to find a detailed list of all the businesses under the Brown family, as well as Ricardo's significant milestones in his Ancient Martial cultivation.

It even included some secrets of the Ancient Martial world.

Marcus skimmed through the contents, deciding to review them more thoroughly later.

His focus shifted to the Brown family's business empire.

It took Marcus a full ten minutes to finish reading the list, leaving him deeply shocked.

The Brown family of Kingston truly lived up to its reputation as one of Uswad's top families, with industries sprawling like an empire.

Their holdings were even more vast than Marcus had imagined, a true commercial empire.

Compared to the Brown family, the Golden Group under Marcus's control seemed like a mere drop in the ocean.

Within the list were several businesses that caught Marcus's attention. He set his sights on a few cutting-edge tech companies.

These companies were part of emerging industries with high technological value, and because there were few investors in this field, their profits were remarkably lucrative.

With Ricardo gone, the Brown family in Kingston was bound to collapse, plunging into a state of chaos for a considerable period.

Marcus planned to take over some of the Brown family's core businesses.

This would allow him to rapidly expand his own business empire.

With this idea in mind, Marcus began calculating. At present, he only had about 10 billion dollars at his disposal.

That amount was far from enough to acquire the Brown family's key businesses.

Ricardo's demise, however, had conveniently delivered a windfall.

After tallying up Ricardo's Spatial Ring, Marcus found over 50 billion dollars inside.

Converted to Uswad currency, it amounted to 350 billion dollars—more than enough to acquire several sizable companies.

Thinking about this gave Marcus a small boost of confidence.

Marcus extracted higher-grade medicinal herbs from Ricardo's Spatial Ring, ground them into powder, and consumed them.

Using the healing techniques from the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire, he began to recover from his injuries.
