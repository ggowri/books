Raphael narrowed his eyes slightly as he watched Marcus step forward.

His body seemed to transform into countless afterimages, and in the blink of an eye, he appeared in front of the high-ranking member of the Shark Tribe who had spoken before.

With a powerful punch, he struck toward the opponent's head.

The man looked horrified, as Marcus' speed was so fast that he couldn't react in time.

In a panic, he quickly raised his hand to defend.

A sharp crack of bone echoed through the air, and his arm was nothing under Marcus' powerful punch.

It was immediately shattered, yet Marcus' punch, unstoppable like a roaring tide, descended without any hindrance, directly landing on his head.

A deafening explosion rang out as the head of the high-ranking member of the Shark Tribe was crushed to pieces by the force of the blow.

His body plummeted from the void, and he died on the spot.

Many people couldn't help but gasp in shock. They never expected that, despite the overwhelming number of Shark Tribe members surrounding him, Marcus would show no signs of retreat, boldly taking the initiative to face them head-on.

And he killed one person directly.

It was nothing short of a direct provocation to the Shark Tribe.

"Kill him!" Rusell shouted through gritted teeth.

His body possessed an incredibly terrifying physical strength.

Before he could even finish speaking, he swung his fist and attacked Marcus madly.

The punch carried an overwhelming force.

In order to pose a significant threat to Marcus, Rusell had even drained his latent power.

As a result, the power of the punch surged dramatically, becoming countless times stronger than his previous attacks. In the blink of an eye, it came crashing down.

"How ignorant!"

Marcus sneered, completely disregarding Rusell's attack.

He, too, threw a punch, and the two Fist Rays collided in midair.

It was still that seemingly light punch, but the power behind it seemed even more terrifying than the one that had crippled Micah.

The next moment, everyone heard a dreadful rumbling sound, as the force of destruction spread out in all directions.

Rusell instantly felt the horror of Marcus' punch.

Under that powerful strike, his body trembled uncontrollably, and the bones in his arm collapsed bit by bit. The overwhelming shockwave traveled up his arm and spread through his body, leaving him with an indescribable sense of fear.

The next instant, a thunderous, resonating sound followed.

Marcus' punch, intending to annihilate all, landed upon Rusell's body.

The latter screamed in agony, unable to resist.

His massive body was blasted away like a cannonball.

While his body was still flying in midair, he was completely occupied by the destructive force, then exploded, turning into a ball of blood mist, and finally merged into the sea.

The scene left everyone in a stunned silence.

Rusell had exhausted his latent power to fight Marcus, hoping to gain the upper hand in their clash.

Yet, in the face of Marcus' overwhelming power, his every move was in vain.

It was still the same seemingly light punch, but it contained endless killing intent.

With just one punch, Rusell was killed.

The shock was beyond words, causing many to feel a deep, unsettling tremor in their hearts.

The remaining members of the Shark Tribe, in particular, looked at Marcus with fear in their eyes.

A high-ranking member from the Shark Tribe said in terror, "Our chief has been crippled, the vice chief killed... How can we possibly be a match for him?"

Many people's gazes froze slightly, and they lost all confidence.

Their two most powerful leaders had been easily crushed by Marcus, rendering them incapable of fighting back.

The rest of them were nothing more than cannon fodder.
