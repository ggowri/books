As Marcus counted to three, the doors to the operating room suddenly swung open!

Jade walked out quickly, pulling off her mask to reveal her strikingly beautiful face.

She took a deep breath, looking utterly exhausted.

"Haha, look at her sighing like that! The surgery must have failed!"

"Exactly! Even Jayden couldn't pull this off, let alone someone like Jade."

"Looks like we're in for some entertainment!"

The crowd buzzed with chatter, their eyes full of mockery as they looked at Jade.

Secretly, they couldn't wait to see Marcus kneel and bark like a dog.

"Jade, go ahead and keep your promise—resign already!" Freya sneered, her face smug with satisfaction.

Seeing Jade step out, Freya wasted no time, sneering triumphantly.

Jayden chimed in, "Don't worry, Freya. If she doesn't resign, I'll make sure she's fired!"

Jade gave them a peculiar look. "What makes you think I should resign? Who told you the surgery failed?"

At her words, both Freya and Jayden froze momentarily before smirking sarcastically.

They thought they must've misheard her.

Freya scoffed, "Don't tell me the surgery was a success. That's simply impossible! I suggest you stop lying, or you'll be exposed soon enough!"

Jade chuckled coldly. "Why don't you go check for yourself instead of asking me?"

Freya and Jayden exchanged a glance and hurried into the operating room.

They found the patient lying calmly on the bed, both legs wrapped in pristine white bandages.

Since orthopedic surgeries couldn't be visually assessed, Jayden immediately called for diagnostic equipment.

As the examination progressed, his expression shifted from disdain to utter shock.

"The recovery is complete... It's actually complete! How is this possible? How did Jade manage to do this?" he stammered.

Jayden's voice trembled as he continued examining the patient.

His face was etched with disbelief as he rubbed his eyes involuntarily.

Yet the results on the screen were undeniable.

Freya heard Jayden's words, and her face went blank.

She hastily grabbed the device to check for herself, and she was dumbfounded!

"Jade... actually succeeded? How is this even possible?"

At that moment, Freya felt like she must be dreaming.

This was a nearly impossible surgery, yet Jade had performed it flawlessly.

To her, it was more shocking than seeing the sun rise in the west.

"Jayden, how did Jade do this? Based on her skills, this surgery should've been impossible," Freya asked.

Jayden shook his head. "I'm not entirely sure, but my guess is that Jade must have been guided by an expert.

"The incision, stitching, anti-inflammation, and debridement techniques were flawless. There's no way Jade did this on her own.

"Even I can't compare!"

Freya muttered to herself, "An expert... Who could have been guiding Jade?"

Freya couldn't figure it out and left the operating room with Jayden.

"Jayden, Freya, what did you see? Was the surgery a success?" Jade asked.

Jayden looked embarrassed but had no choice but to nod and admit the surgery was a success.

Freya, unwilling to concede, said, "Jade, sure, the surgery succeeded, but it's clearly not your work. Someone must have guided you, so it doesn't count!

"I suggest you resign before Jayden fires you!"

Marcus chuckled coldly. "Shouldn't you be the one resigning? How dare you tell Jade to quit?"

At Marcus' words, Freya's expression immediately soured.

"Who do you think you are? What gives you the right to demand my resignation? I'm a certified doctor at Solomia Hospital. You have no authority to question me!" Freya snapped coldly.

She glared icily, conveniently ignoring the bet they had made earlier.

"Didn't we just make a bet? You said you'd resign if Jade succeeded. Why are you going back on your word so quickly?" Marcus questioned.

Freya's eyes darted nervously, and she put her hands on her hips. "When did I make a bet with you? Do you have proof? Besides, it was just verbal—it's not legally binding. I still say Jade should be the one to resign!"

Freya's ability to twist the truth was impressive.

However, the other doctors and nurses watching weren't surprised—they had seen her manipulation skills before.
