But Marcus' big hands were like iron clamps. No matter how hard Blake tried, he couldn't break free at all!

"Kneel!"

Although the sound was not loud, it contained a special energy fluctuation.

That was the spiritual power of a warrior. The spiritual power instantly invaded the mind of Blake.

Because the gap between their power was too big.

Blake instantly felt an irresistible force.

In front of Marcus, he felt so small and humble, and he was filled with fear from the bottom of his soul.

The energy manifestation!

Even Blake was easily subdued by Marcus, so they must be nothing to Marcus.
