Even though Marcus was given a lot of so-called waste raw crystals, he could pick one of the best crystals!

In an instant, the internal structure of all the raw crystals appeared in his sight without any barrier.

Without any hesitation, Marcus quickly selected ten of them within half a minute.

His speed stunned Cooke and Vanessa.

It also indicated that he was indeed equipped with excellent skills in gambling on stones since he could tell they were all waste raw crystals at one glance.

That was why he gave up.

"Are you sure it's your final decision?" Vanessa confirmed with Marcus and looked more confident than before.

"I'm sure." Marcus nodded faintly.

Through the X-ray vision, he indeed noticed the disparity of these raw crystals between the ones in the front yard.

But it didn't mean there was no fine crystal.

These ten raw crystals he just selected would bring him energy crystals with five times more energy than the previous one, and it was quite a good deal to get it with ten billion dollars.

Next, with the help of two stone cutters, the first raw crystal was cut.

As the blue light bloomed, a strong energy fluctuation appeared.

The rest nine would only bring him a miserable loss.

However, things didn't go as she expected.

Then, the stone cutters opened all the rest raw crystals, which all contained at least one fist-sized energy crystal with rather superior quality.

If her suspicion was true, she would suffer perhaps the greatest loss in her life!

In less than half an hour, all the ninety raw crystals were cut.

Then, she looked at Marcus and asked, "May I ask how you managed to do that?"

He chuckled, "Maybe just like what you said. I'm lucky."

Vanessa stamped her feet with anger.

She knew he wouldn't tell her but couldn't do anything with it.

Marcus didn't put forward any conditions to her, and she made all the rules from the beginning.

So, she couldn't make him tell her the secret behind his success.

In addition to Vanessa, Cooke and the stone cutters were all stunned.

He also responded to him with a smile and a nod.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, you're amazing! I can't admire you more!" Cooke said with a trembling voice.

If people thought Marcus merely depended on his luck to gain a raw crystal worth twenty billion dollars with sixty million dollars, they would know that it had nothing to do with luck now.

He accurately picked the only ten raw crystals with superior quality out of all these wastes.

His accuracy reached one hundred percent!

He might be the first one to have such prowess at gambling on stones in Southern Market ever!
