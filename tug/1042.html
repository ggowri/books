"You dare offend Mr. Death? I will kill you today!"

As he spoke, Miles began to punch and kick Selena.

Based on what he had done to Marcus earlier, it was clear that Marcus would never forgive him.

He hadn't expected Selena to add fuel to the fire.

At that moment, he almost wanted to kill her himself.

Selena screamed in pain, never imagining that Miles would be so brutal toward her.

After taking out his anger on Selena, Miles knelt in front of Marcus again, continuing to beg.

However, Marcus didn't even bother to look at him. He said, "You've worked for Royal Entertainment for many years, but from what I know, you haven't contributed anything. Instead, you've been like a leech, embezzling funds, accepting bribes, and using your position for personal gain.

"Royal Entertainment doesn't need someone like you. And on top of that, you tried to kill me. That's something that can never be forgiven!"

With that, Marcus turned his back, refusing to even glance at Miles.

Miles fell into utter despair. He knew that today he had hit a dead end.

His ignorance and arrogance had led him to offend the wrong person.

But as he thought about his inevitable end, anger and frustration consumed him.

That anger quickly turned into hatred.

The next moment, Miles suddenly stood up and grabbed a handgun from one of the bodyguards, attempting to shoot Marcus.

Miles was certainly not a man that went down without a fight.

Since Marcus had taken everything from him, he would rather die with Marcus than live in disgrace.

But before he could pull the trigger, another gunshot rang out.

A sniper's bullet pierced through Miles' temple.

With a powerful impact, his head exploded, and the remnants of his skull and flesh scattered everywhere.

The smell of blood immediately filled the air, sending many in the room retreating in fear, their faces pale.

No one had expected that the once-glorious Executive Vice President of Royal Entertainment, Miles, would meet such a brutal end, shot in the head by a sniper's rifle.

It was a horrific, tragic death.

Selena was so terrified that her face turned pale, especially when she saw Miles' headless corpse. She let out a scream and curled up on the ground, feeling a hot stream surge from her groin. She was so frightened that she actually wet herself!

"You guys clean it up. I'm leaving."

Marcus said, uninterested in Selena's plight, as he spoke to Evander.

Evander, equally shocked by the bloody scene, managed to force a smile and nodded at Marcus.

"By the way, the award for Best Female Singer of the Year goes to Chloe."

Before leaving, Marcus reminded Evander.

This brought a warm feeling to Chloe's heart, and she hurriedly followed him, saying, "Wait for me. I'll go with you."

Marcus glanced at Chloe and teased, "You're the Best Female Singer of the Year. Don't you want to stay and accept your award?"

Chloe blushed and replied, "Those awards are just titles. The truly remarkable people, like you, know when to strike and when to hold back."

After witnessing what just happened, Chloe's mindset seemed to change in an instant.

She became much more mature.

Before this, she had been extremely determined to win the award for Best Female Singer of the Year.

She had even been willing to confront Miles head-on for it.

But after witnessing what Marcus had done, Chloe suddenly saw through many things.

She no longer cared as much about such hollow titles.

The truly powerful don't care too much about fame. They focus on what's inside.

If someone is strong enough, even without those so-called titles, they can still stand out and make a name for themselves.
