From the moment the fight started, not a single one of the fifty attackers had even touched the hem of Marcus' clothes. In less than ten seconds, seven or eight men had already been taken out.

No one had ever seen someone fight like this before. It was like a scene straight out of an action movie, each movement precise, every strike landing with devastating force.

One man, alone, has defeated fifty armed fighters. It's almost impossible to believe. And yet, it has happened right in front of our eyes.

Marcus easily took care of all these people. He hasn't been reckless. He has been confident. Not the kind of false bravado that comes from arrogance, but real, unwavering confidence in his own strength. He has known from the start that Garrick and his men aren't a threat to him.
