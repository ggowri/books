Her hair was disheveled, and her skin was covered with scars. She was wearing tattered clothes and looked listless.

It was Marcus' ex-wife, Abigail!

Abigail followed a group of slaves and kept kowtowing to Marcus to express his gratitude.

After bowing their heads, many slaves ran out of the rainforest, wanting to leave this place as soon as possible.

Abigail also stood up from the ground, but just as she was about to leave, she accidentally saw Marcus.

She trembled and was completely stunned.

Her expression was incredulous, at the same time a little reluctant and stubborn.

"Marcus... Why did you come to the southwest?"

Abigail asked in a hoarse voice.

The middle-aged man and other people in the Red River Mines were stunned.

They all looked at Marcus.

Marcus remained calm and did not show any signs of disturbance.

He said, "I'm here on business."

"They said that you saved us. Is that true?"

Abigail asked with a trembling voice.

Ever since she met Marcus at Kingston's Chaumet shop, she was abandoned and came to the southwest region to develop by chance.

At the beginning, she made a small profit and was doing very well.

But later, she was targeted by human traffickers and sold to the Red River Mines as a slave.

During this period, Abigail endured pain and torture. She was so desperate that she almost chose to commit suicide several times.

Although it was just over a month, the pain had changed her a lot.

Abigail was once a prominent figure in Solomia and the young lady of the Winchester family.

Although her assets were not as good as those top figures, she lived a luxurious and wealthy life.

But everything changed since Abigail cheated on Felix and divorced Marcus.

She became penniless and was deserted by everyone.

Jane had a passionate relationship with Marcus. And even Natalia cut off ties with Abigail.

Abigail thought that with her intelligence and beautiful looks, she would be able to change her situation sooner or later.

But she didn't.

What Abigail didn't expect was that her most miserable and desperate appearance was completely presented in front of Marcus.

This dealt a heavy blow to her self-esteem.

"All the slaves here should thank this gentleman."

The middle-aged man took the initiative to speak.

Abigail looked at the corpse on the ground with mixed feelings.

Combining the sound of gunfire and alarms, she knew what had happened here before.

But in her opinion, this matter was related to Marcus, but he couldn't do it alone.

Abigail thought Marcus knew someone in the military.

It was because the Red River Mines was heavily guarded, and the boss' men were all armed with heavy weapons.

Abigail didn't think Marcus had the ability to destroy the Red River Mines.

However, even so, Abigail felt unbalanced.

She thought, "Why am I getting worse while Marcus is getting better?

Why?

They say God is fair, but I didn't feel it."

Looking at Marcus' arrogant look and thinking about her current situation, Abigail immediately made a decision.

She walked quickly to Marcus and begged painfully, "Marcus, I beg you. Please come back to me, okay? I'm sorry. You still love me, right?

"As long as you agree to me, I can sleep with you tonight. You always wanted it before, and now you can get what you want."

Hearing what Abigail said, Marcus showed a strange look and felt nauseous.

He pushed Abigail away and said, "I didn't expect you to still be so self-righteous.

"Based on what you did to me before, I could have killed you. The reason I didn't do that was entirely because of Natalia. Understand?

"Now in my heart, you are just Natalia's sister. We will never have anything to do with each other again in this life!"

Abigail was thrown away with tears. "No, Marcus. I know you still love me.

"Even if you don't want to admit it, you can't stand by, right?"

The vast rainforest was full of various dangers. Abigail was afraid that she would not be able to get out.

"Fend for yourself!"

Marcus turned around and didn't look at Abigail anymore.
