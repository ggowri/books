The level of power Marcus displayed left many people gasping in disbelief.

Maximilian, Fiona, and Alistair looked visibly uncomfortable.

None of them had expected Marcus to be so good in a fight.

"I didn't expect this guy to be such a powerhouse. No wonder he's been acting so cocky!"

"So what if he can fight? I know this karaoke bar is protected by the Serpent's Coil. If the owner tells them about this, just imagine what will happen to him."

"Yeah, the Serpent's Coil is the biggest underground group in Solomia. Word is every member's got a weapon on them. No matter how tough this guy is, once the Serpent's Coil shows up, he's in for a world of hurt!"

The crowd murmured nervously.

Seeing Marcus take down the security guards, they knew he had just made a bigger mess for himself.

Alistair turned to Maximilian. "Don't worry, Mr. Lockwood. This guy laying hands on my men? This isn't over."

With that, Alistair turned his cold eyes to Marcus. "You realize you've just signed your own death sentence, right?"

Marcus, already losing patience, shot back, "Just say what you need to say. Quit beating around the bush. I don't have time to waste on your nonsense."

Alistair's eyes narrowed. He couldn't believe this kid was so bold, even when facing imminent danger.

"I'm sure you heard what people are saying. Our karaoke bar is under the protection of the Serpent's Coil. I'm calling them now, and you'll regret this."

As Alistair spoke, a smug smile spread across his face.

To him, bringing in the Serpent's Coil was about more than just forcing Marcus to kneel and beg.

"Mr. Ravenshaw, call in the Serpent's Coil now! This guy's way too cocky, he's got to pay for what he's done!"

Fiona chimed in, egging him on, practically eager to see Marcus put down by the gang.

Maximilian nodded in agreement.

With Maximilian's approval, Alistair pulled out his phone and made the call.

"Hello, Mr. Hayes? We've got a situation at the karaoke bar. Can you send some of your guys to handle it?"

"What the hell? Someone's causing trouble on our turf? They've got a death wish. I'm on my way, don't worry!"

Orson Hayes' voice crackled with anger through the phone.

Orson was a captain in the Serpent's Coil, running this entire area with a crew of dozens.

They collected protection money and handled other shady operations.

Alistair's karaoke bar paid a hefty protection fee to the Serpent's Coil every year.

So, anytime there was a problem, the Serpent's Coil had Alistair's back.

This gave Alistair the confidence to handle anything that came his way.

With the Serpent's Coil behind him, he knew there was no one who could touch him.

People from both the legal and illegal sides of things, no one dares to mess with the Serpent's Coil.

Even better, if he could handle this for Maximilian, he would lock down a steady business relationship with him.

Once the SiliconWave Technologies branch was up and running, Maximilian would be a regular at his karaoke bar.

That meant serious money for Alistair.

"You just wait, kid. Mr. Hayes is on his way with the Serpent's Coil. Let's see how cocky you are then."

After hanging up, Alistair turned to Marcus with a cold, mocking smile.

At Alistair's words, the crowd couldn't help but gasp in shock.

No one had expected Alistair to bring in Orson.

Those familiar with the area knew Orson was a brutal, no-nonsense leader who had recently been promoted within the Serpent's Coil.

His ruthlessness, decisiveness, and ability to get things done quickly had earned him the favor of Jalen, who made him a captain and put him in charge of this district.
