Everyone's eyes widened in disbelief.

They were utterly stunned, almost wondering if they were seeing things.

Stephen, the newly appointed president of Sync Group, had just bowed to the young man in front of everyone, addressing him with the utmost respect.

It was simply unbelievable.

Julius, Jake, and Abigail were completely taken aback, their minds racing with questions.

How was that possible?

What was going on?

Why was this happening?

Marcus glanced at Stephen and asked, "Is this guy your nephew?"

Stephen was sweating profusely. "Yes. He's young and foolish, and he offended you. Mr. Fitzgerald, please forgive him just this once!"

Marcus scoffed. "He wanted to break my legs and cut out my tongue. How can I simply let that go?"

Stephen wiped his brow, his expression turning serious.

Just then, Abigail stepped forward. "Mr. Stephen Andrade, he's my ex-husband. He was the chairman of Golden Group, but he was kicked out a long time ago. Don't be fooled by him; he's just someone else's pawn!"

Stephen kicked Abigail in the stomach. "Shut up, bitch! Who do you think you are? Get her out of here!"

Two bodyguards swiftly moved in, forcibly dragging Abigail away and restraining her.

"Mr. Stephen Andrade, you have to believe me! This guy is just a useless freeloader. He's lying to you!" Abigail shouted desperately.

Stephen turned coldly to the bodyguards. "Slap her to shut her up!"

The sound of slaps echoed as the bodyguards struck Abigail repeatedly, leaving her skin red and swollen.

"Uncle Stephen, Abigail is my girlfriend. How could you treat her like this?" Julius questioned, appearing anxious.

Furious, Stephen shot back, "You bastard, how dare you defend that woman? I can't believe I have such a foolish nephew!"

Under Marcus's penetrating gaze, Stephen cracked under pressure. "Mr. Fitzgerald, since he has offended you, he must face the consequences!"

He grabbed a baseball bat from a bodyguard and kicked Julius to the ground.

With a sickening crack, Julius's hand broke, and he screamed in agony.

A second swing shattered his left leg, followed by another blow that broke his right leg.

Gasps filled the room. They couldn't comprehend how Stephen could be so brutal to his own nephew.

They had heard how much he doted on Julius.

Yet now, he had to hit his nephew out of fear of Marcus.

This was enough to show Marcus's noble status.

Julius writhed in pain, his face pale, the excruciating pain causing him to tremble. Despair washed over him.

Stephen dropped the bat on the floor, feeling heartbroken for Julius. But facing Marcus, he had to put on a respectful face again. "Mr. Fitzgerald, are you satisfied? If not, I can kill him right now!"

Marcus glanced at the crumpled figure of Julius on the floor and replied, "Since he's your nephew, spare him."

Stephen then breathed a sigh of relief and quickly thanked Marcus.

Everyone stood in stunned silence. The atmosphere felt heavy and oppressive.

Just one word from Marcus had brought the esteemed president of Sync Group to his knees.

It was clear that Marcus was the real power here.

Marcus stood up and walked over to Josie, who met his gaze with shock.

Despite having witnessed his strength before, she never imagined that even someone like Stephen would show him such deference.

She couldn't help but wonder if Sync Group belonged to Marcus.

The thought only deepened her curiosity about his true identity.

She didn't know Marcus's identity.

But she realized that anyone who crossed him wouldn't end well.

As Josie lost herself in thought, Marcus took her hand and led her out from behind the counter.

Before leaving, he warned Stephen, "Keep your men under control. If this happens again, you'll be fired!"
