Silence enveloped the space as everyone froze, staring in disbelief at Micah, whose trembling body lay among the rubble.

They never imagined that someone as powerful as Micah, having reached Stage Nine, could be defeated by a single punch from Marcus, destroying his cultivation.

The sheer strength displayed was terrifying and frightened the Nuer Tribe.

Initially, they had considered capturing Marcus to prevent him from curing Nadia of Cursed Power.

Even when Raphael lost and Marcus stepped in, they doubted Marcus could match Micah.

Now, they realized their underestimation of Marcus' true power was foolish.

His calm demeanor towards the Nuer and Shark Tribes made sense.

He had never regarded them as a threat.

Nadia and Susan looked at Marcus with admiration, surprised not only by his medical skills but also his formidable strength.

Micah coughed from the ruins, staring at Marcus in disbelief. "How is it possible for you to be so strong? What are you?"

He couldn't believe that the Nuer tribe had a relationship with such a powerful human ancient warrior.

Marcus sneered, "Dealing with someone like you is easy. You don't pose much of a challenge."

He then revealed the cores of five undersea beasts he had killed and said, "These belong to your Shark Tribe, right?"

The Shark Tribe's faces turned grim at the sight of the glowing cores.

One voice trembled, "Saint Envoy! All five of our Saint Envoys were killed by you!"

This revelation shocked the Nuer Tribe, who understood the significance of the Saint Envoy to the Shark Tribe.

Curious, Marcus asked, "You mean these beasts were your Tribe's Saint Envoys?"

He had noticed during their fight that the undersea giants lacked spirit, indicating they were mere demonic beasts without human emotions or intelligence.

He couldn't understand how such a beast could be a Saint Envoy.

The Shark Tribe's expressions darkened, and they looked at Marcus with cold eyes. For a moment, no one answered his question.

Seeing Marcus was very curious, Raphael leaned in and whispered to him.

Marcus immediately couldn't help but burst into laughter.

According to Raphael, it turned out that the men of the Shark Tribe had no rights to mate or reproduce.

All the women in the tribe could only reproduce with Saint Envoys sent from a sacred force outside the Morvalis Ice Wall.

Then they gave birth to children. The boys became soldiers of the Shark Tribe, while the girls were responsible for the reproduction of the next generation.

However, the Saint Envoys, those giant sea beasts, arrived at irregular intervals.

They appeared at intervals of years, decades, or even centuries, leading women to endure endless waiting for the opportunity to reproduce.

Many waited until old age, but not for the arrival of the so-called Saint Envoy.

When Marcus arrived in this underwater realm, he coincided with the emergence of five Saint Envoys, whom he killed.

The people of the Shark Tribe were still unaware of this matter.

Upon seeing Marcus extract the cores from the five giant beasts, extreme anger appeared among the tribe members.

It had been thirty years since the last Saint Envoy had visited the Shark Tribe, meaning no births had occurred in the past thirty years.

This absence severely impacted the tribe's legacy.

The Shark Tribe's women had been hoping for the Saint Envoy's arrival, but their hopes were dashed by Marcus' actions, which eliminated their chance for prosperity.

Despite the tribe's customs, Marcus couldn't help but laugh at their peculiar reproductive rules.

He sneered, "That's really interesting. No wonder you are so bloodthirsty and brutal. You're all descendants of beasts!"

His words further incited the Shark Tribe's anger.

Micah snarled, "You killed five of our Saint Envoys, cutting off our chance to prosper. Holy God won't let you go!"

The Holy God was the entity controlling the Shark Tribe's fate, likely residing beyond the Morvalis Ice Wall.

Marcus realized that his actions had offended someone powerful.

But he didn't care about it. He had no intention of provoking these five beasts, but they attacked him.

He had only acted in self-defense against the five beasts.

If they sought revenge, he would respond with full force.

He always believed that strength could overcome any adversity.

"Mr. Graham, this man has ruined our chance to thrive. We must destroy him!" Rusell exclaimed.

His daughter was now thirty years old.

In the Shark Tribe, women typically lost fertility by forty.

In other words, the arrival of the Saint Envoy was his daughter's only chance to reproduce.

But this opportunity was ruined by Marcus.

It was equivalent to making him without any descendants. He couldn't bear such a blow.

The rest of the Shark Tribe echoed Rusell's sentiments.

Although Marcus destroyed Micah's cultivation with one strike, their numbers gave them confidence in overpowering him.

Micah nodded immediately. His hatred for Marcus was the strongest. Naturally, he hoped to kill Marcus.

A senior figure from the Shark Tribe roared, rallying the crowd to attack Marcus.

Destructive forces surged toward him from all directions.

Raphael frowned, contemplating sending the Nuer Tribe to assist Marcus.

However, Marcus waved him off, saying, "You don't need to intervene. Just watch me kill!"
