Marcus casually said, "Do to him as he intended to do to me."

Bob breathed a sigh of relief and left with his men.

As long as the freshly severed arms were well-preserved, there was a chance they could be reattached.

Although the function would not be comparable to normal arms, it would prevent complete disability.

At the headquarters of TreatMed Pharmaceuticals, Haley's father, Jared Brown, roared into the phone.

Even if it meant offending the Golden Group, he was willing to risk it!

Jane replied, "Then aren't you going to let me in?"

Marcus felt somewhat helpless, thinking, "Sometimes these women are really very inexplicable."
