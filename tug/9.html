The hall crackled with tension.

"Look at you, loser," Abigail sneered. "Keep running your mouth. I want to watch you dig your own grave."

Felix, cradling his broken arm, spat, "Mr. Wesley, this punk's got the nerve to disrespect you. He needs to die!"

Anthony smirked and gave the nod.

One of his men raised a gun, finger tightening on the trigger.

But before he could shoot, a hail of bullets turned him into Swiss cheese. He dropped like a stone, blood pooling around him.

The room fell silent in terror. Everyone turned toward the source of the gunfire.

At the entrance, a stunning young woman strode in confidently.

It was Diana.

Behind her, a dozen women in tactical gear marched in, each packing an MP7.

Anthony's face went slack. He looked up to find snipers on the second-floor balcony, their scopes locked on him.

"You're... you work for the Godfather... Diana..." he choked out.

"Mr. Fitzgerald!"

Diana was dressed in a form-fitting leather dress that accentuated her flawless figure.

She led her squad forward. As one, they bowed deep to Marcus.

Everyone's jaws hit the floor.

The Winchester's live-in son-in-law, claiming to be Golden Group's chairman, had an armed female hit squad bowing to him.

No one could make sense of it.

Abigail spat, "He's just a useless loser. There's no way he's someone important. This is ridiculous!"

She can't even recover from the utmost shock.

"Shut your mouth, wretch!"

Diana snapped, striking Abigail with a fierce slap. The blow sent Abigail stumbling, her cheek swelling instantly.

"How dare you!" Abigail shrieked.

"You asked for it!"

Another slap knocked one of Abigail's teeth loose.

Diana turned away, dismissing her like trash.

She addressed the crowd, "Listen up! Mr. Fitzgerald is Golden Group's new chairman. Anyone who dares disrespect him, die!"

The room went silent as it finally sank in - Marcus really was the new boss.

They were shell-shocked.

No one could believe it. The dependent son-in-law they'd all ridiculed as worthless was actually the new chairman of Golden Group.

The revelation left everyone in stunned disbelief.

Anthony's face darkened.

"You expect us to believe this bullshit just because you say so?" Anthony snarled through clenched teeth.

Diana didn't waste words. She simply tossed a folder into Anthony's lap - the official Golden Group equity transfer agreement.

Anthony's face drained of color as he skimmed the document quickly. He could tell the agreement was true.

Abigail and Felix felt like their world was crumbling.

"Still doubting my position, Mr. Wesley?" Marcus smirked coldly.

With snipers still trained on him, Anthony could only nod stiffly. "No... no doubts."

His fists clenched tightly in frustration.

"Good," Marcus's voice was steel. "Then as chairman, I'm stripping Felix of all positions at Golden Group."

"He's fired, effective immediately. From now on, Michelle Smith will take over as general manager."

Michelle stood frozen, joy slowly dawning on her face as the words sank in.

Felix nearly collapsed, looking desperately to Anthony for help.

"Even if you're the chairman, appointing a general manager so recklessly is not good." Anthony's lips quivered with rage. "Have you ever considered the future of the company?"

Marcus laughed coldly. "That's no longer your concern. Worry about yourself."

Anthony's face twisted with anger, his frustration boiling beneath the surface.

"Fine!" Anthony spat. "Do as you please. Let's see how long you last in that chair!"

He stormed out, his men trailing behind.

Then security escorted Abigail and Felix out.

Abigail looked lost.

What's going on? That humble Marcus ruined all my dream life just like that?

I was about to be the general manager's wife!

Why the man I'd despised, rejected, and called worthless was now the unreachable chairman of Golden Group?

Did I... make the wrong choice?

What have I done all these years...

It was all too late. Marcus and I would never be the same as before.

He will not forgive me...
