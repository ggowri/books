If he lost his position as the City Governer of Solomia, it wasn't just jail time that awaited him—it would drag his entire family down with him.

The weight of this realization crushed Hank's spirit. As he begged Marcus, the arrogance and pride he once had were nowhere to be found. All that remained was fear, desperation, and a desperate plea for forgiveness.

Dexter and his wife watched in stunned silence. They couldn't believe their eyes. The mighty Hank Foster, who had always been their figure of authority, their City Governer, was now kneeling before Marcus like a dog, begging for mercy.

Just moments ago, Hank had arrived with all the swagger of a high-ranking official. But now, he was a completely different person, his pride shattered.

Not just Dexter and the others, but even the neighbors who were witnessing this scene couldn't help but feel a shift in their perception.

Hank had always been an unreachable figure on TV or in the media—someone high above them, above reproach. But now, to see him groveling before Marcus, their image of him was completely upended.

Marcus gave Hank a cold glance, a mocking smile curling on his lips. "Weren't you the one who kept saying Uswad's laws didn't apply to you? Weren't you the one threatening me, promising to make me pay? And now, look at you, begging for mercy. Doesn't it feel ridiculous?"

The room was filled with a ripple of agreement, especially from the neighbors who knew the full story. They couldn't help but scoff at Hank, Dexter and his wife.

If it weren't for the wife's insistence on withholding Marcus's girlfriend Kiera's deposit, none of this would have happened.

Throughout the entire ordeal, the Dexter and his wife had been trying to use their influence and connections to suppress Marcus and Kiera.

If Marcus hadn't had such an important status, there was no doubt that the three people would have crushed him by now.

Many of the onlookers couldn't help but cast sarcastic glances at Hank, who had once seemed so untouchable in their eyes.

Now it was too late. His clash with Marcus had brought the full force of the city military down on him. Uswad's higher-ups had taken notice and had already collected detailed evidence of his wrongdoings.
