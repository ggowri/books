"Marcus, you're seriously going down there to fight them?" Chloe asked, worry creeping into her voice.

This mess started because of her—so why was Marcus acting like it was personal for him, too?

Against a Return Essence stage expert—Acquired Return Essence Realm, Fifth Stage—no street punk stood a chance.
