Marcus gave Maxine a reassuring look.

Then, he turned to Malcolm and said, "Since Maxine has sworn to marry no one but me, today, I have no reason to back down. I'm in this to the end!"

His words shocked the crowd.

The situation in the room had clearly shifted entirely in Malcolm's favor.

At this point, Marcus' involvement seemed not only futile but also highly dangerous. He was risking his life.

No one could understand why Marcus would step forward now.

Matthew, Owen, Hazel, and Stella exchanged grateful looks.

Although Marcus' intervention couldn't possibly change the outcome, his willingness to stand up for Maxine at such a crucial moment revealed his loyalty and deep sense of honor.

"Marcus, playing the hero now isn't the wisest choice. You'd better think carefully about the consequences you'll face!" Malcolm sneered and waved his hand.

At once, the dozens of black-clad men turned their guns on Marcus.

A suffocating tension filled the entire banquet hall.

"This fool has no idea what he's up against. Dad, why not just kill him now?" Arnold chuckled coldly.

If Malcolm took the position of family head, he would become the Burnett family's next successor!

Malcolm nodded in agreement, but before he could give the order, the crowd saw Marcus step forward, his body flashing like lightning until he was right in front of Malcolm.

Before Malcolm even had time to react, Marcus had already seized his throat.

"Drop your weapons, or I'll snap his neck right here!"

Marcus' eyes scanned the black-clad men, his voice cold and unwavering.

Everyone in the room stood frozen in shock.

They hadn't even seen how Marcus moved so quickly.

It was as if he had teleported.

As Malcolm felt Marcus' hand tightening around his throat, his face turned red from the pressure. He bellowed, "Let go of me! Let go of me now!"

While shouting, he subtly gave a signal to the warrior from the Astral Serpent Order.

The warrior slowly stood up from his seat and spoke, "I didn't expect to find someone like you here. From the looks of it, you're just a beginner. As long as I'm here, you won't have any chance to resist. If you want to live, release Mr. Malcolm Burnett, or I won't hesitate to make you regret it!"

"Release him? Let's see if you have the ability to make me!" Marcus scoffed.

The middle-aged warrior was at the Ancient Martial Stage Three, and from his aura, he was much stronger than Ricardo, whom Marcus had killed earlier.

However, after his recent cultivation, Marcus' strength had increased considerably.

His cultivation level now matched the middle-aged warrior's, and with the power of a Wizard and the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire, Marcus could easily crush him!

Marcus had intentionally suppressed his aura, causing the warrior to mistakenly believe he was a mere novice.

"You're truly impossible to reason with. In that case, I'll have to kill you!" The middle-aged warrior's eyes flared with anger.

Summoning his Numinous Energy, he instantly launched a devastating palm strike toward Marcus.

The palm strike was terrifying, blocking out the sky and seemingly engulfing Marcus in an instant!

The crowd gasped, their faces full of fear.

For most of them, this was the first time witnessing an actual warrior's attack, and they couldn't help but tremble with excitement and dread.

Such scenes were usually reserved for movies, and never in their wildest dreams did they think they'd see it in real life.

However, in the face of such a fearsome attack, Marcus stood unmoving, his expression calm.

He was still calm and showed no signs of evading.

The terrifying palm strike, accompanied by a chilling wind, descended upon him like a flash of lightning.

"Celestial Reversal!"

At the exact moment the strike was about to hit Marcus, he uttered these words.

Immediately, his body was enveloped in golden light, creating a barrier that separated him from the space around him.

In the blink of an eye, a powerful force of rebound shot out from Marcus, sending the warrior's palm strike hurtling back toward him!

The strike now rebounded and didn't lose its power. In fact, it became even more terrifying.

The middle-aged warrior's expression twisted in horror, and he tried to dodge, but it was too late!

A thunderous crash filled the air as the palm strike collided with him, sending him flying backward.

He smashed into the banquet hall's wall, leaving a massive impact.

The entire hall shook, and the wall cracked open, sending a wave of fear through everyone.
