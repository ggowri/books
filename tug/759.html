The person before them was the chief of the Shark Tribe, Micah Graham!

Marcus could see Micah's level surpassed Raphael's, equating to an expert at the peak at Ancient Martial Stage Eight.

In this isolated underwater realm, such a cultivation level was nearly unmatched.

Beside Micah stood the deputy chief of the Shark tribe, Rusell Bianchi, a larger and more imposing man whose level was slightly lower, comparable to Raphael's.

Additionally, four other powerful experts behind Micah boasted ranks of level 7 or above, significantly elevating their collective strength over that of the Nuer Tribe.

The faces of the Nuer Tribe members turned pale.

They had anticipated an attack from the Shark Tribe with the impending opening of the space-time gate.

But they hadn't expected such a swift and overwhelming mobilization of the enemy's strongest experts.

This situation felt like a life-and-death battle between the two tribes.

None from the Nuer Tribe surrendered. Instead, they wore expressions of grim determination.

"Who from the Nuer Tribe will step forward to die first?" Rusell taunted, his body suspended in the seawater, arrogance radiating from him as he surveyed the Nuer Tribe.

Elliott stepped up, wielding a heavy iron sword, and charged at Rusell, declaring, "You are so arrogant. Prepare to be destroyed!"

As he swung, a wave of destructive energy surged, and the sword light cut through the water with exceptional sharpness, reaching Rusell in an instant.

Rusell smirked disdainfully and countered with a powerful fist.

A Fist Ray surged forward, colliding with Elliott's sword light.

Elliott felt a violent tremor in his arm as an overwhelming force struck him.

His arm went numb, the sword flew from his grasp, and he was knocked back several feet before regaining his balance.

Rusell sneered, "With this little strength, are you qualified to fight me?"

With a swift step, he closed the distance to Elliott.

As members of the Shark Tribe, they had inherited the characteristics of the underwater sharks. Not only were they extremely fast, but their attack power was also extremely fierce.

In this deep sea area, they could better play their advantages.

He just took a casual step, and in the blink of an eye, his body appeared in front of Elliott.

He unleashed another formidable Fist Ray, resembling a terrifying battle shark, intent on engulfing Elliott.

Panicking, Elliott prepared to defend himself against the oncoming attack.

With a crack, Elliott was hurled into the air, blood spilling from his mouth as he crashed onto the underwater castle, shattering it.

"Father!" grief washed over Susan's face.

She never imagined the vast disparity between her father and Rusell.

"The Nuer Tribe has no fighters left. The deputy chieftain is so incompetent. Who can challenge me?" Rusell boasted arrogantly.

Raphael, with a sharp gaze, handed the scepter to Nadia and stepped forward. "If you want the Nuer Tribe to submit, you'll have to get through me first!"

Before he could finish, Micah emerged from his camp, sneering, "Your opponent is me."

Rusell retreated into his camp.

The showdown between the two tribes was imminent.

And tension thickened in the air.

Since the Shark Tribe's invasion, there had never been a confrontation between the chiefs of the two tribes.

Today, whoever wins or loses would determine the rise and fall of the tribe.

Raphael glanced at his tribe and waved.

In the distance, a massive figure approached—the roc, a legendary cetacean from ancient Uswad.

"Nadia, Susan, if I lose, take Mr. Fitzgerald on this creature. It has great magical power and can break through the confinement array!" Raphael instructed.

They were the Nuer Tribe's last hope.

Even in the face of defeat, Raphael needed to protect their future.

Sadness and anger filled Nadia and Susan. They were ready to fight for their tribe but nodded in agreement.

"Don't worry, none of you will escape today!" Micah declared coldly, launching an attack on Raphael.

The two chiefs clashed fiercely in the giant square.

As the strongest of the two tribes, this battle was naturally extremely fierce.

Everyone unleashed the strongest power they possessed without reservation.

Initially, Raphael held his ground against Micah, but gradually, his inferiority became apparent.

His realm was not as good as Micah's, and he was also cursed, which completely affected his performance.

In a collision, he was shocked and retreated continuously, with blood oozing from the corners of his mouth.

Seizing the moment, Micah sneered, "It's time to end this!"

He charged forward, releasing a wave of destructive energy infused with the Shark Tribe's bloodthirsty aura, aiming to deliver a final blow to Raphael.

As the attack engulfed Raphael, he desperately channeled his power to resist, but it was futile. The destructive force invaded him relentlessly.

"Mr. Stewart!" The Nuer Tribe watched in anguish, longing to retaliate against the Shark Tribe, but their strength was insufficient to intervene.

Seeing Raphael was about to die in battle, they were powerless to do anything.

"Go to hell!" Micah's eyes gleamed with satisfaction as he intensified his attack to finish Raphael.

Suddenly, a stream of destructive light descended from above, accompanied by a deafening explosion that shattered Micah's attack.

Horrified, Micah staggered back, and the onlookers were stunned by the unexpected turn of events, all eyes drawn to the source of the light as a young figure emerged, stepping forward.
