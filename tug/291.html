Under the powerful Devouring Power, Asher gradually ceased his struggles.

His eyes became dull, his skin lost its luster, and his body rapidly dried up, turning into a desiccated corpse.

Marcus panted heavily, horrified and feeling as if he had narrowly escaped death.

The sight of the desiccated corpse filled him with dread.

He had never imagined the Devouring Power he had just comprehended would be so potent.

Asher was an expert at the peak of Ancient Martial Stage Five, and his strength was much stronger than Marcus'.

Had he not used Celestial Reversal to seriously injure Asher, who had initially underestimated him and fought earnestly, he would have been dead.

Thanks to the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire, Marcus managed to turn defeat into victory, devouring Asher.

This made him think of the old man who had given him the pendant three years ago.

The Timeless Warrior's Grimoire was a cultivation manual he gained after the pendant merged with him.

He thought, "If such a simple gift has this kind of power, what strength does the old man possess?"

After absorbing Asher's energy, Marcus felt his meridians fill with power that didn't belong to him, creating an overwhelming sense of expansion in his body.

His abdomen was extremely full of energy.

He knew that with some refinement, this energy would significantly enhance his strength.

The battle had been witnessed by the remaining Ancient Martial Stage Four expert of the Astral Serpent Order.

He looked at Marcus in horror and attempted to escape.

Marcus, however, unleashed a lightning attack and killed him instantly.

With that, Marcus defeated all three Astral Serpent Order experts.

He took the Spatial Rings of the fallen and returned to Asher's corpse and recalled that Asher had mentioned wearing the Silkworm Armour.

This powerful defensive armor would be invaluable for his survival.

As he searched, he found the so-called Silkworm Armour.

On the surface, it looked just like an ordinary silk vest and was made of silk, just like the name.

It could be worn casually but transformed into body armor upon attack.

Recognizing its value, Marcus removed it from Asher and stored it in his Spatial Ring.

He planned to clean it before wearing it.

Afterward, he incinerated the three corpses with his flame magic.

With Asher dead, the rune he had set up became ineffective.

Marcus returned to the mountain pass near the Bayside Highway.

The mountain pass remained undisturbed, and there was no trace of fighting.

The sea breeze blew slowly, the sun was shiny and mild, and it was a quiet and peaceful scene.

He marveled at the power of the rune, which was capable of creating independent spaces for various activities without outside interference.

Besides mastering ancient martial arts, Asher was also a Rune Crafter.

Back at the Bayside Villa, Marcus intended to search Asher's Spatial Ring to see if there were any secrets about the rune.

He was injured and needed to heal quickly.

Without much time to think, he drove his Aston Martin toward Bayside Villa.
