Marcus glanced at Maxine and said, "We are friends. You don't have to apologize to me."

"But you are innocent..."

Maxine felt extremely guilty.

She understood that the Burnett family was one of the four major families in Uswad and that its energy was immeasurable.

Despite having seen Marcus' strength, she was still afraid that he would be drawn into unnecessary trouble.

"If you treat me as a friend, don't say that."

Marcus waved at Maxine.

Though cold and aloof, she was actually very kind.

Whether it was the hijacking or this time when the Burnett family encountered an accident, Maxine's first consideration was not to implicate others.

It would be absolutely a lie that Marcus had no affection for such a kind woman.

He also couldn't bear to see her getting hurt because of family changes.

Therefore, after being inexplicably involved, he might as well wait and see.

If something really happened to Maxine, Marcus would be the first to lend a helping hand.

As one of the four major families in Uswad, the Burnett family had a very deep foundation.

Matthew had been in a high position for a long time, and he was a giant figure who could influence half of the country.

Due to his advanced age, he had already handed over the family business to his two sons.

But his prestige couldn't be ignored.

Today was his 80th birthday, and more than 80% of Kingston's upper classes gathered here.

They came from business, political, and military circles.

Rumor had it that masters from the Astral Serpent Order, an Ancient Martial Art Sect, were present. What a grand event!

The Burnett Villa was located on several acres of land north of Kingston.

The building appeared luxurious, simple, magnificent, and impressive.

At that moment, the courtyard was carefully decorated, and there was a constant stream of guests coming and going.

A magnificent banquet was set in the living room of the Burnett Villa, and people were bustling about.

Matthew, wearing a suit and with snow-white hair, sat in the main seat.

Despite being in his 80s, he had a strong breath, a ruddy complexion, and was full of energy.

Next to him stood two pairs of middle-aged couples with extraordinary demeanor.

They were Maxine's parents, Owen Burnett and Hazel Burnett, and her uncle and his wife, Malcolm Burnett and Ruth Burnett.

Maxine's elder sister, Stella Burnett, came back from Acoton to celebrate Matthew's birthday.

Not far from Stella was a young man in a white suit.

He was handsome, elegant, and noble. He was Malcolm's son, Arnold Burnett, and the only male heir in the Burnett family.

In addition to these direct descendants of the Burnett family, members of the branches also arrived. There were dozens of people.

Owen, acting like the head of the family, and Hazel were greeting the guests who came to celebrate Matthew's birthday.

Malcolm and Ruth stood by and watched coldly.

"How is Shaun preparing? We have waited so long for today. We must make sure everything goes well," Ruth asked Malcolm in a low voice.

His eyes were fierce. "Don't worry. Shaun wants Maxine. I have agreed to marry her into the Whitman family after becoming the head of the family. Shaun will naturally help me unconditionally."

"That's good. When you become the head of the family, the Burnett family business will belong to us, and Arnold will inherit your position smoothly."

Ruth nodded with satisfaction.

Despite being in her 50s, she was still charming and elegant.

Her beautiful eyes betrayed her shrewdness.

Malcolm's ambition was half related to Ruth.

If she had not been instigating behind the scenes, he would not have thought of replacing Owen.

"Don't worry. In order to ensure that everything is perfect, in addition to Shaun, I have also invited experts from the Astral Serpent Order!

"Moreover, I have Mr. Roger Gellar's support for being the head of the Burnett family. This is truly destiny!"

Malcolm was proud. When he mentioned Roger, his reverence was evident.

"Godfather is the most powerful man in the world. Mr. Roger Gellar will help us a lot! But, Malcolm, don't talk nonsense. If others hear it and tell Godfather, we will most likely be punished by him!"

Ruth ignored the experts from the Astral Serpent Order and focused her attention entirely on Roger. She was nervous.
