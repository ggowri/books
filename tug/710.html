Ross, his voice muffled, asked, "Grandfather, is the Bloodthirsty Cult really as powerful as they say?"

Zachary's eyes narrowed as he answered coldly, "Though the Walsh family occupies a major part of the entertainment industry in Uswad, we've never been able to control our own power. We're still not in a position to rival the Bloodthirsty Cult."

The image of him bowing publicly in front of Marcus and slapping his own face filled Ross with fury.

Zachary wore a cold expression. He said, "Of course not. That guy hit my grandson, and I won't let this go easily. I have been in Uswad for many years, and I have built up some connections.
