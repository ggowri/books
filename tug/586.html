Catherine's body quivered, and her mind instantly went blank. That intense feeling of comfort was so overwhelming that it made her feel as if she were in a fairyland.

It took a while before Catherine came to her senses from that feeling.

Her face still retained a rosy flush, and beads of sweat were trickling down her forehead.

"You feel good?"

Marcus asked. He had always been extremely attentive to the feelings of his bedmates, constantly trying to ensure that each encounter enabled them to enjoy the most perfect happiness fully.

Catherine gently bit her lips and nodded slightly, and a shy expression gradually appeared on her pretty face.

"Marcus, why would you say that? You don't seem the least bit shy..."

"We've already done what we needed to do. What is there to be shy about?"

Marcus smiled.

During this period, through successive dual cultivation sessions with Lila, Josie, Melina, Natalia, and Catherine herself, Marcus' cultivation had been steadily improving.

This particular dual cultivation enabled his cultivation to gradually step beyond the minor completion stage of Ancient Martial Stage Seven.

Almost at the same time, Catherine's phone rang.

It was her mother, Laura, calling.

"Hey, Mom. I'm downstairs, and I'll be up in a moment."

"Catherine, you sound a bit out of breath. Are you tired?"

Laura asked, her voice full of concern.

"It's okay, Mom. Don't worry. I'm truly alright... I just took a short jog a while ago, so I'm a bit breathless. I'll be upstairs soon."

Hearing Laura's words, Catherine's pretty face suddenly flushed.

Besides, she glared at Marcus.

After hanging up the phone, Catherine told Marcus, "Marcus, it's all your fault. My mom said I was a bit out of breath... It's so embarrassing."

With that, Catherine got out of the car and trotted upstairs.

"Here, take the car keys."

Marcus quickly stepped forward, handed the car keys to Catherine, and left with a smile.

The next day, Catherine drove a brand new mini car to work. Even though her driving skills were not quite good, this car was easier to control than she had imagined.

Upon arriving at the company, she immediately attracted envious glances from many female colleagues.

"Catherine, you're also early. Which department are you in? After I get familiar with the environment, I'll talk to the boss tomorrow and see if I can help you get a better position."

No sooner had Catherine stepped out of the car than she caught sight of Gina approaching her from a distance.

Catherine greeted her with a smile and said, "Gina, I work in the general manager's office."

Gina was taken aback for a moment. After a short pause, she glanced at Catherine thoughtfully and said, "Catherine, you're truly remarkable. I didn't expect you to be the general manager's secretary.

"That's right. You're not only highly educated but also stunning. It's no wonder the general manager has taken a liking to you."

Catherine simply smiled without saying anything and entered the office building with Gina.

Her office was located on the eighty-eighth floor, and she had an exclusive elevator.

As they reached the elevator door, Catherine said goodbye to Gina, "Gina, I'm heading up first. See you later."

"Okay, go ahead. I have to go through the onboarding process, so I probably won't see you until this afternoon," Gina replied.

With that, the two went their separate ways.

This was Gina's first day at work, and there were numerous procedures she needed to follow, such as filling out forms and being assigned an office.

Most importantly, her employment application form required the signature of the company's general manager.

When Gina had filled in the form, a female staff member from the Human Resources Department informed her, "Ms. Gina Wilson, take this form to the general manager's office on the eighty-eighth floor. Once Ms. Wilson signs it, you can officially commence work."

"Ms. Wilson? Does the general manager also bear the surname Wilson?"

Gina asked curiously.

"Yes, the general manager is quite pretty, and you'll find out when you see her. Anyway, I believe our general manager is the most gorgeous woman in the entire company.

"You know what, Ms. Gina Wilson, you look a bit like her," The female staff member added with a charming smile.

Gina didn't pay much attention to this comment, merely taking it as a courteous compliment.

Shortly thereafter, Gina made her way nervously to the general manager's office on the eighty-eighth floor and knocked on the heavy and armored double doors.

"Come in."

A sweet voice came from inside.

Gina pushed the door open and walked in.

The office spanned an area of two thousand square feet, looking magnificent and capacious. Through the expansive floor-to-ceiling windows, they could take in the entire vista of Solomia.

Gina couldn't help but exclaim inwardly, "Is this the general manager's office? It's truly splendid."

Her gaze then shifted and fell on a large desk not far away.

A familiar person was sitting in front of the desk.

"Catherine, why are you sitting there? Where is the general manager? Hurry up and get away. Be careful not to be saw by the general manager later," Gina reminded Catherine.

When they had first seen each other just now, Gina had mistaken Catherine for the general manager's secretary.

In her estimation, although Catherine was a secretary, it was improper for her to be seated at the general manager's desk.

"Gina, this is my desk. You're here to handle the entry procedures, right? Bring it over, and I'll sign for you," Catherine said with a grin.

Gina raised her eyebrows slightly and responded with a smile, "Catherine, cut it out. How could you possibly be the general manager?"
