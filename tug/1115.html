Rivers' punch glowed with golden light, illuminating the entire cave.

His cultivation was even stronger than an Acquired State Nine expert. Before his Fist Ray descended, Marcus could already feel the terrifying energy contained in this punch.

He felt a stinging sensation on his skin.

However, he didn't mind at all. With a casual sidestep, he dodged the punch and began calculating the moves in their battle.

"First strike!"

Seeing Marcus dodge easily, Rivers instantly burned with rage, the killing intent in his eyes growing even stronger.

He gritted his teeth and said, "I didn't expect you to react this fast, but don't think that you'll survive after dodging this. Your death is next!"

Rivers swung his palm, and a golden light formed in his palm.

The golden light enveloped his body. His form radiated endless energy, like a war god standing tall in this world.

With a casual palm strike, a terrifying Hand Strike materialized in the void, like divine punishment descending, pressing down toward Marcus!

An incredibly terrifying energy exploded in the void, instantly enveloping the cave in its power.

Under the effect of that terrifying energy, Gertie was forced to retreat. She had to release Numinous Energy to resist, yet still felt breathless from the overwhelming force.

However, Marcus seemed isolated from this space, and no matter how powerful the Hand Strike was, it still couldn't affect him.

With a deafening roar, the power unleashed by the Hand Strike caused the ground to tremble, and the walls of the cave cracked all around.

However, such a terrifying Hand Strike still failed to hit Marcus's body.

At this moment, Marcus had already appeared in a different spot.

He stood with his hands behind his back, spotless, and looked at Rivers with a face full of mockery.

"Is this really what you can do? You're way too weak!"

Rivers frowned deeply, his eyes filled with killing intent, and said, "I admit I underestimated you before. It seems I must unleash my full strength to kill you!"

He began to fully mobilize all his potential, preparing to unleash his strongest strike to completely annihilate Marcus!

"But your chance is already gone!"

Before Rivers could react, he saw Marcus's figure become blurred at that moment and then vanish.

The next moment, Marcus had already appeared right in front of him out of thin air!

Rivers was terrified and instinctively launched an attack, trying to force Marcus back.

However, Marcus didn’t give him any chance! He suddenly reached out one hand and directly grabbed one of Rivers' snake heads!

The next moment, a terrifying Devouring Power erupted, instantly enveloping Rivers' body.

Rivers looked horrified, desperately trying to break free.

But he found that his body no longer obeyed him, as if bound by some kind of force.

The next moment, he felt the energy within him rapidly draining away.

Rivers trembled uncontrollably, his eyes filled with terror.

"How is this possible? How can this be? How can you unleash such terrifying Devouring Power? What kind of evil sorcery are you practicing?"

He struggled frantically, using all his strength to unleash his full potential in an attempt to resist.

Terrifying shockwaves echoed continuously, the entire space consumed by destructive force.

However, to Marcus, these forces were nothing more than child's play.

"You'll never have the chance to know!"

A mocking smile curled at the corner of his mouth, then he intensified the Devouring Power.

Rivers stared at Marcus in horror. The golden glow around him gradually dimmed at this moment. His face was deathly pale!

As the Devouring Power intensified, Rivers stopped his struggles.
