If it were just Lord League under siege, Osborne wouldn't lift a finger to help.

After all, he had no ties to Lord League.

The truth was, Lord League was far too foolish; if not for their own blunders, the alliance of the Dark Deity Legion would have been systematically advancing on the Augustine family, where a fully prepared Osborne awaited.

Though Osborne had been secluded for three days, dedicating himself to the creation of a Dimensional Microcosm, his companion, Sebastian, and the rest hadn't been idle. They had mobilized over 200 thousand members of the Draco Armed Services Branch, setting traps and making significant preparations in the area.

If the Dark Deity Legion's coalition came, Osborne was confident he could inflict heavy losses, and with the help of the Prophet Alliance warriors, he could even wipe them out entirely.

However, due to Lord League's reckless actions, the Dark Deity Legion changed their initial strategy. Instead of targeting the Augustine family, they turned their sights on the misguided Lord League.

To make matters worse, nearly twenty elite warriors from the Prophet Alliance were trapped there.

If they perished, the Prophet Alliance would be severely crippled, and Osborne would have to face the Dark Deity Legion coalition alone.

Osborne had a decent relationship with the Prophet Alliance, and Jackie had helped him in the past.

So when Jackie came pleading for help, he couldn't turn a blind eye.

"Tell me where they are!" he demanded.

Without hesitation, Jackie relayed the location of Lord League's Dimensional Microcosm.

A few thousand miles away in Lordia Land.

Osborne locked onto the position and initiated interdimensional travel, taking Jackie with him across the vast distance.

As they landed, Osborne immediately sensed a powerful surge of energy.

It pulsed through the air, the result of a tremendous explosion; though it was over a hundred miles away, its intensity sent shivers down his spine.

It was as if half of Blue Planet could feel the shockwave.

A creeping dread filled Osborne's mind.

"Please tell me that's not the Dimensional Microcosm of Lord League that just blew up," he mused.

Jackie's face drained of color as he felt the energy ripple.

"Oh no! It's over! The Dark Deity Legion has destroyed Lord League's Dimensional Microcosm! That kind of explosive energy can only mean one thing!"

His body trembled.

"Lord League is done for! Anyone trapped inside—there's no way they could have escaped! My predecessors from the Prophet Alliance... they're likely..."

Jackie couldn't finish; fear etched deeper across his features.

Osborne quickly reassured him, "Mr. Flores, don't panic! The elders of the Prophet Alliance are Transcendent Deity warriors; they would've sensed the danger and fled in time!

"Stay here, I'll go and rescue them!"

With that, Osborne set Jackie down and sprinted towards the explosion's source, entering the parallel space by activating his Phantom Steps.

He covered miles in no time, tearing through the air until the sounds of battle reached his ears.

What he saw was chaos incarnate.

The Dark Deity Legion, along with the Pantheon League and Eityreia Organization, had unleashed everything they had—thousands of warriors, all Superior Deity and Transcendent Deity, the latter numbering at least a hundred and fifty strong.

The coalition was far more formidable than Osborne had anticipated.

Meanwhile, their opponents numbered fewer than a hundred.

The explosion of Lord League's Dimensional Microcosm nearly wiped them out entirely. Fewer than a hundred managed to escape in time.

Including the close to twenty survivors from the Prophet Alliance, only about eighty were left.

Each of them was heavily wounded, surrounded by over a thousand Superior Deities and Transcendent Deities from the Dark Deity Legion.

Their eyes were filled with nothing but fear and despair.

Breaking out?

That was a pipe dream!

The tripartite coalition of the Dark Deity Legion was nearly ten times their strength.

What they could do was huddle together, desperately trying to hold out, hoping against hope for some miracle or for reinforcements to arrive.

But deep down, they had already given up.

They knew that even if help came, it wouldn't change the outcome; it couldn't match the might of the Dark Deity Legion's tripartite coalition.

After all, Blue Planet's major powers were few and far between.

With the Dark Deity Legion, the Pantheon League, and the Eityreia Organization now almost annihilating Lord League and trapping half of the Prophet Alliance's elite, only remnants of the Prophet Alliance and Rosmandi's Augustine family were left standing.

Could half a Prophet Alliance and the Augustine family really save them?

They didn't think so.

Utterly hopeless, their hearts sank into a bottomless abyss.
