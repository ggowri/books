"Let me put an end to this. I don't want to waste any more time with you. I am eager to awaken Sebastian and announce to him that I win this battle."

After speaking, Gerardo waved his hand again to give orders.

This time, a full two hundred Class S clones charged out, rushing towards Greyson, Bryce, and the other Class S warriors, unleashing terrifying attacks along with the remaining Tauren Kings.

Greyson and the others had initially been at an absolute advantage, able to stop this group of Tauren Kings from destroying the energy cannons.

Thousands of energy cannons could finally turn their focus to attacking the clones that were slaughtering drones and robots.

The situation was initially favorable for the coalition forces, but with the addition of two hundred Class S clones, the favor shifted instantly towards the Aberrants.

The fewer than fifty Class S warriors suddenly faced a total of two hundred fifty Class S clones, increasing their pressure tenfold.

In the previous moment, Bryce had been swinging the Trident of Poseidon and slaughtering Tauren Kings. In the next moment, he found himself in a bitter struggle, covered in numerous wounds.

Fortunately, Osborne gave him the Crystal of Life.

In fact, every Class S warrior received a Crystal of Life. Although they were injured, the Crystals allowed for rapid recovery.

Despite the continued difficulty in holding their ground, they managed to hold on.

This surprised Gerardo.

"Still not enough to break them? Then, I'll add a little more. Show me how long you can hold on."

Gerardo waved his hand again, sending out another two hundred Class S clones.

The Class S warriors of the coalition forces now had to face over four hundred Class S clones. At this point, they truly could not hold on any longer. But this was not the end.

Gerardo murmured, "Augustines, I'm disappointed. I thought you could last longer. Since that's the case, all of you shall die. The underworld of Rosmandi is mine."

After finishing his words, the last two hundred Class S clones beside him swiftly charged out. Their target remained the Class S warriors like Bryce.

Now, the fewer than fifty members of the coalition forces were facing over six hundred Class S clones, a numerical disadvantage of more than ten times.

The coalition forces could no longer hold on. The energy cannons were no longer assisting the combat drones and robots. They were firing with full force to support Bryce and the others, but they still could not withstand the onslaught.

A Class S warrior from a superpower, facing dozens of Class S clones' attacks, finally could not bear it and was blasted to pieces. Then came the second, the third.

Faced with attacks from such a significant numerical advantage, they could do nothing to defend themselves.

Their defenses shattered, heavy injuries appeared on their bodies, and even the Crystal of Life could not save them in time, as their bodies were directly obliterated.

More and more Class S warriors fell into danger and despair. Wesson's face turned pale.

"No!"

He knew he couldn’t wait longer. Each of these Class S warriors was incredibly important. If they all died, the coalition forces would be finished.

Therefore, Wesson prepared to join the battle.

He was just an ordinary Class S, even weaker than Bryce. His involvement could not possibly change the current situation. But he held the Godfather's scepter.

Wesson raised the Godfather's scepter high, and in an instant, the distant mountain seemed to come alive as a terrifying power gathered atop the scepter, emitting a black ghostly light.

"You shall die now." Wesson brought his hands down, and the black light shot out from the Godfather's scepter, enveloping a group of thirty clones.

Those thirty clones, engulfed by the black light, did not even have time to scream before their bodies rapidly dissolved. All thirty Class S clones perished.

Wesson raised the Godfather's scepter high again. "Kill."

The black light gathered once more and attacked another group of clones.

Another batch of Class S clones died instantly.

Bryce and the others became invigorated. Previously trapped in desperation, they now saw a glimmer of hope.

Although they still faced over five hundred Class S clones, judging by Wesson's current killing speed, as long as he launched another twenty or so attacks, he could take them all down.

"The Godfather's scepter is invincible," someone shouted excitedly.

"Hmph." Just at that moment, a cold snort sounded.

Gerardo suddenly appeared before Wesson. His eyes were filled with greed as he stared at the Godfather's scepter in Wesson's hand.

"Is that the Godfather's scepter?"
