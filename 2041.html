Over there, the God of Light was locked in an intense struggle against Icy One and his four comrades. The very air crackled with the residual energy of their clash, and Osborne could feel the immense pressure weighing down on him.

At his current level of power, he felt utterly unqualified to join that fight.

Just taking down Icy Six nearly exhausted every trick I had up my sleeve. If we faced off again, I might not even manage to defeat him! After all, my absolute forbidden zone has its limits.

If he'd just retreated the moment he had the chance, he could have regained his Ascension Six strength... I wouldn't stand a chance against him!

"I've taken down Icy Six, Icy Seven, and Icy Eight, along with over a thousand Supernal Deity Kings under Lord Malevolence. I'm sure the Paramount Supreme Clan won't let this slide easily! They'll want revenge, and I can only imagine what kind of stronger forces they'll send after me!

After tonight, they might be so enraged that they'll launch a full-scale war against the Human Community, especially since they see no hope of winning in the upcoming competition. If that happens, the God of Light will be too busy managing the bigger picture to protect me.

I'll be on my own! I need to get stronger!"

Osborne's expression turned grave. He desperately wished his cultivation level matched that of the God of Light—an Ascension Seven Supernal Deity King.

Even Ascension Six or Five would suffice!

If he could reach Ascension Four, he might stand a chance of protecting himself and influencing the course of this war.

But alas, he was still stuck at Ascension One.

Without some extraordinary opportunity, even breaking through to Ascension Two would take considerable time.

"I need to think of a way to delay the onset of full-scale war! Even a few days would be better than an immediate outbreak!"

Osborne's mind raced as he brainstormed solutions.

The intensity of the battle surged, the shockwaves growing fiercer.

Both the God of Light and Icy One's group were fully engaged, as if they were fighting for their very lives.

The ferocity of their clash was palpable, even from a distance.

Just then, a spark of inspiration lit up in Osborne's eyes.

"Maybe I can push back the war's start after all! Let's hope I can pull this off!"

"God of Light, it's me, Osborne. I've taken down Icy Six, Icy Seven, and Icy Eight! Not to mention, I've also eliminated three hundred Supernal Deity Kings from the Jackson family who betrayed the Human Community and one thousand under Lord Malevolence."

Not just alive, but he's actually killed Icy Six, Icy Seven, and Icy Eight?

How is that even possible?"

"Of course, I mean a fake death! I'm planning to do this, but I need your cooperation!"
