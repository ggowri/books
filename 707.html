Emma faced fourteen Class AAA Evolved Human.

Among them were five Titans, four Werewolves, three Medusas, and two Tauren.

When Emma realized she had to fight these opponents, she couldn't help but gasp.

"Damn it!"

She couldn't help but curse for the first time in her life.

"I said I could handle two or three Class AAA. But fourteen? I could slap myself for getting into this mess just because I was attracted to someone!"

Emma muttered.

"Osborne, if I survive this, you have to help me unlock Satan's Eye! Or this won't be worth it!"

After saying this, Emma began her attack.

"Freeze!

"Psychic Blast!

"Death's Stare!"

Emma didn't hesitate. She unleashed all her power and trump cards in an instant.

A terrifying cold aura spread over hundreds of yards, covering everything in thick ice.

The fourteen Class AAA Aberrants were no exception.

They were strong enough to shatter the ice layer instantly, glaring at Emma like she was a mere insect.

They hadn't expected Emma to strike first.

"You're done!"

All fourteen Class AAA Aberrants moved, but Emma's Psychic Blast hit their minds just then.

Unfortunately, these Evolved Humans weren't affected by Psychic Blasts.

They seemed unaffected and kept charging at Emma, causing her expression to change.

By then, her right eye's Death's Stare was activated.

A terrifying and eerie white light shot out from Emma's right eye, instantly hitting the two Werewolves in front.

"Awooo!!

"Aaaah!"

Piercing howls filled the air as the two Werewolves suddenly stopped. They rolled on the ground in agony. The blood streamed from their eyes, noses, mouths, and ears.

Soon, they stopped moving.

The life force quickly drained from their bodies.

They were dead!

This stunned the remaining twelve Class AAA Aberrants, who all halted, too afraid to approach.

Two of them had died in an instant?

They stared at Emma, seeing that her face was deathly pale from the enormous energy drain.

The Evolved Humans exchanged glances.

"Don't be afraid! That was her trump card! She can't use it again. If we attack together, we can easily tear her apart!"

Medusa said this, then unleashed a trump card of her own.

Ashes of Death!

Emma immediately sensed something wrong. She barely managed to raise the swallow shield in front of her.

Crackle, crackle!

In an instant, Medusa's attack was blocked by the swallow shield.

This made her expression change drastically.

"Attack together!"

Boom, boom, boom!

Eleven Class AAA Aberrants attacked Emma again. They had guessed correctly. Emma couldn't use Death's Stare again so soon and had to rely on the swallow shield for defense.

Boom, boom, boom!

As the barrage of attacks hit, even the swallow shield began to tremble violently.

Although the swallow shield was a divine artifact, Emma, as a Class AA, couldn't fully wield its power. Against the combined attacks of over a dozen Class AAA Evolved Humans, even the shield was struggling.

The energy absorbed by the swallow shield surged into Emma's body, making her cheeks flush red as she felt like she was about to explode.

She had no choice but to keep attacking, trying to release the overwhelming energy.

But with several Titans blocking her, her attacks couldn't inflict any fatal damage.

Bang, bang, bang!

In no time, Emma became a punching bag for the dozen or so Class AAA Evolved Humans surrounding her. Fortunately, the swallow shield was strong enough, or she would have died multiple times.

Even though the swallow shield absorbed most of the attacks, Emma still had to endure the intense shockwaves. Blood quickly trickled from the corner of her mouth.

"Damn it!"

Emma couldn't help but curse again, her eyes turning bloodshot. She didn't have time to regret her decisions. She felt like a small boat caught in a storm, on the brink of capsizing.

At this moment, all she could do was grit her teeth and hold on.

She could only hope that Osborne would defeat his opponents quickly and come to her rescue. Otherwise, she would surely die here.

Bang, bang, bang!

Emma was repeatedly knocked back. The power of the swallow shield seemed to be weakening under the relentless onslaught, and Emma's injuries were worsening.

She kept coughing up blood, her face growing paler by the second.

"Am I about to die?"

Emma muttered, her mind suddenly flooded with memories of her past twenty-odd years.

From a young age, she had always been the most talented in the Augustine family.

She had awakened three extra energy.

Everyone believed she was exceptional, and she thought so too. She even believed that no man was worthy of her.

And now, she was about to die?

Emma's vision began to blur.

She couldn't hold on any longer.
