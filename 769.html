Bang!

The Water Domain, cast by Andrew, was still covering the arena. In such a situation, Stuart, who was already much weaker, could not stand up to him.

"This is a trap?"

Stuart instantly understood. Andrew was intentionally humiliating Rhett to provoke his hot temper, hoping he would charge onto the arena again and violate the rules.

Anyone who broke the arena's rules deserved to die, even if beaten to death!

At that moment, Stuart's eyes filled with regret.

Just yesterday, he had fallen for a similar trick due to his impulsiveness and was nearly killed on the arena by Roger, a killer from the Eityreia Organization.

If it weren't for Osborne, he would've been dead yesterday.

Now, he deeply regretted not learning his lesson. He had once again fallen into Andrew's trap, putting himself in danger.

Andrew was much stronger than Roger!

Not only was Andrew one of the Godsons on the Global Godson Rankings, but he was also ranked 465. There was no way for Stuart to survive if Andrew wanted to kill him.

"Are you... are you part of the Eityreia Organization too?"

Andrew sneered and replied, "I'm not a hitman! But Roger is my friend. He knew Rhett would challenge me, and he also knew how short-tempered you are. So, we came up with this plan.

"He said that as long as I kept humiliating Rhett, you wouldn't be able to hold back and would jump into the arena. And guess what? He was right. You fell for it!

"Now, it's time for you to die!"

A cold, murderous glint flashed in Andrew's eyes.

"Sigh!"

Down below the arena, Osborne couldn't help but sigh as he watched the miserable sight of Rhett and Stuart on the stage, feeling a bit helpless.

He hadn't expected Andrew and Roger to be friends, and the constant insults toward Rhett were all part of a scheme targeting Stuart.

With Stuart's explosive temper, it was no surprise that he fell for it again.

"Some things just won't let you rest, huh?"

Osborne muttered to himself. He knew it was time for him to step in again.

No matter what, Aleena had asked him to pose as a member of the Lambert Family and come to Tethynia for the Honor Quest Ceremony. He owed her a favor, and there was no way he could just stand by and let Andrew kill Stuart like that.

Having made up his mind, Osborne began walking toward the arena.

"I really just wanted to observe. I had no intention of getting involved. I simply wanted to see the strength of the younger generation and prepare for my own Honor Quest Ceremony in twenty days.

"Why must you force me to act?"

By the time he finished speaking, Osborne had already reached the front of the arena.

At that moment, Andrew had lifted his foot, aiming to crush Stuart's head.

Everyone around, including Jackie from the Diviner League, didn't intervene.

After all, Stuart had broken the rules by jumping into the arena, interrupting the fight between Andrew and Rhett. Even if Andrew killed him, it would be justified.

"Die!"

Andrew's face twisted in a sinister grin, and his foot was about to come down on Stuart's head.

Bang!

A tall figure appeared before him in the next second, throwing a punch that sent him flying backward.

Andrew stumbled back a few steps, then steadied himself and saw Osborne standing before him.

Instead of anger, his eyes lit up with surprise, and he burst into laughter.

"Hahaha!

Stuart struggled to stand, feeling ashamed as he saw Osborne once again stepping into the ring to save him, facing such a powerful opponent.

"Axell, I..."

Osborne cut him off. "Next time, don't be so impulsive! You've been secluded for too long. You don't know how dangerous the world outside can be. That's why you keep falling into their traps. Be more careful in the future."

"Go!"

Stuart felt overwhelmed with guilt, but he said nothing more. He helped the unconscious Rhett up and prepared to leave the stage.

"Axell, be careful!"

"Hmph!"

At that moment, Andrew snorted coldly, saying, "Did I say you could leave?

"Rhett can go. He's a worthless piece of trash. But you two violated the arena's rules. Who said you could leave? Both of you are staying right here!

"I'm going to slowly torture both of you to death!"

As soon as he finished speaking, Stuart's face went pale.

Osborne, however, remained calm and said, "Just keep going. Don't worry about him. I'll hold him off."

Stuart gritted his teeth and took large strides toward the edge of the arena.

"You bastard! Stop right there!"

Andrew attacked fiercely, summoning terrifying torrents of water from his Water Domain to strike at Stuart.
