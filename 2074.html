"It's all your fault for being so weak! Once we reach Umbra Earth, I might not even be able to protect you on my own! That is why I must recruit several geniuses from the World of Racial Convergence to guard you!

At last, Osborne had officially joined Alfreda's exploration team. With this proper affiliation, he could now enter Umbra Earth without worry. Osborne was thrilled. He no longer had to puzzle over how to sneak into Umbra Earth.

The next morning, Osborne arrived at Alfreda's treehouse and knocked on the door.

"Coming!"

Alfreda was already prepared. "Let's go! We head to the Combat Arena. With my strength and reputation, countless geniuses from the World of Racial Convergence will be clamoring to join my exploration team!"

Yet Osborne was not as optimistic. After all, Alfreda was a Nature Elf, and the Nature Elves were rather weak at the moment.

Although Alfreda was an Ascension Five Supernal Deity King, it did not guarantee that many genuine warriors from the World of Racial Convergence would join her team.

"Let's go over there!"

Alfreda pointed in a direction. "That must be the recruitment spot for warriors! They actually set us up in such a remote place? Hmph!"

When they saw Alfreda, their expressions changed instantly, and they began whispering to each other.

"I don't want to make enemies of the Fire Elf Royal Family!"

The murmurs of the crowd were low, yet Alfreda, as an Ascension Five Supernal Deity King, heard every word clearly.

Alfreda remained as confident as ever!

Finally, she leaned in and whispered to Osborne, "I'll tell you a secret, but you must never tell anyone else!

"Now you see just how powerful I am, don't you?"
