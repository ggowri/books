"Mr. Lachlan Swift, did I hear you right? You said you want to help your human slaves regain their freedom and status?"

Lillian was momentarily speechless.

The slave market could indeed help slaves regain their freedom, but it was an uncommon request.

Most people who bought slaves simply discarded them when they lost interest, treating slaves as disposable commodities.

Who would be foolish enough to come here to pay for the freedom of those deemed worthless?

Helping slaves regain their status required a significant amount of money—often not much less than the cost of purchasing a new slave.

In her time working here, Lillian had never encountered anyone willing to go to such lengths for their slaves.

She looked at him, bewildered.

"Mr. Lachlan Swift, are you feeling alright? Is this really you? Or have you suddenly come into a fortune? If your slaves are no longer wanted, you could just hand them over to me. With that, Lillian wrapped her arm around Osborne's again, her gaze flirtatious and teasing.

Osborne felt a wave of disgust wash over him, and without hesitation, he roughly pushed her away.

"Get off me! Stay away from me. Your kind makes me sick! I'm a guest here, and I'm here to help my slaves regain their freedom. As a staff member, you need to assist me immediately, or I will lodge a complaint against you!"

"Lillian, what's going on?" the lead security officer asked, concern etched on his face.

Seeing him, Lillian immediately feigned distress, rushing to cling to his arm. "Yannick, Lachlan has gone mad! I was just trying to help him, and he pushed me! You have to stand up for me!"

"I order you to apologize to Lillian right now, or don't blame me for being rude!"

"Sure, the Swift family is the strongest force in the Human Community of Wild Flame City, but compared to the Rainbow Viper Clan? You're nothing! And against the Demonic Dragon Clan and Soaring Lion Clan? You're just a blip on the radar!

"You want me to apologize?

"Lillian, do you really think you're worth that? You think you can scare me just because you're trying to play nice with this Rainbow Viper Clan guy?

"I'm here to spend my Essence Crystals and become one of your biggest clients!
