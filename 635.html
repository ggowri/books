"Am I already at the bottom?" Osborne looked at his ranking with no visible change in expression, nor did he seem anxious.

He thought, "The war has only just begun. The territories currently occupied by the Aberrants could still be reclaimed later. It is too early to determine who would win or lose in the end."

Osborne hadn't expected that even Vincent, Matthew, and Emma couldn't withstand the Aberrants' attack and had to rely on the solid defenses of the Draco Group headquarters to prevent losing entire coastal provinces.

Osborne murmured, "I need to speed up. If all six coastal provinces fall to the Aberrants, they'll likely push further inland, and Campbell might not be safe either."

He wasn't going to wait passively. Staying in Dampston wasn't just about protecting Clarissa. It was also about becoming a shadowy figure striking against the Aberrants.

But first, Osborne needed to understand the full extent of the Aberrants' strength.

He was currently watching a video Matthew had just uploaded.

Not long ago, the Aberrants had launched a fierce attack on the Draco Group headquarters in Jalmar, which Matthew was defending.

Matthew had recorded the battle and shared it in a chatroom created specifically for the six heirs.

Along with the video, Matthew had posted a message: [Everyone, this war isn't just a competition among us six heirs. It's a fight for the Augustine family's survival. We need to share information and help each other because the Aberrants are much stronger than I anticipated. We must cooperate to combat them. Here's what happened when they attacked me. The situation isn't good, and I can't hold out much longer.]

After reading Matthew's message, Osborne's expression grew serious.

He thought, "Even Matthew sounded so pessimistic."

He quickly clicked on the video.

The footage was from the Draco Group headquarters in Jalmar, which was built like a fortress. Despite this, the Aberrants chose to attack it.

Hundreds of shadowy figures appeared first, transforming into terrifying Werewolves with sharp teeth and claws, each at least Class A-.

They attacked furiously, leaving deep marks on the strong walls.

But it didn't stop there. Next, a group of Aberrants transformed into Titans, each standing at least nine to twelve feet tall and possessing immense strength.

Those Titans were also Class A-.

Then came Taurens and Blood Clan members, over a thousand shape-shifting metahumans, relentlessly attacking the headquarters in Jalmar, creating a nightmarish scene.

However, that alone wasn't enough to make Matthew so despondent.

Osborne quickly realized that these Class A- shape-shifting metahumans were just cannon fodder.

The Aberrants' real main force consisted of identical-looking individuals, including several Medusas.

There were at least dozens of Medusas, resembling clones, each with Class A strength, terrifying petrification power, and formidable combat skills.

Additionally, there were many Class A and above Werewolves and Blood Clan members.

Leading them was a powerful figure in a black robe, clearly a Class AAA.

Although Matthew was strong, he was easily pushed back by this leader.

Eventually, a Class AAA elder from the Augustine family managed to hold off the leader.

Despite Matthew's team eventually repelling the Aberrants' attack, their losses were significant.

After watching the video, Osborne's face darkened. "The King of the Aberrants must have mastered some kind of cloning technology. Otherwise, it's impossible to have so many Medusas, Werewolves, and Blood Clan metahumans."

At this moment, Emma sent a message in the chat forum: [Matthew is right. The Aberrants are stronger than we thought. We six need to cooperate and share information. My situation is similar to Matthew's. The enemy numbers and types are about the same."

Emma posted her battle video next.

Osborne quickly watched it and saw that the situation was indeed similar. The Aberrants used numerous Class A- Werewolves, Titans, and Blood Clans as shock troops, with groups of Class A Medusas, Werewolves, and Blood Clans as the main force.

Class AA enemies were fewer, about a dozen, led by a Class AAA figure in a black robe.

Then Vincent uploaded his battle video.

The situation was similar, but Vincent managed to fend off the enemies more easily.

In the video, Osborne saw Vincent overpowering the Aberrants' Class AAA and killing a Class AA before the enemy temporarily withdrew.

Finally, Lance uploaded his video.

In his footage, he was almost overwhelmed by the Aberrants. The headquarters' defenses were quickly broken through. They had to flee.

Next, Owen uploaded his video.

Owen's situation was slightly better than Lance's. Although he suffered heavy losses, he managed to repel the enemy and keep the headquarters from falling.

However, Osborne knew Owen might not hold out much longer.

"Osborne, what's going on over there? How did you end up in last place with a score of negative thirty-one hundred? Did you abandon all the territories you were supposed to defend without even fighting the enemy?" Matthew asked.
