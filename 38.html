Osborne was shocked to the core, never having witnessed such a bizarre situation before.

How was this possible?

Was this person even human?

A bullet hitting the eye at close range, and he was still alive?

And that strange eyeball managed to stop the bullet?

“I’m going to kill you!” Milo roared in anger, his other eye now emanating an even more eerie purple light, like veins spreading across his face as he stared intensely at Osborne.

In an instant, Osborne felt as though his brain had been struck by a heavy hammer, leaving him dazed and losing control of his body.

But the next moment, a refreshing sensation surged through his Satan’s Eye, instantly clearing his confused mind.

“Huh?” Milo exclaimed in disbelief, looking at Osborne with a hint of doubt.

“You resisted my psychic abilities? You’re not just an ordinary human?”

Now calmed down, Osborne coldly snorted, saying, “Just realizing now? It’s too late.”

With that, Osborne fired off a series of shots.

The bullets rained down on Milo, who was prepared this time, showing no fear on his face.

He sneered, the purple light in his eyes intensifying.

Surprisingly, all the bullets in mid-air changed direction, hitting the wall behind Milo.

“What?”

Osborne was once again startled.

However, Milo grinned wickedly and launched a punch at Osborne’s chest.

But to Osborne, it all seemed like slow motion through his Satan’s Eye.

He effortlessly evaded the punch, causing Milo to strike the wall with tremendous force, creating a gaping hole and shaking the entire building.

Osborne was astounded by Milo’s terrifying strength.

Similarly, Milo was surprised that Osborne could dodge his attacks.

Unable to land a hit, Milo frowned as Osborne skillfully evaded his blows.

As he finally landed a punch on Osborne, Osborne stepped back, unscathed.

Milo couldn’t comprehend how Osborne could resist his psychic abilities, evade his attacks, and display strength comparable to his own.

Momentarily at a loss, Milo found himself unable to contend with Osborne.

Before coming here, he never imagined Osborne would be so formidable.

Should have asked Justin for five million dollars, Milo thought, already contemplating retreat.

Suddenly, his gaze shifted to Anastasia lying on the bed, a sinister smirk appearing on his face.

Ignoring Osborne, Milo charged towards the bed.

Osborne’s expression hardened drastically as he frantically tried to stop Milo. Though he managed to dodge Milo’s attacks, he couldn’t defeat him. His only option was to entangle Milo with all his might, but Milo steadily advanced towards Anastasia’s bedside.

In his attempts to thwart Milo, Osborne inadvertently left himself vulnerable, getting struck twice by Milo and nearly spitting out blood.

“Prey…” Milo growled.

He reached the bedside, extending his claw-like hand towards Anastasia aggressively.

“Stop!” Osborne roared, his eyes crazed with anger as if a lone wolf in a desperate situation.

Unwilling to dodge anymore, he charged forward recklessly, exposing more weaknesses.

While momentarily halting Milo, Osborne was repeatedly injured by him.

This was Milo’s plan all along.

“You dared to harm my eye; I’ll make you pay with yours.” Milo’s eyes emitted a chilling cold light as he fiercely grabbed Osborne’s arm, and his other hand viciously lunged towards Osborne’s left eye.

His Satan’s Eye.

At that moment, Osborne was unable to evade, and Milo’s sharp claw instantly landed on his eye.

But in the next second, a piercing scream resounded.

Upon touching Osborne’s Satan’s Eye, Milo’s hand seemed to be shocked, causing his body to tremble violently.

The purple light in his eyes quickly vanished, his skin wrinkling as if he had aged ten years in an instant.

Seconds later, Milo shoved Osborne away, his eyes filled with terror and fear, no longer concerned with killing Osborne, frantically fleeing outside.

Osborne, astonished, had no idea what had just occurred.

However, his concern for Anastasia prevented him from giving chase.

Seeing Milo about to escape, a tall figure suddenly appeared.

It was Isa.

Isa stood in the way of Milo’s escape route, her expression incredibly cold.

“Get lost!”

Milo, crazed, threw a punch at Isa.

Osborne was instantly alarmed, shouting, “Isa, move aside quickly. He’s a metahuman, his strength is formidable, don’t—”
