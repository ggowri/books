Hannah felt a jolt of disbelief inside her, but she kept quiet, furrowing her brow as she observed the scene unfolding in the palace.

However, reaching mid-stage Deity King was a rare feat.

Most super geniuses from various races, after breaking through to Deity King, had exhausted their potential and found it impossible to advance further.

Those who could push through to mid-stage Deity King were fewer than one in a hundred.

Every mid-stage Deity King could dominate within a powerful faction.

Take the Mystic Feather Clan in Wild Flame City, for example, while they had several Deity King warriors, all of them were at the early stages, with not a single mid-stage Deity King among them.
