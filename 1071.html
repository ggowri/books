"Gerardo, don't waste your energy! It's a Level Five Realm!" Armstrong interjected.

Armstrong continued, "Gerardo, your understanding of Realms is too limited! Don't you see? Level Four Realm is already incredibly solid, as tough as iron. But even iron can be shattered if you hit it with enough force.
