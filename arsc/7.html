Chapter 7

I slapped Layla hard across the face, channeling all the hatred from both my past and present lives.

"You finally figured it out," I said with a smile.

Indeed, I was the one who switched the condom.

After learning that Nathan frequently visited prostitutes, I had my private investigator obtain one of Nathan's used condoms by any means necessary, and brought it home using proper preservation methods.

Later, I deliberately had sex with Matthew and made sure Layla discovered us. Then, I threw Nathan's used condom into the trash.

This was exactly what Layla had been waiting for, so she excitedly assumed it was Matthew's used condom. After retrieving it, she contacted Carter and underwent in vitro fertilization.

That's why she left my house so quickly.

Looking at Layla lying on the ground after my slap, I finally felt some of my hatred subside. I said mockingly, "Since you love being pregnant and using other people's sperm so much, go ahead and take good care of that baby in your belly. I wish you both well."

With that, I left with Matthew.

By now, the reporters who had been surrounding us all rushed toward Layla and Carter. After all, this outcome was shocking news for them too.

The story continued to explode online, with everyone discussing and analyzing how Layla got pregnant. Some internet users even uncovered that Layla had previously been pregnant with a married man's child, which ended in a miscarriage after the man's wife attacked her.

[My God! Layla is a repeat offender. She tried to use pregnancy to gain money and status before, but got beaten until she miscarried.]

[Her husband must be devastated. And now he's been betrayed too.]

[Right. Otherwise, why would her husband be angry enough to hit her?]

As public interest in the matter grew, and after discovering her past had been exposed, Layla finally snapped.

She released a video online—the one from when she tried to seduce Matthew but was flatly rejected. However, since the phone recorded from her perspective, combined with her malicious editing and deliberately added dialogue, the video made it look like Matthew was actually harming her.

This video sparked another round of widespread discussion, with internet users shocked by these continuous plot twists.

Then Layla released another video, declaring: "Matthew's harassment is real. This video is proof. I don't know how many people Matthew and Naomi have bribed to falsify two test results. But after I give birth, I'll personally have the baby's blood tested for DNA. I'm confident that will be accurate. Please wait with me until the child is born."

Watching that video, Matthew's expression nearly twisted with rage. "This woman is truly insane. She's the one who came to my study, suddenly stripped, and tried to seduce me. Now she claims I harassed her."

I let out a cold laugh and said, "Don't worry, Matthew. Since she enjoys putting on a show, let's play along. By the way, isn't your company about to launch a new project? You'll need publicity, right?"

Matthew was stunned. "What do you mean?"

I smiled and said, "Nothing much. I'm just going to save you a fortune on promotional costs."
