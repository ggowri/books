Chapter 4

Upon hearing this name, everyone present froze. After a few seconds of silence, Carter was the first to react, rushing forward to grab the police officer. "What nonsense are you talking about? Nathan is my father. How could this child possibly be his?"

Hearing this, Nathan, who had been causing chaos in the crowd, also froze before emerging from the group.

He pointed at the police officer and shouted, "That's absolute bullshit! Matthew raped Layla, so this child must be Matthew's. What does this have to do with me?"

The reporters present were still in shock.

A male reporter said in astonishment, "What's going on here? Could there be a problem with the DNA test? How could this child possibly be Nathan's?"

A female reporter expressed her disbelief: "This is absurd! A girl gets raped, goes to the police station seeking evidence, and the police station somehow messes up the test results. Isn't this a second trauma for the victim?"

Another man countered, "But DNA test results are rarely wrong. Layla couldn't have actually slept with her husband's father, could she? That's disgusting. I wish I could control my thoughts and not think about this."

As the crowd's discussions grew louder, I displayed a mocking smile.

The bigger the commotion they caused, the better. The more severe the argument, the better. I was curious to see who would end up with a miserable fate in this lifetime.

At this moment, Layla realized something was wrong. She immediately rushed forward and snatched the test results from the police officer's hands. When she saw Nathan's name written at the end, she shouted, "This is wrong! These results must be flawed! This is fake!"

Then, she looked up at Matthew and me, gritting her teeth as she said, "You must have tampered with the results, didn't you? Matthew is so rich, he must have bribed the staff to secretly alter the test results."

Matthew was completely baffled. "I was brought to the police station from my home without even knowing what happened. Who could I possibly bribe? Layla, is something wrong with your brain? Do you have paranoid delusions?"

In my previous life, after learning the results, Matthew had been extremely agitated, believing Layla had tampered with the test results, desperately demanding the police investigate her.

But now, the person accusing the test results of being problematic was Layla.

I let out a cold laugh.

Perhaps my expression was too obvious, as Layla immediately sensed something was off and pointed at me, shrieking, "Naomi, it was you! You planned all of this. No wonder you haven't been nervous at all since being brought in by the police. You were prepared all along."

She let out a cold laugh, then put on a look of sudden realization, turning to the police officer and saying loudly, "I now suspect that Naomi bribed you beforehand to forge my DNA test results. I demand a retest at a larger institution."
