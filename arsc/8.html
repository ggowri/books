Chapter 8

I didn't immediately respond to Layla's video and accusations. Instead, I continuously posted various lifestyle photos on my social media platforms, including my regular shopping sprees and travel pictures.

Of course, in these photos, I deliberately showcased luxury handbags, expensive cars, and mansions—things that obviously flaunted my wealth—to intentionally stir up controversy.

Sure enough, within just a few days, my social media accounts surpassed a million followers, with everyone cursing me in the comments section. Even those who initially attacked Layla started attacking me.

"You're so shameless! You ruined your friend's life, yet you're still showing off."

"She has so many bags and jewelry. It seems her husband is indeed wealthy and powerful, so what Layla said might be true."

"No wonder Layla insisted on keeping the baby to confront you. She knew she was innocent and wanted to prove herself."

"Naomi is so stupid. Once Layla gives birth to that child, the baby will have inheritance rights. At that point, Matthew's assets will have to be shared with that child."

As the attention on this matter grew, I seized the opportunity and directly shared Matthew's company's new products. I also found several business accounts to promote the products. Within just a few days, the attention on Matthew's company's new project reached an all-time high, even directly generating tens of millions of dollars.

At this point, I had Matthew prepare for the launch of the new project while I edited the surveillance footage from our home, especially the segment where Layla deliberately unbuttoned her clothes before entering Matthew's study.

The surveillance video clearly showed Layla's intentions and the entire process. Their conversation was also recorded.

Matthew's disgusted voice came through crystal clear: "Ms. Parker, please behave yourself. I love my wife very much. You'd better abandon your plan. Otherwise, you should move out of my house immediately."

Besides this video, I also published online evidence of Layla wearing my clothes, swapping her high-quality replica watch for my million-dollar watch, and her multiple discussions with Carter about stealing Matthew's used condoms.

The editing was precise, the footage was clear, and the truth was evident.

At this moment, the attention on this matter finally reached its peak. Everyone finally understood all the details of the entire incident and began launching new attacks against Layla.

Layla completely broke down. She called to curse at me, but I hung up immediately and blocked her.

Carter tried to cause trouble outside our building, threatening and insulting me. However, Matthew had security throw both of them out.

I found a lawyer and handed over all the evidence of this incident, asking him to help sue Layla and Carter for malicious defamation and extortion, among other charges.

The lawyer confidently said, "Don't worry. We will definitely win this case."

Indeed, with sufficient evidence, the case progressed smoothly. The court made a judgment in less than a month, ordering Layla and Carter to pay me ten million dollars in damages.

Naturally, they didn't have the money to pay me, so they acted like scoundrels, even going live online to play the victim and earn gifts from netizens.

I had anticipated they would do this. So, I once again found the private investigator from before and had him investigate Carter's creditor, Wyatt Ruiz, and sent him information about Carter's current income.

Seeing the money, Wyatt was excited, like a beast spotting carrion. He even burst into Carter's home, beat him up, and demanded repayment.

However, all of Carter's money was with Layla. Layla even tried to wait until after the baby was born to continue threatening us, so she refused to pay Carter's debt.

Carter was desperate and had no choice but to mortgage Layla to Wyatt. Wyatt grabbed Layla and said with a lecherous smile, "There are people who particularly like pregnant women now. Your wife is so delicate; she'll definitely earn us a lot of money."

With that, he took her away, ignoring Layla's struggles and resistance.

Two months later, when Layla was returned, she was extremely weak. Due to multiple rapes, she had miscarried and lost the baby. She even became ill from this and could no longer have children.

Wyatt directly told Carter, "Your wife was indeed impressive. In just two months, she earned back all the money you owed us. I've brought her back to you. Our debt is settled."

"Okay, thank you," Carter was overjoyed because he no longer had to be beaten by debt collectors.

But Layla's eyes were vacant. She limped into the bathroom and remained silent afterward.

Initially, Carter felt some remorse toward her. But thinking about how embarrassed he was by Layla's pregnancy with Nathan's child, he felt that having her pay off his debt was compensation for him, and he no longer felt sorry.

With this in mind, he happily opened his phone and started sending gifts to female streamers.

At that moment, Layla came out and plunged a dagger directly into his heart.

By the time Matthew and I learned of this news, his company's new project had already earned hundreds of millions of dollars. Matthew was incredibly grateful to me and insisted on buying me cars and houses.

We were shopping when we saw the news report. The emaciated Layla was arrested for intentional homicide, while Carter died on the spot from excessive blood loss.

Their evil was finally coming to an end.

"Naomi, don't look at them anymore. Happy days are waiting for us," Matthew happily held my hand and turned to continue shopping.

I nodded, thinking, "That's right. Without Layla, Matthew and I will definitely live a happier life in this lifetime."

The End.
