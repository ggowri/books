Osborne felt a surge of confidence.

In a typical clash of soul power, he knew he'd struggle against Adolph. Although his own soul power was vast, it paled in comparison to Adolph's.

Even after traveling billions of miles, Adolph's soul power remained formidable.

On top of that, their mastery over soul power was worlds apart; in a fair fight, Osborne's soul power would simply crumble under the pressure.

Even with the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity on his side, he wouldn't stand a chance against Adolph.

But now, Adolph's soul power had detached and invaded his Soul Current Ocean.

That was a death wish!

A detached soul power was like a tree uprooted—without nourishment, it would only grow weaker and more sparse until it ultimately faded away.

"This is impossible!"

Adolph was seething with rage, unable to fathom how Osborne's soul power could be so absurdly resilient, continuously recovering and even growing stronger.

"If this keeps up, I'm going to be drained dry here!"

A flicker of unwillingness crossed Adolph's eyes.

He wasn't foolish; as an Ascension Two Supernal Deity King of soul power, he recognized his precarious situation. If he stayed and fought, he would be worn down by Osborne's relentless soul power.

The smartest move now was to retreat from Osborne's Soul Current Ocean and return to his original form.

He was confident Osborne couldn't stop him!

Yet, as Adolph gazed at the shimmering golden soul power and the unique golden Soul Current Ocean, he felt a pang of reluctance. This golden Soul Current Ocean was too special to let go.

"If I could harness this golden Soul Current Ocean, my soul power might transform into gold, too! Just imagine how powerful I could become!"

Greed filled Adolph's mind.

"Then even an Ascension Three Supernal Deity King wouldn't stand a chance against me! If I break through again, I could become the tenth Dark Sovereign under the esteemed Lord Malevolence—truly a position of unparalleled power!"

Caught in this internal struggle, Adolph was torn between staying to obliterate Osborne's soul power or making a hasty retreat.

At that moment, Osborne sensed Adolph's conflict.

"Oh no! He's thinking about escaping!"

Osborne furrowed his brow. While he was confident he could drain Adolph's soul power here, he knew he couldn't stop Adolph if Adolph decided to flee.

The gap in their abilities was simply too vast.

He hadn't specifically trained for soul power; the strength he had was either a fluke or a gift. There was no way Osborne could block Adolph if he wanted to leave.

The thought of Adolph's soul power invading his Soul Current Ocean, attacking him, and then slipping away unscathed was unacceptable.

"I can't let him get away! If I can't stop his soul power from returning, then I'll just have to destroy his original form! If I obliterate his body, he won't be able to escape!"

With that realization, a spark ignited in Osborne's eyes.

He sprang into action.

A surge of soul power shot out from his Soul Current Ocean, disappearing into the void.

Meanwhile, on the floating mountain above East Zone's Wild Flame City, the atmosphere was eerily quiet. A group of Paramount Supreme Clan warriors were quietly discussing Adolph's absence.

One man said, "Why hasn't Mr. Adolph Eldridge returned yet?

"He's a second-tier Supernal Deity King of soul power.

"His detached soul power should easily handle taking down Theodore's soul power, right? Or could he have stumbled upon something amazing?"

They chuckled among themselves, unaware of the brewing storm.

They watched as Adolph sat cross-legged, eyes closed, with a flickering flame of golden fire dancing before him.

This was a fragment of Osborne's soul power.

Adolph's Soul Spark Oblivion was on the verge of incinerating it completely.

But just then, the flames surged with renewed intensity.

At the brink of annihilation, Osborne's soul power seemed to draw on some hidden reserve, flaring up with newfound vigor.

In the blink of an eye, it shot straight into Adolph's mind.

Adolph's original form was empty, a vast void.

Osborne's soul power slipped effortlessly into his Soul Current Ocean, and without hesitation, it self-destructed!

The silent explosion of soul power went unnoticed by Adolph's body.

However, in that instant, his Soul Current Ocean was decimated!

"No!" At that moment, Adolph's soul power, still lingering in Osborne's Soul Current Ocean and grappling with the decision to escape, felt the catastrophic shift in its original form. It erupted in a mix of terror and fury.

"You... you actually destroyed my Soul Current Ocean?

"You'll pay for this!"

Adolph's roar echoed with murderous intent directed at Osborne.

He realized that with his original Soul Current Ocean in ruins, there was no way back.

His only chance of survival now lay in obliterating Osborne's soul and seizing control of Osborne's Soul Current Ocean!
