Evelynn was proud but not pedantic. Noticing that Julia may not be a match for Osborne, she immediately called on the others to attack him together.

"You are quite arrogant!"

"How dare you! Even in Newport City, there are not many people as arrogant as you!"

"Go to hell!"

The downpour, controlled by Julia, became even more violent at that moment.

The rain became even more frightening, falling crackling and rippling on the martial art aura.

While manipulating Osborne's shadow to alter his physique, Charlie transformed into a giant and punched him wildly.

Evelynn activated Satan's Eye, employing her mind control to assault Osborne and projecting illusions into his mind. At the same time, she unleashed Devil's Light at level two against him.

Meanwhile, the other four immediately deployed all their abilities and exerted their utmost effort to bring down Osborne.

His martial art aura shook violently at that moment, as if it was about to break.

This sparked a glimmer of hope in the seven people, driving them to intensify their attack.

Osborne smiled calmly.

After fighting with Class AA masters like Chris and Bruce, he couldn't muster much interest in opponents like Evelynn, Julia, and Charlie, who were not even at Class A+.

"Damn it. He's much stronger than before!"

How formidable he was!

Even in Newport City, there were not many such individuals among the younger generation!

"You intend to take revenge on me, don't you?"
