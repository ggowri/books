In the end, only eight people chose to accompany Osborne to Celestara.

They were Grandfather Sebastian, sisters Anastasia and Zoe, siblings Bryce and Kendra, and two more: Yulissa and Nathan.

Interestingly, Yulissa could be considered a half-sister to Osborne; years ago, he had disguised himself as Yulissa's brother to infiltrate the Dampston Augustine family.

Even now, she still called him brother.

Osborne thought, "Just eight of us, plus the Blood Demon members, won't even total five thousand! Given this, it seems best to leave this Dimensional Microcosm for the Augustine family.

Should I create another one?

I still don't know what to expect in Celestara, and with Grandpa and the others still at a low level of strength, it's crucial to have a Dimensional Microcosm for safety.

Yes, I'll need to create another one."

Osborne set to work, constructing the new Dimensional Microcosm with ease, his experience making the process smooth. He didn't bother with excessive decorations; instead, he simply transferred the Mount of Gods Manor from the Pantheon League Dimensional Microcosm into the new one.

The Mount of Gods Manor was stunning, with breathtaking scenery at every turn, and it was spacious enough to accommodate thousands of people.

Once finished, he removed the old Dimensional Microcosm from the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity's petals and replaced it with the new one.

As for the Augustine family's Dimensional Microcosm vessel, he'd need to find a secure location for it.

Fortunately, Blue Planet was now safe enough. Once he eliminated the remaining members of the Eityreia Organization and the Dark Deity Legion, there would be no threats left to endanger the Augustine family's Dimensional Microcosm.

Two days passed swiftly.

Two days passed swiftly. The Blood Demon members had all completed their breakthroughs, with two thousand six hundred of them ascending to the rank of Superior Deity.

"It's time to deal with the Eityreia Organization and the Dark Deity Legion once and for all!" Osborne declared as he gathered the Blood Demon members and set out.

Before long, he met up with the members of the Prophet Alliance. With their assistance, they pinpointed the locations of the Eityreia Organization and the Dark Deity Legion's Dark Deities, one by one. The Blood Demon members swiftly dispatched them, eliminating the remnants of these forces without a hitch.

Faced with such overwhelming strength, the enemies had no chance to retaliate, only fleeing in desperation. It took the Blood Demon members a full week to track down and eliminate every last one of them.

Finally, the Prophet Alliance confirmed that no one from these two dominant factions remained alive on Blue Planet, and the extermination campaign came to a halt.

"Godfather, are you really planning to go to Celestara?" Eddie from the Prophet Alliance asked, surprised to hear about Osborne's imminent departure.

Osborne nodded. "Yes, Mr. Pike. Is there a problem?"

Eddie shook his head, then hesitated before saying, "I just didn't expect you to head to Celestara so soon! You seem confident; you must have a solid plan to open a dimensional portal to Celestara!"

"Absolutely," Osborne said.

Eddie continued, "In that case, I'd like to send you a few words of caution on behalf of the Prophet Alliance."

At this, Osborne's expression turned serious.

He had witnessed the Prophet Alliance's prophetic abilities firsthand, and he knew their warnings were not to be taken lightly.

They must have foreseen something significant for Eddie to relay such a message.

Perhaps these simple words could save his life in the future.

"Mr. Pike, please go ahead," he urged.

Eddie's demeanor grew somber as he gazed skyward, murmuring, "Once you reach Celestara, remember this: do not head west! For a whole year, stay away from the west, no matter what happens. If you come near, a great calamity awaits you!

"This warning comes from the Grand Elder of the Prophet Alliance, who insisted I pass it on to you as soon as I confirmed your journey to Celestara."

Osborne's expression grew even more serious at Eddie's words.

"The west? Did the Grand Elder foresee something? Can you elaborate?"

Eddie shook his head. "I can't say anything more!

"I don't know the details myself! All I know is that the Grand Elder insisted I warn you. For a whole year, steer clear of the west!"

He paused, then added, "There's one more thing the Grand Elder wanted me to tell you: be wary of the God of Time..."

At this, Osborne's demeanor hardened dramatically.

"Be wary of the God of Time?"

He mused, "The God of Time is Ophelia!

The Grand Elder of the Prophet Alliance is warning me to be cautious of Ophelia? What exactly has he foreseen?"

At that moment, a sense of foreboding washed over Osborne.

Despite the unease settling in his gut, his journey to Celestara was already set in motion. He knew danger awaited him, but he had no choice but to proceed.

Osborne nodded firmly. "Mr. Pike, please extend my gratitude to the Grand Elder for his warning. I will tread carefully."

With that, Osborne and Eddie parted ways.

Shortly after, he completed his preparations and set his sights on the journey ahead.
