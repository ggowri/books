Osborne's right leg had undergone a transformation, absorbing the vast blood energy of Dark Deity Mosley, evolving into what was known as a Divine Bone.

This Divine Bone was filled with divine power.

Now, as he tapped into its strength, he felt his power surge to more than ten times its usual might. The sheer intensity was staggering; even Osborne didn’t know just how devastating this kick would be.

At that moment, he unleashed the full force of his right leg against the last solar orb.

After a brief silence, the final solar orb blasted violently. Although it was fused with Marlon's body and contained immense power, it paled when compared with the might of Osborne's Divine Bone.

The orb exploded with a huge ferocity. Marlon, still connected to the orb, let out a horrific scream as the impact hit him like a freight train. He passed out before he could respond to the hit.

His bones shattered under the onslaught, and blood spewed from his mouth as he was thrown back, his body crumpling like a rag doll against the arena floor, twisted and unrecognizable.

At that instant, Marlon's Solar Realm crumbled around him.

Even in unconsciousness, he couldn't escape the mental backlash of its collapse, dark blood pooling at the corners of his mouth.

His body lay still as if he were lifeless.

The surroundings turned eerily quiet. All eyes were glued to the arena, watching as Osborne slowly took back his right leg.

The audience felt that their hearts were pounding with the weight of disbelief and shock.

Marlon was defeated!

Osborne won!

With Marlon's defeat, every young warrior on the Godson Rankings had fallen to Osborne.

He had cleared the Honor Quest Ceremony!

The audience was in a state of utter astonishment; even members of the Augustine family were too surprised to cheer.

Finally, someone whispered, "A legend has been born!"

His words ignited a spark that set off a wildfire of excitement throughout the venue. Cheers erupted, and the Augustine family members quickly regained their composure, faces flushed with joy.

"He did it! He won!"

"Osborne won! He defeated Marlon! He defeated everyone!"

"128 wins in a row! He's taken them all down!"

"He's a legend!"

"He's the first from the Augustine family to clear the Honor Quest Ceremony, the first from Rosmandi, the fourth in the world!"

"He won! Oh my gosh!"

Many members of the Augustine family were so overwhelmed with pride that tears streamed down their faces. Yet, even though their tears, they stood tall, feeling very proud.

Clarissa and Emma stared at Osborne, who stood bloodied and battered on the arena.

Osborne was shattered, but there was an undeniable strength in his posture.

Osborne stood tall, like a sword piercing the sky, radiating an aura of invincibility.

Despite his disheveled appearance, he seemed to embody a towering mountain, instilling awe in everyone around him.

Clarissa murmured, her eyes shining with excitement and pride for him, "Osborne, he did it.

"Osborne, you're getting stronger! Don't worry, I'll keep up! I'll work hard and chase after you!"

On the other side, Emma gazed at Osborne with admiration, her eyes sparkling with affection.

"It’s the man I chose!"

Caleb stood nearby, staring at Osborne in awe.

"I always knew he was something special, but I never imagined he'd grow this fast, climb this high. He's so far above me now that I can barely see him when I look up.

Is a legend about to be born, one that surpasses Godfather?"

Meanwhile, Wesson watched Osborne from the high stands, his expression a mix of disbelief. His expectations for Osborne had been modest—just fifty consecutive wins. After all, Osborne was the grandson of Sebastian, and Wesson thought that fifty victories would be a reasonable benchmark for someone worthy of being Sebastian's heir.

But Osborne had completely blown those expectations out of the water.

Fifty wins?

That was nothing!

Osborne had cleared the Honor Quest Ceremony and defeated every challenger in his path!

Wesson exclaimed softly, "Well!

Sebastian, your vision really was spot on!"

A smile crept across Wesson's face.

As Sebastian's butler, he was older than Sebastian, having witnessed the legendary figure achieve ninety-five consecutive wins in the Honor Quest Ceremony over forty years ago, cementing his place in the history of the Augustine family.

Now, Wesson was witnessing Osborne shine even brighter.

If Sebastian was a legend for the Augustine family, a beacon for Rosmandi, then Osborne was destined to be a legend for the entire world!

All the members of the Augustine family looked at Osborne, their faces glowing with pride and excitement.

But just then, a sudden surge of terrifying power erupted in the arena.

A voice roared, breaking the celebratory atmosphere,

"Go to hell!"
