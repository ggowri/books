Avery and Hermione were thrown backward, blood spraying from their mouths as they crashed onto the ground.

Meanwhile, the siblings Neville and Arabela turned their maniacal gaze toward Godfrey and Osborne.

With her cold blue eyes locked onto Osborne, Arabela launched a powerful attack, her palm slicing through the air with lethal intent.

"Watch out!"

Alfreda finally sensed something was terribly wrong. But with her attention divided as she fought off a swarm of Ghostwing Insects, she realized there was no time to intervene; Arabela was too close, and the assault was too swift.

Panic washed over her, leaving her pale and helpless.

Yet, amidst the turmoil, Osborne remained disturbingly calm.

As Arabela charged at him, he didn't flinch. Instead, he vanished from view, reappearing behind her in an instant.

With a swift motion, he grabbed her by the shoulder and delivered a sharp blow to the back of her neck.

"Ugh!"

Arabela let out a muffled groan as her body crumpled to the ground, unconscious.

Godfrey, however, wasn't so fortunate.

He sensed Neville's impending strike but couldn't evade it entirely. His armor shifted and contorted, forming a protective barrier behind him just in time to absorb most of the force.

Still, the impact sent him flying, blood spilling from his mouth as his complexion turned ghostly pale.

"Damn it!"

At that moment, Beverly appeared, her figure slicing through the chaos.

With a dance of her dagger, she effortlessly blocked every strike from Neville, forcing him into a defensive position.

Cyrus rushed in to assist Beverly, ready to help subdue Neville.

"Beverly, look out!" Osborne shouted, but it was too late.

Cyrus had already changed targets, unleashing a savage punch aimed straight at Beverly's back.

She sensed his movement but had no time to dodge.

Just then, a surge of spatial energy enveloped her.

The power whisked Beverly away, transporting her next to Osborne.

Cyrus' punch struck the ground, leaving behind a massive crater in the purple-black earth.

Beverly's heart raced, shock written all over her face.

"Thank you!" She turned to Osborne, awestruck.

She had once considered him a burden in their team but never imagined he would be the one to save her in this moment of danger.

Then her gaze fell on Arabela, who was now unconscious at Osborne's feet.

Shock surged through her.

Arabela was an Ascension Four Supernal Deity King, a powerhouse who had nearly devastated Avery just moments ago. And yet, here she lay—taken down by Osborne?

"How did he manage that?" she wondered inwardly.

"Now's not the time; we need to deal with the crisis at hand!" Osborne urged, pressing on.

Cyrus and Neville were locked in a fierce battle, both unleashing everything they had in a duel that could very well end in death for one of them.

"What the hell is going on?" Beverly asked, her brow furrowed in confusion.

Osborne glanced at the towering mushroom trees, his expression shifting to one of gravity. "If I'm right, these mushroom trees are behind this madness! They've released a cloud of spores—spores that have a potent hallucinogenic effect!

"Neville, Arabela, and Cyrus—none of them possess strong enough soul power, and they fell victim to the madness!"

Their team was faring better than many others, but that was hardly a silver lining.

Among the nine of them, only Neville, Arabela, and Cyrus had succumbed to the hallucinogenic spores from the mushroom trees.

But the situation for the other exploration teams was dire.

Many of their members were completely out of control, driven mad by the spores and attacking their own comrades.

The relentless assault of the Ghostwing Insects compounded the chaos, resulting in a steady stream of casualties.

Beverly echoed, her face pale with concern, "Mushroom tree spores?

"Potent hallucinogenic effect?

"Any Supernal Deity King has a powerful soul power. Yet, the spores of these mushroom trees can plunge them into illusions and madness... This hallucinogenic effect is truly formidable!"

Osborne shook his head, frustration etched on his features. "I've already tried to bring them back to their senses, but it's impossible.

"And as time goes on, we'll be affected too, eventually succumbing to the madness ourselves.

"Even if we don't end up killing each other, we'll be overwhelmed by the Ghostwing Insects!

"The best course of action now is to knock them out and get out of this mushroom forest as quickly as possible. Otherwise, we'll all be caught in this nightmare."

He glanced over at Alfreda, who was valiantly fighting off a swarm of Ghostwing Insects.

Though she was powerful, the sheer number of insects was beginning to overwhelm her, and the tension on her face was evident.

Their nine-member team was on the brink of collapse, teetering on the edge of disaster.

With Neville, Arabela, and Cyrus lost to the madness, and Godfrey, Hermione, and Avery injured, only Alfreda, Beverly, and Osborne remained capable of fighting.

"We need to get out of this mushroom forest immediately!" Osborne insisted.

Beverly nodded, understanding the urgency. "Osborne, you stay safe. I'll try to knock out Neville and Cyrus!"

"Trying to knock them out could put you in serious danger. If they turn their attention to you, it could end badly."

Beverly's expression hardened as she clenched her jaw. "I don't know if I can do it, but it's our only option. We're a team, and we can't just abandon them!

"No matter what, I have to try!"

With determination in her eyes, she prepared to leap into action.

Osborne stepped in front of her, his tone calm but firm. "Hold on. I'm going with you."

"Wait, you?" Beverly looked at him, bewildered.

Osborne smiled, "What? You don't believe in my abilities?"

She replied, her brow furrowed in concern, "It's not that I don't believe in you. But your cultivation level is too low! You're only Ascension One, while they're Ascension Five! They're in a frenzy, and every move they make is with full force. It's too dangerous for you to be anywhere near them!"

Osborne chuckled, "Is that so? I wouldn't be so sure about that.

"No one said an Ascension One Supernal Deity King can't hold their own against an Ascension Five."

With that, he strode confidently toward Neville and Cyrus.
