With a cry of surprise, everyone stood up and looked out the door.

Godfather, the head of the Augustine family, was also the boss of the Draco Group and the king of the underground world in Rosmandi.

However, in recent years, he had nearly ceased all involvement in family affairs. Although he lived in the Augustine Estate, his residence, Mount Celestial Reach, had been designated as a forbidden area.

Even the elders were not allowed to enter there.

As a result, the family members had few chances to meet Godfather. They learned about him mostly from legends and the fact that he was the reason the Augustine family became the top ancient family in Rosmandi.

At that moment, his 80th birthday party was about to begin.

He was finally about to show up amid everyone's expectant eyes.

Even Vincent, Matthew, Owen, Emma, and Lance all rose to their feet obediently and stared at the door with great anticipation.

But the door didn’t budge at all.

When everyone was confused, a faint, elderly voice suddenly sounded from behind them.

"Make yourself at home. Sit down."

Stunned, they all turned quickly, only to see that Godfather had taken his place in the middle.​

None of the thousands of people present noticed it.

Vincent, Matthew, and the others glanced at each other in shock.

There were many top Class AAA masters.

But none of them noticed how Godfather had come in and appeared in the middle.

They didn't dare to think too much and quickly greeted him.

"Godfather!"

"Godfather!"

Godfather waved his hand.

It wasn’t until then that everyone dared to size him up seriously.

He appeared quite elderly, yet his eyes remained sharp. Gazing at him was like observing a bulldog that never quit.

He lounged comfortably on the soft seat, holding a sleek black cat in one hand and gently stroking it with his fingers. With his slicked-back hair, black suit, and black bow tie, he looked like a gentleman.

Next to him was a black and gold scepter.

Vincent, Matthew, and the others fixed their eyes on the scepter, and their breathing immediately became heavy.

They knew that whoever obtained the scepter could become Godfather's successor, inheriting all his powers and becoming the new Godfather.

Godfather glanced at everyone and smiled, "It's just a birthday celebration, which happens every year. Thank you for making it so grand. In this case, let's get started."

"Let me be clear, I don't accept gifts! No matter whose gift it is, don't bring it up. Otherwise, leave on your own."

His request surprised many people.

The people present had all carefully picked some gifts for Godfather, but they did not dare to disobey him and could only put away the gifts.

"Sebastian, why didn't you invite me to your birthday party? I came here uninvited. You're not going to send me away, are you?"

Just as the birthday party was about to start, an exaggerated laugh suddenly echoed from outside the door. The members of the Augustine family instantly grew furious.

This was Godfather's birthday party. Who dared to laugh so wildly? How dared he call Godfather by his name?

They were all baffled.

All members of the Augustine family turned their gaze to the door.

A group of people strolled over.

The leader was Everett Rosenthal, a man around Godfather’s age.

He was tall, like a giant, exuding a powerful aura. He ignored everyone else in the Augustine family and focused solely on Godfather with a smile.

He directed his sharp, sword-like gaze at Godfather.

Behind him was a group of young men and women.

Despite their different appearances, each exuded an extremely formidable aura and was brimming with arrogance. They responded to the Augustine family's glares with disdain and contempt.

Godfather frowned at Everett.

He narrowed his eyes and stared straight into Everett's eyes. Suddenly, everyone sensed a chill and a terrifying wave of power.

Finally, Everett and Godfather snorted coldly in unison, and the tension eased.

Godfather said, "Everett, I didn't expect you to come to Rosmandi. You should have told me earlier. After all, it's my place, and I should have picked you up."

Everett smiled, "Sebastian, I dare not ask you to pick me up. You are the Godfather of Rosmandi. I don't deserve that. But I heard that it is your birthday, so I couldn't help but come over. You won't chase us away, right?"

Godfather replied, "Please take a seat!"

Then he had a row of seats arranged opposite him for Everett and his men.

Everyone in the Augustine family was dumbfounded.

They stared at Everett and his men seriously, guessing the identity of this person.

Judging from Godfather’s attitude, they could tell that he dared not offend the uninvited guest.

"I know who he is!"

At that moment, Lance suddenly said solemnly, "He is Everett Windsor, the legendary master from Yalynch and the head of the Windsor family, the most powerful divine family there! He and Godfather are both Class S Supreme Overlords!"

Everyone was shocked, looking at Everett in disbelief.
