Osborne continued to explore the new ability of Satan’s Eye. He wanted to see if he could control bullets.

Osborne chose to use the Draco Model H mini-handgun for testing because this gun was known to have the fastest bullet speed. It was three times faster than ordinary guns.

“If I can catch the bullets from the Draco Model H mini-handgun, other pistols, guns and rifles will no longer be a threat to me.”

Osborne took a deep breath and pulled the trigger hard.

The bullet flew out and shot toward the distant target at a speed of nearly 666 feet per second, which was extremely terrifying.

The control range of Satan’s Eye was only 33 feet.

In other words, Osborne must react quickly within 0.05 seconds and use the ocular energy to capture and control the bullet.

Other people wouldn’t react in 0.05 seconds.

But Osborne was different. Satan’s Eye could slow down the speed of moving objects. Especially after level three, this ability became stronger.

At this moment, in Osborne’s eyes, the bullet with terrifying speed was shooting towards the target in the distance at a slow speed. He had enough time to react and capture.

The bullet had only traveled less than three feet. The ocular energy burst out and covered the bullet. It was fixed steadily in mid-air.

“I did it…” Osborne was surprised. Using Satan’s Eye to catch the bullet actually worked. It was much easier than he had imagined.

Osborne felt that as long as there were not more than a dozen guns shooting at him at close range at the same time, he could use Satan’s Eye to control all the bullets.

This was a huge improvement in his strength.

His safety would be greatly improved. He could also foresee danger. It would be impossible for anyone to shoot him from behind.

Osborne was full of confidence in his current strength.

Although Osborne was good before, he was not considered a top master. Osborne was not sure he could deal with Isa, Donna, or even Clarissa’s bodyguard captain, Paul.

But now, Osborne was no longer afraid of these people.

Thinking of Clarissa, Osborne couldn’t help but frown.

“I have completed my second successor test. I don’t need to be Clarissa’s bodyguard anymore. When it’s daybreak, I will notify the Radcliffe family that I won’t be coming over.” Osborne made a decision.

Then he thought, “I became Clarissa’s bodyguard for the purpose of completing the successor test. Now that the test is over. I have no interest in being a bodyguard for others. Although Clarissa’s status is noble, I am not bad either now. I have more than 600 million dollars in cash in my account. Luna Jewelry is worth billions of dollars. If I reveal my true identity now, I will be considered a big shot in Mayby City.

“Why would I be Clarissa’s bodyguard? Just because she is beautiful? Every time I see her, my heart beats faster. And, I admit that Clarissa is indeed wonderful. Not only does she have a powerful background and identity, but she also has terrifying supernatural talents. She is an unprecedented possessor of three supernatural powers.

“But being Clarissa’s bodyguard is too dangerous. I am still frightened by yesterday’s attack, especially the metahuman who could control lightning attacks and the dozen or so werewolves. If I stay with Clarissa, I will inevitably face such dangers in the future. I don’t want to cause trouble for myself.”

Osborne made up his mind.

“I’ll contact the Radcliffe family at dawn and tell them that I can’t be Clarissa’s bodyguard anymore.”

But at this moment, his Draco Star Wristlet suddenly vibrated. Osborne looked down and was stunned.

The message showed, [The third test of succession begins! Within seven days, you will become the captain of Clarissa’s bodyguards.]

“What?” When Osborne saw the requirements of the third successor test, he was stunned.

He wondered, Godfather asked me to become Clarissa’s bodyguard captain within seven days? How is this possible? I have confidence in myself. But the time is too short. This is too difficult. The Radcliffe family has no trust in me at all. No matter how strong I am, how can they give me the position of bodyguard captain?

Osborne frowned tightly.

He had just decided not to be Clarissa’s bodyguard. But Godfather gave him a new test. He even suspected that Godfather was monitoring him somewhere.

Osborne smiled bitterly, “Fortunately, I haven’t resigned from the Radcliffe family yet. Otherwise, it would be another trouble.”

The new test for the successor was to become the captain of Clarissa’s bodyguards. Osborne could only accept it because he was not qualified to negotiate terms with Godfather.

Moreover, he had his reasons for having to complete the test.

Osborne began to analyze the success rate of completing the test.

“If I want to become Clarissa’s bodyguard captain, I must first gain the Radcliffe family’s absolute trust. I must also find a way to get rid of the Captain Paul and defeat all competitors. I only have seven days. It’s really too difficult.”
