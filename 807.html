He had little interest in weapons shaped like great swords.

Osborne preferred his own fists or weapons like axes, scythes, and spears. Besides, Abner's divine artifact was merely an Ordinary divine artifact, and any of Osborne's own artifacts were stronger.

Still, it was a divine artifact, and even if Osborne had no use for it, he could always gift it to someone else.

Smiling faintly, Osborne said, "I'm feeling a bit tired now. I'll head back and rest. There's no need for you to see me off."

"Damn it! This jerk is treating our place like a vacation spot!"

"We're dying to make him pay, and he's still lounging around, soaking up the sun? Doesn't he have any respect for us?"

"Does he think he can just disrespect the Williamson family like this??"

"Unbelievable!"

Someone with a cold voice said, "Don't worry, everyone. We've suffered a huge loss, and we won't let him get away with it. He's probably sticking around because he knows he can't leave yet and is waiting for reinforcements."
