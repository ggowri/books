Although Medusa used Death Ash, she paid a huge price.

She could barely stand at this moment.

Her face was incredibly pale. She was trembling slightly, looking so fragile that even a baby could push her over.

When Medusa saw Osborne turn to stone, she sneered.

"Kill him quickly. Don’t let him live!" Medusa said, struggling .

"You don't need to say that. We'll kill him!"

Titan shouted, unleashing his ultimate move as soon as Osborne was petrified.

His massive body turned bright red, as if it were on fire. After gathering power for a long time, all the red light in his body rushed into his right arm.

His right fist now resembled a piece of burning rock, radiating a fiery glow. He then smashed it toward Osborne.

"Self-Sacrificing Punch!"

Boom!

Titan's entire right arm detached from his body, hurtling toward Osborne like a massive missile.

Osborne, being petrified, couldn't dodge at all.

He could only desperately unleash the martial art aura, layering defensive barriers before him. He hoped to weaken the terrifying attack from Titan, who had sacrificed his entire right arm.

Bang!

In the next second, Osborne's expression changed. All his defenses instantly crumbled.

Titan's Self-Sacrificing Punch was far more powerful than Osborne had anticipated, much stronger than Tauren's Bull Charge.

In an instant, Titan's right arm broke through his defenses and slammed into his chest.

Bang!

The violent impact caused Titan's right arm to shatter completely.

But the terrifying force had already surged into Osborne's petrified body. His body was instantly covered in countless cracks, and his internal organs were nearly shattered.

He suffered extremely severe injuries.

Osborne's body was covered in blood. He was on the brink of collapse.

"Still not dead?" Titan exclaimed in shock when he saw this.

He glanced at his left arm but ultimately decided not to sacrifice it.

"Werewolf No. Nine, it's your turn!"

Werewolf nodded. "Don't worry. He's already on his last legs. He's done for!"

After that, Werewolf's body suddenly swelled.

Like a balloon inflating, his body instantly transformed into a terrifying giant wolf, over thirty feet long.

His massive jaws opened wide, seemingly capable of swallowing a house.

Saliva dripped from his mouth, and his sharp teeth gleamed coldly. His claws were like blades, long and sharp, as if they could easily slice an elephant in half.

With one leap, he landed in front of Osborne and pinned him down.

Osborne was in a terrible state.

After being fully petrified by Medusa and taking Titan's Self-Sacrificing Punch, he survived, which was already a miracle.

But now, he was on the verge of death.

Even his consciousness was starting to fade.

He could only see Werewolf, now a giant wolf, standing over him. His claws pinned him down, and he was looking at him like a piece of meat ready to be devoured.

Werewolf's eyes were filled with contempt and mockery.

"You're finally about to die! Ultimately, dying by my ultimate move should make you proud! Die! Death Claw!"

Werewolf raised his claw, which glowed red, and slashed down at Osborne with all his might.

At that moment, Osborne felt the presence of death looming over him.

In his near-death state, there was no way he could defend himself. Even at his peak, he might not have been able to withstand Werewolf's ultimate move.

As Osborne watched Werewolf's massive claw rapidly descending. A wild glint flashed in his eyes.

"Light of Redemption!" He shouted.

Instantly, a beam of light descended from the sky, enveloping Osborne's body in a radiance filled with life and divine energy.

In a split second, all of Osborne's negative conditions, his near-fatal injuries, and his depleted strength were fully restored.

The Light of Redemption cleansed the petrification effect from Medusa.

The severe injury inflicted by Titan's sacrificed arm was instantly healed as if Osborne had never been hurt.

Osborne's strength, worn down by the prolonged battle, was restored to its peak.

And all of this happened in an instant.

Osborne went from the brink of death to peak condition in an instant. The martial art aura surged, and he launched a powerful punch at Werewolf's Death Claw.

Bang!

A terrifying force erupted, and the ground split into several deep cracks. It was carved by Werewolf's Death Claw.
