At this moment, Angela and Agatha finally arrived on this resource-bearing planet.

At this moment, the family gathered together, seemingly with endless things to say. Angela and Agatha couldn't get a word in. So, Osborne simply asked them to gather all the slaves on this resource-bearing planet.

Osborne explained his situation.

He didn’t hide anything from Vivienne and Theodore, telling them about his plans to participate in the Glorious Divine Kingdom Duel, to pay respects to his mother from his past life, and to avenge her.

"Osborne, you've grown up now. Your strength is beyond our imagination! But the one you seek revenge against is even stronger! We won't stop you from seeking revenge! After all, to you, who has regained the memories of your past life, she is just as much your mother as I am!

On Racial Convergence Planet, building a Cosmic Translocator in a city would take many people working together for several days.

But Osborne only took one minute!

"If Osborne were a bit stronger, he might be able to build a Cosmic Translocator with just a wave of his hand in the future!"

She didn't have Osborne's talent.

She didn't know that building a Cosmic Translocator with a wave of his hand was exactly his goal. That way, he could use it while escaping.

"Alright! Then let's set off!"
