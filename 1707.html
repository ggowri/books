At that moment, Zoe chimed in, "Anastasia, come on, stop being so modest!

"Your talent is downright extraordinary. It's the kind of talent that makes me envious! But you really don't need to compare yourself to Osborne. He's on a whole different level!"

Her gaze held a hint of envy as she looked at Anastasia. Despite being identical twins, there was a significant gap in their abilities.

Anastasia's brilliance overshadowed Zoe's own.

"You're at the peak of Deity Overlord now! Back when you were a Junior Deity, you had a Level Five Divine Body, and now you've reached the pinnacle of Level Six. Just a little more time, and you'll definitely hit Level Seven!

"Once you break through to Deity King, your Divine Body will transform again, and you might even achieve a Level Eight Galactic Divine Body! You're a prodigy in your own right!

She knew she could be considered a prodigy, but with Osborne and Anastasia shining so brightly, she felt somewhat "ordinary" in comparison.

In reality, Zoe's talent was among the very best in Celestara!

Even the top seven Deity Kings of the Human Community would be hard-pressed to match her innate abilities.

Otherwise, how could Isa have chosen her as a disciple?

Although Isa had only taught Zoe for a short time, if Zoe didn't have the talent, Isa wouldn't have poured so much effort into her training.

In short, Anastasia was indeed a prodigy.

Not just her, but also Osborne's grandfather Sebastian, along with Bryce, Kendra, and Mila, they all belonged to the ranks of exceptional talents.

They had all forged Level Five Divine Bodies while still Junior Deities.

Although, aside from Mila, the others had used Phoenix Ascension when they broke through to enhance their Divine Bodies.

However, regardless of their other attributes, their Divine Bodies are undeniably formidable.

"Logically, when breaking through to Superior Deity or Transcendent Deity, a Divine Body should undergo a transformation, typically increasing by one level. Yet, when Anastasia, Zoe, Grandpa, Bryce, and Kendra experienced their second transformation, their Divine Bodies didn't improve as expected!

"It looks like Mila's innate talent is the highest among them all! Grandpa and the others seem to have relied on Phoenix Ascension to force their way up!

Celestial Crystals can enhance their potential, and they'll catch up eventually!"

Zoe's face lit up with joy at his words.

Like Zoe, Bryce and Kendra were at the peak of Deity Overlord, boasting Level Six Divine Bodies and elemental essence also at Level Six.

They hadn't rushed to break through to Deity King yet, wanting to wait until their Divine Bodies reached Level Seven.

Their strength was comparable to Zoe's.

Bryce nodded, still grinning.

"Don't worry, we won't hold you back! Sure, we might not be on the same level as someone like you or even Anastasia and Mila, but I think we're pretty solid. And Kendra? She's definitely stronger than I am!"

At that, Kendra's cheeks flushed with a hint of embarrassment.

Just then, another voice chimed in.

"Osborne, I want to stay outside, too, and I want to be by your side."

It was Mila!
