Chapter 228 Gale Fainted

Gale was already drowsy. The hours of surgery were excruciating for him.​

Noticing that it was Paula's voice, he immediately opened his eyes and saw her gazing at him tearfully.

"Mom, I..."

Paula wiped her tears and said, "Gale, I'll take you to see a good show, and then you'll know how wrong you are.

"Afterwards, let's sever ties with each other. Your stupidity will kill you."

Paula called the bodyguards to push a wheelchair over and asked them to lift Gale into it.

She was serious. Gale instantly panicked. "Mom, I didn't give you the shares. It was my fault, but I wanted Penelope and our baby to live a better life. She is pregnant with my child, which is also your grandchild..."

"Shut up! I don't want to hear your nonsense. Whatever you hear or see, be quiet. Go abroad in three days. Your life depends on your luck."

Paula instructed the bodyguards to wheel the drowsy Gale out.

In the emergency exit not far away, Penelope glared at Queenie. "Queenie, Gale is disabled now. Do you want me to marry him?

"Also, was it really Diana who injured his legs?"

Queenie cast a cold, sharp glance at her and said arrogantly, "It doesn't matter whether it was Diana or not. Most importantly, they had a conflict three days ago, and Gale sent her design drawings to Emma.

"Diana wished Gale would die, and this was her motive.

"Moreover, Penelope, you aren't pregnant with his child at all. I help you find a father for the baby, so you should just restrain yourself.

"Why did you question me?"

Penelope sneered, "It seems that I am also your pawn. Half a year ago, you used me to frame Diana and Althea..."

"Penelope, shut up. I did that for your own good. Gale gives you a huge sum of money every month as pocket money.

"But you spent it on supporting your gigolo. Now, Gale's disabled. You should take good care of him."

"Queenie, I will marry Gale on the condition that he is healthy and can make money for me, but he is now disabled. What can he give me? Nothing. I will give birth to the baby in my belly. At worst, I will raise him myself.

"Do you want me to serve a cripple? Dream on."

Penelope turned around and left angrily.

Queenie sneered and went directly to the first floor to see the investigation into Diana.

Gale's face was in tears. He trembled all over. Great pain and sorrow overwhelmed him.

It turned out that everything was a carefully planned scam.

The ones who did him harm were Queenie and Penelope, but not Diana and Althea.

Why was Penelope, a lively and lovely girl, so cruel to him?

He was really, really stupid!

Penelope even used his money to support a gigolo.

Gale, unable to bear such a blow, fainted, tears at the corners of his eyes. It was not known whether this was due to physical or psychological pain.

Paula wept silently. She beckoned the bodyguards over and asked them to take Gale back to the ward.

Downstairs, Diana and Luna were assisting the police with their investigation.
