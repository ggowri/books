Chapter 334 Assault

Cecil was still burning up, but his mind was clear.

He leaned against Diana's chest, feeling the softness and inhaling her sweet scent.

The scent was enchanting and made him feel at ease.

He turned his head slightly, accidentally pressing his face against Diana's soft spot.

His handsome face flushed even more, but Diana didn't seem to notice.

Steven and Alexander, however, did notice.

Steven's gaze darkened, wanting Diana to let go of Cecil.

Alexander pulled Cecil up. "Kid, get up and explain yourself."

Cecil looked confused. "Say what? About what?"

"Diana, I saw you enter the room, so I followed. What's going on with me?"

Diana followed Rita's words directly. "Queenie drugged you to seduce you. You were drugged."

"Damn, these bitches! Isn't serving Mr. Foliot enough? Now she wants to seduce me too?" Cecil stomped his foot in anger but felt weak and slumped onto a seat across from Diana.

He stared at Queenie, shocked. "What's going on? Why are you wearing the same outfit as Diana, even with the same hairstyle?

"Diana, I only came in because I saw you enter the room."

Diana quickly turned to Queenie, stunned. Queenie's outfit was very similar to hers, even down to the hairstyle.

Queenie's scheme to frame Cecil was indeed cunning.

Diana glared at Queenie's deceitful face. "Queenie, you weren't wearing this outfit earlier.

"You put on a wig and the same dress as mine, lured Cecil into the room, and now you're accusing him of assaulting you? Queenie, do you have no shame?"

Queenie almost lost her balance at Cecil's last words.

Her face turned pale. The plan was to lure both Steven and Cecil here with her outfit.

Coincidentally, Steven was an unexpected helper. The outfit he prepared in his car was quite similar to Diana's.

The moment she got the clothes and distanced herself from Jace, she was surprised at how things seemed to go her way.

She approached Steven, determined to make Diana despise him completely.

"Diana, Steven prepared this outfit for me. I just wore the wig to complete the hairstyle. It was Steven's thoughtful gesture, so I couldn't refuse.

"But Cecil's assault on me happened. You weren't here earlier, but Steven and Mr. Foliot saw it with their own eyes.

"Mr. Foliot has already called the police. I want justice for myself, but you're turning it around, accusing me of seducing Cecil? Why would I seduce him? At an event like this, would I joke about my reputation?

"Diana, you've always taken pleasure in framing me. Everyone saw what happened. I'd like to see how you can falsely accuse me now."

Queenie's long lashes were covered with tears, making her look delicate. At this moment, she cried uncontrollably.

She looked both beautiful and fragile, pale and weak, her slender figure evoking pity.

Diana had to admit that Queenie played the innocent role to perfection.

It took Cecil a moment to grasp what Queenie meant. Pointing to himself, he stared at her. "You're saying I tried to assault you?"
