Osborne suddenly topped the points scoreboard, making his five rivals feel the pressure and begin taking new actions to increase their points quickly.

As the war had just begun, Osborne ordered everyone to retreat to Campbell and let the Aberrants take control of Dampston, ensuring the strength of his team remained intact.

They even found themselves bored at the headquarters in Campbell.

Caleb and Elder Janet of the Augustine family found their daily amusement in observing the people at Training Base No. 6 as doing various training sessions at the headquarters.

"Caleb, what do you think of these people?" Janet asked.

Caleb said solemnly, "Although these people refuse to reveal their identities, I know that they are the Godfather's secret armed forces! I never expected them to appear here and become Osborne's men."

"Even we, elders of the Augustine family, cannot come into contact with the Godfather's secret armed forces, let alone command them. Neither could those outstanding young people like Vincent, Matthew, and Emma.

"So, who is Osborne exactly?"

Janet glared at him and said, "If you have something to say, just say it directly. Don't ask me."

Caleb smiled and suggested, "Why don't we exchange our speculations? Let's see if our guesses are the same."

After a moment of thought, Janet nodded, "Alright."

"I don't think Osborne is from the branch bloodline in Dampston. He has a deeper connection with the Godfather!"

After finishing their sentences, they exchanged glances.

"It seems that we are on the same page."

Taking a deep breath, Caleb continued, "On the surface, Osborne is the son of Allen, the former head of the branch in Dampston, and the grandson of Elder Isaac. But after observing and investigating for some time, I am almost certain that Isaac is not Osborne's biological grandfather! In fact, Osborne isn't even from the branch bloodline in Dampston.

"The identity of Osborne as the heir of the branch bloodline in Dampston is just a disguise!"

"But his Satan's Eye can't be faked!"

"Therefore, he is definitely a member of our Augustine family, perhaps even from the main bloodline. It is even possible that he has a direct blood relationship with the Godfather."

"Are you saying that Osborne might be the Godfather's biological grandson?"

Caleb nodded. "Do you remember Theodore, the Godfather's only son? Over twenty years ago, Theodore and his wife Vivienne disappeared together. The Godfather searched for them for over twenty years and never gave up.

Janet looked puzzled. "So, Osborne could be Theodore and Vivienne's child, the Godfather's biological grandson? The Godfather's sole heir?

"Unfortunately, Osborne is still a bit too young. If he were a few years older, perhaps he could really compete with Vincent and Matthew.

"But now, it will be difficult!"

"Mr. Caleb Augustine, something has happened!

"Osborne's points suddenly skyrocketed by five thousand!

"Osborne has topped the points scoreboard!"

"What?"

Caleb was especially embarrassed to hear the news. He had just mentioned how Osborne was too young to compete with Vincent and Matthew.

But now, right after saying that, Osborne's points suddenly increased by five thousand, pushing him to the top of the scoreboard.

Caleb quickly checked the scoreboard.

Seeing Osborne dominate the scoreboard with three thousand points, he took a deep breath.

"How did he do that?

"This kid stayed alone in Dampston without telling us the reason. Previously, it was shocking enough when he obtained one thousand points from ambushing two rare metal mining areas. And now, he has gained five thousand points?

"Could it be that he killed five Class AAA fighters from the Aberrants?"

Janet glared at him. "What are you looking at me for? Contact Osborne and ask him what's going on! We have reached the top, and regardless of how Osborne achieved it, it's good news for us."

Caleb nodded and began to contact Osborne.
