Osborne looked at everyone solemnly.

He announced, "The Aberrants will return to Rosmandi and start a bloody war!"

"I am a member of the Augustine family, in charge of defending the Augustine family and the Draco Group's industries and resisting the attacks of the Aberrants. So, I need your assistance now. Are you willing to fight for me?"

Finishing speaking, Osborne looked at everyone seriously.

At this moment, everyone's expressions became excited.

They were at Training Base No. 6, where they had been training in secret for over 20 years. Therefore, they couldn't wait to engage in real combat.

Now, the opportunity had come.

Suddenly, 20 instructors and five hundred trainees all knelt on one knee before Osborne.

They shouted, "We are!"

Immediately, he smiled.

Not long after, Osborne returned to Mount Seven.

All instructors and trainees at Training Base No. 6 would be led by No. 1 and rush to Dampston in the shortest time possible.

However, they needed some time to prepare.

Training Base No. 6 not only consisted of instructors and trainees but also housed a wide array of weapons and equipment. There were indeed over a dozen sets of Class A defense systems alone.

In addition, there were a large number of Draco Group's most advanced combat robots and combat drones.

For Osborne, all of these were unexpected pleasant surprises.

The combination of the instructors and trainees with these advanced weapon systems was what allowed them to unleash their full potential.

"Training Base No. 6 is much more affluent than Skyefall Security Services! It's a good opportunity to have them join Skyefall Security Services.

"With them, Skyefall Security Services will undoubtedly be considered one of the top security companies in Rosmandi! Which other security company can boast such a large number of skilled individuals and advanced weaponry?" Osborne murmured.

Thinking of this, he felt a little proud.

Osborne returned to Third Estate and thought, "Owen thought that I had no backing and support, which is why he dared to be so arrogant. He will surely have an interesting expression as he sees No. 1 and the others I have on my side."

At this moment, on Mount Seven, the atmosphere had become somewhat calm.

The nine-branch bloodlines of the Augustine family would also participate in the upcoming war against the Aberrants.

And they each had their own heir-in-waiting whom they supported.

The branch bloodlines of Marcelin and Ransford both supported Lance.

Matthew recruited the other six branch bloodlines.

Osborne felt helpless about this.

Matthew's methods were indeed powerful. Not only did he have the most elders supporting him, but he also won over many branch bloodlines, which became his supporters.

"Although I also have the support of Training Base No. 6, it is still far behind the support of Vincent, Matthew, and even Emma!"

"Even Lance has the support of branch bloodlines of Yalynch, Marcelin, and Ransford. The combined strength of these three branch bloodlines is definitely stronger than that of Training Base No. 6."

"Indeed, winning over them and securing the first place in the selection of the heir is undoubtedly a challenging task for me."

His expression became solemn again.

"I must continue to enhance my strength to have a glimmer of hope of winning!"

Finishing thinking about it, Osborne walked into Third Estate.

He prepared to pack up and rush back to Dampston.

But there was still one thing he hadn't done, so he was unwilling to go back now.

Yet, this matter was not up to him to decide.

"He will come, right?" Osborne murmured.

He greeted them and instructed them to pack their belongings.

Osborne then proceeded alone to the top floor of the mansion, absorbing the star power to enhance his strength while patiently waiting.

He believed that the person he was waiting for would definitely come.

Osborne didn't know how long it had been. But he was suddenly startled and felt something unusual.

He turned quickly and looked behind him.

Then, Osborne saw an old figure who had appeared behind him at some point.

This old figure held a black cat in one hand and a gold scepter in the other.

It was the Godfather!

Osborne's breathing suddenly became rapid.

Finally, the Godfather came to meet him.

"So, you know that I was coming?"

The Godfather spoke, and at this moment, his expression was no longer as serious as it was at the birthday party but with a faint smile.

Osborne took a deep breath and said calmly, "You once said that when I completed the fifth test you set for the heir, you would personally come to see me! I believe that if you cannot even fulfill this promise, then you don't deserve to be my grandfather!"

As he finished speaking, the Godfather was stunned.

He didn't seem to expect Osborne to say this.

Ever since he became the underground king of Rosmandi and became the Godfather, no younger generation of the Augustine family dared to speak to him like this.

After a long time, the Godfather sighed, "You're right. I'm not a qualified grandfather or a qualified father. You must have a lot of resentment, right? You must also have a lot of questions about your parents that you want to ask me, right?"

"Now, you are strong enough to know everything! If you want to know anything, just ask me, and I will tell you."

Hearing that, Osborne trembled.

Was he finally qualified to know everything about his parents?

Thinking of this, Osborne clenched his fists.

He stared at the Godfather and asked his first question.
