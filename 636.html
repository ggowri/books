He already had a good sense of the Aberrants' strength. They were indeed formidable, and their attacks on various provincial headquarters were beyond what he could currently handle.

However, Osborne wasn't powerless.

The Aberrants had taken over Draco Group's assets and had to leave some forces behind to guard them.

These guards wouldn't be very strong.

Osborne was ready to stir up trouble.

"Now, the roles have reversed. Defense is always passive. Only attack is the way to go," Osborne murmured, tearing open a dimensional rift with a wave of his hand.

He stepped into the dimensional rift, heading for a rare metal mining area.

This mining area had been taken by the Aberrants a few hours earlier, but before that, Osborne had already evacuated everyone, so there had been no casualties.

Now, Osborne aimed to reclaim the mine.

At this point, he had reached Class AA and could control spatial power far beyond his previous level. Each dimensional shift could cover over 30 miles.

After several interdimensional travels, Osborne arrived at his destination.

It was a rare metal mine controlled by the Draco Group.

Osborne didn't know the exact type of rare metal, but he knew that the Draco Group placed great importance on these mines. They controlled all the rare metals in Rosmandi.

"Let's see how many enemies are still here," Osborne murmured, using his dark elemental power to blend into the shadows and cautiously enter the mine.

With no battle having erupted, everything in the mine seemed normal, except that the machines had stopped working.

Osborne soon picked up on the scent of enemies.

Activating his Satan's Eye, he quickly spotted a dozen or so enemy figures scattered around the mine.

These figures were at least Class A in strength.

Osborne couldn't confirm whether there was any Class AA among them without taking action.

"The Aberrants left only a handful of people to guard the mine? Even if there are Class AA among them, with my current strength, I don't need to worry. I can either eliminate them or easily retreat if needed." Osborne's eyes gleamed with a cold, murderous intent. "But I must act quickly. I need to strike and leave immediately, without getting entangled. Otherwise, if more Aberrants, or even a Class AAA, show up, I might not be able to escape."

With this thought, Osborne decided to go for a stealthy approach.

He opened the dimensional space and took out a scythe.
