From her eyes, two beams of radiant light unexpectedly shot forth.

The light petrified space itself as it passed.

Osborne's face was drained of color, and all thoughts of Venom Lord had been forgotten. He tore open a dimensional rift swiftly in front of Clarissa.

The rift opened like a monstrous maw, attempting to devour the two beams of light into another dimension.

But Osborne's attempt was in vain.

Upon contact with the rift, the beams didn't vanish; instead, it was instantly turned to stone and then crumbled with a thunderous roar.

The beams merely dimmed slightly, still advancing towards Clarissa.

Seeing this, Osborne turned alarmed.

"Clarissa, watch out! Dodge it!"

But how could Clarissa dodge such an attack?

Yet, she stood unafraid, staring down the terrifying beams, and cried out, "Divine and Demonic Wings!"

In an instant, the void shook violently. A burst of intense, dual-toned light erupted behind Clarissa—sacred white and infernal black—forming a pair of extraordinary wings.

One half resembled angelic wings, the other demonic.

The colossal wings flapped, instantly enveloping Clarissa and shielding her.

As the dreadful beams finally struck, they hit the Divine and Demonic Wings.

A bizarre sound echoed. The wings began to calcify as if attempting to turn both the wings and Clarissa to stone.

But before the petrification could finish, the stones seemed to melt, vanishing swiftly.

Simultaneously, an inexplicable force rebounded from the wings.

Medusa's body became encased in stone quickly, only to dissolve away just as fast, her face now a mask of shock.

"How is this possible? Damn it!

"Intelligence failure! The Fatebearer has grown stronger. She's at least a Class B+ metahuman. If we kill her, the backlash could turn me to ash in an instant!

"Retreat! Abort the mission!

"The Fatebearer cannot be killed, not by our organization's hand! It could bring ruin upon us all! Retreat!"

Medusa's face was filled with panic, her eyes reflecting fear for the first time as she looked at Clarissa, enveloped by her wings. She made to flee without hesitation.

"Going somewhere? Not a chance!"

Seeing Clarissa was safe, Osborne breathed a sigh of relief. Spotting Medusa's retreat, he gritted his teeth and shot out a bolt of blue Devil's Light to bar her escape.

But with a mere glance, Medusa turned the blue light to stone, which then shattered.

"Is my fifth-level Devil's Light not even a momentary obstacle for her? She's undoubtedly a Class A master!" Osborne's thoughts were a whirlwind of alarm.

He knew in that instant that he couldn't stop Medusa with his current strength. Swiftly, he redirected his focus to Venom Lord.

"She's a Class A master, but you're not! You dared to assassinate Clarissa. Now, you'll pay the price. You're staying here permanently!"

With those words, the Devil's Light blasted toward Venom Lord.

Seeing Medusa flee, Venom Lord panicked, but surrounded by countless rifts, he was vulnerable and unable to muster speed.

Osborne's assault left him in disarray.

In a flash, he was overpowered by Osborne.

He was merely a Class B+ metahuman. His power to control Necrovenom made him a formidable foe, but it was useless against Osborne.

Osborne's means, however, left him struggling. Barring a miracle, he'd soon be killed by Osborne.

"Medusa, help me!" Venom Lord shouted desperately.

Medusa, nearly out of the castle, turned at his cry, her face a mask of fury as she cursed, "What a waste!"

Despite her anger, she turned, shooting two beams from her eyes that set off Osborne's Satan's Eye in a violent alarm.

He sensed an overwhelming threat. He wasn't Clarissa; he didn't have the Divine and Demonic Wings to shield him from the beams.

Without hesitation, Osborne dove into a dimensional rift, narrowly escaping Medusa's assault.

This gave Venom Lord the opportunity he needed to flee.

As Medusa continued to fire beams from her eyes, more rifts petrified and collapsed, and Venom Lord finally seized his chance to escape.

"Let's go! The Fatebearer has grown. We must relay this news!"

Medusa grabbed Venom Lord's arm, ready to make a swift exit.

Osborne could only watch helplessly as they vanished from the castle.

"My power is still insufficient! If I were a Class A master, I would have detained them no matter what today!"

Osborne clenched his fists, yearning for more strength.

As Medusa and Venom Lord were about to leave the castle and disappear for good, a terrifying surge of energy arrived.

An elderly voice echoed.

"Since you've come, you might as well stay! A fledgling master daring to act out in my domain?

"Subjugate!"
