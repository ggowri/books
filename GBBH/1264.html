"If you're going to give me a massage, do it properly instead of..." Ava's words trailed off. Soon, she was trembling all over.

"This is a proper massage. Feels nice, doesn't it?" Alexander teased with a grin.

"You're impossible," Ava protested half-heartedly.

Alexander turned off the main light, leaving only a soft bedside lamp glowing. His movements grew bolder.

Ava yielded to his touch, her senses heightened in the dim light.

Alexander's desire grew as he watched Ava's gentle surrender.

They held each other close as Ava's delicate fingers traced patterns on Alexander's chest.

Soon, the room filled with soft moans and a man's heavy breathing...

In the middle of the night, Ava was drifting between sleep and wakefulness when she felt Alexander get out of bed.

At first, she thought she was imagining things, but when she opened her eyes, Alexander had indeed left the bed, looking surprisingly alert.

"Why aren't you sleeping? What's going on?" Ava asked, confused by his strange move.

"I'd love to sleep, but someone's up to no good," Alexander replied. A cold smile crossed his face.

"What's wrong? What happened?" Ava knew something was definitely wrong, and anxiety immediately gripped her.

"Someone's decided to cause trouble at this hour. They really don't want me to get my beauty sleep."

Alexander checked the time. It was 2 AM, when the world should be silent and everyone fast asleep.

He threw on his clothes quickly, ready to head out. Ava jumped out of bed and grabbed his arm. "I'm worried. Let me come with you!"

Concern and anxiety were written all over her beautiful face.

Alexander smiled, wrapped an arm around her waist, and they left the bedroom together.

In the entire mansion, besides Alexander, both Charlize and Rosalia were forces to be reckoned with. Their mysterious intruder had already managed to alert both female security guards.

Death was already looming over this overconfident intruder who thought they'd snuck in undetected.

Alexander took the lead while Ava clutched his hand tightly, like a little girl holding her father's hand, afraid of getting lost.

"Where are they? Are you sure we didn't make a mistake?" Ava's voice trembled slightly.

"Don't worry, there's no mistake. Everything is under control."

Alexander smiled, taking off his jacket and draping it over Ava's shoulders. He gently rubbed her back, and immediately, a powerful surge of the Force flowed into her body. The chill vanished instantly. Even with the cold night breeze, she felt completely warm.

Alexander led Ava to the entrance, telling her to wait by the front door.

He took a few steps forward and called out into the darkness, "If you're brave enough to come here, why not show your face? What's with all this sneaking around? Come out on your own. Don't make me force you."

Alexander's tone was casual as if he were chatting with an old friend.

A moment passed in dead silence, with only the faint chirping of insects in the darkness.

Ava frowned. She knew Alexander's abilities. He wouldn't make this kind of mistake. Whoever was hiding out there clearly had bad intentions.

"If you won't come out, don't blame me for what happens next."

Alexander's voice turned ice-cold, filled with deadly intent. He was clearly ready to take action.

Finally, a voice emerged from the darkness.

"Very well, Mr. Hawthorne. I'm impressed. You could sense me even from this distance!"

His apparent admiration for Alexander carried more than a hint of mockery.

Footsteps rustled in the darkness as a middle-aged man in a black uniform emerged from the shadows.

The guy had a long, thin face with small eyes, and thick fur-like hair growing on his neck and arms. Everything about him gave off seriously creepy vibes.

"Heh," Alexander said with a smirk. "If I'm not mistaken, you're from the Sakra Society. What brings you to my place at this hour?"

His tone dripped with confidence and contempt.

"Don't get cocky, Alexander. I'm here on behalf of Mr. White to give you one last chance. Hand over the magic powder formula and join the Sakra Society."

"Mr. White is willing to forgive what you have done before. But if you refuse, you'll learn exactly what kind of terrifying force you've messed with."

"As of today, our chairman isn't just the head of the Sakra Society. He's also become a high-ranking member of the Ghost. You do know what the Ghost is capable of, right? Don't sign your own death warrant!"

The man's face radiated arrogance. He was certain that just mentioning "the Ghost" would make Alexander shake in his boots.
