If it had been an ordinary person, they would have been thrown out of the car after such a violent crash and spin.

But both the driver and Alexander remained firmly seated, unmoving—clearly, the driver was no ordinary man.

He was a formidable expert.

A shadow flitted across the scene, and Loren's withered figure appeared, swiftly crawling through the shattered window and landing behind Alexander.

His left chest was wrapped in thick bandages, now stained deep red with blood.

"Simeon, grab him! That's Alexander! If we kill him, Mr. White promised us five hundred million dollars—five hundred million!"

Loren's voice was frantic, his grip tight around a sharp dagger, which he thrust directly at Alexander's back.

Just as Alexander prepared to dodge, the driver, roaring like a wild gorilla, lunged at him, pinning him down with terrifying strength, trapping him in place.

"Get off me!"

Alexander growled, using the Force to attempt a desperate struggle.

The driver howled in pain as his arm cracked, but Alexander couldn't break free—not yet.

His face darkened with realization; the driver's strength was at least on par with a level-four strength power, a force far stronger than even a silverback gorilla.

"Thwack!"

The dagger pierced the back of the seat, sinking into Alexander's flesh.

It had been so long since Alexander had felt such excruciating pain—especially that cold, agonizing burn that seemed to sink into his bones. It was a pain that rattled him to his core.

Without a doubt, Loren had coated the blade with some kind of poison. Otherwise, Alexander wouldn't have felt such torment.

Even with the Force surging through him, he nearly lost the ability to move entirely.

The driver, seeing Alexander injured, grinned maliciously.

"Ha! I'm a level-four strength metahuman, and I was a world-class weightlifter before all this! With me here, there's no way you're getting out alive!"

Simeon's arms bulged with muscles as thick as a normal person's thighs. He tightened his grip on Alexander, preventing him from escaping Loren's assault.

Loren snarled, his dagger flashing as he aimed for Alexander's neck.

From the passenger seat, Alexander couldn't see what was coming. He had no choice but to jerk his head to the side, narrowly avoiding the strike.

But the blade still grazed his skin, and an intense, searing pain exploded in his neck, nearly causing him to black out. The agony was overwhelming, a sensation so sharp it cut through his every nerve.

Normally, Alexander would have been immune to poisoning, but this time, the poison nearly got the best of him.

His fury ignited, burning like wildfire.

With a roar that sounded almost like a dragon's fury, the Force surged out of him. The blast was so powerful that Loren hesitated, his hand shaking as the dagger nearly slipped from his grasp.

Simeon, face-to-face with Alexander, was blasted by the raw power of the Force. The shockwave sent him reeling, his vision spinning, legs buckling under the intensity.

"You're dead!"

Alexander's eyes burned red, veins pulsing with rage.

He shot a look at Simeon, a single glance that sent the Force to its maximum limit. Blood-red tears streamed from his eyes as his Focus intensified.

Simeon caught in the power and staggered backward, releasing his grip on Alexander and lunging at Loren like a wild animal.

His hands tightened around the dagger, disregarding the cuts on his fingers, blood dripping freely.

"Simeon! Are you out of your mind? What are you doing?"

Loren's voice was filled with fury as he fought against the frenzied Simeon.

He knew something was wrong—Simeon's actions were too erratic, too far from what was expected.

Loren had thought they had this—between his cunning and Simeon's strength, there was no way Alexander could survive.

He was already gravely injured. Anyone else would have been finished.

But then, Loren's expression shifted as he realized the truth.

"No! He should be dead! He was poisoned!"

Loren muttered in disbelief, watching as Alexander remained standing, alive, despite the poison.

The car, already small, had been further distorted by the crash.

Alexander, weakened and poisoned, would have struggled to escape—yet he was still alive.

Simeon, suddenly foaming at the mouth, spasms violently.

His hands released Loren, and he fell back, convulsing on the floor.

"Idiot. Go to hell."

Loren sneered, raising the dagger, and with one swift movement, slashed it across Simeon's throat.

"Thud..."

Simeon's massive head hit the car floor with a sickening thud, his blood splattering across Loren's face, blinding his eyes with crimson.

Everything happened in an instant. Just as Loren was processing this, Alexander spun around and landed a punch square into Loren's left chest.

The sound of bones snapping echoed through the car, followed by Loren's gut-wrenching scream of pain.

Loren let out a violent cough, spewing blood toward Alexander as the force of the blow crushed his chest.
