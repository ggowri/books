The protective shield was shaking more and more. It made cracking sounds like breaking glass.

Cracks appeared all over the shield.

These cracks spread quickly, covering the entire shield.

It was clear that the amulet's protection was about to reach its limit. The energy in the gemstone was almost gone.

If Alexander hadn't become stronger and better at making amulets, this one would have been destroyed by Rock and Monica already.

"Yvonne, you deserve to die. Hand over the amulet," Monica shouted.

"Hand it over to me. It's not too late now," Rock said.

They didn't want the amulet to be destroyed.

The black snake squeezed tighter, and more cracks appeared on the shield. But Yvonne wasn't scared at all.

Everyone thought Yvonne was ready to die, but they didn't know she had someone powerful backing her up.

"I dare you!" a cold voice said from outside.

The voice wasn't loud, but it echoed like thunder. Everyone covered their ears.

"Who is it?" everyone wondered, turning to look at the door.

"Who dares to touch my people?" the cold voice said.

It was like needles in their ears.

Monica and Rock were both shocked.

The sound hit Rock, who was attacking Yvonne.

It was as if thunder was exploding in his ears, accompanied by a sharp, stabbing pain. Rock instantly turned pale and stumbled backward.

The black snake around Yvonne disappeared into black smoke.

Monica and Rock were stunned.

They wondered what kind of witchcraft it was.

They didn't realize it was Alexander.

"Master!" Yvonne shouted happily.

She wasn't affected by the sound.

"Is it really Alexander?" Everyone stared at the door.

They wanted to see what Alexander looked like and if he was as powerful as the stories said.

A tall, handsome figure appeared at the door.

He stood with the sun behind him, making it hard to see his face.

When Alexander stepped into the hall, everyone could see his handsome face.

"It's indeed him." Rock gritted his teeth.

He remembered the battle on Treasure Island.

Alexander's followers had almost defeated him.

Rock put his hand on the Ancient Grimoire, ready to defend himself. He stopped attacking Yvonne and stared at Alexander.

Everyone looked behind Alexander, but there was no one else.

He was there alone, which was kind of an insult to the City of Sorcery.

Alexander remained composed despite facing such a large crowd.

He pranced to Yvonne, as if he were just taking a walk after dinner instead of causing trouble there.

"Alexander, what do you want?" Rock asked, trying to hide his fear.

He didn't know why, but Alexander's presence brought him an uneasy feeling.

Monica coldly stared at Alexander, the biggest enemy of the City of Sorcery.

"Sorry, I'm a bit late," Alexander said casually.

Yvonne was so happy she had tears in her eyes. "Master, I didn't complete my task."

Alexander smiled, "No, you did well. You showed everyone that Rock and Monica are not united.

"They will fight each other eventually even if you don't appear. The City of Sorcery has no future."

His words were provocative, but no one could retort. He was telling the truth.
