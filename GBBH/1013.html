Over a hundred elite members of the Vance family were gathered here. Although individually, they were no match for Alexander, as a group, their presence was formidable.

Asher believed that in such a situation, Alexander would have no choice but to retreat.

But Alexander didn't flinch. Instead, he smirked, radiating an air of unshakable calm.

As the onslaught began, Alexander vanished.

In the blink of an eye, both the bullets and flying blades fell harmlessly to the ground.

The man leading the charge stumbled, nearly losing his balance.

He had been sure he'd severed Alexander's head with a single strike, but now all he faced was empty air. His expression turned to one of shock.

"Ah!"

A horrifying scream erupted from his throat as he looked down.

His chest had suddenly protruded. Alexander had struck him from behind, piercing through his chest.

His ribs and sternum were shattered, and jagged bone fragments jutted out, tearing through his chest like a grotesque protrusion.

Blood poured out in torrents, and the force sent him flying through the air. He collapsed in a heap, everything going black.

With a thud, his body crashed into the crowd like a cannonball, sending people scattering. Cries of terror filled the air.

Alexander moved through the crowd like a ghost. Each strike he delivered took a life.

It was as if he were playing a cruel game of dominoes. The second, the third... bodies fell into bloody heaps.

Within moments, Asher's men were scattered across the floor, lifeless and broken.

The members of Ultimate Alliance had joined in, surrounding Alexander and assisting with the onslaught.

In less than five minutes, over a hundred of Asher's men lay defeated on the ground.

"You... you're so strong. Why have you been hiding it all this time?" Asher stammered, trembling.

For the first time, he truly felt the sting of death. This wasn't West Cemetery.

Back then, there had been figures like Gavin watching, so Alexander had refrained from killing him.

But this was different. This was Ultimate Alliance's territory. If Alexander wanted him dead now, it would be as easy as killing an ant.

"Asher, you brought all this on yourself."

Alexander's cold eyes locked onto him.

Asher felt an overwhelming pressure, his bladder threatening to release.

He had always been arrogant, feeling superior, thinking of himself as a genius.

But now, standing before Alexander, he felt like trash.

Cold sweat drenched his body. His legs shook uncontrollably. He felt he was doomed.

Alexander walked toward him expressionlessly.

The silence in the hall was deafening, broken only by the sound of Alexander's footsteps, each one like a hammer pounding into Asher's chest.

The murder in Alexander's eyes sent a shiver down Asher's spine, making every hair on his body stand on end.

Asher felt as though Alexander was an insurmountable abyss. Any moment now, he could be swallowed up and sink forever, unable to rise again.

He lost all will to fight and bolted for the door.

But strangely, no one tried to stop him. As he neared the door with a look of wild joy on his face, Alexander swiftly kicked out.

A knife flew up from the floor like lightning, piercing Asher's right leg.

"Ah!"

A blood-curdling scream erupted as Asher crashed to the ground, his leg bleeding profusely.

"Mr. Vance..."

A few Vance family members who had survived gasped, their faces draining of color.

They realized it was truly over. If Alexander showed no mercy to Asher, they were certainly doomed.

Alexander walked slowly up to Asher, looking down at him from above as though gazing down upon a beggar from a high throne.

Asher lay there, writhing in agony and fear, his gaze locked on Alexander, his body drenched in sweat.

The terror in his heart was overwhelming. All he could think of was surviving.

"Alexander... I... I was wrong. Please let me go. I can apologize, I can pay you a huge sum of money. What do you say?

"Killing me won't benefit you. If I die, the Vance family will definitely come after you. We could solve this though a better way, right?" Asher pleaded.
