The young flagbearer stood atop his horse. With a light step, the steed neighed sharply as he leaped into the air, closely following behind Josh, heading straight for the estate walls.

Behind them, dozens of men charged forward, hauling several siege ladders directly toward the walls.

On top of the wall, Alexander's expression was ice-cold, his face devoid of emotion as he murmured to himself, "Hm... Strong energy. A level-four warrior... and a level-three warrior."

No one would be in a good mood if their home was attacked in the dead of night. His voice was frigid.

"I'll take the one with the blade. Vegeta, the kid is yours. Logan, Nova, stay with the guards and hold the fort."

Josh landed smoothly on the estate wall and couldn't help but grin. The estate was as good as his!

But just a few yards away, a young man in a blue robe stood watching him coldly, his piercing gaze sharp as lightning, his presence as deep as an abyss. It was Alexander. How effortless it was to find him!

His eyes flashed with a savage gleam. His figure blurred as he suddenly appeared beside Alexander. The air split apart with a deafening roar as his nine-ring broadsword flared with a foot-long crimson glow, slashing straight toward Alexander's neck.

Alexander let out a cold scoff. "You're much slower than Rhodes."

His toes tapped the ground, and in an instant, a blinding green light burst forth. He had already moved behind Josh. A deafening crack of thunder rang out as his elbow, sharp as a spear, struck straight for his back.

His face twisted in shock. "A level-four warrior?!"

With a roar, he spun his broadsword in a wide arc, unleashing a blazing red energy that enveloped his entire body like a raging inferno.

But Alexander suddenly dropped lower, sweeping his leg out in a ferocious kick. The sheer force of it howled like a storm, crashing straight toward his ankles.

Josh stomped down hard. A surge of red energy blasted out as he propelled himself into the air. His broadsword came crashing down with a force like a collapsing mountain, its crimson glow splitting through the night, aiming straight for Alexander's skull.

A glint of murderous intent flickered in Alexander's eyes. He slammed both palms against the ground, causing an eruption of green energy. His body spun like a whirlwind, Arhat Spin! A flurry of relentless kicks bombarded Josh from below.

Each kick was fiercer than the last. Each strike more devastating!

Josh took hit after hit. Blood sprayed from his mouth as he was sent flying like a ragdoll, his entire body convulsing uncontrollably.

"This is it. Die!"

Alexander let out a sharp roar. His foot tapped the ground. He shot into the air like a soaring dragon, charging straight toward the airborne Josh. A piercing, earth-shattering crack echoed.

A devastating whip kick struck Josh square in the back. BOOM! He plummeted like a falling star, crashing straight into the courtyard wall.

Alexander landed gracefully, arms folded behind his back, his sharp eyes locked onto the blood-soaked Josh lying in the deep crater. His voice was barely above a whisper, "I'm no level-four warrior."

Josh's eyes widened in horror. It was like being struck by lightning on a clear day, like having a bucket of ice water poured over his entire body. He was frozen in shock, unable to move, as if he had turned to stone.

The young flagbearer's massive flag spun through the air, shifting between a spear, a staff, and a blade as he fiercely battled Vegeta.

In just moments, Josh had been completely taken down. The young flagbearer's face paled with terror. He let out a desperate roar, "Sun Piercing Arrow!"

He hurled his flag like a shooting star, streaking toward Vegeta before turning to flee toward the outskirts of the estate.

Trying to run?

Vegeta's eyes blazed with fury. Letting out a deep roar, he lowered his stance and stepped forward like a drawn bow. His body twisted, both hands forming claw-like shapes at his waist, resembling the petals of a blooming flower.

Sparks of dazzling electricity crackled around his entire body. Countless radiant silver-white light particles converged in his palms, forming a blinding, mysterious orb of energy.

"Energy... Wave... Attack!"

With Vegeta's thunderous roar, he thrust both palms forward. The blinding energy sphere morphed into a dazzling beam of light, cutting through space and time, slamming straight into the young flagbearer's back.

BOOM! A massive explosion erupted. The young flagbearer's body turned pitch black, and he collapsed to the ground.

"Energy Plot Attack!" In that instant, Vegeta, a level-three warrior, unleashed such immense power. "He definitely reached level-four!" Alexander's jaw dropped, unable to close his mouth for a long while.

Meanwhile, the hundreds of bandits had only just reached the moat.

In the blink of an eye, both the level-four warrior Josh and the level-three flagbearer had been utterly defeated. The bandits immediately fell into chaos.

Suddenly, the estate's drawbridge was lowered, and the gates swung open. Logan and Nova led over a hundred men charging out, cutting down enemies on sight with unstoppable force.

The bandits, though well-trained, had lost their leaders. Their morale was completely shattered.

Vegeta's people were warriors who had fought against the Noah Empire on the Noah Continent. In an instant, the bandits were completely overwhelmed, scattering in disarray.

Vegeta's limbs felt heavy and sore. He barely managed to walk up to Alexander, offering a respectful salute before speaking with pride, "Sir! What do you think of the Energy Plot Attack I mastered?"
