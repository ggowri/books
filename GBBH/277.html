Alexander faced a brutal attack and found himself in a life-or-death situation.

The elite special forces squad worked with impeccable coordination, their movements almost synchronized.

A cold smile appeared at the corner of Alexander's mouth, his eyes turning razor-sharp.

The Force gathered into a swirling vortex inside him, spinning violently and surging with fury.

Alexander unleashed "Force Confinement," and the entire world seemed to freeze in an instant.

Bullets and enemy movements appeared to be in slow motion, like a video played in slow speed, moving at a snail's pace.

But Alexander remained unaffected, moving as fast as lightning.

Johnny and his team noticed nothing unusual, seeing only dozens of bullets tearing through Alexander's body.

"Hahaha!"

They were overjoyed, convinced that Alexander was dead.

But in the next second, Johnny was stunned, wondering if he was hallucinating.

All the bullets seemed to have struck Alexander directly, yet they had missed entirely, and his figure vanished without a trace.

"Damn!"

It was just an afterimage!

Alexander's speed had reached such a peak that it created an afterimage, making them believe he'd been hit.

Suddenly, a figure appeared beside Johnny, throwing a punch.

The punch roared with such force that it emitted a sharp, piercing sound, slamming into his chest.

"Bang!"

Johnny flew backward like a cannonball, crashing hard to the ground.

His chest and ribs shattered completely, blood gushing from his mouth.

Meanwhile, Noah, who had been hit in the leg, tried to crawl backward but was caught by Alexander in two steps and slammed onto the floor.

"Crack!"

The sound of breaking bones and tearing tendons echoed.

Noah's neck snapped, his eyes wide open in terror, filled with fear, as he died unpeacefully.

In the blink of an eye, Alexander had taken down two of them.

Adrian and another teammate, who had rushed forward, were now paralyzed with fear, unsure whether to charge ahead or retreat.

At that moment, gunfire rang out again.

One of them was shot in the chest by Charlize, crashing hard to the ground as blood gushed out.

Adrian snapped out of it, throwing a dagger at Alexander before turning to flee.

Alexander stepped his foot, causing the hard floor to suddenly crack open.

The terrifying recoil launched him forward like a bolt, landing a punch squarely on Adrian's back.

"Ugh!"

Adrian spat blood violently, rolling far across the ground like a ball.

Alexander had no intention of letting him go; Adrian's dagger had conveniently fallen in front of him.

He kicked the dagger, causing a piercing sound as it sliced through the air.

The dagger shot like an arrow, piercing Adrian's chest and pinning him brutally against the wall.

In a flash, the elite squad was reduced to just Johnny, barely clinging to life.

His broken ribs had punctured his lungs, and he was coughing up blood, his heavy breathing resembling a worn-out accordion.

Alexander approached him, looking down with contempt in his eyes.

"Please... don't... don't kill me..."

Johnny stammered, begging for his life.

Alexander shook his head. "I'll deliver you to Schroder as a welcoming gift."

As he finished speaking, Alexander gestured slightly, and the dagger on the ground flew into his hand.

"Swish!"

The dagger pierced straight into the man's heart.

Alexander felt no mercy toward his enemies.

Even in death, the man's eyes were wide open, still filled with terror as they stared at Alexander.

Across the building, William and Charlize were once again amazed by Alexander's skills.

Alexander was simply too fierce; he took down the elite special forces team in no time.

Although they were sniping from the shadows, Alexander had faced the assault of four elite operatives alone, coming out completely unscathed. It's something they couldn't have achieved.

At that moment, Augusta arrived with Marvin, followed by a dozen Fraternity subordinates.

"Clean up the scene quickly! Leave no trace!" Augusta ordered, waving his hand. Everyone swiftly got to work.

But Alexander turned around, his sharp gaze locking onto a woman across the street.

"There's still one who slipped through the net!" he announced.

Augusta and the others were stunned. None of their surveillance had noticed her. How did the Boss spot her?
