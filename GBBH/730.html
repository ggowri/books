There was a clanking sound.

Bruno was shirtless, holding the machete on the blue flame with the iron pincers of his left hand, and swinging the hammer with his right hand to forge it continuously.

After the broken machete was molten, he joined it to a piece of steel. Then, he forged it into a complete machete.

"Well, I mixed it with tungsten steel this time. I'll see how you can still break my machete!"

Bruno sneered.

This machete had been with Bruno for over twenty years. He had a deep affection for it and was not willing to just abandon it.

A truly formidable person would not change their weapons, because the feel of the weapon was unique.

A familiar weapon could definitely make the user much more powerful.

"Alexander, I'll kill you when I fix the machete!"

"Ava, I'm going to make you come to your senses and see Alexander, the asshole, for what he really is. You'll realize who's genuinely doing you good when the time comes."

Bruno muttered to himself, ferocity showing in his eyes.

The tungsten steel was so hard that Bruno had to take a break to regain his strength after hammering only a dozen times.

After hammering for over a hundred times, Bruno began to pant. His physical strength was overexerted, so he looked pale. It was truly hard to repair the machete with tungsten steel.

However, Bruno was absolutely confident that once he finished tinkering with the machete, he would kill Alexander and get Ava.

At that moment, his cell phone rang.

"What's going on?"

Bruno asked angrily when he answered it.

Brandon on the other side of the cell phone was silent for a while before he took a deep breath and asked with a straight face, "Are you done with your mission?"

Bruno sneered and said in anger, "I've fought Alexander. He's truly impressive, and he broke my machete, but don't worry. When I fix my machete, I can definitely cut off his head!"

"Good. Bruno, you must finish the mission. This concerns your mother's future and whether you will feel elated."

Brandon's voice on the other side of the phone became much softer.

Bruno could feel that Brandon, as his elder, the Godfather of the Mafia, didn't genuinely care about him but wanted him to be a loyal dog through the phone.

Bruno replied coldly, "Don't worry. I'll do it when I'm sure."

"Okay. I'll wait for your good news."

Brandon was quite satisfied and nodded with a smile. "As long as you complete the mission, I certainly won't treat you poorly."

After hanging up the phone, Bruno sneered and threw the phone aside.

He was going to kill Alexander not for the sake of the Mafia and Brandon but for himself.

Bruno hated the Mafia and despised Brandon, but he had to fulfill his mother's dying wish.

Also, he was in love with Ava, and he had to make sure that Ava got rid of Alexander!

Bruno grabbed the hammer and started forging hard.

It was at the Saint Group.

Ava frowned, "The plague has been raging lately, and our group had to have a vacation all together. I heard that the city is going to be locked down today, and it will definitely affect our business."

Alexander wasn't surprised at all.

"I already have a way to get rid of the plague. Follow my recipe and ask someone to send over the herbs, and I will create the antidote.

"Then, I will put the antidote powder into the water plant's reservoir. People will simply drink the antidote water to remove the poison from their bodies and regain their health."

Ava was shocked. "You are such a hero!"

She excitedly jumped into Alexander's arms and hugged him tightly.

The two hugged for a while before immediately starting to take action.

At this moment, Loscity, once a thriving paradise, had become a hell on earth.

With the terrible plague spreading wildly, it had become everyone's wish not to be infected and to survive.

In Loscity Hospital, where the situation was most serious, both the halls and the corridors had been filled with hospital beds, lying with dying infected people.

There were no beds for many of the patients, so they had to cower in the corners, some coughing violently, others convulsing all over.

In front of the hospital, Melin was standing guard, her pretty face looking haggard.

She truly hadn't expected the terrible plague to spread so quickly, and the situation had worsened in just one night.
