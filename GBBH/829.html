But within a second, Louise returned to her usual condescending attitude.

She acted like she was a princess high above everyone else, expecting others to look up to her.

Alice was quietly observing Louise.

Hearing Craig comforting Alexander and catching bits of everyone's conversations, she quickly pieced together the history between Alexander and Louise.

"What a foolish woman, letting go of such a great man," Alice thought to herself with amusement. She gently placed a hand on Alexander's leg, offering him silent comfort.

Alexander responded with a smile, his mood instantly lifting.

Back then, Louise had dumped him for being poor and lacking prospects.

She had never even acknowledged him as her boyfriend, treating him as nothing more than a backup plan.

Now, everything was different. Who cared about her being the campus queen?

Any woman by Alexander's side now easily outshone her.

Louise's arrival had suddenly made the atmosphere more lively.

"Alexander, I heard you divorced Jane. Why did you two split up?"

Louise asked, her face full of curiosity. It was clear she wasn't genuinely concerned. She just wanted the juicy details.

Before Alexander could respond, Bobby interjected eagerly.

"Louise, even we didn't know this guy got divorced! It's obvious, though, that his wife must've been fed up with him. A useless bum with no job? Who wouldn't leave him?"

Bobby, trying to act like a big shot in front of Louise, was starting to get carried away.

Even with Alexander's typically good temper and his desire to maintain cordial relations with former classmates, this was going too far.

"Louise, my personal life has nothing to do with you. Stay out of it!

"And Bobby, was the slap earlier not enough? Do I need to teach you another lesson?"

Denise, who had always acted like Bobby's loyal guard, immediately jumped up in anger.

"Alexander, how dare you talk to Mr. Murray like that? And that slut hit him earlier. We haven't even settled that score yet! You're so ungrateful!"

Emboldened by Denise's support, Bobby started shouting again.

"Alexander, you piece of trash! I paid for this reunion, and this is how you repay me?

"You even brought some prostitute to pretend she's the secretary to the CEO of the Saint Group. What a joke!

"Bring that slut over here. I'm going to teach her a lesson!"

Louise feigned shock, pointing at Alice mockingly.

"She's the secretary to the Saint Group's CEO? Her? Don't make me laugh. It's absurd!"

Bobby grew even bolder, reaching out to grab Alice's hair.

"You're asking for it!"

Alexander stood up and swung his arm, landing a heavy slap across Bobby's face. The blow knocked Bobby to the ground.

With a cry, Bobby spat out a mouthful of blood, along with two teeth. He writhed in pain, wailing loudly.

The entire room froze in shock. No one had expected Alexander to strike so fiercely.

Louise pointed at Alexander, furious.

"You've gone too far! Bobby invited you here out of kindness, and you hit him? And so viciously! I knew I had made the right choice by dumping you back then. Thank goodness I did!

"Jane divorced you because you were abusive, didn't she?"

Louise's baseless accusations almost made Alexander explode with rage.

He fixed his icy gaze on Louise, making her break into a cold sweat.

Her back felt like it was freezing, and she started trembling uncontrollably.

"You dare hit me that hard? I'll make you pay for this!"

Bobby finally managed to catch his breath.

Clutching his swollen face, which now looked like a pig's head, he groaned and pulled out his phone to make a call.

"Hey, Shawn, I just got beaten up. Bring some guys over. I'll send you the address!"

After hanging up, Bobby smirked, certain Alexander was doomed.

Glaring at Alexander, he sneered.

"If you kneel down and apologize right now, I'll go easy on you for old times' sake and only break one of your arms."

Alexander was speechless. Faced with such stupidity, he didn't even know how to respond.

Alice, however, couldn't hold back her laughter and thought, "This kind of trash thinks he can break Mr. Hawthorne's leg? What a joke."

But the other classmates didn't find it funny.
