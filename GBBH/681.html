After more than an hour, Betty lay weak in Alexander's arms, gently stroking his firm chest.

"Mr. Hawthorne, Charlize is being discharged tomorrow. Will you be picking her up?" she asked.

"Of course, I'll be there!" Alexander replied without hesitation.

Charlize had been injured in the line of duty, and he wasn't about to neglect her.

After a few more minutes in each other's embrace, Betty left.

Alexander felt the Force coursed through his body, filling him with an invigorated vitality.

Unable to contain himself, he left the study and headed to a small grove nearby.

With a single punch, he tore through the air, his fist force splitting it with a booming sound and driving straight through a tree trunk.

A tree as thick as a bowl cracked and snapped.

That single punch, exerting only a fraction of his strength, had generated a sonic boom and broken a sizable tree trunk.

That was a terrifying power.

Those so-called fighting masters couldn't even block a single punch from him.

Punch after punch, Alexander's strikes came faster and faster. In mere moments, he had leveled numerous trees, his strength far surpassing what it had been before.

Now, he just wanted to test his limits.

His punching speed increased rapidly!

After seventy-two consecutive punches, he finally felt his chest tighten, a breath he could no longer hold in.

Anyone who saw what had just happened would be in utter disbelief. Knocking down over seventy trees in a single go was the feat of a superhuman.

This was equivalent to Alexander defeating seventy-two martial arts masters in one breath.

Alexander was quite pleased with the result.

Over the next few days, Alexander used high-grade gemstones to craft a new batch of protective amulets.

Everyone close to him received one, even the top members of the Ultimate Alliance.

Charlize, in particular, received special attention. Although she was fully healed, her appearance and figure perfectly restored, Alexander decided to help her reach new heights in power.

Using a spell from the Ultimate Legacy, Alexander crafted 108 enchanted gemstones.

Each of the gemstones was engraved with intricate magical runes, faintly glowing with an air of mystery.

"Charlize, I'm going to use this magic circle to help you unlock your full potential. It might be painful. Can you bear it?" Alexander asked solemnly.

Charlize blinked, then nodded resolutely.

"Mr. Hawthorne, I'm not afraid! Go ahead; I can handle it!"

"Good, I promise you won't regret it. Think of this as a reward for your loyalty."

Satisfied, Alexander patted her shoulder.

He arranged the 108 gemstones into a magic circle and instructed Charlize to sit cross-legged in its center, preparing for meditation.

When Alexander activated the magic circle, there were faint arcs of electricity that began to crackle around it, and a mysterious vortex formed around Charlize, swirling steadily.

From within the vortex, the reflections of all 108 gemstones appeared, glowing with an extraordinary radiance.

"Charlize, I'm now raising your potential limits. It will hurt, but you must endure it. Can you handle that?" Alexander asked solemnly.

Charlize's face was set with determination.

"Mr. Hawthorne, I crave strength. Go ahead. I'm not afraid."

"Good!"

Alexander praised her, then began channeling the Force into the magic circle.

The 108 gemstones pulsed continuously, and a surge of life energy emerged from the vortex, rapidly integrating into Charlize's body.

Charlize's vision darkened, her blood seeming to boil, her body on the verge of exploding.

She felt a searing energy flooded into her.

"Ah! It's so hot... it hurts..." she cried out.

"Bear with it," Alexander reminded her. "Didn't you say you desired great power?"

"Yes, Mr. Hawthorne, I'll endure it!"

Charlize gritted her teeth, her face contorted with pain but remaining silent.

Gradually, the pain began to subside, and tiny specks of gemstone light started to glimmer beneath her skin.

Several hours later, the 108 gemstones had lost their luster, shattering into fragments of ordinary stone.

The energy within each gemstone had been fully absorbed by Charlize's body.
