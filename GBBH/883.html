While Keith was engrossed in filming, Alexander arrived in Shiru with Marvin.

It was also home to many skyscrapers, making it one of the most vibrant places in Risun.

"Boss, can we have some fun over there?" Marvin asked, pointing ahead.

He swallowed hard, his eyes filled with longing.

Alexander looked up and immediately spotted the Kabukicho sign.

Alexander chuckled. "Already thinking about women?"

He understood how men thought.

Marvin scratched his head, looking a little embarrassed.

"Let's go. Shadow's already here and waiting for us. Business comes first," Alexander said.

Alexander led Marvin toward Shiru.

Shiru was undoubtedly the heart of Risun's nightlife.

It was a paradise not just for men, but for women as well.

Whether a man sought a woman or a woman wanted an attentive host, as long as you had money, the options were endless.

For the wealthy, Kabukicho was heaven.

Here, the only limit was your imagination.

Alexander and Marvin made their way forward.

Alexander had Shadow come to Risun in secret, not just to deal with the Sakra Society but primarily to target the Mafia.

The Mafia's branch in Risun was located nearby.

The streets were lined with hostess bars, love hotels, and bustling crowds.

Young women in revealing outfits, elegant older ladies, and sophisticated women of high status could all be seen.

Feminine men were also abundant—lacking masculine traits but likely popular male hosts.

Even the air was thick with the scent of alcohol and indulgence.

Marvin's eyes darted everywhere, overwhelmed by the photos of scantily-clad women and the open advertisements. He looked like he'd been hit with a shot of adrenaline.

If Alexander hadn't said no, Marvin would have already run into one of the hostess bars to have fun.

Kabukicho was a hub for various factions, with dozens of groups staking out their territories.

The most powerful among them were the Dragon Gang, the Sakra Society, and the Mafia.

Wherever you went, women generated huge profits, and no one could afford to ignore that.

"This way!" Alexander said.

To avoid being followed, Alexander quickly led Marvin into the crowd.

Soon, the two slipped into an unremarkable alleyway.

Unlike the main streets lined with hostess and host clubs, this alley was filled with upscale clubs and bars.

Even the women soliciting customers here were more attractive than those outside.

Clearly, these were private, high-end clubs catering to a wealthier clientele.

Alexander stopped in front of one of the clubs, ready to enter.

"Hold it!"

A few large men approached and blocked Alexander's way.

"Sir, this is a members-only club. Are you one of our VIPs?"

Alexander casually made the pre-arranged hand gesture.

The men froze, then immediately bowed in unison.

"Welcome, Mr. Hawthorne!

"We didn't realize it was you. Please forgive us, Mr. Hawthorne!"

Alexander gave a slight smile and walked in with Marvin.

With someone leading the way, they encountered no further obstacles as they made their way deeper into the club.

"Mr. Hawthorne!" Shadow greeted enthusiastically.

Shadow hurried forward with excitement.

Alexander patted his shoulder, and the three of them entered a small lounge.

"Is everything ready?" Alexander asked.

Shadow nodded respectfully. "Everything is ready, young master."

He led Alexander further inside.

They passed through a long corridor, entered a bedroom, and then opened a hidden door in the floor.

A secret staircase appeared, and the group descended into the basement.

The basement was at least 86 feet—a small-scale arsenal.

The walls and display racks were lined with the latest automatic rifles.
