"Mr. Bryant, you've emerged from seclusion? That's wonderful!"

A housekeeper-like middle-aged woman rushed to greet him, swiftly opening a grand door for him.

Alexander watched this scene from the gap in the ceiling, his spirits lifting upon hearing "Mr. Bryant".

He had finally found Guzman!

Though they were siblings from different mothers, they were at odds over the position of Godfather.

Moreover, Guzman had repeatedly attempted to assassinate him and his family. Alexander would show no mercy.

The Red Umbrella Group wouldn't completely break ties with him before the potential potion was perfected.

The most likely suspect was Alexander. Had he finally found his way here?

The cruise ship appeared to be under Cloe's control, but she knew that Guzman was the true mastermind.
