Quentin frowned and looked at the time. Almost two hours had passed, but Alexander did not come back.

"Call him," Reynard ordered his men.

Then, Walter quickly took out his cell phone and dialed Alexander's number.

He turned on the hands-free, and the prompt sound of waiting for the connection was audible to everyone.

Alexander didn't answer the phone until it automatically hung up.

Walter called him again, but this time, the call was completely disconnected, and it said that the other party was already on the phone.

Quentin's face immediately turned gloomy, and he thought maybe Alexander really ran away.

Adam laughed triumphantly.

"Mr. Thompson, I am now 100% sure that he is a liar. The fact that Mr. Scott Thompson was awake just now was just a coincidence. It's not that Alexander's credit.

"Mr. Thompson, I advise you to wait patiently for the dark wizard I have hired. He will definitely restore Mr. Scott Thompson to health."

Quentin was silent.

Brittany frowned, not knowing what to say.

"Oh! You've all been fooled. If he wanted to come back, he would have come back a long time ago. It's obvious that he can't cure Mr. Scott Thompson, so he runs away.

"Maybe this necklace is harmful to Mr. Scott Thompson!"

"Brittany, I told you that guy is a liar. I'm doing this for Scott's sake, and this necklace is definitely something harmful."

Scott, lying on the hospital bed, let out a scream and a dark spot appeared on his forehead.

Madison trembled all over and stammered, "This... What's going on?"

Adam's expression was extremely gloomy, and he gritted his teeth and said, "It's really a witch insect... and a witch insect that I've never seen or heard of before. This witch insect is really good at hiding..."

"Madison, you bitch! I told you not to touch the necklace, but you insisted on doing so. You want to kill Scott, right?"

"Adam was helpless and stammered, ""Mr. Thompson, I'm sorry. I have never seen this kind of witch insect. I am just a gray wizard, and we should wait for the dark wizard I invited..."""

"You're just a useless person!"

Adam staggered back several steps, then ran outside, holding his aching stomach.

At the same time, Brittany also got angry at Madison.

"If anything happens to Scott, I will never forgive you. Get out of here now, and I won't have a shameless friend like you anymore!"

Madison was completely frightened and stumbled away in a panic with Adam.

"Scott! You have to hold on. Hold on a little longer. Alexander will be back soon."

Quentin picked up the necklace on the ground and put it back on him.

However, the gemstone that served as the pendant had cracked, and the Esoteric Pattern had been destroyed. It could not gather the vital energy between heaven and earth and could not suppress the runaway witch insect.

"Scott...Scott, you must hold on!"

Brittany's eyes were filled with tears, and her makeup was ruined by her tears.

But the dark area on Scott's body was getting larger and larger. Seeing that his whole body was about to turn dark, the Thompson family was already in despair.

Finally, the figure that everyone was looking forward appearing. Alexander was back!
