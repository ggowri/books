Ava kept calm as she made the demand, "Alright, I'll sign. But I have one condition—the moment I put my name on this, you better have Julian show up and clear the Brilliant Group's and magic powder's names!"

Elliot laughed triumphantly.

"Don't worry about that. Once you sign, the formula will officially be ours. Of course, we'll clear up any 'misunderstandings.'"

Without a second of hesitation, Ava picked up the pen and swiftly signed the asset transfer agreement.

But then she glared at Elliot and yelled, "I'm warning you, if you lay a finger on Lily or Ashley, I swear you'll die a miserable death."

Elliot, still smug from his "victory," laughed even louder with the contract in his hand like a trophy.

"Let's go!"

He waved his men toward the exit.

"Stop! First, you give us Julian."

Suddenly, Alexander burst through the crowd, standing directly in front of them with his arms outstretched.

No matter how much the crowd surged against him, Alexander didn't budge. Instead, he exerted the slightest bit of force, sending the people around him stumbling backward.

The security guards of the Brilliant Group let out a collective sigh of relief. When Alexander showed up, he made them all look small.

Elliot, furious at being interrupted, glared at Alexander and yelled, "Boy, why aren't you coming over to greet me?"

Alexander's hand flew through the air, delivering a stinging slap that echoed with a sharp crack across Elliot's cheek. With a piercing scream, Elliot reeled from the force before he crumpled to the ground with a thud.

"Y-You dare hit me?

"Ava's already signed the agreement, so according to what you agreed on, the formula belongs to us now. Hand it over, or you'll owe us 100 billion dollars!"

Elliot waved the contract in Alexander's face, thinking it gave him the upper hand.

Alexander laughed mockingly. In the blink of an eye, he snatched the contract from Elliot's hand.

The thick document exploded into thousands of pieces of paper, raining down on Elliot and the floor around him.

Elliot's face went slack with shock. He thrust an accusatory finger towards Alexander and muttered, "You bastard! You tore up the agreement in public? How could you! Ah!"

Before he could finish his rant, Alexander stomped on his chest, pressing him into the ground. His sneer was ice-cold.

"I let Ava sign that agreement on purpose to humor you. I wanted you to think you'd won before I take everything from you."

Alexander's smile was wide and almost friendly, but to Elliot, it looked demonic.

"You! You piece of trash! Aren't you afraid of what might happen to your daughter and Lily?"

Elliot tried the same old trick he'd used on Ava, hoping the threat would work on Alexander, too.

Alexander's grip tightened around Elliot's collar, yanking him off the ground. Two hard slaps echoed through the room, leaving Elliot screaming in pain.

"Uncle Elliot, you used a recording of my daughter crying from kindergarten to try and scare me? Who do you think you're messing with? Isn't that a little low, even for you?"

"How did you know about that?"

Elliot's eyes widened in confusion.

Suddenly, one of the black-clad thugs behind him shouted, "Everyone! That's Alexander! He's the one who used the magic powder to harm us! Grab him! Make him pay compensation!"

In an instant, the crowd surged forward and whipped into a frenzy.

"Don't let him get away! Grab him! Make him pay! If he doesn't cough up the money, we'll tear this shady company to the ground!"

"Yeah! Smash it all!"

The companions at Elliot's side leaped into action and corralled the instigators, steering the group toward the inner sanctum of the company.

They were tall, burly, and had a murderous look in their eyes. Anyone with half a brain could see they were spoiling for a fight.

But Alexander just sneered. These guys were obviously from the Vance family.

Bang!

Alexander flung out a powerful palm strike, hitting one of the biggest thugs square in the chest.

The massive man flew backward, sailing over the heads of the crowd until he smashed into the wall. The impact was so strong that he stuck to the wall for a moment before slumping to the ground.

Before the other thugs could react, Alexander slapped them away one by one, sending them crashing into the crowd like they were nothing more than ragdolls.

Within seconds, the floor was littered with bodies, and the surge of attackers was brought to a grinding halt.

The last remaining thug, a tall figure in black, stood frozen in place. As soon as Alexander's gaze landed on him, the man turned on his heel and tried to make a run for it.

But Alexander was faster. He grabbed the man by the neck and yanked him off his feet, holding him in the air like he weighed nothing.

"Julian," Alexander growled, "you really thought you could run?"

With one swift motion, Alexander tore off the man's face, literally peeling away a thin, translucent face mask.

Gasps erupted all around. Ava and the others from the Brilliant Group stood stunned, their mouths hanging open in disbelief.

Underneath the mask was none other than Julian, the warehouse supervisor who had gone missing!
