Human potential is limitless. As described in the teachings of Ultimate Legacy, every person carries within them an energy universe.

There was once a news story about a mother who found her child trapped beneath rubble after an earthquake, barely clinging to life.

Her unconditional love and sense of duty for her child ignited her inner universe in an instant.

Her adrenaline levels skyrocketed to terrifying heights in mere moments!

That mother, relying solely on her own strength, lifted a heavy concrete slab and saved her child.

Marvin was the same now—driven by rage and the bond of brotherhood.

He erupted completely, like an enraged tiger.

His entire body trembled violently, his movements growing faster and faster, leaving behind afterimages as he neared breaking free from his restraints.

The terrifying snakes and venom were closing in on them.

The woman suddenly screamed, "No... My psychic restraints... I can't hold him much longer!"

Locke's face flashed with surprise once again.

Before she could finish speaking, Marvin leapt up, grabbing Shadow as he swiftly dodged all incoming attacks.

"Damn it!" Locke cursed.

Locke's expression turned grim. He had dual abilities—not only could he control venomous snakes, but he also possessed second-level firearm manipulation skills.

His hands moved in a blur, conjuring an invisible, eerie force that churned around him.

The swarm of snakes grew even more agitated, launching a frenzied assault on Marvin and Shadow.

Marvin fired his compact submachine gun relentlessly as he and Shadow retreated under fire.

One by one, the snakes were shot to pieces, their bodies writhing on the ground.

Suddenly, a piercing gunshot rang out.

A sniper bullet grazed Marvin's arm, causing him to stumble and fall to the ground.

Faced with countless snakes swarming them and Locke sniping from the shadows, both Marvin and Shadow felt a sense of despair.

Another shot rang out as Locke's sniper bullet, enhanced by his abilities, flew rapidly toward Marvin's head.

A life-or-death moment!

Bang!

A bullet flew in at a barely visible speed, colliding violently with Locke's shot.

Locke's bullet was instantly shattered, the opposing bullet clearly far more powerful.

"Boss!" Marvin shouted.

"Sir!" Shadow cried out.

Marvin and Shadow cheered in excitement.

Though the battle had lasted only a few minutes, it felt as if decades had passed for them.

For the first time, Locke's expression shifted. "Alexander is here! He's got a sniper rifle, too—be careful!" he warned urgently.

Boom!

Pale flames erupted around Shadow and Marvin, reducing any attacking cobras to ashes.

Even the venom was vaporized by the flames before it could reach them, vanishing without a trace.

Undeterred, Locke directed the snake swarm to continue its attack on Shadow and Marvin while firing at the rapidly approaching figure.

Almost simultaneously, two gunshots echoed.

Locke's bullet, enhanced by his second-level firearm ability, was exponentially faster and more powerful.

Alexander's bullet, however, was infused with a faint aura of the Force.

The two bullets collided with force, shattering Locke's shot into countless fragments.

Alexander's bullet continued its lightning-fast path, striking Locke squarely.

Fortunately, Locke dodged quickly, and the bullet only grazed his left arm rather than hitting his heart.

Still, even the graze tore a chunk of flesh from Locke's arm, leaving it bloodied and nearly useless.

The woman, pale and anxious, said, "I can't restrain him at all... His psychic ability is stronger than mine. I can't do it..."

Locke, with a cold tone, reminded her, "His sniper rifle reloads quickly. You have less than two seconds to attack. If you fail, both of us will be killed by Alexander.

"Hurry up! Use your full power and unleash your strongest ability!"

The woman closed her eyes again, stretching her hands toward Alexander's direction.

Locke shouted, "I've got him locked! Now's the time—restrain him!"

"Ahh!" she screamed.

The woman shouted loudly, clearly using every ounce of her strength. Her voice was piercingly unpleasant.

Locke took aim at Alexander and pulled the trigger with deadly intent.

A bullet tore through the air, reaching Alexander in the blink of an eye.
