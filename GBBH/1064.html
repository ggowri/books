Meanwhile, Trenton had organized his men and was ready to eliminate the members of the Sakra Society.

Their base was located in the Riverside Hotel.

The members of the Sakra Society were not numerous, but with a lot of financial support behind them, they were quite wealthy.

In the deep night, several black cars pulled into the underground parking lot of the hotel.

There were already several armed men waiting. Plenty of women were dragged out of the cars.

Disheveled and embarrassed, they embarked on the nightmare of their lives.

"Hurry up! When you enter this place, there is no chance to go back."

"Don't stand there. Go!"

They forced those women into the hotel aggressively.

A middle-aged man approached Cyclops, the rising star of the Sakra Society, and smiled carefully, "Sir, I have arranged a room for you..."

Cyclops slapped him hard in the face.

"Tell me, how did that bitch Hazel run away? What is going on now?"

"She... All the people we sent didn't come back... The only one who returned soon died. Please forgive me!"

The man desperately begged for mercy, his face as white as paper.

He knew that Cyclops was formidable, ruthless, and murderous.

Cyclops snorted, "You are all losers."

He kicked the man to the floor and said, pointing to a young woman, "Bring her over. I need to vent my anger!"

She shivered and then screamed miserably, "Don't touch me! Kill me... Kill me right now..."

However, she couldn't resist. Two burly men grabbed her and took her to Cyclops.

"Bitch, are you courting death? I asked you to come over, but you still howled to refuse."

Cyclops' horrible expression made the woman weep.

As soon as he waved his hand, someone immediately put on her a dog collar and a dog chain as thick as an index finger.

The chain made of fine steel was strong and heavy.

Cyclops held the chain and looked as haughty as if he were walking a dog.​

The woman was so ashamed and resentful that she longed to kill the devil in front of her. He deceived her.

Cyclops could always coax some women into trusting him by virtue of his good looks and sweet words.

The woman was once the proprietress of the Riverside Hotel, but she was controlled by Cyclops, and the hotel became his stronghold.

It was too late for her to make any resistance now.

All the women were escorted into the hotel, whose basement had been converted into a modern prison by Cyclops.

The compartments inside were exactly like those in a prison, with 24-hour guards and a monitoring system.

As those women drew nearer, their faces darkened even more, and they couldn't help but shiver violently.

They saw a lot of battered women and all kinds of racks and strange instruments of torture. It was like hell.

Cyclops ordered coldly, "Put collars and chains on them. I want to give them a warning!"

Soon, the women were put on chains one by one, and their clothes were stripped off, leaving them naked.

"Listen up, you bitches. From now on, you are my property, and you must obey my orders completely!

"If anyone dares to disobey, I will break her legs and give her to the tramp on the street.

"Do you understand?"

Cyclops' gloomy, threatening voice, combined with his fierce subordinates and the terrible torture devices, made the women tremble.

They all hung their heads, not daring to make a sound.

Cyclops shouted, "Very good. Now, lie down like dogs."

At that moment, the proprietress scolded, "You have gone too far! We are all dignified. If you return the hotel to me, I won't call the police."

All the other women glanced up at her with gratitude and sympathy.

At the same time, they deeply understood that the proprietress was doomed.

"Fucking bitch! How dare you talk back to me in public and threaten to call the police?

"You are seeking death! Since you don't like being a dog, that's fine! I'll show you what kind of woman is my good dog," Cyclops laughed grimly, planning to torture this bitch to threaten others.
