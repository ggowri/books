Oscar tucked the check away with a pleased smile.

"You're a sly one, aren't you? Don't worry; I'll keep my mouth shut."

As Alexander was about to leave, he noticed that Felicity was still in the hall.

"Felicity, didn't I ask Sir Stewart to send someone to take you home?"

"Thank you, Alexander, but I don't want to go back. I'd rather find a place to stay for now."

She looked at him with a hopeful expression. It looked like she was waiting for an invitation to his place.

Alexander could only force a bitter smile, pretending he hadn't caught her hint.

"Not going home might be for the best. It'll save you from their pressure. Sir Stewart, Felicity just graduated from college and hasn't found a job yet. Could I trouble you to help her find a job and a place to stay?"

Oscar readily agreed. To him, this was a trivial matter.

After learning that Alexander might control the magic powder, Oscar had grown even more respectful of him and was eager to keep their relationship in good standing.

Felicity looked disappointed. She watched Alexander leave, lingering as if she wanted to say something, but no words came out.

Before he got into his car, Alexander waved goodbye to her. Soon, his old Toyota vanished into the busy traffic.

Thinking of her family's selfishness and Alexander's selfless help, Felicity couldn't stop the tears from streaming down her face.

Lara, who had been waiting for quite some time, immediately started her car and began following.

"Alexander, I'll see just what you're up to!"

Alexander quickly noticed the car tailing him and pressed down on the accelerator.

His car shot into the opposite lane, darted through an intersection, and sped away.

Lara was caught off guard, nearly ran into another car. She pulled over quickly, her face pale with fear as she steadied herself.

"Alexander, you bastard! I'll expose whatever scheme you and Oscar are plotting!"

Meanwhile, Jane entered the hospital room with a song on her lips, swaying her hips confidently. Her alluring figure drew many admiring glances as she walked.

She handed a document to Sebastian.

"Mr. Tate, great news! Mr. Gellar has agreed to grant Brilliant Group as the major distributor of the magic powder!"

Sebastian's face lit up with delight.

"Ah, Jane, you're incredible. It's a privilege to have someone as capable as you on the team. Once the conference is over, you'll be our new vice president."

Jane's face turned red with excitement.

Just then, Andrew grinned obsequiously and said, "Mr. Tate, we advanced forty million dollars as a deposit for Mr. Gellar. Could we get that back?"

Sebastian's face darkened, and he said tersely, "What? Coming to me just for forty million? Isn't it clearly laid out in the contract?"

"When Mr. Gellar's partnership with us is sealed, we'll give him a billion dollars. Have him pay you back then."

Andrew was left speechless.

Annoyed, Jane chided, "Dad, how could you bring up the forty million dollars in front of Mr. Tate? Don't you have any sense of the bigger picture?"

Andrew shot her a glare but kept silent.

Sebastian waved them off. "You've both worked hard; go take a rest."

Though they wanted to stay and curry more favor with Sebastian, his dismissal left Andrew and Jane with no choice but to leave, looking rather deflated.

"Once I officially become the vice president, I'll make Alexander come begging. Let's see who dares to cross me then!"

Jane gritted her teeth, her eyes filled with a fierce hatred toward Alexander.

In the hospital room, Sebastian kept looking over the contract, growing more pleased with each glance.

"Evan, tomorrow when we meet that Jackson guy, we need to find a way to capture him. We must take over the formula for the magic powder."

Evan's face was lit with excitement.

"Dad, once we control the magic powder, every faction, the Blood Gang, the Cripples, the Kaylon, and the four major families in Loscity, will all be lining up to work with us."

"We'll destroy the Saint Group and take Alexander out!"

Sebastian nodded.

"Of course. And it's all thanks to that foolish Jane. She still dreams of marrying into our family. She's deluded."

"When we merge the Saint Group into us and kill Alexander, your uncle will be the next Godfather!"

"By then, we'll be the most powerful force of all!"

Filled with visions of their bright future, Sebastian and Evan laughed with anticipation.
