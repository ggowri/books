Keith didn't feel embarrassed at all; instead, he looked quite proud.

Marvin, on the other hand, felt uncomfortable. Although he liked women, being teased by a group of them in front of hundreds of people was embarrassing.

"Tsk, tsk, this one is big and dark, but the firmness isn't quite there," muttered the lead female examiner regretfully.

Marvin's chest heaved with anger; if Alexander weren't there, he would have slapped her by now.

Soon, the hundreds of men in the hall were reduced to just a handful of about ten.

Those dismissed for not meeting the sixteen-inch requirement could only watch Keith and the others with envy, their eyes sharp with jealousy.

At that moment, the female examiner took out a flexible ruler and waved it in front of everyone.

The ruler whistled as it moved!

"Congratulations to everyone who passed the first round. Now, it's time for the second round, the endurance test.

"Like this!"

She suddenly swung the ruler and struck a man's waistband, not too hard but firm enough.

"Ah!"

The man let out a pained scream, clutching his waistband and rolling on the ground.

"Useless! I barely used any force. No endurance at all, and you still want to be an actor? Out, out, out!"

The woman called for the unlucky man to be dragged out.

Alexander covered his mouth, almost bursting out laughing.

The female examiner continued, tapping the ruler on the applicants' waistbands with quick strikes.

Cries of pain and groans erupted throughout the room.

That was the most sensitive area for men, and with a strike like that, most of them fell to the ground, clutching their waistbands.

One of them even passed out and was immediately carried out on a stretcher.

Marvin had had enough and quickly hid behind Alexander.

Strangely, the female examiner who had been eyeing Marvin seemed frozen, as if under a spell, unmoving.

Marvin sighed in relief, giving a bitter smile. "Boss, if I did something wrong, just tell me. Don't mess with me like this again."

Alexander burst into laughter.

Keith was the last one to face the examiners.

"Bring it on! I can handle it!"

The female examiner seemed to value Keith highly, saving him for the final test.

"Smack!"

The ruler struck, but Keith stood perfectly still, not even making a sound.

The woman didn't hold back, but Keith endured it.

The pain that would have been intense was dulled by the Force, and Keith was able to withstand it.

"Yes!"

"Amazing! Impressive!"

The examiners were immediately excited.

The woman, not giving up, struck two more times, but Keith remained motionless.

The room buzzed with astonishment; they had never seen a man with such endurance—it was as if he were made of iron.

Keith's heart swelled with gratitude as he looked at Alexander, feeling as if he could do anything for him.

After the two rounds of testing, only five men remained.

The men eliminated in the second round weren't completely out of luck.

They could still receive temporary offers from the Dragon Gang to play supporting roles.

Supporting roles in these films were the lowest tier, where they could only watch without participating or having physical contact with the lead actress.

They were allowed to entertain themselves in front of the lead actress.

At most, during key moments, they could join in for some collective action.

Participating in such scenes let them watch up close and came with decent pay.

There was even a chance to eventually be promoted to a lead role, which made many willing to take the opportunity.

The five men who passed both rounds would become lead actors in the films.

Of course, at first, they would act alongside lesser-known actresses.

But the generous pay and the chance to be with different actresses every day made it feel almost regal.

Eventually, they might even worry that their daily activities would outpace their stamina and nutrition.

Male actors in this line of work often lost twenty pounds within a year due to the frequency and suffered from malnutrition.

Some even developed conditions like nephritis or prostatitis.
