Deep in the picturesque mountains of Mesco stood a wealthy estate, exuding grandeur and wealth.

The property wasn't just a feast for the eyes with its natural beauty and melodious birdsong. It was a fortress bristling with security measures. For miles around, a web of guards and lookouts kept watch, complemented by cutting-edge satellite surveillance.

It was no exaggeration to say that not even a bird could fly in without the owner's permission.

Inside a uniquely decorated study, a tall, bronze-skinned man in his prime was blowing his top.

Surrounding him, over a dozen men and women of varied appearances stood with bowed heads, not daring to utter a peep.

Any outsider catching sight of this group would be floored.

Each person present was a big shot, capable of making entire cities in Mesco quake with just a word.

They froze like deer in headlights, not daring to make a sound.

The man before them was Guzman, the kingpin of the Mesco drug cartel and chairman of the Brilliant Group.

"Sebastian, has Theodore reached Loscity yet?" Guzman asked in a grave tone.

Sebastian quickly replied, "Don't worry. Theodore's in Loscity and has started the operation. We should hear good news soon."

Guzman nodded.

"We need to investigate Nicholas and Daniel's disappearance. If it wasn't The Kaylon, it must be the Saint Group. And we need to look into Betty. Why is she unharmed?"

"Yes, Guzman. Theodore took several of our best men. They'll definitely take out that punk Alexander and seize control of the Saint Group."

"Excellent!"

Guzman slammed his fist on the table with satisfaction.

The table vibrated, sending out a shockwave that made everyone's eardrums ache.

The group winced, wiping away cold sweat while gazing at Guzman with awe.

Guzman's eyes swept across the room as he fingered the small black doll hanging from his neck, grinning smugly.

The voodoo doll crafted by the dark wizard was genuinely miraculous, boosting both his strength and speed by more than twofold.

Guzman even felt like he was getting younger, turning back the clock.

Guzman scoffed inwardly, "A mere bastard dares to challenge me for that position? What a pipe dream."

Everyone suddenly felt a terrifying, cold, murderous intent emanating from Guzman. Their backs broke out in cold sweat as they hurriedly made their excuses and left.

Even Sebastian's face turned ashen.

It wasn't until he was far from the study that he finally breathed a long sigh of relief, feeling truly safe.

As Guzman's most trusted man, second only to the boss himself, his life was far from easy.

Guzman's terrifying eyes seemed to be watching him every second of every day.

The next day, at The Kaylon's headquarters in Loscity...

Ava personally came to visit Oscar to discuss alliance matters.

Oscar chuckled while brewing coffee.

"Ms. Blackburn, as you can see, I'm in great shape. How could I possibly have Parkinson's?"

Ava hadn't expected Oscar to hold a grudge against Alexander still.

"Sir Stewart, my boyfriend, has mysterious abilities. I suggest you get a thorough check-up at the hospital."

Oscar sighed, "Ms. Blackburn, I really can't figure out what it is about Alexander that has you so obsessed. Why do you keep defending him?"

Ava smiled sweetly, "Because he's my boyfriend, of course I trust him. Besides, he can actually help treat my angina."

At the mention of Alexander, Ava's eyes sparkled, and she couldn't help but smile.

Oscar, being experienced in these matters, could tell Ava had fallen for Alexander.

He snorted dismissively, "Ms. Blackburn, after I returned yesterday, I had someone thoroughly investigate Alexander's background."

"He spent five years as a loser for the Fairfax family, living like a dog. It wasn't until he met you that he started throwing his weight around.

"If I'm not mistaken, the half a million dollars for his daughter Ashley's surgery came out of your pocket, didn't it, Ms. Blackburn?

"To put it bluntly, a man like that is nothing but dead weight!"

Oscar finished with a sneer, his contempt obvious.

Ava felt a sudden surge of anger.

She couldn't stand anyone trash-talking Alexander in front of her, always ready to defend him tooth and nail.

Time to give this old coot a piece of her mind!
