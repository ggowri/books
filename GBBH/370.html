"Alexander, how's my grandpa?"

Rebecca asked cautiously.

Quentin also looked at Alexander with expectation.

The doctors, however, smirked with faint contempt, seeming unimpressed.

But none of them dared to speak up; they simply watched, hoping Alexander would fail.

"Though it's critical, thankfully I got here in time. He can be saved."

Alexander channeled the Force into his hands and placed them gently on Ralph's body.

A solemn, heavy energy filled the room, and everyone tensed up, watching intently.

Alexander's Force energy entered Ralph's body, slowly gathering and expelling the toxins that had been spreading inside him.

Gradually, drops of dark, tainted blood began trickling from Ralph's nose and ears.

Seeing this strange sight, everyone held their breath, watching Alexander treat Ralph with mixed expressions.

The doctors, who had initially doubted Alexander, now looked stunned, faces full of surprise.

Even so, they still didn't entirely believe Alexander could cure Ralph.

Meanwhile, Byron arrived at the Hansen Villa in his Ferrari, leading a grand procession of cars.

A dozen black sedans lined up as several men in black suits exited, along with Orion and a few of the Hansen family members.

Led by Orion, they all gathered behind Byron.

"What do you want?"

The security guards blocked their way.

Orion shouted angrily, "Get out of my way! Don't you see I'm back?"

The security guards' faces fell, and they stepped aside.

Ever since Ralph fell ill, Orion had broken free from house arrest and gathered some Hansen family supporters to seize the head position.

"Out of my way, fools!"

Orion kicked down one guard and strode inside.

Byron followed closely, chuckling darkly.

Orion said flatteringly, "Don't worry, Mr. Clifford. Ralph's as good as dead today, and I'll make sure Rebecca goes with you obediently."

A smile had just crossed Byron's face when he noticed a Porsche parked nearby, and his expression darkened again.

Byron was instantly enraged, storming over to the Porsche and kicking it hard.

"Bang! Bang!"

He shattered both front headlights.

Still not satisfied, Byron angrily ordered his men, "Smash it!"

His men grabbed pipes and axes, smashing the car wildly.

"Beep! Beep!"

The Porsche let out a piercing alarm.

"What are you doing? That's Ms. Hansen's car!"

One of the security guards yelled angrily, rushing forward.

Byron gave a vicious wave. "Smash Anyone who tries to stop me with the car!"

With Ralph critically ill, the Hansen family members had split into two factions, most siding with Orion. None could resist Byron's prepared forces.

Cries of pain and calls for help rang out as every person who tried to block Byron was knocked down.

Quentin and Rebecca were both alerted, but Alexander was tied up with the treatment and couldn't leave.

Outside, the car alarms, shouts, and cries of pain continued, a clear sign of trouble.

"What's going on?"

Rebecca hurried outside to ask.

Seeing the smashed-up car and Hansen family members sprawled all over the ground, Rebecca's anger flared.

Seeing Rebecca, Byron and Orion immediately gathered their group and surrounded her.

"Rebecca, this is what you get for hitting me on purpose! Hand over the culprit and come home with me, or I'll make you regret it."

Byron shouted excitedly, without a trace of shame.

Rebecca's face darkened with anger. This guy was not only falsely accusing her but completely twisting the story. It's unbelievable.

"Byron, you're the one who tried to hit us with your car, and now you're blaming us? You can dream if you think I'm going home with you!"

Rebecca spat in Byron's face.

Byron was furious, nearly choking with anger as he cursed and wiped the spit off his face.

Orion quickly stepped forward, putting on an act as if he were protecting Byron.

"Rebecca, you conspired with Alexander to poison Grandpa and tried to cause a car accident to kill Mr. Clifford. These are serious crimes. It's unforgivable.

"As head of the Hansen family, I declare that you are now expelled from the family and handed over to Mr. Clifford to deal with!"
