"Your dad? Like father, like son."

Alexander gave a murderous smile.

He stepped on Jordan's chest and said coldly, "You have the shares of the Silver Shield Security, don't you? If you don't want to die, transfer all the shares to me."

Alexander gave Jordan a kick.

"Write a gratis transfer agreement now."

"No, I..."

Jordan looked around with fright. He didn't want to do that.

He owned 20% of the shares of Silver Shield Security, which was worth nearly 30 million dollars. He couldn't give it away for free.

Alexander chuckled.

"You want money instead of your life? Fine. Go to hell!"

After he spoke, Alexander reached out and grabbed Jordan's neck again.

Jordan screamed with fright. He valued his life more than money, so he shouted, "Don't kill me. Please. Don't kill me. I'll write it right away."

Under Alexander's coercion, Jordan got up and began to write the transfer agreement tremblingly.

"Don't make any mistakes. If you make a mistake, you will die!" Alexander's words were extremely cold, which was like a bolt from the blue to Jordan's ears.

"You are ruthless."

As Jordan held the pen, he wrote and muttered, his forehead breaking out in cold sweat.

"If you say another word of nonsense, I'll cut off your tongue."

Alexander stared at him murderously.

Jordan's heart was aching. He was going to give away the shares that were worth hundreds of millions of dollars for free.

It took Jordan more than ten minutes to write the short agreement and sign it.

After that, he seemed to be drained of strength and collapsed to the ground with a blank look on his face.

Alexander picked up the agreement and read it carefully. He put it away and said with a smile, "Nice. Since you are so obedient, I won't kill you, but for the sake of your safety, you'd better be a fool."

Jordan was shocked and angry. Just as he was about to scream loudly, Alexander slapped him on the head.

Jordan shuddered violently and then fell to the ground with a stupid expression, his mouth and eyes twitching.

"You deserve it!"

Alexander spat at him and went away calmly.

After he got into the car, Emma asked him anxiously, "Did you find them?"

Alexander nodded and said, "Yes. They are with Jordan's Dad Thomas. Let's go and settle the score with him."

Emma said in surprise, "Thomas? He is not simple. He has close ties with the gangsters in Newbrook. I'm afraid it's not easy to deal with him."

Although she knew that Alexander was capable, she was a little worried.

"Don't worry. Let's go."

Alexander didn't take it seriously and drove fast in the dark.

A few dozen minutes later, Alexander parked his car in front of the door of a high-end club.

Although it was late at night, the club was brightly lit and bustling with people.

Alexander headed toward the door alone leisurely.

Before he even reached the door, he was stopped by several security guards.

"We only accept VIP members. Outsiders are not allowed," The bald security guard warned fiercely.

"VIP members?"

Alexander waved his hand indifferently.

"Bang."

The bald man was sent flying away, and everyone present was shocked.

Before they knew what had happened, Alexander had entered the club.

"Stop!"

Several burly men rushed out from somewhere and surrounded Alexander.

These people were burly built and had menacing looks.

Judging from the calluses on their thick knuckles, Alexander knew they were a well-trained elite force of the club.

"Who are you? How dare you to cause trouble here? You're seeking death."

One of the big men pushed Alexander suddenly, roaring fiercely and murderously.

"Why are you barking at me?"

Alexander cursed and launched an attack quickly, grabbing the heads of the two nearby men and slamming them together.

"Bang."

The foreheads of the two big men were dented, and they fell to the ground.

Before the remaining two could react, Alexander kicked them in the lower abdomen, and they fainted in the pain.

Alexander continued to stride forward.

At the end of the hallway, there was a grand door.

A dozen bodyguards were standing at the door.

Alexander headed straight to the door. He had seen everything in the room with his X-ray vision.
