Gale took a deep breath to steady himself. Howard's cries were only making things worse, adding fuel to the fire.

"Shut up!" he barked, his voice harsh, and Howard instantly fell silent, startled by the fury in Gale's tone.

Even the doctors, caught off guard, looked up at Gale, but quickly returned to their frantic work.

Alexander noticed an older man, his hair graying, drenched in sweat. He was guiding the other doctors in their desperate attempts to save Gavin.

The strain on his face was evident, as if he were carrying a heavy burden.

"Dr. Matt, how is my father doing?" Gale asked, his voice grave.

"Mr. Gale Lawson, Gavin has suffered a stroke. The clot is located in the brainstem... It's a very complex area, and I'm afraid the damage may be irreversible.

"Prepare to inject adrenaline, get the defibrillator ready!" Dr. Matt ordered, his hands trembling.

"Okay, there's still hope! We can still save him!"

Dr. Matt's heart sank. He felt as though the ground had been ripped from beneath him. He almost fainted from the shock.

The Lawson family members staggered back, their faces pale, their legs weak.

If Gavin died, not only would the Lawson family fall from grace, but the repercussions would send shockwaves throughout the entire power structure!

"Don't worry. Gavin won't die."
