Seeing this, everyone gasped in disbelief, staring at Alexander.

Everyone knew Brent was a formidable fighter. He was undoubtedly a master, yet Alexander had sent him flying with a single punch.

The wall even cracked like that. Was this even possible for a human to do?

"Who dares to cause trouble at the Pegasus Club?"

Kyle's furious voice boomed from inside.

Alexander's harsh punishment of Brent and the others had finally alerted Kyle within.

Kyle, disheveled and with a flushed face still glowing with excitement, had been in the throes of passion with two women, only to be rudely interrupted, his anger flaring instantly.

Alexander's eyes sharply scanned Kyle—the high-ranking member of the Vanderbilt family, Felix's uncle, and a metahuman with fire power.

Alexander's All-seeing Eyes could see a surge of red energy within Kyle, harboring immense power. This was clearly his fire power.

No wonder he had inflicted such severe injuries on Charlize!

Kyle looked at Bradley, whose face was swollen on one side, bloodied and bruised.

Though it looked terrifying, there was no danger to his life.

Unconscious Brent wasn't so fortunate. He lay there motionless, his breathing growing weaker by the moment.

"Mr. Vanderbilt, you have to avenge me!"

Bradley lay on the ground, trembling, the searing pain on his cheek nearly driving him mad.

Kyle glared menacingly at Alexander.

"Do you know that the Pegasus Club is a property of the Red Umbrella Group? Do you know who I am? You dare to injure my guests. Are you looking for death?"

He had only seen Alexander's photo and, in his rage, didn't recognize him on the spot.

Alexander ignored Kyle completely. Instead, he looked at the others.

"What... what do you want to do?"

The rest were petrified with fear, all hoping Alexander wouldn't turn on them, scrambling to hide.

Alexander pointed at the women and said calmly, "You all leave. This doesn't concern you."

"Thank you..."

The women who had been coerced shuddered, tears streaming down their faces as they ran outside.

Anyone who tried to stop them was slapped away by Alexander, falling to the ground and spitting out blood.

"Whoever dares to block will be shown no mercy!"

Alexander's domineering aura silenced everyone.

All the women left without any objections or interventions, not even from Kyle.

Kyle gnashed his teeth, staring at Alexander.

"I know who you are now. You're Alexander!"

"Finally, you recognize me? Your eyesight seems poor. Tell me. Where have you hidden my daughter?"

Alexander's deep eyes were filled with ruthlessness and murderous intent.

In an instant, the temperature in the room plummeted.

Walls, sofas, tables, chandeliers, floors... A layer of icy frost covered everything in the room.

Kyle and his friends shivered, their hair standing on end.

"Alexander, so you're a metahuman with freezing power? You think that can suppress my fire power? Dream on!"

Kyle suddenly waved his hand, and a torrent of scorching flame erupted, ravaging the room and instantly melting all the frost.

"I thought you were more formidable. Die!"

Kyle laughed triumphantly and stomped his foot.

The solid marble floor shattered.

The powerful recoil propelled Kyle like a launched arrow, charging fiercely at Alexander.

"Die!"

Kyle roared and punched Alexander's head.

His fist was engulfed in flames, terrifying to behold.

In fact, this punch had concentrated all of Kyle's fire power.

Faced with a formidable enemy like Alexander, he had even exceeded his limits, his strength approaching that of a level-two metahuman.

Alexander had used the Force to simulate freezing, leading Kyle to believe he was a level-one metahuman.

Kyle thought this punch, if not able to shatter Alexander's skull, would at least force him into a desperate dodge.

The flames howled!

His fist pierced through Alexander's head, but there was no expected splatter of blood and flesh.

Kyle's smile froze, replaced by horror.
