"I'm Alexander, here to see Ms. Hansen."

Upon hearing this, the two security guards trembled in fear, frozen in place.

They both knew that Alexander was close to Rebecca and that he was a leader of Ultimate Alliance.

"Is there a problem? Can't I come in?"

Alexander's gaze swept over them.

"Apologies, Mr. Hawthorne. Please, go ahead!"

The two security guards immediately apologized and hurriedly stepped aside to let Alexander in.

Watching Alexander's confident back as he entered, the guards wiped the sweat from their brows. The man's presence was simply too overwhelming.

"Alexander!"

Upon seeing him, Rebecca immediately flew into his arms happily.

"You're here!"

Ava and Lynn were also at Rebecca's place. Surprised and happy to see them, Alexander asked, "You didn't think to invite me along when you came here?"

"Oh, you're such a busy man! How could we dare disturb you? Today, Rebecca's moving into her new house, so we came to congratulate her. Who knew you'd come as well?" Ava smiled as she clung to Alexander's arm, explaining.

Alexander nodded repeatedly. He had indeed been very busy lately, and if not for the fact that he hadn't visited Rebecca in a while, he wouldn't have come.

Besides, he had some important matters to discuss with her.

"Rebecca, does your family have several sugar factories?" Alexander asked.

Rebecca looked at him with some confusion. "Yes. So what?"

Alexander smiled, "Perfect. Pack up one of the sugar factories for me. I want to ship all the equipment to Treasure Island."

"Ah? Why?" Rebecca was shocked and asked.

Aside from Ava and Betty, Alexander hadn't shared his future plans with Rebecca, but now, the time had come to give her a warning.

Alexander signaled to Ava, who immediately understood his intention and pulled Rebecca aside to explain.

"Rebecca, here's the situation...

"Sakra Society's Yinson has restarted the Apocalypse Project, planning to turn most of humanity into his slaves. He's also developed enhancement drugs and has already controlled many wealthy elites.

"Alexander believes that it won't be long before global chaos erupts. So, he's been working to turn Treasure Island and Devil Island into doomsday bases."

"What?" Rebecca's eyes widened in disbelief.

She had heard of the Apocalypse Project and knew a little about the zombie outbreak in the suburbs of Loscity.

But she never imagined that Yinson could be so evil.

"Alexander, what do you need a sugar factory for?" Rebecca was still confused.

Alexander smiled, "Sugar not only provides energy but can also be used to make explosives, which is considered military material.

"We can plant sugar crops, but to refine them into sugar, we'll need the factories and workers."

Rebecca suddenly understood and quickly took out her phone to make a call.

"Alexander, I've given the order. Not just the sugar factory, but if there are any resources at the Hansen family that you can use, you can have them for free!"

Alexander nodded in satisfaction. "Good. When the time comes, we'll go to the island together and live our happy life."

Rebecca's face turned pink, and she shyly glanced at Ava.

Ava smiled at her and then playfully rolled her eyes at Alexander.

Alexander chuckled, placing his left hand on Rebecca's thigh and his right hand gently holding Ava's hand.

Lynn, watching from the side, didn't say anything, but her intense gaze met with Alexander's.

Excited, Alexander couldn't help but think of ways to get these three beautiful women to spend time with him. It would be so much fun!

"Don't get any ideas. There are still many guests coming!"

Ava pinched him heavily.

Alexander pretended to be in pain, shouting loudly, which made the three beauties burst into giggles.

To be honest, Alexander truly didn't understand how Ava, Rebecca, and Lynn all got along so well together.

"It must be my charm. They can't resist me." Alexander thought proudly.

"Let's go, we're going swimming!"

Rebecca pulled Ava and Lynn toward the pool at the back.

"Alexander, you should join us!"

"Swimming? Didn't you say there were other guests coming? Is it a pool party?"

Alexander quickly followed them, eager to feast his eyes on the fun that awaited.
