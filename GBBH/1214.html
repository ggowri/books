There was no doubt in Ilona's mind—Alexander's strength far exceeded hers.

"Why... How is this possible? Level-four metahumans are already rare, and the best among us... And now this guy turns out to be one of them too?"

Ilona had already decided to admit defeat. Facing an opponent like Alexander, she stood no chance at all.

Alexander observed everyone's expressions, then waved his hand toward the shooting range.

Dozens of bullets stormed forward like a violent downpour, slamming into the thick concrete wall.

The wall, over three feet thick, was riddled with dozens of holes, swaying unsteadily before collapsing with a thunderous crash under their stunned gazes.

At that moment, the digital screen displayed a total score of 900 points!

In the same amount of time, Alexander had scored more than twice as much as Ilona.

The room fell into dead silence—Ilona had lost miserably!

"Defeated! But I can't believe it. Could he already possess level-six abilities?"

Gael's expression was utterly grim. The thought of having offended a metahuman three levels above him sent chills down his spine.

Everyone else distanced themselves from Gael's group, eager to draw a clear line.

Alexander's abilities could no longer be described as extraordinary—they were terrifying.

If Alexander wanted, he could easily kill everyone present without breaking a sweat.

Brittany and Quentin were both thrilled, unable to suppress their excitement. They hadn't expected Alexander to grow this powerful—it was simply unbelievable.

"I lost!"

Ilona admitted defeat decisively, lowering her head deeply. Her cheeks burned red, feeling as though she'd been completely stripped bare and exposed in front of everyone.

"Alexander, I knew you'd win!" Brittany cheered as she ran over and hugged Alexander tightly.

"Hahaha!"

Quentin laughed heartily, "Amazing! You truly stunned everyone with that performance."

"I'm sorry. I apologize for my earlier recklessness and disrespect."

To everyone's surprise, Ilona put aside her pride and apologized, her tone now incredibly respectful.

As someone who always admired strength, Ilona had accepted Alexander's power after losing and naturally bowed her proud head.

There was even a faint hint of admiration and respect in the way she looked at Alexander.

"Sigh..."

Gael let out a deep sigh, clearly shaken to his core.

He had always thought of himself as a prodigy, but now even his leader, Ilona, had been humiliated by Alexander in front of everyone.

"This is all your fault! Can't you go a single day without stirring up trouble?"

Ilona raised her hand and slapped Gael hard across the face, leaving him dazed.

"Ms. Cox... I'm sorry! It's all my fault!" Gael apologized miserably, holding his face.

After witnessing Alexander's display of power, Gael knew he had no courage to stand against him.

"What's the point of apologizing to me? You'd better earn Mr. Hawthorne's forgiveness, or even I won't be able to save you—unless you have a death wish!"

Ilona's icy tone sent chills down his spine.

Gael's expression turned bitter. He realized Ilona was trying to save his life—if Alexander decided to act, no one could stop him, nor would anyone dare to try.

"I'm sorry, Mr. Hawthorne. Please, for the sake of us being part of the same operations team, forgive me this time.

"I also promise never to bother Ms. Thompson again!"

Gael bowed deeply to Alexander, his demeanor utterly respectful.

"I'm not going to waste my time on someone like you. I just want you to understand—having powers doesn't make you better than anyone else. Everyone is equal!

"You think ordinary people are like ants beneath your feet. Well, I see you the same way—just an ant I could crush with a single step.

"I hope you'll reflect on this and behave better in the future!"

After saying that, Alexander turned and walked away, taking Brittany and Quentin with him.

"Cough!"

Gael was so furious he coughed up a mouthful of blood. Judging by his state, he wouldn't be participating in tonight's mission.

"For a woman... is it really worth it?" Ilona sighed deeply.

She sighed again, glancing enviously at Brittany's retreating figure.

"You two, along with Gael, will spend the next month in solitary confinement!"

Gael and his two companions were led away miserably, not daring to utter another word.

The other team members were deeply reflective. Many of them had shared Gael's mindset deep down.
