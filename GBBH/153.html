"Don't worry, Boss. I'll get it done," Logan assured, taking the check from Alexander.

The Fairfax family had been swindled out of 40 million, with 30 million already landing in the hands of the Godfather, known as "SATAN." It was certainly a lost cause to try and reclaim that money.

Besides, the Fairfax family didn't really deserve sympathy. With this last 10 million, they could still live above the average person's standard.

However, dreaming of any wealthy or elite social circles was out of the question.

Alexander's phone rang again, and he felt a surge of anger. It seemed the Fairfax family clung to him like a relentless specter.

Surprisingly, it was a call from Lily this time.

Over the past few days, Alexander had been busy preparing for the conference, crafting magic powders, and dealing with Sebastian, leaving him no time to visit Lily.

He answered the call with a smile, but his expression quickly turned serious.

It was clear from Lily's hoarse voice that she'd been crying and was deeply upset, even despairing.

Rushing to the hospital, Alexander arrived just as a group of thugs, led by Nick, barged into Lily's room.

Lily sat in a wheelchair, tears streaming as she berated her son, "You ungrateful brat.

"What do you think you're doing? If you try anything, I'll call the police!"

Nick retorted arrogantly, "You old hag, you think you can secretly give all the money to Alexander behind my back?

"I'm here for the house today. Hand it over. I'm your son, your rightful heir.

"Calling the cops won't help you. If I don't get it today, you'll just give it to Alexander later! Now sign over the house to me, and get out!"

After being fired from the bank, Nick had set his sights on his mother's property, convinced that any money Alexander had must have come from Lily.

"I will never sign it over! Get out of here!" Lily shouted, her body trembling with anger.

Nick, however, gestured harshly to the thugs.

"Hold her down. Once she signs, the house is mine, and nobody can take it!"

Lily broke into sobs, frantically trying to break free.

Her injured leg and age left her defenseless against the brutish men.

"Nick, have you lost your mind? I'm your mother! When have I ever given Alexander money?

"Since your father passed, this house is all we have. When I'm gone, it'll be yours. Why betray me like this?" Lily cried, her voice filled with anguish.

"Quiet, you old witch! Stop trying to play the innocent!

"Alexander's nothing but a leech. Without you sneaking him money, he'd be nothing!"

Nick raged on, lifting his arm to strike Lily.

"Stop!" Alexander shouted, lunging forward to grab Nick's arm.

"You bastard, touch my aunt, and you'll regret it."

Nick let out a piercing scream, feeling his arm go numb.

"Alexander, you dare strike me in front of my mom? See, mom, he's hitting me!" Nick yelled shamelessly.

"Yeah, I hit you. So what?" Alexander replied, slapping Nick again, leaving him seeing stars.

Lily covered her face, unable to watch, tears flowing freely.

Seeing her so distraught broke Alexander's heart, and he shoved Nick aside with force.

Nick shouted at the men he brought, "What are you standing around for?

"I hired you to fight, not to spectate!

"Get him, and don't hold back. I'll take the fall if something happens!"

The thugs surrounded Alexander, rolling up their sleeves, ready to take him down.

Lily was terrified and immediately urged Alexander to run.

But Alexander wasn't worried in the slightest. Instead, he charged straight at the group of men.

The first guy he hit went down hard with a single punch from Alexander. A few more kicks and punches later, the remaining thugs were wailing in pain, begging for mercy on the floor.

Nick was frozen in shock, stammering out, "Y... you... don't hit me. My mom's been good to you. You can't hit me..."

His pathetic plea only fueled Alexander's anger.

Alexander grabbed Nick by the collar, pulling him close.

"Now you're hiding behind your mom's name to save your skin? Nick, if it weren't for my aunt's sake, you'd be dead already!" Alexander snarled.
