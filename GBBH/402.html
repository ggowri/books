Ella had no idea danger was looming as she walked into the office, lost in thought.

All the other teachers had already gone home, leaving her alone in the preschool.

A dark figure followed closely, like a snake ready to strike.

Once Ella left the office, the shadowy figure poured a liquid into her cup.

If Ella drank from that cup tomorrow, she'd undoubtedly consume the potion.

The figure smirked darkly.

"Heh heh, Mr. Vanderbilt is determined. Not only is he after Jane, but he won't spare this teacher, either. That brat, Ashley, won't escape for long!"

His goal was to poison Ella so that once she was hooked, he could control her.

Ella was Ashley's teacher; controlling her would give him plenty of opportunities to target Ashley.

"Daddy! Daddy!"

As soon as Ashley got home, she ran into Alexander's arms, begging for attention.

The little girl looked up at him with a sweet smile.

Looking at the adorable little angel, Alexander felt his heart melting.

He pinched his daughter's nose with a smile, saying, "Hey, little piggy, you're back! Were you good at school today?"

"I'm not a little piggy; you're the big piggy, Daddy! I was really good, and Ms. Purnell likes me best!"

Ashley giggled and tried to pinch Alexander's nose back.

But she couldn't reach his nose, so her soft little hands playfully grabbed and squeezed his face instead.

Alexander laughed heartily, happier than he'd been in a long time, as he held his daughter.

Ava watched them with a warm smile, sensing the strong father-daughter bond.

Her heart felt warm and full; this was what family felt like.

Ava suggested, "Since we're off tomorrow, why don't we go to the water park?"

"Yay! I love the water park! That's awesome. I want to go, I want to go!"

Ashley jumped up, clapping her hands.

Alexander agreed right away.

Ding-dong! Ding-dong!

Just then, the doorbell rang.

Alexander checked the security monitor and frowned. It was Andrew and Belinda.

Ava asked in confusion, "What are they doing here?"

Alexander opened the door without a word.

"What do you want?"

Andrew scowled and said, "I'm here to take Ashley out. I'm her grandpa, and I have the right."

Alexander grew more suspicious; this old man showing up out of the blue to take Ashley had to be up to something.

"I don't want to go, I don't want to..."

Ashley muttered, frightened, and hid in Ava's arms.

Andrew was about to lose his temper, but Belinda held him back.

Belinda smiled sweetly at Alexander.

"We're Ashley's grandparents and just want to take our granddaughter out for some family time. Is that a problem?"

Alexander thought it over for a moment, then refused.

"Sorry, Ashley doesn't want to go. Maybe you can come another time."

Belinda's expression changed immediately, and she started yelling.

"Damn you, Alexander! How dare you keep us from seeing Ashley? This is illegal!"

Andrew jumped up, absolutely furious.

"Alexander, if you don't hand over Ashley today, we're not done here!"

Their uproar only made Alexander more suspicious; there was no way he was letting them take Ashley.

"What law am I breaking? I'm the one with legal custody of Ashley. I'm her guardian, not you."

Alexander's words left Andrew speechless.

Belinda snorted, sneering sarcastically, "Fine, you won't let us take her? We'll have Jane come instead. She's Ashley's mother; surely you won't refuse her?"

Alexander didn't respond. Legally, he couldn't prevent Jane from taking Ashley out.

"Just wait. You wouldn't dare keep Ashley from her mother!"

Belinda's face lit up smugly.

Andrew pointed at Alexander's nose and cursed, "You punk, just wait and see if we can take her or not!"

The two of them stormed off, seething with anger.

Ashley trembled in fear, stammering, "I'm scared... I don't want to go..."

Alexander gently stroked his daughter's head and reassured her, "Don't worry, I won't let anyone hurt you!"
