This wasn't just a simple situation; the Porsche surged with terrifying power, going from several hundred horsepower to over a thousand instantly, hitting top speed immediately.

"Bang!"

The Porsche slammed hard into the rear corner of the Lamborghini.

Byron was sneering with smug satisfaction, having no idea that Porsche would suddenly slam into him so fiercely.

The forceful collision sent the Lamborghini flying, rolling several times and scraping against the guardrails with sparks flying before finally skidding to a stop several dozen miles away.

Thanks to Alexander's Force protection, the Porsche only had a slight dent in the front and was otherwise untouched.

The Lamborghini's airbags had fully deployed. While Byron wasn't seriously injured, his face was bloodied as he let out a horrified scream.

Alexander pulled the Porsche to a stop beside him. He rolled down the window, gave Byron the middle finger, and drove off, completely unbothered.

As the Porsche roared away, it left a blast of foul-smelling exhaust, causing Byron to curse out loud.

"You bastard! You crash into my car and flip me off... you're dead!"

Byron struggled out of his seat, cursing as he crawled out of the car.

His newly bought Lamborghini was completely wrecked, totaled by the impact.

A car worth over ten million, gone just like that, and he'd had it for less than a month.

"Damn it, that bastard!"

Byron's heart bled as his rage skyrocketed.

"Rebecca, you brat! You crash my car and run? Ralph's about to die anyway. Let's see how far you get!"

Byron called up a group of Hansen family members, then set off in hot pursuit toward the Hansen Villa.

In the car, Rebecca was thrilled, still buzzing from that massive crash.

"Alexander, you're incredible! You sent that jerk flying. He deserved it! I can't stop laughing!"

Alexander smiled slightly. "People like him need to be taught a lesson."

This was Rebecca's first experience with a collision like that, and she couldn't contain her excitement.

The guy had wanted to force her into marriage, yet now he'd been sent flying. Just thinking about it melted away her accumulated frustrations like snow meeting fire.

"Vroom, vroom..."

The sound of a powerful engine came from behind. Rebecca glanced back in surprise, her expression changing instantly.

"He just won't quit. He's chasing us again!"

Alexander glanced in the rearview mirror, then smiled calmly.

"He's not satisfied? Guess he's got a death wish. He can't catch us."

"Can't catch us?"

Seeing the Ferrari getting closer in the mirror, along with a large convoy, Rebecca looked worried.

Alexander just smiled silently. From the next corner on, the road was full of sharp curves. It's not like a straight stretch where more horsepower gives the advantage.

Straight-line races depended on a car's acceleration, but curves were a whole different game, relying on the driver's skill.

Besides, Alexander's Force and All-seeing Eyes could make all the difference in critical moments.

Alexander floored the gas pedal, and the Porsche took off at full speed.

The Porsche sped ahead, with a Ferrari leading a huge convoy behind it, chasing like a crazed pack of animals and staying on their tail.

Alexander kept his All-seeing Eyes activated, reading every detail of the road and the performance of both cars as the information streamed into his mind.

His powerful mental abilities acted like a supercomputer, instantly calculating the best route for him through the turns and making it harder for the Ferrari to keep up.

The Porsche quickly approached the first sharp turn, and Alexander didn't even hit the brakes, continuing to accelerate as he sharply turned the wheel.

"This idiot's got a death wish!"

Byron saw the Porsche speeding into the curve without slowing down and sneered.

This had to be suicide; there was no way he wouldn't go off the road into one of those ditches.

Under Alexander's control, the Porsche's rear swung out in a perfect arc, with the tires scraping against the road and filling the air with the stench of burnt rubber.
