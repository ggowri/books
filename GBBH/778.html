The raid on Mandale Town had failed.

The Axe suffered heavy losses and lost several loyal wizards.

"Yvonne, I never thought you'd be useless too. It's better that you're gone," Monica thought angrily, her smile fading into a cold expression.

She stayed calm and spoke loudly to the crowd, "Today, we gather to celebrate our 100th anniversary and the safe return of Rock Rossetti."

She continued, "And I have good news. After his time in seclusion, Rock has now advanced to become a white wizard.

"From today on, Rock will be the second white wizard in our City of Sorcery."

Everyone gasped, their expressions complicated.

It was no small feat to become a white wizard. The City of Sorcery's power had grown again, making their own positions weaker and forcing them to flatter the City of Sorcery even more.

The room suddenly felt colder as the guests' spirits sank.

Monica scanned the crowd. "What's wrong? Aren't you happy?"

They quickly bowed and hid their uneasy expressions. "Of course, we are happy. Congratulations to Mr. Rossetti and the City of Sorcery."

"Yeah, congratulations."

"Hmph. You'd better behave from now on. Anyone who dares disobey me will find no place to rest, even in death," Monica sneered, feeling pleased with her authority.

Just then, a dark wizard stepped forward and whispered to Monica, "Ms. Quinn, we've found out that Yvonne and Woodrow's disappearance is related to Alexander."

"Alexander, that pest. Why is he everywhere?" Monica snapped.

She slammed her hand on the table, making everyone around her jump.

"Ms. Quinn, Alexander has become a serious threat. Even the plague couldn't stop him. Let's focus our strength to destroy him once and for all."

"Yes, Alexander took the Ancestral Witch's Cane and the Spirit Bead Bracelet. We must get them back and make him pay."

The wizards shouted, all supporting the idea to eliminate Alexander.

Monica thought carefully about the best course of action.

Suddenly, the doorkeeper announced loudly from outside, "Mr. Rossetti has arrived."

Rock's appearance instantly drew everyone's attention.

He was a white wizard now, an equal in power to Monica.

Everyone looked at him with awe, fear, and respect.

Even Monica stared at him, not blinking.

Rock was different now. Without her protective amulet, Monica couldn't be sure who would win if she and Rock fought.

Back when they discovered an ancient wizard's tomb, they found a mystical poison, the Ancient Grimoire, and a powerful amulet.

Monica had claimed the poison and amulet, while Rock obtained the Ancient Grimoire.

A group of people quickly entered, led by Rock.

He wore a white robe and held the black Ancient Grimoire in his right hand. He was calm and imposing, no longer the disheveled figure seen at Treasure Island.

All the wizards and guests in the hall held their breath in silence.

Monica smiled and waved. "Rock, come here."

Rock was surprised that Monica called him like that in front of everyone. Clearly, she was showing off her higher rank.

Rock walked over and bowed to her.

"Yes, Ms. Quinn?" he asked.

No one noticed his resentment as he lowered his head.

If Monica weren't the ruler of the city, he wouldn't have been with her. She was old and wicked.

Things were different now. He was also a white wizard and had ambitions for the throne.
