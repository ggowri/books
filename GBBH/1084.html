A man in his forties sat on the sofa, wearing a suit and a black eyepatch over his left eye.

He lounged back with his legs crossed, casually puffing on a large cigar, sending clouds of smoke into the air.

As Alexander approached, Lanny, who stood beside the one-eyed man, jumped up and pointed at Alexander accusingly.

"You bastard, you actually dared to show up? Mr. Nicholson, this is the guy who helped Emma! He took Emma and Maxine away!"

Jordan's eye narrowed to a slit, emanating a cold, murderous gleam as he sized Alexander up.

Lanny, his face still bruised and swollen from the beating Alexander had given him earlier that day, glared at him with cold fury.

Suddenly, a desperate cry rang out. "Get out of here! They lured you here on purpose. They won't let me go..."

A slightly balding man was being held by two thugs, his hands bound in handcuffs.

Alexander knew this had to be Maxine's father, Alvin.

"Jordan, I'm giving you a chance. Let him go," Alexander spoke calmly, interlacing his fingers and cracking his knuckles with a series of sharp pops.

Jordan sneered and made a gesture. Immediately, one of the thugs beside Alvin drew a sharp dagger and pressed it against Alvin's throat.

"Watch yourself, man. One wrong move, and he's dead." Jordan's face showed complete confidence in his position.

"If you're so eager to die, I'll grant your wish."

Alexander was absolutely certain he could take them all down before they could harm Alvin.

Jordan yanked Alvin's hair and growled, "He can't save you. Hand over all the technical documents if you know what's good for you.

"Otherwise, it won't just be you who will die. Your wife and daughter will go to hell with you."

His face was twisted with murderous intent as if he would tear Alvin apart with his bare hands.

Despite his pained expression, Alvin remained silent, his jaw clenched tight.

Jordan pointed arrogantly at Alexander. "Get on your knees! I want Alvin to see what happens to people who dare challenge me!"

"Jordan, please, let me go! Please don't kill anyone... Nothing good will come from this..." Alvin screamed hysterically, struggling with all his might.

"Hold him down!"

After barking the order, Jordan advanced toward Alexander, flanked by two hulking enforcers.

Contemptuous smiles spread across their faces as they sized up Alexander like he was easy prey.

"Kill him, Mr. Nicholson!" Lanny shouted excitedly. "Make sure he's dead!"

"Get him!"

At Jordan's command, two thugs lunged at Alexander like wolves on the hunt.

"Back off!"

Alexander let out a cold snort and struck their throats with lightning speed.

With two sharp cracks, the thugs collapsed to the ground, eyes rolling back in their heads. They lay there like lifeless snakes, boneless and still.

Jordan flinched in shock and frantically waved his hand. "Everyone! Get out here, now!"

Chaos erupted in the bar, followed by shouts and the thundering of approaching footsteps.

"Get out! Everyone clear out, now!"

"Who are these guys? We're leaving. Please don't hit us. Don't hit us!"

Chaos erupted in the bar within seconds.

Dozens of men in matching uniforms, each brandishing a gleaming machete, surrounded Alexander with an intimidating presence.

There were over forty of them, their faces fierce and brimming with menace, ready to gang up on Alexander at any moment.

Alexander ignored them completely, moving like a phantom to Alvin's side in a flash. His cold gaze fell on the two thugs holding Alvin down.

Jordan and the others jumped in shock. Alexander's speed was beyond anything they could follow.

"Let him go if you want to live." Alexander's voice cut through the air like ice.

The two thugs felt a wave of murderous intent wash over them, crushing them down like a mountain. They trembled as they backed away.

"Go to hell!"

One of the thugs suddenly roared, driving his gleaming dagger straight at Alexander.

"Ah!"

In a cry of agony, the man's wrist snapped in Alexander's grip, and the dagger fell into Alexander's waiting hand.

The blade whistled through the air as Alexander slashed the throats of the surrounding thugs in one fluid motion.

Crimson blood gushed like fountains as multiple bodies crumpled to the ground, their limbs twitching uncontrollably.

Jordan and his thugs stumbled backward in terror.

Lanny broke into a cold sweat, his eyes wild with fear as he stared at Alexander. "You... you actually dared to kill people?"
