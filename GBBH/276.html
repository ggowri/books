But the rooftop was empty, except for the four of them.

Noah's face was covered in sweat, and he forced a smile.

"Johnny, don't scare me. We're still a few hundred meters away. How could he spot us? It's not like he's got eagle eyes..."

Adrian, standing beside him, wore a serious expression.

"Don't scare yourself. I think he noticed something else and stopped to check. Maybe it's the uneven terrain and the bushes blocking our view."

Johnny kept his gun up, eyes wide open, scanning the surroundings intently.

Meanwhile, an intense sense of danger kept growing.

Noah snapped out of it too, tossing his sniper rifle aside and grabbing a submachine gun.

"Johnny, there are four of us together. Even if Alexander tries something, we have nothing to fear. I'm more worried he won't show up!"

His eyes gleamed with both murderous intent and greed.

"The General hasn't given the order yet, but if we catch Alexander early, we're sure to get promoted."

Adrian nodded repeatedly. "Right, let's take the initiative and grab Alexander's people."

They were the elite team, well-equipped, and carrying out an ambush. Taking down Alexander was not a concern.

But Johnny remained silent, cold sweat breaking out on his forehead.

"Shut up! We've been targeted for a while now..."

"What!?"

The group immediately felt chills, cold sweat covering their backs.

Just then, two small red dots appeared on Johnny's and Noah's chests.

"Damn! Snipers!"

They were shocked, but none dared to move.

Whoever managed to lock onto them silently, right under their noses, had to be as skilled as them, if not better.

On a nearby building, William and Charlize both sneered.

If those two fools made even the slightest move, they wouldn't hesitate to shoot.

Suddenly, Johnny sensed a shadow flash behind him and turned immediately.

"It's you! How did you get up here?"

He glared at Alexander, who had appeared like a ghost, his face turning grim.

All four stared wide-eyed, unable to believe Alexander had appeared silently.

If Alexander had opened fire a moment earlier, they'd all be dead by now.

"Are you Schroder's people? Trying to kill me or capture me?"

Alexander's tone was as cold as ice.

Johnny was flustered, momentarily unsure how to respond, just staring at Alexander.

Alexander, with his hands behind his back, smiled casually.

"Schroder invited me to the banquet just to steal my magic powder formula. If I don't agree, you'll make your move, right?"

"Kill!"

Johnny suddenly shouted, cutting off Alexander's words.

As expected of an elite team Johnny, he pretended to be intimidated by Alexander but had been planning an ambush all along.

He rolled quickly to dodge the sniper's aim, using Alexander's body as cover, and pulled the trigger.

"Bang! Bang! Bang!"

Three bullets shot straight toward Alexander's chest.

His movements were fast enough that Charlize, who had been targeting him, couldn't get a clear shot. He used Alexander to block her line of sight.

"Damn bastard!"

Charlize cursed viciously.

At the same time, William and Noah fired almost simultaneously.

Noah also rolled, unleashing a hail of bullets from his submachine gun at Alexander.

But his thigh was hit by William, leaving a bowl-sized wound with blood gushing out.

Ignoring the wound, Noah emptied his magazine in an instant.

Dozens of bullets covered Alexander's entire body, leaving him nowhere to hide!

The bullets rained down on Alexander at such close range that no one thought he could survive.

Johnny's face twisted into a grim yet pleased smile.

Adrian and another teammate didn't idle either; both drew their combat knives simultaneously, charging at Alexander at full speed.

Getting close to Alexander was the only way to force the hidden snipers to hold fire, afraid of hitting their own.
