The Porsche shot through the first curve at top speed without a hitch.

"What! How is that even possible?"

Byron was utterly shocked.

Pulling off a turn like that should only be possible on a professional track, in a high-performance race car, and with an expert driver at the wheel.

Yet Alexander had done it, executing a flawless drift that had an indescribable elegance.

As a professional racer, Byron knew the level of skill it took.

Those terrifyingly perfect drifting techniques had his heart racing.

"Damn it, who exactly is this guy with Rebecca? Where did he get such incredible driving skills? Could he actually be a king of the racing world?

"No! It can't be... I know all the top drivers..." he muttered bitterly.

Byron mumbled to himself, his expression turning sour.

Then, to his horror, Alexander took on several consecutive hairpin curves without slowing down, drifting through each one at top speed. The sight was both thrilling and mesmerizing.

How was this even possible!?

Byron wondered if he was dreaming; everything felt unreal, beyond belief.

He didn't dare attempt those terrifying drifts and could only watch as the Porsche got farther and farther away, finally vanishing from sight.

Meanwhile, he was only nearing the second turn.

Byron's heart was filled with anger, jealousy, and frustration.

He even stopped his car, furiously pounding the hood in a desperate attempt to vent his frustration.

Meanwhile, Rebecca cheered joyfully, wrapping her arms around Alexander and kissing him repeatedly.

"Hahaha!"

Alexander laughed along, equally thrilled.

As they approached the Hansen Villa, Alexander eased off the accelerator.

"Hang in there, Grandpa. Alexander is here..." Rebecca murmured, overwhelmed with emotion.

She remembered how she used to look down on Alexander and suddenly felt embarrassed, her emotions complicated.

Filled with remorse, Rebecca nestled closely to Alexander.

"Don't worry, leave everything to me," he said.

Alexander patted Rebecca's shoulder, and together, they headed toward the Hansen Villa's entrance.

"Miss, you're finally back.

"Mr. Thompson is here already; he's been waiting anxiously inside."

"Alexander, you're here? Good, that means Ralph has a chance... sigh!"

Quentin hurried out, clearly excited.

"Mr. Thompson..."

"This isn't the military. Call me Quentin, and I'll call you Alexander. I'm leaving Ralph's life in your hands; there's no time to lose."

Though Alexander and Rebecca were obviously close, Quentin wasn't surprised in the least.

With a capable and handsome young man like Alexander, it would've been strange if he didn't have a few women interested in him.

He was also confident that his granddaughter, Brittany, would win Alexander's heart.

Ralph lay motionless in bed, surrounded by advanced medical equipment.

Several doctors were working busily, their expressions grim.

Alexander noticed beads of sweat on the doctors' foreheads; they were clearly under pressure.

"Alexander is here; all of you, step aside!"

Quentin raised his hand authoritatively, and the doctors quickly stepped back, eyeing Alexander with curiosity and doubt.

"He's so young!

Isn't he a bit too young to heal Ralph?"

A few doctors were unconvinced and secretly hoping to see him fail.

Alexander strode quickly to Ralph's side, activating his All-seeing Eyes to scan him closely. It's a far more precise and direct method than Force Sense.

In Alexander's eyes, Ralph's entire body appeared as transparent as crystal glass.

Every bone, muscle, blood vessel, and organ was completely visible.

Alexander noticed Ralph's liver was blackened, clearly poisoned, and it was identical to the poison that had affected Ashley earlier.

It seemed that the Red Umbrella Group was the real power backing Orion.

Fortunately, he had arrived just in time; within an hour or two, Ralph would have succumbed to the poison.
