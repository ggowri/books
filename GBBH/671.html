Alexander swung the Lightsaber and chopped off Dereck's head.

The bloody head rolled on the ground and was kicked away by Alexander.

This man who used fetuses to refine demon puppets deserved this.

Alexander stood proudly, pointing his Lightsaber at the Mafia and the Weaver family, and issued a challenge expressionlessly.

"Any other strong men? Don't waste time. Come on!"

The members of the Mafia and the Weaver family were all quiet. And no one dared to speak.

Paxton and Beverly looked gloomy.

They thought that they were well prepared. With six powerful men plus the wizard, Dereck, they thought they would be able to destroy the Axe.

They had not imagined that all the strong men would be killed by Alexander.

"Bastard, what are you doing?"

Paxton shouted angrily.

The person in front not only did not attack but immediately retreated behind Paxton and let him out.

"What the hell! Do you want to rebel?"

Paxton was so angry that his face turned pale.

The six experts sent by the headquarters were half of the twelve guards of the Mafia. Even the most elite special forces were far from their match.

And Dereck was a ruthless killer.

In desperation, Paxton and Beverly retreated, trying to get away from Alexander.

Alexander stared at them, faced the guns, and walked towards the two calmly.

"What do you want?"

Paxton was horrified by Alexander's murderous intent.

"Kill him now! Kill him!"

Beverly couldn't stand this.

She screamed wildly, raised her gun, and fired at Alexander.

A magazine of bullets was emptied in an instant.

"Kill him! Kill him!"

Paxton also roared and shot at Alexander fiercely.

Fierce gunfire rang out again. Hundreds of guns were fired at Alexander as if to reduce him to ashes.

Alexander had disappeared as if he had never appeared.

All the bullets hit the open ground, leaving craters.

The next second, Alexander suddenly appeared in front of several corpses, picked up guns from the ground with both hands and started firing fiercely.

Alexander had an illusion that he was playing a shooting game.

He didn't need to aim at all. His All-seeing Eyes could lock onto dozens of targets at the same time. And then he could pull the trigger continuously and shoot at the targets.

The two guns had fired all the bullets, and more than thirty people from the Axe had fallen.

Alexander dropped the guns, picked up two more from the ground, and continued firing.

Under the control of powerful strength, as long as Alexander pulled the trigger, someone fell.

He was alone, shooting with guns he picked up, but he unleashed terrifying lethality.

The Mafia and the Weaver family fell one after another.

Paxton and Beverly were panicked. They knew that they would be defeated. Not only would they not be able to destroy the Axe, but they themselves would also be killed.

"Fire! Fire! Kill him now! Or I will kill you," Paxton yelled wildly.

His eyes were full of fear, and he kept aiming and shooting at Alexander, but he could not keep up with Alexander's speed. All the bullets were gone.

Everyone knew how terrifying Alexander was. And they concentrated their firepower to kill him.

The rain of bullets was pouring down like a rainstorm. Alexander used sandbags, brick walls, trees, and other things as cover and quickly approached Paxton.

The Axe had already come to their senses and were cooperating with Alexander's attack.

With Alexander's fast and accurate shooting, the Axe immediately suppressed the enemy.

Paxton felt like he was about to collapse. He yelled and cursed, venting his fear and anger.

"Damn... What monster is this guy? He must be a devil. A devil that came from hell!"

The experts sent by the headquarters died, and Paxton's own men fell one after another.
