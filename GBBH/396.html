Lawrence and his men glared at Alexander, eyes filled with murderous intent.

They absolutely despised Alexander!

Mary felt both startled and thrilled; Alexander had come just in time.

But deep down, she was filled with regret and shame, knowing it was a trap and yet allowing Alexander to walk into it.

As she watched Alexander stride toward her, Mary wasn't sure where her sudden courage came from.

She grabbed a bottle and said, "I'm sorry! I'll drink this to apologize. Let's forget any unpleasantness between us, and please, don't target Alexander."

Mary didn't want Alexander caught up in this; her voice shook, even tinged with a trace of pleading.

Lawrence and Eugene both grinned smugly, their eyes turning even more vicious.

Eugene taunted confidently, "What gives you the right to bargain with me? You can't even save yourself, and you think you'll protect Alexander? Tonight, I'll..."

"Smack!"

Before he could finish, a crisp slap rang through the room.

Eugene hit the floor from Alexander's slap, blood trickling from the corner of his mouth.

Everyone in the room was stunned. The speed was unreal!

Eugene struggled to his feet, staring at Alexander with hatred as if looking at a sworn enemy.

Alexander grabbed Mary's bottle, smashing it on Lawrence's head, letting wine and blood soak his expensive suit.

"Since you love drinking so much, lick the wine off the floor!"

Lawrence, dazed from the blow, bled from his head and took a while to recover.

He pointed at Alexander, shouting, "How dare you hit me? I'm the Godfather's representative. You're a dead man, and no one can save you!

"Are you all blind? What are you waiting for? Take him down!"

Several brawny men lunged at Alexander like beasts.

They swung their fists viciously at Alexander's face and head.

Eager to catch him, they dreamed of earning glory for the Mafia.

"Watch out!"

Mary gasped, but Alexander didn't budge.

Just as a fist was about to hit him, Alexander moved.

Without looking back, he grabbed a guard from behind and tossed him aside.

Almost simultaneously, he struck the two men on either side in the chest.

"Crash!"

The three men flew back at once, crashing into tables and chairs, causing a loud noise and screams of pain.

Taking down three in an instant, Alexander didn't stop, continuing to deal with the lackeys.

Under everyone's horrified gaze, with each step Alexander took, several elites were sent to the ground.

Despite their numbers, Alexander sliced through them like a hot knife through butter, reaching Lawrence with ease.

Bodies were sprawled across the floor, blood pooling everywhere.

Alexander's cold gaze was laced with a deadly aura, making Lawrence feel as if he'd been plunged into an ice pit.

"A devil! This guy is a true devil!"

Lawrence looked sick with fear; he'd known Alexander was strong, but not to this terrifying level.

Next to him, Eugene and the other Blood Gang leaders clenched their teeth, unsure what to do.

"So, you're the Mafia's representative?"

Alexander asked icily.

"That's right! I am. What do you plan to..."

Lawrence replied, his voice trembling.

Alexander slapped him across the face.

"The Mafia's so impressive, huh? Forcing my woman to drink?"

Hearing this, Mary felt her heart was filled with happiness.

Although she and Alexander had only one intimate encounter, it was unforgettable. And Alexander hadn't forgotten her, treating her as his own.

"The Mafia... we have countless experts. You may be strong, but...but..."

Lawrence was so scared he could barely speak.

Alexander's sheer presence was enough to make him break down.

"Forget it, he's representing Satan of the Mafia..."

Mary gently tugged on Alexander's arm, trying to calm him.

Lawrence quietly breathed a sigh of relief. "So, he is afraid of the Mafia after all," he thought.

But Alexander sneered. "The Mafia's exactly who I'm here to crush!"
