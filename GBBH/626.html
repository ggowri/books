Before the young man could finish his words, Brandon grabbed the cup and slammed it in front of Evan.

"Alexander has offended me many times. Apart from that old man, I have never suffered so much.

Brandon was notoriously cruel. He would kill someone's whole family with no mercy.

"Flame, first, you have to find out where Felix is. Second, kill Shadow and that old man as soon as possible.
