"Go ahead, try it," Rock sneered.

"See if your remote has any signal."

Shadow's face darkened as he checked his remote. Sure enough, there was no connection.

Not just the remote was dead. Every communication device, phone, walkie-talkie, and satellite phone all showed no signal.

It had to be the Demon Summoning Array, causing interference across the entire area.

Rock's mocking smile faded, replaced by murderous intent.

"You're all going to die today! After I kill you all, I'll catch that brat Alexander, flay him alive, and take both the Primal Staff and the Spirit Beads for myself."

"Hahaha!"

Rock's laughter boomed arrogantly through the air.

"You'll all become sacrifices to the Great Being! You should feel honored this is your destiny..."

Feeling the pressure mount, Shadow kept his eyes locked on Rock. The Grand Elder of the City of Sorcery was formidable indeed.

"Arrogant fool!"

Shadow bent his knees slightly, gathering power and ready to strike at any moment.

The Demon Summoning Array was active now. They had no idea how Alexander was doing down there, but they needed to take Rock down first.

"BOOM!"

The air exploded as Shadow struck, charging at Rock like a fierce tiger. His punch tore through the air with devastating force.

"Hmph, you think that's enough to kill me?"

Rock opened the Ancient Grimoire, releasing a stream of black mist that collided violently with Shadow's fist.

The clash of these two powerful forces rang out like clashing steel.

"You've got decent martial skills, but too bad you ran into me. To a true wizard, you martial artists are nothing but brute force without finesse!" Rock sneered as the black mist from the grimoire enveloped him completely.

Shadowy figures emerged from the dark fog. Multiple dark-clad figures formed a crescent formation around Shadow, radiating an increasingly sinister aura.

Shadow didn't stay idle, throwing punch after punch.

But no matter how powerful or fast his strikes were, the dark figures blocked every single one, making it impossible to reach Rock.

If Alexander were here, his All-seeing Eyes would reveal these weren't human at all, but monsters formed from dark energy, lacking even human faces.

The dark figures moved with supernatural speed, not only blocking Shadow's attacks but launching counterattacks that forced him back.

Despite being among the most potent fighters alive, Shadow, whose punches could bend steel plates like paper, couldn't get past these mysterious assailants.

"Kill him!"

Rock commanded, pointing at Shadow. More dark figures materialized, rushing at Shadow from every angle imaginable.

"Die!" Shadow roared, spitting out a mouthful of blood.

It was blood from biting his tongue, a technique mastered by true martial arts experts. Their blood, charged with pure yang energy, could dispel evil spirits.

The blood spray hit three of the dark figures, vaporizing them instantly like fire melting ice.

Shadow coughed up another mouthful of blood as he took down five more of the masked attackers.

He staggered backward while Rock, face drained of color, also stumbled back several steps.

Their clash had clearly left both fighters badly wounded.

The remaining five attackers charged at Shadow with murderous intent.

Rosalia and Charlize rushed forward to help, blocking the attackers' path.

"Lord of the inferno, your fierce flames burn away all sin..."

Rock muttered under his breath. As the eerie incantation continued, half his body suddenly withered, becoming skeletal and emaciated.

His sunken face showed exhaustion, covered in intricate black patterns that spread like a spiderweb across his skin.

Dark energy gathered around the Ancient Grimoire, ready to burst forth at any moment.

As Rock continued his chant, his withered body shrank even further.

A black flame the size of a fist emerged from the grimoire, splitting into five sparks that shot into the bodies of the attackers.

"BOOM!"

Black flames engulfed the five attackers, transforming them into something even more terrifying and grotesque.
