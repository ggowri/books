"Quick, put out a bounty for any leads on Alexander. Whoever finds him will get five million dollars!"

After hours of searching with no sign of Alexander, Sebastian felt like he was on the brink of collapse.

It felt as if a drill was spinning in his skull, the pain making him break out in a cold sweat, wishing for death to come.

His bounty announcement sent shockwaves through Loscity.

"We need to dig deep and find Alexander! Not only will you snag five million dollars, but you'll also earn the favor of Mr. Sebastian Tate from the Brilliant Group!"

Countless clueless individuals sprang into action, fueled by adrenaline.

Only a select few knew the truth. Sebastian was desperately trying to plead for his life.

Andrew laughed triumphantly. "Ha! I knew Alexander was done for! With Mr. Sebastian Tate offering five million dollars right off the bat, it's curtains for him!"

Belinda chimed in eagerly, "Absolutely! Our whole family needs to pitch in to find Alexander. Not only is there a hefty reward, but we could also win Mr. Sebastian Tate's favor for the Fairfax family!"

"Finally, that loser Alexander is getting what's coming to him!"

Anger and frustration bubbled up inside her.

"Jane, do you really still have feelings for Alexander? That guy is heartless; he's already off with Ava. Why would he ever take you back?"

"Damn it, I can't stand that smug look on Alexander's face. I want to see Mr. Sebastian Tate break his legs!"

Jane, though her face was still a thundercloud, pulled out her phone and dialed Alexander's number.
