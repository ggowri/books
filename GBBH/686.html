"Mr. Staggs, those two beauties are stunning. I don't think they're part of our 'Reaper' crew, are they?"

"All the better if they're not from our organization. They seem skilled enough; imagine all the moves they could pull off in bed."

Levi smirked, his gaze filled with desire as he looked at them.

Alexander was at the buffet, calmly enjoying his meal, entirely unfazed by the chatter around him.

"Has that guy never seen food before? With two such stunning women by his side, and all he does is eat? What a waste."

"I don't recognize him, but he's definitely not one of our top members. It's annoying just looking at him."

"Exactly. It's a crime to waste such beauties on him. I'm going over there to win one of them over. He probably won't dare say a word."

A middle-aged man finally couldn't hold back and strode confidently toward Rosalia and Charlize, flashing a charming smile.

"Ladies, would I be fortunate enough to buy you a drink?"

He asked smoothly, clearly practiced at approaching women.

Rosalia and Charlize acted as if they hadn't even heard him, ignoring him entirely, as though it were the air itself speaking.

A look of irritation flashed across the man's face, but he wasn't one to give up easily. Moving closer, he tried again.

"Ladies, my name is Lucas Grylls. Would either of you honor me with a dance?"

Lucas' tone held a note of politeness as he bowed slightly, exuding the air of a gentleman from another era.

Rosalia gave him a frosty glance, replying icily, "We're not interested. Please go find someone else and don't disturb us."

Charlize rolled her eyes with open disdain. "Look at yourself in a mirror and see if you're even worth our time."

Laughter erupted from nearby, adding insult to the sting of Lucas' rejection.

"You're being rude!"

Lucas snapped, humiliated and on the verge of losing his temper.

"Come on, ladies, you can't give me a little face? Just one dance from one of you, and I'll leave you alone.

Lucas restrained his anger and made one last attempt.

Charlize sneered. "You think you're capable of making things difficult for us?"

Rosalia, even colder, said bluntly, "Arrogant fool. Get lost."

Lucas' face contorted with fury. As a skilled martial artist and a key member of the organization, he wasn't used to being rejected so publicly, especially in front of his laughing companions. This humiliation was too much to bear.

Unable to contain his rage any longer, he pointed at Alexander, who was still calmly enjoying his food, and demanded, "Is that glutton your man? This guy?"

Laughter erupted through the room, loud and mocking.

Lucas had crossed the line, and Alexander had no intention of backing down.

Truthfully, Alexander had found Lucas' advances amusing to watch and hadn't intervened when he initially approached Rosalia and Charlize.

As soon as Alexander turned toward him, Lucas lunged, moving to strike without warning.

The air ripped as Lucas thrust a gleaming knife toward Alexander's arm with alarming speed, the blade catching the light with a deadly glint.

Alexander scoffed. This guy had a death wish.

"Back off!"

He bellowed, channeling the Force with pinpoint precision, piercing Lucas' right hand like a needle.

Lucas screamed in agony, dropping the knife as blood oozed from a hole in his hand as thick as a finger.

The room fell silent in shock. Everyone knew Lucas had been a champion underground fighter, and yet Alexander had taken him down with a single move.

Alexander stood unfazed, his gaze steely and cold.

"So, you tried to ambush me just because your little flirting attempt failed?"
