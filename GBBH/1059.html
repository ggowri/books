Wayne took action first, trying to kick Alexander in the stomach.​

It was a mighty attack!

If Wayne hit him, Alexander would definitely be blown off his feet. Moreover, he might be killed.

Ariella could not help but exclaim, "Watch out!"

Alexander turned to her and smiled as if nothing serious had happened, "Don't worry. It's no big deal."

Then, to everyone's surprise, Alexander dodged Wayne's kick.

Wayne's face immediately darkened. As his confident attack missed its mark, he realized it was definitely not a fluke.

Alexander was unusually calm. After comforting Ariella, he easily avoided Wayne's kick and did not strike back.

Wayne gritted his teeth and attacked Alexander repeatedly without hesitation.

He used the trump cards he had acquired in underground boxing, throwing punches and kicks with lightning speed and great force.

He believed that even a master couldn't bear his counterattack and that this young man would at least get hurt.

Wayne screamed.

Suddenly, he couldn't take back his fists, and a sharp pain came.

Somehow, his right fist had been caught by Alexander as if by a pair of pliers, and he couldn't struggle at all.

"L-Let go of me..."

In a panic, Wayne looked completely pale.

Alexander sneered, "Let go of you? Weren't you so wild and arrogant just now? Go on!"

Alexander kicked Wayne hard.

Because Wayne's fist was held by Alexander, he couldn't hide even if he wanted to.

Chase and the others were shocked that Wayne was kicked away heavily, like a leaf blown away by a strong wind, flying and slamming on the wall.

Wayne screamed in pain and coughed up blood nonstop.

Everyone from the Obsidian Gang stepped back in fear, and several women started shivering.

Chase hugged Wayne anxiously and shouted, "Wayne! Are you all right?"

Gore and Wayne were both his right-hand men, and now both of them were killed. Therefore, Chase was frightened.

Wayne spewed out another big mouthful of blood, which splashed all over Chase, and then completely lost consciousness.

"Bastard!"

Chase's expression was ferocious, and the muscles of his face twitched. He flew into a rage.

"Come on and kill them!"

He roared like a madman, "Charge! Anyone who kills them will be rewarded with one million dollars!"

The members of the Obsidian Gang came back to their senses and were ecstatic. They rushed to Alexander, Ariella, and Rosalia like hungry wolves.

With a pale face, Ariella remained composed because Alexander and Rosalia gave her great confidence.

Rosalia flickered in front of her to protect her.

"You're all losers!" Alexander snorted with disdain before disappearing.

When his figure reappeared, he had already dashed into the crowd in the Obsidian Gang, just like an invincible guy in a game.

At least one member of the Obsidian Gang was knocked down, either dead or disabled, by each of his casual moves.

Those with guns became the focus of attention and were first attacked and defeated by Alexander.

A group of people surrounded Alexander aggressively, but they didn't even touch a corner of his clothes.

In the blink of an eye, the weapons scattered all over the floor, which was littered with people, and the screams echoed.

Alexander still showed mercy to the members of the Obsidian Gang. He just dislocated their limbs at most instead of killing them.

Ariella, after all, was going to take over this gang. The members, who had been taught a lesson, would naturally serve her with respect.

Chase shivered all over, feeling that he was doomed.

If he knew that Ariella had such a strong friend, he would never dare to steal Ariella's power and position.

Despite having been fighting on Aetheris Street for more than 20 years, Chase had never met such a powerful figure.

He couldn't help trembling at the thought that he was about to face revenge, and a chill ran through him.

Alexander walked to him and smiled, "Chase, now do you agree you are a blind dog?"

There was a dead silence!

Chase stared at Alexander with hatred, with a pale face, and he mumbled something to himself.

Alexander was pretending! This young brat had deceived him by pretending to be a weak gigolo!
