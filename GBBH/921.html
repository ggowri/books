The police officers were unharmed and had the upper hand.

The remaining enemies had no desire to fight. And with Alexander's attack, they would soon win.

When the last enemy fell, Melin and others happily put down their guns and surrounded Alexander.

"Be careful! Go to the elevator first. I'll cover you."

Alexander discovered something was wrong.

He had heard some unusual noises, rapidly approaching from far away. Something was coming out of the undamaged glass jars.

He thought, "Another living dead?"

As a chaotic sound of footsteps sounded, Alexander had a bad premonition.

He said, "Go away. I can protect myself!"

Alexander pushed Melin into the elevator and urged them to leave.

Melin and others were stunned, not knowing why Alexander suddenly behaved like this.

But Alexander's expression was serious. Melin didn't ask in the end. Instead, she hugged Alexander and kissed him before leaving with other police officers.

Several shrill screams were heard from the corner of the corridor, obviously coming from people in the base.

Something was killing people on the base!

Accompanied by a low growl, a group of zombies with highly decayed bodies and red eyes slowly turned the corner.

As soon as they saw Alexander, they pounced on him like hungry wolves at a fast speed.

Alexander saw that they were no longer human, but zombies.

Each one of them looked like a ghost, quite terrifying.

Behind them, there was a zombie with a black body and long fingernails that were as sharp as blades.

Under the command of the living dead, these zombies have surrounded Alexander.

In the eyes of these zombies, Alexander was delicious food.

Alexander sneered contemptuously. They were indeed some brainless monsters who dared to surround him.

He swung his sword and rushed towards them.

The Lightsaber was swung, and white sword beams flashed, cutting through the monsters effortlessly.

These monsters had no sense of pain, were fearless and had only appetite. They were no match for Alexander.

Soon, they turned into fragments.

The only thing that surprised Alexander was that the living dead could command zombies.

He couldn't help but think of Jake who was far away in the suburbs of Loscity. Jake had several living dead under his command, enough to control a small zombie army.

Alexander was worried about Ava and Ashley, so he acted faster and more ruthlessly, chopping the zombies hiding at the back.

The body parts mixed with black blood on the ground looked like immortal maggots, shaking and wriggling.

There was also a zombie head that opened its mouth towards Alexander, as if it wanted to bite his foot.

Alexander didn't even look at it and crushed it.

However, things did not end. As more than a dozen zombies rushed out from deep within the base.

Some of them were wearing researchers' clothes, some were wearing guard uniforms, and some were soldiers wearing combat uniforms.

They were obviously bitten to death and infected by the living dead. They opened their mouths, revealing sharp teeth, and rushed towards Alexander with roars.

Alexander wielded the Lightsaber. And zombies' heads were chopped off.

If they were released, they would eat all living animals and humans, and infect them, causing their kind to grow exponentially.

Alexander thought it should have been a while since Elaine activated the base's self-destruction program.

He didn't want to delay and waved his hand to create Force Burning.

Suddenly, balls of pale flames burned in the corridor, and the scorching flames quickly spread deep into the base.

Suddenly, a loud roar came from the direction of the research center.

A giant ran toward Alexander, carrying the unconscious Elaine in his hands.

The giant's body was highly decayed, his huge eyes flashed red, and his thick limbs were like tree trunks, full of powerful strength.
