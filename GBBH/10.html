Ava shot her a glance.

"Godfather's already claimed me. Does it matter whether I like it or not?"

Emma caught the vivid excitement dancing in her eyes and grew anxious.

"Look, Ms. Blackburn, Alexander's got some tricks up his sleeve, sure. But he's not good enough for you. You should ask the Godfather to reconsider - pick a business tycoon or a political elite instead. A power couple would be much better."

Ava sipped her wine and shook her head.

"I see it differently. Alexander possesses mysterious powers but isn't arrogant; this is humility. He endures humiliation in the Fairfax family for love; this is sacrifice. He volunteers at the nursing home year-round; this is kindness and compassion."

"Good luck finding another man like that these days!"

Emma rolled her eyes. She knew Ava had totally fallen for this Alexander.

She snorted in disagreement. "Jane and Ryan walked all over him, and he didn't even do a thing. What a coward!"

Ava laughed. "Really? You think so?"

"Just watch. He'll definitely make those two jerks pay. Trust me."

Emma shook her head hard. "Well, I can't get it at all!"

Anticipation gleamed in Ava's eyes. "He's not someone people can push around. With his abilities, he'll make something of himself sooner or later."

Emma gestured at the VIPs around them with a sneer. "Make something of himself? Even if he worked his butt off for ten lifetimes, he still wouldn't match any of these people."

Ava's expression turned cold. "Emma, we do get along well, but stay out of my personal life. You should really learn to hold your tongue."

"Ms. Blackburn, I'm just trying to help..."

Emma trailed off, scared but still doubtful.

Ava thought for a moment, then smirked. "I really despise that scumbag Ryan and Jane. Check if the Hawkins Group is actually one of our corporate partners."

Without hesitation, Emma made some calls to check it out.

After a while, she replied, "Yes, Ms. Blackburn. The Hawkins family is indeed a long-term partner of us. They're invited to the ball too."

Ava said coolly, "Cancel all cooperation with the Hawkins family. And revoke Ryan's invitation."

Emma sighed internally. It was obvious that Ava's doing this all for Alexander. It meant she had really fallen hard.

She could only nod and say, "Right away, Ms. Blackburn!"

After making the call, her expression turned weird.

"Ms. Blackburn, it can't be canceled... Alice said that Ryan is a key focus of the new chairman, so we can't cancel the partnership. The chairman specifically requested that they be invited to the ball."

"What!?"

Ava slammed her wine glass down, red liquid splashing across the table like dark blood.

Her face turned serious. Godfather hadn't revealed who the new chairman was. It remained a mystery.

"Ryan's got connections with the new chairman?" Ava frowned. "If Alexander really wants to go up against him, he's screwed."

"Ms. Blackburn," Emma cut in, "don't risk your standing with the chairman over someone like Alexander. I knew he wasn't a good guy. It's not worth it to offend the chairman for him."

"Who knows if the new chairman is Mr. Augustine Hawthorne, or maybe even Mr. Anthony Hawthorne?"

Ava's beautiful eyes flashed with a sharp glint, and then she murmured to herself, "If Alexander refuses to back down, what should I do..."

Meanwhile, Alexander lounged in his chair, legs crossed.

Alice stood before him respectfully, her voluptuous curves showcased perfectly in a tight off-shoulder dress.

Alexander chuckled, "So Ava wants to help me handle Ryan?"

Alice nodded, her expression complex.

"Mr. Hawthorne, should we tell Ms. Blackburn you're the new chairman?"

Instead of answering, Alexander rose and walked to the window, taking in the glittering cityscape.

His tall frame radiated such mysterious power that even Alice, used to dealing with big shots, felt intimidated.

Why do I feel his presence... overshadows even Godfather now?

The ruthless and cold Mr. Augustine Hawthorne and Mr. Anthony Hawthorne can't compete with that.

Am I losing it? She thought.

She shook her head to regain her senses.

But Alexander was oblivious. After becoming a Warrior, he had undergone an earth-shaking transformation without even realizing it.

"Keep my identity quiet for now. And watch Augustine and Anthony's movements."

"Have you found out who was behind the last assassination attempt?"

Though calm and flat, his icy tone sent chills down Alice's spine.
