"I am not someone just anyone can look down on, not even the Morris family."

Felix stood with his hands behind his back, full of confidence.

"Hmph, you sure talk big!"

Though Grayson couldn't stop himself from trembling, he refused to back down.

Felix waved dismissively. "Go and report to your master before you get in over your head."

"Outrageous! Felix, you're going too far!"

Grayson's face turned livid.

He wanted to attack, but knowing he wasn't a match only fueled his frustration.

"Felix, you've got some nerve, causing trouble at my Morris Villa!"

Suddenly, a furious shout rang out.

A tall, imposing middle-aged man stormed out of the courtyard like a tiger.

His eyes were sharp and fierce, and he fixed a hard stare on Felix.

"Felix, you dare run your mouth here? Kneel down and apologize!"

Seeing the newcomer, Grayson was immediately overjoyed.

"Weston, you're just in time! Teach this fool a lesson for daring to disrespect our family."

Weston Morris, though not one of the family's top ten experts like Diego, was firmly in the top twenty. It's far stronger than Grayson.

The gathered Morris family members breathed a sigh of relief, ready to enjoy the show now that Weston was here.

"Weston? What nonsense. His strength is pathetic."

Felix mocked disdainfully.

Weston was furious. "Felix, who do you think you are, acting so arrogant?"

"I'm here to see your master. Move it! Good dogs don't block the way!"

Felix struck out like lightning, his speed power erupting as he moved like a ghost, impossibly fast.

Smack!

Before Weston could react, Felix slapped him, sending him sprawling heavily to the ground.

"You...you coward, how dare you attack me by surprise?"

Weston's face twisted with anger as he shouted in frustration.

Smack!

Felix struck again.

Weston took another brutal slap, blood spurting from his nose and mouth as his cheek swelled like a giant dumpling.

"You! How dare you slap my face? You must have a death wish..."

Covering his face, Weston was filled with a mixture of fear and shock.

"So what if I slap you? Who do you think you are? Go get your master out here to see me."

"Enough!"

An angry voice boomed from deep within the courtyard.

Though far away, the voice was strong and resonant, like a forceful blow right in Felix's ear.

Felix turned pale, involuntarily stepping back.

He never expected the Morris family to have such a terrifying power. A mere word from him had already unsettled Felix.

"Mr. Morris!"

Weston, Grayson, and the other black-clad men all knelt toward the direction of the voice.

Felix was rattled. The last time he'd come to the Morris Villa, he hadn't met the master.

He hadn't realized the master of the Morris family was such a fearsome power!

Felix assessed the situation carefully, realizing he had no confidence in a win.

Felix, who only bullied the weak, immediately drew in his energy and stopped standing so tall.

"Let him in!"

The authoritative voice held no trace of emotion.

Felix couldn't stop himself from shivering.

Just a moment ago, he sensed a terrifying gaze from deep within the Morris Villa, piercing through him.

Felix felt an icy chill spread over him; this was just from a single look.

Martial arts truly held a terrifying power!

Felix estimated that the master's power was at least several times Diego's level.

Weston and Grayson glared furiously at Felix. Disturbing the master was a sign of their own incompetence.

Regaining his composure, Felix let out a cold snort and, with a serious expression, walked into the courtyard.

Weston and Grayson quickly followed him, leading the way forward.

Felix suddenly noticed patches of blood on the ground, and the air still held a faint metallic smell. Clearly, a fierce battle had taken place here.

"What happened here?"

He immediately grew cautious.

Weston gave him a disdainful look.

"Felix, don't think a bit of power lets you run wild in the Morris Villa! If not for the real threat we just faced, you wouldn't have left here in one piece!"
