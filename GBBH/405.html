As soon as they entered, Ashley started clamoring for ice cream.

Ava quickly bought three cartoon-themed ice creams, one for each, which made Ashley ecstatic.

"Daddy, can I have a bite of your Mickey Mouse one?"

Ashley leaned over, took a bite of Alexander's ice cream, and then smacked her lips happily.

"Daddy's ice cream is so tasty!

"Ava, can I have a bite of your Donald Duck too?"

After taking a bite of Ava's, Ashley held up her own ice cream to Ava and said, "Here, you eat mine too!"

"What a sweetie you are."

Smiling, Ava took a bite, leaving a red lipstick mark on the ice cream.

Ashley immediately turned and offered her ice cream to Alexander. "Daddy, you eat too!"

Without a second thought, Alexander licked right over the spot Ava had bitten, erasing the lipstick mark.

He shot Ava an amused smile, and she realized he did it on purpose. Remembering the previous night's intensity, she blushed deeply.

Alexander chuckled, his expression revealing he'd meant it.

Ava rolled her eyes at him, then turned to Ashley and asked, "Ashley, what do you want to go on first?"

Ashley pointed ahead excitedly. "Let's go to the water!"

Alexander looked at the giant pool ahead, with a towering slide dozens of feet high.

One by one, bikini-clad women slid down, hitting the water with a splash and sending droplets flying.

This was the perfect summer activity. It's refreshing and cool.

Ashley wore a cute children's swimsuit, while Ava had on a three-piece bikini, showing off her curves in all the right places. She's both full and slender.

"Daddy, why are you staring at Ava like that?"

Ashley shouted out.

Ava's cheeks flushed pink as she scolded playfully, "Silly, let's go."

She picked up Ashley, turning her back to Alexander.

"Ava, what happened?"

Ashley asked, curious.

Seeing Ava lost for words, Alexander covered his mouth, trying not to laugh.

"It's you guys!"

Suddenly, Alexander heard a familiar, surprised voice behind him.

Alexander's expression shifted. He turned, and sure enough, it was her.

Jane approached with a few young men and women.

Jane was dressed in a revealing swimsuit, her makeup flawless, and she was glaring unhappily at Alexander and Ava.

"Mom, do you want to play with us?"

Ashley asked hopefully.

But Jane looked at a scar-faced man beside her, her expression conflicted.

"Mom, why aren't you saying anything? Mom... what's wrong?"

Ashley was visibly disappointed.

Jane stammered, "Ashley, you go ahead and play. I have something else to take care of. Next time, I'll take you to have fun..."

Ashley let out a quiet "Oh," and fell silent, but she pouted, clearly unhappy.

The other young men and women with Jane, whom Alexander didn't know, were all glancing at him and whispering, obviously talking about him.

Alexander hadn't expected to run into Jane here, and his previously good mood vanished instantly.

He wasn't afraid of Jane, but deep down, he felt a strong aversion to her and had no desire to see her, let alone speak to her.
