Alexander propped his leg up, eating the apple the beauty prepared, and said calmly, "Now that Gary is dead, you don't need to recommend a new leader. Let's establish a family Senior Guild.

"Each of you will be an elder, and all affairs of the Federer family will be decided by your votes; majority rules."

The senior members suddenly became excited, their eyes filled with greedy desire for power.

There was one leader before, but with this Senior Guild, each would have a share of power, which seemed quite appealing.

Then Alexander shifted his tone.

"And I will be your Chief Elder, holding the veto, with Edward as my representative. How does that sound?"

"What!?"

Everyone from the Federer family was stunned.

Alexander was outright stealing; he was treating the Federer family like a juicy piece of meat to take a big bite out of.

This so-called Chief Elder was essentially a disguised leader.

The faces of the senior members turned pale, clearly reluctant, but they dared not oppose.

Edward was equally shocked; he hadn't expected Alexander to make him his representative, no wonder he had mentioned earlier about giving him wealth.

Edward's feelings were complicated; even though he claimed to be the top fighter in the Federer family, he was just in middle management at best.

Entering the true upper level might never be possible if not for today.

"What? Don’t you agree?

"Don't worry, I won't interfere in your Federer family matters as long as you give me 20% of your profits each year.

"And I won't take your money for nothing; I can offer you magic powder at a 30% discount. How about it?"

The senior members of the Federer family whispered among themselves but remained silent.

They were all cunning old foxes, aware that Alexander's sweet words now could lead to unknown results later.

Perhaps once Alexander secured his position in the Federer family, he would take away everything.

There is no limit to one’s greed; they had to be on guard.

Alexander took another bite of the apple, and seeing their continued reluctance, his expression turned cold.

"Since you refuse my offer, don't regret it later."

With that, Alexander struck several of the senior members with a swift hand.

Force surged into their bodies, like an invisible drill stirring within them.

"Ah! My head hurts so much."

"My body aches everywhere! Alexander, what have you done to us?"

They fell to the ground, rolling and moaning in pain.

Their painful expressions terrified Edward and the beautiful woman, leaving their faces pale.

It wasn't until a few guys were in so much pain they couldn't make a sound, gradually becoming motionless and only able to moan weakly on the ground, that Alexander finally retracted the Force.

Instantly, they felt a wave of relief, as if they had just escaped a living hell.

Alexander coldly warned. "If you don't want to experience that kind of pain again, you'd better cooperate with me. Otherwise, I’ll make sure you won't even be able to die."

"Yes, yes, we agree. We’ll cooperate."

"From now on, Mr. Hawthorne will be the Chief Elder of the Federer family, holding veto."

The few of them, with gloomy faces, could only agree.

"Great! Starting today, the matters of the Federer family will be managed by the Senior Guild. Edward, you will be my agent from now on; is that okay?"

Alexander looked at Edward with a half-smile.

Edward bowed deeply.

"I will follow all of Mr. Hawthorne's orders; I wouldn't dare defy you!"

After experiencing the terror of the bloody night, he was already intimidated by Alexander's strength.

Now, seeing Alexander's mysterious abilities and decisive demeanor, he had given up any thoughts of resistance.

To such a powerful figure, submission was the only choice.

"When I'm not around, Edward will be my agent; do any of you have a problem with that?"

Alexander turned to the Federer family members with a sly smile.

"No problem; Edward is one of the family anyway!"

"Yes, Edward is the best among the Federer family. With Mr. Hawthorne backing us up, we'll be unstoppable in Loscity!"

"Exactly, thanks to Mr. Hawthorne, the Federer family will definitely reach new heights."

Several senior members were desperately flattering Alexander.
