The trees and plants touched by the venom emitted black smoke, withering instantly.

Even the hard cement ground showed deep pits with signs of burning.

But Alexander was too fast. The moment the black snake opened its mouth to spray venom, he had already retreated several meters back, hiding behind a large tree.

Seeing the potency of the snake's venom, Alexander became cautious.

Just a single drop could cause serious harm, even with the Force protecting him.

The dark wizard was indeed formidable—not only skilled but wielding such terrifying creatures.

Meanwhile, Drake seemed to have aged ten years in an instant.

His wrinkled old face cracked like a dried-out wasteland, with deep blood-streaked lines appearing.

It seemed the black snake's venom took a heavy toll on his energy as well.

Alexander's lips curled into a cold smile as he flicked more pebbles.

With a hissing screech, two more black snakes were hit, bleeding and thrashing on the ground.

Using his speed, Alexander launched a guerrilla assault. Each time Drake paused, Alexander would strike with Force-powered pebbles, killing a few more snakes.

Furious, Drake commanded the snakes to spit venom in retaliation, but not a single drop touched Alexander.

After a few rounds, the dozens of strange black snakes were reduced to only a handful.

Drake himself was nearly spent, his entire body trembling uncontrollably.

Alexander still looked relaxed. He had only used a fraction of his Force.

When the final black snake was killed, Drake collapsed to his knees, gasping for air.

"This can't be! I'm a dark wizard... how could I lose to you?"

Black blood gushed from Drake's mouth, staining his chest.

"Alexander! Are you really going to kill me? Think carefully—I'm a dark wizard of the City of Sorcery. If they find out, you'll face endless pursuit."

Alexander looked at him with cold disdain.

"You took Guzman's order to kill me. You should have known the price of failure. If it were me on my knees now, would you let me live?"

Drake's face twisted with hatred. He suddenly tore off his robe, revealing his emaciated body.

His skeletal frame was covered in twisted scars, like dried tree bark.

As he chanted a strange spell, the scars on his body began to move, transforming into monstrous faces, as if they were about to crawl out of him.

"Still trying tricks when you're at death's door?" Alexander glared at him, not foolish enough to let him complete his sorcery.

He threw a handful of Force-infused pebbles. Bang, bang, bang!

Poor Drake was hit as if he'd been shot by a machine gun, his body bursting into a bloody pulp, riddled with holes straight through.

Black blood oozed out, filling the air with a foul stench.

Drake lay on the ground, unable to move, spewing black blood from his mouth.

In each mouthful of black blood, small, worm-like black snakes squirmed.

He had nurtured these black snakes in his body, linked to him physically and spiritually.

Each snake's death inflicted severe pain on Drake.

Lying in his own black blood, he was barely breathing.

Alexander walked over, intending to check if Drake had anything useful on him.

As he approached, he suddenly paused, sneering. "Damn wizard, trying to have a black snake latch onto me, huh?"

Drake suddenly lifted his head to glare venomously at Alexander.

In the black pool beside him, several snakes began writhing again, crawling desperately toward Alexander.

Without hesitation, Alexander used the Force to ignite them.

A burst of Force engulfed the black blood, and flames roared up instantly.
