The burly man shot the driver a fierce glare.

"Are you deaf? Mr. Hawthorne said to stop! The Commander instructed us to look after Mr. Hawthorne, no slacking off."

The driver quickly stepped on the brakes, stopping right in front of Madelyn.

Madelyn furrowed her brows slightly, eyeing the convoy warily, certain it belonged to some powerful figure.

At that moment, the car door opened, and Alexander stepped out, smiling warmly.

"Is that you? What are you doing here?"

Seeing Alexander, Madelyn's face lit up with surprise and delight.

"Where are you headed? I'll give you a ride."

Alexander offered with a smile.

Blushing, Madelyn replied, "I'm heading to Tijuanea, just about to catch a flight. You must be busy; there's no need to take me."

Alexander paused for a moment.

"Tijuanea? I'm actually heading there too."

"Really? What a coincidence! What brings you to Tijuanea?"

Madelyn was thrilled.

Alexander chuckled, "I'm going there to treat someone."

"Treat someone?"

Madelyn was a bit taken aback.

"I didn't know you could treat people. That's impressive."

Alexander simply smiled, leaving it unexplained.

They both got into the car and headed straight to the airport.

Along the way, Madelyn talked non-stop, and Alexander learned she was going to Tijuanea because a major client's funds were frozen by Tijuanea Bank, and she was going to handle it.

*****

At the Loscity Airport...

After picking up their boarding passes, Alexander, Madelyn, and several of Quentin's attendants queued up for security checks.

Thinking about Tijuanea Bank's uncooperative attitude, Madelyn couldn't help but feel nervous.

However, she wasn't entirely without hope; she had a distant cousin, Roman Batts, who worked at Tijuanea Bank and was her only chance.

Seeing the worry on Madelyn's face, Alexander could tell what she was concerned about.

If he could cure Quentin's grandson, he could ask the four-star general to help resolve Madelyn's issue. For Quentin, it would be a small favor.

Sitting side by side, their arms and hands occasionally brushed. Madelyn's skin was soft and smooth, and a pleasant fragrance wafted from her, making Alexander feel at ease.

Suddenly, a commotion broke out in the security line ahead.

A crowd of fans holding signs and banners surged forward, jumping and cheering excitedly.

"Marcos, Marcos, we love you!"

"Autograph! I want an autograph!"

A handsome young man in sunglasses was immediately surrounded by fans, causing the security line to be completely disrupted.

"That's Marcos, the hottest pop star in Tijuanea right now."

Madelyn commented indifferently, then shook her head.

Although Marcos was a good singer and very handsome, with skin more delicate than most women's, Madelyn had no interest in celebrity fandom.

Alexander glanced at Marcos, his eyes showing a hint of disdain.

"Marcos, we'll love you forever!"

A group of women holding bouquets and glow sticks screamed and cheered.

"Ugh, can we get through security already?"

Madelyn frowned tightly; these people were simply wasting time.

"Please move! Please make way!"

Alexander had been waiting impatiently and grabbed her hand, striding forward.

Madelyn hadn't even reacted when she saw the crowded people ahead parting like waves hitting a rock, making way for them.

Feeling the warmth of Alexander's big hand, Madelyn's heart began to race, and her cheeks turned red.

She had long been attracted to Alexander and wanted to pursue him, but recently hadn't found the chance to meet him.

Now, just holding his hand made Madelyn feel as if she was intoxicated.

Marcos, surrounded by a crowd, was suddenly pushed aside with a powerful force, and if it weren't for some fans blocking him, he would have fallen.

Alexander held Madelyn's hand, striding through the crowd with confidence.

Marcos was about to curse, but then he saw Madelyn, and his eyes lit up, his gaze becoming intense.

What a captivating woman!

"Excuse me, please make way."

Marcos pushed aside the fans, eagerly pursuing Madelyn.

Once on the plane, Madelyn talked with the flight attendant to switch seats and happily sat next to Alexander, her beautiful face flushed.
