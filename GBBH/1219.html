On the surface, Jonah claimed he was here to get justice for Gael. But his real agenda? He saw Alexander as nothing more than a stepping stone - someone he could use to climb higher up the ladder.

Too bad Jonah was being way too optimistic. Alexander wasn't the kind of guy they could just push around.

The call to assemble echoed through the area as everyone geared up for the raid.

They had fifty metahumans, including Jonah and Ilona, backed by a hundred special ops troops.

The massive sword strapped to Jonah's shoulder was hard to miss, and he was practically radiating killer vibes.

Just one look from Alexander had him breaking into a cold sweat—the pressure was intense.

Jonah's eyes narrowed to slits, a deadly glint flickering behind them. He wanted to use Alexander as a test of strength, and the tougher Alexander turned out to be, the better.

Sure, Jonah might be tough compared to the average fighter, but Alexander was playing in a whole different league.

Everyone except Alexander seemed to be feeling the weight of what was coming.

It made sense—Sakra Society had developed some serious enhancement drugs from the Immortality Serum, creating test subjects with crazy abilities.

Trying to snatch the pure Immortality Serum from their secret base wasn't going to be a walk in the park. They were in for one hell of a fight.

It wasn't for nothing that the command had put together an assault team with fifty metahumans.

After all, Jonah wasn't just good. He was ranked number one among all fifty metahumans in their unit.
