"I am SATAN, the Godfather of Mafia. Do I need you to teach me what to do?" Brandon roared.

Karl was so frightened that he dared not speak.

Then Brandon said, "Tell Bruno that no matter what means he uses, as long as he can kill Alexander, he will be a member of the Hawkins family. I will let him enter the upper echelons of Mafia and become one of the successor candidates."

"Yes, Godfather. Please calm down. I will do it right away." Karl agreed.

Thinking about meeting Bruno, Karl felt a chill on his back, and his whole body stiffened.

Kamdon, known as the evil place, was one of the most chaotic cities.

The underground forces here were complicated. The most powerful group among them was the Redoom Group.

The Redoom Group was full of elites, and the boss was a king-level mercenary. He was so powerful that no one dared to mess with him in Kamdon.

However, someone broke into the Redoom Group alone today.

He was like a devil. Inside the Redoom Group headquarters, blood flowed everywhere, and corpses lay all over the place.

The bodies in the pool of blood were not complete. They were chopped into many pieces, with broken limbs and arms.

They seemed to have seen something horrifying before they died. Their eyes were wide open, and their expressions were ferocious.

The air was filled with a strong smell of blood, and every corner made people want to vomit.

Hundreds of members of the Redoom Group had been killed. Only the leader Francisco Muda and a dozen injured elites of the group were left.

Francisco was a king-level mercenary with top swordsmanship, and the remaining subordinates were also good fighters.

They all had pale faces and stared fiercely at the figure in front of them with bloodshot eyes.

The man was about thirty years old, wearing an ugly mask. Holding a bloody black machete in his hand, he slowly approached with his limp foot.

He looked like the killer in a horror movie.

The people in front of him were the only ones left in Redoom Group.

"Bruno, we have never offended you. Why are you helping Mafia to kill us? Don't you have a grudge against Brandon?" Francisco asked.

Brandon didn't accept Bruno, and every member of the Hawkins family treated Bruno as a bastard.

To fulfill his mother's last wish and to have his mother buried in the family cemetery, Bruno was willing to be a killer of the Mafia.

Bruno had a disfigured face and was a cripple. He was disabled, but he was an extraordinary person.

People who didn't know him couldn't associate him with genius.

Hearing what Francisco said, Bruno replied coldly, "Stop talking nonsense. Just surrender, and I can let you die with less pain."

His tone was very overbearing.

Francisco's face was pale, and his eyes were full of murderous intent.

"Bruno, I admit that you are the most powerful killer. But you must pay a heavy price for killing us."

Francisco suddenly took action.

He squatted down and exerted force. The hard floor exploded like a large spider web crack.

Francisco rushed towards Bruno like a cannonball.

He was indeed a king-level mercenary.

Bruno remained expressionless, facing such a fierce attack.

As Francisco swung his machete, the sharp blade was almost reaching Bruno's head.

With a fierce light in Bruno's eyes, he dodged the attack by moving sideways and drew his machete at the same time.

The sharp blade flashed across Francisco's body, and it was too late for him to dodge.

With a scream, Francisco's right arm flew out.

Blood gushed out, flowing all over the ground.

Before Francisco could come to his senses, the machete flashed again, and a bloody mark appeared on his throat.
