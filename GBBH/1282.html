Next, Alexander began to dissect the mutated bison.

After his previous attempts, he was well aware that mutated beasts were edible, and their meat was surprisingly delicious.

It had been ages since he'd enjoyed tender beef, and with such a prime opportunity before him, he was determined to feast.

"Practice what I taught you!" he instructed, handing Sally the cleaver.

Sally took the knife, her grip firm as she followed Alexander's guidance and started practicing her chopping technique.

Heavy breaths soon filled the air, but she refused to take a break, gritting her teeth and pushing through.

She understood that encountering a powerhouse like Alexander in this post-apocalyptic world was a stroke of luck she couldn't afford to waste.

If he ever left, she needed to be able to fend for herself.

As Alexander sliced the tenderest cuts of meat from the bison and placed them over the campfire to roast, he turned his attention to the beast's impressive horns.

Each horn was over three feet long and thick, resembling the trunk of a small tree.

They glimmered with a faint blood-red hue, displaying remarkable toughness.

He picked one up, channeling the Force to twist it. To his surprise, it didn't break, only bending slightly.

Curious, he pulled out his Lightsaber to test it. The saber sliced through the horn with ease, but he felt a subtle resistance that was unusual.

He had cut through light armor before without encountering this kind of feedback, indicating that these horns were something special.

"What a find!" Alexander marveled, tracing the mysterious spiral patterns on the horns.

He decided to keep them; they might be useful for crafting spears later.

However, they were too long, so he used the Lightsaber to trim them down, leaving just the sharp tips.

The bison's hide was also incredibly tough. While not as strong as the horns, it certainly surpassed the best protective gear available.

After a moment's thought, he skinned the hide off with the Lightsaber and washed it clean with fresh water.

"Alexander, I... I can't go on anymore..." Sally gasped, drenched in sweat.

Her hands trembled from gripping the knife, and her entire body shook with exhaustion.

"Take a five-minute break, then chop another three thousand times!"

Alexander replied casually, focused on organizing the hide and horns while keeping an eye on the meat.

Sally blinked, momentarily stunned.

She didn't dare argue, and after a brief rest, she obediently resumed her practice.

Alexander glanced at her from behind, nodding slightly. He hadn't expected Sally to possess such a strong will; she truly deserved the time he was investing in her training.

By the time Alexander finished his tasks, Sally was sprawled on the ground, her ample chest rising and falling with labored breaths.

The meat on the campfire had turned a perfect golden brown, and Alexander sprinkled some salt over it before waving her over.

"Let's call it a day for practice. Come eat!"

Sally sprang to her feet, her body aching, but the aroma of the roasted beef was too enticing to resist.

"Wow! This is delicious!" she exclaimed after taking her first bite, even though the meat was still steaming hot.

She couldn't help but devour it eagerly, praising it between bites.

Alexander savored the roasted beef as well, marveling at the quality of the mutated bison's meat.

He had never tasted anything so delectable, even with just a sprinkle of salt and a slightly charred exterior.

Before long, they had polished off several pounds of roasted beef.

Alexander patted his slightly rounded belly, then got back to work.

He planned to roast dozens of pounds to take with them, not wanting to waste such a precious resource.

Unfortunately, they couldn't carry the entire bison, and with the heat, the meat would spoil quickly. Salting and roasting it would help preserve it for a while.

Sally hurried over to lend a hand, eager to help.

"Hey!" Sally suddenly exclaimed, jumping to her feet in astonishment.

She flailed her arms excitedly, even picking up the cleaver and performing rapid chopping motions.

Alexander furrowed his brows, perplexed. "What's going on? Have you lost your mind?"

Breathless with surprise and joy, Sally replied, "Alexander, I just realized something amazing!

"Eating this bison meat not only restored my strength, but my muscles don't feel sore anymore. It's like my body is bursting with energy!"

As she spoke, her movements with the knife became faster and more powerful, clearly showing her newfound vigor.

At first, Alexander's expression was serious, but then a spark of excitement began to illuminate his face.

"That's fantastic news! This must be a blessing from above. Who would have thought that consuming mutated beast meat could actually enhance one's abilities?"
