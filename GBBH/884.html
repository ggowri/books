"Damn! They've even got this stuff."

"We'll pick out a few for ourselves and give the rest to the guys. The Sakra Society is a local force in Risun. Wiping them out completely would be tough for us.

While they could pull it off themselves, they certainly couldn't do it with Alexander's ease.

"Boss, I've been itching for some action!" Marvin exclaimed.

Unable to hold back, Marvin began selecting his weapons of choice.

"Tsk, tsk, tsk. Too bad we can't take this beast," he said.

With its terrifying firing rate of thousands of rounds per minute, the Vulcan cannon was an absolute killing machine.

Once fired, it could unleash a storm of destruction, leveling everything in its path.

Aside from someone like Alexander, most mutated individuals would be reduced to a pile of minced flesh under such firepower.

If several Vulcan cannons were combined with elite snipers in a coordinated ambush against Alexander...

Even he would find it difficult to escape unscathed.

Shadow pulled two folding submachine guns from one of the crates.

These compact submachine guns boasted high firing rates and modified oversized magazines, offering a perfect blend of mobility and firepower.

In "Resident Evil," the protagonist Alice favored the kukri dagger, but Alexander believed the cleaving blades packed more power.
