"The seafood and barbecue are tasty and very fresh. Eat some."

Alexander, Rosalia, and Charlize started eating the barbecue buffet. The freshly caught seafood was indeed delicious, completely different from the one shipped inland frozen.

Even the air-freighted seafood served to the wealthy in the interior was far inferior to that caught locally.​

Alexander, Rosalia, and Charlize ate with relish and laughed happily.

The leaders of the forces around them felt uneasy and constantly glanced at Alexander, wondering why he was holding this party.

But they didn't dare to gaze at him for too long. They just caught a glimpse of him and then quickly looked away for fear of attracting his attention.

Yet, he heard all their whispers.

"He is young, decisive, and promising... With such a hero to lead us on Treasure Island, our future is bright."

"Well, it will be good for our business after the laws and regulations are unified across the island."

Alexander smiled slightly, for the vast majority supported him.

"From now on, this guy will dominate Treasure Island. I didn't expect Felix to be so disappointing. Back then, I formed an alliance with the Red Umbrella Group, believing that after Felix won, I would be able to take advantage of him. What a surprise!" a corpulent man in his forties muttered indignantly, and his lustful eyes roamed incessantly over the plump bodies of Rosalia and Charlize.​

"He is not that powerful. He is lucky to be Godfather's illegitimate child. If I were him, I would definitely be more successful than him.

"Fuck! Those two women are pretty. If they were my women, I wouldn't let them eat barbecue but eat my..."

The corpulent man kept swearing with an obscene smile on his face.

"Keep your voice down. Are you seeking death?"

"That's right. If anyone hears you, you're doomed!"

The people around tried to dissuade the corpulent man, and some even stayed away from him, fearing that they might get implicated.

He growled in disdain, "What are you afraid of? Take it easy. I'm so far away from them. How could he hear me? What cowards! Ouch!"

Before finishing his words, the corpulent man covered his bloody mouth and collapsed heavily to the ground.

A long fishbone stuck in his mouth and even broke his two front teeth, and blood gushed out.

After coming back to his senses, he started shouting wildly, "Ouch! Fuck you! Who! Who attacked me? If you have the guts, stand up. Don't hide. I will kill you..."

The people around him stepped back one after another, glaring at him with displeasure and wondering if he were mad to dare to act recklessly here.

The corpulent man didn't take it seriously and scolded, "Why are you running away? What are you afraid of?"

Suddenly, he saw Alexander appear in front of him and met his icy gaze.

The corpulent man shivered and narrowed his eyes in fright.

He swallowed hard and said tremblingly, "W-Was it you who hit me?"

The cold, murderous intent emanating from Alexander had already made the corpulent man sweat profusely, and his entire body was drenched in cold sweat.

"I-I didn't say anything. It's a misunderstanding. It's really a misunderstanding..." the corpulent man explained in a panic, not daring to look at Alexander.

"Will you commit suicide, or should I have you thrown into the sea to feed the sharks?" Alexander asked coldly.

He was just wondering how to establish his authority. To his surprise, this idiot came to his doorstep. He absolutely could not let him off.

His punishment should be a warning to others.

The corpulent man, who had been extremely arrogant just now, suddenly begged Alexander for mercy desperately.

"I'm sorry! It's all my fault. Don't kill me! I was wrong. I'm evil and blind. I'm a bastard..."

Alexander said in a very calm tone, "If you commit suicide now, your whole family will survive. They can leave Treasure Island with some of their property and live a peaceful life.

"If you want me to ask someone to take your life, your whole family will die. What are you going to choose?"

Everyone trembled with fear and turned pale.

At the same time, Rosalia slowly drew out the katana.
