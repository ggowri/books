"Get lost!"

"Alexander, I'll have you thrown out of Loscity!"

At the upcoming ball, he would publicly humiliate Jane and Ryan and make the Hawkins family crumble.

Ava laughed happily. "Silly, just take care of yourself and your daughter first. Call me if you need anything.

"Interesting... She's the CEO of the Saint Group. Doesn't that make her my direct subordinate?"

As dusk settled over the city and the lights came on, Ava attended a grand evening gala. The attendees were Loscity's elite, wealthy moguls, stars, the mayor, senators, and even the police chief.

She swirled her glass of red wine absentmindedly, her gaze distant.

Emma, noticing her distraction, couldn't help but ask, "Ms. Blackburn, have you fallen for Alexander?"
