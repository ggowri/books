"Congratulations, you've passed the first two tests!

"Now, you are officially members of the Dragon Gang. Money and women await you!"

The female interviewer delivered an impassioned and provocative speech.

Keith and the other men were visibly excited, their eyes gleaming with uncontrollable anticipation.

The woman's tone shifted. "However, there's one final test! Whoever passes this will become our top candidate for training."

She sighed theatrically. "It's been a long time since any man passed this last test."

Keith was initially surprised but soon grew confident.

Alexander was curious. What was this final test?

The second test, hitting the groin with a hard ruler, had been bizarre enough, making Alexander wince.

The female interviewer chuckled mysteriously. "Who among you is willing to give up your dignity?"

The hall fell silent, and a few of the men looked apprehensive.

Someone cautiously asked, "What do we need to give up our dignity for?"

"Who is willing to be on the receiving end?"

She walked up to one of the candidates, pressing the ruler under his chin and glaring coldly.

"Well, do you?" she demanded.

The man stammered, "D-Does that mean I'll be hit? And maybe... fed weird things?"

Remembering the sordid scenes from certain movies, the man's Adam's apple quivered, and he couldn't bring himself to agree.

After all, he only wanted to enjoy time with the actresses, not be trampled underfoot or fed filth.

"You're pathetic! Stay a bit player for life!"

The female interviewer shoved him aside harshly.

"Not one of you is willing? I thought we'd find a rising star this time, but you've all disappointed me!

"Don't you have any dreams? Don't you want to be our top trainee?"

The woman shouted wildly, waving the ruler excitedly.

Alexander felt queasy, and Marvin's expression soured. No sane man would agree to this.

Unexpectedly, Keith stepped forward, shouting, "I want to be the top trainee. I'm willing to try!"

He stood tall and proud, facing the shocked stares as if he were a warrior ready to face death.

Alexander was speechless, the thought of Keith possibly being humiliated making him nauseous.

He stood up, patted Marvin on the shoulder, and said, "Leave Keith a contact number for us. I can't watch this anymore. I'm leaving."

With that, Alexander quickly left the hall.

Outside, hundreds of people were still lined up, with more continuously arriving.

Alexander was at a loss. What were these men in the Risun thinking?

Was it so hard to find a decent job? Choosing this kind of bizarre ordeal was just hopeless.

Not long after, Marvin hurried out.

"Boss, I left him the contact info. Where to next?"

Alexander thought for a moment. "Call Norma and tell her I'm at their headquarters."

A few minutes after Marvin made the call, a convoy of black Mercedes cars sped over.

In the middle of the convoy were two black Rolls-Royces.

The convoy stopped in front of the building, and from one of the Rolls-Royces stepped an elderly man in his fifties or sixties, dressed in a black martial arts uniform.

Despite his age, the man walked steadily, head held high, showing no signs of frailty.

His eyes, in particular, shone with a brightness surpassing even that of younger men.

The island nation was known for karate and kendo, and this man was clearly a martial arts master.

But the old man wasn't the main figure. After stepping out, he held the car door respectfully, head slightly bowed, waiting for the real VIP to emerge.

A man in his thirties stepped out of the car.

From the other Rolls-Royce, a young woman emerged.

The elderly man followed the pair, head lowered, his expression full of respect.
