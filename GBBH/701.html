Empowered by the black flames, the five figures in black surged with evil energy, stirring up an icy wind that chilled to the bone.

Rock sneered, "Not so tough now, are you? Let's see how you handle the flames of hell!"

He unleashed his trump card, though it drained his life force to do so.

Seeing the black-clad figures charging with lethal intent, Shadow shouted, "Attack together!"

He, Rosalia, and Charlize formed a triangular formation and launched their counterattack.

"Kill them!"

At the forefront, Shadow let out a battle cry. His body suddenly expanded, muscles bulging until they ripped through his clothes. In a split second, he unleashed hundreds of punches.

His werewolf bloodline pushed to its limits, and his fists packed devastating power as they slammed into the first black figure.

A thunderous explosion erupted from the point of impact between Shadow and his opponent. The shockwave rippled outward violently.

The black figure at the front was completely obliterated, leaving nothing but scattered sparks falling to the ground.

Shadow coughed up a mouthful of blood, his body swaying uncontrollably. He was clearly seriously wounded.

Though Rock's face remained expressionless, blood trickled from the corner of his mouth. The destruction of his shadow warrior had injured him as well.

Rosalia and Charlize also went all out, each taking down one of the black figures.

Sparks littered the ground like fallen stars, with only two shadow warriors remaining.

Rock wasn't just bleeding anymore - he was hemorrhaging. Each fallen assassin took a devastating toll on him.

William, who was covering their rear, had lost all patience. His body became engulfed in a blood mist once again as he surrounded the two remaining assassins with it.

The mist erupted with thunderous noise as black flames repeatedly burst through, only to be smothered by the crimson fog.

Another assassin fell to William's onslaught, leaving nothing but black sparks scattered across the ground.

"Damn it!"

Rock was losing his mind with rage. He'd never imagined that even after using his trump card, he'd end up so severely wounded.

The last assassin managed to break free from the blood mist, but the black flames surrounding him had grown dim and weak.

Charlize let out a battle cry. "Time to end this!"

Her hands moved in a blur as she launched a barrage of throwing knives, each one finding its mark in the assassin's body.

Rosalia followed up with her katana, executing a perfect cross-slash that quartered the assassin.

Even in pieces, the dismembered parts continued to writhe on the ground.

William shouted, "Go to hell!" and engulfed the remains in his blood mist. Within seconds, the assassin's remains dissolved into black sparks and vanished without a trace.

Real fear gripped Rock now. Even if he fought with everything he had, he stood no chance against such powerful opponents working together.

"Is this how everything would end?"

Rock didn't waste another second. He turned and bolted toward the exit like a man possessed.

His bulging eyes were wide with fear as his withered body moved with surprising speed, darting down the stairs like a cornered rat.

Everyone immediately took off after him.

Rock bolted ahead without looking back, quickly reaching the plaza.

He congratulated himself on his brilliant decision as he took in the scene around him. Bodies littered the ground, most of them members of the Red Umbrella Group.

Blood had turned the ground into a slick red surface.

"Catch him!" someone shouted.

Shadow's men quickly closed in, attempting to capture Rock alive.

Rock let out a cold laugh and swiftly opened the Ancient Grimoire. Black mist instantly materialized, spreading across the area.

When the group rushed forward, they found nothing. Rock had vanished into thin air.

"Just missed him! That old fox got away!"

Shadow smacked his forehead in frustration.

Meanwhile, Alexander and Felix had begun their confrontation.

Alexander stood perfectly still while Felix circled him on all fours like a wild animal, moving at incredible speed and letting out inhuman growls.

In a flash, Felix lunged upward, aiming his claws at the back of Alexander's head.

His fingers were long and pale, with pronounced knuckles and jet-black nails that had grown several inches long, gleaming with a deadly sharpness.

Without turning around, as if he had eyes in the back of his head, Alexander casually swung his sword.

With a sharp slicing sound, all ten razor-sharp nails were severed clean off.

Felix barely managed to pull back in time. If he'd been a fraction slower, it would have been his fingers rather than just his nails.
