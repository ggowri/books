Arlene couldn't help but say to Skyla, "Auntie, Alexander is too arrogant. You must not let him die peacefully."

As Arlene spoke, her beautiful eyes sparkled, and her charming smile was very captivating.

Although Arlene was stunningly beautiful, she was also cunning and ruthless.

Skyla chuckled sinisterly, "Don't worry. He has already fallen into my trap. Not only will I take his life, but I will also make him hand over all his property and the formula for magic powder. Only then can I vent my hatred."

"That would be the best, but it's a pity, he's quite good-looking..." Arlene shook her head at Alexander as if she felt quite regretful.

Skyla walked towards Alexander, and the two locked eyes, ready to begin the duel.

Ava was so nervous that her whole body trembled, but she knew she couldn't stop it, so she could only silently pray for Alexander in her heart.

"Let's begin. Ladies first," Alexander said with a very calm expression, his tone more like an order to Skyla.

"Hmph. You'll suffer soon enough. I'll make you suffer a fate worse than death to avenge my son," Skyla cursed, taking out a small black vial from her pocket, confidently saying, "This is my carefully concocted Gut-breaking Powder. Dare you to eat it?"

"Cut the chatter! Give it to me." Alexander took the small vial, opened the lid, and poured all its contents into his mouth.

No one saw that when the poison entered his mouth, an invisible force wrapped around the poison.

Even if Alexander swallowed these poisons, the Force would isolate the poison from his body, preventing any harm to Alexander.

"No. You can't eat that..." Ava wanted to shout, but she was so weak that she didn't even have the strength to speak loudly.

Seeing Alexander swallow the entire vial of Gut-breaking Powder, Skyla laughed triumphantly.

"Hahaha. Alexander, you're doomed. Just less than a gram of this poison can kill several wild oxen."

"This vial must contain over a hundred grams. Just wait for the poison to take effect."

Skyla confidently threatened Alexander.

"If you don't want to die, submit to me and hand over all your property. I'll count to ten, and you'll start feeling the effects."

"One, two, three, four, five..."

When Skyla finished counting ten, Alexander showed no reaction, and the playful smile on his face seemed to mock Skyla.

The discussions quieted down, and everyone stared at Alexander in astonishment.

Everyone thought, "Skyla made such a big deal about Gut-breaking Powder, but Alexander ate a whole vial, and he's completely fine?"

Skyla was dumbfounded, not knowing how this could be.

"You... How can you be fine? Don't you feel excruciating pain in your intestines, like being cut by a knife?"

Even Skyla herself, if she accidentally ingested Gut-breaking Powder without a timely antidote, would suffer from a perforated stomach and die.

Alexander chuckled coldly and said, "Nonsense. Now, it's your turn to eat the poison I prepared."

Skyla's face changed, and she suddenly reneged, "No, you must have cheated earlier. You have to eat it again."

The scene erupted into an uproar, with most people thinking Skyla was shameless.

They had all seen Alexander eat a whole vial of poison.

Augusta was furious, "Bitch. You're actually cheating. I'll kill you."

Augusta was about to charge at Skyla but was stopped by Alexander.

"Alright, I agree. Bring out any other powerful poisons you have, and I'll taste them all," Alexander said calmly, which infuriated Augusta and the members of the Ultimate Alliance.

"Sir, you can't do this."

"That bitch dares to renege. Let's kill her."

The crowd was in an uproar, shouting and preparing to attack the Vanderbilt family.

"Quiet," Alexander said softly, and it seemed to carry infinite authority, immediately silencing everyone.

"Skyla, bring out all the witch poisons you're proud of."

Alexander's sharp gaze was like a blade, making Skyla sweat profusely, and she felt a great insult.

"Don't get cocky, kid. You must have cheated earlier. I definitely can't poison you. If you have the guts, eat this Multipoison Pill." Skyla took out a green pill.
