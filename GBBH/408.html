"Mr. Vanderbilt, where did you go? I couldn't find you anywhere," Jane asked curiously.

A hint of cunning showed in Simon's eyes.

"I went to the bathroom, so it took a little longer."

"By the way, what did Alexander say? Did he agree to let you take your daughter?"

Jane frowned and curled her lips in anger.

"He didn't, and I don't know why. Did someone slip up?"

Simon immediately looked grim.

"Hmph! Anyway, you only have five days left. You can talk to Alexander again."

Jane felt nervous and scared inside, not knowing what to do.

At this moment, Alexander, Ava, and Ashley had arrived to stand in line at the flume ride.

There were too many people in line, so Ava and Ashley were playing games in a nearby lounge, and Ashley giggled from time to time.

Alexander stood watching them with a smile on his face, his eyes full of love and happiness.

Seeing Jane coming over, Alexander's smile disappeared, thinking, "This woman follows me everywhere."

Ava frowned and hugged Ashley tightly.

"What the hell do you want?" Alexander looked furious.

Jane pointed at Ashley and sneered, "Alexander, can't I take my daughter out if I want to? I'm Ashley's mother. Aren't you being overbearing?"

Before Alexander could say anything, Ashley complained, "Mom, I'm going to play with Ava today. You can pick me up some other time, okay?"

Jane was instantly outraged.

"Ashley, I'm your mom, and Ava is a bitch. She seduced your dad, and that's why your dad and I got divorced."

"Shut up!"

Alexander couldn't stand it anymore.

"If you have anything, you can talk to me over there. Don't harass them."

"All right. We'll go to the front and talk for a while. I've got to take Ashley, and I have that right as a mother."

Jane hurried to follow.

After the two quickly walked away, Simon instantly sneered.

He knew Alexander was powerful, and it was a great opportunity for him.

At the side of the path ahead, Alexander looked at Jane coldly.

"You can take Ashley, but I'll arrange for someone to stay with her all the time. If you're okay with that, you can take her whenever you want."

Alexander looked calm and condescending, not allowing Jane to reject him.

"Don't go too far! Would I sell my daughter?"

Jane inexplicably looked guilty as she said this.

Alexander sneered, "You're extremely vain, and your family are even more despicable. You are good at betrayal, so I'll be uneasy."

"That's enough!"

Jane screamed at the top of her lungs.

"Alexander, who are you to look down on me? Have you forgotten how you flattered me back then?"

She was extremely upset that the man she could have bullied at will before was suddenly condescending to her.

"Are you done? I don't want to waste time with you. Still, if you want to pick up Ashley, I'll send someone to follow her.

"Remember, you and your family would better stop screwing around. Otherwise, even for Sir Fairfax's sake, it won't work this time."

With that, Alexander turned away mercilessly.

"Son of a bitch! What are you being arrogant about? What's so great about you?"

Jane was so angry that she was trembling all over.

At this moment, a hint of smugness appeared on Simon's face, who was hiding far away.

"Alexander, so what if you're more capable? I will still take your woman away!"

"Dad! Dad, help! Dad, boohoo! There are bad people catching us. Where are you? Ava got caught by the bad guys. Boohoo!"

Suddenly, Ashley's cries came.

Oops!

Alexander suddenly looked panicked as he ran in the direction the faint cries were coming from.

Luckily, his hearing was too much better than ordinary people. Otherwise, he wouldn't hear such a subtle cry for help at all.

The direction from which the cries for help came was a building in the distance. Why did they suddenly go there?

Alexander's Force exploded, and he rushed over like a gust of wind.

He happened to see Ashley running out of the building while crying and wiping her tears, her face full of fear and looking back every now and then.

"Stop! Little jerk, stop!"

Several men with fierce faces chased after her from the building.

They strode forward, trying to grab Ashley with grim smiles on their faces.
