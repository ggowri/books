It was all over.

Evan's face turned livid, and he was so angry that he couldn't speak.

Alexander smiled and said, "Mr. Tate, you are so rich. You spent thirty million dollars to buy a big rock. Don't waste this pile of rocks. You can transport it back to build a road or something."

There was a burst of snickering.

"Mr. Tate, what should we do now?" Sandy asked, stuttering.

"Bang!"

Evan turned around and slapped her in the face.

"Shut up! If it weren't for you, I wouldn't have bought this stone. Get lost! I don't want to see you."

"Mr. Tate... This is not my fault..." Sandy covered her swollen cheeks and cried.

"Get out!" Evan kicked her to the ground.

Alexander laughed and said, "This is what you sow."

Olivia snorted, feeling very happy about Sandy's being dumped.

Evan turned around and yelled at Alexander, "Brat, if you hadn't deliberately bid against me, would I have spent so much money? You must be responsible for this."

Alexander chuckled. "How about we make a bet? If I can find an ore with a high-grade gem, I win. If I can't, I lose.

"If I lose, I'll pay you fifty million dollars, and if you lose, you'll return the exchange to Olivia. How about that?"

Evan was so angry that his face turned red, and he cursed, "You bastard! If you want the exchange, what's the point of messing with me? Go mess with Mafia."

Alexander looked at Evan with contempt as if he was looking at an ant.

"Aren't you the person in charge of the exchange now? If I win the exchange from you, it is legal. If Mafia dares to come to Loscity to cause trouble again, Ultimate Alliance will not let them go."

Evan shuddered with fear. "Are you from Ultimate Alliance?"

Ultimate Alliance had integrated several major forces and was now the largest force in Loscity.

Alexander said coldly, "Stop talking nonsense. Do you dare to bet? If you don't dare, get out of Loscity. Ultimate Alliance will protect this exchange from now on."

Evan gritted his teeth and said nothing.

He thought that the people from Ultimate Alliance had been lying in ambush outside, ready to rush in at any time, but he hadn't made any preparations.

Even if the twenty or thirty Mafia members left in Loscity were all gathered together, they would be no match for Ultimate Alliance.

Evan thought for a long time and finally agreed.

"Okay, I'll make a bet with you. If you can cut out a high-grade gem, I'll lose. If you can't, you will lose. You have to pay me fifty million dollars, and you are not allowed to interfere with my exchange again."

Hearing these words, everyone stared at Alexander.

"Ignore him. The conditions are too harsh. Don't bet with him." Olivia grabbed Alexander's sleeve, her beautiful eyes full of worry.

Everyone thought that Alexander wouldn't agree. After all, finding an ore containing a high-grade gem was very difficult.

However, Alexander agreed without any hesitation. "Okay, I'll take the bet."

Evan grinned grimly, thinking he was about to turn the tables. He didn't believe that Alexander could find an ore containing a high-grade gem.

"Alexander will lose to Mr. Tate."

"I think so too. He is so young and has no experience. He will suffer a big loss."

"Mr. Tate will make a comeback by winning this bet."

The onlookers were talking about it, ready to watch the good show.

Alexander had already identified some ores through his All-seeing Eyes.

Judging from the light flashing in those ores, they were very likely to be top-grade gems, but he still needed to confirm it.

Alexander paced slowly in the hall, walking around the ores.

A few minutes passed, but he still hadn't made a choice.

Ten minutes passed.

Everyone started whispering, thinking that Alexander had no confidence and would lose.

Evan was a little nervous at first, but now he calmed down.

He sneered, "Brat, weren't you very arrogant just now? Hahaha, if you can't give me fifty million later, I will kill you!"
