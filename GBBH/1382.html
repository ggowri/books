"You..."

Logan was about to charge forward and curse him out, but Alexander quickly held him back. His instincts screamed a warning—this man was extremely dangerous.

The middle-aged man sneered at them. "Even stray dogs and nobodies dare come here to ask for medicine?"

Then, without sparing them another glance, he casually knocked on the door.

Alexander's face darkened, his fists clenching tightly. He had thought that reaching the level-four warrior rank, combined with his mysterious golden energy, golden body, and energy waves, meant he could challenge even half-step level-five warriors. Because of that, he had let his guard down, neglecting further progress.

Now that he was being humiliated, he had no one to blame but himself!

A moment later, the same young priest from earlier opened the door. Upon seeing the man, he immediately swung the gates wide open.

The man glanced around, catching the shocked expressions of the onlookers. A trace of arrogance flickered in his eyes. He lightly flicked open his folding fan, then turned and stepped into Hall of Lives, the doors shutting behind him.

The people who had come with him immediately started bragging.

"Jaryl is a genius disciple of the Inner Court—ranked 86th on the Demon-Slaying List!"

"An Inner Court disciple?!"

"I never thought I'd see an Inner Court disciple in person! They say there are only about ten thousand of them, and each one is a genius among geniuses!"

"Every single Inner Court disciple is trained to vanquish demons and subdue evil..."

The crowd erupted into excited chatter.

"An Inner Court disciple, huh?"

Alexander's fury burned as he narrowed his eyes, a sharp glint flashing in them. "One day, I'll crush you beneath my feet and repay this humiliation!"

The two of them lingered outside the hall for a while, but it felt meaningless, so they decided to leave.

Just then, a group of people approached from the distance.

Leading them was a middle-aged saint, her flowing white robes billowing gently in the breeze. Her long eyebrows arched toward her temples, her ears hung low past her shoulders, and her phoenix eyes radiated wisdom. Her face was serene and kind, exuding a divine presence. In her hand, she carried a jade purification bottle, adorned with a willow branch, looking utterly sacred and dignified.

The onlookers bowed respectfully, their voices ringing in unison. "Greetings, Father Elias!"

She was none other than Elias, the head of the Dominion Church's Great Vehicle Division and the abbess of Hall of Lives.

Alexander and Logan quickly bowed in respect.

Elias was about to pass Alexander and enter the hall when, suddenly, his golden energy in his left chest stirred ever so slightly.

The master halted abruptly and turned to face Alexander. He felt as if those phoenix-like eyes had pierced through his very soul, seeing his past and present with absolute clarity. His heartbeat quickened, a strange sense of unease creeping over him.

Elias's lips curved into a faint smile. She lightly gestured toward Alexander and Logan, saying with a gentle voice, "You two, come with me."

Alexander and Logan exchanged uneasy glances before following Father Elias into the hall.

The crowd outside erupted in shock.

"Father Elias never bothers with trivial medicine requests. Who the hell are those two?"

"Could they be related to her?"

"That guy looks familiar... Oh! Now I remember! He fought Rhodes in the Silver Division match this year! Damn, I lost thousands of coins betting on that fight!"

Elias said nothing, simply leading the two into a meditation chamber.

The meditation chamber had an elegant and ancient charm. At its center stood a towering statue of God, over thirty feet tall. The deity was draped in flowing white robes, standing gracefully atop a nine-tiered lotus platform. A jeweled crownadorned the head, and the crescent-shaped eyebrows framed a gentle yet compassionate gaze, radiating both dignity and serenity. In one hand, God held a jade purification bottle with a willow branch.

"The true God, immeasurably wise, cultivated through countless lifetimes of purity and devotion."

Elias walked straight to the meditation cushions in front of the statue, gracefully sitting in the lotus position. She pressed her palms together in a prayer gesture, gazing at Alexander and Logan with a gentle smile. Several young priests stood silently beside her, their heads bowed.

Alexander bowed deeply. "Greetings, Father Elias. I have come to Hall of Lives today in search of a rare medicine."

Elias's eyes gleamed with a knowing smile as she spoke slowly, "You were born of this land, destined to protect and guide the lost. A single flower contains a world—when the time is right, all will fall into place."

Alexander and Logan exchanged glances, completely lost. Neither had a clue what she meant. Just as Alexander was about to ask...

Elias suddenly gave a firm instruction to one of the priests. "Liora, bring me the small vial."

The young priest named Liora looked up, visibly startled, as if about to say something but held back. He lowered his gaze, then walked over to a golden nanmu wood cabinet, carefully retrieving a small jade bottle. Holding it with both hands, he respectfully presented it to Elias.

Elias gracefully lifted the willow branch from her jade purification bottle, allowing a few drops of milky-white liquid to fall into the small jade vial.

A strange and enchanting fragrance filled the room. The moment Alexander caught a whiff, he felt his mind clear and his vision sharpen. It was as if his muscles, bones, and every fiber of his being were inhaling the scent, absorbing an overwhelming surge of strength. Just that one breath felt equivalent to three full days of intense training!

Alexander knew without a doubt—this was one of the most precious treasures of Hall of Lives. Overcome with gratitude, he carefully took the jade bottle from Liora's hands and placed it into his Dimensional pouch. Then, pulling Logan down beside him, he knelt and bowed deeply several times.
