Even the security guards were displeased with Alexander.

If they let a liar in, what should they do if something happened to Elaina? The consequences would be very serious!

Alexander gazed at the security guards calmly.

"Let me see Orlando. I can cure Elaina."

The leading security captain stepped forward and sneered, "You are a liar. Do you want to see Mr. Gomez? Impossible!"

"That's right! Liars have no right to meet Mr. Gomez. Is this reason enough?"

The security captain shoved Alexander, feeling as if he were pushing a huge rock, and Alexander did not move at all.

"You are quite strong. No wonder you dare to cheat. Get out of here, or I will beat you up!"

Alexander ignored them and walked straight into the villa, but the security captain immediately stopped him.

"Are you fucking out of your mind? Didn't you hear what I just said?

"You liar, do you want to see Mr. Gomez? Dream on! I'll count to three. If you don't get out of here, don't blame me for attacking you!"

"Get out of the way!"

Alexander stepped forward before they started counting.

The security captain's face blushed with anger, and he quickly led his men to surround Alexander.

"Are you leaving or not?"

The security captain reached out for Alexander's arm.

He sneered. As long as he grabbed Alexander by the wrist and twisted it in the opposite direction, Alexander's arm would be injured, and it would take him at least a month to recover.

Alexander stared at him coldly and did not resist.

The security captain grasped Alexander's wrist as he wished and suddenly exerted force, but Alexander remained unfazed.

"Damn it!"

The security captain tried his best, but it was of no use.

Alexander's arm was like a steel pipe, and the captain couldn't shake it despite his efforts.

"You! How do you have so much strength?"

In a panic, the security captain took two steps back tremblingly with a dark face.

Another security guard chuckled, "Captain, what are you doing? Are you feeling weak today because you spent too much time at the bar last night? Let me help you!"

He also reached out for Alexander's arm.

"Get lost!"

Alexander waved his hand casually, as if to drive away a mosquito.

The two security guards suddenly felt a powerful force strike them, as if they had been hit by a speeding truck, and they flew backwards.

There was an uproar at the scene. No one expected Alexander to beat someone in front of the Gomez Villa.

"Stop! Who dares to act recklessly at the Gomez Villa?"

Hearing the majestic voice, everyone was stunned for a moment and then burst into discussion.

"Orlando is here!"

"It's Mr. Gomez. The guy is in trouble!"

Orlando, in his fifties, came out, looking haggard and exhausted, and glared at Alexander.

"Are you sure you can cure my daughter?"

Alexander replied calmly, "I can give it a try, and the chances of success are very high."

"It's inexcusable that you have caused such a commotion in my place. If you can't cure my daughter, I will definitely take revenge on you. Can you afford the consequences?"

Orlando was fierce.

Alexander beat up the security guards from the Gomez family in public. As the family head, Orlando certainly couldn't forgive him easily.

Those black-clad men behind Orlando all stood up and glared at Alexander.

Suddenly, a chilling, murderous aura spread out, and everyone felt depressed and breathless.

Archie wiped the cold sweat and secretly felt glad that he did not act impulsively. Otherwise, he would be in big trouble after failing to cure Elaina.

"Why don't you answer me? Are you scared?" Orlando asked coldly.

Alexander smiled, "If your daughter recovers, I want your dragon bone fragment."

Orlando gazed at Alexander viciously, wishing to kill him.
