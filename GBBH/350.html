Wesley seized the girl by the hand and caressed it.

He chuckled, "I won't charge you any money in the future, but your daughter has to sleep with me. It's a pity that such a beautiful girl has to suffer with you."

"Let go of me! Help!"

In a panic, the girl burst into tears and struggled desperately.

"You bastard! Let go!"

The elderly man came back to his senses, grabbed the fruit knife, and stabbed Wesley's arm hard.

"Ouch!" Wesley screamed, blood dripping from his arm.

The girl took the opportunity to break free and hid behind her father. Her rosy face was now pale.

"How dare you stab me with a knife? Damn it, you're courting death!"

Wesley got furious and ordered his men to overturn the elderly man's cart.

The food and change on the cart were scattered all over the ground.

The elderly man ignored his cart and backed away, holding the fruit knife to protect his daughter.

"You bastards, you deserve to go to hell. God will definitely punish you!"

"Asshole, you injured and cursed me. Go to hell!"

In his rage, Wesley pulled out his gun, aimed at the elderly man, and shot him.

When a gunshot rang out, many people closed their eyes. They couldn't bear to see the elderly man's blood splattered on the spot.

Suddenly, to the surprise of some, Wesley's hand holding the gun was seized by a handsome young man, and the shot missed.​

"Who are you? Let go of me if you don't want to die!"

Wesley threatened Alexander.

He tried with all his strength to free himself from Alexander's grasp, but it was all in vain.​

"I told you to let go!" Wesley roared and attempted to punch Alexander in the nose with his left fist.

But Alexander was countless times faster than him. As soon as Wesley's left hand moved, Alexander grabbed his fist.

Wesley felt as if his hand was being strained by an iron hoop, causing him excruciating pain!

"Ouch! It hurts! Let go. Please let go of me..." Wesley screamed bitterly. He heard the crisp sound of bones breaking in his hand.

Alexander continued to tighten his grip and sneered, "You bastard, do you feel any pain? You are unworthy of being a human. Go to hell and be a slave of the devil."

With that, he added a little more strength.

"Ouch!" Wesley wailed in agony.

"Let go of our boss!"

"Hey, are you asking for trouble?"

Wesley's men confronted Alexander arrogantly and, relying on their large numbers, rushed forward to defeat him.

The first guy just moved closer and immediately felt a sharp pang in his chest.

Then, as if he had been hit by a speeding heavy tank, he went flying upside down through the crowd and landed hard on the ground.​

Alexander kicked the guy away so fast that no one could see his actions clearly.

In the blink of an eye, two more hooligans flew out and lay prone on the ground, unable to get up.

Alexander picked up the knives and forks on the table and threw them at the other hooligans.

The knives and forks shot out, each hitting a hooligan accurately.

Some were stabbed in the thigh, others in the abdomen, and some, covering their bleeding shoulders, collapsed to the ground, wailing.​

In an instant, all of Wesley's men were struck down.

"Ouch! Ouch!"

Wesley was in a great deal of pain, for his right hand had been clung to by Alexander.​

It twisted into a horrible shape and turned black and blue due to extreme lack of blood.

"I was wrong. I was wrong... I'm sorry. Please let me off!"

Wesley begged Alexander for mercy again and again.

Alexander looked at him with a deadpan, murderous expression.

"You are a bastard. If you die, it will be a blessing to the world."

Wesley was almost frightened to death.

He was no longer as arrogant as before, and he pleaded with tears, "Sir, please let me off. I know I was wrong, and I will never do it again!"

At that moment, the elderly man carefully advised, "Thank you. How about letting him off? After all, he's always been a bully on this street. We are ordinary people, and we can't afford to offend him..."

He was afraid that if Alexander left, Wesley would take revenge on him, and there would really be no way for him to survive.
