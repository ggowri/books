Betty stared at the surveillance screen and reminded Alexander, "Mr. Hawthorne, they are getting closer!"

Alexander sneered, "It doesn't matter. None of them can escape."

He kissed Betty on the face and then walked out leisurely.

In another direction, Logan was grinning.

"These Risunese bastards come at the perfect time. I haven't killed anyone in a long time. This time, I'm going to kill them wantonly!"

Logan's eyes were fierce, and his expression was excited.

The position he chose formed a cross-fire point with Marvin. This way, they could completely cover a large area at the entrance of the base.

At this time, Lamont reported to Alexander on the intercom, "All the guards at the base, fifty in total, are in place!"

"Okay, we can't let anyone leave later."

With that, Alexander came to the gate and stood in the middle of the small square at the entrance of the base.

When the Dragon Gang members pried open the door and filed in, they were all shocked.

The captain, Diken Zander, looked surprised and incredulous.

He had met Alexander during the day. He actually saw Alexander standing there and smiling at him now.

Diken thought, "How could Alexander know we are coming? What does he want to do?"

The members of the Dragon Gang all knew something was wrong, and they looked very grim.

But they were not stupid and quickly surrounded Alexander.

They thought if they could take Alexander as a hostage, they would walk out unscathed and could even extort a large ransom.

Alexander smiled, "Do you want to catch me? Let me see whether you have the ability to do so."

Diken roared and pointed his M16 Assault Rifle at Alexander.

More than twenty of his subordinates also raised their guns and aimed at Alexander.

Diken thought, "The members of the Dragon Gang kill people without hesitation. With more than twenty guns, Alexander can't fly away."

They all were full of confidence and thought that even if there was any conspiracy, they could break it as long as they caught Alexander.

Just then, a sound of tearing air rang out, breaking the silence of the night.

A beam of red light shot out and pierced through the chest of a member of the Dragon Gang.

The sharp arrow with fire power shot through him without any hindrance and then pierced deeply into the hard ground.

Under the considerable inertia of the sharp arrow, the corpse fell out.

Before anyone else could come to their senses, two more sounds were heard.

Bullets blew up the heads of two members of the Dragon Gang. Blood, flesh, and brain matter splattered everywhere, and their bodies fell heavily to the ground.

After three of his subordinates died, Diken finally came to his senses and screamed in horror, "Be careful. There's a sniper!

"Everyone, take cover! Everyone, take cover! We're ambushed... Ouch!"

Suddenly, the remaining people panicked. They turned around and ran away in despair, fleeing madly.

No one even wanted to shoot Alexander, as everyone was afraid that the sniper would target them.

"How dare you want to run away now?" Alexander laughed.

Two consecutive gunshots were heard, and two more bodies fell.

"Kill them!"

Marvin put down his crossbow and rushed out directly.

His burly body was like that of a black bear, and his roar was deafening.

He had awakened fire power and strength power. His fists were wrapped in scorching flames, and he could knock his enemies away with just one punch.

The base guards also rushed out and surrounded the members of the Dragon Gang.

As a result, the members of the Dragon Gang begged for mercy before they could even fire a single shot.

"This kind of rubbish actually wants to launch a sneak attack. It's really courting death!"

Logan laughed wildly and kicked his enemy away with just one kick.

After a few guys who wanted to resist were killed, the others all surrendered.

At this moment, more than a dozen corpses were lying on the ground, and blood was flowing like a stream.

Alexander smiled. These trashes were not worthy of his attack. It was enough for his subordinates to step in.

"Bring him here!"

Alexander pointed at a guy who was trembling.

Marvin grabbed his neck and carried him to Alexander like garbage.

Alexander looked at him coldly and asked, "How many people are there in the Dragon Gang in Loscity?"

The man looked up at Alexander, his eyes full of fear.

He had never expected that the outcome of this sneak attack would be so tragic.
