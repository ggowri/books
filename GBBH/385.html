"Are you asleep again? I'm talking to you, dumbass! Cat got your tongue?"

But there was still no response. Calvin and the other two turned to look.

Instantly, all three froze.

A dark gun barrel was pressed right to Calvin's forehead!

The cold, murderous gun had Calvin frozen in place.

The gun belonged to the bald man, who was now slumped over, out cold. Somehow, Alexander was now sitting silently in the van.

"Hey, buddy, what's this about? We're with The Cripples, don't think we've crossed you, so why do this?" Calvin said with a forced smile.

Calvin grinned, hoping to bluff his way out.

Alexander knocked out the two goons with the butt of his gun, then grabbed Calvin by the neck and dragged him to the driver's seat.

"Drive. Take me to the one calling the shots. Maybe you'll live."

Alexander pressed the gun against Calvin's groin and clicked off the safety, almost making Calvin wet himself.

"Don't kill me, please! I'll take you, I'll take you!"

Calvin pleaded frantically, starting the van with trembling hands, and drove Alexander toward the outskirts.

Meanwhile, at an estate on the east side of town.

Eddie, leader of The Cripples, was drinking with two scantily-clad women clinging to him.

"Boss, you're amazing! I want to spend the night with you again."

"Boss, you're the strongest man I've ever known."

The two women leaned into Eddie, seductively stroking his chest.

"Hahaha!"

Eddie laughed happily, feeling on top of the world.

He had been in Loscity for days and finally had a chance to relax.

"Boss, Calvin's car is back."

One of his men ran over to report.

Eddie was groping the women, but his eyes lit up at the news.

"Calvin is our top enforcer, after all. He must've succeeded. Bring that beauty Ava over for me to see."

"Boss, it seems Calvin only brought back the guy, Alexander."

"He captured Alexander? Calvin really did it!"

Overjoyed, Eddie shoved the woman in his lap aside and said excitedly, "Alexander's magic powder formula is priceless. Bring him in right now. I want to interrogate him personally and find out what happened to Joseph and Houtte."

Bang!

Just as he finished speaking, there was a loud crash.

The heavy wooden door flew off its hinges, slamming into the messenger and knocking him to the ground.

The man collapsed, coughing up blood.

Alexander strolled in with a calm smile on his face.

He looked as relaxed as if he were taking a stroll in the park after a full meal.

Eddie was stunned for a moment, then exploded with rage.

"Alexander, do you even know who I am? How dare you cause trouble here!"

Several of his bodyguards immediately surrounded Alexander, brimming with hostility.

Alexander sneered, ignoring the men surrounding him as he strode directly toward Eddie.

"Stop!"

The lead guard, a man with a buzz cut, grew furious at Alexander's dismissive attitude.

"You've got a death wish, causing trouble on our turf!"

He drew a gleaming knife and stabbed it toward Alexander's chest.

Alexander moved calmly, pinching the knife between two fingers and stopping it cold.

"Let go! Let go, damn it!"

The guard cursed, using every ounce of his strength, but he couldn't pull the knife free.

No matter how much the guard twisted the knife, the blade didn't budge, leaving everyone around in shock.

In the next second, Alexander kicked the guy, sending him flying.

The remaining three bodyguards looked frightened and cautiously took a few steps back.

"Alexander, I am Eddie, leader of The Cripples! Today, you're not leaving here alive!"

Eddie cursed through gritted teeth.

Although Alexander's strength surprised him, this was The Cripples' main Loscity base, and reinforcements would arrive soon.

No matter how strong Alexander was, could he really take on dozens, maybe even hundreds, alone?

Alexander asked calmly, "The Cripples' backer is the Godfather, Satan. He's trying to go after my father, isn't he?"

Eddie paused, confused, and asked, "Your father? Who is your father?"
