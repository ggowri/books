The center of Treasure Island was a large square with smooth bluestone slabs on the ground. The ground was slightly white due to the salty and humid sea breeze.

The square was surrounded by a large number of exquisite buildings, including some of the tallest buildings on the island.

It was obvious that this was a privileged area on the island. Not only was it prosperous and exquisite, but it was also managed and cleaned by dedicated people. There was not a piece of paper on the ground, and there was even a group of birds leisurely strolling and foraging in the square.

This scene was completely different from the previous slums.

This place was like a tourist destination, with beautiful sea views, tranquility, and comfort, which made people yearn for it.

Moreover, the people Alexander met all looked like gentlemen on the surface. They were either wearing a well-ironed suit or casual clothes and were very polite.

But Alexander knew that these people were all demons. The trading center was an important industry of the Brilliant Group and a hub of the international black market.

Here, whether it was arms, women, various contraband, or hiring a killer, as long as you had money, you could buy whatever you wanted.

However, the drug business, which was once the hottest, was now in a slump. With the promotion of magic powder, fewer and fewer people were willing to use drugs.

Therefore, this trading center had become an important source of income for the Brilliant Group.

It was said that the daily transaction fees here could bring in tens of millions or even hundreds of millions of dollars.

Alexander had not yet confirmed Guzman's exact location, so he would go into the trading center to check it out as soon as possible.

"Sir, do you want to go in and trade? Admission starts at ten thousand dollars."

A pretty waitress stopped Alexander and looked at him with a smile.

Alexander was stunned for a moment. He didn't expect that the entrance fee would be ten thousand dollars. It was equal to a robbery.

When the beauty saw Alexander, she was stunned for a moment but did not show any displeasure and continued to smile, making people feel comfortable.

This kind of service didn't make him feel like he was on a sin island, but rather in a high-end club.

Alexander took out his card and paid for the ticket, and the beauty became even more enthusiastic.

She could tell from Alexander's expression that he was rich, but this was also his first time at Treasure Island.

She was well-informed and well-mannered, and she knew that she might have met a rich person this time and might even get a generous tip.

So she provided more considerate service to Alexander and introduced the business of the trading center very patiently.

"Sir, my name is Lynne. In addition to weapons, medicines, and various contraband items, the center also provides other services, such as killers or women..."

She approached Alexander shyly and rubbed her plump breasts against his arm.

Alexander felt the wonderful soft and elastic touch, but he just smiled indifferently.

Any woman around him was better than this salesperson, so of course he was very calm.

Lynne couldn't help feeling regretful. It seemed that she was going to miss out on this good deal.

Otherwise, considering Alexander's youth and handsome appearance, she wouldn't mind pleasing him for free.

Moreover, Lynne was even more certain that Alexander must be a big shot who has met numerous women, otherwise, with her looks, no normal man would not be tempted.

"Do you have any meteorites here?"

Alexander asked casually.

Lynne was stunned for a moment, then nodded affirmatively.

"Sir, do you like scientific research? We have thousands of kilograms of meteorites here, but they are very valuable..."

Before she finished speaking, Alexander became excited and immediately waved his hand.

"I'll buy all the meteorites!"

Lynne looked excited.

"Sir, thousands of kilograms of meteorites would cost over 100 million dollars to buy them all. Do you need so much?"

Alexander showed his bank card and said, "I'll pay with my card!"

Lynne took the card with trembling hands. The commission from this big deal was enough to increase her savings significantly.

As long as she saved enough money, she could leave Treasure Island and live in a normal place.

After the payment, Lynne looked at the signature and found out that his name was Edison.

"Mr. Hawthorne, your rank in our trading center has been promoted from level one to the top level nine."

"We have some great things that are not open to the public. Are you interested?"

Lynne asked expectantly.
