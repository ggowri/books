Whether it was the decapitated body or the other unfortunate victims, their wounds were impossibly clean, like surgical cuts.

Rosalia had struck four times, taking down over a dozen opponents.

Peter finally snapped out of his shock and roared, "Damn it!"

A split second later, searing pain shot through his back as blood gushed from the wound.

The combat exoskeleton had partially stopped Rosalia's blade, but it still managed to pierce Peter's flesh about half an inch deep.

Peter transformed into a raging bull, charging at Rosalia with murderous intent.

His terrifying fists cut through the air like missiles, aiming straight for Rosalia's head.

"Attack!"

The other skilled fighters jumped into action, rushing at Rosalia from different angles.

Unfazed, Rosalia met their assault head-on. Her blade danced as she engaged multiple opponents, their movements too fast for the naked eye to follow.

Alexander watched intently, knowing full well these guys would have been dead meat without their combat exoskeletons.

The protective gear was the only thing letting them hold their ground against Rosalia.

Alexander checked the time and smirked. "Almost a minute now."

The remaining gunmen didn't dare shoot at Rosalia for fear of hitting Peter. Instead, they turned their weapons toward Alexander and Charlize, fingers squeezing their triggers.

The dark gun barrels erupted with muzzle flashes as hundreds of bullets rained down like a storm toward Alexander and Charlize.

"Watch yourself!"

Alexander called out to Charlize before launching himself skyward, effortlessly sailing over everyone's heads and dodging the bullets with ease.

While Charlize couldn't match Alexander's graceful maneuvers, she demonstrated her extraordinary skills.

She moved with incredible speed, using trees and rocks as cover, making every bullet miss its mark.

Alexander had now positioned himself in a tall tree several yards away, watching the battle unfold with an amused smile on his face.

The Red Umbrella Group members' faces darkened as they realized just how terrifying Alexander's jumping ability was.

Before they could recover from their shock, Charlize made her move.

In rapid succession, she unleashed a barrage of throwing knives.

Her combat suit was equipped with several knife pouches, each containing twenty blades.

The black-coated blades were laced with arrow frog poison. It was a single cut that meant certain death.

Screams filled the air as gunmen fell one after another, knives buried in their throats and chests.

Alexander's cold voice cut through the chaos. "Ninety seconds have passed!"

Rosalia's pretty face instantly darkened with rage as she shouted, "Go to hell!"

The two katanas, which had been moving like lightning, suddenly began to slow down, their movements becoming increasingly sedentary.

Strangely, her skilled attackers also seemed to move in slow motion, as if someone had pressed the slow-motion button on reality itself.

This moment stretched like an eternity, yet paradoxically felt like the blink of an eye.

Rosalia appeared to be wielding her katanas with intense concentration, each movement precise and deliberate. Her usually rosy complexion had turned pale as snow, her expression gravely serious.

Alexander's eyes lit up with recognition. Rosalia didn't possess supernatural powers – she had mastered the highest level of swordsmanship. It was the essence of authentic martial arts, where extreme speed created an illusion of slowness while being faster than ever.

Peter and his companions were also accelerating, but they couldn't match Rosalia's movements anymore.

Rosalia channeled every ounce of her being into this strike, drawing admiration even from Alexander.

This was the strike that marked Rosalia's true breakthrough.

"Argh!"

Peter, being the closest, was the first to scream.

He desperately tried to block the blade, but no matter how hard he tried, he couldn't match her speed. Rosalia's seemingly slow strike sliced across his throat.

The blade found the gap in his combat exoskeleton, and Peter's head went flying.

Peter's eyes were wide with terror.

"Run!" the other three shouted in unison, turning to flee, but it was already too late.

Rosalia executed two more strikes.

These strikes appeared slow as molasses, but they had already sealed off every possible escape route.
