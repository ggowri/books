Alexander looked angry and said, "Are you from Ultimate Alliance?"

"Yes! The people of Ultimate Alliance are very arrogant now. We'd better not provoke them," someone said.

"Yeah! Keep your voice down and don't get yourself into trouble," another person said.

Many people passing by were talking about it.

Alexander was really angry. He didn't expect others to have such a poor impression of Ultimate Alliance.

Right after Ultimate Alliance became the largest force in Loscity, trash like Sanchez appeared. Alexander felt that it must be properly rectified.

"Get out of here right now and leave your woman here. Otherwise, don't blame us for hitting you." They threatened Alexander viciously and looked at him in contempt.

They were from Ultimate Alliance and had many followers. What did Alexander want to do?

Sanchez and his men were full of confidence, and they were sure that Alexander would not dare to resist and would have to obey them.

He looked cold and had murder intent.

Ava knew that Alexander was mad, so she quickly took a step back and hid behind him.

His companions were all terrified. When they came to their senses, they rushed towards Alexander, howling like wolves.

Everyone tried to persuade them.

Alexander said expressionlessly, "I'm not leaving. I'm waiting for the people from Ultimate Alliance!"

Everyone was stunned and wondered if he was crazy.

Hearing that Alexander was not leaving, Sanchez grinned. "You are courting dead!"
