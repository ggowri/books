This was a huge underground warehouse with a space more than ten meters high and at least tens of thousands of square meters.

The room was neatly filled with green boxes, both long and short.

Inside the box are bullets, grenades, and various firearms.

There were dozens of tall weapon cabinets around, hung with various firearms, including fully automatic assault rifles, various types of submachine guns, and even machine guns.

Not only that, there were also mines for various purposes, gas masks, biochemical protection combat suits, etc.

On the other side of the warehouse, there were even dozens of armored infantry fighting vehicles and a dozen battle tanks parked.

Although these combat vehicles were just outdated weapons, they still had terrifying lethality.

The terrifying amount of weapons stored here was enough to arm an elite combat battalion. Alexander was very happy.

Although weapons could be purchased in Loscity, they were not fully automatic heavy firepower. The heavy firepower here was comparable to that of the army.

Alexander looked over with a smile on his face, and at the same time used Force Sense to perceive his surroundings to see if Guzman had set any traps here.

He suddenly heard a slight noise and his sharp eyes immediately swept towards the corner.

He saw a little mouse hiding in the darkness and looking at this side. After sensing Alexander's terrifying eyes, the mouse trembled and was so scared that it couldn't escape.

Alexander smiled slightly and withdrew the Force that was locking onto the mouse.

Suddenly the mouse regained its freedom and quickly ran away.

Alice and Cloe, who were walking behind him, noticed this scene and were shocked that Alexander had such terrifying strength!

Even mice could not escape his eyes and control.

At this time, Alexander opened a rectangular black iron box.

Inside was a Stinger anti-aircraft missile launcher, equipped with two missiles.

The rich smell of gun oil hit Alexander's nose. These weapons were well-preserved and could be used right out of the box.

Ordinary armed organizations couldn't have anti-aircraft missiles.

Now there were dozens of similar black iron boxes in front of Alexander. It was obvious that they were all anti-aircraft missiles.

"Good!"

Alexander was overjoyed.

"I want to transport a small portion of these weapons and ammunition to Loscity. Is there any way?"

Alexander looked at the two beauties.

"Yes, don't worry, Mr. Hawthorne. We have a special delivery channel that is very safe. Leave this to me."

Cloe had already followed Alice in calling Alexander Mr. Hawthorne, completely displaying a humble attitude of submission.

"Okay, do it quickly." Alexander was very satisfied.

"Yes!" Cloe quickly arranged for people to start transporting weapons and ammunition.

In just over an hour, enough weapons and ammunition to arm an entire company were transported to a container ship at the dock.

Alexander secretly rejoiced that he had made the right decision to come to Treasure Island this time.

Not only did he control Treasure Island and the Brilliant Group, but they also obtained nearly 100 billion of Guzman's wealth. These weapons and ammunition were just bonuses.

After timely blood transfusion treatment, Cloe temporarily regained her ability to move, but her face was still pale and she had not fully recovered.

Alexander patted her shoulder lightly, and a surge of Force flowed into her, instantly cheering up Cloe.

"Cloe, the power I left in your body can help you recover quickly and improve your physical fitness."

"Thank you, Mr. Hawthorne!"

Cloe was overjoyed.

But Alexander suddenly changed his attitude and said coldly.

"But if you betray me, this power will stir in your body, tearing your intestines apart, and no one will be able to save you."

Cloe was so frightened that her whole body was shaking. She lowered her head with a pale face.

"Mr. Hawthorne, don't worry. You saved my life. I am not an ungrateful person!"

Alexander nodded with satisfaction and took Alice onto the ship.

He trusted Cloe, but he didn't trust human nature.

As time went by, the original life-saving grace may fade.

Alexander would see whether Cloe could remain loyal when tempted by sufficiently lucrative benefits.

Alexander would never gamble on human nature but would use more direct means to keep everything firmly under his control.

This was better for Cloe. She would remember who the real master was all the time.
