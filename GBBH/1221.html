Fernando Cobb, the base commander, never saw the attack coming.

He'd just wrapped up an intense "session" with two women and was trying to get some rest when the raid rudely interrupted his good time.

That really got under his skin, and his bloodlust was starting to boil over.

"Kill them all!" he roared. "I don't care who these raiders are—take them out with everything we've got!" Fernando charged forward like an enraged lion, his men falling in behind him.

"Well, well... looks like we found their base commander," Quentin said with a cold smirk as he watched Fernando barreling toward them like a madman.

"Who the hell are you people?" Fernando's tough act faltered when he got a good look at Quentin's crew. These weren't your average raiders—these guys were elite operators, and Fernando knew he was in deep trouble.

"The Sakra Society thinks they can just set up a secret base in Newbrook without our permission?" Quentin's voice dripped with contempt.

"Did you really think we wouldn't notice your little operation? That we'd just let it slide?"

He leveled his gaze at Fernando. "Hand over the pure Immortality Serum, and maybe—just maybe—I'll let you walk away from this."

Fernando's face twisted into an ugly grin, his eyes blazing with fury. "You pieces of trash think you deserve the pure Immortality Serum? Keep dreaming!"

Alexander stepped forward, fixing Fernando with an icy stare. "Since you won't cooperate, I'll have to do this my way. Trust me. You'll talk eventually."

He strode toward Fernando with the kind of confidence that made everyone hold their breath. The air around him practically crackled with deadly intent.

"What the hell?" Fernando's tough guy act faltered for a split second as he picked up on Alexander's menacing vibe. This was going to be a fight for survival.

Fernando glared at Alexander, demanding, "Hey, young man, are you running this little operation?"

Before Alexander could answer, Jonah and his men burst out from the sides, weapons ready. "On your knees, Fernando!" Jonah barked. "Or we shoot to kill!"

His metahuman squad charged in like predators going after prey, cutting down Fernando's men with brutal efficiency.

"Don't get cocky," Fernando roared, pulling out his saber. "You want the pure Immortality Serum? You'll have to get through my knife first."

With nothing left to lose, Fernando went for broke.

The saber flashed through the air in a deadly arc, slicing clean across a metahuman's throat. Blood sprayed like a burst pipe as the Level Two metahuman clutched his neck and crumpled to the ground. Just like that, Fernando had taken one of them out with a single strike.

Quentin stood there stunned. He hadn't expected Fernando to be this powerful.

As Fernando lunged forward with his saber again, sporting a twisted grin, Alexander barked out. "That's enough!"

Alexander sprang into action, closing the distance between them in a flash as he reached for Fernando's saber.

There was no doubt about it—Fernando had to be another test subject jacked up on enhancement drugs.

His blade skill, speed, and raw power were seriously impressive—easily on par with a level-four metahuman.

When he saw Alexander trying to grab his blade bare-handed, Fernando let out a cold laugh. "Are you crazy? Looking to lose those fingers?"

Fernando's saber sliced through the air with a sharp whistle as he thrust it straight at Alexander's chest, moving so fast it literally made the air scream.

Instead of drawing his Lightsabers, Alexander simply pressed his index and middle fingers together and touched them to the blade of the saber.

The military-grade steel saber—the kind that's supposed to be practically indestructible—snapped clean in half the moment it met Alexander's fingers.

"What the hell are you?" Fernando's face went pale as he scrambled backward, wildly swinging his broken blade in a desperate attempt to keep Alexander from closing in.

"It's pointless. I've already taken down several of your test subjects. Your resistance is like bringing a knife to a gunfight. It was completely useless."

Alexander moved like a shadow, his fingers dancing through the air as he unleashed invisible blades of Force energy. Each strike was like a phantom sword, precise and deadly.

The devastating assault shattered Fernando's broken blade before slamming into his chest.

Blood sprayed everywhere as the attacks punched clean through his body, leaving gaping wounds one could see straight through.

Fernando crashed to the ground with a sickening thud, his eyes wide with disbelief.

He couldn't accept that he'd been defeated so easily, and he glared at Alexander with pure hatred and resentment.

"Now, tell me where the pure Immortality Serum is," Alexander demanded, standing over Fernando's broken form.
