Jane pulled out the divorce papers and threw them in Alexander's face, her expression full of disdain.

"Sign the papers and get lost. I'll pretend your earlier words were just hot air. This engagement party is too classy for someone of your low status."

The crowd suddenly understood and began whispering and snickering.

"So, that's Jane's useless husband. First time I've seen him."

"Pathetic. Can't even keep his own woman. What a disgrace!"

Everyone ridiculed Alexander without mercy.

Alexander could barely contain his emotions.

"Five years. I've endured you all for five long years, and finally, it's over!"

For five years, he had lived like a servant in the Fairfax family, eating leftovers with no dignity or respect, living a life worse than a dog.

But now, the nightmare was coming to an end.

"Ryan, if you want a woman as unfaithful as Jane, take her. I don't care one bit."

With that, Alexander deliberately pulled Ava closer to him.

Ava's face blushed slightly, but she gently touched Alexander's cheek in response.

Seeing this, Jane was so furious that she nearly jumped into a rage. Ava was far more beautiful than her in every way.

"Alexander, how dare you cheat on me!?"

Alexander chuckled. "Jane, you were the one who betrayed me and forced me into a divorce. And now you have the nerve to accuse me?"

Jane was at a loss for words, her momentum completely stifled.

"Hmph!"

She turned her attention to mocking Ava.

"You really are shameless, Miss. Hugging a piece of trash, I threw it away. It's laughable.

"He's jobless, a drug dealer, and his daughter is in critical condition in the hospital. He has no future! Don't let him deceive you!"

Ava's disdain for Jane deepened. "What a terrible person!" she thought.

She tightened her grip on Alexander's arm and declared proudly.

"I don't care! Only women who are weak, greedy for wealth and power, would think of depending on a man. I find Alexander to be a good man. He's kind, capable, and loves his daughter deeply."

Jane's face turned beet red with fury upon hearing such praise.

"You're blind! If you don't listen to reason now, you'll suffer for it later."

Meanwhile, Ryan couldn't stop himself from eyeing Ava with greed. She was stunning, far superior to Jane in every aspect.

Although Ryan was a business partner of the Saint Group, he had never met Ava in person and had only seen her in work-related photos.

"Miss, you look very familiar. Have we met before?"

Ava gave him a scornful glance.

"You might have seen me, but I don't know you."

"Really? Then we must have a special connection."

Ryan was excited. Since he had successfully stolen Jane from Alexander, he believed he could do the same with Ava.

Adjusting his suit, Ryan tried to appear more gentlemanly and introduced himself.

"I'm Ryan, CEO of the Hawkins Group. I'm 29 and already have assets worth over a hundred million dollars."

Ava, seeing right through his intentions, replied dismissively, "That's nothing to brag about. There are plenty of people richer than you."

Ryan's face flushed with embarrassment, but he quickly regained his composure, patting his chest.

"In three days, I'll be attending the Saint Group's ball. Given my long-standing partnership with the company, I have a good chance of being appointed Vice President. The Saint Group controls a fortune worth billions of dollars."

"If you're interested, we could have dinner sometime."

Jane's face darkened with anger. Ryan was going too far, openly hitting on another woman in front of her.

Ava giggled, covering her mouth as she laughed sweetly.

"So, your name is Ryan, the CEO of the Hawkins Group? I'll remember that."

Ryan's face lit up. Clearly, he was far more impressive than Alexander, and he thought Ava was interested.

Jane, on the other hand, was fuming. "Darling, what do you think you're doing, flirting in front of me?"

Ryan awkwardly scratched the back of his head, realizing it wasn't the right time to pursue Ava.

Jane turned back to Alexander and pressed, "Stop wasting time and sign the papers!

"Bringing her here to show off is pointless. A woman like that would never fall for you."

"You think I don't see through this? You must've paid her to act as your girlfriend to protect your fragile ego, right?"

Alexander found Jane's behavior increasingly repulsive. The woman he once knew was gone, replaced by this selfish, spiteful person.

"Jane, once I sign my name, you will regret this for the rest of your life."

"Enough with the bravado. Stop pretending. Just sign the papers already. I don't want custody of our daughter, she's your problem now."

Jane couldn't wait any longer.

She was impatient to finalize the divorce, even indifferent to their daughter, just eager to marry Ryan as soon as possible.

Without further delay, Alexander signed his name on the divorce papers and flung them at Jane.

The papers, imbued with a touch of The Force, hit Jane's face with the impact of a hard slap.

Jane clutched her burning cheek, both shocked and furious.

"You dare hit me!?"

The room fell into dead silence. No one could believe Alexander had the audacity to strike back at the Fairfax family.

"You hit my woman?"

Ryan, furious, charged at Alexander, ready to throw a punch.
