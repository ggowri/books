At that moment, the portly Travis Stark chimed in, "Mr. Chapman probably just wants to play it safe. After all, Stephen is known as the top fighter in Sakra Society. But no matter his strength, I could take him down easily."

As he spoke, he snapped his fingers, conjuring a small flame that danced between them.

The Flame power shifted as Cesar and Dariel's expressions grew serious.

A metahuman wielding level-three Flame power was indeed awesome, compounded by Travis' credentials as a wrestling champion.

Seeing their serious faces only inflated Travis' ego, and a grin spread across his chubby cheeks.

Alexander replied coolly, "You're quite arrogant for someone with only level-three fire power. I wonder what someone with level-nine power would be."

Travis erupted in fury, slamming the table as he prepared to confront Alexander.

Karson stepped in quickly, soothing them. "You're all my guests, so let's keep the peace for my sake. Despite his age, Alex is incredibly skilled. I have to admit he might even be better than me.

"So, with Alex joining us, I'm sure we can defeat Stephen."

The room fell silent as Cesar, Travis, and Dariel exchanged looks, clearly doubting Karson's words and blaming Alexander's youth for his bravado.

Karson cleared his throat and began to introduce the three men to Alexander.

He gestured to Cesar, saying, "This is Mr. Cesar Dussel, a martial arts expert of the highest caliber."

Then, he pointed at Dariel, "This is Mr. Dariel Garvey. He's not only gifted with supernatural power but is also a champion boxer, having knocked out opponents 30 times in a row."

"And this is Mr. Travis Stark," Karson continued. "He's a three-time gold medalist in wrestling and holds level-three fire power. Truly unbeatable!"

All three were indeed impressive, and it was rare to encounter such strength in one place. Karson clearly put a lot of effort into gathering them here.

Karson then smiled, gesturing towards Alexander. "Alex is a bit unassuming, but I want to introduce him properly to avoid any misunderstandings.

"This is Mr. Alexander Hawthorne from Loscity, the creator of the magic powder."

The trio was visibly taken aback, their gazes fixed on Alexander.

They were well aware of the magic powder and had heard of Alexander's accomplishments, yet they were shocked to see someone so young associated with it.

The experts fell silent. While they weren't overly enthusiastic about Alexander, their attitude shifted to a more respectful stance compared to their earlier demeanor.

Alexander maintained his composure. "Mr. Chapman, you're too kind. There's no need for such introductions. I'm confident they won't be able to deal with Stephen."

The words ignited a fire within the three experts.

As a martial artist respected wherever Cesar went, he'd never encountered a youth as audacious as Alexander.

"Mr. Hawthorne, what makes you think we can't handle Stephen?" Cesar scoffed, his hand slamming down on the table in front of Alexander.

In an instant, his palm sank deep into the wood like a branding iron, creating a clear imprint as he withdrew his hand, leaving a gaping hole behind.

Cesar's move shocked everyone except Alexander.

"Mr. Chapman, it's not that I want to be kind, but he is too full of himself.

"Who does he think we can't deal with Stephen? If it weren't for your sake, I would have already knocked some sense into him!" Cesar growled, his eyes glaring daggers at Alexander, filled with intent to harm.

While Karson was a skilled fighter, he couldn't help but be impressed by Cesar's power.

The clean cut of the palm print was not only piercing but also smooth, as if someone had polished it meticulously.

"Impressive, Cesar! I have no doubt you could take down Stephen on your own," Karson said confidently.

Cesar puffed up with pride while Dariel rolled his eyes, and Travis also appeared unimpressed.
