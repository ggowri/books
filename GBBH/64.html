The passionate and warm mysterious Force surged over Ava's body, and she felt an unprecedented pleasure that she had never experienced before.

Ava couldn't help but feel limp all over, no longer aggressive under the fierce kisses of Alexander.

Alexander had laid the car seat flat, and the two of them were about to make out in the car.

Beep!

Suddenly, a burst of urgent car horns was heard, followed by the powerful roars of the engine.

Alexander looked up sullenly and saw a blue sports car coming with several black cars.

They didn't look friendly.

Ava was startled and widened her eyes.

"These are the cars of the Brilliant Group. Are we surrounded?"

At this moment, Theodore got out of the sports car.

He was five feet nine. Although he was gangly, he was dressed smartly.

Alexander comforted Ava, "What will be, will be. Don't worry. I'll deal with them."

He opened the car door and got out first.

Ava stood beside him compliantly. As long as Alexander was around, she was not afraid.

Theodore went up to them arrogantly with a group of people.

"Hello, Ms. Blackburn. I am Theodore Tate from the Brilliant Group."

He smiled and extended his hand to Ava.

Alexander couldn't help but frown slightly. Why didn't Betty inform him that Theodore had come to Loscity?

Damn it!

Betty couldn't have betrayed him. It was very likely that something had happened to her.

Ava ignored Theodore's hand and said coldly, "Mr. Tate, why did you come to see me with so many people?"

Theodore cast a cold glance at Alexander.

"Are you Alexander, who is dating Ms. Blackburn?"

"Yes, I am Alexander."

Alexander looked Theodore in the eye without showing any sign of weakness.

Theodore looked closely at Alexander and couldn't help but smile contemptuously.

Alexander looked plain and weak. How could he compete with his uncle to be the successor of Godfather?

The disappearance of Nicholas and Daniel might not have anything to do with Alexander. Betty did not lie. The Kaylon might have taken the two men away.

Theodore stretched out his hand and said, "Mr. Hawthorne, nice to meet you."

Alexander knew that Theodore had bad intentions, but he shook hands with him without any fear.

When their hands met, Theodore suddenly tightened the grip on Alexander's hand, trying to make a fool of him.

Alexander couldn't help but sneer. Theodore had overestimated himself.

The next second, Theodore's smirk turned into a mixed expression of shock, anger, and pain.

He felt as if his hands were tightly clamped by an iron hoop, and his finger joints crackled under the pinching force.

Theodore struggled desperately, trying to pull his hand back.

However, Alexander held it tightly, making him unable to withdraw.

Alexander deliberately asked, "Mr. Tate, why do you look so upset? Would you like me to take you to the hospital?"

Theodore flew into a rage and wished to skin Alexander alive.

Alexander again tightened his grip and clenched Theodore's hand more fiercely.

Theodore finally threw away his pride and shouted angrily, "Why are you holding my hands so hard? Let go of me. Let go..."

Alexander sneered contemptuously.

"Didn't you want to launch a sneak attack? You've overestimated yourself."

He shook off Theodore, who fell heavily to the ground and moaned in pain, holding his bruised right hand.

The people from the Brilliant Group rushed over and helped Theodore up.

Ava was very happy to see Theodore defeated. She said with a smile, "Mr. Tate, you're a grownup. Why can't you walk steadily?"

"Stop pretending. It's obvious that he..."

Theodore gritted his teeth and trailed off. Being inferior to Alexander in strength was not something to boast of.

He took a deep breath and tried to calm down.

"Ms. Blackburn, the Brilliant Group is eager to recruit talented people. We would like to invite you to join us. Your annual salary will be doubled."

Ava said indifferently, "Thank you, but I won't leave the Saint Group."

Theodore immediately stopped pretending and revealed his true colors.

"Ava, if you don't leave the Saint Group, the Brilliant Group will not spare you. What a pity. You're such a beautiful and charming woman."

"If you are willing to be my woman, I'll triple your salary and make you a senior executive of the Brilliant Group. How about that?"

Theodore continued to tempt Ava.
