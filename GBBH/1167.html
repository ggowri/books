Preston's right arm had already been broken by Alexander earlier, but it could have healed.

Now, Tristan's blade had completely severed it. The limb dropped to the ground, fingers still twitching slightly. Blood gushed out like a fountain.

Between the pain and shock, Preston's eyes rolled back, and he passed out cold.

The crowd watched in stunned silence as Tristan dropped the blade and fell to his knees before Alexander, pressing his forehead to the ground in a deep bow.

"I've failed to teach my son properly. Please punish me!"

The dozens of tough guys behind him dropped to their knees as well.

It was wild—these same men who'd stormed in looking ready for a fight were now groveling on the ground. Everyone else in the room could barely process what they were seeing, like their eyes were playing tricks on them.

All eyes locked on Alexander. The crowd knew this guy had to be someone seriously powerful to make Tristan and the Ultimate Alliance crew back off like that.

The looks coming his way were a whole mix of emotions—confusion, curiosity, fear, and respect.

Alexander glanced at the unconscious Preston, then at Tristan, trembling on his knees, about to say something.

But Ava grabbed his arm and whispered, "Let it go. The guy's already lost a hand. That's enough."

Alexander nodded. "Fine, we're out of here."

They walked out of the bar hand in hand, with people scrambling to clear a path for them.

Tristan stayed frozen on his knees, not daring to move a muscle. Only after Alexander and Ava were completely gone did he finally let out a massive sigh of relief and collapse onto the floor.

Right then, Preston came to, whimpering. "Dad... I'm your son... Why did you... why did you cut off my hand?"

"Where are they? Where's that bastard?" Preston yelled, totally clueless about the situation.

"Shut up! If you want to live, then shut your damn mouth!" Tristan roared, looking like he wanted to strangle this worthless idiot right then and there.

He waved his hand dismissively. "Take him to the hospital. Once he's patched up, I never want to see him in Loscity again!"

Two burly guys grabbed Preston from both sides and started dragging him out.

"No! Dad, what are you doing? Dad, I'm sorry! I'm sorry! Please forgive me!" Preston screamed, his face turning ashen.

One of the guys leaned in close to his ear with a nasty grin. "You know who that guy was just now? He's..."

Though the guy's voice dropped to a whisper at the end, Preston felt like his brain had short-circuited. Everything went blank like he'd been struck by lightning.

"He... He's your boss?"

Preston completely deflated, like all the fight had drained out of him.

He'd harassed the Ultimate Alliance leader's woman, and he'd gotten off with just a broken hand and his life intact. It was quite a dumb luck.

"I can help with the pain and get your body back to a healthy state."

"Really?" Ava was amazed. She never would have guessed Alexander could treat something like this, too. It seemed almost magical.
