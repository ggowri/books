"This young man is probably an amateur. I don't believe he can succeed."

"Yeah, if it was so easy to find high-grade gems, we would have made a fortune long ago."

"That's right. He is doomed to lose."

Hearing everyone's discussion, Olivia showed anxiety in her beautiful eyes.

"Alexander, you must win."

Finally, Alexander selected a piece of ore. Through his All-seeing Eyes, he could see a strong light in the center of the ore.

There must be a high-grade gem in it, even a top-grade gem.

Alexander waved to the staff next to him and said, "I'll choose this one."

The staff member took a look at the sign on the stone and said in confusion, "The material of this ore is not good, and the chance of it containing a gemstone is extremely small, so its price is only five thousand dollars."

Olivia quickly persuaded, "Alexander, you'd better not choose this cheap ore. Generally, the chances of cutting out gemstones from expensive ores are higher. How about we choose an expensive one?"

"Shut up!"

Evan shouted viciously, "This is a bet between him and me. Don't interfere. Everyone saw that he chose this one, and he can't change. I am waiting for him to give me fifty million dollars."

He couldn't help laughing.

"You idiot. You chose a worthless piece of scrap ore. Do you want to cut out a high-grade gem from this ore? Impossible."

Evan laughed triumphantly. He was confident that he would win.

Olivia was so anxious that she was about to cry, but she didn't know how to help Alexander.

Alexander glanced at Evan coldly and smiled.

"Don't worry, I won't change my mind. I'll choose this one. The price doesn't mean anything. The stone you just bought cost thirty million dollars, but what did you get in the end?"

Evan was so angry that he trembled all over.

"I will buy it. Cut it open." Alexander waved to the staff member.

Evan sneered, "You treat a piece of scrap ore as a treasure. You are a bumpkin."

"Shut up! If you keep talking, I will beat you up."

Alexander was annoyed, and he stared at Evan with cold eyes, which made Evan feel deep fear in his heart.

Evan gritted his teeth and dared not speak.

The staff member started cutting the stone with a cutting machine.

The onlookers all shook their heads, not interested in this ore at all.

"Haha, there are no gems in this kind of ore. Was this ore picked up from the roadside?"

"It's possible. Sometimes, the organizers would pick up some stones and use them as ores."

"This boy is doomed to lose. There's no way he can win."

The person who was cutting stones also looked sad.

He had cut open many ores, so he could tell at a glance that this one did not contain any gems.

"Stop!"

Alexander suddenly stopped him and then drew a line on the ore with a pen.

He said, "Cut in this direction and stop after this. Then use a grinder. Start from this side and grind all around."

The staff member rolled his eyes speechlessly.

Didn't Alexander make things difficult for him?

"What do you mean? You know there is a gem inside, so you don't use a cutter to avoid damaging the gem?"

Everyone burst into laughter.

"Haha, he is acting. I think he wants to delay time and try not to lose so quickly."

Evan kept mocking Alexander, "It's just a piece of scrap. Just cut it open. If there is a gem inside, I'll eat the stone."

Alexander pointed at the line firmly. "Listen to me, start using the grinder after cutting here."

The staff member was helpless. He thought there was no need to use a grinder, but since Alexander asked for it, he had to follow his request.

As the grinder was turned on, the sound of violent friction was very harsh.
