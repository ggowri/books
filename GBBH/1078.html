"What... what are you all standing around for? Get them! Seize them all!" Lanny stammered out his orders.

The corporate security guards immediately surrounded Alexander and the other two.

Emma's eyes were filled with glistening tears that instantly spilled down her cheeks. Her lips trembled as she pleaded, "Run! I'll hold them off!"

Alexander gave a confident smile. "Don't worry, ma'am. I'll protect you both. Let's find somewhere more private to talk this through."

"Can we even get out of here?" Emma stared at Alexander in disbelief.

"Mom, don't worry. Alexander's fighting skills are incredible. They don't stand a chance against him," Maxine rushed to explain.

Emma's eyes widened with a mix of shock and hope as she focused on Alexander.

Lanny stepped forward, pointing at Alexander with arrogant contempt. "Hey punk, do you even know where you are? You dare try to steal a woman from Silver Shield Security? Got a death wish?"

"Get out of my way," Alexander replied icily. "Don't make me do this the hard way."

"You! You're asking for death!" Lanny, already seething from the earlier confrontation, completely lost his composure. "Grab them all!" he snarled through gritted teeth.

Two security guards reached for Alexander. "Back off!"

With just those few words, Alexander swung his hand in a devastating slap. The guards went flying with screams of pain, crashing hard onto the ground. Each coughed up a mouthful of blood, along with several teeth.

Alexander's foot slammed into Lanny's chest before the remaining security guards could even react.

Despite being over six feet tall and nearly 220 pounds, Lanny went flying backward from the impact.

The other guards froze in terror, staring blankly, too scared to make a move.

"Let them go for now," one of them muttered.

Alexander escorted the mother and daughter to his car and drove away swiftly.

"Don't worry, ma'am," he said as he drove. "Since they're after Mr. Duncan's technology, they won't harm him for now. I'll get you both somewhere safe first, then I’ll go to rescue Mr. Duncan."

Emma suddenly burst into tears with a loud sob. "Thank you... If you can really save my husband, you'll be our family's savior..."

"Mom, don't worry." Maxine hugged Emma comfortingly. "Alexander will make it happen!"

"Ah..." Emma let out a deep sigh. "Your father worked so hard on his research, trying to give us a better life. He finally achieved a breakthrough, but we never imagined Silver Shield Security would stoop so low."

"Mom, don't worry," Maxine said, her eyes glistening with tears. "Alexander will definitely rescue Dad."

Emma glanced at Alexander as he drove. Though still somewhat doubtful, she nodded firmly.

Suddenly, her phone buzzed. Looking at the screen, she saw it was her husband calling. Her heart leaped with joy as she exclaimed, "It's your father! Could he have escaped?"

Emma quickly answered and put the call on speaker.

Then, a creepy voice filled the car. "Trying to run? You really think you can escape? Listen up! I have Alvin. Be at Frenzy Bar at 8 PM. If you don't show up, Alvin will lose a leg.

"And don't even think about calling the police unless you want to plan his funeral!"

Sinister laughter echoed from the other end of the line.

"It's Jordan!" Emma and Maxine were terrified.

The laughter from the phone grew louder, more maniacal. "Emma, Maxine, I know you're listening. You two are quite the attractive pair, mother and daughter. That's why I won't kill you.

"Maxine, if you want your father to be safe, come to the bar tonight. Convince him to hand over the technical documents. Then you two ladies sleep with me, and I might spare you."

Jordan hung up with a sinister laugh.

"That bastard!" Maxine exploded in anger.

A slight smile crossed Alexander's face. "I'll send you two to a safe place to stay. Tonight, I'll meet Jordan at the bar."

"Alexander, are you sure you can handle this alone?" Maxine asked worriedly. "Should we call the police?"

Alexander chuckled, "If the police could help, wouldn't you have called them earlier?"

Maxine fell silent, her furrowed brow betraying her concern.

After a moment, Emma hesitantly spoke up, "Mr. Hawthorne... Why are you helping us? Is it just because of my daughter?"
