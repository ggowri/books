Rosen screamed in pain, "You bitch! If you have the guts, kill me!"

Melin warned, "I'm not someone to be messed with. If your men dare to move, I'll kill you first."

Rosen didn't expect her to be so determined, and he became even angrier.

He trembled violently from anger and pain.

"Don't worry about me. Attack her together and catch her!" he ordered.

Two muscular men immediately charged at Melin.

Melin stared at them.

They burst into action, moving very fast.

Their muscles bulged with explosive power.

"Get her!" the two men roared.

Melin was daunted.

With their speed and strength, she couldn't handle even one of them.

She finally realized that killing Rosen wouldn't help.

Plus, he would resist during the process.

Melin quickly backed away.

The two men grinned and charged at her.

Soon, Melin was cornered, and she still had handcuffs on.

She stared at the two men intensely.

She bent her knees slightly, lowered her shoulders, and got into a fighting stance.

Melin took deep breaths, forcing herself to calm down and focus on the enemies.

Rosen was now protected by his men.

They surrounded Melin so she couldn't escape. The two muscular men approached her from both sides.

Melin's back was covered in cold sweat. She felt like she was being watched by a pack of hyenas.

Alexander could see that although Melin was skilled, she was no match for these elite fighters.

These people were killing machines.

"Mike, I can handle this woman alone," one of the men said, stopping his partner.

"Okay, remember not to hurt her. We have plans for her later. A woman with such a good figure must be enjoyed," his partner said with a grin, stepping back.

"Officer, you should stop resisting. You'll only get hurt," the man said with a twisted smile.

The scars on his face were terrifying.

Before he could finish, Mike suddenly moved.

He leaped from the side, moving incredibly fast, and tried to grab Melin.

Alexander was disgusted by their cowardly tactics. They had the advantage in numbers but still resorted to sneak attacks.

Mike's attack was fierce.

Melin could only dodge it as best as she could.

"Officer, you can't escape!" Mike laughed wickedly and continued to attack.

"Damn it!" Melin was cornered again.

Her hands were still cuffed.

She used all her strength to elbow Mike in the head.

"Not bad, you've got some moves," Mike smirked, blocking her elbow with his own.

Her elbow hit Mike's arm, but he only slightly moved aside and quickly stepped forward to face her.

Melin was shocked and instinctively kneed Mike in the stomach.

This strike used all her strength. If she missed, she would lose her balance.

Unexpectedly, Mike was prepared. He sidestepped and punched Melin in the right rib.

With a cry of pain, Melin was thrown to the ground.

She held her right side and sweated in pain.

The man was faster and stronger than her.

Although Melin's fighting skills were not inferior, the handcuffs greatly affected her performance.
