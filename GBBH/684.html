Alexander pondered for a moment, realizing he needed to rush to Treasure Island. His showdown with Felix was fast approaching.

He quickly gathered the top members of the Ultimate Alliance, instructing Charlize and Rosalia to select a team of five hundred elite fighters to accompany him to Treasure Island the following day.

Meanwhile, he assigned Marvin and Betty to protect Ava and Ashley.

Just then, his phone rang, and upon seeing the caller ID, he frowned slightly. It was Shadow.

Alexander wondered, "Why would he suddenly be calling?

Is it another order from the old man?

Or is Felix stirring up trouble behind the scenes?"

A flurry of thoughts ran through his mind.

"Mr. Hawthorne, let's meet tomorrow in Tijuanea. There's something important."

Shadow said briefly before hanging up.

Alexander was puzzled, but since he was already planning to head toward Treasure Island, he decided he could make a quick stop in Tijuanea first.

The following evening, bathed in the glow of a stunning sunset, a large convoy of ten buses and several luxury cars made its way to an estate on the outskirts of Tijuanea.

As the convoy stopped, Alexander stepped out of the lead BMW 7 Series.

At that moment, the estate was heavily guarded, with armed soldiers densely packed around it, and it was impossible to tell how many watchmen were on duty secretly.

Light and heavy weaponry glinted menacingly around the grounds, and black muzzles were aimed directly at Alexander's convoy.

In addition to the guards, Alexander's All-seeing Eyes detected several powerful figures inside the mansion.

There were over a hundred strong individuals, all metahuman or martial arts experts, gathered for what appeared to be a lavish banquet in the dining hall.

A young man in a suit approached with a polite bow. "Please follow me."

Alexander signaled Charlize and Rosalia to accompany him, leaving the others outside.

He trusted that Shadow, being the most loyal bodyguard to the Godfather and having expressed his support, would not attempt any harm.

The banquet hall was immense, spanning thousands of square feet, with several smaller lounges and VIP rooms surrounding it.

The men and women at the event were all engaged in polite conversation, exuding an air of sophistication that was clearly high society.

Even the servers, despite their role, were dressed immaculately and conducted themselves with dignity.

The male servers wore sleek black tuxedos, while the female servers were dressed in seductive bunny costumes, looking irresistibly charming.

Occasionally, one of the attractive servers, male or female, would be led into a private room by one of the powerful guests, not to be seen again for some time.

The atmosphere at the banquet, though sophisticated, was tinged with a decadent allure. It was a surprising sight, given the strength and influence of those in attendance.

"Mr. Hawthorne!"

Shadow spotted Alexander right away and approached him with a warm smile.

"Shadow."

Alexander greeted, extending his hand.

Shadow lowered his head, grasping Alexander's hand with both of his, a gesture of surprising humility that caught Alexander off guard.

Though Shadow had previously expressed his support, Alexander hadn't expected such respect since he had not yet formally inherited the Godfather's title or earned the feared moniker "Reaper."

"These are Rosalia and Charlize," Alexander introduced his companions.

Shadow laughed heartily. "Mr. Hawthorne, you've got me envious! If I were twenty years younger, I'd give you a run for your money with the ladies!"

Then, before Alexander could ask any questions, Shadow continued.

"The Godfather sent us here. He no longer supports Felix and has chosen you as his successor."

Shadow's expression was complex as he spoke, his eyes moistening slightly.

Alexander was taken aback, a mix of emotions washing over him rather than the excitement he might have expected.

For the first time, he felt a faint, unfamiliar sentiment toward this father he had never met.

Scanning the room, Alexander didn't see the elderly man he had envisioned.

Shadow bowed respectfully and explained, "The Godfather had some urgent matters to attend to but will arrive shortly."

He then went on to clarify why so many powerful individuals had gathered.

"The Godfather intends to help you take Treasure Island decisively. Mr. Anthony Hawthorne's alliance with the City of Sorcery to construct a Demon Summoning Array has infuriated him.

"Back in the day, the Godfather and Rock were bitter enemies; they even killed members of each other's families. There's blood vengeance between them. That Anthony Hawthorne would disregard this to ally with Rock has left the Godfather deeply disappointed.

"More importantly, the Godfather admires your abilities and character!"
