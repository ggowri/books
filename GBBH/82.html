Augusta was the first to shed his pants, revealing the old scar on his knee.

A jagged line ran from his thigh down to just below his kneecap, a testament to the brutal injury he had endured.

The larger pieces of shrapnel had been removed, but a few tiny fragments were still lodged dangerously close to the nerves in his knee.

If those shards were disturbed, they could damage the nerves and render his leg useless—a risk even the best specialists were unwilling to take.

Alexander hadn't prepared any anesthetics; instead, he instructed Billy to bring over the magic powder.

He said, "Augusta, this magic powder works better than regular anesthetics. But since it's an old injury, removing the shrapnel might still sting a bit. Just bear with it."

"Boss, this magic powder is incredible! I feel great all over. Just do what you need to do; I can handle it," Augusta replied, a grin spreading across his face.

Nodding, Alexander used Force Sense to pinpoint the tiny pieces of shrapnel before picking up the prepared needle.

As he slowly inserted it, the Force coalesced around the needle, emitting a faint white glow. The others watched in awe, their respect for Alexander growing.

Though the needle pricked his skin, Augusta only furrowed his brow, silently enduring the discomfort.

Alexander felt a surge of satisfaction; a strong will often equated to a strong fighter.

With a swift motion, he withdrew the needle, revealing a tiny piece of shrapnel the size of a sesame seed stuck to its tip.

It was astonishing.

Alexander continued, extracting three small fragments in total, each one a testament to his skill.

"Ah! That feels so good..."

Augusta's eyes lit up, joy radiating from his face.

The persistent ache in his knee faded, replaced by a sense of lightness he hadn't felt in ages.

Unable to contain himself, he flexed his leg, excitement bubbling over.

"Boss, how did you do that? I feel like I'm already healed! This is unbelievable!"

Alexander cautioned with a smile, "Easy there! No need to rush. You'll need to rest for at least three days before you can start pushing it again.

"And while you're going to feel a lot better, don't expect a full return to normal just yet. But getting to eighty or ninety percent is a solid win."

"I'm already more than satisfied with that. Thank you, boss," Augusta said in gratitude.

Apart from the simple words of thanks, this former soldier didn't know how else to express his appreciation.

The other three men, who had been somewhat anxious, now looked at Augusta with renewed hope, their anticipation for their own treatments ignited.

"I'll take a look at your injuries next," Alexander said, moving to assess them individually.

Each of their wounds varied in severity. Logan had a shoulder injury, Marvin had a wound in his abdomen, and Charlize had a hurt back.

Charlize's injury was relatively minor, but the others were serious. The cumulative effects of their untreated injuries left them in a precarious state.

Marvin's situation was the worst; he had developed necrosis in his abdomen due to a piece of shrapnel that couldn't be removed.

No wonder Charlize teased him about not lasting more than two minutes in bed. Such injuries would definitely take a toll.

"This area is particularly sensitive due to the nerve endings. If it weren't for the Force, I wouldn't dare to treat it," Alexander thought, assessing the situation.

"Don't worry! Your injuries may look severe, but they're all treatable, and you can fully recover," Alexander reassured them confidently.

"Really? I can fully recover?"

Marvin's face flushed with excitement.

He had thought he'd be stuck like this for life, so hearing Alexander's promise filled him with hope.

"Absolutely!" Alexander confirmed.

Marvin's gratitude was palpable.

Alexander chuckled. "Just so you know, this area is pretty sensitive, so it might sting a bit."

Marvin thumped his chest confidently. "Don't worry, boss. Just go ahead and treat me. If I even make a sound, I'm not a man."

Alexander nodded and got to work, treating each one of them carefully.

Hours later, Alexander was drenched in sweat but finally allowed himself a satisfied smile.

Continuous treatment was no easy task, and he'd need some serious meditation to restore his Force.

"Thank you, boss!"

Augusta and the others expressed their gratitude profusely. They were deeply appreciative but were at a loss for how to repay him.

If Alexander asked them to take on any task, even something as extreme as taking lives, they would do it without hesitation.
