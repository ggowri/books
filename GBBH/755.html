After all, this was the headquarters of the Ultimate Alliance. Alexander's challenge to a one-on-one duel was more than ideal for Bruno.

"Alexander, don't you dare run!"

Bruno pursued him swiftly.

Before anyone could fully register their movements, Alexander and Bruno had vanished.

"Are they both that fast?"

"Stop doubting. Our boss will win for sure!"

Charlize's tone was resolute, but her heart was filled with worry.

Alexander and Bruno raced towards the outskirts of Loscity, dashing through the streets like gusts of wind, causing a commotion.

Soon, they reached a secluded path in the suburbs of Loscity.

The area was lush with vegetation and rarely frequented by people.

They entered a quiet grove by the roadside, stopping almost simultaneously.

Alexander remained calm, his breathing steady as he leisurely observed Bruno.

Bruno, on the other hand, was slightly out of breath, his chest heaving from the sprint.

Alexander wore a teasing smile.

Bruno couldn't withstand Alexander's piercing gaze, feeling a chill down his spine.

He drew his blade. The blood-red machete was unsheathed.

Alexander remained still, intrigued by Bruno and his blade.

"Kill!"

Bruno roared, stomping the ground with force. The impact caused the solid earth to cave in.

The powerful reaction propelled Bruno towards Alexander like a bullet.

In the next moment, Bruno appeared right in front of Alexander.

Alexander casually sidestepped, moving to Bruno's flank.

But Bruno seemed to float as if weightless.

He appeared to be drawn to Alexander, moving slowly. In reality, his speed was so fast that it was beyond the grasp of ordinary people.

A blood-red blade of light appeared, slicing towards Alexander's throat.

Alexander didn't use his Lightsaber. Instead, he punched out.

His fist was enveloped in the Force, creating an incredibly tough protective shield.

The last time, Alexander had shattered Bruno's blade with a single punch.

The fist collided with the machete, and this time, the blade didn't break.

Bruno was thrown back by the force, snapping a small tree.

Alexander swayed but remained standing firm.

However, the ground beneath his feet cracked, spreading out like a spider's web.

Just the aftermath of their clash was this powerful.

Clearly, their strength and speed had far surpassed ordinary humans, even meta-humans, demonstrating the terrifying potential of human capabilities.

"Kill!"

Bruno launched another attack.

He leaped like a ferocious tiger and swooped down like a nimble eagle, raising his blade high above his head and slashing down toward Alexander's skull.

The red blade of light seemed like a waterfall descending from the heavens, intending to split Alexander in half from top to bottom.

Alexander stood his ground. This time, instead of using his fist, he drew his Lightsaber.

A dazzling white light flared, blocking the bloodied blade.

With a faint sound, the Lightsaber effortlessly severed the machete.

Bruno retreated in shock, staring at his broken blade in disbelief.

"What's going on? My blade was made of tungsten steel. How could it break so easily!"

Alexander ignored him and thrust his Lightsaber.

The blinding sword light almost prevented Bruno from opening his eyes.

He dodged with lightning speed but didn't entirely escape the Lightsaber, and his left arm was severed.

At the same time, Bruno hurled his broken blade at Alexander.

The distance was too close. Alexander didn't expect Bruno to forgo dodging and instead opt for a mutually destructive move.

The broken blade grazed Alexander's shoulder, blood immediately flowing down.

Alexander let the blood drip to the ground, staring at Bruno with cold eyes.

Bruno clutched his severed arm. He knew he had lost.

With the instincts of a wild beast, he knew he couldn't avoid that sword thrust, so he chose to inflict some damage in return.

But Alexander was too fast. In the end, Bruno lost an arm, while Alexander was only scratched.
