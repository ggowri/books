Grace's curiosity was piqued.

She hadn't anticipated Asher's extravagant gesture of preparing tens of millions dollars in jewelry along with a mysterious final gift. It was genuinely heartfelt.

Yet, when she glanced at the small gift box from Alexander, a sweet smile spread across her face.

She valued his gift, regardless of its cost, and felt joy at whatever he gave her.

Asher confidently commanded, "Bring it up!"

"Yes, Mr. Vance," his men responded in unison.

Several burly men escorted an elderly man who held a silver gift box with both hands. All eyes were on the box, clearly expecting something remarkable inside, as indicated by the heavy security.

The anticipation for Asher's grand final gift hushed the hall, making even the sound of a pin dropping audible.

Everyone was eager to see the gem within the box.

Alexander, however, sneered inwardly, having already discerned what lay inside.

Taking the gift box, Asher strutted to the center of the hall, casting a smug glance at Alexander.

Ave urged, "Asher, open it quickly. We want to see the treasure."

"Yes, Mr. Vance. Please open it quickly. We want to see it."

"We can't wait any longer, hurry up!"

Everyone started to urge him on.

The more they urged, the more pleased Asher became. After whetting everyone's appetite, he finally slowly opened the gift box.

Suddenly, a beam of light shot up into the sky, and everyone's eyes were attracted by the dazzling ring.

The ring featured a large diamond surrounded by countless smaller stones, radiating a captivating light.

Moreover, it exuded a noble aura and carried a hint of the mysterious and heavy sense of history.

Everyone who saw this ring was involuntarily attracted, and a greedy expression appeared on their face.

This diamond ring was so precious and amazing.

Even Grace was intrigued, asking, "What kind of ring is this?"

"This is the Eros Ring," Asher declared. "Wearing it brings the blessing of the God of Love for a happy romance! It's a divine artifact."

He flaunted the ring while eyeing Alexander provocatively.

Seeing Alexander remain composed, he felt a twinge of annoyance.

Alexander recognized the ring was no divine artifact. It was the Cursed Ring of an ancient wizard.

Its long history was shrouded in secrets even modern wizards might not uncover.

Alexander, possessing the Ultimate Legacy with notes from a powerful ancient wizard, saw through it immediately.

Ancient wizards crafted cursed jewelry to extend their lives.

Anyone wearing this ring would not find love. Instead, it would drain their life force, allowing the wizard to prolong his existence while ensuring the wearer wouldn't die.

Anyone who wore the ring would definitely die miserably.

"Gentlemen, I spent a billion dollars on this Eros Ring to propose to my fiancée," Asher announced. "Only such a precious artifact is worthy of her. Grace, will you marry me?"

Asher proposed to Grace in public. He knelt on one knee in front of Grace with the ring in his hand and looked at her expectantly.

There was a sudden cheer at the scene.

"Mr. Vance is so romantic! The Eros Ring is worth a billion dollars. How sincere!"

"What a perfect couple! Wishing them happiness!"

"The Vance family is on the rise. Their future is limitless!"

Everyone was captivated, oblivious to Alexander, as Asher's ring stole the show.
