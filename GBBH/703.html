Alexander had just returned to the ground level of the plaza, but a terrifying force suddenly enveloped him. The air itself seemed to thicken, pressing down on him with an almost physical weight.

In a flash, Shadow and the others watched as a black figure materialized behind Alexander like a phantom, its hand shooting out to grab his throat.

Though they stood hundreds of feet away from Alexander, everyone felt the violent ripple in space.

Faced with that horrifying black hand, no one could move. It was like being trapped in quicksand with tons of weight pressing down on them.

"Felix has made his move!"

Shadow's face went pale as he stared at the black hand.

"Kill them!!"

The Red Umbrella Group's captives erupted in excited cheers at the sight.

"Finally, we're saved!"

Their voices rang out with triumph. "Victory will be ours! Treasure Island belongs to us!"

Their excitement was through the roof, pinning all their hopes on Felix.

Alexander maintained a calm expression as he faced the giant black hand reaching for him.

He muttered under his breath, "Borrowed power is always just borrowed. Still, this evil Force is impressive. Clearly, there's a lot in this world I've yet to discover..."

His voice brimmed with confidence as he added, "If this isn't even the true evil entity, what harm can it do to me?"

BOOM!

The black hand lunged at Alexander but crashed against an invisible Force shield, unable to break through.

Despite his outward composure, Alexander's heart raced as he faced this unprecedented challenger.

With a flash, his Lightsaber ignited and wreathed in flames as the Force surged through it.

The heat was so intense that Shadow and the others could feel it burning from hundreds of feet away.

"Evil will always be evil!"

Alexander gripped his Lightsaber, and his entire presence transformed dramatically.

He thrust his blade straight into the center of the black hand.

For a moment, the hand and Lightsaber were locked in a standoff. Then, the hand began to crumble, disintegrating piece by piece until it vanished entirely in the pale flames.

Gripping his Lightsaber with both hands, Alexander channeled every ounce of Force power he had and brought his blade crashing down toward Felix's head.

It was Alexander's first time unleashing his full power. The Force stored within him burst forth like a flood.

His level-two Lightsaber reached its maximum potential, blazing so bright it could blind anyone who dared look directly at it.

Among the thousand people surrounding the plaza, most couldn't bear to look. Many felt their knees buckle, dropping to the ground with a thud.

Alexander's strike destroyed Felix's hand, while the Force engulfed Felix's body in searing flames that crackled and hissed.

"Kill him! Kill him!"

Felix's rage exploded as dark energy surged outward, extinguishing all the flames.

Alexander struck relentlessly, his sword darting like lightning toward Felix.

Felix roared continuously, charging like an enraged bull, using his dark powers to defend himself while viciously ramming toward Alexander.

BOOM!

The two terrifying forces collided violently.

The Lightsaber wrapped in intense flames clashed against the dark energy. They compressed, devoured, and destroyed each other like water meeting fire, incompatible and locked in eternal conflict.

The entire plaza trembled as invisible ripples spread outward in concentric circles like stones dropped in still water.

Suddenly, a piercing scream echoed from the plaza's center as the dark energy rapidly dispersed.

Felix's furious voice rang out.

"Kid, I won't forget this! You'll die by my hand eventually. When I truly enter this world, I'll skin you alive!"

As his words faded, Felix crashed heavily to the ground, his body shattering into pieces.

Alexander wiped the blood from the corner of his mouth and gave a bitter smile. Though he'd killed Felix, he hadn't escaped unscathed.

Against such supernatural evil, his power still fell far short.

The Red Umbrella Group's captives were struck speechless with fear. With Felix dead, their only options were surrender or death.

Cloe moved in with her team, securing all the prisoners.

Everyone looked at Alexander with complex expressions mixing excitement, awe, and deep anticipation for the future.

A thunderous roar filled the air.

The sound of helicopter blades grew louder as they approached.

A black helicopter swooped in, landing swiftly in the plaza.

Shadow quickly straightened his clothes and said excitedly, "Mr. Hawthorne, the Godfather's here. He must be coming to name you as his successor!"
