"Boss!"

Xavier strode towards Alexander excitedly.

"Boss? Mr. Ulysses, who are you calling? Is the head of the Ultimate Alliance here?"

Waylon was dumbfounded.

He knew that Xavier, who had just been a nobody in the industry, had risen rapidly to become a big shot after representing the Ultimate Alliance.

"If I can become a member of the Ultimate Alliance, then..." Waylon murmured to himself, full of expectations.

At that moment, Alexander walked over, and Waylon roared, "Damn, you are so bold. You hit me yesterday and my sister today. You are doomed!"

"Mr. Ulysses, this guy..."

Waylon had a flattering smile.

However, instead of casting a glance at him, Xavier pushed him hard, causing him to stagger.

"Get lost!"

Waylon was confused and embarrassed with a livid face, wondering what was going on.

"Boss, I just got back."

Xavier hugged Alexander passionately.

What!?

Waylon was stunned.

With a chuckle, Alexander patted Xavier on the back. Among his subordinates, except for the women, Xavier was the only one who dared to embrace him in public.

Waylon stammered, "Mr. Ulysses, i-is he your boss? The boss of the Ultimate Alliance?"

Xavier snorted coldly and said contemptuously, "Waylon, haven't you always wanted to meet our boss? This is our boss, Alexander Hawthorne!"

Waylon's mouth was wide open in shock, and he took several steps back in fear.

"Waylon, please - whoever he is, you have to help us get revenge!" His sister clutched his arm desperately.

"You idiots! Do you have any idea what you've done?"

SLAP! Waylon's hand cracked across his sister's face.

"I keep telling you to keep your head down, but you never listen! Are you trying to destroy me too?"

"Apologize to Mr. Hawthorne - now! Or I'll kill you myself!"

The couple deflated like punctured balloons, suddenly very quiet.

Alexander watched the show with an amused smirk like he was enjoying street theater.

"Apologize if you want to live!"

Waylon kicked his sister hard.

"I'm sorry... I'm sorry... I remember now. It was our son who was at fault. He fell and injured himself. I'm sorry..."

The couple apologized repeatedly and then ran away in shame with their son.

"Mr. Hawthorne, I'm so sorry. Yesterday was a mistake... I'm really sorry. I don't know Lynn is yours. I'm sorry. I was out of my mind!"

Waylon apologized to Alexander while slapping himself in the face to beg for mercy.

"Get out of Loscity, and don't harass Lynn again, or I'll castrate you!" Alexander warned, his tone revealing murderous intent.

"Okay. I'll leave right away, right away..."

Waylon breathed a sigh of relief, turned around, and ran away.

Alexander kept his eyes upon him fiercely. Waylon felt a chill down his back and ran faster in fear.

Unexpectedly, he tripped and face-planted right into a pile of dog shit. The stench was so bad he nearly puked.

Alexander and the others laughed out loud.

But Waylon didn't bother to stay any longer, he got up and rushed out in an instant.

Half an hour later, Alexander and Xavier arrived at Food Street, where more than a dozen shops served breakfast.

Various food was available, such as spaghetti, hamburgers, ravioli, and pizzas, all of which were fragrant with tempting aromas.​

Alexander ordered two plates of spaghetti, two hamburgers, and two plates of ravioli. Then, he and Xavier chatted while eating.

Xavier bought several bottles of beer.

"Alexander, drink with me!"

Alexander asked with a grin, "Drink in the morning? Are you upset about something?"

Xavier made no reply. He first filled Alexander's glass and then his before gulping it down in one go.

Alexander drank a few glasses with him and asked again, "Xavier, you know I don't like beating around the bush. Tell me what is going on. I will definitely help you if I can."
