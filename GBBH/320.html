The first-class seats were in pairs, with Madelyn seated by the window and Alexander by the aisle.

Quentin's guards were storing luggage and then took seats behind them.

"Walter, what kind of skills does this Alexander guy even have? Do we really need to serve him like this?"

One guard complained.

The leader, Walter, with striking blue eyes, sighed and replied, "Don't say that. It's an order, and we have to follow it."

"Hmph!"

The other guard could only sit down, pouting.

At that moment, Marcos boarded the plane in a hurry.

His seat was in the same row as Alexander, just across the aisle.

Marcos stared at Madelyn, taking in her graceful figure, alluring charm, and long, beautiful legs...

He couldn't help but swallow, taking a couple of gulps.

Not only Marcos, but the men around were all focused on Madelyn.

Even though several other women in the area were also attractive, besides Madelyn, they seemed like mere stars beside the moon.

Marcos wasn't just a popular singer in Tijuanea; his father was one of the biggest drug lords there, and he'd been with many models and actresses.

Madelyn's looks and figure rivaled any celebrity's, and she had an even more sophisticated aura.

Marcos couldn't help but imagine spending a wonderful night with this beauty.

He couldn't believe he'd met such a stunning woman while passing through Loscity—he couldn't miss this chance.

With his status and confidence, he felt sure he could easily win over this beauty.

As for Alexander sitting beside her, he didn't consider him a competitor at all—what threat could this nobody pose?

Marcos walked over, smiling, and asked, "Lovely lady, may I sit here?"

He pointed at Alexander without caring whether Alexander wanted to switch seats or not.

Madelyn gave him an icy look.

"What do you mean?"

Marcos immediately pulled out a stack of dollars and tossed it at Alexander, sneering. "Take this money and go sit somewhere else."

Alexander was speechless—this guy was unbelievably rude.

Seeing this, Madelyn grew furious. "What do you mean? Do you think money makes you superior? Who cares about your filthy cash!"

Marcos was taken aback, his confidence shaken.

"Miss, I'm the famous singer Marcos from Tijuanea. My father is Caleb; surely you've heard of him?"

He even tried to suavely brush his hair back.

"I don't know you, now go away. I have absolutely no interest in you; stop bothering us."

Madelyn picked up the money thrown at Alexander and tossed it back at Marcos with a hard glare.

Alexander kept a smile throughout, watching Marcos get humiliated.

Laughter erupted at the scene as everyone mocked Marcos' arrogance and incompetence, and Marcos immediately became enraged.

"Ma'am, don't you think that was a bit much?"

Marcos attempted to grab Madelyn's shoulder, but Alexander intercepted him.

"Don't harass my friend again, or I won't be so polite."

"Hmph! Who the hell are you?"

Marcos glared at Alexander in fury.

Alexander's attire was ordinary, and in terms of status, he surely couldn't compare to Marcos, who quickly regained some confidence.

"Brat, you're really pushing it. Give up your seat now, or you'll regret it."

Alexander's expression grew stern; this shameless guy just wouldn't stop.

"Are you deaf? I told you to get out of the way!"

Marcos actually raised his arm, aiming a slap at Alexander.

Walter leaped up, ready to assist, but Alexander had already seized Marcos' hand, immobilizing him.

"What... what are you doing? Let go... it hurts!"

Marcos cried out, feeling his hand clamped like an iron vice, growing tighter and tighter.

"Ow! Let go..."

In the next second, his pleas were abruptly cut off.

Alexander grabbed him by the neck and lifted him into the air like a hawk clutching a chick.

Marcos' face quickly darkened, his eyes nearly popping out.
