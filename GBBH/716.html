The doctors, nurses, and police were all fully equipped with protective gear - goggles, masks, isolation gowns, gloves, and shoe covers.

Despite their complete protective equipment, everyone was pale-faced with pounding hearts, wondering if they'd be the next to get infected.

Melin barked orders to her subordinate.

"Summarize the situation and report to the chief immediately! We need more personnel to prevent riots. And remember that! Protect yourself at all costs. Don't get infected!"

When no response came from the young officer, she realized he was frozen in fear.

Melin shot him a fierce glare. "What are you standing there for? Get moving!"

"Oh! Yes, yes, Ms. Ward, I'll report to the chief right away."

Snapping out of his daze, the young officer suddenly grabbed Melin's arm.

"Ms. Ward, it's dangerous out here. Please be careful..."

Behind her protective mask, Melin's expression was hidden, but her eyes crinkled with a hint of amusement.

Seeing his worried look, she sighed internally, patted his shoulder, and said softly.

"Kid, I've got a boyfriend. Just take care of yourself."

The young officer's face fell, leaving him at a loss for words.

"Help! Murder! Somebody help!"

Suddenly, piercing screams erupted from the other side of the ward.

Melin's expression hardened as she snapped, "Stop wasting time! Report to the chief and get backup here now. I'll check it out."

Without another word, she drew her gun and rushed toward the screams with the other officers.

With trembling hands, the young officer began to make the call...

The next day at Loscity Airport...

Most flights had been grounded due to the terrifying epidemic.

Countless people crowded the terminal, desperate to escape the city and the deadly plague.

A private jet touched down on the runway, and after coming to a stop, Alexander was the first to disembark.

Rosalia and Charlize followed close behind him. Shadow, Augusta, and William had stayed behind at Treasure Island to assist Cloe.

Alexander frowned deeply at the sight of the packed terminal. Men, women, the elderly, and children all crammed together in a chaotic mess.

He couldn't believe how much Loscity had changed in just a few short days.

As the three of them barely made it out of the airport, Alexander's eyes landed on someone he'd rather not see.

It was Jane!

There she was, dragging a large suitcase, looking completely worn out. Next to her sat Belinda, sickly and miserable, with an expression of utter despair.

Some sketchy-looking skinny guy in an airport uniform was trying to chat Jane up, wearing a smug expression.

Alexander let out a cold internal laugh, barely sparing them a glance before dismissing them entirely.

Jane had cut all ties with him. If it weren't for Ashley's sake, the Fairfax family would've been wiped out long ago.

Charlize whispered, "Jane's definitely trying to find connections to leave Loscity. Looks like she's about to get scammed. What an idiot."

"Just ignore them. Let's go," Alexander said.

Alexander strode forward with purpose.

"Alexander, wait!"

As they passed by, Jane spotted Alexander immediately and rushed over with excitement.

Alexander stopped in his tracks, his face expressionless. "What do you want?"

"I... I need to ask for your help... Could you help us leave Loscity? Please, for Ashley's sake, help us escape this terrible epidemic!"

Jane stammered her desperate plea.

Alexander heaved a deep sigh, remaining silent.

"This woman is heartless," he thought, "worried only for her own skin and not even considering Ashley."

"Who the hell are you?"

The skinny man glared at Alexander with hostility.

He'd been sweet-talking Jane, using his airport job as bait to have some fun with her, but this guy had come out of nowhere to steal his thunder.

Jane replied with a complicated expression, "He's my ex."

Her eyes drifted to the two stunning women by Alexander's side, both dripping in luxury and designer brands. Her heart twisted with jealousy and envy, mixed with deep regret.

If she hadn't cheated back then, if she hadn't abandoned Alexander, she would've been the one enjoying his wealth now.
