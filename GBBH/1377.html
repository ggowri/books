Behind him stood a young man, towering over six and a half feet tall, incredibly obese, his body layered with rolls of fat. His hair was tied in a high topknot, and he wore nothing but a pair of blue-and-white striped shorts around his waist. His bare feet stomped over the rocky ground without a hint of discomfort.

Behind them stood over a thousand armed men, carrying blades and rifles.

When they arrived at Tiger Gang, they found the gates wide open, completely deserted. The fat Risunese man grinned at the middle-aged man beside him.

"Klein, looks like Josh is gone. I bet everyone in this Gang has already fled!"

The tall, thin middle-aged Risunese man patted the fat man's arm with smug satisfaction.

"Adonis, looks like we've secured another Gang. The Sacred Spirit Gangordered us to take control of all the bandit dens in the Oxhead Mountains. At this rate, it won't take long!"

The two flattered each other as they stepped into Tiger Gang, leading a small group of men inside.

With a thunderous boom, a massive wooden gate suddenly crashed down from above, sealing off the entrance completely.

A lean but imposing figure stood defiantly atop the Gang walls, his fierce gaze locked on them. Holding a massive nine-ring broadsword, his entire body radiated an aura of raw ferocity. It was none other than Josh.

Suddenly, a battle cry erupted! Logan and Ivan, leading hundreds of armed fighters, charged out from the inner courtyard. Some surrounded the Sacred Spirit Gang fighters trapped inside, while others rushed up to defend the walls.

The Sacred Spirit Gang's men were thrown into chaos—those inside were surrounded, while those outside couldn't break through. No one knew if their two leaders were alive or dead.

At that moment, Adonis let out a loud, boisterous laugh.

"Hahaha! I never thought an old, sick tiger like you still had tricks up your sleeve!"

Dressed in a pink robe, Klein sneered wickedly as he pointed at Josh.

"Kneel and pledge loyalty to us now, and we might let the past slide!"

Clap, clap, clap!

Suddenly, the sound of slow, deliberate applause echoed. Alexander emerged from the main hall, clapping as he strolled forward with a smirk.

"A pink beanpole and a sumo wrestler—what a pair of Risunese clowns."

Hmm? Another one?

Klein's sharp, angled eyebrows twitched. His beady eyes gleamed with malice as he locked onto Alexander and murmured,

"Adonis, take care of the old tiger. Be careful with this one—his energy is powerful!"

Adonis' fat cheeks trembled. He shot Josh a sideways glance and let out a low grunt. Then, his thick legs—each as wide as a grown man's waist—stomped the ground.

Boom! The ground trembled beneath him.

His massive body defied gravity, launching into the air like a crossbow bolt—his round head like an arrowhead, his bulky body the shaft, and his limbs the fletching—as he hurtled toward Josh.

Josh roared, his nine-ring broadsword surging with blazing red protective energy, and swung down with all his might toward Adonis' head.

The two clashed instantly, matching each other blow for blow.

Inside the Gang, the battle erupted between both sides, while outside, the Sacred Spirit Gang's forces launched their assault. Logan and Ivan led their men in a fierce defense, holding the walls without retreating.

Klein's beady eyes flashed with a vicious gleam. With a light tap of his toes, he glided toward Alexander, moving as effortlessly as a dragonfly skimming the water.

Alexander's eyes gleamed sharply. With a swift yoga twist, he instantly shifted to Klein's side, lowering his stance. His leg, as straight as a staff, carried the faint roar of thunder as he swept it toward Klein's ankle.

Klein's eyes flickered with brief disdain. He tapped his toes against the ground and leaped into the air, letting out a sharp cry. "Levitating Technique!" A strange and mysterious energy spread around him. Though his movements seemed slow, he soared into the sky at an astonishing speed.

Klein's energy was incredibly strong! At the very least, he was at the peak of a level-four warrior.

Levitating Technique!

Alexander's mind raced. His body shone with a vibrant blue protective energy, his eyes glowing with intensity as he locked onto Klein. He clenched his fists lightly, positioning one in front and one behind, lowering his stance into a solid guard with his feet firmly planted.

Klein smirked coldly. Raising his index and middle fingers to his temples, he suddenly let out a fierce roar.

"Solar Fist!" In an instant, an intense white flash, brighter than the sun, blasted straight at Alexander. His eyes, fixed on Klein, were instantly overwhelmed by blinding light. The next moment, his vision turned completely white—then total darkness. He couldn't see a thing.

At that moment, Klein channeled his energy into his fingertip. With a sudden thrust, a brilliant white beam shot from his finger like a laser, hurtling straight for Alexander's chest!

Damn it! If he has the Levitating Technique, then of course he has the Solar Fist! And probably the Piercing Wave too!

Alexander was temporarily blinded, frustration boiling inside him.

In the blink of an eye, he could sense an overwhelmingly powerful energy surging right toward his chest!

Alexander let out a low snort. Crossing his palms in front of his chest, he traced a circular motion. His protective energy flared up violently. With a powerful shove, he unleashed Seismic Cleave—a move from the Muscle-Tendon Classic. A dense surge of deep blue energy erupted in thick, cascading waves, crashing toward Klein like an inverted galaxy!
