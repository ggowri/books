Though Alexander appeared imposing, in Asher's eyes, it was as if Alexander had walked into his own grave. To Asher, he was already as good as dead.

Alexander turned his gaze toward Asher, murderous intent in his soulful eyes, a cold smile tugging at his lips.

"Asher, you're not leaving here today. And neither is Axel. Neither of you will escape."

His words, dripping with killing intent, seemed to chill the air around them, dropping the temperature by several degrees.

Asher snorted dismissively, his voice dripping with contempt.

"Alexander, do you think this is still West Cemetery? Today, I'm fully prepared, and the only one who's going to die here is you.

"Hand over the magic powder formula, and perhaps I'll spare you some suffering.

"And as for the women around you, whether it's Ava or Betty... they're mine. They're all mine! After you're dead, I'll take good care of them. Don't worry."

A sinister laugh escaped him, his eyes gleaming with greed and desire.

Alexander shook his head. He realized that Asher still hadn't realized the danger he was in.

"Asher, I will kill you!"

Asher laughed derisively, not taking Alexander's threat seriously at all.

Two men, each radiating cold, murderous intent, stepped forward from behind Asher. One possessed level-two strength power, and the other, like Windwolf, was a metahuman with level-two speed power.

"Mr. Vance, let's stop wasting time with this guy. Let's just kill him!"

"Alexander, I've heard you're strong, but the three of us can easily handle you."

"You're an idiot! Anyone who dares to insult Mr. Vance will be killed."

With his two powerful companions at his side, Windwolf regained his confidence.

He had already forgotten the moment when Alexander had shattered his knife with a tiny stone.

"Kill him!"

Under Windwolf's command, the three men arranged themselves in a triangular formation, their cold eyes locking onto Alexander, ready to strike.

In their eyes, Alexander was already a dead man.

"Are you three serious? Do you think you can fight me?"

Alexander couldn't believe how ridiculous this was. He wasn't just a level-two metahuman anymore. At his current strength, even a level-five metahuman wouldn't stand a chance.

"Shut up. Don't act so cocky in front of me!"

Asher snapped, a cruel smirk spreading across his face.

"Take him down. I want to have some fun with his women in front of him..."

Before he could finish, a flash of fierce light flickered in Alexander's eyes. "Die!"

He made the first move.

However, Asher wasn't worried in the slightest.

Windwolf and his two companions were already on high alert. The moment Alexander moved, they roared in unison, "Grab him!"

Before they could even react, Windwolf shot forward like a bolt of lightning, blocking Asher's path.

The gleaming edge of a knife flashed!

Windwolf now held two knives, one in each hand, and he viciously stabbed toward Alexander's chest.

At the same time, his two metahuman companions attacked from either side.

One, just as fast as Windwolf, slipped behind Alexander with a knife in hand, aiming for his back.

The other, wearing heavy knuckle gloves, swung a punch that roared through the air, aimed straight at Alexander's head.

As a level-two strength metahuman, his punch could shatter rocks.

He was confident that even if he didn't land a direct hit, grazing Alexander's head would be enough to give him a concussion.

As Alexander faced the three metahumans closing in, Betty and the others' expressions changed dramatically.

Now, they understood that Asher had been prepared all along. The earlier attack by Windwolf had just been a distraction.

The combined strength of three metahumans was far more terrifying than Windwolf acting alone.

Asher's face twisted into a mocking grin as he watched the scene unfold, his confidence unwavering.

He had no doubt that his three powerful men would easily capture Alexander.

But then, in the blink of an eye, everything changed.

The smile vanished from Asher's face. Panic crept into his expression.

Windwolf and his companions' attacks landed on Alexander, but they struck nothing but air. It was only an illusion.

Alexander had already disappeared. All they had attacked was the air.

Windwolf didn't even have time to react. He turned and found Alexander right behind him, as silent and sudden as a ghost.
