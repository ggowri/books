Meanwhile, everyone saw the strange black demon puppet riding on Alexander's neck and pinching his throat hard.

His face turned pallid, and he showed no reaction with his eyes shut.

Dereck chuckled triumphantly, "Alexander, weren't you very awesome just now? I'll give you a chance. If you hand over the Ancestral Witch's Cane and the Spirit Bead Bracelet, maybe I will let you survive a few more days!"

He was full of confidence. As long as the demon puppet took action, everyone would be killed.

Alexander was very powerful. As long as the demon puppet took his life and sucked his blood, its strength would be greatly improved.

"What should we do? Mr. Hawthorne seems to be dying!"

"This wizard is formidable. Is it true that the Axe has no choice but to perish?"

Malone and the other Axe members looked pale and despondent.​

Alexander trembled violently as if he was about to fall down in the next second.

All the Axe members had lost hope and felt helpless.

Wizards were indeed the strangest and most terrifying beings.

Especially at the sight of the ferocious demon puppet, they couldn't help but break out in cold sweats and were completely in despair.

Even the Mafia and the Weaver family silently watched the scene with wide eyes.

The demon puppet pulled out a bloody knife, put it on Alexander's neck, and attempted to cut off his head in one move.

Dereck grinned and said, "You are really capable. You actually can hold on for so long."

Seeing that Alexander was still conscious, he showed a fierce expression on his skinny face and roared, "Kill him!"

At the urging of his master, the demon puppet uttered a strange chattering sound, and its pupil-less eyes suddenly widened.

Its gray eyes, as big as walnuts, bloodshot and full of murderous intent, contrasted with the ugly, twisted scars on its face, making it look hideous and ghastly.

The blade had cut through Alexander's skin, and he was on the verge of death.

The weird supernatural power was indeed terrible, and even a Warrior couldn't resist it.

When the skin on Alexander's neck was scratched, the slight pain at once woke him up.

He violently activated the powerful Force within him, which surged and turned into the Force Burning!

A huge white flame emerged and struck the demon puppet directly.

The terrifying heat of the Force Burning could destroy all evil, and like a maggot clinging to a bone, it enveloped the demon puppet tightly.​

Despite its struggles and shrieks, it couldn't get rid of it.

Even the bloody knife was quickly melted in the flame.

The demon puppet exploded and was reduced to ashes in the white flame.

In the end, its head only lasted a few seconds longer and let out a shrill scream.

The demon puppet was burned and disappeared without a trace.

At the same time, Alexander's All-seeing Eyes saw a pretty and adorable baby waving at him with a smile in the air.

It was clearly that demon puppet. It was the little boy's remaining soul, purified by Alexander's Force Burning, and he was completely freed.​

That wisp of soul floated away into the sky with a laugh and was soon lost in the clouds.​

Dereck spat out a large mouthful of blood, staining his entire chest red.

The demon puppet that symbolized his life was successfully purified by Alexander. He was but a shell now and would soon be dead.​

Dereck flopped down heavily on the ground, breathing weakly, and his already skinny frame became even more shriveled.

Only his fierce eyes were fixed upon Alexander as if he were eager to bite him to death and eat him.​

Everyone was stunned. They had just believed that Alexander would definitely be doomed.

To their surprise, he suddenly fought back, purifying the demon puppet and killing the wizard.

There was a dead silence!

No one spoke or fired a shot.

Stanley swallowed hard and glanced at Alexander with excitement and admiration.

Despite knowing that Alexander was very strong, he didn't expect him to be so mighty that he could even eliminate a wizard who possessed a demon puppet.

Alexander breathed a long sigh of relief. It was so dangerous just now. He almost got killed.
