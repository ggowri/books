Alexander unleashed the Force, suddenly stomping the ground.

"Boom!"

The hard marble floor exploded violently.

Alexander leaped high into the air, and all the bullets aimed at him missed.

"The guns are impressive, but they're useless against me."

A cold, merciless voice rang out.

Then he descended from the ceiling with a gleaming dagger.

In a second, cries of agony, pleas for help, and the sound of bodies hitting the ground echoed throughout the hall.

Severed limbs and spurting blood were everywhere.

In the blink of an eye, more than a dozen people lay scattered across the hall.

None of the remaining bodyguards from the Federer family survived, and the stench of blood filled the air, making the pampered seniors of the Federer family feel nauseous.

"Are you... are you even human?!"

Gary could hardly believe his eyes; he deeply regretted provoking this monster.

His will shattered, Gary turned and ran.

"Think you can escape?"

Alexander suddenly appeared in front of him, landing a punch to Gary's gut.

Gary clutched his stomach and fell to the ground, crying out in pain while blood poured from the corner of his mouth.

"Gary, did you think you were powerful, that the Federer family was something great? In the face of absolute power, you're nothing.

"To me, the Federer family is just a group of lambs waiting to be slaughtered!"

Alexander scoffed derisively.

The high-ranking members of the Federer family trembled in fear, too scared to speak up, worried Alexander would target them next.

As for whether Gary would die, they didn't care, as long as they could survive.

Edward knelt on the ground, too afraid to look up, not wanting to attract trouble.

"You dare to bully my woman?"

Alexander lifted Gary up and delivered another punch to his stomach.

"Ah..." Gary screamed, blood beginning to spray from his mouth.

"And threaten me with Ashley?"

Alexander punched him again on the same place.

Gary curled up like a shrimp, losing control of his bladder.

Suddenly, a foul smell filled the hall.

"What a joke! The head of the Federer family is nothing but trash!"

Alexander casually tossed Gary to the ground with a thud.

Three punches have left Gary in agony, and he hasn't died on the spot, only able to groan in pain.

After a while, Gary convulsed violently a few times and then lay completely still.

Alexander came today to eliminate any threats; he won't allow Gary to live.

"Please don’t kill me! Mr. Hawthorne, it's all Gary's plan; we had no idea about it."

"Yes, it was all Gary acting on his own. We only found out later and it's too late."

Several senior members of the Federer family knelt down, desperately pleading for living.

Since Alexander dared to kill Gary, he could easily take their lives too.

They understood the consequences of silence; Alexander had no reason to let them go.

Alexander scoffed and declared proudly,

"There’s a chance to give you a way out, but it depends on whether you'll grasp it."

The Federer family is the most powerful of Loscity's four families. Although they lost most of their elite tonight and even their stronghold was taken, they were not yet done for.

If he killed these senior members, the Federer family would become a mess, which wouldn't benefit Alexander at all.

If he found a puppet to take over Gary's place and control the Federer family, he could maximize his own interests.

Alexander smiled and said, "Gary betrayed the family and caused serious losses. Everyone in the Federer family agrees to strip him of his title and expel him, right?"

"Yes, yes! Mr. Hawthorne is right. Gary was unforgivable; we'll make an announcement tomorrow!"

The seniors nodded vigorously, too afraid to voice any dissent.

Alexander swaggered over to the couch and glanced at the terrified beauty, pointing to the fruit platter on the coffee table.

The beauty trembled but quickly understood and hurried to peel an apple.

Her hands shook so much that she nearly cut herself several times.
