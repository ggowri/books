Early the next morning.

As for men, the better they were, the more women would be around them.

Especially the scar on his cheek, which extended from the corner of his eye to his neck. The scar was twisted like a centipede and was very scary.

More than a dozen big men in black all bowed their heads and saluted the bald man.

The man snorted with a hint of arrogance.

"This is Mr. Farrow from Underworld of Kingsdon!"

The onlookers were horrified and hurried away as if they were avoiding the plague.

Hoson was a big boss in the gang. He was ruthless, killing people without blinking an eye. Everyone who knew him had to respectfully call him "Mr. Farrow".

His subordinate pointed to the hotel.

Alexander must be served carefully, otherwise, Hoson would be in trouble.

"Okay, Mr. Hawthorne!"

It was so embarrassing that a gangster had to please an ordinary waiter.

"Thank you!"

"Hoson, come in."

"Hoson? Could it be that he is the gangster boss?"

"What is the origin of Mr. Hawthorne inside? Even Mr. Farrow is so humble facing him."
