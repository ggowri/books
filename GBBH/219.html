Kevin gave her a playful squeeze on her butt and grinned. "Why bother with a movie? I'll wrap this up in ten minutes, and then we'll hit a five-star hotel buffet. After that, some real fun."

"You're such a bad boy," she laughed, teasing him back.

They flirted openly, oblivious to anyone else around them.

Meanwhile, all the shop owners on Gemstone Street gathered. The Ulysses family had long controlled the street, supplying goods to these shops, so everyone wanted to stay on their good side.

"Dad, the Carter family is here!" Angelica pointed out as the group approached.

Aiden's expression turned deadly. "Let's go meet them."

With the shop owners in tow, the Ulysses family went to confront the Carters.

"Isaac, it's been a while. Good to see you're still... kicking," Aiden greeted, his words laced with irony.

Isaac snorted. "Of course I am, Aiden. I wouldn't dream of leaving this world before you do."

Aiden's demeanor shifted to anger. "Isaac, you dare to challenge my family's authority and eye the management of Gemstone Street? This is unacceptable! Today, we settle this. A duel. The winner takes control of Gemstone Street, and the loser pays ten million dollars. Agreed?"

Isaac smirked confidently. "Agreed!"

From behind Isaac emerged a burly man with calloused fists, clearly a seasoned fighter. Aiden patted Kevin on the shoulder, full of anticipation.

"Kevin, we're counting on you. Don't let us down!"

Kevin thumped his chest with confidence. "Aiden, I've got this!"

As Kevin stepped into the ring with the strong man, the tension in the air was palpable. The crowd buzzed with excitement, eager to see who would prevail.

The two fighters exchanged initial blows, quickly escalating into a fierce battle. Kevin's skill was evident as he soon forced his opponent to retreat.

"Go, Kevin!" Angelica cheered, excitement bubbling over.

The Ulysses family watched eagerly as victory seemed within reach. Isaac's face darkened, realizing his expensive hire was no match for Kevin.

Emboldened by the cheers, Kevin deliberately left an opening. The strong man seized the opportunity, rushing in, only to be met with a powerful uppercut to the chin from Kevin. Kevin followed with a flurry of punches, knocking the man out cold.

Cheers erupted. Aiden and Angelica laughed in triumph. Kevin stood victorious in the ring, raising his fist. "Who dares challenge me?"

"I do!" came a cold voice, cutting through the noise.

Silence fell as everyone turned to see Julia and Aiden approaching. Aiden glared at Julia. "What's this about, Julia?"

Julia met his gaze calmly. "As a member of the Ulysses family, I have the right to challenge the head according to clan rules."

Aiden scoffed. "You think you can take my place? Who's going to beat Kevin? Your son, Xavier?"

He laughed dismissively.

Xavier bristled but was held back by Julia, who smiled. "Not my son, but someone I've hired."

Aiden sneered. "Kevin's one of the best in Loscity. Who could you possibly hire?"

Kevin smirked. "Bring them on. I'll take them down."
