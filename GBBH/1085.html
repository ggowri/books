Alexander ignored him, grabbing the handcuffs on Alvin's wrist and twisting them.

Crack.

"Are you okay? My last name is Hawthorne. Your wife and daughter asked me to rescue you," Alexander said with a smile.

Alvin turned red with excitement, not knowing what to say.

Alexander turned around and went up to the frightened Jordan.

"Jordan, bear in mind killing him is easier for me than crushing an ant."

He was not stupid, knowing with Alexander's strength, it would be useless even if he had more people on his side.

Alexander looked at Lanny and said with a smile, "Villain, today I will kill you as a warning to the rest."

His cold murderous aura frightened Lanny, who was trembling all over.

"Plop."

Lanny apologized and slapped himself in the face.

Alexander ignored him, giving him a kick.

"Bang."

Lanny flew backward like a bowling ball, knocking several people to the ground and fainting.

After a burst of exclamations, a silence fell upon the place.

Although Jordan outnumbered Alexander, he was rooted to the ground as if he were cast a spell.

Alexander glanced around with sharp eyes.

"Jordan, this is a warning. If you dare to covet the All-purpose Bullet-resistant Jacket, you'll end up like Lanny."

The people behind cast awed, angry, and unconvinced gaze at Alexander, but he didn't even look back.

"Bang."

Jordan breathed a sigh of relief. He had been fearing that Alexander would kill him.

At this time, a subordinate came over and asked sulkily, "Mr. Nicholson, shall we give up like this?"

In the hotel room, the Duncan family hugged each other and cried bitterly.

"Dad, we are finally safe. Thank you, Alex."

Maxine looked at Alexander gratefully with tears in her eyes.

Emma and Maxine looked at Alvin in surprise, realizing something was wrong.

"Well..." Alvin sighed heavily.

"Mr. Hawthorne, you are an expert. I didn't expect you to notice it..."

"During my captivity, Jordan not only tortured me for the information but also injected me with an addictive potion that was fiercer than drugs in an attempt to completely control me.

"What? A mind-controlling potion?

"Jordan is despicable!"

The Duncan family was shocked and furious, and Maxine even jumped up in anger.

He screamed and rolled on the ground, even shouting that he would go out to see Jordan. He looked more miserable than those drug addicts.
