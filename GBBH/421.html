In the packed bar, Betty stood out like a jewel, drawing every man's eye.

The music grew heavier as the night wore on.

Bodies moved wildly on the dance floor, drinks held high, as people let loose.

They were all there seeking release, letting their hidden demons run free.

Alexander sat next to Betty, secretly enjoying how other men looked at them with envy.

While Betty closed a deal with a client, Alexander nursed his whiskey.

The Force let him drink as much as he wanted without getting drunk - unless he chose to.

He smiled warmly at her over his glass.

She'd catch his eye now and then, flashing that sweet smile of hers.

"Mr. Mansfield, it's settled then. You will officially become a partner of the Saint Group, and I'll offer you a ten percent discount."

"Thank you, Miss Rowe. I really appreciate your support," Burton Mansfield replied, nodding eagerly, overly accommodating.

Although Burton was polite to Alexander, Alexander couldn't help but notice the occasional flicker of disdain in the man's eyes.

Alexander didn't mind. In many people's eyes, he was just Betty's kept man, especially since the magic powder formula came from her.

After concluding the business deal, they toasted one more time before Burton excused himself.

Alexander wrapped an arm around Betty and began to move his body to the rhythm of the music.

Betty had a unique scent that thrilled him.

It was rare for Betty to come to a bar with him, and she was clearly enjoying herself, her sultry dance moving in sync with the music.

Not far away, a few men exchanged lewd glances and deliberately squeezed closer to Betty.

The leader was a rotund man, his belly protruding like that of a pregnant woman, his jiggling cheeks wobbling as he moved.

Alexander had already noticed these unsavory characters, his expression quickly turning cold as a dangerous glint flashed in his eyes.

The man and his companions circled Betty, occasionally brushing against her before grinning as they stepped away.

Although Betty had been drinking, she was very much alert and felt the harassment, quickly backing away toward Alexander.

The rotund man, undeterred, continued to invade her space, and Betty's temper flared.

Slap!

With a swift motion, she slapped the man hard across the face, making him dizzy.

The man clutched his stinging cheek, realizing he had failed to take advantage and had been humiliated.

"Fuck! Little beauty, you've got some guts!" he spat, opening his arms to grab Betty.

"What do you think you're doing?"

Alexander kicked the man away, sending him flying.

Crash!

The man tumbled through the crowd like a bowling ball, knocking over several patrons and causing chaos in the bar.

"Ow! Ow!"

The man wailed as he struggled to get back on his feet.

"You son of a—do you know who I am? You're asking for trouble!"

His companions surrounded Alexander, looking menacing.

Alexander merely smirked. To him, these men were less than grass.

"Fatso, I'll give you one minute to disappear from my sight, or you won't like the consequences."

Betty had already taken refuge behind Alexander, feeling blissful with her protector by her side.

"Who do you think you are? You think you can play tough? You don't even know who my boss is! I'm going to make you pay!"

The man was defiant, believing he had been ambushed, and picked up a bottle to charge at Alexander.

The bottle whizzed through the air, but Alexander casually punched the man in the face.

"Ah!"

A piercing scream echoed throughout the bar.

The man fell to the ground, blood gushing from his face, showcasing the severity of Alexander's blow.

"You little brat, you dare to hit Albert? You're asking for death!"

The man's cronies snapped back to reality, drawing sharp daggers and rushing toward Alexander.

The sharp dagger slashed towards Alexander's throat with extreme viciousness.
