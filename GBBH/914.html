The man was just a researcher and didn't notice Alexander silently trailing behind him.

"Melin, don't blame me. You were the one who was so persistent with your investigation at the Locity Police Department. In the end, you were caught by Jake, weren't you?

She didn't dare believe Alexander would show up.

"Stop!"

"Touch her, and I'll kill you right here."

The entire room fell silent. Yannick froze, his body going rigid.

He quickly spun around, his eyes locking onto Alexander, who had suddenly appeared behind him.

"Ah! Alexander! How did you get here?"

Melin's heart skipped a beat. She couldn't believe her eyes.

"Mr. Hawthorne!" The four officers, just as shocked, let out a collective gasp.

But Yannick's face drained of color as he stared at Alexander with growing terror.

"Help me!"

Melin, unable to control herself, screamed out in pure relief.

The man she had given up on seeing again had appeared, just when she needed him most, like an angel sent to save her.
