At that very moment, Sebastian was being rushed into the hospital, cradling his head and crying out in pain.

It felt as if a power drill were burrowing into his skull, and he wished for nothing more than to end the agony with a single bullet.

News of the Brilliant Group president's mysterious illness quickly spread throughout the hospital.

In the end, they had no choice but to inject Sebastian with a hefty dose of painkillers, which provided only slight relief.

The relentless drilling sensation in his head persisted, overshadowing even the pain in his injured hand.

Sebastian couldn't shake the memory of Guzman's ruthless nature; the man was a true devil.

But then again, wasn't Alexander a terrifying devil in his own right?

Chaos ensued in the hospital room.

The feeling of a drill in his brain was a relentless, cruel form of torture.

Had it not been for the potent painkillers, Sebastian would have succumbed to the despair of taking his own life.

A renowned neurologist, whose fee was astronomical, rushed to the hospital at night and conducted a series of examinations.

"Dylan, what's in my head? Am I cursed, or is it some illness?"

Sebastian collapsed onto the hospital bed, despair enveloping his heart.

At that moment, Dylan cautioned, "Mr. Sebastian Tate, based on my speculation, your condition cannot be delayed any longer.

"If we don't find an effective treatment within the next couple of days, with a decline in your body's functions, multi-organ failure could follow... That would be perilous..."

Dylan handed Sebastian a notice of critical condition.
