He had hit only an afterimage. It wasn't Alexander.

"Damn it!"

Kyle quickly retreated backward.

Alexander had suddenly appeared like a specter, striking Kyle in the chest with a palm strike.

With a crisp sound of breaking bones, Kyle let out a shrill scream, thrown more than ten feet away.

In front of everyone, Kyle, a strong member of the Red Umbrella Group and a level-one metahuman, was defeated by Alexander in a single move.

"Bastard!"

Kyle cursed, struggling to get up and fight back.

Alexander kicked him onto the ground, stepping on his chest as if crushing an ant.

Kyle, feeling as if his bones had been removed, could only collapse under Alexander's foot, emitting cries of agony.

"Mr. Vanderbilt!"

"How can this be... Someone help Mr. Vanderbilt. Help! Help!"

The men in the room screamed in terror.

Kyle, who they had regarded as god-like, was now being trampled underfoot.

"Run! Run!" someone shouted.

They all turned and stumbled towards the exit.

However, with a flick of Alexander's hand, the Force knock on the door.

The door shut tightly. No matter how hard they pushed, the door wouldn't budge, as if welded shut.

"What... what do you want to do? We didn't kidnap your daughter. We're not part of the Red Umbrella Group."

"Please don't kill me. I have a wife and children. I just met Kyle by chance. We're not close..."

They crawled before Alexander, begging for mercy.

Alexander dared to commit violence in front of them and wouldn't let them leave, which meant he had no intention of sparing them.

Alexander snorted coldly, a hint of purple flashing in his eyes. His All-seeing Eyes had been activated, scanning the eyes of several people.

The next second, Kyle's cronies all became dazed, expressionlessly rigid, like wooden figures.

They fell to the ground one after another, frothing at the mouth, incontinent, and convulsing violently.

A cold smile played on Alexander's lips. He hadn't killed them but had affected their mental worlds with Force Control.

When they woke up again, they wouldn't remember what had just happened, and their intelligence would greatly regress, possibly even degenerating into imbecility.

These scumbags deserved such punishment!

"Don't pretend to be dead. Tell me. Where is my daughter? If you speak, maybe I can spare your life."

Alexander kicked Kyle.

Kyle vomited blood, grinning at Alexander maliciously.

"You'll never find your daughter, never... I won't tell you even if I die."

Kyle remained incredibly arrogant.

Alexander's anger erupted like a volcano. He stomped on Kyle's right hand.

With a series of crisp sounds, Kyle's shrill howl echoed in the room.

His right hand was completely ruined, without a single intact bone.

"Will you talk?"

Alexander stepped on Kyle's left hand.

With another round of cracking sounds, Kyle's left hand was also destroyed.

Though in unbearable pain, Kyle still said nothing. Instead, he revealed a ferocious and evil grin.

Seeing this, Alexander hesitated no more. He grabbed Kyle's head and twisted it in the opposite direction.

Kyle's head turned, facing backward.

The expression of pain and despair on his face seemed to solidify as he died, staring with lifeless grey eyes.

Having killed Kyle without obtaining any concrete information about Ashley, Alexander's anger intensified.

When he opened the door and stepped out, the corridor was packed with security personnel.

Dressed in matching black suits and wielding machetes, axes, pistols, and other weapons, they glared menacingly at Alexander.

With nowhere to vent his rage, Alexander bared a fierce grin at them.

He pressed the hilt, and his resplendent Lightsaber hissed to life.

The next moment, brilliant beams of light blossomed in the corridor.

Whether it was the sturdy machetes and axes or the fragile bodies, all were rent asunder by the Lightsaber.
