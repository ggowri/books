As the morning sun rose, a thunderous roar erupted across the island, echoing like a detonation.

A dozen agile figures charged toward the headquarters of the Red Umbrella Group, ruthlessly taking down its members in their path.

These were the elite fighters under Shadow, clad in combat armor, serving as the vanguard assault team.

The decisive battle was finally about to begin!

"Combat armor is truly extraordinary; each of them is stronger than I am!"

Rosalia exclaimed, her beautiful eyes wide with shock.

"Fortunately, this type of combat armor is still difficult to mass-produce; it's so rare that even the Red Umbrella Group doesn't possess any."

Charlize was also taken aback; the scene before her completely overturned her worldview.

If this combat armor became widely available, ordinary humans could leap into power.

Just imagine how formidable skilled fighters like them would become with this armor!

They could probably tear through a tiger with ease!

And he believed that with the ongoing advancements in technology, there would surely come a day when combat armor could be produced the masse.

Alexander chuckled to himself; not only was he unbothered by this basic combat armor, but even the more advanced suits from science fiction didn't intimidate him. He could cut through them effortlessly with a single swing of his sword.

He didn't rush into action but calmly observed the battlefield.

The dozen elite fighters in combat armor moved swiftly and struck hard.

However, with Alexander's All-seeing Eyes, he could effortlessly lock onto them, tracing their movements and identifying their weaknesses.

Immediately, dozens, even hundreds, of effective attack strategies flashed through his mind.

As Alexander grew stronger, his All-seeing Eyes operated like an omniscient system, adept at grasping the changes on the battlefield and making optimal decisions.

Even if the Red Umbrella Group had combat armor, Alexander wouldn't need to lift a finger. He could unleash the Force and, using the All-seeing Eyes and the Force, easily control the minds of the wearers.

However, even if Alexander could control the enemies, he couldn't seize the combat armor.

This high-tech product wasn't solely controlled by the wearer; it also responded to commands from the central control room.

So, even if Alexander could dominate the wearers, the central control room would trigger self-destruct mechanisms on the armor.

The vanguard team dispatched by Shadow, enhanced by their combat armor, was unstoppable, wreaking havoc everywhere.

"We're currently in a favorable position; the Red Umbrella Group hasn't made any significant moves yet. Let's flank them from another direction for a two-pronged attack."

Alexander initiated the pre-arranged battle plan as he led Rosalia and Charlize toward a nearby building.

That was the hideout of Augusta, William, and five hundred elite members of the Ultimate Alliance.

Originally a small force affiliated with the Red Umbrella Group, this location lay on the eastern side, close to the Group's stronghold.

However, the leader of this faction had already been subdued by Augusta, allowing them to remain undetected and poised for a surprise attack.

"Stop! Who are you?"

As Alexander, Rosalia, and Charlize reached the halfway point, they were suddenly confronted by members of the Red Umbrella Group, led by a man clad in a full black battle exoskeleton.

His exoskeleton was simpler and more rudimentary compared to the combat armor, clearly a notch below in technology.

However, it still significantly enhanced strength and speed.

Even ordinary fighters equipped with this low-tier exoskeleton could see a substantial boost in their combat effectiveness, allowing them to contend with more powerful foes.

At the forefront of the Red Umbrella Group was a burly, bald Black man, his body adorned with red tattoos, resembling a monstrous figure of red and black.

"Are you from the Brilliant Group? How dare you ambush us, the Red Umbrella Group! let's take them down!"

The bald man roared, charging forward with a menacing aura.

"Mr. Hawthorne, I'll handle him!"

Rosalia declared confidently, stepping forward to take on the challenge.

At this moment, not only the bald man but several other members of the Red Umbrella Group, fully armed, were rushing towards them, aiming to encircle Shadow's rear.

Among them were several skilled fighters equipped with low-level battle exoskeletons, each one significantly bolstered in strength.
