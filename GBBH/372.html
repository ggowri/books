Orion staggered as he got up, one side of his face swollen badly.

"Cough, cough..."

He spat out a mouthful of blood again.

"Are you all blind? What are you standing around for? Get him! Teach him a lesson!"

"Everyone, attack him! Make sure he's done for!"

Orion and Byron shouted their orders at nearly the same time.

Around thirty or forty men from the Clifford family and Orion's crew charged at Alexander like a pack of hyenas, ready to tear him apart.

"Pathetic!"

Alexander charged into the crowd like a tiger.

"Ah!"

"Crack!"

Screams and the sound of bones snapping filled the air as the men collapsed one after another without even grazing Alexander's clothes.

"Yes! Good job!"

"Teach these jerks a lesson!"

Members of the Hansen family on Rebecca's side clapped, cheering excitedly.

They had been at a disadvantage, watching Rebecca get mistreated, feeling as though the family's dignity had been trampled.

Now that Alexander was stepping in, they felt a sense of justice.

As an Intermediate Warrior with All-seeing Eyes, Alexander completely overpowered them, even with dozens on the opposing side.

In less than a minute, every one of them lay defeated on the ground, unable to get up.

Alexander stood proudly as if he were a Sovereign looking down on the world.

"What...what are you planning to do?"

Byron was completely rattled, seeing Alexander's overwhelming strength.

His men were supposed to be elite, each one able to take on several opponents with ease.

But against Alexander, they were pitifully weak, as easy to defeat as chickens.

Alexander snorted coldly and strode toward Byron.

"Stay back! Don't come closer! I... I'm the Clifford family's heir...my father is the head of the Clifford family...we're one of the four major families in Loscity...ah!"

Before Byron could finish, Alexander slapped him hard again, sending him stumbling to the ground.

"The Clifford family? That's nothing!"

Alexander sneered, lifting his foot and stepping down on Byron's right hand.

"This is the hand you used to touch Rebecca, isn't it?"

Alexander pressed his foot down harder.

"Crack! Crack!"

Everyone could hear the clear sound of bones breaking.

Byron let out a shrill scream as his right hand was crushed underfoot.

"And earlier, you deliberately crashed into my car, didn't you?"

Without hesitation, Alexander stomped on Byron's left hand, breaking it as well.

"Ah! My hands, my hands...you're dead...you're asking for it...I won't let you get away with this..."

Byron watched in horror as his hands were destroyed, pain and panic overtaking him until he passed out.

"Take him and get out!"

Alexander kicked Byron, sending him straight out of the Hansen Villa gates.

The Clifford family members stumbled after him, knowing they'd face severe punishment if Byron died.

"Orion, now it's your turn."

Alexander looked at Orion, his face expressionless.

Orion's whole body shook; he was nearly scared out of his mind.

The Hansen family members who had supported him scattered, quickly switching sides to stand with Rebecca.

Just then, Quentin emerged with Ralph, leading a large group of people out.

When Orion saw Ralph alive, his face went pale, and he turned to run.

Ralph shouted angrily, "Grab that beast. He's the one who poisoned me!"

Alexander caught up quickly, landing a hard kick on Orion's backside.

Orion fell headfirst, his face slamming into the ground, one side covered in blood.

"Still trying to run? Think you can escape?"

Alexander hauled him up and punched him in the stomach, nearly making him throw up.

Ralph came over, leaning on his cane, glaring at Orion.

"You are my blood, one of the Hansen family. Why did you poison me?"

Orion lifted his head, smiling with spite.

"Ha ha ha, if you thought of me as your grandson, why didn't you give me the family head position? Why give it to that bitch Rebecca..."

Ralph's face turned livid with rage as he raised his cane and struck him forcefully.

Orion yelped and fell flat on the ground again.

He remained defiant, shouting at the top of his lungs.

"Ralph, answer me! Why are you so unfair? Why shouldn't I inherit the Hansen family? How is Rebecca better than me?"
