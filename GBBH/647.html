"Wait a minute, I'll be there right away!" Alyssa hung up the phone, but her expression was as solemn as before.

"What happened?" Alice asked with concern.

Although they had only known each other for a couple of hours, Alice treated this nice lady as her friend.

"It's okay, just some trivial things..."

With a forced smile, Alyssa obviously didn't tell the truth.

However, those who knew him well knew that despite his pure appearance, his faith in God, and his amicability, anyone who really pissed him off would be torn apart. About thirty people were killed by him.

The anxiety made his gentle face look so grim.

Alyssa slammed the table in anger.

"How dare these bastards treat us as sheep!"

Everyone present had a very solemn expression. The Mafia alone was stronger than them.

Its allies were as powerful as the Axe and sometimes could even overshadow them.

Five oligarchs of the gem trade in Mandale Town were the Mafia, the Weaver family, Southern Mining, the Glory Association, and the Axe, which was the weakest.

Now that the Mafia had allied with the other three giants, plus some small gangs that would like to carve the Axe up, they were too vulnerable to their attacks. Everyone present was worrying about their doom.

"The Axe will tell the Mafia that we're wolves!" she roared.

It greatly boosted morale.

"If they want our lives and our money, let's see who is with more guts! They will come back with countless bodies of their people!"

At this moment, Stanley received a call.

"Oh, shit!" Stanley's face changed drastically.
