Alexander chuckled darkly.

"You want to steal my magic powder formula, take my woman, and take down the Saint Group. Don't you know who my father is?"

Eddie's face turned pale, pointing at Alexander in shock.

"Wait, you're... you're that old man's son?

"How dare you!"

Before Eddie could finish, a sharp dagger was pressed to his throat, just inches from piercing his skin.

The two women screamed, backing into a corner in terror.

No one else dared to move, fearing Alexander might kill Eddie with a single strike.

"Wh-what...what do you want?"

Eddie's eyes were wide with fear, and he was frozen in place, terrified of dying.

Alexander asked calmly, "Tell me, what's Satan's real name, and where is he?"

"You're trying to find Godfather? Heh..."

Eddie sneered, slowly regaining his composure.

After all, he was a seasoned boss, and the Cripples were a powerful force in the Underworld.

"Alexander, I'll admit I underestimated you. Joseph and Houtte must've died by your hand, right?"

"Enough talk!"

Alexander moved the dagger, slicing a deep cut on Eddie's throat, and blood began to flow.

Eddie screamed again, clutching his neck with a look of agony.

"Alexander, don't think I'll betray the Godfather. I'll never tell you, even if it kills me."

Alexander smiled broadly.

"Good, very good. For a gang leader, you're a loyal dog. In that case, I won't force you. I'll just send you to hell to meet Satan."

The sharp blade pressed against Eddie's throat, ready to end him.

The deadly killing intent made Eddie tremble uncontrollably. He wet himself in fear, and the sharp stench filled the air.

"Stop! Let go of our leader!"

Three bodyguards rushed at Alexander from different directions.

One swung a machete, aiming for Alexander's head.

The other two attacked from the sides, lunging at him like wolves, trying to pin him down.

Alexander didn't even blink, casually swinging his dagger.

"Slice!"

The dagger, glowing faintly with Force, sliced through the machete and then effortlessly slashed across the attacker's chest.

Without hesitation, Alexander swung the dagger back again.

A faint glow of the blade swept over the two attackers from behind, and they froze as if under a spell, motionless.

The next second, a thud sounded.

The three bodies dropped to the ground, cut cleanly into six pieces.

Blood gushed out like a spring, spreading a crimson bloom on the floor.

The two women screamed, clutching their heads before fainting in terror.

Eddie trembled uncontrollably.

"Let me go. I'll pay you. Name your price."

Just then, chaotic footsteps and shouting sounded from outside the hall.

Dozens of Cripples members swarmed in, armed with machetes, axes, and guns, surrounding Alexander with murderous intent.

Seeing his men finally arrive, Eddie was overjoyed.

His expression turned, and he threatened viciously, "Alexander, you're surrounded. I don't care how good you are; how many can you kill? You'll die by my hand eventually, so I'd advise you to behave!"

"Oh, you think you've got numbers? Why don't you look at who else I brought?"

Alexander gestured.

Another wave of rapid footsteps came from outside the mansion as a hundred men in black stormed in, instantly encircling Eddie's men.

Marvin and Logan led them, having rushed over at Alexander's command to surround the mansion.

Eddie and his men went pale.

Alexander sneered. "You dared to lay a hand on my woman. Just for that, you deserve to die a hundred times!"

A terrifying killing intent filled the air, making the room feel several degrees colder.

Eddie shivered uncontrollably, his face ashen.

"Alexander, the Cripples are under the protection of the Mafia. Our Godfather, Satan, is more powerful than ever. Don't dig your own grave!"
