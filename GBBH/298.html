"Let's go," Alexander reminded.

Melin quickly helped Frank towards the exit.

He was seriously injured and had a high fever.

He relied on Melin to support him. Although Melin's injury had healed, she was still a woman and her strength was limited, so they moved very slowly.

The group of thugs got restless again, surrounding them and looking for a chance to attack.

"If anyone comes near, he dies first!" Alexander threatened, holding Rosen in front of him.

Rosen desperately cried out, "Don't come near. If anyone comes, I'll kill them."

The thugs stopped in their tracks, glaring at Alexander.

Alexander smirked. If it weren't for worrying about Melin and Frank's safety, he would have sent all these thugs to hell.

Melin finally managed to help Frank into the police car. She opened the car door and waved frantically at Alexander.

"Get in the car!" she shouted.

Alexander dragged Rosen and quickly rushed towards the car.

As the thugs tried to chase them, Alexander suddenly turned back and swung Rosen around like a windmill, knocking the thugs down.

"Let's go!" Alexander laughed, throwing Rosen into the trunk and jumping into the car.

Melin stepped on the gas, and the car sped away.

Just then, a dart flew out from a nearby bush.

It hit Melin in the right chest.

Melin grunted in pain but managed to control the car and keep driving.

Soon, several SUVs chased after the police car.

A short while later, the police car stopped in a quiet grove, hiding from the pursuers.

Frank had fainted from his injuries and the shock.

Melin felt dizzy and weak, slumping in her seat and gasping for breath. Her face was flushed.

Alexander quickly used the Force to check Melin's condition.

She had been hit by a dart, and the dart was poisonous.

Alexander was surprised as Melin's current state was similar to when Betty was drugged.

"Officer, you've been poisoned. And this poison requires a man to treat," he said.

"Needs a man?" Melin was confused at first.

Then her face turned redder.

"I've already called for backup. The doctor and support will be here soon. I don't need your help. Let the doctor treat me," she said.

Even though she had a good impression of Alexander, she couldn't accept that kind of treatment.

Besides, the dart had hit her right chest, a very private area.

Even if she adored Alexander, she couldn't let him see it so casually.

Alexander sighed and smiled bitterly, "Do you think I want to help you? I have a girlfriend. The ambulance will take at least an hour to find us, and then it will take another hour to send you to the hospital for diagnosis.

"By the time the treatment starts, it will be two or three hours later. According to my judgment, you can't wait that long."

Melin was feeling stranger. Her body was burning hot, tingling, and very uncomfortable.

She couldn't help but moan, "Take off your mask. Let me see your face."

Alexander hesitated for a moment but still took off the silver mask.

Melin stared at Alexander's handsome face, and her heart beat faster.

"I didn't expect you to be so handsome," she said, feeling a warm current rising from her belly.

She felt itchy and couldn't help but moan.

"If I don't let you treat me, what will happen?" she asked.

Alexander thought for a moment and said, "It will damage your nervous system. You might become stupid or turn into someone who only wants men."

"What?" Melin was shocked.

Alexander explained, "Ordinary antidotes won't work for this poisoned dart. That's the purpose of making this special dart.

"And I think the person who made it has some perverted hobby, so they use this dart to harm women."
