Alexander swung the rifle off his back and opened fire on the ten red gorillas charging toward him.

With his enhanced senses, he didn't even need to aim. The bullets streaked out like lightning bolts.

Though they were just assault rifle rounds, his Force powers amplified them to devastating effect.

The rapid gunfire was punctuated by several agonized howls.

Alexander had already taken down multiple red gorillas with perfect headshots.

Their headless bodies lay scattered on the ground in pools of blood—a gruesome sight.

Alexander maintained a distance of about 250 feet from the gorillas, and their defenses were far weaker than the Mutated Monkey King's skull.

Before they could even reach him, he'd already eliminated more than half of them.

The combat abilities of an Advanced Warrior went far beyond what ordinary people could imagine.

Instead of meeting the charging gorillas head-on, Alexander kept backing away while picking them off—like a pro gamer kiting a boss in an MMO.

One by one, his bullets found their marks until all the remaining red gorillas lay dead.

The beast tide might have been fierce, but they were no match for Alexander.

After dispatching the group of red gorillas, he pushed deeper into the heart of the beast tide.

Any mutated beast that got too close to Alexander ended up dead, their bodies carved into pieces on the ground.

Their unnaturally tough bodies continued to twitch and struggle even after death.

Once Alexander had eliminated all dozen-plus red gorillas, the beast tide's momentum visibly weakened. A large portion of the mutated beasts began to move erratically as if they'd lost their coordination.

A rush of pure exhilaration surged through Alexander's body.

Just like sex, killing could bring its own kind of thrill, and Alexander was savoring every moment of it.

He knew why these dozens of red gorillas had come for him. The mysterious figure behind them was panicking, afraid Alexander would smoke them out of hiding.

Suddenly, the beast tide split into several groups.

The largest group continued charging toward Louvian Port, while another surrounded Alexander. The final group abruptly turned tail and fled.

They seemed to be protecting their mysterious leader, trying to get them safely away from the battlefield.

But Alexander wouldn't let them off that easy.

He smirked. Victory was within his grasp. All he had to do was chase down and expose this mystery person.

This would not only crush the beast tide but also blow the lid off the Sakra Society's entire conspiracy.

As the horde split up, the mutant beasts surrounding Alexander were clearly terrified. After watching him slaughter their companions, they just circled him nervously, too afraid to attack.

Those red gorillas' deaths were fresh in their minds, and fear had taken hold.

Alexander unleashed the Force, extending his Lightsaber to its full length. He picked up his killing pace and charged after the fleeing group.

Any mutant beast that dared to block his path met a swift end.

Some of the creatures had already started to flee.

The horde surrounding Alexander couldn't slow him down. He picked up speed like a hunter closing in on his prey, keeping them firmly in his sights.

Finally, Alexander caught up to them, cutting through the fleeing mass of beasts like a sharp sword. The mysterious figure at the center began to panic, and chaos spread through the entire horde.

A victorious smile crossed Alexander's face. His hunch had been right. Subduing that mysterious person would end this beast invasion once and for all.

Suddenly, his eyes lit up. With his incredible vision, he spotted ten red-furred gorillas huddled together, apparently shielding someone.

Alexander could just barely make out a figure in a black cloak—the mysterious person he'd been hunting.

The figure carried a dark, bulging pack on their back. Whatever was inside remained a secret.

Alexander wasn't about to let this troublemaker escape. Today, he would give it everything he had.

"You won't get away!"

Alexander let out a battle cry and charged forward, swinging his Lightsabers like spinning windmills.

His Lightsabers tore through everything in their path, leaving mutant beasts in pieces and rivers of blood in their wake.

The ten red gorillas suddenly scattered in all directions, abandoning their post.
