It was clear Nate had no idea that Ava was now the CEO of the Saint Group.

Ever since the Saint Group became the exclusive distributor of the magic powder, the company's valuation had shot up, surpassing 150 billion dollars, and it was still climbing.

As CEO, Ava's salary and dividends put her annual income in the hundreds of millions of dollars. Why on earth would she care about Nate's little offer?

Plus, Ava was proud and ambitious. Nate trying to buy her with money wasn't just insulting—it was downright degrading.

"Ugh!" Ava spat in disgust.

"A million dollars? Please, that's pocket change. You're like a moth thinking it could date a flame."

Nate's expression twisted further, his frustration evident.

Alexander decided to step in. "Alright, fine. Let's settle this. I'll play along so you get it once and for all."

"Good! I'll let you go first, so later you can't say I didn't give you a chance."

Nate's confidence was sky-high.

"Sure, I'll go first," Alexander grinned.

He started adding twenty-kilogram plates onto the barbell—five on each side. Including the original plates, it totaled four hundred kilograms.

Nate snorted in disbelief. "Seriously? You're trying to show off? Forget four hundred—can you even lift a hundred kilograms?"

What a joke. Not even Olympic champions had lifted three hundred kilograms before.

Ava stared at the barbell, her eyes wide—four hundred kilograms?!

Even though she believed in Alexander, that kind of weight was hard to imagine.

Alexander flashed Nate a smile.

"Watch closely. If I win, you disappear from our lives—forever."

The next second, he grabbed the barbell with one hand, smoothly lifting it above his head.

He did it effortlessly, not breaking a sweat or even breathing hard.

"How about that?"

Alexander stood there, one-handedly holding up the four-hundred-kilogram barbell, still able to talk with ease.

Nate stood frozen, jaw hanging open, wondering if he was dreaming.

The current world record for weightlifting was around two hundred seventy or eighty kilograms.

But Alexander effortlessly lifted four hundred kilograms with just one hand—a truly staggering display of strength.

If he were to use both hands, he could likely lift eight hundred kilograms, leaving the current world record in the dust.

"Is that enough for you?"

Alexander set the barbell down without effort as if it weighed nothing.

Nate couldn't help but stammer, "Are... are you even human?"

Alexander shot him a cold glare.

"Less talking. I'm done. Your turn."

Nate's face went pale, his eyes darting around as he avoided Alexander's stare.

He never thought he'd be humiliated in what he thought was his specialty and in such a decisive way.

"Nate, what do you think of my boyfriend's strength? You'll never be on his level, so do us both a favor and stay away from me."

Ava's voice was full of disdain, her gaze cutting into Nate.

Nate's face flushed red, his eyes filled with frustration, but he couldn't argue.

Forget one-handed four hundred kilograms—he could barely lift two hundred with both hands.

"Fine, you win."

Nate shot Alexander a hateful look, then turned and stormed off.

Watching Nate leave, Alexander chuckled softly.

What could Nate do? Everything came down to raw power.

"I just helped you get rid of a pretty big annoyance. Don't I deserve a thank you?"

Alexander teased, a playful grin on his face.

"Ugh, you're such a pain! I'm all sweaty—let's go shower first."

Ava pouted, pressing her full figure against Alexander's chest.

Her chest bounced slightly as if eager to break free from the tight yoga outfit.

Desire flared within Alexander, and his All-seeing Eyes allowed him to see through Ava's clothes, taking in every inch of her smooth, soft skin.

Ava could feel the heat of Alexander's gaze, her body shivering, legs almost giving out beneath her.

Without a second thought, Alexander scooped Ava up and carried her toward their suite.

Along the way, they drew plenty of stares.

Especially Ava—her stunning figure, full chest, slim waist, rounded hips, and long, toned legs had more than a few men staring in awe.

The moment they entered the room, Alexander locked the door and hung the "Do Not Disturb" sign.

Ava casually slipped out of her yoga clothes.
