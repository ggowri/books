"Ah!"

Windwolf screamed in agony as Alexander grabbed his arms and snapped them with a twist.

In a blur of movement, Alexander's fingers locked onto the throats of Windwolf's two companions like an eagle's claws.

Two sounds of breaking necks echoed simultaneously. The two metahumans crumpled to the ground, their lifeless bodies slumping like discarded bags.

Windwolf roared in fury, still unwilling to accept defeat. He kicked toward Alexander, forcing himself through the pain.

But Alexander was faster. The moment Windwolf's foot moved, Alexander grabbed his leg, twisting it with a brutal grip. Then, he hammered his fist into Windwolf's face.

With a shrill scream, Windwolf fell to the ground, half his face swollen, his hands twisted and broken. He howled in agony.

Alexander placed his foot on Windwolf's chest, his voice cold and merciless. "To me, taking your life is no greater task than squashing an ant."

Everyone froze, a chill running down their spine. Alexander's overwhelming aura paralyzed them with fear, making them unable to move.

Asher's gaze was fixed on Windwolf, who was still writhing beneath Alexander's boot, blood pouring from his mouth.

His face was full of disbelief and shock.

He was shocked that the three metahumans had teamed up, and yet they were easily defeated in an instant.

Asher was drenched in cold sweat, his clothes soaked through.

He had always prided himself on being the top young genius, but now, he realized he was nothing compared to Alexander.

He had known Alexander was strong, but he had always believed that he could defeat him. He'd never taken Alexander seriously.

Now, he realized how wrong he had been.

Alexander was far stronger than any of the intel had suggested. He was at a level of power that was almost terrifying.

Windwolf and his companions had failed, and now an even greater number of members of the Ultimate Alliance were surrounding them.

These people were nothing like Betty's subordinates. Each of them radiated a chilling and murderous aura, and none of them were any weaker than Windwolf and his crew.

They stood there, their chilling and murderous glares focused on Asher. Just their presence was enough to send waves of fear through him.

"It's over! We're surrounded!"

Asher's face turned ashen, his eyes wide with terror.

He wanted to take down Alexander, but he had no confidence left.

Asher swallowed hard, his legs trembling uncontrollably. His eyes filled with pure and unmasked fear as he looked at Alexander.

Betty knew Alexander was strong, but she hadn't seen him fight in a long time.

The average members of the Ultimate Alliance had never witnessed his true power.

What had just happened was beyond their wildest imagination. They didn't expect that Alexander had become a force so terrifying.

The three metahumans had been taken down in the blink of an eye.

"Alexander, dare you to kill members of the Vance family?"

Asher screamed, though he tried to be brave, his voice full of desperation.

He wasn't begging for mercy. He knew this was a fight to the death, and if he was going to survive, he had no choice but to gamble everything.

He yelled at his subordinates, "Go! Kill them all! I'll reward 200 thousand dollars for a kill!"

"Kill Alexander, and you'll get 100 million dollars. Plus, you'll become a senior manager in the Vance family. You'll enjoy the family's annual dividends!"

Asher wasn't just a tough character. He knew how to use money and power to his advantage. At a moment like this, he didn't hesitate to offer his subordinates hefty rewards.

For money and power, people would do anything.

The atmosphere instantly became charged. His previously deflated subordinates, fueled by greed, suddenly came alive.

Now, they only had one goal, which was to kill for money and maybe rise to the top of the Vance family.

They were all ready to gamble with their lives.

"I'm going to kill him. Don't you dare get in my way!"

One of the men charged forward, exploding with fury.

The group howled like wolves, some readying their guns, others brandishing knives, charging wildly at Alexander.

Gunfire erupted, the flames from the muzzles lighting up the scene!

Blades gleamed in the air!

All the Vance family's attacks were now focused solely on Alexander.

These men, carefully picked by Asher, were elite warriors. Although they didn't have metahuman abilities, their skills were top-notch. They had killed someone before and weren't afraid of violence.

Blinded by greed, they attacked like wild animals, their ferocity terrifying.
