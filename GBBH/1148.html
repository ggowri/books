"Come, everyone, let's take a seat and enjoy the feast!" Karson said.

After a few drinks, the previously serious mood lightened considerably, thanks to the alcohol.

Within hundreds of miles of the castle, a number of elite mercenaries were on duty, fully armed and alert to their surroundings.

The castle itself was equipped with hundreds of surveillance cameras, all active and on high alert. Even a bird wouldn't slip past unnoticed.

The five of them gathered around, eating and chatting, while Alexander remained quiet, savoring his food.

Karson and Cesar discussed pressing news, particularly their interest in Sakra Society's Immortality Serum.

Alexander sighed inwardly upon hearing this. If even these individuals were fixated on immortality, he could only imagine how much more the truly powerful and wealthy craved it.

This only solidified Alexander's resolve to accelerate the development of Treasure Island and Devil Island, turning both into doomsday strongholds.

Before long, Yinson would bring catastrophe to the world.

The camaraderie among Karson, Cesar, Dariel, and Travis grew, showing no signs that they were preparing for an imminent threat.

Alexander, however, remained relaxed, not taking Stephen seriously at all.

Karson, Cesar, and the others stayed vigilant, their senses heightened as they discussed and drank, always aware of their surroundings, like springs coiled tight, ready to spring into action at a moment's notice.

Suddenly, a faint howl echoed outside.

Karson immediately stood up, his expression turning grave as he peered outside.

A guard rushed in panic etched across his blood-soaked face. "Mr. Chapman, bad news!"

He barely got the words out before collapsing to the ground and passing out.

Everyone caught a whiff of the strong blood odor wafting from behind him.

"Who did this?" Cesar shouted angrily, turning toward the door.

In unison, they all turned to see a dark figure materialize silently in the entryway.

Karson's voice turned icy. "Stephen!"

Stephen came, clad in a black robe, two sabers sheathed in his belt. Though the sabers were not drawn, they radiated a deadly aura.

"Mr. Chapman, you were bold enough to make me make this trip." Stephen looked at Karson with a smile, then glanced at Cesar and the others.

With a sneer, he taunted Karson. "Are these the so-called allies you've gathered? What a shame. All those guards outside are dead."

Karson's face paled. He had deployed dozens of mercenaries to secure the perimeter, never anticipating they'd all be wiped out so easily by Stephen.

It was only when Stephen made his entrance that Karson realized just how much stronger Stephen was than he had imagined.

Cesar, Dariel, and Travis immediately stood, glaring at Stephen.

Their powerful energies coalesced, aimed directly at him.

Only Alexander remained unperturbed, continuing to enjoy his meal.

Using his Force Sense, he had gauged Stephen's true strength, which was on par with Felix's.

But unfortunately, it was nothing in Alexander's eyes.

In the past, Alexander had been an Intermediate Warrior and managed to defeat Felix; now, as an Advanced Warrior, the situation was even more in his favor.

Yet, even if Cesar, Dariel, and Travis combined their strengths, they would still find themselves outmatched by Stephen. Without Alexander's intervention, they would definitely be killed.

If Alexander decided to take Stephen down without using his Lightsabers, they might have to trade blows for some time.

But if Alexander unleashed his full power, Stephen wouldn't stand a chance.

"Mr. Chapman, surrender control of Sunrise Group to Sakra Society and join us. I might still spare your life," Stephen said arrogantly.

Karson's anger flared. "Forget Sakra Society! You're all just a bunch of losers. If you want my company, come and take it!" he yelled.

With the backing of three experts, Karson felt confident in victory.

"Are you serious? This is a world, where strength reigns supreme. You think these so-called allies of yours are worth anything?" Stephen retorted, pointing derisively at Cesar, Dariel, and Travis.
