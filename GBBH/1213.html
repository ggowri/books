Bullets carved graceful arcs through the air, knocking down target after target.

Many spectators held their breath, watching Ilona's performance with awe. Some even wondered silently, "If I am facing her, how many shots will it take for her to bring me down?"

However, her firearm only held a little over twenty bullets. Yet, the digital screen showed her score already surpassing 300 points—which meant Ilona had hit 30 targets.

As more targets appeared, her gun should have been out of bullets. But strangely enough, she was still taking down targets.

Ilona wasn't firing anymore. Instead, she extended her fingers, controlling the bullets she had already shot.

One by one, the bullets danced through the air, piercing targets under her command.

"320 points!

"350 points!

"380 points!

"400 points..."

The bullets moved like living, graceful sprites, whirling and circling endlessly.

Finally, the two-minute shooting time ended. Ilona finished with an astonishing 460 points.

The crowd fell silent, awestruck by Ilona's terrifying mastery over firearms.

Especially during the final moments, her ability to control dozens of bullets at intricate angles left everyone convinced they wouldn't stand a chance against her.

"Awesome! You're incredible, Ms. Cox!"

"You're the best, Ms. Cox!"

Gael was beaming with excitement, clapping enthusiastically.

Ilona turned to Alexander, a teasing smile playing on her lips.

"Well? Are you going to admit defeat now, or do you want to challenge me?

"If you surrender now, out of respect for General Thompson, I'll let it slide. Just admit you were wrong and apologize."

Alexander's lips curled into a cold smirk.

"You think you've won? I haven't even started yet. Honestly, you and Gael are about the same—both have a screw loose."

Alexander tapped his temple mockingly.

Ilona's face turned pale with anger as she clenched her fists tightly, itching to teach Alexander a lesson.

Alexander ignored her, picked up the gun, and fired without even bothering to aim.

"20 points!"

His first shot was no ordinary one. The bullet, wrapped in the Force, demonstrated extraordinary flight capability and hit two targets at once.

The bullet didn't stop there—it continued toward another target.

Alexander fired a second shot, and soon the second bullet joined in the hunt for the targets.

When Ilona had started controlling bullets, she wasn't nearly as fluid as Alexander, nor had she used such powerful abilities from the beginning.

Alexander controlled the gun and bullets as if they were extensions of his body.

Bang! Bang! Bang!

Alexander fired off all his bullets in quick succession.

Everyone stared wide-eyed, dumbfounded, unsure of what Alexander was trying to do.

Moments later, over twenty bullets began to whirl and dance around the target positions.

Every target that appeared was immediately taken down by the bullets.

"100 points!

"110 points!

"150 points!"

His score skyrocketed. Each bullet moved with agile and unpredictable trajectories, like busy little bees, taking down every target no matter how many appeared.

The targets were released automatically, and the faster they were hit, the quicker new ones appeared.

"330 points!

"380 points!

"400 points!"

In less than a minute, Alexander had already taken down 40 targets, and his pace was still accelerating.

Everyone was stunned, completely dumbfounded. Could it be that Alexander also had advanced firearm power?

Brittany couldn't help but gasp, her beautiful eyes sparkling with delight.

Quentin trembled with excitement, clenching his fists just like many of the team members, his expression brimming with joy.

Gael, however, was completely floored. He now realized just how massive the gap between him and Alexander was—killing him would be as easy for Alexander as eating or drinking.

"Phew!"

Ilona took several deep breaths, barely managing to steady her trembling body.

As someone with level-four firearm power, she knew that to perform at Alexander's level required at least level-five abilities, possibly level six or higher.

Alexander had appeared completely at ease, clearly not even using his full power, while she had exerted herself to her absolute limit.

By the end, she had nearly depleted all her internal energy, with sweat covering her palms and soles.
