It was as if it was not a solid fortress but a piece of delicious fat meat.

This fortress was the command center of the Jedi City. After thousands of years of operation, it had robust defenses.

The walls are covered with Force cannons and various defensive weapons.

The sky was gray, and the remaining two thousand people in the Jedi City were there.

The armors were covered with dents and cracks and stained with blood, but they were still strong.

The light of the Lightsabers had dimmed, but they were still extremely sharp.

The only worry was these last two thousand Warriors. The worst of them were Advanced, and most of them were Experts.

They were exhausted, but the Warriors would never lower their proud heads.

Everyone had a blank expression on their face, and no one backed down,

They gripped their Lightsabers tightly, and their angry, cold, and slightly sad eyes stared at the terrifying orcs that were approaching and spreading all over the mountains.

These orcs had ferocious faces, were over nine feet tall, and had immense strength, and the animal skins covering their bodies were comparable to the hardest armor.

The most terrifying was that their pain sensitivity was dull, so they were not afraid of death.

The million-strong orc army had lost more than 200,000 of them, which was paid for by the lives of 8,000 Warriors.

Outside the fortress, the orcs were shouting wildly, but on the top of the city, there was total silence!

The oppressive atmosphere of the approaching decisive battle was like the dark clouds in the sky.

Alexander suddenly found himself standing on the wall, tightly gripping the atomic crossbow in his hand, his handsome face still somewhat childish.

Sharp eyebrows, bright eyes, a high nose bridge, and angular lips, this was himself in his dreams.

He was the son of the city lord, Edgar Lester. He just turned eighteen this year and this was his first time to go to the battlefield.

Edgar had a nervous expression on his face. There were too many orcs, and they were everywhere in the mountains.

They held terrifying maces, giant axes, and machetes in their hands and rode various ferocious beasts such as war wolves, porcupines, crocodiles, etc.

"Don't be afraid, son. You are my descendant, and you have noble blood flowing in your body!"

Marcus patted his youngest son, his white hair and beard fluttering in the wind.

"Don't worry, Dad. There are only Warriors who die standing, and no losers who live on their knees!"

Edgar's young face was full of determination.

"Good boy!"

Marcus praised his son and his eyes became moist.

They stared at the orcs, who were getting closer and closer to the fortress, their eyes full of contempt.

Edgar was a little anxious. He could already see the ugly faces of the orcs and wondered why his father hadn't given the order to fire.

He didn't know if he could still survive. Freya was looking forward to his return...

Freya was so beautiful... He wondered if he could see her again.

Edgar was thinking wildly in his mind, and a happy smile couldn't help but appear on the corner of his mouth.

"Load!"

Suddenly, he heard a roar and woke up immediately.

He quickly and skillfully loaded an arrow into the atomic crossbow. When the arrow was loaded, it immediately emitted a dazzling light.

Edgar raised the crossbow with both hands and aimed at the orc in front.

Marcus was not panicked at all. He raised his arm and ordered in a loud voice, "Hold on! Hold on! Fire!"

Thousands of arrows flew out in response, tearing through the air and then plummeting down like a storm.

At the same time, hundreds of Force cannons spewed out beams of light, which fell among the orcs and created balls of light that shot up into the sky.

Miserable screams came one after another from the orcs, and the light directly vaporized more orcs.

"Projectile!"

"Free Fire!"

Marcus commanded the battle calmly.

Edgar secretly glanced at his father. His wrinkled face now seemed a dozen years younger, and his eyes were full of murderous intent.

Edgar was already very familiar with the atomic crossbow in his hand. Arrows of light whizzed out and pierced through the bodies of the orcs one by one.

Edgar was particularly delighted to see these beasts falling one by one.

This arrow was to avenge his elder brother!

The second was to avenge his second sister, who cared about him the most!

The third was for his uncle!

The fourth arrow was for every friend who died in battle!

The fifth was for the glory of the Warriors...

Edgar's face turned pale due to excessive use of the atomic crossbow in a short period, which led to excessive use of the Force.
