Meanwhile, Shadow felt completely at ease.

Under Alexander's direct command, the team had fought their way to the central elevator shaft.

The elevator went down more than ten floors underground. Instead of taking it, Alexander's team had to clear each floor using the stairwell.

Each level was guarded by the Red Umbrella Group's elite forces, but these small fries weren't worth Alexander's effort. Shadow and Rosalia made quick work of them.

The slaughter continued as they descended. The stairwell transformed into a bloody path to hell, littered with bodies sprawled in every direction, blood flowing freely.

Alexander soon reached the lowest level.

The Red Umbrella Group's control room was located here. It was their most crucial facility.

BOOM!

Alexander's sword sliced through the control room's blast-proof door.

The reinforced door, designed to withstand rocket attacks, was cut through like butter.

The entire floor began to shake as an eerie, supernatural presence filled the underground space from above.

Alexander's expression turned grave. The evil force was about to descend.

"Kill them!" Shadow yelled, leading the charge into the control room.

Over a hundred members of the Red Umbrella Group screamed and scattered in panic.

Some tried to draw their weapons but were cut down before they could even pull the trigger.

Strangely, Felix and Rock were nowhere to be found.

With a wave of his hand, Alexander signaled his men to storm in and round up everyone from the Red Umbrella Group.

"You've got nowhere to run now!"

Shadow ordered his men to bring a large package of remote-controlled explosives, which they placed strategically around the area.

The combined blast would be powerful enough to send the Red Umbrella Group's headquarters sky-high.

Whatever Felix and Rock were up to, they'd be nothing but ashes after an explosion of this magnitude.

The ground trembled with increasing intensity, making the entire control room shake.

Alexander gestured to his men. "Everyone clear out now. We detonate in five minutes!"

"Kill them!"

A rage-filled battle cry suddenly pierced the air.

Hana appeared, half his body covered in blood, swinging a broken katana as he lunged viciously at Alexander.

Alexander dodged effortlessly and asked in an icy tone, "Where's Felix?"

Hana's teeth ground together audibly as he snarled, "I'll kill you all! One's worth my trouble, two's a bonus!"

He swung his blade again, launching another desperate attack at Alexander.

"You're asking for it!"

Alexander didn't even bother using his Lightsabers. Instead, he struck out with his bare fist.

His fist collided violently with Hana's broken katana.

The blade shattered into countless pieces. The explosive force sent the fragments flying back into Hana's body, instantly riddling him with dozens of bleeding wounds.

Despair and agony twisted Hana's features. He'd retreated underground after being wounded by Augusta, only to end up even worse off.

"Fuck!" he roared furiously, lunging at Alexander again like a rabid monster.

Alexander, tired of the confrontation, struck Hana across the face with devastating force.

A scream pierced the air as Hana flew backward, coughing up blood before crashing to the ground, motionless.

A close inspection would reveal Hana's completely shattered jaw and caved-in face. He was dead.

No one would believe that a top-tier assassin from the Risun Sakra Society could be killed with just one slap from Alexander.

Alexander gestured sharply. "Everyone out, now!"

Shadow, Rosalia, and Charlize didn't dare argue, rushing toward the stairwell.

Halfway up, a dark figure suddenly blocked their path.

"Rock!" Shadow gasped, shocked to find their escape route ambushed.

"Well, well, well. Been waiting for you folks," Rock sneered. "None of you are leaving. You'll all become sacrifices for the Great Being.

"You thought destroying the outer array would stop us? Dead wrong!"

Rock let out a sinister laugh as he opened the Ancient Grimoire, chanting under his breath.

The control room rumbled as a massive pentagram materialized, blazing with light and flames.

Felix descended from the ceiling, landing in the center of the array, fixing Alexander with a murderous glare.

Shadow immediately pulled out the detonator.

"Move it, old man, or we all go up in flames together."
