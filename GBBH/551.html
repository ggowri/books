Following Clay's shout, the dragon head stick began to absorb the black energy from Elaina's chest.

As it surged, her eyelids fluttered a few times, as if they were about to open.

Orlando shook with excitement.

"She's saved. Elaina's saved."

Everyone's eyes widened in astonishment and admiration for Clay.

Alexander remained composed and waited for the result.

Elaina let out a long breath. Though unconscious, she obviously had normal reactions.

Moreover, her pale face became much rosier. Orlando finally breathed a sigh of relief.

Clay smiled triumphantly and withdrew the dragon head stick.

"I have almost cured Ms. Elaina Gomez. Let her get more sleep to recover her strength. She will be back to normal soon."

Overjoyed, Orlando wiped his tears vigorously. "Thank you, Mr. Burch. Thank you, Mr. Burch!"

Clay glanced at Alexander icily.

"I have cured Ms. Elaina Gomez. Apologize to me!"

Alexander didn't obey at all.

Orlando immediately ordered, "Come here, make him apologize to Mr. Burch."

The bodyguards swarmed forward and were about to attack Alexander menacingly. Suddenly, a terrifying aura appeared, trapping them all and making them unable to move.

Alexander asked calmly, "Clay, did you really cure Ms. Elaina Gomez?"

"The facts are right in front of you. Why don't you admit it?"

Clay was full of confidence.

Suddenly, a doctor exclaimed, "Oh no!"

The ECG monitor began to beep, and Elaina's blood pressure plummeted.

"It's terrible. Ms. Elaina Gomez's heart rate and blood pressure are dropping rapidly!"

"Look, Ms. Elaina Gomez is covered in that black energy..."

Everyone was stunned, gazing at Elaina.

Elaina stiffened, the dark energy covering her face, and she began to convulse.

The most terrifying thing was her face. It had been rosy just now, but now, it was getting darker and darker. In just a few seconds, it was as black as charcoal. It was very scary.

"Elaina!"

Orlando suddenly came back to his senses and rushed over to hug Elaina tightly like a madman.

"Why is it so cold? Oh no! Why is her body so cold?"

Elaina's body was as cold as a dead person's.

"Impossible! Wasn't she healed just now?"

Clay panicked and quickly checked the dragon head stick.

"Someone is playing tricks! Someone must be playing tricks!"

His eyes were wide open as Clay glared at everyone present, but he still didn't take Alexander seriously.

At that moment, the ECG monitor emitted a long, shrill sound, and the ECG graph unexpectedly changed from a faint curve to a straight line.

"Ms. Elaina Gomez is dead!?"

The sudden and drastic change shocked everyone.

"Elaina, you can't die! You can't die!"

Orlando cried in despair and hugged Elaina tightly.

He lost his only child and felt like the end of the world was coming.

"Let me check on her again. It's absolutely impossible..."

Clay hurried over and examined Elaina carefully.

He sensed the black energy emanating from her body. It was clearly the witchcraft he had cast. Why couldn't he get it back with the dragon head stick?

Clay muttered to himself in horror, "How could this happen? It's absolutely impossible. Impossible..."

Orlando grabbed him tightly and asked angrily, "Clay, what happened? Why did my daughter die?"

"Mr. Gomez, this is impossible. Ms. Elaina Gomez will definitely not die. I clearly cured her just now," Clay stuttered, at a loss.

"How ridiculous!"

At that moment, a contemptuous voice rang out. Although it was not loud, everyone heard it.

They pivoted around and saw that it was Alexander.

He mocked mercilessly, "Clay, you caused Ms. Elaina Gomez's death, but you still claimed it was impossible. You are just a loser!"

Clay was furious. "Shut up! You have no right to speak here!"

Alexander sneered, "You killed Ms. Elaina Gomez, and you stopped me from mentioning it. However, facts speak louder than words. You ended Ms. Elaina Gomez's life with your actions."

"What a damn future star! What a disgrace!"

Alexander's sharp words irritated Clay so much that he screamed.
