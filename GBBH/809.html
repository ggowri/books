Aidan's chest was simultaneously pierced and charred.

After letting out a short, piercing scream, Aidan fell heavily to the ground.

He never would have thought that Alexander would suddenly appear in front of him, and even the hardest shield in the world could not withstand a single strike from him.

After Alexander killed Aidan with one sword, he sneered and vanished into the darkness.

"Asshole, you dare to kill my brother!" Casper roared in anger, putting all his strength into the fight.

There was a series of clatters from his combat armor, and four jets of flame spewed from the energy pack on his back, suddenly increasing Casper's speed by several times.

"Go to hell!" Casper, like a bolt of lightning, caught up behind Alexander, raised his battle knife, and roared as he fiercely slashed at Alexander's back.

The material of this battle knife was not only special metal, but the blade also incorporated the most advanced technology, inlaid with countless tiny diamond particles, giving it a powerful cutting ability.

Even light armor could be penetrated by this battle knife.

"Where is he?" Casper's strike landed on nothing; just a moment ago, he had clearly seen Alexander right in front of him, but in an instant, Alexander vanished.

Holding the knife with both hands, he panted heavily while cautiously searching all around for any trace of Alexander.

"Casper, watch out!" suddenly, Kermit shouted and forcefully pushed Casper away.

An incredibly dashing man was descending from the sky.

Alexander leaped high into the air silently; due to his incredible speed, no one noticed.

He seemed like a deity descending from the heavens, thrusting his sword down from mid-air.

"Kill him!" Kermit roared in anger, brandishing his battle blade that could cut through iron like butter. The sharp edge, embedded with countless diamond particles, reflected the cold moonlight.

At the same time, the combat armor spewed out flames once again, causing Kermit's speed to increase dramatically.

Kermit's strike instantly became several times faster, the sharp blade thrusting toward Alexander's abdomen.

Alexander, his entire body in mid-air and unable to dodge, seemed as if he would be pierced by Kermit in the next second.

At the critical moment, Alexander smiled and said, "Not bad. I didn't expect the Dragon Gang to have such advanced combat armor that can significantly increase speed. It's a pity you met me."

Then, he utilized the Force, performing the Force Confinement.

Ever since Alexander acquired the ability to see through objects and reached the peak of an Intermediate Warrior, he hadn't used this move for a very long time.

At this moment, the entire world seemed to have stopped, and even time itself had come to a standstill.

To Alexander, everyone's movements became slower than snails. The expressions on their faces, whether fierceness, excitement, thrill, or fear, were all so vivid and clear.

Only Kermit's speed was a bit faster than the others, but only by a tiny bit.

If others were snails, he was a turtle.

In his eyes, everyone's movements had slowed to the extreme.

Alexander instantly spotted several flaws in Kermit's attacks. Like a ghost, he appeared behind Kermit and, with one sword strike, decapitated him.

The world instantly returned to normal. Kermit's head flew into the air, his face contorted with a grimace, while his body and sword crashed heavily to the ground.

"Kermit!" Casper, seeing his brother's tragic death, instantly teared up.

They were the three major leaders of the Dragon Gang and also brothers. In the blink of an eye, only he was left, all alone and pitiful.

Even though they were Risun warriors, possessing firearm power and wearing combat armor, two of them were quickly killed by Alexander in a three-against-one situation.

"I must avenge them!" Casper gritted his teeth, wanting to tear him apart.

He lifted the machine gun with his left hand, gripped the battle knife with his right, and unexpectedly turned around, charging towards Augusta and Marvin.

Subsequently, Casper opened fire fiercely.

Augusta and Marvin reacted quickly and dodged.

The others fell to the ground in unison, rolling continuously to avoid the dense hail of bullets.

However, in the face of Casper wielding firearm power, some were a bit too slow, and several fell into pools of blood.

"I'll slaughter you!" Casper laughed maniacally and continued to fire fiercely.

He knew he couldn't beat Alexander, so he could only kill the others to vent his hatred.
