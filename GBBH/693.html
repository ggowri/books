The speed wasn't slow at all. In fact, it was so blindingly fast that Rosalia's movements left afterimages in the air, creating an illusion of slow motion.

Two swift slashes at the same spot, and Rosalia's blade tore through the combat exoskeleton.

The opponent's body split in two along with the exoskeleton, crashing heavily to the ground.

The exoskeleton's power cell ruptured, sparking and igniting in a massive explosion.

The other two experts were engulfed in the fiery blast, their combat exoskeletons exploding in a chain reaction.

A massive mushroom cloud erupted at the scene, sending devastating shockwaves in all directions.

The Red Umbrella Group members were blown to bits, scattering flesh and blood everywhere.

Like a ghost, Rosalia swiftly took cover behind a boulder.

Her face was pale, her body trembling uncontrollably. Those few strikes had clearly drained most of her energy.

Yet Rosalia felt incredibly refreshed, as if every cell in her body had been supercharged.

She had always been a martial arts expert, and Alexander's magic circle had helped unlock her potential. Now, she had broken through to a level surpassing even level-three metahuman.

Just how many tiers she could go toe to toe with would need to be tested in actual combat.

Just then, two survivors from the Red Umbrella Group emerged from the edge of the blast zone and took off running.

Though they had barely escaped death, the explosion's aftermath had left them covered in soot, their clothes in tatters, and bleeding from various wounds.

"Trying to run?"

Two flashes of razor-sharp light pierced through their hearts with deadly precision.

"Heh."

Rosalia's beautiful eyes glinted with murderous intent as she sneered.

Alexander approached, clapping his hands with a smile.

"Less than two minutes. Impressive. I'm pleased."

"It's all thanks to your training, Sir!"

Rosalia and Charlize bowed their heads respectfully.

Alexander smiled. "Come, let's join Augusta."

Several minutes later, Alexander arrived with Rosalia and Charlize at Augusta's position.

The buildings, just over half a mile from the Red Umbrella Group's plaza, had been converted into forward operating bases.

Five hundred elite Ultimate Alliance fighters stood ready for battle. At the same time, Cloe commanded over a thousand Brilliant Group members to form a perimeter, ensuring no Red Umbrella Group members could escape after their defeat.

Not only had Augusta awakened her werewolf bloodline, but William had also undergone a massive transformation.

Through dedicated practice of Alexander's meditation techniques, he had awakened his vampire bloodline.

While William couldn't match Augusta in raw combat power, he possessed the ability to control thousands of vampire bats simultaneously.

These bats had been hiding in the island's dense jungles and dark underground caverns until William summoned them, forming a terrifying army of winged creatures.

As soon as Alexander appeared, Augusta and William rushed out with their subordinates to greet him.

Rosalia and Charlize looked at them in surprise, noticing that Augusta and William's auras were just as powerful as their own.

William squinted, sneaking a few glances at Rosalia and Charlize before quickly lowering his head, not daring to look longer.

Ever since awakening his vampire bloodline, his resistance to beautiful women drastically decreased, and he found himself having inappropriate thoughts about Rosalia and Charlize.

But the moment he remembered they were Alexander's women, he straightened up and kept his eyes down.

Alexander gave a faint smile, fully aware of William's thoughts.

William was already under Force Control, with a trace of the Force lurking in his mind. All Alexander had to do was think about it, and William would be in agony.

So, no matter how strong William became, he wouldn't dare betray Alexander. Betrayal would only lead to his doom.

"Here, I brought you some protective talismans. They'll shield you from magical damage for a while," Alexander said, pulling out several jewel talismans.

"Thank you, Sir!"

Augusta and William exclaimed excitedly, taking the jeweled necklaces from Alexander's hand and carefully putting them on.

Alexander then used his All-seeing Eyes to examine William, discovering a crimson energy inside his body, redder than blood itself. It must be William's awakened vampire bloodline.
