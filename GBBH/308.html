Alexander's instincts told him that these two opponents were formidable, and their teamwork was almost flawless.

Just as he began to retreat, they suddenly accelerated, appearing right where he had just stood.

A flurry of blinding blade flashes sliced through the air, barely missing him.

The speed of both sides was astounding, and in the blink of an eye, they had already exchanged a move.

"Wow!"

The two Shadow Warriors were astonished because they were the strongest in the team.

They had thought that with their combined attack, Alexander would have no chance of evading, but he had easily sidestepped it.

They felt a growing sense of urgency as his retreating speed was impressively quick, almost as if they couldn't keep up.

The expressions of the other Shadow Warriors shifted as well. Despite being caught off guard, Alexander managed to dodge their strikes with ease.

If it had been any of them, they would have already met their end.

The two leading Shadow Warriors momentarily faltered, but their resolve quickly returned as they pursued Alexander.

The glint of their Kukri daggers flashed as they closed in on him again.

Yet, Alexander stood his ground, a smirk of contempt appearing on his lips. Clearly, he wasn't planning to retreat any further.

"You're asking for trouble!"

The two Shadow Warriors felt a surge of confidence, convinced that Alexander couldn't evade them this time. They could almost visualize him being torn apart by their blades.

But as their sinister smiles appeared, their expressions changed drastically.

"Back away! Now!"

They shouted in unison, an instinct honed through life-and-death encounters.

But it was too late. Their attacks had been fully committed, leaving no room for retreat.

Suddenly, Alexander crouched low and struck at the gap beneath them with a fierce sweep of his leg.

The force erupted, making his leg feel harder than steel.

Crack!

In an instant, two sharp sounds echoed.

The Shadow Warriors' calves were broken as they crashed to the ground, their daggers flying from their grasp.

As they struggled to recover, Alexander appeared before them. He was holding a Kukri dagger, cold and merciless, resembling a reaper.

With a flash of his blade, their heads rolled away, blood gushing from their severed necks.

With the two Shadow Warriors dispatched, Alexander scanned the remaining opponents.

Schroder was pale with fear.

The remaining five Shadow Warriors wore serious expressions, drawing their weapons.

The basement was crowded with dozens of people, and in the confined space, the lethality of their melee weapons was amplified.

They brandished daggers, knives, claw hammers, and even heavy chains, which swung threateningly.

"Attack!"

Alexander commanded as he focused on the Shadow Warriors.

Augusta and Marvin charged toward Schroder, brandishing their swords and slicing through the chaos.

Blood spattered as they cut down anyone in their path, quickly overwhelming Schroder and pushing him into a corner while rescuing Jane.

The five remaining Shadow Warriors did not intervene, their eyes fixed on Alexander.

Despite their numerical advantage, they felt intense pressure, as if each faced Alexander alone.

When Augusta and Marvin made their move, the tension rose even higher.

Both Augusta and Marvin exuded the same energy as the others, clearly indicating that they were also Shadow Warriors who had used the power elixirs.

Their presence was even more intimidating and pure.

Thanks to Alexander's refinement of harmful substances from the power elixir, Augusta and Marvin gained strength that surpassed that of the others.

The lead Shadow Warrior, a bald man, shed his shirt, revealing a muscular torso.

Veins bulged on his arms, and his muscles were sharply defined, brimming with explosive power.

"Guys, we can't go back alive unless we kill him!"

The bald man swung his heavy hammer, rallying his four companions as they charged at Alexander like a pack of wolves.
