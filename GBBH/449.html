The Lightsaber whistled through the air as if tearing space itself.

With a single strike, Alexander retracted it and strode into the room.

The onlookers were curious why the eight bodyguards hadn't fired and didn't seem to intend to stop Alexander.

Eight bodies lay in a pool of blood, along with their guns, all cut in half.

Inside the room, the atmosphere was lively, with music and noise blending into a cacophony.

Several people turned their heads, staring at Alexander in surprise as he entered.

They were utterly unaware that death had come, thinking he was a guest invited by Kyle.

Alexander scanned the room but didn't find Kyle.

A dozen men and women, dressed provocatively, were sitting and caressing each other. They not only touched and flirted openly, but some had already begun to undress in public.

These men, all dressed in designer brands, looked like wealthy playboys.

The women, willingly or not, were all dressed in revealing, sexy attire with fiery figures.

A few women had stiff expressions, tears on their faces, and even red palm prints, clearly indicating they were coerced.

They had no power to resist and could only submit to the men's demands.

A woman, her dress torn off, screamed in fear and ran towards Alexander, arms wrapped around her chest.

Alexander gently supported her, and she couldn't help but wail.

"Bitch. Where do you think you're going?"

Several men chased after her, aggressively trying to drag her away.

"Please, let me go. I don't want this. No!"

The woman struggled desperately.

"Stop!"

Alexander bellowed, silencing everyone.

"Are you even men, bullying a woman like this?"

Everyone was stunned for a few seconds before bursting into laughter.

A man with red hair contemptuously glared at Alexander, even poking him hard in the chest with his finger.

"Who are you to meddle in my business?" Bradley Patel taunted.

"Mr. Patel, I don't know this guy. Do you know him? Some nobody, looking for trouble!"

"He probably snuck in. If Mr. Vanderbilt knew, he'd be dead already."

The others laughed at Alexander, mocking him, not taking him seriously.

Alexander slapped Bradley.

Bradley flew like a cannonball, crashing into the wall.

The impact was so great that the entire room shook.

Bradley spat out a mouthful of blood and fell to the ground, unmoving, his condition uncertain.

Instantly, a deathly silence fell, everyone staring in shock.

"Boy, how dare you commit violence here?"

A tall and burly middle-aged man jumped up, rushing towards Bradley in a frenzy.

In fact, Alexander had noticed him as soon as he entered.

This middle-aged man was the strongest present, with a subtle murderous aura in his eyes, indicating he was a killer.

He had been sitting silently in the corner of the sofa, drinking, looking out of place.

Unfortunately for him, he encountered Alexander. Alexander's strength was far beyond his reach.

"Mr. Patel, are you okay? Wake up!"

The man shook Bradley anxiously.

Bradley finally opened his eyes, his face filled with resentment.

"Brent, you're our family's top fighter. Don't just stand there. Kill him... kill him!!"

"As you wish, Mr. Patel!"

Brent charged at Alexander.

"Die!"

He quickly threw a punch, smashing towards Alexander's face.

Alexander advanced instead of retreating, stepping forward.

It seemed like a simple step forward, but his speed was unimaginably fast, several times faster than Brent, who had started first.

In an instant, before Brent could react, Alexander punched him in the stomach.

Brent flew like a cannonball, smashing into the wall, then crashed to the ground.

This time, even the solid wall was covered in a dense network of cracks, like a giant spider's web.
