Alexander smiled faintly, standing against the wind, his deep eyes piercing through the night, gazing at the Black Dragon Gang's camp in the distance.

"I'll go. Launch the attack in five minutes!"

The next second, Alexander appeared a hundred feet away with astonishing speed.

Then, no one could see where he was.

"He's so fast!" everyone exclaimed as his speed was totally beyond their expectations.

"I'm a little worried about him," Marvin muttered to himself a little worriedly.

Augusta said with a confident smile, "Don't worry, this is child's play for him."

Although Alexander was completely out of sight, everyone had their eyes wide open, fully focused on the direction where he disappeared.

As time ticked by, the five minutes seemed as long as a century, yet also seemed to pass in an instant.

The Dragon Gang's camp remained silent as if no battle had taken place.

"Let's rush over at top speed!" Augusta waved his hand excitedly.

Immediately, over a thousand people rushed towards the camp.

The sound of footsteps and startled birds and beasts broke the original silence.

Everyone tightened their grip on their guns; the battle was about to begin.

Over a thousand people rushed out of the jungle, forming a dense, dark mass.

Logically speaking, the Dragon Gang should have spotted them by now and opened fire fiercely.

Strange things happened; there was not a single sound within the camp, let alone any attacks.

Soon, Augusta and his subordinates successfully surrounded the camp. The Dragon Gang did not fire a single shot, let alone use any howitzers; they were completely silent.

At this point, many people had already caught the scent of blood in the sea breeze.

They were all seasoned elites, battle-hardened, and most sensitive to the smell of blood.

They began to occupy the camp, discovering numerous bodies that were still slightly warm in each of the guard posts.

There were also many bodies lying haphazardly behind rocks, in tents, and among the bushes. The gruesome scene was indescribable.

As they delved deeper into the camp, the stench of blood in the air grew stronger, and the number of corpses increased.

Everyone was shocked; they didn't expect Alexander to launch a massacre.

Someone made a rough estimate that at least one-third of the Dragon Gang's force, which numbered in the thousands, had been killed, and there were still many bodies that had not been counted.

They followed behind effortlessly as if they were on a sightseeing trip.

"Who's that? Enemy attack!"

Suddenly, a roar erupted from the tent in the very center of the camp.

Immediately after, several gunshots rang out, followed by a loud boom, and the entire tent was torn to pieces.

Three tall and burly men appeared; they were the Napper brothers, wearing combat armor. Their armored bodies gleamed with a faint light.

After rushing out, they found that half of the camp was already destroyed, and enemies surrounded them everywhere. They immediately let out a roar of fury, "Kill them all!"

The three brothers, all metahumans with special abilities, could push the combat effectiveness of their combat armor to the extreme.

They raised their rapid-fire machine guns, spewing out terrifying streams of fire, launching a fierce attack on a ghostly figure not far away.

However, as soon as the guns fired, Alexander had already vanished under the cover of night, refusing to engage in a direct confrontation with them.

At this moment, the entire camp was enveloped in the intense sound of gunfire.

Augusta, along with his subordinates, began to launch a frenzied assault, pouring fierce firepower onto the enemy like rain.

"Son of a bitch, you just launched a sneak attack! Face me!" Casper's eyes were already red with urgency.

The camp was already destroyed, with heavy losses among the subordinates; it was definitely impossible to occupy Devil Island.

But as a level-two metahuman, wearing combat armor that significantly boosted combat effectiveness, Casper was far from willing to give up.

So, Casper locked onto Alexander and opened fire again.

A hail of bullets rained down, but his target had vanished.

"Casper, watch out!" Kermit warned his brother, drew his battle knife made of special steel, and dashed towards Alexander.

"Kermit!" Aidan roared, raising the specially made shield to cover Kermit.

With a dull sound, the shield that could block machine gun bullets was easily penetrated by the Lightsaber.
