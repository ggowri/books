"Diego, what do you think of Felix?" Barry asked suddenly.

In the distance, the beach stretched out toward the blue sea, with flocks of seagulls soaring through the sky—a breathtaking view.

Joyce took out a test strip, placed a few drops of coffee on it, and watched it closely for any reaction.

"You're overly cautious. The Kaylon runs this place. Who would dare poison us here?

"I've been thinking about that as well. He's not as simple as he appears.
