Gary's voice dripped with menace as he warned, "Young man, I strongly advise you to dial it back.

"Think about the consequences of your words and actions!

"You may have struck it lucky with the magic powder, but it's just a tool for making money. The Federer family can still thrive without it.

"And let's not forget, Ms. Blackburn is a pragmatic woman; she won't jeopardize her interests with the Saint Group over you."

Alexander chuckled derisively.

"If this is an auction, why are we wasting time with all this chatter? The highest bidder wins!"

Gary, seething, sank heavily into his chair, clearly outraged.

David leaned in, his voice barely above a whisper. "Gary, are we really just going to let this go?"

"Don't worry, I'll take care of this for you. By tomorrow, Alexander will be a non-issue!

"But I hope the Federer family is ready to team up with us against the Saint Group when they retaliate."

"Alright, it's a deal."

Although Alexander couldn't hear their conversation, he noticed the furtive whispers and knew they were plotting against him.

The Federer family had considerable power, but with his current standing, he had no reason to fear them.

Just then, a man beside Alexander leaned in; concern etched on his face.

"Mr. Hawthorne, it might be wise to let the Federer family have this gem. Otherwise, you could be inviting serious trouble."

"Exactly. The Federer family has a long memory for grudges. It's just a rock; why not go with the flow and hand it over?"

Seeing his stubbornness, the others fell silent, exchanging resigned glances.

He and Ava climbed into a sleek Rolls-Royce, leaving behind a crowd of envious onlookers.

If it weren't for the magic powder formula, I'd have already turned him into a hornet's nest."

"Ms. Blackburn, why don't you be a good girl and raise your hands in surrender? Otherwise, don't say I didn't warn you!"

He envisioned himself getting his hands on the magic powder formula and taking down Alexander, after which Ava would be at his mercy. He had no intention of letting her slip away.

Before Houtte could make his next move, Alexander quickly moved to shield Ava, positioning her behind the side of the car.

Houtte taunted, swaggering with a newfound arrogance, "Alexander, what are you hiding for?

"Aren't you supposed to be the tough guy? Let's not forget it was you who got Joseph killed. Why don't you come out and face me?
