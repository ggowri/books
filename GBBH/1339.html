At the Palace in Darwin, the Silvermoon Gate served as the main entry and exit point for the palace staff and guards, and it was bustling with activity.

Servants and maids from various departments were coming and going, attending to their duties. Among them, a dashing young general clad in armor and a soldier in a white robe followed a purchasing servant toward the Silvermoon Gate.

On Oak Avenue, the busiest street in Darwin, a "man" and a woman walked closely together, making their way through the throngs of people.

A beautiful maid, her silver hair pinned up and dressed in a flowing green gauze skirt, leaned on the arm of a striking young "man," whispering, "Your Highness... Oh, I mean, sir, you're truly remarkable. We always manage to slip out without anyone noticing."

The handsome young "man" in white waved his folding fan and replied, "Lana, don't just play around. I heard there's some amazing Risunese juggling going on up ahead. It should be fun."

Not far off, Alexander, Logan, and Jalen strolled casually along Oak Avenue.

Jalen exclaimed, "I just remembered! Someone at the manor mentioned there's a Risunese juggling troupe performing nearby. It sounds really interesting. How about we check it out?"

Logan replied excitedly, "Sure, let's go see it!"

Alexander nodded in agreement. The trio asked for directions and headed straight toward the Risunese circus.

At the end of an alley on Oak Avenue, a large courtyard came into view. The area was filled with a lively crowd and the sound of continuous applause.

A banner at the entrance displayed bold letters proclaiming "Risun Circus." Nearby, a group of Risunese was selling tickets, charging one coin for three people.

"That's pretty expensive," Alexander grumbled.

After purchasing their tickets, they entered the courtyard, which was about a hundred feet long and wide.

Inside, there were men, women, the elderly, and children—probably thousands of people crowded together. Some were sitting or squatting in the front, while others stood in the back.

In the center of the courtyard was a large wooden stage, complete with a grand curtain behind it.

A juggling act was currently in progress, featuring two Risunese performing a fire-blowing trick that sent bursts of flames shooting into the air, eliciting cheers and shouts from the audience.

Jalen joined the crowd in cheering, while Alexander and Logan found it rather dull. After witnessing modern, high-tech juggling performances, the Risunese display felt a bit childish to them.

They exchanged glances, sharing a mutual understanding of their boredom.

Following the fire-blowing act, the next performances consisted of acts that were popular in that era, such as unicycling and stilt walking.

Just when Alexander and Logan were starting to doze off, a short Risunese emerged from behind the curtain. He sported a high bun, small eyes, and an unsightly big black mole on his right cheek, which had a few black hairs protruding from it. He was dressed in a white hunting robe.

The Risunese announced loudly in a strange accent, "Thank you all for your support! Up next is our most thrilling and magical show: the Disappearing Act!"

The crowd erupted in excitement, clapping enthusiastically—it seemed the show was quite a hit.

The Risunese pointed to a young man in white, saying, "Now, we invite the young man in white to join us on stage. Everyone, give him a warm welcome!"

The young man in white appeared somewhat reluctant, but a few Risunese dancers coaxed him forward with sweet smiles, encouraging him to participate. After a moment of hesitation, he stepped onto the stage.

As Alexander took a closer look, he recognized him as the boy who had argued with Julius on the street that day.

He nudged Logan and said, "Hey! It's someone we know."

"Who?" Logan asked, following Alexander's gaze and seeing a handsome young man.

A chill ran down Logan's spine as he touched his own backside. Fearfully, he looked at Alexander. "You don't actually like him, do you?"

Alexander slapped him on the shoulder and shot back, "Get out of here!"

At that moment, several Risunese on stage rolled out a large cabinet and set it up in the spotlight.

An older Risunese appeared, acting mysteriously as he waved a stick-shaped prop, gesturing theatrically before instructing the young man in white to step inside the cabinet.

Once the cabinet door closed, the old man screamed hoarsely and pointed at the cabinet with his stick.

The cabinet door swung open, revealing a beautifully dressed Risun dancer stepping out, while the young man in white had vanished.
