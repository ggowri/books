Earlier, Alexander used the Force to see through the water and cast his fishing rod, reeling in the massive fish in one go.

This time, Alexander cast the fishing rod again, but without using the Force or his vision. He relied on bait and waited patiently.

The others carried the tuna away, leaving the deck quiet. Only Alexander and Norma remained.

The fresh sea breeze swept through Norma's hair and light chiffon wrap, her graceful figure appearing ethereal, like a goddess about to take flight.

She lightly touched Alexander's bare chest and laughed softly.

"Mr. Hawthorne, you're in such great shape!"

Norma picked up sunscreen and gently applied it to Alexander.

Her delicate fingers brushed against his chest, back, and arms... It was incredibly soothing.

Soon, a few attendants brought a small table onto the deck.

The table was laden with dishes: tuna sashimi, grilled fish, savory fish soup, and fresh salad, all mouthwateringly aromatic.

With the sparkling waves, the luxury yacht, fine food and drink, and Norma's breathtaking presence, Alexander felt like a true winner in life.

After finishing the meal, Alexander and Norma rested for a while before going for a dive.

Both were captivated by the beauty of the underwater world.

Vibrant corals, schools of colorful fish, scuttling crabs, and unhurried turtles created a scene straight out of a fairytale.

It was Norma's first-time diving. Amid the stunning and dreamlike underwater scenery, she felt a mix of awe and nervousness.

But with Alexander by her side, Norma felt much more at ease.

She stayed close behind him, her heart brimming with warmth.

Holding her hand, Alexander couldn't help but admit that Norma was truly stunning.

In the crystal-clear waters, Norma's diving suit hugged her flawless figure like a sultry mermaid moving gracefully.

"Oh no! A shark!"

Suddenly, Norma froze in fear, her eyes wide, and grabbed Alexander, frantically swimming toward the surface.

A massive tiger shark charged at them, its jaws gaping to reveal rows of razor-sharp teeth.

Tiger sharks were vicious predators known for attacking humans, responsible for most shark-related injuries.

If Alexander and Norma were ordinary people, there would be no doubt—they'd be dead.

Alexander pulled Norma behind him and launched himself like a torpedo toward the tiger shark.

Just as the tiger shark's massive jaws were about to close around him, Alexander dodged nimbly, causing it to miss.

Taking advantage of the moment, Alexander delivered a powerful kick to the shark's head, using the Force to shatter its skull.

The tiger shark rolled backward several meters as the water around it turned a deep red.

A gaping hole in its head gushed torrents of blood.

Its lifeless body floated toward the surface, leaving a blood-stained sea in its wake.

Tiger sharks moved in packs, earning them the nickname "wolves of the sea."

Soon, the heavy scent of blood began to attract other tiger sharks from kilometers away.

Although Norma hadn't noticed the other sharks yet, Alexander had already spotted over a dozen tiger sharks.

He could hear panicked screams from the yacht, confirming that the shark pack had been noticed there as well.

Alexander glared at the yacht's smirking pilot, his eyes filled with deadly intent.

This tiger shark hadn't appeared by chance. Someone had dumped bloody bait into the water while they were diving, luring the shark to them.

The yacht pilot had planned to kill them using the sharks.

"Let's go!"

Alexander grabbed Norma by the waist and unleashed the Force, propelling them forward at incredible speed.

The immense power made him shoot toward the surface like a launched arrow.

Terrified moments ago, Norma felt reassured in his arms and clung to Alexander tightly, tears streaming down her face.

Holding each other close, they rapidly rose to the surface.

Around them, over a dozen tiger sharks swarmed from all directions, drawn by the scent of blood.

The scene was horrifying yet strangely breathtaking.
