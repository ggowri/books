The wind was howling!

The colonel dared to confront Alexander head-on.

Alexander smiled in disdain. The colonel was arrogant and conceited.

The opponent was about to hit Alexander in the chest. If Alexander didn't dodge, his ribs would definitely be broken.

But Alexander was faster. He reached the side of the colonel's body like lightning and grabbed his right wrist.

The colonel's wrist was broken, and he screamed in pain.

Then, Alexander punched him in the stomach. The colonel fell to the ground, holding his stomach, and curled up in pain.

"Damn it. I'll kill you..."

The colonel had a ferocious expression. He took out a potion from his pocket and injected it directly into his body.

The black liquid quickly entered the colonel's body. It was the first generation of immortality potion.

Alexander was a little surprised. He didn't expect that the colonel was so fearless.

The generation of immortality potion had serious side effects. If a normal person used it, he would at least be the living dead.

But for a short period of time, he was immortal.

The colonel had broken one of his hands, but after being injected with medicine, he seemed to be fine.

His eyes turned red, and he laughed wildly.

"You want to kill me? I am immortal. You are the one who is going to die, and I will live forever until the day the world is destroyed!"

The colonel grinned.

His broken hand was twisted.

Seeing the colonel's strange changes, Alexander did not take action immediately. He wanted to see how this guy turned into a living dead.

The colonel had lost his sense of pain and his mind, roaring like a wild beast.

Just as Alexander was observing, the colonel pounced on him.

His nails had grown long and sharp, and had turned black, obviously poisonous.

Alexander casually blocked the colonel with his assault rifle, but the gun body was actually cut off by the colonel's nails.

There was a white light. Alexander finally took out the Lightsaber.

The colonel felt his hands tremble and discovered that all of his fingers were gone, cut off by the Lightsaber.

The colonel, who had already begun to transform into a living dead, was not afraid at all. With red eyes, he pounced on Alexander again and opened his mouth wide to bite Alexander.

It was obvious that the colonel had lost his sense of pain. Otherwise, he would lose his ability to fight.

Moreover, he had lost his normal thinking and become crazy.

Alexander sneered and stabbed with the Lightsaber.

His movements were graceful and elegant, like a noble performing.

Everyone, who saw this scene, felt that Alexander's movements had a strange beauty.

The colonel's two hands were cut off and fell to the ground.

The blood flowing from his wound was no longer red, but black.

Alexander's Lightsaber stabbed the colonel in the heart, leaving a hole in his left chest.

However, the colonel did not feel any pain. He just took a few steps back and continued to charge towards Alexander with a ferocious laugh.

"Immortality? It's just a monster!"

Alexander snorted coldly, shook the Lightsaber in his hand lightly, and a powerful force burst out.

The colonel's chest was hit hard by the Force, and a big hole was blown out in his chest.

Half of his body disappeared, turning into debris, wriggling in the black blood.

The colonel still didn't fall down, staggered, and roared madly.

Alexander didn't kill him because he wanted to see how strong the living dead was. Now, it was time to end it.

The colonel's head was cut off and flew out.

The headless corpse immediately stopped moving and fell to the ground.

The colonel was killed, and his soldiers lost their fighting spirit. Under the fierce attack of Melin and others, they fled in panic.
