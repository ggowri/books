In the corridor, the guards were knocked out one by one by Alexander before they could even raise the alarm.

Alexander's dark pupils were emitting an enchanting glow.

He had an aura of supreme power, which made people feel afraid.

Alexander even directly hypnotized those Rottweilers without fighting.

At this moment, Alyssa and a dozen other people were all staring intently at the large LCD screen.

No intruders were found, and everything seemed calm.

"Ms. Perry, I think that guy was just bragging. Look how long it's been, but no one has come in."

Some people couldn't bear it anymore and started to complain.

"We are on high alert. He couldn't sneak in. He must have entered by chance before."

"With this level of security, it's impossible to get in by force, let alone sneak in!"

Everyone was talking about it without respect.

They thought it would be a fantasy for Alexander to sneak in under such strict security measures.

Amid the discussions, Alyssa remained calm, but she did not stop them from talking.

She still maintained her confidence and expectations in Alexander, hoping that he could sneak in.

If Alexander made it, the Axe would be saved!

Alyssa couldn't explain why she was confident. Maybe it was because of her intuition. After so many years of struggling, she thought intuition was the most reliable.

"Hasn't he started yet?"

Alyssa finally couldn't sit still anymore, so she picked up her phone and called Alexander.

However, the phone beeped for a long time, but no one answered.

What the hell was Alexander doing?

Alyssa frowned. This was totally illogical. Something must have happened.

Could it be that Alexander had already come in?

Alyssa was suddenly very excited, and a smile appeared on her face involuntarily.

At this time, Alexander had already arrived at the door of the conference room in a leisurely manner.

There were eight fully armed guards at the door. When they saw Alexander suddenly appear, they were so shocked that they opened their mouths wide.

"Stop!"

The captain was about to stop Alexander.

Alexander gently pointed his finger at the captain's forehead.

The captain was stunned for a second, then fell to the ground with his eyes closed.

The other guards were knocked down by Alexander one after another before they could sound the alarm.

At this time, the people in the conference room also saw the image on the LCD screen, and there was an uproar.

"Crack!"

Alexander pushed open the door of the conference room and walked in calmly.

Everyone was shocked, staring at Alexander with incredible eyes.

Alexander was composed as if he had just done something insignificant.

Even Alyssa, who believed in Alexander, was speechless. Under such strict defense, Alexander reached the door silently.

More than a dozen pairs of eyes were staring at the surveillance screen, but they didn't notice Alexander at all.

This was a miracle!

Alyssa couldn't suppress the doubts in her heart.

"Mr. Hawthorne, you are amazing. How did you do it?"

More than a dozen pairs of eyes were focused on Alexander's face. They were waiting for his explanation.

Alexander glanced at everyone and smiled indifferently.

"Don't care about this. You just need to know that I can complete the plan, and you just need to follow my arrangements."

Alyssa nodded vigorously with excitement and was very excited.

No one minded that Alexander didn't tell them about the secret behind this. After all, no one would casually reveal their abilities.

"Mr. Hawthorne, I'm sorry, we shouldn't have asked such childish questions."

Alyssa quickly apologized sincerely.

Other leaders also bowed and apologized.

Alexander smiled. In the face of absolute strength, some things needed no explanation.

"Now what do you think of my plan? I will kill the leaders of those forces, causing them to fall into civil strife, and then the Axe Gang will take the opportunity to swallow them up!"

Alyssa pumped her fist excitedly.

"Your plan is great. This time, we will unify Mandale Town."

Everyone present no longer looked gloomy but waved their fists excited, with murder intention in their eyes.
