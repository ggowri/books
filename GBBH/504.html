Alyosha and Eva were not only Danielle's loyal subordinates but also her closest friends, making it hard for her to decide.

She raised her gun again, shouting, "Let them go, or I'll shoot!"

The thug ignored her and kept counting, "Seven... eight... nine... ten! Ready to resist till the end? Fine, you're heartless! This one's dead because of you!"

"Bang!"

A piercing gunshot echoed as a man beside Danielle was shot in the head, his body collapsing heavily like a broken log.

His assault rifle fell into the blood, and his body twitched...

Danielle's expression changed; she hadn't expected them to be so ruthless.

"Sh-sh-sh!"

Hidden windows opened on all sides of the hall.

Dozens of sleek, black gun barrels aimed at Danielle and her team.

"Damn it!"

Danielle's face was grim; she hadn't anticipated an automatic weapon system.

Those thick gun barrels were fully automatic, controlled by an intelligent system capable of tracking and targeting with near-perfect accuracy.

Danielle genuinely feared they'd start shooting; if they opened fire, she stood no chance. Her entire team would be wiped out.

"Captain, we can't put down our guns!

"If we put them down, we're done. We have to fight our way out."

A team member shouted angrily.

Danielle frowned, gripping her gun tightly.

Capturing Felix was now impossible, but she still wanted to save her team, putting her in a difficult position.

"One! Two! Three! Four..."

They kept counting.

Their tone was mocking; every ten counts, they killed another Phoenix Team member.

Danielle knew that the people from the Red Umbrella Group were ruthless and deadly serious, especially with hostages.

She waved her trembling hand and finally made a decision.

"Prepare... to retreat!"

The Phoenix Team immediately formed a circle, aiming their guns at the people from the Red Umbrella Group as they slowly retreated.

The people from the Red Umbrella Group were furious.

"Trying to run? You think you can escape?"

Two figures charged forward, eyes filled with rage, like enraged bulls, each smashing the head of one of Danielle's team members with a single punch.

They were strength-type metahumans!

Danielle's expression changed drastically, and she could no longer bear the pressure. She raised her gun, clenching her teeth.

"I'll put down my gun if you let my team go!"

With that, Danielle dropped her gun, raised her hands, and walked slowly toward them.

"Captain!"

"Captain, come back! It's dangerous!"

Her team members were in an uproar, with several men rushing to hold Danielle back, stopping her from risking herself.

"Bang!"

With a loud bang, one of the metahumans struck again, sending two men flying with a single punch.

Such terrifying power made everyone flinch in fear.

"Leave the women, kill all the men!"

One of the burly metahumans shouted.

"Let's fight them!"

Danielle quickly reached for her gun.

But as her fingers touched the gun handle, a fierce wind hit her, and she felt herself spinning as she was thrown back.

"Rat-tat-tat!"

A burst of gunfire erupted, followed by a series of pained cries.

All of Danielle's team members, except for a few women, lay in pools of blood.

When the dust settled, Felix finally walked out.

"Welcome, Mr. Vanderbilt!"

The people from the Red Umbrella Group all knelt before Felix in unison.

Felix's cold gaze swept across them, sharp as a blade, making everyone tremble uncontrollably.

Now possessing the power of a level-two metahuman, with the potential to reach level three on his own, Felix was truly a formidable force.

Felix waved his hand coldly.

"Shackle the women; drag the rest out."

The blades hidden in her undergarments were found and confiscated.

She and a few female team members were handcuffed, linked together in a long iron chain.

It was over!

Danielle's face went pale; she knew she had completely fallen into their clutches with no chance of escape.

Felix stood with his hands behind his back, looking disdainfully at his beautiful captives.

"Line them up. I have questions for them."
