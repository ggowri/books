As for Cyclops' Cadillac, because of its strong bulletproof surface, it managed to break out of the rain of bullets and ran away after knocking a car that got in its way.

"Crap! Fuck!"

Trenton was mad and wanted to order his men to chase Cyclops, but Alexander stopped him.

"Tell your men to take down the rest of his thugs in this building. I'll handle Cyclops."

Then, Alexander flashed dozens of miles away and soon got out of Trenton's sight.

Trenton was simply dumbfounded and even forgot to follow his order.

After a while, Trenton roughly rubbed his eyes and cursed with a horrified expression, "Fuck! Is he even a human being? Chase, don't blame me for not avenging you. I'm just too weak to offend a monster like Alexander."

Meanwhile, Cyclops urged his driver, Jet Gordon, "Hurry! Speed up! We've rushed out, so don't let them catch up with us!"

Jet was also very excited about their survival and stepped hard on the accelerator. The Cadillac dashed forward like a rocket.

"Boss! Haha! They lost us! We're finally safe!" said Jet.

Cyclops also breathed a sigh of relief.

Then, he gritted his teeth and said in profound hatred, "That goddamn Chase Burke. He took so much money from me, and he still attacked me. He will pay for this!"

"Hey! What is that?" Suddenly, Jet screamed.

Cyclops looked up and saw a man standing on the street ahead, walking towards their vehicle with his arms open.

The person getting in their way turned his back to a street lamp, which cast a shadow on his face, making Cyclops unable to see who he was.

"What an unlucky day! Since this loser wants to kill himself, just hit him with the car!" Cyclops ordered the driver fiercely.

Jet, originally intending to step on the brake, decided to keep on stepping on the accelerator after seeing this weird scene.

The Cadillac's engine revved up, and it severely sped up.

With the roar of the engine, it dashed out but hit nothing. It was like that man in front of their car was just an illusion.

"What's the fuck going on? Was there something wrong with my eyes?" Jet asked in shock.

"Fuck! What the hell?" Cyclops looked very panicked.

"What's that?!" Suddenly, a dark shadow appeared on the hood of the car and blocked Jet's view.

He freaked out and screamed, and the car bumped into the green belts in jolts.

Cyclops realized it was an attack in a moment and pulled his gun to shoot that shadow.

Within several seconds, he emptied his bullets and also broke the car windows.

However, that shadow disappeared.

"Idiot! Stop the car!"

Cyclops shouted at Jet, but it was too late as the Cadillac crashed hard into a lamppost.

Jet flew out of the broken car window and fell to the ground, being unconscious.

Due to the huge inertia, Cyclops crashed hard into the car seat and bruised his face.

"Damn it! What kind of monster are you? Get out! I'll kill you!"

Cyclops was trembling as he reloaded his gun and provoked this mysterious enemy.

The next second, someone directly pulled the dented car door off, and Cyclops suddenly saw Alexander's mocking smile.

"Who the hell are you? What do you want?" He glared at Alexander in horror.

Alexander just said calmly, "Due to Norma's request, I'm here to take Hazel back. By the way, do you really think you can escape after hurting Hazel and so many other women? I don't think so."

"Go to hell!" Cyclops roared and fired at Alexander.

Amid the fierce gunshots, Alexander disappeared again, and of course, all his bullets missed.

At this very moment, Cyclops was on the verge of losing his mind as he couldn't understand why this freak could dodge all his bullets with such incredible speed.

"Ah!"

Then, Cyclops heard himself letting out a scream. Alexander gripped his neck and pulled him out of the crushed car, and Cyclops also lost his gun.

With a single hand, Alexander lifted him and gripped his neck harder and harder.

As if there was an iron hoop around his neck, Cyclops felt his lungs were out of oxygen, and his face also turned purple.

He said, "Um... Please..."

Despite his struggle and desperate plea, Alexander just kept exerting more strength on his hand.

After flapping its limbs violently for a few seconds, Cyclops' head drooped and remained motionless.

Snorting coldly, Alexander threw his dead body on the ground like garbage and directly walked away.
