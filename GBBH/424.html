Alexander remained calm and composed.

"You're hitmen here for that one-hundred-million-dollar bounty on the Dark Web?"

"Stop wasting time! Are you coming with us quietly, or do we need to make you?"

The man in the suit sneered, cracking his knuckles with a loud snap.

A glint of danger flashed in Alexander's eyes as he pulled Betty behind him for protection.

Antony, leaning heavily on a cane, hobbled in front of Alexander and ordered coldly. "Don't resist. Come with us!"

His voice was as frigid as an Arctic wind, making Betty shiver uncontrollably.

"Don't be afraid. Wait for me in the car for a few minutes; it won't take long."

Alexander reassured her, giving her a gentle pat on the back. A surge of Force filled her.

Betty felt the warmth wash over her body and immediately stopped shivering.

"I'll wait for you!" she replied, and with a determined wiggle, she dashed into the car.

"Kid, what did you just say? You'll deal with us in a few minutes? That's so arrogant; I'll make you cry!"

The old man, Antony, felt deeply insulted.

Alexander waved dismissively. "Enough with the chatter; I've dealt with hitmen before. Let's get this over with!"

The suited man grinned wickedly.

"We're in the top one hundred hitmen on the Dark Web. If you come quietly, we might leave you intact.

"As for the girl, she's quite attractive; I'll take her for myself."

Alexander was amused by their audacity.

"You two are pathetic. I wonder why you are so bold, but since you won't make a move, I'll take action."

With a commanding shout, he unleashed a wave of Force that roared like a lion.

A brilliant white beam burst from his mouth, streaking like an arrow toward the old man.

Force Breath!

Back when Alexander was just starting out as a Warrior, this move could blast through concrete.

Now, as a seasoned fighter, his Force Breath had become something else entirely - a searing blast that felt more like a dragon's fury.

"What is this?!"

The old man was stunned and terrified. After Alexander's roar, he was already shaken, and now the intense heat was upon him.

He barely had time to swing his cane at the wave of energy.

Crack!

The cane, forged from steel, shattered on impact.

The elderly man's hands were splintered and bleeding as the Force Breath slammed into his chest.

He was thrown backward, crashing to the ground, and after spitting out several mouthfuls of blood, he lay still.

The suited man was dumbfounded, never expecting that Alexander could take out his partner with just a breath of white mist.

He wanted to flee but dared not turn his back to Alexander, knowing it would be his death.

With wide eyes, the man produced two short daggers about a foot long and charged at Alexander.

The sharp blades whistled through the air, fierce and lethal!

"Child's play."

To the man's shock, Alexander reached out and grabbed the blades.

The man rejoiced, thinking he had the upper hand, not realizing he'd made a grave mistake.

Clang!

Suddenly, the gleaming daggers snapped like crisp chips, falling to the ground in countless pieces.

Meanwhile, Alexander's hand remained unscathed.

Despair washed over the man. This was no ordinary person!

At that moment, he cursed the bounty poster. If he had known how terrifying Alexander was, he wouldn't have come for a hundred million, not even for one billion dollars.

He had completely lost his will to fight and turned to run.

"Running? Did you get my permission?"

In a flash, Alexander vanished.

Moving so fast that only a shadow remained, he appeared behind the man.

"Die!"

He struck the man's back with a powerful blow.

Boom!

The torrent of Force surged through, exploding outward.

The man screamed as his clothes shredded, blood spraying everywhere as he collapsed to the ground.

Two formidable killers, lured by the bounty from the Red Umbrella Group, were effortlessly sent to their doom by Alexander.
