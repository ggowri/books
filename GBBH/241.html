Peter said dully, "Today is our group's turn to guard Mr. Butler. When we left, Mr. Butler was still in the office. However... I saw him preparing weapons and clothes. He should leave soon, but I don't know where he's going."

At this time, Tom added, "I heard that Mr. Butler has a mistress outside. That woman is very favored. Mr. Butler goes to see her every Friday night. Today is Friday, so Mr. Butler will definitely date with that woman."

Alexander's eyes brightened.

What John took the initiative to see shouldn't be a mistress but the hidden Guzman.

"How long have you not seen Guzman?" Alexander continued to ask.

Peter replied truthfully, "It's been a long time... I remember the last time I saw Mr. Bryant was half a month ago... We used to see him often at the trading center."

Alexander further confirmed that Guzman was not hiding underground in the trading center.

"Okay, pick up your weapons. Now continue to find the stripper. Keep going... You will forget everything in a while. This is just an illusion..."

Alexander commanded them with a bewitching voice while swinging the pocket watch.

Peter and his companions walked towards the red-light district in a daze.

Gradually, they disappeared into the night.

After a while, Peter suddenly rushed over, looking around in horror.

The rest of the group also came over.

"Peter, why did you run back?" Tom asked curiously.

Peter rubbed his temples hard and asked in confusion, "Don't you guys feel like we forgot something? We just met someone here... Something happened, but I can't remember anything!"

He looked around in horror, but there was no one around, with no clues.

The others laughed and said, "Peter, did you daydream? Aren't we going to find the new stripper? What could happen?"

Peter slapped his forehead and laughed awkwardly. "Maybe I drank too much and took some magic medicine before. It should be an illusion."

He took a few more glances at the dark alley and left.

When the figures of the group disappeared, Alexander appeared from the shadows with a sinister smile on his lips.

His Force Control was getting stronger, and it could erase someone's memory of a certain period.

Even someone with Peter's strength couldn't restore the truth.

According to Peter and Tom, John was very likely to leave the trading center tonight.

Alexander continued to lurk, waiting patiently for John to appear.

While observing the trading center, he let his thoughts wander, entering a trance-like state, perfectly blending into the darkness.

Occasionally, stray cats and dogs passed by. With their keen senses, they didn't notice a human hidden in the shadows.

"Guzman has done many evil deeds and has tried to kill me many times. He must die! I must take over Treasure Island. This is an international hub for black market transactions and is extremely important. If I can seize a batch of potential potions from the Red Umbrella Group, it would be best to help Augusta and others improve their strength..."

Alexander pondered the next plan while waiting patiently for John to appear.

He was like a tiger lurking in the dark. As soon as the prey appeared, it would be killed in one blow.

After more than an hour, it was already early morning.

The lights on the island became fewer. Only the red-light district was bright, dressing up the nightlife of Treasure Island.

At this moment, Alexander was not tired or irritable because of the wait, but his energy was adjusted to the peak.

His deep eyes were bright, staring at the trading center without blinking.

Finally, three black cars drove out of the underground parking lot of the trading center in a row.

They drove in three different directions, which made Alexander a bit caught off guard.

Fortunately, he had the Force and immediately sensed that there was no breath of John in any of the three cars.

Alexander sneered, "John is really cunning. Unfortunately, no matter how cunning the fox is, it can't escape the hunter's grasp!"
