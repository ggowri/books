Buzz! Buzz! Buzz!

Alexander's phone rang, showing that it was Jane calling.

He didn't need to answer to know she was calling about Andrew's arrest.

Feeling indifferent, he hung up.

A moment later, Jane called again. He hung up once more.

After several attempts, he finally answered.

"Alexander, why did you let my dad get arrested? Even though we're divorced, he was your father-in-law once. How can you be so heartless?"

As soon as the call connected, Jane launched into a barrage of accusations.

"It's not me who got him caught. He was trying to help the Red Umbrella Group kidnap Ashley, and you're in cahoots with them, aren't you?"

Alexander's tone was icy.

Jane faltered, her anger boiling over.

"Alexander, we signed an agreement with the Red Umbrella Group. Ashley must participate in the research, or we owe them a billion dollars!

"If Ashley doesn't go, are you going to pay that billion?"

Alexander was angry.

"I can't believe how despicable you've become, willing to sell out your own daughter."

"Shut up, Alexander! You have no right to lecture me!

"No matter how eloquently you put it, I have every right to take Ashley. Then you'll need to go to the police to explain that my dad wasn't trespassing or attempting a kidnapping and sincerely apologize to him.

"Otherwise, I won't let this go!"

Alexander was speechless, filled with a deep disdain for Jane.

"Since we can't settle this with words, let's see who has the stronger position. You won't take Ashley; try it if you dare."

Jane seethed with rage.

"Do you think you can intimidate me?

"I have the backing of the Red Umbrella Group. I'll rise to the top, and our family will be the new elite in Loscity. When that time comes, don't say I didn't warn you..."

Before she could finish, Alexander hung up.

Jane's face turned pale.

Bang!

She smashed her phone to the ground.

Belinda, looking desperate, asked, "Jane, we only have three days until our agreement with the Red Umbrella Group expires. What do we do now?"

"Phew!"

Jane exhaled deeply, unable to calm herself.

"Alexander won't let me take Ashley, so I'll target someone else. Ava and Ashley both have bodyguards, but that jerk Alexander values Lily.

"If we capture her, he'll think twice!"

Belinda initially felt hopeful, then frowned. "But Jane, Loscity is almost under Alexander's control. Who would dare help us?"

"Humph!"

Jane scoffed.

"I don't need anyone else. I'll ask Bowman for help."

Belinda was taken aback. "Bowman is your ex-boyfriend; you broke up. Will he help you?"

Jane smiled confidently.

"He's always been under my thumb. If I ask him to deal with an old woman, he'll definitely assist!"

Belinda's spirits lifted; she finally saw a glimmer of hope.

Afterward, Jane dressed up carefully, donning a stylish outfit and high heels, and headed to a bar.

"Hey, Jane! What brings you here today?"

A bald man greeted her with a grin, his eyes roaming over her.

"Bowman, I need your help. Are you unhappy to see me?"

Jane playfully swayed, making his eyes widen.

"Of course! Even after all these years, I haven't forgotten your charm. You've always been my favorite!"

Bowman smirked, stepping closer.

Jane leaned into him, saying, "I was blind to marry that jerk Alexander. If only I had married you instead."

Bowman perked up.

"Jane, now that you're divorced, are you willing to be with me?"
