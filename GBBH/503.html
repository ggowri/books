The guards were happily chatting away.

Now, fully geared up, Danielle had an added air of power, looking both fierce and dashing.

To many men's regret, Danielle was married and had just had a baby, still in the breastfeeding stage.

Felix was now worth a hefty one billion dollars alive!

Despite recently having a baby, Danielle showed no signs of weight gain and looked exceptionally fit and curvy.

Her waist was as slender as ever, her skin was even more smooth and soft, and her full chest, due to nursing, appeared particularly rounded and enticing.

Even Danielle's own team members couldn't help but sneak glances at their stunning captain.

The Phoenix Team had a solid reputation in mercenary circles, not only for their skills and record.
