The genius of this Mafia family possessed the terrifying combat strength of a wild beast, yet still, he was no match for Alexander.

"Your defeat is sealed. Do you still intend to resist futilely?"

Alexander gazed at him with indifference.

Bruno spat out a mouthful of blood.

He glared at Alexander, his chest burning with raging battle intent.

"I will kill you!"

Like a cornered beast with no way out, Bruno lunged at Alexander with ferocity.

Alexander couldn't help but marvel at Bruno's sheer might; he was indeed extraordinary by birth, but alas, it was all in vain.

Bruno collided with an invisible wall of the Force and couldn't get any closer to Alexander.

Alexander slapped Bruno hard across the face, sending him flying more than twenty feet away.

Bruno lay on the ground, his cheek swollen and discolored, spitting out blood mixed with several teeth.

"You dare to slap me? Alexander, you shall die..."

Bruno was trembling with rage. This was an unspeakable humiliation.

"Do you still refuse to yield?"

Alexander tilted his head slightly, his eyes filled with disdain and arrogance.

"I've spared you twice out of consideration for the fact that you didn't ambush Ava. If you continue to be ungrateful, don't blame me for ending you."

His cold tone, laced with murderous intent, seemed to lower the temperature around them.

Bruno panted heavily, propping himself up with one hand, his chest heaving.

Gradually, his grotesque and vicious expression disappeared, replaced by a strange calm.

Neither the bloodied stump of his arm nor the throbbing pain in his cheek affected him.

Bruno stared at Alexander and suddenly grinned.

"You don't deserve Ava. You're garbage who deceives women's feelings. Don't think you've won! I've lived in the jungle for over twenty years, living a primitive life. Every time I faced danger, I came out unscathed. Countless beasts have fallen to my hands."

As he spoke, he tore off his tattered clothes, revealing dozens of terrifying scars.

"Look at this. A brown bear left this one. Its swipe almost spilled my intestines."

Bruno pointed to several twisted, centipede-like ugly scars on his stomach.

"In the end, that brown bear became my sustenance for the winter. The taste of roasted bear paws was quite delightful."

He reminisced with a chuckle, then pointed to two round pits on his chest.

"That was from a wild boar weighing over four hundred pounds. Its tusks were indeed formidable, almost impaling me. In the end, I cured it with rock salt and firewood, turning it into delicious smoked meat that could be stored for a long time.

"Another time, I was swallowed by a giant python... You'll never know what that feels like... The dark, enclosed passage, the corrosive acid of the python's stomach... That time was really dangerous. I felt like I was going to die...

"In the end, I still managed to cut open its belly with a dagger, watching it roll and convulse on the ground. It took a full two hours for it to die completely."

Bruno took great interest in introducing the scars on his body, his cold gaze fixed on Alexander all the while.

"Do you think you've won now? Alexander, I warn you. Our battle has only just begun!

"I don't seek anything from Brandon, but to fulfill my mother's wish, I'm willing to pay any price. You're just a stumbling block on my path to success. I will crush you bit by bit!"

With that, Bruno's aura suddenly intensified like an insurmountable mountain.

Alexander clapped his hands with a smile.

"Very well. I didn't expect your will to be so strong, to rise in adversity. No wonder you dared to come to kill me."

Alexander laughed heartily, with a hint of regret.

"What a pity. You have a wish to fulfill, and I have something to protect. Perhaps we are the same kind of people, but by some twist of fate, you are destined to die.

"Not only will you die, but Brandon will die too. The position of the Mafia Godfather is mine!"

Alexander's resolute words caused Bruno's face to change. He didn't expect Alexander to have such ambition, even wanting to annex the Mafia.
