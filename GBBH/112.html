"Come on, Felicity's still young. It's normal for her to have hard feelings. She just doesn't know me well enough yet. Once we start dating and get to know each other better, I'm sure she'll come around.

"Now, let's eat and chat. Sure, I'm older, but that maturity is my edge. Once Felicity sees who I really am, she'll accept me."

Jackson was determined not to let Felicity slip away, and his forced cheerfulness managed to lighten the atmosphere.

Felicity felt trapped, her mind racing like a thousand ants crawling under her skin.

She thought, "Not a single person in the Fairfax family is on my side! Wait! Didn't I just see Alexander? Could he really be here?"

"Alexander, the auction is still on the way. After this magic powder conference, you can gather some funds. If you need financial help, I'm here for you."

"What's wrong? Not willing? You think you can play the game without putting anything on the table?"

Jackson slammed the glass on the table with force.

"Fifty hundred million is just pocket change to me—I can make it in a single day! I just need you to show me your sincerity. But now I see it loud and clear! You've got none!"
