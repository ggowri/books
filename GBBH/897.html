But Edgar was reluctant to stop. He tried his best to concentrate the remaining Force and continuously shoot out light arrows.

There was only anger and the pleasure of revenge in his heart.

More and more orcs fell, and in the blink of an eye, tens of thousands of corpses were piled up in front of the fortress.

However, the Force power of the Warriors would be exhausted. Without a replacement team, their bodies gradually became weak, and the speed at which they fired their light arrows lowered.

At the same time, the sound of the Force cannons gradually died down.

Although the Force cannons could amplify the power of Force attacks, they also consumed the gunner's Force.

But there were too many orcs swarming up like ants.

Finally, some orcs stepped on the corpses, approached the fortress, and began to attack the city wall with the Force shield.

The magic circle provided the Force shield of the city wall, but it also consumed energy.

Replacing gems took time, which was the biggest flaw.

"Shoot!"

There was a roar from the wall.

Light arrows shot down one after another, killing all the orcs near the city wall, leaving only broken limbs and blood on the ground.

"Kill! Kill these damn Warriors."

A ferocious and hideous orc general, wielding a huge mace and roaring in frustration, jumped onto the city wall that was dozens of feet high.

"Piss off!"

Marcus roared and slashed the Lightsabers in his hand, hitting the orc general's head.

The orc general who had just climbed onto the city wall only had time to hold his mace horizontally.

"Bang!"

The Lightsabers cut off the mace without any hindrance and passed directly through the orc general.

The orc general's huge head was split into two halves.

Scarlet blood mixed with white brain matter burst out, and the orc general fell directly from the wall without a grunt.

Seeing more and more orcs attacking the fortress, Marcus waved his hand.

"Burn them!"

A group of Warriors poured barrels of fuel down and then used the Force to ignite it.

The fuel burned fiercely, and coupled with the power of the Force itself, the flames were even more terrifying.

Suddenly, the densely packed orcs were burned to ashes, and a cloud of smoke mixed with a strange stench rose.

Any orc who came close would be burned and screamed in agony without even a sound.

Finally, the Warriors in the fortress got a chance to catch their breath.

This time, the orcs lost nearly 20,000 people, but compared to the million-strong army of the orcs, it was only a tiny part.

Two hours later, the orcs launched a more violent attack.

After paying the price of tens of thousands of lives again, they finally broke the fortress wall.

"Kill! For the glory of the Warriors!"

Edgar no longer had the Force to use the atomic crossbow.

He brandished his Lightsabers and rushed towards orcs who had just rushed onto the wall...

Blood was spreading and the pungent smell of blood filled the air.

The two sides fought for a long time, and the sun rose and the moon set.

After a day and a night, the orcs' corpses had piled up on a small hill.

Those orcs who were climbing up the wall like ants no longer needed any leverage and could rush up the wall simply by stepping on the corpses of their own people.

Edgar had a blood-stained cloth wrapped around his arm because an orc accidentally cut off a piece of his flesh.

There was also a cut on his leg. The skin and flesh of the wound were rolled up, and the blood and flesh had turned black after drying. It was excruciatingly painful when he moved.

If it weren't for the other Warriors who risked their lives to protect him, he would have died in the battle long ago.

To save him, many Warriors fell down and never got up again.

Tears welled up in his eyes. Edgar tried to hold them back but failed.

His father said that men shed blood but not tears. They were Warriors!

Due to overdrawing his body, Edgar's body had become numb, and he had no feeling. He wielded the Lightsabers mechanically.

He felt like he was walking lightly and was on the verge of collapse.

But the anger burned in his chest, like a volcano that was accumulating energy and could erupt at any time. He wished he could burn those damn orcs to death.

Blood was flowing, and it seemed as if his body was about to break apart.

It hurt!

Suddenly, a mace flew over and hit Edgar's chest hard, turning his body into a bloody mess.

He slumped to the ground, and the Lightsabers dimmed and lost their brilliance.
