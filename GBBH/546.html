They were in the midst of a vigorous discussion. Some were excited, some shook their heads, and some sighed.

There was a reward notice posted at the gate of the Gomez Villa, and a group of security guards stood next to it, their eyes wide open, scanning the crowd vigilantly.

Someone exclaimed, "The Gomez family is so rich. They offer a reward of 50 million dollars to cure Elaina!"

"Fifty million dollars is not the point. Whoever can treat Elaina will have the opportunity to become friends with her. Later, the guy might marry her and make a fortune. The Gomez family is a wealthy family with assets of tens of billions of dollars."

"Too bad I'm not a famous doctor, an expert, or a professor. Otherwise, I will definitely seize this opportunity to change my destiny."

Everyone chatted with each other excitedly.

Alexander took a glance at the notice. Orlando must be desperate. Otherwise, he wouldn't have offered the reward.

At that moment, a black Mercedes-Benz drove up, and a middle-aged man in a suit got out of the car.

With a black suitcase, he grinned with delight while reading the notice.

Someone recognized him and kindly reminded him, "Archie, you have to think carefully. Although you are a medical expert, Ms. Elaina Gomez's strange disease is really difficult to cure!"

Archie glared at the guy.

"I have been practicing medicine for more than 20 years, and I am now a professor. Don't I have a chance?"

The guy smiled, "Archie, I don't look down on you. But have you heard of Matthew, one of the best medical experts in Kingsdon?"

"Did Matthew come here, too? No way! His professional knowledge is indeed better than mine."

Archie was quite modest.

"Matthew was helpless with Elaina's illness. As a result, Orlando lost his temper on the spot, calling him a liar. In the end, Matthew was beaten out.

"The Gomez family is originally a medical family, and Orlando is also the descendant of a famous doctor, but he couldn't treat Elaina's illness. Archie, do you really have to go in and get beaten?"

While persuading Archie, the guy laughed and sized him up, obviously not optimistic about him.

Archie was stunned and stammered, "If that's the case, then forget it. It turns out that the 50 million dollars is not easy to earn..."

Suddenly, a roar came from inside the Gomez Villa.

"Get lost!

"What an incompetent doctor!"

A doctor in a white coat was pushed out viciously by a group of security guards.

Seeing this, Archie wiped the cold sweat from his forehead, feeling glad that he did not act rashly.

"More than a dozen doctors went in these few days, including a Nobel Prize winner in medicine. They were all powerless and were kicked out by Orlando."

"They failed to cure Elaina and even worsened her condition, so Orlando got furious."

"Although 50 million dollars is tempting and Elaina is a beautiful lady, no one can get them!"

Everyone talked about it.

Some doctors from other places, who had heard the news and had come here to try their luck, immediately became dejected.

"Excuse me!"

Alexander squeezed through the crowd and gave it a gentle push, releasing so much force that those standing in front retreated to the sides like grass blown over by the wind.

"Let me in. I can cure Ms. Elaina Gomez."

Alexander's confident attitude instantly caused an uproar at the scene.

Everyone, including the Gomez family's security guards, stared at him with wide eyes, looking him up and down.

Seeing that he was so young and dressed in ordinary clothes, they immediately disdained him.

Archie couldn't help but ask, "Which hospital do you work in? Which university did you graduate from?"

Alexander replied calmly, "I am not a doctor, and I have never been to medical school."

His reply caused another uproar at the scene.

"I can tell from his appearance that he is a real liar and definitely not a doctor."

"He must want to try his luck. It doesn't cost anything anyway. Maybe he will succeed."

"I'm afraid he will be beaten out. The Gomez family will kill such liars."

Everyone mocked Alexander with contempt.

A security guard yelled fiercely, "Damn, you're not a doctor, and you've never studied medicine. How dare you treat Ms. Elaina Gomez? Are you fucking crazy?"
