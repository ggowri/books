Amanda clutched her stomach, collapsing to the ground in pain.

Alexander stopped Marvin, who was about to strike again. He stepped toward Amanda, looking down at her coldly.

"Amanda, since you're a woman, I'll give you a chance. If you're willing to be my spy, I'll let you go."

Amanda lifted her head, glaring at Alexander with hatred.

"You want me to betray the General? Dream on!"

Alexander chuckled and gestured to Marvin.

Marvin sneered as he grabbed Amanda and dragged her into a nearby thicket.

Shortly after, Marvin returned with Amanda's ID badge, along with the badges of four others, completing an elite squad of five.

Alexander held the five shiny badges and grinned.

"This is my gift for Schroder!"

At the Hansen family's banquet, Andrew stood by the entrance, muttering curses against Alexander.

"Hmph! Why hasn't that bastard Alexander shown up yet? Did he run away? Damn it, if he dares to run, I'll make his daughter pay..."

The people nearby looked at Andrew with disdain. Alexander's daughter was his own granddaughter, and yet he showed no familial loyalty.

The banquet had been underway for nearly an hour, yet there was still no sign of Alexander.

Schroder was growing impatient, glancing at the entrance from time to time.

Jane giggled smugly.

"General, I think Alexander and Ava are too afraid to come. They must be hiding or have already fled Loscity."

Suddenly, gunshots rang out, and Schroder's expression changed instantly. He tried to contact the elite squad, but there was no response.

"What's going on? Amanda isn't responding either..."

Schroder knew something had gone wrong and grew anxious.

He quickly ordered his men, "Go check on Amanda and the others immediately, and bring Alexander and Ava to me!"

"General, what's the rush? Did you need me for something?"

Suddenly, a magnetic male voice rang out.

The moment Jane heard the familiar voice, she trembled and instinctively stepped away from Schroder.

Her emotions were complicated; she both hated Alexander and deeply regretted her choices.

Sure enough, it was Alexander, walking in with his arm around Ava's waist, smiling brightly.

Alexander looked dashing, and Ava was stunningly elegant, drawing everyone's attention.

He met Schroder's fierce gaze without fear, walking over with a confident, dazzling smile that captivated everyone.

Following behind Alexander were Augusta, Marvin, William, and Charlize.

The four wore expressionless faces, their cold eyes fixed on Schroder. Their synchronized steps radiated a sharp killing intent, like blades and swords combined.

"Clatter!"

Alexander tossed Amanda and the others' ID badges at Schroder's feet, smiling as he said, "General, I ran into some bandits on the way here, so I'm a bit late. Hope you don't mind."

Seeing his mistress Amanda's badge, Schroder's eye twitched uncontrollably as he growled, "Alexander, you dared to mess with my woman? Damn you!"

Alexander calmly shot back.

"General, do those bandits have something to do with you?"

Schroder was stunned, unable to respond.

His secret deployment of special forces to ambush Alexander couldn't be exposed, or he'd face severe consequences back at the headquarters.

The room fell silent, everyone watching in stillness.

No one had expected Alexander to immediately deliver a blow to Schroder.

Schroder, who had been so commanding just moments ago, suddenly seemed helpless in front of Alexander, unsure how to react.

Seeing this, Jane's expression turned grim.

She recalled how Ryan, Sebastian, and others who had been high and mighty, were all trampled under Alexander's feet.

"Could it be that even Schroder can't handle Alexander?"

Jane's heart was filled with deep fear and regret. Was her original choice really wrong?

At this time, Andrew was the first to jump up, pointing at Alexander and cursing, "Alexander, how can you speak like that? Apologize to the General immediately!"
