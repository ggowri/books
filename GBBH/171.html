They were the goddesses of Loscity, the stuff of countless dreams for many men.

What made it even more infuriating was how Alexander seemed completely absorbed in his food and drink, as if nothing else mattered.

Ava and Lara occasionally poured him drinks, and their attention only fueled the jealousy of the onlookers, many of whom would have gladly traded places with him.

Despite his irritation, he couldn't confront Alexander. After losing their last bet, he was forbidden from pursuing Ava any further.

"Are you guys planning to make a move on Alexander? He's got the magic powder formula under his control, and he's riding high right now. You'd find it tough to take him down."

Houtte's smile widened, a glint of mischief in his eyes.

"What if I could get my hands on that magic powder formula?"

Orion perked up, curiosity piqued. "What do you need from me?"

"If I manage to snag the magic powder formula, how about I get a stake in the Hansen family's businesses?"

Those emerging industries were cash cows, and the Hansen family had poured immense resources into nurturing them.

They were on the brink of reaping the rewards, and letting the Cripples get involved could spell disaster.

"Listen, if you let me in, I'll get rid of Lara for you and help you become the head of the Hansen family."

While he held a certain advantage in the Hansen family, he wasn't the top heir.

If Ralph decided to pass the title to Lara, he would face significant opposition.

Houtte was over the moon, clinking glasses with Orion in a show of solidarity.

Meanwhile, Alexander had noticed Houtte and Orion huddled together, whispering conspiratorially.

Whatever scheme they were plotting, he didn't care.

In the face of his overwhelming power, all their conspiracies would crumble like sandcastles against the tide.

At that moment, Alexander caught a whiff of several powerful presences concealed within the private booths. Clearly, these individuals were the elite of the Kaylon, and the atmosphere surrounding them was charged with an undeniable tension.

Surrounding them were more than twenty imposing figures, clearly there for the security of the auction items.

Inwardly, Alexander mused to himself about Kaylon's impressive strength.

Oscar announced with an air of enthusiasm, "Ladies and gentlemen. The Kaylon warmly welcomes you to tonight's auction!

"We've carefully prepared a selection of exquisite jewelry and invaluable antiques. May you all find the treasures you seek!"
