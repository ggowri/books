Augusta had already taken action, hitting the bald guy and the skinny guy with his huge fists behind their heads, respectively.

With a thud, the two men fell silently to the ground.

William was even more decisive as he pulled out his silenced pistol and fired two shots.

He was supposed to be a gunman. After his power improved, the speed of his gun was comparable to that of a super cowboy in the movie, and his bullets never missed their targets.

The remaining two were immediately shot in the shoulder, covering their shoulders and about to scream, but they were pinched by Augusta's neck and fainted.

The four guys who had been so cheerful just a few moments before all fell to the ground.

Augusta hurriedly tied them up and dragged them to the secluded woods to torture them severely.

To stay alive, they had to transfer all the money in their accounts to Augusta, which added up to over a million dollars.

Augusta hadn't been on Treasure Island long, but he had already made almost ten million dollars by doing this.

After the transfer, Augusta searched the four men and found four bottles of Level 2 power elixir, and of course, it was diluted.

"Sir, spare our lives! We've already done what you asked, so shouldn't you let us go?"

The bald man kept pleading.

The other three looked at Augusta expectantly.

"Well..."

Augusta sneered, walked over to them calmly, and tied them together with a rope.

"What do you want? Sir, you swore you'd let us go..."

The bald man was anxious.

Augusta said expressionlessly, "I didn't say I wouldn't let you go, but I didn't tell you what I would do."

The four heard the first half of the sentence with a sudden burst of relief, but the second half made them feel like they had fallen into hell.

"Let them go! It's up to them whether will survive or not."

Augusta waved her hand.

A few henchmen yanked the four men, who were bundled up into a ball, and threw them straight over the cliff into the sea.

William watched it all with a grin on his face.

"We didn't break our promise, and it's up to their destiny whether they live or not."

"Ha-ha-ha!"

Augusta smiled and patted William on the shoulder. Then, he gave him two bottles of Level 2 power elixir.

The two excitedly poured it all into their mouths and sat cross-legged as Alexander had taught them to meditate.

Alexander taught them Warrior's way of meditating on a whim, and he wondered if it would work on ordinary people.

After a period of experimentation, both Augusta and William found it useful.

They boosted their strength faster, and even if they took more Level 2 power elixir, there were no so-called side effects.

Of course, it would be almost impossible for them to cultivate the Force and become a Warrior if they tried to rely on this meditation method.

After just over ten minutes, Augusta suddenly opened his eyes.

His eyes in the darkness were filled with amazement as he felt that his physical qualities had improved a lot more. Although no mutant abilities had appeared, his power had increased again.

Augusta took hundreds of thousands of dollars out of the money he had just grabbed and distributed it to his men.

Everyone was excited and looking forward to the next action at once.

"Come on. Let's continue!"

The two led a patrol around the Red Umbrella Group as they kept robbing and killing their enemies.

The people missing from the Red Umbrella Group were increasing in number, and they could never find the enemy, so they had to blame it all on poor security.

They organized a security team in the manner of the Brilliant Group and started patrolling.

But it was ineffective, and instead, several patrols went missing.

Augusta and William became increasingly powerful through their battles.

The Red Umbrella Group had set up traps twice, and hundreds of people surrounded them or were killed by the two of them.

On the contrary, the Red Umbrella Group lost dozens of people, and they were furious at once.

Treasure Island was an important location for the Red Umbrella Group, and they were researching power elixir on the island, so they couldn't afford to lose it.

But as the balance of power tipped, the Red Umbrella Group was at a disadvantage on the island.

With each passing day, Augusta and William were stronger than the day before.

They walked like real ghosts on Treasure Island, capturing and killing the people of the Red Umbrella Group and taking their deposits and power elixir.
