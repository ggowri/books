Alexander paid no attention to Sienna or her group. Instead, he focused on comforting Ashley with gentle patience, gave Ava a warm embrace, and only then turned back to face Sienna with ice in his eyes.

"You harassed my woman and my daughter and then tried to attack me. So why wouldn't I beat you?"

As Alexander spoke, he began walking toward Sienna.

Each unhurried step was like a hammer blow to her already frayed nerves.

"Stay back! What do you think you're doing?" Her voice rose in panic. "I'm Sienna Rhodes of the Newbrook Rhodes family. Don't you dare lay another hand on me!"

A contemptuous smile crossed Alexander's face as he continued his advance, completely unfazed by her threats.

"Stop right there! How dare you hit Ms. Rhodes, you bastard!"

The security guards finally snapped into action, moving to block Alexander's path. They had failed in their duty to protect Sienna and were desperate to make amends.

"Teach him a lesson! Beat him up!" Sienna screamed, her face contorted with rage.

The other women joined in, berating the security guards. "What are you waiting for? He just hit Sienna! Get him!"

"Slap their face—him, that woman, and the kid!"

The guards, fueled by the women's demands, charged at Alexander with renewed vigor.

But despite being outnumbered, Alexander viewed them as nothing more than insects beneath his feet.

He didn't even bother to move as they approached, a contemptuous smile on his face.

One guard managed to land a solid punch to Alexander's chest.

His triumphant grin lasted only a split second before excruciating pain shot through his arm. The force of hitting Alexander's chest had ricocheted back through his own limb.

"Argh!" The guard collapsed to the ground, clutching his mangled right hand, writhing in agony.

The other bodyguards joined in, raining blows on Alexander.

Yet he stood there like a statue, making no move to fight back, simply taking their assault in silence.

But everyone who struck him paid a steep price. Their attacks seemed to rebound with devastating force—hands snapped, feet fractured, leaving them either writhing on the ground in agony or curled up like wounded animals.

In reality, their blows never truly connected. Alexander's body was surrounded by an invisible force field, and when mere mortals tried to breach it, the consequences were brutal.

"What the hell is this guy made of, steel?"

"Motherfucker, he can't be human!"

The women backed away in terror.

Alexander ignored them completely, continuing his slow, deliberate advance toward Sienna.

"Stop him! Somebody stop him! What are you all waiting for? Do something!" Sienna's voice cracked with panic.

The heavyset woman had managed to get back on her feet. She circled behind Alexander, grabbed a microphone stand from the stage, and swung it at him with all her might.

The stand shattered against Alexander's body like glass hitting concrete.

The woman screamed in pain, blood streaming from her hands where the metal had torn into them.

Alexander halted, fixing the heavyset woman with an icy stare.

She began trembling uncontrollably and stumbled backward in terror, losing her balance and tumbling off the stage.

The crowd erupted into chaos once again.

The audience was stunned by the handsome young man's composure. Despite being surrounded by multiple attackers, he handled the situation with remarkable ease.

They were also disgusted, even outraged, by Sienna's group's tyrannical and shameless behavior.

Meanwhile, Sienna was scrambling backward, her face contorted with fear and malice.

"Someone protect me! Don't let him near me!" she yelled. "I'll pay a million dollars to whoever breaks his legs. And I'll even be your girlfriend!"

Faced with Alexander's overwhelming presence, Sienna had completely lost her composure, resorting to using money and her looks to entice help.

Her offer immediately sparked excitement among several men in the crowd below.

This was their chance to get in Sienna's good graces. If they succeeded, they'd get both money and a beautiful girlfriend.

"Ms. Rhodes, are you serious about that offer?" a burly man called out, already cracking his knuckles in anticipation. "The million dollars and being your boyfriend—that's a real promise?"

Sienna continued sweetening the deal, trying to entice someone to help her. "I swear I'm telling the truth! Break his legs, and I'll make your dreams come true.

"I'll get the best lawyers to protect you. You won't go to jail for breaking his legs—you'll just become my boyfriend."
