As Alexander delicately carved into the gemstone, it transformed before his eyes, radiating a grand and mysterious aura.

A wave of excitement surged through him, inspiring him to continue the intricate work.

The Energy Convergence Matrix required one hundred and eight Spellweave Patterns, and he had just completed his first.

The second followed smoothly, then the third and fourth.

Alexander was hitting his stride, becoming more adept with every stroke.

But when he reached the seventh pattern, he felt the Force draining too quickly, causing his carving knife to tremble slightly.

Suddenly, the completed patterns on the stone blazed with bright yellow light.

Without warning, the gemstone shattered with a deafening crack, disintegrating into dust.

Alexander stared blankly, disbelief washing over him. He hadn't expected his very first attempt to end in failure.

"Thank goodness I haven't recklessly tested with the premium gemstone. Okay, let's do this again. I need to get better at this!"

Undeterred, Alexander set to work again, even more careful this time.

Yet he only managed to carve twelve patterns before a wave of confusion washed over him, and the gemstone exploded once more, reduced to scrap.

Wiping the sweat from his palms, Alexander reflected on his recent failures, mentally replaying the lessons learned.

Holding the carving knife in the air, he practiced the movements, tuning into the precise techniques needed.

With his eyes shut, Alexander aimed to relax and attune himself to everything around him.

This included the chirping of crickets, the distant hum of engines, and the rustling of leaves as a wild cat stealthily crossed the yard, and so on.

Despite his closed eyes, these sounds painted a vivid movie in his mind, each detail clearer than ever.

He gained a deeper understanding of the Spellweave Patterns and grasped the remaining first-class gemstone with determination.

As he pressed his thumb down on the knife, a drop of blood welled up from his fingertip.

With the crimson liquid staining his knife, he began to carve into the yellow gemstone once more.

The intricate Spellweave Patterns blossomed under his skillful hand, his movements smooth and flowing like water.

At that moment, he transcended into a state of almost ethereal concentration, his spirit intertwined with the night sky and the gemstone itself.

This time, he successfully etched thirty-six Spellweave Patterns into the stone.

When the thirty-sixth was complete, an immense energy surged from the gemstone as it seemed to inhale the very essence of the universe into its depths.

Realizing he had reached his limit, Alexander stepped back; his current abilities allowed for only thirty-six patterns, which was still far from completing the full matrix.

However, this was good news—the stone, imbued with these patterns, would aid him in accelerating his mastery of the Force and would also offer some defensive properties.

He traced the surface of the transformed gemstone, unable to contain his excitement.

It pulsed with a gentle yellow glow, the beautiful patterns shimmering with an otherworldly charm.

"This is tough!" Alexander sighed, staring longingly at the premium gemstone, knowing he'd have to leave it untouched for now.

"I'll take my time. I can always find Xavier to get some more stones for practice. I'm sure I'll be able to carve the full Energy Convergence Matrix eventually."

Suddenly, a wave of dizziness swept over him, a fatigue slamming into him like a freight train. He felt weak and spent, as if he had been deprived of sleep for days.

"I'm so tired; this is draining!" Alexander muttered, his face pale as he fought against the overwhelming exhaustion weighing down on him.

Despite carving for only half an hour, Alexander had drained all the Force he could command and was mentally exhausted.

He settled into a meditative pose to recover his strength.

At that moment, the yellow gemstone in his hand shuddered, releasing a surge of mystical energy from its thirty-six Spellweave Patterns, which enveloped Alexander.

Immediately, he felt an enhanced capacity to absorb external energy.

Previous meditations had been like finding tiny streams, but now, with the help of the Spellweave Pattern, those streams had widened into small rivers.

Alexander greedily absorbed the energy from the surroundings, refining it all into the Force, which he stored within himself.
