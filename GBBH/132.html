Alexander called Augusta to investigate Jackson's details, focusing on tracking down the his location.

After setting things up, Alexander sat down to meditate.

As he entered a state of deep concentration, he felt the Force he'd used was completely restored.

However, most of the Force left by James in his body remained frozen, like ice in the Arctic, waiting to be slowly absorbed.

When Alexander finally opened his eyes, it was already midnight.

Augusta had just sent over a location. "Boss, that Jackson is hiding at a nearby motel. The guy thinks the most dangerous place is the safest."

Alexander chuckled. Jackson had no idea about the Force mark on him.

By now, the Fairfax family had left, and Alexander drove his beat-up Toyota into the night.

Before long, a black Mercedes appeared from the opposite direction, following from a distance.

Alexander's heightened senses quickly picked up the tail.

He squinted his eyes, ready for some action. "It's likely Sebastian's men. Is it the dark wizard he hired?"

Alexander kept driving, pressing the gas and sped toward the motel.

By 11 p.m., only a dim orange light illuminated the motel's two-story entrance, with no staff in sight.

Alexander slipped in quietly, where Augusta met him and pointed upstairs.

Following his lead, Alexander reached Room 206.

"Keep watch downstairs. Let me know if anything happens." After instructing Augusta, Alexander leaned in to observe through the door crack.

Inside, he saw Jackson on the bed, wrapped around a naked woman, both in deep sleep.

Judging by the messy sheets, they'd clearly had an intense evening.

Alexander gripped the handle and turned it gently, using the Force to break the lock effortlessly.

As he entered, neither woke up—they'd exhausted themselves.

Moving to the bed, Alexander pressed a nerve on the woman's neck.

She immediately fell into a deeper, unshakeable sleep.

"Who's there?" Jackson jolted awake, staring in fear at Alexander.

"Awake? Hand over the money, and I'll let you die without pain." Alexander's voice was calm.

"It's you!" Recognizing Alexander, Jackson's face turned grim.

He hadn't expected him to find him so quickly.

"Alright, alright, I'll give you the money... it's just some money. Here, I'll give it to you," Jackson stammered, reaching under his pillow.

Alexander saw through the act and smirked. This man was still trying to resist.

The next second, Jackson pulled a gun and aimed it at Alexander, ready to pull the trigger.

With a vicious look on his face, Jackson felt confident, though his speed seemed childishly slow to Alexander.

Smack!

Before Jackson could fire, Alexander slapped him hard, sending him flying off the bed and crashing to the floor, blood pouring from his mouth.

"Don't kill me... I already gave most of the money to Mr. Satan. I still have ten million here. You can have it... take it all."

Jackson trembled as he pulled out a check from under the bed.

"Satan... are you one of his men?" Alexander's eyes flashed with surprise.

Satan was a Mafia godfather, infamous for scams, drug trafficking, kidnapping, and murder—the only one who could rival "Reaper", Satan’s father.

"Yes, yes, I work for Satan. Please, let me go. You won’t want his wrath." Jackson begged, deeply regretting his decisions.

If he'd known Alexander was this capable, he'd never have dared to impersonate him.

Alexander's lips curled into a wicked smile.

Seeing the smile, Jackson grew hopeful, thinking his life might be spared.

"Since you work for Mr. Satan, that's all the more reason to end you here. I'd rather not leave loose ends."
