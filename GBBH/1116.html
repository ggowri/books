"What do you want from me?"

Loren's voice was hoarse, barely a whisper.

The longer he held his position, the more his nerves frayed, the quicker his strength drained, and the more his weakness became exposed.

Alexander remained unmoved, his face expressionless and calm.

"Yinson starts with the world leaders, causes global chaos, and then reactivates the Apocalypse Project. Am I right?"

Loren's eyes widened in shock. "How... how did you know?"

Alexander chuckled coldly, "Today's just not your lucky day. Go wait for Yinson in hell. He'll be joining you soon enough."

At that, Loren's heart twisted with fear.

Without wasting another second, Alexander unleashed his unseen Force, and with a single motion of his hand, the entire space seemed to freeze.

Time itself felt suspended, everything around them frozen in place.

Loren's eyes bulged as he struggled, trying to move, but his body was completely paralyzed under the pressure of Alexander's power.

His right hand, which still gripped the dagger, was helplessly controlled, twisting until the blade pointed directly at his own heart.

"Please... don't kill me! Yinson sent me... it's not my fault..."

Loren's face contorted in a mix of fear and rage as he desperately pleaded for his life.

The dagger, cold and sharp, pressed against his left chest, slowly sinking deeper.

"No! Please, I beg you!"

Loren's cries echoed with desperation, the sound so loud that Melin clamped her hands over her ears.

"Stop struggling. The more you fight, the worse it will get."

Alexander sneered, his hand rising to strike again, the Force amplifying as he gestured with a single motion.

"Whoosh!"

Blood splattered across the room as Loren's own dagger stabbed into his chest, driven by the immense force.

At that moment, the excruciating pain jolted Loren's body back into action, and with sheer willpower, he pulled the blade away, ignoring the agony.

The dagger hadn't pierced his heart, but it had sunk deep into his chest, blood pouring from the wound like a crimson fountain.

Loren had been gravely injured but not dead—yet.

"Ah! It hurts!"

The pain seemed to push him over the edge.

Loren's eyes burned with madness as he realized there was no escaping now. With a sudden, reckless motion, he reached into his pocket and pulled out a small vial of pale red liquid. He jabbed the needle into his arm, injecting the entire contents of the vial into his veins.

The color of the serum was familiar to Alexander, and his expression darkened instantly. This was a diluted form of the Immortality Serum.

It seemed Yinson had been busy, making new advancements in the serum's use.

The effects of the red serum were immediate. Loren's body seemed to surge with renewed vitality. His blood, once flowing freely from his wound, slowed to a stop, and his energy seemed to amplify.

His strength had skyrocketed. What was once a metahuman power comparable to level four had now reached an entirely new level.

"I'll kill you!"

Loren roared in fury, hurling the dagger toward Melin with lethal speed.

Alexander caught off guard and yanked Melin out of the way just in time. The dagger tore through the air with a force so strong that it pierced the wall behind them.

Without missing a beat, Loren leaped backward with a speed that defied belief, his body vanishing into the night like a phantom.

Alexander cursed under his breath, surprised by Loren's cunning.

Melin dashed to the window, gun in hand, and fired.

"Bang! Bang! Bang!"

The deafening shots echoed through the building, startling everyone in the police station.

"What's going on?"

"Who's shooting?"

Suddenly, the blaring sound of alarms filled the air, and Oliver and his team rushed to the scene.

"You take care of Melin. I'll go after the assassin."

With that, Alexander darted after Loren, following the trail of blood.

He couldn't let the old man get away—not when Loren was so dangerous, stronger than Rosalia or Charlize.

In the dark of the night, Loren's speed was remarkable.

Despite his small, frail frame, he moved like a creature of the shadows, faster than Alexander had anticipated.

But Alexander was faster. He closed the gap between them, relentlessly pursuing his prey.

Realizing he couldn't outrun him, Loren swung around, tossing several small, black, spherical objects behind him.

Grenades.

Alexander dove behind a large tree just in time.

"Boom! Boom! Boom!"

The explosions rocked the street, sending plumes of smoke into the air.

The shockwaves tore through the area, and several innocent bystanders were caught in the blast, their screams filling the air as blood stained the pavement.

Alexander's face twisted in frustration. "Letting that old bastard get away..."

The situation was bad—Loren had used everything he had to escape, including the deadly serum, and now was out of reach.

But Alexander wasn't disheartened. Loren had already taken serious damage, and there was no way he'd get out of this unscathed. He wouldn't survive long—especially after the red serum had given him a temporary boost.
