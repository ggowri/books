Hugh was no amateur; he recognized that Alexander's grip was anything but ordinary.

But the speed of Alexander's move left him no time to change tactics.

Just moments ago, Hugh had been brimming with confidence. Now, he had to throw all caution to the wind.

Summoning every ounce of strength, he felt like he was pushing himself to the absolute limit.

The two hands clashed violently, and a series of sickening cracks echoed in the air.

Hugh's fingers shattered upon impact, his hand contorting grotesquely.

Agonizing pain surged through him, and he instinctively clutched his mangled hand, stumbling back in shock.

Alexander, relentless, pressed forward, landing a forceful palm strike right on Hugh's chest.

Hugh barely let out a grunt before he collapsed, unconscious.

The sight left Sebastian completely dumbfounded.

The so-called top fighter of Loscity was taken down in mere moments by Alexander.

Stepping closer to Sebastian, Alexander said coldly, "Sebastian, still not ready to talk? Where's Guzman hiding?"

Sebastian tilted his chin defiantly. "Go ahead, kill me. I won't betray Guzman!"

He was trying to exude a sense of bravery.

"Such loyalty, even in the face of death," Alexander replied with a cynical laugh.

He then added, "But let's see how you handle something worse than dying."

Alexander slapped his palm against Sebastian's forehead, and the Force surged into him like a swarm of needles piercing his brain.

Sebastian stifled a groan. The acute agony racing through him felt unlike anything he had endured before.

It was as if a legion of daggers had taken residence in his skull, rapidly spreading pain throughout his body.

Every muscle, every bone, felt like it was being put through the wringer of the harshest torture.

The agony seemed to have settled deep within his soul, tormenting him mercilessly like a relentless shadow.

Sebastian desperately wanted to scream, but an invisible weight seemed to crush his throat, rendering him unable to voice a single word.

He crumpled to the floor, curling into himself as if trying to protect his very soul from the onslaught of pain.

Alexander eyed him with utter indifference. This was a man who had committed countless sins and had previously plotted his demise. He felt no sympathy for someone like Sebastian; the torment Sebastian was dishing out was far more fitting than he deserved.

Most people feared death because of potential pain, but Sebastian was now experiencing suffering that made the prospect of dying seem merciful.

Such an unending torment would be unbearable even for the demons of hell!

A few excruciating minutes later, Sebastian's body was convulsing, foam bubbling at his lips.

He was on the brink of breaking. With a rough kick to the side, Alexander withdrew his Force.

Sebastian lay on the ground, gasping for air, drenched in cold sweat, creating a puddle underneath him.

"Ready to spill your guts? Do you still want to play the tough guy? Trust me, you won't be dying anytime soon unless I let you."

A smile graced Alexander's lips, but it was anything but kind.

"You... you're a monster! An outright monster!"

Panic filled Sebastian's eyes.

He had never encountered anything like the torment Alexander had unleashed on him.

The suffering Sebastian endured now exceeded anything he experienced in the hospital by tenfold, a hundredfold; it felt as if his soul had descended into the very depths of hell, where even the torments there would pale in comparison to this anguish.

"Still holding on to that stubbornness, huh?"

Alexander raised his hand again.

Desperation flooded Sebastian's heart.

"Do you truly want to be loyal to Guzman?"

Alexander's patience was wearing thin as he seized Sebastian by the throat, dragging him along like a ragdoll.

"No! Please, don't!"

Sebastian was painfully aware of the consequences of keeping silent; he feared the torture could drive him to madness.

"Speak!"

Alexander's voice boomed like thunder rumbling overhead.

Sebastian's breath came in rapid bursts, his chest heaving.

"Alright, alright! I'll tell you! Guzman is crafty, and though he trusts me, I can't always pinpoint his location.

"Especially after coming to Loscity, we only communicate by phone..."

Before he could finish, a fierce glare from Alexander cut him off. "Don't even think about pulling a fast one!"

Sebastian waved his hands frantically; desperation etched in his mien.

"No! Don't kill me! I know where he spends the most time. Guzman has invested heavily in a secret base out on the water... It's on an island just about 350 miles from Tijuanea in Mexico..."
