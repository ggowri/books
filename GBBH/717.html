The guy was clearly trying to show off in front of Alexander, flexing his masculine dominance.

He'd almost sealed the deal with Jane and was prepared to have some fun in his office.

But as soon as Alexander showed up, Jane got all nervous, which pissed Ken off and made him want to challenge Alexander.

"Mr. Lakers, please don't..."

"Jane, what's going on? We had a deal: you spend a few days with me, and I get you both on that plane. Are you backing out now?"

"Do you two want to leave Loscity alive or not?"

Ken's face twisted into an unpleasant smile.

"Better do as I say. Play nice, and I'll make sure you both get out of Loscity safely. Otherwise, you'll get infected: organ failure, tissue damage, the works."

Jane was at a loss for words. She couldn't bring herself to face Alexander in this situation.

Seeing Jane's compliance, Ken's smirk grew more confident.

"What's wrong with spending a few days with me? Afraid your ex-husband will interfere? He's just your ex! He has no right to stop you.

"If this guy tries anything, I'll have security teach him a lesson right away!"

As Ken bragged, he kept making unwanted advances toward Jane right in front of Alexander.

He was both trying to provoke Alexander and satisfy his twisted ego.

While Alexander remained still, Charlize stepped forward and landed a sharp slap across Ken's face.

"You... you actually hit me? Do you know who I am? I'm the manager here!"

"Why did you hit our manager?" they demanded aggressively.

Charlize cut in with an icy tone. "We're from the Saint Group. What exactly do you plan to do about it? Are you looking for trouble?"

"The Saint Group?"

The two guards were already frozen in place, fear etched on their faces. They forced out an apologetic smile towards Alexander.

"Sorry, sorry! It's a misunderstanding, all a misunderstanding... We, uh, we need to get back to our posts."

They shot Ken a dirty look before practically sprinting away.

Ken bowed deeply to Alexander, practically folding himself in half. "I'm so sorry, I had no idea you were with the Saint Group. It was all a huge misunderstanding."

Jane watched the scene unfold with mixed emotions churning inside her.

Seeing that Alexander was no longer at the bottom of the social ladder made her feelings even more complicated. She felt a bitter cocktail of regret and sadness.

At that moment, Alexander stepped toward Ken.

Ken jumped like a startled rabbit, keeping his head bowed low in nervous anticipation.

"I'm sorry, I'm so incredibly sorry... Please be merciful... I was wrong. I promise I won't bother Ms. Fairfax anymore, and I'll make sure they get on that plane."

He kept apologizing profusely, trying to appease Alexander with apparent fear in his voice.

"If I ever see you again, you'll end up as fish food at the bottom of the lake."

Alexander's voice dripped with murderous intent, his tone so cold it seemed to drop the temperature several degrees.

Ken trembled violently, cold sweat soaking through his clothes.

After delivering his threat, Alexander shot one last penetrating look at Jane before walking away with the two women.

Despite Jane's hopeful gaze following him, Alexander didn't look back once. He felt no attachment to this woman. He only felt disgusted.
