Yinson, deep in the underground base, vaguely felt a great threat and looked stern. He never expected these people to be able to control such terrifying weapons.

At the same time, on the endless ice field in the north of Raquel, several large-scale military buildings with a sci-fi look stand on the vast plain.

There was Raquel's intercontinental ballistic missile early-warning base. This hidden base couldn't be found on civilian maps.

On military maps, this area was marked with a striking red line. Without special permission, no one could enter this area within three miles of the base.

The base was a complex of buildings on the ice field. It extended hundreds of feet deep into the hard permafrost.

There were not only the most advanced strategic early-warning radars that could detect targets thousands of miles away but also a launch base for dozens of intercontinental missiles carrying atomic warheads.

The most powerful country on the earth had tens of thousands of atomic warheads.

This base on the ice field was on duty all year, without any slack.

Roderick Byrne, the commander of this base, was sitting on a tall seat in the central control room, looking coldly at the data on the circular LCD screen.

For the sake of his family and himself, he wanted to survive.

Roderick's face was pale with a tinge of ashen color. Just half an hour ago, he received a warning from the military department.

In order to eliminate the monsters, atomic weapons would be launched globally immediately.

"Damn it!" Roderick cursed with a ferocious expression.

Raquel had fallen into the monster crisis, and many small and medium-sized countries were deeply mired in it.

Roderick thought, "Destroy the whole world? These crazy lunatics. Go to hell!

Maybe they're just drunk or have some mental abnormality. But it's said that all countries around the world have received this warning. It should be a prank, right?"

Roderick tried to calm down, but he could hardly convince himself.

All countries around the world had received the warning sent by Rudolph that the earth would be destroyed along with those monsters by atomic weapons.

One hour after receiving the warning, the astronauts in Moon Walker, the only still-operating space station, issued a warning that hundreds of intercontinental missiles had been launched.

That was not all. In just a few minutes, hundreds of intercontinental missiles had been launched, and their landing points covered all regions of the world.

In particular, some powerful countries were the key targets.

The astronauts reacted quickly. Immediately, they used the space-based weapons deployed on the space station and in space orbit and successfully shot down dozens of intercontinental missiles.

However, a small part of the remaining ones still reached their target locations, raising huge mushroom clouds.

[Counter-attack with all your might! Activate the emergency mechanism and stop them from launching more missiles!] This was the order Roderick received.

Streams of flames soared from the ice field. This was the place closest to Adcock. In less than half an hour, these terrifying missiles could hit the opponents' capitals.

For a time, more and more glows flickered in the night sky. The number of missiles fired by several parties this time was as many as over five hundred.

The images transmitted back by the monitoring system on Moon Walker were like a fireworks show.

On the blue earth, huge flames rose, like a beautiful yet destructive fireworks show.

On the electronic map, centered around capitals, together with several continents, a bloody and terrifying death net was formed.

Just when the first round of atomic weapons was launched, the highest-level emergency shelter warning was immediately issued.

On Treasure Island, it was in complete chaos.

"Hurry into the underground shelter!" Augusta and a group of fully armed team members protected Ava, Betty, and many other high-level officials, entering the underground shelter in an orderly manner.

"Alexander!" Standing at the entrance of the shelter, Ava looked into the distance, her beautiful eyes filled with tears, looking extremely pitiful.

"Alexander will be fine. He has his fortune." Augusta looked solemn as he pressed the electric button.
