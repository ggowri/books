A few people rushed forward to help him up. The purple-robed disciple was pale, his clothes torn, and blood trickled from the corner of his mouth. He trembled, unable to say a word. The others exchanged glances, then silently turned and left the stone chamber.

Alexander watched them go, deep in thought. Tigris was at least a level-four warrior. If he could tame the beast, it would be a huge asset. But could he really pull it off...

Determined, he clenched his fists. A surge of protective energy erupted around him as he tapped his toes against the ground. Bang! Bang! Bang! His figure shot forward like a streak of lightning, ghostly yet swift, gliding toward the cave.

He focused his gaze. A massive tiger, nearly half his height, stood before him. A bold character was etched on its forehead, its body covered in black-and-white stripes. Its tail lashed like a whip, and its glowing red eyes burned with a chilling ferocity.

Tigris pressed its paws into the ground, releasing arcs of white lightning. A deafening wind roared as the beast lunged like an arrow straight for Alexander's throat.

Alexander's eyes flashed with icy determination. "Hah!" he exhaled sharply. His arms shot up like iron pillars, protective energy surging, blocking both of Tigris's claws midair.

A glint of mischief flickered in Tigris's eyes. Its massive jaws snapped open, spitting out a blinding, bowl-sized ball of lightning straight at Alexander's chest.

Alexander's pupils shrank. Letting out a sharp cry, he twisted his body into a snake-like motion. In a split second, he sidestepped the attack, his elbow shooting forward like a spear, aiming straight for Tigris's skull.

Tigris let out a deafening roar, like a thunderclap in a clear sky. The force of the sound sent Alexander's head spinning, momentarily dazing him.

The sharp crack of a whip rang out. A blurred shadow streaked toward Alexander's head at an unimaginable speed.

Danger! Alexander roared, his muscles swelling. A storm of wind and thunder erupted around him. Waves of protective energy surged from his palms like a raging tide, crashing toward Tigris.

Boom! Boom! Boom! Tigris was sent flying through the air.

Finish it while it's down! Alexander rooted his feet, his body twisting. His hands formed an energy sphere at his waist, crackling with dazzling lightning.

Countless orbs of blinding silver-white energy coalesced in his hands, forming a radiant sphere out of thin air.

"Energy Plot Attack!"

Boom! The impact erupted in a brilliant explosion, sending blood spraying in all directions.

Alexander laughed happily and couldn't resist crouching down to hug Tigris. Its fur was warm and incredibly soft.

"Ugh! Stop licking me..."

"Well, my coins and martial virtue points are gone... Guess I'll skip the Treasure House and check out the trading fair instead."
