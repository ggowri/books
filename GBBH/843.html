"Boss is amazing!"

Keith didn't understand why Shirley had suddenly changed her mind, but he was thrilled.

Alexander pushed open the doors and stepped inside, startled by what he saw.

There were fewer people inside than outside, but still at least two or three hundred men were there, all hoping to be actors.

It made sense—they'd earn high pay and enjoy themselves, as the actresses were all well-trained in their craft.

The men in the hall had stripped down for physical evaluations; this was the first round Keith had mentioned.

"You three, come over here!"

A young woman in a black professional suit, holding a clipboard, was clearly one of the examiners.

When she spotted Alexander, her face lit up with surprise.

"Hurry up, take off your clothes so I can measure you!"

The woman eagerly reached for Alexander's belt, eyes glinting with anticipation.

Alexander took a step back. "I'm not here for the audition. Let them undress."

"Oh, my God! Boss, you want me to undress too? Are you asking me to be an actor as well?"

Marvin looked stunned, a slight blush appearing on his dark face.

Alexander held back a laugh and said coldly, "What? You dare disobey my orders?"

Marvin, unable to refuse, started unbuttoning reluctantly.

Being confident in his physique, Marvin didn't mind at all.

Keith was filled with anticipation, eager to redeem himself. He quickly undressed without hesitation.

The woman glared at Alexander in annoyance. "Then why are you here?"

She was about to call for security to remove him, but Alexander's gaze hypnotized her.

She seemed unable to see Alexander anymore and instead focused intently on Marvin and Keith.

Alexander stood off to the side, watching the hundreds of undressed men with a bemused smile, feeling as if he were observing from a superior vantage point.

The other interviewers, seeing that the supervisor didn't ask Alexander to undress, assumed he was a high-ranking inspector from the company and worked even harder.

"What is this, a worm or a pinky finger? How dare you come to audition with that? Get out!"

One interviewer shouted angrily, raising a baton and striking an embarrassed older man hard.

"Ow! Please, stop hitting me... Please hire me, it's my lifelong dream..."

The older man cried out, pleading as he was struck.

The interviewer grew angrier, wishing he could hit the old fool again. How could someone with that size satisfy anyone?

A few staff members lifted the old man and punched him in the stomach several times until he spat out foam, dragging him out mercilessly.

Seeing this, Keith felt a surge of emotions.

He recalled his first time applying; although he hadn't been hit, he was harshly mocked and kicked out.

But now, things were different—he had improved.

Keith felt deep gratitude toward Alexander.

"Thirteen inches? What are you doing here? Get out!"

"Fourteen inches? Not qualified!"

"Fifteen inches? Still not enough!"

A dozen female examiners, holding measuring tapes in one hand and handling the candidates with the other, measured with practiced precision.

Their eyes widened as they focused intently.

"Oh! This one is great—nineteen! Excellent!"

The lead woman measured Keith and let out a delighted cheer, as if she had discovered new land.

"Nineteen inches? Not bad, not bad. It's been a while since we've had such a promising candidate."

Several interviewers gathered around, squeezing Keith's waistband with interest.

Keith's body tensed, swelling to its maximum, like a fierce dragon.

"This gentleman is impressive too—reaching a remarkable twenty inches. No wonder, being well-built."

"Today wasn't in vain; we finally found two promising candidates."

The female examiners whispered excitedly among themselves, alternately handling Keith and Marvin for comparison.

A few even approached and smelled.
