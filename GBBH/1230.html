Time seemed to stand still as everyone froze in their tracks. Augusta and his group, despite all their combat prowess, found themselves completely paralyzed. They couldn't even twitch a finger.

Cold sweat soaked through their clothes as the truth sank in. They'd always known Alexander was tough, but this was way beyond anything they could have imagined. His power wasn't just next level—it was off the charts entirely.

"Yes, Mr. Hawthorne," they stammered in unison, practically falling over themselves to agree.

Alexander gave a satisfied nod. He'd made his point crystal clear—between this display of absolute power and the Force control, Augusta's crew wouldn't even think about crossing him.

At that moment, they felt like they were standing before some kind of God—one wrong move, one wrong look from him, and they'd be goners.

"Alright, enough small talk," Alexander said casually like he hadn't just scared them half to death. "Let's get started with your meditation training."

Augusta and the others could barely contain their excitement as they hung on Alexander's every word.

Following his guidance, they all found themselves naturally settling into cross-legged positions on the floor, starting their meditation the Warrior way.

Alexander kept busy during their session, channeling the Force as he moved from person to person, his touches helping to enhance their physical capabilities.

William closed his eyes and smiled mysteriously. "Man, this is incredible. It's like being reborn. I can feel the life force just flowing through me."

Logan and Charlize joined in, everyone buzzing with excitement as they shared their experiences.
