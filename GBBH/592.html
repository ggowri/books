"How dare you, an evildoer, act so presumptuously!"

One step!

Two steps!

Three steps!

As Alexander approached, Patrick felt as if he was being pressed down by a mountain and was unable to raise his head.

"What do you want to do?"

Patrick's lips turned pale, and he kept backing away.

"Come on. I'll let you make the first move!"

Alexander was calm. He did not take Patrick seriously at all.

"What! You want me to attack first? Alexander, you are too contemptuous of me."

Patrick felt humiliated and angry.

Clay suffered a great loss, so Patrick did not dare to be careless.

Patrick stared at Alexander's head and shoulders.

Before a person made a movement, his head and shoulders would have slight tremors, which served as a warning.

Different from Patrick who was nervous, Alexander was unusually composed.
