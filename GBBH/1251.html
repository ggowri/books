"What wish? Go on, spill it," Fiona asked, her brow furrowed in confusion.

Oswald Borden, the young man, stammered, "I've never slept with a woman before. If I die now, I'd really be kicking the bucket without having lived..."

"Are you kidding me? So you want to sleep with me?"

Fiona's face flushed crimson as she yanked him by the collar, pinning him against the wall.

"Fiona, I'm sorry! If you're not interested, that's fine. I wouldn't force you," Oswald stuttered, clearly shaken, his body trembling slightly.

"Fiona, please don't misunderstand! I'm not going to run away... Just trust me, alright? I'm genuinely worried about dying without ever having been with a woman. That would mean my life as a man was all for nothing!" Oswald grumbled, pushing her away.

"You little punk! I thought you were my buddy, and here you are thinking about sleeping with me!"

Fiona's anger flared as she swiftly snatched up the knife, placing the blade against Oswald's neck.

The sharp edge broke the skin, a bead of crimson blood oozing out due to the tension in the air.

"Oswald, I've seen guys like you before—young, impulsive, and utterly unreliable. Don't even think about taking advantage of me, or I swear I'll gut you!"

Fiona glared at him, her eyes fierce.

"I get it, I get it! I messed up, okay? Just let me go, and don't let anyone see this! Please, hear me out!"

Oswald's back was soaked with cold sweat, and he shivered at the precariousness of their situation.

"Just promise me you won't act like a creep again!" Fiona reiterated, though her tone softened a bit, her cheeks still flushed.

"Fiona, I messed up. I thought I might die today, and I have feelings for you, so I blurted out that request... Please don't be mad. I won't say anything like that again."

"Listen here, kid. Looks like you've really grown up, thinking about women and all. Did I spoil you too much when you were younger? I'm eight years older than you, you know!"

Fiona found herself momentarily speechless, her cheeks turning an even deeper shade of red.

Oswald had a point, and deep down, she knew it. She wasn't exactly hard on the eyes—her sun-kissed skin, curvy figure, and the aura of a mature woman all added up to something undeniably attractive.

Seeing her quiet, Oswald sensed his chance.

With a sudden burst of boldness, he pounced forward, wrapping his arms tightly around Fiona.
