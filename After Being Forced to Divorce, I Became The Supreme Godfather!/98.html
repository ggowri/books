Chapter 97 Breath of Death

However, given the current critical situation, Jane couldn't afford to be so particular anymore.

She held the long whip, swinging it rapidly.

This time, Jane unleashed a combat power even stronger than before.

Earlier at the West Gate, her plan was similar to Anthony's: defend and then wait for Carolyn's support.

However, Carolyn didn't arrive for a long time, and she suspected that Carolyn was probably in trouble.

Then, the werewolves attacked fiercely once again. Jane knew they couldn't hold out, and the situation became increasingly dire.

Therefore, she abandoned the West Gate to come to Anthony's aid, or rather, to rescue him.

Her original intention was to take Anthony away from the Turner Manor.

Unfortunately, Anthony was too badly injured and needed Rose's treatment.

Given the circumstances, her mission was to buy enough time for Rose and Anthony.

A fight to the death was unavoidable!

Under full exertion, Jane displayed combat prowess beyond imagination.

As the leader of the Dragon Guild, Jane was also a legitimate Metahuman, with her strength ranking between Level A and Level B.

Moreover, Jane possessed extremely rich combat experience and an innumerable array of combat skills.

After all, Anthony's technique crystal originated from Jane!

Perhaps her talent didn't match Anthony's, enhanced by the Devil Eye, but she had a broad range of expertise.

While Jane was fiercely engaged in battle, not far away, a black whirlwind suddenly erupted!

Accompanying the whirlwind was an overwhelming aura of malevolence!

She thought, "Mason has made his move!"

Jane was taken aback and, not daring to be careless, immediately retreated several steps.

However, she underestimated the speed of the whirlwind. Mason almost instantly closed the distance about a hundred feet!

The malevolent aura hit her in the face!

Jane swung her arm, and the long whip exploded with a sonic boom in the air before coming down with a fierce strike.

The power of this whip was formidable!

"Trifles!" However, what made Jane gape in astonishment was that Mason sneered, casually flicking his black robe to completely neutralize the power of the whip!

Her whip was powerful, but even though the black robe seemed as soft as a ball of cotton, it possessed an incredibly strong defensive power!

Jane was taken aback, never expecting that Mason would so easily defuse her attack.

Before Jane could come to her senses from the shock, Mason came at her like the wind, his sharp wolf claws extending from beneath the black robe.

The wolf claws gleamed with a sharp, cold light.

Jane trembled, the aura of death causing an intangible fear to assault her mind.

The wolf claws slashed across her upright chest, and blood splattered!

A searing pain hit Jane as she clutched her chest and lifted her foot to force Mason back!

"Beauty, prepare to die!" Disregarding Jane's attack, Mason said with contempt as he kicked Jane away.

Then, he transformed into a whirlwind once again, charging forward!

She thought, "Shit!"

The black whirlwind was as swift as lightning!

Mason's speed left Jane with no way to avoid his attack!

"Beast! I, Carolyn Turner, am here to take your life!"

At the critical moment when Jane's life was at stake, Carolyn's loud voice echoed from not far away!

Then, a surge of yellow sand surged, obscuring the sky and blocking out the sun!

In an instant, the yellow sand formed a towering wall that rose from the ground, standing directly in front of Jane.

The black whirlwind slammed into the wall of sand, with countless grains of yellow sand falling onto his head.

As a Level C Metahuman, Carolyn's elemental power was unpredictable!

Caught off guard, Mason crashed into it, let out a moan, and then turned his head with blazing anger to look back.

Not far away, Carolyn approached in large strides, wielding a sword.

She was enveloped in swirling sand and wind, resembling a war goddess!

"That's impossible! How could you possibly escape..."

Seeing it was Carolyn, Mason was greatly shocked and instinctively took a small step back.

Carolyn, the spokesperson of the Turner family and a Level C Metahuman, had already instilled fear in Mason.

"Beast! Prepare to die!"

However, the furious Carolyn didn't give him a chance to speak.

She roared to summon endless sands!

Her elemental power was incredibly strong!

The swirling sands, filled with awe-inspiring power, swept through like a plague of locusts, crushing any enemy in their path!

Amidst the swirling sands, a massive golden sword gradually took form, capable of tearing the sky asunder!

The terrifying might was earth-shattering, as if it were about to annihilate everything!

"This is a Level C Metahuman? That's terrifying!"

"Indeed, Ms. Carolyn Turner is a godlike presence; this single strike is enough to turn the tide of the battle..."

The massive sword materialized out of thin air, causing a great stir at the Turner Manor. Many members of the Turner family were moved to tears of excitement.

She is the strength and soul of the Turner family!

With Carolyn around, all difficulties could be overcome!

Under the massive sword, the werewolf army trembled in fear, howling to the sky, unable to stop retreating!
