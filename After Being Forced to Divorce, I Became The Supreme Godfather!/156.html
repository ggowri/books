Chapter 155 Blood Shadow Saber

"Anthony?" Necros smirked coldly, flicking a stone between his fingers, raising an eyebrow as he looked at Anthony.

Anthony's heart skipped when he saw the stone.

It was the very one he had thrown at Necros, and yet, he'd caught it with his bare hand. That alone spoke volumes about how strong this guy was.

Anthony's eyes sharpened, wariness creeping into his mind. He asked, "Who are you? A hitman the Smith family sent, or someone from the Evil Spirit Legion?"

Necros stared at Anthony, a sly grin playing on his lips, and shot back, "Does it really matter?"

He seemed completely unfazed, exuding confidence in his strength.

Anthony let out a dry chuckle, shrugging. "Guess it doesn't."

Before he could even finish his sentence, Necros raised his arm, smoothly drawing a jet-black machete from his waist.

The sound of the machete scraping the scabbard was sharp and grating, cutting through the silence of the valley like nails on a chalkboard.

Necros eyed the machete, then casually asked, "Archlord Iscairn mentioned you killed NT-681?"

Anthony frowned, clearly puzzled.

It was obvious that NT-681 was a codename, something the Evil Spirit Legion used often.

But Anthony had taken out plenty of their people and still had no clue who this particular codename referred to.

Noticing Anthony's confusion, Necros continued, as if it were no big deal, "He was the one leading the assault on the Turner family. His codename was NT-681."

So, it was referring to Arthur.

When Anthony realized that Necros was from the Evil Spirit Legion, he was slightly shocked.

The Legion was fast. He had barely entered the mountains, and they were already on his tail. It seemed they'd been following him all the way from Yoland.

Still pretending not to know, Anthony asked, "So you're with the Evil Spirit Legion?

"Do you guys only use codenames, or do you actually know each other's real names?"

He asked, partly to be cautious.

If Necros knew Arthur's real name, it might be time to rethink the one he'd been using.

He still wasn't sure who this Archlord Iscairn was, but he had a feeling it might be Arthur's informant.

"A puppet. Names don't matter," Necros said dismissively, his tone calm and uninterested.

Anthony wasn't in a hurry either. He threw the question back, "What about you? No name? Just another puppet?"

Necros' expression darkened, and his voice took on an edge of anger. "Remember this. My name is Necros."

"Necros, huh?" Anthony smirked, raising an eyebrow.

"Is that a nickname? Sounds like it's supposed to be fancy. Does it make you feel more important? A high-level puppet?"

"And what about Archlord Iscairn? An even higher-ranking puppet?"

Necros' temper snapped. "Enough!"

"You're asking for death!"

With a swift motion, his sleeve flicked, and the stone he had been playing with shot through the air with deadly force.

It flew like a speeding bullet, the sheer power behind it enough to make the air crackle with energy.

Anthony was ready. With a quick shift of his foot, he effortlessly dodged the stone that flew toward him.

It zipped past, and a flash of cold light aimed straight at his face. Necros' massive frame moved in a strange, almost unnatural way, speeding toward him.

His machete shimmered in the night, cutting through the air with lethal intent.

"A Warrior, huh?" Anthony smirked, muttering under his breath.

Then, he kicked into Whirlwind Step, darting sideways with a speed that left a blur behind him.

But just as he moved, Necros transformed into a streak of light, blocking his escape route in an instant. His machete came down with deadly precision.

Anthony's heart skipped a beat at the insane speed. The cockiness he'd felt earlier was gone, replaced by the full focus of a man facing a real threat. He pushed his speed even further.

The machete slashed through the air, missing him by a hair's breadth. A sharp gust from the machete cut across his cheek, leaving a burning pain.

Necros was clearly a B-level Warrior, fast as lightning and deadly with his sword.

Anthony kept dodging, but Necros wouldn't relent. His machete carved through the air, a flash of light followed by a burst of cutting force. It was fierce and unstoppable.

When it became clear that normal swordplay wouldn't do the trick, Necros' whole body trembled. A crushing aura exploded from him.

"Blood Shadow Saber!"

Necros shouted as a second machete appeared in his left hand.

In a split second, his battle intent soared, and the air around him felt sharp enough to cut through stone.

With two machetes now, his speed was even more overwhelming.

In the dark night, it felt as if he'd become one with the shadows, his machetes dancing and flashing with terrifying precision.

After a dozen exchanges, Anthony, just a little too careless, felt a sharp sting across his chest as one of the machetes grazed him.

A cold rush filled his chest as his shirt was torn open, leaving a deep gash.

If the machete had been even slightly slower, it might've torn through him entirely, and he wouldn't be standing here anymore.

"NT-681 was a joke... Go to hell!" Necros' face darkened as he muttered to himself.

Without wasting a second, he charged again, faster and more vicious than before.

The speed of his attacks kept increasing, and Anthony's dodges were becoming more desperate. It wasn't that his Whirlwind Step wasn't fast enough. It was just that Necros was too strong.

From their brief exchange, Anthony could tell this guy wasn't just good with one style of machete.

The way he attacked told him he was blending at least a dozen different techniques, each one powerful in its own right.

Honestly, Necros was the toughest and most relentless Warrior Anthony had faced yet.
