Chapter 148 Fun

Most of the guards of the Smith family were ordinary people who came from major security companies or were trained by the Smith family.

However, they could not be considered warriors, but they were a little stronger than ordinary people.

It was not about strength but numbers!

"Everyone!

"Let's kill Anthony and split the money..."

One hundred million dollars was a huge sum of money for many ordinary guards. How could they not be tempted?

Soon, the crowd rushed towards Anthony.

At this time, Anthony was chasing the Black Cloak.

For a Level B Warrior, the Black Cloak's strength and defense were impressive.

Even though he was seriously injured, it was still not easy to kill him.

Boom!

The crowd rushed up.

Long swords flashing with cold lights stretched out from the crowd and slashed at Anthony.

Their power was not strong, but Anthony did not want to be hurt.

Therefore, he gave up the Black Cloak and turned to face the crowd.

Dariel, who was not far away, looked gloomy and strode over.

However, before he could make a move, Anthony shook his head at him, signaling him not to take action.

Dariel retreated.

The attack of so many people might have a little effect on people of average strength.

However, for a strong man like Anthony, it was of no use at all.

Dariel knew very well that Anthony's fighting ability was extraordinary.

At the very least, this group of people was far from enough to hurt Anthony!

Bang!

The crowd rushed forward, but Anthony handled it well.

Muffled sounds followed one another.

A group of ordinary security guards were vulnerable.

Anthony's one move was enough to knock down several people. Soon, there were no less than ten people lying under Anthony's feet!

The violent scene was jaw-dropping.

Despite being besieged by hundreds of people, Anthony was able to handle the situation with ease. Instead of being at a disadvantage, he became more courageous!

The crowd fell to the ground and wailed.

"How could this happen? I..."

Not far away, Alvin was completely dumbfounded. His face changed again and again, and he took a few steps back in fear.

He couldn't help but be afraid!

Hundreds of people didn't even have the chance to get close to Anthony. In just a few moments, more than half of them fell down.

There was no way they could have cornered Anthony.

"We are doomed."

"Anthony is too strong. Who can stop him?"

"Look at his expression. He will not stop until the Smith family is destroyed today!"

"Of course. Anne slandered and insulted him before. Now that he has the chance, he must take revenge."

"I can guarantee that if the Smith family can't stop him, Anthony will destroy them..."

"Why hasn't the Evil Spirit Legion come yet? If they don't come, something terrible will happen..."

...

Anthony was killing people in all directions with unstoppable force. Everyone was panicked.

There was a lot of discussion. For a moment, many people didn't know what to do.

"Let's go!"

"It is not appropriate to stay here for long..."

"The Evil Spirit Legion has not arrived yet. There must be a problem. Let's leave..."

"Yes. Innocent people might get hurt. Let's go..."

Many people did not dare to stay and slipped away in fear.

With the first one, there would be the second and the third one.

After a while, most of the crowd had left, and the few people left were all hesitating.

Anne was shocked!

The purpose of this family dinner was to enhance the cohesion of the Smith family and to unify Yoland with the help of the power of the Evil Spirit Legion!

If all these people left, everything would be in vain.

"Mr. Hastings!

"I'll give you what you want. Immediately!"

After a while of consideration, Anne didn't dare to delay. As she spoke, she started transferring money to Arthur.

In this case, even if she could save the Smith family, their prestige would plummet!

Arthur accepted the money very quickly.

He inadvertently raised his head and looked at Anthony in the crowd.

Anthony also glanced at Arthur. His hand movements quickened.

Just now, the reason why Anthony showed mercy was to wait for Arthur.

The payment had been received. And the fun began!

"Lunatic!

"Don't be so presumptuous. Prepare your death!"

Arthur suddenly rushed out. And with a loud shout, he landed on the ground with a bang.

Bang!

Dust and smoke rose up and rushed in all directions with tremendous force.

In an instant, the crowd gathered around Anthony was shocked and took several steps back. Even Anthony couldn't help but take a step back.
