Chapter 74 Unstoppable

In the banquet hall, Alvin showed no remorse and sneered, "Anthony, you will definitely die this time.

"Ms. Turner is so beautiful. If such a good woman dies because of you, wouldn't it be a pity?

"Apologize to me and call me grandpa. If I am satisfied, I can ask the Evil Spirit Legion to spare your life. How about that?"

Carolyn's intention of personally participating in the battle was to boost the morale of the Turner family.

However, in Alvin's view, it was the Turner family's last gasp. Otherwise, why would the leader of the Turner family take action personally?

Moreover, Carolyn had taken away all the personal guards, so Alvin was arrogant once again.

Hearing his words, everyone looked at Anthony.

"Are you tired? Sit down and rest for a while," said Anthony.

He ignored Alvin and pulled Rose to sit down, comforting her. "Don't worry. The power of the Turner family is enough to resist the Evil Spirit Legion."

Rose nodded. "Anthony, don't worry. As long as you are in the Turner Manor, no one can hurt you."

Anthony also nodded, and Rose once again took the initiative to hold his hand.

The enemy was at the gates, but the two were still not in a hurry but comforting each other in public, which made Alvin furious.

"Anthony. You bastard, you are about to die, and you still dare to bluff," said Alvin.

He jumped up and continued angrily, "Everyone, the Evil Spirit Legion outside has gathered because of this bastard.

"Carolyn... Ms. Carolyn Turner was worried about the Turner family's reputation and couldn't say it out loud directly.

"Are you going to watch as such a bastard destroys the Turner family? Besides, look. How long Dariel can hold out?"

Hearing his words, many people looked at the big screen.

Indeed, Dariel and others outside the gate were utterly unable to withstand the werewolves' attack.

The situation was critical, and it wouldn't be long before the werewolves broke through the gate if it continued like this.

Seeing how ferocious the werewolves were, many members of the Turner family became a little restless.

Noticing everyone's expressions, Alvin continued, "Everyone, once the Evil Spirit Legion breaks through the gate, we will all be in trouble.

"Anthony is a bastard. His death is not a pity. But what about us? Why should we die because of him? Why should we be buried with him?

"In my opinion, we should capture him and hand him over to the Evil Spirit Legion for disposal.

"I can guarantee that the Evil Spirit Legion will not hurt the Turner family as long as you hand over Anthony."

Everyone present thought his words were correct.

Anthony was a bastard, and his death was not a pity.

Moreover, Anthony had just met the Turner family by chance. Why should the Turner family die with him?

"Ms. Turner..." Finally, a middle-aged man stood up.

"How dare you!" Rose's eyes became angry, and she scolded, "Are you still a member of the Turner family? Wasn't what my aunt said just now clear enough?

"As the heir of the Turner family, I declare once again that the Turner family swears to stand together with Anthony and live and die with him.

"If anyone dares to shake the morale again, don't blame me for being rude."

Rose, who had been soft, was furious.

Everyone in the Turner family was frightened by her words and dared not say another word.

Alvin was still unwilling and wanted to say something else.

But Rose turned around and said, "If this pair of bastards dare to spread rumors and confuse the public again, beat them to death."

"Got it." A dozen of Rose's guards stepped forward, staring at Alvin and Anne.

Seeing such a scene, Alvin finally stopped and dared not speak anymore.

He knew that as Rose's guards, these people had probably been dissatisfied with him for a long time.

Thinking Alvin was just a clown, Rose ignored him.

When she turned around, she found that Anthony was staring at the big screen with a frown. So she asked, "What's wrong?"

"Nothing." Anthony retracted his gaze and smiled at her, signaling her to be at ease.

However, Mason, outside the gate, had disappeared.

Boom.

Rose was about to say something when the Turner Manor suddenly trembled.

The considerable movement was like a mountain collapsing and the earth splitting apart.

On the big screen, they could see thick smoke and blood mist outside the gate.

Looking closely, the guards had used a powerful weapon, and a shell hit the werewolves hard.

Amid the smoke, blood and flesh were scattered, and wailing was heard everywhere.

When the smoke cleared, only three of the dozen werewolves were left, and they were missing limbs and suffered heavy losses.

"Kill them!" Seeing the werewolves were frustrated, Dariel rushed forward first.

After a fierce battle, only a dozen of the thirty or so people brought by Dariel were left.

If they hadn't used their most powerful weapon, they wouldn't have been able to stop those werewolves.

However, using the most powerful weapon was, to a certain extent, a humiliation to them and a denial of their strength.

Dariel thought, "Damn it."

"Kill them!" It was not just Dariel who had red eyes. The few remaining guards also had red eyes and rushed toward the remaining werewolves with roars.

Just as they were about to kill, another wolf howl was heard from the corner.

Immediately afterward, more than a dozen ferocious-looking werewolves rushed up with bared fangs and claws.

Dariel was horrified and shouted, "Retreat! Retreat now!"

But it was too late.

The werewolves had planned it long ago. More than a dozen of them were like tigers descending from the mountains, unstoppable.

A cloud of blood mist flew up, and more than half of the dozen or so guards left were killed tragically in an instant.

"Damn it. You bastards. I'll fight you to death," roared Dariel.

His eyes were red, just like a wild beast. He tore the werewolf apart with his hands.

Unfortunately, there were too many werewolves.
