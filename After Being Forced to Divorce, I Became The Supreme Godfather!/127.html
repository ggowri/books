Chapter 126 No Rest Until Death

"The Zodiac?"

Anthony's interest was piqued, but Seraph paused deliberately.

Seeing Anthony's impatient look, he continued, "The day before yesterday, they went on a rampage, taking down several security companies one after another.

"Yeah, just like challenging martial arts schools, they went from one to the next, beating them all.

"The next day, they made a big show of establishing Draconic Shield."

"But here's the kicker, the heads of those defeated security companies personally went to congratulate him..."

Anthony couldn't help but laugh.

Arthur was indeed one of a kind. Only he could come up with such a plan.

Starting a security company by taking down another one... what a genius!

However, Anthony wasn't too concerned about that. Instead, he asked, "What about the situation in the Virstino Mountains? Any leads?"

"Yes.

"But we were wrong in our initial assumption.

"The werewolf assassin you caught admitted he was from the Evil Spirit Legion's Southwest Camp and trained in the Virstino Mountains.

"That led us to believe the Southwest Camp was in the Virstino Mountains, but that's not the case.

"Our investigation shows only minimal Evil Spirit Legion activity in the Virstino Mountains.

"I later contacted Arthur, who confirmed several locations where they previously trained and hid out.

"To put it simply, while the Evil Spirit Legion had people hiding in the Virstino Mountains, the Southwest Camp itself isn't located there.

"And currently, there's almost no trace of the Evil Spirit Legion in the Virstino Mountains. They've either gone deep underground or left altogether."

Three days had been enough time for Seraph to investigate the situation at Virstino Mountains thoroughly.

However, these findings weren't what Anthony had been hoping for.

As Seraph had mentioned, Anthony had expected to make some significant discoveries in the Virstino Mountains.

He'd specifically warned Seraph to be careful, thinking there would be a large concentration of Evil Spirit Legion members there.

But now, they were entirely off base.

Had all their efforts been for nothing?

No!

Something wasn't right!

After a moment of deep thought, Anthony spoke up. "Based on what you're saying, has the Evil Spirit Legion given up on the Turner family?

"It's not like them to throw in the towel so easily.

"Plus, the Evil Spirit Legion took quite a hit in the battle with the Turner family.

"They lost a bunch of assassins and their Virstino Mountains operation was exposed. Why would they just walk away from all that?"

"Well..." Seraph hesitated, at a loss for words.

Anthony's concern wasn't unfounded. The Evil Spirit Legion lived by one notorious motto. "No rest until death!"

"Hah!"

When he saw Seraph was speechless, Anthony let out a chuckle and waved his hand. "Alright, don't get too hung up on the Evil Spirit Legion.

"Having you investigate early was just a precautionary measure. It's great if you find something, but no big deal if you don't.

"Since the Evil Spirit Legion never gives up, they're bound to make another move.

"So what we need to do is actually quite simple.

"Wait and watch, let them walk right into our trap."

"Um..." Seraph looked confused.

"Mr. Johnson, what do you mean?"

Anthony smiled wryly and explained, "Anne has not only rallied various families to her side, but she's also planning a grand banquet to announce her allegiance to the Evil Spirit Legion, right?

"Just imagine, if you were the Evil Spirit Legion, would you miss such a golden opportunity?

"Someone publicly pledging allegiance to an evil force... that's never happened before. The Evil Spirit Legion will definitely send someone.

"So all we need to do is wait patiently for them to come to us. Get it?"

Seraph paused for a moment before breaking into a flirtatious giggle.

"Oh Mr. Johnson, you're so brilliant at both strategy and combat. I'm absolutely crazy about you..."

"Go away!" Anthony waved his hand in disgust as Seraph tried to grab his arm.

"Cut that out! Just keep your eyes peeled and let me know the second anything happens."

"Yes, Mr. Johnson."

Unfazed, Seraph grinned and latched onto his arm again.

Her generous assets pressed against him shamelessly, making his blood boil.

Anthony had planned to stop by the Dragon Guild, but Seraph's antics had put him in such a foul mood that he decided to head straight to the Turner family instead.

"That bastard!

"Screw him!

"Nothing but trash talk from that lowlife!

"A worthless piece of garbage, talking big like that!"

Meanwhile, at the Smith family residence, Anne nearly smashed her phone after hanging up, cursing up a storm.

She couldn't stand Anthony's condescending tone and the way he'd dismissed her ultimately.

Even after everything that had happened, she still saw him as nothing but a worthless fool.

"Give it a rest," Alvin said lazily from his spot on the couch.

"Why waste your energy on a dead man walking?

"In three days, Anthony better not show his face. If he dares to come, he's as good as dead!"
