Chapter 122 Aaron Hastings

"You?"

Anthony looked at him and couldn't help but laugh.

Was this guy jealous that he bestowed names upon others?

But Anthony said nothing, thinking for a moment before speaking. "If you don't like the name Arthur Hastings, you can always go by 'Aaron Hastings' from now on."

"Aaron Hastings?"

Arthur murmured the name, and then his face lit up with delight. He bowed, saying, "Thank you for the name, Mr. Johnson."

"Arthur, come with me. The rest of you stay here."

Without wasting any time, Anthony led Arthur, or rather, Aaron, into the Celestial Palace.

As they entered, Seraph greeted them with a smile. "Congratulations, Mr. Johnson, for successfully completing the second phase of the Chosen Path."

Her words were serious, but Anthony couldn't shake the feeling that Seraph's gaze carried an odd, almost seductive allure.

It was hard to deny—whenever he saw Seraph, she always made his heart skip a beat.

Her wavy golden hair cascaded like a waterfall, soft and voluminous.

Her golden V-neck gown clung to her curves, accentuating her graceful figure in the most alluring way.

Her every movement radiated a unique, fiery sensuality.

Anthony smiled, though his thoughts were far from romantic. He knew Seraph was a rose with thorns—beautiful, yes, but not someone he could easily get close to.

To truly win her over, one would first have to remove her thorns—but Anthony wasn't in any position to do so yet.

Seeing that Anthony was unphased, Seraph leaned in closer, her voice dropping to a whisper. "The second-phase task is complete, and your reward is upgraded..."

"Really?"

Now intrigued, Anthony asked, "What reward?"

"Of course, it's me...

"Besides, this time you can really enjoy me anytime..."

She gave him a teasing smile, flipping her golden hair.

Anthony's lips twitched, and he shot her an irritated glance.

But to his surprise, there was no warning from the Devil Eye.

Did this mean she was telling the truth?

Could the reward truly be... her?

Anthony shook his head.

He wasn't in the mood for such things at the moment. Calmly, he walked past Seraph and entered the main hall.

While Anthony remained composed, Arthur, following behind, couldn't help but gawk.

She was too beautiful, too alluring!

This fiery, seductive blonde woman called him... Mr. Johnson?

Noticing Arthur's disarray, Anthony wasn't surprised. It was hard for any man to resist the charm of a woman like her.

Seraph giggled, turning to Anthony. "Mr. Johnson, when you first arrived here, you were just as stunned as he is now..."

"Ahem."

Anthony cleared his throat to mask his embarrassment.

Arthur quickly snapped back to his senses, his face reddening in awkwardness.

To ease the situation, he pulled a map from his pocket and handed it to Anthony. "Mr. Johnson, here's the map you asked me to prepare."

After the battle at the Phantom Hollow, Arthur informed Anthony that they had been hiding in the Virstino Mountains prior to that.

This information had also been confirmed by the werewolves they had captured.

Additionally, a base for the Evil Spirit Legion was located within the Virstino Mountains, known as the Southwest Camp.

Arthur wasn't sure how many werewolf assassins were stationed there, as they tended to operate in smaller, scattered groups.

They rarely gathered together, and the communication between them was limited.

The Virstino Mountains were just over a hundred miles from the Turner family Manor.

The Evil Spirit Legion's presence there was a major threat to Yoland.

And now that the attempt to attack the Turner family had failed, the Evil Spirit Legion would certainly not rest.

Whatever their motives, they were sure to strike again.

Furthermore, Arthur was the grandson of the Godfather and the future heir of the Godfather Group.

The Evil Spirit Legion was the number one enemy of the Godfather Group.

So, even though he wasn't actively planning an attack, Arthur knew he had to remain vigilant and gather intel on the Evil Spirit Legion.

Knowing his enemy was key to winning any battle.

Anthony briefly looked over the map before passing it to Seraph. "Send someone to investigate the Virstino Mountains. If you find any leads, report back to me immediately.

"Make sure to operate in secret. We don't want to alert them."

For now, Anthony didn't have the strength to confront the Evil Spirit Legion head-on.

Yes, he was the Godfather's grandson, but as the Godfather had told him, he wouldn't receive much help until he completed the Chosen Path and the Dragon Ring task.

So far, only Seraph and Jane were by his side.

This was why he had gathered loyal followers like Arthur. Draconic Shield was his true source of strength.

He also knew the Godfather's test for him wasn't just about completing the Dragon Ring's mission.

Along the way, he needed to demonstrate enough power to show he was worthy of being the next leader.

And this power wasn't limited to just his physical strength.

"Yes, Mr. Johnson," Seraph replied.

Seeing Anthony's serious expression, she put aside her playful attitude and thought for a moment before asking, "What about the others? Do you have any plans for them?"
