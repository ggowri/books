Chapter 90 Insurmountable

"Fuck!" Anthony cursed in his heart.

However, he quickly pulled back his leg at that moment and immediately dodged to the left.

As expected, the moment Anthony pulled back his leg, a blade about one foot long extended from beneath Mason's black robe.

The blade flashed with a cold light, slashing through the air fiercely!

This powerful slash carried considerable force.

This force coalesced into a single entity, transforming into an energy wave that shot out several feet before striking the ground forcefully.

In an instant, the floor shattered, and dust was stirred!

Anthony focused his gaze and saw that the hard marble had turned to powder. A knife mark over three feet long was left on the ground, looking extremely terrifying!

He was greatly shocked.

If it weren't for the Devil Eye's warning, his kick would not only fail to injure Mason but also result in him losing a leg!

His head might have been shattered by this energy wave!

Mason was too cunning!

Anthony pretended to be weak, and he chose to deal with him in the same way.

Anthony secretly prepared a decisive move for victory, and so did Mason!

What appeared to be an impulsive attack driven by rage actually contained a shocking determination and wisdom.

And Anthony had to admit that Mason's skills were clearly stronger than his.

If it weren't for the help of Devil Eye, he would have died long ago.

With another fatal strike failing, Mason was also stunned.

Just now, Anthony lifted his leg but retracted the attack midway.

This behavior seemed too bizarre to Mason.

Mason couldn't figure it out.

This attack was supposed to be impossible to miss.

But, Anthony managed to dodge it.

Anthony repeatedly avoided his fatal attacks in a bizarre way.

While Mason was stunned, Anthony reacted quickly and once again thrust Dragonfang at Mason.

The timing was perfect, and the speed was incredibly fast.

At this moment, Mason had just landed and was still in a state of disbelief and shock.

Anthony didn't believe he could react to this strike!

However, before Dragonfang could cut Mason's neck, a foot suddenly extended from beneath the black robe!

He heftily kicked Anthony's chest.

Both Anthony's strike and Mason's kick were executed with speed that reached its peak.

Everything happened so fast that the Devil Eye didn't have time to warn.

Anthony completely withstood the attack and was sent flying off his feet.

A Level B metahuman's speed, strength, and reaction time are nothing to be trifled with.

The power of Leopard Avatar had already made Anthony much stronger.

However, this kind of strength seemed to still have a significant gap compared to that of a Level B metahuman.

He thought, "Damn it!"

Collapsing to the ground, Anthony spat out a mouthful of blood.

After that, he huddled miserably in the corner, spreading his hands in a posture of surrender.

"Anthony!" Not far away, Rose was shocked, screaming and instinctively rushing towards Anthony.

"Ms. Turner, don't go over there!" Helen was startled and desperately held back Rose.

Then, with a ghastly pale face, Helen ordered, "Hurry! Escort Ms. Turner to the safe room, hurry!"

Her screams were mixed with sobs, and Helen was truly terrified. However, even in her fear, she still struggled to hold onto Rose.

Carolyn couldn't come to rescue them.

Anthony was severely injured and unable to fight, while over a hundred werewolves were preparing to tear them apart.

The Turner family was on the brink of destruction!

"You have no means of retaliation left! Prepare to die!" Mason roared to the sky, bellowing as he rapidly charged towards Anthony.

As he sprinted, his black robe flapped wildly in the wind, his speed so fast that it was terrifying.

After such a fierce battle, Mason had also witnessed Anthony's cunning. Therefore, even though Anthony appeared to be in a desperate situation, Mason did not let his guard down!

At this moment, although Anthony seemed weak, he was secretly watching him, with a blue light flickering above his left eye.

He gathered all his strength from his entire body, focusing it recklessly into his arm.

According to the Devil Eye's deduction, Anthony only had one chance!

He must go all out.

Then, Mason swung a long sword, slashing down fiercely!

However, when the long sword was less than three feet away from Anthony, he suddenly trembled, and the power of the Leopard Avatar erupted abruptly.

In the next second, Anthony lunged forward like a leopard!

His speed was so fast that it even left a strange afterimage!

It flashed, mingled with a glint of cold light.

While dodging the sword, Anthony jabbed at Mason's throat with Dragonfang at an ingenious angle!

"You despicable bastard!" Realizing Anthony's attack, Mason was taken aback and sharply turned his head as he shouted.

The dagger flashed past, and Dragonfang, being incredibly sharp, cut through Mason's shoulder with unstoppable force!

In an instant, blood spurted from Mason's shoulder!

But in the next second, Mason countered back and punched Anthony away.

At such a close distance, Anthony had no choice but to brace for the attack.

Unfortunately, this slash failed to hit Mason's vital spot.

Otherwise, even if Anthony couldn't kill him just now, it would have been enough to severely wound him.

He failed to grab the last chance.

The gap in strength was simply insurmountable!

Anthony thought, "Fuck!"
