Chapter 143 Initiative

The strong wind blew in their faces, containing extraordinary power. The gathered crowd subconsciously took a few steps back.

However, before the dust cleared, Anthony and the Black Cloak each retreated.

Every step the two took was so powerful that the ground could not support them at all and broke apart.

This scene was so shocking that many people in the crowd gasped.

The power of a Warrior was brutal!

The punch was also a fight of sheer force.

The key point was that after one punch, Anthony and the Black Cloak each retreated several steps.

Evenly matched?

No way.

Anthony could fight on equal terms with the famous Black Cloak?

Moreover, the Black Cloak also displayed his proud skills.

The crowd was dumbfounded again.

Everyone was shocked.

The Black Cloak was quite famous in Yoland, and his strength was extraordinary.

Anthony?

He was just a piece of trash.

Therefore, for them, once the Black Cloak made a move, Anthony would surely die.

But now, the two separated as soon as they touched each other. The powerful force was so strong that they took several steps back.

Even though they were not Warriors, they could see that in this round, the two were evenly matched!

No way.

"Impossible! What kind of skill is that?"

Not to mention that other people were shocked, even the Black Cloak couldn't help but frown and looked at Anthony in shock.

The Black Cloak was not only surprised by Anthony's strength, but also by his skills.

He was sure that Anthony's skills were stronger than his!

The Black Cloak thought, "But how was that possible?

Anthony is just a loser. How could he have such powerful skills?"

"Black Cloak!"

Not far away, Alvin's face darkened, and he said unhappily, "What are you doing? Hurry up and kill him!

"He's just a piece of trash. No need to show mercy. Kill him right away!"

Alvin had absolute confidence in the Black Cloak's strength. He would never believe that Anthony could be on par with Black Cloak.

Therefore, he thought the Black Cloak showed mercy!

"Yes, sir!"

The Black Cloak agreed confidently, but his eyes were always fixed on Anthony, not daring to move away!

Moreover, he stopped underestimating Anthony.

Even though Anthony's power relied on drugs, he was indeed very strong.

Seeing the Black Cloak nod, Anthony laughed. "Don't you know that you shouldn't easily promise something you can't do?

"You knew you were no match for me, but you still agreed to it. You're embarrassing me."

The Black Cloak's face darkened, and he cursed angrily, "You bastard, how dare you speak so shamelessly!

"I admit that I underestimated you before, but I won't do that again!"

"Really?"

Anthony said seriously, "Well, be careful!"

He finally took the initiative.

"What?"

"He dares to take the initiative to attack?"

"Is this true? He can't be so arrogant just because the Black Cloak was careless for a moment, right?"

"Yeah, he is too ignorant. He took the initiative to get himself killed."

...

The crowd began to talk and looked at Anthony with disdain.

Before, Anthony had been on the passive defensive, which was a sign of weakness for someone who did not understand the fight.

Taking the initiative to attack was what the strong did.

And in their eyes, Anthony was obviously not a strong man.

The battle outside the gate became more and more exciting, and everyone's attention was drawn to it.

In the yard, Anne was silent. Ever since Ben was defeated, her expression had been gloomy.

At this moment, seeing Anthony taking the initiative to attack, Anne finally stopped hesitating and turned to walk towards Arthur.

"Mr. Hastings, what do you think of Anthony's strength?"

Anne's face returned to its former charm. She spoke with a smile.

She seemed to be calm and composed.

However, her performance could not deceive Arthur.

Arthur was still holding the glass in his hand. He shook it and said thoughtfully, "It's not bad. He relied on drugs to force his potential, barely a Warrior."

"Well."

His words obviously made Anne relax a lot.

However, Arthur said, "But it seems that the Black Cloak could not win."

Anne was stunned and asked, "Why?"

Arthur smiled, "You should know Anthony better, right?

"Maybe in your heart, he is a loser, but you have to admit that he is not a reckless person, right?

"If that's the case, he chose to take the initiative and must be very confident that he can defeat the Black Cloak.

"That's why you asked me for my opinion."

After speaking, Arthur raised his glass, drank the wine in one gulp, and stared at Anne steadily.
