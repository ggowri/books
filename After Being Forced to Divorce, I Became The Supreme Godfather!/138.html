Chapter 137 A Small Fry

Anthony couldn't help but laugh at Timothy's arrogant demeanor.

"These guys must have rocks for brains," he thought.

"Why are you laughing?

"What the hell are you laughing at?"

Enraged by Anthony's smile, Timothy charged forward, shouting, "Get on your knees, you piece of shit!"

Whoosh!

Timothy's fist, fueled by fury, shot out with lightning speed.

Back in the day, such a punch would have definitely knocked Anthony down.

Unfortunately for Timothy, the current Anthony was no longer the same pushover, a fact Timothy remained blissfully ignorant of.

Seeing Anthony unfazed, a smug grin spread across Timothy's face.

Imagine the notoriety he'd gain if he could bring Anthony to his knees with a single punch right here in front of everyone!

BAM!

Just as Timothy was gloating internally, Anthony suddenly moved.

All Timothy saw was a foot shooting towards him like lightning.

The next instant, a searing pain exploded in his chest as if he'd been slammed by a speeding train. He let out a yelp and went flying backward.

Before Timothy could even process what happened, he was sent flying over 20 feet, smashing through a wall and landing in the Smith Manor's courtyard.

Timothy violently coughed as he crashed to the ground, spitting out a mouthful of blood.

"You... you, you..." he sputtered, struggling to lift his head and look at Anthony, trying to speak.

But his injuries were too severe. After a few desperate attempts, he failed to utter a single word. His head slumped to the side, and he passed out.

"What?" The crowd, which had been reveling in the spectacle moments before, was now stunned. They instinctively took a few steps back.

It was too sudden. Timothy had been so confident when he launched his attack that everyone had assumed Anthony was doomed.

But in the blink of an eye, Timothy was the one taken down!

"Loser!"

He turned his cold gaze to Anne standing behind the crowd and spoke in a deep voice. "Anne, do you know why I came today?

"It's because I want everyone to know what you really are nothing but a whore who begged me to fuck you."

Was the Smith family really conspiring with the Evil Spirit Legion to take over Yoland?

Anne's ambition was undeniable, but she was way out of her league.

"You! You Bastard!"

Seeing Anthony's outburst, Anne's face twisted into an ugly snarl. She waved her hand and snapped, "Ben, what are you waiting for?

"Kill him! Now!"

Anne had no intention of explaining, not that she needed to anyway.

After all, today, Anthony was as good as dead. Once he was gone, who would care about anything he'd said?

"Anthony! You worthless piece of trash!"

Ben stepped out from the crowd, his eyes blazing with murderous intent. "You're nothing but a junkie who relies on drugs for power, and you dare cause trouble for the Smith family?"

"Do you really think that just because you've pumped yourself full of drugs, you can look down on everyone?

"Today, I'll show you just how much of a difference there is between drug-fueled power and natural strength!"

As Ben spoke, his body tensed, and a series of sharp cracking sounds erupted from his bones.

Powerful energy coursed through his limbs, radiating an impressive aura.

"Wow!"

"A Warrior? Did Ben break through?"

"No, not quite Warrior level, but Ben's power is almost there..."

"This kind of strength is practically Warrior class. Anthony's toast now, that loser..."

"You got that right. With a Warrior at the helm, the Smith family's power is climbing to new heights. What was Anthony thinking, causing trouble here?"

"Hah! He's just trash who got a temporary power boost from some drugs. Talk about overestimating himself..."

The crowd was initially shocked by Ben's overwhelming aura.

Then their expressions turned gleeful as they looked at Anthony, eager to watch him fail.

To ordinary people, Warriors were legendary figures whose power was beyond imagination. No one dared to underestimate a Warrior's strength.

And Anthony was just a loser who, at best, was a regular guy who got a bit stronger using some enhancement drugs.

The gap between an average person and someone nearly at Warrior level was evident to everyone.

But Anthony just laughed internally at their reactions.

"Nearly at Warrior level? Practically to a Warrior?

What nonsense logic was this? A Warrior was a Warrior! What did 'practically' even mean?

These ignorant fools."

Being close to Warrior level wasn't the same as being a Warrior. The difference in power was like heaven and earth!

Besides, Ben wasn't even a real Warrior yet. But even if he was, why should Anthony be afraid?

"Anthony!"

With power surging through his body, Ben glared at Anthony with wide eyes and roared, "Don't accuse me of bullying! Aren't you able to boost your strength with drugs?

"If you don't want to die pathetically, I'll give you plenty of time to take your drugs first!

"But," he sneered, "I'll make damn sure you learn that drugs are just drugs, and trash will always be trash, just like you."

Anthony chuckled softly, hands casually tucked in his pockets. "Just get on with it, why all the bluster?

"Do you really think I need drugs to deal with a small fry like you?

"Ben, aren't you getting a little ahead of yourself?"
