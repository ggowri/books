Chapter 131 The New Head of the Smith Family

The Smith Manor was decked out in festive decorations at the crack of dawn, with music and excitement filling the air.

Anyone passing by might have mistaken it for a wedding celebration.

Outside the main gates, a sea of people gathered. Luxury cars lined the driveway as the city's elite arrived in their finest attire.

This banquet was no small affair. The Smiths had managed to attract over eighty percent of Yoland's most powerful families. The significance couldn't be understated.

"Ladies and gentlemen!" Anne called out.

"Did any of you ever imagine that the Smith family would have such a day? Where we'd command the attention of the entire city?"

Standing at the main table in the estate's grand hall, Anne cut an imposing figure in her blue evening gown, holding a wine glass with aristocratic grace as she addressed the family elders.

"We never dared to dream..."

"Such grandeur was beyond our wildest imagination before..."

"Indeed, the Smith family's rise to power and control over Yoland is all thanks to Ms. Smith's exceptional capabilities..."

"Absolutely right. If it weren't for Ms. Smith's brilliant decision to ally with the Evil Spirit Legion when we were backed into a corner, the Turner family would have isolated us completely."

"Yes, yes, the Smith family's current success is entirely due to Ms. Smith's contributions..."

Anne's single question had set off a cascade of enthusiastic praise from the elders, their excitement palpable in the air.

As the Smith family's influence grew, their status as family elders naturally came with plenty of perks.

Besides, they weren't wrong - they'd never dreamed the Smith family could reach such heights.

After all, the Smith family was just nouveau riche, worth only a few billion.

Among the crowd below were actual elite families worth tens of billions.

Yet even they had to bow down to the Smith family now!

After today, the Smith family would officially join the Evil Spirit Legion. Their meteoric rise was practically guaranteed!

Soon enough, they'd indeed surpass the Turner family and rule all of Yoland!

Anne smiled coolly, tilting her head back with utter disdain.

Her proud posture resembled that of a haughty canary.

But then her brow furrowed, and she asked impatiently, "Where's Grandpa? Why isn't he here yet?

"What if Mr. Foster arrives early? It would be terribly rude if Grandpa, our family head, isn't here to greet him."

Anne might be the Smith family heir, but she wasn't yet the head of the family.

However...

Last night, Anne had gone to see the family patriarch, her grandfather Galvin Smith.

She'd lied, claiming Mason had said that if they were to accept the Smith family, they needed to deal directly with the family head.

She claimed they didn't know much about the Smith family yet and only knew Anne.

Or rather, they only trusted Anne.

The implication was clear. "Man, it's time for you to step down!"

As the leader of the Smith family, Galvin was no fool. He understood Anne's intentions perfectly.

Besides, Anne had done the Smith family a tremendous service.

And since she was already the Smith family's heir, Galvin saw no reason to play games.

He'd promised to announce his retirement today publicly and formally name Anne as the new head of the Smith family.

So when Galvin was late, Anne couldn't help feeling annoyed.

"What's with this older man who should've retired ages ago putting on airs?"

"Sir Smith has arrived!"

Just as Anne's patience was wearing thin, a commanding Rolls-Royce Phantom pulled up outside.

Out stepped an older man with white hair, wearing a burgundy suit and leaning on a dragon-headed cane.

Despite his advanced age, he radiated vitality and energy, his bearing projecting the unmistakable authority of someone born to lead.

It was Galvin, the head of the Smith family.

He was a self-made man known for his ruthless tactics, which had single-handedly elevated the Smith family to its current heights.

As soon as Galvin stepped out, family heads, both big and small, swarmed around him, showering him with endless flattery.

The Smith family's rise was unstoppable, and no one dared look down on any of its members now.

Much less its patriarch himself.

Galvin received all the flattery with a warm smile, like a kindly grandfather greeting each guest personally.

"Ladies and gentlemen!

"First, I want to thank you all for taking time from your busy schedules to attend this modest family gathering."

After this brief pleasantry, Galvin got straight to the point. "I'd like to take this opportunity to make an announcement.

"Given my advanced years, I no longer have the energy to manage the Smith family's business empire.

"My granddaughter, Anne, has shown exceptional intelligence since childhood and is more than capable of taking on this responsibility.

"Therefore, I hereby announce my retirement, effective immediately, and officially name Anne as the new head of the Smith family!"

With these words, Galvin took Anne's hand and guided her to the front while he took several slow steps back.

The ceremony was simple, almost rushed.

It was necessary, as they didn't know when the leader of Evil Spirit Legion would arrive, so they couldn't afford to waste time.

Anne didn't mind the simplicity. She strode forward proudly, looking down at the crowd with an air of superiority.

"Wow!"

"Just like that? So sudden!"

"Ms. Smith is becoming the head of the Smith family?"

"Congratulations to Ms. Smith on inheriting the Smith family, and to Sir Smith on his well-deserved retirement..."

"Yes, yes, congratulations to Ms. Smith and Sir Smith..."

The crowd erupted in a buzz of excitement. After the initial shock wore off, they all scrambled to offer their congratulations.
