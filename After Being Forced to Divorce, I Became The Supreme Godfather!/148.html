Chapter 147 Reward

He was not to be hired or fight lightly.

He did not accept temporary employment.

Alvin was stunned, while Anne asked, "Mr. Hastings, unless what?

"Just tell me what conditions you have. As long as you can capture Anthony, everything will be easy!"

Anthony was so strong that Anne was terrified.

With such strong combat power, she didn't think the Smith family could withstand him, so she placed all her hopes on Arthur.

At the very least, Arthur looked confident. And even after seeing Anthony's fighting ability, he still looked calm.

She thought Anthony would definitely not be his opponent.

Just as Anne said, as long as Anthony could be captured, she would agree to any conditions.

"No problem."

Arthur smiled calmly, took a sip of red wine, put two hands together, and made the number ten.

Anne was stunned for a moment and said blankly, "What does this mean?"

"Five hundred million dollars a year."

Arthur said, "And we need to sign a contract for at least ten years. In other words, for five billion dollars, I can guarantee the safety of your family for ten years!"

"What?"

Before he could finish, Alvin exclaimed, "Aaron, you are a fucking bandit, right?"

The bandits were not as cruel as Arthur.

"Really?"

Arthur was not angry. He leisurely shook the red wine glass and looked at Anne.

He knew that Alvin had no say in the matter, so Arthur simply didn't bother to pay attention to him.

"Well..."

Anne was also shocked and said, "The price is indeed a bit outrageous.

"Five hundred million dollars. Do you know how many Warriors I can hire to look after my house?"

Arthur nodded and said, "I know. Then, please invite them quickly, or it will be too late."

...

Anne froze.

The situation was urgent. Anthony was right in front of us, full of murderous intent.

Anne didn't have time to invite the other Warriors.

"God!"

Alvin was furious and shouted angrily, "Aaron, you are adding insult to injury!"

"No."

This time, Arthur shook his head and said, "First of all, you should know that I am a businessman.

"When doing business, we need to examine the situation carefully. The price is determined based on your current situation.

"Besides, I didn't say you had to hire me, did I?"

Alvin was speechless. Looking at Arthur's arrogant look, he wanted to beat him up.

Anne was hesitant. She looked at Arthur with anger but also a bit of unconcealed appreciation and agitation.

What a man!

Only with strong power could one have a strong voice. A real man should be so tough.

What about Alvin?

He was so weak.

Anne couldn't help but want to agree to Arthur's conditions, but the money...

Just for Anthony? Was a loser worth half a billion dollars?

As if he had guessed Anne's thoughts, Arthur said, "Ms. Smith, I want to remind you that the money was spent not for Anthony but for the Smith family.

"Given the hatred between you and Anthony, if you don't stop him in time, maybe he will really go on a killing spree..."

"You're just taking advantage of the situation. Shut up!"

Before Arthur could finish, Alvin cursed angrily, and then said, "Anne, don't listen to him!

"Anthony is just a piece of trash, not worth the money. Besides, there are so many people in the Smith family and the Blake family. We can capture Anthony!

"Don't forget all his strength is just due to the drugs. After fighting for so long, the effect of the drugs should have been almost consumed.

"Without the support of drugs, how many people can he beat? Thirty? Fifty? Or a hundred?

"Where is the Blake family's security?"

Without waiting for Anne to answer, Alvin shouted loudly.

"We are here!"

Dozens of people in the crowd stood up.

Moreover, there was a large group of people not far away, gathering rapidly.

They were members of the Blake family, or in other words, Alvin's personal guards.

There were about forty or fifty people.

But there were many people in the Smith family.

After Anthony's fierce battle, the Smith family had gathered.

There were at least seventy or eighty people. Together with the Blake family, there were at least hundreds of people.

Moreover, this was not the entire strength of the Smith family. All of them add up to over a hundred.

Anne's eyes lit up, and she nodded, "Yes! I have forgotten this!

"He's just a piece of trash. So what if he's a good fighter?"

Anne waved her hand and ordered, "Listen, do everything you can to surround and kill Anthony!

"Whoever can kill him will be rewarded one hundred million dollars!"

"Yes, Ms. Smith!"

The high reward had many guards coveted.
