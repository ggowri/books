Chapter 138 A True Warrior

"Screw you!" Ben snarled with a twisted smirk. "You piece of trash, talking big even when you're about to die!

"Since you're so eager to die, don't blame me for what happens next.

"I swear I won't rest until I've killed you!"

As his words died in the air, Ben's body tensed like a drawn bowstring before he launched himself forward.

His speed was breathtaking.

Most of the onlookers could barely track his movement. He was nothing but a blur of light rushing toward Anthony.

"Loser! Kneel!"

The wind howled as Ben roared, his fist cutting through the air like an unstoppable force!

Anthony stood perfectly still, seemingly paralyzed with fear. Ben was sure this punch would have his opponent groveling like a beaten dog!

But as Ben continued his battle cry, he saw a foot appearing in his vision, growing larger and larger by the millisecond!

Then, that foot connected with his face—a perfect, solid hit!

With a high kick, Ben went flying backward even faster than he'd charged forward!

In the blink of an eye, Ben yelped as he flew a dozen feet through the air and crashed hard into the wall.

The immense impact caused the wall to collapse with a roar, and Ben enveloped in a thick cloud of dust, flew into the Smith Manor courtyard.

"Holy shit!"

As Ben went airborne, the crowd gasped in shock. Their eyes bulged, and their jaws dropped like they'd just seen a ghost.

"How was this even possible?

Ben, who was practically at Warrior level, just got kicked and sent flying?

What kind of steroids was Anthony on? How did he get so strong?"

The crowd took a few intuitive steps back, completely stunned.

Their smug expressions of schadenfreude quickly transformed into shock and disbelief.

"You bastard! You fucking sucker-punched me!"

While the crowd was still reeling, Ben roared and burst out of the courtyard.

Upon closer inspection, blood was streaming from Ben's nose and mouth, and there was a very prominent shoe print plastered across his face.

Someone in the crowd couldn't hold it in anymore and almost burst out laughing.

"Yeah, it had to be a cheap shot!"

"Must've been a sucker punch. How else could a loser like Anthony be this strong?"

"That's what losers do! He resorts to dirty tricks. That doesn't take any real skill!"

Seeing Ben's wounded pride, several members of the Smith family immediately jumped in, hurling verbal abuse at Anthony.

"A sneak attack?"

Anthony's face darkened, and he sneered. "He was the one who attacked first. My counterattack is considered a sneak attack? Are you all blind?"

His retort left the Smith family members gritting their teeth, yet unable to refute his words.

"Screw you! Don't get cocky, Anthony!"

Ben stepped forward, advancing with large strides as he snarled, "I let my guard down earlier, but don't think I'll go easy on you this time."

After taking that kick, Ben's eyes were blazing with murderous intent, like a wild beast ready to strike.

"Oh really?"

Anthony smiled coolly and replied, "In that case, you'd better fight at full strength.

"For just a Level A Warrior, you're sure putting on quite an act. What's with all the pretense?"

Ben's heart skipped a beat, his face flickering with surprise.

"How did he know?"

Anthony was right. Ben's power wasn't just approaching Warrior level.

He had recently broken through. He was now a full-fledged Warrior!

Just as Anthony said, Ben had been hiding his true strength.

Judging by their reactions, Anne and the Smith family higher-ups must have known about this.

Sure enough, when Anthony dropped this bombshell, Anne and the others' expressions changed dramatically.

The Smith family hadn't really intended to keep this a secret forever. They were just waiting for the right moment for Ben to make a spectacular revelation.

For instance, once the Evil Spirit Legion representatives arrived, they planned to recommend Ben and have him demonstrate his Warrior powers in public.

It would surely elevate the Smith family's standing in the Evil Spirit Legion's eyes.

But they never expected that Anthony, whom they considered worthless, would see through their plan and expose it publicly.

Damn it all!

"Fine!"

After a moment of silence, Ben sneered, "Well, well... so this loser managed to see through my true abilities.

"Since that's the case, let me show you, you worthless nobody, what a true Warrior's power looks like!"

As Ben spoke, he unleashed the power within him. In an instant, the stone tiles beneath his feet shattered.

A powerful aura swept across the area, stirring up winds and clouds of dust.

"Whoa!"

"A Warrior? Ben is actually a Warrior!"

"Holy shit! A real Warrior! Captain Ben has broken through!"

"This is insane! Captain Ben's been holding back all this time!"

"Anthony's dead meat now. Whether he fights dirty or uses those sketchy pills, there's no way he can match a Warrior!"

"He's just trash anyway, never stood a chance. All he knows are cheap tricks..."

Ben had broken through, and the crowd was stunned.

A true Warrior's strength far surpassed that of an Expert who was merely approaching Warrior level!

The crowd buzzed with excitement, falling over themselves to praise Ben and the Smith family.
