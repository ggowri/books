Chapter 102 Beyond Redemption

However, the moment Carolyn fell to the ground, the sand-crafted dagger sliced through Mason's throat with unstoppable force.

The dagger flashed past, leaving a mist of blood in its wake.

Mason let out a howl. The immense impact nearly made him collapse. He stumbled a few steps before regaining his balance.

Carolyn looked up and sighed inwardly, "Too bad."

Mason's reaction speed was too quick.

Although the dagger wasn't infinitely powerful, it was swift, striking from a tricky angle, and Carolyn had launched it as a surprise attack.

As the head of the Turner family, Carolyn would have disdained to ambush a level-C metahuman.

But now, with the Turner family in dire straits, she had to cast aside such scruples.

She had thought this strike would kill Mason, but he evaded the attack.

The dagger grazed his neck, tearing a nearly four-inch wound that bled profusely, yet not life-threatening.

Carolyn felt a surge of despair and lay there, powerless, on the ground.

"Damn it! You wicked woman! Go to hell!"

Mason was furious, roaring as he raised his foot and stomped the ground with force.

With a muffled thud, a boulder the size of a human head suddenly flew up, hurtling towards Carolyn's head.

Carolyn was nearly drained, covered in blood, her strength utterly depleted, and her face terrifyingly pale.

Under normal circumstances, Carolyn wouldn't have given a stone a second thought, as it couldn't harm her in the slightest.

But now, this relatively small stone could take her life.

As Carolyn closed her eyes, ready to burn her essence, a figure flashed over, standing in front of her and shattering the incoming stone with a single claw strike.

"Ms. Turner! Retreat! You should retreat to the safe house first!"

Upon closer look, it was Dariel. His stature was imposing, nearly a foot taller than before.

His clothes were in tatters, revealing his muscular body, riddled with scars and sparsely covered with hair.

Moreover, his eyes gleamed with a bestial ferocity.

This was his werewolf form!

Judging by Dariel's appearance, he was on the verge of complete transformation.

Carolyn's heart leaped with joy, but the next moment, she couldn't help but feel sorrow.

Dariel seemed to be evolving, but the situation for the Turner family was dire.

Even if he did evolve, it might not make a difference, and he could still end up dead.

Dariel was fiercely loyal to the Turner family and had contributed significantly to their cause on numerous occasions.

Setting aside past battles, in this very fight, Dariel had already put his life on the line.

"Curse you all. You will all die!"

Before Carolyn could ponder further, Mason cursed and charged again.

"Quick! Escort Ms. Turner to retreat!"

Dariel's eyes darkened. He abruptly spread his arms wide, his biceps bulging with veins.

"Retreat? You wish. Die!"

As Dariel's words fell, Mason sprinted towards them.

After such a prolonged battle, Mason still appeared ferocious and full of strength.

Everyone noticed this and found it strange, though they didn't know why.

"Damn it! Bastard, bring it on!"

Dariel roared with bloodshot eyes, his gaze about to burst from its sockets as he met Mason in combat.

However, Dariel's charging steps were noticeably unsteady, or rather, staggering.

Seeing Dariel about to sacrifice himself, Carolyn shouted, "Dariel! Come back!"

But it was too late.

With a single strike, Dariel was sent flying back. Differently, his chest now bore a gruesome wound.

The wound was so deep that it exposed the bone, a mess of blood and flesh.

The werewolf's sharp claws had nearly torn open his chest from top to bottom. The flayed flesh was horrifying to behold.

"Come quickly!" Carolyn rushed over and shouted, "Someone! Medication for Dariel! Hurry!"

As Carolyn was shouting, another figure flew over from the distance.

It was Jane!

Jane's red dress was even more crimson now. It was stained red with blood.

"Ms. Ronald!"

Carolyn's face drained of color in shock, and she yelled, "Someone! Bring the medicine! Quickly..."

"Ms. Turner..."

An elderly man in a white coat trembled as he said, "We... we have no more medicine..."

"What?"

Carolyn's eyes turned red with anger as she scolded, "No more medicine? What does that mean?

"What are you all for? Why is there no medicine?

"How could the Turner family run out of medicine? Bring the medicine now!"

Carolyn's eyes were bloodshot as she raged furiously.

But in the end, Carolyn was overwhelmed with sorrow, tears silently streaming down her face.

She turned her head to look around. Everywhere she saw were werewolves with terrifying fangs, howling.

The vast Turner Manor was in ruins, blood flowing like rivers and piles of corpses.

The Turner family was heading toward decline beyond redemption.
