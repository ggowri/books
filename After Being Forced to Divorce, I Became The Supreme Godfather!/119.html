Chapter 118 Hopeless

When the Turner family appeared, their intimidating aura and murderous intent were clear. Alvin and the others instinctively lowered their heads.

"What are you doing?

"Do you want to fight me?"

However, Anne was unfazed. She stood proudly and scoffed. "A bunch of brainless fools! What's there for you to be so cocky about?

"Do you really think that if Anthony didn't join Mr. Foster, he'd have made it back alive?

"Do you think that such a useless man is your family's savior?

"Better watch your backs—he might betray you, and you wouldn't even see it coming!"

Anne stood tall, exuding confidence.

"Exactly!"

Alvin, who had been shrinking back, straightened his back and spoke up. "Anthony definitely joined Mr. Foster. Everything was just an act!

"He came back to convince you all to join Mr. Foster, or he's planning to secretly undermine the Turner family. You'd better watch out!"

No matter what, Alvin wasn't about to let Anne steal the spotlight.

He, being a grown man, certainly didn't want to be overshadowed.

"Anthony!"

Anne ignored Alvin and shouted at Anthony. "If you're still a man, tell me, did you join Mr. Foster?"

"Heh."

Anthony smiled helplessly and said, "Do you think everyone is as cowardly as you?

"Willing to abandon their dignity and sell their soul just to survive?

"Let me warn you all—don't let yourselves be used as pawns in the service of evil!

"The Evil Spirit Legion has done countless wrongs and is despised by all. One day, they will be eradicated.

"If you don't want to be implicated, it's best to distance yourselves from certain people, or else you'll face the consequences!"

As for Anne, Anthony couldn't be bothered to waste words on her, but the others from Yoland seemed not completely beyond saving.

Anne was enraged. She cursed. "Anthony, you're nothing but trash. Stop spreading lies!

"You really think you can take down the Evil Spirit Legion? What are you, some kind of hero?

"Don't think that because you deny it, it's going to disappear!

"You've joined the Evil Spirit Legion, and that's an undeniable fact. Otherwise, you wouldn't have survived to come back!

"Do you think that without Mr. Foster around, you can say whatever you want?

"Just wait. I'll meet Mr. Foster myself and expose your lies!

"And as for your Turner family...

"Seven days from now, I'll host a banquet at the Smith Manor and officially announce that families from Yoland are joining the Evil Spirit Legion!

"You'd better make up your minds quickly. If you're still stubborn after seven days, the Turner family will once again be drenched in blood!"

The banquet, initially meant to celebrate their "wise" choice of aligning with the Evil Spirit Legion, had now taken on a different tone.

Now that Mason hadn't returned and the werewolf army had retreated, Anne was a bit confused.

However, she didn't believe that Anthony would defeat Mason.

Despite not seeing Mason, Anne was confident that after seven days, the banquet would still take place, and Mason would arrive at the Smith Manor.

When that happened, the Smith family would become the leading power in Yoland, a force that could rival the Turner family!

Perhaps, one day, they might even surpass them.

As for Anthony?

Hmph! Just a trash talker—his lies would be exposed sooner or later!

"Wait?

"A banquet? Joining together?"

When Anthony heard this, he frowned and looked at the other people around Anne.

He was somewhat familiar with the families of Yoland, and it wasn't an exaggeration to say that Anne had already managed to win the support of over 80% of Yoland's families!

If these families actually did join the Evil Spirit Legion, it would indeed be a loss for Yoland.

However, Anthony wasn't concerned that they would truly join, because they didn't even have that opportunity.

The reason he frowned was that he felt the Smith family's banquet might be a good chance for him to act.

"Hmph!

"Anthony, are you scared?"

Seeing Anthony frown, Anne sneered and arrogantly said, "Just a moment ago, who was it that boldly said they would eliminate the Evil Spirit Legion?

"Since you're so brave, the doors of the Smith Manor will be open to you seven days from now. Are you daring enough to come?"

As she spoke, Anne glared fiercely at Anthony.

At that moment, Rose frowned and instinctively squeezed Anthony's hand.

A banquet at the Smith Manor?

And they are inviting the families of Yoland to publicly announce their allegiance to the Evil Spirit Legion?

Wasn't this obviously a trap?

Anthony subtly squeezed Rose's hand, signaling her not to worry. He then turned to Anne and said, "Alright, I'll come to the banquet seven days from now."

"Fine!

"You bastard!"

Seeing Anthony agree, before Anne could say anything, Alvin shouted. "You said it yourself. If you don't show up, you're nothing but a coward!"

"Alright.

"You can leave now."

With a wave of his hand, Anthony's guard team, led by Dariel, immediately expelled Anne and her group from the Turner family Manor.
