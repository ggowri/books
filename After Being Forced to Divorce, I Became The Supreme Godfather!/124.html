Chapter 123 Changing One's Appearance

Seraph, personally dispatched by the Godfather and tasked with closely assisting Anthony, was far more than just a pretty face.

Therefore, she knew that since Anthony had recruited such a large group of people like Arthur, he must have some plan in mind.

Anthony merely smiled and said, "For now, you don't need to get involved with establishing Draconic Shield.

"It's just a security company. If he can't handle this small task, how could he be trusted with more important responsibilities in the future?"

He turned his gaze to Arthur.

Arthur was shocked, and he immediately replied with a serious tone, "Mr. Johnson, please be assured. I have a plan. Within three days, Draconic Shield will definitely be officially established."

Perhaps due to the soul contract, Arthur didn't dare to defy Anthony.

Moreover, he was afraid of doing anything that might displease him.

Anthony wasn't particularly concerned about these matters and instead said, "Establishing Draconic Shield is just the first step and the easiest one at that.

"The next phase is to control Yoland's prominent families secretly. That's the real key.

"What are your thoughts on this?"

"Well..." Arthur hesitated for a moment, then said, "I believe the most critical issue right now is manpower."

"I'm not interested in ordinary people, so my plan is to find an opportunity to recruit from the Evil Spirit Legion..."

As he spoke, Arthur hesitated again, nervously glancing at Anthony.

"Oh?" Anthony raised an eyebrow, intrigued.

"You mean continue undermining the Evil Spirit Legion?"

"Yes."

Seeing that Anthony wasn't angry, Arthur let out a sigh of relief and explained, "Since Mr. Johnson wants to use Draconic Shield as a stepping stone, the strength of Draconic Shield is crucial.

"The Evil Spirit Legion doesn't know about my situation yet, which is a golden opportunity we can't miss.

"I plan to contact as many of my old acquaintances as possible within three days and select suitable personnel from among them."

"Good." Anthony nodded with satisfaction, then turned his attention to Arthur's facial scars.

After a moment of consideration, he asked Seraph, "Can these scars be removed?"

Arthur's scars crisscrossed, covering half his face, making him look particularly grotesque.

The critical issue was that this hideous appearance was highly distinctive.

The establishment of Draconic Shield held immense significance for Anthony, so he had to be extremely cautious.

Changing one's appearance was the most prudent solution.

Moreover, Arthur's plan was clearly to operate covertly, and his face would make stealth impossible.

"Child's play," Seraph said casually, glancing over.

"Forget a few scars, even if his entire face were destroyed, I could restore it to its original state."

Godfather Group possessed the world's most cutting-edge technology and the most exceptional, comprehensive research team.

Scar removal was child's play for them.

"Really?

"You...you mean the scars on my face can be removed?"

The news that his facial scar could be removed initially stunned Arthur, but then he was overcome with joy.

Such a large scar was indeed hard to accept, and judging by his facial features, Arthur wasn't a bad-looking guy.

His reaction suggested he'd long been self-conscious about the mark.

It would be best to remove it, especially since he would be in the public eye.

Anthony smiled and said to Seraph, "Go ahead and make the arrangements. The sooner, the better."

"Alright," Seraph replied with a nod.

She stood up, her curves swaying as she gracefully exited the room.

Arthur couldn't help but stare.

Anthony snapped his fingers, bringing Arthur back to attention. "Seven days," he declared. "You have only seven days."

"Huh?" The sudden declaration took aback Arthur, then quickly bowed. "Yes, Mr. Johnson.

"Within seven days, I will ensure Draconic Shield makes a name for itself in Yoland."

"No."

Anthony shook his head. "If you're just looking to make a name, you only have three days."

"Within seven days, I want Draconic Shield fully established, with a comprehensive security system and a team of top-tier security personnel!

"Only then can Draconic Shield be ready to accept mission assignments and officially provide services to Yoland's families."

"Um..."

Arthur scratched his head helplessly, nervously saying, "But, Mr. Johnson, Yoland's families already have specific security companies.

"We're a new company with no reputation and no connections. I'm afraid we might not..."

Before he could finish, Anthony cut him off with a wave of his hand. "Seven days from now, the Smith family will host a gathering.

"They'll invite representatives from all of Yoland's major families. This is our opportunity. I'll personally work to dismantle the existing security teams.

"So in seven days, Draconic Shield must have enough strength to replace other security companies.

"As for reputation, I'll negotiate with the Turner family. With the Turner family backing you, isn't that reputation enough?"

Wow!

After this speech, Arthur was stunned.

Arthur was stunned by these words. If what Anthony said was true, he would barely have to do anything himself and focus on recruiting and building his strength!
