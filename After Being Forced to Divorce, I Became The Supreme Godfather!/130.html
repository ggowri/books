Chapter 129 Go Down Together

"Haha!"

Rose gasped, a mix of nervousness and panic in her voice, which only made Anthony laugh harder.

She'd already spilled the beans and only now realized her slip of the tongue.

"But it's okay..."

Seeing Anthony doubled over with laughter, Rose smiled and added, "After all, I belong to you, Anthony. Eventually, you'll be running the Turner family anyway, right?"

"Huh?

"Yeah, yeah, of course..."

Anthony froze for a moment before quickly nodding his head.

"Run the Turner family?" he scoffed inwardly.

His future was set as the Godfather! How could the mere Turner family ever contain his ambitions?

After some thought, Anthony asked, "So after all these years of development, have they found anything in the Mines?"

Rose shook her head helplessly and said, "The Mines are in terrible condition. Plus, there are always poisonous creatures, dangerous animals, and traces of bandits.

"Development has been crawling along. If Grandfather hadn't specifically insisted, we would've given up on it long ago."

"I see..."

Anthony mulled this over for a moment before saying, "Well, in that case, there's no need to worry about it right now.

"When we have time, we can go check it out together. And if Vincent keeps being stubborn, we might as well kick him out of the family!"

Rose hesitated before speaking. "Let's wait until Aunt Carolyn gets back before jumping to conclusions. We don't know the whole story yet.

"Maybe Vincent was just blowing off steam. Give it some time, and things might settle down on their own."

"Alright."

Anthony didn't push the matter further after Rose's comment.

Still, he couldn't shake the feeling that it wasn't that simple. Vincent was the type who'd stop at nothing to get what he wanted. He wouldn't back down easily.

They rested for a while.

Soon enough, Anthony got his second wind, and Rose recovered, too. They spent the next few hours lost in passion.

This time, Anthony advanced to the eighth position of his unique technique, and Rose was noticeably more confident and eager, wholly caught up in the pleasure.

After their exhausting night, Anthony and Rose slept well past morning.

Helen had no choice but to knock on their door, informing them that Carolyn had returned and had been waiting for them for quite a while.

After freshening up, they headed to the main hall.

Inside, they found Aunt Carolyn, along with several Turner family elders, all wearing grave expressions.

After they entered, Rose whispered that the three elders seated below were previously in charge of the Mines.

They took their seats at Carolyn's right hand, and Rose asked, "Aunt Carolyn, why the urgent summons? Is something wrong at the Mines?"

Whether due to the crowd or something else, Carolyn shook her head. "No, the Mines are running fine for now.

"Vincent was just a bit too eager to prove himself. It's understandable—no need to worry too much.

"I called you here because of Anthony."

"Me?"

Anthony stared at Carolyn, wholly puzzled.

"Yes." Carolyn nodded.

"Late last night, we received solid intel that the Smith family has made contact with the Evil Spirit Legion.

"And Anne has been going around, swearing up and down to every family that the Evil Spirit Legion will definitely send representatives to this family banquet!

"We've also heard that you're on their hit list, Anthony. Word is, they're not just coming for the banquet! They're planning to take you out while they're at it!"

Carolyn directed these last words straight at Anthony.

Rose jumped, startled by this news, and instinctively grabbed Anthony's hand.

Anthony just smiled and said, "Aunt Carolyn, are you trying to convince me to skip the Smith family's banquet?"

"Exactly!" Carolyn cut straight to the chase.

"After what happened last time, the Evil Spirit Legion won't be taking any chances.

"If they're bold enough to send someone, it's got to be serious. That's why I want you to stay here with the Turner family.

"The Turner family has weathered every storm in Yoland for hundreds of years.

"If the Evil Spirit Legion is dead set on destroying the Turner family, then so be it! We'll go down together if we have to.

"Bottom line is, this all started because of the Turner family, and we'll do whatever it takes to ensure your safety."

Carolyn's words carried an unwavering resolve and dead seriousness.

Anthony couldn't help but be deeply moved by Carolyn's dedication.

She hadn't even considered that if the Evil Spirit Legion attended the family banquet, it would signal their acceptance of other families' allegiance.

If that happened, the Turner family would face an uphill battle in Yoland.

No!

To be more precise, the Turner family would have no place left to stand in Yoland at all!

Yet despite all this, Carolyn's first instinct was to protect Anthony at any cost.

"Thank you for your kindness, Aunt Carolyn."

Then, Anthony continued, "But you don't need to worry.

"I must attend the Smith family's banquet. Besides, given the current situation, staying away isn't really an option.

"However, I can promise you this, Aunt Carolyn. The Evil Spirit Legion won't find any foothold in Yoland.

"If they don't show up, fine. But if they do, I'll take them down one by one, pair by pair!"

Anthony was hoping the Evil Spirit Legion would show up. It would make things so much easier!
