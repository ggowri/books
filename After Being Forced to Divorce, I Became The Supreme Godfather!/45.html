Chapter 44 Going Solo

In a show of sincerity, Jane left the storage chip completely unprotected. The moment Anthony activated his Devil Eye and touched it, a wealth of information flooded his senses.

He found extensive materials including The Collection of Firearms Skills of the Special Forces, The Secret of Close Combat of the Royal Guards, and Disguise and Invisibility...

It was as if he stumbled upon a treasure trove. Without pausing to sift through the details, he quickly initiated the copying and storage process.

Before long, the invaluable information from the chip was transferred to the Devil Eye and seamlessly integrated into Anthony's mind.

He felt a slight pressure in his head as he checked the remaining storage capacity of the Devil Eye. There wasn't much space left. The last time he had stored a vast amount of knowledge about spiritual medicine, and now these new skills took up 90% of the device's capacity.

If he wanted to absorb more information in the future, he'd need to upgrade the Devil Eye.

But he had no idea how to do that.

He couldn't dwell on it now; he needed to head back and digest this knowledge as quickly as possible.

"Thanks, I'll take it. Bye!" Anthony said excitedly as he turned away.

Jane's eyes widened in surprise.

She looked at the chip still in her palm, confused. Anthony clearly refused it, so why did he say he took it?

She pondered and realized he might just have appreciated her kindness. As for the skills in the chip, he didn't care at all.

It made sense to Jane.

Despite the immense energy, effort, and money she had invested in acquiring these skills, they meant nothing to Anthony, who was destined to become the Godfather.

She felt foolish for offering such a trivial gift and vowed to find something truly worthy for him next time.

Back in Rose's room, Anthony eagerly opened his Devil Eye, ready to explore the knowledge he had just stored.

Alongside firearm and close combat techniques, he found an array of other valuable skills: Killing Kick, Invincible Punch, Eagle Claw Attack, and more.

Suddenly, one name stood out among the skills: Seduction Trap, Male Version.

What the hell was that?

He recalled Jane's enchanting dance that had mesmerized the werewolf and wondered, "Does Jane want me to learn the male version of Seduction Trap?

Do I need to perform a striptease to attract women?"

The very thought of himself dancing and performing seductive moves in front of Rose made him blush.

But that wasn't the main issue.

The most shocking discovery lay at the end of the skill catalog: a section labeled "Practice of Sex."

Anthony couldn't help swallowing.

He hesitated but ultimately couldn't resist opening the directory. Instantly, vivid images surged into his mind—men and women, all looking blissful in each scene.

It was a collection of sexual techniques.

"Is Jane dropping hints?" Anthony wondered.

Imagining the joy of using those 108 sexual techniques on Jane sent his heart racing, nearly causing him to lose his composure. He was tempted to rush to her for a little experimentation.

But he forced himself to stay calm and focused, diving into the study of these skills.

With the assistance of the Devil Eye, he was a sharpshooter and a close combat expert.

However, he knew those were the Devil Eye's abilities, not his own.

Once the energy of the Devil Eye ran out, he'd revert to being an ordinary person, vulnerable and easily defeated.

He understood he needed to master these skills and not rely on external powers.

Determined, he dedicated himself to learning them so that if the Devil Eye failed, he could still handle himself in a fight.

If he encountered challenges, he wanted to be self-sufficient and not have to activate the Devil Eye, saving its energy for critical moments.

The next day, Anthony was set to meet Rose at Turner Manor.

Unaware of the dangers that awaited him, he must prepare meticulously.

He studied every minute.

The next morning, when Jane learned of Anthony's plan to go to the Turner Manor, she felt worried.

"The Turner family has declared you the assassin who targeted Rose. Everyone sees you as a foe. It's too risky for you to go alone," she said. "It would be wise to wait until the werewolf wakes up, interrogate him, and clear your name before heading there."

Anthony recognized the wisdom in her advice; it was the best course of action at that moment.

Yet he shook his head. "I have a bad feeling that Rose is in danger or being wronged. I need to get there as quickly as possible to help her.

"Let's split up. You handle the interrogation of the werewolf and track down the four-fingered guy. I'll dig into things from inside the Turner family.

"Let's meet at the Turner Manor."

Jane replied, "Today is Rose's 20th birthday, a significant day when the Turner family will announce her as the official heir and allow her to partake in family affairs. They've invited dignitaries from across Yoland for the celebration.

"The crowd will be diverse, and the situation will be complex. Mr. Johnson, you must be careful. I promise I'll arrive with the werewolf and answers before the party starts, and we'll regroup then."
