Chapter 141 Kill Him

Ben's wails were heart-wrenching!

Anne couldn't help but feel dazed. A Warrior was no match for Anthony.

She thought, "Are the drugs from the Godfather Research Institute really that powerful?

This drug turns a loser into a Warrior?

So scary."

For an ordinary person like Anne, this was simply unbelievable.

"Anne!

"Will you beg me or not?"

Anthony stood proudly among the crowd, looked at Anne, and asked again.

"Well..."

At this moment, Anne was in a state of confusion.

Anthony's strength made it a little difficult for her to accept it for a while.

Because in her heart, Anthony was a loser, a man who deserved to die, a man who should beg for mercy!

But now, he had transformed into such a powerful person.

"Don't be afraid."

Just when Anne was at a loss, Alvin came over and whispered, "Don't forget that the more powerful the drug, the stronger the side effects.

"Besides, being strong for a short time doesn't mean anything."

Anne took a deep breath and nodded. "Yes, I almost forgot.

"The power of the drug, after all, is not his own power. He is just a powerful loser..."

Anne frowned and said worriedly, "But it would be disgraceful of us not to teach him a lesson in public."

Alvin said, "Don't worry. He will definitely die today."

He smiled and glanced at Anne proudly.

He was proud because he knew Ben was no match for Anthony.

At the beginning, Alvin didn't know that Ben had successfully became a Warrior, so he was certain that Ben would lose.

Even if Anthony relied on drugs, his enhanced power was obvious.

Ben couldn't beat him.

Alvin was surprised that Ben became a Warrior.

However, he was still certain that Ben was no match for Anthony.

This was indeed the case. And what Alvin was waiting for was Ben's defeat.

In fact, ever since they got engaged, Anne had always been superior to him and had taken the lead in everything.

After all, Alvin was the heir of the Blake family. And the status of the Blake family was much higher than that of the Smith family.

Why?

Besides, Alvin was a grown man. He didn't want to let Anne take the lead.

In short, Alvin felt angry. And now was the time for him to vent his anger.

The Smith family couldn't stop Anthony.

However, the Blake family could do it!

"Black Cloak!"

After glancing at Anne, Alvin held his head high and waved to the Black Cloak not far away.

"Yes, sir."

The Black Cloak understood and strode over.

Alvin said, "Kill Anthony, and I'll reward you one hundred million dollars!"

The Black Cloak grinned and said respectfully, "You are too kind. It is my duty to help you solve your problems."

Having said that, the Black Cloak still had a greedy look on his face.

The Black Cloak had said that his duty was to protect the Blake family.

Then, the Black Cloak did not hesitate, jumped up, rushed towards Anthony and shouted, "Loser, do you still remember me?"

Anthony looked back and couldn't help laughing.

A Black Cloak!

Anthony wanted to fight with the Blake family's Shadow Sentinel!

At the beginning, Anthony caused a scene at the engagement banquet, after which the Black Cloak attacked with great strength and almost caught him.

If Anthony hadn't been quick-witted and used his pistol to force the Black Cloak to stop, he would have been in trouble!

Even so, the Black Cloak left a very deep impression on Anthony!

Or rather, it was the power of the Warrior that impressed him.

It was Anthony's first contact with a Warrior.

He was surprised by the Warrior's strength.

However, times had changed. This time, Anthony wanted to teach him a lesson!

Anthony turned around and smiled, "It's you, the old guy who was stopped by my gun..."

"Brat! I'm going to kill you!"

Before Anthony could finish, the Black Cloak became furious, shouted, and approached him.

To be precise, the Black Cloak had been looking for an opportunity to teach Anthony, this ignorant guy, a lesson!

The reward was secondary.

Boom!

Unlike Ben, the Black Cloak was quick. As soon as he finished speaking, he shook his arms.

Warrior!

"The Blake family's Shadow Sentinel has been famous for a long time."

"Yes. It is said that as early as ten years ago, he became a Warrior and has a lot of experience."

"Anthony is ignorant. He dares to speak so recklessly in front of the Black Cloak."

"Humph! He is smart. He was going to die anyway, better at the hands of the Black Cloak than by the Evil Spirit Legion."

"It can be considered that he was brave enough to resist, and his death was worthy..."

...

When they saw the Black Cloak taking action, everyone exclaimed in surprise.

Everyone knew this was a Shadow Sentinel that had been famous for a long time.

Moreover, his strength was extraordinary. And he was far superior to Ben, the new Shadow Sentinel.

"Kill him!

"Must kill him..."

At this time, Ben, who was not far away, wiped the blood off his face and screamed crazily.
