Chapter 186 Kneel

The offer seemed tempting on paper. Not only would Anthony escape death, but he'd also gain control of the Turner family and become Yoland's leader.

Mordred's proposal was the kind of fast-track to power that would make most people's heads spin.

Vincent and Anne had proved that.

But for Anthony, it meant nothing.

As the grandson of the Velmor Godfather and heir to the Godfather Group, Anthony knew better. The Evil Spirit Legion and the Godfather Group were mortal enemies, like oil and water, they'd never mix.

Asking him to join the Legion was like asking a cat to swim.

Of course, Mordred didn't know any of this. That explained his smug confidence in making the offer.

Anthony let out a soft chuckle, tightening his grip on Dragonfang. "You know what?" he said with a hint of mockery in his voice. "I might consider joining the Legion... if you sweeten the deal a bit."

"Oh?" Mordred raised an eyebrow, taking the bait. "What else could you possibly want?"

Anthony walked forward with casual confidence. "Kneel before me and swear your loyalty. Maybe I'll think about it," he said with an exaggerated drawl, his mockery becoming even more obvious.

"You're asking for death!" Mordred's face twisted with rage before Anthony could even finish his taunt. He charged forward like a bull seeing red.

Each footfall from Mordred's massive frame thundered against the ground like war drums. As he charged, waves of murderous intent rolled off him, growing stronger with each step.

Anthony threw his head back and laughed, standing his ground. His grip tightened on his weapon as he rushed to meet Mordred head-on.

Inside him, his dragon power surged desperately, pushing itself to the limit.

A silvery-white glow spread across Anthony's skin, shimmering like scales in the sunlight.

In an instant, they closed the gap. Mordred swung his blood-red spear in a deadly arc.

Anthony met the challenge, his whole body tensing as Dragonfang burst with an icy blue light. He thrust the blade toward Mordred's midsection.

But there was a dangerous difference in their weapons. Dragonfang was barely eighteen inches long, while Mordred's crimson spear stretched nearly ten feet. In a weapon clash, reach could mean everything.

Realizing he was outmatched, Anthony couldn't risk a head-on clash. As the spear descended, he executed a Whirlwind Step, his body twisting at an impossible angle to avoid the deadly thrust.

Though the spear missed his vital points by inches, Mordred managed to deflect Anthony's Dragonfang in the same instant. Anthony saw his chance.

In a split-second decision, he made his Dragonfang vanish.

His fingers clenched into a tight fist, which began to glow with an otherworldly light.

His mortal flesh channeled an incredible burst of power and speed.

The surprise attack caught Mordred off guard, landing squarely on his shoulder. "Damn," Anthony thought. He'd been aiming for Mordred's heart, but his opponent was just too fast.

Even more frustrating, Mordred's massive frame barely swayed from the impact. His defense was absolutely insane!

"You fight without honor!" Mordred growled. "Die!"

At such close range, however, Mordred's spear had become more of a liability than an advantage.

The spear in his hand vanished as his massive fist came crashing down.

But Anthony had anticipated this move perfectly. Before the punch could land, he pivoted sharply, driving his elbow straight into Mordred's chest.

The lightning-fast counter caught Mordred completely off guard. The elbow strike, even more devastating than a punch, forced him to stumble backward. Yet Mordred managed to keep his footing.

Before Mordred could retaliate, Anthony spun into action again. He launched himself into the air, rotating a full 180 degrees.

His right leg whipped around like a steel cable, cutting through the air as it aimed for Mordred's throat.

The speed and fluid connection of his moves left Mordred stunned.

The air crackled with electricity as Anthony followed up with another strike. His fingers spread wide like a tiger's claws, sparks dancing at his fingertips.

He slashed across Mordred's chest, tearing through his shirt and leaving bloody scratches on his dark skin.

It was Killing Kick!

Anthony seized his moment, launching himself into a perfect 360-degree spin before unleashing a devastating roundhouse kick aimed straight at Mordred's head.

The powerful strike connected, finally sending Mordred's massive frame flying through the air.

The sheer force behind the blow carried Mordred through three massive trees, each thicker than a man's body, splintering them like toothpicks.

Without missing a beat, Anthony gripped Dragonfang and charged forward with incredible speed.

The sword gleamed with an eerie blue light while his eyes burned with unmistakable killing intent.

Though his relentless attacks hadn't broken through Mordred's defenses yet, they had him completely overwhelmed.

If bare hands couldn't crack that defense, Anthony reasoned, maybe Dragonfang would do the trick.

Just as Anthony had predicted, his flood of attacks had pushed Mordred into a corner. His fury was building. One moment of carelessness had put him at a disadvantage he couldn't overcome.

This was it—time to end this!

Anthony rushed in for the kill, his murderous intent reaching its peak.

"Argh!" Mordred's voice boomed across the battlefield. "You bastard. I'll tear you apart!"

But as Anthony closed in, Mordred let out an earth-shattering roar.

Suddenly, a mysterious force exploded within him as the crimson mist began to rise from his body.
