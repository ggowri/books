Chapter 149 Escape

"What?"

Anthony's face darkened, and he pretended to be surprised and asked, "Who are you? How dare you meddle in my business?"

Arthur said, "I am Aaron Hastings from Draconic Shield!

"I'm here to kill you on behalf of the Smith family! Go to hell!"

Arthur came with great momentum, waved his hand, and rushed towards Anthony.

Anthony countered the opponent's moves and refused to retreat. For a moment, the dust was flying.

"Wow!"

"Aaron? The boss of Draconic Shield?"

"So strong! It seems that this he is even stronger than the Black Cloak."

"Yeah, it seems like Anthony is going to lose the fight..."

After more than a dozen rounds, Anthony gradually fell into a disadvantage. Arthur became unstoppable!

Anne finally breathed a sigh of relief and sneered, "This time, Anthony will be killed!"

Alvin was also still in shock and nodded, "If that's the case, the money is worth it.

"We can kill Anthony and also win over such a master."

While the two were talking, Anthony became anxious and shouted angrily, then seemed to use all his strength to punch out.

This punch forced Arthur to retreat. And then, Anthony seemed to be about to run away.

"Stop him!

"Don't let him get away!"

Seeing that Anthony was about to run away, Anne became anxious and shouted, "Mr. Hastings, kill him!"

"He can't get away."

Arthur smiled coldly, and then quickly chased after Anthony.

However, Anthony's speed was so fast that he almost rushed out of the Smith Manor.

Unexpectedly, Arthur trembled, and then howled.

Immediately afterwards, his speed increased several times in an instant. In a blink of an eye, he caught up with the running Anthony and kicked him.

With a fierce kick, Anthony was kicked several feet away!

However, Anthony used the force of this kick to leap a few hundred feet forward and rushed out of the Smith Manor.

"Werewolf?"

"Mr. Hastings is a werewolf? No wonder he is so strong..."

"Metahuman! I've heard that everyone in Draconic Shield has special skills..."

Arthur howled. And the crowd was shocked once again and started discussing.

However, after Anthony rushed out of the Smith Manor, Dariel rushed over. Without saying a word, Dariel burst out with all his strength and stopped Arthur.

The two fought and retreated, but Anthony had already disappeared.

"Aaron.

"The Turner family will not let this matter go!"

Dariel also imitated Anthony and fled after saying a cruel word.

"Coward!"

Arthur cursed angrily in the direction where Anthony and Dariel were fleeing.

"Wow!"

"Mr. Hastings is awesome! So strong!"

"The Metahuman is indeed powerful. Mr. Hastings is so majestic..."

"We are finally not afraid of Anthony. If he dares to come again, we will beat him to death..."

Anthony fled in panic, and Dariel was also driven away.

There was a deafening cheer from the crowd.

Arthur stood proudly, like a god, enjoying the cheers and admiration of the crowd.

"Thank you, Mr. Hastings..."

The most excited person must be Anne. She walked over quickly and smiled charmingly at Aaron.

Arthur said, "You are welcome. I'm just doing what I promised.

"Anthony has escaped.

"Besides, he won't dare to come back in a short time. I'll take my leave now."

After saying that, Arthur prepared to take his people away.

"Wait a moment..."

However, Anne got anxious and pulled him back, saying, "Mr. Hastings, why are you so anxious? Can we talk about the next step of cooperation?"

"What?"

Arthur pretended to be confused and turned around to ask, "What do you mean? Don't you think the cost is too high?"

"Well..."

Anne smiled helplessly and said, "Mr. Hastings, we can talk about it, right?"

"No!"

Arthur shook his head decisively and said solemnly, "Five hundred million dollars a year. No room for negotiation."

...

Anne was stunned for a moment.

The cost was outrageous.

However, everyone had seen Arthur's strength. Anthony, who defeated the Black Cloak and killed many people, was just defeated by Arthur.

Moreover, Arthur was a Metahuman.

Besides, Anthony escaped, not died.

If one day Anthony returned, and Arthur was not there, the Smith family would suffer.

The cost was ridiculously high, but this amount of money could save the Smith family from disaster. It was actually not a loss.
