Chapter 100 Potent Healing

The battle outside was earth-shattering.

The dull roar was unceasing, and the ground trembled violently.

The heavy door of the safe house, crafted from fine steel, shut out all the chaos the moment it closed, leaving the interior tranquil and peaceful.

"Anthony..."

Rose was frantic with worry, disregarding everything else as she helped Anthony to the bedroom.

She desperately activated her healing power to tend to Anthony's wounds.

However, Anthony's injuries were too severe.

Rose had already used her healing power once before, and her strength was significantly depleted.

The remnants of her power were utterly insufficient to heal Anthony.

"Anthony! Please don't!"

Rose realized that as well. Her heart ached, and she was frantic, wiping away tears while desperately trying to ignite her power.

"Ms. Turner..."

Helen couldn't bear it, rushing over to hold Rose back.

"No. I have to save Anthony."

"Ms. Turner, please calm down."

Helen pleaded urgently, "Ms. Turner, I beg you. You're already very weak. If you force your power, it might cost you your life."

"I'm not afraid. I'm not afraid to die if it means saving Anthony."

Rose pushed Helen away once more, her gaze unwavering.

Then, she shuddered, and a fine mist enveloped her arms.

The mist traveled down her slender arms, gathering in her palms and transforming into a mighty healing power that surged into Anthony's body.

However, Rose's complexion also visibly paled, her weakness becoming increasingly apparent.

"Ms. Turner. Please don't."

Helen was terrified and began to wail, "Ms. Carolyn Turner said that unless it's absolutely necessary, you must not burn your essence to heal others..."

Before Helen could finish, a stream of blood suddenly gushed from the corner of Rose's mouth.

But Rose paid no heed, still desperately igniting her power.

Helen tried with all her might to pull her away, but it was to no avail.

"No. Don't..."

The potent healing power flooded into Anthony's body. He gradually regained consciousness, slowly opening his eyes and shaking his head at Rose.

"Anthony... Thank goodness, Anthony, you're finally awake."

Rose screamed in delight, throwing herself into his arms, crying tears of joy.

Rose then waved to Helen and said, "Helen, you may leave us."

Helen was taken aback, staring at Rose in confusion. She advised, "Ms. Turner, please don't do anything rash..."

"Leave now." Rose's tone became stern before Helen could finish.

"Yes, Ms. Turner."

Helen reluctantly exited the bedroom.

Anthony disregarded the situation and said to Rose, "Ms. Turner, I won't die just yet. You must stop using your superpower. Otherwise, you will die."

"No." Rose shook her head and said somewhat awkwardly, "Anthony, actually, I have another way to save you..."

Rose's pale face uncontrollably flushed with embarrassment.

Anthony's heart skipped a beat.

After Rose's treatment, he had recovered quite a bit, and his mind was much clearer.

After a moment of shyness, Rose asked softly, her face red, "Anthony, do you love me?"

Did he love her?

Rose was the acknowledged goddess of Yoland, the dream lover of countless men.

Did Anthony love her?

"Yes, I love you!"

Anthony nodded seriously without hesitation.

If he didn't love her, why would Anthony risk his life to protect her?

It was because of the Chosen Path but also because of his love for her.

In their brief time together, Rose had fallen irrevocably in love with Anthony, and he with her.

Seeing Anthony nod, Rose blushed and asked again, "Then do you want me?"

Heaven knew how much courage it took Rose to ask that question.

"I want you."

Anthony nodded again without hesitation.

By this point, he had pretty much guessed what Rose's method was.

The union of a man and a woman was the most effective way to heal.

In fact, Anthony was also eager to unite with Rose.

The second task of the Chosen Path was to win over Rose.

Once he completed the task of the Chosen Path, he would not only receive unexpected rewards, but his Devil Eye could also potentially make a breakthrough.

When the Devil Eye evolved to 2.0, Anthony's strength would soar.

In that case, the werewolf army outside would be no concern at all.

"Anthony... be gentle. Don't hurt me..."

Anthony's heart fluttered as Rose, blushing, nestled into his arms.

Her body was soft and warm.

A faint fragrance wafted into his nose, instantly arousing Anthony's body.

Seemingly considering Anthony's injuries, Rose took the initiative, gently removing his tattered shirt from the battle.

Her warm hands slowly explored his abdomen, clumsily undoing his belt.

After that, Rose stopped. She whispered, "Anthony, you do it..."

Anthony swallowed hard and turned over, pressing Rose beneath him.
