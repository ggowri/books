Chapter 210 The Turner Family's Savior

Just as Mordred had said, Astaroth had merely been careless for a moment.

Even so, Anthony's reaction and counterattacks had left him astonished.

However, Astaroth was a Level B Warrior, blessed with immense natural strength and extraordinary defenses.

Thus, after being forced back by Anthony, Astaroth's rage surged, and his fury-fueled return boosted his combat strength to a terrifying level.

Empowered by his overwhelming strength, Astaroth dodged Anthony's fierce attack and landed a punch that sent Anthony flying.

As Anthony was thrown through the air, a surge of alarm shot through him.

Astaroth's power was indeed formidable.

If this had been back when he first encountered Astaroth, Anthony doubted he could have lasted even ten rounds.

But that was then, and this was now.

Although he appeared disheveled after being struck, there wasn't the slightest hint of panic on Anthony's face. Instead, a fiery fighting spirit burned brightly in his eyes.

His entire body seemed to swell with power, muscles bulging to the point of nearly tearing apart his already tattered and battered clothing.

Through the tears in his clothes, the raised veins crisscrossing his body could be seen clearly, like a sprawling web covering him from head to toe.

"You little punk!"

Astaroth charged toward him, bellowing, "It was just a moment of distraction, and you dare spout such arrogant nonsense?"

"Today, I'll make you wish you were dead!"

As he charged, a ferocious energy erupted from Astaroth, enveloping his towering frame.

His massive form seemed to grow even taller, and his muscles swelled to a degree even more exaggerated than Anthony's.

"Spit," Anthony turned his head and spat out a mouthful of dust, then curled his lips into a wicked smirk.

He sneered, "Your strength is indeed impressive, but it's far from enough. Not nearly enough."

As his words fell, Anthony raised his fist abruptly.

It collided squarely with Astaroth's punch. The moment they struck, a dull, deafening thud echoed in the air.

Beneath their feet came a crisp cracking sound, this was the sound of rock breaking beneath the thick layers of dust.

This was, after all, a mining area, and the layer of dust beneath them was several inches deep. Looking closely, one could see both men's feet had sunk deeply into the ground.

"Impossible! How could you..."

After the punch landed, Astaroth's expression changed drastically, his eyes wide with shock.

Before he could finish his sentence, Anthony let out a cold chuckle and said, "You think you were the only one who could amplify your strength?"

"You think you're that strong? Let me show you what real power looks like!"

As Anthony's words fell, he gave a sudden shake of his arm, releasing a surge of tremendous energy that forced Astaroth back three steps.

Without hesitation, Anthony's shoulders trembled, and his already bulging muscles swelled further. He took a step forward, choosing to go on the offensive.

His muscles seemed on the verge of exploding, and his entire body radiated an overwhelming aura of power.

Astaroth's face darkened, and he instinctively threw a punch to the counter.

But his hurried punch lacked sufficient force.

With a muffled thud, Astaroth stumbled back another step, his heart filled with unparalleled shock.

Yet before he could process it, Anthony struck again and again, each blow fiercer than the last, like an unstoppable avalanche.

"You bastard!"

Astaroth roared in fury, shouting, "I'm gonna..."

"Shut up!"

Before he could finish, Anthony's sharp voice interrupted him.

Leaping into the air, Anthony's right leg lashed out like a whip, slicing through the air with a sharp, whistling sound as it aimed directly for Astaroth's face.

His attacks came like a relentless tide, one after another.

Fists, kicks, elbows, knees, shoulders, every part of Anthony's body seemed to become a weapon for assault.

His strikes were swift and seamless, their transitions smooth and fluid.

Despite Astaroth's reliance on defense and his self-proclaimed invincibility due to his immense strength, he was now being forced back step by step, completely unable to fight back against Anthony's onslaught.

To Astaroth, Anthony didn't seem all that powerful on the surface. But every time he attacked, Anthony's moves seemed to perfectly counter his own.

All his raw strength was rendered useless, and he could only rely on his exceptional defenses to endure wave after wave of Anthony's relentless assaults.

In the distance, the onlookers were dumbfounded. Eyes widened in disbelief, they stared at Anthony as though they had seen a ghost.

Earlier, one could still attribute the situation to Astaroth's carelessness. But now, this was clearly something entirely different.

Anthony's physique was a full head shorter than Astaroth's, yet his attacks were far more ferocious.

"Hahaha..."

"Mr. Johnson is going to win! He's going to win!"

"Ms. Carolyn Turner, Astaroth isn't a match for Anthony! We finally have hope!"

"Hmph! Vincent, your schemes have failed. Mr. Johnson won't let you off the hook!"

Seeing Anthony pushing Astaroth back step by step, many of the Turner family elders, and even Dariel, couldn't suppress their excitement.

Amidst the crowd, Carolyn finally let out a sigh of relief. Anthony's strength was like a potent shot of adrenaline, reigniting a flicker of hope in her heart.

"Silence!"

As the crowd's excitement grew, Mordred's face darkened, and he barked, "Astaroth is merely warming up. This pathetic fool can't be your savior!"

"Hmph! Astaroth could crush him effortlessly. Just wait and see..."

Before Mordred could finish, a strange booming sound echoed from the distance.
