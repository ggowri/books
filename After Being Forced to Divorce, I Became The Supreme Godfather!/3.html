Chapter 3 The Chosen Path

"Chosen Path? Successor?" Anthony asked, his face a picture of confusion.

Michael noticed his look and explained, "Listen up—becoming Godfather ain't a cakewalk. It's a gauntlet of death traps and mind games. That's the Chosen Path.

"Beat these trials, and you're the next supreme ruler. Everyone bows, hailing you as their king. Money, power, pussy - snap your fingers, it's yours."

Anthony's heart raced, blood pumping hot.

When I make it, Anne and Alvin are dead meat!

Every sucker who laughed at me's gonna regret breathing!

Michael let out a cold, mirthless chuckle. "Slow your roll, kid. Before you hit the top, you're gonna eat shit and almost die. One slip-up? Game over.

"Top dogs walk alone. I won't save your ass. Even if you're bleeding out, that's on you. Your cross to bear. I'd stand by and let fate take its course."

Anthony's face went stone serious.

Michael continued, "Look, if you're too scared of the Chosen Path, that's cool. You're still family. I'll set you up nice - live like a trust fund baby, zero worries.

"Now, tell me your choice!"

Under Michael's laser stare, Anthony went deep in thought.

The Chosen Path could get him killed, especially solo.

Living rich and lazy had its perks, but that boring life felt like a total prison, which might destroy his soul.

Though the Chosen Path was sketchy as hell, the payoff was God-tier. If he could survive the test, he would become the next powerful Godfather!

Everything he wanted - his for the taking.

Better yet? He could hit back at those scums who humiliated him.

The memory hit like a truck - his wife throwing him out while flaunting her side piece. Rage burned through his veins, turning his vision blood red.

Like a caged wolf, Anthony's fists clenched as he roared.

"Hell yes! I'm taking the Path! I'll crush these trials and become the next Godfather!"

Michael's eyes flashed approval. "Good. Now get your Dragon Ring. That's step one on your journey."

Dragon Ring?

Anthony's mind drifted back to that somber-hued ring he'd worn since he was a kid. Left index finger. Never knew what it was made of.

When he married Anne, she'd bitched about how ugly it was until he took it off. So, he'd stashed it in their bedroom jewelry box.

Hard to believe that simple ring was a precious token left by Michael.

Now he had to get it back.

"I'm going now!" Anthony jumped up, ready to bolt.

"Hold up." Michael raised his hand. In an instant, a blue hologram screen popped up, looking straight out of sci-fi.

"See where you stand right now."

The screen lit up with Yoland's hottest gossip online.

[BREAKING: Limp-dick Anthony marries Smith princess—can't get it up for 3 years!]

[LOSER ALERT: Anthony got kicked to the curb with nothing. Most pathetic dude ever?]

[POWER COUPLE: Anne & Alvin's engagement bash tomorrow. Big money meets bigger money!]

[WANTED: Anthony jacked Smith millions in jewels. Kill him, treasure's yours!]

[Shadow Tigers calling dibs on Anthony's head!]

[Axe Gang: That punk's dead meat!]

[GET HIM - GET RICH!]

It got even crazier.

Some psychos wanted to publicly chop his junk off. Called him a disgrace to men everywhere.

Anthony just stood there, shell-shocked.

That bitch Raina must've run her mouth to Anne. Now Anne and Alvin had cooked up this shit storm.

They were framing him for the jewel theft, trying to get the gangs to do their dirty work and eliminate him.

Michael shot him a look. "Hitting Smith Manor for that Dragon Ring is like walking into a death trap. Those hunters want your head. Whether you live or die - it's all on you now.

"But here's the deal - you're halfway up this mountain. You can take any ride from the garage. Good luck."

Anthony's brain kicked back into gear.

Smart players needed solid plans. Charging in like some dumbass hero? Pure suicide.

Between the Smith and Blake hitmen, plus those trigger-happy thugs drooling over the jewels, he'd be Swiss cheese.

Tomorrow night was perfect - Anne and Alvin's engagement party would have everyone distracted. Security was bound to slacken. Perfect time to slip in and grab that ring...

The next night, Anthony transformed into a deadly predator, ready to hunt.

In the garage, his eyes locked onto a sick Hellfire Bike in the corner - matte black with wicked skull designs crawling up the frame. His face split into a wolf's grin.

Motorcycles were his thing. Truth was, he'd even paid bills as a stunt rider back in the day.

He straddled that beast and pulled on a mask, feeling like Ghost Rider incarnate.

The engine screamed when he twisted the throttle. Then he was gone, tearing down that mountain road like a demon straight out of hell.
