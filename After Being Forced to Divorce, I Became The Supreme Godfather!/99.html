Chapter 98 You Shall Die

Carolyn arrived, exuding an awe-inspiring presence that was nothing short of world-shaking.

In the blink of an eye, a colossal sword stood suspended in the air as if ready to cleave the heavens and the earth.

Then, with devastating force, it swung towards Mason.

Mason's face drained of color, and he let out a roar. Without a moment's hesitation, he retreated as fast as he could.

In an instant, he reigned in his tyrannical power, channeling everything into his legs.

And so, Mason unleashed a speed that was nothing short of extraordinary.

His billowing black robe flapped noisily as he seemed to teleport dozens of feet away.

However, before Mason could touch down, Carolyn barked, "Trying to run? Halt!"

The formidable sword suddenly flipped its blade, shooting out like an arrow.

In a flash, the sword reached over a hundred feet, catching up to Mason, who was desperately fleeing for his life.

With a thunderous crash, the sword fell from the sky, causing the earth to tremble.

A terrifying wave of energy swept across an area of over a hundred feet. The ground split, with horrifying fissures spreading in all directions.

The immense impact kicked up an astonishing cloud of dust and sand.

The werewolf army, over a hundred feet away, couldn't dodge in time and was sent tumbling.

Visible dust and debris, like a violent gust of wind, overturned the werewolf ranks and destroyed structures within its reach.

The ground cracked open with fissures several feet deep, a sight to behold.

Once the dust settled, a massive crater, dozens of feet wide and several feet deep, appeared on the ground.

Endless cracks radiated from the crater.

Yet, in the enormous pit, there was no sign of Mason.

Jane's eyes widened in shock as she muttered in disbelief, "Is he dead? Turned to dust?"

Just as Jane was thinking this, a wail came from the edge of the pit.

She turned to see a figure covered in dust and grime emerging from the rubble. It was Mason.

The immense impact had torn his extremely durable black robe to tatters, revealing his muscular body and hairy limbs.

Seeing Mason crawl out, Jane was aghast.

Could he have survived that?

Not far away, Carolyn was also taken aback, a look of helplessness on her face.

But she didn't hesitate for long.

Before Mason could fully climb out of the pit, Carolyn flicked her wrist, and the sand and debris at the edge of the pit shot into the sky.

In the blink of an eye, a swirling tornado formed, creating a powerful gust within dozens of feet.

Huge marble blocks on the ground flew into the air as if they were made of foam.

Distant iron fences shattered, their pieces converging towards the tempest.

Mason was aghast. His voluminous black robe flapped wildly in the tornado's grasp.

The tornado, like a gaping maw, seemed intent on devouring all in its path as it approached Mason.

As the tornado neared, a powerful force of tearing through the air.

This force was unstoppable, threatening to rip Mason apart.

Mason retreated desperately, but the suction of the wind was too strong.

He grabbed onto a railing, only for it to be uprooted.

He clung to a tree, only for it to be snapped in half.

The terrifying tornado annihilated everything in its path.

"Damn you. You dared to deceive me. You shall die. You shall die! I'll make you suffer!"

At the critical moment, Mason, furious and desperate, cursed loudly.

His vast and highly durable black robe was finally torn apart, sucked into the tornado, and instantly reduced to shreds.

The power of the tornado was so terrifying that if it struck, Mason would undoubtedly be shredded into pieces.

Unfortunately, when the tornado was just thirty feet away from Mason, Carolyn suddenly convulsed and spat out a mouthful of blood.

The unstoppable tornado instantly dissipated significantly, then continued to unravel.

The immense power dissipated at that moment.

"No!"

Carolyn's eyes turned bloodshot as she shouted.

She forcibly took a deep breath, and in an instant, the dissipating tornado rose into the sky once more.

But the next second, Carolyn spat out blood again, and the tornado unraveled once more.

"Ms. Turner! Don't!"

Seeing Carolyn about to exert her superpowers desperately, Jane stopped her. "Mr. Johnson just said he had a way to resolve everything."

Carolyn was taken aback, then asked with a face full of surprise, "Really? What method does Mr. Johnson have?"

"I have no idea."

Jane shook her head helplessly. "Mr. Johnson didn't finish speaking before he fainted.

"I've sent people to escort Mr. Johnson and Ms. Rose Turner to a safe house. I believe that once Mr. Johnson recovers, he will definitely have a plan.

"So, all we need to do is to guard the safe house and protect Mr. Johnson and Ms. Rose Turner's safety at all costs."

"Understood."

Carolyn took a deep breath, thought for a moment, and nodded resolutely.
