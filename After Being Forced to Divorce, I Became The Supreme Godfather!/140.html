Chapter 139 Don't Waste It

Ben's breakthrough left everyone stunned.

The crowd erupted in praise, almost elevating Ben and the Smith family to legendary status.

Anne stood tall in the crowd, her pride swelling as she called out to Anthony, "If you're scared, Anthony, there's still time to kneel and beg for mercy.

"I'm generous. My earlier offer still stands! Kneel and beg, and your life will be spared."

As she spoke, Anne walked outside.

They faced each other from a distance, with Anne deliberately taking a commanding posture as if ready to accept Anthony's submission.

"Hahaha..."

"What are you waiting for, you worthless fool? Kneel!"

"Ms. Smith is showing you mercy, giving you chance after chance. Don't waste it!"

"That's right! You're about to die, just kneel already!"

"Kneel now, it's better than dying!"

Anne's appearance stirred up the crowd once again.

Emboldened by Ben's status as a Warrior, they pressed closer, eager to witness Anthony's submission.

"Hahaha..."

Unexpectedly, Anthony threw his head back in laughter. After a moment, he addressed Anne, "Funny thing. My earlier words still stand, too.

"I said you will beg me to fuck you. If you want to kneel now, I won't stop you..."

"Ah!"

Before he could finish, Anne exploded with rage, her eyes blazing as she screamed, "Ben! Kill him! Kill him now! Tear him apart!"

"Yes!"

Ben's eyes blazed with fury. "Loser! Go to hell!"

With a thunderous roar, Ben charged at Anthony once again.

In his rage, each of Ben's footsteps shattered the floor beneath him.

His rampage kicked up clouds of dust, his killing intent palpable in the air.

The true power of a Warrior was absolutely terrifying!

The ground trembled, sending spectators scrambling backward in fear.

"Thunder Fist!"

After his wild charge, Ben's fist shot upward like a rocket.

As his power surged, his fist seemed to grow even larger.

Now the size of a punching bag, his fist was wrapped in a deadly aura that could split stones and create sonic booms.

"Thunder Fist? Is that a Warrior technique?"

"Hmph! Anthony is just a worthless trash, but at least Ben's giving him the honor of dying to a real technique."

"Yeah, dying to a move like that he should count himself lucky..."

A Warrior's technique allowed them to compress their power through their meridians before releasing it in a devastating burst.

As Ben unleashed his Thunder Fist, the crowd gasped in amazement, confident that this would be Anthony's end.

Anthony remained frozen in place, appearing stunned.

In reality, though, he was contemplating whether to test out a combat technique of his own.

Why not?

With that thought, Anthony quickly selected a specific combat skill from his mental repertoire.

It wasn't that Anthony was being slow. Ben's movements were just laughably sluggish to him.

Even without activating his Devil Eye, Ben's movements appeared to Anthony like a slow-motion movie scene.

The gap in their abilities was just too vast.

After his breakthrough, Ben was barely a Level A Warrior, while Anthony was a solid Level B Warrior!

Plus, Anthony's combat experience and mastery of various fighting techniques far exceeded Ben's.

"Super Fist!"

Despite his casual demeanor, Anthony's movements were anything but slow.

As Ben's fist came flying in, Anthony met it head-on with his punch, clean and precise.

"You worthless piece of trash! You're asking for death!"

Seeing Anthony dare to meet his punch directly, Ben let out a cold laugh.

"Anthony must be insane! What's he thinking?"

"He dares to go fist-to-fist with Ben? Doesn't he know Ben's a boxing champion?"

"Plus, Ben just reached his peak. That punch of his must pack twenty years of training behind it..."

Ben sneered as the crowd buzzed with similar comments.

Just as the whispers reached their peak, Anthony's fist came crashing down.

The collision of fists thundered like a sonic boom!

What followed was the sickening crunch of breaking bones and an agonized scream.

"Argh!"

Everyone watched in shock as Ben went flying backward.

A spray of blood followed his trajectory through the air.

Anthony's punch had been so devastating that it had shattered Ben's fist, broken his hand, and left blood everywhere!

Yet there stood Anthony, precisely as before, not having moved an inch!

The punch had happened so fast that barely anyone had seen how it went down.

All they knew was that Ben, who had confidently unleashed his Thunder Fist with full force, had been sent flying by a single counter-punch!

Ben was a legitimate Warrior!

One round and his arm was brutally broken?

Wasn't this the same as being crippled?

The Smith family had finally produced a Warrior, and before they could even bask in the glory for a few minutes, he was ruined!

And to make matters worse, the one who crippled Ben was a notorious loser!

The crowd, which had been buzzing with gossip and schadenfreude just moments before, fell instantly silent as if someone had flipped a switch. You could hear a pin drop.
