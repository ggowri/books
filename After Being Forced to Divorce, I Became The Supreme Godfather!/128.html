Chapter 127 Be Ours!

"Not coming?"

Anne's face darkened as she snapped, "He wouldn't dare! Besides, with Yoland locked down tight, where does he think he can run?"

"That's true..."

Alvin nodded, then frowned. "Still, we can't underestimate Anthony's abilities, no matter what.

"Something's off about that kid lately. His strength doesn't seem natural.

"Mr. Foster is a Level B Metahuman, yet Anthony held his own for so long..."

"Hmph!" Before he could finish, Anne cut him off with a contemptuous snort.

"He's just trash-pumped full of drugs. How strong could he really be?

"Who knows? He might drop dead within three days anyway."

Alvin ignored her anger-laden words and continued, "Draconic Shield has been making waves lately, taking down several security companies right after their debut.

"They're calling themselves the Zodiac, and word is each member is stronger than the last. If we could get them on our side..."

"Hah!"

This time, before Anne could speak, Ben's cold laugh rang out from nearby. "Mr. Blake, got a problem with us brothers?"

"Anthony's nothing but garbage propped up by drugs. So what if he got lucky and lasted a few rounds against Mr. Foster?

"Give me whatever drugs he's taking, and I bet I could hold my own against Mr. Foster too!"

Ben, the head of security at the Smith family, was an Expert whose skills nearly matched those of a Warrior.

His abilities were nothing to sneeze at.

Seeing Alvin praise Anthony so highly and deliberately invite outsiders for protection naturally rubbed him the wrong way.

Alvin frowned, giving Ben a contemptuous look.

However, out of respect for Anne, he held his tongue and said instead, "That's not what I meant. I'm just trying to cover all our bases.

"It's not just the Smith family, the Blake family has been considering this matter too."

At these words, Black Cloak, who had been standing silently behind them, spoke up. "Mr. Blake, how come I haven't heard anything about this?"

Yoland's Draconic Shield was making waves, and their reputation was undoubtedly impressive.

Black Cloak knew this much for sure.

But he'd never heard anything about the Blake family planning to change their security team.

As a full-fledged Warrior, Black Cloak's power ranked among the top in Yoland.

"Heh."

Seeing Black Cloak speak up, Alvin gave a helpless smile and said, "Black Cloak, what's this about? Worried about being replaced?"

"If that's the case, you're overthinking it. You've served the Blake family loyally for years. They won't do you wrong."

"No, Mr. Blake, you've misunderstood my meaning," Black Cloak said with a stern expression, shaking his head.

"I know a thing or two about the Draconic Shield you mentioned earlier. The Zodiac is powerful, yes, but their background is murky at best.

"A large group of mysterious yet powerful individuals suddenly showed up in Yoland. Something doesn't sit right with me.

"So if Mr. Johnson truly intends to make contact with them, I'd advise extreme caution."

The warning was well-intentioned and reasonable.

Before Alvin could respond, Anne nodded and said, "Black Cloak is right. The Zodiac's sudden appearance and unclear motives are concerning. We'd better not rock the boat right now.

"Besides, with Black Cloak and Ben's team holding down the fort, what trouble could a lowlife like Anthony possibly cause?

"If they can't handle Anthony together, then the Draconic Shield's people probably can't either."

"Ms. Smith makes excellent points."

Black Cloak was the first to speak up respectfully. "Don't worry, Mr. Johnson, Ms. Smith. If Anthony dares show his face, I'll be the first to end him!"

His words carried a fierce intensity.

The incident where he'd slipped up and been bested by Anthony in front of everyone still burned in his mind.

"I have complete faith in Black Cloak's abilities," Alvin declared.

Seeing everyone's unanimous agreement, Alvin reluctantly nodded.

After a pause, he added, "Still, let's be honest here. Everyone's seen what the Zodiac is capable of.

"They're a force to be reckoned with. Since we're inviting all the prominent Yoland families to this gathering, why not extend an invitation to the Draconic Shield folks, too?

"First off, making allies with powerhouses like them can't hurt, right?

"Second, we could use this chance to convince them to join Evil Spirit Legion with us.

"If we pull this off, it'll be a major win. Mr. Foster would definitely reward us handsomely, don't you think?"

Before anyone else could speak, Alvin turned directly to Anne.

"That's right!"

Anne's eyes lit up at his words. "Brilliant! Why didn't I think of that?"

"They're newcomers with no connections in Yoland. We won't even need to sweet-talk them much. If they join us, perfect.

"And if they refuse? One word from us, and no matter how strong they are, they'll lose their foothold in Yoland!"

"Exactly!" Alvin nodded. "Once that's settled, their background won't matter anymore.

"After joining Evil Spirit Legion, we'll all be family. Yoland will soon be ours for the taking!"
