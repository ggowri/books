Chapter 135 Do It Again

"Kneel down?"

Anne's angry shout echoed throughout the Smith Manor.

Moments later, Anne and Alvin strutted over, surrounded by their entourage.

"Well, well...

"Look who we have here, huh? Turner family lapdogs..."

Anne pushed through the crowd and spotted Dariel, she sneered as she shot Anthony a cold look. "Trash will always be trash. I was wondering how you dared to act so arrogant.

"It turns out you have the Turners protecting you, you coward!"

Alvin swaggered over, laughing scornfully. "Seems like I was right," he sneered. "You're nothing but a pretty-boy freeloader.

"A grown man hiding behind a woman's skirt. What a coward!"

Anthony had indeed arrived, but he brought the Turner family with him.

Seeing this, Anne and Alvin naturally assumed Anthony had pledged allegiance to the Turner family.

"How dare you!

"Alvin, do you have a death wish?"

Before Anthony could even respond to their mockery, Dariel exploded with rage.

Completely forgetting Anthony's earlier warning, she clenched her fist, ready to punch Alvin's lights out.

"Calm down."

Anthony stepped in to block his path. "Captain Stein, let's not be hasty.

"Everyone knows Mr. Blake isn't a real man anyway. Why waste your time on someone like that?"

"What?"

His words sent a ripple through the room, and many instinctively glanced at Alvin.

Quite a few people had seen the video that Anthony had publicly released at the engagement banquet.

News of it had spread throughout Yoland in the past few days, but everyone had been hesitant to mention it, given the Blake family's influence.

Yet now, in front of everyone, Anthony had brought it up again, and right in front of Alvin!

"You!

"You bastard, Anthony! I'll fucking kill you!"

As expected, Alvin exploded at the mention of it, his eyes bulging with rage as he lunged forward.

"Got a death wish?"

Before Alvin could reach him, Dariel stepped forward to shield Anthony.

"Fuck!"

Alvin was seeing red now. "Dariel, don't think being from the Turner family means you can look down on everyone.

"This is Smith family territory, not your Turner family playground!

"And what's so great about the Turner family anyway?

"Mr. Foster will be here soon. If the Turner family doesn't wise up, Mr. Foster won't let you off easy!

"If you know what's good for you, get out of my way right now. Otherwise, you'll be the first one I take down!"

The Evil Spirit Legion was coming!

Those words sent a chill down Dariel's spine. He had to admit this was a genuine concern for the Turner family right now.

The very reason Carolyn sent Dariel was precisely for this.

"Hah!"

Noticing Dariel's darkening expression, Anne smirked, crossing her arms. "Looks like the Turner family can barely keep themselves afloat, huh?

"Anthony, I really wonder how a worthless piece of trash like you got the Turner family to go all out for your sake.

"The Turner family might be able to protect you now, but they can't shield you forever.

"And from today on, the Turner family will lose their foothold in Yoland completely.

"Soon enough, the Smith family will take their place and become the sole rulers of Yoland."

Perhaps because she'd received news about the Evil Spirit Legion first, Anne was already treating herself and the Smith family as members of the organization.

Or rather, she'd already fantasized about their imminent future, rising to power, soaring to the top, becoming Yoland's rulers!

She'd even started treating these grand delusions as reality.

Anthony let out a sneer, waving off Dariel once again. "I've never needed anyone's protection, and the Smith family will never replace the Turner family. Never!"

"Stop acting tough, Anthony!" Anne let out a cold snort, staring at him with contempt. "Don't need anyone's protection? Weren't you running for your life from Mr. Foster just the other day?

"Weren't you hiding behind a woman like a coward?

"Why keep up this act when we all know the truth?

"You came to the Smith family today because you're scared Mr. Foster will go after you, right?

"Let's cut to the chase! Get on your knees before me, the head of the Smith family. Bow down and beg for mercy.

"If I'm in a good mood, I might consider sparing your worthless life. You can stay as my obedient dog!"

Anne's arrogance was becoming impossible to hide.

Her haughty expression showed how desperately she wanted Anthony to kneel and prostrate before everyone.

Alvin burst out laughing and joined in the taunting. "She's right, Anthony. Why keep pretending when we all know what you are?

"Everyone knows you're just a freeloader living off others!

"Rose isn't here anyway, so just bow down and save your skin. Makes sense, right?"

"Yeah!"

"Mr. Blake is right! Get on your knees!"

"A freeloader like you has no pride to protect anyway!"

"What are you waiting for? Kneel down already..."

What started with Alvin's taunts quickly spread as he encouraged others to join in. Soon, the whole crowd was shouting in unison.

Anthony's gaze darkened as he stepped forward, a cold smirk playing on his lips. "Anne, since you love kneeling so much, why not do it again?

"After all, you've done it before—begging me to fuck you, right?

"Alvin, I bet you didn't know this, huh?

"Anne, my ex-wife, your fiancee. Even after you two got engaged, she was on her knees, crying and begging me to screw her.

"Oh, my bad.

"I almost forgot.. you're not much of a man, are you? That's why you can't satisfy her. So, of course, she'd come back to me, begging for what I could give her, isn't that right?"
