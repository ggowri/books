Chapter 101 Armageddon

Helen left the bedroom, her mind a whirlwind of confusion.

"Oh no. Could Ms. Turner..."

After closing the door, Helen realized something, and her face drained of color. She was about to push the door open again but then hesitated.

As Rose's maid, Helen knew she had an ultimate healing method.

And this method only worked for the man she loved.

It didn't matter who the man was. What mattered was that he was the man she loved.

However, Helen also knew that Rose had only been with Anthony for a few days. Could her feelings for him genuinely be called "love"?

What if it wasn't love?

"Wouldn't Ms. Turner be at a great disadvantage then?" It was Helen's first thought.

And she stopped because she had another thought.

Their few days together were brief, but Helen could see the sincerity in Rose's feelings for Anthony.

Moreover, during the recent battle, Rose's concern for Anthony and her willingness to do anything for him seemed to be enough to prove her love.

In the end, Helen muttered to herself, "Love is such a strange thing."

She simply sat on the sofa, propped her chin in her hands, pouted, and gradually got distracted.

She had never been in love. To be precise, she had never been in love with a man.

So, Helen didn't quite understand.

As Helen's thoughts wandered, a loud rumble came from outside.

Even sitting in the safe house, Helen felt the ground beneath her feet tremble.

There were also occasional blood-curdling howls of wolves.

The thunderous commotion made Helen's heart race.

At the same time, she couldn't help but glance at the bedroom door, wondering, "Does this take a long time?"

Perhaps out of curiosity, Helen found herself standing up, approaching the door, and listening closely.

"Ah! Anthony, faster. I'm coming..."

The door had good sound insulation, but as Helen listened closely, a series of moans clearly reached her ears.

"Oh, so embarrassing!"

Helen's face flushed with embarrassment.

She quickly scampered back to the sofa, not daring to approach the door again.

The rumbling outside grew more intense. The earth shook as if it were the end of the world.

The werewolf army pressed on.

The Turner family fought a retreating battle until they had nowhere left to retreat.

Not long ago, the Turner family's medical team arrived. Carolyn took emergency medication to heal and repair her injuries.

The Turner family's medicine was very effective, incomparable to the healing drugs on the market.

However, the werewolf army didn't give them that much time.

How could Dariel alone resist them?

With no other choice, Carolyn and Jane, who were halfway through their treatment, had to join the battle again.

But the sword move she used earlier had almost exhausted all of Carolyn's strength.

The subsequent battle reduced her combat power significantly.

At this point, after another fierce battle, Carolyn was exhausted, her strength depleted.

On the contrary, Mason fought with increasing vigor, as if he had endless energy.

This battle really terrified Carolyn.

The werewolves were not only numerous but also had extraordinary strength.

The Turner family had panaceas that could restore their strength in a short time.

However, the werewolf army seemed to be able to recover their strength in an extremely short time through some means as well.

And from their situation, their recovery speed seemed much faster than the warriors of the Turner family.

In this case, even with the medical team's help, the Turner family still couldn't hold back the werewolf army.

"Men, the Turner family is about to collapse. Charge!

"Kill one member of the Turner family and get a reward of ten million dollars. Kill!

"Break into the safe house, capture Rose alive, and get a reward of one hundred million dollars! Kill Anthony, and get a reward of one hundred million dollars!"

The Turner family retreated outside the safe house. Mason, while fighting Carolyn, excitedly raised his arm and shouted.

Great rewards inevitably attracted brave men, and werewolves were no exception. Mason's words instantly boosted the morale of the werewolf army.

They seemed to be on steroids, staring with green eyes, brandishing sharp claws, and charging forward once again.

"Carolyn, you must die today!"

With the army pressing down, Mason roared and fiercely swung his long knife towards Carolyn.

"Shit! You damn beast."

Carolyn cursed, not willing to show weakness. She swung her arm, instantly condensing a formless sandstorm.

The sandstorm coalesced into a long sword, slashing towards Mason.

Such an attack was no match for Mason.

He casually swung his long knife, shattering the sandstorm, and the long knife fell to the ground.

Carolyn knew she couldn't defeat him and quickly retreated backward.

However, Mason was faster than her.

In a flash, a strike aimed straight for Carolyn's head.

"Beast! Go to hell!"

As the long knife was about to fall, Carolyn's eyes widened, and she quickly waved her hand to cast a spell.

In the blink of an eye, the scattered sand condensed into a four-inch-long short sword.

Though small, the short sword contained immense power. It was like a thin bolt of lightning, heading straight for Mason's throat.

After casting this sword, Carolyn once again spat out blood.

Her legs trembled, and she uncontrollably fell to the ground, her face turning pale.
