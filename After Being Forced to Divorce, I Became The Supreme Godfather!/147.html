Chapter 146 Anxious

Retreat?

It was too late!

Anthony took a step forward.

The violent force swept in all directions in an instant, and an incomparably sharp aura suddenly enveloped the Black Cloak!

The Black Cloak had no way to avoid it!

The man in Black Cloak was helpless, but he made up his mind and poured all his strength into his claw.

Bang!

In a flash, the two separated as soon as they touched.

There was a dull crash, like the sound of rocks collapsing. Under the impact, the Black Cloak retreated three steps in succession.

However, before he could steady his steps, Anthony took advantage of the situation and pursued him.

Anthony's forward pounce was ferocious!

"Where did you learn this technique?"

Seeing Anthony's tiger claws rushing forward, the Black Cloak was frightened and retreated in panic while screaming.

Eagle Claw Attack?

The Black Cloak was very proud of it. It was his most secret skill, and he would never show it to anyone unless it was absolutely necessary!

It had been many years since he last used it. But when he used it again, it was easily cracked by a loser.

Anthony's Thunder Claw was obviously more powerful and fiercer!

And Anthony's own strength was far beyond the Black Cloak's imagination!

Squeak!

Puff!

The Black Cloak retreated in panic, while Anthony took advantage of the victory and became more and more courageous.

The Black Cloak was gradually defeated, and his clothes were torn apart bit by bit.

If he dodged in time, he would not suffer any flesh injuries. However, if he failed to dodge in time, Anthony's claws would leave a bloody mark on his body!

In mild cases, the skin and flesh were torn and blood gushed out. In severe cases, it might even hurt the bones!

Fortunately, the Black Cloak was fast and strong enough!

Otherwise, Anthony would kill him!

"Wow!"

"What the hell? The Black Cloak..."

"Oh my god! Can't the Black Cloak beat him?"

"What kind of skill is this? Is Anthony also a Warrior?"

"That's not right. The Black Cloak is a Level B Warrior. Could it be that Anthony is also a Level B Warrior?"

...

Everything changed so quickly that by the time the crowd reacted, the Black Cloak had already started running away miserably.

The crowd was horrified and couldn't help but exclaim and talk.

No one could have imagined that the Black Cloak was no match for Anthony.

Moreover, he was being chased.

"Help me..."

After a while, the Black Cloak could not bear it anymore and rushed towards Alvin, shouting.

He was defeated.

A Level B Warrior was beaten so badly by Anthony.

The Smith Manor was filled with guests. But at this moment, everyone was stunned, looking at the Black Cloak in disbelief.

The Blake family's Shadow Sentinel, swore firmly that he would kill Anthony.

But this was the result.

So surprising!

...

Alvin in the crowd had a gloomy face, showing shock, fear, and disbelief.

The Level B Warrior was the Blake family's greatest reliance!

But now, he was defeated by Anthony.

What the hell!

"Why is this happening?"

Anne was also dumbfounded, staring blankly at the Black Cloak and Anthony who was and becoming more and more courageous.

She seemed not to recognize Anthony. How could this strong, domineering and powerful man be Anthony?

Half a month ago, this man was still a loser who allowed her to beat and scold him!

But now, he beat the Black Cloak so badly.

Anne walked quickly to Arthur and said, "Mr. Hastings, please subdue this lunatic, Anthony.

"The half a billion dollars will be paid within three days. If you can kill Anthony, I will pay an additional one hundred million dollars!"

Anne was anxious.

Anthony was unstoppable.

Moreover, Ben was disabled, and the Black Cloak was no match for him. It was impossible for the other security personnel, even if they were numerous, to stop Anthony!

In this case, if she wanted to beat Anthony, she had only asked Arthur for help!

Anne reacted quickly and made decisions decisively without any hesitation.

The cost and bonus were indeed exaggerated.

Even for the Smith family, it was a lot of money.

However, since it concerned the reputation of the Smith family, she couldn't care so much.

Besides, she was now the head of the Smith family, and using the money was not a big deal.

As long as she could save the reputation of the Smith family, it would be worth it!

"Yes."

Alvin also walked over quickly and said, "Mr. Hastings, please take action quickly and capture Anthony.

"As long as you can capture Anthony, everything will be fine. We'll pay!"

Alvin was in danger and might die.

Therefore, he could not care so much. If he could capture Anthony and keep Alvin, a Level B Warrior, with the cost of half a billion dollars, it would not be a loss.

"Excuse me..."

However, in the expectant eyes of the two, Arthur smiled helplessly and shook his head, saying, "Our rule is not to be hired, not to fight lightly.

"Also, we do not accept temporary employment unless..."

"What?"

Alvin exclaimed, and his eyes widened.
