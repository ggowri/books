Chapter 94 Valiance

In just twenty minutes, Anthony, who was seriously injured, had already recovered.

Outside the main gate, Mason, who was sitting on a stone healing, felt very confused.

If he had only recovered, it would be fine. But Anthony was extremely fierce.

He rushed out, unstoppable, wielding Dragonfang and single-handedly caused more than a dozen werewolves to die in howls.

He relied solely on brute strength to repel the army of werewolves that were about to rush through the gate.

Mason and the howling army of werewolves were both stunned.

Among the onlookers, Anne was also shocked, and Alvin was already beyond stunned.

Anthony's prowess shocked everyone.

Compared to these two, the gathered crowd stared at Anthony with even more surprise, fear, helplessness, and confusion.

This battle had many ups and downs, and their emotions were more thrilling than a roller coaster ride.

Their previous submission was purely out of necessity, a temporary measure to save their lives.

To be honest, they sincerely hoped that Anthony could repel the ferocious werewolf army.

Because, as long as he repelled the werewolf army, they wouldn't have to join the Evil Spirit Legion or give up all their possessions.

Unexpectedly, the unpredictable battle left them in a state of shock and at a loss for what to do.

Therefore, these people were at a loss for words for a moment and chose to remain silent, observing the situation.

"He must have taken more drugs!" They remained silent, but Anne glared with fury and shouted, "Otherwise, how could he recover so quickly? Damn it! How is this drug-dependent loser still not dead?"

The others were anxious, and in truth, Anne was no different.

However, she hoped that Anthony would die and that the werewolf army would raze the Turner Manor to the ground.

In this way, she could fully leverage the power of the Evil Spirit Legion to unify Yoland and become its dominant ruler!

"She's right! Everyone, don't worry." Alvin hesitated slightly before adding, "He must have taken more drugs; otherwise, he would have died long ago!

"However, with such frequent drug use over such a short period, he won't last much longer.

"Even if Mr. Foster doesn't kill him, he will die from his bloodline fracturing!

"He's doomed; even God can't save him!"

Clearly, Alvin misunderstood everyone's thoughts, thinking that they, like him, were eager to join the Evil Spirit Legion.

However, upon hearing him say this, the others also felt that hope was very slim.

Relying on drugs to activate superpowers, regardless of the strength, this is entirely a way of fighting that burns one's life.

The stronger the burst of power, the stronger the backlash from the drug effects.

From this perspective, Alvin was right; Anthony wouldn't live much longer.

"kill!" "Mr. Johnson is mighty! Brothers, kill..."

"Guys, let's rush together to kill all these beasts..."

While everyone was preoccupied with their own thoughts, not far away, Anthony's heroic actions greatly boosted the morale of the Turner Guards.

Dozens of people fought desperately, forcing the werewolf army to retreat from the second checkpoint to the first one!

As the werewolves entered the range of machine guns and cannons, accompanied by a dull rumble, the werewolves outside the gate were once again plunged into chaos and death.

The situation turned around, and the Turner Guards also entered a frenzied state. Unconsciously, they pursued the enemy beyond the gate, showing a determined stance to press their advantage and wipe out the werewolf army.

But they numbered only in the dozens.

Meanwhile, although the werewolf army was forced to retreat, their casualties were not significant, and they still numbered nearly a hundred.

"Stop! Don't rush out! Get back and defend the gate!" Anthony was shocked and rushed to the front of the crowd, stopping these irrational people from advancing.

If they dared to rush out, they would surely be surrounded by the wolf pack and torn apart!

However, if they guarded the gate, with the support of the firepower near the gate and the advantage of its narrowness, they could still defend for a while.

Once they rushed out, none of them would survive!

Anthony's shout instantly sobered up the Turner Guards, who quickly retreated.

As the crowd retreated, Anthony held Dragonfang and stood at the forefront of the crowd.

A gust of wind swept through, carrying with it swirling yellow sand, making Anthony's figure appear inexplicably majestic.

Right now, he was facing over a hundred werewolves baring their fangs and eyes glinting with menace. They were ready to pounce at any moment and tear Anthony to pieces.

Anthony squinted his eyes and instinctively tried to grip Dragonfang tighter, but his arm couldn't stop trembling.

In fact, Anthony's legs were also trembling uncontrollably.

It wasn't that he was afraid; rather, he was struggling to hold on.

His strength was once again depleted.

The reason he could still stand despite his exhaustion was purely due to his willpower, gritting his teeth to endure.

The pungent smell of blood surged into his nostrils and mouth. Anthony gritted his teeth and forced the blood back down.

He couldn't spit out the blood, retreat, or even fall down!

Mason said, "You're such a fighter..."

Anthony stood proudly, and Mason jumped down from the boulder, holding a long knife, a cold smile spreading across his face.

As he spoke, Mason approached, the long sword in his hand glinting with a cold light.

His eyes, a deep emerald green, also glinted with a bloodthirsty gleam.
