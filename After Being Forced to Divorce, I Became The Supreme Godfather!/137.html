Chapter 136 A Golden Opportunity

The crowd's chatter was cut off by Anthony's words, which hit like a thunderbolt out of the blue.

"Holy shit!"

In an instant, the crowd fell dead silent, and everyone was struck dumb.

All eyes instinctively darted to Anne's face before inevitably shifting to Alvin.

"Anne?

Begging Anthony to fuck her?

And after getting engaged to Alvin? How messed up was that?"

"Bullshit! You're full of crap!"

The crowd's piercing stares infuriated Alvin. His eyes burning with rage, he roared, "You bastard! You son of a bitch! If you dare spout any more nonsense, I'll kill you!"

"Nonsense?" Anthony let out a sneer. "Whether it's nonsense or not, why don't you ask your fiancee?"

"Shut up!" Alvin bellowed, his face contorted with fury. "Do you think I don't know? You've been married for three years, and you haven't even touched her!

"Does this kind of thing even need to be asked?" he added with a dismissive wave of his hand.

"Oh really?"

Alvin was fuming with rage, but Anthony remained unfazed, as calm as ever. He spoke with a casual smile. "How am I supposed to know there's a mole on the inside of her thigh?

"Or that there's a scar below her left breast? Oh, and she also mentioned how every time, you're soft as a pillow, no fun at all. Am I right, my dear ex-wife?"

As he spoke, Anthony deliberately turned his head and shot Anne a lewd grin.

"You!"

"You bastard!"

Anne, now wholly enraged, snapped, "If you say one more word, I'll cut out your tongue myself!"

"Oh? Don't want me to talk?"

Anthony chuckled, clearly enjoying himself. "So, does that mean you're admitting you begged me to fuck you?"

"You! That's a lie!"

Anne was flustered, denying fiercely. "You're nothing but trash! Stop spewing nonsense!"

But her words faltered.

Anne tried her best to explain, but Alvin's expression darkened.

Anthony was absolutely right!

The mole on Anne's inner thigh was in the most private, hidden spot.

There was no way anyone could see it unless she was completely undressed!

The critical issue was Alvin knew deep down that he genuinely couldn't satisfy Anne.

It was the harsh truth!

Lies might not always hurt, but the truth could cut like a blade!

As this thought crossed his mind, Alvin's eyes began to redden, and he instinctively turned to Anne.

At that moment, Anne was desperately trying to defend herself, but her frantic excuses came off as weak and unconvincing.

Under the gaze of the crowd, Anne was visibly panicked.

The problem was, she had knelt before Anthony in the past—completely naked, no less.

Anthony's brutal honesty left her no room to hide. Who could withstand such a blow?

"Anthony! You bastard!"

Seeing Anne flustered beyond measure, Ben, the head of security, shouted angrily, "How dare you smear Ms. Smith like this in front of everyone? I'll beat you to death for this!"

With those words, Ben leaped forward, crossing several yards in an instant as he charged at Anthony.

Ben was no lightweight. His attack came with explosive force and overwhelming momentum.

Anthony couldn't help but sneer internally.

This punk had bullied him plenty back in the day.

Just as Anthony was about to make his move, Dariel took a step forward.

That single movement from Dariel, before he even acted, was enough to make the charging Ben tremble in fear.

He was the captain of the Turner family's security and a Metahuman!

Even Ben, with all his arrogance, knew full well that Dariel was considered one of Yoland's most powerful fighters.

Dariel could kill him with ease, so he didn't dare to make a move against Dariel.

However, while he feared Dariel, he had no such fear for Anthony. Stopping in his tracks, he sneered coldly, "Hmph! Pathetic loser.

"Dragging along a dog from the Turner family, and you're acting all high and mighty?

"If you're even half a man, tell him to step aside, and let's settle this one-on-one!"

"Fuck! You're asking for it!"

A dog from the Turner family? The moment those words left Ben's mouth, Dariel's face darkened with fury.

"Stand down!" Anthony ordered, raising his hand before Dariel could make a move.

"Yes, Mr. Johnson."

With that, Dariel immediately fell silent and stepped back respectfully.

"Ben!"

Seeing Dariel step back, Alvin's eyes bulged with rage as he shouted, "Destroy him! No, kill him!"

"Yes! Kill him!"

Anne joined in with the command.

Anthony's words had thoroughly enraged them both.

"Captain, let me handle this!"

When Dariel backed down, Timothy's face lit up with excitement as he strode forward. "Without the Turner family's protection, he's nothing but trash."

"Captain, why use a sledgehammer to crack a nut? This loser isn't even worth your time. I can take care of him myself!"

With all eyes on them, everyone was eager to prove themselves, and Timothy was no exception.

Besides, as he'd said, Anthony was just a worthless nobody! It was practically a golden opportunity!

Ben was thinking the same thing.

Since Timothy had already volunteered, he simply smirked and said, "Fine, he's all yours."

Timothy said excitedly, "Don't worry, Captain. He's just a junkie! Taking him down will be like taking candy from a baby!"

"Hey! Anthony! Are you ready to die?"

Timothy's eyes blazed with confidence and fury as he glared at Anthony.

Sure, he was the Smith family's vice-captain, but moments ago, Dariel had kicked him flying like a ragdoll.

That humiliation in front of everyone had stung!

He didn't dare lash out at Dariel, but Anthony? That nobody wasn't even worth considering as a threat!
