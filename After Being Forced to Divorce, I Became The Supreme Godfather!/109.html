Chapter 108 Devil Eye Upgraded

Anthony had long been aware that Mason was concealing a blade beneath his black robe.

Moreover, this wasn't the first time Mason had employed such a tactic.

In the past, when Anthony's strength was insufficient, he would either be unable to dodge in time or be put in a very embarrassing situation by this sudden attack.

However, times had changed.

Seeing the blade thrust towards him once again, Anthony was not panicked but instead let out a cold laugh.

The moment the blade fell, Dragonfang appeared in Anthony's hand.

Then, with a speed even faster than before, Dragonfang struck towards Mason.

Dragonfang flashed with a cold gleam. In an instant, Mason's blade was as fragile as tofu, breaking into two pieces.

"What?"

Mason was shocked and exclaimed in disbelief.

Anthony's strike was not only astonishingly fast but also seemed to have made Dragonfang much sharper than before.

Before Mason could recover, Anthony followed up with a kick, as fast, accurate, and ruthless as ever.

Mason was kicked dozens of feet away, slamming into the mountainside.

Under the immense impact, the hard mountain rocks cracked bit by bit, with dust and debris flying everywhere.

Mason's burly body nearly embedded itself into the cliff wall.

In the dust and smoke, he actually spat out a mouthful of blood.

Anthony's kick carried an immense force.

If Anthony didn't want to kill him for the time being, he could have ended his life with a single blow.

"What do you think?"

Anthony stood with his hands behind his back, looking down at Mason. "Do you want to talk now, or should I beat it out of you?"

"Damn it! I will tear you apart!"

How could Mason admit defeat so easily? He roared to the sky, his muscles swelling and bones creaking.

In the blink of an eye, his wide black robe bulged high, almost about to tear.

A bloodthirsty and brutal aura swept over them.

He was not only a level-B superpower but also a werewolf.

Anthony's gaze sharpened, and his pupils flickered with a mad and fiery light.

The transformation into a werewolf took place in an instant.

Mason leaped forward like a giant wolf descending from the sky, his fangs terrifying and his claws gleaming with a cold light.

"Go to hell."

Fully transformed into a werewolf, Mason came aggressively, carrying an unstoppable murderous intent.

Seeing Anthony unmoved, Mason opened his bloodstained maw and roared at him.

Anthony stared at him calmly and silently, not at all panicked.

When they were less than a hundred feet apart, Anthony suddenly shuddered all over and roared to the sky.

This roar was like a dragon's roar, earth-shattering and deafening.

At the same time, the illusion of a dragon flashed above Anthony's head.

The dragon brandished its claws and teeth, soaring in the sky.

In an instant, violent winds raged.

The terrifying and astonishing power seemed to come from ancient times, suppressing all areas.

Within a hundred feet, flowers and grass were annihilated, and trees collapsed as if it were the end of the world.

Mason, who had been running confidently, suddenly shuddered all over, and his body shrank by more than half in an instant.

His astonishing power was instantly shattered under the dragon's roar.

Mason fell to the ground and curled up, shivering.

The werewolf body that had almost burst through the black robe just now quickly shrank.

Mason turned into an ordinary person, curled up under the black robe, shivering and not daring to raise his head.

The dragon's roar was one of the powers of Anthony's Leopard Avatar, evolving into the Dragon Avatar.

It contained a terrifying force. Werewolves were no match for it.

Not long ago, when Anthony was on the brink of death, Rose desperately revived him.

In fact, at that time, Rose also knew that pure healing power could not save Anthony's life.

There was only one way: the union of a man and a woman.

The Turner family had a precept. Healing metahumans all had the most powerful healing method, but it was only applicable to those who genuinely love each other.

Moreover, if both parties loved each other, they would benefit immensely.

In the safe house, Rose took the initiative to unite with Anthony.

Anthony was pleasantly surprised because he knew that once they united, he would complete the second task of the Chosen Path.

Sure enough, after that, the Devil Eye indicated that the second stage task of the Chosen Path had been completed.

The Devil Eye was now version 2.0, and its power had been enhanced.

It could invade electronic devices with intermediate protection levels. Data deduction, physical enhancement, and other functions had advanced to the intermediate level.

Anthony's Leopard Avatar evolved into the Dragon Avatar, possessing the power of dragons.

His Energy-absorbing Breathing also evolved to level two.

The cultivation method was not only related to Anthony's overall strength but also to the duration of the Devil Eye.

The higher the level of the method, the longer the Devil Eye lasted.

In addition, another star lit up in the Dragon Ring, representing that Anthony had become a two-star member of the Godfather Group with greater authority.

Promoted to a level-two member, he could receive a reward of 100,000 gold coins, and the Dragon Ring Space would expand tenfold.

In the Dragon Ring Store, more powerful items had been unlocked, such as the special, enhanced Inferno Gatling.
