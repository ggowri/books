Chapter 116 The Foolish People

"Yes, yes...

"Hurry, let's kneel and greet Mr. Foster..."

As soon as Anne spoke, Scarlet immediately reacted, signaling for everyone to kneel.

As she spoke, she casually glanced at Anthony, who passed by them.

His well-defined muscles and sharp features radiated intense masculinity.

Striding forward with confidence, cold yet commanding, he exuded an air of power and authority that was simply captivating.

For a moment, Scarlet felt a bit dazed, her mind drifting back to the time in Anne's apartment, where she and Anthony had shared moments of intense passion.

Feeling uneasy, Scarlet dryly swallowed, instinctively pressing her legs together.

"Yes."

"Everyone, quickly kneel and greet Mr. Foster..."

"Hurry, hurry, Mr. Foster will be back soon. We can't afford to offend him..."

Scarlet knelt down, and soon, others followed suit. A chorus of voices echoed as they knelt with sincerity and urgency.

Anthony, observing the devotion and efficiency with which they knelt, couldn't help but glance back in mild disbelief.

So many foolish people, yet not one of them was outside the inner circle of Yoland's elite.

The city was ruled by these individuals, and that was a troubling thought.

His resolve solidified: he had to change the fate of Yoland.

As the others knelt, Anthony pushed through the crowd and walked straight to Rose's side.

They exchanged a smile, and Anthony gently wrapped his arm around her slender waist. Surprised, he said, "My love, you've broken through too?"

"Yes," Rose replied with a sweet smile, leaning her head against his shoulder. "How are you? Did you get hurt?"

Anthony shook his head, teasing her. "After your sweet nurturing, I feel like I have boundless energy. I'm stronger than ever..."

As he spoke, he playfully rubbed her waist.

Rose shuddered slightly, her cheeks turning pink as she mumbled. "Stop it..."

She thought to herself, "He was already as strong as an ox before, and now even stronger? That would be incredible!"

Their public display of affection drew envious glances from many.

Rose was recognized as the goddess of Yoland—beautiful as an angel, with an ethereal aura.

Many were excited just to glimpse her beauty, and seeing Anthony hold her so intimately made them wish for that kind of attention.

However, not everyone was pleased.

"Anthony!"

Alvin growled through gritted teeth. "You worthless fool! Get over here and kneel down!"

"Rose!" Anne snapped, her voice filled with venom. "You too, get over here and kneel before Mr. Foster!

"Anthony, how dare you! Now that Mr. Foster had defeat you, kneel now!"

Anthony shot Anne a weary glance and impatiently replied, "Shut up. Who told you that I've submitted to the Evil Spirit Legion?

"I told you that I killed the werewolf leader. If you want to kneel, you can kneel as long as you like..."

"You're talking nonsense!"

"Anthony, stop bluffing!"

Before Anthony could finish, Alvin interrupted. "How dare you speak like this?

"If you hadn't submitted to Mr. Foster, how could you have survived?"

"Exactly!" Anne chimed in. "Oh I see. You must have pretended to submit, and then returned early to try to take the Turner family and run, right?

"Well, how dare you!

"How dare you play tricks on Mr. Foster? You're asking for death!"

Furious, Anne turned to the werewolf army, barking orders. "Why are you standing there? Surround them! Don't let them escape!"

"Right!"

"Surround them! Anthony is trying to run away!"

Alvin jumped up, shouting. "Just to be safe, capture them, or just kill them outright..."

Bang!

Bang! Bang!

Before he could finish, Anthony pulled out the Inferno Gatling.

Without a word, he fired a quick burst at the werewolf army hiding behind cover!

Boom!

The solid wall exploded with a thunderous sound, countless bullets shredding it to pieces.

The werewolves behind it screeched as they scrambled to flee in every direction.

"Get lost!"

With a furious roar, Anthony shouted. "Otherwise, I'll kill every last one of you!"

The Inferno Gatling—a weapon of mass destruction!

The blinding blue glow of the barrels and the terrifying bullets that could crush rocks and split stones created an awe-inspiring spectacle.

The werewolves hesitated, shivering in fear. None dared approach.

After all, the power of these bullets was so immense that even a single hit would turn them to dust without a chance to fight back.

They may be werewolves, but they were not fearless warriors willing to die without a second thought.

Moreover, without their leader, they were just a scattered group.

Whoever confronted the Inferno Gatling would die instantly!

"Don't be afraid!

"His Gatling may be powerful, but he only has one!"

Anne, seeing the hesitation of the werewolves, issued another order. "Attack from all sides at once! He won't be able to stop all of you!

"Just charge and kill him! Do it!"

"Shut up!

"Who do you think you are, giving orders to us?"

This time, before Anne could speak, one of the werewolves snapped. "You've been yelling nonstop! Why don't you go up yourself?"

"I..." Anne was stunned, her mouth hanging open, unable to respond.
