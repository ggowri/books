Chapter 3202

Following the sound, Jasper arrived at the door of a utility room at the end of the hallway.

The sound of the piano came from this room.

Just as she was about to open the door, someone else opened it first.

Then, a person, about the same height as Jasper, suddenly darted out of the room. After bumping into her, the person quickly ran towards the safety passage on the side and disappeared in an instant.

Jasper was hit and fell to the ground. The nanny quickly approached and asked her, "Miss Jasper, are you okay? Did you get hurt?"

Jasper shook her head and looked towards the utility room with the door open.

It was quiet inside the room.

As Jasper entered it, she saw a piano.

Jasper was puzzled for a moment. She wondered if the person who was playing the piano just now was the same boy who bumped into her and ran away earlier.

The nanny helped her up, and just then, a voice suddenly sounded, "Jasper, what are you doing here?"

Looking up, Jasper saw William running towards her with a nervous expression.

"Did you fall just now?" he asked.

"Yeah, someone bumped into me and I fell," Jasper answered. She then turned to her nanny who was walking beside her, and asked, "Coty, did you see the guy who ran into me just now?"

"No. I'm sorry, miss," the nanny apologized. "The kid was running too fast and I was only focused on you, Miss Jasper."

Jasper looked regretful upon hearing this. "I didn't see what he looked like either. He ran too fast. I wish I had caught up with him just now!"

William asked, "Do you want to find the guy who bumped into you just now?"

"Yes!" Jasper's expression became excited again. "William, that guy played the piano so well just now! He should be around our age. Although his skills may not be that good, I can tell he’s talented..."

At that moment, Jasper was still talking about how she felt when she heard the piece. So, she didn't notice the flash of panic in William's eyes.

At the thought that Jasper might be attracted to the boy who was playing the piano just now, William forced himself to snap out of it. He couldn't bear to keep thinking about it.

Jasper and William became friends because he could play the piano and she liked him for it. And he didn't want her to become friends with another guy and shift her attention to him.

A sense of panic spread within William as he thought about it, and he suddenly reached out and grabbed her hand.

"Oh? William, you're hurting me," Jasper cried out in pain.

Hearing that, William realized that he had been holding her hand too tightly. He quickly loosened his grip, but still held her hand.

"We should go back soon, or else the adults will come looking for us," said William.

"Oh." Jasper's gaze lingered on the emergency exit.

She still regretted not catching up with the little boy earlier.

"The song just now was really nice. If only you were with me, William, you could have listened to it with me. Maybe we could have even stopped the boy!" Jasper exclaimed.

William's body trembled slightly, then he took Jasper's hand and said, "Let's go, otherwise your parents will worry about you."

Then he took her hand and led her towards the main hall of the wedding banquet, while the nanny followed closely behind.
