Chapter 3461

 

Veronica and Daniel entered the temple, but since it was not allowed to light incense inside the hall, they just paid their respects without doing so.

Veronica is looking at the majestic Buddha statue in front of her, with her hands clasped together and her eyes closed, praying sincerely.

Meanwhile, Daniel stood on the side, staring blankly at Veronica, feeling as if the distance between them was growing farther and farther.

How should he bring it up?

If he really said these words, how would she look at him? Would she think that he was only dating her for some ulterior motive?

When Veronica opened her eyes and looked at Daniel, she said in a slightly strange way, "Why do you look a bit weird?"

"Strange?" Daniel was surprised.

"Right, the way you're looking at me seems cautious, like you have something to say," Veronica said. "If you do have something to say to me, why not just say it?"

"I...I don't really have anything to say," Daniel chuckled nervously and said, "I was just curious about what wishes you made earlier."

Veronica said, "I can't say that. Don't they say that wishes won't come true if you speak them out loud?"

The wish she just made was for a smooth relationship with Daniel and for Mason to be safe, healthy, and happier.

Daniel didn't ask any more questions and continued to accompany Veronica as they walked around the temple. However, in the end, he didn't say the words he wanted to say.

When Daniel returned home, he was met with the anxious faces of his parents.

"Daniel, how did Zixi say it? When did she bring this up with Mason?" asked Mr. Cross anxiously.

"I didn't talk to Veronica," said Daniel.

"What, you didn't say?" Old Mr. Cross looked surprised and then scolded, "How could you not say it? Do you... do you want me to go to jail?"

"Yes, Daniel, this matter cannot be delayed!" Mrs. Cross, the elderly woman, repeated.

"I...I understand. I will go talk to Veronica about this," Daniel promised.

But the next day, when he arrived at the entrance of the Barlow family's neighborhood, he was invited to a private room in a hotel.

The person in the private room was none other than Mason.

"Mason, why do you suddenly want to see me, and... um, in a place like this?" Daniel asked nervously.

After all, his family was in dire need of a business deal with the Reed family, and the person he had originally planned to ask for help from, Veronica, was now standing in front of him.

"Then why did you go to the Barlow family?" Mason asked.

Daniel became even more nervous, "I... I just wanted to find Veronica..."

"Is it because of your father's embezzlement?" Mason asked calmly.

As soon as he said this, Daniel was stunned, his pupils suddenly contracted, and he looked at the person in front of him in disbelief!

He... actually knew!

But the problem was... how did he know?! Could this be the Reed family's Mason? Even secrets from other families could be easily known.

"What's wrong? Am I wrong?" Mason's expression remained unchanged, in stark contrast to Daniel's shock.

"Why...how do you know?" Daniel murmured.

"I not only know about your father's corruption, but I also know that someone is using your father's name to pursue a business deal under the guise of the Reed family," said Mason.

Daniel's throat felt a burning sensation as he realized that the things he had been carefully considering were already known by the other party.
