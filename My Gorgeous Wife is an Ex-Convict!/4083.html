Chapter 4081

You need to differentiate between your feelings for your idol and your feelings for your loved ones, and know which is more important.

It's precisely because her mother could differentiate between the two that even now, her father is still at ease with her mother's fan behavior and even helps her mother with it.

Although occasionally, her father would get jealous, her mother would always soothe his emotions.

Thinking of her mother's usual behavior of soothing her father's emotions, Tina raised her hand and stroked Chandler's head.

Chandler was slightly stunned.

Eugenia, who was standing next to them, widened her eyes.

What was Tina doing? The way she was stroking Chandler's head, it was like she was petting a puppy!

And it was in front of so many people!

Eugenia noticed that it wasn't just her who was watching Tina and Chandler, but everyone in the studio was looking over, even Kit and Weldon.

"So don't be jealous, okay? Be good, I love you the most!" Tina said while stroking Chandler's head.

Eugenia had the urge to faint. Come on, did Tina really treat Chandler like a puppy?

Chandler's appearance made people think of a fierce beast.

A CEO-level figure being coaxed like this by a woman in front of so many people... what if Chandler gets angry?

Just as Eugenia was trembling with fear, Chandler whispered, "Alright, if you can really do what you say, then I won't be jealous."

"Of course!" Tina took the initiative to kiss Chandler on the lips.

Although there were other people around, she really wanted to kiss him right now!

Tina has always been the type of person who does what she wants, regardless of the opinions of those around her.

Chandler's body suddenly trembled, his eyelashes drooped slightly, and he bent down slightly to match Tina's kiss.

It was strange, because when he watched her filming earlier, he didn't feel good. He had a feeling of discomfort, like something was gnawing at his chest.

But now, that uncomfortable feeling had completely disappeared, leaving only his affection for her and a desire to touch her more!

She seemed to have a magical power that could soothe all his anxieties.

Was it because of her frankness? Or was it because she... was the one he was destined to be with?!

The sight of a man and a woman kissing in public didn't make people feel uncomfortable at all. On the contrary, it was a beautiful scene, as if it was only natural for them to be together.

Weldon tightly held his camera. If it weren't for the fact that one of the people involved was Chandler, he might have already lifted his camera and captured this moment.

Although he knew that Tina was Chandler's fiancée, he didn't expect Chandler to let her kiss him in public.

Hunter valued etiquette and family rules, and Chandler was the future leader of the family. But now he was acting like this!

If Hunter's old-fashioned people saw this, who knows what they would think.

Weldon looked at the scene in front of him with a mocking gaze.

And at Kit, not far from Weldon, he was also watching the scene!
