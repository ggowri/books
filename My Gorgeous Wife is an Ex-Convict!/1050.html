Grace was stunned, not realizing that Brian already had plans for the evening.

However, when she asked him to bring her here, he had said yes without any hesitation! Grace felt heavy-hearted.

It seemed as if she was owing him more and more.

"Mr. Hart." Stella's manager came over and said hi to Brian, the man who was supposed to have something to do today. It was because of this that he brought Stella to the gala to meet some people in the entertainment industry.

"Well... Brian, why don't you show me around and meet some people with me? I'll feel better if you accompany me." Stella feigned nervousness.

Grace did not bother to watch Stella pretend and turned her head to look around. She suddenly trembled when she saw the people she wanted to see tonight!

It was Martin Weiss and Paisley Daniels. 'There they are!'

Grace hurriedly said to Brian, "I see the people I'm looking for." Then, she walked in Martin Weiss and Paisley Daniels's direction.

Looking at Grace's back view as she took hurried steps, Brian suddenly felt a sense of loss.
