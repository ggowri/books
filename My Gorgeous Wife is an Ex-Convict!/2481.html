Chapter 2479

Grace rushed to the washroom and leaned over the sink to throw up.

"Blaargh... Blaargh..." The sound of vomiting was so clear in the washroom that she almost seemed to be throwing up everything in her stomach.

Unbeknownst to her, a man outside the bathroom was worried about her while she was throwing up like crazy in there.

Brian's indifferent face was now full of worry.

The crown prince of the entertainment industry had never been worried about any woman, except her.

Every move she made affected him.

He kept telling himself that she was now living a good life with Jason, and that was enough for him.

The person she loved was Jason. Her affection for him was nothing more than something that stemmed from childhood memories. He knew this, but his instincts surpassed his reason.

The moment he saw her uncomfortably rushing into the washroom, he could not help following her.

The sound of vomiting inside made him restless. He was afraid that something was wrong with her.

When Grace finally emerged from the washroom, Brian quickly went forward and asked, "What came over you? Are you okay?"

Grace looked at Brian with a flicker of shock in her eyes before shaking her head. "I probably thought of something bad in the past when I heard Amber Mitchell talking about the conspiracy. It must have caused some discomfort in me. I feel better now after throwing up."

Brian's eyes darkened. 'It must be the car accident she was involved in… She didn't die in the crash, but she spent three years in prison for it.'

He found out later that she had suffered during her time in prison. What was he doing at that time? He was dating women who looked like her and living a life everyone envied.

"I'm sorry I didn't find you sooner," he murmured.

Grace said, "It's not your fault. Besides, the people responsible have already gotten the punishment they deserve."

However, he did not feel good when she said so. He only felt sorrier.

He was sorry he missed her.

"Where's Jason? Why didn't he come with you today?" he asked as he changed the subject.

"He went back to Emerald City with the kids yesterday," said Grace.

He was surprised at that. "He left you alone in Deer City?"

"What's there to worry about?" Grace replied calmly.

Brian's eyes flickered. He knew she understood what he meant. He did not expect Jason to leave Deer City first and leave Grace alone when he was still here.

If it were in the past, Jason would never have done this, but now... Was Jason not afraid that he would take the opportunity to approach Grace and steal her heart?
