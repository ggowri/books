Chapter 938

The trial would begin in a few days. Grace felt powerless as she thought of this.

When she came back to her senses, she found that the car had stopped at a styling shop Jason had taken her to before.

Grace was shocked, and she asked the driver, "Why are we here?"

The driver simply said, "Mr. Reed is waiting inside."

Grace said nothing and got out of the car.

As soon as she entered the shop, the shop manager greeted her and said enthusiastically, "You must be Miss Cummins. Please follow me."

Grace followed the shop manager to a room on the second floor where Jason was sitting on a couch with a light purple gown hanging on a hanger next to him.

It was a beautiful gown with deep purple and white crystals inlaid, making it look elegant and classic.

"Accompany me to a banquet tonight. Try on the gown," said Jason.

"A banquet?" Grace was stunned.

"It's just an ordinary banquet. There's no need to be too nervous," said Jason.

Grace had accompanied Jason to a banquet before, but it was not a very good experience.

She wondered if anything would happen at the banquet today.

Grace went into the changing room and changed into the gown.

She had to admit that Jason was considerate in choosing her gown. The scars on her body were all covered up and hidden.

The only thing that was showing was probably her hands.

The joints of her fingers were slightly deformed. They looked alright from a distance, but once you looked at them closely, you could notice the deformities.

It was the only thing the gown could not hide.

Jason's eyes flashed with amazement when Grace walked out of the changing room.

When he saw the gown, he thought it suited her, so he bought the limited one-piece only designer handmade gown.

She indeed looked beautiful now that she was wearing it.

"Beautiful." He stepped forward with a smile.

Grace pressed her lips together and said nothing.

He picked up the jewelry that was lying in a jewelry box nearby and put on a white pearl necklace for her.

Grace looked at the string of pearls. Each was large, round, and shiny with no flaws. The necklace was probably worth tens of millions.

Grace looked at herself in a full-length mirror. She looked beautiful, elegant, and poised. No one would know she was just a paralegal.

After he dressed her up, she now looked like a doll.

"Sis, do you like it?" he murmured, leaning over from behind while bringing his face close to her cheek.

Looking in the mirror, they looked as intimate as a couple.

The staff was stunned. 'Did Mr. Reed call her... Sis?'

'What's going on here? When Mr. Reed first brought the lady over, she seemed to be Mr. Reed's girlfriend!'

Although the staff member was full of shock and speculation, she dared not ask anything.
