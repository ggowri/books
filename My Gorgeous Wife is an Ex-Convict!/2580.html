Chapter 2578

"Well…" Amber Mitchell nodded. "I’ll be studying archaeology in Emerald City for at least three years. After that, I’ll consider whether I want to stay in Emerald City."

"You're not going back to Deer City?" Kyla asked again.

'Deer City...' There was a flicker of gloom in Amber Mitchell's eyes. Deer City was a sad place for her. "I’ll go back to Deer City to pay my respects during Tomb Sweeping Day, Winter Solstice, and Herman's death anniversary. I’ll also go back if my son needs anything."

In her opinion, Herman and her son were the only ones in Deer City holding her back.

The fact that she sold the shares of Mitchell Group was the best proof she wanted to leave Deer City.

"How's Simon?" Kyla asked with concern.

"The doctor said that he's doing okay. Perhaps he can leave the hospital healthy after a period of treatment," she said.

"That's good." Kyla breathed a sigh of relief. As a mother, what scared her the most was something bad happening to her children. "Does he know you're his biological mother yet?"

Amber Mitchell shook her head. "I don't know if he knows. I've only met Simon a few times. Sometimes, I think it would be better for him not to know that I'm his biological mother."

After all, the feud between the adults was so intense that the child might not be as happy as before if he found out about it.

"Well, enough about me. Trista is so cute!" Shifting the subject, Amber Mitchell began to tease the baby in Kyla's arms.

Although born premature, the little one had grown to two and a half kilograms after a period of careful feeding in the incubator. She could be fed like regular babies now.

The little one looked tender and adorable.

Kyla said, "She's still a little skinny. She drinks less milk than other babies who are born full-term. It's a pity she has been drinking baby formula since birth and has never had her mother's milk."

"You’ve done enough for her. She'll understand your hard efforts when she grows up," said Amber Mitchell as she took out a red envelope. "Alright, it's my first time meeting the baby today. I'm giving her a red envelope for good luck!"

Kyla immediately rejected it and said, "That's inappropriate. Take it back!"

Amber Mitchell chuckled and said, "It's for the baby, not you. You're just keeping it for her. You gotta give it to her later! Besides, I've sold my shares. I have enough money to last me for several lifetimes. You don’t have to be conscious about my money.”

Kyla then accepted the red envelope.

"Here, let me carry the baby. She's so cute. I want to carry her." Amber Mitchell seemed like she could not wait to carry her.

Kyla then carefully handed her daughter to Amber Mitchell.

Amber Mitchell hugged the tiny and soft body carefully. Watching the little one pouting in her arms and spitting bubbles… Her eyes grew red.

'Was Simon like this when he was born? Was he this adorable too?'

However, she had never even held Simon once since he was born.
