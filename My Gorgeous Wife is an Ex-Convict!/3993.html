Chapter 3991

The strong scent of perfume emanating from the woman filled Mick's nostrils.

It was so different from the scent on Nancy.

Damn it, even at this moment, he still thinks of her!

"Are you really willing to do anything for me?" Mick asked the woman in front of him.

He could see too much makeup on her gorgeous face.

Nancy, on the other hand, only wore light makeup, which made her more pleasing to his eyes.

"Yes," the woman quickly assured him.

"Then hug me," Mick said.

The woman was stunned again.

She had prepared herself for the possibility of Mick making demanding requests.

But now, the young master of the Reed family only asked her to hug him?!

"What, don't want to?" Mick asked.

"No, no!" The woman quickly reached out and hugged Mick, afraid that he would change his mind.

At this moment, not only the woman, but also the people around them were stunned by Mick's request.

Everyone saw the woman's hands wrapped around Mick's waist, leaning against his chest.

Mick lowered his head and rested it on the woman's shoulder.

Time seemed to stand still.

Have you ever seen the Reed family's young master holding a woman like this in such a situation?

Many people immediately felt envious and jealous, thinking that this woman might have gained a higher status because of this.

Even the woman's heart couldn't stop beating rapidly, feeling like she might have just hit the jackpot.

If Mr. Reed took the initiative to hold her, then he must be interested in her!

But the woman was still caught up in her excitement when Mick's voice suddenly rang in her ear, "That's enough, let go."

What?!

The woman was stunned.

"Mr. Reed, what are you..."

"I said that's enough, you can let go now!" Mick said impatiently.

The woman reluctantly let go, her face full of confusion.

Mick didn't even look at her, pouring himself a glass of wine from a nearby bottle and downing it in one gulp.

Why did he feel nothing but disgust during the embrace just now?

He had wanted to use this hug to drive Nancy's image out of his mind.

But why did it make Nancy's image even clearer in his mind?

"Mr. Reed, if you didn't like the hug just now, I can..." The woman didn't finish her sentence before Mick's face darkened. "Did you not hear what I said earlier? Get lost!"

The woman looked lost, her previous pride now replaced with defeat.

She left the private room dejectedly.

Meanwhile, other women were eager to try their luck.

After all, Mick had let the previous woman hug him, so she must not have pleased Mr. Reed!

They believed they could do better.

But when these women approached him, Mick impatiently said, "I want to be alone, so everyone leave now!"

Everyone looked at each other and no one dared to stay in the private room.

Mick was left alone in the room, finally able to catch his breath in the silence.

Suddenly, he laughed. The laughter was clear and heavy in the quiet room.
