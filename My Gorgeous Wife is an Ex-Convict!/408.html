Chapter 407

"No, I just happened to meet... someone I know. He settled things out." Grace hesitated.

"Your friend? We must thank him, then! Why don't I buy some presents later so you can take them to your friend?" Kyla Corbyn said hurriedly.

"It's all right," said Grace as she quickly changed the subject by asking about Nelson's cochlear implant.

"In a couple of days when Nelson's medical examination report is out. He can install the cochlear implant if there's no problem." Kyla Corbyn could not hide her joy when she talked about this. "The hospital also said I was financially challenged and could apply for a discount."

"That's great," said Grace. She knew that Jason had communicated with the hospital. Grace was delighted at the thought that Nelson could hear soon.

The earlier incident was like a short episode, and it was now over.

What Grace did not expect was that by evening, Kyla Corbyn stopped her and asked, "Was it Brian who helped us solve the blackmail problem today?"

Grace looked at her in surprise, wondering how Kyla Corbyn knew this.

Kyla Corbyn saw her expression and immediately knew it was true. She said, "A customer was having dinner here just now and saw the video of you arguing with those blackmailers when you delivered their food earlier this noon. Brian appeared later on, didn’t he? I saw him in the video too."

Kyla Corbyn said and looked at Grace with a little more doubt in her gaze.

She already had doubts about Grace's relationship with Brian when he came to the store to wait for Grace previously.

However, in the video today, Brian sent a huge shock to her heart as he even went down on one knee to tie Grace's shoelace.

She remembered Brian to be an indifferent person. All those short-lived girlfriends only accentuated the ruthlessness of his nature.

Even though Brian doted on every girlfriend he had ever dated, the so-called ‘doting’ was only in terms of money and resources.

She had seen him with one of his girlfriends before, but he would look at her with little emotion. It was as if he was looking at a toy that interested him rather than a person.

However, in today's video, she clearly felt that Brian’s eyes were full of Grace.

‘When I asked Brian not to tell anyone about seeing me, Brian must have said yes because of Grace!’ thought Kyla Corbyn.

"There's a video online?" Grace was shocked.

"Yes, let me show you," said Kyla Corbyn while she took out her phone. After typing into the search bar though, she found nothing. The web page even suggested that the content of her search did not exist.

"How can this be?" Kyla Corbyn said with a frown.

Grace also took out her phone and typed in some keywords to search for the video, but she could not find any videos of her and Brian.

Grace pursed her lips tightly. If Kyla had seen it, it would only mean that the sudden disappearance of these videos was because some force had intervened and removed the relevant content online.
