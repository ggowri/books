Chapter 3717

His body stiffened, and her lips, with her own warmth, pressed against his.

And her words just now lingered in his ears.

"Mason...Mason..." How long had it been since he heard that name?

She said she wouldn't hate him, no matter what. Was it true? Then why did she say she hated him when she misunderstood him before?

What kind of feelings did she have for him in the past? If it was just familial love, why was it so easy for her to hate him?

Could he believe her words now?

Why did he choose this kind of hypnosis in the first place? If he wanted to forget his feelings, wouldn't it be better to forget all memories related to her as well?

Why choose to remember everything that happened between them, but forget the emotions he experienced during those events?

It's like watching a movie of his own performance, but without the same state of mind he had during the actual performance.

And now, the woman who occupies the vast majority of his past memories is approaching him, kissing him, and doing things that she could never do in his memories.

After a moment, Veronica wanted to pull away, but her lips only slightly left his before his hand grabbed the back of her head and pressed her lips back onto his.

She was startled. "Mason, you..."

But before she could finish her sentence, his kiss invaded her mouth, deepening the kiss.

"Mmm..." She moaned, her hands instinctively pushing against his chest, but not pushing him away. She continued to passively accept the kiss.

She didn't know how long it had been, but when the kiss finally ended, Veronica was panting, her face already red, and she didn't even dare to look at Mason.

"Why didn't you push me away?" he suddenly asked.

She was stunned and didn't react for a moment.

"This isn't the first time I've kissed you. I've kissed you before, but back then, you would push me away. Why not now?" he asked.

She adjusted her breathing, her fingers unconsciously pulling on her own clothes. After hesitating for a moment, she looked up at him. "Because we're supposed to be trying, right? So...so..."

She pursed her red lips. "Mason, I won't push you away again!"

He stared at her. "Some promises shouldn't be made too early, because you don't even know if you can keep them."

"I can definitely do it!" she said firmly, her dark eyes shining with determination. "I will definitely be able to do it!"

He looked at her eyes in surprise, and his calm heart began to stir because of her words.

He...was really easily influenced by her!

At this moment, he even wanted to believe her words, to believe that she would never push him away in the future, to believe in her promise!

"Well, Veronica, don't forget what you said today. If you can't do it in the future, I won't let you off the hook!" His voice rang out calmly.
