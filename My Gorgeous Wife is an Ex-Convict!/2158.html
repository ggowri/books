However, Veronica Barlow seemed pretty skillful at carrying the baby.

"You're good at carrying the baby," praised Nelson Corbyn.

"Oh, now?" asked Veronica Barlow.

The confinement nanny hesitated a little. "You're too small. Can you carry her?"

"Yes! I won't drop Tina," Jasper said affirmatively.

Nelson Corbyn thought about it and said, "Let Jasper sit on the couch. It's safer for her to carry Tina that way."

The confinement nanny accepted the suggestion after thinking it over, so she asked Jasper to sit on the couch before carefully handing the baby in her arms to Jasper Reed.

Nelson Corbyn and Mick Reed sat on either side of Jasper Reed. Nelson Corbyn reminded her now and then, "Keep your arms steady. Yes, that's it. Your arm should support the baby's head. The baby will feel comfortable if you hold her well..."
