Chapter 4017

"I don't understand, how could you fall in love with me?" Nancy asked. "Is it because I helped Jasper and you mistook gratitude for love?"

"Nancy, I'm not a child. I know the difference between gratitude and love," Mick replied.

"Then I don't understand even more. You used to hate me, didn't you? You even hated me so much that you didn't want to see me!" Nancy said.

"Yes, I did hate you," Mick murmured. "Because I had truly invested my feelings in a woman for the first time, only to be used. That's why I hated you and didn't want to see you. Every time I saw you, the feelings in my body kept reminding me of how much I missed you."

Mick paused for a moment before continuing. "But at that time, I couldn't accept it. I couldn't accept that I still had feelings for you, so I kept denying it. But when you jumped off the cliff, I was overwhelmed by fear. My mind went blank, and my first reaction was to jump after you and save you!"

If it weren't for the Reed family's bodyguard catching him, he might have really jumped!

Nancy listened to Mick's confession in a daze, unable to react.

"In the days I spent searching for you, I finally realized that I love you. That's why I risked my life to find you after you fell into the sea. That's why I dreamt of you every night and kept thinking about our past. And every night, I had to hold onto your luggage to fall asleep!" Mick said.

"What?" Nancy was stunned. Was her luggage still with Mick in Emerald City?

"Nancy, I love you. You should never doubt that," Mick said with such seriousness and solemnity.

At that moment, his expression was so sincere and solemn!

And his lips deeply kissed the palm of her hand.

As if he was making a vow to her!

...

Mick's love for her was something Nancy had never expected.

Her mind was in chaos, and all she could say to Mick was, "I think... I need to rest and be alone for a while."

After meeting so many people and receiving so much information today, she felt exhausted.

"Okay," Mick said. "Rest well."

After speaking, he stood up, tucked in Nancy's blanket, and left the hospital room.

As soon as he stepped out of the room, Jasper hurried over. "Big brother, how's Nancy doing?"

"She's a bit tired and wants to rest," Mick said.

"What about the baby in her belly..."

"It's mine," Mick said.

His unquestionable tone told his family not to doubt who the father of the child was.

Jasper breathed a sigh of relief.

If this child wasn't his brother's, there would probably be another storm.

After hearing his son's words, Jason did not show a relaxed expression, but instead said, "Let's first listen to what the doctor has to say."

"What's wrong? Is there another problem?" Jasper felt the seriousness in his father's tone.

After hearing the doctor's analysis, Jasper understood why his father had spoken with such a serious tone.
