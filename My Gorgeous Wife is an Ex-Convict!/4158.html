Chapter 4156 Her Good

"Mm." Tina responded and took the initiative to kiss Chandler.

But after a moment, she went from being the one taking the initiative to being passive.

Chandler held the back of Tina's head with one hand and kissed her forcefully, with such desire and urgency, as if proving his words from earlier.

Chandler finally released his lips after kissing Tina until she was almost out of breath.

Tina's face was flushed as she gasped for air, looking at the handsome face in front of her.

"Do you want me?" he whispered, his husky voice exuding a special charm.

She nodded, her rosy lips forming the word "yes".

In the next moment, he lifted her horizontally and walked into the bedroom.

Carefully placing her on the bed, he slowly removed his clothes, fully exposing himself to her.

As long as she desired him, he would willingly give himself to her.

He was afraid that he wasn't good enough, not beautiful enough, unable to attract more of her attention.

He feared that her feelings for him were merely the confusion of adolescence, unable to last, and that someone else would make her love them more in the future.

Does loving someone always involve such anxiety and insecurity?

Today, at the scene of the fire and in the hospital, he also experienced an indescribable emotion when he saw those injured and suffering.

In the past, he wouldn't have cared about anything; even if someone died in front of him, he wouldn't have felt a shred of pity.

But now, things were different.

Was it because she stayed by his side today, or was it because... for some other reason?

In the midst of their intimacy, he seemed to want to give everything to her, causing her to constantly sink deeper...

In the following days, there was a heated discussion online about the fire incident.

Chandler gained praise from many people for his handling of the fire aftermath, but of course, there were also many people who questioned whether Chandler was just putting on a show.

Some people even criticized online, saying that the fire happened because the Hunter Group's factory didn't have adequate fire safety measures.

However, these criticisms disappeared with the release of reports from the fire department and the police.

The fire department determined that the fire was not accidental, but deliberate!

As a result, the police also began investigating who intentionally caused the fire.

After dealing with the factory's matters, Chandler also planned to visit the Hunter family and inform the elders of the incident.

"I want to go with you," Tina said. "After all, I went with you last time, and now I also know the secret that you're guarding for the Hunter family. If I go with you, your grandfather shouldn't kick me out again, right?"

"There's no need to visit the Hunter family. You've been there once, so you should know what kind of place it is," he said.

She said, "I feel like even after we get married, I should go there often. So why not familiarize myself with it now?" The key reason is that she always feels that the Hunter family gives off a cold feeling.

It's as if that mansion hides countless secrets.

Although she now knows some of those secrets, she still wants to understand him more, to truly enter the Hunter family and face his family!

"Technically, you shouldn't enter the extended family before we get married," Chandler pondered.

"But you've already been there once, so a second time wouldn't hurt," she insisted.
