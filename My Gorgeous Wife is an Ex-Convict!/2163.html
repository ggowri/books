Seeing that William refused to apologize no matter what she said, Jasper snapped. "If you don't apologize, I won't play with you anymore. I don't want to be your friend."

This was the biggest threat to children!

William's face instantly paled.

He reached out to grab Jasper and tell her that he only tried to push Nelson Corbyn away because he heard a sound. He never touched Nelson Corbyn. Nelson Corbyn tried to dodge him, so he lost his footing and fell down the stairs.

His hands caught nothing but air.

They were empty!

At this moment, the maids around him looked at him with scorn, disgust, and disdain.

He winced a little as he slowly lowered his hand. He hung his head low as he pitifully returned to his room.

The room was so quiet.

He had never found the room so quiet before.

'Is Jasper gonna stop thinking of me as a friend? At the end of the day, I'm just a toy. How could I be her friend... How could I...'

William cowered in the corner. His entire body was curled up into a ball. 'It's so cold... I feel so cold...'

...

When Jason came back in the evening, Grace told him what had happened at Lina's place today.

Jason raised his eyebrows when he heard that William had pushed Nelson downstairs. "Did he really push him?"

"Jasper and Mick saw it, so that’s probably the case. Nelson didn’t deny it either. I asked William afterward and he said he did push Nelson, but it was because he heard a sound heading toward Nelson,” said Grace.

"A sound?"

"He couldn't tell me the details, so I decided to check out Lina's place again. I'll also have to go to Nelson and ask what exactly happened," said Grace.

"Is Nelson okay?" asked Jason.

"I called Kyla and asked. He's okay so far, but some test results won't come back until tomorrow," said Grace. Then, she asked Jason a little hesitantly, "Do you think William pushed Nelson down the stairs on purpose?"

Jason's eyes glimmered slightly before he suddenly said, "Jasper and Nelson are close, aren't they?"

"Yes, why did you mention that all of a sudden?"

"William likes Jasper very much too," he said.

Grace paused for a moment before understanding what he meant. "No way. Are you saying William pushed Nelson down the stairs because he was jealous that Jasper's close to Nelson?"

Jason said, "After all, it's hard to tell what's going on in one’s mind, not to mention one’s emotions. Even adults have trouble controlling their emotions, let alone a child. William has always been with Lily. Maybe he took after Lily's character."
