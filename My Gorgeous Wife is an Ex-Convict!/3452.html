Chapter 3450

 

However, Mason hasn't been staying at home these days, which makes Veronica feel uneasy. They used to go to school and come home together.

But these days, she's been alone, and it's making her uncomfortable.

Although at school, Mason seems to be the same as usual, sitting quietly in his seat, and going to the school cafeteria with her for lunch.

The only difference is that these days, there's another person eating with them, Daniel.

Veronica remembers the first day when Daniel smiled and said to Mason, "Mason, I'm dating Veronica now, so I'm your sister's boyfriend!"

When Daniel said this, Veronica felt stiff all over, afraid that Mason would be upset, especially since she hadn't even told him yet!

Previously, Mason was quite opposed to her liking Daniel. She had intended to tell him at an appropriate time, but unfortunately he went to live with the Reed family.

The result is that she still hasn't spoken up now.

But to her surprise, Mason didn't react in an exaggerated or disgusted way. He simply said, "Oh, I see. So Mr. Cross is Veronica's boyfriend now, huh?"

Mason... doesn't reject Daniel anymore?

Veronica secretly looked at Mason, but couldn't tell anything from his expressionless face.

After lunch ended, Veronica and Mason returned to the classroom. She couldn't help but ask, "Mason, are you willing to accept Daniel?"

Mason raised his beautiful eyes and asked, "Why should I accept Mr. Cross?"

"But just now you..."

"He is Veronica's boyfriend, and although I don't like him, it doesn't mean I have to oppose him," said Mason. "Since he is the person Veronica likes, I will try to get along with him."

Upon hearing this, Veronica finally breathed a sigh of relief and a smile of relief appeared on her face. "That's great! Mason, thank you!"

Her smile suddenly became unbearable to him.

He used to love seeing her smile, but now it's because of someone else.

"Veronica, if one day I stand against Daniel, who would you help?" Mason asked abruptly.

Veronica froze, the question was like asking who to save first if two important people fell into the water at the same time.

It's not appropriate to help either of them.

"That day will never come!" Veronica thought for a moment and replied.

"But what if it does?" he insisted.

"If it really does, then I will try to resolve the conflict between you and not let that happen," Veronica said. "I believe that where there's a will, there's a way!"

Mason suddenly smiled and said, "Yes, I also believe that where there's a will, there's a way!"

He believed that one day she would realize that the person she should be with in this life is not Daniel, but him!

"By the way, Mason, when are you coming back to live here?" Veronica asked again.

Mason said, "I want to stay with the Reed family for a few more days."

"Is that so?" Veronica murmured.

"Isn't it what Veronica would most like to see? My family and I strengthening our relationship a bit more?" he said.

She felt a sudden suffocation. Yes, wasn't she hoping that he could spend more time with his family? But now that it had actually happened, she felt a sense of disappointment.

What's wrong with her?!

Veronica forcefully shook her head and forced a smile, "Well, you can spend more time with your family, that's a good thing!"

She should be happy and offer her blessings, that's the right thing to do!
