Chapter 4178 Tina's Choice

Tina felt a sense of unease rising within her.

But before she could speak, a loud explosion erupted.

Immediately after, a second explosion followed...

The three-story building began to collapse in an instant.

In just a moment, many people were trapped beneath the rubble.

Tina and Eugenia were also trapped beneath the rubble.

Tina smelled the scent of blood.

Her body was filled with intense pain, making it hard for her to determine where exactly it hurt.

"Eugenia... are you... are you okay?" Tina asked.

"I... I'm still alive!" Eugenia trembled and said, "Tina, we won't die here, right?"

Just as they were rescued, they suddenly encountered this situation again, causing Eugenia's spirit to almost collapse.

"No, we won't die, we will be saved," Tina said firmly.

Her voice slightly reassured Eugenia.

But then, suddenly, an even louder explosion occurred, and shouts from outside could be heard.

"Oh God, it's the dam, the dam has been blown up!"

"How could this happen? The water from the dam will come rushing down!"

"Quick, quick, save them!"

Tina's heart skipped a beat. Was the dam blown up? Was it Mr. Perkins who did this?

So, Mr. Perkins meant it when he said they would all go down together!

It seems that Mr. Perkins had planned for every possibility!

The rocks that were pressing down on her and Eugenia were moved aside, but at the same time, the rushing water surged towards them in an instant!

"Ah!" Tina and Eugenia were both swept into the raging current almost at the same time.

A SWAT officer managed to grab hold of Eugenia, while Tina held onto Eugenia.

The three of them were like a chain.

However, in the waves of rushing water, it was extremely difficult.

Tina saw that the SWAT officer holding onto Eugenia was about to give in.

After all, the chances of survival were too low while dragging two people in the water.

Eugenia cried out, "Tina, hold on, don't let go!"

But if Eugenia didn't let go, then the three of them might all die in the water!

Tina mustered her last bit of strength and said to Eugenia, "Eugenia, I'm sorry... for involving you. Please tell my parents that it was my fortune to be their daughter. I hope in my next life, I can still be their child. And... tell Chandler I'm sorry, I couldn't become his wife..."

"I won't tell, I won't tell! Don't let go!" Eugenia shouted desperately.

Tina smiled weakly; she knew Eugenia was kind-hearted, so she would eventually tell.

Slowly, Tina released her grip, and all she could hear was Eugenia's screams.

But this was the best choice she could make right now.

Three people dying or one person dying, no matter how you thought about it, it was more cost-effective for one person to die.

She just felt sorry for her parents' nurturing kindness.

And... sorry, Chandler.

She had promised Chandler so solemnly that she would save him! She would give birth to their child and save him!

But now...

"Tina!" a hoarse voice suddenly resounded, carrying a sense of fear that made one's heart tremble.

Tina's body was swept away by the surging waves, heading towards the distance.

She struggled to open her eyes and looked towards the sound.

She saw a figure desperately running towards her, trying to jump into the current.
