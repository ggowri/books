This is the ending she most wants to see!

When Valda fell into the water and couldn't be found for a year, she thought she would never see this scene again!

Fortunately, Valda has returned!

Grace and Jason sat back down, and Jasper leaned in close to Grace again. He continued with the previous question, "Mommy, can you help me find that boy?"

"Which boy?" Jason asked with great interest.

So Jasper repeated the incident of hearing the piano music in the hallway and the person running out of the storeroom.

Jason's eyes flickered with a hint of thoughtfulness, "So, does Jasper really want to find that boy?"

"Mmm, I want to hear him play the piano again," the little one nodded vigorously.

Jason looked at William standing next to Jasper and asked, "What about William? Do you want Jasper to find that boy?"

William's body suddenly stiffened and met Jason's gaze.

In an instant, he felt as though the other person had seen through all of his thoughts.

Every time he was looked at like this by Uncle Reed, he felt like he couldn't hide anything and felt a heavy sense of oppression.

He certainly doesn't want Jasper to find that boy, nor does he want her to develop feelings for someone else's piano and then... develop feelings for that person!

Because... he used to be like this!

If he didn't have a piano back then, maybe Jasper wouldn't have been so interested in him!
