Naturally, they looked at her with surprise after finding out she was a comic artist.

It was as if they did not expect the crown prince of the entertainment industry to have a regular comic artist as his girlfriend.

Suddenly, there was some commotion at the banquet hall's entrance.

Brian turned his head to look and froze. Valda Theller, who was holding his arm, was the first to notice something was wrong with him.

Valda Theller looked up to see two figures walking into the banquet hall. Many people walked toward them.

Those two were... Jason and Grace.

Valda Theller had met Grace in the shopping mall before, while Jason... Valda Theller found his photo online afterward.

'They're here, and Brian...'

She subconsciously looked up to Brian and saw him looking straight at Grace.

Jason looked over as if sensing something. Then, Grace also looked over.

Instantly, the four people looked at each other from afar under such circumstances.

"Valda, let's go say hello." Moments later, Brian's voice rang in her ears. However, his voice sounded slightly tense.

'Tense?'

Valda Theller froze. 'Brian tenses up too?'

However, she could feel that the arm she was holding had stiffened since Grace appeared.

'He... must be really nervous.'

Biting her lip slightly, Valda Theller's hand slipped from Brian's arm to his hand. Then she opened her fingers to grab his hand.

He paused, turned his head slightly, and looked at her as if he was surprised.

Valda Theller said sternly, "Brian, I'm your girlfriend."

That sentence seemed to make Brian's nervousness fade.

It was unbelievable. She thought she was the one who would scared or nervous, yet she was the one motivating him now.

"Yeah, you're my girlfriend," he muttered before lifting his feet again and leading her to Grace and Jason.

"What a coincidence running into you here," said Brian.

"It's quite a coincidence," said Jason as he sized up Valda Theller.

"This is my girlfriend, Valda Theller." Brian introduced before saying to Valda Theller, "This is Jason, the president of Reed group. The one next to him is his wife, Grace."

"Hello, Mr. Reed," Valda Theller said naturally before looking at Grace. "Hello, Mrs. Reed. We met once. I didn't expect to meet you again."

"Yeah." Grace nodded. "You can call me Grace. I'll call you Valda." Grace liked Valda Theller.
