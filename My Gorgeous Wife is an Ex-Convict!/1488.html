Chapter 1487

"If you believe me, remove those people and just let Huang Qian follow me to protect me like before. You don’t have to get so many people to follow me every time I go out.” It made her feel like she was being monitored by a lot of people.

"No." He rejected her immediately.

"Why?" she asked.

"In any case, you need to have a lot of people around you to protect you,” Jason said in a deep voice. He did not want to tell her about Secretary Wang or Lily's escape.

After all, some of it was just his speculation. He was still unsure whether it was true. She was due to have a cesarean section in a week, and he was afraid it would be bad for her health if she got worried.

A sudden feeling of disappointment struck Grace's heart. He kept saying he believed her, but... he still did not believe her.

"Jason, is there so little trust between us? What do I have to do for you to believe that I won't leave you?" she asked, unconsciously raising her voice.

He stared at her. Those eyes almost seemed to see through her. "Then kiss me."

"What?" She was stunned and was a little at a loss all of a sudden.

"Kiss me. I'll believe you won't leave me if you kiss me."

'Just give me some confidence so I can firmly believe that you really love me!'

It seemed that his love for her was deepening, yet it felt as though he was the only one growing attached to her in this relationship.

She only married him because of the kids and Lina. Later, she was willing to love him again because she wanted to give the children a complete and happy family. Even when she said she would forgive him, he could not help wondering if it was also for the children?

Did she forgive him just so the children could have a complete family?

Was that why she said she would forgive him but her body still resisted him?

Grace stared blankly at Jason and took a long time to digest what he had just said.

'Did he just ask me to... kiss him? But I simply can't touch him.' Whenever she touched him, her body would almost instinctively stiffen and she would even... feel nauseous!

Her silence made him laugh mockingly at himself. "You can't do it, right? I'll remove those people when you can do it."

He was about to turn away as he spoke.

"Wait!" she said suddenly as she subconsciously reached out to grab him!

She did not want him to just walk away like this. It made her feel that there might be a misunderstanding between them if she just let him leave.

She did not want any misunderstandings!

"You'll believe me if I kiss you, right?" She grabbed his arm.

Instantly, her body stiffened.

'Never mind. It's alright. I’ll forget about the past. The man before me isn't the Master Reed who ignored me back then; he’s Jay, the man who loves me deeply!'

Grace kept telling herself as she kept her hand on Jason's arm.

There was a slight look of surprise on Jason’s face. Out of the corners of his eyes, he glanced at the hand holding his arm. Moments later, the word ‘yes’ was gently uttered from his thin lips.

Grace took a deep breath, took a step forward, and put her hands around Jason's neck.
