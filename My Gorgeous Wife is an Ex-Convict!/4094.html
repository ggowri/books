Chapter 4092

After Tina was asked to leave the room, Hunter's servant arranged for her to have a room to bathe in and even prepared clean clothes for her.

Tina wasn't sure if she should praise Hunter's attentive servant for this.

After she washed off all the medicinal smell from her body, she was led by the servant to an antique room.

In front of the room sat an old man.

Tina immediately recognized him as Chandler's grandfather and Hunter's current patriarch, whom she had met during her engagement to Chandler.

"Hello, Grandpa Hunter," Tina greeted him.

Mr. Hunter responded expressionlessly, "How did you come here?"

"I came to see Chandler, so I found my way here," Tina replied candidly, without revealing that she had snuck in.

"You should know Hunter's rules," Mr. Hunter said sternly.

"I know, but I will marry Chandler soon and enter Hunter's old mansion. It's just a matter of time. So, is it okay for me to come in earlier?" Tina shamelessly asked.

Mr. Hunter frowned slightly. This girl looked bold and confident, but her words were so entangling.

"Regardless, rules are rules. I will have someone escort you out later, and don't come in again before you and Chandler get married," Mr. Hunter said.

If this girl wasn't Chandler's fiancée and the one who could save Chandler's life, she would have been thrown to the wolves by now!

In any case, she must marry Chandler in the future, have children smoothly, and continue the Hunter family line!

The longer she stays with Hunter, the more likely she is to find out his secrets. This could lead to some unexpected changes in their planned marriage!

"But I still have to meet with Chandler, he asked me to wait for him!" Tina quickly said.

Although she was mentally prepared to be sent home by Hunter after being caught, she didn't expect it to happen so soon!

She had only spoken a few words with Chandler.

However, Mr. Hunter obviously ignored Tina's words and called for a servant to come in and escort her out.

"Wait, I don't..." Tina protested.

Hunter's servants, one on each side, lifted her up. Although she knew some martial arts, this was Hunter's territory and she was an intruder. It wouldn't be wise to fight back.

Besides, there were so many of Hunter's servants that she wouldn't be able to handle them all!

Just as Tina was thinking about how to persuade the old man to let her stay with Hunter, Chandler's voice suddenly rang out.

"Let her go!"

The servants holding Tina looked to the old man for instructions.

The old man frowned and said to Chandler, "She broke the rules!"

"Rules are made by people. Whether she stays or goes is up to me!" Chandler replied.

The old man's frown deepened. "Aren't you afraid that letting her stay here will lead to unexpected changes?"

Chandler took Tina from the servants and said to the old man, "If anything unexpected happens, I will take responsibility for it myself."
