Chapter 2487

Guilt flickered in Louise Mitchell's eyes, but she said calmly, "It really has nothing to do with me!"

George Gregory stared at Louise Mitchell intently. The next moment, he raised his hand and grabbed Louise Mitchell's hand before pulling up her middle finger.

As if realizing something, Louise Mitchell immediately shouted, "No, George. I'm your wife! Would you rather believe the woman who betrayed you than me?"

However, Louise Mitchell soon gave a piercing wail. Sounds of bones cracking rang out.

Her middle finger had been snapped.

"Does it hurt?" George Gregory asked indifferently.

Louise Mitchell covered her right hand, "Why... Why? I'm your wife! And she... she's the one who betrayed you. Did you break my finger for her?"

Louise Mitchell shouted with grief and anger.

"She betrayed me, but that doesn't mean you can cut off her finger like that. Your pain isn't a tenth of what she suffered then. If it isn’t because you’re my wife, do you think you’ll still be able to keep this finger?” George Gregory said coldly.

Louise Mitchell shivered suddenly. He had never forgotten Amber Mitchell.

"Do you believe everything she says? How dare you treat me like this for her? I'm the one who loves you with all my heart!"

"Did you think I didn't check before I came? The subordinates who were with you when you cut off her finger have confessed to everything," said George Gregory.

Louise Mitchell's heart sank. "I only wanted to help you out. A woman like her isn’t worthy to wear the ring you gave her!”

George Gregory looked coldly at the woman screaming in front of him.

However, there was no emotion in his heart no matter how pained her expression was.

He... did not love this woman because the one he loved... was Amber Mitchell, the woman who betrayed him!

"I gave her the ring. Even if she doesn't deserve it, I should be the one to take it back. I didn’t need you to meddle in this. Besides, she was still my fiancée when you cut off her finger. Who gave you the right to cut off her finger?"

He looked condescendingly at her. There was not the slightest heartache and reluctance in his cold eyes.

His gaze made Louise Mitchell feel like freezing water had drenched her from head to toe, making her feel cold all over.

"What's so good about Amber Mitchell? Why are you still obsessed with her when she has betrayed you like that? Why do you despise me when I love you wholeheartedly?" Louise Mitchell shouted.

George Gregory was silent.

Even he wanted to ask himself why... he was still obsessed with Amber Mitchell.

In the following days, news broke online that the doctors who diagnosed Amber Mitchell as mentally ill had been detained by the police.

The police were investigating the case further.

Although the police did not release any further information about the situation, it was all over the internet. Many of them pointed their fingers at the Mitchell family.
