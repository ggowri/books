Chapter 3592

She shook her head innocently.

"So how did you end up in those photos with him?" he asked.

Jasper pursed her lips and didn't hide anything from Mick. She explained how she had suspected Roger was Lawrence, approached him, and obtained a DNA sample.

"So, he's not Lawrence?" Mick asked.

"No, he's not," Jasper said. "I was the one who kept mistaking him for Lawrence."

"But what if the DNA test showed that he was Lawrence? Between him and William, who would you choose?" Mick asked suddenly.

"Wha...what?" Jasper suddenly became stuttered.

"What, William hasn't confessed to you? I thought he would have made a move after entering college," Mick said casually.

Jasper's face turned red all of a sudden, and for a moment, he didn't know what to say.

Looking at his sister's appearance, Mick said thoughtfully, "It seems that he has made a move. What about you? Haven't you accepted?"

Jasper was taken aback, "You know he likes me?"

Mick rolled his eyes and said, "Everyone knows he likes you."

"I'm not talking about just a normal liking, but...you know, the kind between a man and a woman," she said, afraid of her older brother misunderstanding.

"I'm talking about the liking between a man and a woman. Otherwise, what do you think I meant?" Mick said. "Anyone can see that he likes you in that way."

Jasper blinked her eyes. Was she really too slow to realize it? If William hadn't explicitly said it, she wouldn't have thought about it in that way.

"So, did you say yes to him or reject him?" Mick continued to ask.

Jasper looked embarrassed and tried to change the subject, "Brother, when did you become so nosy?" Normally, his brother didn't like to listen to this kind of emotional gossip.

"I just feel sorry for William," Mick said.

"Sorry?"

"Yes, he likes someone, has liked her for so many years, but he never dared to confess. Isn't that pitiful?" Mick said, at least in his opinion, he couldn't do it himself.

But on second thought, how many people can keep a secret crush in their hearts for so many years?

Jasper was still thinking about what his elder brother had said until he boarded the plane the next day.

Is it pitiful...when everyone knows that you like someone, except for the person you like? It sounds quite pitiful.

He had said that he liked her for a very, very long time.

And then, without realizing it, their feelings had developed into a romantic attraction.

But she still only saw him as a childhood playmate, without ever considering anything more.

"What's wrong? Why are you staring at me like that?" William, who was sitting next to her, asked.

"N-nothing, I was just thinking...you like me, but I had no idea. Do you feel sorry for yourself?" She immediately felt uneasy after saying this and quickly added, "I was just talking nonsense. Just forget what I said!"

As she spoke, she hurriedly picked up the nearby water glass and drank from it, almost burying her face in it.

He saw this and smiled slightly, "I don't think I'm sorry for myself. I'm happy to like you."
