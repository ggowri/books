Chapter 1573

Compared to the previous sales assistant, the manager's attitude was much better.

"Get me a new sales assistant. Since this sales assistant doesn't want to serve me, then there's no need to force her," said Grace.

"I'm sorry you had a bad experience." The manager apologized and called for another staff member who looked young.

Grace glanced at the name tag pinned to their chest. They were a junior sales assistant, probably someone who had just started working in the boutique.

She smiled and briefly told them what she was looking for as well as the colors she liked. The rookie sales assistant started helping Grace look for suitable gowns.

Lina said, "That's it? That staff member earlier wasn’t even remotely professional."

Grace said, "That person isn't even afraid of complaints. If she's not afraid, why bother? Don't worry, she'll be sorry in a minute."

Lina appeared to be thinking about something.

Just then, the rookie sales assistant came over and told Grace that there were several gowns that would suit her. However, one of them was a limited edition and she was not in a position to let Grace try it on. However, Grace could have a look at it first. She would ask the manager if Grace liked it.

Grace looked at the photo of the gown the sales assistant showed to her. It was a black gown that looked elegant and mysterious. Due to the diamond inlays, the black dress did not look monotonous. One could even call it dazzling.

It was the perfect gown for her first appearance in five years! Lina said with amazement, "Wow, Grace! It's even better than the silver one you had your eye on earlier."

"I'll take it. I like it and I want to try it on," said Grace.

"Okay, wait a minute, please," the sales assistant said.

After a while, they brought the black dress over and led Grace to the fitting room. Lina sat in a chair in the lounge and waited.

Just then, Lina saw a figure walk into the boutique. The sales assistant who had mistreated them earlier greeted them with a respectful and warm attitude.

"Miss Lynch, you’re here. I've been waiting for you. What do you plan to buy today?"

"I came to look at the gowns. I saw a black limited edition gown that looked pretty good in the brochure you sent me," said Irene Lynch as she enjoyed the sales assistant’s meticulous service.

She was the boss here, and these people had to respect her. She could now afford the things she could not afford before.

She was also qualified to buy the limited edition items from these luxury brands!

"Okay, have a seat. Let me show you the dress first," the sales assistant said.

Irene Lynch went to the lounge area. She frowned a little when she saw Lina. She recognized that she was the same friend who was with Grace last time.

"What are you doing here?" asked Irene Lynch.

Lina shrugged and asked, "If you can be here, why can't I?"
