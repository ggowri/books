Martin Weiss said, "What if the situation still isn’t optimistic after the consultation? Are you still going to have the baby?"

"Then let's see how bad it is!" replied Kyla.

Martin Weiss was instantly overwhelmed with regret.

If treating Kyla like that back then was one of the things he regretted, then knocking her up after getting drunk was another thing he regretted.

If he had known she would get into such danger after getting pregnant, he would never have knocked her up. He even... tried to stop her when she tried to abort the baby!

Martin Weiss just wanted to slap himself hard, and he did!

Grace saw slap marks on Martin Weiss's face when she saw him.

"Did Kyla do that?" asked Grace. After all, in her opinion, only Kyla would be able to leave slap marks like that on Martin Weiss's face.

"I did it myself," said Martin Weiss.

Grace was at a loss for words all of a sudden.

Martin Weiss said, "I came to see you today because I need your help with something. If you're willing to help me, I’ll spare no effort to help you or the Reed family whenever you need me in the future! Take it as though I’ll owe you a big favor. What do you think?"

It was rare for the head of the Weiss family to say such a thing.

Grace frowned. "If you want me to help you with something related to Kyla, then I'm afraid I can't help much." She thought he wanted her to help him put in a good word to Kyla and bring him and Kyla back together.
