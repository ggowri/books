However, the silence was enough to make Lily understand something.

"Haha! Sean, you're the same as before!" Lily laughed bitterly, her eyes full of intense hatred. "Why did I fall in love with a bastard like you? You're just cruel and heartless. The Stevens family benefited so much from you dating me! But now, you're going to kick me aside?"

Now that the Atkinson family was in such a mess, the Stevens family’s fate would probably not be any better!
