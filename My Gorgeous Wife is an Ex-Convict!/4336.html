Chapter 4334 Beautiful Eyes

On the day of the birthday banquet, Harley, dressed in a water-ink blue dress, followed his parents to the banquet venue.

When she followed her mother and watched her father carefully leading her forward, while her mother limped, a surge of emotion surged in her heart.

In her memory, her father never despised her mother's lameness and even took more care of her mother.

And from childhood to adulthood, she always heard a voice saying, 'How can someone like my mother deserve such a perfect father? Sooner or later, my father will dump my mother and find a more suitable woman!'!

But over the years, the father's love for his mother has been increasing.

Love has never been born out of matching.

She envies her parents' love, but also feels sad about her own love. She loves Lornell, but Lornell chose her only because of her perfect match?

Because of her identity, she was worthy of the Quentin family, so when he chose to save her, he directly said he wanted her to be his fiancée.

But for so many years, he did not gradually fall in love with her, and his attitude towards her remained unchanged for so many years.

I don't like it, but I don't dislike it either, it's like just treating an ordinary acquaintance!

Is this kind of engagement really what she wants?

Entering the arena, the parents arrived in front of the Quentin family and began chatting with each other.

At this moment, Harley also saw Lornell, dressed in a black suit and paired with a white shirt. Even though it was just an ordinary color scheme, wearing it on him highlighted his indifferent yet mysterious temperament even more clearly.

Naturally, it also attracted the attention of many young women around it.

Even though this birthday banquet was not planned for a grand occasion, the Quentin family already had a wide network of people, coupled with their excellent medical skills, many people wanted to please the Quentin family, so they naturally found a way to attend this banquet.

And there is more than one woman who is interested in Lornell among them.

At this moment, Lornell walked forward after seeing Harley and said, "You're here."

"Hmm. "She responded.

"You look very beautiful today, "Lornell said.

Harley smiled slightly and said, "Thank you."

Although he was praising her for her beauty, there was no ripple in his eyes, just an ordinary routine.

As they chatted, Harley's gaze unconsciously remained on Lornell's face.

Why does she love him?

Is it because he saved her in a moment of kindness when he was a child? Or is it because of his appearance, which happens to be the type she likes? That's why she's attracted to him?

Especially his eyes, she still remembers how beautiful they were when she first saw them when she was a child!

It seems that after watching it for a long time, one will become addicted to it.

Suddenly, another pair of eyes flashed through her mind, one of the few that could surprise her, beautiful and profound, with a melancholic feeling.

That's Calvert's eyes!

She only saw each other twice, but didn't really see their appearance, because each time, they were beaten black and blue.

The only thing I can see clearly is the other person's eyes.

"What are you thinking? "Lornell's voice suddenly rang out.

Harley suddenly regained his senses!
