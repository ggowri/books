Chapter 4013

"Don't talk about taking care of me. First, take care of your health. You still have a little one in your belly. If you're not healthy, how can you give birth to this little one?" Miss Fowler worried.

Although she didn't have much education, she understood some of the things the doctors said during their rounds.

They said that Nancy had too many injuries and it would be difficult to give birth to the child.

But because of her current condition, it would also be difficult to have an abortion. One wrong move could lead to various complications.

So that's why she kept delaying going to the clinic.

"I will cooperate with the treatment and do everything I can to recover as soon as possible," Nancy promised.

After Miss Fowler left the ward, Nancy lowered her eyes and looked at her swollen belly.

When she was transferred to this hospital with such fanfare and seen by so many top doctors in the county, she had a suspicion.

The only ones who could give her this kind of treatment in this unfamiliar city were the Reed family.

Did the Reed family know she was here and knew about her situation, so they offered to help?

In a few days, perhaps the police in Emerald City would come here to ask her about her involvement in the case.

By then, would the Reed family know about her pregnancy? Would Mick know?

Thinking of this, Nancy couldn't help but think of the time when Mick handed her birth control pills, his dark eyes full of indifference.

At that time, he made her take the birth control pills in front of him, just so he wouldn't have anything to do with her again.

This child was never what he hoped for!

If he knew about her pregnancy, would he force her to have an abortion?

Her child...was her only blood relative in this world, and she wanted to keep this child no matter what!

Mick...

Every time she thought of him, her heart still ached.

How much longer will it take to ease this pain? She closed her eyes and fell asleep unknowingly.

In her sleep, she seemed to hear rapid breathing, and then... felt a cold and trembling hand touching her face.

Who was touching her? Whose hand was it?

She slowly opened her eyes, and moonlight streamed into the room through the window.

What she saw was the familiar handsome face.

It was Mick.

However, he was different from the Mick she remembered. At this moment, there was a strange emotion on his face, as if he cared, as if he was concerned, and more like he had found hope in despair.

His hand was trembling, gently stroking her face, as if he was afraid that a little more force would break her.

His lips were also trembling, whispering her name, "Nancy... Nancy..."

He kept calling her name over and over again.

Was she dreaming?

This was a Mick she had never seen before.

It must be a dream, otherwise how could it be so unreal?

"Mick..." Nancy murmured, struggling to lift her arm.

For her, even lifting her arm was extremely difficult at the moment.

But she still wanted to touch him, even if it was only in her dreams!
