Chapter 4195 The One I Love Most is You

Tina bought some things in the mall, and in addition, Kit bought her a lot of nutritional supplements suitable for pregnant women, as if he was afraid she would continue to suffer from malnutrition.

In the following days, Kit rented a nice detached house in the local area. It had a yard with many flowers and plants, which made people feel much better.

The house was nicely decorated, and with a little arrangement, it felt very warm.

"Let's live here for now. After you give birth to the baby... " Kit hesitated for a moment, "at that time, we can find another place."

"Won't we go back to our previous home?" Tina asked, puzzled.

In her opinion, since she was carrying Kit's child and from some of Kit's words, it seemed that they were not married.

But even if they were just boyfriend and girlfriend, it was very likely that they would live together at the stage of pregnancy.

Kit lowered his gaze. "I sold our previous home because I don't think we'll go back to that city anymore. So in the future, we'll have to buy a new home."

Another lie!

He just didn't want to take her back to Emerald City because once they returned, all these lies would be easily exposed.

Fortunately, Tina believed his words, but she wondered, "Why won't we go back?"

"Because I have broken ties with my father. I don't want to be controlled by him anymore, so I don't want to go back to that city," he explained.

She paused for a moment and asked, "What about your mother?"

"She died a long time ago. I grew up in an orphanage, and my father only came to get me when I was 8 years old. He thought he could give me fatherly love."

"But it was just another form of exploitation."

"If he didn't have this decent appearance, my father would never have adopted me."

"And after I left the entertainment industry, I lost my usefulness to my father."

"He took back the house he had given me and most of the money I had earned over the years. I was left with only a couple hundred thousand."

"This money is enough for me to live a normal life. The extravagant lifestyle I used to have is no longer possible."

"But I don't regret it because I want to live life on my own terms."

"Now, I'm grateful for this decision. It's why I left Emerald City and found Tina in a place far away from there!"

"Tina, you're the only one I have left now," Kit murmured. "Even if it's just for a short time, I hope you can feel happiness by my side."

"I already feel happy now," Tina said.

Kit embraced the person in front of them, using it as a shield to hide their own bitterness. If one day, the Hunter family or the Stephenson family were to find them or if she remembered the past.

Will she still feel happy then?

"Remember, you are the person I love the most in this world. No matter what the future holds, it will always be you," Kit whispered.

"Me too!" Tina buried her face deeper into Kit's chest.

Once upon a time, a person wandered anxiously in that unfamiliar village every day.

But now, with Kit by her side, she finally wasn't as afraid! She felt that life was becoming more and more stable.

She lived a peaceful and orderly life, and Tina felt happy every day.

However, alongside her happiness, deep down in her heart, there was always an unshakable sense of urgency, as if her body was urging her to do something.

But she didn't know what exactly she should do!
