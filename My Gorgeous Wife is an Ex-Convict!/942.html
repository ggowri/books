Chapter 941

'Now that I've made this decision, there's nothing to regret!

'Besides, my marriage to Lily would be the best for the Stevens family!'

"Alright, let's go over there and say hello to President Wang and the rest," said Sean, trying to stop the quarrel.

However, Zoe did not want to let it go. "Brother, what do you mean? Can you stand Grace insulting the Atkinson family? Can't you stand up for Lily?"

"Have you forgotten your lesson?" Sean snapped.

"That's because she had Jason back then, but now she has broken up with him. Speaking of which, she's just a worn wh*re now," Zoe spoke loudly, wanting everyone around them to hear her.

Moments later, everyone had turned to look at the scene. There were even a few sympathetic and curious looks, but Zoe did not think much of it. She just thought the crowd was sympathizing with Grace.

Zoe became more enthusiastic as she spoke, "Jason's weird too. He was interested in a woman you dumped. Amazingly enough, he lost interest in the worn wh*re. Grace, I heard you were on your knees in prison, licking and eating leftovers like a dog. Why don't you perform—"

However, before she could finish, someone slapped her in the face. Zoe fell to the ground and subconsciously covered her cheek. It took a while before she came to her senses. "Who was it? Who dares to slap me?"

Zoe shouted. Her mouth and cheeks were tingling with pain as she spoke while her face burned.

She came to this banquet with her brother and future sister-in-law, wanting to see if there was anyone suitable for her in the upper-class. After all, her dating life had suffered after she became a cripple.

She refused to compromise and marry someone ordinary. The husbands of her friends were all rich and powerful or at least had hundreds of millions of dollars worth of fortune.

Therefore, to be slapped on the ground in such a manner was nothing short of humiliating for her. How could she hope to leave a good impression on the single men at this banquet now?

"It seems that the Stevens family don't remember their lessons." The elegant voice, accompanied by its bleak tone, immediately made Zoe shudder. Then, she caught sight of a tall figure.

'That's…' Zoe's pupils immediately constricted as she looked at the man in surprise. It was Jason!

However, Jason did not even bother looking at her. He just glanced at Sean and Lily before asking Grace, "Did they do anything to you?"

His tone was completely different from what it was before. It was gentle.

"We just chatted a little," Grace replied in a soft voice. Zoe's vicious remarks did not affect her at all.

Seeing this, Sean and Lily knew that Grace still shared a relationship with Jason after all.

Whether or not they had broken up, Jason was still Grace's backup!

With Jason around, no one could lay a hand on Grace.
