Chapter 3693

At this moment, Tina was still grumbling in the car, "Why did you pull me up so quickly? Today is Jasmine's birthday, and I wanted to go shopping with her a little longer."

"It's already so late, do you not fear danger while wandering around?" Chandler retorted impatiently.

"But we have you and Mason with us, how could we possibly be in danger?" Tina replied.

It must be admitted that her flattery was quite good, at least for Chandler, it was effective.

Chandler glanced at the person sitting next to him and suddenly asked, "What kind of manga did you lend to Jasmine? Yaoi?"

Tina chuckled nervously, "Not entirely, I also gave her some manga that are very youthful, energetic, and positive, about striving hard."

"Such as?" he asked.

"Such as..." She began to rack her brains, and finally managed to name a few manga books about sports.

"Jasmine is still young, don't always give her those 'kinds' of manga. If you lead her astray in the future, see how your parents will deal with you," Chandler warned.

Tina grumbled, "The manga I read is not a big deal. My mom even watches them with me and says the boys in them are handsome!"

"..." Chandler was speechless for a moment. It seems that he still needs to communicate with his future mother-in-law in the future. "Do you also like the love in those manga books?" he asked.

"What?" She was stunned.

"The love between men, do you like it?" he asked.

"I like that kind of love, but it doesn't matter if it's between a man and a woman," Tina said. "I think as long as it's two people who truly love each other, age, gender, wealth, can all be overcome."

As she spoke, a dreamy expression of longing appeared on her face. At her age, she may not yet understand what true love is, but she has a naive yearning for it.

"And what about you? Would you be willing to overcome everything for someone you love in the future?" Chandler asked.

"Me?" Tina blinked and turned to Chandler. "But in the future, I'm supposed to love you, Chandler. Do I need to overcome anything else?"

Chandler's heart suddenly shook. The car stopped at a red light, and his gaze fell on Tina. "You 'have' to love me?"

"Of course. Isn't that what I'm supposed to do?" she said. "You and I are engaged, and this engagement was arranged since I was born. You also saved my mother's life. Without you, both my mother and I might not be alive."

Chandler felt a complex emotion rising in his chest. He hesitated for a moment and said, "Don't you feel uncomfortable? Because I saved you and your mother, your engagement has to be tied to me?"

"No," Tina shook her head. "I think it's great. I have a fiancé like you. My classmates envy me because you're handsome, a famous university student, rich, and talented... everything is perfect."

Chandler tightly pursed his lips. She felt good and didn't feel any sense of restraint because she hadn't truly fallen in love with him yet.

She hadn't fallen in love with him, she just saw him as a habitual presence, which was why she could say such things.

If one day she fell in love with someone else, would she feel that he had used his life-saving act to force her into an engagement, binding her for life, and that it was a despicable act?
