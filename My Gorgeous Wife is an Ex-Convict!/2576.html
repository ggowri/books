Chapter 2574

Turning around, he walked away from her door. However, he had only taken a few steps when he suddenly fell flat on his face.

His face was pale, and he closed his eyes. He had seemingly fainted.

...

George Gregory only felt as though he had a very long dream.

In the dream, he and Amber Mitchell were about to get married. Herman Moss had gone abroad. There was no accident, no suspicion, and Amber Mitchell was never admitted to a mental hospital.

They were still in love. Besides that, to his delight, she had gotten pregnant. A few months after their marriage, she gave birth to a boy.

They were a happy family of three.

The dream was so beautiful that he was reluctant to wake up.

Once he woke up... what he had to face was the harsh reality!

"George, George..." There was a voice calling his name!

"Son, how did this happen all of a sudden? What's going on?" Someone was sobbing. It was his mother's voice.

It was time... to wake up!

George Gregory slowly opened his eyes and saw his parents' faces.

"Dad... Mom... What are you..."

"What happened to you? Who did this to your hand? Did Amber Mitchell have someone break your hand?" Old Madam Gregory asked quickly.

The mention of Amber Mitchell made George Gregory's expression change suddenly. "Where's Amber Mitchell? W-Where is she? W-Why am I here?"

"She sent you to the hospital, but now she's detained by the police at the police station," said Old Madam Gregory.

"Detained? Why is she detained at the police station?" George Gregory asked hurriedly.

"She's being held on suspicion of intentional injury!" Old Madam Gregory snapped.

However, George Gregory's face turned paler as soon as she said this. He pulled off the thin blanket to get out of bed.

"What are you doing? Stay in bed! They just got your fingers in a cast. It'll be all for nothing if you move. It'll affect your fingers' function!" Old Madam Gregory said hurriedly.

Old Master Gregory also pressed George Gregory's shoulder. "Listen to your mother and go back to the bed!"

"I broke my fingers myself. Amber Mitchell has nothing to do with it. Even if she wanted to cripple my hands, I would’ve willingly let her do it! I'm going to the police station to explain things!" George Gregory said. He could not let Amber Mitchell suffer any more injustice.

However, it surprised Old Master Gregory and Old Madam Gregory as soon as he said this.

"What? You broke your fingers yourself?" The old couple looked at their son in disbelief.

"Yes, I broke them myself. Louise Mitchell cut off one of Amber Mitchell's fingers. Even if I break all five of my fingers, I still can't make up for that finger!"

It was the greatest pain to him!

"You... You..." Old Madam Gregory was so shocked that she was at a loss. She finally spoke after a long while, "Louise Mitchell cut off her... finger, not you. Why did you have to do this?"
