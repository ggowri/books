The baby? So, Miss Stephenson has already given birth to the baby?

If we calculate the dates, this is premature!

The baby's condition is probably not good either.

Without waiting for the secretary's reaction, Chandler went straight into the elevator.

The secretary looked at the closed elevator and suddenly realized, could it be that Mr. Hunter intends to drive there himself?

That town is at least 700 kilometers away from here!

At the same time, Kit tightly held the phone in his hands after ending the call.

He knew that it was all over!

All his dreams, all the lies, it was all over!

When Tina finds out the truth, how will she look at him?

"I'm sorry... Tina, I'm sorry..." he repeated in a pained voice.

But the person who can forgive him is still unconscious.

Chandler drove alone, heading towards the hospital address Kit mentioned.

Along the way, he didn't even know how he managed to drive, his mind filled with one thought: to see Tina as soon as possible!

Tina, his Tina, and their child!

Eight hours later, when he finally arrived at the hospital, it was already midnight.

"I want to see Tina, which ward is she in?" Chandler asked the duty nurse in the inpatient department.

The duty nurse was startled, "It's already the patient's rest time now. If you want to visit a patient, please come back tomorrow..."

"I want to see Tina, where is she?" Chandler's voice became more fierce, his eyes filled with madness.

The nurse was frightened and felt a wave of fear spreading from the depths of her heart. She was considering whether to call security when suddenly several people hurriedly walked towards them.
