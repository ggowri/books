Chapter 1032

He was stunned.

She continued saying, "If… If I really get pregnant someday and the Stephenson family isn't stable yet, we don't have to get married. You don't have to marry me with some grand gesture. I can give birth myself and protect the baby. Just let me have my day once you find it safe!"

Hadwin Stephenson was stunned. "You..."

"I'm not afraid of rumors. They can't hurt me at all! I want you forever. For better or worse, I want it!"

Her voice was so crisp, and the look in her eyes was so determined.

Hadwin Stephenson immediately held Lina into his arms.

His Lina was stronger and braver than he had imagined. He had belittled her, thinking she needed his protection. It never occurred to him that she could be independent!

"I just realized the woman I fell in love with is so great!" Hadwin Stephenson chuckled and said, "It's great to have fallen in love with you!"

He only wanted to open up a world of possibilities for her so that she could always be happy and carefree!

…

Grace did not expect Jason to bring her to the mourning hall in Reed Residence.

It was a mourning hall for Jason's father where his memorial tablet was. Grace had only come here twice.

However... Jason brought her over without saying anything. He just stared at the tablet with his eyes lowered. He was holding her hand so tightly that she could not even leave.

"What on earth did you bring me here for?" she asked.

"Do you remember what you said to me here?" he asked slowly.

She was stunned as memories of the past recurred to her.

"It's been a long time. I don't remember anymore," she said. Even though this was only a lie, she would eventually forget it someday!

She would forget everything about him!

"You don’t remember?" He chuckled suddenly. It sounded a little harsh in the mourning hall. "But I remember it very well."

Jason said while slowly turning around. His eyes were as dark as the deep night as he stared at her face.

The way he stared at her made her a little uncomfortable, so she turned away and said, "Thank you for the two bodyguards' help, but it's late now and I need to go home! If you want to discuss the past with me, you needn't. I think I made that very clear last time."

However, he merely took her hands in his and pressed them against his cheeks.

She stiffened slightly. Her hands were full of the warmth from his face. His hands were pressing the back of her hand so that she could not move.

He was forcing her hands, pressing them against his face. If one ignored his hands, it looked like she was holding his face out of her own will.
