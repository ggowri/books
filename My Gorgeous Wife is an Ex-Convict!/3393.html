Chapter 3391

 

As time passed by second by second, finally, when Harley slowly opened her eyes in the ICU ward, the people waiting outside were overjoyed.

"She's awake, she's awake! That's great, she finally woke up!" someone exclaimed excitedly.

Valda couldn't stop her tears from falling, her daughter had finally woken up! God knows how afraid she was that her daughter would never wake up again.

At this moment, even Brian's eyes were filled with tears.

Although Harley had woken up, she had only just woken up and fell asleep again after a moment of awakening.

After the attending physician examined Harley, he finally announced that the little one was out of danger.

This finally lifted the heavy stone in everyone's hearts.

The people then left one after another, agreeing to visit the little one again when her condition improved.

Outside the ICU ward, it also became quiet.

Valda sat on a chair, leaning against her husband's shoulder, quietly watching her daughter through the glass wall.

These days, she has looked at her daughter countless times through this glass wall, but her mood has already changed completely.

"Brian, Harley has finally woken up," murmured Valda.

"Yeah, she'll recover and before long, she'll be just like she was before," said Brian.

"Will she blame me? I didn't protect her well, but I also let her have another engagement. She's so young that she probably doesn't even understand what an engagement means," murmured Valda.

Marriage, in fact, also means being tied down, especially for the Quentin family of the Crypt families. If they really want to refuse the marriage in the future, it won't be that simple!

However, in that situation, she was unable to say no at all.

Moreover, Lornell proposed the engagement to save Harley. If it weren't for this engagement, why would the Quentin family have intervened?

Contradiction...

"I don't know," Brian said. "Harley won't blame you. She's always been cheerful and optimistic. Besides, I think Lornell is a good kid. At least he genuinely wants to save Harley. Maybe the future of these two kids will be bright."

"Hopefully..." murmured Valda, unable to resist closing her eyes.

These days, she has been too tired, and at this moment, the big stone in her heart has finally fallen, allowing her to truly relax.

In just a moment, Valda had already fallen asleep leaning on Brian's shoulder.

Brian turned his head slightly and looked down at his wife beside him. He said softly, "Valda, don't worry. No matter what happens to our child in the future, I will protect you and our child. Even if something unfortunate happens, I will do my best to turn things around."

He will do everything in his power to protect his family.

When Lornell came to see Harley again, he was still being observed in the ICU, but he had been awake for quite some time.

Because of the limit on the number of people allowed in the ICU ward, and also because Lornell, despite being engaged to Harley, is not considered a family member in legal terms.

So he could only watch from the outside.
