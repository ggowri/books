Chapter 3504

 

"Because you look like you're feeling really sad," she said.

He paused, is he feeling very sad from his expression now?

She lifted her hand and lightly brushed his eye corner with her fingertips, "It's like you're about to cry."

His hand suddenly grabbed her finger, "If I really want to cry because I can't get something, what would you do?"

"Cry because you can't get it?" She laughed, "Are you a child? Wait, I didn't see you cry when you were little because you couldn't get something!"

"As you grow up, some things will always change," he said.

"Sure." She thought for a moment and said, "As long as it's not something impossible or morally wrong, I will help you get what you want so that you won't cry anymore!"

"Really?" He looked at her with a meaningful gaze, "Then, if I really want that thing in the future, please help me!"

His gaze made her feel a little embarrassed, and her face inexplicably turned red.

The way he stared at her just now was like the male lead in those movies and TV shows staring at the female lead, which made her increasingly uncomfortable.

Subconsciously, she suddenly lifted the other hand that he didn't catch and directly covered his eyes with it.

"What's wrong?" He stood still, letting her cover his eyes with her hands.

"Don't look at me with that kind of gaze like you did just now!" She said, that kind of gaze would make her feel a strange feeling that she couldn't even explain to herself.

His body seemed to stiffen slightly, and after a moment, he slowly said, "Okay!"

She finally removed her hands from his face, and his gaze at her did indeed return to normal.

She breathed a sigh of relief and asked, "By the way, what is that thing you wanted in the end?"

His eyelashes trembled slightly, and with half-closed eyes, he said, "You'll know in the future."

"Okay, if you really can't get it, you must tell me! I don't want to see you cry!" She said in a carefree manner.

"Okay, I'll definitely tell you!" He said.

What he wanted was her love!

As long as she was willing to give it to him, he would cherish it with all his time for the rest of his life!

After the nurse finished changing Veronica's medication, she left the room. A moment later, Mason walked in.

He didn't say anything, just looked at her quietly.

"What's wrong? You're not saying anything?" she asked curiously.

He pursed his lips and after a while, he said, "Lionel can be released on bail tomorrow."

"Then my dad and the others should be able to breathe a sigh of relief," Veronica said.

"But for now, it's only bail. As for whether he will be convicted or not, we have to wait for the court's verdict," Mason said.

"Will he... go to jail? Or maybe a juvenile correctional facility?" she asked. After all, she had been with Lionel for so many years and knew that he wasn't really a bad person, just a bit spoiled by his stepmother and always hanging out with bad friends, neglecting his studies and pretending to be an adult in society.
