'If that man did this, who... on earth is he? How did he find out so much in three days and put the information in my study without anyone knowing?

Rhea Oliver's face was full of disbelief, but it was pointless no matter how she cried and explained. Stewart Watson even asked the servants to chase her out of his residence.

Rhea Oliver looked blankly at the closed iron gates. Recalling what Stewart Watson had said, she shuddered instantly.

'Who on earth... is Valda Theller's boyfriend?

'If Valda Theller's boyfriend was the one who found all this information, could he... have something to do with the trouble my family's business is facing?

'This... No way! Even if the man wears an expensive watch and has a lot of money, there's no way he could have done this!'

She denied it repeatedly in her mind, but she trembled more and more. The expression on her face looked increasingly panicked too.
