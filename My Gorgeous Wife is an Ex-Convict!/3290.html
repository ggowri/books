Valda couldn't help but laugh when she heard it. It does seem that way!

"Are you planning to take Lina with you to the premiere of that person's movie?" Brian asked.

"Not just Lina, but also Grace, Camille, Kyla, and Amber," Valda counted on her fingers while asking.

Brian watched as Valda counted on her fingers and mentioned so many names, but she didn't mention his name at all.

"Are you not taking me with you?" Brian asked.

"Hmm?" Valda blinked in surprise and looked at the other person. "You're going too? But I thought you weren't interested in art films."

After all, the movie that Kering is going to release this time is an art film.

"Shouldn't a husband accompany his wife to watch a movie? Or is it that you prefer your friends to accompany you instead of your husband?" Brian asked.

Right now, his face is very close to hers, and his warm breath is spraying on her ear, making her feel a tingling sensation in her ear.

"Where...where is it?" she said.

"Okay, then on that day, I'll accompany you as well," he concluded.

Suddenly, Valda let out a cry of "Ah!" Brian immediately became nervous and asked, "What's wrong?"

"Babe...the baby is kicking me," said Valda. She is now 6 months pregnant and can clearly feel the fetal movements.

Brian lifted his hand and placed his palm on Valda's belly, feeling the movements of the baby inside.

"It seems like a lively child," said Brian.

This statement is not wrong. Since Valda felt the fetal movement, she has been able to feel it several times a day, indicating that the baby is quite active.

"Did you misbehave when you were a child?" Valda asked curiously.

"Before the age of three, I don't remember anything. You'll have to ask my parents about that. But since I have memories after the age of three, I don't think I was mischievous."

She guessed that his "not mischievous" meant that she was actually very mischievous.

"I wonder whose personality this child will have in the future," Valda muttered.

"Hopefully like you," Brian said.

For him, it would be best to have a mini Valda. It would feel great.

Valda thought about herself. Before her parents passed away, she was quite mischievous and was scolded by her parents often, or had to eat bamboo shoots and shredded pork.

But later, her parents' sudden death made her grow up overnight. She was no longer mischievous, but more silent.

Actually, if possible, it would be very happy to continue being mischievous under the care of her parents.

"Then the child will look like you and have my personality, how about that?" Valda said, thinking for a moment before adding, "Of course, the intelligence should be like yours too!"

He was a top student. If the child had his intelligence, it would be as easy as eating cabbage to study in the future. How great!

Brian smiled and said, "Okay, whatever you say."
