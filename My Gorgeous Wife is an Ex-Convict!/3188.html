Chapter 3186

"Didn't you want me to talk to Brian? I can call him right now. If you have something to say, you can tell him yourself," said Valda.

"You..." Lexi felt that Valda was clearly trying to embarrass her and show her that she was close to Brian.

Jealous and angry, she raised her hand and directly knocked Valda's phone off her hand.

The phone fell to the ground. Meanwhile, the call got through.

Lexi didn't notice it. She just stared at Valda and snapped, "Valda, I’m warning you. Solve this problem or I’ll maim your other leg!"

Valda was angry, too.

Although Lexi had been disrespectful to her before, she let it slide, considering that she was Brian’s cousin.

However, this time, Valda didn’t intend to let her go. Lexi was so vicious, so Valda decided to teach her a lesson.

"You can go ahead and try, see if you can cripple my other leg too. Lexi, you're already an adult. If you commit a crime, I will definitely send you to jail without mercy!" Valda stated fearlessly.

Hearing that, Lexi was so infuriated that she couldn't come up with a retort. She then raised her hand, trying to hit Valda.

Valda instinctively took a step back to avoid it. The next second, to her surprise, someone else grabbed Lexi's wrist.

Instantly, Lexi cried out in pain.

"How dare you try to cripple Valda's leg?!" A cold voice suddenly rang out.

Valda turned her head and saw that Brian had appeared beside her, holding Lexi's wrist tightly.

"Brian..." Lexi cried out in pain, "My hand...it's going to break...let go..."

"Tell me, how do you plan to cripple her? Valda is the love of my life. How dare you threaten her like that?!"

Lexi immediately panicked. At that moment, Brian looked like a complete stranger to her.

The Brian she knew before was cold and distant, but he wasn't as aggressive and intimidating as he was right now, as if he would kill her the next second.

"I...I'm sorry, Brian. I wasn't thinking straight. I was just talking nonsense. I didn't really mean to do anything. Please spare me, Brian. I won't say those things again..." Lexi stammered, her face turning pale.

The intense pain in her wrist made her feel like passing out, but at the same time, the pain kept her from fainting.

At this moment, the girls who had previously gone to the bathroom with Lexi also came over and were stunned by the scene.

They all wondered why Brian was glaring at Lexi and grabbing her wrist like that. After all, Lexi kept bragging that she and Brian were close.

For a moment, no one dared to step forward.

After all, Brian was like the king of the entertainment industry. If they made him mad, they would have to say goodbye to showbiz forever.
