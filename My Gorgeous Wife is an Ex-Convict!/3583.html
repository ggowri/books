Chapter 3581

 

"Do you like it?" William guessed from her focused expression.

"Hmm," Jasper nodded.

"Let me take a picture for you," William offered.

"No need, I can take it myself," Jasper declined.

"Is there a difference between my photo and yours? Besides, it's been a while since I gave you a gift you like. If you like this, let me take a good picture for you," he insisted.

William thought to himself, maybe when she looks at this composed music, she will also think of him.

When the host announced the start of the auction with a starting price of $100,000, William immediately raised his sign. After several rounds of bidding, the final price settled at $1.2 million.

If the sheet music is indeed authentic, then $1.2 million is not too expensive. However, if it is not authentic, then it is really not worth it.

Jasper looked at the final price and playfully stuck out her tongue. "Will your allowance be enough for this purchase? If not, I can lend you some."

At home, their mother sets a monthly allowance for them to prevent them from developing a habit of spending recklessly.

Although they also have an unlimited card, they usually do not use it unless there are special circumstances.

William chuckled, "After I went to college, my family's money could be used freely."

Jasper blinked, "Did Uncle Barlow allow you to use the Barlow family's money freely?"

"Yes," he replied, "My father let me gradually take over some of the family's business, and I also have other investments that make money, so I have quite a bit of money with me."

Jasper blinked again. They were the same age, but she was still spending her family's money while he... had already invested and made money. There really was a gap between people.

"What's wrong?" He looked at her change in expression and couldn't help but ask.

"It's just... all of a sudden, you seem like a grown-up," she muttered. It was like she was still standing in the same spot while he had already moved forward.

"Isn't that a good thing?" he looked at her gently. "Growing up means becoming stronger and being able to protect the people and things you care about."

Jasper couldn't help but smile at his words. "Yes, it is."

As they exchanged smiles, Roger, who was standing in a nearby corner, watched them.

In his eyes, there was a hint of jealousy that he didn't even realize he had.

This sheet music was anonymously donated by him. Jasper was indeed moved by it, and William didn't hesitate to buy it.

A million-dollar fortune, just to win a smile from the princess.

After today, this will probably become a story that people love to talk about.

The tragedy of the Abbott family has long been forgotten by people's memories.

Jasper... why is she desperately searching for Lawrence? Is it just to ease her own conscience?

If Lawrence were still alive, then her sense of guilt would be lighter.

With this thought, a mocking smile appeared on Roger's lips.

After several more auctions, it was Jasper's donated item's turn.
