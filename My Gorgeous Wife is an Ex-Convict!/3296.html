For now, let's have Lawrence and Jasper's piano lessons separated for a while. After this matter has settled down, we can let the two children have lessons together again."

In those beautiful peach blossom eyes, there is a deep desire flashing at this moment.

After being married for many years, his gaze naturally made her understand. "Okay, I'll listen to you."

As her voice trailed off, he immediately scooped her up and carried her towards the large bed.

No matter how many years have passed, her attraction to him remains so strong that he feels he will never be enough for her.

"Grace, I love you," he whispered as he kissed her lips.

"I love you just the same," she replied, loving him more and more every day!
