Chapter 3031

After about 20 minutes, the Gregory family arrived, led by George.

When George saw Brian among the people with his son, he was clearly surprised.

Brian was one of the people who helped Amber before. Without him, Amber might not have been rescued from the Mitchell family so quickly.

And now, did Brian save his son again? George walked up to Brian and said, "Did you help my son and prevent the traffickers from taking him away? Thank you!"

In a previous phone call with the police, George learned that his son had almost been abducted by human traffickers on the street.

Brian looked at George in front of him. This man looked more tired and thinner than the last time he saw him. Although Grace was dressed in a suit and had neatly combed hair, there was a lifeless look in his eyes.

That is a lifeless feeling that seems to only exist in people who have lost faith in life.

"It's just a small favor, no need to thank me. But if you had really lost the child, Amber would probably hate you even more," said Brian. "However, today's incident can also be a lesson for the child. He should know better about danger in the future."

When George heard the name Amber, his body trembled and his thin lips quivered. After a long time, he hoarsely asked, "Is she okay?"

"I'm not sure, all I know is that she's in Emerald City right now," said Brian. "If you want to find out, why don't you investigate yourself? It should be easy to find out, right?"

A bitter expression appeared on George's lips.

Yes, if he investigates, he can easily find out how Amber is doing now.

But... he didn't dare.

Because he doesn't know what kind of answer he wants to get.

If she doesn't live well, he feels bad, but if she lives well, he also feels bad.

And with such a large sum of money, she will probably live very well in Emerald City, which also means that she no longer needs him.

To her, he is just the person who causes her pain! If it weren't for their child, who is a common bond between them, she would probably want to treat him like a stranger.

George approached Simon and Valda, glanced at Valda, then at Brian, and asked Brian, "Who is this?"

"My girlfriend, Valda," Brian introduced, then turned to Valda and said, "Valda, this is Simon Gregory's father, George."

George was surprised to learn that Valda was Brian's girlfriend.

After all, men like Brian have many options when it comes to finding a girlfriend, such as high society ladies or popular female celebrities. However, Valda appears quite ordinary in comparison.

Brian's gaze towards Valda was gentle.

This made George realize that Brian truly cares about this woman, with genuine feelings rather than casual dating.

The atmosphere surrounding the two individuals is relaxed and pleasant.

In the past, there was a similar atmosphere between him and Amber, but... it was ultimately shattered by his own hands.
