The word "breakup" seemed to stimulate him, and he suddenly smiled. "Yes, we broke up, so you can go love Raymond Bowman again, right?"

As he spoke, his lips heavily kissed hers.

The intense and domineering kiss made her almost breathless.

No, this can't go on, or else she will...

Her teeth bit down, and a bloody smell filled her mouth.

Finally, his kiss ended, and he straightened up. A touch of crimson appeared at the corner of his lips, looking so shocking.

"Valda Theller, do you resist me like this? You would rather kiss Raymond Bowman than kiss me?" His pitch-black eyes stared at her, questioning.

"I...I didn't kiss Raymond Bowman..." She denied again, but before she finished speaking, he had already taken out a stack of photos from his pocket and slammed them on the ground.

"So, you still want to say you didn't? Are you going to tell me that these photos are fake? Or that the person in these photos isn't you?" He spoke sternly.

Valda Theller crouched down and picked up the photos from the ground, finally seeing them clearly.

It was at the wrap party, when she used the excuse of going to the bathroom and stood at the end of the corridor talking to Raymond Bowman.

But she didn't expect that someone had secretly taken photos at the time.

And some of these photos looked like Raymond Bowman was holding her and kissing her, just like a borrowed scene in a movie or TV show.

"The person in these photos is me, and I was indeed talking to Raymond Bowman at the time, but..." She picked out a few photos that looked ambiguous from different angles. "But these few photos are not what they seem. At that time, he didn't hold me or kiss me. It was just a matter of angle, which created this illusion."

"Is that so?" He said, but his expression clearly didn't believe her words.

Valda Theller felt a sourness in her heart. "Okay, even if he really kissed me and held me, so what? Brian Hart, we have already broken up. Even if I really slept with Raymond Bowman, you have no right to question me!"

"So, are you planning to sleep with Raymond Bowman?" His voice suddenly became cold, and a layer of gloom covered his handsome face.

An instinctive sense of crisis reminded her to escape, and her body had already instinctively moved back step by step.

"Brian Hart, we...we have already broken up. Please leave my house..." Valda Theller's voice trembled.

"I don't agree." He said, "Valda Theller, I don't agree to break up, and you can't break up with me either!"

As he spoke, he took a big step forward, picked her up horizontally, and walked towards her bedroom.

Valda Theller's face suddenly turned pale, and she immediately understood what Brian Hart was going to do next.

"No, I don't want to...Brian Hart, put me down!" She shouted, desperately trying to jump out of his arms.

But his strength made her struggles seem useless.

He threw her onto the bed.

Before she could sit up, his body had already pressed down on her.

"Valda Theller, I told you not to lie to me, and you can't break up with me. I won't let you be with Raymond Bowman. I won't!" He spoke, and his lips kissed hers again.
