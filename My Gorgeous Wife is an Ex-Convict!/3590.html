Chapter 3588

 

However, Jasper didn't know if she could be happy again. Lawrence's situation was like a pain that she couldn't erase from her heart.

In this lifetime, could she still find Lawrence?

If she couldn't find him, could she still be happy?

The next day, Jasper went to the chemistry department again and found Roger.

"Can we find a place to talk?" she asked.

He looked at her with a deep gaze and after a moment, he said, "Okay."

The two of them walked to a secluded place on campus, and Jasper finally stopped and turned to face him.

Roger was quite tall, over 180 centimeters, with a slender figure and a handsome face that gave off a good feeling.

Sunlight filtered through the treetops and fell on his face. He had short black hair, a full forehead, a straight nose, and thin lips with a water-like hue. However, what was most attractive about him were his eyes - beautiful and flawless, like a gorgeous obsidian.

This was the image of Lawrence that she had imagined he would grow up to be.

But no matter how much she imagined, reality was like a bucket of cold water poured over her head.

"I'm sorry," she said.

"Sorry?" he raised an eyebrow. "Why are you suddenly apologizing?"

"You were angry that day at the welfare home, right?" Jasper said. "Because I always treated you as Lawrence and made you uncomfortable. But from now on, there won't be such a thing. I won't treat you as Lawrence anymore, and I won't do anything that makes you dislike me."

However, her words made his brows furrow involuntarily. "What do you mean by that?" he asked.

"Just what I said," Jasper said. "I won't impose my subjective will on you anymore, and I'll try not to bother you. As for the unpleasant things I caused you before, I'm sorry and I hope you can forgive me."

"What if I don't forgive you?" he asked.

Jasper was stunned, looking at Roger in a daze.

He suddenly gave a cold laugh, "Jasper, in this world, not everything can be forgiven. There are always some things that are unforgivable!"

Jasper's expression darkened.

Yes, in this world, there are always some things that are unforgivable!

If Lawrence were in front of her right now, would he forgive her? Probably not.

Because of her impulsive decision to end their friendship, because she didn't lend a helping hand when he needed it the most!

"So, what do I have to do for you to forgive me?" Jasper asked, looking up.

"Date me," he said.

"What?" She looked completely shocked.

"Date me, and then I'll forgive you for mistaking me for Lawrence all this time," Roger said.

"That's impossible!" she exclaimed.

"Is there anything wrong with it?" he said with a half-smile. "I think I'm not bad-looking, and besides, you don't have a boyfriend now, and I don't have a girlfriend either. If we date, is there anything taboo?"

Jasper frowned. "I just think that dating shouldn't be treated as a condition for a transaction! The two people who date should at least like each other!"

"But I like you," he said.

Huh? She was stunned, only hearing him say, "Haven't I been saying that I like you and want to date you?"

She stared at him blankly, and he was looking at her at the same time.
