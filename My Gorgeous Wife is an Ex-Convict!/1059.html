Chapter 1058

Grace did not know how long it took, but when the kiss finally ended, she gasped like a fish on a chopping block.

Her long hair was a tad unruly, and her cheeks were blushing. Her serene almond-shaped eyes were glaring at him. Finally, her eyes were no longer as calm as usual. It was as if he finally existed in her eyes again.

Even though there was anger in her eyes, he was still fascinated.

He raised his hand and gently stroked her hair. Then, his lips came closer to her ear as he said in a hoarse voice, "Forget about falling for Brian..."

'Yes, I won't allow her to fall for Brian. She can only fall for me!'

A chill ran down her spine. "It's my business who I fall for—"

However, before she could finish, his lips were on hers again, swallowing all her voice...

...

There was a bit of a commotion at the charity gala too. After all, there had been a lot more commotion earlier.

When Lina found out that Jason cleared the area and had a conflict with Brian, she was completely stunned!

'Jason? It's alright if Jason came to the gala, but why would he seal off the area? Is it because of…'

Lina suddenly thought of Grace. Her best friend said she was leaving earlier. Could she have run into Jason?

'Did Jason seal the area for... Grace?'

This idea crossed Lina's mind, but she thought it was absurd even just imagining it. Even so, her heart was still racing.

She took out her phone and quickly dialed Grace's number, but no one answered her call!

Lina's expression changed. 'Did something bad… happen to Grace?'

Hadwin Stephenson said to Lina, "Don't worry too much. Check with Brian first."

"Yes, yes, I should check with Brian first!" Lina said quickly before hurrying toward the exit while Hadwin Stephenson followed closely after her.

When they got to the exit, there was already a crowd. Lina saw a lot of reporters around Brian. They were frantically taking photos, but not one of them dared to interview him.

There was someone in a lilac gown next to Brian, but it was not Grace!

Standing next to Brian, Stella looked at his right hand that was dangling at his side. It was unnaturally twisted. "Brian... is your hand okay? Why don't I accompany you to the hospital?"

However, Brian paid her no attention. His thin lips were pressed into a straight line as he told his men, "Get these reporters out of here. I don't want to see any rumors online about what happened tonight!"

"Yes!" his men responded, and the security guards began driving the reporters away.

Stella gritted her teeth in displeasure. She had hoped to show off her relationship with Brian in front of the press and get onto a trending post online so that her coursemates in university and the film crew would not taunt her behind her back all day long!

Now there was one less chance!
