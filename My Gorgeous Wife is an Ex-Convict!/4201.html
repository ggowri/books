Chapter 4199

So the next day, Kit took Tina to the mall for shopping.

When buying baby clothes, Tina seemed very happy, emitting a maternal tenderness that made Kit somewhat mesmerized.

Basically, whatever Tina liked, Kit bought.

Looking at the several large bags filled with stuff, Tina couldn't help but ask, "Did we buy too much? Do you have enough money?"

After all, since they started living together, she hasn't seen Kit go out to work.

And Kit had told her before that he had quit the entertainment industry, so now their expenses are all covered by Kit's savings.

"It's enough!" Kit smiled and said, "Don't worry, I have enough money. If it's not enough in the future, I can go out to work."

"Go back to being a singer?" she curiously asked.

He shook his head, "Just find some ordinary job. But don't worry, there's no problem in supporting you and the baby."

He didn't plan to return to the entertainment circle, as there were too many dark things happening there.

"I'll go out to work too when the time comes." Tina smiled and started talking about her dreams for the future.

They continued talking until they entered the elevator.

Kit listened and felt that it was so wonderful. If it really happens in the future, how great it would be.

At this moment, suddenly the elevator stopped abruptly, and Kit immediately had a bad feeling.

Did the elevator malfunction?

And then, the lights in the elevator suddenly went out, and the elevator became pitch black.
