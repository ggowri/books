However... the old Mason, although he spoke briefly, would still act affectionately towards her.

But now, he wouldn't do that kind of intimate affection anymore, would he?

After the movie ended, Jasper and William came over again.

"Sister Veronica, it's still early, how about having afternoon tea together?" Jasper said with a smile.

Veronica looked at Mason and didn't see any reluctance on his face. His smile was so charming that she couldn't refuse him. "Okay then," she said.

The four of them went to a dessert shop in the mall and sat down.

Mason, Jasper, and William were all good-looking and stood out in the crowd. Veronica, on the other hand, was quite ordinary-looking.

As Veronica put it, she was invisible in a crowd.

The four of them ordered some desserts. Jasper's eyes were clearly shifting between Veronica and Mason, while William seemed uninterested in Veronica and Mason and kept holding hands with Jasper.

It wasn't until the desserts arrived that William let go of Jasper's hand.

"Sister Veronica, have you made up with Mason?" Jasper couldn't contain his curiosity and asked directly.

"Well... I guess so," Veronica replied hesitantly. They should be considered reconciled now.

"Then you should come to Stanford University's campus festival with us. It's quite fun!" Jasper invited.

"Campus festival?" Veronica was puzzled.

"Your school should have one too," Jasper said.

Veronica shook her head. "My school had one a few years ago, but they haven't held it in recent years."

"Then you should come to Stanford University and check it out. Mason can be your guide!" Jasper said with a sly smile, clearly up to something.

"No need. Mason probably doesn't like such lively occasions," Veronica said quickly. She knew that Mason preferred quiet places.

"It's not that I don't like it. I can be your guide for the campus festival," Mason said.

Veronica looked confused, while Jasper happily exclaimed, "Great! Sister Veronica, remember to come and join us at the Stanford University festival!"

"Okay," Veronica replied.

The Stanford University festival... Thinking back, she and Mason had welcomed many festivals together. At that time, she loved strolling around the festival and introducing everything to him non-stop.

And he, like a loyal little tail, always followed behind her.

But at some point, everything changed.

Now... it's like repairing those lost pieces bit by bit.

When the two girls finished their afternoon tea, they went to the restroom together, while the two boys sat at the table, staring at each other.

"Are you serious?" William suddenly asked.

"What makes you think I'm not serious?" Mason retorted.

"I'm just worried about Jasper. She's your sister, but she also has a good relationship with Veronica," William said, implying that he was afraid Mason might change his mind later and hurt Jasper's feelings.

Mason remained silent and only spoke after a moment, "I am serious, but I can't guarantee what the final outcome will be."

William raised his eyebrows and asked, "Even if you were hypnotized, do you still want to pursue that person?"

But this time, Mason didn't answer.
