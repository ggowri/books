Rather, she was nothing to him!

If Master Reed did not want Grace to stay in Emerald City, how could Grace still be in front of Master Reed like this?

When Jason and his men left, Lina rushed forward and tore the waitress' hand away from her best friend's arm. "It's alright that you pissed Jason off, but why drag Grace into this? You deserved what you got when a woman like you tried to get to the top by using someone else! I won't let you off the hook easily if you ever harass Grace again!"

Lina said and dragged Grace toward Hadwin Stephenson's car. Hadwin Stephenson followed the two women.

However, when they almost got to the car, Lina suddenly said, "Grace, why is your hand… trembling?" Yes, it was faintly trembling and was a little cold.

"I..." Grace was about to speak when nausea suddenly rose from her stomach. She pushed her best friend away, squatted down on the roadside, and threw up.

She vomited badly. She threw up everything she had eaten before.

"Grace, are you okay?" Lina hurried forward and asked with concern.

Grace had no time to answer her best friend. She continued to vomit until she felt her stomach was empty and there was nothing more to vomit.

Lina quickly took out a tissue and handed it to her best friend.

Grace wiped her lips and gasped before saying, "Maybe it was that woman's hand... I... feel a little sick..."

Lina did not ask any more questions, knowing that Grace may have been reminded of when she had her fingernails pulled out.

Although that woman deserved it for what she did, it still reminded her best friend of that painful time.

"Then get some rest after you get back. Don't overthink it," said Lina.
