Chapter 3125

Next, Brian found a restaurant and Valda brought up the topic of house hunting for the day. "We saw a new house today, it's in the same building as Amber's, but I saw a unit on the first floor while Amber saw one on the ninth floor. Amber also told me about the neighborhood and I think it's pretty good."

"Do you want to buy that house?" Brian asked.

"I have an idea. I'll ask the agent to negotiate a lower price first. Then, you and Camille can go together to take a look at the house for me," said Valda.

"Okay," he said, lowering his gaze.

If she buys the house, it means she will take in Lisa, who saved her life. That also means he can't live with her after all.

Although he was extremely grateful to Lisa, the thought of possibly being unable to get closer to her made him feel even more confused and uncertain.

I don't know how to get closer to her, or what he should do to help her let go of this emotional burden.

All he can do now is to persistently pursue her.

As he thought of this, he couldn't help but feel a sense of self-mockery.

He, who had once despised such persistent pursuit, had now resorted to it himself!

But this was Valda... the Valda he couldn't let go of no matter what.

Valda watched as Brian ate his food with his head down.

He had rushed over so hastily just because she had interrupted their phone call. And when he arrived and hugged her, that trembling feeling seemed to still linger in her body.

This man cared so much about her. Why did she still doubt that his feelings for her were only out of pity?

Valda, are you really that lacking in confidence? She murmured to herself in her heart.

If she continued to feel inferior because of her limp, both she and Brian would suffer in the end. So why not take that step forward?

Even if the outcome was not good, it would only be as painful as it is now. But what if the result was good?

If she stopped here, she would definitely regret it in a few years. She would regret being too insecure and lacking the courage to take this step.

"Brian," she softly called his name.

"Hmm?" He slightly raised his head, and his phoenix eyes looked straight at her.

"I love you." These three words, spoken from her mouth, also represented her determination at this moment.

She decided to let go of her insecurity. Even though she had a disability, as long as he loved her, she would be with him. No matter how big the storms in the future, she would bravely face them.

Brian was stunned, and then his phoenix eyes burst with an incredulous light.

"Valda, what did you say?" He was a little excited and cautious, afraid that things were not as he thought.

"Brian, I love you, so let's get married, okay?" She asked, wanting to form a family with him, wanting to have children for him, and wanting to be with him forever and ever.

He looked at her in a daze, and the next moment, two lines of clear tears slid down his face.

"Okay, let's get married!" He said hoarsely, excitedly taking her hand and kissing it, sprinkling kisses on her hand and fingertips... "Valda, we're getting married, I, Brian, am going to marry Valda!"
