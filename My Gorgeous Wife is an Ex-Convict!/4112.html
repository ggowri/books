Chapter 4110

However, Tina never expected that two days later, her visit to the infertility clinic would become a hot topic on social media. Along with this, the poster she shot with Kit and the photo of her helping Kit in the alley also became trending.

Rumors online started to spread that Tina and Kit had a close relationship and were secretly trying to conceive. Some even speculated that Kit was infertile, which was why Tina went to the clinic.

In short, there were all kinds of rumors online.

Tina felt helpless and frustrated about all the speculation. Come on, why do people have to guess like this? She's engaged to someone else!

Of course, some people also found out about her engagement to Chandler, and the rumors online turned into her cheating on him and Chandler being cuckolded.

However, the Stephenson family and Hunter quickly suppressed the rumors.

Tina was called back home to face her parents' questioning, and Chandler was also summoned to the meeting.

Hadwin looked back and forth between his daughter and son-in-law, "What is going on here?!"

"Um, I assure you that Kit and I have no inappropriate relationship. I'm just his fan, and helping him in the alley was just a small gesture!" Tina quickly explained.

Hadwin rubbed his forehead and stared at his daughter. "I'm not asking about you and Kit. I want to know why you went to the infertility clinic. What's going on?"

Tina was quick to respond. "Of course, it's to have a baby!"

"A baby? You want to have a baby now?" Hadwin stared at his daughter, and even Lina looked surprised.

"Yes," Tina replied.

Hadwin was taken aback. In his eyes, his daughter was still a little girl. How could she be thinking about having a baby?

Lina quickly patted her husband's chest and turned to her daughter and Chandler. "You two didn't accidentally cause a pregnancy, did you?"

Tina thought to herself, "If I did get pregnant, why would I need to go to an infertility clinic, Mom?"

Exactly! Lina looked at Chandler suspiciously. Could her son-in-law have some hidden issues in that area?

As for why she didn't think about her own daughter, it was all due to the filter of a mother's love.

Chandler blushed slightly. He knew exactly what his future mother-in-law's gaze meant, but he couldn't explain this kind of problem!

In fact, he was just as shocked when he saw the news trending!

When he saw that Tina had gone to the infertility clinic, he even choked on his coffee.

"Mom, Chandler doesn't have any problems in that area!" Tina quickly clarified.

Chandler was a bit speechless. This kind of clarification always made him feel strange.

"How do you know he doesn't have any problems?" Hadwin's voice suddenly rang out.

"Of course it's because I'm always with Chandler..." Tina's words suddenly stopped.

Oh no, she let it slip!

And there was a tense atmosphere in the air, Hadwin's expression changed!
