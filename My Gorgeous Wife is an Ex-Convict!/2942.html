Chapter 2940

She pursed her lips and set aside her sketchbook, silently watching him on the hospital bed.

Would she miss him like this?

And what about him? How long would it take for him to find someone he could love?

She was afraid of her future jealousy, which could ruin their present happiness. She was afraid that he loved Grace too much and would never love her.

Actually...she was really afraid of a lot of things in this relationship.

But...was it really going to end like this? Valda Theller's hand couldn't help but stroke Brian Hart's cheek, feeling his warmth in her palm.

"Brian Hart, can I really do this?" She murmured, asking him and herself.

Just then, Brian Hart's brow furrowed and he shook his head from side to side, looking uneasy. "Valda Theller...don't..."

Valda Theller was taken aback. Was he having a nightmare? And he dreamed of her?

Then she saw his forehead break out in a thin layer of sweat, and the temperature of his skin in her palm seemed to be rising.

"Brian Hart!" she shouted.

But he remained asleep, murmuring, "Don't...don't...Valda Theller...you can't, absolutely can't..."

Valda Theller frowned. What did he dream of? What did she do in his dream that made him say "absolutely can't"?

But in his current condition, he might have a fever. The doctor had warned her to be especially careful about whether he had a fever.

Thinking of this, Valda Theller quickly pressed the nurse button next to the bed and waited for the nurse to come.

But just as Valda Theller pressed the button, Brian Hart, who had been closing his eyes, suddenly opened them and looked at her with his dark eyes.

"You...you're awake?" Valda Theller was stunned. "You seem to have a fever. I've pressed the nurse button and the nurse will be here soon..."

Before she could finish her sentence, he sat up suddenly and hugged her tightly.

"...Brian Hart?" Valda Theller was surprised.

"Valda Theller!" he urgently called her name. "You can't...you can't jump down...absolutely can't!"

She was confused. What was he talking about? Jump where?

"You...you let go first, I...I'm not going to jump anywhere," she shouted, but didn't dare to struggle easily, afraid of hurting his wound.

But his arms tightened around her, making it harder for her to breathe, and his urgent voice kept calling her name, as if he was afraid of something.

Even this embrace was trembling, because his body was shaking.
