Chapter 3994

Inside the private room, there was finally only him left!

Mick closed his eyes and reached towards the part of his body where his blood was concentrated. The image that appeared in his mind was surprisingly Nancy's.

...

It had been a month since she resigned, and she had already arranged with the landlord to sublet her rental.

She had packed all the luggage she needed to take, bought her high-speed train ticket, and in two days, she would completely leave Emerald City.

As a small person like her, leaving a city would have no impact on the city whatsoever.

It was like a drop of water leaving a pond, and no one would care!

Only two days left!

Nancy walked aimlessly on the street.

In the last two days she had in Emerald City, she wanted to take a good look at the city where she grew up and where she met Mick.

In the future, she might never set foot in Emerald City again.

When she passed by the People's Park in Emerald City, her footsteps suddenly stopped.

This was the place where she had wanted to come with Mick.

When she saw the news, it mentioned a hundred-year-old tree in the People's Park that many young people had worshipped as a wishing tree.

Many people would come to the tree to make wishes. Even the park had set up a wishing fence next to the tree. Tourists could buy wishing ribbons launched by the park, tie them to the fence after making a wish.

In Nancy's opinion, this was more like a revenue-generating project launched by the park.

However, there are many posts online that say this wishing tree is very effective.

So, Nancy mentioned it to Mick at the time.

And he said, "Then let's go together in the future, make a wish, and see if it really works."

But there was no "future" between them in the end.

After a while, Nancy arrived at the wishing tree.

Because this tree is very famous online, it was not difficult to find.

At the fence under the tree, Nancy saw many ribbons of different colors tied up. Were there thousands or tens of thousands?

As she stood under the tree, several other tourists were also there.

Some female tourists even had their hands clasped together, obviously making a wish.

It seems that women always believe in wishing more.

Nancy went to the small shop next to the tree and bought a ribbon for wishing.

Then she clasped her hands together under the tree and made a serious wish.

First wish: Mick can be safe and healthy in the future.

Second wish: Mick can be happy and joyful!

Third wish: One day in the future, Mick can find someone he truly loves.

Even when making her third wish, Nancy still feels a sharp pain in her heart.

She knows that she and Mick can never be together in this lifetime!

But she still feels the pain.

How much longer will it take for her to let go of her feelings for him?

Or will she hold onto this hopeless love for the rest of her life, until she's old and gray and lying in her coffin?

After making her wish, Nancy opens her eyes and walks to the fence, tying the wish ribbon onto it.

If her three wishes can really come true, that would be great.

As she turns around after tying the ribbon, she suddenly freezes at the sight of a figure not far away.

Why is Mick...here?!
