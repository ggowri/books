Hadwin Stephenson looked out the window toward the apartment floor Lina was staying on.

'She's in that apartment right now. What is she doing? Is she still mad that I caused her to be in such a predicament?'

"Aren't you going upstairs, Mr. Stephenson?" the driver asked somewhat curiously.

"No, I'll just stay here for a while," muttered Hadwin Stephenson. He still maintained the same sitting posture and looked quietly at the apartment floor through the window.

'Tomorrow... I can clear Lina's name tomorrow!'

...

The next day, news broke out that Katherine Jackson had been dating a mystery man for years. She even kept him as a secret lover.

They soon found out his identity. He used to be a male stripper in a nightclub. However, he now lived in a luxurious mansion and had people waiting on him when he went out.

The reporter even claimed to have followed Katherine Jackson for several years and had many photos of Katherine Jackson's private meetings with him.

Some of them were posted online, of course.

In these photos, Katherine Jackson was seen being intimate with that man, even kissing him passionately.

The reporter also said they had something juicier!

Katherine Jackson was surrounded by reporters when she finally left Hadwin Stephenson's mansion. Looking at the photos they showed her, she instantly understood that this was the ‘show’ Hadwin Stephenson had mentioned.

The news about the male stripper was true. She had indeed been trying to conquer Hadwin Stephenson for five years, but it did not mean that she was ascetic.

She naturally had needs and would want to see other men kneel at her feet and worship her like a dog!

However, these photos were two to three years old! Katherine Jackson did not believe that a reporter had taken these photos as they would have immediately asked her for money upon taking these juicy photos!

Hadwin Stephenson probably gave these photos to the reporter who broke the news!

'I can't believe Hadwin Stephenson… has been so calm this whole time!' Katherine Jackson found the man increasingly unfathomable and frightening. Why did she ever set her eyes on him?

"Miss Jackson, do you have an explanation for these photos?"

"You're so intimate with this man while dating Mr. Stephenson. What relationship do you have with this man? Are you cheating on Mr. Stephenson?”

"What about your engagement to Mr. Stephenson?"

They bombarded Katherine Jackson with a series of questions. She now had a taste of Lina's predicament back then.

'Is Hadwin Stephenson trying to give me a taste of my own medicine?'

However, Lina had Hadwin Stephenson to rescue her. No one would help her. She even needed to ‘perform’ as best she could!

The next day, a piece of even more exciting news broke out. The Stephenson family had canceled their engagement with the Jackson family!
