Chapter 2033

Lina urgently said, "Dad, the glass is full. It's spilling!"

Mr. Sweeney then came to his senses and quickly stopped.

The married couple looked at Hadwin Stephenson and asked in unison, "You're getting married?"

Hadwin Stephenson answered affirmatively, "Yes, but Lina is too far along in her pregnancy. We can't have her be tired out by the wedding, so I plan to get the wedding license first. We'll have the wedding after the baby is born. I wonder if you're okay with that, Aunt and Uncle?"

"Yes, yes, of course!" Mr. Sweeney and Mrs. Sweeney said with a smile. Their daughter's marriage had always been their biggest concern.

The married couple had wondered when to make a subtle mention of marriage, but Hadwin Stephenson just said it right away.

"Do you have any requirements? I’ll do whatever I can."

"All we want is for you to treat Lina well," said Mr. Sweeney and Mrs. Sweeney.

Hadwin Stephenson gave a slight nod when he heard that. "Okay, I'll play it by ear then."

They had a peaceful dinner. Once it was over, Hadwin Stephenson took the initiative to clean up the dishes and carried them to the kitchen to wash.

Mrs. Sweeney looked approvingly at her future son-in-law. Not many people with his identity and status were willing to do the dishes.

"You’ve found a good husband, so your father and I are relieved," Mrs. Sweeney said to her daughter emotionally.

Lina smiled and said, "Mom and Dad, Hadwin and I will be fine. You don't have to continue worrying about me. I'll be good to you two. You can go anywhere you want, even around the world."

"Around the world? We'll have to look after your baby when they're born!" said Mr. Sweeney and Mrs. Sweeney. Although Hadwin Stephenson would find the best nanny for the child, they still wanted to take care of their grandson or granddaughter.

Mr. Sweeney also joined the conversation with a smile. "Hadwin's a good kid. He has never been arrogant with our family. Don't bully him after you marry him!"

"How could I possibly bully him?" mumbled Lina.

"Bully?" Hadwin Stephenson's voice rang suddenly.

Lina looked in the direction and saw that he had already done the dishes and was walking out of the kitchen.

"Nothing!" she said quickly.

"Then take your household registration booklet and ID. Sleep at my place tonight and we'll register tomorrow morning," said Hadwin Stephenson.

"We're registering tomorrow?" Lina was a little overwhelmed. "Isn't that a little too soon?"

"Then when do you want to register?" Hadwin Stephenson raised his eyebrows and asked in reply.

"It's better to do it as soon as possible. Do you want to wait until the baby is born to register?" Mr. Sweeney smacked his daughter on the forehead.

Mrs. Sweeney hurriedly took their household registration booklet and her daughter's ID. She shoved them into her daughter's hand and whispered in her daughter's ear, "Hurry and register. We'll hold you solely responsible if you don't register tomorrow!"

Lina was speechless. 'How desperate is Mom to get me married?'
