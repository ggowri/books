Chapter 2845

When the bus arrived at the bus stop and the door opened, Valda Theller saw Brian standing there.

He wore a white shirt with partial black stripes, black trousers, and leather shoes. He looked elegant and noble.

He attracted attention like a prince in a fairy tale.

Valda Theller got off the bus and immediately felt everyone waiting for the bus looking in their direction.

"How long have you been waiting?" she asked in a mutter.

Brian said, "Not too long. My car is parked nearby. Where do you want to eat tonight?"

"Why don't... we buy some ingredients from a nearby supermarket and go to my apartment for a simple hotpot?” she said. They had bought ingredients and cooked themselves before.

"Alright," said Brian.

Therefore, they went to a nearby supermarket and started shopping for hotpot ingredients for the evening.

However, Valda Theller would look at Brian from time to time while shopping in the supermarket. When Brian looked at her, she immediately avoided him.

'I can't ask... Can I ask if he took off my gown last night?' Valda Theller lamented in her head.

Even if he did, he was probably just worried she would not be comfortable sleeping in the gown.

However, the incident would haunt her if she did not ask.

After shopping at the supermarket, Brian drove to Valda Theller's apartment. The two got out of the car with the ingredients.

"What's the matter? You seem to be taking peeks at me every now and then. Do you have something to tell me?" Brian's voice rang out suddenly.

She quickly denied it and said, "Ah... It's... It's nothing. I... I just think you look good, especially in black and white."

"Really?" He stared at her thoughtfully.

She nodded, but he still saw through the guilty expression.

Brian looked down and said nothing as he entered the elevator with Valda Theller and arrived at her apartment door.

Valda Theller opened the door, and the two went in.

"Put the ingredients on the marble counter in the kitchen," said Valda Theller before putting down the things she was holding. She suddenly felt a shadow loom over her. Brian came close and pressed her against the marble table.

"You know what? You're a terrible liar. You have a guilty look on your face whenever you lie," he said as his thin lips parted.

Valda Theller bit her lip awkwardly and looked flustered.

"So you might as well just tell me anything you want to say and save me all the guessing, okay?" Brian continued saying, his dark phoenix eyes staring straight at her.

She felt her heart beating fast again at that moment, and his eyes seemed to see right through her.

Taking a deep breath, Valda Theller blushed and said, "Actually... I just wanted to ask... whether you changed me out of my gown and into my pajamas... last night… because... because I don't remember anything..."

It took her a lot of effort to say that.
