Chapter 654

Grace was now wearing those clothes! This made Stella Lindsay feel a little uncomfortable.

"I was just going to look for a change of clothes for you, but you've already changed," Stella Lindsay said wearily, then she took Brian's arm lovingly before continuing, "Let me introduce you, Brian. This is..."

"It's all right, we know each other," said Brian.

Stella Lindsay thought Brian only knew Jason, but he knew Grace too.

Suddenly, a wave of panic struck Stella Lindsay. Then, she comforted herself. 'It's all right, Grace doesn't even remember what happened that year!'

Since Brian found her, it meant that Grace did not tell Brian anything.

As long as she could play the role of the person who saved Brian as a child, then it was hard to say what she might get.

Grace's eyes fell on Stella Lindsay's arm that was on Brian's arm. She could not help frowning a little. The intimacy was a bit too much as her cousin had a husband!

However... She had not seen her cousin-in-law ever since she got back to the Lu family's house.

Brian seemed to notice it and pulled back his arm. Stella Lindsay looked a little embarrassed, but then smiled and said, "Grace, you probably don't know that Zhang Quan and I have divorced."

Zhang Quan was Stella Lindsay's former husband and a blacksmith.

Grace was stunned. 'Divorced? That's so sudden.' However, she sort of knew why when she saw Brian standing next to Stella Lindsay.

Her cousin had long been dissatisfied with marrying only a blacksmith. Brian's sudden appearance must have given her some ideas.

After all, Brian was an excellent candidate for a husband in the eyes of the socialites in Emerald City, let alone her cousin who was from a small town.

Brian was especially persistent in looking for her cousin. All those girlfriends he had were only substitutes for her cousin.

Come to think of it, her cousin's idea seemed to make sense.

However, Brian's attitude toward her cousin seemed just alright. Grace even found it a little estranged.

Although her cousin seemed to have high hopes for Brian, whether they could be realized depended on Brian!

"How did you meet Grace, Brian? Did you know her through Mr. Reed?" Stella Lindsay asked insinuatingly.

"Just by coincidence," said Grace. Then, she turned to Jason and said, "I'm a little hungry, Jay. Would you mind going out with me to find something to eat nearby?"

"Sure," Jason said softly, taking Grace's hand. Then, he said to Brian and Stella Lindsay, "If you'll excuse us."

Brian stared blankly at their backs. Did he not know long ago that they were in a relationship and was even about to get married?

However, why did the sight of them walking side by side make his heart ache?

Even when he saw her in a wedding dress today, he felt suffocated. She was so beautiful in a wedding dress, but it was for another man.
