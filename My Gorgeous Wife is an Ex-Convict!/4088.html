Chapter 4086

However, on the occasion of Hunter's ancestor's wedding, that woman suddenly appeared at the wedding and pointed at Hunter's ancestor and the bride, saying, "I curse your descendants, who will never have a good ending, who will welcome death in pain, and who will become monsters feared by all!"

The woman was later driven away by Hunter's people, and her so-called curse was not taken seriously at the time, as everyone thought it was just the ramblings of a crazy woman.

But later on, when strange things began to happen one after another to Hunter's people, no one thought it was just ramblings anymore!

It was...a poisonous curse!

That woman was a bug master who used insect breeding to cast spells.

Afterwards, Hunter spent a long time searching for a way to break the curse.

As the generations passed, Hunter's wealth accumulated more and more, but his direct descendants became fewer and fewer.

Finally, when it was almost too late, he found a way to break the curse.

And this church was built at that time.

Every year during Qingming and Winter Solstice, a worship service is held in this church.

In the church, an old man stands in front of a portrait.

The portrait is of a woman in ancient costume, bright and beautiful, and it is the portrait of the woman who cursed Hunter all those years ago.

A woman like this can love someone to the bone, but when she hates someone, she can be viciously poisonous.

"You're back," Mr. Hunter turned around and looked at Chandler standing behind him.

"Yes," Chandler replied.

"How's your health been during this time?"

"It's still the same, although I occasionally have some painful episodes, I can still bear it," Chandler said.

"The medicinal bath is ready. After Tina graduates, you and she should get married as soon as possible and have a child soon. You should know that only the child you have with her can save you."

Chandler lowered his eyes slightly, "I understand."

"Okay, you may go now," Mr. Hunter said.

The tone was formal, like that of a superior and subordinate.

And for this type of conversation, Chandler was already quite familiar with it.

In Hunter, it seemed that everyone wore a restrained mask. The so-called family affection and warmth seemed to be nonexistent in Hunter.

Or perhaps it should be said that even if there was family affection in Hunter, it was faint, as if everything done was just for the continuation of the family's bloodline.

And he was just a tool for continuing the bloodline.

After leaving the church, Chandler came to the medicinal bath pool.

The servants had already arranged everything inside the room, and as soon as he entered, there was a strong smell of medicinal herbs.

Chandler looked at the blood-like pool with a heavy gaze.

He was all too familiar with this scent, having had to soak in this type of medicinal bath every so often since he was young.

Only this type of medicinal bath could calm the poison in his body.

Yes, poison.

The woman who poisoned Hunter used a special kind of poison that can be passed down through bloodlines to future generations.

It could be passed down to the next generation or even several generations later.

The only known treatment is a medicinal bath, but it only treats the symptoms and not the root cause.

To truly cure the poison, a specific person needs to have a child with someone who has the poison in their bloodline. The placenta of the child born from this union has the power to cure the poison.

The placenta needs to be processed into medicine and ingested to truly cure the poison.

Therefore, anyone in the Hunter family who has inherited this poison will go to great lengths to find the specific person.

Those in the Hunter family who have the poison in their blood will have a special connection to the specific person.
