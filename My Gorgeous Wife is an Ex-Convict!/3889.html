"What's wrong? You seem tense," Mason spoke up.

Her body had stiffened, and he could feel the tension even though he was just holding her feet.

"Everyone's watching, so maybe..."

"You feel embarrassed?" he asked.

Her face turned a bit red, clearly he had hit the nail on the head.

"But why should you feel embarrassed? We're going to be husband and wife, and everyone knows that. Even if we act more intimately, it's only natural, isn't it?" he said.

He was right, but...she still felt embarrassed!

Veronica blinked, once again feeling that Mason was a bit clueless in certain ways, and was definitely different from her.

He seems to rarely blush or feel shy, and he doesn't care about other people's gazes.

But that's just him, the him that she has always liked!

Although her face is red, Veronica doesn't try to retract her feet. Instead, she tries to ignore the gazes around her and lets him continue massaging her feet.

Anyway, let the people around them look! She'll just pretend those people don't exist.

Thinking this way, her gaze naturally becomes more focused on his face. The light falls on his face, making his features more clear and three-dimensional.

His long eyelashes cast a shadow on his eyelids because of the way they droop.

His straight nose and sexy, pale lips. This is him, only 20 years old, but they're getting married!

He's only lived a quarter of his life and is about to embark on the most brilliant part of it. Does he think it's too early to get married?

What will their married life be like in the future?

Soon, they will have their own child. How will their lives change then?

Sometimes, in her eyes, he looks like a child. After all, she often remembers what he was like when he was younger.

What kind of father will he be?

Also, he seems different today, more mature. Is it because of the light makeup or his hairstyle?

Her hand couldn't help but stroke his black hair.

Mason's body suddenly stiffened, and he looked up at her, "What's wrong?"

"It's nothing, I just suddenly feel like you've grown up," Veronica murmured, "If we hadn't grown up together, I wouldn't have thought of you as a little brother for so long. If we had met in high school, well... maybe I would have fallen in love with you at first sight."

Maybe then they wouldn't have gone through such a big circle!

After all, his appearance really does fit her aesthetic.

"I prefer a slow burn to love at first sight," Mason said, "You once said that love at first sight is just being attracted to someone's looks, right?"

"Haha!" Veronica couldn't help but laugh, it really seemed to be the case!

"And I think it's great that I can be your little brother for so many years. And in the future, I'll be your husband, so I'm afraid not many people will have this experience," Mason said seriously.
