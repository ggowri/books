Valda stared at the man in front of her, "You're really... shy."

"Mhmm," he responded.

"But...what are you shy about?" she asked dumbfoundedly.

Someone like him, who had been served by countless people since childhood, should not be shy about just taking off his clothes, right?

"I'm afraid you'll think my body is not good-looking, afraid you won't like it, afraid you'll be disappointed..." He had never had such worries before, but now, in front of her, he had this kind of "fear".

Valda was stunned. Did he...even know what he was talking about?

With his figure, he could easily rival those international supermodels! Broad shoulders, narrow waist, and long legs - so many people envy him.

Many people would just drool over this kind of figure, but he was actually afraid that she would think it's not good-looking?

"Are your standards for yourself...a bit too high?" Valda stammered, "Your figure is simply top-notch. How could I possibly think it's not good-looking?"

"Do you really think it's good-looking?" he asked.

She nodded vigorously, "If your figure is not good-looking, then what am I? A third-class cripple? Look at you, you're lean and strong, with muscles, but not too exaggerated."

As she spoke, her fingertips even directly poked and rubbed his chest and abdominal muscles.

"And your body proportions are great, with a beautiful neck and shoulder line," she said, her fingers tracing the areas she was complimenting. "Your body lines are very attractive, and with your head-to-body ratio, you're about 9 heads tall. Your legs are straight and your hips..."

As she spoke, her voice trailed off. Oh no, was she really discussing his body with him?

"Anyway, your body...is very good," she summed up, blushing and lowering her head to continue hastily wiping him down.

"Do you like it?" His voice rang out above her head.

Though shy, she was honest. "...Yes, I do."

"Good, as long as you like it," he murmured.

She couldn't help but look up at him again, seeing the soft smile on his face. It was as if her liking it was the greatest satisfaction to him.

She stared at the smile on his lips, feeling a bit foolish...

Finally finished wiping him down, Valda hurried back to her own bedroom, washed up, and lay down on the bed.

Just as she closed her eyes, all she could see was Brian's figure - the way he looked when she brushed past him, and the sensation of his body still lingered on her fingertips...and then she couldn't fall asleep no matter what.

Is she going to have insomnia tonight?

Valda thought to herself, and just then, someone gently pushed open the door to her bedroom.

With a sudden shiver, she sat up and saw Brian walking in.

"Are you not sleeping?" he asked.

"Yup... Yup, I haven't slept yet. How about you? Why did you come over?" Valda quickly tugged at her nightwear and asked.

"I can't sleep," he said, "so I came over to see you."

What? Look at her?

Valda stared at Brian, and they both locked eyes in a staring contest.

Time passes by quietly like this.

Although his face is very handsome, it's not the same thing to keep looking at it like this in the middle of the night.

Valda thought to herself and couldn't help but say, "Um...aren't you going back to your room?"

"You go ahead and sleep, I'll keep watching." He said.

But the problem is, how can she sleep when he's looking at her like this!

As if sensing her embarrassment, he said, "I'm just afraid that when I wake up tomorrow morning, you won't be in this villa anymore, so...I can't help but want to look at you. It makes me feel more at ease."
