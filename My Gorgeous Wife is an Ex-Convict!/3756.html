Chapter 3754

At this moment, Mason took out his phone directly, dialed a number, and said to the other end of the phone, "Driver Zack, come to the second teaching building of the school now."

Then, Mason put away his phone and looked at Vanessa with a cold and indifferent gaze, "You can see the confrontation you wanted to see soon, but after that, you will also have to pay the price for what you said."

An uneasy feeling enveloped Vanessa. What was going on? Why was this little white face so calm?

To find Veronica's financial backer for a confrontation, how could this be possible? This was a lie that would be exposed with just one poke. Wasn't this little white face afraid of being even more embarrassed later?

"Who do you think you are, saying these things..."

Before Vanessa could finish her sentence, the voice of the head of the department rang out urgently, "You must be Mason. I didn't expect you to come to our school. The school will deal with the rumors about Veronica as soon as possible!"

The department head walked quickly to Mason's side, speaking warmly.

Vanessa's eyes widened in surprise. Did the department head know this "little white face"?

"Do you know him, sir?" Vanessa couldn't help but ask. "He's the kept man of Veronica's outside. Don't let them fool you!"

The department head was furious at this accusation. "Do you even know what you're talking about?!" he scolded.

The term "little white face" made the department head feel dizzy. If he angered Mason from the Reed family, who knows what kind of consequences the school would face!

Especially with so many people around to hear the term "little white face"!

"Enough!" the department head rebuked. "I don't know how you can speak so casually like this. Mason is from the Reed Group and has nothing to do with what you're saying."

This statement caused a commotion among the surrounding crowd.

After all, the Reed family was famous in Emerald City!

How could Mason from such a wealthy family be a "little white face"?

Vanessa was stunned, her eyes wide with disbelief.

"How is it possible that Veronica knows someone like him?" Vanessa blurted out her thoughts without thinking.

Mason looked at her coldly. "Why couldn't she know someone like me? It seems you haven't learned your lesson from last time."

His icy tone and disgusted gaze made Vanessa shiver, remembering the scene of being dragged out of the restaurant that day.

That scene had once been a great shame to her, but now it seemed that even more unfortunate things were waiting for her.

If the person in front of her is really the Reed family Mason, Vanessa can hardly imagine what her fate will be.

"Mason, did you call me over for something?" a man walked up to Mason and asked.

As this man arrived, the murmurs of the onlookers grew louder. Vanessa stared at the man in disbelief.

This man was the one who was keeping Veronica, but now he was addressing Mason as Mr. Mason?!
