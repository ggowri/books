Chapter 4293 No Divorce

Tina was stunned for a moment.

Yeah, why?

She had asked herself the same question.

When she saw him suffering so much from the curse, all she could think about was how to make him feel better.

She even forgot about his past deception and the fact that she should hate him.

"I don't want to see the person I used to love suffer like that." After a long while, she gave this answer.

He smirked and laughed, "You said it yourself, it's just 'used to love'. Since you don't love me anymore and we're divorced, we have no relationship besides Quinn. So my affairs have nothing to do with you!"

His words were another rejection.

"It does matter!" Tina took a deep breath and looked directly at him. "We are not divorced, you are still my husband!"

"What?" His response was like thunder, shocking Chandler.

Tina bit her lip. "Yes, we are not divorced yet. I haven't submitted the divorce papers, so legally, we are still married!"

Chandler took a long time to speak. "Why haven't you submitted the papers?"

"I don't know." She awkwardly admitted.

But that was the truth. She really didn't understand why she hadn't submitted the papers, considering that she was the one who wanted the divorce!

Perhaps it was because every time she thought about their divorce, she felt a sense of emptiness in her heart. She was afraid that she would regret it!

If her memories were to return in the future, she might regret divorcing him!

As Chandler looked at Tina in front of him, his emotions gradually calmed down.

"Tina, you don't want me to be affected by the curse anymore and want to have another child to lift the curse. I appreciate that. But I don't want to have another child just because of that. It would make me greedy and give me false hope. It would make me selfish and take away things I shouldn't take."

"You probably don't know how much self-control I need to ignore you when you appear in front of me, and how much self-control I need to have a calm conversation with you as if nothing happened. It's a delicate balance that I have finally managed to maintain, and once it's broken, I don't think I can do it again!"

Tina listened in astonishment. She didn't expect Chandler to say such things.

"So, you're restraining yourself even now?" she asked in surprise.

"Yes," he replied. "Every time you appear in front of me, it feels like a test. So I hope you can try not to appear in front of me as much as possible."

Tina suddenly felt a pain in her heart when she heard his words. This pain seemed to be growing stronger.

So it hurts so much when he keeps rejecting her.

"In that case, don't restrain yourself!" The words blurted out.

He was stunned. "Did you understand the consequences of what I just said?"

"I understood!" she said. "So I think, since that's the case, let's try to start over. Just this once, without any more lies! Don't be nice to me or treat me gently just because I can lift the curse for you!"
