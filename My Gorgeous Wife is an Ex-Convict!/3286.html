Chapter 3284

William took it and said, "I really like all of your gifts."

"Would you like to open and take a look?" Jasper asked with a smile.

"Wait until we get back to Deer Capital before opening them slowly," he said, looking at the pile of gifts she had prepared. He then noticed a card with the name Lawrence written on it.

William hesitated for a moment and asked, "Are you also going to give a gift to Lawrence?"

"Of course, he's my friend!" said Jasper naturally.

William's expression dimmed slightly as he knew that the people who receive gifts from her on Christmas are the ones she considers important.

Are there not many people who receive gifts from her as a friend, besides her family? But now, is Lawrence one of them?

"Jasper, you have many friends, but... can you promise me that I am your most important friend?" William spoke up.

Jasper looked at the other person with a hint of confusion in his eyes, wondering what was going on.

William's heart sank a little. "What's wrong? Is it because of my request? I...I just..."

As soon as he finished speaking, she interrupted and said, "William, your words are strange. Of course, you are my most important friend!"

Upon hearing this, William was stunned, and the nervousness he had been feeling suddenly disappeared.

He suddenly raised his hand and hugged the other person. "Yes, I said something strange. Jasper is also the most important person to me..." The last few words of his sentence were muffled in his mouth.

To him, she has always been more than just his best friend, she is the most important person to him! More important than his dad, his grandparents, or anyone else!

Everything he is doing now is for the future when he can stand by her side and truly be with her.

"Jasper, I will work very hard, very hard... to become someone who deserves her, instead of being known as the son of a criminal by others!"

On Christmas Day, Jasper went to look for Lawrence after school.

As soon as Lawrence left the school gate, he was stopped by the driver of the Reed family and was taken to the car where Jasper was sitting.

"You...how did you come here?" Lawrence said in surprise.

"Today is Christmas, and I got you a gift," Jasper said with a smile. Suddenly, his smile faded and he leaned in close to the person's cheek, furrowing his brow and asking, "Why do you have a bruise on your face?"

Lawrence suddenly looked embarrassed, he had been... beaten by his mother.

Although it's not as popular as it was at the beginning, there are still some traces left.

"It...it was me accidentally hitting the door frame. It...it will be better in a few days," he stuttered, telling a lie in front of her, making him feel extremely guilty.

"I see. Well, be careful when you walk from now on," Jasper cautioned. He then took out his own gift, put on a smile again, and handed the gift over to him.

Lawrence looked at the gift in his hand with a slight surprise. The gift was wrapped nicely, even the wrapping paper on the outside was very beautiful.

"Well... but I didn't prepare your gift," he suddenly felt embarrassed. Actually... he did prepare it. He was still thinking about how to give her the gift.

But now, seeing her with such a beautifully wrapped gift, he suddenly felt a bit embarrassed to present his own gift.

My own gift seems too shabby compared to it.
