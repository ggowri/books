When the servant walked into the room with the hangover soup, they saw Brian's long fingers massaging Valda Theller's head, and Valda Theller seemed to be enjoying it.

The servant widened their eyes and looked at the scene in front of them in disbelief.

After all, any servant who had been working here for a certain amount of time knew the crown prince was sexually reserved. It was said that the crown prince had a woman in his heart, so he would not have feelings for other women.

The woman in the crown prince's heart was the Reed family's mistress. Therefore, even the servants had said privately that the crown prince would not find any other women.

However, last night, the crown prince brought a woman back to the mansion and was... now massaging her head. This did not seem like what the crown prince would do!

When Brian caught sight of the servant's dumbstruck expression, he frowned. "Put the hangover soup on the nightstand and get out."

"Yes." The servant came to their senses, put the soup down, and left the room.

Brian took over the soup and handed it to Valda Theller. "Drink this and your headache will soon disappear."

Valda Theller took over the bowl. With two hands holding the bowl, she drank it gulp by gulp. She felt comfortable after drinking the warm soup.

"Thank you for saving me... yesterday," said Valda Theller.

"I was late, or you wouldn't have had to suffer." Brian's face darkened as he asked, "Have they... treated you like this before?"

Valda Theller's face was gloomy. "It's all in the past. I didn't expect them to do such a thing after graduating from university for so many years!"

"Yeah, how unruly. Who do they think they are? The judge?" Displeasure flickered in Brian's eyes.

He had known from his research that she was taunted in university, but it was only described in a few sentences. He witnessed it himself last night.

He was even angrier after witnessing it.

"But you saved my life yesterday. Camille told them about you and said you're my boyfriend. I think they won’t dare to do anything to me anymore," said Valda Theller.

His dark phoenix eyes looked at her. "Do you think that's enough?"

"Huh?" She froze, still in a trance.

"It's not enough. It's not nearly enough," muttered Brian. Watching Valda Theller, who was in a trance, he toned down the chill in his eyes. "Alright, hurry and finish the soup. It won’t taste good if it gets cold."

"Okay." She quickly finished her soup.

He said, "Your change of clothes is by the bed. You can go to the bathroom next door if you want to take a bath. It has everything."

She blushed when she saw the pile of clothes beside the bed, which also included lingerie.

However, she blushed more when she looked down at what she was wearing.

"Umm... My... My clothes..." She was even stammering.

The clothes she wore were not her own but a new nightgown she had never seen. In other words, someone changed her clothes for her last night after she got drunk.

"Yeah. I got someone to change your clothes for you," he said.
