Chapter 2630

He thought she was only a tool. After all, she was not the one he had fallen in love with at the time. He was only using her to complete a bet with another woman.

However, when he saw her taunted at school and even humiliated in public, he felt a painful pang in his heart.

A person who had once been cheerful in front of him had become tremblingly fearful of her surroundings.

He knew she was being bullied and became the laughing stock of the school.

He caused all this. It happened to her because he used her. However, he still did not explain anything at school even when he saw her so miserable.

He hurried abroad as an exchange student half a year later as if to escape all this.

However, the scenes of her being bullied by others still appeared in his mind from time to time even after going abroad. She had imperceptibly held a place in his heart.

He would wonder how she was doing and whether she was alright.

It never occurred to him that the woman he did not care about but only wanted to use could not be erased from his mind.

Even when other women were friendly to him, deliberately approached him, and made intimate gestures, her face would recur to him.

He could not get rid of her!

"Raymond Bowman, have you ever thought you might have fallen in love with Valda Theller?" His former university classmate and current best friend Chang Mingyu had said to him.

'I've fallen in love with her?' He found it ridiculous as soon as he heard it.

'How could I have fallen in love with Valda Theller? That woman has never been my cup of tea. Besides, I was attracted to another woman at first.'

"How is that possible? Stop joking around!" he said.

"How else would you explain it? Why do you still think of her after all these years? Is it really because you feel guilty? If so, you have a lot of people to feel guilty about. Since childhood, many women have been rejected by you. Why don't you feel guilty about them?"

Therefore, he found out about her whereabouts, secretly investigated her current situation, and asked Keith Sharp to take him on the blind date.

He only wanted to take this opportunity to reconnect with her, but he did not expect her to have a boyfriend.

She did not have a boyfriend when he investigated her!

'Where did this boyfriend of hers come from? And she's in love with him? I was the one she loved!'

The thought of this sent another pang of pain through his heart.

He thought she would hate him, try to avoid him, or rush up and beat him when they met.

He never thought she would not hate him.

She did not hate him because she had no feelings for him anymore! Her love for him was gone, but he...

If there was karma in this world, perhaps this would be it!
