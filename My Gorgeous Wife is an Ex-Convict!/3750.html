Chapter 3748

"Veronica, what did you say?!" Vanessa glared angrily, "Do you know who you're talking to?!"

"So, do you think you're noble? Only you can criticize others, but others can't criticize you?" Veronica retorted.

"I am indeed more noble than you, so what?" Vanessa said, her eyes filled with not only anger but also disdain. In her eyes, people like Veronica were nothing but lowly dust.

Although she was being kept by someone now, once this kind of thing was exposed, she would never be able to hold her head up again!

She would see how unlucky Veronica would be then!

And now, the first thing she had to do was-"Manager, where is the manager of your restaurant?" Vanessa suddenly grabbed a waiter.

The waiter went to find the manager, and after a moment, the manager walked over and asked, "Is there anything Miss Thompson needs?" After all, Vanessa was a regular here, and the manager knew her.

"Well, you see, I paid for their table today. After all, my classmate usually does compensated dating and brought her gigolo here to eat. The price is a bit too much for her to bear, so as a classmate, I kindly helped her out."

Vanessa deliberately raised her voice when speaking, obviously wanting everyone around to hear, and the group of girlfriends standing beside her even burst into mocking laughter.

After hearing Vanessa's words, the manager looked at Veronica's table and was completely stunned.

What...what is this woman talking about? Saying that the female companion brought by Mason from the Reed family is...doing compensated dating? And then saying that Mason is a gigolo?

Oh my god, if the Reed family really investigates, not only will this woman be in trouble, but even their restaurant will be implicated!

Thinking of this, the manager's face turned pale, and he immediately said to the waiter beside him, "Call the security guard over."

"Yes!" The waiter hurriedly ran off, and Vanessa's face flashed with surprise before turning into a smug expression. The manager must be planning to kick Veronica and that pretty boy out of the restaurant.

People like Veronica dining here only lower the restaurant's standards!

As for the pretty boy, if he apologizes properly, Vanessa wouldn't mind spending some time with him. After all, his face is quite to her liking.

However, what Vanessa didn't expect was that when several security guards arrived, the manager pointed directly at her and said, "Kick her and her companions out of the restaurant."

"What?!" Vanessa and her friends were shocked. "Manager, what are you saying? You want us to leave?"

"Yes, this restaurant doesn't welcome you, and you will be permanently blacklisted." The manager scolded the security guards and urged them to take action.

The security guards then said to Vanessa and her friends, "Ladies, please come with us."

"I'm not leaving. What can you do? Your restaurant has no right to refuse customers. And I will go to the Consumer Council to sue you. If you don't give me an explanation today, you won't even be able to keep your restaurant open!" Vanessa said.

"Then you can try!" The manager said stubbornly, urging the security guards.

After all, the longer they stayed, the more they would anger Mason.
