Chapter 4107

"Don't you have anything you want to say to me?" he avoided eye contact, afraid to look at her.

Tina blinked. "I think it's better for us to have a child sooner rather than later. If I get pregnant, I can take a break from school and continue my studies after the child is born. Besides, I'm already of legal age to get married and have children. It's all good!" she said eagerly.

Chandler was stunned, looking at Tina with surprise. He never expected her to say something like that after learning the truth about having a child. She didn't seem angry at all, but rather... eager to have a child with him!

"Do you know what you're saying?" he asked.

"I know," she nodded. How could she not know when she had said the words herself?

"Now that you know the truth, aren't you angry? Aren't you furious? Why aren't you blaming me?" Chandler yelled, suppressing his emotions.

"Because I'm really not angry or upset," Tina said. "When our families arranged our engagement, it was because I'm a curse breaker and Hunter can save me and my mom. So it was a mutually beneficial transaction from the start!"

The terms of the deal were clear from the beginning, so she didn't feel like she was being used.

"And you've been so good to me all these years," Tina continued. "Besides my parents, you've been the person who's been with me the longest!"

"That was just using you!" Chandler said hoarsely, avoiding her gaze again. "I just... wanted you to like me, wanted to slowly cultivate a relationship with you, wanted you to not be able to leave me..."

She didn't say anything.

There seemed to be a silence in the air.

He laughed bitterly, "You must think I'm despicable, wanting to use your feelings to tie you down since before you were even born..."

"But I don't think it's that despicable," Tina's voice rang out again. "Although you wanted to use feelings to tie me down, the feelings you put in were not fake!"

She knew better than anyone how he had treated her over the years.

She could tell whether it was true or false!

"On the contrary, I think it's pretty good. Fortunately, you could sense me when I was still in my mother's womb, fortunately I have this constitution, so my mother and I could be saved, and I could survive! And then there's you, accompanying me all the way..."

She spoke sincerely, every word like a warm current, flowing into his heart, extinguishing his anxiety, panic, and pain...

Chandler stared at her in a daze. He had imagined many times what her reaction would be when she found out the truth.

Even if she wanted to cut ties with him now, to leave him forever, he was mentally prepared for it!

But he never expected her to describe his purpose as "pretty good," to describe this deceitful relationship that had a purpose from the start with those words!

"In my opinion, there is no such thing as liking or loving someone for no reason in this world. Just like how my parents love me because I am their daughter, and I love them because they are my parents," Tina continued, "If you are good to me because I am the one who can remove the parasites from your body, then I think by now, there must be more than just that reason. If you don't have any other feelings for me, then you should be thinking of finding a way to marry me soon and have children as soon as possible!"

Instead of postponing the wedding date again and again!
