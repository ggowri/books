Chapter 1606

'She won't become Young master Reed's weakness?'

An indescribable feeling filled Secretary Wang's heart. Old Master was afraid that Young master Reed's rival could easily manipulate him if he had a weakness, which might lead to the downfall of the Reed family. Therefore, his last words to him were to get rid of Grace.

However, Secretary Wang decided to save Grace at the last moment because Grace had chosen to sacrifice herself to save Young master Reed.

Five years later, the woman would still do the same.

However, Jason only felt something pressing on his chest after hearing what Grace said. He could not breathe.

He instantly grasped Grace's hand. "Even if someone does use you to threaten me one day, I won't let you kill yourself! Do you hear me?"

Grace looked at Jason but did not answer him as he wanted.

"Grace!" His voice unconsciously rose. He wanted an answer from her no matter what.

She said, "Then we'll never let that day happen. Jay, at least I can promise you that I won't give up on myself until the very last minute."

He stared at her and felt a sudden pain in his head. Something seemed to flash through his mind, but it was too quick and he could not grasp it!

Secretary Wang's voice broke the silence. "Grace, even though you’ve said so, I can't tell you the whereabouts of the child because... they were already dead upon birth."

"How is that possible?" Grace asked in disbelief.

"Why not? Where do you think the child is otherwise? I don't have any children here!" Secretary Wang said coldly.

Grace stared blankly at Secretary Wang. 'The child...' Secretary Wang did not have any children around him, so... did her child actually die five years ago?

They were born dead!

Even though she... had thought of this possibility, it still hurt so much that she could not breathe when she heard it. It was too heavy for her to bear.

She remembered all three babies moving in her belly. She still...

Grace suddenly blacked out. She fell into familiar arms, and the last words that escaped her mouth were... "I'm sorry."

'I'm sorry. I'm a mother but failed to protect my child.

'I'm sorry. That child was supposed to come into the world but... didn't even get a chance to see it.'

As their mother, she only found out he or she no longer existed in this world five years later!

Grace fainted while Jason stared at Secretary Wang. "Is that child alive or dead? You'd better think it through before you answer. I'll make your life a living hell if you dare lie!"

"Does it matter whether they're alive or dead? I don't have any children here. That's all I can say. I won't tell you the rest," said Secretary Wang.

Jason pressed his thin lips together tightly, picked Grace up without a word, and walked out of the house.

Terrence and those subordinates were waiting outside. As soon as he saw Jason coming out, he rushed forward and said, "Young master Reed, is Young Madam..."

Jason said, "She fainted because she got too emotional. Inform the police about Secretary Wang's whereabouts."
