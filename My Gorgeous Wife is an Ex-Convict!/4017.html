"How can you not go back to Emerald City? You're pregnant with your elder brother's child, so of course you have to go back to Emerald City!" Jasper blurted out.

After all, Nancy's bulging belly made it obvious to anyone that she was pregnant.

And before they came here, they had already learned all about Nancy's situation in the past few months.

They knew that she had been discovered and had been in a coma for five months, during which time she was found to be 25 weeks pregnant.

Nancy's body suddenly stiffened. "No, the child is not Mick's!" This was almost without thinking, instinctively coming out of her mouth.

Suddenly, the atmosphere in the room became awkward, as if it had suddenly fallen silent.

Jasper opened his mouth, wanting to say something, but for a moment he didn't know what to say.

It was Mick who spoke, "You guys go out first, I'll talk to Nancy alone."

The people in the ward filed out.

Only Mick and Nancy were left.

Mick stared at Nancy. "Why did you say the child isn't mine?"

"Because... the child really isn't yours," Nancy said.

"The child is 25 weeks old now. Counting the days, it should be the time we had sex!" Mick said.

"Mr. Reed shouldn't think that one time is enough to get pregnant, and besides, you saw me take birth control pills with your own eyes," Nancy said.

In any case, she couldn't let Mick think that the child was his!

She wants to keep this child, her only family.

"If it's not mine, then whose is it?" Mick asked.

"You don't need to know that. Mr. Reed, have you forgotten that we agreed to be strangers with no relationship in the future?" Nancy tried to remain calm.

Her words made Mick's heart skip a beat.

"Are you blaming me for how I treated you back then? I can apologize, I'm sorry, I shouldn't have..." Mick tried to say.

"No!" Nancy interrupted Mick.

His humble attitude towards her at this moment made her uncomfortable.

"I'm not blaming you. I'm the one who should apologize to you. You were kind to me back then. I just want to say..." She took a deep breath. "The child is not yours, it has nothing to do with you."

"Then who is the child's father? Tell me!" Mick said. He didn't believe there was another man when they were together.

Besides, he knew that when they had sex, it was her first time.

"Just a stranger, I don't know him. After we separated, I had a relationship with that person because of an accident, and that's how I got pregnant." She made up a story.

Making up a non-existent person would make it impossible to prove otherwise.

"Anyway - this child is mine, and I will raise him/her well. The child only has a relationship with me!" Nancy said.

Mick stared at Nancy and suddenly said, "If you want to know if the child is mine, it's easy to do a DNA test after the child is born."

His tone sounded like he was sure the child was his.

"You--" Nancy stared at Mick, momentarily speechless.

If they really did a DNA test, then everyone would know who the child's biological father is.

"This is my child, you have no right to do this!" she protested, feeling guilty.

But this only proved who the child belonged to!
