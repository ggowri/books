Chapter 4285 Looking for Her

Harley paused, turning around in surprise when she saw Lornell coming out of the car and walking towards her.

"How did you..." she was a bit confused.

Weren't they supposed to pretend they didn't know each other? Why was he taking the initiative to greet her?

"I came here today specifically to find you. Are you planning to ignore me and leave?" Lornell walked up to Harley and said.

"You're looking for me?" she blinked, not quite understanding what was going on.

He looked at her bewildered expression, furrowing his brow slightly, and grabbed her wrist. "Get in the car, let's leave this place."

After all, he didn't have time to explain here and be watched by the people around.

Harley was led into the car by Lornell, and the car quickly left the school gate, leaving behind a crowd of onlookers still marveling at the scene that just happened.

After all, Harley was known as the princess of the school's entertainment circle.

The Hart family owned half of the national film and television industry, and her father, Brian Hart, could almost be called the emperor of the entertainment industry.

There were rumors that because Harley liked a star when she was a child, that star received resources that would be almost impossible to obtain in this lifetime. Just because of her words, he got famous nationwide.

One could imagine the position the Hart family held in the entertainment industry. Therefore, many stars wanted to get to know this little princess of the Hart family.

After all, if they were lucky enough to be noticed by the little princess, they would have a smooth sailing-career in the entertainment industry, gaining both fame and fortune.

"Could that person just now be some kind of celebrity?" someone speculated.

"Unlikely, most celebrities can't afford such a car." someone else said.

"Could it be an heir of some wealthy family?"

"But if a wealthy heir like that is so handsome, they would have been trending already."

Various discussions were going on, while Harley sat in Lornell's car, trying to guess why he suddenly came to her school today.

"Did you see me in the restaurant at lunch today?" Lornell asked.

"Yes," Harley replied.

"Why didn't you say hello?" he asked.

"Your classmates were there, it wouldn't have been appropriate for me to approach and greet you," Harley explained.

"What's inappropriate about it?" he countered.

She hesitated for a moment, then bit her lip lightly. "Alright, I understand. Next time I see you, I'll come and say hello."

If she didn't mention their engagement and just introduced herself as his friend, she should be able to greet him. He probably meant that too.

"By the way, how did you end up with Tina?" Lornell asked again.

"What's so strange about me being with Tina?" Harley retorted.

"She lost her memory, so you shouldn't have any connection with her. Isn't it strange that you had a meal with her at the restaurant?" Lornell said.

"Tina asked me out for a meal today and asked me some questions about your family," Harley said.

"My family?" Lornell raised an eyebrow.

"She asked if I knew anything about the Quentin family, and I just mentioned some things that everyone knows. But later on, we talked about recovering her memory. Oh, by the way, can the Quentin family help Tina recover her memory?"

Lornell pondered for a moment and said, "If there is a forceful stimulation, perhaps it will be possible."
