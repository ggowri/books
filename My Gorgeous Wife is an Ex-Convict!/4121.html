When he stepped out of the nanny car today and looked up to see her, something seemed to slowly spiral out of control.

Bringing her onto the nanny car and keeping her there was just to prevent her from being photographed today at the film studio, to avoid further rumors from spreading, right?

"Kit, are you ready? We're about to start shooting," the assistant director said.

Kit gathered his thoughts. He shouldn't be thinking about anything else right now. All he had to do was focus on the shoot. This was his first step into a new field. He could only succeed, not fail!

At this moment, Tina was still in the nanny car, watching everything inside. The assistant got out of the car with Kit, leaving only her inside.

Wasn't Kit afraid that she would mess with things inside the nanny car? Tina thought to herself.

Was this... the trust of an idol towards their fan?

The glass on both sides of the nanny car was covered with a special film. You could see outside from inside, but you couldn't see inside from outside.

So Tina could see the outside scene through the glass, but unfortunately, she couldn't see the filming scene outside!

Just then, Eugenia called, "Tina, where are you? You've been in the bathroom for too long."

Tina was sweating profusely. So much had happened that she almost forgot Eugenia was still waiting for her!

"Something happened earlier, and now I'm in Kit's nanny car," Tina didn't hide anything from her good friend.

Eugenia was someone she could trust!

"What?" Eugenia was extremely surprised. "How did you end up in Kit's nanny car?"

Tina explained the whole situation to Eugenia.

"So, he's worried that someone might take a picture of you coming here, and it would make things even more complicated between you two," muttered Eugenia.

"That's probably it," Tina said. "But in the nanny car, we couldn't see anything that was being filmed. I really wish I could see it!" Tina felt a great sense of regret.

It was such a rare opportunity to be so close to the filming location, but she couldn't see anything.

"Wait a minute!" Eugenia said, ending the call.

Before Tina could react, Eugenia called back on video and aimed her phone at the filming location.

Because it was a long distance, there were many things that couldn't be seen clearly, but at least it was better than just sitting bored in the nanny car.

Tina chatted with Eugenia while watching the "live broadcast" on her phone until the door of the nanny car opened and Kit walked in.

"What are you doing?" Kit asked.

"Uh, just having a video call with a friend," Tina said, but she deliberately adjusted the phone camera so it wasn't facing Kit.

Eugenia, on the other hand, heard Kit's voice and immediately exclaimed, "It's Kit! I saw him get on the nanny car just now! Can I talk to Kit?"

Because Tina was using the speakerphone, Kit naturally heard all of this.

Tina looked at Kit awkwardly. "My friend wants to talk to you. Is that okay? You've met her before, it's Eugenia. You even took a photo with her and gave her an autograph. She's your loyal fan, and she's here with me today!"

Tina was afraid that Kit would refuse Eugenia.

After all, Eugenia is an even bigger fan of Kit than she is!
