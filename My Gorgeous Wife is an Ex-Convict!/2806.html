"It... It's broken... Ouch!" Rowena Santiago shouted.

"Y-Young Master Hart, how could you do this to an elderly?" Wynne asked as he quickly helped his mother to get up.

Brian said coldly, "So what? I told you, no one can hit Valda in front of me."

"Young Master Hart, don't be charmed by her. She's a Jayx. Her parents would still be alive if it weren't for her! You can ask her if she killed her parents. She's a Jayx!"

Seeing that things had come to this, Wynne simply had a falling out with Valda Theller. Since Valda Theller was so stubborn, he would not give Valda Theller an easy time.

Brian felt Valda Theller trembling, and her already pale face was now paler.

Wynne ‘earnestly’ said, "Young Master Hart, she's a Jayx who will only bring you bad luck. Be careful! It's like how my dad got fired from his old job and could only get a strenuous job soon after she came to my house. My mom was usually in good health, but she fell seriously ill because of her. I didn't do well in the senior high school entrance examination and the national college entrance examination either—"

"So you think it's all her fault?" Brian interrupted.

"It... It's all because of her. We also had someone look into her four pillars of destiny. She's bad luck!" Rowena Santiago added.

"It's funny how you have to blame someone else for your own problems." Brian stared coldly at the mother and son pair. "Then I can tell you now that Valda's lucky. If you didn't have her four pillars of destiny protecting you, you would’ve ended miserably!"

Rowena Santiago and Wynne instantly shuddered as fear rose in their hearts.

They then realized that the man standing before them was the crown prince of the entertainment industry who was famous for his cruelty and indifference.

The crown prince had never been kind!

With panicked looks, the mother and son pair almost left in a hurry.

Brian looked down at Valda Theller and said, "Don't mind what they said."

Valda Theller looked down and pursed her lips for a long time before saying, "What if I do mind?"

'Yeah, how can I not mind? After my parents' death, I've been called a Jayx so often that I even doubt myself sometimes.'

"If you mind it, I'll find a way to make sure they can never criticize you again," said Brian.

Valda Theller stared blankly at Brian, not expecting him to say that.

"What's the matter? You don't think I can do it?" he asked as he raised his eyebrows.

She shook her head. "No, you can do it. It's just..." She took a deep breath and said after summoning her courage, "I don't care what they say about me, but I'll cause something bad to happen to you someday if I'm really a Jayx."

His eyes glimmered before he said indifferently, "I’ve never believed in superstitions, and neither should you."

"But—"

"Do you expect me to believe that and break up with you?" he interrupted her.
