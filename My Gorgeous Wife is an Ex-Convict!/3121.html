Valda noticed Amber's suspicion and whispered, "Actually, Brian's cooking is quite delicious, don't worry."

If it weren't for Valda liking this child, he would have definitely thrown away this unpleasant kid.

"When you guys went out earlier, I heard from Amber that Simon has come to Emerald City several times this year with George, and during the winter and summer breaks, Simon stays with Amber. At least George hasn't completely cut off their mother-son relationship."

"George is probably still hoping to get back together with Amber," Brian said. He didn't really like George's personality.

If you love someone, how could you not go see them even once, knowing that they have been suffering in a mental hospital for so many years?

If George had just taken a glance at Amber during those years, he would have understood the kind of suffering she had gone through. Perhaps the situation wouldn't have turned out like it is now.
