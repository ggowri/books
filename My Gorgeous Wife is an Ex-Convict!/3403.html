Chapter 3401

 

Looking at Grace's worried expression, Jason said, "Okay, don't overthink it. No news is good news, which means Lawrence is likely still alive."

Grace sighed, "Yes, Lawrence is very likely still alive."

I just don't know if Lawrence will ever be able to appear in front of them again in the future.

Time passes day by day, and the triplets' birthday is naturally a very important event for the Reed family. Jasper is wearing a beautiful white dress, looking like a fairy fallen from the mortal world.

At the age of 14, one can already be considered a young adult, and this person looks poised and graceful.

She has long black hair, curved willow leaf eyebrows, and a pair of almond-shaped eyes that look clear and bright. Her delicate face, swan-like neck, and the developing body of a young girl all make her appear to be transitioning from a girl to a young woman.

Jasper looked at herself in the mirror. Today was her birthday, and she should have been happy, but deep down, there was always a hint of regret.

How great it would be if Lawrence were here!

Before, she had told Lawrence that if it was her birthday, she wanted him to give her a gift!

But every year, he has never given her a birthday present.

The door of the room was knocked on, and the makeup artist who was doing Jasper's makeup went to open it.

Outside the door stood William.

At this moment, William walked in. When he saw Jasper, he was momentarily stunned and a bit dizzy.

In recent years, her facial features have gradually matured and she has become more beautiful.

In his eyes, she has always been beautiful, whether it was when she was a child or now.

Although they have been living in different cities for the past few years and haven't met often, they frequently video chat and he often sees her and her various expressions.

He thought he wouldn't be too surprised no matter how she dressed up tonight. But when he actually saw her, his heart couldn't help but contract violently for a moment, as if reminding him of something, and his gaze almost couldn't move away from her face.

"William, what's wrong?" Jasper asked as he approached.

William snapped out of his thoughts and said, "Nothing...just wanted to ask how you're doing with the preparations for the banquet."

"I'm all set. Let's go down together," Jasper said.

"Okay," William replied.

As they walked out of the room, William whispered in a voice only they could hear, "You look very beautiful tonight."

"Thank you," she smiled slightly.

But... because she's too beautiful, he feels uneasy.

He's afraid that too many people will come to like her, and he's afraid that he's not strong enough yet to have her!

Although there's only a few hours' drive between Emerald City and Deer Capital, he still feels it's too far away.

If... if they could be closer, if they could see each other every day...

That would be so great!

However, he also understands that the patience he's showing now is all for the future, so that he can be more worthy of her.

"Jasper, what do you think our future will be like?" William suddenly said.

"Our future?" Jasper was taken aback.

"Yes, our future." He stopped walking and turned to look at her.

At fourteen years old, William is already much taller than Jasper.
