Chapter 1497

Grace’s tears came streaming out. She had forgiven him! In fact, she had already forgiven him long ago!

As soon as his thumb pressed down on the device, the timer displayed the time and it started counting down.

Grace knew that this countdown was for an explosion. Right now, there was only two minutes left.

Did she only have two minutes left with Jay?

In these two minutes, Jay could still change his mind! Jay could leave!

“Terrence, get someone to pull Jay away!” Grace took a deep breath and ordered Terrence.

Terrence was stunned for a moment. If he pulled Master Reed away… it meant that the bombs buried in front of Grace’s car would explode immediately!

“Whoever dares to pull me away, I’ll have their whole family buried alongside me. I mean what I say. Whoever dares, come and try!” Jason coldly voiced out.

After what he said, the people around remained motionless out of fear, while Terrence was left in a difficult position!

“Terrence, you can leave. There’s no need to stay by my side,” Jason said and then moved his gaze toward Grace, “Grace, I’m doing this out of my own volition. I’m doing it willingly.”

Willingly? However, she did not want him to die!

Suddenly, an idea came to Grace’s mind. Actually, this problem could be solved. She had another choice. She looked at her hand that was tied to the handbrake and came to a decision.

“My babies, I’m sorry… Mommy is being selfish. “ Grace lowered her head and muttered, her gaze moving toward her bulging belly. “I want some company… but Mommy loves Daddy a lot. And I don’t want Daddy to die.”

Grace took a deep breath, her fingers clutching onto the handbrake tightly. She faced Jason and said, “Jay, it’s enough. What you’ve done for me, it’s enough!

Jason’s eyes widened as he realized something. He shouted loudly, “Grace, no! I don’t give you permission to do so, you hear me?!”

Grace’s hand had already pulled on the handbrake forcefully at that moment.

The next moment, the car went in reverse. More of the car was slowly suspended in the air with the car tilting backward.

“I forgive you, but it’s not because of our children.” Grace looked at the handsome yet pale face. On his face, there was terror, panic, and pain!

He was the man she loved the most!

“So, please keep living!”

The next moment, she could feel the whole car slipping downward. Through the front windscreen, she could see the azure sky.

She was selfish, so selfish to the point she was using the lives of three children in exchange for Jay’s.

However… Even though she knew it was selfish, she still did it! She had picked that man up from the streets, but after that, he gave her a home.

She knew that for a period in the future, he would be depressed.
