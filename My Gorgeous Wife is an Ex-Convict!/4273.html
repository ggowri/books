Chapter 4271 Seeking help from the Quentin family

Adele didn't say anything else because she understood that at this point, there was no way to salvage their already-ended marriage.

"Can Chandler really lure out the gu?" she asked, worried.

"I will go to the Quentin family again," Mr. Hunter, who was silent just now, suddenly spoke up.

"The Quentin family?" Colin was surprised. "But the Quentin family..."

Like the Hunter family, the Quentin family kept a low profile. However, due to Chandler's connection with Tina, the Hunter family gradually started to make appearances. On the other hand, the Quentin family remained even more mysterious.

The Quentin family excelled in medicine and possessed many secret techniques that were not widely known. Even in this era of advanced medicine, the Quentin family still held a special position among the Crypt families, indicating the extent of their medical prowess.

The relationship between the Quentin family and the Hunter family in the Crypt family was not good, but it wasn't bad either.

However, convincing the Quentin family to help Chandler lure out the gu wouldn't be an easy task. Even if the old Mr. Hunter personally intervened, given the Quentin family's eccentric temperament, they might not agree to help.

"What if the Quentin family refuses?" Colin expressed his concern.

"I will offer conditions that the Quentin family would be willing to accept," the old Mr. Hunter said.

Upon hearing this, Colin couldn't help but be taken aback. It seemed that obtaining the Quentin family's agreement would require a considerable price to be paid.

But for now, to save Chandler's life, they had no other choice.

"Then I will accompany you to the Quentin family," Colin said.

"No need, I can go by myself!" the old Mr. Hunter said.

At this point, whether Chandler could be saved might depend on the Quentin family.

Tina learned from her parents about the compensation terms proposed by the Hunter family for their divorce.

What surprised her was that her parents had agreed to all of them.

Lina explained, "Quinn will spend four months with the Hunter family when she grows up. I know that you won't bear to be separated from your daughter, but the conditions proposed by the Hunter family are not harsh! And..."

"Mom, I understand!" Tina said. "I just didn't expect that my divorce would cause such a loss for you!"

"It's just money," Hadwin said. "We're doing all this just to truly live happily."

At that moment, Tina didn't feel happy. When Chandler signed the divorce agreement, it felt like a piece of her heart was also empty!

It was already a month later when she saw Chandler again.

At the Hart family's banquet, Tina saw Chandler.

The Stephenson family, the Hunter family, and the Hart family all had a good relationship, as they were familiar with each other.

After Tina returned to Emerald City, the Hart family visited her several times. Despite losing her memory, Tina got along well with Harley and felt like she was a lovely sister.

Today was Harley's birthday, so the Hart family naturally invited relatives and friends to celebrate. It wasn’t strange for Tina to see Chandler here.

To her surprise, Chandler today looked thinner than the last time she saw him, with a paler complexion. He appeared somewhat sickly.

Wasn't he only tired and not seriously ill when he was hospitalized last time? So why did he look like this now?

Tina couldn’t help but wonder.
