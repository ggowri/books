After seeing Mick come out, Nancy put down her glass and stood up, feeling a bit uneasy. "You... have you finished washing up? Then let's... hic... go to bed..."

She murmured, feeling like she wasn't as nervous after drinking.

The effects of alcohol made Nancy lose her previous restraint, and she became more casual in her behavior.

Nancy began to pull at Mick's bathrobe, and one hand even slipped inside, touching his skin.

Mick raised an eyebrow. The last woman who tried to touch him had her hand broken by him.

Nancy's movements were extremely clumsy, but they didn't disgust him. In fact, the places she touched him made him feel a rush of blood.

Did his physical reaction mean that he had feelings for her?

Thinking this, Mick's eyes became deeper. "Why did you drink?" he asked.

"I was... afraid... afraid of being nervous... and not performing well..." she slurred.

If she were still sober, she would have used different words, but she overestimated her alcohol tolerance. Now that she was drunk, she spoke without thinking.

Fortunately, even though she was drunk, she still remembered her goal.

"Performing well?" Mick couldn't help but laugh. It was the first time a woman had said something like this to him. "Do you think your performance is good now?"

"I will... try to make you comfortable, so... so... don't dislike me..." As she finished speaking, she pulled open his bathrobe and kissed his chest.

Mick's body suddenly stiffened. This feeling was somewhat unfamiliar to him and made him a little uneasy, as if some kind of balance was about to be disrupted.

Kiss after kiss, she showered them on his chest, collarbone, and shoulders... as if igniting a fire within him.

As she kissed him, she kept murmuring, "Don't... don't dislike me... I'll do well, I'll... make you happy..."

Mick lowered his eyes and looked at the flushed and blurry-eyed woman. Even if she was drunk, did she still want him to like her?

Her words seemed to place herself in a humble position, as if she would do anything to gain his affection.

Was it because she really liked him, as she said, or was there something else?

After all, too many women desired him, not only as a person, but also for the Reed family behind him!

"Do you really want me to like you?" he asked in a low voice.

"Of course... I want you to like me... I really like you... hiccup... me!" She said, raising her head, wrapping her arms around his neck, and then pressing her lips hard against his.
