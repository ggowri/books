Chapter 1380

Kyla Corbyn gently lowered her eyes. "Since it’s like this, I don't regret meeting Martin Weiss in this life. Maybe it's fate... But I just hope I won't meet Martin Weiss again in my next life."

Grace pressed her lips together. Even if Kyla had accepted her fate, she would not accept it.

She must do something to keep Kyla alive!

...

Grace came to Reed Group after leaving the hospital.

The company's receptionist and security guards recognized her. After all, the image of Grace standing at the entrance to meet the president last time was still fresh in their minds.

They had thought the president would not want to meet with her last time, but in the end, the president asked Secretary Jackson to take her to his office.

Therefore, they were a little unsure whether to let her in this time.

Just as Grace wanted to call Jason to explain the situation, she saw Terrence passing by not far away. She immediately shouted, "Secretary Gao!"

After seeing Grace, Terrence immediately hurried forward and said, "Young Madam, why are you here?"

What he addressed her as immediately shocked the receptionist and the others who were following Terrence.

"I'm here to see Jay, but I forgot to call him and tell him before coming over," said Grace.

"Master Reed is probably in a meeting right now. Why don't I accompany you to Master Reed's office and you can wait for him there?" Terrence said respectfully.

Grace nodded. Then, Terrence said something to the people around him before going into the elevator with Grace. He even pressed the elevator button himself.

Although Terrence was only a secretary and not a senior executive in Reed Group, many senior executives were respectful to Secretary Jackson because he had been working with Master Reed for many years.

Secretary Jackson had never been so respectful to a woman before.

The way he had called her ‘Young Madam’ and the way she had called out ‘Jay’...

The rest of the crowd looked at each other. A possibility had occurred to them, and the disbelief on their faces became more obvious.

'Is this Jay... Master Reed? Secretary Jackson seemed to be taking that woman to Master Reed!'

"Well... Uh... Young Madam seems to be pregnant," someone said suddenly.

It suddenly occurred to everyone that although the woman's limbs were thin, her belly was indeed protruding. However, it was not protruding much.

'If that woman is pregnant, then the baby in her belly is... Master Reed's?

'If that's true, the news will set the whole city alight!'

Grace followed Terrence to the president's office, which she had been to several times before. As such, she did not need Terrence to accompany her.

"You can go deal with your matters now. I'll just wait here," said Grace.

"Okay, you can always look for the secretary outside if you need anything," Terrence said and left the room.

Grace was the only one left in the large office.

She looked around the office. Her identity and situation now were different from the last time she was here.

At that time, she had come here to beg him to help with Lina's hospital bills, and her heart had been full of despair.
