Chapter 4220

A worried expression appeared on Tina's face, "What about the placenta? What about your illness?"

Chandler felt strangely comforted in that instant, "You're worried about me?"

Tina was stunned, staring blankly at the person in front of her.

Yes, she was actually worried about him just now!

Was it because he said he was her husband, the father of her child?

But didn't she remember him at all? The current him was like a stranger to her.

"Are you worried about me?" his voice repeated again in her ear.

That voice carried a strong longing.

At this moment, a strange feeling suddenly surged in her heart, and for a moment, she couldn't say any denying answer.

"Yes, I am worried. After all, the placenta could have cured your illness." She spoke softly.

He suddenly smiled, a smile that rarely appeared on his expressionless face, and tears filled his deep eyes.

This was a side of him she had never seen before.

She felt unable to take her eyes off his face. He was smiling, but it seemed like he was about to cry.

She even felt like his tears were about to overflow in the next moment.

Tina couldn't help but raise her hand and stroke Chandler's eye corner.

He looked at her with a puzzled gaze.

She suddenly realized what she had done, "I just thought you were about to cry!"

"I wasn't crying," Chandler said softly, but his eyes were filled with tears.

"But you're now..."

"I'm just very happy," he said in a low voice, "because you're worried about me, so I'm happy!"

Happy?

Tina was momentarily stunned. What was there to be happy about?

"But now that the placenta is gone, what about your illness?"

"It's okay if the placenta is gone, then it's gone." As long as she is safe, it's enough for him!

When Tina fell asleep at night, her mind was still thinking about Chandler, and even his teary eyes kept appearing in her dreams. When she woke up, she even felt a bit dazed.

Why did she care so much?

Even seeing the tears in his eyes made her heart ache.

Another day passed, and Tina finally saw Kit.

They met in a hospital room.

Kit was lying in bed, while Tina was sitting in a wheelchair.

Tina knew that Kit's injuries were actually worse than her own.

She had heard the nurse say, in a semi-conscious state, that during the accident, Kit had used his body to shield her from part of the impact, protecting her.

Otherwise, she and the baby might not have survived!
