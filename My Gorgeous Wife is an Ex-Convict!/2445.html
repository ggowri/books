Just as the police were about to rush in, the screen that was used to announce queue numbers suddenly lit up. A video appeared on it.

She had put a mini spy camera on her collar. She was afraid that she might miss something when she saw Amber Mitchell again, so she recorded it. She did not expect it to become evidence one day.

Kyla yelled, "Here's my proof! You'll know how the Mitchell family tortured a normal person into becoming mentally ill after you watch this video!"

Kyla targeted the Mitchell family. She had to buy time. The most important thing now was to buy time.

Everyone's eyes were focused on the big screen while Louise Mitchell panicked. When was the video taken? Amber Mitchell seemed to only sit still and keep her head down. What proof kind of proof is that? What was going to happen next?

However, the more assertive Kyla was, the more uneasy Louise Mitchell's heart became.

"George..." Louise Mitchell called to her husband in a whisper but found George Gregory staring at the screen seemingly with all his attention.

Louise Mitchell gritted her teeth in hatred and said to the police, "This video is unnecessary. Captain Wang, how long are you going to stay here? If anything happens to my sister, I won’t..."

However, several people suddenly rushed over before she could finish speaking. They raised their cameras to film the scene.

The expressions of the people on Louise Mitchell's side changed, while Grace's looked calm because Kyla had already told them she would be inviting the press over when she came. Hence, they were all mentally prepared.

"What's with all these people? Get out. Kick them out!" Louise Mitchell instructed the Mitchell family's men, her uneasiness growing stronger.

Kyla lunged forward and said, "I invited these media! Since it's proof, more people should see it so they can judge whether Amber Mitchell should be given a mental evaluation!”
