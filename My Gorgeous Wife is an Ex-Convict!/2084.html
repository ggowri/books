Chapter 2082

Lina's face looked paler and paler.

Just then, the strange boy suddenly stretched out his hand and pressed his small palm on Lina's belly.

Kyla and Lina were startled. Kyla raised her hand to remove the little boy's hand from Lina's belly.

However, the middle-aged man standing behind the little boy suddenly said, "Don't touch him. You can't afford the consequences of touching Little Young Master."

Kyla froze. The man spoke so confidently. It was as if something bad... would happen if she removed the little boy's hand.

Just as she hesitated, Lina gasped and said, "Kyla... It... It's alright..."

She could feel the strong and rapid kicking of the baby slowing down and becoming normal after the boy put his hand on her belly.

Kyla did not touch the little boy when she saw that Lina was seemingly getting better.

The little boy withdrew his hand after a while. He looked up and stared at Lina. "What would it take for you to give her to me?"

Lina froze. She was still in a trance. When she saw the boy's fingers pointing to her belly, she realized the child was talking about the baby.

Although the little boy and the middle-aged man behind him looked rather peculiar, Lina thought they did not mean any harm.

Especially since her baby’s kicking calmed down a lot after the child touched her.

"This baby is my treasure, so I can't give them to you," Lina said with a slight smile.

"You can't?" The boy frowned. "But I want to be with her. I can give you anything you want."

Looking at the little one's seriousness, Lina could not help laughing. "The baby's still in my belly, and I don't even know if they're a boy or a girl. Yet you want to be with the baby?"

"It’s a girl!" the boy said with great certainty.

Lina looked surprised. She did not even know the gender of the baby, but the child was so sure that she was pregnant with a girl.

The child was too serious at making things up.

Kyla was also visibly surprised.

Lina then quipped, "If you want to be with my daughter, you can marry her after she's born!"

The little boy blinked his beautiful eyes. Then, he turned to the middle-aged man and asked, "If I marry her, can I be with her all the time?"

The ‘her’ referred to the baby in Lina's belly.

The man replied respectfully, "If a man and a woman reach the legal age and become husband and wife, they can stay together all the time."

"Alright!" The little boy looked up at Lina again. "I’ll marry her when she’s of legal age."

"Huh?" Lina was instantly dumbfounded, unable to keep up with the little boy's eccentric thinking.

The man standing next to the little boy looked as if everything his little young master said was right.

Just then, Grace had already driven over and said to Lina and Kyla, "Get in the car. We’ll go to the hospital."
