Chapter 4273 Eavesdropping in the Garden

Tina was shocked.

Was she looking at Chandler with the gaze of someone who likes him?

Like... that can't be!

Even if she had some liking for Chandler in her heart before, after knowing his true intentions in approaching her, she no longer likes him!

"You misunderstood, Maybe it's just because he was someone I liked before I lost my memory, but I don't remember anything from the past anymore," Tina said. "I don't want to be bound by past feelings."

Harley couldn't help feeling sorry for Chandler, "But in that case, isn't Chandler pitiful? He loves you so much."

Tina remained silent, does he love me? Unfortunately, Chandler's feelings for her are not pure love.

It's hard to say who is more pitiful between her and Chandler now.

Harley seemed to realize his rudeness and quickly said, "Tina, I'm sorry, I didn't mean to say you shouldn't get a divorce, I just..."

"I understand." Tina smiled faintly, "I'm going to take a breath in the back garden."

"Then I'll accompany you," Harley said.

"You are the protagonist of the party today, don't accompany me. I'll take a breath and come in later." Tina said, walking towards the back garden.

The meticulously maintained garden, although it was night, was not pitch black because of the many hanging lamps and lamp posts placed in the garden. Instead, it gave a different beauty than during the day.

Tina walked into the garden, the silence here was different from the hustle and bustle in the hall, which allowed her to relax a little.

Maybe because she and Chandler both attended this party today, she could always feel many gazes on her.

Even those people whom she might have been familiar with before.

But for her now, most of these people could be considered as strangers whom she had only met a few times.

Just as Tina was looking at the scenery in the garden, suddenly footsteps could be heard not far away.

Tina frowned, turned around, and hid in a dark corner nearby.

At this moment, she just wanted to be quiet, she didn't want to greet anyone, nor did she want to face the various strange gazes of others.

Originally, she thought that the person would quickly pass by, but when the footsteps got closer, they stopped.

Then a young man's voice rang out, "Have you really decided to have no more involvement with Tina?"

Tina's body suddenly stiffened, could it be that among the people who came, there was...

"Yes!" When the familiar voice sounded, Tina's body couldn't help but tremble.

It was Chandler!

"If she helps, the curse on your body can be easily resolved, but you actually plan to bypass this shortcut, it really makes people puzzled."

"I don't want to force her."

"So you got a divorce?" the other person sneered, "If it were the old Tina, after knowing your situation, I don't know if she would still divorce you."

"She wouldn't," Chandler said in a low voice.

The other person's voice carried a hint of surprise, "She wouldn't? Does the old Tina know about the curse?"

"Yes, she knows," Chandler said with a nostalgic smile on his lips. It was really good back then.

"If a person loses their memory, they lose their emotions as well. It seems like her feelings for you aren't really strong," the person continued.

"Lornell, don't say that. It's because I'm not good enough," Chandler said softly.
