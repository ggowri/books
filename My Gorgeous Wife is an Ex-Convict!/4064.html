The next day, Tina wore a high-necked shirt to school. Eugenia took one look and immediately gave her a sly look, "Did you really have sex with your Chandler brother last night?"

Tina gave her friend a direct glare, "Sometimes, don't assume things so easily. Also, you're a beautiful girl, and that kind of look doesn't suit you at all!"

Eugenia was puzzled, "Then why are you wearing a high-necked shirt? It's not even cold today."

"Is there a rule that says you can't wear a high-necked shirt when it's not cold?" Tina retorted.

Eugenia rubbed her nose, "Sister, it seems like you're not in a good mood. Why don't you tell me what's bothering you? Maybe I can give you some advice."

Tina thought it was a good idea and agreed.

Chandler's reaction yesterday kept her up all night.

She had been so proactive, but he just left without a word. Was it out of respect or because he saw her grow up and couldn't see her as anything other than a sister, despite her being engaged?

Or maybe he had always said he liked her, but didn't truly love her?

"What do you call it when a woman makes a move on a man, but he still leaves her alone in the room and walks away?" Tina asked.

Eugenia widened her eyes. "Are you talking about you and Chandler?"

"You know it, why do you have to say it so clearly?" Tina complained.

"I get it, I get it!" Eugenia quickly said. "Are you sure the woman made it clear enough?"

"Very clear!" Tina gritted her teeth. She had already made it clear enough. If he still didn't get it, she didn't know what else to do.

Eugenia thought for a moment and analyzed, "Either Chandler is old-fashioned and wants to wait until the wedding night, or he's just not interested in you, or..."

"Or what?" Tina perked up her ears.

"Or he likes men!" Eugenia said.

"Cough...cough!" Tina almost choked on her own saliva. "How is that possible?"

"I'm just guessing!" Eugenia said. "Or maybe he's just not into sex."

Tina remained silent, seemingly pondering her friend's words.

"By the way, aren't you supposed to be taking some COS photos this weekend? Can I come along? You have so many fans online, and I promised them some behind-the-scenes footage of you," Eugenia said.

"Okay," Tina didn't have any objections.

Taking photos of some anime characters in cosplay was just a small hobby of hers. At first, she thought it was interesting to dress up as her favorite anime characters with exaggerated costumes and makeup.

Unfortunately, many of the characters she liked were male, but luckily she was tall enough to pull off the male characters without looking awkward.

She just took some photos and found it fun, so she posted them on her page. Unexpectedly, they became popular.

Fortunately, no one dug up anything about her family background, or it could have been even more popular.

On the day of the shoot, Eugenia, who was supposed to follow and take some behind-the-scenes footage, disappeared while Tina was getting her makeup done. She hurried back just before the shoot.

"Tina, do you know what? Kit is shooting next door to you! Hurry up and take some photos. Maybe we can see him when he leaves!" Eugenia said.
