Chapter 790

The next moment, the cake in her hand had fallen to the ground.

Not far from the restaurant's door, a tall figure had stepped out of the car and was approaching her in the moonlight step by step.

His hair was still meticulously combed, and he was wearing a crisp suit. His cold and stern face gave people a sense of abstinence, while his deep and narrow eyes seemed to see through people.

When you were stared at by those eyes, others could not help but feel scared as if they were being stared at by a beast.

Now those eyes were upon her!

Kyla only felt that the blood vessels in her body seemed to have frozen suddenly, and the only thing left was the suffocating cold!

Martin Weiss!

Did the man finally appear in front of her?

Perhaps when she fled from the mall that day, she had a vague premonition that she might not escape him this time. He would eventually find her.

'I just didn't expect it to be... so soon!'

Everything had happened too quickly that she felt breathless.

"It’s been a long time." A cold voice rang in the night.

Her body could not help but shiver. 'It’s been a long time? Indeed, I haven’t seen him for a long time, except for a glance at his profile at the mall the other day.

'When was the last time before that? Right, when he appeared in court as a witness and testified against my 'crime'.'

He used his testimony to push her into the depths of hell.

He said, "Kyla, make amends for your sins!"

'Sins? What am I going to make amends for? I'm not guilty at all!' However, no matter how much she complained, he said she had ‘sinned’, so she needed to suffer for it.

'If I was really guilty, then my greatest sin was to have loved him!'

Now, the man who sent her to prison said that it had been a long time. Subconsciously, she just wanted to get away from the man.

'I want to get far away from him and never see him again!'

Kyla immediately walked to get past Martin Weiss.

However, only after two steps, her arm was gripped hard by a hand. "I haven't asked you to leave yet. Where are you going?"

Kyla stiffened and turned to meet Martin Weiss's cold eyes. "Mr. Weiss, you don't seem to have the power to decide whether I can leave or not."

He stared at her, and the air around them seemed to be oppressive. "I haven't seen you in a few years, but you've become quite eloquent. Looks like you had good food in prison."

It was a light sentence, but it made Kyla feel like a sharp weapon had thrust into her heart.

'Good food?

'Does he know how hard it was for me to get a proper meal in prison?

'Does he know that when I was pregnant with Nelson, I was afraid that I didn't have enough to eat and the baby in my tummy would be malnourished, so I did many humiliating things in return for other people's leftovers?

'Does he know that after I gave birth to Nelson, I was so hungry that I couldn't even produce milk, so I could only let Nelson drink the crudest rice soup…

'No, he doesn't know any of this!'
