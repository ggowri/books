Chapter 3594

 

"Huh?" Jasper was taken aback and glanced at William standing beside him from the corner of his eye. Then he replied somewhat awkwardly, "No...not yet."

"Then consider how about our William?"

"Ahem..." Jasper almost choked on her own saliva.

"My old bones, I don't know how much longer they can hold up. What I fear most is that if I close my eyes one day, our William won't even have a girlfriend," the old man sighed.

Jasper quickly said, "How could that be? William will definitely have a girlfriend!"

"Really?" The old Mr. Barlow's eyes lit up.

Jasper suddenly realized that her response just now sounded like she was saying she would be William's girlfriend, as if she was talking to her own grandfather.

However, facing the old man's hopeful gaze, she could only force herself to say, "Really."

"That's good, that's good. Then, when William has children, I can also close my eyes," the old man said again.

Boom!

Jasper's face turned completely red.

William quickly said, "Grandpa, that's enough, let's not talk about this anymore."

"Okay, I won't say anything then," the old man said, giving his grandson a look that said "I'm helping you out." This left William feeling both amused and exasperated.

After all, the old man was still sick and soon became tired, falling asleep again.

Jasper and William left the hospital.

"Don't worry about what my grandfather said earlier," William said.

"Um...about what I said to my grandfather just now...," she bit her lip, feeling a bit uneasy as she considered what words to use.

"I know you're only saying this for your grandfather's sake, and I won't regret anything because of it. I'll continue to wait for you to think it over and give me an answer, and you don't need to rush to respond," said William.

What he wanted wasn't for her to reluctantly be with him due to a moment of embarrassment, but for a lifetime together!

Therefore, he didn't mind waiting!

His words made her breathe a sigh of relief. He seemed to always be like this, not putting pressure on her.

"William, do you know? You're a lot like a spring breeze," Jasper said.

"Spring breeze?" He raised his eyebrows slightly.

"Yes, it's warm and gentle, it doesn't give people pressure. Being with you gives a very comfortable feeling." She said.

He chuckled, probably only she would feel this way.

His interactions with other people were not like this.
