Chapter 2560

"But..." Grace almost blurted out about Martin's liver donation.

However, she held back the urge.

Kyla was still recovering and Trista was still in the incubator. Not wanting Kyla to have mood swings, Grace decided that she would tell her the truth later.

"No buts. I'm afraid you're about to suffer again in this pregnancy," said Kyla.

Grace said, "My suffering is nothing compared to yours. After all, the threat to my life is minimal. But I'm willing to suffer for the baby."

"Yeah, for the baby..." Kyla said emotionally.

Maybe only mothers could understand their love for their children.

"Just like Amber… Simon is her child and is currently sick. It seems that only Amber's blood can save the child. I think she’ll do anything to save him."

Amber wasn’t around when her child grew up, but she was his mother, which was something no one could change.

Grace nodded in agreement at that. Back when she knew the truth from Amber when they were on the phone, Grace was stunned for quite a while.

However, what surprised her more was that Amber had chosen to marry Herman.

After all, Herman died so many years ago, yet Amber chose to become his widow.

As Grace thought of it, she couldn’t help but wonder if Amber would ever be happy or dwell on the past for the rest of her life.

When Grace left the hospital and returned to Reed Residence, she saw Jason lulling their two children to sleep in the room.

Mick and Jasper were lying side by side. They were covered with a thin blanket, and they hugged each other as they fell asleep. It was a beautiful sight.

"You're back," said Jason as he sat up and looked at Grace.

"Yeah." She nodded. "I stopped by the hospital with Kyla to see Trista when I went to see her today."

"How's Trista?" Jason asked.

Grace said, "She's doing okay. She can be discharged in two weeks if everything goes well. Speaking of which, I almost told Kyla that Martin donated his liver to her, but I was afraid she would get intense mood swings. I thought I'd wait until Trista is discharged."

Jason said, "Suit yourself. You can decide how you want to handle it. Did you throw up when you were outside today?"

"I feel better now," Grace replied as she looked down at her belly. "Maybe the baby feels bad for me and wants me to throw up less."

Sorry flickered in Jason's eyes.

He thought to himself, If the pregnancy threatens your life, I won’t hesitate to get rid of the baby just to save you, Grace. Even if you resent me and hate me for it, I’ll still do it! I don’t want to lose you, Grace...

"If this baby feels bad for you, they should keep you safe," said Jason.

Grace burst into laughter. "Alright, it's not that dangerous. Besides, I have the most authoritative obstetricians and gynecologists in Emerald City to take care of me. I'm not so worried now. As for Amber..."
