Chapter 2593

Mallory Malcom entered the mansion and met Martin Weiss, who was sitting on the couch going through the company's documents.

"Chairman Weiss, I just met your wife outside the mansion. Mrs. Weiss would like to meet you," said Mallory Malcom.

Martin Weiss's hand holding the pen paused instantly.

She still came!

She came even though he had made it clear on the phone with the lawyer that he was not going to see her.

He had deliberately stopped himself from thinking about her and removed her from his mind. However, her voice and face kept recurring to him whenever he calmed down.

Everything that ever happened in the past flashed past his mind one by one.

'I was hypnotized! I've forgotten this relationship. Why does this woman still dominate my mind?'

"Chairman Weiss?" Mallory Malcom could not help asking after Martin Weiss's long silence.

"Since she wants to see me, I'll see her. Take her to the living room. I'll go downstairs to see her," said Martin Weiss,

"Yes, I'll bring Mrs. Weiss in," Mallory Malcom said quickly.

Although others did not know what Chairman Weiss's attitude was toward Mrs. Weiss, he knew that Chairman Weiss cared about Mrs. Weiss even though he seemed indifferent.

Otherwise, why would he instruct him to help make arrangements for Mrs. Weiss when she came to L City back then? He even went to the hotel often to see Mrs. Weiss and spent some time with Mrs. Weiss in Emerald City.

As far as he knew, Chairman Weiss had donated his liver to Mrs. Weiss in Emerald City! As a result, he was now in poor health and had to recuperate in the mansion.

Not many men would sacrifice their health for a woman. Even old married couples might not be able to do that.

Mallory Malcom arrived at the entrance and took Kyla inside.

Once they reached the living room, Mallory Malcom said to Kyla, "Mrs. Weiss, wait here for a moment. Chairman Weiss will be downstairs soon." With that said, he dismissed the servants and left.

It was suddenly empty, and Kyla became nervous.

She was not nervous on the way in or when security stopped her at the mansion’s entrance. Earlier, all she could think about was seeing him. She wanted to see how he was doing. She had so much to say to him.

However, she suddenly became nervous as she did not expect to see him soon.

Footsteps suddenly rang out.

Kyla stiffened instantly as she looked toward the stairs, where a slender figure descended the stairs step by step.

It was... Martin Weiss!

Kyla looked blankly at the man who stepped down the stairs.

He was dressed in a suit and tie with his black hair combed neatly. His graceful step was not as slow as she imagined. He seemed to look the same as before.

However, when he came down the stairs and walked slowly toward her, she noticed that he had lost weight and his face was a little pale. Even the suit seemed baggy on him.

For a moment, Kyla only stared at Martin Weiss until his voice sounded. "Did you come from Emerald City to find me just to stare at me like this?"
