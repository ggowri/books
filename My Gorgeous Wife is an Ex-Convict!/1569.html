Chapter 1567

After closing the web page, Lina sent a message to her blind date that night. The most important thing now was for her to go on blind dates!

Maybe she could find someone she liked who could also reciprocate her feelings.

At night, Lina went to the plaza in tMason Swansonty center at the appointed time and met Anthony Sherman, her blind date today. He was said to be the department head of a big company.

After Lina saw him in person, she could not help exclaiming to herself, 'The blind date Mom set up for me this time... is a little too good.'

Anthony Sherman was dressed in a suit and tie. He wore gold-rimmed glasses. He looked well-mannered and was indeed good-looking. Her mother said he was two years older than her. He was 35 but looked like he was in his early 30s.

"Hello, you must be Miss Sweeney. Nice to meet you," he said.

"Hello," Lina said with a smile.

After exchanging some pleasantries, they reached a restaurant.

Lina originally just wanted to have a meal and chat at a random restaurant, but the restaurant Anthony Sherman took her to was not cheap. It was a restaurant with a good dining environment. Of course, the prices here were not cheap either.

"Miss Sweeney, you can order whatever you want," he said politely.

Lina thought about it and ordered some relatively cheap dishes. Anthony Sherman's thoughtful eyes flickered for a moment, but he did not say anything and ordered a few more dishes.

"Miss Sweeney, why did you agree to go on blind dates? Do you think you have to marry someone because it's time?" asked Anthony Sherman.

"It's not that I have to marry someone. After all, it's not like you can't live without marriage, but... I don’t want my family to worry and think there’s something wrong with me. Won’t it be nice if I actually meet the right person on a blind date?" said Lina.

Anthony Sherman muttered, "Really? Then we're in the same boat. I don't want my family to worry too."

"That's good. We're really similar," said Lina.

Anthony Sherman smiled.

They had a good chat during the meal. Although they were in different fields, they could find common topics to talk about.

For example, the two of them had similar tastes in TV shows. They enjoyed those cheesy romance dramas. In particular, they had been watching the same TV show recently.

"Do you think... it's strange for a man to enjoy watching these romance dramas?" Anthony Sherman asked, sounding as though he was a little embarrassed about it.

"What's so strange about that? Many romance dramas are written by men," said Lina.

However, she was a little surprised after learning that Anthony Sherman never had an official girlfriend. After all, this man was not bad-looking. He had a good job, a house, and a car. It surprised her that he was her blind date today.

'He’s never had a girlfriend. Are all the women around him... blind?'

"Are you sure you’ve never had a girlfriend?" she asked.

"Yes," he answered.

"Is it because you have high standards?"
