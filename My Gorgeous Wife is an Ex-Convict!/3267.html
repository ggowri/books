Chapter 3265

"You're pregnant, why didn't you tell me? Were you planning to keep it a secret if I didn't come to your studio?" Camille said angrily to Valda in her studio.

"It's not like I was intentionally keeping it from you. It's only been a little over a week since I found out, and because it's still early in the pregnancy and the fetus is not stable yet, I wanted to wait until it was more stable before telling anyone," Valda explained.

Upon hearing this, Camille's expression immediately turned nervous. "How is the fetus now? Is it stable?"

"For now, it's okay. The blood test results have doubled, but we still need to check every week," Valda replied.

"That's good," Camille breathed a sigh of relief. "But now that you're pregnant, it's best to reduce your workload so you don't overwork yourself."

She knew how much her friend wanted this child, so now that she was pregnant, it was natural to protect her and the baby.

Valda also nodded. "I will try to finish the comic I'm currently serializing before I give birth, and after that, I plan to take a year off."

"Sure," Camille readily agreed. "I'll go back and see how I can adjust your current serialization process for you."

After finishing their work conversation, they started talking about the baby in Valda's belly.

"You're pregnant, how is Brian reacting?" Camille asked curiously.

"Be careful, and he talks a lot more now than before. I didn't know before that he could be so chatty," Valda chuckled.

After all, in her memory, he had always been a man of few words. However, after they started dating and their relationship improved, he did speak more, but not excessively.

And until now, she only just found out that he too can be a bit of a complainer.

Camille chuckled at the comment, "I'm actually looking forward to seeing what Brian will be like as a dad in the future."

Suddenly, Valda imagined Brian holding a baby... Hmm, it seems like she's starting to look forward to it too!

Although Valda had originally planned to wait until after the first three months had passed before telling her godmother and in-laws about her pregnancy, Brian took care of Valda's diet and daily life, and even had the butler hire a chef to make special dishes for pregnant women. He also accompanied Valda to prenatal check-ups and even placed some pregnancy-related books on the office desk.

How could the old Mr. Hart and the old Mrs. Hart not have guessed it!

So he went straight to his son's office, closed the door, and began to interrogate him.

"Is Valda pregnant?" Mrs. Hart asked directly without beating around the bush.

"Okay," Brian replied readily.

Mr. and Mrs. Hart, who are elderly, exchanged a joyful glance with each other, both expressing happiness in their eyes.

Valda is pregnant, which means they will have a grandchild! They have been envious of their old friends who already have a third generation, so this is exciting news for them.

Although they didn't pressure their son to have a baby after getting married, they have always been longing for one deep down. And now... they finally see a glimmer of hope!

"How long has it been?" asked Mrs. Hart again.

"It's almost been 6 weeks," said Brian.

"6 weeks? How is the fetus doing now?" asked Mrs. Hart anxiously, while Mr. Hart also looked on nervously.

Brian, on the other hand, calmly said, "Everything is fine."
