Chapter 4170 Buried for a Lifetime

When did she become important to him? It seemed that originally, she was just a fan to him, a fan who was slightly different from the others!

But gradually, thoughts of her started occupying his mind more and more.

He couldn't help but want to see her, and even... touch her.

Even though he knew she already had Chandler by her side, he still couldn't help but wonder... if he had met her earlier, would he have had a chance?

Tina didn't expect Kit to call her.

"Can we meet?" Kit said softly over the phone.

Tina thought for a moment and replied, "Okay, where should we meet?"

The next day, Tina arrived at the meeting place she had agreed upon with Kit.

It was a secluded scenic area in the outskirts. Because it was a weekday, there weren't many people around, and it was very tranquil.

"There shouldn't be anyone to bother us here, and we don't have to worry about being photographed by reporters," Kit said to Tina. "Thank you for agreeing to meet me."

"Why did you ask me to meet?" Tina asked.

Her unfamiliar tone made Kit's face darken. "Do you hate me now?" he asked.

Tina looked at him but didn't say anything.

"I'm sorry for my impulsiveness last time. It was my dirty thoughts that influenced how I saw you," he apologized in a hoarse voice.

Her gaze made him feel particularly uncomfortable. It felt like something was blocking his chest, making it hard for him to breathe.

Tina looked at the person in front of her. While she was angry when she misunderstood him at first, it seemed like she didn't care anymore.

"It's okay, I've already let it go," Tina said calmly.

Kit pursed his lips awkwardly, "Will you stop liking me from now on?"

Tina was taken aback, staring directly at Kit.

Kit said dryly, "I only meant... am I no longer your idol?"

"I don't know," Tina replied truthfully, feeling a bit complicated about her feelings for Kit since that incident.

On one hand, she was angry that her good intentions were so misunderstood by Kit, feeling like a joke.

But on the other hand, she also pitied him.

Kit forced a bitter smile, "Seems like I'm really foolish." Foolish enough to push her away with such actions.

"Anyway, I've let it go and I hope you have a successful career in the future," Tina said. "I might still go and see the movies or concerts you act in."

After saying that, Tina turned around to leave.

"Wait!" Kit called out. "Besides wanting to apologize, I asked you out today to ask if you're really getting married to Chandler."

"Yes, the wedding will be held in about a month," Tina said. Although it was a short timeframe, it was not a problem for families like the Stephenson family and the Hunter family, who were from prominent backgrounds.

Even with just a month, they could have a grand wedding.

"Do you... love him?" Kit asked.

"Very much," Tina answered with a smile. When it came to Chandler, her previous distance seemed to have turned into sweetness.

A complex look flashed in Kit's eyes. It turned out, he really had run out of time!

That love, which couldn't be expressed in time, might just have to be buried in his heart forever!
