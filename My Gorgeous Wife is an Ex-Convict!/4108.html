Chapter 4106

Instead of going back to the Stephenson family, Tina went straight to Chandler's place.

Chandler hadn't returned yet, but she could unlock the door with her fingerprint and come and go freely.

Tina sat on the sofa in the living room, waiting for Chandler while thinking about what Weldon had said to her today.

A child...

If the placenta of a child could really be used as medicine to completely get rid of the worms in Chandler's body, should she have a child earlier?

Although she originally wanted to be mentally prepared enough to become a mother before having a child.

But if it was for the sake of solving Chandler's health problems, then it would be good to have a child earlier.

Anyway, it would be her and Chandler's child, and she would love this child very much!

"Tina? Why are you here?" Chandler's voice suddenly sounded in the living room.

Tina suddenly snapped out of it and realized that Chandler had already come back.

"I missed you. I'm staying at your place tonight," Tina said.

"Your parents will worry," Chandler said. "Let's have dinner at my place and I'll take you back later."

"No, I want to stay at your place," Tina pouted. "And I've already talked to my parents about it."

Chandler looked at her indulgently. "Alright, it's up to you. What do you want to eat tonight?"

"Anything is fine!" Tina said.

The dinner was cooked by Chandler himself.

Tina was also very impressed with Chandler's cooking skills.

Hmm, her fiancé was simply a jack of all trades, and he was like a fierce dragon in bed...

Okay, she got a little carried away! Tina's face turned slightly red.

"Why aren't you eating? Not hungry?" Chandler asked because she had been sitting there without eating much.

"No...it's delicious! I'm just thinking about something," Tina hesitated and tentatively said, "I ran into Weldon today and he gave me the final photos from our last shoot."

Chandler frowned, seeming to dislike her meeting with Weldon.

"You don't need to pay attention to him," Chandler said.

"But the favor he asked me for was to give him a ring with a red and blue gemstone if I ever saw one at the Hunter family's place. He also said that the ring wasn't worth much, he just wanted it as a keepsake," Tina said. "Have you seen this ring before? Does it have any special meaning?"

To Chandler's surprise, his face changed when he heard this. "Did he say anything else?" he asked.

Tina pursed her lips and replied truthfully, "He also mentioned the issue of the gu worms and why I have to have a child."

Chandler's face turned pale and his eyes were fixed on Tina. "What did he say exactly?" he asked, his voice trembling.

At that moment, Tina felt like his voice was shaking.

"He said that I have to have a child and that the placenta can be used as medicine to completely get rid of the gu worms in your body," Tina said.

Chandler's face had turned gray and defeated.

She now knew all of this.

He had planned to explain everything to her when the time was right.

But now... everything had happened so suddenly.
