Chapter 585

Through this glass wall, one could see the hubbub in the center of the hall outside. There were singing and dancing performances by various resident singers.

Compared with the noise outside, the private room was strangely quiet.

Brian poured himself a cup of tea, sipped on it, and stared coldly at the uproar outside that was alien to him.

The door was pushed open, and the sound of footsteps rang in the private room.

Brian turned his head slightly and looked at the visitor. "You're here. Would you like a cup of tea?"

"No thanks." Jason walked to a chair opposite Brian and sat down. "I came to tell you a few simple words. I'll leave right after."

Brian raised his eyebrows slightly. "Really? What do you want to tell me?"

"It's your business to break up with Evelyn, but don't get Grace involved. I have limited patience and won't let Grace be targeted again and again." Jason warned.

Brian's eyes glinted. "What do you mean?"

"Today, Evelyn's parents went to Grace and said you broke up with Evelyn because of Grace. Is the reason Evelyn's parents think so because of Evelyn or you?" said Jason.

Brian gently twirled the teacup in his hands. The fragrance of tea diffused through his nose along with the heat and gradually filled the whole room. "Does it matter?"

"Since I'm here to see you, of course, it's important," answered Jason.

Brian lowered his eyelids and was silent. He did not immediately answer Jason.

Jason's voice once again rang in the private room. "Does your breakup with Evelyn have anything to do with Grace?"

Brian's hands trembled almost unnoticeably. It was a subtle gesture, but Jason noticed it.

Jason's eyes darkened slowly, and an almost stagnant vibe pervaded the private room.

After a long time, Brian slowly raised his phoenix eyes and met Jason's. "What if I say it does matter?"

Instantly, Jason's eyes looked like they were covered with ice. "Do you want to die?"

"She seems to mean a great deal to you. However, if I really want her, then you can't stop me even if you're Jason," Brian said, looking into his eyes.

Immediately, there was tension in the air.

"You can't afford her." There was an air of danger around Jason.

Brian emptied his cup of tea. "She's not the one I'm looking for. Otherwise, there's no way I'll let go even if I have to fight you. Therefore, even if I've broken up with Evelyn, I'm not going to do anything about Grace."

"Well then, I don't want Grace to get into any more trouble because of you." Jason finished talking, stood up, and walked straight out of the private room.

He had the answer he wanted tonight.

Maybe he should be glad that Brian did not know that Grace was the person he was looking for. Even so, Brian was genuinely tempted by Grace.

If Brian had not been too obsessed with the person he was looking for, he would have...
