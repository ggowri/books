Chapter 3605

Jasper raised his hand and hugged William back.

"I'm here," she said softly. "William, no matter what happens, I will always be by your side. So, please don't turn off your phone without saying anything anymore. Don't make me unable to find you. It makes me anxious!"

Upon hearing this, he held her even tighter. After a while, he slowly uttered a word, "Okay!"

... After they came down from the cemetery, Jasper said to William, "I'm hungry."

"Then let's find a nearby place to eat first," William said.

Because they were in the vicinity of the cemetery and it was already late, there were limited dining options nearby. Eventually, the two of them found a small, shabbily decorated shop.

"If you don't want to eat here, we can drive a bit further and probably find more places," William suggested.

"No, this place is fine!" Jasper said.

After they both sat down, they placed their orders.

William took out his previously turned-off phone, turned it back on, and saw several missed calls and messages.

And just from Jasper's phone calls alone, there have been 5!

"You made 5 phone calls?" he looked at her.

"I was worried about you. You suddenly turned off your phone, and I couldn't find you anymore," she said.

"Then how did you find this place later?" he asked.

"First, I checked the school's surveillance footage and found that you left the campus. Then I asked Terrence to help me check the road surveillance, and finally found out that you came to the cemetery," she said.

A hint of apology flashed in his eyes, "I'm sorry for making you go through all that trouble."

"So, next time you can't just turn off your phone like that!" she said.

"I won't do it again in the future," he replied.

Just then, his phone rang and he glanced at the caller ID, slightly furrowing his brows.

"Dad, what's up?" he said.

On the other end of the phone, Philip said, "Are you okay?"

"Did you find out?" William asked.

"You're my son, of course I would know about your situation," Philip replied.

Although the father and son didn't explicitly mention what the specific situation was, they both understood what they were referring to.

"Do you want me to help?" Philip offered.

"No, I can handle it myself." If he couldn't handle even this, how could he take over the Barlow family's business in the future?

His identity couldn't change, and the fact that Lily was his mother couldn't change either.

If he wanted to take charge of the Barlow family's enterprise in the future, he would only face more scrutiny!

"Okay, then you handle it yourself, but don't forget, the Barlow family is your support!" Philip said.

William's nose tingled slightly, "I know!"

He was grateful that Philip was his father. Although there weren't many intimate gestures between them, he knew that his father and the Barlow family were truly good to him.

After ending the call, he looked up and saw Jasper's bright eyes looking at him.

"What's wrong? Why are you looking at me like that?"

"When you were on the phone with Uncle Barlow, it seemed like you were about to cry," she said, "Your nose is a bit red."

"You're mistaken," he said, feeling uneasy.

She rested her chin on her hand and said, "If you really want to cry, you can cry on my shoulder. My shoulder will always be there for you."
