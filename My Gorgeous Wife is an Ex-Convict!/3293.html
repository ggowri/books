Chapter 3291

After the movie ended, the group met the creators of the film up close, including Kering.

Kering greeted Brian and Valda with a smile, "Mr. Hart, Mrs. Hart, hello. I didn't expect you to really come."

Valda smiled and then introduced her group of people.

Grace, Kyla, and Amber were okay and not overly enthusiastic, but Lina was different. Her eyes were shining with an incredible passion, as if she was meeting her idol as a fan.

Of course, Kering is now one of Lina's idols, although... um, she actually has quite a few idols.

When Hadwin saw his wife's reaction, he felt frustrated because he knew that she had probably forgotten all the promises she made to him earlier upon seeing Kering.

As a dedicated fan, Lina brought Kering's photo album and posters for him to sign. She expressed her intention to keep them safe, which surprised Kering.

However, when Lina expressed her desire to take a group photo and give a hug, Hadwin finally pulled his wife aside and said to everyone, "Sorry, Lina and I have something to attend to, we'll be leaving now."

And everyone was used to this already, after all, only Lina could make Hadwin jealous like this.

They've seen this kind of trick before.

"Oh, I'm fine," Lina struggled to say.

"You have something to say," said Hadwin with certainty.

"That...at least let me take a photo with Kering first, I haven't taken one yet...Hadwin..." Her voice trailed off, indicating that Lina's wish to take a photo was temporarily unfulfilled.

Everyone recognized each other and smiled.

Brian bent down and whispered in Valda's ear, "It's a good thing you didn't try to hug Kering, otherwise I might have taken you away too. Valda, the only man you can hold onto is me."

Valda's face blushed slightly again. His words seemed to imply something.

Right at that moment, Grace's phone rang. But after answering the call, her expression suddenly changed. "Okay, I understand. I'll be back soon."

Everyone was confused. Kyla asked, "Grace, what's wrong?"

"That's the kid who learned to play the piano with Jasper. His mother has some issues, so I need to go back and take care of it first," Grace said, and then hurriedly left.

"Is it the boy named Lawrence?" Valda asked. Sometimes when Valda talked with Grace, Grace would mention that boy.
