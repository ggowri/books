Chapter 3332

 

Finally, Valda took Harley.

"Harley, that's a nice name," said Hadwin. "I heard Valda had a smooth delivery this time. Among the few of us, your wife had the smoothest delivery, didn't she?"

Is it smooth? Brian imagined the scene of Valda giving birth and broke out in a cold sweat for the rest of his life. At that moment, he truly understood what it meant to be powerless.

No matter how much money or power one has, it cannot replace the suffering of losing the person they love the most.

"Brian murmured, 'I feel like giving birth is really painful for women.'"

"Yeah, but there's no way to replace it," Hadwin said with a heavy heart. "It's just that it hurts so much, and yet some people still want to have a second child. With so many children, what's the point?"

As the man with the most children present, Jason listened and smiled, "I think it's great, more children bring more blessings."

"Is that so? You seem to do well when competing for attention with your children at home," pointed out Hadwin without hesitation.

Okay, favoritism is indeed a problem. Even Jason couldn't find the words to say for a moment.

Just then, Grace and Lina brought the baby out, and Jason and Hadwin finally saw the baby.

"Come and take a look, the child is so cute!" Lina said, "So adorable! I really want to have one myself just by looking at it."

Hadwin gave Lina a look, "Don't even think about it. If you have another one, you'll be an elderly mother."

Lina was speechless, "I'm just thinking about it, I don't really want to have one." She also knew that she was getting older now, and having a child was not that easy.

Jason looked at the baby, who was chubby and strong. Compared to his own daughter when she was born, the baby looked much better.

Thinking of his own daughter being born prematurely, Jason's eyes dimmed. His daughter had stayed in an incubator for a long time before slowly recovering.

However, even so, her physical condition was still weaker than that of ordinary children, and she was more prone to illness. Even a small cold was more likely to develop into pneumonia than other children.

Feeling Jason's change of mood at the moment, Grace raised her hand and gently pulled her husband's arm.

Jason turned his head and met Grace's caring gaze. He smiled slightly.

After being married for many years, even if they don't say anything, they understand each other.

"After Valda," Jason said after a moment, looking at the newborn Harley.

"How did you figure that out?" Grace asked curiously.

"With more children, you naturally start to see the resemblance," Jason replied.

"..." Grace suddenly didn't know what to say. Why didn't she see it?

"But since the baby's eyes aren't open, it's unclear whose eyes they resemble," Jason added.

Brian's face lit up when he heard that the baby looked like Valda.

After Jason and the others left, Brian turned to Valda and said, "Jason said the baby looks like you."

"Like me?" Valda was a little surprised.

"Yeah, he said the baby's bone structure looks more like yours," Brian said. "We just don't know whose eyes the baby has. It would be great if they looked like yours too."

Valda chuckled, "Don't you think it would be a shame if your beautiful eyes didn't get passed down to your children?"

"But me, I prefer looking into your eyes," said Brian.

Valda felt a wave of sweetness in her heart. Although she thought her eyes were just ordinary, well... this is probably what they mean by "beauty is in the eye of the beholder".

"If everyone was like me, then where would your genetic traits be displayed?" Valda said.

"Blood type, the child's blood type, is the same as mine," said Brian.

Valda chuckled and then lowered her head, looking at the little baby in her arms. "I hope our Harley can open his eyes soon!"

She really wants to see what the baby looks like with its eyes open, and she also wants the baby to see her as its mommy as soon as possible.
