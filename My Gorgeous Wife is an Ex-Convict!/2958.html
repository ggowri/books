Chapter 2956

Zelda sounded like she was speaking up for Valda, but in reality, she was belittling her.

"You mean what she’s wearing is fake?" Brian asked and narrowed his eyes.

"Yes, she's wearing the knockoff of Chanel's exclusive spring collection. The price tag is 2.8 million dollars. How could she possibly afford it?" Zelda quickly added, hoping to embarrass Valda.

With a sneer, Brian said, "I asked my assistant to buy it for her. If it were a knockoff just as you mentioned, I should sue Chanel for selling knockoffs."

Zelda was stunned when she realized what Valda was wearing wasn’t knockoff at all. Still, she couldn’t believe it.

Brian turned his head at this moment and said to the waiter next to him, "Please call your manager over."

"Okay, sure," the waiter replied, knowing that Brian should be some rich dude.

After a while, the manager came over. As soon as he saw Brian, his face changed and he hurriedly greeted him, "Mr. Hart, what can I do for you?"

"Well, I'm having dinner with my girlfriend and I don't want it to be too noisy here. Can you please ask those two people to leave?" Brian said calmly.

Upon hearing this, the manager immediately put on a serious face and said to Zelda and Ginger, "I’m sorry but we can no longer serve you here. Please leave."

Zelda was surprised while Ginger said unhappily, "What do you mean by that? I am a VIP here. How dare you ask us to leave? Where’s your boss? I need to speak to them!"

"Even if I ask my boss over, they will still ask you to leave!" The manager said bluntly, "Both of you, please leave immediately."

Hearing the argument, the surrounding guests all looked over in this direction.

Zelda and Ginger refused to leave, so the manager called a few employees to kick them out.

And they attracted even more attention from bystanders when they were forced to leave the restaurant.

Irritated, Zelda couldn’t believe she was so humiliated in front of Valda.

She snapped, "I...I'm going to sue you! The restaurant will go out of business for good. Just wait and see!"

"Okay, go ahead, no one is stopping you," said the manager.

After speaking, he turned around and led the staff back into the restaurant.

Standing at the entrance, Zelda and Ginger looked at each other in confusion.

"Who exactly is Valda?" Ginger asked after finally coming back to his senses.

"Valda... she's just an ordinary person!" Zelda answered.

"But how could an ordinary person like her afford a 2.8 million dollar outfit? You don't even know what kind of friends she has, and you spoke so recklessly. Ugh! You’re so stupid!" Ginger said angrily, then turned and left.

Meanwhile, he felt lucky that he didn’t bump into anyone he knew at the restaurant. Otherwise, he would’ve made a bigger fool of himself.

Angry and sad, Zelda also wanted to know who exactly was Brian. She still found it hard to digest the fact that Valda met someone so rich and generous.

Thinking of this, she became more jealous.
