Chapter 2951

Valda stared blankly at Brian's face, which was so close to her. And the more she looked at it, the more mesmerized she was.

She couldn't help but exclaim secretly.

Gosh! You're so damn good-looking, Brian! I mean, look at your skin! It's so smooth. And your eyelashes are so long and thick.

At the thought, Valda cautiously touched his eyelashes with her fingertips, feeling the dense lashes brushing against them.

Suddenly, Brian opened his eyes and stared straight at her.

Valda instantly stiffened as if she was caught red-handed.

"I...I just wanted to touch your eyelashes...I think they're...um, really nice." She awkwardly withdrew her hand with an embarrassed expression.

His eyelashes trembled slightly as he asked, "Do you like my eyelashes?"

"Yep." She blushed and responded. And she thought, If only my eyelashes were as thick and long as yours, that would have saved me a lot of mascara.

"Well, if we have children in the future, our kids will probably have thick eyelashes just like I do," he said.

Hearing that, Valda blinked in puzzlement. It took her a while to realize what he meant by that. Immediately, her face turned redder.

"I...I'll go freshen up first, and then I'll go to the studio!" Valda said hastily, quickly getting out of bed and heading towards the bathroom.

She was afraid that if she stayed in bed with him any longer, her face would uncontrollably turn even redder.

After washing up, she had breakfast with Brian.

He asked, "Are you going to the studio? I can give you a ride."

"No, thanks. I can take the subway there by myself," she said.

"I’m your boyfriend. Isn’t it normal for a boyfriend to send his girlfriend to work? You’d better get used to it," he said.

Hearing that, Valda was happy and excited. After all, it was the first time she had been feeling that she was actually in a relationship with Brian.

"What about your injury?" she became worried again.

"The driver will do his job. After a few days, I will drive you to work myself," he said.

When the car stopped at he destination, Valda opened the car door. Just when she was about to get out, Brian suddenly grabbed her and buried his face in her shoulder.

"What's wrong?" She asked curiously, "Did something start bothering you again?"

"No," he murmured softly.

"So, you are..."

"I just don’t feel like letting you go like this," he murmured, wishing he could keep her by his side all the time.

At that moment, it hit him that he seemed to have become more and more reliant on her.

Valda was stunned, wondering if his words could be counted as sweet talk as Brian had seldom said stuff like that to her before.

"I...I'm just going to work," She stammered.

"I know, I'll come pick you up after you finish work," he said, finally letting go of her hand.

"Ummm... Well, bye!" She said and stepped out of the car.

Suddenly, as if she had remembered something, she leaned in and gave Brian a kiss on the cheek. After that, she shut the door and quickly walked into the building with a red face.

Brian kept staring at her back until her figure completely disappeared from his sight. It was only then that he suddenly came to his senses and gently touched the spot where she had just landed a kiss.
