Chapter 3093

A while later, Grace took a step forward and hugged Valda tightly, exclaiming, "Valda, you're finally back! Thank goodness!"

As her words trailed off, sobs followed, and Grace couldn't help but cry as she held onto Valda.

Valda slowly raised her hand and hugged Grace back, saying "Yes, I'm back, Grace. Don't cry. I'm here now, aren't I?"

But Grace cried even more sadly because Valda clearly wasn't okay, she was hurt so badly.

"I'm sorry, I'm sorry..." Grace kept apologizing, "Valda, I'm sorry, I'm sorry for causing you so much pain."

"Stop it," Valda comforted. "Grace, we were both victims. If anyone should be sorry, it should be Whitney and Phoebe. But they have already faced legal consequences."

At that moment, Grace realized that Valda might be the kindest person she had met in the world. And Brian was head over heels for her probably because of it.

Valda’s kindness wasn’t that eye-catching at first. But when you got to know her better and spent more time with her, you would find yourself gradually immersed in it. And before you knew it, you would probably become reliant on it.

"By the way, how is Jasmine doing?" Valda changed the subject.

"She's doing well, I'll bring her to see you next time," Grace said, releasing Valda.

Valda raised her hand and wiped away the tears from Grace's face, "It's okay, Grace. You really don't have to feel like you owe me anything."

As she said this, her gaze also turned towards Jason and Brian standing beside her, "None of you owe me anything. Really. I don't want everyone to always feel like they owe me something."

"Anyway, the fact is that you saved Jasmine. So, the Reed family owes you a favor. If you need any help from us in the future, just tell me," Jason said seriously.

Valda looked at Jason, hesitated for a while, and then smiled and said, "Okay, thank you."

"I should be the one saying thank you," said Jason.

"Valda, can you tell me what happened to you this past year? Where were you? How did you come back?" Grace had many questions she wanted to ask.

Valda didn't hide anything and told them everything that happened in the past year. Of course, she heard about what happened during her coma from Lisa.

Although she was trying to downplay everything as much as possible, Grace still felt extremely uneasy when hearing it.
