Hattie Robson and Taylor Carline were left standing there like insignificant air.

Hattie Robson’s angry look turned into a look of disbelief when she saw the Bentley.

‘This car... is not cheap. If it's the top of its series, then the price is not something a regular person can even imagine. Who is this friend of Lina?!

‘And that man. The last look he gave was so cold. When I think back about it now, it gives me the creeps.’

Lina said that she assumed that things would end after she had given her warning, but he tried to pull her and make advances on her.

That was when Hattie Robson appeared and blamed Lina for seducing her boyfriend!

Nobody in the company knew that these two were having an office romance!

The situation became a mess and they ended up in the police station.

“Aren’t you together with Hadwin? Why didn’t you look for him to bail you out?” Jason’s voice suddenly resounded within the car.
