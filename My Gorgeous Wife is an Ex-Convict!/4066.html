Chapter 4064

While the two were chatting, Zabulon suddenly walked over with his senior sister to Tina and Eugenia.

"Tina, can you help me with something?" Zabulon asked.

Tina was taken aback, "What do you need help with?"

"Well, since you haven't taken off your makeup yet, I want you to change your clothes and take a few photos with Kit to help him find his groove. Of course, since this is just a spur-of-the-moment idea, the client may not use the photos in the end, but if they do, you will be paid accordingly," his senior sister said.

Eugenia was even more excited than Tina when she heard this, looking at Tina with anticipation.

Tina knew that if she didn't agree, Eugenia might make things difficult for her.

"Okay," Tina agreed. After all, she also wanted to try what it feels like to shoot with her idol.

For her, it was also a fresh experience.

Tina was taken to change her clothes and the makeup artist touched up her makeup.

When everything was ready, she walked out and Eugenia was immediately impressed.

"Oh my god! Tina, you look...so handsome!" Especially with the wig, black shirt, and matching pants with red gem accents, it exuded a neutral beauty. "If you were a man, I would really be willing to be an enemy with Chandler! The enemy of the rival!"

Tina was speechless. "Okay, do you have to be so exaggerated?"

"It's really good-looking! It makes me so excited that my best friend is going to shoot with Kit!" Eugenia was still in an excited state.

Tina walked up to Kit. "Hello, Kit, we meet again!"

"You are..." Kit frowned, obviously not recognizing Tina.

He was only informed by his assistant that someone else would be shooting with him later.

He didn't really care who he was shooting with, after all, since his debut, he had countless experiences shooting with other models or celebrities.

But when he saw the person in front of him, he was stunned.

This person's temperament and makeup were very eye-catching, making it hard to look away.

"We met at the Reed family's wedding. You even gave me an autograph, remember?" Tina reminded him.

"Is it you? Miss Stephenson?" Kit said.

After all, Tina was the only one who asked for his autograph at that event.

But is this person in front of him really the same as Tina? It was hard for him to imagine.

"Yes, it's me!" Tina grinned. "I'm glad to be shooting with you today. I hope the rest of the shoot goes smoothly!"

"I hope we have a pleasant collaboration," Kit said in a formal tone.

Even if a rich young lady has the best makeup and styling, how good can the photos be if it's just a hobby?

Kit lowered his gaze, hiding the disdain that flashed in his eyes.

At this moment, in his eyes, Miss Stephenson probably bribed the photographer to shoot with him because she was a fan.

However, as the photographer said, "Let's begin!" Kit was suddenly stunned.
