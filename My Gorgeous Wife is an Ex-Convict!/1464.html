Chapter 1463

He was silent. His eyes met her almond-shaped eyes, and the word ‘yes’ finally came out of his thin lips.

"Is it true that you've always known the truth but chose not to say anything because of... the deal with the Atkinson family?" she continued asking.

His lips trembled even more. Each of her questions seemed to cut him with a sharp knife, making him feel like he was being executed.

However, the saddest part was that he deserved this execution. He could not even defend himself!

"Yes or no, answer me!" She raised her voice, and her expression became agitated.

He said, "Yes... But Grace, I..."

"Jason, how could you do that? You let someone innocent go to jail for money? You even... You knew what the Atkinson family did to me when I was in prison!" However, he chose not to look into it and just let the matter be.

She previously thought he did that because he had mistaken her as the perpetrator in the car accident.

However, now she knew that she had made a fool of herself.

It turned out... he had known about the truth the whole time. He knew that she was innocent and wronged, but... he did nothing. He did not even have a shred of pity!

"I'm sorry... Grace, I'm sorry..." Jason kept apologizing. He wanted to go up and hug her.

However, she suddenly pushed his hand away. "Sorry... It turns out that someone's fate is just this to you. Do you know I almost died in prison? How could you lie to me and fool me like that... How could you..."

Grace frowned suddenly, covering her bulging belly with her hands. She was flustered by the wave of pain in her belly.

'Nothing... Nothing bad must happen to the babies!'

She knew that her agitation had probably caused the abnormality.

"Grace, what’s happening?" Jason quickly grabbed Grace and asked her nervously. Brian also looked worried.

"My belly... hurts..." Grace mumbled with difficulty, a thin layer of sweat already breaking out on her forehead. "The babies... The babies..."

"I'll get you to the hospital right away!" Jason immediately picked Grace up and rushed out of the main house. Brian followed them too.

Grace only felt the pain getting worse and worse. She began to blackout. All she could think about before fainting was... ‘Nothing bad must happen to the babies no matter what!’

...

Grace was sent into the emergency room where the doctor carried out a quick examination. Outside the emergency room, Brian looked at Jason who was extremely nervous and did not know what to say.

He had never seen Jason like this before.

Only things related to Grace could make Jason so nervous.

Brian laughed at the thought. 'And I’m the same. I'm nervous because of Grace and almost got into an accident when I followed Jason's car here.’

He just hoped that the babies in Grace's belly would be okay.

It would be too much for Grace to bear if anything happened to the babies, and he did not want to see her sad!
