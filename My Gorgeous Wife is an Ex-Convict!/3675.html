There are many wealthy families in Yemen.

"Well, yeah, now that you mention it, Tina and Chandler are childhood sweethearts too. But who knows if it will turn into love in the future," Lina said, as her daughter was only 13 and still in the innocent stage of love.

Tina was engaged to Chandler since birth, and no one knew what the future held for them.

Chandler had developed feelings for Lina's daughter, but Tina...

"There are other childhood sweethearts too," Kyla said. "Harley and Lornell are also close. Maybe they'll end up like William and Jasper."

Valda smiled. "They do get along well."
