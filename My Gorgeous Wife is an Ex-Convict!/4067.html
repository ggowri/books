Chapter 4065

Because in that instant, the atmosphere around Tina changed completely.

If the original Tina, even with makeup, gave Kit a feeling of being naive and sweet, but at this moment, he suddenly wasn't so sure.

Was this woman in front of him really naive and sweet?

Why did she exude a dark feeling in that instant, like chaos in hell, giving people a shivering sensation?

But at the same time, while shivering, all eyes were drawn to her, making people unable to stop looking at her.

It was as if her every glance, every breath, every simple movement gave people a strong sense of presence, making the world in other people's eyes become her own!

Even when he came to his senses, he heard the photographer say, "Stop, Kit, what's wrong? Why can't you keep up with Tina's movements?!"

"Sorry!" Kit came back to his senses and said, he was distracted just now!

"Don't worry about it. It's our first time working together, and we haven't gotten used to each other yet. I'll cooperate with you in a bit." Tina winked at Kit.

At this moment, in Kit's eyes, she had become that silly and sweet girl again.

"No need, I'll just cooperate with you!" Kit said. After all, he was a professional, and the girl in front of him was just a rich girl playing around.

"Oh, you're willing to cooperate with me? That's great!" For Tina, this was clearly a pleasant surprise!

So, when filming resumed, Tina immediately wrapped her arm around Kit's waist.

Kit's body stiffened, but he quickly suppressed his shock because he realized that Tina was really focused on the shoot and not trying to do anything inappropriate.

At the moment she wrapped her arm around him, her gaze turned cold as ice, fixed on the camera.

Their actions were flirtatious, but her eyes were colder than anyone else's.

This stark contrast also stirred up Kit's emotions.

At least he was a professional, and he couldn't lose to this rich girl!

So Kit gradually got into the zone, their bodies moving closer and then apart, light and dark, the contrast becoming more and more apparent!

Between the two of them, there was an atmosphere that no outsider could penetrate, and everyone's eyes were inevitably drawn to them.

When the photographer finally shouted "cut," they stopped all movement, and everyone seemed to snap out of their trance.

"Thank you for shooting with me. It was a precious experience for me! I'll continue to support you in the future!" Tina smiled at Kit.

Kit looked at the person in front of him, still a little dazed. He felt like he was still immersed in the shoot.

It was a thrilling feeling!

How long has it been since you felt this way while shooting a poster?

Every time he posed for a poster or a photo album before, he felt like a puppet that could be manipulated at will as soon as the camera was pointed at him.

But just now, he didn't feel that way!

Under the lights, Tina walked over and stood next to Eugenia, while Kit continued to shoot.

After shooting with Tina just now, Kit obviously found his rhythm and the shooting went smoothly!

Eugenia watched the shoot and whispered to Tina, "Do you know how amazing you were just now? You and Kit looked like a couple, and the scene we just shot was unforgettable!"

Her voice was full of excitement!
