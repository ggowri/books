Chapter 3601

Harold and Camelia watched the backs of the two people, looking at each other in surprise.

"That girl just now...she's Jason and Grace's daughter, right? I remember reading about her in the news, her name is Jasper."

"Yes, that's the child."

"So, she and William..." Camelia hesitated.

Harold sighed bitterly, "It's all a tragedy! A curse!"

Even in that short moment just now, he could tell that his grandson was afraid that Jasper would find out about their identities.

To their grandson, their existence is a sin, a shame!

He remembered that the news mentioned that their grandson and the Reed family's girl were childhood friends who grew up together, and they had a good relationship!

The Atkinson family and the Reed family, who were once supposed to be in-laws, turned into enemies. Who would have thought that their descendants would develop such a strong bond.

It can only be said that things are unpredictable!

At this moment, Jasper and William entered the elevator. Jasper asked curiously, "Isn't Building 3 the hospital? Are they here for a medical check-up? But why are they carrying water and snacks?"

"Maybe they are here to thank a doctor, it's not really important, you don't need to think too much about it," William said.

Is it really like that? But Jasper has a strange feeling, as if William doesn't want to mention these two old people.

However, she quickly suppressed this strange feeling.

Perhaps she's overthinking it, after all, those two old people are just unrelated strangers!

...After returning from Deer Capital to Emerald City, Jasper Grace continued her classes as usual.

The posts about her and Roger's rumors on the forum have also been deleted, and it didn't have much impact on her university life.

After all, there were not many people who dared to point fingers at her in front of her!

As for the whispers behind her back, Mason didn't care.

From a young age, there have been plenty of people talking behind her back. If she constantly worries about what others say, she'll never be able to live her life!

One afternoon during a public class, Jasper couldn't help but notice that many of her classmates were looking back at her, and the way they looked at her seemed quite strange.

She was puzzled, wondering if her rumored relationship with Roger on campus hadn't ended yet. Or perhaps there was a new post about her on the school forum?

As the class ended, Meave rushed up to her and said, "Jasper, something's not right. There's a post on the forum, it's about..."

"About me?" Jasper interrupted, "What is it this time? Did someone take a picture of me with another guy and post it on the forum?"

"No... it's not that!" Meave quickly replied, "Well, it does have something to do with you, but it's mainly about William!"

Jasper froze, "About William?"

She quickly took out her phone and opened the school forum.

At that moment, the most popular post on the forum had a title that read, "The Son of a Criminal Turns out to be the Prince of Stanford University!"
