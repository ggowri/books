Chapter 4172 Suspicious Person

Chandler answered the call and listened for a moment. His face slightly changed, and he said, "Okay, I'll come over this afternoon."

After ending the call, Tina asked, "What happened?"

"It's about the fire at the factory last time. The investigation report is out. It was a deliberate arson, and the police have arrested a suspect. However, this suspect was also manipulated by someone else, and they can't determine who that person is. So, the police want me to come over." Chandler explained.

Upon hearing this, Tina immediately said, "Then I'll go with you!"

Chandler looked at Tina and said, "It might be boring."

"No, it won't be boring. It's a big matter concerning your factory. And now that we're married, it's my factory too! I care about our 'family' business, so how could it be boring?" Tina said.

The mention of "family" gave Chandler a special feeling, as if it brought them closer.

In the afternoon, they arrived at the police station. The police explained the situation regarding the arrested suspect and the fire, and then asked Chandler if he had any enemies.

Although they couldn't uncover the mastermind behind the suspect, they did get some useful information. When the suspect was contacted by the person using a voice-changing phone, they said something.

"Since Chandler and that bitch embarrassed me, I'm going to make him suffer too!"

Tina listened, furrowing her brow. Bitch? For some reason, she had a feeling that the insult was directed at her!

But had she and Chandler done anything to make someone want to seek revenge by humiliating them?

Chandler had several competitors in his business, and it was natural for there to be hidden grudges. However, she couldn't think of anything that would warrant being called a bitch or being humiliated.

"If I come up with any useful leads, I'll contact the police promptly," Chandler said.

"Alright," the police nodded.

As they left the police station, Tina turned to Chandler and asked, "Did we do anything together that would make someone want to humiliate us?"

"Why, do you think that the person the suspect referred to as a bitch is you?" Chandler asked.

"Otherwise, have you done something embarrassing with someone else?" Tina asked.

"It's not impossible," Chandler thought to himself. He didn't want Tina to be the person behind the arson, because that would mean she could also be the target of that person.

If that person has dealt with him now, then they might go after Tina next.

"But I still think it might be related to me," Tina said, wondering if this was a woman's intuition.

On the way back, Tina couldn't stop thinking about whether she had offended anyone recently. And especially if she had offended someone along with Chandler.

If we exclude the people at school, then it would be someone she has encountered in her life.

In her life...

Suddenly, Tina remembered someone who made her sick to her stomach.

"I got it!" she suddenly exclaimed to Chandler. "Could it be Mr. Perkins?"

"Mr. Perkins?" Chandler was puzzled.

"The one we had dinner with before, the one we met with Kit! Wasn't he planning something inappropriate with Kit, and then I beat him up? And after you arrived, he didn't dare to touch me anymore!" Tina said. "Now that I think about it, Mr. Perkins is highly suspicious!"
