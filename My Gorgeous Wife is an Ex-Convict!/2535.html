Chapter 2533

"Give it back to me!"

Amber snapped while reaching out her hand to George.

At that moment, all she could see was the bracelet he was holding.

Hearing that, George was overwhelmed by disappointment. He couldn’t believe that Amber refused to look at him the whole time, treating him as if he was just a stranger to her.

"Amber Mitchell! You cheated on me with Herman back then! And now, you’re holding onto the bracelet he gave you? No way! I’m getting rid of it!"

With that, he reached out his hand to the side. And there was a drain beside him. Once the bracelet fell into the drain, it would be difficult for Amber to recover it.

Amber yelled, "No! George, don't you dare! I won't let you go if you drop it!"

"Oh, really?" Suddenly, he sneered, "Who do you think you are, huh?

Her face instantly paled. "Herman gave it to me and you snatched it from me. I’m calling the cops! You’ll be arrested for robbery!"

"Robbery?" George smirked, and his gaze grew colder.

"How much is the bracelet? I can pay you back. Hell, I'll even double it. You really think they'd call that a robbery? A rich man like me doesn’t need to rob a bracelet!"

Shocked, Amber closed her eyes. She knew George was determined to give her a hard time by bringing up the old grudges.

After much consideration, she realized that giving in to him might be the only way to save the bracelet.

"Fine! What do I have to do so that you'll give it back to me?" She asked.

George said coldly, "There's nothing you can do! Have you ever thought of me when you and Herman made me the laughingstock of Deer City?"

"I told you, Herman and I never crossed the line!" Amber shouted.

However, George still looked at her in disbelief.

"You don't believe me, do you?" Amber asked.

"How about making a bet? Lie down on the road and see if the drivers can spot you. I'll believe you if you survive. But if you get run over by a car, that will be on you!" George said nonchalantly.

Amber's heart sank when she heard this.

She took a deep breath and closed her eyes as if she was deep in thought.

Moments later, she opened her eyes and replied, "Okay, I'll take the bet! If I survive, give me back my bracelet!"

After that, she stared at the bracelet again before setting off down the street without hesitation.

It was already dark.

The drivers often drove a little faster on the street due to the lack of traffic. Under the dim streetlights, if they weren't cautious, they would hit Amber.

Determined to get back the bracelet, Amber had no choice but to risk her life.

After all, it was the most precious thing that Herman left her.

Later, when she stood in the middle of the road, all she could think of was -- It would be great if she got to see Herman in heaven after she died.
