After a moment, the person put away the telescope and turned to look at the woman sitting in the chair.

"Phoebe, you want me to pretend to be Grace and infiltrate the Barlow family tomorrow. What if it doesn't work? I'll be in trouble, but you can escape easily! Why should I believe you that I'll be safe and get a lot of money in the end?"

With that, Whitney glared at Phoebe.

Phoebe smiled slightly, "I trust you. You're determined to see Valda suffer, right? We both loved Brian and hate Valda, so my plan is the best plan."

As she spoke, she looked at Whitney's face, which was similar to Grace's. With the right clothes, hairstyle, and makeup, Whitney could almost pass for Grace.

But unexpectedly, Brian loved Grace so much that he didn't want Whitney to stay. He would rather let Whitney leave Emerald City for fear of Valda's misunderstanding.

"Besides, you heard them today. Tomorrow, Grace and the others are going to the zoo. You can visit the Barlows with a suitable excuse. No one will suspect anything," Phoebe said.

A few seconds later, she added, "Or do you want to chicken out at this point? Then you'll only get a regular job with low pay in the Hart Group. Are you willing to settle for that?"

Phoebe's words sparked the determination within Whitney.

Whitney thought, How could I settle for that? Back then, Brian saved me and treated me well, making me think I had climbed the social ladder. My friends thought the same, so they were constantly flattering me. But in the end, I became a laughingstock!

I didn't even mind being a substitute just to stay by Brian's side. But Valda ruined everything! So, I'm taking her down!

"Okay, I promise you, but you have to keep me safe and give me a billion dollars, otherwise you won't have it easy either!" Whitney warned.

Phoebe smiled, "Of course, no problem. I promise to keep my word."

Meanwhile, Phoebe wanted to make Valda's life a living hell, make Brian hate Valda, and show him how ridiculous his feelings for Valda were.

The Cross family was ruined because of Valda, so now she was going to ruin Valda too.

***

The next day, Valda and Brian met up with Jason, Grace and the others at the entrance of the zoo.

There were four adults and four kids among them.

Simon and William were both from Deer Capital, but Simon knew the place better than them. After all, he grew up there.

Simon had been to this wildlife park many times with his family before, so he managed to show them around. He knew where to find different animals so well that he didn't even need to look at the map.

Jasper was quite impressed, and her gaze fell on Simon from time to time.

Sensing her admiration, Simon feel very pleased.

Although William was a bit unhappy about it, he didn't feel that bad when he felt Jasper's small hand in his hand.

Anyway, he would try to know this place better, so the next time they came here, he could also guide them around like Simon did this time.

It had been such a long time since Valda last visited a zoo, so today's visit was quite enjoyable for her.

And as she looked at those cute animals, cute cartoon images of them kept popping up in her mind. Perhaps after getting home, she could paint some animal posters or stuff.

In the morning, they wandered in the zoo. At noon, they went to a nearby restaurant to have lunch.

During mealtime, they could see a stream outside the restaurant, where several small boats were waiting to serve tourists.
