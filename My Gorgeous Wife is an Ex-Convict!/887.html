All the noise seemed to have cut off from them.

Although the Stephenson family was not based in Emerald City, both would lose if they really wanted to go against the Reed family.

Moreover, Hadwin might look elegant and charming, but he was actually ruthless and cunning.

Otherwise, how would a b*stard become the Stephenson family's patriarch?!

Hadwin wanted to give the girl's eyes a good washing. He wanted to wash away all those eye-pricking images!

'Good, she's not too drunk to recognize me!'

Lina was still holding the little vest that she had managed to grab.

She was holding it like it was a treasure. If he tried to grab it by force, he would attract the attention of onlookers.

Hadwin thought about it before returning to the driver's seat. He then started the car to leave. He planned to grab the vest from Lina and throw it away later.

In the rearview mirror, Hadwin watched as another black car drove in the opposite direction. It was Jason's car!

He could vaguely see Jason through the window as he sat in the back seat holding Grace in his arms.

'Why break up if he can't get over her?' Hadwin thought to himself, then his eyes fell on the heartless girl beside him.

'Anyway, Lina said she loves me, so... I'll never break up with her!

'I'm the only man she can ever love.

'She can forget about liking other men!'

In the back seat, Jason looked at Grace who was leaning in his arms.

'Is she drunk? She's probably drunk. If she isn't drunk, she wouldn't be lying so intimately in my arms. She would only be too eager to establish boundaries between us.'
