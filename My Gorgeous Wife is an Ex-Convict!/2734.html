"You must be hungry. Try and see if you like these. I'll get something else if you don't." Brian's voice rang in her ears.

"These will do," Valda Theller said hurriedly.

The man’s beautiful peach blossom eyes glimmered slightly as Jason smiled. "Yeah, it's indeed something worth feeling happy for."

Since it was Brian's choice, he would let Brian go on with this.

Having two sons and two daughters would be like having the best of both worlds.

"Alright, I'll get it for you," said Jason, his eyes full of love for her.

"Brian, you've had a lot to drink," Valda Theller could not help but remind.

He looked at her and chuckled. "I can hold my liquor. This is nothing."

She stared at him. He was smiling, but his eyes looked as if they were about to cry.

'Is it because of... Grace?' When she was eating just now, she could see that Grace was happy and Jason took great care of her.
