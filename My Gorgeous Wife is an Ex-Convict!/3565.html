Chapter 3563

 

"If you really want to avoid struggling for a few decades, you can. Even if we don't date, I can promise you that," Jasper said.

His pupils suddenly shrank, looking at her in surprise.

"I can talk to my dad and have the Reed family sponsor you after you graduate, so you don't have to worry about your career development," Jasper said.

"Why?" he asked in a hoarse voice.

She pursed her lips. "Because your face looks a lot like my friend's."

He suddenly let go of her hand, as if he had used all his strength!

Jasper stumbled back two steps before finally regaining his balance.

"So, Miss. Reed is looking for a stand-in?" Roger sneered.

Jasper said, "No one can replace Lawrence, and I don't want you to be a substitute. I just hope that someone who looks similar to Lawrence in my memory can live a good life."

Consider it as a way to make up for the regret that she couldn't make Lawrence's life better before.

At that time, if she hadn't been so determined to break up with Lawrence, maybe everything could have been different.

"Live a good life? Miss. Reed is quite generous. It seems that the Lawrence you mentioned didn't have a good life," Roger said.

"Think whatever you want," Jasper said. "Also, I came to talk to you today to say that I will only date people I like and who also like me."

After saying this, she walked out of the piano room.

Roger stood still for a while before raising his hand and touching his own cheek. When she touched his cheek just now, he felt flustered and didn't know what to do.

Was it because of his face that she easily agreed to let him stop struggling for a few decades? Was this a way to make up for the guilt she felt towards the former Lawrence?

She knew very well that the former Lawrence had lost everything and had fallen into hell!

People who have never tasted sweetness often don't feel the bitterness.

But if someone gives you something sweet, and then takes it back halfway, you can no longer bear the bitterness!

"Jasper, who do you like? William? Then let's see who you will choose in the future..." he whispered.

A few days later, William gave Jasper a preliminary investigation report.

Roger grew up abroad and was admitted to Stanford University as a special student due to his outstanding performance in chemistry. He is also the only heir of the Elliott family overseas.

The Elliott family is a relatively low-key family overseas, with few members, but they have accumulated wealth quickly over the years through multiple patents.

Before the age of 14, Roger was often sickly and did not attend school. Instead, he received lessons from a private tutor at home. It wasn't until he turned 14 that he officially entered school.

As a result, there is hardly any information available about Roger before the age of 14.

Accompanying this information, however, are photos of Roger taken every year since he turned 14.

Jasper looked at the photos and saw that 14-year-old Roger looked more like her memory of Lawrence.

But this information also indicates that Roger is not Lawrence.

Is it really true that people can look alike?

"Do you have any pictures of him before he was 14?" Jasper asked.

"Not at the moment," William replied. "The Elliott family has never revealed any pictures of Roger before, as if they deliberately hid them."
