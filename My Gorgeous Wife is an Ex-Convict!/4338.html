Chapter 4336 Not So Liked

Harley said lightly, "I'm doing quite well here."

Tina looked at the person in front of her, who was a few years younger than her. Although her memory had not yet recovered, she also knew that they had been childhood playmates before.

Just because of the age difference, we don't spend much time playing together.

But deep down in her heart, she had a natural liking for Harley, and she must be a very nice person.

She is now able to reconcile smoothly with Chandler, and Chandler has not led out the insect in such a brutal way, and Harley has also helped her.

"Since you like Lornell, why don't you take the initiative? "Tina said." Emotions sometimes rely on striving. If you really like that person, then you should be more proactive."

"Is it like the way you chased Chandler back then? "Harley said, and soon she realized she had said the wrong thing and couldn't help but apologize." Sorry, I forgot about you and my memory hasn't been restored yet."

"It's okay, "Tina smiled." Although I haven't remembered the past, I've also learned a lot about my past with Chandler. You're right, just like when I chased Chandler."

"Harley laughed mockingly and said, "But Tina, I'm different from you. Chandler has always liked and loved you since then. Your proactive pursuit will naturally receive a response, but I..."

She paused, turned her head, and turned her gaze to Lornell, who was talking to someone. "Lornell, he doesn't like me. For him, the engagement with me was just a matter of curiosity and necessity. If I hadn't been dying and needed the Quentin family to heal me, I'm afraid I wouldn't have had this engagement at all."

Harley's tone was filled with a sense of sadness.

Tina listened and remained silent for a moment: "So what about you? What do you think? If you really like him so much, then..."

"Actually, I realized that I don't seem to like it that much anymore, "Harley suddenly smiled, Tina, thank you for enlightening me. To be honest, I envy you and Chandler. You grew up together, faced difficulties together, and finally got together again. However, not every marriage contract made at a young age will have good results in the end. In those movies and TV dramas, many unmarried couples who have made contracts also end up breaking their contracts."

Tina was startled and said, "Do you want to break your engagement with Lornell?"

Harley's tone of voice clearly suggests this idea.

"The engagement of the Quentin family cannot be easily dissolved, "Harley said slowly." Perhaps as you said, since you like it, you need to work hard."

For now, she is already trapped in a contradiction.

On the one hand, she knew that Lornell didn't love her, and even didn't have that kind of male-female relationship with her, but it was just a habit.

Similarly, she also understood that emotions are not something that comes with hard work. Just one person working hard cannot succeed.

On the other hand, there was still a reluctance in her heart. She really liked Lornell, but after being treated with indifference time and time again, this liking gradually cooled down.

However, terminating the engagement would bring significant losses to the Hart family, and as a last resort, she did not want to terminate the engagement like this.
