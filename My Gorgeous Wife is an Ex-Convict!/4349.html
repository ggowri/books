Chapter 4347 Let's Talk About Breaking Off the Engagement

Calvert looked up to see a handsome young man walking straight towards him.

Harley looked at the man who had just arrived, "How did you end up here?"

"I came looking for you," Lornell said. Just now, at the school gate, he overheard some students gossiping about her having a meal with a boy at a snack bar. He decided to check it out, and to his surprise, it turned out to be true.

"Do you need something?" Harley asked.

Lornell raised an eyebrow, "What do you think I'm here for?"

Harley pursed his lips, probably because he was about to break off the engagement!

"Or are you going to continue having dinner with him and we'll talk about our stuff another day?" Lornell said.

Harley's brow furrowed slightly. She didn't want to drag out this matter of breaking off the engagement any longer. The sooner it was resolved, the better!

She turned to Calvert with an apologetic look, "Sorry, I have something to take care of today and need to leave early. Next time, I'll treat you to a meal, okay?"

"Alright," Calvert responded in a low voice.

"Let's get going then," Lornell said to Harley. Before leaving, Lornell shot a quick glance at Calvert from the corner of his eye.

Lornell's gaze was indifferent and superior.

Calvert stiffened. He often saw this kind of gaze in those who held power.

This guy, he was on the same level as Harley, wasn't he?

But Harley was different, he never looked at him that way.

Harley and Lornell stepped out of the snack bar, with Lornell's car parked not far away.

"Let's get in the car first," Lornell said. "I don't want to discuss what's next just anywhere. After all, if some ill-intentioned people overhear us, it could get a bit tricky."

Harley nodded.

The two of them got in the car, and Lornell drove it over to the club.

Once they were in the private room, Lornell casually asked Harley, "What would you like to drink?"

"No need, let's just get straight to the point," Harley said.

Lornell suddenly let out a cold laugh, "What? You don't even have time to have a drink with me now? But you do have time to dine with other men. Don't tell me you're breaking off our engagement because of that young boy."

Harley's eyes widened in surprise, "My decision to break off our engagement has nothing to do with Calvert!"

How could Lornell have been so imaginative?

"His name is Calvert, right?" Lornell asked, "Since he's not the reason you want to break off the engagement, then tell me, what is the reason?"

Harley looked at the person in front of him, took a deep breath, and said, "I don't want to marry someone just because it's convenient!"

Lornell raised an eyebrow, "What's wrong with 'suitable'?"

Harley gave a bitter smile, "It's not that it's bad, it's just not good enough. I hope my future marriage will be like my parents, where they love each other!"

After hearing what Harley had to say, Lornell was stunned for a moment, but then suddenly burst into laughter.

Lornell's laughter was laced with mockery.

"I can't believe you're so naive, Harley. How many people do you think have a love like your parents in this world? It's like some people's parents hit the jackpot. Do their children also believe they'll be as lucky as their parents and hit the jackpot again?" Lornell stopped laughing, looking indifferently at Harley sitting on the sofa.

Harley's eyelashes fluttered slightly, "You might be right, but for me, if a future marriage doesn't have love, then I'd rather not have that marriage at all!"
