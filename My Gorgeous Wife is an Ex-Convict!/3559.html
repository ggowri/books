Chapter 3557

 

Just then, the door of the private room was suddenly pushed open, and a tall figure walked in.

Someone in the private room saw Roger and exclaimed in surprise, "Hey, Roger, didn't you say you weren't coming? Why are you here?"

Roger smiled faintly and replied, "It just happened to be free, and I felt a bit bored, so I came over."

Jasper heard the voice and looked up, meeting Roger's gaze. For some reason, she suddenly had a feeling that he had come specifically for her.

Immediately, she silently criticized herself for overthinking things.

How could he have come for her? He himself said he came because he was bored!

But why did he come here...was it just to join in the fun or was he looking for a girlfriend? Jasper's mind was filled with speculation.

Roger was already popular among girls due to his handsome appearance, and now, naturally, girls flocked around him.

Similarly, many girls surrounded William, even though his attitude seemed cold. But Grace saw many girls persistently trying to strike up a conversation with William, after all, he was the eldest son of the Barlow family!

Although there were many rumors about Stanford University between Mr. Barlow and Jasper, they were not in a relationship. However, if anyone could catch Mr. Barlow's eye, then even if they couldn't marry into a wealthy family, there would still be many benefits.

At this moment, Jasper seemed like a bystander, just watching the boys' side, where William and Roger were surrounded by almost half of the girls who came to the mixer today.

However, the number of people on both sides is about the same, and it is difficult to tell who is more popular.

Jasper didn't expect that after a moment, she would become the subject of gossip.

This was because a girl asked Roger, "Roger, do you like any girl?"

"Yes," Roger replied directly.

"Who is it?" Suddenly, many girls became curious.

Roger didn't answer, he just looked towards Jasper.

Jasper was eating fruit and almost choked on it when he met Roger's gaze.

"Cough...cough..." Jasper quickly swallowed the fruit, finally able to catch his breath.

Onlookers saw their eye contact and teased, "Roger, you don't have a crush on Jasper, do you?"

Suddenly, everyone in the room focused their attention on the two of them.

Jasper blinked her eyes, feeling that the onlookers around her had too vivid imaginations. A few days ago, she wanted to get to know Roger better, but he looked at her with disgust. How could someone like that possibly like her?

Just as Jasper was about to deny it, Roger's voice suddenly sounded, "Yes."

There was a sudden gasp from the surrounding crowd, and even Jasper herself gasped!
