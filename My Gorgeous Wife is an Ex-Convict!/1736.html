Lina had dry-cleaned the clothes she wore when she left Hadwin Stephenson's mansion that day and went to Stephenson Group's office building in Emerald City, intending to return them to Hadwin Stephenson.

She went to the reception desk and explained her intention. She thought she would have to go through a lot of trouble to meet Hadwin Stephenson, but as soon as the receptionist heard that she was here to return the clothes, they spoke respectfully and warmly without asking for her name. "You must be Miss Lina. President Stephenson said you can go see him right away!"

The receptionist led Lina to the elevator and helped her press the floor number as they spoke, "The president's office is on the 32nd floor. You'll be led to President Stephenson's office when you arrive on the floor!"

Lina did not expect Hadwin Stephenson to have told his employees about her arrival. When she reached the 32nd floor, there was already someone waiting at the doors.

"Hello, Miss Sweeney. I'm President Stephenson's secretary. Please come with me," they said.

Lina followed the secretary to Hadwin Stephenson's office door. After gently knocking on the door twice, the secretary twisted the doorknob and said to Lina, "Miss Sweeney, please enter."

"I can go in?" asked Lina. After all, although the secretary had knocked on the door twice, there was no response from Hadwin Stephenson.

"The president said you can enter at any time," the secretary said.

Lina pressed her red lips together and went in.
