Chapter 2476

Her remarks were aimed at those who were helping Amber Mitchell.

Amber Mitchell's gaze went cold. "Trusting my family is why I ended up like this. Outsiders rescued me and proved that I'm a normal person. Tell me who I’m supposed to believe now."

"Sis, you’ve misunderstood. You were ill at the time. The doctor's diagnosis report was clear, and there was more than one doctor involved. It’s impossible that they were all wrong in their diagnosis, right?”

Just then, someone among the reporters exclaimed, "Gosh, a doctor confessed online that the Mitchell family bribed them, so they diagnosed Amber Mitchell to be mentally ill without going through an examination."

"Gosh, really?"

"If that's true, did the Mitchell family intend to drive their eldest daughter insane?"

"Are the conspiracy theories online true? Did the Mitchell family imprison Amber Mitchell in a mental hospital and made her out to be a mental patient even though she was fine just to gain her shares?”

The Mitchell family turned pale at the conversation around them.

The reporters even raised their microphones and asked them all kinds of tough questions.

A reporter asked the Mitchell family what they thought of the doctor's confession on the website while displaying the page on their phone.

Louise Mitchell grabbed the phone and looked at the words on it.

The doctor who confessed was Amber Mitchell's attending doctor, and the doctor even listed their financial transactions with the Mitchell family. They even claimed that they had audio recordings.

The recordings had yet to be released, but Louise Mitchell was already covered in a cold sweat.

'What the hell is going on here? Didn't I tell the doctor to lay low for a while? I made it clear I’d pay them a good sum of money once this is over.'

However, this confession had now become evidence against the Mitchell family!

A car drove up to the hospital’s entrance. The bodyguards at Amber Mitchell's side immediately formed a path to let her get into the car as soon as they saw it.

Even if the reporters wanted to interview Amber Mitchell, they were blocked by the bodyguards.

Amber Mitchell got into the car and saw Grace.

Grace did not seem surprised by everything happening outside the car.

"Did you know this was going to happen?" Amber Mitchell asked.

"I didn't know the Mitchell family was coming until a while ago. It's why I got the bodyguards to escort you over," said Grace.

"The doctor's confession online..."

Grace said, "We found the doctor when you were having your consultation. He confessed because we already had evidence against him. If he confessed, he could at least face a shorter sentence. When I learned that the Mitchell family was coming over, I asked the doctor to make an online statement to humiliate the Mitchell family further."

If the Mitchell family had not come over today, the doctor's statement would only have been published a few days later.

Amber Mitchell looked at Grace with gratitude. She had helped her more than she expected.

She had expected to be on her own after the consultation, but now... things seemed a little different than expected.

"Thank you," she said.

Grace said, "You've thanked me many times. But I can't help you much after this. I'm returning to Emerald City in a few days, but I'll introduce you to some lawyer friends before leaving. You can consult them if you have any legal problems. They’ll help you get justice."

"You've done so much for me," said Amber Mitchell. However, what she wanted was not only justice for herself but also… Herman.
