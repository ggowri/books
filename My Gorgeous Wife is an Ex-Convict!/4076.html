Chapter 4074

Kit immediately thought of the last time at the wedding, when Tina helped her friend get an autograph, and he did write "TO Eugenia"!

So, was this assistant her friend?

Kit's gaze fell on Tina, who was talking to Weldon.

Since the last shoot, he had been thinking about Tina from time to time, thinking about the scene that day, and thinking about her appearance at the time.

Neither male nor female, neither god nor demon.

It caught his entire attention!

Even afterwards, he went out of his way to ask the photographer for the set of photos taken with Tina and saved them on his phone.

When he was alone, he would stare at the photos in a daze.

Why? Perhaps because Tina, who took those photos, was so different from her usual self that it was shocking and left a deep impression?

Or was it because he had been struggling with his emotions, but with Tina's guidance, he suddenly entered the state of mind needed for the shoot?

Although he was the professional, and Tina was just an amateur, he felt like he had lost to her!

"Kit?" Eugenia's voice sounded in Kit's ear. "What's wrong?"

"Huh?" Kit suddenly snapped out of his daze and quickly took Eugenia's autograph board. "Okay, I'll sign it for you right now!"

Kit quickly signed his name on the board.

Meanwhile, Tina felt a strange sensation from the photographer, Weldon, who was looking at her. His gaze wasn't like that of a photographer looking at a model, but rather filled with curiosity, surprise, and even... a hint of disdain.

Disdain?

Was it because she wasn't at a professional level?

But she heard from Zabulon that Weldon specifically requested her!

If it wasn't for Weldon's request, she wouldn't have had the opportunity to work with Kit again!

So why did Weldon's gaze contain such disdain?

"I saw the photos from your last shoot with Kit and thought they were good, but last time you had a more neutral look. This time, I want you to present a more feminine appearance," said Weldon.

"What kind of appearance?" Tina asked.

"Cute and innocent, yet charming and alluring," Weldon replied. "There should be a bit of contradiction, like in your last shoot, but this time I want to see a harmonious contradiction in you."

Tina frowned. She had always cosplayed male characters from anime and even dressed neutrally in her last shoot with Kit.

But this time, it was different. She was going to be photographed as a woman.

"Of course, if you can't do it, you can leave now and I can bring in other candidates," Weldon said.

"Since I'm here, I might as well do the shoot," Tina smiled.

She wasn't one to back down from a challenge, and she was curious about portraying a more feminine appearance.

"Alright, go get ready," Weldon said.

Just as Tina was about to leave, Weldon suddenly said, "By the way, I heard you're the daughter of the White Maple Group and Chandler's fiancée."
