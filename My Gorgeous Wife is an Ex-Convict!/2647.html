Chapter 2645

Just then, the producer saw Camille and Valda and waved to them.

"Come on, let's go and say hello," Valda said before taking her best friend by the hand and walking toward the producer.

Watching Valda walking over, Raymond fell into a trance.

Today, Valda wore a long mint green dress. Her long hair was tied up in a neat ponytail. She looked like herself when she was in college.

In particular, the faint dark circles under her eyes reminded him of the time when he often saw her with dark circles back then.

At that time, when he asked her if she did not sleep well, she would yawn and say that she had been drawing comics before the deadline.

"Do you have to work so hard on comics?" Raymond asked her.

"Well, I love comics. Why would I get tired of something I love?" With a broad smile, Valda answered.

Whenever she spoke about comics and the stories she created, her eyes always glistened like the bright and beautiful stars in the sky.

"Here, let me introduce you to CEO Raymond. Mr. Bowman is the one who made the additional investment in our film. With his investment, our film can become better,” the producer introduced with a laugh, bringing Raymond’s mind back to reality.

Valda and Camille were shocked. They already knew that someone had made an additional investment in the film, but they did not expect it to be Raymond.

So, they both wondered if it was simply a coincidence, or Raymond’s intention.

Instantly, Valda looked at Raymond with vigilance.

"And we have Val..."

The producer was about to introduce Valda and Camille when Raymond cut him off, "No need for introduction. I know Valda and Camille. We used to go to the same school."

The producer smiled. "What a coincidence. This is your reunion, then."

"Yeah, it's not easy for us to be reunited." Raymond chuckled.

Valda maintained a smile out of politeness.

After chitchatting for a short while, the producer left with several others, leaving only Raymond, Valda, and Camille.

Camille's fake smile was gone immediately. She asked, "Raymond, what do you want?"

"What is that supposed to mean?" Raymond asked in reply.

"Why did you invest in this film?" Camille asked.

"You should thank me for the investment. Otherwise, you wouldn’t have been able to afford Gloria's salary at all," He replied.

Hearing that, Camille did not know what to say.

"Thank you for investing in the film, Mr. Bowman," Valda chimed in indifferently.

Then she turned around, wanting to leave with Camille.

Raymond raised his hand to stop her and said, "I mean no harm in investing in this film. I only hope that the film based on your original work can be a success. I sincerely apologize for what happened."

Valda said, "I told you, I accept your apology, but that doesn't mean I forgive you. Mr. Bowman, you chose to invest in the film. Whether you make or lose money is also your matter. I don't need you to invest in anything because of me. I don't think we're that close."

With a snort, Camille uttered, "A sincere apology? Why didn't you explain to the whole school why you hurt Valda like that, then? And now, you’re telling us it’s a sincere apology? How funny!"

At her words, Raymond's expression froze.
