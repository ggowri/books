Chapter 3545

 

The afternoon after Jasper's second class ended, she came to the library to find some books and materials. As she sat in her chair looking at the materials, she caught a glimpse of a figure out of the corner of her eye. The figure was carrying a stack of books and sat down in a nearby seat, flipping through the pages and taking notes.

That is... Roger!

Jasper immediately recognized the person. They were separated by a few tables, but her gaze kept drifting towards him.

It really looks like... like Lawrence!

If he really is Lawrence, that would be amazing!

Last night, as soon as she closed her eyes, she could hear him playing the Transcendental Etude No. 12 Chasse-neige.

Suddenly, her eyes met with a pair of deep, beautiful black eyes. Jasper was taken aback as she realized that Roger had lifted his head and was looking directly at her. Their gazes locked!

Was she caught peeking?

Jasper's heart raced, but she didn't look away. She continued to stare straight at him.

After a moment, his eyelashes trembled slightly, and he lowered his gaze. He gathered his books and materials, stood up, and walked towards the library door.

Almost instinctively, Jasper followed him out. By the time she snapped out of it, she was already walking behind him.

What caught her eye was his back.

Broad shoulders, narrow waist, and a straight spine, but his walking posture was casual.

He was quite tall, around 180 centimeters, just like William. She used to wonder what Lawrence would look like and how tall he would be when she found him, not as a 10-year-old child anymore.

At that moment, Roger, who was walking in front, suddenly stopped. Jasper was lost in thought and bumped into his back.

Ouch!

She covered her nose and staggered back a few steps, while he turned around and his gaze fell on her. "You've been following me all the way, is there something you need?"

"Huh?" Jasper was taken aback. Her following him seemed like an unconscious action, and she didn't even think much about it when she chased him out of the library.

"If there's nothing, I hope you won't follow me like this again," Roger said, turning around to leave.

"Wait!" She suddenly took two quick steps forward and blocked his way. "I followed you because you look a lot like a friend of mine!"

Upon hearing this, he furrowed his brow. "Lawrence?"

"Do you know him?" She was surprised.
