And he knows even the most specialized terminology, sometimes even better than the professional translators present.

"How many languages have you actually learned?" Valda asked in a moment of pause.

"Just six languages, and for the others, I only know a little bit," he replied.

"Six countries?!" Valda gasped. If he only knows a little bit of the other languages, then he must be fluent in these six languages?

He's really a language genius!
