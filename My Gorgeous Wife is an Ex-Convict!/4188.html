Chapter 4186 Escape

She took a deep breath and walked briskly ahead.

She is now a mother, willing to take any risks to protect her unborn child.

Just as she was about to leave the village, suddenly, the flickering flames of the ritual extinguished!

She exclaimed in her mind, "Oh no!"

This meant that the ritual ended earlier than she had anticipated.

She had to be quick! Quick to escape!

Before Drogo and the others returned home and discovered her escape, she had to leave the village as soon as possible!

Suddenly, it started drizzling from the sky!

She forced a bitter smile. The rain would only slow her down and make her escape even more difficult!

Could it be that fate was going to throw even more obstacles her way?

It was particularly difficult for her to run with her big belly. Even walking briskly was already a heavy burden on her body.

She didn't know how long she had been walking, but finally... she walked out of the village, only to find an even longer road ahead.

She remembered hearing someone say that if she walked a long road outside the village, she would reach the town where there was a police station and many people.

If she made it to the town and found someone from the police, then she would be saved!

Even if she couldn't remember anything, the police would help her, help her find her family, and... the father of her child.

What kind of person was the father of her child?

Although she couldn't remember, whenever she thought about it, she felt a sense of nostalgia. She must have loved the father of her child!

Just then, she heard the sound of hurried footsteps behind her, along with flashlight beams and people shouting, making her heart race even faster.

They were chasing after her!

She had to be quick, she had to be fast!

She had to escape from here quickly!

"Here! I see her, she's up ahead!" someone shouted.

Then more footsteps echoed.

"We can't let her get away!"

"If she escapes, the Drogo family will be finished! Where will Drogo find such a beautiful and lively daughter-in-law again!"

"The Drogo family promised that whoever helps them catch their daughter-in-law will be rewarded with 1000 dollars!"

1000 dollars, in this backward village, was a big amount of money. The villagers who came to catch her became even more excited upon discovering her trail, their eyes shining!

As the crowd chasing her got closer and closer, she stumbled and fell to the ground.

Worriedly, she looked at her belly, relieved that she had been on all fours and her belly hadn't hit the ground.

But her elbow joints and knees were throbbing with pain.

However, at a time like this, she couldn't care less about her injured body. All she had to do was run!

She had to run, even if it was for the sake of the child in her belly!

Her body seemed to have an instinct telling her that no matter what, she had to protect this child, because this child was important to her...

This kind of importance seemed to come not only from maternal love, but also from some other reason.

But she couldn't remember!

The footsteps behind her were getting closer and closer. Just as it seemed like she was about to be caught, suddenly, she saw a car approaching not far away!

The bright headlights stung her eyes!

But at this moment, the car lights were her last hope of escaping from here!

She exerted all her strength, waving her hand towards the car and running towards it.

The car slammed on the brakes just a few meters away from her.

Bathed in the light of the car headlights, she looked straight at the man sitting in the driver's seat through the windshield!
