"Just look at me like this and raise your hands to help me with my collar," he said, still leaning over slightly.

Valda Theller's face reddened. Like an obedient student, she raised her hands and randomly fixed his suit collar and shirt collar. "Like this?"

"Yes," he said. His lips curled slightly. It was as if he was watching her with a gentle smile.

However, she knew the gentleness was only an act.

Without looking up, she could feel many eyes on her.

"Umm... A lot of people are looking at us," she whispered.

"I know. Let them watch," he said. That was his purpose today.

Just then, a voice rang out. "Hey, how enviable it is to watch you two's public display of affection."

Valda Theller turned her head and saw Patrick, whom she had seen at the party with Brian's friends. However, Patrick had a new female companion with him today.

"There you are." Brian straightened up and greeted Patrick.

The two chatted casually, but Patrick had no intention of introducing the female companion beside him to Brian and Valda Theller.

In his opinion, she was only a woman he was fooling around with. They took what they needed from each other, and there was nothing serious about it.

"Valda is so beautiful today that I almost didn't recognize her." Patrick's eyes fell on Valda Theller. "It seems Brian is quite serious about you."

Patrick called Valda Theller ‘Valda’ like he was already familiar with her.

Valda Theller smiled sheepishly. Brian wrapped his arm around Valda Theller's waist, pulling her nearer. "I’ve always been serious about Valda."

"Yes, yes!" Patrick repeated.

Brian looked at Valda Theller and said, "Why don't you take a seat? I'll get you something to eat."

"Okay," she replied.

Patrick also turned to his female companion and said, "Sit with Valda." With that said, he walked to the other side with Brian.

Valda Theller and Patrick's female companion looked at each other. Finally, she spoke up first.

"Hello, what's your name?"

Valda Theller smiled and said, "My name is Valda Theller. You can call me Valda. What about you? What's your name?"

"My name is Wei Jayg. You can call me Jasperg," she said.

Valda Theller looked at her. She looked to be in her early 20s and younger than her. She could indeed call her Jasperg.

"Valda, by the way, Mr. Hart and you are..." Wei Jayg looked inquisitive.

"We're a couple," Valda Theller said naturally.
