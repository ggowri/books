Chapter 3064

"Do you think Valda only wanted to save the child? What she really wanted to save was..."

Before Jason could finish his sentence, Grace interrupted him with a scolding, "Jason, don't say anymore!"

Jason paused and looked at his wife.

Grace pleaded with him, "Jason, please stop. It's our fault, everything is our fault! Valda disappeared because of us!"

Meanwhile, Jasmine in Grace's arms was crying loudly, frightened by the situation.

Grace quickly comforted her daughter, "Jasmine, don't cry. Uncle Brian didn't mean to scare you just now!"

Jason looked at Brian and said, "I won't let you lay a finger on Jasmine. If you really want to harm Valda, you can come after me instead."

"Fill my destiny?" Brian suddenly laughed, but with that mournful laughter, two lines of clear tears flowed from his reddened phoenix eyes.

Actually, he is the one who deserves to be sentenced to death!

"In fact, I'm the one who should die, it's all my fault! I'm the one who let Valda down! It's me who deserves to die!" Brian shouted, feeling a sharp pain in his chest.

These days, the pain seems to have already enveloped him, even to the point where it's so intense that he feels numb.

However, there was never a "most painful" before, only "more painful"!

He thought he was so numb from the pain that he couldn't feel it anymore.

But now... he feels the pain again.

Ouch! The pain was so intense that he clenched his fists and repeatedly pounded his chest, saying "It's me, it's all my fault..."

"Brian, don't do this!" Grace handed the child to Jason, took a quick step forward, and grabbed Brian's hand. "Don't do this. If Valda finds out you're acting like this for her, she'll be hurt and upset."

"Feeling sad?" Brian murmured, "But do you know? I've made her feel sad too many times. Why...why was I so foolish before? Why didn't I treat her better when she was by my side? Why did I always make her feel sad?"

Brian keeps blaming himself.

"I knew exactly what she wanted to hear from me, didn't I? I knew she had been waiting for me to fall in love with her! She was so eager, but I didn't say anything! I had already fallen in love with her a long time ago, but I foolishly thought I hadn't! Hahaha... Is there anyone in the world more foolish than me? Why, why did she have to fall in love with a fool like me!"

Brian was crying in pain, his body trembling and curled up like a desperate beast. It seemed that for him, the remaining time was just waiting for death.

Grace couldn't stop crying. In front of her was Brian, and she didn't even know what she could say to help him pull himself together.

Maybe... only Valda can do it!

Grace looked at Jason beside her, but he just shook his head slightly, his eyes telling her that nothing needed to be said right now.

Because...saying anything now is useless!
