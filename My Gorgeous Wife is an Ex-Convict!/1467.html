Chapter 1466

Jason was silent and did not say a word.

Kyla Corbyn said, "Whatever it is, don't hurt Grace if you care about Grace and love her deeply."

Jason's eyelashes fluttered a little. A long time passed before he said, "The last thing I want is to hurt her."

...

Brian walked into the ward and saw Grace in hospital clothes. She was half-sitting on the hospital bed. She still did not look good.

"Sorry," he said. He had never thought of letting her find out about the truth in such a way. She had almost miscarried and was even hospitalized.

He had planned to keep the truth to himself and have Lily imprisoned and punished in other ways.

However, most of the time, things would not work out as planned.

Just like back then... He had wanted to be with her, but he did not trust her when she needed help most.

He had pushed her to Jason himself!

Now, it was useless even if he held countless regrets in his heart.

Grace said, "You have nothing to apologize for. I just want to see the contents of the email that Old Master Reed sent you. I hope you can forward a copy of that to me if possible."

Brian seemed somewhat surprised. "You want to see the email?"

"Can I?" she asked.

He bit his lower lip lightly. "Sure. But your health..."

"I know my health well. I'll keep my emotions in check," she said. She deserved to know about the truth!

Everyone else now knew about the truth, but she was the only one kept in the dark.

Brian pulled out his phone and forwarded the anonymous email he had received to the email address Grace provided.

After doing this, Brian said, "Grace, I don't know what will happen next, but... if you need my help one day, just ask. My phone number will never change. I can help you with anything."

He would do anything for her if she asked!

Grace shook her head. "Brian, I told you to get over me. Now, I'm still going to say the same thing. You should get over some things, or you'll be stuck in the past forever. You’ll only end up wasting your feelings."

Brian smiled wryly. 'Get over?'

It was easy to say those two words but difficult to actually do it.

He could not nor did he want to get over her. He was willing to stay stuck in the past even though it was a waste of his feelings and his feelings would never be reciprocated.

He... was still reluctant to get over her.

Brian left, and Grace asked the medical staff looking after her to ask Jason for her phone.

After all, she had been rushed into the hospital due to an emergency. She did not have her phone with her now.

Half an hour later, the medical staff gave her the phone.

When handing her the phone, the medical staff whispered to her, "Mrs. Reed, Mr. Reed is still outside the ward. Do you want him to come in?"

"No," she said. Perhaps she was not yet ready to see him.
