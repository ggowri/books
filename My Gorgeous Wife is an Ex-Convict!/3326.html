Fortunately, someone else's child will also be her son-in-law in the future. It is said that Lina is hoping to transform her own daughter's stubborn genes with Chandler's good learning genes.

However, although prenatal education by reading fairy tales may seem useless to Brian, given the existence of a negative example like Tina.

But he still reads a few stories to the unborn child every day, and can't help but imagine the scene of holding a soft little one and telling stories after the child is born.

He has imagined this scene many times since Valda became pregnant.

Just then, Valda, who was listening to the fairy tale, suddenly changed her expression and let out a sharp scream.

"What's wrong?" Brian immediately became nervous. "Are you uncomfortable somewhere? Or is your leg cramping again?" He said, trying to lift the blanket covering her legs.

Valda's face turned pale and she said, "No... it's not cramping. I think, maybe, my water broke."

Upon hearing this, Brian quickly told Valda to lie down and placed a pillow under her to prevent more amniotic fluid from flowing out. Then he immediately made a phone call.

Before this, Brian had prepared various emergency plans, so Valda quickly got into a professional ambulance and headed to the designated hospital.

Valda couldn't help but sigh again as she looked at the car.

In her previous understanding, if her water broke or there was an emergency, she would call 120 and an ambulance would be dispatched.

As a result, he went ahead and bought an ambulance with all the necessary equipment on board. He even hired qualified medical personnel who are available 24/7 to provide emergency assistance.

So at this moment, even though Valda's water has broken, she is feeling quite calm now, except for the initial panic.

After all, her beloved husband has already made complete preparations for her sake.

Brian is actually feeling nervous, in contrast to her relaxed demeanor.

Even though the doctor next to him had already told Brian that Valda was doing fine, Grace's face remained pale and her hands were trembling slightly.

Even so, he tried to calm Valda down by saying, "It's okay, don't worry. You will be able to give birth to the baby smoothly."

To take a step back, even if there is a real issue and a choice needs to be made, all he wants is for her to be okay.

He can give up everything else!

He didn't say these words in front of her.

Valda thinks that something is up right now, and it seems to be him instead.

"I will take good care of giving birth to the child. I am aware of the precautions, as well as the steps and procedures involved in childbirth," Valda said as she reached out and held Brian's hand.

His hands are very cold at the moment, and with the trembling, one can imagine his nervousness.

"It's okay, Brian. I'll be fine," Valda reassured him. As a man who was always in control, Brian had never seen him so nervous before. But Valda knew that his anxiety was all because of her.

"Yes, everything will be fine, we will be safe," Brian lowered his head and kissed Valda's hand, as if trying to calm his emotions at the moment.
