Chapter 3950

Although the expressions on the secretary and the high-level executives behind her didn't change much, their gazes all focused on Nancy's delicate hand held by Mick.

Nancy's face turned red when she saw so many people coming in, and she wanted to pull her hand back.

But Mick didn't let go, instead he tightened his grip on her hand, "Don't move!"

"Um...you guys have business to discuss, right? No need to massage me, I can do it myself," Nancy said awkwardly.

"Do you know the acupoints on your hand?" he asked.

"..." She was speechless, she really didn't know.

"It'll just take a little while longer, won't delay much time," Mick said.

So, under the gaze of the high-level executives, Nancy received a hand massage from Mick. In order to avoid moaning out loud, she had to desperately suppress the urge to shout, making her face even redder.

Finally, Mick stopped his hand movements, and Nancy felt like she had just gone through a disaster.

While Mick was discussing business with the executives, Nancy left Mick's office.

The soreness in her hands from being massaged seemed to have turned into a warm sensation, and her finger joints felt more comfortable as she moved them.

It seemed that the massage earlier had been quite effective.

Nancy went to the restroom, and as she was about to open the door to leave the stall, she heard someone outside say:

"Mr. Reed has been keeping that woman in his office a lot lately. She doesn't seem to be a socialite or anything. I wonder who she is."

"Could she be some kind of minor celebrity? But she doesn't even look that good!"

"Women like her won't last long by Mr. Reed's side."

"In a little while, she'll realize that it's not easy to enter the Reed family. A man like Mr. Reed can have any woman he wants! This woman is just daydreaming."

The voices outside gradually faded away until there were no other sounds in the restroom, and Nancy finally came out.

Although the words she had just heard were hurtful, she was mentally prepared for them. In the eyes of outsiders, this was probably how she appeared.

But she had never daydreamed, because she was always clear about her goals.

When she achieved her goals in the future, she would eventually leave Mick's side. This experience was just a memory in her life.

After leaving the restroom, Nancy walked to a VIP waiting area on this floor. There were many Reed Group internal publications on a nearby display rack, and Nancy casually picked up one to read.

When she turned to a page in the publication, she was surprised to see a photo of Mick.

It was a photo from an e-sports competition. Based on the text and accompanying images, it seemed to be a competition that the group had held, and the winner had played a one-on-one match with Mick as a post-competition activity.

In this individual final, Mick directly defeated the champion.

Seeing this, Nancy remembered that it was indeed the case. At that time, she was still in college and heard about it from her dormitory mates.
