Chapter 4160 Have some snacks

Adele looked deeply at the woman in front of her, as if she saw her own reflection from years ago.

Back then, she also had the courage to believe that love only required unwavering courage!

But in the end...

"Regardless, you would be willing to help him relieve his pain, right?" Adele asked.

"Yes!" Tina said without any hesitation.

"Good. Just don't forget the words you said to me today," Adele said, then turned and left, leaving Tina confused.

What... did she come to find her just to say these things?

Was it to test whether she was truly committed to Chandler and willing to have his child?

After a while, Chandler returned.

"Did you finish paying your respects?" she asked.

"Hmm," he said.

"Do you always have to go to pay respects every time you come back?" she asked curiously.

"More or less. If I'm staying at the Hunter family, then on Friday nights we would pay respects," Chandler said.

"But the person in the portrait didn't harm your family, yet you still insist on paying respects to her?" Tina asked, puzzled.

"It's said to appease her anger, and over time, it became a tradition for the Hunter family," Chandler replied.

"When you're paying respects, do you ever secretly curse the person in the portrait?" After all, it was because of this woman that he was in so much pain.

"I used to, but not anymore," Chandler said.

"Why not anymore?" she asked, curious.

He looked at her with tender eyes, "If the parasites didn't exist in my body, then I wouldn't have met you. I wouldn't have noticed your existence when I saw Lina, I wouldn't have saved you, and I wouldn't have known that you are such a lovely person worth loving!"

So, gradually, his mindset started to change.

Before she was born, he would complain about the unfairness of fate. Every time the pain in his body flared up, he would resent why he had to have such a body, why such a curse had to exist.

But later, as she spent more time with him, he even began to feel fortunate that fate bound him to this curse, connecting his destiny with Tina's.

Suddenly, Tina felt her heart race. Facing the gaze of the man in front of her, she had an urge to forcefully press him down beneath her!

However, before she could act on this impulse, the sound of a knock interrupted them. This time, it was the maid delivering late-night snacks.

"Madam asked to send some late-night snacks for the young master and Miss Stephenson," the maid said.

"I'm feeling a bit hungry," Tina quickly said, trying to divert her attention. Thankfully, she didn't really lay a hand on Chandler earlier, otherwise she would've been caught red-handed by the servant.

Even though she had a thick skin, being in the man's house made it inappropriate.

"Just put it down," Chandler said.

"Yes!" The servant placed the tray of food on the coffee table in the bedroom.

Once the servant left, Tina started enjoying the snacks. She had to admit, the Hunter family's snacks were indeed delicious! She couldn't help but want to have another one.

"Do you like them?" Chandler asked.

"Mm, they're quite tasty," Tina replied, picking up another one and offering it to Chandler's lips, clearly intending to feed him.

Chandler raised an eyebrow slightly. He didn't really like the snacks at home, but at this moment, he obediently opened his mouth and took a bite.

Just as he was about to take another bite, Tina suddenly trembled and dropped the snack on the floor, accompanied by a moan from her mouth...
