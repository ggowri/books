Chapter 3599

But her next words made him breathe a sigh of relief.

"It's your grandfather's illness that you're worried about, right? I think he's doing okay mentally, and the doctor also said that his physical condition is good. He just needs to stay in the hospital for a few more days and then he can come back," Jasper said.

He paused for a moment. Did she think... he was playing the piano so late at night because he was worried about his grandfather?

But it was fine, at least he didn't need to come up with any more excuses.

So William nodded and said, "I... am worried about my grandfather."

"Okay, don't worry! Otherwise, I'll have my dad find some specialists from Emerald City to come and consult with your grandfather?" she said, as if comforting a child, and patted his head.

"No need for that. Since the experts from Deer Capital said there's no big problem, it should be true." William let go of Jasper's hand and placed it on his cheek.

Her warm palm made him feel at ease.

"Jasper, thank you."

She chuckled, "What are you thanking me for? I haven't done anything to deserve your thanks."

"Just being by my side now is enough for me to thank you," he said.

Thank her for being there at this moment, which prevented his emotions from getting out of control.

Without her, perhaps his emotions would have been even more shattered at this moment.

She smiled and said, "Okay, it's getting late now. Let's go back to our rooms and sleep. I want to go to the hospital to see my grandpa again tomorrow!"

"Okay," he replied.
