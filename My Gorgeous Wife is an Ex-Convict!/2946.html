Chapter 2944

"When you two get married in the future, you must give me a matchmaker's red envelope," Grace joked.

Valda Theller smiled, wondering what her future with Brian Hart would be like.

After chatting with Grace for over an hour, Valda Theller got up to leave. As she walked down the stairs, she saw Jason Reed coming back.

"Valda Theller, long time no see," Jason Reed said, looking up at her.

"Not really," Valda Theller replied. "I came to see Grace and the baby today."

"I see," Jason Reed suddenly said. "I heard you and Brian Hart broke up?"

Valda Theller stiffened. "Mr. Reed, what do you want to say?"

"I don't mean anything else. I just hope it won't affect Grace's recovery. Everything else is not important to me," Jason Reed said slowly.

Valda Theller looked at the man in front of her, who could easily turn the world upside down in Emerald City, but for him, all that mattered was Grace.

As a woman, how could she not envy that?

"I think the same way," Valda Theller said.

"Don't you hate Grace?" Jason Reed asked. Valda Theller had heard Brian Hart talking about Grace in the hospital that day.

"If I hated her, I wouldn't be standing here now. I don't hate her because love is never about right or wrong. I just envy Grace and regret why the person who met Brian Hart when we were young wasn't me," Valda Theller said.

"I have the same regret," Jason Reed said. "I also regret why Grace didn't meet me when we were young."

At that moment, Valda Theller suddenly felt that Jason Reed was not so unattainable.

"Grace doesn't know that you and Brian Hart have broken up yet. At least I hope you can continue to pretend to be in a relationship with him in front of her until she recovers. Consider it a favor I owe you," Jason Reed said.

For ordinary people to make Mr. Reed of Emerald City owe them a favor was truly a rare opportunity.

"No need," Valda Theller shook her head. "Maybe Brian Hart and I don't need to pretend in front of Grace."

"Oh?" Jason Reed raised an eyebrow. Did that mean she was planning to get back together with Brian Hart? "Aren't you afraid it will be a difficult road?" he hinted.

She pursed her lips. "It may be difficult, but I still want to try."

After saying that, Valda Theller walked away. Jason Reed watched her leave and smiled slightly. Brian Hart... he really had good taste.
