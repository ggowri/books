Chapter 3788

"Your phone was off, and I couldn't find you, so I came here to try my luck and see if you were here." Veronica's voice trembled slightly as she spoke.

Although she had mentally prepared herself for his anger and perhaps even fury, when she actually saw him, the look in his eyes that kept people at a distance still made her heart feel like it was being stabbed.

"What do you want from me?" Mason's voice sounded again.

"I... I don't want there to be any misunderstandings between us, so I wanted to find you and explain what happened at the hospital today." She steadied herself and spoke.

"What's there to explain about the hospital?" He said nonchalantly.

"There's plenty!" She said urgently, "I really don't care about Daniel, I don't have any feelings for him anymore. I just don't want you to get into unnecessary trouble because of beating him up. The person I'm worried about is you!"

"If you're really worried about me, then you could have left with me when I did. Why did you stay in the hospital with Daniel?" he asked.

"Well...there was no one else around at the time, and I needed to find doctors and nurses to treat his injuries," she quickly replied.

"And after that? After the doctors and nurses came, you still stayed with him? You waited until his injuries were treated and talked to him for a while before leaving the hospital?" Mason continued.

Veronica's heart skipped a beat.

How did Mason know that she only left the hospital after Daniel's injuries were treated and she talked to him? Did he see everything?

"You..." she opened her mouth, but didn't know how to answer.

"Are you wondering how I knew? Do you know how long I waited for you at the hospital entrance?" he said coldly.

He waited for her at the hospital entrance, hoping she would come out and tell him that he was the most important person to her.

But time passed by and she didn't show up.

He told himself that she would come out when he counted to 100.

But even after counting to 10,000, she still didn't come out.

So he closed his phone, drove his car aimlessly on the streets of Emerald City, not knowing where to go.

His emotions were once again in turmoil.

It was like the feeling he had when they first separated.

He thought he would never experience that feeling again, but he did.

He thought that by hypnotizing his memories, he could avoid feeling the pain of that emotion again.

But reality made him feel it again.

Is this punishment for him? Punishing him for not learning his lesson, even though he had been hurt by Veronica before, he still rushed to her to get hurt again!

"Yes, I'm sorry..." She stammered, "I waited in the hospital until it was confirmed that Daniel's injury was not serious before leaving. I was afraid that he would really have a serious problem and you would get into trouble. I didn't mean to..."

But before she could finish her words, he suddenly interrupted her, "You don't need to explain anything to me."

"I'm telling you this because I don't want you to misunderstand. Daniel and I really don't have anything..."

"Even if you really have something with him, it has nothing to do with me." He said coldly.

A sense of unease surged in Veronica's heart, "What do you mean by that?"

"It means we're breaking up." He said these words in a cold tone, and there was no expression on his handsome face, only indifference.
