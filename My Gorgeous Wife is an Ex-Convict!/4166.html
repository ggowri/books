Chapter 4164 Let's Get Married

"I know," Chandler replied, "but I want to marry Tina. Besides, marrying her won't hinder her studies. After we get married, she can continue doing what she loves and wants to do. I won't stand in her way."

"So just because you want to marry, I should marry my daughter to you right now?" Hadwin gave him a look that said, "Don't even think about it."

Tina couldn't help but speak up, "Dad, it's me who wants to marry Chandler as soon as possible. Chandler was forced to agree because of me."

Hadwin turned to look at his daughter, "You forced him to marry you?"

"Yes," Tina quickly nodded.

Hadwin frowned, "What sweet words did Chandler say to you that made you speak up for him? Let me tell you, a man's sweet words are the least trustworthy!"

Tina had the urge to comment, did Dad forget that he himself was a man?!

And she heard from Mom that Dad used to say sweet words to Mom too!

"Um... I swear, Chandler really didn't say any sweet words!" Tina quickly raised her hand to assure.

"Then why do you want to marry Chandler as soon as possible?" Hadwin said impatiently.

"Because I might already be pregnant, Dad. Don't you want your grandchild to be born out of wedlock? Or do you want me to get married with a big belly?" Tina touched her belly and said.

Hadwin almost choked on his breath and it took him a while to extend his hand, pointing at his daughter, "What did you say? You're pregnant?"

"Um... there's a possibility," Tina added, after all, she still didn't know if she was actually pregnant or not, "But in the Hunter family, I had sex with Chandler without protection, so..."

Although Chandler told him that his mother drugged her in the pastries, so she acted that way after eating them.

But in fact, she felt that even if there was no drug, maybe she wouldn't be able to resist anyway.

The drug simply unleashed her desires, and she had always hoped to marry Chandler and have children early.

So Tina wasn't really against Auntie Adele drugging her.

After all, the other party did it with the hope that Chandler could resolve this source of pain sooner.

But before Tina finished speaking, Hadwin had already grabbed Chandler by the collar, glaring at him angrily, "Is this how you followed my instructions?"

"I'm sorry," Chandler apologized.

"You should be sorry!" Hadwin punched Chandler directly in the face.

Chandler staggered back a few steps, his cheek red and his lips bleeding, showing a hint of blood.

Tina was dumbfounded, watching her father about to throw a second punch. She quickly stepped forward and stood in front of Chandler. "Dad, I... I already said it earlier. It was me who forced myself on him. Chandler was coerced."

Hadwin sneered, "If he didn't want it, could you really force him? Tina, move aside!"

Men understand men the most. In Hadwin's eyes, Chandler deliberately seduced his own daughter!

"Enough!" Lina, who had been standing by, suddenly spoke up. "If Tina and Chandler truly want to get married, then let them!"
