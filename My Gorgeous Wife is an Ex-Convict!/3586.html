Chapter 3584

 

Jasper frowned, not understanding what he meant.

Suddenly, Roger asked, "Can you play 'On My Way'?"

Jasper was taken aback. This was the theme song from the animated series "Pleasant Goat and Big Big Wolf". She had watched the show when she was a child, and had even played this particular tune on the piano.

Especially now that she was at the welfare home, she had prepared many songs suitable for children before coming.

"Sure," Jasper replied.

"Then let's play together, four hands on the piano. Is that okay with you?" he said.

"Huh?" Jasper was stunned. Before she could react, Roger had already taken her wrist and led her to the piano, where they both sat down.

Playing a four-hand piano piece is not a difficult task for her.

However, she has never practiced with Roger before and is unsure if they can coordinate well.

After Roger explained each person's part, he said, "Let's begin."

As his voice fell, his fingers had already pressed down on the keys.

Beautiful and lively music flowed out from his fingertips.

Jasper pursed her lips and began playing on the piano keys, in coordination with Roger's playing.

Although it was their first time playing a four-hand piece together, they had an unusual sense of harmony, as if they had played together many times before.

The children listened with great interest, and some even sang along with the music, happily.

After the performance, Roger complimented Jasper, "You played well."

"Not at all, you coordinated well too," she replied. "Do you...um, often play four-hand pieces with others?"

"No, you're the first," he replied.

Her eyes were filled with surprise. So, he had never played a four-hand piece with anyone else before?

But their performance just now was so...

"But after playing with you, I feel like we have a good understanding in our piano accompaniment, don't you think?" he said.

They did have a good understanding, considering it was their first time playing together like this, and the end result was quite impressive.

Even Meave, who was listening on the side, pulled Jasper over and whispered, "Have you been in contact with Roger privately? Your four-hand playing is too in sync."

"No, today is the first time," Jasper replied.

Meave was amazed, and Roger went on to play several more pieces for the children. When there was a break in the middle, Jasper handed out small gifts he had brought for the children.

One little girl who received a gift didn't leave right away, but kept staring at Jasper and asked, "Do you like Roger?"

Jasper almost choked on his own saliva and asked, "What do you mean by 'like'?"

"So, do you want to marry Roger?" the little girl explained.

Jasper was sweating profusely. Are children nowadays so mature at such a young age? She's only a few years old and already asking these kinds of questions!

"Of course not," Jasper quickly replied.

The little girl looked at her skeptically, clearly not believing her words.
