Chapter 4264 May You Have a Safe and Happy Life

Tina looked at the divorce agreement handed to her and suddenly felt a sense of bewilderment.

Is this really what she wanted?

Is it really okay to divorce Chandler like this?

"So according to the contract, when I am in good health and the doctor considers me fit for pregnancy, we will go to the hospital for artificial insemination..."

Before she could finish her sentence, he interrupted her.

"No need," Chandler said.

Tina was stunned. She never expected Chandler to say that.

"But you said..."

"Yes, what you heard that day was only part of it. Breaking the curse doesn't necessarily require the umbilical cord blood and placenta of a child. There are other ways, albeit more difficult. But as you said, the birth of a child should not be solely to save someone's life. It should be because two people love each other. I don't want this child to resent me for the reason of its birth, and I don't want you to have this child for such a reason!"

So there are other methods?

After hearing this, Tina didn't know whether to feel relieved or not.

But there was a hint of disappointment in her heart!

It was as if the bond between them had diminished!

Oh my God, Tina, what are you thinking! She shouted at herself in her mind.

Isn't it better this way? Since she chose to divorce him, their bond should be as little as possible!

"Since you put it that way, then I won't say anything," Tina said, getting up to leave.

"Tina!" Chandler also stood up. "Wait!"

She turned to look at him. "Is there something else?"

"Can I hug you again?" he asked.

She hesitated for a moment, and he quickly added, "Just a hug. I won't bother you anymore. Even if I want to visit our daughter, if you don't want to see me, I can have someone bring Quinn over and then send her back to you at the agreed time."

His tone was almost humble.

Tina felt her heart become heavy!

If possible, she didn't want him to speak to her in such a humble tone. It only made her feel uncomfortable.

"Okay!" she agreed and opened her arms willingly. "Just a hug. And even though we're divorced, if you want to visit our daughter, you can come anytime. I won't consider it a bother!"

He stared at her and walked up to embrace her!

The person he loved the most!

He had always thought that there would be a good ending between them, but he never expected that their ending would lead to them becoming strangers.

If they didn't have a daughter, maybe in the future, they would truly just be two strangers.

The future...

Thinking about this, Chandler self-mockingly wondered if he even had a future.

But there was no need for Tina to know these thoughts.

All he could do was set Tina free and not let her be trapped in his destiny.

Chandler held Tina tightly, feeling the warmth and breath in his arms, feeling that she was alive!

At least, as long as she was living well, that was the most important thing. Everything else didn't matter.

"Tina, whether you believe it or not, I love you. I wish you a safe and happy life!"
