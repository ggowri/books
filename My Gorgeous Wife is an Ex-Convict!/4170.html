Chapter 4168 Buying Hot Searches

Even Tina, who was used to Chandler's behavior, was stunned by his devout prayer.

In that moment, she found it almost impossible to take her eyes off him.

When Chandler stood up and looked at Tina, she suddenly snapped back to reality.

"What's wrong?" he asked.

"I didn't expect you to kneel and bow. I thought you didn't believe," she said.

Chandler smiled faintly, his smile melting away his usual coldness. "Since it's said that the more sincere the heart, the stronger the bond, let's make our bond even stronger."

Tina couldn't help but embrace Chandler. How could she not love a man like him?

After leaving the temple of Cupid, Tina took Chandler to the Marriage Hall.

Here, various marriage-related merchandise is sold, and many couples come here for good luck.

Tina spotted a string of bracelets made of alternating white crystal and red agate. They looked quite nice.

And the meaning was quite good - "Eternal unity."

"What do you think of this bracelet?" Tina asked.

"It's nice," Chandler replied.

"Then let's buy two strings and each wear one," she said with great interest.

It would be nice to wear couple bracelets.

He gently said, "Okay." His eyes were full of fondness.

Tina bought two strings of bracelets, and they each wore one.

The bracelets were slightly more feminine, and Tina looked at Chandler in his neat suit, wearing such a bracelet on his wrist. It gave off a feeling of mismatch.

But Chandler didn't think it was inappropriate at all. On the contrary, his lips curved into a shallow smile. "I hope this bracelet can really make us stay united forever!"

"Of course it will!" Tina said.

Just then, a internet celebrity next to them was filming a dancing video. Many onlookers were taking photos. Among them, someone was taking photos and stepping backwards, about to collide with Tina. Chandler quickly raised his hand to protect her.

Tina was already quite tall among girls, but being protected like this by Chandler, her whole body was almost engulfed in his embrace, giving off a delicate feeling.

"Be careful!" Chandler warned the person who was stepping back and taking photos.

"Y-yes, sorry," the person quickly apologized.

This was originally just a small incident, but Tina didn't expect that it would become a hot topic the next day.

The moment when Chandler protected her was captured and uploaded online, along with the scene of Chandler devoutly kneeling and bowing to Cupid.

At first, people were discussing Chandler's handsome appearance and boyfriend material.

Then someone started digging into Chandler's identity and discovered that he was the CEO of the Hunter Group, and the financial and political power behind him was difficult to estimate.

Furthermore, someone pointed out that the woman Chandler protected was Tina, the heiress of the White Maple Group and Chandler's fiancée.

Suddenly, the popularity soared and eventually became a hot search.

Of course, Tina also contributed to this hot search. Seeing the popularity, Tina decided to buy another hot search, indicating that the two of them are about to get married.

Chandler looked at the hot search Tina bought and couldn't help but cry and laugh, "You bought a hot search?"

"Yes," Tina nodded candidly.

"Why did you buy a hot search?" he asked.
