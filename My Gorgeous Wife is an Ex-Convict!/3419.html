Chapter 3417

 

Daniel couldn't help but furrow his brow.

At that moment, a teacher heard about the fight and rushed over. Upon seeing some people lying on the ground, the teacher immediately approached and asked, "What's going on? Are you guys okay?"

Daniel gave a bitter smile, "There might be something important."

"Who did it?" the teacher asked.

"It's Mason!" a classmate exclaimed.

The teacher was taken aback, and his face immediately turned pale. His gaze towards Daniel and his group was filled with worry.

After all, that is the second son of the Reed family! Getting into a conflict and even fighting with someone, if the Reed family were to retaliate against these students because of this...

It's easy to imagine the consequences that these students, and even their families, will face.

After seeing the teacher's expression, Daniel suddenly remembered something. Oh right, the boy who fought with them is named Mason.

The surname Reed...

He had heard before that the second son of the Reed family was studying in their school as a sophomore.

The Reed family refers to the first wealthy and influential family in Emerald City.

At that time, when he heard this gossip, he didn't think much of it. After all, even if he was a rich young master, he had no connection with him.

After all, he never intended to curry favor with anyone!

He just didn't expect that the other person turned out to be Veronica's "little brother", and they even had a fight today.

When he thought of the Reed family, Daniel's heart sank.

Veronica was dragged by Jasper to a secluded spot in the school.

"Where are you taking me?" Veronica asked, feeling the pain in her wrist from his grip. "Mason, stop and let's talk!"

His footsteps finally stopped, and he turned to look at her.

She breathed a sigh of relief and said, "Can you please let go of my hand? You're hurting me!"

He hesitated for a moment, then lowered his head and loosened his grip on her hand. He saw the red marks on her wrist, which he had caused.

"I'm sorry," he murmured, carefully lifting his hand and gently touching the red marks on her wrist.

Veronica said, "You should be apologizing to the people you hit earlier, not me!"

This statement caused his expression to suddenly change. "Do you think it was my fault?" he asked.

"It was your fault. You can't just hit people for no reason," she replied.

"But I didn't like them talking about you dating that guy," he said, his voice sounding muffled and clearly unhappy.

"They were just joking around. It doesn't mean anything," she said.

"What if they weren't joking? What if that guy really wants to date you? Would you agree?" he asked.

"Hmm?" Veronica paused for a moment, she hadn't really thought about this question before.

It seems that from childhood to adulthood, no boy has ever pursued her. During her time in elementary and middle school, he almost completely occupied her time.

Actually, she has thought about what kind of boyfriend she would have in the future.
