Chapter 1968

Emily Flowers was on the phone with the mother of the Tang family's heiress, explaining last night's accident.

"Don't worry. Since it didn’t work this time, there's always another chance. I hope your daughter will be my daughter-in-law. Yesterday was just a small accident..."

Before she could finish, she heard the butler's voice. "Young... Young Master, why did you bring so many people here? Did... Did something happen?"

Then, Hadwin Stephenson's cold voice sounded. "Get lost!"

Emily Flowers looked up and saw her son walking in with a group of his men. Her expression changed slightly, and she said to Mrs. Tang on the other end of the line, "Excuse me, Mrs. Tang. I have something to attend to. I'll talk to you later."

After ending the call, Emily Flowers looked at her son and said, "Hadwin, what are your intentions for coming here with such a large group of people?"

Hadwin Stephenson walked to the couch and sat down. He said to the men behind him, "Wait outside."

"Yes." The men filed out. Now, only Hadwin Stephenson, Emily Flowers, and the butler were left in the living room.

Emily Flowers gave the butler a look, and he left the living room as well.

Hadwin Stephenson looked indifferent. He casually took the newspaper Emily Flowers was reading earlier from the coffee table and looked through it.

Although one could read the news online now, Emily Flowers still maintained the habit of reading newspapers.

Compared to Hadwin Stephenson's indifference, Emily Flowers felt uneasy. Her son probably brought so many people here to question her about what had happened yesterday.

The older her son grew, the more unpredictable he became. At some point, she could no longer figure out what was on her son's mind.

"Hadwin, you've asked everyone to leave. Is there something you want to tell me?" asked Emily Flowers when she already knew the answer.

Hadwin Stephenson seemed to say nonchalantly, "I do have to tell you something, Mom. I hope you can temporarily live abroad and then come back after a few years."

"What?" Emily Flowers instantly stood up and stared at her son in front of her. "What did you say? Are you kicking me abroad?"

"I’m not kicking you there. I'm just 'asking' you to go there," Hadwin Stephenson said indifferently.

Emily Flowers narrowed her beautiful eyes that were adorned with delicate makeup. "What if I don't want to? I'm doing well here. I don't want to go abroad."

"If you won't do it, I'll have to have people stationed outside your mansion at all times. I'll have them removed whenever you agree to move," said Hadwin Stephenson.

Emily Flowers immediately snapped. "Stationed? Are you trying to lock me up in this mansion? Like a prisoner?"

"Think of it however you want to, Mom," replied Hadwin Stephenson.
