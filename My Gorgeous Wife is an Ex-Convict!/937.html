"I wanted to see you, so I didn’t care about the time. Hadwin, I think I'm lucky that our feelings are mutual," she said, her voice sounding sweet.

Compared to Grace, she was much luckier, and therefore, she felt even more sorry for Grace.

"Why do you say that all of a sudden?" Hadwin looked at the person in front of him. He was pleased to hear these words from her, but he could not understand why she was saying these all of a sudden as if she had gotten inspired.

"I went to see Grace today. She said she and Jason are back to being brothers and sisters again because of some things. I know Grace wants to get over Jason, but this situation will only make it worse," said Lina.

Lina said angrily after a pause, "I don't know what Jason is thinking. He seems to care about Grace a lot, but he's the one who wanted to break up with Grace. They all said women are unpredictable, but I think he's the unpredictable one."

"Jason is still in love with Grace," Hadwin said suddenly.

"What?" Lina was stunned. "He's still in love with her?"

"Yes." He had seen the way Jason looked at Grace after breaking up. As a man, he could feel that Jason's feelings for Grace were not over at all. Instead, it was... bordering on madness.

"Then why did he want to break up? He said he was tired and wanted to stop loving her!" Lina asked in confusion.

"You'll have to ask him this." Hadwin rubbed Lina's forehead. "Alright, don't get involved in other people's business."

"Other people? It's Grace!" Lina frowned disapprovingly. 'Grace and the others are not the same. We're besties, even sisters!'

"Yes, yes!" answered Hadwin, knowing how important Grace was to the woman. Even Hadwin would often get jealous because of that.

He suspected that if one day he and Grace fell into the water at the same time, the girl would not hesitate to save Grace first.

"Hadwin, let's not break up for no reason, alright?" she asked.

He only found it awkward. 'What does she mean by breaking up for no reason? We can never break up!'

"Have you forgotten? I told you that if we’re really dating, then we would never break up! Lina, if you dare break up with me, you're dead meat!"

Lina finally recalled it. 'We made a promise never to break up at the beginning of our relationship!'

"Hehe!" She smiled, then moved her hands from his waist to his neck before wrapping them around to make him bend down a little. She stood on her tiptoes and kissed him!

This kiss was spontaneous and passionate. All of a sudden, his eyes darkened as he opened his mouth to respond to her kiss…

She was the only one he allowed to do as she pleased.

Once the kiss was over, Lina wanted more. Her round cat-like eyes stared at Hadwin. Licking her lips, she said, "I think we can have a deeper kiss."

Raising his eyebrows, he looked at her. "Do you know what happens when you kiss a man in the middle of the night like this?" He was not rational enough to maintain his self-control after her ‘deep’ kisses.

He even had to desperately restrain himself from pinning her down on the bed during the kiss earlier.

She blinked, then blushed as if she understood what he meant. However, after a moment's hesitation, she stood on her tiptoes and wanted to go in for a kiss again.

His long fingers touched her lips, and the tips of their noses were almost touching. He asked hoarsely, "Aren't you afraid?"
