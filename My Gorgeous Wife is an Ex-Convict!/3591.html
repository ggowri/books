But Roger also said he liked her, yet his gaze didn't give her the same feeling of being liked. Instead, it made her feel a sense of danger.

His face was close, his breath spraying on her face, and his sexy lips slowly opened, "So what do I have to do for you to believe me?"

"Even if the other person doesn't like you back, it doesn't matter?" she asked, puzzled.

"It's okay, because I know she will eventually fall in love with me," he said with a confident look in his eyes.

Jasper was stunned. The way this person spoke made it seem like he was telling the truth and truly believed it.

"You...are really confident," she chuckled nervously.

"Is it bad to be confident?" he asked in return.

Her face immediately turned red. "What makes you so sure that I will like you?"

"Do you believe in destiny?" he asked.

"What do you mean?"

"From the moment I first laid eyes on you, I knew that we were destined to be together," he replied.

"Yes, they are destined to have a doomed fate with each other!" he silently said to himself.

When Jasper returned to the dormitory, he saw the other three roommates staring at him.

"What's going on?" she asked, confused.

Jasper felt a headache coming on and asked, "What are you guys talking about?"

"Someone posted your photos on the school forum. You're not planning to deny it, are you?" Meave said, pulling up the forum post and showing it to Jasper.
