Chapter 3551

 

Just then, Jasper's phone rang and he checked to see that it was a call from William.

When she answered, William's voice came through, "Jasper, where are you? I heard from your dorm mates that you didn't come back to the dorm this afternoon?"

"I'm outside now, it'll be a while before I can get back to school," she said.

"Then I'll wait for you and we'll have dinner together," William said.

Just as Jasper was about to agree, Roger next to her said, "It's already dinner time, let's find a place nearby to eat first."

"Huh?" Jasper was taken aback, and William on the other end of the phone also heard Roger's voice.

"Jasper, who are you with?" he asked.

"I'm with Roger now, um... William, why don't you skip dinner with me and I'll come back to school after I finish eating outside."

On the phone, after a moment of silence, William said, "Okay, do you want me to come pick you up later?"

"No, it's okay. I'll come back after I finish eating," Jasper said.

After ending the call, she looked up and saw Roger looking at her with an indescribable expression.

"What's wrong?" she asked.

"Nothing," he shrugged. "The person who called you earlier, was it your friend?"

"Yeah, we've known each other since we were kids. His name is William," she said, watching his reaction.

But to her disappointment, his face remained calm after hearing the name.

"Have you heard of this name before?" she asked.

"I have heard of it," he said.

She immediately asked excitedly, "When did you hear of this name?"

"William, your childhood friend Jasper's name is well-known in school. You can find out about him just by checking the school forum," he said.

"I see," she sighed. Ever since their scandalous photos were posted on the school forum, the popular threads were all about speculating the relationship between the three of them.

"How else do you think I found out?" he retorted.

She touched her nose and once again guessed whether he was Lawrence or not. She wondered if his "I don't know" was intentional concealment.

"Let's go eat," he said, leading the way.

She quickly followed behind him.

The two of them found a noodle shop. The shop was small with an old-fashioned decor, but it was quite crowded inside and had good business.

Roger found a seat and Jasper sat down with him. However, when it came to choosing what kind of noodles to order, she just stared at the menu in a daze.

"What, don't want to eat noodles here? Do you think the environment is too poor? The food is too simple?" Roger taunted.

She looked up at him and asked, "Do you really dislike me?"

"What do you mean?" he asked in return.

"Because you always seem to enjoy mocking me," she said. "I don't mind eating noodles here, and I don't think the environment is bad or the food is simple. I didn't always live a privileged life. Before I was five years old, my mom and I ate simple box lunches and sat on the side of the road eating bread..."

Although those were memories from before she was five years old, many of them were still clear in her mind, and she remembered what life was like back then.
