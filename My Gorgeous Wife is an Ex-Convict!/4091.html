Chapter 4089

Soon after, two people entered the room, chatting while cleaning.

Tina considered revealing herself and explaining her presence, but then heard one of the cleaners say:

"The young master came back today and is currently taking a medicinal bath. I heard that a servant tried to sneak in during his last bath to seduce him, but was scared off and went crazy. No one knows where they went after Hunter dealt with them."

"How did they go crazy? Did something happen inside?"

"Who knows? But during the young master's medicinal baths, no one else is allowed to be inside. I heard that if anyone dares to peek, their eyes will be gouged out."

"That's...terrifying!"

Two cleaning maids were chatting, and Tina, who was hiding under the desk, shuddered!

They mentioned Chandler.

And Chandler is now taking a medicinal bath?! And it seems quite mysterious according to what they said!

"Anyway, when the young master is taking a medicinal bath, no one can enter!"

After the two maids left, Tina crawled out from under the desk, pondering over their conversation just now.

Anyway, Chandler is now taking a medicinal bath, and no one else can intrude!

But... that's other people, so it shouldn't matter for her! Tina thought to herself.

Of course, the most important thing now is - how can she find Chandler?

Fifteen minutes later, Tina unexpectedly found the place she wanted to find the most.

Originally, she was just trying her luck. After all, the mansion was so big that she might have been discovered by Hunter's other people before finding Chandler.

But she didn't know whether to say that she was lucky or not.

She actually found Chandler first.

However, at this moment, Tina's nose was filled with the pungent smell of medicine. She saw a familiar figure not far away, soaking in the medicinal water. The smooth back that she remembered was now covered with black lines.

What's going on?

"Get out!" A voice suppressed with pain surged from his throat.

Tina's body suddenly trembled. She had never heard him make such a sound before, as if he was in pain, and even his voice was trembling.

Is he feeling very uncomfortable?

What happened?

And what are these black lines on his body?

Is it because something is wrong with his body that he needs to soak in this kind of potion?

One question after another kept popping up in Tina's mind.

Her feet couldn't help but move forward.

"Did you not hear me when I said not to tell anyone?" Another stern rebuke was followed by a splash, as if someone had stood up from the bath, and Tina felt the pungent potion splash all over her body!

Just then, there was a knocking sound outside the door. "Young Master, we found someone breaking in on the surveillance. Are you okay? Do you need us to come in?"

For a moment, Tina didn't know whether to describe the scene as chaotic or quiet.

Outside the door, the knocking and questioning continued.

Inside the room, however, there was silence, as Chandler stared at her in shock.
