"What, am I embarrassing you by surprising you like this?" Mick looked at Nancy's stunned expression and asked.

"Yes, I'm surprised." Nancy stammered, but what surprised her even more was - "You look so..."

"So?" Mick frowned slightly, not understanding what she meant.

"So beautiful." She finally managed to say.

He was taken aback, and the blush on his face became even more apparent!

Obviously, he didn't expect her to say something like that.

Beautiful? Since he started middle school, it seemed like no one had used that word to describe him, although he had heard a lot of compliments about his appearance, and the word "beautiful" was more often used to describe women.

But hearing it from her mouth at this moment gave him a strange feeling.

"Beautiful? Do you think I'm beautiful like this?" He leaned down, his face close to hers.

"I think the way you blush is very charming." Nancy said shamelessly.

"Can't I make you feel that way when I'm not blushing?" He asked, his warm breath spraying on her face.

"Normally, of course... um, I'm also very moved, but I feel like you're different from usual, and I haven't seen this side of you before, so I'm especially... moved. That blushing face just makes me want to kiss you hard and take a bite..."

Nancy quickly explained, but as she went on, she started sweating profusely.

Oh my god, what was she even saying? Would he think she's a pervert for talking about kissing and biting him?

"So, do you want to kiss and bite me?" He raised his hand and lightly touched her lips, and that handsome face seemed to easily evoke her deepest desires.

"Yes." Almost without thinking, the word slipped out of her mouth.

"Good." He said.

Huh? Nancy snapped out of it. What was he saying? Good?

What was good?

The next moment, Mick had already leaned in to her lips, "Then it's up to you, everything I have is yours, whether you want to bite or kiss, it's all good!"

Nancy stared at the man's flushed face in front of her.

Her heart felt even itchier!

Everything he had was hers?

When she heard this, she felt a warm feeling in her heart.

She kissed his cheek, "Mick, everything I have is yours too!"

It seemed like her love for him was growing more and more!

It's overflowing like it's too much...

...

A month later, Nancy was discharged from the hospital before the child.

Her physical recovery was good, although not yet fully recovered, but the rest needed time for slow adjustment.

Because of Nancy's current physical condition, she needed someone to take care of her, so she moved into the Reed family's mansion and stayed in Mick's room.

As for their daughter, Mick named her Sophia Reed.

When Nancy asked why they named the child "Sophia," Mick said, "Because this child was born too quickly, even before we were ready, so I hope that in her future life, she can slow down a bit. Sometimes, slowing down may be a kind of blessing."

"She already has a father like you, which is already a blessing." Nancy said, knowing that he would protect their daughter's life in the future.

"Having a mother like you is her blessing." Mick gazed at Nancy, his eyes becoming deep, and his voice becoming hoarse. "It's also my blessing."
