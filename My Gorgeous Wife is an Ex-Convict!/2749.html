Chapter 2747

'Yeah, Grace will be relieved if I can fall in love with Valda Theller. I won't be stuck in a hopeless relationship again, and my parents will be relieved too.

'But can I really fall in love with the woman in front of me? She doesn't annoy me, and I even find my time with her relaxing.'

Valda Theller stared at Brian with a shocked gaze. She thought Brian would give up on the idea after she said this. She did not expect him to... say so.

"What... What did you say? You want to date me while considering marriage?" she asked, finding her voice slightly out of tune.

"Yes," he answered confidently.

"Do you want me to... love you? Will you love me too?" she asked again.

"Yes, I will try to fall for you," he said.

"But can you fall for me?" She was doubtful. After all, he was so in love with Grace. Could he fall for another person?

He spoke honestly, "I don't know. I don't know if I can fall in love with you. It’ll be a gamble for you and me. No one can predict the outcome."

Valda Theller bit her lip. She could not believe that Brian agreed.

She was glad he said that now. Yeah, no one knew what would happen in the end.

His cold voice said, "Valda Theller, do you want to love me? Let's be a real couple. Make me fall in love with you!"

Thump! Thump! Thump!

She could hear her heart beating very clearly.

'Should I refuse? Or agree?

'Can I really make this man fall for me? Can I get him to move on?' She gazed at him deeply. After a long pause, she heard her voice reply, "Well, we're a real couple now."

It was only because... she did not want to lose him!

...

After dinner, Valda Theller was still deep in trance even though Brian had left the apartment.

'Am I really dating... Brian now?

'No more acting, but a real relationship? Brian even promised to try to fall for me!'

Valda Theller looked at her red face in the mirror after walking into the bathroom. She took a deep breath and said to herself, "Come on. Let's fight... for this!"

'Let's fight for this relationship!'

She knew she had fallen for Brian. She had never felt this emotion again after Raymond Bowman hurt her.
