Chapter 4188 What are you to me?

Finally, Kit drove away from the scene with Tina.

Along the way, Tina sat in the passenger seat, her head down, feeling restless.

Kit occasionally glanced at Tina from the corner of his eye.

Even though it had been over half a year since she disappeared after falling into the water, Emerald City was still searching for her. However, everyone had already lost hope, hoping only to find her body.

The Stephenson family had even prepared a grave for her, but they hadn't put her name on the tombstone.

Perhaps there was still a trace of hope deep in their hearts.

And he never imagined that he would find her faster than the Hunter family and the Stephenson family!

Because the Hunter family and the Stephenson family were mainly searching for Tina's whereabouts in Emerald City and the surrounding counties and cities.

So he deliberately stayed away from those areas.

After he left the entertainment industry, he simply followed the waterway where Tina disappeared and drove downstream.

Originally, he had only heard that there was a ritual in the village where Tina escaped tonight, so he wanted to go and take a look. He didn't expect to encounter Tina.

It was like... it was arranged by fate.

Kit took Tina to a small town dozens of miles away and checked into a small hotel in the town.

 

Tina looked up and met Kit's concerned eyes. "Tha... thank you..."

Kit led Tina into the hotel room.

It was a double room, but quite spacious. Besides the two beds, there was a small sitting area with a sofa and a coffee table.

Seeing Tina's uneasy expression, Kit explained, "You can sleep inside. You can choose any of the two beds. I'll sleep on the sofa. The reason I didn't book two rooms is because you're pregnant, and I'm worried about any unforeseen circumstances. If I were in another room, I wouldn't be able to know. So... it's just for your safety."

Tina looked up and stared at the person in front of her.

Her anxious heart was calmed by his words.

This man was the one who brought her out of the village, the one who saved her. And for some reason, he gave her a familiar feeling.

Moreover, when this man got off the car, he called her Tina, so... "Do we know each other?"

"What?" Kit was taken aback.

"Do we know each other?" Tina asked.

"You... don't know me?" Kit widened his eyes and looked at Tina.

Tina bit her lip. "I... lost my memory. I don't remember anything from before. Because I was saved by the Drogo family, everyone in the village says I'm Drogo's wife. But... I know, I'm not Drogo's wife at all, and the child in my belly is not his!"

Kit looked at Tina in shock. She lost her memory... she actually lost her memory!

"You don't remember anything from before?" he asked.

"Yeah." Tina nodded. "Every time I try to remember things from before, I get a headache."

"You... are called Tina!" Kit murmured.

Tina, was that her name? Tina wondered to herself. "And what about you? What's your name?"

"I... I'm called Kit," he said.

"Then what relation do you have with me?" Her memory was a blank slate, and right now she seemed like a naive child, asking him.
