Chapter 4112

Finally, Hadwin shut his mouth and stopped talking.

Lina took command and said to her daughter and future son-in-law, "You two, sit down first before you speak!"

Tina and Chandler obediently sat back in their seats.

Lina then asked, "You two aren't married yet. What if you get pregnant?" It was a very practical question.

Tina couldn't help but think to herself that she actually wanted to get pregnant!

But she couldn't answer like that in front of her mother, so she said, "I will take responsibility!"

"What responsibility will you take?" Hadwin glared at his disappointing daughter.

Tina's shoulders couldn't help but shrink, but she persisted, "Of course, I will take responsibility for marrying Chandler as soon as possible!"

"You're still in college!" Hadwin said.

"You can still get married while in college!" Tina muttered, after all, there are people who get married while in college!

Chandler spoke up, "There won't be anything like this happening again before Tina and I get married. As for the wedding date, it's up to the Stephenson family. If Tina is willing to marry me, I will take good care of her for the rest of my life. If Tina doesn't want to, I will still bless her and won't force her!"

Chandler naturally understood Hadwin's concerns because he had the same worries.

He was afraid that Tina was too young and might not be able to distinguish her feelings.

He was afraid that she might mistake their years of companionship for love and regret the marriage if she met someone she truly loved after getting married.

After hearing Chandler's words, Hadwin's expression eased, "Can Hunter agree to what you said?"

"You don't have to worry about Hunter. I can make the decision!" Chandler said directly.

Tina was angry and stared at Chandler, "Chandler, do you still think I will regret getting married to you? Do you really not trust my feelings?"

"Tina, I just..."

Tina suddenly stood up and walked to Chandler, who was still sitting in his chair. She looked down at him and said, "Although I'm eight years younger than you, I know my own feelings. I know who I want to spend the rest of my life with. Your constant search for a way out only makes me feel that you are not the one who loves me enough!"

After saying this, she turned to her parents and said, "Mom and Dad, whether I marry Chandler or not, I will have his child. There will be a child of Chandler and me in this world, and you will be grandparents no matter what!"

After speaking, she didn't look at anyone's reaction and went straight upstairs.

Hadwin and Lina were surprised. What did she mean? Did she want to have a child even if they didn't get married?

"Is Tina really not pregnant?" Hadwin stared at Chandler and began to suspect that his daughter was already pregnant.

Lina quickly said, "I'll confirm it later. Don't be angry. Chandler has made everything clear, and he has given the decision-making power to the Stephenson family."

Lina was still very satisfied with her future son-in-law!

He is willing to take responsibility, but he won't force Tina. On the contrary, his own daughter is more willful!

Only Chandler understands the true meaning behind Tina's words just now!

She was telling him that no matter what, she will save him, right?!
