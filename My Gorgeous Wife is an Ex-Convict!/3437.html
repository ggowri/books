Chapter 3435

 

So, the girls at school also started to pursue Mason one after another.

Every day, there are girls who give love letters or gifts to Mason, but he always throws them away.

However, those girls are not discouraged. After all, he is the second son of the Reed family. If they can really catch him, their destiny could be changed forever!

Look, isn't Veronica the perfect example?

If Mason only treats Veronica as a sister, Veronica can live such a good life. If there is a chance to become Mason's girlfriend, wouldn't it be better than Veronica's current situation?

After school, Veronica watched as Mason packed his backpack. Another love letter fell out of his drawer, along with a beautifully wrapped gift box. It must have been a carefully chosen gift from some girl.

Mason simply picked up the love letter and gift, and threw them directly into the trash can at the back of the classroom.

Veronica looked and couldn't help but say, "Why don't you open it up and take a look? Maybe this girl could be someone you like?"

"I won't." He said with absolute certainty.

"You haven't even seen the love letter the other person wrote, let alone met them. How do you know you won't fall for them?" She asked.

But he looked at her with unwavering eyes and said, "I am certain that I won't fall for this person."

Because for him, there was already someone he liked, and this person was the most important person in his life, someone he would never give up to anyone else!

Even if he had to resort to any means, he would get this person!

Under his gaze, her heart suddenly fluttered, and she quickly avoided his eyes and said, "Okay, let's go."

Mason didn't say anything and just picked up their backpacks and headed towards the school gate.

But when they reached the bottom of the teaching building, they ran into Daniel.

Daniel looked at the two of them, seeming hesitant.

"What's wrong, Mr. Cross?" Veronica asked.

Daniel felt a bit embarrassed and said, "Actually, I just wanted to ask if you're free this weekend to come watch me play basketball. Our school team has a game against No. 3 Middle School."

Veronica remembered that she had promised Daniel that she would go watch him play a game, but for this weekend... She looked a bit embarrassed and glanced at Mason standing next to her. After a moment, she said to Daniel, "I had originally planned to go to the bookstore with Mason this weekend."

"I see." Daniel's expression seemed a bit disappointed, but then he said, "If you don't mind, how about coming to watch me play basketball? After the game, I can accompany you to the bookstore to buy books. The game won't take too long."

Although Daniel had conflicts with Mason in the past, he naturally hoped to have a better relationship with him because Mason is Veronica's "brother".

"Hmm?" Veronica furrowed her brow upon hearing Daniel's suggestion.

After all, she knew that Mason didn't like Daniel. He had even destroyed the ointment that Daniel had given her twice before. They had even had a cold war for several days because of Daniel's relationship. So it was clear that Mason would never agree.
