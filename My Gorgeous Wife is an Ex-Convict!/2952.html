"I... don't feel like it's a force," she gathered her courage and met his gaze.

Or perhaps, she was also looking forward to it.

"Later... you can stay a little further away from me. I'm afraid I might accidentally touch your wound if I sleep in a bad position," Valda said.

"I'll be careful, but even if I do touch it, it's not a big deal," he said.

Who says only men are impulsive? Women are the same.

If she didn't come back to him, then his future life wouldn't be happy anymore.

Valda thought that she wouldn't be able to sleep if Brian was next to her, but surprisingly, she slept soundly until morning.

His breath surrounded her nose, giving her an inexplicable sense of security and allowing her to sleep more soundly.
