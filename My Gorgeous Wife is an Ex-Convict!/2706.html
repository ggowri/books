Chapter 2704

"So, don't fall in love with me. Just so you know, even if you force me to continue to be with you by jumping off the building, I’ll only stand by and watch," Brian uttered with a soft tone.

His voice was so melodious, but the words he blurted out were so harsh and cold.

Valda knew he was warning her not to fall for him. He was intimate with her in public today, but it was just an act to make people believe they were a real couple.

Brian had given all his love to the woman he loved, so he had no more to give to others.

...

*Meanwhile, at L City*

Kyla looked at the date on the phone, feeling her heart sink.

It had been more than two months since she came here, however, she had made no progress.

Martin was still cold to her.

Then Kyla thought to herself, Maybe I’ve already missed the one and only chance to fix our relationship. And there’s no way back for us.

Well, I’ll sign the divorce in 25 days. By that time, Martin will have nothing to do with me. Maybe, these are the final days I got to spend with him. When the time comes, one of us will end up in L city, while the other will leave for Emerald City.

Gosh... It might take us years to see each other again.

After collecting her thoughts, Kyla went to the kitchen and said to the cook, "I'd like to cook dinner for Martin today."

"But..." The cook hesitated.

"I'm still the lady of the house now. Can't I cook dinner myself?" Kyla pressured him.

After Martin declared her the mistress in front of the servants, they had been respectful to her.

The cook said nothing more and left the kitchen.

Kyla then started cooking. At the very least, she still wanted to do something for Martin as his wife.

She made noodles for him last time, but he did not even take a bite. This time, she hoped he would at least have some of her food.

Back when they were dating, Kyla told Martin she would cook for him and they would have their meals together after they got married.

Now that their marriage was coming to an end, she wanted to realize her dream at least.

An hour later, Kyla made a table of food.

These days, Martin's meals were brought to the study by the servant. He had been eating alone. Hence, the servant was going to take the dishes upstairs.

Kyla stopped them and said, "I’ll ask Martin to come down and eat."

With a confused look, the servant said, "But Mr. Weiss ordered..."

Kyla interrupted, "We should at least give it a shot. If he still doesn't come down to eat, you can bring the food upstairs."

"Okay," the servant answered.
