Chapter 2864

"Got it!" said Valda Theller. She would like to meet Brian no matter what!

However, her mood now differed from the night she went to his mansion to see him!

...

At the hospital, Kyla put away her phone and looked at Brian who was still anxiously waiting outside the operating room.

When she and Jason sent Grace to the hospital, they did not expect Brian to get news about it and arrive at the hospital not long after.

Grace had to undergo a C-section because her water broke early.

However, there were too many risks to giving birth to the baby, and it was unclear whether Grace could keep her uterus.

Kyla was uneasy as she thought of this.

They were standing outside the operating room, hoping that the doctor would come out and give them some good news.

It was quiet outside the operating room.

Jason and Brian stared closely at the operating room. There were obvious concern, anxiety, and unease on their faces.

Who would have thought that the two men who ruled Emerald City were currently waiting so anxiously for a woman?

However... Kyla had a heavy feeling in her heart when she thought of Valda Theller's call earlier. How would Valda feel if she knew Brian was standing outside Grace's operating room now?

No girlfriend would want her boyfriend to care so much about a woman he once loved.

'What about Brian? Does he love Valda?' She would have thought Brian was still in love with Grace if she had not known he already had Valda as his girlfriend.

The light above the operating room door finally dimmed. The door opened, and the doctor walked out.

Jason and Brian ran to the doctor at about the same time.

"How’s my wife?" Jason asked. Grace mattered the most to him at the moment.

"Both mother and daughter are safe. Although Mrs. Reed can keep her uterus, she suffered massive bleeding during the operation. The heavy uterus damage may require long-term treatment in the future. The specific situation depends on the patient's recovery after surgery. She might require further resection if we have no other choice," the doctor said.

Jason breathed a sigh of relief. It was good news. He would try to provide her with the best treatment.

A nurse carried out a small baby girl and held her in front of Jason to look.

Jason looked at the baby in front of him. Vernix still covered her red body.

'This is the baby Grace tried so hard to give birth to...'

Jason took the baby from the nurse.

"Jason, is this what you wanted? You put Grace in danger for a baby. You put her life on the line. She might even have to go to the operating table again!" Brian's voice rang out suddenly.

The sight of the baby girl stung his eyes.

Jason looked up at him. "Brian, this is my and Grace's decision. Who are you to say that to me?"
