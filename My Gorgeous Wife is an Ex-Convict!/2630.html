Raymond Bowman's face turned pale. Boring... was what he had used to describe her.

Valda Theller looked at the person in front of her coldly. "I did hate you back then. But I don't hate you anymore. After all these years, there's no point in hating someone irrelevant."

"Irrelevant?" Her words made him paler.

"What else could it be? Am I supposed to think of you as important?" she asked as if she was amused.
