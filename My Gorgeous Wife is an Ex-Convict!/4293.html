Chapter 4291 Tina's Decision

"Because you deserve to be loved, I don't want to easily give up!" Valda said.

All the past hardships and challenges have now turned into sweetness.

In this lifetime, her marriage is very happy, and now, she only hopes that her daughter can also have a happy marriage.

Brian gently kissed Valda.

Even after so many years of marriage, his feelings for her have become even stronger.

"Valda, I love you!" he whispered.

He didn't know how many times he had said these words, but for him, it was still the most genuine expression from the bottom of his heart.

He wanted her to understand how much he loved her!

And he wanted her to know how happy he was now.

"I love you too, very much," she responded.

Even when they both have white hair in the future, she will still love him just as much!

Valda leaned against Brian's embrace, thinking this way.

Tina arrived at the Hunter Group building and informed the staff that she wanted to see Chandler.

The staff naturally recognized Tina and immediately made a phone call through the internal line.

After a short while, the staff personally escorted Tina into the elevator and thoughtfully pressed the floor button.

If Tina hadn't insisted that the staff didn't need to accompany her, they probably would have entered the elevator together.

Alone in the elevator, Tina looked at the constantly changing floor numbers, and her mood became anxious.

She was about to meet Chandler in a moment, and she had something to say to him...

The elevator arrived at the floor, and Tina stepped out.

The secretary was already waiting at the elevator door and immediately said, "Miss Stephenson, please follow me!"

In the company, some executives and those close to the CEO already knew that he had divorced his wife, so now they naturally referred to Tina as Miss Stephenson.

Tina followed the secretary to the door of the CEO's office.

The secretary pushed open the door, and Tina walked in.

Chandler was in his office, and as soon as he saw Tina enter, he stood up and asked, "Would you like something to drink?"

"No, thank you," Tina replied.

Chandler then asked the secretary to leave.

The secretary closed the door, and there were only Tina and Chandler in the office.

"Do you have a reason for coming here? I remember I mentioned before that since we're divorced, I hope we can avoid seeing each other as much as possible," Chandler approached Tina and said.

"I came today to talk to you about something," Tina took a deep breath and said, feeling more anxious at this moment.

"You have something to tell me?" Chandler raised an eyebrow slightly, "Is there something we haven't clarified between us?"

"I want to have another child with you," Tina said.

As soon as this statement came out, even though Chandler had always been very composed, he showed a shocked expression, "What?"

"I mean, I want to have another child with you!" Tina repeated.

Chandler's expression became unpleasant, "Why?"

"The curse of the Hunter family brings you too much pain, and it's also dangerous to invoke the witch. I've thought about it for a long time, and it seems like having another child is the least risky and relatively safest choice," Tina said.

She had been thinking about this for days, and now that she was in front of him, it meant that she had made up her mind!
