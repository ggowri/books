Chapter 536

"Sure," said Jason. It was no trouble for him to take one more child to the premiere. "Are you sure he can understand a movie?"

Grace thought about it. It seemed that the main appeal of the film was that it was for both young and old, though Nelson was a little younger and did not know much about sounds nor languages.

However, watching a movie... Um, was always a new experience. Plus, she would be able to whisper hints or speak sign language to express what was happening on the screen.

Grace found it a lot more fun with that in mind! She looked forward to seeing Nelson watch the movie later.

Therefore, Grace spoke in sign language and asked the little one whether he had seen a movie before.

Then, the little one shook his head and said he had not seen any.

"Why don't you and I go watch a movie later?" asked Grace as she used sign language to speak. She was afraid the little one would not understand.

The little one nodded his head, his face lighting up with excitement.

Although the little one knew about movies only in the literal sense, his face was expectant.

Looking at Nelson’s excited face, Grace could not help feeling a bit sorry. If only they were watching an animation with Nelson today.

If Nelson was interested in movies, then she would watch animated movies with him next time.

The car drove to the premiere, but it did not stop at the main entrance. After all, there were quite a few reporters at the main entrance right now. Jason knew that Grace did not like to attract the news media's attention.

Moreover, he had always disliked reporters himself.

A security guard was waiting at the side door. As soon as Jason's car arrived, he respectfully opened the car door.

Jason, Grace, and Nelson got out of the car and went inside.

There were no reporters inside, just celebratory wreaths in the auditorium and guests in attendance.

As far as Grace could see, these people were all familiar—top celebrities one would see on the screen, some well-known local directors, and even some people you would normally only see in political news.

Grace was stunned. Although Howell Webb was now quite popular, Howell Webb seemed to be far from having such an impact.

On second thought, it suddenly became clear to her that the director of this film was one of the three famous directors in the industry. They had won many honors abroad.

This time, it was clear that many of the big shots attending the premiere were doing the director favors.

While Grace got a lot of attention at the last banquet, there was also a lot of chatter in the high society about how they should not offend Jason's new girlfriend.

However, at the end of the day, it was not something everyone knew about.

Now, Grace was lining up alongside Jason with a child. They were a sight that attracted a lot of attention.

In particular, those who knew Jason felt like their eyes were popping out.

What was going on? Why was Emerald City's fearsome Young Master Reed now attending such an event with a child?
