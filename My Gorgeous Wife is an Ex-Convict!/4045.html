Chapter 4043

Miss Fowler nodded repeatedly, tears in her eyes.

Originally, she just pitied Nancy and did everything she could to save her, knowing that every life was precious.

But who could have thought that she would receive such a reward.

Although she had no children of her own, she now had Nancy's child, which was worth it!

At one of the tables at the wedding banquet sat Grace's friend Lina and her family, including their daughter Tina, who is now a 20-year-old young woman.

Tina's appearance is a blend of Lina and Hadwin's features. Her oval face and dark round eyes, tied up in a simple ponytail, make her look cute and lovely.

There is a hint of elegance in her eyebrows, but her eyes soften that elegance.

However, if she were to enhance her eyes with eyeliner to make them look narrower, it would highlight that elegance.

Recently, Tina has taken a liking to cosplay. Because of her height, which is 170cm, she cross-dresses as a man, and she can't get enough of it, thanks to her friends' encouragement.

As a result, Tina has been doing well in this circle. Whenever she has free time, she takes a few cosplay photos or videos and posts them online, and she has gained quite a few fans.

However, Tina's personality is completely different from the beautiful men she cosplays as.

In the words of her friend Eugenia Massy, if Tina's fans knew what her true personality was like, half of them would probably be disappointed!

The real Tina is nothing like the characters she cosplays as, who are either cold, arrogant, or crazy.

Instead... she loves beautiful people, regardless of gender. For Tina, as long as they are beautiful, that's all that matters.

And once she gets to know a beautiful person, she likes to tease them. Eugenia herself has not escaped Tina's clutches and has been teased by her countless times.

Fortunately, Eugenia has gotten used to it over time.

Tina also likes to have a crush on beautiful people. Even though she already has a stunning fiancé like Chandler by her side, she still likes to idolize celebrities.

Eugenia once said, "If God gave me an unmarried husband like Chandler, I wouldn't look at any other man."

But Tina replied, "Wouldn't that be too boring? Beauty is meant to be appreciated in abundance!"

"Aren't you afraid your fiancé will get jealous?"

"Chandler is very understanding, he can handle it!"

"Understand my ass!" Eugenia thought to herself. In Eugenia's eyes, Chandler was extremely good-looking and treated Tina well, but deep down...well, he was sly!

This kind of man could only be a friend. If he became an enemy, it would be too scary!

If Chandler ever started to care, Tina would probably suffer!

At the moment, Eugenia was sending messages to Tina, reminding her, "Don't forget to get Kit's autograph for me. If you don't get it for me, we're done tomorrow!"

Kit was a member of a new idol group and also Eugenia's current favorite idol.

Kit's real name was Kit Ingram, and his family was also a prominent one. He was invited to attend the Reed family's wedding, so Eugenia asked Tina to get his autograph for her.

"Got it, don't worry. I want Kit's autograph for myself too!" Tina replied to her friend's message.

"Thanks, sis. But aren't you afraid Chandler will get jealous if you also get Kit's autograph?"

"Haha, impossible. If Chandler really gets jealous, I want to see it!" Tina replied.
