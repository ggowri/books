Chapter 3305

 

Did Taelyn meet Valda in the restroom and then had a conversation with her?

As soon as Brian thought of this, his face changed and he rushed straight into the bathroom, afraid that Taelyn might do something harmful to Valda.

After all, when he was in a relationship with the other person, he provided them with some resources. However, after they broke up, Taelyn's resources gradually decreased and he was no longer as successful as before.

Although he and Taelyn had a peaceful breakup, it has been many years since then and Taelyn's life is currently going well. Therefore, it is unlikely that she would go after Valda.

However, whenever something involves Valda, he loses his ability to make rational decisions and only wants to rush to her side as soon as possible.

When Brian rushed over to the sink, he saw Taelyn reaching out her hand towards Valda.

He suddenly rushed forward and stood in front of Valda, "Taelyn, what are you going to do?!"

Taelyn was stunned, her hand even frozen in mid-air. "Brian?! You...you're here too?"

"What exactly do you want to do to Valda?!" Brian questioned again.

"I..." Taelyn was still a bit stunned, but Valda peeked out from behind Brian and said, "Brian, Miss Taelyn didn't do anything to me. We were just talking about you."

Now, Brian was stunned, "Talking about me?"

"Yes, like where you used to like going, what your hobbies were, and uh, how many women used to chase after you..." Valda said, feeling a bit embarrassed.

Facing his ex-girlfriend, she couldn't resist her gossiping heart and asked about his past.

Because that was the him she wasn't familiar with! It was the him before they met.

She wanted to know more about him!

Brian pursed his lips, looked at Valda, then at Taelyn, "Is that all?"

"What else could there be?" Valda muttered, "Do you want to fight when you meet?"

Her words made him feel a bit helpless.

Taelyn looked at the two in front of her and said to Brian, "Long time no see."

"It has been a long time," Brian said.

"I just saw your wife and couldn't help but start a conversation with her," Taelyn thought for a moment and said, "I guess I was really curious about what kind of woman could make you want to marry her."

Brian stared at Taelyn with cold, piercing eyes, as if he was scrutinizing the truthfulness of her words.

Taelyn couldn't help but feel even more emotional.

As expected, he was still as cold and distant as he was back then, but... just now, seeing him rush in with a worried expression on his face, it was difficult for her to connect him with the person he is now.

Or perhaps it should be said...his anxiety and concern were only for Valda.

And for others, in his mind, they don't carry that same weight.

"I just had an interesting chat with Mrs. Hart. I think I understand why you married her," Taelyn chuckled. "I wish you both happiness."

She said, "So, let's talk again when we have the chance," to Valda.

"Ah, okay," Valda responded.

Taelyn walked away, and Brian turned around to face Valda. Suddenly, he bent down and rested his head on her shoulder, wrapping his arms around her waist. He let out a long sigh and said, "I'm glad you're okay."
