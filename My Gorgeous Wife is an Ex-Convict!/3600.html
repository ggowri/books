Chapter 3598

At the moment, he was bowing his head, his fingers moving quickly on the keys, as if he was completely immersed in this music and hadn't noticed Jasper's arrival.

At first, Jasper thought William was simply playing the piano, but later she realized that he was constantly repeating the same piece, and even...if you looked closely, you could see a pained expression on his face.

"Enough!" Jasper stepped forward and pressed his fingers on the piano keys, stopping him from playing further.

William seemed to snap out of it and looked at Jasper, somewhat breathless as he asked, "You...what are you doing here?" He seemed to be struggling to control his emotions.

"I couldn't sleep, so I came to find you and saw you playing the piano," she said, taking his hand and noticing that his fingertips were slightly red.

He must have been playing for a long time!

"What's wrong? Did something happen? You rarely play this kind of music, and you keep repeating it," Jasper asked.

William's eyes flickered, and he withdrew his hand from hers with some unease. "Nothing happened, I...I just played randomly."

"You're lying!" she said.

He was startled, and his hands involuntarily clenched into fists as he nervously looked at her.

Did she...notice something?
