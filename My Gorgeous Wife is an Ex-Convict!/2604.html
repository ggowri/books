"Really?" He suddenly got up and released her hand. Then, he lifted his hands and removed his white shirt in front of her.

She immediately caught sight of the hideous scar again. It was a clearer view than earlier.

She looked blankly at his injury while feeling the urge to cry. She was already tearing up. "Does the wound still hurt?"

"It's been more than two months. Does it hurt for you?" he asked indifferently.

He was not in pain, so he must be fine. "I'm sorry I made you suffer so much for me..."

Martin Weiss said coldly, "I didn't do it for you but to fulfill my wishes before the hypnosis. Now that you've seen the wound, don’t take off my clothes randomly anymore."

He said as he bent down, and their cheeks were so close together. His fingers grabbed her jaw, and he brushed the tip of his thumb against her lips. "Believe me. I have no interest in you!"

With that said, he turned around and left the yard.

Kyla looked at Martin Weiss's leaving figure. Her wrist still held the lingering heat from when he grabbed her hand.

From his speech and body movements, he was distancing himself from her.

He had agreed to the three-month bet, but... he was reluctant.

'A waste of time? Three months? Is it really a waste of time?'

Kyla smiled bitterly.

She was in a position where she could not hear what they said, but... the way Mr. Weiss looked at the woman was different from his usual indifference.

It was as if there was something hidden deep under his eyes, or rather... he was suppressing something!

What was Mr. Weiss suppressing?
