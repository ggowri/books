Chapter 1029

"Are you disappointed?" Lina asked in a mutter.

Hadwin Stephenson smiled faintly and said, "It's alright if you're not pregnant. We'll have children in a few years. Alright, you can come out."

Lina said helplessly, "I can't! I’m having my period and I don't have any sanitary pads with me. Why don't you go out and buy me some?"

Hadwin Stephenson's expression froze. "Buy you some sanitary pads?" It was the second time his voice went out of tune tonight.

"Yeah. Do you want me to come out all bloody?" she said, emphasizing the word ‘bloody’.

"I'll ask my servants to buy some," he said.

She quickly said, "No! It's embarrassing to get your servant to buy such a thing. Why don't you buy it yourself? You know what? When I was in school, I envied girls who had boyfriends who would buy sanitary pads for them. I even imagined having a boyfriend who would buy some for me."

Hadwin Stephenson could not understand why she would envy having a boyfriend who would buy her sanitary pads and fantasize that her own boyfriend would buy her some too.

'Are sanitary pads, uh, that important?'

However, when he saw the expectation in her eyes, Hadwin Stephenson knew he would give in again this time.

Therefore, he could only say, "Wait here."

"No problem!" She immediately smiled sweetly and added, "Buy me night use pads from Whisper!"

Hadwin Stephenson was rendered speechless and had an indescribable feeling in his heart.

It seemed he kept buying things he did not usually need tonight. The problem was... he kept getting weird looks from people.

When Hadwin Stephenson was at the check-out counter with two packs of sanitary pads, he was once again greeted by a strange look from the cashier.

After paying the bill, he heard the cashier say to the cashier beside her, "Did you see that? That guy just bought sanitary pads!"

"He probably bought them for his girlfriend or his wife."

"Hey, a man who's willing to buy sanitary pads is a good man!"

"I'm so envious of that woman!"

Therefore, Hadwin Stephenson realized it was not Lina's logic that was bizarre but women’s in general!

When Lina came out of the bathroom after putting on the sanitary pad, she was clutching her stomach. She frowned slightly, her face looking paler than before.

"What's the matter? Are you feeling uncomfortable?" Hadwin Stephenson asked with concern.

"Just a little stomach ache," said Lina. She met Hadwin Stephenson's concerned eyes and hurriedly explained, "Don't worry. I get cramps the first two days of my period!"

He pondered for a moment and asked, "What would make you feel better?"

"I feel better after drinking some brown sugar ginger tea, but do you, uh, have the ingredients?" she asked.

"I'll get someone to buy some right away if I don't have them!" he said. He ordered his servant to make some brown sugar ginger tea while making her lie down on the couch with a cushion on her waist and a little blanket over her stomach to make her feel comfortable.
