Chapter 3155

"Are you planning to resume the serialization of the manga that you previously worked on?" asked Camille.

"Yep," Valda replied, "I don't want that story to end like that. I hope it can be a story with a proper beginning and end."

"But it will be difficult. After all, lesser-known series that come back usually end up with a hasty wrap-up unless it's a hot favorite," Camille explained.

She then added, "You are Brian's fiancée now, and he will help you with this, but he won't do a losing business deal just for you!"

"Got it. If it doesn’t work out, I will wrap it up," Valda said, "But I really like the manga. It's a serialized comic story that I started after I met Brian. So I hope that it can have a good ending too."

"Okay, I'll help you get on with it. Actually, readers like your story. And the publisher even planned to publish it. But it didn't happen because you went missing at that time. Maybe if you start the serialization again, it will have a good outcome!" Camille smiled and stood up, saying, "I'll go back to my office."

"By the way, don't forget to come over to my new house tonight for the party," Valda reminded her.

With a smile, Camille uttered, "I’ll be there. Don’t worry. So many important people are attending the party today. This kind of gathering is rare, and I can't miss it."

In the evening, Valda hosted a small gathering in her newly purchased house, and she prepared a buffet for dinner.

Brian also brought over some of the cooks and servants from the villa to help her with it.

Amber and Simon were the first ones to arrive. The little guy even prepared a gift, which was a homemade greeting card.

"Here you are, Valda! The card is not worth any money, but you know what my teacher said? It's the thought that counts!" Simon said to Valda while handing her the card.

With a grin, Valda replied, "Oh, thank you, Simon! I really like the card you made, and I will keep it safe!"

Upon hearing her say this, he finally showed a satisfied expression.

A while later, others also arrived.

Grace came with her triplets and Veronica Barlow. Grace didn’t bring her youngest daughter along for fear that she wouldn’t be able to take care of her.

Kyla and Martin brought Nelson with them. And Hadwin and Lina came.

With six children and a bunch of adults, Valda felt that the living room, which was originally spacious, now seemed crowded.

Fortunately, the house was on the ground floor and had a fairly large yard. The children were all playing in the yard, making the living room less crowded.

Looking at the people in the room, Camille was awed. After all, she was with the rich and powerful at the moment. And she had only seen people like Jason, Martin, and Hadwin in the news before.

If she took a photo of them and posted it online, it would surely cause a sensation.

At the moment, a few women gathered together were chatting about their children and discussing Valda and Brian's upcoming wedding.

"By the way, you're living with Brian now. If you get pregnant, will you have to move up your wedding date?" Lina asked.

Valda's face turned red, "Um... I don't think so."

"Did you guys use protection?" Lina asked.

Valda's face turned even redder. Most of the time, they did use protection, but occasionally, in moments of impulsiveness, they forgot about it.

But Valda was too embarrassed to answer that question.

"You know what. Accidents happen. Just let nature take its course," Lina said.
