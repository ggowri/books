Chapter 3091

"And you, do you really only treat me as a friend? Look at me now and answer me," he said.

Valda stared at the man who was so close to her. At that moment, his handsome face was not as cold and distant as usual. And she found it hard to take her eyes off it.

"I know you still love me. So, are you planning to deceive me or deceive yourself? Are you sure this is really what you want? Just being a friend to me?" Brian sounded quite seductive as he uttered.

"I...I just wanted to..."

She almost used her last bit of willpower to say something that went against her heart when his lips landed on hers, cutting her off.

Suddenly, Valda felt her mind go blank. His kiss seemed to take away all her senses, and she actually quite enjoyed it.

That night, when they were making love, Valda kept telling herself that it would just be a one-night stand.

Also, she told herself that Brian was drunk, which was why he said these things and did these things to her. And she took it as a stolen moment of happiness.

The next day, when Valda woke up and saw Brian lying next to her with his eyes closed, she suddenly didn’t want to let him go.

The truth was, she had always been deeply in love with him. But sadly, she didn’t know if she still deserved him.

She gently lifted her hand and slowly reached out towards the face that was so close to her. In fact, he was the person she was most reluctant to part with in her heart.

Her fingers lightly brushed his cheek, feeling his warmth.

"Brian, thank you," she whispered, thanking him for the night they had.

But just as she was about to pull her hand away, his other hand quickly grabbed her wrist, and Brian slowly opened his eyes.

"What do you want to thank me for?" He asked calmly.

Valda's face immediately turned red. "You...you heard me?"

"Mm, I heard you thanking me earlier. So, tell me, what do you want to thank me for?" he asked with a smirk.

Her face grew even redder as she felt embarrassed.

"Well, ummm... I just wanna thank you for not giving up looking for me," She finally managed to explain after a while.

"But you deliberately avoided me even though you returned to Emerald City!" he said, pulling her hand to his lips and kissing it. "From now on, you're not allowed to hide from me anymore. If you don't wanna move to my place, then I'll move in here with you. How about that?"

Valda widened her eyes in shock. "You...you want to move in with me?"

"Yes. Anything wrong with it? Don't tell me you only wanted to sleep with me," Brian said.

Valda was stunned as Brian had seldom said stuff like that.

"Last night, we both acted on impulse. So, just let it slide." She stammered.

"But what if I tell you that I wasn't acting on impulse? Would you still want to let it slide?" Brian asked.

"But you were clearly drunk yesterday..."
