Chapter 1044

Brian did not expect Grace to come to him. After all, she always seemed to keep her distance from him.

However, he was somewhat surprised when he knew why she came to him.

"You want to go to the charity gala tonight?" asked Brian.

"Yeah. Can I?" Grace asked eagerly.

Brian said, "Why not? But you have to let me know why you want to attend the gala so suddenly. You're not after some celebrity, are you?"

After all, several top celebrities were attending the charity gala, so the event was attracting a lot of fans.

However, if she was really after some celebrity, he could give her a hand.

People in the entertainment industry would show him some respect.

"I want to meet Martin Weiss and Paisley Daniels. I heard they'll be attending the charity gala tonight," said Grace.

"Meet them?" Doubt flashed across Brian's eyes. "They're attending the charity gala, but you and them..."

"It's a little personal. It has to do with a friend of mine." Grace stopped short.

"Alright, I'll set it up. Although time is a little tight, we'll make it!" said Brian.

Grace was stunned. 'Time is a little tight? What does that mean?'

Half an hour later, she finally understood what Brian meant when he said time was tight. Brian had someone drive a minivan over, and one of Emerald City's top stylist, Sam, styled her in the car.

"You'll attract a lot of attention if you don't dress up and show up at the charity gala in what you’re wearing now," said Brian.

Grace knew that everyone would dress up formally for galas like this. Her casual clothes were out of place. She went to Brian at a last minute’s notice, so now she had to get her makeup done in the car as they headed toward the gala’s venue.

"You're such a hassle, dragging me here to do someone's makeup! Is this your new girlfriend?" Sam joked. He seemed to know Brian well.

"No," Brian said lightly.

The answer was just as Sam expected. After all, it was not easy to be the crown prince's new girlfriend.

Although she looked pretty good, she was not exactly stunning. He had also noticed her hands that bore many calluses. The skin on her hands was a bit rougher than other women, and the joints of her fingers were slightly deformed. One could tell she did a lot of rough work.

He was also surprised. He wondered what the crown prince had to do with the woman in front of him. After all, a woman with such hands seemed to have nothing to do with the crown prince.

However, the next thing Brian said made Sam's hand, which was filling in Grace's eyebrow, tremble. It almost ruined her makeup.

Brian said, "She's the one I love."

Sam immediately widened his eyes. He looked at Brian before looking at the woman in front of him. 'Is this... the one the crown prince loves?'

Sam remembered how the crown prince confessed in an interview that he had someone he loved, but the woman had not accepted him.

'Therefore... she hasn't accepted the crown prince?'
