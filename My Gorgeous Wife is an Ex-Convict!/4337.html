Chapter 4335 Neglected People

"I didn't think much! "She said.

At this moment, a beautiful young woman in a red dress walked forward and said to Lornell, "Lornell, long time no see, how have you been lately."

"It's still the same as before, "Lornell replied.

Harley watched as she knew this woman, a relative of the Quentin family named Ursula, who had also played with them since childhood.

Of course, it's basically Ursula and Lornell playing, and as for her, Ursula wouldn't pay much attention to her unless she was necessary, just treating her like air.

Just like at this moment, she and Lornell were standing together, but Ursula only greeted Lornell without saying hello, as if she hadn't seen her.

And Lornell didn't say anything, as if he had become accustomed to this scene.

Isn't it just a habit? Harley smiled wryly and then said lightly, "Take your time talking, I'll go eat something first."

After speaking, she didn't look at their reactions either, so she walked away directly.

Lornell looked at Harley's back and unconsciously frowned.

Ursula whispered, "Lornell, your fiancée seems to be getting impolite. She doesn't even say hello to me, but it's not surprising. Who made her the princess of the Hart family? Of course, she's extraordinary."

Lornell turned his head and looked at Ursula with a smirk on his face. "Didn't you also say hello to her?"

Ursula said awkwardly, "I... I just..."

"Alright, you don't need to explain anything to me, "Lornell said lightly as if it was just an insignificant matter.

Ursula breathed a sigh of relief.

It seems that Lornell still doesn't care about Harley!

Although Harley holds the name of his fiancée, it has been so many years and Lornell's attitude towards Harley has not changed much, so what tied the two together is just a marriage agreement.

Although she and Lornell were more intimate and had a better time, it was only because Harley was the daughter of the Hart family that

Thinking of this, Ursula's heart was filled with reluctance.

At this moment, someone else came to Lornell's side to say hello, while Ursula, like Lornell's fiancée, followed Lornell's side and talked to those who came to say hello.

Harley stood not far away, watching the scene expressionless.

She had actually seen too much of this situation. At first, there were various pains in her heart, but later on, she gradually became numb.

Even her parents would occasionally ask her if she wanted to terminate her engagement.

If you really want to lift it, then the Hart family can naturally pay some price, and it's okay to consider it as a return for the Quentin family's life-saving kindness.

But she couldn't make up her mind.

Because once the engagement is terminated, there really won't be any relationship between her and Lornell.

Now, the only thing that maintains their relationship is a marriage agreement.

Based on Lornell's feelings for her, after breaking the engagement, the other party is likely to completely treat her as a stranger.

At that moment, a voice rose: "Harley."

Harley turned his head and saw Tina's face.

"Tina. "Harley looked at Tina and it was normal for Tina to come to the Quentin family's birthday banquet.

"Why don't you go to Lornell's side and instead stand here alone? "Tina asked.
