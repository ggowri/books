Chapter 2851

Whitney Cooper clenched her teeth in hatred. She could have fooled Brian if it were not for Valda Theller.

"I'm free to like Brian, Miss Theller. You have no right to interfere," said Whitney Cooper.

"It's your freedom to like someone. But there’ll be a problem with your integrity if you destroy people's relationships! Miss Cooper, if you're here to steal my boyfriend, I have to say that I don’t welcome you here. Please leave!" Valda Theller said unceremoniously.

Whitney Cooper choked. Her face with delicate makeup was almost distorted.

"Didn't you hear her?" Brian turned to look at Whitney Cooper. "You’re not welcome here. Besides, I already told you it's never going to happen between us! If you don't want to work abroad, you can resign. Hart Group doesn't support idle people!"

Whitney Cooper pressed her red lips tightly. She could tell Brian was angry. "I... I’ll leave. Brian, don't be mad at me. I know it's my fault. I'm sorry I upset Miss Theller."

She solemnly said to Valda Theller, "Miss Theller, I'm so sorry."

Whitney Cooper left after saying that.

Valda Theller finally released Brian's arm slowly. "Just now... I..." She bit her lip, feeling flustered and embarrassed.

Brian said, "It was the first time I saw you so overbearing, but you're my girlfriend. You have the right to say those things to her."

Valda Theller's eyelashes quivered slightly. She did not say those words because she was his girlfriend but because she was jealous.

She was jealous of the past Whitney Cooper mentioned.

Even though she knew Brian probably only thought of Whitney Cooper as a substitute, her jealousy could not be controlled by reason.

"She does look like Grace," Valda Theller murmured for a while.

"Yeah, she does," said Brian as he looked at Valda Theller's hesitant look. "If you have anything you want to ask, you might as well go ahead."

"How did you happen to be here today?" She asked after some hesitation.

"I knew Whitney Cooper had come to Emerald City, so I had people keep an eye on her. Then I got word that she came here, so I came," said Brian.

Whitney Cooper looked like Grace, so he let her off the hook.

"Then... Why didn't you ask her to be your girlfriend? Why me?" she asked a second question.

"Do you think I should’ve asked her?" Brian asked rhetorically.

"She... looks like Grace..." There was a faint tremble in her voice. She knew there were probably questions she should not be asking, but she could not help wanting answers.

Brian said slowly, "Yes, she looks like her, but I don't want a substitute. You know what? I've had a lot of substitutes... before I found Grace. I kept looking for her substitutes, but the one I really want won’t ever belong to me."
