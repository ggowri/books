Chapter 1021

Kyla Corbyn gently coaxed him, and soon, the little one fell asleep. Kyla Corbyn then muttered, "I just hope Nelson won't be discriminated against when he starts kindergarten."

"Who dares discriminate against him? I'll go to the kindergarten and stand up for him!" Lina immediately said. She did not believe she could not handle a few children.

Kyla Corbyn smiled. She envied Lina's character sometimes. She was just as fearless as she used to be. However, she could never be the same after everything she experienced.

"Grace, thank you so much for the custody case," said Kyla Corbyn.

"I've already talked to the firm and Lawyer Carter has agreed to help. Don't think about it too much," said Grace.

Kyla Corbyn nodded. Speaking of which, she was surprised that Martin Weiss chose a court in Emerald City to hear the case. She thought he would choose a court in S City.

After all, S city was his territory.

Since he had chosen Emerald City, she did not have to travel back and forth between two cities, which saved her a lot of effort.

"Now that you've sold the small restaurant, are you going to get a job?" asked Grace.

"I'll set up a food stall for now. I'll take care of the rest after the lawsuit is over," said Kyla Corbyn.

"A food stall? A roadside food stall?" Lina was surprised.

"Yeah, I start in the evening and close around 3 A.M. I've been doing it for more than a week, and it's doing okay. The money I’m making is sufficient to support me, my mom, and Nelson," said Kyla Corbyn.

"Isn’t running food stalls hard work?" asked Lina.

"A little, but the jobs I can do are limited. I'm just setting up a stall where I cook some noodles and appetizers. The money I earn from doing these is alright. Besides, I've set up food stalls before I opened the small restaurant, so I have some experience," said Kyla Corbyn.

Lina was dumbfounded.

Grace knew how hard life could be for a woman who had been released from prison. She had trouble finding a job too when she was released from prison. Besides, Kyla had to make a living for her mother and son as well.

Kyla Corbyn looked at the time and said, "It's almost time to set up my food stall, so I'm not going to eat dinner with you today."

Grace said, "I'll go with you. I'm free anyway."

"I'll come with you too!" Lina interjected suddenly.

"However, the place is... a bit far. I'm riding a scooter, so you..."

"I'll drive Grace to the destination and wait for you there. Just tell me the specific location," said Lina.

Therefore, the three of them headed to the place where Kyla Corbyn set up her food stall.

Once they were there, Kyla Corbyn began to lay out some folding tables and chairs, along with ingredients, tools, and so on. Grace and Lina also gave her a hand.

Once they set up everything, Lina was already sweating and could not help admiring Kyla Corbyn more.

She was already exhausted after doing it for a while, not to mention Kyla Corbyn who usually did everything herself.

The customers had also started to arrive. Kyla Corbyn expertly cooked the food while Grace and Lina helped her wipe the tables and serve the food.
