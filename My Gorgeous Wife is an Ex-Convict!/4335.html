Chapter 4333 He Doesn't Love Her

Calvert held the medicine and stared blankly at Harley's departure.

Is it because he looks too pitiful? So the princess of the Hart family came out to help him and buy him medicine?

These medicines seem particularly heavy in their hands.

When facing Harley, he always feels a sense of inferiority, which makes him want to escape.

But when Harley really left, he had an urge to catch up with her and catch her.

At this moment, Calvert forcefully suppressed his impulse until Harley's figure completely disappeared before his eyes before he walked toward his dark and dilapidated home.

Perhaps this cannot be called home at all.

It's just a shelter for him!

When he opened the door, a familiar musty smell once again flooded into his nose.

The rotten and moldy smell in this dilapidated house always accompanies him.

Perhaps his whole person is also slowly decaying like this room.

The more I grow up, the more I broaden my horizons and understand how difficult it is to change my life!

He took off his coat and looked at the bruises on his body, new and old as if there were always wounds on him.

And the wounds on his body have never been cared about.

Just Calvert's gaze fell on the drugs, which Harley gave him.

Does this mean that she cares about his wounds?

On the other side, Harley sat in the car, looking at the message sent by the secretary of the Quentin family on his phone.

A week later, the Quentin family will hold Lornell's grandfather's birthday banquet and ask if she will attend.

Harley smiled wryly. Throughout, many things about the Quentin family have been sent messages or emails by the secretary, rather than Lornell communicating with her.

This makes her feel like an outsider.

Alternatively, in the eyes of the Quentin family, she is now just Lornell's fiancée and not married, so she is indeed an outsider.

So for Lornell, does she also exist as an outsider?

Harley responded and of course, she will attend the birthday banquet.

And the Hart family has already prepared the gift.

And it wasn't until the day before the banquet that Harley received Lornell's phone call: "Tomorrow I'll pick you up and attend my grandfather's birthday party together."

For an instant, Harley had an indescribable complex feeling in his heart, "I have already told my parents that tomorrow I will go to the banquet with them."

These days, she has been waiting for his call, but he hasn't called yet.

The secretary of the Quentin family was also explaining some of the attendance matters at the banquet to her.

It's like he can't even spare a little time for her!

In the eyes of others, fiancée should have been an intimate identity, but for him, she is probably only stronger than strangers.

"Is that right? That's good. "Lornell's voice came faintly from the other end of his phone, and he hung up immediately. The phone kept hearing the sound of ending the call.

Harley only felt bitter in his mouth.

It seems that he was just saying it for real, and even after she refused, he just replied briefly and ended the conversation.

His attitude is undoubtedly silently telling her that he doesn't love her!

Harley gently lowered his eyes and looked at the phone in his hand... Yeah, she knows better than anyone the fact that he doesn't love her, right?
