"Thank you, Shirley. I'm sure you'll be happy too in the future," Veronica said.

Wedding photos are an important step before getting married.

Since Veronica's belly is not yet visible, they quickly completed this step. However, to prevent her from getting too tired, the wedding photos were almost all arranged indoors. They only took one set of outdoor photos, and in the team, there were also obstetricians, nurses, and a nanny assistant to take care of Veronica.

Overall, Veronica felt like a VIP panda with the treatment she received during the photo shoot. She didn't even have to lift a finger to drink water, as someone would bring the cup directly to her lips.

Several people worked on her makeup and hairstyle at the same time, which greatly saved her time.

After everything was done, Veronica looked at herself in the mirror wearing a white wedding dress, with exquisite makeup, and almost couldn't recognize herself.

It must be said that top makeup artists and stylists are different. Although the person in the mirror is still her, it doesn't give an exaggerated feeling, but it's different from usual.

It's like enlarging the beautiful parts of her facial features, making her more prominent and stunning.

"Are you ready?" a voice sounded behind her. Veronica turned around and saw Mason walking towards her.

He was wearing a black suit, a white shirt, and a white bow tie, looking like a prince in a fairy tale.

No, to her, he was always her prince!

"Okay, let's go," Veronica said.

Mason reached out his hand to Veronica. "Let's head over to the studio."

She placed her hand in his, and they walked towards the studio.
