Jasper suddenly reached out and hugged William, burying his face in William's arms.

"When Veronica told me to hide and distract the kidnappers herself, I was really scared. I was afraid that something bad would happen to Veronica while I desperately ran down the mountain to find help and save her. I was afraid that Veronica would end up like Lawrence's parents."

Jasper's voice began to choke up.

When she uttered the name "Lawrence," William's body tensed up.

Even after so many years have passed, she still hasn't forgotten Lawrence.

Once, he thought that with the passage of time, Lawrence would gradually fade away in her heart.

But now it seems that not only has it not been forgotten, but it has become even more profound.

The matter of Lawrence and the Abbott family is like a perpetual pain in her heart. Perhaps only finding Lawrence in the future can relieve her pain.

But...if we really do find Lawrence...

When this possibility crossed his mind, he suddenly felt afraid.

Once they find Lawrence, will their relationship remain the same as it is now? Will she belong to him in the future? Or if Lawrence reappears, will he lose her?

At this moment, he suddenly held her tighter in his arms.
