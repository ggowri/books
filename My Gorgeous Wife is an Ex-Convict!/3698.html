Chapter 3696

Even she was surprised that she couldn't grab his arm. She had felt like she had him, but she didn't.

"Your suit..."

"This is my unwanted trash. What's the point of returning trash to me?" Jasper said coldly, finally looking directly at her as Angelica had hoped.

But his gaze made Angelica's heart skip a beat.

Because his eyes were too cold and sharp, as if he could see through all her intentions, even...making her feel like he was calling her trash, not just the suit.

Angelica forced a dry smile. "Veronica, my assistant, found this suit. I didn't know you had thrown it away. I wanted to help her out since she's so busy with work and school. I also wanted to apologize for last time when I accidentally spilled pasta on your clothes..."

She shifted all the blame onto Veronica, making herself look both innocent and wronged.

Mason's gaze darkened at the mention of Veronica's name.

The air around them suddenly felt suffocating. Just as Angelica thought Mason wasn't going to say anything, his thin lips parted and he asked, "Is she your assistant?"

"Ah, yes...she is," Angelica quickly replied. "Actually, I used to have another assistant, but she was just a temporary worker. Recently, my other assistant had to take a leave of absence due to illness, and I heard that Veronica was working part-time to support herself. I thought she deserved a chance, so I let her be my temporary assistant. This way, she could earn more money than just doing odd jobs."

Angelica continued to portray herself as a kind-hearted person.

Mason spoke coldly, "Since she was the one who picked up the clothes, she should be the one to return them to me!"

After saying that, Jasper left on his own.

Angelica stood there in a daze, watching Mason's back. She felt a lump in her throat and couldn't breathe properly.

What was this supposed to mean?

Did he want Veronica to return the clothes?

Angelica had thought that even though Mason seemed cold, she could still strike up a conversation with him while returning the clothes. But it turned out like this!

It seemed like she was just a clown from beginning to end.

...

The next day, Veronica looked at the suit that was thrown in front of her and hesitated. "Miss Kinsman, what is this for?"

"This suit. Mason said it was garbage and didn't want it. Since you picked up the garbage, you should return it to him personally!" Angelica said unkindly.

Veronica lowered her gaze and felt a bitter taste in her mouth. Garbage? He thought it was garbage because it was the suit that had protected her from the pasta. In his eyes, she wasn't worth that kind of gesture.

"What are you staring at! Listen carefully, you will return this suit to Mason and explain the situation from last time. It was your fault that I got angry and threw pasta, don't let Mason misunderstand me, got it?"

Veronica's eyes dimmed, "I understand."

"Okay, you can leave now," Angelica said.
