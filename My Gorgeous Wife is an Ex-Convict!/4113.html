Chapter 4111

Tina nervously closed her mouth and frantically tried to think of an excuse to get out of it.

But Hadwin obviously didn't give his daughter time to come up with an excuse, and looked directly at Chandler, "Did you do something to Tina?"

Chandler met Hadwin's gaze, "I..."

"No...no, Chandler didn't do anything to me." Tina quickly interrupted.

Hadwin, who was rarely angry, shouted at his daughter, "I didn't ask you, I asked him!"

"Yes, I did." Chandler gave a simple and straightforward answer.

The air suddenly seemed to freeze, and Hadwin suddenly rushed forward, grabbing Chandler's collar, "Is this how you treat the Stephenson family's trust? I trusted you to be with Tina, she's only a few years old, and you're treating her like this. You're not even married yet! Have you thought about the consequences of this?"

Tina and Lina looked at Hadwin's angry expression, both dumbfounded.

But fortunately, they were mother and daughter, and their tacit understanding was enough. They both rushed forward without saying a word, one pulling her husband back, and the other squeezing in between her father and fiancé, trying hard to separate the two.

"Husband, calm down first, and figure out what's going on. Chandler is someone we've watched grow up, he's not that kind of person!" Lina helped her future son-in-law.

"How can I calm down about something like this!" Hadwin said angrily.

He cherished his precious daughter, who was already 20 years old, but in his eyes, she was still a little girl. Even though she had been engaged to Chandler for many years, to Hadwin, it was just an engagement.

They hadn't even set a date for the wedding yet!

But today, he suddenly discovered that his carefully nurtured daughter had been taken away by someone else without his knowledge. How could he not be angry?

Just then, Tina shouted at her father, "Dad, you've got it all wrong. Yes, Chandler and I had sex, but I forced him to do it! He didn't want to, but I made him!"

The air froze once again.

Hadwin, who had been full of anger just a moment ago, almost couldn't catch his breath after hearing his daughter's words.

Lina, on the other hand, looked at her daughter with interest. "You took the initiative?"

"Yeah." Although Tina was usually carefree, she blushed when talking about this kind of thing with her parents.

"You're my daughter after all. When you need to take the initiative, you should take it!" Lina patted her daughter's shoulder.

Tina was delighted. Her mother's words meant that she was being supported and that her mother was on her side!

"Lina, how could you say that!" Hadwin was unhappy.

"You heard it yourself. It was Tina who took the initiative. Don't blame Chandler." Lina was still very satisfied with her future son-in-law.

"If a man refuses, how can a woman take the initiative? He could have refused!" Hadwin blamed Chandler for not refusing.

In any case, it was all Chandler's fault!

And his precious daughter was just an innocent little white rabbit!

Lina was puzzled. Her usually steady husband was acting like a stubborn child.

"Then why didn't you refuse my advances back then?"

Boom!

With Lina's shout, the room fell silent again.
