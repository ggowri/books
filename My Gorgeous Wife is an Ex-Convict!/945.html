As soon as their eyes met, a hand suddenly covered Grace's eyes. All she could see was pitch-black.

"Don't look. I don't like you looking at other men so seriously." The cold voice rang faintly in Grace's ears, making her shudder.

"Stop fooling around. Take your hand off," said Grace.

"I'm not fooling around," muttered Jason. Those bright eyes were now staring coldly at Brian who was not far away.

Brian could not help frowning when he saw Jason with Grace.

'What's going on? Didn't they break up? Why is Grace with Jason?'

The two men looked into each other's eyes. One pair was full of confusion while the other was full of cold jealousy.

Jason knew he was jealous. He was jealous when she looked at Brian so attentively.

Rather, he was more envious of her past with Brian. After all, the man knew her years before him and had even been looking for her all this time.

Despite all he had done... she still remembered Brian and her memories with him.

However, what surprised him was that she did not seem to have told Brian about it. Otherwise, the woman standing beside Brian today would not be Stella!

Grace shouted, "Jay! I didn't mean to look at Brian. I can't see anything if you do this."

His hand seemed to be just touching her eyes, but when she tried to pull it away, she could not.

"Don't fall for Brian." His voice rang in her ears again.

She was speechless. She had no plans to fall for Brian at all. "I won't."

"Good," he muttered as he looked at Brian who was coming this way, then he slowly took his hand away.
