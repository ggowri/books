After returning to Brian's villa, Valda solemnly collected these two things.

Especially since these jewelry pieces are quite expensive, she would probably be nervous wearing them, afraid of damaging or losing them.

"Well then, I'll make sure to find more opportunities for you to wear these jewelry pieces," Brian joked.

Valda wrapped her arms around Brian's neck and said, "Brian, I suddenly feel so happy right now." But she was also afraid that this happiness was only temporary, and that their happiness together may not last forever.

The more happy she felt, the more afraid she became deep down inside.

Brian bent down and kissed Valda on the lips, saying, "I want to make you even happier, Valda...I want you to be happy because of me..."

Without realizing it, his feelings for her had grown even deeper, and he wanted to give her the best of everything and make sure she never had to worry.

In the private room of the bar, Patrick looked at his friend.

His usually indifferent face seemed to have softened a bit, and his sexy lips were slightly upturned with a hint of a smile.

"Are you in a good mood?" Patrick asked.

"Mmm, not bad," Brian admitted readily.

"Because of Valda?" Patrick asked, looking at his friend's eyes. He suddenly felt like he had asked a stupid question. Who else could it be but Valda?

Recently, due to Valda and Brian's breakup, this guy completely gave off an unwelcoming vibe and the atmosphere around him was extremely cold.

Every time he goes to the Hart Group, a bunch of executives come to him to complain.

During that time, he was afraid to get too close to Brian, in case Brian didn't like him and he would have nowhere to turn if that happened.

"By the way, a few days ago I tried to meet up with you but you were busy. How come you have time today?" Patrick touched his nose and changed the subject.

"Valda's studio has to work overtime today," said Brian.

Patrick suppressed the urge to roll his eyes and reluctantly returned to Valda.

"Oh my god, what kind of love potion did Valda give you?" Patrick exaggeratedly acted like he was about to faint. "Do I have to schedule our meetings around her availability now?"

"You can also understand it this way," said Brian.

Patrick was speechless for a moment, but then he said, "By the way, you guys aren't going to break up again, are you?"

"No," Brian said with absolute certainty.

He wouldn't let Valda break up with him again! He was determined to grow old with her!

When he first started dating Valda, it was because of Grace's hope. Grace wanted him to let go of his hopeless feelings and start a new relationship, so he found Valda.

But this time was different. This time, he wasn't doing it for Grace, but for himself.

Just then, his phone rang and Valda's name appeared on the screen.

Brian answered the phone and heard Valda's voice on the other end. "Hey, Brian? I'll be off work in about half an hour."

"Okay, I'll come pick you up," Brian said.

"Okay, see you later," Valda said.
