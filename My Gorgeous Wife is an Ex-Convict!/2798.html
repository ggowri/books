"I only said that at the press conference to dispel the rumors as soon as possible. Weiss Group's share price had dropped due to those rumors, causing a lot of losses. It's more effective for me to say those things instead of Martin Weiss or to have Weiss Group release a statement," said Kyla.

Grace said with concern, "So you and Martin Weiss..."

"We'll pretend to be a loving couple for a while. We'll find another excuse to announce our divorce once this is over," Kyla said before smiling. "Alright, don't worry about me. Whether it’s pretending to be a loving couple with Martin Weiss or divorcing Martin Weiss is my own choice. No one forced me. I wanted to do it."

"Are you sure?" Grace asked.

Kyla's gaze softened. "I love him. I don't want Weiss Group, which he has worked so hard to build, to suffer any more losses, so I'm willing to pretend to be a loving couple with him. However, I'm also willing to divorce him and set him free because he doesn't love me back."

Her tone was calm when she spoke. Their love and hatred had turned into peace again.

Perhaps it was the best outcome for her and Martin Weiss.

"By the way, have you seen the news about Brian and Valda?" Kyla asked as she changed the topic.

Grace knew what news Kyla was referring to. "Yes."

"Judging from the photo, Brian must be serious about Valda. I had doubts before, but I think my doubts are unnecessary now," Kyla said with a smile.

"So am I. I was afraid that Brian would find any random woman because of what I said to him, but it doesn't look like that now. And even though I've only met Valda a couple of times and haven't spent much time with her, I can tell she's sincere. Perhaps only such a sincere woman can move a person like Brian," said Grace.

"Yeah, so you should focus on looking after your unborn baby. I'm waiting for you to give birth to your baby safely and be their godmother," said Kyla.

After Kyla left, Jason walked in. Looking at the look on his wife's face, he smiled. "Alright, it seems we have nothing to worry about anymore."

"Yeah, Kyla has her own plans. Besides, no one forced Kyla to do it. She did it willingly," said Grace, who was also relieved to know this.

"Worry less about other people's business. The doctor also said you shouldn't have mood swings," Jason said softly.

"Got it. I’ll take good care of the baby in my belly!"

"No, your main concern is to take good care of yourself," Jason corrected her. She mattered the most to him!

Grace stared at the person in front of her. Her figure was reflected in his beautiful peach eyes as if she meant everything to him.

She knew he probably would not survive either if anything happened to her.

She selfishly wanted the baby and dragged him into it.

"You're right. My main concern should be taking good care of myself," she murmured. She had thought that it did not matter whether she existed.

However, he had let her know how important she was to him!

Grace moved her body, trying to roll over to lie flat. She had just moved her body when she suddenly gasped and frowned.

"What's the matter?" he immediately asked nervously.
