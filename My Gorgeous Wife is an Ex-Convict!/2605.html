Chapter 2603

Lina's keen eye saw the ring on Amber Mitchell's left ring finger. "What's with the ring?"

"It's my wedding ring," Amber Mitchell replied with a faint smile.

Lina asked in surprise, "A wedding ring? You're married?"

Amber Mitchell admitted graciously, "Yes, I'm married. I'm married to a man who died trying to save me. Though he's dead, he's the only husband I have. I feel content to be able to marry him after all these years."

Grace said nothing as she had already learned about this from her phone conversation with Amber Mitchell previously.

Lina looked surprised. When she heard Amber Mitchell talking about her husband, the nostalgia and contentment on her face made her heart ache.

"You must love your husband very much," she murmured.

"Yeah, I love Herman." Amber Mitchell smiled. "There's nothing better than being with the one you love. I'll never have this chance, and I can only be with Herman in my heart, but Kyla still has the chance. I really hope she and Martin Weiss can have a happy ending with her trip to L City this time.

Speaking of Martin Weiss, Lina could not help but ask Grace, "Is it true that one’s memory could only be restored after saying the password?"

"In principle, yes. Of course, there’s another way. There’s a chance of recovery if one is forcefully triggered." It was how Jay got all his memories back.

However, Jay and Martin Weiss were different. Jay lost part of his memory after being hypnotized, while Martin Weiss remembered everything except his feelings for her.

"Could we get the password from the hypnotist?" Lina asked.

Grace shook her head. "I've asked. The hypnotist doesn't know Martin Weiss's password because Martin Weiss himself had asked for the hypnosis. Hence, Martin Weiss set the password himself too."

"…" Lina was speechless. "In other words, unless Martin Weiss tells us the password himself, no one will know what it is."

"Yeah. Unless Martin Weiss wants to lift the hypnosis himself, we have no other way," said Grace. Martin Weiss was willing to donate his liver to Kyla a second time, but he still chose to leave her and return to L City. One could tell that Martin Weiss did not want to lift his hypnosis at all.

He did not want to lift the hypnosis and recover his feelings from back then. Martin Weiss was the one taking charge of the situation here.

They had no idea if Kyla could make Martin Weiss change his mind during her trip to L City this time.

The three sighed. God knew what password Martin Weiss had set!

Over in L City, Kyla was also pondering the same question.

Standing in the study, she looked at the impatient-looking Martin Weiss and said, "I... I only came in to have a word with you. I'll leave once I’m done."

"I thought I made it clear to you last time we were at the yard," said Martin Weiss.

Kyla took a deep breath and suddenly said, "Martin Weiss, I love you. I love you very much!"

Martin Weiss stiffened slightly and frowned. "I told you. Whether you love me or not doesn't matter to me anymore."

Kyla bit her lip before saying, "Martin Weiss, I like you."

Martin Weiss frowned deeper.
