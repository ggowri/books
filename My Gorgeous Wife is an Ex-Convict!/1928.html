Chapter 1926

It was because she knew how hard it was.

Mason's past was different from that of ordinary children. Secretary Wang's method of education was to completely destroy a child's innocence.

Fortunately, Mason met Veronica Barlow. The little girl had given Mason warmth and something to hold on to.

Apart from Veronica Barlow it was difficult for Mason to open his heart up to others.

However, Mason was worried about her and Jay after seeing the news. Did that mean that they were slowly starting to have a place in Mason's heart?

Maybe Mason would accept them one day.

Grace walked up to Mason and crouched down with her eyes looking straight into the little one’s eyes. "I'm happy that you came to see us."

Confusion flickered in the little one's eyes as if he was wondering why the one he should call Mommy was happy.

'Wasn't she hurt? Shouldn't she be sad?'

He slightly raised his hands toward Grace's neck. Veronica Barlow wanted to stop him, but it was too late.

His small hands grasped Grace's neck. As there was still gauze around her neck, his grasp touched her wound. Her brows began to frown.

Jason saw this and roared, "Mason, let go!" He knew his son had hurt Grace.

The little one seemed to be startled by Jason's shouts, and his little body suddenly tensed.

Grace hurriedly soothed the little one and whispered, "Jay, Mason is just concerned about my injury."

With that, she gently took Mason's hand again and kept his fingers off the gauze around her neck but gently laid it flat on her neck.

Grace said with a smile, "See, it doesn't hurt this way. Mason, my injury will heal soon. As long as I don't touch the wound, it won't hurt much."

The little one's dark eyes stared at Grace. "It won't hurt?"

"Yes, it won't hurt," said Grace.

Then, she saw a faint smile on the little one's lips, making his indifferent face appear friendly.

Jason looked at Mason and somehow got lost in a trance.

Half an hour later, Jason ordered his staff to escort Veronica Barlow and Mason safely back to the He family’s house. Finally, the ward was quiet again.

Grace asked Jason, "What were you thinking when you were looking at Mason just now?"

Jason pressed his lips together gently, surprised that she even noticed.

Looking at Jason's hesitant face, Grace said, "Jay, we're husband and wife. You can tell me anything."

Jason then slowly said, "Mason... looks more and more like that woman now, except for his eyes..."
