Chapter 3405

 

"I'm afraid of you? Don't make me laugh. I can say it again and again. You're just a criminal's son. Do you think being the young master of the Barlow family makes you special? No matter what, you can't erase the fact that you have the blood of a criminal running through your veins..." Yvonne said recklessly, not knowing any better.

Just as William was about to make a move, a hand moved even faster than him.

Yvonne suddenly heard a loud and crisp slap, and in the next moment, she covered her cheek in disbelief.

The person who hit her was none other than today's protagonist - Jasper!

"Yi... Jasper, did you hit me?" Yvonne asked.

"Yes, so what if I hit you? If you dare to say such things, why wouldn't I dare to hit you?" Jasper said, feeling his palms burning.

This was the first time she had ever slapped someone, and even she didn't know how she had managed to do it.

She only heard what the other person said, got very angry, and then slapped them.

"He...his mommy is a criminal, and she even harmed your mommy. I'm speaking up for your family, why did you still hit me?" said Yvonne.

Jasper stared at the other person and said, "Our family matters don't require your help, and today is my birthday banquet. I don't welcome you to attend, so please leave immediately. If you don't leave, I will have the security guards escort you out!"

She said this without any room for maneuver.

Yvonne was furious, "You... want to kick me out?"

"Can't I still catch up with you?" Jasper asked in response.

This statement made Yvonne's face turn red and white. After a moment, she glared at Jasper and William with hatred, then turned around and left.

Instead of being forcibly removed by security later, she would rather find an excuse to leave early on her own and save face.

But Yvonne never expected that Jasper would protect William like this.

Let's wait and see!

Yvonne thought to herself that she would eventually get revenge for today's humiliation.

After Yvonne left, Jasper turned to William.

At this moment, William lowered his head, his long eyelashes covering his eyes, making it impossible to know what he was thinking.

Jasper held William's hand and said, "Let's go. I have some new friends who haven't met you yet and want to get to know you."

But William didn't take a step, instead he suddenly said, "Do you... still want to be friends with me, be with me?"

"What?" Jasper didn't understand at first.

William raised his head and looked at Jasper with a steady gaze, "Like the woman just said, my mother is the one who harmed your mother. I am the son of a criminal. Would you still want to be with me?"

Jasper raised his eyes and looked at him. At this moment, his face was pale, and his gaze towards William was even cautious and nervous.

Jasper thought for a moment, then reached out his hand and extended it towards William's hand.

As her hand was about to touch his, he shuddered slightly and seemed to want to avoid it.
