Chapter 4200

Kit's body tensed up.

Because of his claustrophobia, at this moment, the fear caused by the darkness spread through his body.

Even though he kept telling himself to be strong, and not to be afraid, he still needed to protect Tina and the baby in Tina's belly.

But his body was trembling badly.

"Are you afraid of the dark?" Tina's voice came from beside him.

"I..." he struggled to speak, but his voice was trembling with just this one word.

As if he was silently admitting to this answer.

"Don't be afraid." Tina gently said, reaching out her hand and tightly holding Kit's hand.

He was startled, feeling the warmth transmitted from her palm.

"Someone will come to repair the elevator, it will be fine soon, don't worry, I'm here with you..." her voice continued to come steadily and powerfully.

His heart gradually calmed down.

It was as if no matter what happened, she would be by his side.

When the lights in the elevator came back on, the elevator started moving again. Kit looked up and looked at the person standing beside him.

On that handsome face, there was a gentle smile, and in her eyes, there was still concern for him!

In an instant, he couldn't help but be stunned, his gaze unable to move away from her.

It had always been her!

In the past, she had helped him overcome this fear in a pitch-black store.

And now, it was still her who helped him drive away all his fears in that pitch-black environment just now.

How could he not love her, with her being like this?
