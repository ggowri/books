Chapter 3060

This Brian is unlike any he has ever seen before.

"Brian, do you really love Valda?" Jason murmured.

Brian's body suddenly trembled, and in his phoenix-like eyes, there seemed to be an endless amount of pain.

"Love...yes...yes, I love her...I love her so much..." At this moment, he finally spoke clearly and confessed his love for her.

"Why am I so foolish? I clearly love her, so why can't I bring myself to admit it? She's been wanting to hear me say 'I love you' all this time! But...when she asked me before, why didn't I say it? Why? Why?!"

At this moment, he intensely hates himself,

Why didn't he have the courage to confirm his love for her back then? Why didn't he speak up at that time?

Even when she was engulfed by the waves, he never said "I love you" once in his life.

Will she be disappointed? Will she be sad?

Until her death, she thought he had never loved her!

No, she won't die, she definitely won't die!

"Jason, find Valda for me, find her, I want to tell her I love her, I want to tell her..." At this moment, Brian was shouting like a madman.

Jason seemed to see himself back then.

After Grace fell off the cliff with the car, he almost went crazy with pain.

Only those who have experienced it can understand that kind of pain.

But in the end, Grace and the children returned to his side.

But can Valda still come back to Brian alive?

"I love her, I love her, haha... I love her..." Brian was shouting, crying and laughing at the same time, his handsome face twisted in madness.

At that moment, Jason suddenly had a feeling that if Valda was really no longer in this world, then Brian would go crazy.

Become a complete and utter madman!

For three consecutive days, Valda was not found. Only a piece of torn cloth from her dress was found downstream on the second day.

This piece of fabric, however, is treasured by Brian and he wraps it around his wrist, always carrying it with him.

On the third day, both Phoebe and Whitney were caught.

These two individuals had originally planned to escape to a foreign country and had already made careful arrangements.

However, in the end, it was all for naught.

After being arrested, Whitney shifted all the blame onto Phoebe, claiming that she was merely being used and that Phoebe was the mastermind behind everything.

All the plans were made by Phoebe.

Regarding this, Phoebe neither admitted nor denied it. She simply made a request to meet Brian!

She will only confess everything after seeing Brian.

When Brian heard Phoebe's request from the police, he agreed to it.
