Chapter 2792

"What happened?" Brian asked, looking at Valda Theller's suddenly serious expression.

"It's about Kyla," said Valda Theller as she held the phone between herself and Brian so they could both watch the news.

Kyla was in some hall, surrounded by reporters who kept raising tough questions. All of them seemed to focus on ‘divorce’ and ‘illness’.

Kyla's answered the reporters, "We're not divorced. Martin Weiss and I are in a stable marriage, and as to why he brought Shana Nicholls to the banquet you mentioned that day, it’s because Shana Nicholls is my husband's private nurse. I wasn't feeling well that day, so she attended it instead of me. I didn't think it would lead to a misunderstanding."

"Does that mean Mr. Martin Weiss has a problem with his health?"

"My husband needed a nurse because he donated his liver to me twice. I probably wouldn't have survived if it hadn't been for my husband. He's doing well. Otherwise, the doctors wouldn't have discharged him. We hired a professional nurse to take care of him because I wanted someone to take care of him when I'm not around," replied Kyla.

"Is it okay that you two live apart? Since your husband donated his liver to you twice, shouldn't you stay with him and take care of him yourself?"

"I gave birth and had the second liver transplant surgery simultaneously, so I'm not well enough to care for a patient. I stay in Emerald City because I want to care for my mother, and my eldest son goes to school here. I hope to move back to L City after my eldest son finishes his studies this semester."

"I heard you run a store. You're Martin Weiss's wife. The Weiss family can easily afford a better house for you and your children. Besides that, you won't have to take care of your children and run a store at the same time."

"My mother mainly runs the store. I'm only helping her out. I might be Martin Weiss's wife, but my mother doesn't want to rely on the Weiss family and wants to earn her own living. As her daughter, I can't say much about it."

"Then why are you living in a humble house behind the store with your children? Besides, you seem to have lived there for years, and you only got married about a year ago but your eldest son is already 10 years old. Does this mean you two don't really have a good relationship and maybe you wouldn't have gotten married if you hadn’t gotten pregnant with your second child?"

"I got pregnant with our second child because we have a good relationship. I had low self-esteem and thought I wasn’t good enough for Martin. But I gradually got over it because Martin was persistent. I'll move back to L City and live with Martin in the future. I also want people to stop speculating about our relationship!"

Kyla's answer was almost textbook, giving a picture of marital stability while also shaping a good image of her husband, Martin Weiss.

Only then did Valda Theller realize that Kyla's husband was Martin Weiss, the chairman of Weiss Group in L City.

The Weiss family of L City was affluent!

"Kyla's husband donated his liver to Kyla twice, so they must have a good relationship," murmured Valda Theller. Then, she asked Brian, "Do you know Martin Weiss?"
