Chapter 1245

With the Reed family's wealth, even if the babies were born premature, they would receive the best care, so their chances of survival were much higher.

"But have you ever thought about what will happen to your body?" His voice rose unconsciously.

"If I’m going to take a risk anyway, I want to risk it with my kids. I'm their mommy. Why should they take the risk while I'm safe and sound?"

He froze when he heard the word ‘mommy’.

His mother had left him behind when he was badly injured.

However, the woman in front of him was going to risk her life for her children.

'She's a very different person from my mother... She would be a good mommy if she became one, and our kids won't be like me, will they?'

"Jay, I really want to keep these three children.

"You can say that I'm too idealistic or that I'm too emotional and irrational, but... you don't know how desperate I was when the doctors told me I would never be able to conceive. I was afraid I would end up alone in the world without even a family!

"But now that I finally have three children, three more family members who are related to me by blood, I really... can't give up a single one. I know it’ll be risky, but I want to fight for them. I want to fight for myself and the children!"

She told him everything on her mind, holding nothing back. There was a determination in her eyes.

"You'd risk your life for the children, wouldn't you?" His voice rang faintly.

"Yes!" she answered him without hesitation.

"Then you..." His voice broke off, and the rest of the words got stuck in his throat. He did not go on.

"What is it?" She blinked her eyes in confusion.

"Nothing." Jason looked away. For a moment, he had the impulse to ask her if she would risk her life for him too!

Was he expecting something? Expecting her to do the same for him?

How ridiculous!

Even if she was willing to stay with him and work on the marriage with him, it was only for the children and to give the children a happy family.

"It's late. It's time for bed. We'll talk about the babies when we go to the hospital tomorrow!" Jason said coldly.

They did not turn off the lights. The two lay on the bed as the soft light filled the room.

Grace knew Jason was indulging her by leaving the lights on to let her sleep.
