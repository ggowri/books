"Stop. Stop it! Do... Do you know who Valda's boyfriend is? It's Brian. Brian won't let you off easily if you treat her like this!" Camille Strickland said.

However, to Camille Strickland's disappointment, the crowd laughed instead of stopping. "Camille Strickland, you should at least make up a decent lie. How dare you say Valda Theller's boyfriend is Brian? How ridiculous."

"Yeah, my husband is Jason if her boyfriend is Brian!"

'Besides that, the actors who participated in the strike were replaced afterward. If it’s someone who’s capable of doing that... Could it really be Brian?'

Barbara Schuman was shocked at the thought of this but immediately denied it.
