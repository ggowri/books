Chapter 1048

In particular, most of the boy bands were produced by Hart Group's talent agency.

Lina kept telling him that he was the most handsome and best-looking man, and she was crazy about him. However, she watched their videos all the time, making him grit his teeth in jealousy.

If he tried to hide her phone from her, she would put on a pitiful look and say that she only had such one hobby she enjoyed in life. When he heard this, he would be embarrassed for having used such forceful means.

Hadwin Stephenson felt that Lina had him by the short hairs!

"I didn't expect you to be interested in these things, President Stephenson," said Brian.

The corners of Hadwin Stephenson's mouth twitched. "I just think there are too many boy bands, but most of them are similar. I hope your company can consider producing more girl groups."

Lina was not interested in women. It would be alright if they produced more girl groups.

Brian raised his eyebrows as if he was surprised to hear Hadwin Stephenson say such words.

"You seem to care a lot about the entertainment industry, President Stephenson. Do you have a girl group you fancy?"

Before Hadwin Stephenson could speak, Lina's voice rang. "Hmm? Hadwin, which girl group do you fancy? Why haven't I heard you mention this?"

Hadwin Stephenson had a headache. He did not want to make himself a joke in front of outsiders, so he said, "Didn’t you want to meet some celebrities tonight? I saw a few just now. Do you want me to take you to them?"

"Huh? Who are they?" Lina's eyes lit up immediately. Then, she gestured to Grace that she would see her later and quickly followed Hadwin Stephenson to ‘meet’ the celebrities.

After all, it was why she attended the charity gala today!

Grace walked over to Brian and said, "You… Uh... Don't have to worry about me. I can do just fine here by myself."

Brian said, "It's alright. I'll stay here with you. By the way, is your friend a fan?"

"Yeah." Grace could not help smiling at the mention of Lina.

Brian looked at her smiling face in jealousy. He could see that she was important to her. 'When will she smile because of me?'

"Will you smile because of me one day?" The words escaped Brian's mouth.

"What?" Grace had not wrapped her head around it.

"Nothing." Brian smiled faintly.

Just then, a figure suddenly came over. "Brian, didn't you say you wouldn't attend the gala? Why..."

Halfway through the sentence, Stella realized it was Grace standing next to Brian. The words were immediately stuck in her throat.

"Grace, why are you here with Brian?" Stella glared at Grace. There was jealousy in her eyes, especially after seeing the purple gown she was wearing.

She had her eye on the gown for a while now and even asked Brian to give it to her, but Brian bought her another purple dress instead.

Even though the other dress was also expensive, this particular purple gown was still on her mind.

She did not expect Grace to be wearing it today.
