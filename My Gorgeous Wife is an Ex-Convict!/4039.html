"It's probably better if we sleep separately, otherwise I'm afraid I won't be able to resist," Mick said uncomfortably.

Nancy blinked and immediately understood. "Um...if you really can't resist, I can help you," she said, blushing.

Although she was still in postpartum recovery and couldn't have sex, there were still some things they could do.

"No need, you just rest," Mick said.

"Don't you want me to help you?" She leaned closer to him, her sweet scent driving him crazy.

"It's not that, I just don't want you to get too tired," he reluctantly said.

He had always been proud of his self-control, but in front of her, it was fragile and easily broken.

"But I want to," Nancy said. "I want to touch you and be closer to you. Besides, don't think of me as too delicate. It won't tire me out."

"But..." He still hesitated.

"Mick, it's not just you who has desires. I can't resist either," Nancy said. "So, can we sleep together? I want to fall asleep with you holding me."

He looked at her and hoarsely said, "Okay..."

After a night of intimacy, Nancy fell asleep contentedly.

And Mick looked at his sleeping wife and couldn't help but smile bitterly. It seemed that even this wasn't enough to satisfy him!

His desire for her was far greater than she imagined!

But as long as she was okay, nothing else mattered.

If she also has this need and desire for him, then he can suppress his impulses and try to satisfy her as much as possible.

"Nancy, I love you, and you will always love me... okay?" His low voice echoed in the air.

In response, there was only the sound of her peaceful breathing.

A smile unconsciously appeared on his lips.

There were no conflicts between mother-in-law and daughter-in-law or between sisters-in-law.

Jasper was eager to take care of Nancy. If Nancy felt uncomfortable in any way, Jasper was the first to get anxious.

Nancy had a good impression of her mother-in-law, Grace.

Although Grace was a highly successful lawyer and the head of the Reed family outside, she had a kind of inexplicable closeness to Nancy.

Perhaps it was because of their similar experiences.

So, in some ways, this mother-in-law was the one who could best understand Nancy's feelings.

Grace also took care of Nancy very well, not being overbearing, and even consulting with Nancy on some matters.

Especially with regards to the wedding, she respected Nancy's opinions.

Like a kind elder, she listened to Nancy's thoughts and gave appropriate advice. This way of getting along made Nancy feel very comfortable.
