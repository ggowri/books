Chapter 3778

Veronica was stunned, staring at the person in front of her, completely unaware that he was the one who caused him the most pain back then!

"I'm sorry," she said, her voice trembling.

"Okay, I accept," he replied.

She blinked her eyes, was that it?!

Well, he accepted her apology, which was the best outcome, but why did she feel like something was off?

It was like...too casual.

No, it was more like the weight of these things had been building up in her heart for so many years that it had become too heavy.

If only she had understood her feelings earlier, they wouldn't have wasted all these years, and he wouldn't have been hypnotized.

What kind of feeling was it to remember everything that had happened but not feel the same emotions anymore?

Did he really feel nothing as he claimed?

Or was it...a feeling too heavy for him, just like her?

Veronica suddenly stood up and hugged Mason. "We must never be apart again, okay?"

"Never?" He lowered his eyes and rested his head on her chest. "There's nothing in this world that's 'never.'"

Such promises were as elusive as the word "forever."

"Then let's try our best to make 'never' come true," Veronica said.

"Don't you think that if we work hard, we won't have to be apart anymore?" he asked.

"Because I love you, and you love me too. As long as we trust each other enough, nothing can separate us except death," Veronica said.

Mason suddenly looked up, his beautiful black eyes staring straight at Veronica.

"You love me, and I love you?" His voice was huskier than usual.

"Yes," she said, meeting his gaze without flinching. "Or did I say something wrong?"

His eyelashes trembled slightly, and his gaze became even deeper. After what seemed like an eternity, he finally said, "No, you're right. You love me, and I love you."

It turned out that his feelings for her, even under hypnosis, would still fall in love with her again.

Only her! Because she was the one he was destined to love.

Veronica felt as if her whole body was enveloped in a warm embrace. In this moment, she couldn't help but lower her head and kiss his lips.

She loved him, really loved him.

But she seemed to have realized this fact a little too late. If she had realized it earlier, their pain would have been much less.

From now on, she would love him better! She would work hard to heal the wounds of the past and make them happy.

This kiss, initiated by her, was gentle at first, but after he took control, it became dominant and intense, as if all their emotions were poured into this kiss.

This kiss made her almost breathless. Just when she thought her heart was about to stop, his lips finally parted from hers.
