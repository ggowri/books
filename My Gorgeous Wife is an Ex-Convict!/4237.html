Chapter 4235 Does He Really Love Her?

Chandler's expression becomes somewhat stiff. The pen in his hand, which was signing documents, draws a long line on the paper.

"What did you say?" he looks at her, surprised.

"I say, how did our first time making love happen?" she asked, curious.

Chandler's expression seemed somewhat strange. "Why would you want to know that?"

"Why wouldn't I want to know? After all, the first time making love is an important thing for anyone," Tina said.

Chandler's face showed a conflicted expression.

"What's wrong? Did our first time happen when I didn't want it to?" Tina speculated.

"No!" Chandler quickly denied it.

"So it did happen when you didn't want it to?" Tina guessed again.

"No, it's not that!" This time, the answer was slower than the last.

So Tina understood what was probably going on.

"So, it really did happen when you didn't want it to?" she said.

"No, don't overthink it," he sighed, putting away the pen in his hand.

"Then tell me, how did it really happen?" Tina asked.

"It was because I wasn't feeling well, and you wanted to help me, so we made love. For me, I didn't feel unwilling or unwilling, but I just felt that we shouldn't have rushed into it in that situation." He felt sorry for her.

If he could, if he could have restrained the pain at that time...

"Because of a physical reason?" Tina pondered, "Were you drugged at that time?"

These past two days, she watched a web series where the male lead was drugged and then made love to the female lead.

Chandler pinched his nose. "It's really not like what you're thinking. It was because I wasn't feeling well, and I wasn't drugged or anything."

It was just that the Hunter family curse had flared up, causing excruciating pain in his body.

And the only person who could make his body not hurt was her!

"But if you weren't feeling well, and I made love to you to help, why does it sound like I'm some kind of beast?" Tina grumbled.

Chandler listened to her increasingly outrageous words and frowned. "Tina, never speak of yourself like that. Do you know how grateful I am to you? How guilty do I feel towards you? Originally, you could have had more choices, but you were confined to me."

Confusion flashed in Tina's eyes. "Why do I have trouble understanding what you're saying?"

"If you can fall in love with me again in the future, then you will understand," Chandler said.

Tina pursed her lips. In other words, he would only explain the things she didn't understand, the things she couldn't understand after she fell in love with him?

"What if I don't fall in love with you?" she asked again.

He smiled faintly. "Then you don't need to understand the things I just said, right? If you don't fall in love with me and want to end this marriage, you can do so at any time."

If she truly couldn't love him, then he could set her free.

He placed the choice in front of her with those words.

Tina felt a bit dazed for a moment, a wrenching feeling surging up once again.

Why, even though she feels that this man should love her, and everyone around them says that he loves her, why can he so easily give up their marriage?

Does he truly love her, then?

At this moment, Tina suddenly has this doubt!
