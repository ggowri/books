Chapter 3597

"Just now...when I gave them the money, we chatted a bit and I heard them mention it," he replied.

"Then why don't we think of another way to help them?" she suggested.

William took a deep breath. "Jasper, everyone has their own way of living in this world. If we try to help everyone completely, we'll never have enough time. We gave them money just now, and that should at least relieve their financial worries for a while. We can't be responsible for their entire lives."

Jasper sighed at his words.

Although his words sounded harsh, she knew he was telling the truth.

That night, Jasper stayed at the Barlow House.

She couldn't sleep that night, so she got up and walked out of her room, heading to William's bedroom to see if he was asleep. However, when she opened the door, she didn't see him in the room.

Jasper felt strange. It was already late, so where could he be if not in his room?

Exiting William's bedroom, Jasper caught a glimpse of another room not far away from him from the corner of his eye.

It was... the music room.

Does William go to the music room?

As far as she knew, William didn't play the piano very often now, only occasionally.

Nevertheless, Jasper still walked up to the music room and gently pushed open the door.

The intense sound of the piano suddenly filled her ears.

Jasper couldn't help but furrow her brow. The piece William was playing now had a fierce and dark tone, filled with a sense of contradiction that could even give someone goosebumps involuntarily.

She rarely heard him play such pieces because she preferred light and bright or lyrical music, so he usually played more of those types of pieces.
