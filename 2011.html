Ten thousand Celestial Deity Level elites, all composed of prodigies from the Paramount Supreme Clan and the top ten Upper Races.

On the other side, God of Light, the God of Light, was caught in hesitation and conflict.

At the very least, the elite prodigies of the Sanctum of Light were unable to gain any real advantage.

"Once the full-scale war begins, the Nine Dark Sovereigns will be the greatest threat to me! Well, technically... it's the Eight Sovereigns now. Icy Nine is already dead!

It was carnage!

The Human Community had now achieved two consecutive victories!

Though, it was just a small improvement.

After all, nearly all ten thousand of the elite prodigies from the Sanctum of Light had been wiped out!

Only a few dozen had survived!

God of Light felt devastated, but she didn't dare show it. She could only suppress her grief deep inside.

Osborne could sense God of Light's sorrow. He wanted to tell her that those people weren't really dead—that he could revive them anytime...

But in the end, he held back.

"The fact that I can resurrect others... the fewer people who know, the better. Even if she's God of Light..."

Up next was the Deity Overlord Level battle, and representing the Human Community would be ten thousand peak Deity Overlord elites from the Hudson family!

Could they win this battle?
