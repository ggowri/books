Thousands of blood-red locks of hair were absorbing massive amounts of blood energy from Mosley, the Dark Deity.

Osborne's flesh and blood had already soaked up a considerable amount, with every cell now pushed to its limit, like an inflated balloon ready to explode.

With no other choice, Osborne steered the blood energy into his bones, which were composed of stronger, denser cells.

The vast energy was quickly absorbed by his skeletal cells, making them rapidly grow stronger.

A look of surprise and joy appeared on Osborne's face.

He thought, "Indeed, the cells of my bones are more powerful, able to absorb more blood energy without easily collapsing. Maybe I can really suck Mosley dry!"

Osborne gritted his teeth, persevering.

Meanwhile, Mosley was on the verge of insanity. Her blood energy was draining rapidly, and she was powerless to stop it. She now felt the despair that Osborne had experienced before.

She screamed, "What the hell is this?

"Damn it, damn it all! Let me go!

"I am the future Mosley, the ruler of the stars, a true deity.

"How can I die in the hands of a mere mortal?"

As her blood energy continued to drain, Mosley's body gradually withered, her breath became weaker, and her life force rapidly faded.

She begged, "Please, just let me go!

"I am a true deity. If you release me, I can give you everything you desire.

"I can make you the ruler of this planet.

"I can even take you into the dazzling space, where you can witness the prosperity of countless alien races. Please, I don't want to die."

Despite Mosley's pleas, Osborne remained unmoved.

He was in a precarious situation himself.

As Mosley's blood energy was absorbed by his bones, he began to feel that they, too, were reaching their limit.

The terrible power surged within his skeleton, making it stronger but also pushing it to the brink of collapse.

A faint glow emitted from his bones, indicating the escaping energy.

"Damn it!"

Osborne clenched his fists. If his bones couldn't hold the blood energy, everything he had done would be in vain.

His bones might even explode like bombs, leaving him with no flesh or blood remaining.

Sweat beaded on Osborne's forehead as he frantically searched for a solution.

Even his skull was starting to emit light as it absorbed the vast blood energy and teetered on the edge of collapse.

Mosley, noticing his condition, suddenly broke into laughter.

She laughed, her face twisted with malice.

"Foolish mortal, you dare to absorb my blood energy with your fragile body? Even though I am heavily injured, and my power is a fraction of what it once was, I am still a true deity! My blood energy is not something a mere mortal like you can handle.

"You're going to be blown to smithereens!

"Your body, your bones, will explode like balloons!

"Hahaha, you're finished!"

Mosley suddenly ceased resisting the blood-red locks that were draining her blood energy and even actively funneled more of it into Osborne's body through her mouthpart.

Her eyes were filled with ice-cold insanity.

She wanted to push Osborne's body to its limits and make it explode!

If his body couldn't hold the energy, it would erupt, allowing her to easily reclaim it.

She screamed repeatedly, her voice filled with malice, "Die!

"Let the energy overwhelm you!"

As Mosley's mouthpart frantically poured blood energy into Osborne, his body began to bulge at a visible rate. Cracks appeared on his flesh, and blood oozed out.

Even his bones, now emitting a bright glow, seemed to groan in pain as cracks rapidly spread across them, threatening to explode at any moment.

Panic surged through Osborne's mind.

He thought, "This is the end!

Mosley's blood energy is too powerful for my body and bones to handle.

I'm going to burst!

What can I do? Is there any way out?"

His brain raced, searching for a solution.

"Die!" Mosley snarled.

She wore a disdainful smile as she envisioned Osborne's body exploding.

But just then, a sudden spark of inspiration flashed through Osborne's mind.

"Maybe... there is a way!"
