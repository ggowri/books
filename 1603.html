Kenneth's words sparked a glimmer of excitement in everyone's eyes, their breaths quickening with anticipation.

Most of the people present lacked powerful backgrounds; otherwise, they wouldn't have been sent to support the perilous mission at Divine Peak Nine.

Many had never even laid eyes on mid-grade Essence Crystals, relying solely on the meager military stipend from the Wild Flame Army to fund their training.

Now, Kenneth was telling them that they might find a Celestial Crystal atop Divine Peak Nine!

What was a Celestial Crystal?

It was a source of energy far superior to even top-grade or Ultimate Essence Crystals.

Rumor had it that a single Celestial Crystal contained the energy equivalent to 100 million low-grade Essence Crystals, and it was far more refined.

Just one piece could propel any practitioner to at least the Deity Overlord realm!

But that wasn't all.

Celestial Crystals had a unique effect that no other Essence Crystal possessed. They could enhance one's talent and break through bottlenecks.

For those stuck at a plateau for years, feeling like their potential had been drained, obtaining a Celestial Crystal could easily shatter those barriers and elevate their abilities to new heights.

Thus, Celestial Crystals were legendary treasures, priceless and elusive.

Anyone among them who managed to secure one from Divine Peak Nine would have their fate transformed, rising to become something greater than they ever imagined.

And aside from Celestial Crystals, Divine Peak Nine also held Miracle Elixirs that could elevate one's realm and Ultimate divine artifacts!

Just acquiring any one of these items could drastically change their destinies.

The crowd's breaths turned shallow, their eyes gleaming with longing.

Even Osborne's heart raced at the thought.

Celestial Crystals!

Miracle Elixirs!

Ultimate divine artifacts!

Despite his own collection of treasures, he felt powerless against the allure of these items, and Kenneth's words only fueled his excitement about Divine Peak Nine.

Kenneth, noticing the eager expressions around him, couldn't help but smirk.

"I suggest you all temper your enthusiasm.

"To put it bluntly, if these treasures were so easily obtainable, would they even be within your reach? Do you think the Paramount Supreme Clan doesn't want them? Or that the geniuses from the Upper Races don't have their eyes on these prizes?

"They know full well that there are countless treasures atop the divine peaks, yet they hesitate to venture here. Why? Because Divine Peak Nine is incredibly dangerous!"

Kenneth pointed at his scarred face. "Do you see this? I can honestly tell you that to this day, I have no idea how my face ended up like this! I never even set foot on Divine Peak Nine. I was just on the outskirts when I suddenly passed out!

"When I came to, I was left looking like this! Even my foundation got mysteriously damaged, and I've been stuck ever since, unable to advance my cultivation.

"I, Kenneth, was ruined by this hellish place called Divine Peak Nine! If it weren't for my greed for the treasures here, if I hadn't been so arrogant to take this risk, I would at least be at the pinnacle of Deity Overlord by now, maybe even a Deity King warrior!"

As he spoke, his face contorted with anguish, the long, centipede-like scar rippling as if it were alive, sending a chill through the onlookers.

"So if you don't want to end up like me, then stay put in the base! If you must act, stick with the main group.

"Even then, it's still dangerous... Otherwise, nearly half of the previous army of 400 thousand wouldn't have fallen! At least you'll be safer moving with the crowd.

"That's all I have to say! Don't let your greed ruin your lives! Divine Peak Nine... it's not a divine peak; it's a bottomless abyss!"

With that, Kenneth turned on his heel and left, leaving an eerie silence in his wake.

The crowd remained frozen, the weight of his warnings settling heavily upon them. No one spoke; they just stared into space, their faces pale and troubled long after Kenneth had disappeared.

Meanwhile, in Athena's room, she stood before a one-way mirror, watching the gathering crowd on the deck, observing Kenneth as he walked away, and then her eyes narrowed at Osborne, who was mingling among the people.

A cold fury flickered in her gaze. "Finally, we've reached the base of Divine Peak Nine!

"Osborne, you bastard, you've turned Calvin into a waste of space, and I'm going to make you pay for it!

"Just wait for the day you die in misery!"

She muttered under her breath, then pivoted to face Calvin, who sat slumped on the couch, looking dejected and hopeless. A wave of anger welled up in her chest.

"Enough with the gloom and doom! Just because I can't cure your divine power essence doesn't mean it can't be restored!"

At her words, Calvin jolted upright, his eyes wide with surprise.

"What did you just say? My divine power essence can still be restored?"

Athena nodded. "In normal circumstances, it would be impossible—unless the Goddess of Life were to revive you! But here, at Divine Peak Nine, there are Miracle Elixirs capable of healing any ailment..."
