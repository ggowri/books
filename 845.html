Osborne's final burst of power was unleashed with reckless abandon. He was no longer mindful of the toll it was taking on his mental strength.

Mosley's mouthpart had penetrated deep into Osborne's body, draining his blood energy, but she was taken aback by the ferocity of his counterattacks.

Yet Mosley didn't flinch. Her massive frame, enveloped in divine power, was bracing itself to withstand the onslaught.

The Dissolving Light shot forth, striking Mosley directly.

The elemental light should have been particularly effective against her evil form.

Though Osborne had pinned high hopes on it, he was quickly disillusioned.

The Dissolving Light could not pierce through the formidable divine power shielding Mosley.

With the severe injuries, Mosley's divine power was reduced to less than one ten-thousandth of its peak strength. Even this diminished divine energy was enough to repel Osborne's Dissolving Light.

The light vanished, only to be replaced by the terrifying Thunder of Judgment.

Bolts of lightning crashed down, engulfing Mosley's colossal form with a thunderous, crackling noise.

Mosley's divine power defense fluctuated violently but remained unshattered, absorbing the full brunt of the thunderous assault.

A look of disdain appeared in Mosley's immense eyes.

Meanwhile, the space around Mosley began to collapse.

The Spatial Collapse was taking effect.

Mosley sensed a hint of danger but made no effort to evade.

She allowed the power of the collapse to envelop her vast body.

The space disintegrated completely. Mosley remained at the core of the collapse, but as the dust settled, she emerged unscathed. Her divine defenses, however, were shattered.

"What?!"

Osborne stared in shock. Spatial Collapse was arguably his most potent attack, yet Mosley had withstood it at its core without any apparent injury.

The sight left him in utter despair.

He thought, "This is what being divine means?

This is beyond terrifying! If Spatial Collapse can't touch her, what else do I have?"

At that moment, Osborne felt hopeless.

He could feel his blood energy being rapidly drained by Mosley's mouth part. He was growing weaker and more exhausted, unable to move at all.

Osborne bit his tongue again, fighting to stay conscious and avoid slipping into unconsciousness.

"I refuse to believe I can't kill you!

"Come on, fight me!"

Osborne roared, activating Thunder Realm, Spatial Domain, and Spirit Realm all at once.

"Thunder of Judgment!

"Mental Shatter!

"Spatial Collapse!"

As he summoned all three realms, the massive drain on his mental energy nearly emptied his reserves, leaving his face pale.

But Osborne had no time to worry about that. With the realms in place, his attacks were even stronger than before.

"Hmph!"

Even Mosley felt a bit of a threat this time, but she still didn't dodge.

Her divine power surged again, and a tremendous force erupted, instantly shattering all three of Osborne's realms.

Osborne, already drained to the brink, felt the backlash of his own power, his vision blurring with stars and his head throbbing painfully.

His blood energy was completely depleted, leaving him unable to even spit out a drop of blood.

Though his Realms had been shattered, Osborne's attack had already erupted, landing on Mosley. Even her divine power couldn't fully withstand it this time, leaving her severely wounded.

Her already battered body grew even more broken.

Mosley let out a furious roar. "Ah! Damn little insect, still struggling even as you die? You're able to hurt me even on the brink of death? You will die!"

A hum filled the air as the suction force from Mosley's mouth part intensified.

Osborne's blood energy was rapidly drained, and his vision started to fade to darkness.

He felt he was on the verge of passing out.

No matter how desperately Osborne bit his tongue, it was futile. His mental strength pool was depleted, leaving him unable to even use a basic power.

His blood energy drained away, and his body felt as frail as that of an eighty-year-old.

"Is this how it ends?" Osborne murmured to himself, truly feeling the edge of despair.

Mosley, being a Dark Deity, was formidable even at a fraction of her full power. Osborne knew he was no match for her.

He cast a final glance at Kendra and Yulissa, sighing deeply, "I'm sorry, I can't save you."

"Go to hell!!"

Osborne slowly closed his eyes, and at that moment, all his blood energy was siphoned away by Mosley's terrifying mouth part.

Mosley's eyes gleamed with excitement.

"Hahaha, such a massive amount of blood energy! Once I digest this, I'll be able to break free! I am back!"
