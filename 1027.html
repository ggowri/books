"What's going on?"

Osborne restrained the impulse to rush out and call an urgent meeting, instead focusing on Kendra's ashen face.

Kendra looked like a ghost, visibly shaking.

"What's happened to Bryce?"

Bryce was no ordinary warrior. He led the Likreia Mountain pirate crew, boasting over 8,000 pirates—all Class A or higher. They ruled a vast stretch of ocean.

In the Godson Rankings, Bryce had always ranked in the top ten, but that was before he'd broken through to Class S.

The idea that even someone like Bryce could be in danger was unnerving.

Since he knew Bryce was in trouble, Osborne couldn't just walk away; he needed to dig deeper and see if he could help.

He owed Bryce a debt of gratitude; Bryce had saved Osborne's life not once but twice during his early, vulnerable days.

He could die if not for Bryce’s help.

Now Bryce’s in trouble, he wouldn't stand idly by if he could help.

"What exactly is happening?!" Osborne raised his voice, stepping closer to Kendra and patting her shoulder gently.

Kendra was once a girl brimming with boundless optimism and a zest for life, sometimes a funny one. She had an insatiable thirst for adventure and play, so much so that she went to great lengths to sneak out, even disguising her true capabilities.

One day, this playful charade backfired when she was captured by pirates and confined in a cage.

Then Osborne intervened and rescued her from the cage. From that moment on, she decided to stay by Osborne's side.

She had been full of laughter, always sharing jokes with Yulissa and Queenie.

But now, her face was pale; her body trembled uncontrollably.

The warmth from Osborne's hand seemed to snap her back to reality, but her worry was evident.

She said, "Osborne, something's happened to my brother!

"A huge group from the Aberrants attacked Likreia Mountain! Everyone is dead! My brother was severely injured and being chased by several Class S warriors. I don't even know where he is!

"Will he... will he die?

"I need to go save him!"

Without hesitation, she made a move to leave, but Osborne firmly grasped her shoulder.

He said, "Kendra, hold on a second.

"It's over a thousand miles to Likreia Mountain. Even if you take off now, you couldn’t catch them! Besides, the ones coming after him are Class S! There's more than one!

What can you really do out there? Can you even save him?

"If you find him, you might just become his burden!"

Kendra froze, her face paler. "What... what should we do then?"

"I'll figure something out," Osborne declared, his expression hardening with resolve.

Just moments ago, he had received the alarming news of a full-scale attack by the Aberrants. Its elite forces had appeared in Dampston, Skyefall, and other coastal provinces.

This onslaught was unlike previous ones; the Aberrants was dismantling all the major powers along its path.

Whether Ancient Families or hidden super-forces, no one was spared.

The Aberrants wasn't just making a power play against the Augustine family or the Draco Group; It aimed to dominate the entire Rosmandi region.

Every superpower within those borders would either bend the knee or be ruthlessly killed.

If Osborne's hunch was right, the Aberrants would conquer province after province until it had complete control over Rosmandi, forcing every superpower to submit.

Eventually, it would reach Newport City for a final showdown with the Augustine family.

That meant, for the moment, the Augustine family was safe.

Osborne mused, "Then I have to go save Bryce! He saved my life before; now he's in danger, and I can't just stand by!"

He turned to Kendra. "I'll go with you.

"Your powers can track him, right? You can sense his energy? Just lead the way, and I'll help you find him!"

Kendra's eyes lit up with hope.

Tears brimmed in her eyes as she looked at Osborne.

"Thank you, Osborne! Thank you so much!"

Osborne replied, waving his hand dismissively, "Let's move! We don't have a moment to waste."

Bryce was being hunted down by a group of Class S warriors. If Osborne guessed correctly, they were likely a squad of Class S clones.

Though Bryce was a prodigy, having just broken into Class S, he had given the Trident of Poseidon to Clarissa, leaving him without his most effective weapon.

Faced with a group of Class S opponents, his chances of holding out were slim.

If they didn't act fast, they might be too late to save him.

"Let's go!" Osborne said, slicing through the air to open a dimensional rift.

He grabbed Kendra's hand and they left.
