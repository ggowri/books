Chapter 99 Beg for His Help

His face remained expressionless, but his eyes betrayed a storm of complex emotions.

His heart ached like a thousand needles as he watched Aurora being brutally beaten.

Yet he made no move to stop it.

He despised Aurora's stubborn nature and her refusal to bend or break.

Only by breaking her spirit and crushing her pride would she finally learn to behave.

His demands were simple. He just wanted her to be ordinary and obedient, nothing more.

He patiently waited for Aurora to beg for his help.

He would take her under his protection without hesitation when she asked.

His eyes remained fixed on Aurora.

The emotion in his gaze was unmistakable.

"Beg me!

Just beg me, and I'll help you!"

Through the pain and humiliation, Aurora slowly raised her head and met Adolph's gaze.

She understood the meaning in his eyes and saw the coldness, anticipation, and barely contained cruelty.

At that moment, her heart turned to ice as her disgust for him reached new depths.

Beg him?

She would rather die than give him that satisfaction.

A mocking smile played on Aurora's swollen face as she stared at Adolph like he was a pathetic clown.

Adolph felt his heart clench painfully as if squeezed by an invisible hand.

He opened his mouth to explain, but the words died in his throat.

No explanation could suffice, and his eyes betrayed a flash of panic and embarrassment.

His feet moved instinctively to help Aurora, but her icy glare stopped him cold, crushing any urge to intervene.

He steeled himself and resisted helping her, his face returning to its mask of indifference. However, his clenched fists revealed his inner turmoil.

Judy mistook Aurora's smile as mockery directed at her, and her rage instantly boiled over.

"You slut," Judy snarled. "How dare you laugh at me? If you love seducing men so much, maybe we should strip you naked right here."

She lunged forward, clawing at Aurora's clothes like a woman possessed.

The two socialites holding Aurora's arms joined in, their manicured nails leaving bloody scratches on her smooth skin.

There was a loud ripping sound.

Aurora's dress tore open with a violent tear.

Aurora's elegant dress was now in tatters from the violent assault by Judy and the other wealthy socialites.

Her body was barely covered, with her smooth back and delicate shoulders exposed and covered in scratches and bruises.

The shredded fabric hung from her limbs, swaying chaotically with her desperate struggles.

Judy's face was contorted with madness, her eyes bloodshot like a rabid animal.

Her hands showed no mercy as she continued to tear at Aurora, each pull unleashing her deep-seated hatred.

"You slut, I'll teach you a lesson today!"

The other socialites joined in, raining down kicks and punches on Aurora without restraint.

"Beat her! Beat her!"

The blows rained down on Aurora like a storm.

She instinctively tried to shield her body with her hands, but the crazed women attacked every exposed inch of her, venting their cruel intentions.

Her face was twisted in agony, tears mixing with blood as they rolled down her cheeks, her tangled hair plastered to her face in a pitiful sight.

The sounds around her faded, and her vision grew dim.

Finally, Judy seemed to tire and stepped back, pointing at Aurora, who lay on the ground as she shouted:

"Get her up! Since she loves seducing men so much, let's throw her into the party and let those men get a good look at her!"

At these words, the socialites' faces showed hesitation.

One of the women hesitantly spoke up, "Mrs. Hawke, this isn't right. It's Madam Hawke's birthday celebration today."

Judy's eyes turned cold as she glared at the socialite. "I'll handle this. What are you afraid of?"

Under her pressure, the two socialites gritted their teeth. They grabbed the half-conscious Aurora, dragging her toward the banquet hall.

Aurora's lifeless feet scraped against the floor, leaving trails of blood.

Her torn evening gown kept falling apart as they dragged her, leaving her body almost completely exposed.

Adolph stood rooted to the spot, watching the scene unfold with turmoil in his heart.

He wanted to step in and stop them, but Bertha quickly blocked his path.

"Adolph, we can't offend the Hawke family. Isn't that why you came to this party to build connections with them?"

Adolph's eyes filled with conflict as he watched Aurora being dragged further away, his guilt and pain reaching unbearable levels.

But Bertha's words left him torn. His feet remained frozen in place as he helplessly watched Aurora being led to what would surely be public humiliation.

"Aurora, it's not that I don't want to help you.

You brought this on yourself.

You were too stubborn; you provoked Mrs. Hawke and refused to beg for mercy.

This humiliation is the price of your difficult personality."

He kept making excuses to justify his inaction.

Eventually, he managed to convince himself.

He even started believing that Aurora had tried to seduce someone's husband and deserved this beating.

Simon watched in horror as Aurora, barely clothed and covered in bruises, was dragged away.

He knew Julian's brutal nature all too well, and his blood ran cold at the thought of how Julian would react seeing Aurora in such a state.

Julian's ruthlessness was legendary throughout Putnam.

Years ago, when a pregnant Judy confronted Julian's mother, the shock caused her death.

Julian, only five years old at the time, responded by pushing the pregnant Judy down a flight of stairs, causing her to miscarry.

Not stopping there, he had arranged for doctors to remove Judy's uterus, ensuring she could never have children again.

Though he seemed to have mellowed over the years, showing a gentler face to the world, Simon knew better. The monster was just better hidden.

Simon couldn't bear to imagine what Julian, a man approaching thirty who had finally found a woman he cared for, would do now that Judy had tortured her like this. Even worse, Simon feared becoming a target of Julian's rage himself.

He struggled desperately against his captors, ignoring the scratches and bruises covering his own body.

"Let her go if you want to live!" Simon roared as he charged toward Aurora.

But before he could get far, Bertha quietly stuck out her leg.

Simon couldn't dodge in time and flew forward, crashing hard onto the ground.

His chin slammed against the hard floor, his teeth cutting into his lip as blood immediately began to flow.

Simon lay there, the pain so intense that he lost consciousness.

Aurora hit the ballroom floor with a dull thud as they threw her down mercilessly.

The once-cheerful chatter of party guests died instantly, replaced by a shocked silence.

Anderson frowned at the scene and said, "Judy, what the hell are you doing?"

His voice carried a note of reproach, clearly displeased with this spectacle.

Judy laughed it off and said, "What am I doing? I'm catching this homewrecker red-handed!"

She pointed at Aurora with a wild, triumphant look. "Everyone, look at this slut who's been sleeping with my husband!"

Anderson's face darkened as he quickly covered her mouth, hissing, "It's my mother's 80th birthday, for God's sake! What's wrong with you!"

Judy yanked his hand away and announced to everyone in the room, "This shameless whore used every dirty trick to seduce my husband and destroy my marriage!"

Her voice echoed through the ballroom, drawing all eyes to the scene.

But before anyone could follow her accusing finger, a cold voice cut through the air. "Since when did you become the star of today's celebration?"

Judy's body stiffened, and as she slowly turned around, she found Julian behind her.
