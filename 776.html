Quasi-Divine Rankings!

Godson Rankings!

The Diviner League issued both rankings, and Osborne had only heard of them. This time, he finally got to see the full list.

The Quasi-Divine Rankings only had 100 individuals on it.

To be ranked, one had to be an S-class Supreme Overlord, and among the best of them. For instance, Wesson Augustine, the Godfather's butler, though an S-class Supreme Overlord, was not on the list.

Osborne quickly scanned the Quasi-Divine Rankings.

As expected, the number one spot was held by Helios Thornberry, named after the Sun God. More than 200 years ago, he was the first to host the Honor Quest Ceremony, defeating all challengers until none dared to face him. He cleared the ceremony without opposition.

His name was highlighted in bold gold letters by the Diviner League.

It was incredibly dazzling!

For some reason, seeing that shining golden name stirred something strange inside Osborne, a sudden impulse.

"If my name appears here one day, glowing in gold like this...

"That would be quite something!"

With that thought, Osborne quickly shook his head.

This was the number one spot on the Quasi-Divine Rankings. It was practically the strongest person on Earth, the one closest to being a god, unmatched by anyone else.

As for him, he had only just entered the Godson Rankings.

He was far, far away from this level.

Not to mention him. Even the legendary powerhouse from Rosmandi, the Godfather, was far from reaching this rank.

The Godfather was only ranked 75th on the Quasi-Divine Rankings.

Additionally, the King of the Aberrants held the 60th spot.

Osborne continued to skim through the list and soon came across a familiar name.

Reginald Hastings!

The Deputy Leader of the League of Divine Descent and patriarch of the Hastings Family from Marcelin. He was also one of the suspects who might have kidnapped Osborne's parents.

His ranking on the Global Quasi-Divine Rankings was much higher than the King of the Aberrants.

He had made it into the top 50.

Ranked 46th!

Osborne couldn't help but take a deep breath, his face turning grim.

Because, at his peak, the Godfather had only been ranked 51st!

That meant Reginald was even stronger than the Godfather at his best!

Osborne clenched his fists.

"If he really is the one who took my parents, then to take revenge and rescue my mother, I'll need to surpass the Godfather at his peak!"

Osborne bit his lip, feeling the immense pressure.

Soon, that pressure became even greater.

Because he spotted yet another familiar name.

Emperor Dare!

He was the top assassin of the Eityreia Organization, who was also a potential suspect in his parents' abduction.

Emperor Dare's ranking on the Global Quasi-Divine Rankings was so high that it made Osborne feel uneasy.

Ranked 11th!

He was just shy of breaking into the top 10.

In other words, if Emperor Dare was the one who kidnapped Osborne's parents. To exact revenge, Osborne would have to become one of the top 10 strongest people in the world.

Osborne took a deep breath, trying to calm himself.

"I don't care how high your ranking is or how strong you are! If you really took my parents, I will find you, and you will pay for it!"

Muttering to himself, Osborne shifted his gaze to the second list.

Godson Rankings!

For Osborne, the Quasi-Divine Rankings were far out of reach. Even the person ranked at the bottom could easily kill him a hundred times over.

It was the Godson Rankings that he needed to focus on.

Everyone on the Godson Rankings was a top AAA-class. The top 10 were even said to be able to fight against S-class Supreme Overlords, earning the title of Quasi-Overlord.

As he scanned the list, Osborne quickly found a familiar name.

Bryce Clarkson!

Kendran's brother, and Bryce Clarkson, the leader of the pirates at Likreia Mountain!

Bryce ranked 6th on the Global Quasi-Divine Rankings!

"Bryce is ranked 6th on the Quasi-Divine Rankings? With the power of the Trident of Poseidon at sea, no wonder he doesn't even fear the King of the Aberrants."

Osborne took a deep breath.

"I wonder if Isa's name is on here, too. She's AAA-class, and even Bryce admires her. She should be ranked on the Rankings as well."

Osborne searched for Isa's name.

Osborne even thought that with her strength, Isa deserved to be ranked first on the Godson Rankings.

However, the first name on the Godson Rankings wasn't Isa, but someone with the codename Lord.

"Lord?"

Osborne muttered, "That's definitely not Isa. Her codename is Night Witch, but it's strange... why isn't she in the top 10?"

Osborne quickly scanned through the Godson Rankings.

Out of the 500 names, Osborne searched three times but couldn't find Isa listed anywhere.

Even Axell was now ranked 465th, but Isa was nowhere to be found.

"Weird!"

Osborne frowned deeply. It didn't make sense. How could someone with Isa's strength not be ranked?

"Could it be... has Isa already broken through to S-class Supreme Overlord?"
