Reeling from her shattered mental strength forced Ebony to spew blood, and her complexion drained to a ghostly pallor. Yet, the pallor of shock overshadowed her pain as she gaped at Osborne in disbelief.

Yet, in her estimation, Osborne's strength was trifling.

She believed his ascension was a mere testament to the mediocrity of the Augustine family's younger generation.

Thus, she held him in no regard.

Even if she discovered his realm had transcended from Class AA to Class AAA, her indifference remained unshaken.

She held the conviction that she occupied the 398th position on the Godson Rankings. With the Solar Realm at her command, vanquishing Osborne would be not just possible but trivially easy.

Yet, before she could unleash the full power of the Solar Realm, Osborne had decimated it.

Her shock was palpable.

Even Clarissa stood dumbfounded.

She thought, "Ebony, who had bested me with the Solar Realm, was now helpless against Osborne's might?"

Her mind raced.

Otherwise, Ebony would not have triumphed.

"I'll kill you!"
