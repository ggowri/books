"Who?! Who's there?!"

Osborne scanned his surroundings, trying to pinpoint the source of the voice.

His expression darkened with gravity.

"With this level of mental strength disturbance, how is it that someone else can approach here without me sensing them?

Whoever this person is, their power must be extraordinary!

And they even know about the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity! Are they here to steal it from me?"

Osborne's instincts ramped up as he braced himself for a fight.

Suddenly, the voice sounded again. "Hmph! No need to look around so frantically, kid! I'm right here!"

Osborne froze, realizing that the voice was emanating from the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity!

He mused, "The Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity... it has gained sentience?"

"Are you the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity?"

"Hmph! What kind of eyes do you have? Take a closer look. I'm not the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity; I'm merely on top of it!"

Osborne quickly shifted his gaze, and to his astonishment, he saw a faint, translucent figure perched delicately on one of the Lotus' radiant petals. A soul entity!

This ghostly figure, no bigger than a thumb, was dwarfed by the vivid crimson glow of the Lotus.

No wonder Osborne had missed it.

As the details of the soul entity became clearer, Osborne gasped, recognizing the features.

Crimson hair flowing like blood!

Sharp claws, glinting teeth!

A pallid, almost ethereal complexion.

"You're Cain, the progenitor of the Blood Clan?!" Osborne blurted out.

The soul entity paused, its expression shifting in surprise.

"I can't believe someone still knows me! Yes, I am Cain! But that's not exactly true anymore. I'm not the Blood Clan progenitor anymore; the one who possesses the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity is!

"It has been stripped from my body—it no longer belongs to me!"

As he spoke, Cain's eyes sparked with intense fury and hatred.

"The Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity was taken from you?"

Osborne's shock was palpable.

He thought to himself, "No wonder I got my hands on the Blood Cross; someone must have torn the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity from Cain, robbing him of his immortality.

Otherwise, even with just a remnant soul, the power of the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity would have enabled him to quickly regenerate, making him immortal."

Cain barked with indignation, "Exactly!

"That damn bastard ripped my Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity away, condemning me to a mortal existence! If I hadn't acted swiftly, my fragmented soul would have been lost—thankfully, I managed to escape into the protective embrace of the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity, using its power to shield me; otherwise, I would have been long dead!

"It's all because I drained the life out of over 10 million people in a divine city, sucking them dry. And that bastard wanted to kill me for it!

"Hmph! It was all just a pretext!

"He was just looking for an excuse to murder me and seize my Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity! One day, I swear I'll hunt him down and make him pay!"

With Cain radiating hostility and vengeance, Osborne's expression remained grave.

He mused, "Ten million lives in a divine city? All die? No wonder they call him the God of Sin!"

Osborne's gaze hardened as he took in Cain's expression.

He had taken many lives himself, but only those of enemies. The thought of slaughtering innocent people, let alone wiping out millions just to quench his thirst for blood, was abhorrent.

This was a monster before him, a true devil.

With a chilling demeanor, Osborne remained silent, but Cain seemed oblivious, lost in his memories.

"Ha! That bastard tried to rip the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity from me, desperate to fuse it into himself. But he failed! The Lotus is too powerful; not even a Deity King could pull it off successfully!

"And here I am, still breathing! While I may not be able to wield the Lotus' full power, I can definitely stir up its energy.

"If he dared to force a fusion, I could provoke the Lotus' power into a frenzy! In that case, even a Deity King would be doomed, slipping into reincarnation for another life."

Cain's expression shifted between pride and regret as he spoke, but Osborne was too stunned to retort.

It felt like he had stumbled upon a colossal secret.

He gasped, "The one who tried to strip the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity from you was a Deity King? Which one?"

Cain's eyes flickered with malice and hatred at the mention of the Deity King.

"Hmph! Even if I told you, would you understand? But since you're so curious, I'll enlighten you. That Deity King is none other than the God of Night!

"The so-called mightiest Deity King, the God of Night! He's a real piece of work. If it weren't for him, I wouldn't be in this sorry state!

"Listen up, kid. I know you want the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity. I can help you, but you have to agree to one condition!"
