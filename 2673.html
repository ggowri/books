"Osborne!"

Osborne finished speaking and continued observing the jungle with Satan's Eye.

Most importantly, Osborne still couldn't sense any energy fluctuations at this moment.

Those people were all panicking.

They wanted to rush out of the jungle but couldn't. At that moment, the branches behind them swiftly came toward them and wrapped around their bodies.

The branches, like sharp blades, easily pierced through their defenses and stabbed into their bodies.

In an instant, their bodies froze and could no longer move.

Weston, Quinn, Naomi, May, Mason, Brenda, and over thirty other Proto-imperial Empyrean Sacredness warriors were pierced by the branches, suspended in mid-air, and eventually dragged back into the depths of the jungle.

The scene was utterly terrifying.

If Osborne hadn't stopped them, they would have entered the jungle long ago.

And their fate would be no different from those pierced by branches and dragged into the depths of the jungle!

It was Osborne who saved them once again!
