"Solar Realm! Activate!"

Without a moment's hesitation, Marlon unleashed the Solar Realm, determined not to give Osborne a second to recover. Despite his bravado about defeating Osborne even at his peak, Marlon had no intention of allowing Osborne that opportunity.

Marlon had been continuously calculating when Osborne could use the Light of Redemption again.

Twelve minutes remained.

"Twelve minutes—plenty of time to finish you off!"

Marlon sneered as the Solar Realm enveloped the entire arena.

Above Osborne, ten blazing solar orbs appeared, radiating intense heat.

Almost instantly, a searing power washed over Osborne, forcing him to maintain his martial art aura to withstand the onslaught.

"Osborne, your time in the Honor Quest Ceremony has been impressive, but ultimately, you'll be defeated by me and become nothing more than a stepping stone for me!

"Now, it's time for you to lose!

"Solar Descent!"

With a swift motion, Marlon directed one of the radiant sun orbs to crash down upon Osborne. It descended like a meteor, its approach sending waves of pain through Osborne's body even before impact.

The sheer force made his martial art aura fluctuate violently, threatening to break apart.

Osborne's expression shifted to one of grave concern.

"What a powerful attack!"

He shouted, focusing all his strength as he prepared to counter the falling sun orb.

The impact reverberated through the arena as Osborne's energy collided with the descending orb, shattering it into a brilliant explosion.

Yet, even in victory, a wave of scorching energy surged into Osborne's body, forcing a grunt of pain from his lips as his skin flushed a deep crimson.

The burning force clawed at him, igniting every nerve.

"Get off me!" Osborne roared, struggling to expel the searing energy from his body.

Gritting his teeth, he charged toward Marlon.

He thought, "I have to take the initiative!"

Marlon's Solar Realm was formidable, which was already at Level Three, and while Osborne could theoretically shatter it, doing so would drain far too much of his limited strength.

At thirty percent of his peak, he couldn't afford that kind of waste.

Osborne's plan was simple. He needed to land a solid hit on Marlon. If Osborne could connect and unleash his Explosive Force, he was confident he could inflict serious damage, causing the Solar Realm to collapse in the process.

But Marlon wasn't about to make it easy. He wasn't foolish; he knew how dangerous Osborne's attacks could be.

So when he saw Osborne barreling toward him, he simply smirked.

"Solar Fusion!"

In an instant, Marlon vanished from sight.

Osborne's eyes widened in alarm as he looked up, only to see Marlon merging with one of the solar orbs high above.

Now, he looked like the sun itself, looming down at Osborne with a chilling smile.

"Osborne, you can't defeat me! In my Solar Realm, all your tricks are meaningless!

"Now, it's time for you to concede!

"Solar Burst!"

With a thunderous roar, all the solar orbs exploded with blinding light, unleashing a torrent of devastating solar beams that rained down like a storm of deadly swords.

The sheer intensity of the onslaught was overwhelming, leaving Osborne with nowhere to hide.

Osborne's face darkened as he frantically defended himself with his Martial Art Aura, while unleashing a barrage of punches. With his formidable power, he attempted to shatter the endless solar swords in the void.

Yet, the solar swords seemed inexhaustible, crashing down with terrifying might. Osborne was pushed back, step by step, as his strength rapidly drained away.

In a blinding flash, Osborne was swamped by an endless sea of solar swords.

Watching from below, the crowd was awash in a storm of light, unable to discern Osborne's form.

The relentless onslaught sent tremors through the arena, threatening to crumble the very foundations.

The intensity of the solar swords left many in shock, convinced that they themselves couldn't withstand even one strike.

And yet, Osborne faced an endless, unrelenting assault.

The spectating members of the Augustine family stood up, worry etched on their faces.

"It seems he'll never make it."

"This attack is too fearsome. Not even a true Class S Supreme Overlord could not block such an assault without breaking Marlon's Realm!"

"We always thought Marlon claiming a spot in the Godson Rankings was just hot air, but now it's clear it's the truth!"

"Marlon is truly formidable!"

"Osborne is on the verge of defeat!"
