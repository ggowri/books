"What is the origin of the light elemental essence that Isa gave me? Why is it so powerful? Why is it so high in level? And why is there a special force that restrains most of its power?"

Osborne couldn't figure it out.

As he struggled to fuse it into Devil's Light, his mauve level seven light transformed from deep purple to black, ultimately becoming a pure black that could devour light itself.

Black was the color of the eighth level Devil's Light.

However, the fusion wasn't ended because Osborne's light element essence had not yet completely fused into Devil's Light and was still improving its level.

Suddenly, amidst the extreme blackness, the Devil's Light transformed again, becoming completely transparent.

"What is this?" Osborne wondered, surprised but focused on continuing the fusion.

He felt immense pressure on his body. Cracks appeared on his skin, bleeding as they expanded.

Despite the pain, he pressed on.

Soon, he turned into a bloody man.

"I can't go on like this. If I continue to fuse, my body will inevitably collapse. I must continue to improve my physical fitness!"

He gritted his teeth and activated his Satan's Eye.

He used his ocular energy to frantically capture the star power and devour it. Every time he devoured a bit of star power, his body would become a little stronger.

But it was far from enough.

Osborne clenched his teeth. Normally, he could only capture star power within a radius of several dozen yards, absorbing more would freeze his body.

However, to prevent his body from collapsing, he disregarded the freezing effects and frantically absorbed star power from a radius of hundreds or even thousands of feet, devouring it as if his life depended on it.

As a result, his body quickly became encased in ice, but the cracks that had plagued him finally ceased.

Seeing the effect, Osborne intensified his efforts to absorb star power, aiming to enhance his physical fitness.

In this way, time passed slowly.

One day.

Two days.

While ordinary elemental essences typically fused into Devil's Light within minutes, Osborne took three full days to fuse the light elemental essence.

During these three days, his body threatened to break apart repeatedly, but his desperate absorption of star power kept him intact.

At last, the fusion was complete.

Osborne's Devil's Light remained colorless and transparent, yet its energy fluctuations astonished even him.

"What a powerful Devil's Light!" he marveled. "Is it still at level 8?"

An eighth-level Devil's Light had never been seen before, and the potent light elemental essence had caused remarkable mutations.

Osborne was unsure of its exact level but felt confident in its strength.

He controlled the Devil's Light and reintegrated it into his martial art aura.

Buzz!

A faint reddish aura enveloped him, and though still at Class AA, the aura was significantly stronger than just days prior.

"If I had this martial art aura a few days ago, Duke Medusa's petrification power would have been no threat. Her attacks wouldn't have harmed me!"

"Moreover, I have been consuming a lot of star power these days, and my body has become stronger and my strength has become greater. Now, even if I still can't beat Duke Medusa, I am definitely capable of fighting!"

"But..."

Osborne frowned. "This power keeps me safe from Duke Medusa, but defeating her is still a challenge."

"I must have more means!"

Thinking of this, he resolved to enhance his major power.

He had many major abilities.

Isa had previously suggested he focus on spatial, thunder, and psychic power, which Osborne agreed with.

But at that time, he didn't have the dark power and light elemental power.

Now, he recognized the importance of dark and light elemental power, especially the light elemental power.

His light elemental essence was exceptionally powerful, allowing him to effortlessly master two intermediate uses of light elemental powers, Dissolving Light and Light of Redemption.
