Osborne lacked precise measuring tools, relying instead on rough estimation, which left plenty of room for error.

"Over twenty times the increase!" Osborne breathlessly exclaimed, feeling the exhilaration course through him.

His body had reached a peak, a limit he could sense—without a breakthrough to Class S, any further improvement seemed impossible.

An ordinary Class AAA had a physical strength of just 0.1, while prodigies like the Lord only achieved 0.6.

Yet here he stood, boasting a strength level of 220!

That was thousandfold more than a typical Class AAA!

This newfound limit stemmed from his unique nature; after all, it was the merging of the soul of powers with his body that allowed for such extraordinary results.

Osborne realized that his limits far surpassed those of any other Class AAA.

It was the newfound limits that made him acutely feel the barrier separating him from Class S.

At that moment, he felt a powerful opportunity for a breakthrough.

Deep within, a vast reservoir of energy surged, just waiting to be harnessed. However, due to reaching his current limits, he couldn't absorb it.

As long as Osborne took one more step forward, he could easily break into Class S and absorb that energy.

"Break the limits and break into Class S?" Osborne murmured.

He felt that if he simply meditated for a few days, he could manage the transition without help.

"Should I go for it?" Osborne wondered, the conviction quickly taking root in his mind.

After all, with such a golden opportunity at hand, how could he possibly hesitate?

At first, he had thought the leap to Class S would be a daunting task. He had planned to wait for Clarissa to wake up and use her fate power to help him.

But now, knowing he could achieve this on his own, why should Osborne rely on someone else's strength?

The rigorous cleansing of fate power would only strengthen him; once he broke through to Class S, that very process would have even greater benefits.

"Then it's settled! I'll break through!"

Osborne's eyes sparkled with determination.

Before start, he contacted First, the head of Blood Demon, to ensure the Augustine family was safe and sound. Bryce and Kendra had returned unharmed.

Osborne also let Wesson know he'd be entering seclusion for the next few days so he could work on breaking into Class S.

With preparations complete, Osborne began his retreat.

But first he needed a secure place to do that!

Osborne pulled up his Draco Star Wristlet.

"This place is nearly 2,000 miles from Newport City and about 1,500 miles from the Aberrants' base. There's not a single nation within a radius of 300 miles! I should be safe from any interruptions here!"

"But who brought me here? Did they know I was planning to go into seclusion?

Could it have been Isa? I called out for her right before Gerardo tossed me into the Human Slave tank, but she didn't show up immediately.

Only Isa has the means to save me and think ahead, securing me such a safe spot for my breakthrough. But why hasn't she come to see me before leaving?

Is it because she's lost faith in me?"

His expression darkened. Isa had been clear. She wouldn't intervene with the Aberrants. She wanted Osborne to handle his troubles on his own.

But Osborne didn’t do as she told. He called for her help again.

True, Gerardo has managed to capture me because he possesses spatial power as well, and his abilities are a level stronger than mine. He has the perfect advantage to counter me.

But that doesn't excuse the fact that this is the second time I've been taken captive!

If only I had been more cautious! If only my spatial power were stronger! Then, I wouldn't have fallen into Gerardo's trap. I wouldn't have let Isa down!

This will be the last time I'm captured by an enemy!

I've been too arrogant, too inflated in my self-worth. From now on, I refuse to let this happen again! Once I break into Class S, all my energy will go into honing my spatial power. I need to make sure I can escape when the time comes—no more falling back on Isa for help!"

But for now, he had something more pressing to focus on.

"First... I have to break through Class S! Once I accomplish that, my strength will skyrocket! Even the top-tier Class S opponents won't stand a chance against me! I have to make this move quickly, then I'll head back!"

Then he began.

Osborne found the barrier to Class S visible.

"It's time to break through!"

He needed to control his power, channeling it to strike directly at the barrier.
