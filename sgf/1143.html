"What? Handle the Beast Tide?"

He asked, frowning, "How are you planning to do that?

"This Beast Tide is brewing. There are at least 100 thousand Ferocious Beasts within a radius of several dozen miles, possibly even more! Just the Class A beasts alone were in the thousands—this is already what you'd call a medium-sized Beast Tide.

Even if all three of them were top-tier Class S fighters, they'd be outmatched and forced to retreat.

But they weren't just three people, were they?

Each of the over four thousand Blood Demon members was now armed with this powerful weapon!

In short, the Blood Demon members had become stronger than ever!

As they swiftly ascended above the Lostridge Mountains, each member radiated the aura of Class S, converging together like a sword poised to pierce the sky, instilling a deep sense of dread.

Within a six-mile radius, every Ferocious Beast sensed their overwhelming presence and was immediately thrown into a state of panic. Some cowered on the ground, while others fled in a chaotic scramble.

When Cillian and his siblings caught sight of the Blood Demon members and felt the sheer power emanating from them, their faces turned pale, their bodies stiffened, and terror washed over them.
