Mose's strength was formidable!

But what made Mose truly terrifying was his killing technique. As a top-tier assassin, the closer the distance, the deadlier he became.

Even Osborne had to be cautious.

Osborne was prepared. If Mose made sudden moves, he would instantly use spatial manipulation to stretch the space between them.

For most, it would be difficult to unleash their abilities that quickly.

But Osborne could do it.

Isa used a special technique to fuse Osborne's soul of powers with his body, allowing him to use his abilities far faster than others.

Especially for moves like spatial stretching, which required no energy buildup, he could activate them instantly.

That's why he dared to face Mose without fear of his threats.

Both Osborne and Mose's bodies were tense.

It felt like their battle could break out at any moment. When it did, it would be deadly life or death, winner or loser.

Many people noticed the tension between them, glancing over in surprise.

"Hmph!"

In the end, Mose didn't make a move.

He let out a cold snort, reining in his murderous intent. "Very well! Axell, you've piqued my interest. If I don't get to mount your head in my trophy room, I won't sleep peacefully!

"As I said, our battlefield isn't here. It's out in the wild!

"The moment you leave the Williamson Castle will be the day you die!"

With those icy words, Mose stopped paying attention to Osborne and turned toward the arena.

Osborne smiled faintly, completely unbothered by Mose's threats.

"I'll be waiting."

After speaking, he, too, turned his gaze toward the arena.

At this moment, the Honor Quest Ceremony had begun. The head of the Williamson family had made a speech, but Osborne didn't pay any attention to what he said.

His focus was still on Mose.

Even though Mose had said he wouldn't strike here and would assassinate him outside.

But how could Osborne fully trust that?

It could very well be Mose's scheme. He would lull Osborne into lowering his guard and then launch a deadly attack.

Osborne had no intention of dying in vain.

The Eityreia Organization, the top-ranked assassin group in the world, feared by countless powerful factions, definitely had real skills.

As confident as Osborne was, he wouldn't underestimate Mose.

The crowd erupted in noise at that moment, and everyone turned to the arena.

"It's Cuthbert Williamson!"

"The strongest of the younger generation, ranked 36th on the Godson Rankings!"

"He's finally making an appearance!"

"The Honor Quest Ceremony is truly beginning!"

The crowd buzzed with excitement.

Osborne looked at Cuthbert and immediately felt an overwhelming power emanating from him, like a massive mountain. His aura was immense.

"I hope all of you will push me, help me break through to S-class!"

As he finished, a brilliant light flared in his eyes.

"Come on!"

As soon as Cuthbert spoke, the crowd below the stage grew eager to fight.

Who wouldn't be tempted by an ancient artifact and five trillion dollars?

Even though they knew their chances of defeating Cuthbert before he was severely weakened were slim, the prize made it worth a shot.

And with no rules regarding life or death on the stage, they had nothing to fear.

Facing a top-tier fighter like Cuthbert was a rare opportunity in itself!

"Cuthbert Williamson, I challenge you!"

At that moment, a figure leaped onto the stage, facing Cuthbert with intense determination.

"Cuthbert, let's fight!"

He was at the Class AAA but hadn't mastered domain power yet. Even so, he was no ordinary AAA fighter.

Without hesitation, he unleashed his full power.

"Take this!"

Boom!

His terrifying attack caused the ground and sky to tremble, rushing toward Cuthbert with ferocity.

Cuthbert's expression remained calm.

Just as the attack was about to hit him, Cuthbert countered with a casual punch, shattering the fierce strike.

The shockwave from his punch hit his opponent, sending him flying off the stage with a scream, unable to control his body.

Cuthbert!

Wins!
