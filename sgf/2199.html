Should I really tell you that my Chaotic Spiritual Root lets me siphon energy from the void, restoring my Sacred Power?

What a formidable Malevolence Tower! Its defenses truly live up to its reputation as a top-tier Apex divine artifact—it's practically on par with a quasi-Sacred Artifact!
