"Have you lost your mind? Become your subordinate? Listen to you? You think you're someone special? You're just a lowly human! I'd rather die than take orders from you!

"What the hell! What have you done to Mr. Ingemar Harrison?"

"He dares to harm Mr. Ingemar Harrison! We should take him down together! We have to save Mr. Ingemar Harrison!"

Osborne sneered. "You think you can take me on together?

It was a revelation that came too late.

Here's hoping everything goes smoothly!"
