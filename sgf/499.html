"Mr. Clarkson, what happened to you? Why are you looking at me like that?"

Osborne felt a chill run down his spine under Bryce's gaze, thinking something must be wrong.

Bryce quickly cleared his throat to cover his embarrassment and smiled, "It's nothing. I was just a bit shocked. I didn't expect you to have killed Chris and Bruce."

Osborne said, "I just got lucky. Thanks to you supporting me from the shadows, I was able to fight freely. I will arrange for someone to transfer the ten billion I promised to your account."

Bryce was startled.

"Don't. Please don't. I didn't do anything. In fact, I was held up by other matters and just arrived. How could I accept your money?

"If you give me money, it means you don't treat me like a brother."

Osborne was taken aback by this.

"Bryce doesn't want that money? That's ten billion!"

As for Bryce saying he had been delayed and had just arrived, Osborne didn't believe it at all.

"He definitely arrived early. Otherwise, how would he know I killed Chris and Bruce?"

Osborne frowned.

"Mr. Clarkson, this is something we agreed upon long ago. The deal is a deal. I am not someone who breaks promises."

Bryce was terrified.

He thought, "If I dare to accept the money, Isa will definitely find me again, and Likreia Mountain and I will be in trouble."

He quickly said, "No. Since we hit it off, I consider you a brother. If you feel the same, then let's not mention money again, or else I will get angry."

Bryce pretended to look angry.

Osborne hesitated for a long time, finally nodded, and did not mention money again.

"Mr. Clarkson, thank you."

"Don't call me Mr. Clarkson. From now on, I'll call you Osborne, and you'll call me Bryce."

At this point, Bryce hesitated.

"Now that Osborne and I are brothers, can I address Night Witch by her name? I won't get beaten up for this, will I?"

Bryce suddenly felt a bit regretful.

But Osborne already spoke. "Alright, Bryce."

Bryce smiled, but it looked more like a grimace.

Just then, Clarissa finally returned.

Clarissa curiously examined Bryce, then walked over to Osborne and gently hugged his arm.

Bryce was instantly stunned.

He asked in surprise, "Osborne, who is this?"

Osborne smiled and took Clarissa's hand to introduce her. "This is Clarissa, my girlfriend."

"Clarissa, this is Bryce, the pirate leader of Likreia Mountain, the absolute ruler of this sea area. Last time, it was Bryce who saved me."

Clarissa greeted, "Hello, Bryce."

Bryce was already dumbfounded. He glanced at Osborne, then at Clarissa, his mouth agape in surprise.

"Your girlfriend?"

He thought, "Night Witch just told me Osborne is her man, yet Osborne has another girlfriend? Does Night Witch know? She must know. And she actually didn't oppose it?"

Bryce looked at Osborne with admiration in his eyes.

"Osborne is truly a man. He not only has such a powerful girlfriend like Night Witch, but he also has a second one. Meanwhile, I... As a dignified maritime overlord over forty years old, I still don't have a single girlfriend."

Thinking of this, Bryce suddenly grabbed Osborne's arm and said seriously, "Osborne, teach me."

"What? Teach you what?"

Osborne was confused and didn't understand what Bryce meant.

"Teach me how to pick up girls. How did you win over such a beautiful Clarissa? And Night... Anyway, you have to teach me."

Osborne felt awkward.

Even Clarissa blushed.

"Well, maybe it's because I look relatively handsome?" Osborne replied with a smile.

Bryce seriously examined Osborne.

Osborne truly was handsome. Especially after breaking through Class A, his charm became even more outstanding, and his tall, strong physique was like that of a statue.

"Then I'm not bad either..."

Bryce muttered, ultimately saying nothing more.

He looked at Clarissa, thought for a moment, and then showed a resolute expression. "Osborne, since we are friends, let me give you both a gift."

Osborne was extremely surprised to hear this.

"Bryce wants to give us a gift? What could it be?"

Bryce presented the enormous harpoon in his hand, known as the Trident of Poseidon, to Clarissa.

"The Trident of Poseidon has accompanied me for over thirty years. Clarissa, please accept it as my gift to you."

After speaking, Bryce forcibly handed the Trident of Poseidon to Clarissa.

Clarissa was stunned.

Osborne was also left dumbfounded.
