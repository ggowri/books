"Kill them all!"

As the number of enemies dwindled, the pressure on Osborne eased significantly. Even when the remaining foes banded together to attack, they couldn't break through his martial art aura.

The transformation had revealed its true power—Osborne was untouchable, while his strikes were lethal.

The scene was bizarre.

A Class AAA warrior was relentlessly hunting down a group of Class S clones who were completely unable to fight back.

Anyone witnessing this would be left speechless; it was rare for even a Class S to handle a group of Class AAA with such ease.

"Ahhh!"

Finally, the last Wolf King met its end under Osborne's hammer; its massive head shattered into a gruesome mix of blood and brains.

Osborne breathed wildly.

The fight hadn't been tough; Bryce had drawn the bulk of the enemy's attention, making things much easier than Osborne had anticipated.

But that didn't mean he was without fatigue.

Every move he made was a full-powered effort, so now he felt the ache in his arms and the depletion of his mental strength.

But there was no time to rest.

Osborne was in a hurry to eliminate these Class S clones because he could see Bryce was struggling.

Bryce was up against twenty-six Class S clones.

After a brief initial burst of energy, he had been forced into a defensive position.

And it was a losing battle.

Fortunately, they were fighting in the vast ocean, which boosted Bryce's water elemental power. Plus, thanks to the Crystal of Life Osborne had given him, he was managing to recover soon.

Otherwise, Bryce wouldn't have lasted this long; he would've been dead long ago.

Still, Bryce was in rough shape.

At first, he could hurl insults at his enemies, but now he was being tossed around like a rag doll, barely able to catch a breath.

Bryce spat another mouthful of blood, his face as pale as a sheet. Everywhere ached.

Bryce thought, "Damn it! I'm gonna die!

Why the hell did I push my luck? Osborne told me to hold off twenty enemies, and what did I do? I went and attracted twenty-six!"

He regretted that decision, big time.

Facing twenty or twenty-six enemies might not seem like a huge difference, but the intensity was on a whole different level.

If he had a second chance, Bryce wouldn't make that mistake again.

Bryce was sent flying again, feeling like he was on the brink of death.

Even though the Crystal of Life was replenishing his vitality, it wasn't enough to heal his wounds in time.

He didn't even have time to check on what was happening with Osborne.

"Osborne, if you don't come and save me soon, I'm toast! But then again, you're a Class AAA facing off against twenty-five Class S guys. You're probably in worse shape than I am—maybe you've already kicked the bucket!"

As he struggled to defend himself, his thoughts began to drift.

But then, just when he thought all hope was lost, he saw a figure leap in front of him, blocking all incoming attacks.

It was Osborne!

Bryce's jaw dropped.

Osborne flashed a casual smile. "Mr. Clarkson, I'm still alive! And I'm not in that bad of shape—just warming up here!"

Bryce's eyes widened in disbelief.

He glanced in another direction, and his eyes met the grisly scene of over twenty corpses scattered on the ground, most with their skulls crushed to pieces.

"Damn!" Bryce gasped, stunned by the sight.

He thought, "How long has it been? Osborne has already taken down over twenty Class S clones?

And by the looks of it, he barely got a scratch!

Is Osborne even human?

We each faced over twenty enemies. I'm a Class S, and Osborne's just a Class AAA. I can't even take down a single enemy; I'm just defending and getting pummeled, battered and bruised, almost at death's door.

But Osborne has wiped them all out?

Why the gap is so huge?"

Bryce was dumbfounded. He knew Osborne was a prodigy who had topped the Godson Rankings and flawlessly cleared the Honor Quest Ceremony.

Over two centuries, only four individuals had ever perfectly cleared the Honor Quest Ceremony.

But even so, Bryce hadn't imagined Osborne would be this insanely powerful!

"Mr. Clarkson, snap out of it! Can you still fight? Let's work together and finish them off before something goes wrong!" Osborne urged, snapping Bryce back to reality.

Seeing Osborne effortlessly deflecting the attacks of over twenty Class S clones with nothing but martial art aura and still managing to talk, Bryce was utterly convinced.

He quickly replied, "I'm good to go!

"These motherfuckers just had their way with me. Now that you're here, I finally get my revenge!

"Let's kill 'em all!"
