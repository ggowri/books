"Domain Suppression Stele can suppress energy flow within a radius of up to a million miles. However, it doesn't mean that activating the Domain Suppression Stele will necessarily suppress energy flow in the entire million-mile radius. The specific range of suppression can be controlled."

Because he knew that Osborne summoning him meant a battle!

Just now, he suddenly felt a strange force sweep through his body, permeating every corner around him, and then something strange happened.
