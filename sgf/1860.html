Osborne surveyed the villa, taking in its location at the heart of the Hudson family territory.

It was clear that Tristan and his father were core members of this prestigious lineage, likely part of the main bloodline.

The Hudson family was a sprawling entity like the Augustine family of Blue Planet. Beyond the main bloodline in Newport City, the Augustine family boasted numerous branch lines.

However, the Hudson family was larger in scale.

With over five million members across its main and branch lines, about three million resided in the Hudson Valley, while the other two million were scattered—some stationed at the border, others working in the mines, and many more spread across various family enterprises.

It was said that at its peak, the Hudson family had over 10 million members.

However, that glory was long gone, and the family had suffered greatly, especially at the hands of the Dark Deity Legion, which had claimed nearly a million lives in recent years.

Tristan burst through the villa's grand entrance, urgency in his voice.

"Dad! Are you okay? I brought him! Dad!"

"I'm fine, still hanging on!" Seth's weak reply came, but the tone quickly shifted to one of fury.

"Bring him here! I want to see him now!"

Osborne, caught off guard, felt a mix of confusion and concern.

He thought, "Tristan's father sounds weak—he must be seriously injured. But didn't Tristan acquire the white Miracle Elixir? Why hasn't it healed him?

And why is there such anger in his voice?

Is it directed at me?"

Furrowing his brow, Osborne followed Tristan into the room, where he found Seth lying on the bed.

The sight made him frown.

The middle-aged man looked incredibly frail, not from physical wounds but from the reckless use of the Hudson family's forbidden technique, which had drained his life force to a dangerously low level.

To Osborne's estimation, if nothing changed, Seth had no more than half an hour left.

This realization puzzled Osborne.

He turned to Tristan, concern etched on his face. "Tristan, where's the white Miracle Elixir? Why hasn't your dad recovered? He doesn't look like he's taken it at all!"

Osborne's expression grew serious.

He couldn't shake the suspicion that Tristan had hesitated to give the elixir to his father.

If that were true, his respect for Tristan would plummet.

He thought, "No matter how precious the elixir is, it can't compare to his father's life!"

Tristan bit his lip, his eyes glistening with unshed tears, but he remained silent.

Osborne's frown deepened.

He opened his mouth to speak again when he noticed Seth staring intently at him, Seth's expression shifting from weakness to a growing fury.

"Theodore!" Seth's voice erupted in a hoarse growl, filled with rage.

If it weren't for his frail state, Osborne would have sworn he was about to leap out of bed.

Osborne was taken aback.

He thought, "What's happening? Why is Tristan's father so furious at me? It looks like he wants to kill me!"

"I've finally found you, Theodore!" Seth continued, his eyes burning with intensity, his finger trembling as it pointed at Osborne, teeth clenched in anger.

Both Osborne and Tristan stood frozen, shock evident on their faces.

Then, as if struck by a bolt of lightning, Osborne's body jolted as a memory resurfaced. He looked at Tristan's father with disbelief, piecing together the implications of Seth's fury.

Osborne's voice trembled as he spoke, "You... you know me? No, wait!

"What I mean is, do you know 'Theodore'? Do you recognize this face? Who are you? How do you know 'Theodore'?"

Anxiety surged through him.

He was currently using his father's youthful appearance and name.

The fact that Tristan's dad recognized him sent a jolt of excitement through Osborne.

But his words left Seth momentarily stunned.

"What... what do you mean?" Seth managed to stammer.

Not one to miss a beat, Seth quickly pieced together what Osborne was implying.

"So, you're saying you're not 'Theodore'? This face of yours is a disguise?"

Seth's brow furrowed tightly, his mind racing.

He asked, "Then what's your connection to 'Theodore'?"

Osborne hesitated for a moment, but determination replaced his uncertainty.

He had finally stumbled upon crucial information about his father and wasn't about to let it slip away. "That's right! 'Theodore' is my alias! This face is a disguise!"

He took a deep breath, locking eyes with Seth. "Theodore Augustine is actually my dad. This face? It's how he looked when he was younger. My name is Osborne Augustine!"

As the words left his lips, he studied Seth's reaction, hoping to glean some insight from Seth's expression.

Seth was left speechless, staring at Osborne in shock.

With a blank stare, he fixated on Osborne, his body trembling violently. His cheeks flushed a deep red, the color of either intense fury or overwhelming exhilaration.

Seth stuttered, his voice quaking, "You... you're Theodore's son? Then... what's your mother's name? What's her name?"

"Her name is Vivienne Hudson," Osborne replied, his heart racing.

Tristan gasped, "What?"

"It's really her!" Seth exclaimed, disbelief etched across his face.
