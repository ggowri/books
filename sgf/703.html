After Luca finished speaking coldly, Osborne, Clarissa, and Emma's expressions all changed.

Were there more enemies?

Luca had been waiting for Clarissa and Emma to show up. He hadn't even been serious before, just playing around.

Osborne frowned and scanned the area with Satan's Eye, but he saw nothing. Besides Luca and a few surviving Class AAA Aberrants, there was no one else in sight.

But just then, a figure suddenly emerged from the shadows behind a nearby rock.

It was a man in his twenties.

The man wore a mask, revealing only his eyes. He stared at Osborne, filled with hatred and defiance. Osborne found the gaze familiar, but couldn't immediately recall who he was.

As he appeared, more figures emerged from the shadows.

One.

Two.

Three.

Ten.

Another ten Class AAA Aberrants stepped out of the shadows. Two of the Titans held Donna, Barlyn, and two others, all unconscious and covered in blood.

The four were completely unconscious. Their bodies were bloodied and tortured, dangling like toys from the Titan's hands.

This scene left Osborne completely stunned.

"How is this possible?!

"I've scanned that area countless times. I didn't see anyone or sense any presence. How could they have hidden so many people there?"

Osborne frowned deeply.

"Aunt Donna! Father! Grandpa! Barlyn!"

Clarissa saw Donna and the others, her face full of worry. Her eyes reddened as she clenched her fists at the sight of their tortured bodies.

She gritted her teeth and immediately prepared to act.

"Stop!"

The masked man said coldly, "If you make a move, I'll kill them immediately!"

His voice was hoarse, clearly disguised.

Clarissa's body trembled violently, her face full of worry.

Osborne squeezed her hand, signaling her to stay calm.

He looked at the area where the masked man had appeared, suddenly realizing something.

"I know now! It's the Dark Realm!

"You've been hiding in the Dark Realm all along, so I couldn't detect you. Even though you're wearing a mask and disguising your voice, if I'm not mistaken, you're Palmers Rosenthal, the Godson of the Rosenthal family from Yalynch!

"Half a month ago, at the Godfather's birthday party, we fought, and you used the Dark Realm. But you were too arrogant, and I beat you before you could show your full strength."

Osborne stared intently at the masked man in front, his expression icy.

"I didn't expect you to collude with the Aberrants! Has the Rosenthal family allied with them?"

As he finished speaking, anger flashed in the masked man's eyes.

He replied coldly, "Didn't think you'd figure it out so fast! Yes, I'm Palmers! But I'm here not because the Rosenthal family has allied with the Aberrants, but simply because I can't stand you!

"I want to fight you again!"

Palmers' eyes burned with battle intent. "Last time, I lost only because I was careless and didn't use my full strength! This time, I want revenge! Osborne, today we'll settle who's stronger and who survives!

"Do you dare accept?"

Powerful energy surged around Palmers.

Osborne could sense that Palmers, like him, was only Class AA, but he felt even more threatening than the Class AAA Aberrants.

"Settle both victory and death?"

Osborne muttered.

He glanced at Luca and the newly appeared ten Class AAA Aberrants, his expression extremely serious.

At this moment, their situation was extremely dangerous.

The enemy's strength was overwhelming!

To kill him, the Aberrants had sent twenty Class AAA Aberrants.

Although he had killed four of them and two others had exhausted themselves using their ultimate moves, there were still fourteen Class AAA Aberrants ready to fight.

Fourteen Class AAA Aberrants were terrifyingly strong!

In addition, there was still the formidable Luca.

He was the sixth disciple of the King of the Aberrants, wielding the divine artifact, the giant axe. His power was immeasurable. Even when he hadn't used his full strength, Osborne had struggled to resist him.

Now, Palmers wanted to settle the score with him, too.

Although Palmers was only Class AA, he had mastered the Dark Realm, a high-level application of powers. Within that realm, his strength would be incredibly dangerous.

Osborne, to this day, had not mastered any high-level power techniques.

The threat Palmers posed was no less than that of Luca.

In short, the enemies they faced now were overwhelming in both numbers and strength.

Meanwhile, they only had three people on their side!

Three Class AA fighters!

How could they possibly win?

Osborne felt immense pressure at that moment. He turned to look at Clarissa and Emma.

Both of their expressions were equally serious.

"Too scared to fight? Then just surrender."
