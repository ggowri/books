The opposite of darkness was light.

Therefore, the element that could counter dark elemental was naturally light elemental.

Coincidentally, Osborne's light elemental was immensely powerful.

Although he couldn't sense any light element in the Dark Realm, his elemental essence of light itself carried a massive amount of light power.

"Maybe, I can actually break through the dark element!"

Osborne had an idea and immediately began to try it out.

He activated his elemental essence of light.

Instantly, he felt the agitation within his elemental essence of light. It seemed that his light elemental essence was displeased with the lack of light in the environment.

It was angry and restless.

Then He felt that a powerful surge of light energy was eager to burst out.

Meanwhile, after Osborne was shrouded by Palmers' Dark Realm, Clarissa and Emma could no longer see his figure, nor could they sense his presence.

Their expressions changed, filled with worry.

They had their opponents, so they couldn't go to Osborne's aid.

"Fatebearer, show me your power! Let me see how capable you are!" said Luca.

Luca gripped the giant ancient axe, sneering at Clarissa.

Clarissa gritted her teeth. "Wanna know how capable I am? You'll find it out when you're dead!"

As she spoke, a pair of enormous wings suddenly appeared behind Clarissa.

The wings spread over 164 feet wide. One side was holy white, and the other was eerie black.

It was Divine and Demonic Wings.

Clarissa's body slowly floated into the air. She gripped the Fate Staff tightly, pointing it at Luca.

"Wail of Fate!"

Clarissa whispered, and instantly, a wave of energy burst from the Fate Staff, rolling towards Luca like a sonic wave.

The endless wails echoed in the sky.

Luca's expression changed as he felt his blood begin to boil.

He frowned and snorted coldly.

"What kind of trick is this? Let me break it with one swing!"

Luca suddenly raised the giant ancient axe and slashed forward. The sound waves were instantly scattered, and the energy from the axe slashed towards Clarissa.

Clarissa's expression changed.

After just one exchange, she felt Luca's terrifying power. She finally understood why Osborne had such a tough time facing Luca.

The terrifying power he possessed could destroy everything.

Clarissa's Divine and Demonic Wings quickly folded, protecting her in layers.

Boom!

The giant axe slashed against the Divine and Demonic Wings. The wings trembled and flickered, but ultimately managed to hold.

"Not bad defense!"

Luca was a bit surprised, but not panicked.

"But how many strikes can you block? Today, I'll capture you alive and take you to the great King of the Aberrants!"

After saying this, he raised the giant ancient axe and slashed down again.

Bang!

Bang!

Luca's terrifying attacks overwhelmed Clarissa. She could only use her Divine and Demonic Wings to defend herself.

The wings, made of energy rather than a physical entity like Osborne's Devil's Light, started to shatter and dissipate under Luca's relentless strikes.

If this continued, Clarissa would be in danger.

She bit her lip, hiding behind the Divine and Demonic Wings' protection.

Suddenly, she gripped the Fate Staff tightly, pointing it at Luca, shouting, "Retribution for Fate!"

At that moment, Luca had just swung the giant ancient axe down hard.

In that instant, the Fate Staff released a wave of energy, seemingly warping time and space.

A terrifying phantom of the axe suddenly appeared in the void, slashing down towards Luca.

Luca was startled and tried to dodge but found it impossible.

Instantly, the massive axe slashed down on him.

Luca's body was sent flying. A ghastly and terrifying wound appeared on his chest, nearly exposing the bone, with blood gushing out.

He struggled to get up, looked down at the wound, and was shocked.

"Retribution of Fate? It actually rebounded my attack completely, and it's even stronger than my original strike!"

Luca's expression turned grim.

"Impressive! Luckily, I wasn't using my full strength just now. Otherwise, the rebounded force might have killed me! But you can't use that kind of attack many times, can you?"

A cold smile appeared on Luca's face.

Clarissa's face was a bit pale.

The Retribution of Fate technique indeed consumed a lot of her mental strength. With her current power, it would be difficult to use it again in the same battle.

She looked at Luca, who was injured but not seriously, and felt a bit regretful.

"Don't worry about how many times I can use it. It's enough to kill you! That’s all that matters."

Luca snorted coldly at her words.

"Oh, really? Die!"

Luca attacked again.

This time, he held back from using too much power, fearing that Clarissa would use the Retribution of Fate on him again.

He began to repeatedly wear down Clarissa's strength, aiming to exhaust her.

For the moment, Clarissa's situation stabilized.

Compared to her, Emma's situation was much more dire.
