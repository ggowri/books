"Donna, how dare you trick me? You installed the Draco Group's Class A defense system without telling me, tempting me to take the initiative to break in and test its strength for you.

It made him even angrier.

Even abroad, Draco Group's Class A defense system had an excellent reputation, and its strength had long been recognized.

Unless a group of Class B+ warriors joined forces or a Class A warrior took action, it would be difficult to break through head-on.

Although Apollo was confident, he was only at Class B. He couldn't break through its defense with a group of bodyguards at Class D.

However, as long as the Radcliffe family isn't crazy, they will never dare to hurt me!"
