"Welcome back, our ancestors!"

A low hum filled the air, and an immense wave of energy surged through the hall, pressing down on the gathered crowd.

Their faces were flushed with excitement, yet their bodies remained pinned to the ground by the overwhelming force.

In the next moment, a tall figure emerged from the coffin.

It was a man, his presence commanding and electric.

After stretching his limbs, he turned his gaze toward the throng of Deschanel family members, a smile creeping onto his face.

He declared, his voice resonating through the hall, "Finally, I'm free!

"I've slumbered in the Sanctuary Casket for a millennium, while the world outside has changed for eons. What a long, long time it has been! But at last, I, Joseph Deschanel, have awakened, restored to my power as the world reclaims its balance!"

"Descendants of the Deschanel family, we welcome you, ancestor Mr. Joseph Deschanel!"

"Descendants of the Deschanel family, we welcome you, ancestor Mr. Joseph Deschanel!"

Joseph's smile widened. "Who is the head of the family? Look up and speak to me!"

Damian quickly raised his head, his voice respectful. "I am Damian, a humble descendant. Before you, I dare not call myself the head of the family."

Joseph regarded him with a calm demeanor. "Three years ago, Terrence Deschanel, the Deity Overlord of the Deschanel Clan, sensed the awakening of life within the Sanctuary Casket. He took it upon himself to force it open, preparing for our return.

Damian's heart swelled with pride at Joseph's words.

"Indeed, Mr. Joseph Deschanel! The Deschanel family today is comprised of the descendants Mr. Terrence Deschanel left behind three thousand years ago. We have diligently guarded this ancestral land, never daring to slack off, fulfilling his legacy. We are honored to welcome you back today!"

Joseph nodded, satisfaction gleaming in his eyes.

"Excellent! But for now, let's call forth more of our clan's deities!"

With that, an overwhelming power erupted from Joseph, sending shockwaves through the hall.

The energy crashed against the stone coffins, causing a series of intense vibrations.

In the blink of an eye, about a third of the hundred coffins began to tremble violently.

Rumbling sounds echoed as the lids scraped open, unleashing a torrent of power that surged through the air.

Figures began to rise from the stone coffins—men and women of all ages, each strikingly beautiful, their features bearing a striking resemblance to one another.

Most astonishingly, every single one of them radiated an aura of terrifying strength.

Gods! They were all gods!

They couldn't contain their excitement, bowing deeply and shouting in unison.

"Welcome back, revered ancestors! Welcome back, revered ancestors!"
