Clarissa's lips parted, a spray of blood staining the air. Her face drained of color in an instant.

Her eyes, wide with shock, locked onto Ebony.

The Solar Realm had shattered the Divine and Demonic Wings, a first since Clarissa had mastered them.

Even Luca, the disciple of the King of the Aberrants wielding the divine artifact Axe of Strength, had failed to break her wings with a single strike.

The attack power of Ebony's Solar Realm was terrifying.

Without hesitation, Clarissa drew the Fate Staff, gripping it tightly. The Fate Staff erupted with a force that repelled all assaults.

"You've got some tricks up your sleeve!"

Ebony smiled, unperturbed, "But in my Solar Realm, you stand no chance. Now, witness my true power.

"Solar Descent!"

Ebony gestured, and a blazing sun materialized above her, plummeting towards Clarissa like a meteor.

Before the impact could reach Clarissa, a piercing pain surged through her, rendering her helpless to resist.

She clenched her teeth.

"Retribution for Fate!"

Boom!

A strange power enveloped the Fate Staff, altering the sun's trajectory to hurtle towards Ebony.

Ebony was shocked.

"How is this possible? This is my Solar Realm! How can you control my power?"

Panic-stricken, Ebony fought back.

Suddenly, another sun blazed into existence, crashing into the first with a thunderous roar. The collision triggered a cataclysmic explosion, the shockwaves threatening to shatter the very fabric of the Solar Realm itself.

Ebony was slightly disheveled, but her eyes burned with wrath.

"Damn it! I underestimated you! You nearly shattered my realm. But you won't win this.

"Solar Burst!"

Boom!

The remaining eight suns above Ebony unleashed a torrent of light, condensing into swords that relentlessly targeted Clarissa.

The onslaught was overwhelming and inescapable.

Clarissa bit her lip hard.

"Blessing of Fate!"

An invisible wave of light swiftly cocooned Clarissa, like a transparent orb of luminescence encasing her. Innumerable solar swords descended upon this protective sphere, sending shimmering ripples across its surface.

Yet, the barrage of solar swords appeared relentless, ceaselessly draining the energy of the light orb, which shrank ever smaller under the relentless assault.

Despite the eight suns dimming with each passing moment, it was clear that Clarissa's defenses would shatter long before their light would fade.

A sneer crept onto Ebony's face.

"You're merely at Class AA. It's astonishing you've held out this long. If you reach Class AAA, I might not be your match because you could easily defeat me.

"Unfortunately, you're not there yet, so you can't best me.

"Defeat!"

With a graceful flick of her wrist, Ebony summoned the eight suns, which blazed with a newfound intensity. Their light surged, transforming the solar swords into radiant beams of power, gleaming with formidable strength and an almost palpable energy.

Bang!

Clarissa's defenses shattered.

The solar swords struck her, hurling her away. She coughed up blood, and her face was ghostly pale.

Collapsing to the ground, Clarissa was immobilized.

Ebony snorted, appearing beside her and seizing her shoulders.

"Now, you're not going anywhere.

"Come back to Marcelin with me!"

As Ebony dismissed the Solar Realm, she dragged Clarissa towards the car.

"Release Clarissa!"

Donna, Dylan, and others rushed forward to prevent Ebony.

Ebony gracefully waved her hand.

Boom!

In an instant, a colossal force swept through, hurling everyone away with such intensity that they crashed to the ground, coughing up blood from the impact.

"Since you're Clarissa's family and she'll soon marry my brother, I'll spare your lives. But if you dare to challenge me again, I won't hesitate to kill you all."

With an icy tone, Ebony concluded her words and stepped into the car.

"To the airport, now. Back to Marcelin!"

The convoy departed.

Struggling to their feet, Donna, Dylan, and the others could only watch the direction Clarissa had been taken. Their anger was impotent, yet their options were exhausted.

Their strength proved woefully inadequate; they were utterly powerless to rescue Clarissa.

"What do we do?"

Panic gripped them.

"Find Osborne!"

Donna shouted, "Contact Osborne immediately! Clarissa has been taken by the Thornberry family of Marcelin. Only Osborne can save her now!"

With that, she began dialing Osborne's number.

At that very moment, Osborne was nearing Cloudsea City, on his way to the Radcliffe Estate, when Donna's call came through.

Osborne yelled, "What did you say?

"Someone has kidnapped Clarissa? And they're heading for the airport?"

His face darkened, and a storm of fury and resolve brewed within him.

"They're looking for death!"
