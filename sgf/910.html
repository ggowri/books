Osborne poured every ounce of his strength into a single, explosive attack.

The air crackled with energy, sending shockwaves radiating outward in all directions.

But in the blink of an eye, a figure was sent flying across the arena.

It was Osborne!

He soared through the air, while Isa took a couple of casual steps back, her expression as calm as ever, completely unaffected by the force of his strike.

Osborne landed heavily on the arena floor, feeling completed drained of strength. Blood spilled from his mouth as he collapsed, his body refusing to move.

So this is what defeat felt like.

Osborne had given it all—no holds barred, even unleashing the power of his right leg's Divine Bone. But in the end, all he managed to do was push Isa back a couple of steps.

He lost.

And it was a resounding defeat.

Before today, Osborne had felt somewhat confident, believing he had a decent shot—maybe a thirty or forty percent chance—of taking Isa down. He thought, at the very least, they would have a nail-biter.

But now, seeing the reality of the battle, he realized just how formidable Isa truly was.

There was no contest.

With a wry smile, Osborne slowly sat up, shaking his head in disbelief.

Just then, Isa approached him. As he opened his mouth ready to speak, she beat him to it.

"You won!" Isa declared.

Osborne blinked in confusion. "What?"

"I said, you won! I lost. Your last move forced me to tap into a stronger power, which means I broke our agreement. So, by the rules we set, I'm the one who lost," Isa explained.

She pouted slightly. "Should I have known you'd level up this fast, I wouldn't had agreed to hold back! Now that you've beaten me, you're definitely going to pull some mischief on me."

Osborne was left speechless, trying to wrap his head around her words.

He thought, "I won?

But... Isa just pushed me around! How could I be the winner when I felt completely helpless?"

But Isa didn't give him a chance to process.

She grabbed his arm and pulled him to his feet. "Come on, stop looking like an idiot!"

Leaning closer, she whispered in his ear,

"Now that you've won, what are you planning to do with me?"

Heat rushed to Osborne's cheeks at her words.

Isa found it amusing, chuckling.

"Anyway, I'm not leaving today. I'll be waiting for you in your room! If you miss this chance, you might not get another one! Don't keep me waiting!"

She dashed away from the arena, her voice echoing behind her.

"I concede!" Isa shouted, not caring about the stunned expressions of the crowd as she quickly disappeared from view.

Osborne stood there, dumbfounded, watching her leaving figure.

Suddenly, the spectators below snapped back to reality, erupting into a frenzy of chatter and disbelief.

Isa conceded.

To them, it was baffling. They had seen Osborne struggle against Isa, taking hit after hit, never gaining the upper hand.

How could she declare herself the loser?

Even the members of the Augustine family, who should have been celebrating, felt a strange mix of emotions.

While Isa might have conceded, they all knew that Osborne still had a long way to go before he could truly stand on equal footing with her.

This victory didn't feel like a victory at all.

Osborne couldn't help but wear a wry smile.

He had hoped to defeat Isa today, to prove himself, but instead, he was left with a stark reminder of just how far apart they were in strength.

"I'll get her later," Osborne thought.

At that moment, Jackie stepped onto the arena, eyeing Osborne with a peculiar expression.

"I hereby declare the end of Osborne's Honor Quest Ceremony! No one has defeated him, making Osborne the fourth person to successfully clear the Honor Quest Ceremony!"

As he finished speaking, members of the Augustine family bursted in cheers.

Apart from the fierce battle between Osborne and Isa, his performance was nothing short of astounding. He was like a legendary figure rising swiftly to prominence.

Even though he'd been thoroughly beaten by Isa, the spectacle had brought them a sense of pride. It was clear to anyone with eyes that there was something special between Isa and Osborne.

"He did it!"

"Osborne has perfectly completed the Honor Quest Ceremony!"

Osborne let out a sigh of relief. No matter how ridiculous Isa's eventual concession might seem, according to the rules, he had secured a victory. He had successfully completed Sebastian's Seventh Inheritance Trial.

As the audience began to leave, Osborne glanced up at the audience stands, sensing the weight of their gazes.

Rumen and Everett were watching him intently, their expressions gloomy.

They didn't say a word but quickly exited.

Osborne exhaled, feeling a mix of tension and relief.

Suddenly, his Draco Star Wristlet vibrated, catching Osborne's attention.

He looked down, surprise flickering across his face.

He thought, "So soon? My Honor Quest Ceremony just ended, and now I'm getting the rewards for the Seventh Inheritance Trial?"

Surprised, Osborne quickly checked the rewards awaiting him.
