Osborne’s face turned pale, and he quickened his pace towards the door.

But in the next moment, Ophelia called out to Osborne.

“Stop!” Ophelia shouted, her face dark with anger.

She looked at the woman speaking—a drop-dead gorgeous beauty in her twenties with striking features, a full chest, and killer curves.

“Ophelia, Justin just texted me. The bombs on Osborne are fake! Justin saw that loser sneaking into a junk shop,” Penelope quickly said. “He made those fake bombs with scrap materials from that shop. That bastard tried to scare us with fake bombs for money!”

The Caldwell family was furious, glaring at Osborne like they wanted to tear him apart.

“This bastard almost swindled twenty million from us!”

“He deserves to die!”

They had been fearful of the bombs moments ago. Now, they all wanted Osborne dead.

As the bodyguards closed in on him, he suddenly burst into laughter.

“Hahahaha!”

Everyone was puzzled.

“This bastard dares to laugh? Justin Caldwell found out his secret, and he still has the nerve to smile?”

“Is he not afraid of death?”

“Without the bomb threat, he’s not leaving here alive. He must die today!”

Ignoring their words, Osborne sneered, “I’m laughing at how stupid you are.”

“You loser, what did you say?”

“Osborne, how dare you do such a thing? We may have been married, but don’t think I won’t kill you,” Ophelia spat angrily. “Idiot!”

Osborne felt Ophelia’s murderous intent, but he remained calm.

“Justin claims my bombs are fake. If you believe him and attack me, I’ll blow us all to pieces without hesitation. If you die, Justin gains the most.

“He’ll take your place as the new heir of the Caldwell family, controlling its billions.”

Ophelia’s face changed instantly at Osborne’s words.

Her beautiful features were now filled with doubt and suspicion.

Was Justin lying to her?

Were Osborne’s explosives real? Was Justin planning to use Osborne to kill her and take her place?

It seemed…

Very possible!

The Caldwell family members were also filled with doubt.

Only Penelope didn’t buy Osborne’s story.

Ophelia’s face darkened with fury.

“Osborne, you have one minute to prove it, or I’ll kill you.”
