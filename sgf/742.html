Osborne thought, "An average Class A master might barely be able to muster enough energy for a single activation of the super energy pistol."

"Let's start by injecting the equivalent energy of an ordinary Class A master."

Osborne controlled the flow of energy. Once the super energy pistol was activated, he quickly aimed at a Class A humanoid target in the distance.

The Class A target was made from exceptionally sturdy materials, with a defensive capability comparable to that of an ordinary Class A warrior.

If he could destroy this Class A target, it would mean the weapon was lethal enough to kill a real Class A master.

Just as Osborne was about to pull the trigger, Kash stopped him.

"Mr. Augustine, you should test it on a Class AA target instead!" Kash quickly suggested.

Osborne was taken aback. "Are you sure?"

Kash nodded firmly.

Osborne nodded back and adjusted his aim towards a nearby Class AA humanoid target.

He pulled the trigger!

With a soft click, Osborne felt a terrifying energy compress into an energy bullet inside the super energy pistol and shoot out from the barrel.

The bullet moved with such speed that Osborne could barely see it. Almost instantaneously, it struck the Class AA humanoid target.

The energy bullet pierced straight into the target.

With a loud explosion, half of the Class AA target's body was blown to pieces. If it were a real person, they would have been killed instantly.

Osborne stared at the scene in shock.

He had only used the amount of energy equivalent to a normal Class A warrior, yet he inflicted a fatal blow on a Class AA target.

He thought, "Does this mean that an ordinary Class A master, using the super energy pistol at full strength, could have a chance to defeat a Class AA warrior?

A Class A defeating a Class AA? That's incredible!"

The gap in power between Class A and Class AA was enormous. Even if ten Class A masters joined forces, without a special combined attack strategy, they would stand no chance against a Class AA opponent.

But now, the super energy pistol could give an average Class A master the possibility of defeating a Class AA master!

"Amazing!"

Osborne was truly amazed. He realized that the power of the super energy pistol far surpassed that of a standard energy pistol, and this was just its basic power.

He hadn't even injected energy at full capacity.

"Let's try injecting full power!"

Osborne took a deep breath and had Kash prepare ten Class AAA humanoid targets, lining them up in a row and spaced a hundred yards apart.

Kash felt a bit distressed.

Each Class AAA target was worth a fortune, with the material costs alone running into the hundreds of millions of dollars. And now, Osborne wanted to use ten of them all at once.

However, he didn't hesitate and quickly set up the targets.

Osborne took a deep breath and once again channeled energy into the super energy pistol. This time, he went all out, pouring almost all his energy into it.

He felt a slight weakness as a result.

The Divine Bone Crystal embedded in the super energy pistol began to tremble. With so much energy activated, it condensed into a terrifying energy bullet.

Osborne took a deep breath.

He knew that the energy within the Divine Bone Crystal was essentially divine power of exceptionally high quality.

In other words, this energy bullet should more accurately be called a divine power bullet!

"How powerful could a divine power bullet be, charged with all my energy?"

Osborne muttered to himself as he fiercely pulled the trigger.

With a series of explosive sounds, the fearsome divine power bullet shot out, instantly piercing through the first Class AAA humanoid target, leaving a large bowl-sized hole in its chest as the target exploded.

The divine power bullet's momentum seemed undiminished as it continued to penetrate the second, third, and fourth Class AAA targets.

After a series of deafening explosions, all ten Class AAA humanoid targets were pierced through by the divine power bullet. Their bodies exploded into fragmented pieces of metal.

The divine power bullet finally struck the wall, blasting a three-foot-wide hole into it.

The sight left both Osborne and Kash utterly stunned.

"Mr. Augustine, your energy is incredibly powerful!

"You're just a Class AA, but your energy is at least twice as strong as that of an ordinary Class AAA! Oh my, it's a good thing you're only Class AA. If you were Class AAA and used the super energy pistol at full power, even these walls wouldn't be able to hold.

"Even a Class S Supreme Overlord might get injured!"

Hearing Kash's words, Osborne looked down at the super energy pistol, his eyes gleaming.

He thought, "If I had this super energy pistol earlier, I wouldn't have been in such a dire situation when faced with the Class AAA Evolved Humans of the Aberrants.

With it, I could have easily wiped them all out!"

"Kash, I'll take this super energy pistol!

"Make more of them as quickly as you can! I want every member of my Blood Demon Organization to be equipped with a super energy pistol!"

After speaking, Osborne's eyes sparkled with excitement.
