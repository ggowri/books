"Attack!"

Osborne commanded his Dark Undead, fully aware of the key difference between it and the Spatial Clone.

While the clones shared a mental link with him, essentially a division of his own consciousness. However, Dark Undead were independent.

They were like mindless "weapons," requiring direct orders to take action.

With the command was issued to attack Diana, Dark Undead sprang into motion.

Their speed was very fast.

In the blink of an eye, the Dark Undead stood before Diana, moving so fast that even Osborne was taken aback. Being incorporeal, like a soul-state Deathborn, allowed for this incredible speed.

This Dark Undead was already comparable to a middle or late stage Deity Overlord warrior at full throttle.

"Very impressive!"

Osborne was pretty satisfied, and he was eager to test Dark Undead's attack and defense stats.

Understanding her role in this test, Diana didn't strike back immediately.

Instead, she only shielded herself with divine power and let those Dark Undead attack her at will.

Rumbling...

The Dark Undead struck with fierce intensity, unleashing a barrage of dark energy that slammed against Diana's divine power defenses, slightly affecting her shield.

Though the attacks were powerful, they were still not able to break Diana's shield.

Even so, Diana still felt pretty surprised.

As a late-stage Deity Overlord, she could fight with two peak-stage Deathborns all on her own. Even a peak-stage Deathborn could not penetrate her divine power defenses.

But this Dark Undead could make her shield tremble. Its power was no insignificant feat. This indicated an attack strength roughly on par with a typical mid-stage Deity Overlord.

"Great!"

Osborne was satisfied with its attack power.

"Let's try its defense power now!" Osborne urged, thrilled by the display.

With that, Diana unleashed her attack. The Dark Undead, not a mindless creature, didn't just stand there and take it. It smartly dodged and attempted to counterattack, but the gap in their power was too vast.

A few moments later, Dark Undead was attacked by Diana and disappeared in the void.

"Defense is a bit lacking!

I'd say it's equal to an early-stage Deity Overlord, but with impressive speed and dodging skills that surpass the typical early-stage Deity Overlord's, even better than a standard mid-stage warrior.

Overall, I'd say Dark Undead is close to mid-stage Deity Overlord, just slightly weaker, though not by much!"

Osborne couldn't help but be thrilled by the assessment.

Now that he had upgraded Undead Cataclysm to Level Three, he could summon up to 1,000 Dark Undeads.

One thousand Dark Undeads, all with mid-stage Deity Overlord's strength.

Osborne thought, "If I use them to build a strong legion to attack enemies together...

If they launched a coordinated attack, even peak Deity Overlords would struggle to withstand it.

Diana certainly wouldn't be able to defeat them.

If I summon enough of them, that could really threaten a Deity King warrior!"

He said, "My soul power is strong enough to ensure that the Dark Undead I summon is powerful enough! Their effects are right up there with my clones!"

Ecstatic over his newfound advantage, Osborne realized that Undead Cataclysm granted him another trump card. In crucial moments, it could provide a real shock to a Deity King warrior.

"Now, let's try the Eclipse of Eternity!"

Osborne cast the Eclipse of Eternity on Diana. An engulfing darkness folded over Diana's area, shrouding her in a heavy veil of eternal night. Underneath this blanket of shadow, she couldn't see, hear, or feel anything.

All she could do was brace herself against the relentless and oppressive tide of dark energy washing over her.

No matter how hard she tried, she found herself utterly trapped by the darkness chains!

After a mere ten seconds, Osborne stopped casting Eclipse of Eternity.

At that moment, Diana was in a mess. As the darkness faded, she gasped for breath, and her eyes filled with shock and panic.

"What was that technique?" she asked.

Osborne replied, "That was the Eclipse of Eternity! It's the same technique that Lord Malevolence used to wipe out ninety percent of Celestara's lives. But trust me, I'm nowhere near his level."

Diana's eyes widened with disbelief.

"That technique! No wonder it's so terrifying. I felt despair as if the entire world had abandoned me. No matter how hard I fought, I couldn't break free.

"Anyone weaker than you caught in your Eclipse of Eternity wouldn't stand a chance of surviving."

As she spoke, her gaze shifted to Osborne, filled with awe and admiration.

His power felt like an unfathomable abyss.

Dark Undead!

Eclipse of Eternity!

Suddenly, a thought struck her. "Wait a minute. Your dark elemental essence... Did you really just break through to Level Seven?

"It feels way more powerful than what I'd expect from a typical Level Seven elemental essence. Even Angelo, the Deity King, his mind control power didn't make me feel so hopeless."

Osborne smiled. "Maybe I'm just a bit special."

He didn't explain much.

"Let's go ahead! We've already wasted enough time here. We need to catch up with Angelo and the others."
