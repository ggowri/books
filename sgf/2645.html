Everyone who knows about Necropolis City must die!

He hopes Adriana can prepare in advance.

As for why Osborne is unwilling to use the key to open the Eighth Skyfall Casket, it's because his purpose in coming here is to stop Frederick from breaking through the cultivation level!

Otherwise, if Frederick really breaks through to Imperial Empyrean Sacredness, it will be difficult for Osborne to seek revenge.

Osborne is doing all this to stop Frederick from breaking through!

As long as Frederick doesn't break through, Osborne is confident he can take revenge.

On him, there were dozens of Imperial Artifacts, ranging from low-grade Imperial Artifacts to mid-grade, top-grade, and even stronger ones.

Frederick never does anything he's not confident about!
