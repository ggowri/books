Even Osborne could easily see the flaws in the so-called "evidence." After nearly a century of living, how could Wesson not see through it?

His anger flared as he confronted Rumen. "How shameless can you be, Rumen?

"Those wearing the Augustine family attire aren't even from our family—they're just your hired goons, aren't they? You're after the rare metal mines of the Draco Group, and now you're staging this charade to find a chance to strike at us!"

The elders of the Augustine family echoed Wesson's outrage.

"Shameless!"

Rumen merely scoffed, saying, "You call it a charade? The people who took my daughter were wearing your family's attire. They are your people!

"If you don't hand over my daughter, don't blame me when things get ugly!"

With that, a surge of power erupted from him, sending shockwaves through the air.

Wesson's expression hardened, but he stood his ground. "Rumen, I won't deny your strength—you're a top-tier Class S powerhouse! If you go all out, none of us in the Augustine family can stand against you.

"But if you think that'll make us beg for mercy, you're sorely mistaken! If you want a fight, then we'll give you one!"

With a sudden clap of his hands, Wesson summoned eight figures from the buildings behind him. Eight women emerged, each radiating an aura of power.

Osborne's eyes widened as he recognized one of them.

"Ms. Hernandez!"

His grandfather's maid, Serena, looked even more formidable than before, her strength seemingly renewed.

She smiled at Osborne, her confidence palpable.

"Osborne, it's great to see you again. Your growth is astonishing; in just a few months, you've reached this level of power.

"Now's not the time for catching up, though. We'll chat once we've dealt with these intruders."

Serena's gaze turned icy as she focused on Rumen.

Osborne glanced at the other seven women beside Serena, sensing their immense power—some even stronger than Serena herself.

Three of them were undoubtedly Class S.

A thrill of excitement coursed through him, but Rumen's expression darkened.

"I've heard that Sebastian took in eight maids, each with exceptional talent and strength. So, you must be them.

"Didn't expect you to be this powerful! Three Class S? Six top-tier Class AAA? Impressive, but do you really think you can stop me? That's downright laughable!

"Since you refuse to hand over my daughter, then let's fight!"

Rumen's gaze turned steely as he looked at Wesson and the three Class S women. "Everett, I'll take care of these four Class S fighters. You handle the rest of the women and the Augustine family elders. Sound good?"

Everett smirked. "Just a bunch of Class AAA trash. I can handle them."

"Perfect, then let's get to it!"

In an instant, Rumen launched himself into action, unleashing four beams of light aimed at Wesson and the three Class S women.

Their faces shifted to shock as they quickly braced themselves.

All four were pushed back a step, their expressions grave.

Rumen laughed coldly. "Let's take this fight to the skies so we don't turn the Augustine Estate into rubble!"

With that, he shot upward like a rocket.

Wesson exchanged glances with the three Class S women, all of them nodding resolutely.

"Godfather, stay alert!" Wesson called back to Osborne before he and the three women shot into the sky after Rumen.

They were going to attack Rumen, but Osborne's heart sank.

Despite their numbers, he wasn't optimistic about their chances. Rumen's power was overwhelming, and four against one might not be enough.

Osborne wanted to assist Wesson, pulling on the Thundergod Gloves and gripping the Volt Hammer.

However, three figures stepped into his path.

They were Scarlett, Bradley, and Marlon!

Rage burned in their eyes, which were filled with hatred and murderous intent. Enclosing Osborne, they declared, "Osborne, your opponents are us!

"On the arena, you defeated us, shamed us, now it's our turn for revenge! We'll defeat you, kill you!"

As they spoke, their hands clenched mighty divine artifacts, and a surge of power enveloped them.

Osborne snorted, saying, "You? Looking to take revenge on me? I won against you on the arena, and I'll win again here!"

"Fight!"

Without wasting a moment, Osborne lunged forward, intent on swiftly dispatching the trio before moving to support Wesson and the others.

"Volt Hammer!"

With a mighty swing of the Volt Hammer, bolts of lightning crackled and crashed toward Bradley, who stood in his path.

Scarlett, Bradley, and Marlon were at the pinnacle of the younger generation, but among them, Bradley was the weakest.

Osborne aimed to take him down first to create an opening.

His plan was sound, but the trio wasn't about to give him the chance. In an instant, three powerful Realms enveloped him, trapping him in a cage of power.
