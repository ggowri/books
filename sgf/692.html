"You really have a good idea."

If you go to the enemy's territory alone, what if something unexpected happens? What about the Augustine family? What about the Draco Group?"

"Osborne, if I don't return, you won't have to continue the rest of the successor test! You will directly become the new godfather and take my place!"

Godfather seemed to be telling him about his final affairs.

Godfather was a legend of Rosmandi, the boss of Draco Group, and the king of Rosmandi's underground world.

Osborne was his grandson!

When Godfather grew old, Osborne would inevitably take his place and become the new Godfather!
