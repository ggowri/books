"Fast, but not faster than me!" Kerwin sneered, unflinching as Osborne's fist nearly grazed him.

Osborne's smirk widened.

"Knew you'd fall for this! Literally suicidal!"

His lightning elemental essence, at its peak Level Five, made Judgment Thunder Drake devastatingly powerful with a penetrative force that exploded.

Kerwin, treating it as ordinary, was signing his own doom.

Kerwin's punch connected, but instead of shattering, the dragon pierced through his divine power defenses with terrifying force.

Osborne's grin widened.

Lightning swirled around him, condensing into a smaller yet more intense dragon on his arm.

Kerwin was utterly crippled by Osborne.
