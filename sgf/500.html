Osborne looked at Bryce in disbelief, thinking he must have misheard.

"Bryce wants to give the Trident of Poseidon to Clarissa? Why? The Trident of Poseidon is the legendary weapon of Poseidon, a true divine artifact. Bryce was only called the Supreme Overlord because of the Trident.

In reality, without the Trident of Poseidon, Bryce's power was merely Class AAA.

How could Bryce possibly give away the Trident of Poseidon? If it were me, I would never do such a thing."

Clarissa was also shocked, holding Poseidon's Trident with a bewildered expression.

She looked at Osborne for help.

Osborne frowned and asked, "Bryce, did you drink too much?"

Bryce grinned.

"Do you think the Trident is too precious? Am I out of my mind to give it away?"

Osborne hesitated for a moment and nodded.

But Bryce said seriously, "Don't worry. I know what I'm doing. In fact, I am reluctant to part with the Trident of Poseidon because it has made me so powerful.

"But now, I must give it away.

"Because someone told me that if I wanted to break through, I had to give it up. Only by relinquishing my dependence on it could I truly push my limits and become the Supreme Overlord."

Osborne frowned, "Then you could give it to your sister Kendra. After you break through, it will still be yours."

Bryce shook his head with a smile.

"First, Kendra's extra energy is unique. She cannot use the Trident of Poseidon, so giving it to her would be a waste.

"Second, if the Trident were with Kendra, I could retrieve it at any time, which wouldn't serve its purpose. I would still subconsciously rely on it.

"Third, and most importantly, this is why I want to give it to Clarissa."

Osborne was confused. "What reason?"

Bryce's expression became serious. "Because Clarissa is a Fatebearer. Fate is the most mysterious thing. Everyone's fate seems predetermined, yet it appears mutable.

"If I give it to the Fatebearer, I believe the Goddess of Fate will look after me even more."

After saying this, Bryce laughed.

Osborne felt speechless. But after considering it, he nodded. "Then thank you. We will temporarily keep the Trident of Poseidon for you."

Bryce quickly nodded.

He took another glance at the Trident, his eyes filled with reluctance, but he still averted his gaze.

"You should leave here quickly. I need to deal with the battlefield. I cannot let anyone find out Chris and Bruce were killed by you, or you will be in trouble.

"As for how to use the Trident, I will send it to you later."

Osborne was somewhat touched.

But in the end, he said nothing more, just replied, "Thank you. We will remember your kindness and repay it in the future."

Osborne took Clarissa and drove away in the yacht.

Bryce watched the two disappear, gradually revealing a sad expression.

"My Trident..."

Bryce shouted in reluctance, then gritted his teeth and began to handle the surrounding battle traces, leaving behind his power fluctuations.

Just as he was about to leave, a figure suddenly appeared in front of him.

Bryce was startled. Upon realizing it was Isa, he immediately adopted a respectful demeanor.

"Lady Witch, why are you back again?"

Isa answered, "I never left. You are clever to have given the Trident to the Fatebearer."

Bryce's eyes lit up.

"I just took a gamble. Could this act really benefit me?"

Isa chuckled, "Of course, and greatly. However, it's not the kind of benefit you think. You'll know when the time comes.

"Anyway, you did well. Go back and refine properly. Do not get involved in the upcoming war with Rosmandi. At least, don't participate until you break through Class S.

"I hope the next time I see you, you will be a in Class S expert."

With that, Isa turned to leave.

Bryce suddenly asked, "Lady Witch, do you know the Fatebearer is Osborne's girlfriend?"

Isa stopped in her tracks.

She turned her head and coldly looked at Bryce, saying, "So what if I know? None of your business. I think you need a lesson."

With that, Isa kicked Bryce into the sea.

Her voice continued, "Although the Fatebearer is also his girlfriend, I am his chief girlfriend."

Moments later, Isa had disappeared without a trace.

Bryce emerged from the water, his face filled with shock.

He mumbled, "Chief girlfriend? This world is truly crazy.

"But Osborne really is a genius."

Bryce revealed an envious expression.

Not long after, Osborne and Clarissa finally returned to the Augustine Estate.

With their return, Dampston was about to undergo a complete transformation.
