Before long, Osborne and Anastasia left Divine Peak Nine behind.

Even from a great distance, Osborne's gaze was drawn to the Divine Peak Nine base, where he spotted a crowd of unfamiliar figures.

With the power of Satan's Eye, he could clearly see their levels of strength, even discerning their unique powers.

Osborne murmured to himself, "The talented warriors from all clans have arrived, and they're impressive, to say the least!

"This is going to be interesting! There are still two days until the challenge begins. I'd better set up the arena."

With that, Osborne suddenly waved his hand.

The air crackled with energy as he unleashed a powerful technique.

"Spatial Cleaving!"

A section of Divine Peak Nine, roughly six miles in diameter, was abruptly cleaved away, hovering in mid-air under Osborne's control.

"This will be the arena for our showdown," he declared confidently.

Once he completed this task, Osborne's divine power surged.

Over six thousand miles away at the Divine Peak Nine base, a massive shadow appeared in the sky—Osborne's divine power projection, looming over the area with an air of dominance and disdain.

Instantly, everyone at Divine Peak Nine was stirred into action.

They rushed outside, gazing up at Osborne's colossal figure, and their expressions shifted to one of shock.

"It's Theodore's divine power projection!

"Theodore is nearby!" someone exclaimed.

They quickly recognized the shadow as Theodore's, thanks to Stellar Pursuit Mandate issued by the Bowman family.

"What is he up to?"

"Does he dare to provoke us with a divine power projection? He's asking for trouble!"

"Where is his real body?"

"Find him!" Many shouted in anger.

Unfortunately, their attempts to sense Osborne's actual location yielded nothing.

Just then, Osborne's voice thundered through the air.

"Greetings, talented warriors of all clans! The arena for our challenge has been prepared, located to the east of Divine Peak Nine. Just head east, and you'll find it.

"In two days, the Seven Young Deity Kings of Human Community will face off against all of your young warriors! But let me make this clear—only those who have reached the Deity King level are welcome to challenge us!

"Those who haven't attained Deity King status should save themselves the embarrassment! We only accept challenges from the elite of your clans!

"I hope you all rise to the occasion and don't let me down."

As Osborne's voice reverberated through the Divine Peak Nine base, the expressions of the gathered warriors hardened dramatically.

Those who hadn't reached the Deity King level felt their blood surge, and their power nearly boiled over, struggling to contain themselves.

Shock rippled through the crowd.

"This guy is just a divine power projection, and he's causing such a reaction? What kind of strength is this? Damn it!"

Many of the young warriors turned pale with anger.

Only those who had achieved the Deity King level remained unaffected by Osborne's voice, though they still furrowed their brows, staring at the imposing figure above.

At that moment, another colossal shadow appeared in the sky, even larger than Osborne's projection, looming over him.

"Theodore, you're too arrogant!"

"In two days, I will personally defeat your Seven Young Deity Kings! I will avenge Alfred. Just wait!"

With that, the massive shadow launched a fist toward Osborne's projection.

In an instant, Osborne's projection shattered into a million fragments.

"It's Tristram!"

"Tristram, the first heir of the Bowman family! Even though he's younger than Alfred, he's already broken through to the Deity King realm and is nearing the mid-stage. If Tristram steps in, the Human Community's Seven Young Deity Kings don't stand a chance."

"The Human Community's Seven Young Deity Kings are doomed!"

"In two days, they'll be beaten to a pulp by the geniuses of the other races!"

"Exactly! How dare those lowly humans to challenge the other races?"

The crowd buzzed with loud and heated discussions.

Just then, Osborne's shattered projection reappeared in the sky.

His gaze locked onto Tristram's projection, and he smirked.

"Tristram? Early-stage Deity King at its peak? You're only brave enough to show off against my divine power projection! In two days, you won't even be worthy of facing me.

"If the so-called geniuses of the other races are all at your level, I suggest you all just surrender now. Save yourselves the embarrassment."

With that, Osborne's projection moved, throwing a punch straight at Tristram's projection.

Tristram's projection let out a cold snort and swiftly countered, and his fist met Osborne's with a thunderous clash.

Even though both were merely projections of divine power, the force of their collision was terrifying, like a deafening thunderclap.

In the stunned silence of the crowd, Tristram's projection shattered under the impact of Osborne's punch.

"Pathetic." Osborne's icy voice rang out.

His projection now loomed over the gathered geniuses of the various races, and his expression dripped with disdain.
