The effects of the blue Miracle Elixir exceeded Osborne's expectations.

He had initially worried that half of the Elixir wouldn't be enough to push his dark elemental essence from the peak of Level Six to Level Seven.

After all, he had faced a big challenge when breaking through to Level Seven with his space elemental essence, and he knew how tough it could be.

But soon, Osborne's concerns faded away.

He could feel the blue Miracle Elixir working its magic, rapidly boosting his dark elemental essence. It surged forward, hitting a barrier before breaking through in record time.

However, the potency of the Elixir was nearly spent.

When the effects finally settled, Osborne found his dark elemental essence firmly established at the early stage of Level Seven, not far from mid-Level Seven.

"I can't believe my dark elemental essence has surpassed my space elemental essence! The power of this blue Miracle Elixir is amazing! If only I could get more!"

He felt so good.

Even the core energy of the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity to enhance his elemental essence didn't compare with the effects of the blue Miracle Elixir. The blue Miracle Elixir did a much better job!

It wasn't that the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity was not good enough. Instead, it just wasn't the right fit. In terms of energy, the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity was surely more superior than blue Miracle Elixir.

"I still own four lines of core energy of Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity. I've been hesitating about whether to use them to boost my elemental essence, but now that seems like a waste.

"I'll save those four cores to enhance my Satan's Eye instead.

"Can they push my Satan's Eye to Level Ten?" Osborne was curious.

Unfortunately, he didn't have time to do that right now. Upgrading his Satan's Eye to Level Ten would help him a lot, and his Infinite Devouring Eyes would be enhanced.

His speed to break through realms would also get faster.

"No new technique with my dark elemental essence breaking through Level Seven?" he murmured to himself.

Just then, an overwhelming surge of information flooded his mind.

"It's coming!"

This wave of information felt far more massive than what he had experienced when his space elemental essence broke through to Level Seven, leaving him astonished.

His brain raced to process the influx of knowledge.

As Osborne processed the information, vivid pictures began to flash before his eyes.

He saw glimmers of light in the darkness, terrifying battles, endless screams, and rivers of blood.

It felt as if he was locked in combat with a fearsome opponent, both of them engulfed in shadows, colliding amidst a torrent of dark energy.

In that chaotic struggle, he sensed himself being defeated, the echoes of countless gasps ringing in his ears...

Osborne gasped for air as the pictures swirled around him. They were hazy, yet the emotions were crystal clear.

The pressure from that mysterious rival made him feel hard to breathe.

He frowned and thought, "What the hell is that?

Why are these visions surfacing in my mind? Is it a side effect of my dark elemental essence breaking through?

Forget it! I need to stay focused now."

Osborne pushed aside his confusion and continued to absorb the information flow.

Finally, he got what he wanted!

The new dark power techniques!

Just like when his space elemental essence had broken through, this time, he was rewarded with three new dark power techniques.

Eclipse of Eternity!

Undead Cataclysm!

Abyssal Spear!

Osborne quickly set to work mastering these new techniques.

Eclipse of Eternity unleashed a dark power that brought shadows over an entire area. Those people in his shadows whose strength was inferior to his would lose their senses completely. They couldn't see, hear, or feel anything. The terrifying dark energy washed over everything like a relentless tide, wiping out all lives.

When the Eclipse of Eternity was coming, it felt like the world was reverting to chaos!

The endless night or darkness was a catastrophe!

Osborne's eyes widened, and he was panting again.

He remembered all too well what Eclipse of Eternity meant.

When Lord Malevolence led his army to invade Celestara, in an instant, this very technique cloaked the entire region for millions of miles.

In the blink of an eye, over ninety percent of Celestara's inhabitants had perished.

"Eclipse of Eternity! That's the technique!

The same technique that Lord Malevolence used to destroy Celestara! Turns out it's the result of dark elemental essence breaking through to Level Seven!" he realized, and his look turned serious.

Lord Malevolence used this technique to destroy Celestara! Countless lives had been killed because of it!

Osborne murmured, "This is terrifying!

"But right now, my power can only extend the effect to an area of about 30 miles. Lord Malevolence, on the other hand, could blanket all of Celestara in eternal night.

"Oh my God! I master the same technique as Lord Malevolence! But I also found the big gap between us when using this same technique... He's more powerful."
