"Holy Light of Redemption!"

It was as if he had never fought at all.

"So what if I lost my Light Realm? I can still beat you!"

Osborne sneered at Cuthbert.

Boom!

At that moment, Osborne's Light Realm finally shattered completely.

Osborne felt the backlash of mental energy, but his mind was strong enough, and thanks to the Holy Light of Redemption, his mental energy was at its peak. The backlash wasn't enough to cause him serious harm.

Cuthbert froze, staring in shock as Osborne's body fully healed and his condition returned to top form. He felt shocked.

Osborne had forced Cuthbert to self-destruct his Power Realm!

Yet Osborne had used his Light Realm to push Cuthbert to the brink!

"Hiss!"

"Even if I have to burn through all my strength, I will beat you!"

Cuthbert had resolved to go all out.

Yet Osborne was like an immovable stone. His Martial Art Aura was incredibly resilient, enduring over a hundred of Cuthbert's attacks without breaking.

Bang!

Pfft!

Everyone watching could tell that Osborne had been seriously injured.

Osborne was going to lose!
