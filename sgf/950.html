No matter what George did to him, Osborne clenched his teeth and remained silent, eventually shutting his eyes altogether.

It was as if the one being tortured wasn't him at all, which only fueled George's rage further.

Before long, Osborne's body bore the marks of brutal punishment, bruises, and cuts marring his skin.

George's expression shifted from anger to concern; he feared he might push Osborne too far and end up killing Osborne.

The real challenge was figuring out how to break free.

After all, he was Sebastian's grandson, the last direct descendant of the family.

But Sebastian was still recovering from his injuries. If he showed up here, he'd be no match for Gerardo and could easily end up dead.

If that happened, everything would be over—the Augustine family, the Draco Group, and Osborne's own value would vanish.

The best outcome would be his own death.

"I have to find a way out of this!"

With his Class AAA strength, breaking free was utterly impossible.

Without any power, Osborne couldn't fight back—not even against the iron cage that held him.

He forced himself to calm down.

Gerardo had mentioned he would wait five days. If Sebastian didn't show up by then, he would turn Osborne into a war puppet.

Naturally, Osborne would never submit, so he had to devise a plan to escape within that timeframe.

Osborne pressed on desperately, his determination ignited.
