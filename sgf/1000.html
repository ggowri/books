Osborne couldn't help but feel a surge of satisfaction as he assessed the defensive capabilities of his martial art aura.

Although he had previously tested it with the Goddess of Grain's Scythe, he needed this moment to truly confirm its strength.

Blocking the full-force attacks of two Class S assassins from the Eityreia Organization solidified his belief: his martial art aura had genuinely transformed!

Both his offensive and defensive capabilities had reached terrifying new heights—far surpassing the Class AAA level.

In fact, many Class S warriors wouldn't possess such formidable power.

A smirk crept onto Osborne's face as he locked eyes with the two Eityreia assassins.

"Since you can't kill me, I guess it's my turn! Now, go to hell!"

In the blink of an eye, Osborne made his move. Within his Light Realm, the two assassins had nowhere to run.

They could only engage him head-on, but their attacks and defenses paled in comparison to Osborne's. Without the advantage of their Stealth Art, their fate was sealed—death awaited them.

With a fierce burst of energy, Osborne unleashed the full might of his martial art aura, obliterating the two Class S assassins in mere seconds.

With that, every last assassin from the Eityreia Organization lay dead at his feet.

The Lambert family members watched in awe, their admiration for Osborne palpable as they collectively released a sigh of relief.

But Osborne knew better than to let his guard down.

"Everyone, we need to get out of here—now! These guys were just the advance team."

"The real threats are Sin and Crimson, the top Class S fighters!"

"We have to leave immediately!"

As he spoke, Osborne began to absorb the residual blood energy in the area, storing it within the Blood Cross.

In less than ten minutes, over a hundred Class AAA Eityreia assassins and three Class S assassins had died here, leaving behind an overwhelming concentration of blood energy.

"Let's move!"

Just as Osborne prepared to lead the Lambert family away, a chilling voice sliced through the air.

"Do you really think you can leave?"

Before he could react, a terrifying attack erupted.

The Light Realm Osborne had yet to retract trembled violently, shattering like glass and vanishing into thin air.

The backlash from the mental strength strain hit Osborne hard, causing him to spit out a mouthful of blood, his face paling.

Osborne looked up, anxiety gripping him as he spotted two furious figures rapidly approaching from about five hundred yards away.

The attack that had shattered his Realm had come from the male figure—Sin!

Sin and Crimson were here!

Wiping the blood from his lips, Osborne wore a grave face. He knew there was no chance of escaping now. Sin and Crimson were top-tier Class S fighters, even stronger than the likes of Gerardo and Sebastian.

In just a couple of breaths, the two were within striking distance, less than a hundred yards away from them.

Leave? Not a chance!

Even with his spatial power, there was no way he could pull off interdimensional travel in front of these two.

Fighting was his only option.

Fortunately, Osborne sensed a hint of disarray in Sin and Crimson's energy. They were clearly injured and not at their peak strength.

This was Osborne's chance!

He glanced at Greyson and Roy, who looked pale and terrified. But when they met his gaze, they steeled themselves, determination replacing fear. They knew they had to stand with him.

In the presence of Sin and Crimson, anyone below Class S was utterly powerless. Even the residual shockwaves from their battle could spell death for the unprepared.

Greyson and Roy, though trembling with fear, stepped forward, ready to confront the two Class S powerhouses.

Sin and Crimson stood amidst the sea of bodies, their expressions chillingly cold and seething with rage.

They locked their eyes on Osborne, their murderous intent palpable.

Sin and Crimson roared in unison, "In just ten minutes, over a hundred of our men have been wiped out! It seems we underestimated you!

"But mark my words, no one is leaving here alive today!

"You killed our daughter and our subordinates; now, you will all pay! Every single one of you will face the relentless wrath of the Eityreia Organization until there's nothing left of you!"

The Lambert family could only tremble in fear as they absorbed the overwhelming aura of death emanating from the two assassins.

A chilling sense of despair settled over them, creeping into their hearts like a shadow.

Suddenly, Osborne let out a cold snort, breaking through the suffocating atmosphere of dread.

He fixed his gaze on Sin and Crimson with a faint smile. "You know, your daughter said something very similar to me before I blew her head off. And just a little while ago, your underlings echoed those very words, only to find themselves dead at my feet."

With that, Sin and Crimson froze, momentarily taken aback. But it didn't take long for their rage to boil over even more.

"Osborne, you're digging your own grave! You killed my daughter; now, I'll make it my mission to kill you!"
