Judith looked at the sullen Valentin and asked, "Mr. Gibson, what did you say? What did I do wrong? Why are you firing me?"

Valentin sneered, "I'm the general manager of Skyefall Security. As for what you did wrong, think about it yourself. Anyway, less talk, get lost now."

Judith's face turned red, her fists clenched.

Everyone else was stunned.

They didn't expect Osborne's words to come true so quickly.

They thought, "Judith got fired? Can Osborne really predict the future?"

Donna, Barlyn, and Clarissa looked at Osborne in shock.

Osborne just smiled and said nothing.

"We'll see."

Judith said viciously and turned to leave.

But he just blurted it out. How could Valentin agree?"

For the Radcliffe family, 500 million dollars wasn't much. Clarissa's safety was the priority. As long as they could rent the Class A defense system, Clarissa's safety would be ensured.

Even if it cost more, she would agree.

To be precise, Osborne did it.

Osborne casually proposed cooperation and succeeded.

Isn't it too easy?"

According to the bet, she couldn't stop Osborne from pursuing Clarissa and had to facilitate it.
