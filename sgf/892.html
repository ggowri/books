Scarlett chuckled coldly. "Looks like I overestimated you! Your tricks may work against weaklings, which is why you've racked up 99 consecutive wins. But when it comes to a real powerhouse, you're clearly out of your depth!"

Osborne's previous opponents had all been unable to match his sheer strength. His attacks were devastating; anyone who got hit by him was bound to be injured and quickly defeated.

But Scarlett was a different story.

In her Plant Realm, her power surpassed his, and with her Realm at Level Three, Osborne found it nearly impossible to break through her defenses. She had the upper hand.

"If this is all you've got, then it's time for you to admit defeat!"

With a wave of her hand, the trees and vines in her Plant Realm sprang to life, launching a barrage of attacks from all directions, nearly overwhelming Osborne.

His martial art aura flickered under the relentless assault, leaving him with no choice but to defend.

Meanwhile, Scarlett vanished from her throne, slipping into Stealth Mode.

Calmly, Osborne focused on warding off the incoming onslaught while activating his Satan's Eye, hoping to pinpoint Scarlett's location.

To his surprise, he couldn't find her anywhere.

It was as if she had completely disappeared.

Not even his Satan's Eye could detect her presence, and Osborne felt no spatial fluctuations.

"Interesting," Osborne mused.

After all this time at the Honor Quest Ceremony, racking up 99 wins, he finally encountered a worthy opponent.

Osborne understood that Scarlett wasn't just boasting; her power was indeed formidable. Her position at number 100 in the Godson Rankings was simply a result of her being a killer who hadn't seriously pursued a higher rank.

With her current strength, she could easily contend for a spot in the top twenty.

"But do you really think you can take me down like this? You're underestimating me!"

His expression hardened.

If he could shatter Scarlett's Plant Realm, he could turn the tide of the battle. He had at least four options to achieve that.

First, unleash a Realm more powerful than Scarlett's—his Light Realm.

Second, resort to Brute Force to break through.

Third, tap into his Vampire Transformation or the Breath of Celestial to boost his power.

Fourth, harness the strength of his right leg's Divine Bone.

As he weighed his options, he realized his Light Realm had reached Level Four.

If he activated it, he could easily demolish Scarlett's Level Three Plant Realm.

But that was his trump card, and he didn't want to reveal it too soon.

Most Class S experts hadn't mastered a Level Four Realm, and using it might draw unwanted attention.

Osborne's right leg's Divine Bone was also a last resort—his true ace in the hole that he couldn't afford to expose just yet.

Brute Force would cost him a significant amount of energy.

With more than twenty challengers waiting behind Scarlett, each more formidable than the last, including Marlon, Osborne wanted to conserve his strength.

So, there was really only one option left.

Osborne let out a low snort and said, "Scarlett, you've got some real skill. Since that's the case, I'll get serious too!

"Vampire Transformation!"

As Osborne finished speaking, his body began to undergo a dramatic transformation. Terrifying power surged from the Blood Cross, merging seamlessly with him.

His hair grew wildly, cascading into a flowing mane of blood-red strands that resembled streams of fresh blood.

Two sharp fangs emerged from his mouth.

His fingers extended into long, dagger-like claws.

His face turned pale and became more angular, accentuating his menacing appearance.

At that moment, Osborne felt an exhilarating lightness in his body, yet an overwhelming surge of strength. With a single punch, he shattered the surrounding vines and trees with ease.

His physical power had more than doubled.

"Now, I'll blast apart your Plant Realm and force you out!" Osborne declared with determination.

Without hesitation, he unleashed his formidable power on Scarlett's Plant Realm, causing it to tremble violently.

The primal forest around him began to collapse.

Osborne's strength was so formidable that even Scarlett's Plant Realm, which had reached Level Three and was a web of intertwined power, could not withstand it.

Finally, under the relentless assault from Osborne, the Plant Realm was on the brink of collapse.

Just then, Osborne's Satan's Eye began to pulse wildly.

A strong sense of danger surged through him.

"Finally decided to make a move, huh?" Osborne scoffed, quickly scanning for Scarlett's position to launch a counterattack.

But what he found left him stunned.

"Nothing?"
