Sin was hurled back once again. Even though he fought with every ounce of strength, the relentless onslaught from nearly a thousand attackers overwhelmed him, forcing him to splatter blood across the ground.

But the real casualty was Crimson.

Crimson was already seriously injured from Osborne's previous attack; if it weren't for her tenacious vitality, she would have surely died countless times by now. Despite Sin's desperate attempts, he couldn't completely protect her from the onslaught.

Crimson was rocked by another devastating blow, more blood and shredded internal organs spewing from her mouth.

The vitality in her eyes dimmed rapidly, slipping away like sand through her fingers.

Seeing her condition ignited a furious rage within Sin.

"You're asking for death!" Sin roared, fixing a murderous glare on Osborne and the Lambert family.

But the Lambert family wasn't daunted. They continued with attacks, determined to end him once and for all. Hundreds of strikes covered Sin, overwhelming him.

Sin's fury erupted.

"Ah!! If it's death you want, then I'll give it to you! Now!"

With that declaration, an explosive power surged through him.

A massive Realm erupted from Sin, crashing forth like a tidal wave, scattering every attack aimed at him.

This was a Plant Realm.

The Plant Realm enveloped an area spanning a mile, filled with towering trees and massive vines.

The trees and vines had come alive, writhing and twisting in a frenzy.

"Die!" Sin growled, commandeering the enormous vines and roots to lash out at Osborne, Greyson, and the rest of the Lambert family.

Each vine stretched over a hundred yards long, snapping towards them with terrifying speed.

Osborne became the prime target of Sin's wrath.

Half of those daunting vines and roots barreled straight toward him.

He summoned his martial art aura, bracing for the onslaught.

But even that couldn't prepare him for the deluge of strikes to come.

His martial art aura vibrated violently. While a single vine couldn't break his defenses, hundreds raining down in a row were another story. No matter how strong his aura, it couldn't withstand such a barrage.

"What a nightmare!" Osborne thought, his expression darkening, dread settling in.

He had seen the devastation of Scarlett's Plant Realm, but what Sin showed was much stronger.

"This is a Level Four Realm!" He realized, recognizing the monumental power it held.

His own Light Realm was at Level Four, but Sin's Plant Realm dwarfed it in strength.

Plus, Sin's mastery was beyond Osborne's.

For the first time, he was up against someone else's Level Four Realm.

Even Osborne was struggling to defend it, let alone the others.

Greyson and Roy were both sent flying by the monstrous vines and roots while blood erupted from their mouths.

The Lambert family banded together, dozens, even hundreds, working together to block a single root or vine. But they were still overpowered. Some bled like fountains and others even burst under the strain.

In an instant, Sin's Realm inflicted catastrophic losses on the Lambert family.

Osborne's face was etched with horror as he witnessed the chaos.

Osborne yelled, urgency in his voice, "Everyone, give everything you've got! "

"Hold nothing back! Now! We need to shatter his Realm immediately, or we'll all die here!"

He knew that taking down Sin's Level Four Realm by himself was impossible—he could only rely on the strength of the Lambert family.

Trusting Osborne's command, the members of the Lambert family sprang into action without a second thought. Some even abandoned their defenses to bombard Sin's Plant Realm with attacks.

Many among them went a step further and tried to detonate their own realm in a desperate bid to break through Sin's defenses.

Seeing this, Osborne decided it was time to act.

For the first time, he unleashed three Realms in one time.

"Spatial Domain! Thunder Realm! Spirit Realm!"

Then, he detonated them.

The force erupted like a thunderclap.

With nearly a thousand fighters joining together, complemented by dozens of Realms exploding all at once, Osborne was convinced Sin's Level Four Plant Realm couldn’t bear it and had to crumble.

But then, an icy grip of horror seized him.

To his utter shock, despite the violence of the blast, Sin's Level Four Plant Realm still held firm.

"How is this possible?!"
