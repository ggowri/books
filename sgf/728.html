Everyone knew that Osborne had snagged the top spot in the heir selection process, which meant he was set to become the first heir of the Augustine family.

His authority would soon surpass that of most elders, granting him a slew of privileges within the family.

In contrast, Owen had only managed to secure fourth place.

According to the rules, he was no longer even a potential heir, leaving him with no privileges whatsoever in the Augustine family.

If Owen dared to provoke Osborne, he could easily find himself tossed into the dungeon, and no one would bat an eye.

Owen was well aware of this, so he kept his mouth shut.

But that didn't quell his resentment; if anything, it only fueled his anger.

Vincent said, his voice dripping with disdain, "Is this how it's going to be? Bullying with your newfound power?

"Owen might be scared of you, but I'm not! I'm here to challenge you, Osborne! I'm going to show everyone that you don't deserve to be number one; you're just lucky!

"Are you brave enough to take me on? If you're too chicken, just lock me up!"

Vincent shot a provocative look at Osborne.

Just as Osborne was about to respond, a graceful figure stepped forward, blocking his path.

It was Emma.

"Vincent, if you want a challenge, why not take me on instead? After all, you're fifth, and I'm second. If you can't even beat me, what gives you the right to challenge Osborne?

"If you're not satisfied, then fight me first. If you win, then you can go after Osborne," Emma said, her tone dripping with confidence.

Vincent's expression darkened at her words. "Emma, you've got some nerve challenging me! I'll admit you've got talent, but do you really think you're my equal?"

Emma smiled, "You won't know until we fight. It's not just about talk! I think it's time to pass on that 'strongest of the younger generation' title you're holding onto."

Vincent gritted his teeth. "Fine! If you're looking for trouble, I'll gladly oblige!

"Let's settle this! Once I beat you, I'll go straight for Osborne.

"I'll show everyone that you two are nothing compared to me!"

With that, Vincent stormed off toward the training ground.

"I'll be waiting for you both!"

As soon as he left, the crowd erupted in chatter. Concerned murmurs surrounded Emma as people gathered around her.

"Emma, why did you step in? This is between Vincent and Osborne! You're still young, and we all believe you'll surpass Vincent one day, but it's too soon for this!"

"Vincent is the only Class AAA among the younger generation. His strength ranks high even among the elders. You can't take him on!"

"Emma, you're being reckless!"

Despite their warnings, Emma waved her hand dismissively.

"I respect Osborne's first place, so why shouldn't they? If they can't accept it, I'll make them!

"And who says I can't take Vincent on?"

With that, Emma shot a quick glance at Osborne, her eyes softening for a moment before she turned and made her way to the training ground.

Osborne followed closely behind.

Soon, the training ground was packed with spectators, all eyes on Vincent and Emma as they prepared to face off.

"What's happening? Why are they fighting? There's a huge age gap between them! Even if Emma is the most talented in the Augustine family, she can't possibly match Vincent!"

"Honestly, if she can hold her ground for five moves, that'll be impressive!"

Vincent sneered at Emma, his confidence unwavering. "Go ahead, Emma! I won't say I'm picking on you—I'll even let you have three free shots!"

Emma raised an eyebrow at the comment, choosing not to respond verbally.

Instead, she unleashed her power, casually casting Freeze power on Vincent.

In an instant, Vincent was encased in a massive block of ice.

The crowd was stunned into silence.

"Class AAA! That's the aura of a Class AAA!"

"When did Emma break through to Class AAA? She was only Class AA yesterday!"

"Wow, she's barely twenty-five and already at Class AAA? Truly the most talented in the Augustine family. Vincent was close to forty when he reached Class AAA, right?"

"Whoa, this fight just got interesting!"

Despite the uproar from the crowd, the battle continued unabated. Vincent's power surged as he managed to break free from Emma's Freeze power.

But before he could say anything, Emma waved her hand again.

Freeze!

Vincent was frozen once more.

Emma smirked. "It took you this long just to break my Freeze power. And you think you can challenge Osborne? What a joke!

"Just give up already!"

With that, Emma unleashed a Psychic Blast.

The intense psychic assault penetrated Vincent's mind. As Vincent came out of the ice, he coughed up blood, his face pale and clearly injured.

"I admit defeat!"
