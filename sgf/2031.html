"They all must die!"

"So this is the power of an Ascension Seven Supernal Deity King? It's truly terrifying! Just the force of his aura makes me feel paralyzed. I'm nowhere near his level yet! Fortunately, his opponent is the God of Light!"

God of Light said, "Want to kill the Human Community's Seven Young Deity Kings? You're not ready for that!"

"You're being naive!

All of them were Supernal Deity Kings!

Another thousand of them!

"Icy One, have you lost your mind? You Eight Dark Sovereigns come to take down the Human Community's Seven Young Deity Kings, and now you bring along a thousand Supernal Deity Kings?
