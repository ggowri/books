Osborne had disguised himself for so long just for this one attack. He unleashed his strongest power, aiming to kill Chris with a single blow.

He knew that if he failed this time, he might not get a second chance.

Chris clearly did not anticipate such a situation.

Even an experienced Class AA like Serena would be significantly affected after three attacks from him, losing much of her combat power and ability to retaliate.

Osborne, being only a Class A, should have sustained even heavier injuries.

Therefore, Chris had no idea that Osborne was pretending. He was completely unaware that Osborne had not been injured at all from the beginning.

When Osborne exploded with power, Chris was momentarily stunned.

Just as he tried to react, Osborne used Satan's Eye to project an illusion at him. Even though Chris was a psychic metahuman, he couldn't avoid being affected, experiencing a moment of confusion.

Although he quickly recovered, Osborne's attack was already upon him.

Chris found himself unable to dodge.

Moreover, Osborne had also used ocular energy to firmly control his body, preventing him from evading.

But Chris did not panic. Instead, he revealed a cold smile. He positioned the massive Death Sickle in front of him.

"Nice trick, but unfortunately, it won't work.

"I refined Stretch Soft Force, so you cannot break through my defenses and harm me..."

Before Chris could finish his sentence, he suddenly felt a terrifying power penetrate his defenses, piercing his body like a needle.

Chris's body trembled violently from the excruciating pain.

His face contorted as he roared, "You actually refined Point Blank Force, and you've already reached proficiency level. How is this possible?"

Osborne smirked, "There's nothing impossible. Time for you now."

After breaking through Chris's defenses with Point Blank Force, Osborne showed no hesitation and launched another attack.

This time, it was the Ninefold Tide Crash.

His terrifying power manifested as nine tidal waves of attacks. As the force struck Chris, they merged into one, greatly amplifying the damage.

A massive blood hole appeared in Chris's chest. Osborne's fist had penetrated Chris's body, crushing all his internal organs.

Chris widened his eyes, his face filled with disbelief.

His strong life force prevented him from dying immediately. He struggled to lower his head and gaze at the huge blood hole in his chest, his eyes filled with unwillingness.

"I... I actually died at your hands? I..."

Chris's mouth bled profusely, and he no longer had the strength to grip the Death Sickle. His body collapsed with a thud.

Osborne grabbed the Death Sickle and felt a strange power connect with him.

He showed a look of surprise.

"This Death Sickle's power? I can actually use it directly?"

After thinking this, Osborne sharply turned to Bruce.

Bruce's face was filled with disbelief. He was originally engaged in battle with Clarissa, and although he had not used his full strength, he held an absolute advantage.

He had no worries about Chris losing.

He knew very well how bizarre Chris's attacks were and believed that Osborne had no chance of winning.

Especially when he saw Chris quickly beating Osborne to the point of spitting blood and turning pale, he stopped paying attention to their fight.

It wasn't until Osborne suddenly erupted and Chris let out a scream that he looked back.

Unfortunately, he was too late to save Chris.

Osborne's attack had directly pierced Chris's body, shattering all his organs.

Chris was doomed. No one could save him.

"How is this possible?"

Bruce was shocked and forcefully knocked Clarissa away.

"How could you possibly be Chris's opponent? How could you kill him?"

Osborne sneered, "Why couldn't I kill him? Now he is dead, and I am still alive. Most importantly, it's your turn next."

Osborne swung the Death Sickle with force.

The mental attack struck Bruce.

Bruce suddenly spat out a mouthful of blood, his face turning pale, and his spirit became listless.

He looked at Osborne in shock.

"You can actually use Chris's Death Sickle? What is going on?"

Osborne also didn't know what was happening.

After swinging the Death Sickle once, he felt somewhat spiritually weak.

"No wonder Chris's attack frequency wasn't high

Such an attack, while bizarre, consumes too much energy.

However, it is still very useful."

Osborne took a deep breath and swung the Death Sickle toward Bruce again.

Bruce spat out another mouthful of blood. His soul was torn apart, and his spirit became even more listless, his face filled with fear and shock.

"How can you actually use the Death Sickle repeatedly? Even Chris couldn't do that. Damn it!"

After Bruce spoke, he suddenly turned to flee.

With Chris dead and his soul heavily injured, their actions had already failed.

If he didn't escape now, he would die too.

"Wanna escape?"

Osborne stared at Bruce's retreating figure, tore open a dimensional rift, and stepped inside.

The next second, he appeared right in front of Bruce.
