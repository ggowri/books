The grand arena for Osborne's Honor Quest Ceremony was set at Arena Number One, nestled high within Challenge Peak.

This expansive venue comfortably accommodated tens of thousands.

Early that morning, attendees of the ceremony, representatives of prominent superpowers, arrived in droves.

Leading the event was Jackie from the Prophet Alliance, while Wesson stepped in to represent the Augustine family, as their Godfather was unable to attend.

With only one Supreme Overlord in the family, Wesson took it upon himself to maintain order and oversee the proceedings.

The crowd swelled with each passing moment.

Most attendees were young forces from various superpowers, boasting a minimum rank of Class AA, with many reaching Class AAA.

A good number of imposing Class S Supreme Overlords were also present, their mere presence casting a sense of awe and intimidation.

Among them were Rumen from the Thornberry family and Everett from the Rosenthal family.

At least seven or eight Class S powerhouses had appeared, each exuding an overwhelming aura that felt like an abyss, intimidating anyone who dared to come close.

Within this sea of strength, the Secluded Families of Rosmandi had arrived, along with representatives from the thirty-six Divine Families across the globe.

Even the Eityreia Organization sent delegates.

The atmosphere in Arena Number One was electric, alive with anticipation and a hint of rivalry.

"Ha! Ever since I arrived in Rosmandi, that Osborne hasn't shown his face! I hear he's only Class AA? Can you believe the Augustine family is hosting an Honor Quest Ceremony for him? Don't they fear it'll turn into a spectacle of shame?"

"Right? I'm here just to witness the disaster in the making! Imagine if the Augustine family's big event spirals into a total embarrassment!"

"Previously, we'd have no chance, but now with Osborne at Class AA, who knows? Maybe one of us could take him down and snag that divine artifact along with 500 billion!"

"Ha! I'll be the first to challenge him! Imagine, taking him down in the very first round! That would be legendary!"

"Count me in as well! I want that first shot!"

The crowd buzzed with mocking chatter, their taunts woven with disdain. Meanwhile, the Augustine family representatives felt the weight of those scornful looks, their expressions darkening with worry.

They sensed the tension building, particularly because the Prophet Alliance hadn't updated the Godson Rankings, still listing Axell at number 205 instead of Osborne.

As a result, many in attendance were oblivious to Osborne's true strength and were eagerly waiting for a show.

Suddenly, the atmosphere shifted as whispers rippled through the crowd.

"Look, it's Marlon!"

"Marlon has arrived!"

"Marlon has a ranking of 40 on the Godson Rankings! But I've heard he's strong enough to break into the top twenty, maybe even the top ten!"

"Among the younger generation, only Cuthbert ranks higher than him, but rumor has it he's had some setbacks!"

"Marlon could very well be the strongest young fighter out there! And that woman with him? That's his sister, Ebony—a powerhouse in her own right at rank 398."

As the crowd fixated on Marlon, he strode in with an air of arrogance, his demeanor cold and dismissive.

The pair made their way to front-row seats, instantly commanding attention.

Ebony scoffed, her voice laced with irritation.

"That pathetic Osborne has been MIA these past few days! No chance to teach him a lesson! I hope he wins a few matches so I can personally take him down and claim that divine artifact and 500 billion!"

Marlon chuckled, a glint of confidence in his eyes. "Don't worry. You'll get your shot. If the arena doesn't give you a chance, I'll make sure you have one. Osborne won't escape you forever."

Ebony's face lit up with delight.

Just then, a masked woman settled beside them.

Scarlett glanced at Marlon with a subtle smile. "Marlon, you seem to have gotten stronger. We should find a chance to spar sometime!"

Marlon regarded Scarlett with a serious expression.

"In the ring, you wouldn't stand a chance against me! Out in the wild, though, I couldn't win you."

Scarlett smirked. "So you think in the wild you just can't win me? You're quite confident. I feel different. I may not beat you in the ring, but out there, I could kill you."

Marlon snorted dismissively, saying, "Scarlett, you're way too cocky! If you think you can kill me, you're mistaken!"

She shrugged, unfazed. "Well, let's just see if we can set something up."

With that, she turned her attention back to the arena.

By now, everyone who needed to be there had arrived.

Jackie stepped into the spotlight, greeting the crowd with a grin. "I declare the Honor Quest Ceremony officially underway!

"Let's welcome today's main star! Osborne!"

As Jackie announced him, Osborne walked up to the stage with a calm demeanor.

"Boo!"

"Ha! What a joke."

"Honor Quest Ceremony? More like a shame fest!"

"Don't go down in the first match!"

As Osborne took his place on the stage, the crowd erupted into a chorus of jeers and mockery.
