He thought, "So, I'm really not Emperor Frederick's son! My parents actually come from the deeper parts of the universe!"

"I will find you!"

Then, they died!

In their memories, he learned more things.

Michelle indeed came from deeper in the universe. Callista was right, but she didn’t know that Michelle was actually connected to the Royal Family, being one of them, which is why she hid among them.

This is also why, after her death, Frederick was willing to have her buried in the Royal Sepulcher.

"Don't even think about leaving us behind and going alone! We don't agree! We are your women. Wherever you go, we will follow you and never be apart from you again!"

Clarissa, Emma, Austin, Adriana, Anastasia, Zoe, Kendra, and the others also nodded firmly at this moment.

"Yes. Wherever you go, we will follow you and never be apart from you!"

He couldn't help but smile and said, "It's so good to have you with me!"

Osborne took a deep breath, waved his hand, and said, "Let's go! Let's explore deeper into the universe together!"

"Let's set off!"
