At this moment, Osborne felt a bit helpless.

He hadn't intended to get involved and certainly wasn't interested in challenging the Godson Rankings. After all, he was currently disguised as Axell.

Even if he made it onto the Godson Rankings, it would be under Axell's name.

Osborne couldn't help but wonder how Axell would react if he found out his name had appeared on the Godson Rankings as one of the Godsons.

Osborne shook his head, dismissing the thought.

After all, he was only using this disguise for a few days. Once he left Tethynia, he'd return to his real identity.

"Axell Lambert from the Lambert family of Rosmandi, victorious!

"Axell, congratulations on defeating Andrew Perry. You will now take his spot on the Godson Rankings, 465th among the Godsons!

"Please, take your seat!

"The challenges from others will continue."

Jackie approached with a smile, announcing Osborne's victory and motioning for him to sit.

Osborne hesitated for a moment, but then nodded and walked to the seat that had belonged to Andrew, slowly sitting down.

The surrounding Godsons all had their eyes on him.

Osborne didn't care. His expression remained calm.

The challenges continued after that.

Osborne's victory over Andrew had renewed the confidence of the crowd below. Soon, more people stepped onto the stage to challenge their chosen opponents.

Osborne silently observed the ongoing challenges.

He noticed that sitting in this elevated position, looking down at the others, gave a certain sense of satisfaction.

"Your name is Axell? Isn't the Lambert family known for their fire elemental powers? Yet you've unlocked spatial abilities and even mastered the Spatial Domain. Impressive."

At that moment, the man sitting to Osborne's left spoke up.

Osborne glanced at the badge on the man's chest.

He was Ron Adams, from the Adams family in Marcelin. He ranked 460th on the Godson Rankings.

"The Adams family from Marcelin?"

Osborne raised his brow. The Adams family was also part of the Divine Family and a very powerful one at that. This man was ranked even higher than Andrew.

"Thanks for the compliment."

Osborne replied calmly.

Ron smiled lightly. "We should spar sometime. Spatial abilities are extremely rare, maybe one in decades. I'd love to test the strength of your Spatial Domain myself."

Osborne smiled faintly. "The opportunity will come."

With that, he turned his attention back to the ongoing battles, not wanting to continue the conversation.

A glimmer flashed in Ron's eyes as he continued, "But be careful. Rhett angered a key figure in the Eityreia Organization. That person ordered a hit on several important members of your family as retaliation.

"You messed up that person's plan by defeating Roger and Andrew. You'll likely be one of their main assassination targets and will draw so much attention.

"The Eityreia assassins...

"Even the League of Divine Descent avoids them. Plenty of S-Class Overlords have fallen to their assassins.

"You're strong, but... you'd better be careful."

After saying that, Ron fell silent.

Osborne furrowed his brow. So Rhett had offended someone high up in the Eityreia Organization?

And now, that person had ordered the deaths of key Lambert family members in retaliation?

And he would be one of the main targets?

What a disaster, through no fault of his own.

However, remembering that the top Eityreia assassin, Emperor Dare, was likely the one who kidnapped his parents. Osborne's eyes gleamed with cold determination.

"The Eityreia Organization, huh?

"If you want to kill me, bring it on!"

Osborne kept watching the matches. Unfortunately, all the remaining challengers failed. Aside from him, no one else broke into the Godson Rankings.

"Alright, since there are no more challengers, today's Godson Rankings challenge is over!

"Next, it's time for you Godsons to challenge each other to improve your rankings. Anyone interested can begin now."

Jackie said calmly.

"I want to challenge!"

A cold voice suddenly broke through.

Emi stood up and pointed at Osborne, her voice icy. "I challenge him!"

Osborne was momentarily stunned.

Emi was challenging him?

"Hmph! How could an AA-class weakling get onto the Godson Rankings? Fine, but being ranked above me? That, I can't accept!

"I, Emi Byrd, refuse to let an AA-class trash rank higher than me! So, fight me!"

With that, Emi had already stepped onto the platform, glaring at Osborne with fierce intent.

Everyone turned their gaze toward Osborne, amusement flashing across their faces.

Osborne sighed, feeling somewhat helpless, as he stood up.
