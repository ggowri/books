A sinister smile spread across Athena's face as she reveled in her newfound scheme.

She felt she had found the perfect way to ensure Osborne's demise.

"Oh, you want to take a half-month break? Not on my watch! I'll make sure you spend every moment of it gripped by fear and anxiety!" she laughed, a dark thrill coursing through her.

Without wasting a moment, Athena sprang into action. Although she was just a leader of ten thousand and had to answer to many others at the Divine Peak Nine base, she had connections.

Being a member of the Upper Race Soaring Lion Clan's Huntington family, she had an uncle named Edison, a brigadier general in the Wild Flame Army, who was a peak Deity Overlord.

His position ranked second in the Wild Flame Army, and he had the authority to set missions.

Before long, with Edison's influence, a new mission was established.

This was a combined task that required exploration of the outer regions of Divine Peak Nine, along with mining in a designated Essence Crystal area.

This particular mining zone was notorious; it was the same one where Celestial Crystals had been discovered.

Previously, a Deity King warrior had led a team of dozens of Deity Overlord warriors to extract Celestial Crystals, only to be met with a massive Deathborn assault.

The outcome had been disastrous—nine Deity Overlord warriors perished, and the Deity King was severely injured, leaving only Diana and the Deity King to return alive.

Because of that fateful mission, Diana had earned the label of the cursed woman.

Now, Athena and Edison were sending another team to explore the same treacherous area, but this time, there would be no Deity King warrior leading the charge.

Two individuals were specifically mandated to participate—Osborne and Diana.

Other warriors could sign up if they wished, and anyone who joined the mission was guaranteed at least 30 thousand Merit Points.

The mission was set to commence in two weeks.

Regardless of how many members were recruited, they would leave for Divine Peak Nine to explore the Celestial Crystal-rich mining area.

Thirty thousand Merit Points could be exchanged for three second-class merits!

Normally, such a task would attract throngs of eager participants, as exploration missions, despite their dangers, offered incredible rewards.

As long as they made it back alive, they would complete the mission and secure their points.

Plus, the chance of finding Celestial Crystals made it all the more enticing.

As soon as the mission was announced, excitement rippled through the four major military districts. Many were eager to take on the challenge and test their luck.

But when they saw that two participants had already been confirmed, their enthusiasm quickly fizzled out.

Diana—the cursed woman would be there, too. No one wanted to risk their necks alongside her. The whispers of her past failures hung in the air like a dark cloud, snuffing out any hope of glory for those who dared to dream of joining the mission.

"The cursed woman is going back to Divine Peak Nine for another mission? Is she out of her mind? That's just asking for trouble!"

"Last time, she nearly got a Deity King warrior killed!"

"Because of her, nine Deity Overlord warriors lost their lives!"

"I heard she was responsible for Emmett's death, too, along with a dozen other guards! Anyone who teams up with her is practically signing their own death warrant!"

"I'm definitely not getting involved in any mission she's part of!"

"Anyone who isn't afraid of dying can go right ahead. I'm out of here—I want to live a little longer..."

"Count me out too..."

"No way am I going, no matter how many Merit Points are on the table!"

"And that Osborne fellow who's going with her? He's as good as dead!"

"Anyone without Deity King-level strength who dares to accompany that cursed woman to Divine Peak Nine is just asking for it! Even Deity Kings end up injured!"

"Let's get out of here..."

After the mission announcement, despite the enticing Merit Point rewards, no one stepped forward to volunteer. The fear of being dragged down by Diana, the so-called cursed woman, kept everyone at bay.

This reaction was exactly what Athena had anticipated.

A cold smile crept across her face.

"Osborne, you're going to explore Divine Peak Nine with that cursed woman? I can't wait to see how you meet your end! You think you can outsmart me? You're not nearly clever enough! I've got countless ways to make your life a living hell!"

With that, Athena sent Osborne a message detailing the mission.

She intended for him to spend the next two weeks living in fear.

Meanwhile, when Osborne received Athena's message about the mission, his brow furrowed in irritation, a cold glint of menace flashing in his eyes.

"Athena, you're playing with fire! Forcing me to team up with Diana to explore the Celestial Crystal mine at Divine Peak Nine?"

His expression darkened.

He was well aware of the mine's notorious reputation.

A Deity King warrior had been gravely injured there, and nine Deity Overlord warriors had perished.

Osborne's face was a storm cloud of frustration. "I was planning to wait until I was stronger before exploring Divine Peak Nine with Diana, but now, thanks to Athena, I have no choice but to move up the timeline..."
