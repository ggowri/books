"You should know the location of Wild Flame City's Mystic Feather Clan, right? Start talking, and maybe you can redeem yourself."

"Mr. Goodrich, are you serious? Do you want me to go back to Mystic Feather Clan and confess to the Bowman family? Why on earth would I do that?

We don't need to fight at all. After all, it seems your young talents are too scared to face the Human Community's Seven Young Deity Kings in a fair fight, so they send an old man like you to wear us down. Are you afraid of losing to us?"
