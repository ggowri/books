At that moment, Osborne unleashed his powers with effortless grace and breakneck speed. With a mere thought, he could unleash multiple types of power attacks simultaneously.

The thousands of flying Ferocious Beasts surrounding him were unable to breach his web of attacks; they fell one after another, their bodies either plummeting to the ground or vanishing into thin air.

Osborne felt a surge of satisfaction.

However, he knew that his true strength lay not in these flashy power attacks.

While they were impressive and effective against foes of similar or lesser strength, they would be insufficient against a true master.

His greatest assets were his body—his raw strength, defense, and speed.

Now was the time to test just how much he had improved during his three-month slumber.

In an instant, Osborne retracted his power attacks.

In that brief moment, he had already taken down over five thousand flying beasts, but nearly 10 thousand still remained, a daunting sight.

As Osborne withdrew his power attacks, the remaining beasts surged toward him once more.

"Looking for trouble, are we?" Osborne sneered, a confident grin spreading across his face.

He unleashed his martial art aura, enveloping himself in a fierce energy, and charged headlong into the throng of beasts.

This was close-quarters combat!

"Osborne, be careful!" Anastasia and Zoe gasped, their faces paling in alarm.

The thought of him plunging into such a mass of flying beasts was terrifying.

Who would dare to dive into the heart of a beast swarm?

From a distance, he could use power attacks to whittle down their numbers, and if things got dicey, he could always retreat or fight while backing away.

But charging into the pack meant he'd be surrounded, and that was a death wish!

Not only did Osborne have to endure countless attacks simultaneously, but he also couldn't effectively dodge or retreat. Even the most formidable individuals would eventually be worn down and defeated!

Even though these flying Ferocious Beasts were relatively weak, every single attack would chip away at Osborne's defenses. One beast might not break through, but what about a thousand, or 10 thousand, or even 100 thousand?

No matter how strong his defenses were, they could eventually wear him down, leaving him vulnerable to being torn apart.

That was the terrifying nature of a beast swarm.

An army of ants could take down an elephant.

Anastasia and Zoe were frantic, their fear momentarily forgotten as they readied themselves to help Osborne. They were determined to jump in and pull him out.

But just then, Osborne's calm and confident voice reached their ears.

"No need to worry, I'm fine! They can't touch me. I just want to test my physical strength!"

Hearing this, Anastasia and Zoe hesitated, their instincts screaming at them to act, yet they held back.

Concern etched on their faces, they prepared themselves.

If Osborne showed any signs of faltering under the onslaught, they would rush in without a second thought.

But fate had other plans; it seemed they wouldn't get that chance after all.

Osborne plunged into the swarming horde of flying Ferocious Beasts, letting their relentless attacks rain down on his martial art aura. The aura, faintly glowing with a crimson hue, effortlessly deflected every assault.

Be it a mere hatchling or a seasoned veteran among the beasts; none could dent Osborne's formidable defense.

Hundreds, even thousands of attacks converging on his aura scarcely made a dent, scarcely sapping its resilience.

If anything, the regeneration of his aura kept pace with the onslaught, rendering Osborne effectively invincible.

He could stand still all day, and not a single one of these 10 thousand beasts would breach his martial art aura.

Osborne couldn't help but chuckle at this realization.

"Compared to three months ago, my martial art aura's grown even tougher! Now, even the top-tier Class S warriors would find it hard to break through.

Maybe only a true deity could manage it... and even then, they'd have to contend with my bodily defenses!"

With that thought, he abruptly retracted his martial art aura, letting the barrage of attacks land directly on his skin and flesh.

Roaring explosions filled the air as the beasts closed in, claws and beaks ripping at him, while others launched condensed air blades from a distance.

Thousands of attacks thudded against Osborne, yet none could leave a mark. Not a single scratch!

Osborne threw a single punch. Instantly, the force erupted, vaporizing hundreds of the beasts into crimson mist.

"Damn, this is powerful!"

He hovered mid-air, absorbing the assaults from all directions, his spirits soaring.

He thought, "Both my defense and strength have skyrocketed tenfold compared to three months ago! Speed and reflexes have at least tripled!

Overall, my power's at least a hundred times greater now!

Even though I'm still technically a top-tier Class S, I could take on a hundred of them simultaneously.

And if I ever cross paths with a true deity, I'd dare to face them head-on!"
