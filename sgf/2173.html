Ascension Nine!

In fact, in Celestara Planet, only the God of Night had ever reached Ascension Nine; no one else had even touched Ascension Eight.

While the God of Light, God of Death, Goddess of Life, God of Time, Goddess of Fate, and God of Space were all hailed as the Seven Great Deity Kings of the Human Community, their peak cultivation levels had only ever reached Ascension Seven.

Even now, they were merely back to their former strength—still stuck at Ascension Seven.

Faced with Maddox's Ascension Nine level, how could they not be shaken?

This was another super warrior on par with Lord Malevolence from a thousand years ago!

Back then, although Lord Malevolence had been defeated and sealed by their combined efforts, he had still managed to kill the Goddess of Life and the Goddess of Fate, severely injuring the God of Death, God of Time, and God of Space—so badly that they had to enter the cycle of reincarnation to heal.

The God of Night and the God of Light had also been gravely wounded, stalling their cultivation for centuries, even causing regression.

It was clear just how powerful Lord Malevolence had been.

Now, as Maddox showcased his strength, he mirrored that very same power.

Clarissa and the others couldn't help but recall the brutal battles of the past, their faces paling at the memories of the devastating outcomes.

A thousand years ago, they had the God of Night leading the charge.

But now? The God of Night was still absent, and they were left to fend for themselves.

Did they really have what it took to stand against Maddox?

At that moment, uncertainty loomed over them all.

"Death Ripple!"

But just then, a vibrant green light surged through the air, enveloping them.

It was Austin!

Even so, Emma and Clarissa's faces remained grim.

Maddox was just too powerful!

What do we do now?!
