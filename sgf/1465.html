These twenty young male Human Community slaves were prime candidates for dangerous and labor-intensive tasks like mining.

Within Wild Flame City, there were only a dozen large mines, but many medium and small ones operated under various factions.

To dig for resources, they needed slaves.

For the powerful factions, these Superior Deity Human Community slaves were the best high-level miners. They could even fight off the Deathborn to protect other workers, making them invaluable. How could anyone pass up such an opportunity?

After all, female slaves could be used for various purposes, while male slaves were typically limited to dangerous, labor-intensive work.

As the price hit two million and 500 thousand, the competition began to dwindle.

It was unlikely they would fetch more than three million.

"I'll bid four million!"

Silence fell.

The auction hall grew eerily quiet as everyone turned to look at Osborne, their expressions a mix of surprise and skepticism.

If Osborne hadn't already proven his financial power, they might have thought he was just trying to stir up trouble.

He jumped from two million and 500 thousand straight to four million?

Who spent money like that?

So, no one dared to challenge him this time.

Osborne had snatched up every single one of the first six batches!

No matter what they bid, Osborne would always outpace them by at least a million, snuffing out any hope of winning the auction.

In total, he had spent 30 million Essence Crystals, acquiring nearly 200 human slaves!

How could he possibly compete with that?

He wasn't afraid of anyone's wealth.

After all, the money he spent was practically free; he didn't bat an eye at the thought of it. Even if he did run out, he could always sneak into the mines of these major factions and haul away all their Essence Crystals!

Anyone who dared to cross him would pay the price.

With that thought, Osborne wore a calm smile, his demeanor even more relaxed.

At that moment, the seventh batch of slaves was auctioned off to him for five million Essence Crystals.

Then came the eighth batch.

But this time, it wasn't a full batch of slaves; instead, only three women were brought up for bidding.

Three Human Community women!

Three stunning women, all at the Celestial Deity level!
