"Even in the Deity King level competitions, not a single name from the Hudson family!

"You're talking nonsense!

"Which of your factions has suffered greater losses than the Hudson family?

"Hmph!"

"You think you can threaten me? So what if I say the Hudson family is betraying the Human Community? Prove me wrong if you can!"

"What's going on here?
