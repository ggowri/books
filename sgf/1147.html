Having tested his physical strength and defenses, Osborne felt very confident.

Back when he was merely Class AAA, he had already managed to defeat numerous Class S opponents, even holding his ground against a dozen or more at once.

Now, after the enhancement from Gerardo's top-tier cultivation liquid and a transformative breakthrough into Class S, his body had transformed.

Coupled with three months of relentless refining from immense fate power, he had reached the pinnacle of what a Class S could achieve. There was no further room for improvement.

At this moment, Osborne was confident that even if faced with a hundred top-tier Class S enemies, he could handle them with ease, potentially taking them all down.

He even believed he could stand toe-to-toe with deities, and perhaps even defeat them.

Standing in mid-air, he allowed the countless flying Ferocious Beasts to assault his body, lost in thought.

"Next step is forge a Divine Body! By refining my bones and flesh with sheer power, I can transform them into Divine Bone and Divine Flesh, paving the way to the divine realm!

I can feel it; if I set my mind to it, crafting Divine Bone and Divine Flesh will be a walk in the park, making me a true deity!

But what I'm aiming for isn't just any old Divine Body.

An ordinary Divine Body isn't worth a dime! Sure, I could keep refining and leveling it up later, but if it's mediocre from the start, I'd boost it to a Level Three Divine Body at best.

If my Divine Body takes shape as a Level Three in the beginning, then leveling it up three times could get me to a Level Six Divine Body!

And if I start at Level Six, there's a chance I could reach a Level Nine Cosmic Divine Body!

In short, the higher the level when the Divine Body forms, the greater the potential for future growth.

But I'm not aiming for a Level Seven Solar Divine Body, or a Level Eight Galactic Divine Body, or even a Level Nine Cosmic Divine Body.

No, my sights are set on the Omnipotent Divine Body—something beyond Level Nine!"

Osborne's eyes sparkled with determination.

He continued to ponder, "Isa once mentioned that the Omnipotent Divine Body is merely a legend. Many Deity Kings are stuck at just a Level Seven Solar Divine Body. The higher-tier bodies like the Level Eight Galactic Divine Body and Level Nine Cosmic Divine Body are incredibly rare, almost mythical.

But with the Breath of Celestial, there's a chance to forge the ultimate Omnipotent Divine Body!

The catch? My Divine Body has to be at least a Level Seven Solar Divine Body as it forms! Only then can I hope to refine it into an Omnipotent Divine Body!

Level Seven Solar Divine Body... that's a tall order."

Osborne's expression turned serious.

It was clear that even some Deity Kings only managed to achieve a Level Seven Solar Divine Body after millennia of effort.

And here he was, needing to reach that level the moment his Solar Divine Body took shape!

It was practically an impossible feat!

Most people entering the divine realm ended up with a Divine Body devoid of any rank, often failing to even reach Level One.

Only a handful of prodigies could manage to create a ranked Divine Body, and even then, it was usually just Level One or Two.

Level Three was exceedingly rare.

If that were not the case, the God of Star wouldn't have been so ecstatic about Isa remaking her Divine Body into a Level Three.

Achieving a Level Four Divine Body upon becoming a deity was a mark of sheer genius, a sign of potential to become a Deity King!

Yet here was Osborne, aiming to achieve a Level Seven Divine Body right as he ascended to godhood!

No one had ever managed it before.

But Osborne was determined to give it a try. Isa had mentioned that crafting a Divine Body using the Breath of Celestial had the potential to transcend limits.

Osborne trusted Isa.

However, Isa also warned that such a body could just as easily turn out to be nothing.

But Osborne chose to ignore that part.

"First, I'll wipe out this Beast Tide, then return to forge my Divine Bones and Divine Flesh, and make a push for the godly realm!"

Having made up his mind, Osborne sprang into action once more.

He began throwing punch after punch, each with devastating force, shaking the grounds. Within a radius of several hundred yards, every flying Ferocious Beast, regardless of its rank, was obliterated into a mist of blood, leaving no remains behind.

In a blink of eye, Osborne had unleashed dozens of punches.

The nearly 10 thousand flying Ferocious Beasts around him exploded instantly into a crimson haze that stained half the sky red.

This scene left Anastasia and Zoe, who had been anxiously watching, utterly dumbfounded.

And Cillian and his three companions, from a greater distance, rubbed their eyes unbelievably.

After all, that was a horde of tens of thousands of flying Ferocious Beasts—many of them Class A Dark Exotic Beasts—a nightmare even for top-tier Class S warriors.

Yet Osborne casually dispatched them with one punch after another, reducing them all to a bloody fog.

"He's practically a god!" Cillian and his companions marveled, their faces glowing with admiration as they gazed upon Osborne.
