Not long after, Osborne hung up the phone.

He put the box with the Originium into the Dimensional Space.

"Let's goto the other two rare metal mines! I always feel that these Originiums are very important. We must snatch back the other two rare metal mines. And we need to stop them from taking the Originiums away!"

Osborne's expression was extremely solemn.

The call with Godfather was very short. Godfather didn't know what Originium was, but he still felt something was wrong.

He told Osborne that he would rush to Dampston immediately.

Of course, Osborne wouldn't waste time here.

He killed the two Marquis Titans, grabbed Clarissa's hand, quickly tore the space crack, and headed towards the second rare metal mine.

Osborne could interdimensional travel, and his speed was much faster than taking a plane.

With his full effort, they arrived at the second rare metal mining area several hundred miles away in just a few minutes.

He discovered that the members of the Aberrants here were retreating rapidly.

Osborne's face changed.

"They are retreating! It seems they already got what they want! However, we can't let them leave like this!"

"Kill him!"

With that, Osborne attacked again.

This rare metal mining area was guarded by two Duke Werewolves. They had an excellent sense of smell.

Osborn hadn’t made it to the top when it came to the dark power.

Just as he approached and wanted to assassinate, he was discovered by the Duke Werewolves in advance.

Osborne's assassination attempt failed for the first time.

But he didn't care.

He chose assassination because he thought it was more efficient and could kill the enemy with the least effort. 

That didn't mean he was afraid of the Duke Werewolves in a head-on confrontation.

The Duke Werewolves' attacks had stronger penetrating power, and their ultimate move, Death Claws, could completely break his defense and cause him huge damage.

But that was all.

With Osborne's current strength, dealing with a Duke Werewolf alone would not consume too much energy.

After just a few minutes, he killed the Duke Werewolves at the cost of some minor injuries.

And Clarissa's speed was a little faster than his.

After dealing with the strongest Duke Werewolves, Clarissa and Osborne started their slaughter and soon killed all the strong men of the Aberrants.

This time, Osborne had a target and quickly found the second metal box.

There were also hundreds of Originiums in this metal box.

Osborne put the Originiums into the Dimensional Space, and without any rest, he rushed to the third rare metal mining area.

Unfortunately, this time, things didn’t turn out the way he had expected.

The third rare metal mining area was now deserted, and there was no person from the Aberrants.

Osborne frowned tightly, wondering if he’d been there too late.

At this moment, Osborne suddenly felt a terrible force descending from the sky.

Then, Godfather appeared in front of them.

Godfather was holding the black and gold Godfather's scepter, his expression extremely solemn.

It was the first time that Clarissa met Godfather. She was a little nervous.

Feeling the terrifying aura from Godfather, her body trembled slightly.

Even though Clarissa was already a Class AA warrior at this time, and could even easily defeat Class AAA warriors, she still felt fear when facing Godfather.

Osborne quickly held her hand and soothed her.

Godfather seemed to have noticed Clarissa's fear, and instantly restrained his aura of strength, becoming like an ordinary old man.

He smiled at Clarissa and said, "You are Clarissa, the Fatebearer of the Radcliffe family? Not bad. You are progressing very fast! When I saw you, I couldn't help but think of someone."

After saying this, a trace of pain flashed in Godfather's eyes.

Osborne immediately knew that the person Godfather was thinking of must be his mother.

Vivienne Augustine!

His mother, Vivienne, was extremely talented back then.

She had already reached Class AAA at the age of 20. She was even more talented than his father, Theodore.

And Clarissa was also twenty years old this year.

Although her realm was not yet Class AAA, her strength had already reached that level. It was natural that Godfather would think of Vivienne when he saw Clarissa.

Thinking of his mother, Osborne also bit his lips.

"Godfather, I'm Clarissa."

Clarissa finally calmed down and bowed respectfully to Godfather.

Godfather smiled and waved his hand, "Little girl, you don't have to be so polite! From now on, we are all a family. You can call me Grandpa."

"Huh?"

Clarissa was stunned.

Until now, she still didn't know that Osborne was Godfather's grandson.

Osborne touched his nose, looking a little embarrassed.

"Clarissa, I'll explain this to you later!"

"Godfather, you should take a look at these Originiums! I feel that they are very special! The King of the Aberrants launched this war not to destroy the Augustine family or take back Rosmandi, but just for the Originiums!"

After saying that, Osborne took out an Originium from the Dimensional Space.
