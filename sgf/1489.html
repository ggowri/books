Surprise lit up Osborne's face as he absorbed the results of his training.

The enhancement of his lightning elemental essence through the core energy of the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity exceeded his wildest expectations.

He had anticipated that consuming one core would only elevate his lightning elemental essence from Level Four to the mid or late stages of Level Five.

Given his previous experiences with spatial and dark power essences, that seemed reasonable.

However, the outcome was astonishing. After fully absorbing the core energy, his lightning elemental essence soared straight to the peak of Level Five, tantalizingly close to breaking into Level Six.

Osborne mused, "If I had a bit more time, I bet I could push my lightning elemental essence into Level Six without needing another core. Just relying on my own strength.

Why was this boost so significant?

Could it be that my lightning elemental essence isn't as powerful as the spatial or dark essences? So, using the same core energy resulted in a greater enhancement?"

It made sense to him.

Power levels varied; spatial and dark powers were incredibly potent and rare, while Thunder Power, despite its reputation for sheer attack strength, paled in comparison when matched against those.

At least, that was how Osborne saw it. To him, Thunder Power felt like just another elemental force, nowhere near as formidable as spatial or dark powers.

Otherwise, he wouldn't have abandoned Thunder Power to focus on mastering spatial and dark powers.

Even though Osborne's Thunder Power, though mutated, paled in comparison to the might of space and dark powers.

"Too bad my lightning elemental essence is just shy of breaking into Level Six!

If only I had that extra edge, I'd feel much more confident taking on Fabian. Still, the peak of Level Five should suffice!

After all, when I first broke through Level Five with Thunder Power, it unlocked two brand new techniques for me—Judgment Thunder Drake and Thunderform Flash!"

Osborne quickly absorbed the intricacies of using Judgment Thunder Drake and Thunderform Flash in his mind.

Judgment Thunder Drake was an incredibly aggressive attack move, an upgraded version of the Thunder of Judgment. It could summon an endless torrent of lightning to form a thunder dragon that would charge at enemies, delivering not only immense penetration power but also explosive force.

With his current strength and the peak of Level Five lightning elemental essence, casting Judgment Thunder Drake would likely leave any typical Celestial Deity warrior either dead or severely injured.

Yet, against a Deity Overlord like Fabian, it wouldn't be enough.

Osborne didn't expect to defeat Fabian outright; his goal was simply to keep Fabian occupied, to be a thorn in his side.

That was where Thunderform Flash came into play.

This technique allowed Osborne to coat his Divine Body with lightning, significantly boosting his defenses. More importantly, it transformed his body into a bolt of lightning, granting him terrifying speed.

"Thunderform Flash will give me the edge I need! My speed should rival any Celestial Deity warrior who specializes in agility. And that's exactly where Fabian falls short; even as a Deity Overlord, he'll struggle to keep up with me.

Now, without resorting to my hidden cards like space, dark, and time power, I finally have the capability to clash head-on with Fabian."

With that thought in mind, Osborne dove back into his training.

In the secret chamber, where time accelerated a hundredfold, he quickly mastered Thunderform Flash.

In no time, a flash of lightning zipped through the spacious chamber, darting left and right so swiftly that it was nearly impossible to follow with the naked eye.

When the lightning finally came to a halt, it revealed Osborne, surrounded by dark purple arcs of electricity, embodying the essence of a war god.

Osborne exclaimed, a grin spreading across his face, "I've finally got it!

"But this is just the beginning. If I can push Thunderform Flash to initiate mastery level, mastery level, or even refined level, my speed will skyrocket!"

He glanced at the clock.

"There are still forty minutes until the slave market closes. I'll dedicate thirty minutes to honing Thunderform Flash. In here, that's equivalent to five hours!

Hopefully, I can elevate it to the initiate level!"

With determination, he sprang back into action.

Once again, a streak of lightning zipped around the chamber, its speed increasing with every passing moment.

Finally, after what felt like an eternity, five hours had flown by.

Osborne came to a halt, satisfaction and confidence radiating from him.

He was proud of his talent; in just five hours, he had advanced Thunderform Flash from a novice level to initiate mastery, doubling his speed in the process.

Now, he felt ready.

He muttered, "Time to face the world! Betty, Darren, and the others must be anxiously waiting for me.

"It's time to confront Fabian and the other factions. Today marks the first step for me and Spark of Blaze to rise in Wild Flame City!"

With that resolve, Osborne gathered his divine power defenses, slowly opening the door to the chamber.

As he stepped outside, countless eyes turned toward him, curiosity and anticipation shimmering in their gazes.
