Medusa, Titan, and Werewolf were all staring at him. Seeing him frozen in a charging stance, they instantly felt irritated.

"Damn it! I didn't expect that Tauren would win in the end. We got outdone by him! But what's with this pose? It's annoying!"

Titan voiced his frustration.

Medusa was also displeased. The fact that Osborne, whom they couldn't defeat, got badly injured and defeated by Tauren left her feeling uneasy.

"Tauren, hurry up and finish him! Don't waste time.

"You won. No need to strike a pose," Medusa called out.

Tauren seemed not to hear them at all. He remained silent, still standing there. He was frozen in his charging stance.

Finally, they all sensed something was off.

"What's going on?"

"Did Tauren knock himself out?" Werewolf murmured.

"No, something's wrong!"

Vampire, who hadn't made a move yet, suddenly shouted.

"Tauren's aura is fading fast! No, he's already dead!"

"What?"

Vampire's words left everyone in disbelief. After carefully sensing Tauren's aura, their faces showed shock.

Tauren was dead!

Osborne smirked coldly at this moment.

Tauren was indeed powerful. His thick hide was the toughest defense Osborne had ever encountered.

And his brute force charge, though simple and direct, was too fast to dodge easily.

Once hit, even Osborne couldn't defend against it.

Tauren was the most dangerous of the five opponents to Osborne.

In theory, he was also the hardest to deal with.

Unfortunately, Osborne had a technique specifically for opponents like Tauren.

And that was...

Deadly Force!

The Deadly Force that Isa taught Osborne allowed him to channel all his force directly through Tauren's tough hide, striking his insides.

In that instant, even though Tauren had charged Osborne, sending him flying and even breaking his arm.

Osborne had already landed a punch on Tauren's head. The immense force went through his hide and turned his brain to mush.

Tauren died in that moment.

Because of his four legs, he remained standing until the last of his strength left him.

And now...

Boom!

Tauren's massive body collapsed with a thud, lifeless.

"He's dead?!"

They were all stunned. None of them expected Tauren, the one with the strongest defense, to be dead.

They replayed Osborne's punch in their minds.

That punch didn't even look that terrifying. They felt confident they could have blocked it, at most getting a minor injury.

Why did Tauren die instead?

They couldn't figure it out.

But their expressions toward Osborne had shifted. There was no more casual disdain, only serious, cautious looks.

Osborne had just killed one of their own!

This meant Osborne was no longer the insignificant pest they thought he was. He was now a deadly snake, capable of killing them. No longer a plaything!

"Looks like we can't keep toying with him anymore!"

"Let's all attack together! We'll kill him and avenge Tauren!"

"Kill him!"

Tauren's unexpected death had shaken the remaining four. They could no longer stay calm.

They now felt the threat he posed.

With a single command, they all attacked simultaneously, unleashing their powers against Osborne.

All four of them were Class AAA. If it were one-on-one, Osborne wouldn't fear any of them. Even if he couldn't win, he wouldn't lose either.

But with all four attacking at once, Osborne instantly felt overwhelming pressure.

Medusa's eyes gleamed, and she unleashed the Deadly Petrification with a sharp cry.

In an instant, half of Osborne's body turned to stone. Although his martial art aura kept the petrification from spreading further, it severely hindered his movements, making it hard for him to dodge.

Meanwhile, Titan swung his massive fist, aiming straight for Osborne's head.

Werewolf wasn't far behind. He lunged forward. His claws rapidly slashed, piercing Osborne's martial art aura and wounding his body.

However, the most sinister attack came from Vampire.

Osborne hadn't fought Vampire before, so he wasn't familiar with his techniques.

From a distance, Vampire waved his hand toward Osborne.

Osborne suddenly felt his blood boiling violently. His veins surged with chaotic energy, causing intense discomfort.

It was as if his blood wanted to burst out of his body and rush toward Vampire.
