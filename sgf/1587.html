"Donald, just focus on getting better here. Don't worry about anything else! I swear, I'll make them pay for what they did to you!"

Meanwhile, over at Laird's mistress' courtyard...

"It was Donald Saxton? A centurion from the Wild Flame Army? Are you sure there's no mistake?"

"General, there's no mistake. That Wild Flame Army centurion badge we found? It belongs to Donald Saxton. We even showed his photo to Madam Lena, and she confirmed it was him.

"The timeline matches perfectly.

"What are you saying? You want me to just let this slide? Pretend it didn't happen?"

"General, if things get blown out of proportion, it could be bad for you. If this escalates, Madam Christine is likely to find out about Lady Lena. You know what Madam Christine's temper is like. If she finds out you've got another woman, she'll be furious."

"Fine. As long as that brat ends up dead, that'll have to do. I can't let Christine find out about Lena, so I've got no choice.

Herman Saxton, blazing with fury!

A bunch of fools—can't even take out one kid without screwing it up. But that brat deserved it. He dared to touch my woman; his death is justified! And Herman wants revenge? Does he think I'm some pushover?! he thought.
