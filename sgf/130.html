"You bastard, what nonsense are you talking about?"

And Clarissa...

Once she decided on something, she was even more stubborn than Barlyn.

Let Osborne be the bodyguard captain?

If he became the bodyguard captain, he would have more time to be with Clarissa. They would be inseparable.

That was something she absolutely could not accept.

It was also something the Radcliffe family would absolutely not accept.

But with Barlyn insisting on resigning, was there a better choice than Osborne?

At least not for now.

Osborne was not a professional bodyguard. If the deputy captains and other bodyguards didn't respect him or cooperate with his work...

They all had the opportunity to become the captain?

Osborne took a deep breath, looking serious.

He didn't expect Donna to play this trick. She gave Harris, Violet, and Sharon a chance to become the captain. Why would they vote for anyone else?

They would definitely vote for themselves!

Under these circumstances, it was almost impossible for Osborne to get two out of the three votes.

He had to do something.
