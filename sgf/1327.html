Eddie paused, clearly taken aback by Osborne's words.

Then he chuckled, saying, "No wonder the Grand Elder of the Prophet Alliance advised me to treat you with respect, just like I would treat him. I was skeptical at first—after all, you are just a rising talent, not unlike many others during the Age of Gods. But now I see why the Grand Elder made that decision."

With that, Eddie bowed politely to Osborne once more.

"Since you already know the source of this calamity, we can rest easy and save our breath. However, I'm still in the dark about its origin—would you mind enlightening me?"

Osborne was a bit surprised.

He mused, "The Grand Elder of the Prophet Alliance told Eddie to be courteous to me?

Could he have predicted something?

But it seems the Prophet Alliance aims to be friends rather than foes, so there's no need to be overly cautious."

He nodded and said, "Of course, let's discuss this further in a more comfortable setting at the Augustine Bastion."

With a wave of his hand, Osborne opened a dimensional portal leading directly to Augustine Bastion's guest chamber.

"Mr. Pike, please."

Eddie smiled, seemingly unfazed, and stepped through the portal with his entourage, showing no fear of any potential harm from Osborne.

Soon, they found themselves in the guest chamber of the Augustine Bastion.

Eddie couldn't hide his astonishment. "Such dense energy! It must be a hundred times more potent here than outside. This place is truly a paradise for cultivation!"

The members of the Prophet Alliance were equally impressed.

Among them was an acquaintance of Osborne's—Jackie.

He had presided over Cuthbert's Honor Quest Ceremony and Osborne's own, lending a helping hand in the past.

Jackie, who had since ascended to deity status, was also part of Eddie's delegation, likely as a gesture of goodwill to reassure Osborne.

However, it turned out to be unnecessary.

Standing quietly at the back, Jackie watched as Osborne and Eddie exchanged pleasantries. He marveled at how even Eddie showed such respect to Osborne.

He couldn't help but reflect, "How much has changed!

In less than a year, Osborne has reached this level.

At Cuthbert's Honor Quest Ceremony, Osborne was just a Class A warrior, a mere junior in my eyes. If not for my intervention, he might have been taken down by a Class S warrior from the Williamson family.

Now, Osborne's power is unfathomable to me, reaching heights I can hardly imagine."

At that moment, Jackie noticed Osborne looking his way and quickly bowed to show respect.

Osborne, caught a bit off guard, sidestepped and said, "Mr. Flores, you're being far too generous. You're my senior and have helped me before. I can't possibly accept such formalities from you."

Jackie was taken aback, and a wave of gratitude washed over him.

Osborne hadn't forgotten his kindness.

Jackie replied earnestly, "It's only right! The Grand Elder of the Prophet Alliance has instructed us that you're to be treated as our most honored guest and friend. As merely a junior member in the Alliance, it's my duty to show you respect."

Osborne was once again surprised, thinking, "What exactly has the Grand Elder of the Prophet Alliance foreseen to issue such instructions, treating me as their most honored guest and friend?"

Though puzzled, Osborne knew this could only be beneficial for him.

He said, "I'm humbled by the Grand Elder's high regard. I must visit and thank him in person when the opportunity arises."

After personally pouring Jackie a glass of water, Osborne turned his attention back to Eddie.

"Mr. Pike, let's discuss business. Before I reveal the source of the calamity, may I ask about the Prophet Alliance's strength? How many Transcendent Deity warriors can you deploy? How about Superior Deity warriors?"

Eddie hesitated.

Normally, the strength of the Prophet Alliance was a tightly guarded secret. Revealing such information could be risky, leaving them vulnerable if Osborne ever turned against them.

However, recalling the Grand Elder's words to treat Osborne like their most honored guest, even as respectfully as the Elder himself, Eddie decided to be forthcoming.

"Godfather, without awakening our elders who exceed Transcendent Deity status, the Prophet Alliance can currently field about twenty Transcendent Deity warriors and fifty Superior Deity warriors."

Pride flickered in Eddie's eyes as he spoke, echoed by the expressions on the faces of the other members of the Prophet Alliance.

In their view, such strength was more than capable of impressing Osborne.
