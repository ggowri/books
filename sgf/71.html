Alison did not hesitate. The uncut diamond that Wesley bought for 15 million dollars only turned into a bunch of garbage. She would not be disappointed even if nothing was cut out of the six pieces of uncut diamonds. They bought them for free anyway.”

Alison didn’t create suspense and just lifted the cover directly.

Suddenly, everyone gathered around.

The uncut diamond in the cutting machine had been cut into two halves. Two cross-sections appeared in front of everyone.

Then, everyone was stunned.

“Purple! Its color is purple.”

“Oh my god. It’s a violet diamond.”

“It’s so big!”

“This is worth at least tens of millions of dollars!”

In an instant, everyone exclaimed. They breathed rapidly when they saw the glass-type violet diamond.

Alison was also stunned by the beautiful and noble violet diamond.

She seemed to have thought of something and looked at Osborne in shock.

She suddenly remembered that Osborne had asked her about the price of the violet diamond before selecting this piece of uncut diamond.

And she told Osborne that she had seen a set of violet jewelry weighing a total of seven ounces. It was worth 100 million dollars.

And the violet diamond definitely weighed seven ounces. Moreover, the color was more noble.

If she designed the jewelry and put it at auction, the price would definitely be beyond 100 million dollars. It also had 35 ounces of glass-type diamond. This piece was worth at least two hundred million dollars.

They didn’t spend a penny on these six uncut diamonds. Wesley gave them it for free. Now, the first piece had been cut into a top-quality diamond worth at least 200 million dollars.

Wesley was also dumbfounded at this time.

His eyes were red. His face was distorted and full of anger.

Why did I spend 15 million dollars to buy a pile of garbage? Wesley thought. But Osborne cut out a violet diamond from the junk material. It is worth hundreds of millions of dollars. And I gave him uncut diamonds for free.

Wesley pressed his hand on his heart. He felt dizzy and wanted to vomit blood.

Ophelia was jealous at this moment.

She stared at that diamond. Why didn’t this violet diamond belong to her? If she could design a whole set of jewelry with it, she would definitely become the most dazzling person. But there was no if. The violet diamond belonged to her competitor.”

This made Ophelia even more jealous.

Alison looked at Wesley and Ophelia with pride and sneered, “I’m sorry to disappoint you. We didn’t cut out any garbage from the uncut diamond. We cut out the best violet diamond and such a big piece of glass diamond.”

Wesley and Ophelia didn’t want to talk at this time.

Alison suddenly felt bored and looked at Osborne.

Osborne remained calm the entire time. After all, he already knew what was in the uncut diamond. There was no surprise at all.

“Open the others,” Osborne said calmly.

Alison agreed with a smile and quickly opened the covers of the other five cutting machines one by one.

None of the five uncut diamonds had the violet diamond. But each one was cut into top-quality diamonds. They were almost all glass-type diamonds. There was even a tiny piece of Imperial Green.

In an instant, the entire casino was in an uproar.

They came to uncut diamond casinos all year round. They knew the price of top-quality diamonds. The total cost of top-quality diamond cut from these five uncut diamonds was definitely more than 100 million dollars.

With the glass type and violet diamonds, they were worth three hundred million dollars.

It was the price of raw materials. If top designers designed them into top-notch jewelry, it was normal for the price to double.

Alison couldn’t hold back any longer and burst into laughter. The Ashford family’s refusal to supply Luna Jewelry during this period put her under a lot of pressure.

And now, all the pressure was gone.

With this batch of top-quality diamond, it would be enough for Luna Jewelry to operate for a period of time. The most important thing was that they hadn’t spent a penny on these top-grade diamonds worth at least 300 million dollars. The six pieces of uncut diamonds were all given by Wesley.”

At this moment, Wesley really vomited blood. His eyes were red, like a wild beast. His fingers were trembling, and he couldn’t speak. Even his body was shaking slightly.

Ophelia also felt like vomiting blood.

Luna Jewelry was the Caldwell Group’s biggest competitor. She married Wesley for two reasons. Firstly, they wanted to have a stable supply of diamonds. Secondly, they tried to suppress Luna Jewelry.

But now that Luna Jewelry had this batch of top-quality diamonds, the effect of her suppression was completely useless.

“How could it be?” Ophelia was unwilling to accept such a result. She suddenly glared at Osborne angrily.

It was all Osborne’s fault. He selected these six pieces of uncut diamonds for Alison. If it weren’t for Osborne, Alison would not have been able to obtain them. If Osborne helped Ophelia choose… she would be the one laughing triumphantly.

Ophelia was furious. She could only glare at Osborne fiercely.

Osborne ignored Ophelia and looked at Wesley with interest.

He laughed and said, “Wesley, are you vomiting blood now? I still have a piece of uncut diamond that I haven’t cut open. If you see what’s inside, you probably would faint.”
