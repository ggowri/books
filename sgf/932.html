"Wait, we have backup?" Serena questioned, her face a mix of disbelief and intrigue.

Osborne flashed a subtle smile. "Ms. Hernandez, you'll find out soon enough."

With that, he tore open a dimensional rift once again.

Before long, Osborne guided Serena and her four companions through to Blood Demon Island.

The backup he referred to was none other than the over five thousand members of the Blood Demon organization, all of whom he had been training here.

As they descended onto the island, they made no effort to conceal their presence, and it wasn't long before the Blood Demon members noticed Osborne's arrival.

In an instant, all the Blood Demon members rushed over.

"Mr. Augustine!"

"Welcome back, Mr. Augustine!"

Osborne smiled, gesturing for everyone to rise.

The five women stood there, dumbfounded, as they took in the staggering sight of five thousand Blood Demon members.

Their eyes widened in disbelief; they exchanged glances and gasped, realizing that the weakest among them were all Class A!

They thought, "Five thousand Class A warriors?

Where does all this power come from?"

A sense of pride swelled within Osborne.

These members of the Blood Demon had initially been secretly trained by Sebastian and divided into ten different training bases, and their skills were nothing to write home about.

But recently, thanks to Osborne's influence and the power of Blood Cross, they had all been transformed into Blood Clan members, with even the weakest among them reaching Class A.

The number of Class AAA fighters among them exceeded ten, while more than a hundred had achieved Class AA status.

While this strength was still a far cry from the Draco Armed Services Branch, let alone the Aberrants, the potential within these fighters was enormous.

As Blood Clan members, with Osborne's provision of Blood Cross' enriched blood energy, they could rapidly advance without the usual barriers.

Recently, after absorbing a Dark Deity's blood energy, the Blood Cross had become even more potent and dynamic.

Osborne planned to consolidate the abundant energy within the Blood Cross into blood energy, allowing the Blood Demon members to absorb it.

This would undoubtedly boost their power significantly.

Without a moment's hesitation, he sprang into action.

The Draco Armed Services Branch was still on the run from the Aberrants; the sooner he got there, the more lives he could save. Delay could mean their total annihilation.

Osborne tapped into the potency of the Blood Cross.

After imbibing the Dark Deity's blood energy, the Blood Cross had gained a new level of vitality, enabling it to store even greater power.

He began to condense the blood energy before him—one drop, two drops, then a hundred, two hundred.

It didn't take long for countless droplets of blood energy to form in front of him, the sheer number reaching over five thousand.

These new droplets were not only more dynamic but also denser in energy than those Osborne had conjured before.

The Blood Demon members stared at the sight, eyes turning red with anticipation. They felt an overwhelming compulsion to transform, and one by one, they shifted into their Vampire forms.

Once again, Serena and her group found themselves in awe, not fully comprehending Osborne's actions.

But witnessing over five thousand individuals morph into Vampires sent shivers down their spines.

They stood silently, choosing not to interrupt as they observed the spectacle unfold.

"Go!" Osborne commanded, pouring the energy from the Blood Cross into the thousands of droplets.

With a gentle flick of his wrist, the blood energy shot forth, each droplet landing perfectly before its respective Blood Demon member.

"Absorb it immediately!" Osborne commanded.

At his order, the Blood Demon members sprang into action, each of them gulping down the blood energy as they began to rapidly refine and absorb it.

With each member at least at Class A, their absorption rate was impressive.

However, the sheer magnitude of the blood energy was overwhelming; it took a full two hours before the last Blood Demon member finally finished absorbing it.

In an instant, the energy radiating from that member surged dramatically, elevating their status from Class A to Class A+.

By this time, Serena and the others stood utterly stunned, their faces a mixture of shock and disbelief.

What had they witnessed during these two hours?

Over five thousand members of the Blood Demon had absorbed the blood energy, and nearly all of them had broken through to the next level!

Every single one of them had reached at least Class A+.

The number of Class AA members, which had only been a little over a hundred, skyrocketed to an astonishing 1200.

And the Class AAA members, which had previously numbered just twelve, now surged to a hundred!

They thought, "A hundred Class AAA?

Twelve hundred Class AA?

Every single one of the five thousand is above Class A+?

Such power might still fall short compared to the Aberrants, but when measured against the Draco Armed Services Branch, they are definitely on equal footing.

How is this even possible?!"
