An hour slipped by in the blink of an eye.

The God of Star had reached his limit; his body was becoming almost transparent, as if he were on the verge of vanishing from existence.

With a heavy heart, he knew he had to retract the Level Five Light Realm.

"From here on out, it's all up to you. I can't help you anymore. Whether you survive or not is in your hands now!"

With that, the God of Star sank down into a meditative pose, and the Level Five Light Realm dissipated in an instant.

Gerardo, witnessing this, couldn't help but let out a cold chuckle. He ordered, "After them! Find them and wipe them out!"

He didn't rush in himself; instead, he unleashed his power, sending it probing toward Challenge Peak and Mount Celestial Reach.

Excitement flickered in his eyes.

He thought, "A god in a weakened state... If I could capture him, I wonder if I could turn him into my Human Slave. It's worth a shot!

The Augustine family is definitely hiding something.

A few centuries ago, they were just another ordinary superpower. But ever since they moved here, their strength has skyrocketed!

And then there's the Godfather's scepter.

Its power is unmatched! It's even more potent than a top-grade divine artifact—probably on par with a supreme divine artifact!

But here's the catch: the Godfather's scepter only shows its true strength within the Augustine Estate. Outside of it, it's just an ordinary top-grade divine artifact!

This means the Augustine family must have a secret!

Now, let's dig a little deeper..."

Gerardo's power swept over Challenge Peak, but he found nothing. In the dungeon, there was a fortress, blocking any energy from seeping through.

Frustrated, he reluctantly shifted his focus to Mount Celestial Reach.

Suddenly, he froze in surprise.

"Wait, there are still people here? That energy... it's strong. Class S level! Let me go check it out!"

Gerardo locked onto Anastasia's presence and sped toward Mount Celestial Reach.

Meanwhile, Armstrong hadn't left either. He sent his men to hunt down the fleeing Augustine family and other factions while he remained behind.

When he saw Gerardo rushing toward Mount Celestial Reach, he followed suit without hesitation.

As for Sin, the moment the Level Five Light Realm vanished, he had already led a group of Eityreia Organization assassins using Stealth Art to pursue the Augustine family.

Fueled by rage over Osborne's murder of his wife and daughter, he vowed to eliminate everyone connected to Osborne without a moment's pause.

Hunting was their specialty.

At that very moment, nearly two thousand miles away from Newport City, a desolate island in the middle of the ocean erupted with violent winds.

The gales whipped up the waves, sending them crashing outward in furious circles.

The entire island trembled under the pressure.

A terrifying force suddenly erupted from the island, dark clouds rolling in overhead, flickering with lightning as if doomsday had arrived.

Thunderous sounds erupted from the island, rhythmic and pulsating, like the beat of a heart.

With each rumble, a wave of sheer power surged forth from the ground, echoing the island's ominous energy.

That was the sound of Osborne's heartbeat! After four grueling days of relentless effort, Osborne finally shattered the elusive Class S barrier that had seemed just within reach.

As the barrier crumbled, a powerful transformation began to unfold within him.

Osborne felt himself ascending!

His body and soul surged with energy, both in a state of upheaval and metamorphosis.

An overwhelming sense of euphoria washed over him, flooding his senses with pure bliss.

This transformation and elevation lasted for a full hour before it gradually subsided. The storm clouds that had gathered above the island dissipated, the lightning ceased its furious dance, and the island returned to its tranquil state.

Osborne suddenly snapped his eyes open.

A brilliant light shot forth from his gaze. His left eye glowed with the eerie radiance of Satan's Eye, a soft blue hue illuminating the surroundings, while his right eye gleamed with the iconic glow of Medusa's Gaze.

A piercing brilliance emanated from his right eye, striking the surface of the sea. In an instant, the water began to change, transforming into stone before his very eyes!

"What the..."

Osborne sat frozen in disbelief.

He could feel it; he had ascended from Class AAA to Class S, experiencing monumental enhancements in every aspect of his being.

But Osborne hadn't anticipated that after breaking through to Class S, his right eye would undergo such a bizarre transformation, granting him the power of Medusa—the ability to petrify anything in his sight!

This was the unique power of Medusa!

"Could it be the result of Gerardo's cultivation serum? Did those concoctions contain the heart's blood of Goddess Medusa, triggering this mutation in me?"
