I'll give you a chance to gather the funds, but you can't leave. I'll take you to a room where you can contact someone to get the money sent over immediately!"

With that, Tiana led Osborne to a private room.

"You've got ten minutes! If you can't come up with four million... no, not four million, but ten million within that time, don't blame me for not giving you a chance.

Once inside, he closed the door and cast a divine power to envelop the entire room, ensuring that no one outside could perceive what was happening within.

He was doing this to retrieve the money from his Dimensional Space.

Most of his Essence Crystals were stored there.

Tiana wanted to verify his financial status, but he couldn't just pull out Essence Crystals in front of everyone.

If he used his spatial power openly, it would reveal his abilities and expose his true identity.

So, Osborne needed a safe environment to extract the Essence Crystals first before allowing Tiana to check.

Four million Essence Crystals was a significant sum, and he had to handle it discreetly.

Even if Osborne switched to top-grade Essence Crystals, he still had around four hundred pieces. However, Osborne was reluctant to expose his top-grade assets too easily, so he decided to pull out mid-grade Essence Crystals instead.

To gather four million low-grade Essence Crystals, he would need a total of 40 thousand mid-grade Essence Crystals!

To gather 40 thousand mid-grade Essence Crystals required quite a bit of space. A regular bag simply wouldn't cut it, and even if it could hold that amount, it wouldn't be practical to carry around.

Most people preferred the convenience of storing their Essence Crystals in a bank, exchanging them for banknotes.

But Osborne had Dimensional Space, so he had no intention of going through the hassle of converting his assets.

"It looks like I'm going to need a Dimensional Bag! My spatial elemental essence is already at Level Six peak. Creating a Dimensional Bag should be a piece of cake!"

With that in mind, he sprang into action.

A Dimensional Bag was essentially a pouch whose interior space was transformed into a miniature Dimensional Space. It looked like an ordinary bag on the outside, but inside, it held a whole other world.

Such Dimensional Bags were considered special divine artifacts, extremely valuable and rare nowadays, as no one knew how to produce them anymore.

Most of what existed were remnants from the past.

Osborne retrieved an ordinary bag and harnessed his spatial power, quickly constructing a small Dimensional Space within it.

Next, he extracted some mid-grade Essence Crystals from his own Dimensional Space and loaded them into the newly created Dimensional Bag. Once he was set, he dispelled the divine power surrounding him and opened the door to step outside.

Tiana was waiting just outside the door.

As he emerged, the onlookers of the auction were all snickering, their faces twisted with disdain as they regarded Osborne with a mix of scorn and amusement.
