All the members of the Augustine family gathered to bid farewell to Osborne, Sebastian, and the others.

"Matthew, I'm leaving the Augustine family in your hands!" Osborne said, his tone serious.

Matthew nodded vigorously, his expression grave. "Don't worry, Godfather! I'll do everything I can to protect the family.

"You all stay safe out there!

"Once you've settled in Celestara, make sure to come back to Blue Planet and bring us along! We want to see what Celestara is like!"

Osborne smiled and nodded, saying, "Absolutely!"

He quickly scanned the faces of the Augustine family before turning to leave.

Following closely behind him were Sebastian, Anastasia, Zoe, Yulissa, Queenie, Nathan, Bryce, and Kendra, all of them disappearing swiftly from the sight of the family.

It wasn't until they had been gone for quite some time that the Augustine family members began to disperse.

"Ah!" Sebastian stood beside Osborne, hidden in the parallel space. Once the Augustine family members had finally scattered, he couldn't help but let out a sigh, a hint of sadness etched on his face.

Compared to Osborne, Sebastian had a much deeper emotional connection to the Augustine family.

The thought of parting ways with them and heading into the unknown of Celestara weighed heavily on his heart.

"Don't worry, Grandpa! We'll establish ourselves in Celestara quickly! Once we do, we'll come back and bring everyone over, making the Augustine family the strongest family in Celestara!" Osborne reassured him.

Sebastian's mood lifted a bit at this, and he smiled, "Alright! But you've got to put in the effort! Celestara is no Blue Planet. Making the Augustine family the strongest there will depend on you; I can only support you! But I believe you can do it!"

With that, Sebastian gave Osborne's shoulder a firm pat. "Let's go! It's time to set off!"

Osborne smiled back, "No rush; we've got a few more people joining us."

"A few more people?" Sebastian asked, curious.

Before long, three figures appeared, moving quickly toward them—God of Star, Divine Shadow, and Mila.

"Wait a second..." Sebastian exclaimed, narrowing his eyes at Divine Shadow.

"Who is this woman? She looks so familiar! There's something about her that feels incredibly known to me, yet I'm sure I've never met her before."

Osborne couldn't help but chuckle at this.

Instead of answering, he stepped out of the parallel space, bringing the others with him to stand before Divine Shadow.

Their sudden appearance startled Divine Shadow. When she saw Sebastian standing beside Osborne, her expression changed dramatically, and she almost turned to flee.

But just as she began to turn, Osborne called out with a grin, "Grandma Divine Shadow, where do you think you're going? We're heading to Celestara! You're not having second thoughts, are you?"

At his words, Divine Shadow froze in place.

Sebastian, on the other hand, was utterly stunned.

"Divine Shadow?!

"You're really Divine Shadow?! No wonder I felt that strange familiarity! You can take human form? And you look so young!

"Is it really you?"

Sebastian's surprise was palpable as he stared at Divine Shadow's face, unable to look away.

From a young age, he had treated Divine Shadow as a pet, and she had been a part of his life for as long as he could remember. With her, Sebastian had no secrets.

In the past, he had thought of Divine Shadow as just a cat, so he never felt anything extraordinary about her.

But now, he was shocked to discover that she could transform into a human!

And what a stunning human she was!

Divine Shadow stood frozen, her cheeks flushing slightly under Sebastian's intense gaze. Unable to help herself, she shot a fierce glare at Osborne.

With a soft, almost inaudible "Mmhmm," she consented.

Sebastian, finding the confirmation, was once again stunned into silence.

An awkward tension suddenly filled the air.

Realizing the need to break the silence, Osborne quickly said, "Grandpa, Grandma Divine Shadow, let's walk and talk, shall we?"

He then tore through the fabric of space, and in the blink of an eye, the group found themselves at the vast chasm of Felona—a distance of over six thousand miles traversed in an instant.

Osborne murmured, "This is it! The shortest route to Celestara lies here.

"I can create a temporary dimensional portal right into Celestara from this very spot!

"But we know nothing about Celestara. Once the portal is open, everyone, enter my Dimensional Microcosm; I'll take us all in together."

The group nodded in unison.

With that, Osborne set to work. Drawing upon the collective power of the Blood Demon members, his realm soared to the peak of Transcendent Deity.

The dimensional portal he conjured stretched deep, reaching a staggering depth of 120 thousand miles without hitting a limit.

Finally, at nearly 140 thousand miles, he encountered a barrier—the very boundary of Celestara.

All it took was the right application of spatial power to breach this barrier, and they would be one step closer to Celestara.

"This is it," he thought, his expression shifting to one of utmost seriousness.

"Everyone, be ready! We're about to cross into Celestara!"
