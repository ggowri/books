It's still in its juvenile stage...

It must absorb and merge with all eight of its offshoots to truly grow into a new World Tree! Only then can it wield the genuine power of the World Tree, the kind of strength that can support the entire cosmos!"

"An offshoot of the World Tree! Among the nine offshoots, this one is the last to sprout! It's the ninth offshoot!" he thought.

The World Tree was undeniably superior!

Even its offshoots were stronger than the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity.
