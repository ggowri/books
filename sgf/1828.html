Orlando's panic intensified. His body trembled as he stammered, "You... You can't be serious!

If only he didn't have such immense vitality—if only he could die faster and escape this living nightmare of torture.

It took what felt like an eternity, but eventually, Orlando's body was reduced to a ghastly white skeleton, every last scrap of flesh stripped away. Only then did the life finally drain from his hollow eyes.

Orlando was dead.

Even a Deity King at the initial stage couldn't withstand Zoe's soul power attacks.

"Osborne, it's all taken care of!"

"Let me see if the Radiant Era Syndicate has truly betrayed the Human Community. I need to find out if it was them who nearly wiped out the Pioneers of Starlight a year ago."

"Just as I suspected!
