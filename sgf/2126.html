"Wait, what?!" Osborne exclaimed, utterly bewildered.

The Infinite Devouring Eyes had been quietly residing in his Dimensional Space, silently consuming and breaking down the energy from Sacred Crystals to aid in his cultivation level.

So why had it suddenly appeared, and of its own accord, no less?

"Could it be... for the Radiant Golden Ore?" Osborne murmured to himself.

The Infinite Devouring Eyes was a mystical technique he had acquired when Satan's Eye broke through to Level Nine.

Its power was directly tied to the strength of Satan's Eye—the stronger the latter, the more formidable the former became.

Now, as Satan's Eye displayed a keen interest in the Radiant Golden Ore, it seemed unable to absorb it directly. "Is it asking for the Infinite Devouring Eyes to help?"

With that thought, Osborne sent the Radiant Golden Ore soaring toward Satan's Eye.

In the blink of an eye, an immense force of suction erupted.

The Radiant Golden Ore instantly merged into the Infinite Devouring Eyes.

The colossal eye flickered with a soft golden glow for a brief moment before falling silent again.

"Did it really absorb it?" Osborne stood there, stunned.

However, after absorbing the Radiant Golden Ore, the Infinite Devouring Eyes showed no further signs of change. Osborne felt no difference in his Satan's Eye compared to before.

He frowned in frustration, thinking, "Was that it? Maybe it's because one Radiant Golden Ore isn't enough?

Perhaps it needs more to trigger a change in the Infinite Devouring Eyes or Satan's Eye?"

Determined, Osborne pulled out all the Radiant Golden Ore he had collected.

Each Radiant Golden Ore was like the eyes of the Mud Golem; he had defeated twenty-eight of them and gathered a total of fifty-six pieces, all of which were now in front of him.

The next moment, the Infinite Devouring Eyes unleashed a powerful force, drawing all the Radiant Golden Ore toward it and merging them into its depths.

In an instant, a brilliant golden light erupted from the Infinite Devouring Eyes, transforming its previously dark and ominous appearance into something almost sacred, bathed in a soft golden hue.

At the same time, Osborne suddenly felt his Satan's Eye begin to throb violently.

The intense pulsations shocked him, as if his eyeball might pop right out of its socket. A trickle of blood tears unexpectedly streamed from his eyes.

"What's happening?! Is the Radiant Golden Ore toxic?!"

Panic surged through Osborne.

He couldn't afford for Satan's Eye to malfunction. A significant part of his success stemmed from its power.

Without Satan's Eye, he might have remained an inconspicuous ordinary man on Blue Planet, thrown out of the Caldwell family after his divorce from Ophelia, never to rise again.

It was Satan's Eye that had brought him to this point.

Even now, without Satan's Eye, Osborne was an Ascension Two Supernal Deity King, still one of the top warriors in this cosmos.

But Osborne was unwilling to lose Satan's Eye.

Other abilities were manageable, but the super vision and X-ray vision granted by Satan's Eye were irreplaceable. Losing them would be a significant blow.

And then there was the Infinite Devouring Eyes!

This ability actively consumed and broke down energy, allowing Osborne to elevate his cultivation level without wasting countless hours.

Without it, he might still be just an ordinary Deity King.

In short, Osborne could not let anything happen to Satan's Eye.

As the abnormal activity of Satan's Eye caused him to panic, he forced himself to calm down and began to carefully sense the changes occurring within.

The violent pulsations of Satan's Eye sent sharp pangs of pain through Osborne, but he hardly noticed.

Instead, a look of delight spread across his face as he sensed something incredible—with each beat, Satan's Eye was growing stronger!

Having already reached the pinnacle of Level Ten, it had been a long time since Satan's Eye had experienced any improvement. However, after absorbing fifty-six pieces of Radiant Golden Ore through the Infinite Devouring Eyes, it was slowly becoming even more powerful.

The most immediate change was in its X-ray vision ability, which was rapidly enhancing.

Previously, even at full strength, Osborne could only see 16 feet deep into the Corrosive Marsh.

Anything beyond that was a mystery.

But now, his X-ray vision was being enhanced.

As Osborne focused on the marsh, he could see much deeper.

In no time, Satan's Eye was able to penetrate down to thirty-three feet!

This meant his X-ray vision had effectively doubled in range.

And the enhancement didn't stop there.

Not only was Satan's Eye improving, but so was the Infinite Devouring Eyes.

Inside it were twelve Sacred Crystals, which it had been tirelessly working to break down, aiding Osborne in raising his cultivation level.

The energy contained within a Sacred Crystal was immense, and even the Infinite Devouring Eyes needed time to completely absorb and decompose it.

The speed at which it could break down energy was limited, but now, Osborne sensed that this rate was rapidly increasing.

Previously, it took him ten days to decompose the energy from a single Sacred Crystal. Now, that time had been cut in half—just five days to fully break down one Sacred Crystal.

This meant Osborne's cultivation level was also set to rise at double the speed.

And the best part? This enhancement wasn't finished yet.

Osborne felt a surge of joy, thinking, "The Radiant Golden Ore truly works on Satan's Eye! It's leveling up again!

My Satan's Eye, already at Level Ten, is on the brink of becoming... Sacredness Level!

A Satan's Eye at Sacredness Level?!"

He was bubbling with anticipation, but he knew that fifty-six pieces of Radiant Golden Ore wouldn't be enough to push Satan's Eye past its limits into Sacredness Level.

The gap between Level Ten and Sacredness Level was vast, filled with challenges that made breaking through incredibly difficult.

Yet at this moment, Osborne felt a glimmer of hope.

"I need a massive amount of Radiant Golden Ore!" he thought, excitement coursing through him.

The energy from the fifty-six pieces was being consumed rapidly, and the pulsations of Satan's Eye began to slow, indicating that the enhancement was nearing its end.

Having tasted the thrill of rapid improvement, he was eager to push Satan's Eye to the Sacredness Level.

The advantages that would come with it were undeniable.

Finally, the changes in Satan's Eye came to a halt.

Compared to before, its X-ray vision ability had tripled. It was now capable of seeing down to sixty-six feet into the Corrosive Marsh.

The Infinite Devouring Eyes had also tripled its energy decomposition speed.

Osborne felt he was on the cusp of breaking through to Ascension Three.

As he opened his eyes, a spark of determination flashed within them.

"Radiant Golden Ore must be a unique treasure of Umbra Earth! I need to gather as much as I can to help Satan's Eye complete its transformation into Sacredness Level! If I miss this chance, who knows when I'll get another opportunity?"
