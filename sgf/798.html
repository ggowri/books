"Die!"

Cuthbert shattered Osborne's shoulder with a punch, immediately throwing another towards his chest. He was determined to defeat Osborne completely, even killing him wouldn't matter.

In theory, the Honor Quest Ceremony was only about victory, not life and death.

But accidents happen in combat, and no one would blame him if he accidentally killed his opponent.

However, in that moment, Cuthbert sensed something was wrong.

His punch landed heavily on Osborne's chest. In an instant, Osborne's body seemed to shatter, with blood and flesh flying everywhere.

But on closer inspection, none of it was real. It was all an illusion created by manipulating light!

What he hit wasn't Osborne's real body.

It was just an illusion, crafted by Osborne's control over light.

It was so lifelike that Cuthbert hadn't realized it at first.

"Heh, nice punch. Quite powerful," Osborne's voice suddenly sounded behind Cuthbert.

Cuthbert spun around to see Osborne standing there, unharmed.

Cuthbert's expression darkened completely.

"How is this possible?

"You created a fake body with light? And you managed to fool me?"

Cuthbert clenched his fists tightly.

Osborne smiled lightly. "Is it that hard? I don't think so. My Light Realm has more tricks than that! Our battle is only just beginning."

As soon as Osborne finished speaking, Cuthbert felt the light around him start to warp and bend.

Osborne's figure, originally in front of him, suddenly appeared to his left.

Osborne waved his hand, sending beams of Dissolving Light scattering randomly in all directions. In an instant, these beams reappeared right in front of Cuthbert, aimed directly at his vital points.

Cuthbert had no time to dodge.

Thud!

Bang!

Over a dozen Dissolving Lights struck his chest simultaneously. Despite Cuthbert's formidable defenses, the pain forced him to stagger back.

His face turned even darker.

"Twisting light? No, this isn't ordinary light manipulation! A regular light elemental metahuman couldn't distort light enough to trick my perception!

"How did you do it?"

Osborne chuckled. "Want to know? Too bad, I'm not telling you."

With that, Osborne unleashed another barrage of Dissolving Light.

These beams seemed to shoot randomly in all directions, and Cuthbert's perception confirmed the same.

Yet, in the next instant, they abruptly appeared in front of him again, targeting his vital spots.

Caught off guard, Cuthbert couldn't dodge and had to rely on his body to withstand the attack.

Thud!

Bang!

Once again, the force of the attack pushed Cuthbert back. He glanced at the scorched marks on his chest, his face darker than ever.

Osborne's methods were proving troublesome.

Gritting his teeth, Cuthbert rushed at Osborne, determined to strike his real body.

But he soon realized he couldn't get near Osborne. The twisted light made it impossible for him to lock onto Osborne's position. What appeared to be a straight path was actually distorted, causing him to move farther away the more he tried to close in.

"Damn it!"

Cuthbert was furious. Osborne's Light Realm was far too troublesome.

He couldn't land a hit on Osborne and couldn't avoid Osborne's attacks, becoming nothing more than a target.

If it weren't for the relatively weak power of the Dissolving Light, he would have already lost.

"This can't go on!

"If I want to defeat him, I must destroy his Light Realm! Looks like I'll have to self-destruct my Strength Realm!

"Even without my Strength Realm, I can still beat him! But if I self-destruct it, reaching an eighty-win streak will be impossible, and even seventy wins might be hard!"

Cuthbert gritted his teeth.

"But at the very least, I have to defeat him. It'll be a disgrace if I can't even reach fifty wins! How will I ever aim for Class S?

"I have to defeat him."

Determined, Cuthbert began to channel his Strength Realm, colliding with Osborne's Light Realm.

"Self-destruct!"

Cuthbert roared as his Strength Realm disintegrated, unleashing a terrifying force that surged toward Osborne's Light Realm.

In that moment, Osborne's Light Realm seemed to be hit by a tidal wave, with light bending and flickering chaotically.

Crack! Crack!

Osborne furrowed his brow.

The power of Cuthbert's realm explosion was immense. Even though Osborne's Light Realm was stable, it couldn't withstand the impact and started to disintegrate rapidly.

Yet, Osborne remained calm.

He didn't attempt to use his psychic strength to stop his realm from collapsing because he knew it would be futile and would only cause more backlash.

Osborne's Light Realm was on the verge of shattering.

Cuthbert, blood seeping from the corners of his mouth, smiled coldly.

"Without your Light Realm, you're finished!"
