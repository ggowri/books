Osborne nodded and said, "Then you need to hurry. If the Magma Devourer returns to its lair, we'll be in trouble."

He can steal memories from other World Tree offshoots. Among them, one offshoot's memory contains a way to deal with the Magma Devourer.

He needed to calculate the positions of all the silicon-based neural nodes in the Magma Devourer's body to identify a few key ones that were crucial yet located just on the surface.

Those silicon-based neural nodes are the Magma Devourer's fatal weakness.

As long as they can destroy those silicon-based neural nodes, the Magma Devourer will immediately lose its power or even die outright.

In fact, this is not just the Magma Devourer's weakness but the weakness of all silicon-based lifeforms.

After mastering this method, Osborne can calculate the weakness of any silicon-based lifeform he encounters in the future and focus his attacks on those weak points.

This way, he will be able to kill them more easily.

Osborne decided to learn this method from Primordial after returning. Although silicon-based lifeforms are rare, no one can guarantee he won't encounter them in the future.

Every silicon-based lifeform has terrifying defense and immense vitality. Under normal circumstances, they are extremely hard to kill. If he masters this method, it will become much easier.

But now, it's too late to learn this method temporarily.

Osborne can only hope that Primordial quickly identifies the Magma Devourer's weakness.

At this moment, the Magma Devourer had already ventured deep into the volcanic region, getting closer and closer to its nest.

Although Magma Devourer isn't fully grown either, it's already at advanced Empyrean Sacredness, almost nearing Empyrean Sacredness Lesser Perfection."

Can Flash really stall Magma Devourer? With such a huge gap in their cultivation levels, won't Flash get crushed to death by Magma Devourer in one stomp?"

Osborne was still hesitating, but Magma Devourer was already quickly approaching its nest.

The next second, Flash dashed out wildly, as fast as lightning.

Flash is actually confronting Magma Devourer head-on!

Clara and Leroy were also very tense. All their eyes were focused on Flash.
