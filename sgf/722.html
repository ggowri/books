"Relax? Don't resist?" Emma murmured, her body still tense.

Gradually, Emma felt her emotions settle, and her stiff body began to loosen.

Osborne said with a slight smile, "Good.

"Why do I have to do that?" Emma finally voiced her confusion.

Emma bit her lip.

At this moment, if Osborne had wanted to harm Emma, she wouldn't have been able to react at all.

Happy because his plan was working flawlessly; if Osborne wanted to unleash a mental attack and manipulate Emma's memories, it would be a piece of cake.

But troubled because Emma actually trusted him this much.

The thought of harming someone so trusting made Osborne's heart heavy.

He gritted his teeth but resolved to proceed with his plan.

With Emma completely defenseless, Osborne knew what he had to do next: alter her memories, change how she felt about him, and reshape her thoughts.

It wouldn't be easy, but it was the only solution he could think of.

Osborne activated Satan's Eye, crafting a brand-new memory for Emma.

All he had to do was overwrite her memories of today, and everything would be sorted out.

"You're being outrageous!" Emma shouted.
