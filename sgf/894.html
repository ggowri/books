Transforming into a Vampire and unleashing the Breath of Celestial, Osborne felt his reaction time, speed, and strength surge exponentially.

His speed was mind-blowing.

Scarlett, still reeling from the last exchange, clearly hadn't anticipated this new level of power. She attempted to retreat with quick reflexes, but it was too late.

Osborne delivered a powerful kick, unleashing a force that was nothing short of terrifying.

In an instant, Scarlett was sent flying, spewing blood from her mouth as her body crumpled under the impact. Her spine had been severely compromised.

Osborne's strength eclipsed even the peak prowess of Cuthbert.

After Osborne's transformation into a Vampire, his power more than doubled, and with the Breath of Celestial, it surged again.

In this state, he could threaten ordinary Class S Supreme Overlords without any martial arts techniques.

Scarlett, while formidable, relied heavily on her Stealth Art and the Level Three Plant Realm; her physical abilities were no match for Osborne's overwhelming force.

Just like that, Scarlett was badly injured, blood pouring from her lips as she flew backward over a hundred yards.

Before she could even hit the ground, Osborne appeared directly in front of her, icy indifference etched across his face.

Another calculated kick sent Scarlett's body slamming down onto the arena like a rag doll, her consciousness slipping away before she could utter a single sound.

With her defeat, the Plant Realm dissipated into nothingness.

A collective gasp echoed through the crowd gathered to watch the fight.

Everyone rose to their feet in disbelief as they took in the sight of the unconscious Scarlett and the undeniably victorious Osborne.

Shock painted their faces.

Just moments prior, they had seen Osborne struggling and desperately losing ground.

They believed he was on the verge of defeat, his energy rapidly draining against Scarlett's tireless onslaught.

It seemed impossible for him to overcome her—her Level Three Plant Realm and devastating Stealth Art positioned her as a substantial threat.

But, in the blink of an eye, the situation had flipped completely.

Most onlookers hadn't even registered the moment Osborne turned the tide until he had delivered one brutal kick after another, leaving Scarlett battered and unconscious.

Osborne had emerged victorious!

The crowd erupted into a cacophony of cheers and gasps.

Even the Class S warriors on the elevated platform stared wide-eyed in astonishment.

The Augustine family members' joyful cries rang out above the rest. Many had tears in their eyes, overwhelmed by a mix of disbelief and exhilaration.

"He won! He actually defeated Scarlett!"

Osborne's feat not only ended the match but also secured him a remarkable hundred consecutive victories, marking him as the first legend in the Augustine family's history to achieve this milestone and the tenth in the world.

If anyone had suggested just hours earlier that Osborne would accomplish such a feat during the Honor Quest Ceremony, none would have believed it.

It would've felt like nothing short of bragging rights—an impossible dream.

Yet, here Osborne was, defying all expectations and completing a legendary hundred consecutive victories while everyone else had counted him out, convinced he'd somehow turn this prestigious event into a disgrace.

Each face among the Augustine family radiated pride, a swelling sense of honor filling the air.

"Princess!"

While the ecstatic family celebrated, someone dashed to Scarlett's side, scooping her up.

An elderly woman glared at Osborne, her face etched with fury as she assessed Scarlett's severe injuries.

"Damn you, Osborne! How dare you hurt Scarlett? Do you even know who she is? You're finished!"

Osborne merely scoffed in response.

"I couldn't care less who she is! She stepped into the arena, which makes her my challenger. In the ring, punches and kicks are blind; her injuries are just proof of her weakness."

The old woman's anger only intensified at his words.

"Fine, fine! Just wait, Osborne! The Eityreia Organization won't let this slide! You think you can hurt Scarlett and walk away unscathed? You're dead meat!

"Just you wait!"

With that, she cradled the unconscious Scarlett and vanished in an instant.

Osborne frowned but ultimately chose not to pursue her.

Taking a deep breath, he began to absorb a massive amount of star power, working to replenish his energy.

Although he had triumphed over Scarlett, the battle had drained him significantly; Osborne had expended a great deal of strength activating both Spatial Domain and Spirit Realm.

Now, more than twenty opponents awaited him, none of whom could be underestimated.

Among them was Marlon, someone even stronger than Scarlett.

Osborne knew he couldn't afford to let his guard down.

He focused on recovery, but his challengers had no intention of giving him any breathing room.
