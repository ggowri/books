"You have one minute to decide! After that, if you still refuse to surrender and hand over the treasures and resources, I will order all warships to open fire!"

Moreover, even if the prey is very powerful, with a Mid-grade Sacred Artifact warship, they could escape much faster.

The safety of their pirate group would be greatly enhanced.

As for Angela, Osborne, and Agatha on the warship, he didn’t take them seriously at all!

If they dare to resist, we'll launch a direct assault and kill them all!

If they surrender, we'll take the loot first, then kill them afterward!

We'll just toss their bodies into the vast cosmos!"

"Osborne, I'll go out and kill him!"

He is a worthy opponent.

Even for his self-created Solar Sacred Arrow—Human Arrow, Earth Arrow, and Heaven Arrow—he only needed to use the weakest, Human Arrow, to easily defeat any opponent.

He doesn't even need to use the Earth Arrow.

Opponents of the same cultivation level could no longer put any pressure on Osborne.

He's worthy of me using the Earth Arrow!

Only when the arrow hit the enemy would the Sacred Power erupt instantly!

This is the Earth Arrow!

The Human Arrow can push Osborne's Sacred Power to the limit.

But the Earth Arrow compresses the Sacred Power, making the arrow more powerful.

While compressing, the Heaven Arrow also absorbs the power of heaven and earth.

He killed a mid Empyrean Sacredness warrior with a single arrow?!
