Duke Medusa's expression soured upon hearing this.

"Do you think you can kill us? You're dreaming! You won't live long enough. The Aberrants will ensure you die miserably."

Osborne shrugged. "I've heard those words too many times. When I was just Class B, a Count Medusa said the same. Then a Marquis Medusa echoed it, and now you, a Duke, are saying it too."

"But I'm still alive and well, aren't I?"

"You may be powerful, but you can't catch me! When I grow stronger, I'll come for you, and I'll smash your head!"

With that, Osborne stepped back toward the dimensional rift.

Because at this moment, he sensed several powerful auras approaching.

These auras were no weaker than that of Duke Medusa.

If he was surrounded by these people, he would have absolutely no chance of escaping.

"Damn it!"

"You stay here!"

When Medusa saw Osborne was about to leave, she became furious. She sensed her companions were closing in. In just a few moments, Osborne would be surrounded by them and have no chance to escape.

At this moment, how could she be willing to let Osborne leave?

Her eyes blazed with intent to petrify him, but it was too late.

Osborne smiled faintly. "Wait for me. None of you can escape!"

Then he vanished, leaving Duke Medusa unable to sense his location.

Her expression darkened.

Swoosh!

At this moment, several imposing figures appeared beside her.

"Duke Medusa No. 6, where is that little bug? Didn't you lock him down?" A loud voice sounded.

The person who spoke was a Titan. His body was so huge, at least 33 feet tall, resembling a Tyrannosaurus Rex with rough skin and a menacing face. His feet were like two pillars and his fists were like a big stone.

Just looking at him, one could feel the strong pressure.

Duke Medusa No. 6 shot him a cold glance. "Duke Titan No. 7, are you questioning me?"

"It's your fault for arriving late. That damned bug has escaped. He has a special power and eliminated my Medusa Mark. I can't sense him anymore."

"He's at least 30 miles away. We can no longer pursue him."

Duke Titan No. 7's expression darkened. "You let a little bug run away? You're useless! How can you follow the great The King of the Aberrants?"

Duke Medusa No. 6 bristled. "Duke Titan No. 7, are you looking for a fight? I'll turn you into stone!"

"Do you think I'm afraid of you? I'll crush you!" Duke Titan No. 7 said coldly without showing any weakness.

"Enough, stop arguing." A massive black wolf spoke up.

He was Duke Werewolf No. 9!

"What we need to do now is to quickly capture that little bug! He attacked two rare metal mines and killed six Marquises and a dozen Counts."

Duke Vampire No. 8 added, "We must find and tear him apart! He's only at Class AA, but very strong. Marquises and Counts won't be enough."

"Only we dukes can kill him."

Duke Tauren No. 7 said, "We must act quickly. We must find him even if we turn Dampston upside down!"

After speaking, the five suddenly fell silent.

They all wanted to kill Osborne, but he had already escaped. How could they find him?

"He'll likely attack the rare metal mines again or even the Dampston headquarters! Our best bet is to ambush him," Duke Vampire No. 8 suggested.

"I agree!" Duke Medusa No. 7 nodded. "He has spatial powers. Even if we can find him, we may not be able to catch him. The only way is to set a trap and let him fall into it!"

"We can request an ancient magic weapon that can freeze space. If he dares to show up, he won't escape and will surely die!"

"I agree!"

"I agree!"

"I agree!"

The five Dukes of the Aberrants reached a consensus and began planning a trap for Osborne, waiting for him to take the bait.
