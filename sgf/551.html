Everyone turned their heads and saw Rodney slowly approaching them.

Rodney gave a calm smile.

"No, I'm here to propose a deal."

Tony frowned, his face filled with suspicion. "A deal? We're competitors. What kind of deal could we possibly have to discuss?"

Rodney looked at him disdainfully. "Are you sure you're my competition? Let's be honest—you've already been eliminated. Even if you hadn't been, did you ever stand a chance of claiming the Heir's Emblem and becoming the heir?"

Tony and the other three men looked furious, but none could refute his words.

"We may have lost, but you have no chance either! It's obvious that the branch bloodlines from Yalynch, Marcelin, and Ransford have teamed up. They're already strong individually, and now that they've formed an alliance, they're planning to monopolize both the Heir's Emblems. You won't stand a chance."

Rodney nodded with a smile. "You're not wrong. If nothing unexpected happens, I would have no chance either. But the unexpected has already happened, hasn't it?

"Osborne is that unexpected factor.

"He eliminated all four of you right from the start, shattering your hopes. That gives us a basis for cooperation."

Tony and the others looked uneasy.

"What kind of cooperation are you proposing?"

"It's very simple. Lend me your men to help me seize the Heir's Emblem, and I'll make sure you're handsomely rewarded."

"That's right!"

Rodney's voice was steady. "Even though you four have been eliminated, together, your branch bloodlines still have forty strong men. Add that to the eighteen from my branch in the Norlend, and I'll have a formidable team of fifty-eight.

"With so many helpers, I'm confident I can wrest the Heir's Emblem from Osborne's hands and fend off Sam and David."

"In the end, you'll benefit, I'll become the heir, and we'll all be pleased."

The four men exchanged glances, intrigued.

"What kind of reward are we talking about?"

Seeing that they were interested, Rodney leaned in and whispered a few words.

Their eyes immediately lit up.

"Alright! We agree. We'll lend you our men, but you have to keep your promise and help us get revenge. Osborne must be thoroughly humiliated!"

The four men gritted their teeth, filled with resentment.

Rodney, seeing their agreement, laughed. "Don't worry, I won't let you down. Osborne won't get away this time, even with his spatial power. He won't escape!

Rodney's forces swelled to nearly sixty men, making him the contender with the most helpers in the entire Branch Bloodline Competition.

"How dare he!"

"I get it now. He stayed behind to launch a sneak attack and take out some competitors to ease his pressure!"

"He actually has spatial power!"

"And he managed to escape!"

"Damn it!"

"Have some dignity!"

The supporters of the four branch bloodlines began cursing wildly at Isaac, Nathan, and the others, venting their frustration.

"Just you wait!"

How was he supposed to stand a chance?
