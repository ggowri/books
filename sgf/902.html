Marlon understood all too well that if his Solar Realm shattered, he would be defeated. In Osborne's Level Four Light Realm, he had no hope of winning.

After all, Level Four Realm was something that most Class S warriors hadn't even mastered!

It was said to be as solid as iron; with his current strength, Marlon knew he couldn't possibly break through Osborne's formidable defenses.

Fighting within Osborne's Level Four Realm?

That was looking for death!

Marlon realized he had to unleash a final, desperate strike before his Solar Realm crumbled completely. If he could severely injure Osborne, he might just turn the tide in his favor and claim victory.

He murmured, "I cannot lose!

No one among the younger generation can defeat me! I am invincible! I will follow in my great-grandfather's footsteps and become the strongest on Blue Planet!"

Marlon gritted his teeth, controlling the nine solar orbs as they blazed toward Osborne like fiery meteors.

Yet, deep down, he felt that wasn't enough.

In a sudden burst of determination, he charged into the last solar orb, merging completely with it. Channeling all his power, Marlon amplified the orb's strength, ready to unleash everything he had.

A rumble!

The nine solar orbs crashed down like a crazy, looking like the end of the world. This was Marlon's ultimate attack—he was holding nothing back, aiming to decide the outcome in one shot.

Below the arena, the audience was on their feet, shock in their faces.

They knew the moment of victory between Osborne and Marlon was coming.

Would Marlon's desperate strike be enough to defeat Osborne?

Or would Osborne withstand the assault, snuffing out Marlon's hopes and clearing the Honor Quest Ceremony, becoming the fourth person in history to do so?

Everyone held their breath, waiting for the result.

Would it be Marlon's triumph or a birth of a legend?

The terrifying solar orbs were suddenly upon Osborne, their overwhelming power undeniable.

Even Osborne had to admit they were formidable.

"Too bad, this won't be enough to take me down!"

With a cold huff, Osborne finally made his move.

At this point, he didn't care about exhausting his energy; he just needed to block Marlon's desperate attack so he would be the winner. Winning was all that mattered, even if it meant pushing himself to the limit.

With that thought, Osborne unleashed his own power!

A terrifying force erupted from his body, crashing into the first solar orb.

In an instant, the orb exploded under the sheer force of Osborne's punch, sending terrible shockwaves rippling outward like a disaster unfolding.

Osborne staggered back, his martial art aura flaring wildly around him.

His expression was cold and sharp as he unleashed a flurry of punches, shattering each falling solar orb one after another.

Each explosion sent Osborne reeling, blood trickling from his mouth, but he showed no signs of slowing down, keeping fighting.

The crowd below was utterly stunned, watching Osborne demolish those terrifying solar orbs while he himself was forced back, blood staining his lips.

Everyone was nervous and kept wondering, "Who will come out on top?"

Finally, Osborne managed to obliterate eight of the solar orbs in the void, sustaining significant injuries in the process, but for him, it was nothing.

At that moment, the final solar orb merged with Marlon unleashed an even more terrifying power, crashing down with a thunderous roar.

Marlon's voice rang out, wild and triumphant.

"Osborne, it's time for you to die!

"I am the strongest among the younger generation. You're just a stepping stone on my path!"

With every ounce of his strength channeled into the solar orb, Marlon elevated its power to a horrifying degree, at least three times stronger than an ordinary solar orb.

He was confident that even a true Class S Supreme Overlord would be unable to take it and would surely be seriously injured.

In the blink of an eye, that terrifying solar orb loomed before Osborne, with Marlon's body merging seamlessly into it, radiating a divine presence. Marlon smirked with arrogance.

But Osborne was smiling, too.

He could feel the overwhelming might of the solar orb and understood that ordinary methods wouldn't hold up against it.

But rather than panic, he fixed his gaze on Marlon with a cold laugh.

Osborne taunted,

"Marlon, you think you're the strongest among the younger generation?

You think you're worthy?

"Time for you to lose!"

With that, Osborne launched his right foot with tremendous force. In that instant, his Divine Bone flared to life, and a gentle divine power surged through him.

The power of the Divine Bone fortified Osborne's kick.

Then…

His right foot slammed down hard onto the solar orb merged with Marlon.

At that moment, time seemed to freeze.
