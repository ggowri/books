Isa's voice rang out icy cold.

"If it's not over, then you know what you need to do, right?

This is Osborne's Honor Quest Ceremony, and yet here's an old fossil trying to bully him on stage?"

The cold sweat began to bead on his forehead as Rumen looked at her, disbelief written all over his face. He even rubbed his eyes.

"You... you are? Who are you?" Rumen stammered.

Isa let out a mocking laugh.

"You don't know who I am? I wondered where your eyesight was going! Who am I? I'm 'Night.'"

At her words, Rumen stood frozen.

The cold sweat on his brow doubled, and he found himself instinctively taking steps back. Below the stage, the crowd was equally stunned.

"She's Night?"

"Oh my god! She's the one who just skyrocketed to the top of the Godson Rankings? I thought she'd be some old powerhouse, not a young woman!"

"Wow, it's been ages since someone from the younger generation topped the Godson Rankings!"

The murmurs of disbelief spread like wildfire through the crowd.

"It makes sense now why Osborne insisted his Honor Quest Ceremony wasn't over. He must have known Night would show up. There seems to be something special between them."

Isa ignored the chatter, her icy gaze locked onto Rumen.

In a voice only he could hear, she said,

"I warned Everett from the League of Divine Descent before. Osborne is my man.

You can go after him, but only if you're in the same generation. If anyone from a higher rank or generation dares to bully him, they'd better watch out!

Looks like Everett didn't fill you in, did he?"

Her tone turned even colder, causing Rumen's expression to darken.

He whipped around, glaring at Everett on the platform. It was true. Everett hadn't told him about this. He hadn't mentioned that Night Witch had such a connection to Osborne!

If Rumen had known that Osborne was Night Witch's man, he never would have gone after Osborne like this!

Everett quickly turned away, pretending not to see Rumen’s questioning gaze.

There was no way he would spill that information to Rumen.

The last time he was in Rosmandi, he had intended to challenge Sebastian but ended up getting thoroughly beaten by ‘Miss Night’.

Even though Everett was a top-tier Class S Supreme Overlord, he still had his pride.

Getting beaten by Isa wasn't the most humiliating thing, but he'd prefer it if no one ever found out.

Rumen, now visibly flustered, explained in a tone only she could hear,

"Ms. Night, I didn't know. Everett never told me! I had no idea Osborne was connected to you. I was wrong; I'll leave right now and won't interfere with the younger generation again."

He started to back away.

"Did I say you could leave?"

Isa's icy tone stopped him in his tracks.

Rumen froze, fear creeping back into his expression.

Isa smirked,

"You think you can bully my man and just walk away without paying a price? What kind of world do you live in?"

Rumen's face paled.

"What... what should I do then?"

"Slap yourself twice and then get out! Don't make me do it myself, because if I step in, it won't be just a couple of slaps."

With that, Isa dismissed him.

Their conversation had been a private exchange, and others couldn’t hear them. If the crowd had heard it, they would have been left utterly speechless, jaws dropped in disbelief at the reverse.

Osborne was oblivious to the exchange between Isa and Rumen; all he could see was Isa's icy glare directed at Rumen, making it clear she was ready to strike.

A surge of concern washed over him.

"Isa, be careful! He's a top-tier Class S Supreme Overlord. He's incredibly powerful!"

Osborne rushed to stand in front of her, his face filled with worry.

Feeling Osborne's protective presence warmed Isa's heart, and a smile broke through her earlier chill.

"It's alright. Mr. Flores is here to keep things in check, right? With him around, Rumen won't dare to make a move!"

As she spoke, Isa turned toward Jackie, who was looking increasingly rattled.

"Mr. Flores, am I right?" Isa pressed.

Jackie, clearly flustered, nodded vigorously.

"Of course! The Honor Quest Ceremony isn't over yet. Rumen is breaking the rules, and I will punish him..."

But Jackie's words were cut short as Rumen suddenly raised his hand and landed two hard slaps across his own face.

The sound echoed through the arena, each slap landing with enough force to draw blood from the corner of his mouth.

Rumen stammered, "I... I'll take responsibility!

"I was wrong. I'm leaving right now and promise to respect the rules of the Honor Quest Ceremony."

Without another word, Rumen hastily retreated from the stage, practically fleeing.

The crowd stood in stunned silence with all eyes wide with disbelief.

Murmurs began to spread.

Rumen? A top-tier Class S Supreme Overlord, ranked in the top fifty of the Global Quasi-Divine Rankings.

He just slapped himself? He's actually admitting he was wrong?

Osborne, still surprised from the surreal turn of events, turned at Jackie in shock.
