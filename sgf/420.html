Osborne's body was unable to move, and he could only watch Chris approach him.

Faced with such a situation, all of his skills were ineffective because the gap was too big.

Serena, Bruce, and Chris were not ordinary Class A masters because their strength exceeded this level.

People like them were like gods to ordinary people.

Serena, who was far away, was still attacking Bruce frantically, wanting to get rid of Bruce and save Osborne. But unfortunately, she couldn't do that.

She was entangled by Bruce.

Seeing Osborne being caught by Chris, Serena showed a pale face.

"It's over!"

"It's over!"

Both Serena and Osborne thought this in their minds.

A terrifying undercurrent suddenly appeared in front of Chris. Before Chris could react, he was hit by the undercurrent and flew out with a bang. He rolled on the seabed for a long time before finally stopping.

Osborne, who was caught by him, escaped at this time.

"Who do you think you are? Why don't I dare to attack you?"

He didn't know this person.

This harpoon was huge.

Chris' Death Sickle was already huge, as tall as two adults, but this man's harpoon was twice as big as Chris' Death Sickle.

The top of the harpoon was divided into three sharp blades, from which emanated a terrifying wave of power.

Chris also looked at the man in surprise, attracted by the huge harpoon in his hand.

"What you hold in your hand is the legendary ancient weapon, the trident of God of the Sea. You are the pirate leader of Likreia Mountain, Bryce Clarkson."

Was this man Bryce, the leader of Likreia Mountain Pirates, the largest pirate group in this sea area?

Was he Kendra's brother?

Osborne thought, "Why did he come here? Why did he save me?"

He was confused.

Bryce smiled and said to Chris, "You are right."

After saying that, he no longer paid attention to Chris, who had an ugly expression on his face but turned to look at Osborne unscrupulously.

"Brat, someone asked me to help you.

"Mr. Clarkson, who asked you to protect me? Your sister Kendra?"

Bryce was stunned.

"How do you know my sister Kendra? She lost her temper and ran out to play. Even I couldn't find her. Do you know where she is?"

Was Kendra really Bryce's sister?

From what Bryce said, the person who asked Bryce to protect him was not Kendra.

Could it be her?

At this moment, a beautiful figure appeared in Osborne's mind.

Isa.

Kendra once said that her brother Bryce admired Night Witch very much.

Night Witch was Isa's code name.

This arrogant woman.

Osborne was stunned.

This was indeed Isa's style.

He said angrily, "The King of the Aberrants? Do you want to scare me? I am not afraid of him. Even if he is the king, he is not the king of this sea area. In this sea area, I am the king!

"On the shore, I can't defeat the King of the Aberrants, but on the sea, does he dare to fight with me? Even if I can't kill him, I'll make him seriously injured."

Suddenly, terrifying undercurrents hit Chris and Bruce.
