Zachary couldn't help but chuckle.

Osborne woke up Anastasia, Zoe, and the others before pulling out a Dimensional Bag.

Among them, only he and Anastasia had Thunder Power. Since she didn't want the bow and arrow, he'd take them.

At the very least, they would be easier to handle than the Supreme Malevolence Sword.

"Now, let's go meet my family!"
