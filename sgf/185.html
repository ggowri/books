"Why?"

"Clarissa, do you hope Skyefall Security will win?" asked Donna.

"You are really something, dude!

As the eldest son of the Chevalier family, Apollo had a strong background and influence, and he was also outstanding enough. To the elders of the Radcliffe family, only Apollo could bear Clarissa's fate power.

If they got married, Apollo would use Clarissa's fate power to become the most powerful person in Rosmandi. He would also lead the Radcliffe family to become more powerful.

He thought, "Is Clarissa telling me she loves me?"
