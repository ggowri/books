The voice of Deathborn King thundered like rolling thunder, even reaching Osborne six miles away.

He stood frozen.

"Can Deathborn speak?" he murmured, recalling his previous encounters with these creatures, none of whom had ever uttered a word. They seemed to communicate through some unique and silent method.

This was the first time he'd heard Deathborn's voice, and it sent a shiver down his spine.

"I will offer your flesh and blood as a sacrifice to our great Master!"

Osborne thought, "Who was this Master?

Do all the Deathborns serve one master? They slaughter all living beings and devour their flesh to offer sacrifices to their Master?

And why did he call us as 'evil outsiders'?"

Osborne's mind raced with questions, and his expression was mixed of confusion and intrigue.

Meanwhile, Angelo frowned and spoke up. "Deathborn King, we stumbled into your territory by accident! We mean you no harm. If you let us go, we'll leave peacefully."

Deathborn King sneered at him.

"You invaded my realm and killed my subjects! Now do you think you can just leave? You evil outsiders deserve to die! You will all be offerings for my Master!

"Now, go to hell!"

With that, Deathborn King unleashed a wave of terrifying power toward Angelo and his group.

Angelo scoffed, saying, "Do you really think I'm scared of you? If you want to kill us, bring it on! Try me!"

In an instant, Angelo retaliated.

A fierce battle erupted.

Angelo held nothing back, unleashing his full strength, and his mind control power surged like a tidal wave.

He knew they had to eliminate Deathborn King quickly if they wanted any chance of survival.

If the Deathborn army arrived, escaping would be nothing but a dream.

Edison and the others understood the dire situation and joined the fray, but their attacks barely grazed Deathborn King, serving only to help Angelo fend off the surrounding patrols.

The clash of Deity Kings was intense and terrifying.

Osborne watched, captivated, as the two Deity Kings fought with each other.

He felt the energy radiating from both fighters and compared it to his own strength.

He couldn't help but think, "They're strong, but not that overwhelmingly.

Seems like they're not much stronger than me.

I'm not saying they're weak. It's that I've grown stronger.

Having broken through to the early stages of Deity Overlord, with both my space and dark elemental essences hitting Level Seven and boasting a Level Nine Divine Body, I may still be a quasi-Deity King, but the gap between me and a regular Deity King is practically non-existent.

I could probably take on a Deity King myself!"

Excitement surged through Osborne at the thought, sparking a desire to jump into the fight.

However, he soon received word from his clone that the Deathborn army was gathering within the kingdom and rapidly advancing toward them.

The numbers exceeded 30 thousand and were only increasing.

Osborne's expression darkened.

"Angelo and Deathborn King are about equally matched in strength. There's no way Angelo can take him down in a short time.

In less than ten minutes, the Deathborn army will be here!

By then, Angelo won't stand a chance against the King. Even if he survives, he'll be gravely injured. And that's not what I want!

The Deathborn King must die!

Only then can I obtain the death scent liquid to fully restore the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity!"

As these thoughts raced through Osborne's mind, he made a decision.

He had to help Angelo. Together, they would take down Deathborn King.

"Diana, stay here!"

Diana's eyes widened in alarm. "Osborne, what are you trying to do? We just need to do nothing! Angelo and his guys are dead meat!"

Osborne shook his head, and his expression was resolute. "Whether Angelo lives or dies doesn't matter to me! But for some specific reason, Deathborn King must die!

"Right now, Angelo can't take him down alone. I need to step in and help him."

"What?!"

Diana's worry deepened.

"Are you going to fight the Deathborn King? He's a Deity King!"

Osborne smiled, a glint of determination in his eyes. "Exactly! It's the perfect time to test just how far I've come compared to a Deity King!

"Anyway, I need to do it. Take care of yourself!"

With that, Osborne's features began to shift.

Soon, his look had a striking resemblance to the Tobey he had dealt with earlier.

Gripping the Supreme Malevolence Sword, Osborne felt an invigorating surge of power coursing through him.

The sword's sharp energy amplified his strength, making him feel invincible.

"Wait for me here!" he shouted, dashing out.
