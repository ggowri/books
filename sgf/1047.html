"Ophelia, what are you doing back here?" Gerardo frowned as he looked at her.

This was the storage place for divine corpses, and he was in the middle of extracting the heart's blood from one of the fallen gods.

"You said you hated it here. The sight of these corpses makes you feel uncomfortable."

Ophelia nodded.

She disliked being in the place. Somehow, seeing the four divine bodies filled her with sadness.

After her first visit, she made it twice until today.

She said, "Mr. Aniston, I have a question about my training and I can't wait any longer! I know you're waging war against Rosmandi and planning to take down the Augustine family!

"I know Osborne's true identity—he's the grandson of Godfather Sebastian and the core of the Augustine family. If you destroy them, you won't let Osborne go, right?

"I've said I want to take my revenge on him by myself! But I don't want to interfere with your plans, Mr. Aniston. So, I hope to break through to Class S before you attack the Augustine family!

"Then I can join you in the assault! I want to defeat Osborne myself, to humiliate him and take my revenge!"

Ophelia's eyes burned with determination.

Gerardo's expression grew troubled.

"Listen, Ophelia. There's no need to rush. You're progressing at a fast pace! You're my most promising disciple! Right now, the important thing is to build a solid foundation rather than just chase after quick breakthroughs.

"Trust me, if you continue to train, you'll become one of the top Class S warriors in no time—surpassing even me!"

He paused, then added, "As for Osborne... you don't need to worry.

"If you want to take revenge personally, I'll capture him alive! I'll bring him right to you, alright?"

Ophelia frowned tightly at his words.

Gerardo said, "Look, what I'm about to do is really important! I promised I'd help you get your revenge, and I would keep that promise!

"Just wait for me. Once I finish what I'm doing, I'll come find you and help you strengthen your abilities."

After a moment of hesitation, Ophelia finally nodded.

"Fine! I'll head back! But remember, Mr. Aniston, you have to leave Osborne for me! I want him to regret everything he did to me!"

"I promise you, I will!" Gerardo smiled reassuringly.

As Ophelia turned to leave, Gerardo didn't notice the coldness that suddenly appeared on her face, her eyes flashing with anger.

She thought, "Gerardo, you're still deceiving me!

Osborne is already locked up in a Human Slave cultivation tank. In just three or four days, he'll be your Human Slave.

Yet you act like I don't know everything and promise to capture him for me to deal with!

You are lying to me!

If it has to be this way, then don't blame me for what I’m going to do!

Gerardo, the reason I seek you as my mentor is simple. You have the power to make me strong—strong enough to finally take my revenge on Osborne.

Since you want to turn Osborne into a Human Slave, cutting off my revenge, I'll change things my own way!

You think I don't know you wiped out the entire Caldwell family? I've just been pretending, taking the time to build my strength!

You made me do this!"

Ophelia stormed into her room, her heart raging with fury, yet her face remained eerily calm.

She needed to a plan.

There was no way she would allow Gerardo to turn Osborne into a Human Slave, stripping him of his consciousness and memories.

Ophelia mused, "I have to save him!

Hmph! Osborne, you never expected that in your darkest hour, I would be the one trying to rescue you, did you? When you finally learn the truth, will you be shocked?

Will you regret turning me down?"

A smirk crept onto her lips at the thought.

"But saving you won't be easy! I need a good plan. Gerardo is no pushover!"

Ophelia fell into deep thinking.

This was the heart of the Aberrants' base, and even as Gerardo's disciple, she was under tight restrictions. Sneaking Osborne out without Gerardo catching wind of her intentions was very tricky.

But it's not impossible!

She just needed to wait for the right moment.

Every few hours, she would freeze time and slip away to the Human Slave cultivation chamber, checking on Osborne to ensure he hadn't been enslaved.

Three days passed.

Finally, the moment Ophelia had been waiting for arrived!
