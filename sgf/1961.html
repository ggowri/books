In the West Zone's Sanctum of Light, the breathtakingly beautiful God of Light stood by the window, gazing out at the landscape with a heavy heart.

A deep sigh escaped her lips as she murmured, "God of Night, where on earth are you?

"If you don't return soon, we won't be able to hold the West Zone! The last refuge of the Human Community is about to be overrun by the Dark Deity Legion..."

She bit her lip, determination flickering in her eyes.

"But don't you worry, I'll do everything in my power to protect it, even if it costs me my life! God of Death, God of Life, God of Space, God of Time, and God of Destiny have all faced death before. Perhaps it's my turn now..."

With that thought, her brow furrowed again, calling out, "Come here!"

In an instant, a shadowy figure materialized behind her.

"The esteemed God of Light, how may I serve you?" the figure inquired.

"Any updates from the Sanctum of Night?" she asked.

The figure shook its head. "No new developments.

"God of Time is still stuck outside the Sanctum of Night, but Isa and her group have ignored her completely, not even granting her entry. God of Time is furious, but she can't breach the Sanctum of Night's defenses and is left waiting outside."

God of Light sighed heavily, "What a mess! God of Night, if you don't return soon, it won't just be the West Zone in trouble. Your Sanctum of Night will be in jeopardy too."

Turning serious, she added, "Keep a close watch on the situation at the Sanctum of Night! If God of Time and Isa end up clashing, I want to know immediately! No matter what, God of Time died for him once before. We can't let her come to harm! At the very least, she must not die!"

The figure's expression shifted, concern evident. "The esteemed God of Light, are you suggesting that God of Time might struggle against Ms. Bowin? That shouldn't be the case. Her strength is nearly fully restored!"

God of Light let out a cold laugh. "Nearly restored? Sure, with time power, recovery can be quick! But even if she were at her peak, she still wouldn't stand a chance against Isa!

"Not just God of Time. Even I wouldn't be a match for Isa!"

The figure gasped in disbelief.

"What?! How is that even possible?"

God of Light waved a hand dismissively. "Just know this much! There's no need for further questions. Once God of Night returns, everything will become clear! I wonder what Isa is up to in the Sanctum of Night. I can't even reach her! If she would just step up and take charge, it would make everything so much easier...

"If she were willing to emerge, even if Lord Malevolence himself led an assault on the West Zone, we could hold out for a considerable time and avoid this dire predicament."

With a weary gesture, she rubbed her temples.

"Keep your eyes glued to the Sanctum of Night! The moment Isa shows up, let me know!"

The figure, still in shock, nodded and hurried off to carry out the orders.

God of Light continued to gaze out the window, her thoughts heavy.

"God of Night, where are you? Can this risky plan of yours truly succeed? It's far too dangerous! If you fail, what will we do? What will become of the Human Community in Celestara? I can only hope you find success..."

With a final deep breath, the God of Light returned to the main hall. "Gather the Dukedom Level Powers for a meeting! I have a feeling that the Dark Deity Legion will launch a full-scale attack within a month!"
