"Why?"

Was it because it was so dangerous this time that she was scared?

She couldn't have declined to help him out of fear of danger.

Isa's face turned cold.

"Isa, I know what to do! Thank you for reminding me. I really don't have the mindset of a strong man right now, but I will work hard!"
