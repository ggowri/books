"That's right, a divine artifact!"

Luca smiled faintly and said, "To kill you, I have to bring out the big guns!

"Now, take this axe!"

Without any hesitation, Luca gripped the axe with both hands, raised it high above his head, and then it burst with dark light as he swung it down at Osborne.

In that moment, Osborne felt the earth shake violently as if an earthquake had struck the island, sending massive waves across the sea.

Even before the axe came down, Osborne sensed a force locking onto him. It felt like no matter how he tried to dodge, he couldn't escape the blow.

In that instant, Osborne felt the presence of death.

"What a powerful attack! What kind of divine artifact is this? It can lock onto me? It won't stop until it sees blood?"

Osborne's face turned grim. He had no choice but to unleash all his strength and punch forward.

Boom!

The next second, a violent tremor erupted. The axe carved a massive fissure through the island, like a canyon, and Osborne was sent flying. A deep wound ran from his forehead to his stomach, with blood pouring out.

His fist was torn to shreds, with bone visible beneath the blood and flesh.

Luca looked at Osborne and smiled lightly.

"I only used forty percent of my strength, and you're already struggling? Looks like you're about to die!"

After saying that, Luca raised the axe high once more.

Osborne took a deep breath, drawing in all the air from hundreds of yards around him.

The Breath of Celestial!

In an instant, Osborne felt his body surge with power. The fourth layer of the Breath of Celestial increased his strength by eighty percent.

"Kill!"

Just then, the giant axe came down again, giving him that same inescapable feeling.

Osborne could only grit his teeth and brace himself for impact.

Boom!

The axe fell once more, this time creating an even larger fissure, almost splitting the island in half. The attack extended into the sea, causing massive waves.

Osborne was sent flying into the sea.

His body was trembling violently, nearly split in two by the blow. The severe injuries made it difficult for him to even stand.

"Am I going to die?"

Osborne gritted his teeth and glared at Luca.

The divine artifact in Luca's hand was too powerful. Osborne could tell that Luca hadn't even used his full strength, and he already couldn't withstand it.

"Osborne, you've disappointed me!"

Luca chuckled again. "I thought this fight would be interesting, but it turned out to be boring. So, I'll send you to hell!

"Kill!"

Luca raised the axe high and brought it down again, as if he were cleaving the heavens.

At that moment, Osborne truly felt the presence of death.

He could only watch as the giant axe swung down toward him.

This time, his body wouldn't be able to take it.

But just then, a figure suddenly appeared in front of Osborne. She threw a small shield, which instantly expanded into a massive one with the image of a flaming divine bird engraved on it.

The Swallow Shield!

It was Emma!

At the crucial moment, Emma finally arrived. She raised the divine artifact, the Swallow Shield, to block the axe's attack.

Boom!

The next second, the axe slammed into the Swallow Shield, making it shudder, but it didn't break. A terrifying shockwave spread out in all directions.

At the same time, a powerful energy surged through the Swallow Shield and into Emma's body.

Emma's face flushed red, as if she had drunk too much.

"Such powerful energy. Take it back!"

Emma shouted as she threw a punch. A massive force erupted, making Luca's face change as he blocked with his axe.

Bang!

Luca staggered backward, his face pale.

He stared at Emma, utterly shocked.

Emma then turned to Osborne, frowning. "Are you okay? Why didn't you call us if you couldn't handle it? If I hadn't arrived in time, you'd be dead."

Osborne gave an awkward smile in response.

"They're really strong. I was worried you guys would be in danger if you came."

Emma rolled her eyes.

"You knew they were strong and still didn't call us? Were you just planning to get yourself killed?"

As she was speaking, Clarissa finally arrived.

When she saw Osborne's battered state and the deep wounds on his body, nearly split open, her eyes immediately reddened with worry.

She quickly waved her hand and cast Divine Healing.

Once.

Twice.

Three times.

After casting Divine Healing three times in a row, Osborne's injuries finally started to heal significantly.

Only then did Clarissa let out a sigh of relief.

She gripped Osborne's arm tightly and scolded him, "Osborne, how did you get hurt this badly? Why didn't you call us for help sooner?"

Osborne awkwardly rubbed his nose.

Just as he was about to speak, Luca burst into loud laughter.

"Haha! You finally decided to show yourselves! Now that everyone's here, I won't hold back anymore.

"Everyone, come out now!"
