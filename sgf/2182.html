Phew! This guy is just a projection! His power level is still at Supernal Deity King, and he lacks any Sacred Power. Nothing to fear, then.

Atticus' power is at least on par with an Ascension Eight Supernal Deity King. Heck, it might even be on the level of an Ascension Nine one!

If Osborne hadn't merged with the fragment of the World Tree, he'd be in a real bind. He'd have no choice but to rely on an absolute forbidden zone to bring Atticus' cultivation level down a notch. Then, Osborne'd have to go all-out just in the hopes of getting the upper hand.

Clearly, he hadn't anticipated that Osborne could so easily shatter his attack.

"Damn you, human anomaly! You're dead man!
