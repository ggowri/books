A dozen elite warriors quickly surrounded Hannah and Osborne, and the color drained from Hannah's face.

These weren't members of the Bowman family; they were part of the family's elite guard, hailing from various races. The Bowman family, as a Paramount Supreme Clan, held immense prestige, attracting countless individuals eager to attach themselves to their power. Joining the guard was the only way in.

The requirements to be recruited were stringent.

Besides loyalty, candidates had to possess exceptional talent and potential worthy of the resources and time the Bowman family would invest in them.

The warriors standing before Hannah and Osborne had all been forged from the family's vast resources, and they were all at the peak of the Deity Overlord realm.

Each one posed a serious challenge for Hannah, who was only in the mid-stage of that realm. She had already pushed herself to her limits to break through their encirclement, and her injuries left her in no condition to make another escape.

Yet, Hannah refused to give in, her body tense and ready to fight to the last breath.

She remained wary of Osborne as well.

Even now, Hannah couldn't shake the feeling that the man standing before her was an imposter.

At that moment, the members of the Bowman family guard finally turned their attention to Osborne, confusion etched on their faces.

"Hey, who are you? Hannah, you actually have a helper?"

Hannah frowned, concern creeping into her mind.

Osborne seized the moment to speak, "Hannah, didn't you say you were surrounded by dozens of peak Deity Overlord warriors? Why are there only a handful here?"

Hannah froze, astonished. She stared at him in disbelief.

That was something she had only mentioned to him through her terminal.

No one else but Osborne should know that.

Hannah thought, "Could this really be Osborne?"

Panic surged through her. She would rather believe that this Osborne was an enemy in disguise than face the possibility of him being here, putting himself in danger.

"You! You really are Osborne? How did you get here?

"Didn't I tell you not to come and rescue me? Are you out of your mind? Do you really think there's a difference between facing dozens of peak Deity Overlord warriors and just a few? With our strength, we can't take them on. We'll die!"

As she spoke, her eyes glistened with unshed tears, the weight of her fear and frustration threatening to spill over.

Osborne smiled gently, "Looks like you're not doubting my identity anymore. Don't get all teary-eyed; we'll be fine! There's a big difference between facing a dozen peak Deity Overlords and dozens of them. If there were dozens, it would be tricky to take them all out, and a few might slip away.

"But with just a dozen, we can kill it all."

His confidence was palpable, but Hannah stared at him in disbelief.

"You think a dozen peak Deity Overlords are just going to be easy pickings? Osborne, what makes you so sure?"

In her eyes, Osborne seemed slightly weaker than she was, and taking on even one peak Deity Overlord would be no small feat, let alone a dozen.

Even if they teamed up, they wouldn't stand a chance against such formidable opponents.

Moreover, she could sense that Osborne was not in great shape.

He had expended nearly a third of his blood essence, transforming over 400 thousand people into Blood Clan members back at Divine Peak Nine base. That alone had drained him.

And to rush here using interdimensional travel across nearly six million feet? The toll had been enormous.

So why was he so confident against a dozen peak Deity Overlord warriors? Hannah couldn't wrap her head around it.

The guards of the Bowman family erupted in laughter at Osborne's words, their expressions dripping with disdain.

The leader of the guards stared at Osborne with a mocking grin. "Can you believe this guy?

"He thinks he can take us all out? Ha! A mid-stage Deity Overlord trying to wipe out a dozen of us? We're the elite guard of the Bowman family; every single one of us is top-notch!

"You've got guts, I'll give you that! I don't know who you are, but if you want to save Hannah, you're headed straight to hell with her!"

With a flick of his wrist, he signaled to his comrades, their faces twisting into sinister smiles as they readied to attack.

Hannah's expression hardened instantly, and she leaped in front of Osborne, her eyes wide with urgency.

"Osborne, you stay back! I'll hold them off! You need to find a way to escape!"

Osborne felt a surge of emotion at her words.

He placed a reassuring hand on her shoulder, pulling her protectively behind him.

"No need for that! I'm here now, and it's my job to protect you. As long as I'm around, nobody's going to harm you!"

With that, he turned his icy gaze toward the group of guards.

"Hannah is my friend. You hurt her, so get ready to face the consequences!"

As Osborne spoke, a dark dimensional portal materialized in front of him.

"Guys, you want to challenge the geniuses from the various clans? Step through!"
