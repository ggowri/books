She really took a huge loss!

Over 130 contestants died on Abyssal Planet.

In this sense, those who survived were all lucky.

The fifth place on the leaderboard is Dustin, with a total score of only 6,400 Kingdom Duel Scorepoints, while the sixth place is Mckinley, with 6,300 Kingdom Duel Scorepoints.

Seventh place...

Eighth place...

Ninth place...

Tenth place...

Osborne and the other two also heard the crowd's whispers but didn't care at all.

These people were just envious and jealous.

This wouldn't make him weaker, nor would it make Brenda stronger.

At this moment, Osborne was still thinking about how to keep his massive Kingdom Duel Scorepoints after returning to Racial Convergence Planet.

His Kingdom Duel Scorepoints were just too many!

They would definitely attract the envy of many warriors...

On the surface, they might not dare to make a move against him, but given the chance, they would surely try everything to take him down and seize his Kingdom Duel Scorepoints.

I must come up with a plan.

Clara and Leroy's Kingdom Duel Scorepoints also reached three billion, enough to exchange for several Legendary Sacred Artifacts in the Glorious Divine Kingdom's treasury.

They too would attract the envy of warriors.

Even if it were an Empyrean Sacredness Great Perfection...

They still wouldn't catch up with them!
