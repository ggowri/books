Osborne's taunts ignited the rage in both Sin and Crimson. The thought of their daughter, whose life snuffed out by Osborne, added fuel to their fury.

With a dangerous aura of trying to kill, they were poised to strike.

At that moment, beneath his facade of bravado, Osborne was tensed, ready for an onslaught.

His provocative words are his strategy to incite anger in both adversaries.

If they lost control of anger, they would be easy to make mistakes that he desperately needed to exploit.

As top warriors in class S, their power was much greater that Osborne didn’t think he stand a chance to win if they didn’t make mistakes.

However, it was risky, too.

Their rage could also increase their strength, making them more formidable than ever, just like now.

With fury running through their veins, Sin and Crimson lunged at Osborne, their divine artifacts glinting ominously.

"I'll make you wish you were never born!" Sin growled.

His speed so fast that he was right before Osborne as he talked, his dagger aimed straight at Osborne's right shoulder.

Seems like Sin intended to relish in Osborne's suffering, not killing him at once.

That was exactly what Osborne had hoped for.

But he underestimated Sin's speed, the relentless energy on the dagger was about to strike his shoulder before he could react.

Osborne's only option was to unleash his martial art aura to defend himself.

Martial art aura!

The dagger met his aura.

After the transformation of Osborne's martial art aura, his defense became unbreakably strong. Even the Class S assassins from the Eityreia Organization, using all their might, couldn't break his defenses.

But under Sin's attacks—one of the top-tier Class S warriors—Osborne's martial art aura couldn't make another miracle.

Sin's divine artifact dagger, after expending eighty percent of its strength, finally pierced through Osborne's martial art aura and plunged deeply into his right shoulder.

Blood gushed out, splattering onto Sin's face—a sight he met without flinching, even reveling in it as he licked the crimson droplets with a sadistic grin.

Crimson joined the fray, her dagger finding its target in Osborne's left shoulder, blood erupting.

Crimson roared, her voice a whip-crack of fury,

"You killed my daughter! I'll break your limbs and torture you so much that you regret every second you live and beg for forgiveness!"

In the next second, both Sin and Crimson drew their daggers back and aimed for Osborne's thighs.

Greyson and Roy yelled, finally snapping to reality,

"Stop! "

"Get away from him."

They charged forward, desperate to pull Osborne away from two torturers by attacking at them.

"Back off!" Sin bellowed, using a fistful of power to send a punch barreling through the air toward them.

There was a deafening explosion as they were flung backward, blood spewing from their mouths as they tumbled to the ground, pale and shaken.

Greyson and Roy struggled to keep their feet, with disbelief in eyes.

The gap in power was huge.

They thought, "A casual punch from Sin sent us flying! Is this the prowess of a top-tier Class S? It is terrifying.

Even with our combined efforts, it is evident we stand no chance against Sin.

And there is Crimson right beside him, her strength nearly matching his.

How can we even hope to fight back?"

Luckily, their desperate intervention had managed to draw Sin's attention away from Osborne, leaving only the furious Crimson set to deliver more punishment.

With a fierce force, Crimson thrust her divine artifact dagger toward Osborne's left leg.

But in that split second, Osborne snapped into action.

He wrapped martial art aura tightly around his right leg and drew upon the power of the Divine Bone within it.

"You old hag, you think you can shatter my limbs? Not without some consequences on your end! Get back!"

Osborne spat with a cold look.

Osborne channeled every ounce of his power into a single kick. The force was terrifying, and it burst forth with a faster speed than Crimson's dagger’s.

Caught off guard by the unexpected retaliation, Crimson had no time to dodge. Instead, she pressed on with her attack, thrusting her dagger toward Osborne's leg.

The collision was explosive.

Osborne's kick landed against Crimson's shoulder, sending her reeling backward. But at the same time her dagger found its mark, puncturing through his defenses to sink deep into his left leg.

Fresh blood burst from Osborne, but he remained unfazed.

Crimson was also bleeding, full of shock as she processed the sheer force in Osborne's kick.

She wouldn’t expect that Osborne's right leg bone had already transformed into a Divine Bone. Even a thread of divine power was beyond her.

A sickening thud echoed as Crimson crashed to the ground. Her right shoulder was drooping limply. Osborne's kick had crushed the bone, leaving her in agony.

It ignited a wild fury in her, her anger morphing into a relentless thirst for revenge.

"I'm going to kill you!" Crimson shouted and lunged at Osborne once more.
