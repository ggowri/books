Chapter 510 The Guard

Before long, Felix arrived at the restaurant as scheduled.

He wore a brand-new designer suit, a gleaming gold Hermès belt, and polished black shoes, carrying a heavy briefcase in his hand.

"Mr. Wallace, just a little token of thanks for dispatching your troops to protect our headquarters."

Felix smiled as he opened the briefcase, revealing it packed with crisp dollar bills.

Francis responded with a cold smile, "No need for thanks. The Red Umbrella Group is an important partner of the military. It's our duty. If there's anything else you need, just ask!"

Felix's eye twitched slightly. He was just baiting him to make requests to take advantage.

"Come, come, have a seat," Francis gestured.

The stern man immediately picked up a wine bottle, pouring a glass for Francis and Felix.

"Cheers, to a fruitful partnership!"

Francis raised his glass with a sly smile.

Felix said nothing, clinking his glass in a sullen toast.

They drank leisurely, like old friends at a casual gathering.

But underneath, each was scheming how to persuade the other and get what they wanted.

Finally, Francis spoke again, a glint of menace in his eyes.

"Felix, we've always had a good relationship, so let's be frank. You want me to deploy troops to protect the Red Umbrella Group and team up against Alexander, don't you?"

"Exactly!"

Felix agreed immediately, springing up from the couch, visibly excited.

"Alexander killed so many of my men. And that bounty is definitely his doing. It's either him or me... I will definitely get revenge!"

Francis just gave a disdainful laugh.

"Revenge? How would you manage that? Even those two old-timers, Quentin and Theodore, called me to advise you to apologize, pay compensation, and help restore Alexander's daughter's health.

"After all, Alexander is an important military partner and holds an official rank of Colonel. Quentin and Theodore are watching us. I won't personally go against him."

Felix's face darkened as he replied, "Apologizing and paying up is out of the question. I'll never apologize, and I sure won't help that brat."

With his hands pressed firmly on the table, he stared unblinking at Francis, speaking with utter resolve.

"Mr. Wallace, if you can send troops to protect my people, then I'll have no worries. I can take out Alexander and seize all his product formulas!"

Felix's gaze flickered with a strange light.

Now a level-three metahuman, he couldn't resist using his hypnotic power on Francis, trying to influence his decision.

"How dare you! Felix, what are you doing?"

The man, who had been watching Felix, shouted in fury.

As a fellow level-three metahuman, he could sense even the slightest energy shift, immediately detecting Felix's power aimed at Francis.

He threw a powerful punch, his fist the size of a boulder, shooting toward Felix's head like lightning.

The punch was so fast it tore through the air, even making a booming thunder sound.

"A level-three power-type metahuman!"

Felix was startled, instantly regretting it.

He'd hoped to use hypnosis on Francis to get what he wanted, but hadn't expected Francis' guard to be a level-three metahuman, stronger than him.

Just as Felix was about to clash with the man... "Stop!"

Francis' furious shout stopped them both in their tracks.

"Hmph!"

Francis shot Felix a cold glare, then waved for the man to step back.

"Felix, you're far too brave, daring to use powers on me? Do you think the Red Umbrella Group can match the strength of the military?"

Francis' expression had turned hostile, as if he might strike at any moment.

Felix cautiously stepped back a couple of paces, looking visibly embarrassed.

He never would have guessed that Francis would have a level-three metahuman guard by his side.
