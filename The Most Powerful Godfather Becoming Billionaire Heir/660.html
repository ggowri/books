Chapter 660 Increase in Strength

When the guards outside rushed in, they saw Barton's body slumped on the bed, with blood and brain matter staining the sheets.

"Ah! Mr. Estevan is dead!"

The guards screamed and trembled with fear.

Alexander did not go far but hid on the ceiling, quietly watching the development of the situation.

This time, he wanted to make the southern Mining leaderless.

The guard rushed out and shouted desperately in the corridor, "Mr. Estevan is dead! Someone come quickly!"

The other people in the building were immediately alarmed.

"Mr. Estevan!"

Seeing the tragic scene of Barton being shot in the head, everyone panicked and quickly called other senior executives of Southern Mining.

The lights were on in the mining headquarters, and everyone knew that Barton had been assassinated.

More than a dozen senior executives of Southern Mining were present, all with their bodyguards.

They looked at Barton, who was dead in bed, with different expressions. Some were surprised, some were afraid, and some were worried.

Alexander looked at them coldly. The top executives of Southern Mining were more rational than the people of the Glory Association and did not panic the first time.

Even though Barton was dead, they were still discussing matters like how to catch the murderer and strengthen defenses.

Alexander sneered. No matter how smart they were, they could not imagine that the murderer was right above their heads.

The gun in his hand fired. It was a gun with an extended magazine.

"Bang!"

After Alexander emptied one magazine, he reloaded another one. Dozens of bullets were emptied in a few seconds.

Before more than a dozen senior executives of Southern Mining could react, they had their heads blown off, leaving a bloody mess.

Alexander chuckled and walked out through the window.

The entire southern Mining was in chaos. All the senior executives were assassinated, and the others were helpless.

Alexander notified Alyssa again, and then he created chaos at the headquarters himself.

More than a dozen houses were set on fire, power supply facilities were destroyed, and the entire headquarters was plunged into darkness.

Gunshots rang out everywhere, accompanied by shrill screams.

There were even huge explosions at gas supply facilities and oil depots, sending up fiery red mushroom clouds that lit up the night sky.

The loud rumbling noises that came one after another alarmed everyone in Mandale Town.

Alexander alone disrupted the entire southern Mining.

The more than 200 members of Southern Mining were stunned. They couldn't find the enemy, nor did they know how many enemies there were.

Dozens of people were killed or injured by Alexander, and the rest were unable to organize an effective defense.

They could only watch as their companions fell one by one with screams and explosions and fires everywhere. It was a doomsday scene.

"It's all over!"

Everyone in Southern Mining felt the good times were over.

When Alyssa and the Axe arrived aggressively, all the people in the southern Mining immediately laid down their weapons and surrendered.

They even felt more at ease, as their lives were no longer in danger after surrendering to the Axe.

For these grassroots members, it didn't matter whether they worked for Southern Mining or the Axe as long as they could make money.

At this moment, the sky was beginning to get brighter.

Alexander made a fuss all night and temporarily stopped his actions.

Mafia and other forces must have been on guard. The Axe had been busy all night, although they were excited, they were exhausted.

The Axe's current priority was to quickly absorb the manpower and wealth of the two major forces.

After dawn, Mandale Town was in an uproar!

No one could have imagined that the Axe was so powerful. In just one night, the Glory Association and Southern Mining were swallowed up by the Axe.

It was unbelievable!

Although the Axe had just gathered the remaining members of the two major forces, their combat effectiveness might not be very high, but their strength had indeed increased dramatically.

Moreover, in Paxton's eyes, the Axe became threatening and even terrifying.

Even the Mafia couldn't swallow up the two major forces overnight. Paxton temporarily retreated, and other forces immediately withdrew from the alliance against the Axe.

Paxton wanted to figure out what happened last night.

Why did the Axe suddenly become so powerful? And he heard that they didn't lose a single person, which was hard to understand.

"Alyssa, that bitch, hides her power so well!"

Paxton talked to himself, his face looking very gloomy.
