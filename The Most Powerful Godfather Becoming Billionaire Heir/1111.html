Chapter 1111 The Motorcycle Hitman

"Wait! Did you know they were there the whole time? Why didn't you tell me earlier? You did that on purpose, didn't you?"

Grace blushed.

Alexander smirked, confident.

"I'll leave first. You don't need to see me off. If Howard still thinks he can get to me, I won't go easy on him.

"With the chaos coming, keeping someone like him in the Lawson family is a disaster."

Grace paused for a moment and then nodded firmly. "Okay!"

She threw herself back into Alexander's arms, holding on tightly, reluctant to let him go. She looked like a lovesick girl, unwilling to part from him.

Alexander felt a deep sense of satisfaction as he saw how completely she had fallen under his control.

After sharing another brief moment, he finally opened the car door and stepped out.

Meanwhile, Howard had spotted Alexander, and his face turned dark and fury.

"It's him! Alexander! Damn it. I'll kill you..."

Though Grace didn't get out of the car, she watched Alexander leave, her face flushed, eyes sparkling with the unmistakable look of a woman who'd just been well-loved.

One of the bodyguards exclaimed, "Mr. Lawson, that guy took Ms. Lawson away! What should we do now?"

Howard gritted his teeth, his voice dripping with venom.

"That slut's face is all blush, her eyes are all teary, and she definitely just got satisfied. That damn whore!

"I've been pursing her for years, and now she dares to sleep with another man. Alan Peterson, gather the guys and follow him. I want him dead."

The bodyguard quickly nodded. "Yes, Mr. Lawson. We'll take care of it."

Alexander walked ahead, unfazed, with a cold smirk as Howard and his men followed from a distance.

Crash!

Suddenly, deafening motorcycle engines roared from behind.

Motorcycles came speeding up, quickly closing in on Alexander.

Alexander narrowed his eyes and thought Howard really didn't know when to quit.

Whoosh!

The lead motorcycle zoomed forward, closing the gap behind Alexander.

The rider swung a metal pipe high in the air and brought it down hard toward Alexander's head.

Just as Alexander turned around, the pipe was nearly upon him, just inches away from his head.

"Dare to offend Mr. Lawson? Die!"

The rider sneered, putting all his strength into the strike, intent on smashing Alexander's skull.

Whoosh!

The pipe sliced through the air, but it hit nothing. Alexander had already vanished.

The rider's smug grin froze.

He couldn't believe it. He had only struck air. He wondered where that guy went.

In an instant, Alexander appeared behind him like a ghost. He grabbed the rider by the collar.

At this moment, the motorcycle was speeding forward, and the rider was violently yanked off the bike by Alexander, crashing hard to the ground.

Bang!

The motorcycle crashed straight into a large tree by the roadside.

The rider's helmet was shattered, with fragments sticking to his head and face, digging deep into his flesh, causing him to scream in agony.

"Everyone, get him! Kill him!"

Amidst the roaring of the motorcycles, the tattooed, burly man at the front was furious.

Wielding a machete, he seemed to be commanding an army as he charged toward Alexander.

But Alexander remained calm and collected, standing still in the middle of the road.

The Force surged within him, heightening his vision, hearing, and reflexes beyond human limits.

Six more motorcycles sped in from all directions, the riders ruthlessly attacking Alexander.

Iron rods and machetes were swung at him with full force.

It seemed Alexander was about to be beaten to a pulp, blood splattering everywhere, and Howard watched eagerly, cheering with excitement.

Boom!

Crash!

The sounds of impact rang out one after another.

Then, to Howard's shock, a few of the motorcycles seemed to hit an invisible wall, flying off like waves crashing against rocks, tires up in the air.

Including the tattooed man, several riders were thrown to the ground, bloodied and motionless. He was not sure if they were alive or dead.

Alexander smiled and clapped his hands and then turned his gaze toward Howard's hiding spot.

Howard was completely stunned, trembling uncontrollably as if he were in a nightmare.
