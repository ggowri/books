Chapter 665 We Admit Defeat

Alexander didn't care at all.

"Come on, all of you, together, and stop dawdling!"

After a moment of hesitation, Alyssa warned her men, "Remember, this is just a sparring match. You must not injure Mr. Hawthorne."

They agreed loudly and then excitedly surrounded Alexander.

The people around him were laughing and talking, believing that so many of them could easily catch him.

Despite his great fighting skills, he definitely couldn't handle dozens of people rushing towards him.

"We're ready. Be careful!" Alyssa reminded Alexander again.

"Let's get started. Don't waste any more time."

Alexander beckoned at them. It was too easy to deal with them.

Realizing that he didn't take them seriously, they got angry.

Several strong men roared and charged at Alexander, relying on their numbers.

Others also darted towards him. Suddenly, they closed in on him, all yelling aggressively.

"You are too slow!" Alexander smiled contemptuously. He dodged and then disappeared from the spot.

Those men missed their target and glanced around in shock, searching for him.

They had no idea how he had passed through the crowd and got behind everyone.

"I-Is this still the speed of a human? He's too fast. I can't see it clearly at all!"

Alyssa couldn't help but curse.

Her competitive spirit was fully aroused, and she soon ordered her men to surround Alexander.

"Too slow! Move faster. Faster!"

Alexander moved nimbly in and out of the crowd. No one could even touch him, let alone catch him.

There were apparently dozens of people trying to capture him, but it turned out to be a farce. No matter how hard they tried, running and sweating, they could not reach him.​

Throughout, Alexander did not make a move. He was relaxed and at ease, surrounded by dozens of people, as if he were playing a game.​

Sometimes, he patted some of them on the back of the head, shoulders, or vest, scaring them to jump.

If it was an actual combat, it meant they had been killed by Alexander.

They were in chaos, running and panting, but they couldn't touch him at all.

Alyssa finally couldn't help it and waved her hands with a bitter smile.

"Stop! Everyone, stop. We can't catch up. Forget it. We admit defeat!"

Everyone stopped, propped their knees on their hands, and bent over, gasping for breath, full of admiration for him.​

Despite knowing him as a formidable figure who had killed the leaders of two major forces in one night, they had never witnessed his strength personally.

Today, they personally experienced how powerful he was and were suddenly even more in awe of him.

If it had been a real fight just now, they would have been wiped out long ago.

"Well, the friendly match is over. Let's have breakfast."

Alice pulled Alexander and then walked into the villa with Alyssa.

She just watched the whole game and laughed heartily.

Meanwhile, Xavier came downstairs, and the four of them chatted while eating breakfast.

Alyssa said to Alexander, "A new batch of ore will arrive today. Its quality is good. Maybe there will be top-grade gems. How about going there and taking a look?"

He beamed with joy. He just came here for orange and purple top-grade gems.

"Okay, I'll definitely go!" Alexander agreed immediately.

After breakfast, Alyssa drove to an ore warehouse of the Axe with Alexander, Xavier, and Alice.

In fact, the warehouse was an open-air storage area of more than 2.5 acres.

It was surrounded by concrete walls more than ten feet high, with guard towers at intervals and electric fences hanging from the walls.​

Coupled with the 24-hour surveillance cameras and dozens of heavily armed guards, the warehouse was unbreakable.

Dozens of heavy trucks carrying gemstone ores of all shapes and sizes were being unloaded on the grounds, stirring up a cloud of dust.

Alexander was quite surprised to see so many ores for the first time.

His All-seeing Eyes swept over and immediately found glowing ores everywhere.

Many ores contain gems, even high-grade ones.

Just as Alyssa had said, the batch of new ores from the mine was indeed of high quality.
