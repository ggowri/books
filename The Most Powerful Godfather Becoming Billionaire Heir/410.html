Chapter 410 Coward

"How dare you?"

A roar suddenly came from outside.

Just as Daniel was surprised, there was a loud bang.

The thick door of the room flew off the frame and hit the wall so hard that it shook the entire room.

"Who's that?"

Daniel jumped in shock as he turned to the door.

His henchmen were unexpectedly dumped in and left on the ground like trash, their eyes closed as they remained motionless.

"Who are you?"

Daniel cursed, and he never feared them.

Alexander held Ashley's hand and slowly walked in.

The temperature in the room seemed to drop several degrees, even as the air seemed to freeze.

"Damn it!"

Alexander's tone was as cold as ice.

Daniel couldn't control his shivering. Alexander's eyes seemed like swords, and Daniel looked very panicked all of a sudden.

"Asshole, what do you want? I'm the supervisor here, and this is my place."

"Fuck off!"

Alexander kicked Daniel in the stomach.

Daniel covered his stomach and fell to the ground as he shivered.

He tried to resist, but he was so weak from the pain that it was hard for him to even move his fingers.

"Ava!"

Ashley pounced into Ava's arms and hugged her as tightly as she could with her little hands.

Ava had tears in her eyes and hugged Ashley tightly.

"Are you okay?"

Alexander stepped right over to Daniel and looked at Ava tenderly.

Ava shook her head gratefully.

"I'm fine. Thank goodness you got here in time."

Then, Ava looked at Daniel indignantly. Not only did he try to kidnap Ashley, but he tried to rape me. Damn that asshole!"

"Okay, I'll handle this. Take Ashley and wait for me outside. Be good."

Alexander patted Ava on the shoulder, and his gaze on Daniel became extra fierce.

Ava knew there were things later that Ashley shouldn't see.

She didn't ask anything and rushed towards the outside with Ashley in her arms.

As Ava got halfway there, she turned and looked worriedly at Alexander again, wanting to say something.

Alexander smiled and waved his hand. "Don't worry. This asshole won't do anything to me."

When Ava and Ashley left, Alexander walked towards Daniel, who was lying on the ground, his eyes were so cold that it was as if he was looking at a dead man.

At this moment, Daniel no longer looked as arrogant and mighty as he did just now.

In a dead silence, there was only Daniel's heavy and panicked gasping.

Alexander was silent, but his expression became increasingly grim.

Those who were familiar with Alexander knew that he was truly furious and that cruel punishment would await Daniel.

Just as Alexander was about to beat him up, there were suddenly noisy footsteps outside the door.

Immediately afterward, Jane and Simon appeared in the doorway.

They could see Daniel sprawled out at Alexander's feet and several other large men falling to the ground in confusion.

Simon immediately looked enraged, not realizing that the result was the complete opposite of what he had imagined.

Seeing Simon appear, Daniel was like seeing a savior, and they rushed to call for help.

"Simon, please save me!"

"What is it to do with me?"

Simon knew something was wrong, and he hurriedly turned and ran away.

"Can you escape?"

Alexander blocked him quickly and mocked, "So you two are behind this. Damn it!"

Jane immediately yelled, "Alexander. What did I screw up? Don't slander me!"

Unfortunately, Alexander was no longer the man who allowed her to insult him at will.

He glared at Jane in disgust and casually waved his hand. Jane felt a huge invisible force crashing over her and fell to the ground as she cried out.

"If you tell me the truth, maybe I can spare your life!"

Alexander's tone was so calm that it was like he was talking to a close friend he hadn't seen for years.

But the more he did so, the more Simon felt an unprecedented fear.

Cold sweat kept coming out from his forehead.

"You've misunderstood me, and I really have nothing to do with this."

Simon was still shirking his responsibility.

Daniel glared at him grudgingly, not realizing that Simon, with his powerful background, was acting like a coward.

"Tell me, why were you doing this?"

Alexander coldly continued to ask.

Though he hadn't done anything yet, his invisible rage continued to intensify, crushing them as if they were mountains.

Daniel broke out in a cold sweat and completely slumped to the ground like a confessing toad.
