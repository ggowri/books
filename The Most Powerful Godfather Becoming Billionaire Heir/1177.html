Chapter 1177 Royal Chef

"Thank you for all your hard work during this time." Alexander patted William's shoulder.

"It's my pleasure working for you," William replied with a beaming smile.

Cloe glanced at the time and said cheerfully, "Boss, there's a famous restaurant on the island that is said to have a Michelin three-star rating. Let's have lunch there."

Alexander turned to look at her. Cloe had done her makeup beautifully today, making her already stunning face even more charming.

She wore a white shirt and a fitted skirt, topped with a knee-length trench coat.

Her voluptuous and sexy figure peeked out, with graceful curves that particularly attracted men's gazes.

William chimed in, "Boss, I heard that restaurant is great. It has a chef who is particularly skilled.

"That place is always crowded. Logan and I went there once, and it took us a long time to get a table."

Although Alexander wasn't very interested in gourmet food, seeing how highly Cloe and William praised the restaurant piqued his interest.

Alexander walked ahead while Cloe gently leaned against him.

William lagged a couple of steps behind, keeping a vigilant eye on their surroundings.

"Boss, you've been so busy lately. Finally, you have time to see me."

Cloe's expression showed a hint of melancholy.

Alexander smiled and pinched her cheek.

"Thank you for taking care of everything for me here. Seeing Treasure Island thrive, I know you have put in a lot of effort.

"I have already asked Ava to prepare to move the Saint Group's headquarters from Loscity to Treasure Island.

"I believe in less than a month, and Treasure Island will truly become our base."

Cloe was excited upon hearing this. "Great! That way, I can see you every day."

Then her expression changed, and she quietly asked with concern, "Boss, will Yinson really cause a global catastrophe?"

Alexander sighed seriously, "You'll see soon enough."

The two then fell silent, and the atmosphere grew heavy.

Cloe couldn't help but link her arm with Alexander's, smiling as she said, "I'm sorry. I shouldn't bring up such unhappy topics. You're rarely here, and I still mention such disheartening things."

Her watery eyes fixed on Alexander, thinking, "He is my first man and will definitely be my last."

Alexander smiled, and the feeling of satisfaction within him was indescribable.

Walking down the commercial street of Treasure Island, Alexander felt nostalgic.

This place, which used to be dirty and chaotic, had now transformed into a bustling marketplace.

Though people came and went, lively and busy, there was not a scrap of paper in sight, and the order was well maintained.

Before long, the three arrived at the popular restaurant.

"Logan had made a reservation. I heard that the chef comes from a lineage of imperial chefs and has inherited culinary skills, which is why he receives such acclaim," Cloe explained to Alexander.

"An imperial chef? Not bad. It seems I have good fortune today to eat what the emperor dined on." Alexander was also looking forward to it.

William swallowed hard and said proudly, "Thank goodness Logan reserved a spot in advance. Otherwise, we wouldn't be able to eat here even with money. There's a limit on the number of guests served daily, and they only accommodate according to the reservation times."

Alexander nodded. "Good, it should be great then."

As the three discussed, they walked into the restaurant.

The restaurant was decorated with a traditional imperial atmosphere, featuring exquisite murals, luxurious lighting, and lavish carpets.

A server at the entrance raised a hand to stop Alexander and said with a smile, "Our restaurant operates on a reservation basis. May I ask how many of you have a reservation?"

"Yes, they are with me," Logan called out from inside.

He stood near the entrance, watching the situation outside. Upon seeing Alexander and the others, he immediately raised his hand with the reservation card.

"Oh, so you are Mr. Ridley's esteemed guests. My apologies. Please come in." The server quickly allowed them through.

"Boss, long time no see." Logan approached with a beaming smile.

Alexander patted his shoulder, offering some compliments.

A young woman dressed in court attire led the way, guiding the four of them inside.

The restaurant was spacious, even featuring green plants, fountains, fish ponds, and more, creating a beautiful and artistic environment.

"Mr. Hawthorne, hello!" Several executives from Treasure Island had been waiting there. As soon as they saw Alexander, they immediately nodded and bowed, smiling flatteringly.
