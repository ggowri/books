Chapter 990 This Is a Crime

Asher sneered arrogantly, "That coward Alexander didn't even dare to show up. What a useless loser!

"Even if he's too afraid to come, I'll still dig up this grave and make sure Alexander cries over it.

"Start digging!"

With an excited wave of his hand, Asher commanded his men to begin.

Immediately, a group of them raised their shovels, ready to dig.

"Stop! You can't do this! The deceased have found peace. How can you desecrate their grave? This is an act that will send you to hell!"

Lily cried out in desperation, nearly fainting on the spot.

"Heh..."

Asher showed a sarcastic smile on his face.

"Who let Alexander go against me? If Alexander wants me to stop, he better come here and bow in front of everyone to beg me."

"Dream on!"

Ava spat on the ground in front of Asher, her eyes blazing with defiance.

Enraged, Asher shouted, "Dig! Dig it up and feed the bones to the dogs!"

"Anyone who dares stop us will die!"

Ava glared at him, her eyes filled with an icy determination.

"Go ahead and try. Let's see how you kill me."

One of the men stepped forward, raising his shovel high, ready to strike the ground.

"Ah!"

The man suddenly screamed and fell heavily to the ground.

A bullet hole had pierced his forehead, and blood and brain matter splattered everywhere, resembling a smashed watermelon.

"Sniper! There's a sniper! Take cover!"

Asher and Axel immediately ducked behind their bodyguards for protection.

The rest of their men scattered in panic. Some of them dove behind tombstones, others into bushes, and a few simply fled.

"Damn it! How dare you resort to assassins!"

Asher roared in fury, drawing his pistol and pointing it at Ava.

"Stop! Who dares fire a shot?"

A loud, commanding voice rang out as Oliver arrived, leading dozens of police officers to the scene.

Seeing Oliver, Asher's face lit up with relief.

"Mr. Benton, you're just in time! Arrest these people immediately! They've set up a sniper to kill us. Look, here's the evidence!"

He pointed at the lifeless body on the ground.

Oliver glanced coldly at Asher.

He said, "As the police commissioner of Loscity, I guarantee that this city will remain safe and secure. Anyone breaking the law will be held accountable."

Asher nodded in agreement, satisfied. "Exactly, Mr. Benton! Whoever breaks the law should be arrested!"

Lily and Ava's group paled, thinking the police commissioner was siding with Asher.

It seemed like not only would the grave be desecrated, but they might also end up arrested.

With a commanding gesture, Oliver commanded, "Surround them!" Dozens of police officers rushed forward and surrounded Asher and his group.

Asher froze in shock before erupting in rage.

He shouted, "Oliver, what do you think you're doing? They hired the sniper to kill my men, and you're targeting us instead?"

Axel, trembling with anger, pointed a finger at Oliver and scolded, "Oliver, do you know who I am? Are you trying to lose your job by offending the Vance family?"

Oliver's expression tightened. As a member of the Vance family, Axel represented one of the empire's most powerful clans, a force Oliver couldn't afford to antagonize.

But recalling Gavin's backing, Oliver summoned his courage. He thought, "Success often comes with risk, and this is a gamble I am willing to take."

Oliver snorted coldly and said, "Mr. Axel Vance, Mr. Asher Vance, I don't yet know which side hired the sniper, and I don't have evidence to arrest anyone.

"But do you think being part of the Vance family gives you the right to desecrate graves and seize other people's burial sites?

"You are committing a crime!"

His righteous declaration stunned everyone present.

Lily and Ann cheered with joy, realizing Oliver was on their side.

Even Ava's expression turned complicated.

Though she was the president of the Saint Group, even she couldn't influence the police commissioner. This was all thanks to Alexander.

Asher was about to lash out when Axel stopped him.

With a sinister grin, Axel pulled out a document and said, "Oliver, here's the contract for our acquisition of West Cemetery, along with the renovation permits.

"We are conducting legal renovations, and this grave must be removed. If you obstruct us, you're breaking the law, and your position as police commissioner will end here!"
