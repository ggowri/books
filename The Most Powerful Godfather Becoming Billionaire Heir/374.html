Chapter 374 Get Out

"Oh, Ms. Blackburn, you're infatuated with that worthless leech Alexander. Aren't you afraid I'll just have him taken out?

Ava shyly grabbed Alexander's wandering hand, her cheeks flushing.

Though she longed for Alexander, this was still the office, and she didn't want to become the subject of office gossip if anyone saw them.

But Alexander was unfazed; he pressed her down onto the chair.

He was incredibly strong; no wonder he always made her feel like she was in heaven.
