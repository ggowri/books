Chapter 495 Kill Them All

Alexander glared coldly at the bald man dressed in black. His voice was as icy as steel. "You dared to lay a finger on my daughter?"

In the blink of an eye, Alexander was already standing before the man.

The bald man's face twisted in terror. He was a lightning-type metahuman, one of the fastest—second only to speed types.

But in Alexander's presence, he felt as helpless as a child learning to walk.

Smack!

Alexander raised his hand and slapped him hard across the face.

The force sent the man flying backward, his face grotesquely contorted under the impact. Even his eyeballs seemed to bulge from the blow.

Before he could even hit the ground, blood spurted from his mouth in thick streams.

Finally, he crashed into a wall several meters away, motionless, like a broken doll.

The Red Umbrella Group members gasped in horror, their confidence shattered. The remaining two metahumans immediately launched an attack on Alexander.

One wielded fire, the other ice—two opposing forces crashing toward Alexander from either side.

Swish!

A dazzling white blade flashed through the air.

The two level-one metahumans froze in place.

Just as confusion flickered across their faces, blood erupted from deep cuts at their waists, and their bodies fell in two heavy halves onto the floor.

Alexander waved a hand, his voice chilling. "Kill them all. Leave no one alive."

It was only then that Marvin and Logan snapped to attention. With a fierce nod, they charged forward alongside members of the Ultimate Alliance.

The Red Umbrella Group's strongest fighters had already been eliminated by Alexander, leaving the remaining members in utter panic.

They turned to flee, but both ends of the corridor were already blocked by the Ultimate Alliance, leaving no escape.

The floor was painted with the blood of Red Umbrella's agents, their bodies strewn across the ground.

Every plea for mercy was met with swift, unrelenting execution.

In a matter of moments, the corridor had transformed into a hellish scene.

Alexander's expression was like stone as he turned to Betty. "What happened here? Where's Ashley?"

Betty jolted, snapping out of her daze.

"Hurry! Get to the operating room and save Ashley! The doctors here have been bought off by the Red Umbrella Group—they're planning to dissect her to obtain first-hand research data.

"It's my fault...I should have foreseen their scheme. When they said she needed emergency treatment, I let them take her into surgery. But then I got the intel, and Red Umbrella's people showed up as well!"

"Damn it!"

Without a second thought, Alexander bolted toward the operating room. His murderous intent filled the hallway, sending shivers down the spine of anyone in his path.

The metal doors to the operating room were shut, glowing with the red "In Surgery" light.

Alexander could hear the doctors' conversation inside, and his All-seeing Eyes revealed the scene.

Ashley lay on the surgical table, her little face ghostly pale, eyes closed, unmoving.

"Paul, what are you doing? The patient can still be saved, but you're preparing for a dissection. This is murder!" protested a middle-aged doctor with a square face.

Paul smirked, utterly dismissive.

"Yohan, do you have any idea who's behind this request for data? The Red Umbrella Group. Surely, you understand what that means.

"And besides, when we're done, you'll be paid five million, along with a host of other benefits."

But Yohan remained resolute in his refusal.

"No way! I won't allow you to dissect a living patient. No amount of money can convince me otherwise.

"We're doctors, not murderers! I won't allow this dissection!"

Paul laughed mockingly.

"This little girl's body contains research data that the Red Umbrella Group desperately needs. Whoever obtains first-hand information will be set for life.

"Yohan, this is the opportunity of a lifetime! I thought I'd bring you in on this because of our past, and you should be grateful. But instead, you're opposing me?"

Yohan bristled with indignation.

"The patient is still alive! This isn't research; it's murder. I won't allow it. If you insist, I'll report this."

Paul sneered.

"Report it? Are you out of your mind? This is the Red Umbrella Group we're talking about. You think reporting it will help? Her life is worth less than a single strand of their hair."
