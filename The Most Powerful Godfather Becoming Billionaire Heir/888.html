Chapter 888 The Assassin's Legacy

"Die!"

Flame roared furiously, unleashing his full strength.

Shadow felt a massive force hit his hand instantly.

Flame swung his katana down again with brutal force.

The blade flashed past as Shadow barely dodged it, but a chunk of flesh was scraped off his right hand holding the dagger.

His quick reflexes saved him; otherwise, he would have lost his hand entirely.

Shadow quickly retreated, avoiding Flame's next three strikes.

Against such a master swordsman, Shadow struggled to defend himself with just a small dagger.

"Flame! How are you still alive? You belong in hell!" Shadow shouted, his eyes blazing with fury.

Both he and Flame came from the same secretive family of assassins. They had different legacies but became equally elusive killers.

One day, Flame betrayed the family for money, drawing powerful enemies who wiped out the clan.

Shadow was the sole survivor, and from then on, they became bitter enemies.

Flame joined the Mafia and became their top-ranked hitman.

Shadow, meanwhile, became Alexander's father's personal bodyguard.

The two glared at each other, both slipping into assassination mode.

The tension was suffocating. A single mistake could mean being torn apart the next second.

"Heh... Shadow, you're the last one who slipped through the cracks. I won't let you go. I'll kill you for sure!" Flame said, his grin turning wild.

Flame's expression grew increasingly manic.

He stuck out his tongue, licking the blood off his katana, and howled madly, "I love the taste of blood—it's delicious... Soon, I'll carve out your heart, slice it up for sashimi, and savor it slowly!"

Watching Flame lose himself further, a woman in the shadows of the trees frowned and said,

"Mr. Welsh, Flame's losing it again... If he keeps going, killing and eating people, he might slaughter everyone around him."

Locke observed calmly, a contemptuous smirk on his face.

"Let him lose it. A lunatic is still just a lunatic. They may not fear death, but they aren't as clever as sane people."

Meanwhile, Marvin's palms were drenched with sweat as he muttered to himself, "Boss, when are you coming in?"

He knew he and Shadow were at a disadvantage. If it came to an all-out fight, they might not stand a chance against these three.

"Kill!"

Flame roared wildly, spinning his dual katanas into a terrifying wheel of blades as he charged at Shadow.

"Hmph!"

Instead of retreating, Shadow lunged forward, firing his submachine gun at Flame until the clip was empty.

Flame moved like a nimble viper, evading every bullet.

Shadow hurled the empty gun at Flame. With a flurry of metallic clangs, Flame sliced it into pieces.

The two collided violently, and Shadow's figure disappeared into the storm of spinning blades.

Blood splattered everywhere, accompanied by cries of pain.

They attacked each other relentlessly, their movements so fast that the darkness seemed to hold a dozen figures battling at once.

Crack!

One of Flame's katanas snapped.

He hurled the broken blade at Shadow and, gripping his remaining katana with both hands, lunged at lightning speed.

Shadow's disadvantage lay in his single dagger. After deflecting the broken blade, he couldn't evade in time.

Flame's katana stabbed into his shoulder, the tip piercing through to his back.

The impact pinned Shadow to a tree, excruciating pain radiating from his shoulder through his entire body.

Crimson blood gushed out like a fountain.

Shadow screamed in agony, feeling as though the blade had torn his body apart.

" My blade is poisoned—you're as good as dead!" Flame taunted gleefully.

Flame had the upper hand and reveled in his dominance.

"You're the last one who slipped away, and today, you're finally in my grasp," he sneered.

Shadow suddenly lunged forward, plunging his dagger into Flame's abdomen.

Amid Flame's scream, Shadow retreated into the forest shadows, still impaled by the katana in his shoulder.

"Damn it! You're not getting away!"

Blood poured from Flame's stomach, but the dagger hadn't gone deep. His injuries were far less severe than Shadow's.

Flame picked up the broken blade from the ground and quickly gave chase.

The two disappeared into the darkness, one after the other, as Flame's furious roars echoed through the forest.
