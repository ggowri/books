Chapter 447 The Pegasus Club

Alexander, however, remained calm, unaffected by the high temperature.

Another day and night passed. Under the influence of Force Burning, the hilt and the gem finally fused perfectly together.

The hilt radiated a gold and silver light, with Esoteric Patterns emerging and shining, very cool.

Alexander exhaled.

The first-level Lightsaber was finally crafted. This oval hilt was, in fact, a magical Lightsaber.

Alexander immediately wanted to test whether the power of the Lightsaber was as formidable as in sci-fi movies.

With a light flick of his hand, a white beam suddenly emerged from the hilt. Silver and gold lights intertwined, extremely gorgeous.

In his excitement, Alexander swung his hand casually.

The tool cabinet opposite, like a frail blade of grass, was cut in half by a single strike.

Sawdust flew everywhere. Whether it was a hammer, or a wrench, or even a jack, everything the Lightsaber touched was severed.

Moreover, a deep sword mark appeared on the wall, terrifyingly profound.

"Cool!"

Alexander was very satisfied. Although it was a first-level Lightsaber, it did not disappoint him.

With this Lightsaber, Alexander was confident in withstanding modern heavy weapon fire.

Even if a shell flew over, he could instantly cut it in half.

Ordinary light armored vehicles would be sliced open by the Lightsaber.

If facing a main battle tank, Alexander estimated he would need to upgrade the Lightsaber to level two.

But upgrading to a second-level Lightsaber required a large number of top-grade gems to enhance the energy core. Although Alexander now had funds and manpower, it would also take time to collect top-grade gems.

Top-grade gems were not something he could buy on a whim. He might secure some quickly if going to gem mines.

As Alexander contemplated upgrading the Lightsaber, he contentedly prepared to leave the basement.

He checked the time. It was now the evening of the third day of his seclusion.

Alexander eagerly opened the door and immediately saw Ava, Alice, Betty, and Rosalia. They all looked exhausted, their faces etched with deep worry and anxiety.

"What's wrong? What happened?" Alexander asked immediately.

Ava said with guilt, "Ashley! Ashley was kidnapped by the Red Umbrella Group... They demand we hand over the magic powder formula and want you to exchange hostages personally."

"What! Where was Charlize? What was she doing?"

Alexander was shocked, and his anger flared.

Alice quickly explained, "Charlize was severely injured by Kyle from the Red Umbrella Group and is being treated in the hospital. Kyle is a metahuman cultivated by the Red Umbrella Group, possessing terrifying fire power."

A murderous intent burst in Alexander's eyes.

"Where is Kyle?"

Alice said without hesitation, "Pegasus Club!"

Without a word, Alexander strode out, with Ava and the others following closely behind.

Alexander ordered, "You all wait outside later. Don't let anyone escape."

Soon, dozens of black cars streamed out, speeding towards the Pegasus Club.

The Pegasus Club was one of the most upscale clubs in Loscity, with a strong background.

However, few people knew that the Pegasus Club was backed by the Red Umbrella Group.

Tonight, Kyle was entertaining a group of shady friends at the Pegasus Club.

"The Red Umbrella Group dares to kidnap my daughter. They're courting death!"

Alexander could no longer contain himself.

It was already nine in the evening, the prime time for nightlife.

The Pegasus Club was ablaze with lights, with luxury cars filling the entrance.

Rolls-Royces, Bentleys, Ferraris, and other luxury cars seemed like the cheapest low-end goods here.

"Stop! Who are you?"

As Alexander stepped onto the stairs of the Pegasus Club, he was blocked by several burly men.

They were strong and ugly, their faces twisted with a murderous aura, clearly men who were used to killing. They were all elites of the Red Umbrella Group.

"Get lost or die!"

Alexander didn't bother wasting time with these minions and simply waved his hand.

A terrifying Force erupted. Those burly men flew backward more than ten feet as if hit by a speeding tank.

Each had a distinct handprint on their face, swollen and red, strikingly conspicuous.
