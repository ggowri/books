Chapter 574 The Escape

It turned out that Diego had transparent threads wrapped around his fingers, connected to the flying knife handles, allowing him to control them and make them spin at will.

The knives flew back even faster than before, and Alexander barely dodged them in time.

Alexander was taken aback. Diego was extremely skilled, and his attacks were tricky and hard to predict.

Diego was even more shocked. He had launched a full-on lethal attack, yet hadn't managed to touch Alexander at all.

"Damn it!"

Diego swung his hands, launching several more knives.

In an instant, over a dozen knives were flying around Alexander, aiming to strike.

"Clang!"

Alexander casually drew his lightsaber and swung it.

A white blade flashed through the air, producing a terrifying sound like thunder and wind roaring together.

Two knives split cleanly in half and dropped to the ground.

"Clang! Clang!"

Almost instantly, more knives shattered in quick succession, sliced by the lightsaber and falling to the floor.

In the blink of an eye, Diego and Alexander had exchanged several rounds, and more than half of Diego's knives lay destroyed.

"This is bad!"

Diego sensed intense danger.

In that split second, Alexander let out a piercing, resonant cry.

He stomped hard, shattering the marble floor beneath him.

From the point of impact, web-like cracks spread, and a powerful, invisible shockwave surged out.

The entire room trembled. Whether Diego, Barry or the men from The Kaylon, staggered, losing their footing.

Alexander, unaffected, swung his sword toward Diego.

"Mr. Morris, run!"

Diego retreated instead of advancing, twisting his body to block the blade with two of his knives.

"Clang!"

The knives were sliced through, and the blade grazed Diego's waist.

Diego's upper body lifted off as he attempted one last move, reaching fiercely for Alexander's face.

His ten fingernails were blackened, clearly laced with poison.

But Alexander was too quick, dodging like lightning. Diego couldn't even touch him.

Diego's body, severed in two, fell heavily to the ground.

Blood flowed freely across the floor, filling the air with a sharp metallic scent.

Everyone's faces turned pale; if they had faced Alexander, they would have fallen even faster than Diego.

"Kill him!"

Barry finally snapped out of his shock. The battle between Alexander and Diego had only lasted a moment, leaving no chance for others to intervene.

As a level-three metahuman with speed abilities, Barry's fist had already reached Alexander's face.

Just as everyone thought Alexander would be hit, he flashed a sly grin.

With a sudden burst of Force, Force Confinement activated!

This strike posed a real threat, but only because Alexander hadn't been using much of his power.

With Force Confinement active, the entire world around Alexander slowed down.

Even Barry's fist moved at a snail's pace, inching closer little by little.

At this rate, it would take half a day just to reach Alexander.

Barry's fierce expression was crystal clear, yet Alexander's movements remained almost unaffected.

As everyone looked on in a daze, Alexander and Barry passed each other.

Barry let out a scream, blood spraying as he was flung backward, rolling out of the room.

"After him!"

"Don't let him get away!"

The crowd rushed out of the room.

Alexander quickly followed.

Joyce had already broken under his pressure and confessed everything. Alexander now understood the skills of Diego and Barry, and he saw why Felix would want them as allies.

Alexander wondered how many more powerful fighters like them the Morris family had; the Morris family was clearly no ordinary force.

He couldn't let Barry escape back to Felix. Letting them get away would spell trouble down the road.

"Vroom! Vroom!"

The underground parking lot echoed with the powerful roar of an engine as a silver sports car shot out and quickly disappeared into the depths of the dark night.

"Damn it! They got away."

Cole growled, swinging his fist angrily.

Just earlier, Barry had sprinted toward the underground parking lot. Felix, quick on his feet, had already stolen a car, filled it up, and was waiting.

When he saw Diego staggering over, Felix realized things had gone wrong.

Without hesitation, Felix started the car, taking Barry with him as they sped away into the night.
