Chapter 1089 You Can't Escape

The room was in a mess, and Alvin and Maxine were gone.

"It must be Silver Shield Security."

A murderous look flashed across Alexander's eyes, and he took Emma straight to the headquarters of Silver Shield Security.

In the car, Alexander called Quentin and used the military's information network to quickly locate Jordan.

Jordan was in an upscale community in the western suburbs of Newbrook. He had a luxurious villa covering an area of ten thousand square feet.

The villa had not only a garden and a swimming pool but also extremely strict security.

Security guards patrolled the gate around the clock, and there were the most advanced surveillance cameras, facial recognition, and laser scanning.

With such strict security measures, it would take a lot of effort to break in, let alone sneak in.

But for Alexander, it was a piece of cake.

It was late at night. Alexander parked the car in a secluded grove and told Emma to stay in the car.

"Be careful!"

Emma hugged Alexander tightly, reluctant to let go.

Alexander smiled and touched her breasts, opening the car door and disappearing into the night like a ghost.

Emma quickly closed the car door and turned off the lights, waiting quietly while praying.

In the deep darkness, Alexander dodged the security guards and the infrared surveillance cameras like a gust of wind and sneaked into the villa without being noticed.

He sensed Jordan's presence on the second floor.

Alexander went upstairs quietly and saw through the wall with his X-ray vision. Jordan was sleeping like a log in a luxuriously decorated room.

There were two naked young women lying beside him. Evidently, the three of them had a passionate time and exhausted themselves, so they were sleeping soundly.

Alexander snorted coldly and looked at the door.

He saw a secret infrared alarm device installed on the door. As long as Alexander appeared at the door, the alarm would go off.

Normally, ordinary people were not able to detect this kind of secret alarm device at all.

Alexander was a high-level Warrior, and his perception was far more acute than that of ordinary people.

Before Alexander reached the door, he raised his hand and pointed at the alarming device.

The invisible Force destroyed it like a bullet.

Alexander turned the doorknob.

Crack.

The sturdy lock was snapped off, and the door opened.

"Who is it?"

Jordan woke up suddenly and sat up in fright, looking at Alexander in a daze.

"Where are Alvin and Maxine?"

Alexander appeared in front of Jordan like the wind and looked at him coldly.

When the two women were about to scream, Alexander slapped them on the head like a bolt of lightning.

The two women rolled their eyes and fell down, motionless.

"You. What do you want? I warn you. I have more than 30 bodyguards here. You can't escape."

Jordan threatened Alexander with feigned confidence.

Alexander sneered and grabbed Jordan's throat, tightening his grip.

"Ah! Help. Help..."

Jordan wanted to call for help, but he could hardly make any sound.

Due to the acute lack of oxygen, his face turned ashen, and his limbs were wriggling.

No matter how he struggled, he couldn't get Alexander to release him. Instead, he felt the grip on his neck tightening.

Jordan made gurgling sounds and rolled his eyes. He was about to be strangled to death.

"Plop."

Alexander threw Jordan to the ground like throwing away garbage.

Jordan covered his neck and panted heavily for the hard-earned air.

Alexander looked down at him and asked coldly, "Tell me. Where are Alvin and Maxine?"

The muscles on Jordan's face twitched a few times, and he suddenly reached under the pillow and yelled, "Help! Help!"

Before he could finish shouting, Alexander grabbed his neck again.

"Don't kill me. No. Don't."

Jordan couldn't breathe, and his face quickly turned black. He rolled his eyes and struggled in vain.

"I'll tell you. I'll tell you."

Alexander loosened his grip, and Jordan collapsed to the ground, almost wetting his pants out of fright.

"I don't want to die. Please don't kill me. In fact, I was forced to do this. My father ordered me to do this. I am the chairman in name, and my father is the largest shareholder of Silver Shield Security."

"Alvin and Maxine were sent to my dad. Please. Don't kill me. Don't kill me."

Jordan knelt to Alexander, knowing his life was in Alexander's hands.
