Chapter 92 Rejection

Alexander felt a tightening in his pants, a mix of embarrassment and desire.

He forced a smile and said, "Hold your horses; we've got bigger fish to fry. You don't need to worry about Billy; I'll handle him. Just sit tight and wait for the fallout."

Betty's heart raced at his words, and she nodded before quickly turning to leave.

As she walked away, her hips swayed enticingly, and Alexander couldn't help but feel a surge of heat rise within him.

It wasn't that he didn't want her; he just felt guilty about Ava.

After all, the last time with Betty was just a matter of detoxification.

He pulled out his phone and called Augusta, assigning him the task of dealing with Billy.

After all, Augusta had been introduced to him by Billy, and it was a good opportunity to test the loyalty of Augusta and his crew.

Once he hung up, Augusta's face lit up with excitement.

Marvin, sensing something was up, quickly asked, "Captain, what's going on?"

Augusta grinned, saying, "We finally get a chance to thank the boss! That scumbag Billy has the audacity to betray the boss. Let's show him how to behave."

"Sounds like a plan! I've got nothing better to do," Marvin replied, flashing a wicked grin that revealed his gleaming white teeth.

Just then, Alexander's phone buzzed.

It was Jane calling. He almost hit decline but noticed her message. She wanted to meet to discuss their daughter.

Reluctantly, he agreed to meet her.

Half an hour later, he arrived at the agreed spot, where Jane was already waiting.

She had clearly put in some effort, dressed in a sleek black skirt, her makeup flawless, exuding an alluring scent with every gesture.

"Alexander, Sebastian has put a five-million-dollar bounty on your head. You need to be careful! I don't want anything to happen to Ashley's father. I hope you stay safe," Jane said, her tone unusually concerned.

Jane's shift in demeanor surprised him. After witnessing Alexander's strength multiple times, she genuinely regretted her past actions and was hoping to use Ashley as a bridge to reconnect with him.

"I'm busy. What do you want? Spit it out; I don't have all day," Alexander replied curtly, his patience wearing thin.

Old habits died hard.

Even if Jane had turned a new leaf, Alexander's feelings for her had long since faded. Some things, once lost, could never be reclaimed.

To his surprise, Jane didn't lash out.

Instead, she adopted a soft, almost pleading tone. "Alexander, I'm here to apologize. I was blind to everything before. It was my fault. I shouldn't have cheated, shouldn't have pushed you to divorce, and I definitely shouldn't have walked away from our daughter..."

Tears streamed down her face as she continued, "I was wrong. Please forgive me!

"I really understand now; I'll change. Can't we just be a family again? Ashley needs both of us."

She leaned into him, wrapping her arms around him.

As she sobbed, Alexander felt a wave of conflicting emotions wash over him.

He had anticipated this moment, knowing that once he embraced his identity as the Godfather's illegitimate son and obtained the Ultimate Legacy, Jane would eventually regret her choices.

But he hadn't expected it to come so soon.

"Jane, we can't go back to how things were. If you truly understand your mistakes, then respect my boundaries," Alexander said firmly, pushing her away.

Stunned, Jane stood there, her once-beautiful face shifting between shades of red and white.

"I... I've apologized. Can't you just forgive me?"

It seemed she couldn't grasp the reality of his rejection.

She had humbled herself, offered a heartfelt apology, and made a desperate plea for forgiveness.

Suddenly, an angry shout pierced the air.

"Alexander! I'll tell Ava everything!"

A blue sports car screeched to a halt by the curb. Rebecca rolled down the window and furiously pointed at Alexander.

"Damn it! So you think you're so slick, going out with your ex-wife and hugging her in public? I'm definitely going to tell Ava and expose your hypocritical mask!"

Without waiting for Alexander to respond, Rebecca sped off, leaving him standing there.

The confrontation left Alexander feeling anxious about Ava's possible reaction, darkening his mood considerably.

"Just go. We're never getting back together. If you truly regret your actions, then at least ensure you're a good mother to our daughter. Make sure Ashley doesn't lack for maternal love."

With that, Alexander turned on his heel and walked away.
