Chapter 785 Kill Him

They all knew that the strong were respected in the City of Sorcery. Only Monica was a white wizard before, so everyone acknowledged that she was the city lord and the master of the City of Sorcery.

But now that Rock had also become a white wizard, the relationship between the two had become very delicate.

Of course, if Yvonne hadn't used the amulet as bait today, she wouldn't have allowed the two to fall out openly.

Under Alexander's planning, the distrust and mutual fear between Monica and Rock broke out ahead of time.

Rock's eyes were filled with murderous intent.

He said coldly, "Alexander, you are too arrogant! You are not allowed to sow discord between Ms. Quinn and me. Today, I will kill you in front of everyone!"

Monica pointed at Alexander. "If you kill him, I will share the power and wealth with you!"

Rock became excited at once. He didn't expect Monica to give in so quickly.

Although half of the power couldn't satisfy Rock's ambition, Rock did not want to fight with Monica for the time being.

"Kill him! Kill him!"

Hundreds of wizards roared wildly.

Rock was like a tiger about to start hunting, with murderous intent in his eyes. He locked onto Alexander and was ready to attack at any time.

Alexander smiled contemptuously, "It's still uncertain who will be killed today."

Rock couldn't bear it anymore, thinking, "This guy is too arrogant."

He slammed the Ancient Grimoire hard, and black gas enveloped his body. He pounced on Alexander like a devil from hell.

"Kneel!"

The black gas suddenly expanded and turned into a giant palm that pressed down toward Alexander.

The evil aura surged like a tide, submerging the entire hall. The air around Alexander seemed to solidify.

The attack of the white wizard was indeed powerful. But Alexander stood there motionless as if he was frightened.

Rock grinned grimly. The giant black palm was about to smack Alexander to the ground.

"Master!"

Yvonne screamed in fright. Her pretty face turned pale.

Alexander sneered, "How dare you compete with me!"

A bright light flashed and cut the giant black palm in half instantly.

When Alexander swung the Lightsaber, all the wizards involuntarily stiffened up and broke out in cold sweat. Even the soles of their feet were soaked with sweat.

Alexander just stabbed forward.

Without any tricks, the saber pierced through all the black gas and directly pierced Rock's left arm.

"Ouch!"

A shrill howl sounded. Rock, who had just been arrogant just now, had his left arm cut off by Alexander's saber.

Blood gushed out like a fountain, and Rock's expression was terrible.

He raised his broken arm violently, and his blood splattered onto the Ancient Grimoire.

It was like a drunkard craving for liquor. It absorbed all the blood and immediately burst out with an unprecedented, terrifying aura.

Even Monica felt this terrifying evil aura, and her face turned pale.

A jet of inky black mist rushed out from the Ancient Grimoire and instantly condensed into a giant black ghost head, which opened its bloody mouth and flew towards Alexander.

Such a weird and terrifying scene made the wizards present tremble in fear. Was this the devil from hell?

Alexander swung his saber without hesitation.

Black energy burst out from the ghost head, and the two violent forces collided fiercely, creating a hurricane in the hall.

Everyone staggered and couldn't stand steadily.

Those who were closer flew backward and fell heavily to the ground.

That terrifying black ghost head was actually split in half by Alexander's saber, just like cutting a cake with a knife, effortlessly.

Rock's expression changed drastically. He and the ghost head were one, and he immediately felt that his body was cut in half by a sharp saber, painful.

He turned and wanted to run away.

"Do you want to run? Can you?" sneered Alexander.

The Lightsaber was swung towards Rock's back, and a scorching hot saber light rushed out.

The terrifying energy cut Rock in half in an instant. Even the hard floor was cracked into a bottomless groove, emitting scorching heat.

Alexander unleashed Level 2 Lightsaber's strongest energy, making it indestructible.

The two halves of Rock's body were still twisting on the ground, and black gas appeared at the wound, trying desperately to stick the two halves together.
