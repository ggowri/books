Chapter 220 Destroy Him

Julia looked at Kevin.

"Young man, there are always people better than you. Don't think you are invincible."

Kevin said impatiently, "You're bragging. Hurry up and call the people you invited.

"Humph, there is no one in Loscity who can beat Kevin!"

Angelica chimed in.

Alexander stood out from the crowd.

He said, "Kevin, I will fight you on behalf of Julia."

Aiden looked angry.

Alexander laughed and said, "What? Are you scared?"

Angelica started cursing.

"You bastard! My dad invited you to dinner yesterday and gave you gem. How dare you help Julia? You are so ungrateful and shameless."

Alexander glanced at her and said sarcastically.

"You are the ones who have no shame. Don't you want to take the gem back?

"As long as you beat me, I will return the gem to you and compensate you ten million dollars. How about that?"

Angelica said, "Okay! It's a deal. But I don't think you have so much money."

She looked down on Alexander.

Kevin injured his ankle yesterday, and he originally suspected that Alexander was responsible.

But the victory made him lose his mind. Now he was ambitious and had the arrogance to beat anyone.

Kevin pointed at Alexander arrogantly.

"Stop talking nonsense. I'll teach you a lesson today."

Angelica clapped her hands excitedly. "Yes! Kevin, break both of his legs to help me get revenge."

Kevin said, "Yes, I want him to spend the rest of his life in a wheelchair."

Murderous intent flashed in Kevin's eyes.

Aiden also nodded. "Okay, this is a life-and-death fight. There are no restrictive rules. Everyone is on the ring voluntarily, no matter if they die or get injured."

Alexander didn't want to speak and went straight to Kevin.

Xavier asked worriedly, "Mom, will Alexander win?"

Julia, however, was full of confidence.

She said, "Xavier, you don't know his strength. He's one of Loscity's best fighters."

Xavier stared at Alexander nervously, wanting to say something but stopping himself.

He was not as well-informed as Julia, so he didn't know what Alexander had done.

Julia only knew that Alexander had a strong background, was backed by the president of the Saint Group, and had once defeated Joseph of the Cripples.

Very few people at the scene recognized Alexander.

"Isn't that guy Alexander? The one who's been famous lately."

"Yes, it's him. I heard that he broke Joseph's hand on the spot. Why does he come here?"

"Don't let him hear us. He controls magic powder now, and all the major families and companies have to respect him. He is a big shot in Loscity."

"Nonsense! He can't develop magic powder. Ava must have given him the formula. Without Ava, he is nothing."

Several people were talking with envy and jealousy, as well as the gloating feeling of wanting to see Alexander get beaten up.

Alexander said, "You just said you were going to break my legs?"

Alexander looked at Kevin coldly.

He hit Kevin in the ankle with a stone, yesterday. Alexander didn't use much force, or Kevin would have been badly hurt.

Kevin had a look of disdain.

He said, "Are you scared? Apologize immediately, and I will consider letting you go."

Aiden shouted to Kevin, "Kevin, stop talking nonsense. Destroy him!"

Angelica echoed, "Kevin, don't be soft-hearted. Beat him up!"

Kevin said, "You should not offend my girlfriend! Come on!"

Kevin roared angrily and tried to grab Alexander's throat.

Alexander's expression became even colder when he saw Kevin's move.

Alexander was motionless, as if he was frightened by Kevin. And there was a sudden commotion.

Kevin felt that he had a sure win.

However, the next second, Kevin's confident shot missed the mark.
