Chapter 455 Rescued

Alexander, however, paid no heed and strode towards the manor.

"Halt! Anyone who disobeys the military order will be shot without mercy!"

Amid angry shouts, a dense array of gun barrels aimed at Alexander.

Not only were there these hundreds of soldiers, but Alexander keenly sensed a dozen snipers targeting him.

With a flick of his hand, Alexander's Lightsaber materialized out of thin air.

The resplendent blade light was so intense that the soldiers couldn't look directly at it, all revealing expressions of fear and shock.

Then, a powerful aura erupted from Alexander, unstoppable and unbreakable.

The leading colonel, though armed, turned pale and weak-kneed, retreating several steps.

"Stop. Stop right there! Otherwise... we really... will shoot..."

The colonel stammered, unable to articulate his words clearly.

Alexander ignored him, calmly advancing towards the manor with his Lightsaber in hand.

Even if they fired, Alexander was unafraid.

With the Force and Lightsaber, coupled with the swordsmanship from the Ultimate Legacy, even energy beams from laser weapons would be split apart, let alone bullets.

Alexander took a step forward, and the colonel and his soldiers retreated a step.

Alexander advanced three steps, and they retreated three steps as well.

They were many and heavily armed, but Alexander alone was the one controlling the situation.

Everyone was dumbfounded, unable to comprehend this scene.

"Alexander, wait!"

A familiar, desperate cry rang out.

Ava rushed over, tears streaming down her face, and hugged Alexander tightly from behind.

"Please wait. Just wait... Don't take risks. Wait for General Thompson and the others to come out, okay? I... I don't want to lose you..."

Ava's tears soaked Alexander's back.

Alexander's body stiffened, and his cold expression gradually softened.

He could disregard anyone for Ashley, but he couldn't ignore Ava, Lily, and so many lovers and friends.

Finally, Alexander sheathed his Lightsaber.

The atmosphere, which had been tense to the extreme, instantly eased. The colonel and hundreds of soldiers wiped their brows in unison.

They were the ones with the advantage, yet in the face of Alexander, they couldn't help but feel fear, drenched in cold sweat.

It was simply inconceivable!

Soon, Quentin and Theodore emerged with smiles.

Quentin was leading a lovely little girl. Upon seeing Alexander, Ashley ran towards him crying.

"Daddy! Daddy!"

Alexander's eyes moistened as he held Ashley tightly.

Quentin laughed and said, "It's okay. Don't be afraid. Ashley is so cute."

"Thank you!"

Alexander nodded indifferently, not offering them a smile.

Ava and the others also breathed a sigh of relief. Conflicting with the military was clearly not a wise move.

At that moment, the manor's door opened again.

Jane and Belinda, along with more than a dozen members of the Fairfax family, were driven out, looking disheveled and miserable.

Jane's hair was disheveled, and her face was bruised, clearly having been severely disciplined.

She turned and lunged inside, shouting, "Felix, you bastard. Give us back our property!"

But she was kicked down.

"Get out! Get out and stay out!"

Belinda sat on the ground, wailing.

"Jane, we don't even have a place to live now. What are we going to do?"

It turned out that Felix had taken away the last of the Fairfax family's assets. The Fairfax family had become really destitute, the poorest of the poor.

Jane looked at Alexander helplessly.

"Serves you right!" Alexander spat in front of her and got into the car with Ashley.

The vast convoy stretched out, speeding away.

Jane became furious, screaming madly.

"Alexander, stop! Can't you help me for your daughter's sake?"

In the car, Alexander comforted Ashley while using Force Sense to examine her. Fortunately, he found no injuries.

But he still felt a lingering worry. Felix had handed over Ashley so readily. Was there some conspiracy at play?
