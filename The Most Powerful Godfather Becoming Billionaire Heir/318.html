Chapter 318 Jealousy

Alexander thought for a moment and said, "No worries. I'll help you create the illusion that you escaped. Once you return, just say you seduced me and found a chance to flee."

"Alright, I'm one of the highest-ranking Shadow Warriors in the Red Umbrella Group, so they won't reject me if I go back.

"Oh, right. I need to take care of this body."

Rosalia started dragging the body of the muscular man away.

Alexander waved her off and effortlessly threw the body into the yard, where he cast a blast of the Force at it.

White flames sprang up immediately, engulfing the body, and burning intensely in the pouring rain.

In no time, the body turned to ash before their eyes, washed away by the rain until not a trace remained.

"Is this... Hellfire? It can burn even in water and incinerated him so quickly..."

Rosalia was utterly shocked, her eyes wide in disbelief.

What a terrifying fire that was!

Even a high-temperature crematorium would take hours to reduce a body to ash.

Alexander's mystery and power shook her to the core once again.

Early the next morning, several military-plated SUVs pulled up in front of the villa.

"Mr. Thompson's men are here. You be good and wait for me to come back."

Alexander patted Ava on her rounded hip.

Ava held onto him tightly, forcing a smile as she said, "Please, stay safe!"

Seeing the tears welling up in her eyes, Alexander felt bad. He quickly hugged her and gave her a gentle kiss.

"Don't worry, I'll come back safe and sound. I'm counting on you to look after Ashley and Lily."

Ava nodded firmly.

After a tender moment together, Ava saw Alexander off to the car.

Charlize and Augusta stood behind them, their emotions complex.

Last night, Schroder, consumed by grief, committed suicide in the hospital, leaving a letter accusing Alexander of being the killer.

Given that Schroder was a lieutenant general in the Northern Command, Alexander would be in serious trouble if he couldn't cure Quentin's grandson.

Ava watched Alexander's car drive away, feeling lost and vulnerable in a way she never did as the usually decisive and efficient CEO.

Suddenly, Charlize asked, "Ms. Blackburn, if one day he were to leave you, would you be able to take care of yourself?"

Hearing this, Ava snapped. "Charlize, do you know what you're saying?

"Don't think just because you slept with him, you can be his woman. Men as exceptional as him have many women around, but I'm the only one he truly loves!"

Charlize turned red, unable to find words.

She hadn't expected Ava to be so perceptive about her relationship with Alexander and handle it so gracefully.

"Hmph, overestimating yourself!"

With the stance of a victor, Ava held her head high and walked back into the villa.

Charlize rolled her eyes and muttered, "What's there to be smug about? If I'd met him first, he'd love me more..."

"Shut up!"

Augusta snapped at her.

"What's the matter, Captain? You scared me!"

Charlize clutched her chest in annoyance.

With a stern expression, Augusta warned her, "Charlize, I don't mind you being with him, but if you show disrespect to Ms. Blackburn or jeopardize her safety, you'll face consequences."

Charlize went pale, realizing she'd crossed a line this time.

Marvin hurried over to Charlize with a sly grin, saying, "Charlize, maybe you should just be with me. I'll promise to love only you!"

"Ugh! Aren't you the one who goes to the bar every night, hitting on strippers?"

Charlize shot him a scathing look, then solemnly assured Augusta, "Don't worry, Captain. I have my own principles and would never let personal desires endanger Ms. Blackburn's safety. If it came down to it, I'd die before she does."

Augusta's expression softened, and he gave Charlize a firm pat on the shoulder.
