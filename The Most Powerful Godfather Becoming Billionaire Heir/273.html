Chapter 273 Dreaming

"Congratulations, General, for winning such a stunning beauty like Ms. Fairfax!"

"Yes, it's the General's charm, indeed."

Many people chimed in with compliments, while also being extremely polite to the Fairfax family, even trying to please them.

Schroder smirked with a sinister smile.

This kind of woman was only suitable for a fling; his main intention was to use her to deal with Alexander.

Meanwhile, Jane thought she and Schroder had fallen for each other at first sight. In front of the crowd, she nestled in his arms without any hint of embarrassment.

Andrew and the others immediately stood a little taller, suddenly feeling confident again.

The Fairfax family was never considered a major force, repeatedly beaten down and reduced to the status of nobodies.

But now, they believed that Jane's connection with Schroder would bring them success and revive the family.

"Everyone, quiet down!"

Schroder raised his hand, signaling for silence.

The crowd instantly fell silent, shifting their focus to Schroder.

"I've come to Loscity on behalf of the military for two reasons. First, to collaborate with Orion, the future head of the Hansen family, to establish a joint venture..."

Before he could finish, the crowd erupted into an uproar.

Schroder's endorsement meant military support for Orion's claim to head the Hansen family, making him the clear heir.

Orion couldn't hide his excitement, his face beaming with joy.

Meanwhile, Lara was pale, her hands tightly clenched, leaving deep marks on her skin.

Most of the Hansen family members quickly gathered around Orion, surrounding him like stars around the moon, seeing him as the future head.

"The second reason is for the magic powder. I announce that from today, it will be a strategic reserve for the military, and no one is allowed to sell it without my permission!"

Schroder's words struck like thunder, leaving everyone stunned.

The crowd whispered among themselves, some happy, others worried.

Those who disliked Alexander and competed with the Saint Group grinned with joy.

Most people present were agents or distributors of the magic powder, and they all glared at Schroder.

Although Schroder represented the military, he had already harmed their interests.

Cutting off someone's income is like taking their lifeline!

Schroder scanned the room, taking in everyone's expressions, and sneered.

"Don't panic, everyone. I won't cancel the current agents or distributors for the magic powder. You'll be able to get supplies from the military."

Many people breathed a sigh of relief; as long as their profits weren't affected, it didn't matter to them who controlled the source of the magic powder.

Some even began to gloat.

"That punk Alexander is finished. The military will definitely confiscate his formula."

"Exactly, you can't be too arrogant. This is karma catching up with him."

"With the military involved, no matter how powerful Alexander is, he's got no way out now!"

"The Saint Group will have to behave now, instead of using its monopoly on magic powder to push us around."

Amid the chatter, everyone thought Alexander and the Saint Group were done for.

"Hahaha!"

Andrew burst into wild laughter.

"I knew that punk Alexander wouldn't have a good end. Karma has finally caught up with him."

He gritted his teeth as if Alexander owed him millions.

Jane felt a mix of emotions. There was a hint of worry, but mostly she was gloating and feeling pleased.

Andrew nudged Jane, giving her a wild look.

Jane quickly smiled and said, "General, if Alexander hands over the magic powder, could the Fairfax family become the exclusive distributor?"

"Shut up! The Fairfax family isn't qualified to be the exclusive distributor of magic powder."

Schroder cut her off impatiently, glaring at Jane with cold eyes.

"You need to understand your position. I'm here representing the military. Do you want me to abuse my power publicly, giving others an excuse to attack me?"

Jane's face turned pale, and she quickly apologized, "General, I'm sorry. I-I didn't mean to. Please forgive me..."

The previously proud and arrogant Andrew and the rest of the Fairfax family suddenly deflated like burst balloons.

Orion found it secretly amusing. To Schroder, this woman was just a plaything, yet she dared to dream of becoming the exclusive distributor of magic powder. What a delusion!
