Chapter 230 Recommended

Alexander felt his whole body was extremely heavy as if it was filled with cement.

But after slightly using the Force, his mind immediately became extremely clear and his body quickly recovered.

Robert was a very good hypnotist!

Alexander had never suffered such a strange attack before and was almost defeated by the opponent.

Unfortunately, Rober could only make Alexander feel dizzy, which was the limit of Robert's ability.

If he could hypnotize a Warrior, that would be incredible.

Beads of sweat were already oozing out of Robert's forehead. He knew that if he failed, he would suffer severe backlash.

Alexander launched a counterattack, the Force flowed to his eyes, and a strange light flashed in his pupils.

Robert's eyes suddenly went dark and he felt an excruciating pain in his chest.

"Puff!"

He spat out a large mouthful of blood and staggered back several steps.

"Mr. Hawthorne, this is a misunderstanding..."

Robert quickly explained, his eyes full of shock and horror.

"Mr. Hawthorne, I had no ill intentions. I just wanted to assess your strength."

Robert explained in a panic that if his hypnosis ability was cracked and counterattacked by the other party, he would become a lamb to be slaughtered.

"Mr. Milton? What on earth do you want to do?"

Alexander was very unhappy that Robert hypnotized him.

If he couldn't resist the other party's hypnosis, all his secrets would be revealed, and he might even be controlled by the other party.

"Mr. Milton! I am a member of the Brilliant Group and a top client of the trading center. Why are you being rude to me? What is your intention?"

Alexander continued to question angrily.

His eyes were filled with murderous intent. He looked like he was ready to attack at any time.

"I'm sorry, please don't misunderstand me! This is my fault. I didn't explain it clearly. Everything is my fault..."

Robert's face turned pale and he hurried to explain.

"Mr. Hawthorne, you have passed the test of strength. I wonder if you are interested in working at Treasure Island?

"I can recommend you as the security chief of the trading center!"

There was a moment of silence in the hall. Lynne was so surprised that she couldn't speak. She covered her mouth with her hands.

"Oh? You want to recommend me?"

Alexander was delighted, it was a good opportunity.

The security chief of the trading center did not hold a high position but had real power. With this identity, he could find Guzman's hiding place as soon as possible.

Alexander did not agree immediately, but asked, "What will be my salary?"

When Robert heard him ask this, he finally believed Alexander's identity completely.

If an undercover agent wanted to sneak in and cause trouble, he would agree without hesitation to such a good opportunity.

Robert was happy in his heart. If he could recruit a strong man, it would be a credit to him.

"Supervisory level employees can enjoy high salaries and benefits, purchase any product at a 10% discount, and can also use the internal intelligence system."

"And a strong man like you will definitely be able to reach a higher level in the future!"

Robert enthusiastically introduced the salary, and then asked expectantly, "Mr. Hawthorne, what do you think?"

Alexander pretended to hesitate for a while before nodding and agreeing, "Okay, I can try."

Robert was overjoyed.

"Mr. Hawthorne, before officially taking office, the general manager of our trading center has a small assessment. I hope you don't mind..."

As Robert spoke, he observed Alexander's expression.

Seeing that Alexander didn't say anything, Robert was very embarrassed. He was afraid that Alexander would be angry, so he explained again.

"Don't worry, Mr. Hawthorne. With your ability, you are more than qualified to be the security director. The assessment is just a formality."

Alexander snorted lightly, and the atmosphere suddenly became more tense.

A bone-chilling chill emerged, permeating and sweeping across the entire room.

Robert felt as if an iceberg was pressing down on him. His legs trembled uncontrollably and his whole body felt cold.

He was very afraid that Alexander would get angry and attack, which would be a big trouble.

"Well, since it's just a formality, I'll go with you to meet the general manager."

Alexander nodded indifferently, looking dominated.
