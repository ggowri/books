Chapter 842 Body Enhancement

"Ha ha ha!"

Marvin couldn't hold back and burst out laughing.

Alexander suppressed his laughter and whispered, "Well, have you measured it? How many inches?"

Keith's face flushed deeper, too embarrassed to answer.

Alexander managed to stifle his smile and, with a serious face, pressed further, "Don't be shy, just say it. How long?"

"I... I've measured it... only twelve inches... so I don't pass. The minimum is sixteen, and eighteen is considered excellent.

"Boss, I shouldn't go in. I'm sure I won't pass again."

Keith looked utterly defeated, sighing heavily.

Alexander covered his mouth, a slight twitch betraying his amusement.

"Medically speaking, twelve inches isn't extremely short. Don't worry. I have a way to help. You can go ahead with the interview confidently."

With that, he channeled the Force into Keith's body, allowing it to modify him from within.

The human body is made up of bones, muscles, blood vessels, nerves, and internal organs.

The male anatomy is primarily composed of spongy tissue, and the Force could infiltrate it, aiding it to expand to more than double its usual size when stimulated.

The effect was temporary, but Alexander believed it would show results quickly.

This Force in Keith's body would not only increase size but also allow him to control duration.

Whether it was lasting two to three minutes or enduring for dozens, Keith would be in control.

But there was a cost; Keith's body wasn't as strong as Alexander's.

Prolonged use of this Force would gradually damage Keith's body, shortening his lifespan by about ten years.

Alexander didn't mention this side effect for now, planning to tell Keith later.

If he refused, Alexander could withdraw the Force.

Yet Alexander knew no man would give up such an enhancement after experiencing its benefits.

Trading ten years of life for doubled length and time control was a deal most men would accept without hesitation.

Keith not only overcame his physical shortcomings, but the Force within him transformed his entire demeanor.

Previously, Keith's face was pale, his eyes lifeless, with dark circles prominent.

Now, Keith was full of energy, standing tall and looking far more presentable.

Keith didn't know what Alexander had done, but he felt a mysterious warm current moving through his body, filling him with boundless strength.

Especially between his legs, which felt significantly larger than usual.

"Boss, thank you!"

Keith's face was a mix of shock, excitement, and gratitude.

"Boss, you're my savior. I'll be the best undercover agent. I'll face anything, no matter how tough."

He had already been loyal to Alexander; now he was unwaveringly devoted.

Alexander gave a slight smile. "Enough talking, let's go."

Just then, Shirley led them to the entrance of a large hall.

"Over here, fill out these forms first."

She handed three forms to Alexander.

"Wait, not you!"

Shirley snatched the form from Keith's hands.

"You've applied before and failed the physical requirements! Why are you here wasting time again? Do you want me to beat you?

"Get out before I have to use force!"

Shirley pulled out her baton again, glaring fiercely at Keith.

Keith's expression shifted, and he said firmly, "Don't worry, this time I'll pass. I'm over eighteen inches now—definitely excellent."

"Pfft! Do you think you're an earthworm? That thing doesn't just grow to eighteen inches because you say so! Get lost!"

Shirley scoffed, raising her baton.

"I am over eighteen inches now. If you don't believe me, test it!"

Keith stood tall, full of confidence.

Shirley's expression changed as she asked, "Did you get some enhancement surgery? We only hire natural applicants; no surgery allowed."

Alexander spoke up, "Since he claims to be over eighteen inches, why not let him try? It won't waste much time."

A sharp glint flashed in Alexander's eyes, and Shirley, influenced by the Force Control, paused before murmuring, "Alright, come in."
