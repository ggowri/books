Chapter 898 Still Alive

The blood dyed the ground red...

The sky was spinning, his head was buzzing, and his vision was getting darker and darker...

Was he going to die?

Edgar, who was seriously injured and dying, couldn't help but look at his father not far away, only to see a group of high-ranking orc generals surrounding him.

The armor on his father's body had turned completely red, with his blood and more of that of the orcs.

He swung his Lightsabers, and the orcs fell one by one.

"He is the Master Warrior, Marcus. He must be captured alive!"

"Catch him and make him kneel!"

The orc generals fell one by one, but the remaining ones were not afraid at all and rushed forward.

Finally, Marcus was accidentally hugged by a senior orc general behind him.

Instantly, the orc generals rushed forward. They had massive bodies, and compared with them, Marcus was like a child.

But a group of orc generals could not suppress him. After Marcus twisted off the heads of three of the orc generals, he finally ran out of strength.

Many orc generals rushed forward, and they were tightly entangled.

Marcus twisted off the head of an orc general again, but at the same time, a sharp machete was inserted into his body.

"Son, remember this! There are only Warriors who die standing, no warriors who live kneeling!"

Marcus roared at Edgar, then grabbed two senior orc generals and rolled down from the fortress wall.

In a daze, Edgar heard the screams of the orc generals and the groans of his father.

"Dad..."

Two tears of blood flowed from Edgar's eyes.

He reached out and groped on the ground, trying to pick up his Lightsabers.

The Lightsabers was not his, but was left by Jabir. Jabir had just had a child, but orcs killed his wife and child.

Three days ago, he died together with a squad of orcs.

Although he reached the Lightsabers, Edgar didn't have a trace of the Force in his body to activate it.

He gritted his teeth and tried desperately to activate the Lightsabers, but his chest felt extremely painful, and he went into shock.

There were only a few Warriors left in the fortress, and they kept falling.

The orcs suffered heavy losses, but they were still boundless, engulfing the entire fortress...

It had been quite a long time before the unconscious Edgar was kicked awake.

"This guy is still alive!"

An orc found the dying Edgar and kicked him hard again.

Edgar then vomited mouthfuls of blood.

"Stop playing. Just chop off his head. The captain has captured a beautiful woman with a great body, like a doll. If we go over quickly, maybe she is still alive and we can still have some fun."

"Really? You didn't lie to me?"

The orc's eyes widened in surprise.

"I heard her name is Freya. Everyone is lining up. I don't think she will last long. Let's go over there quickly. Maybe we can still play with her once. If we go too late, she will be played to death."

"A Warrior's woman is the most delicate flower. I must enjoy her."

The two orcs were so excited that their saliva was flying.

"Freya... They caught Freya..."

Edgar suddenly raised his head.

What a terrifying pair of eyes they were. His entire eyeball was blood red and the pupils could no longer be seen.

He turned his head suddenly and looked in a specific direction.

Although they were thousands of feet apart and there was a wall blocking the way, his gaze penetrated everything, and he saw everything clearly.

In that familiar room, several orcs were surrounding a naked woman and caressing her... They were laughing obscenely...

There was a long line of orcs waiting outside, each of them drooling at the sight of the woman.

Freya didn't move at all, with no expression on her pretty face. She just let them bully her, with blood flowing from the corners of her mouth.

In an instant, she seemed to sense something, turned her head, and looked in the direction of the fortress wall with a hint of a desperate smile on her face...

"Don't worry. I'll stomp this guy to death, and then we'll get in line."

The orc raised his foot and stomped hard on Edgar.

"Well?!"

The orc was shocked. No matter how hard he tried, he couldn't step on it.

A terrifying burning force burst out from Edgar's red eyes.

"Ah!"

The two orcs screamed and were instantly burned into firemen, burning fiercely like two giant torches.
