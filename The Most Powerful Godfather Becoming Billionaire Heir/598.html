Chapter 598 Test

Moreover, Felix had a good background and was capable.

Megan was used to intrigues and the law of the jungle, so she was much more mature than ordinary women. And she was ambitious.

When Felix saw Megan, a gentle smile immediately appeared on his face. He looked like a considerate and perfect husband.

"Megan, aren't you putting on makeup? I'm just thinking about you. Come here."

Megan sat in Felix's arms obediently, hugged his neck and said coquettishly.

"Felix, our wedding day is coming soon. I'm so happy! You must be nice to me in the future. I will make you happy."

As she spoke, Megan asked for a kiss. She was so cute and beautiful that any man would be captivated by her.

Smiling, Felix reached his hand into her clothes and kneaded her breast vigorously.

He said, "Baby, I am touched that you can marry me. The Red Umbrella Group is facing difficulties now. Your family is my greatest help. And you are so kind. I will love you!"

Felix shed a tear.

"Felix, you are so kind. If the leader knew about this, he would be very pleased."

Megan was still a little naive. Seeing Felix being so affectionate, she was actually moved and about to cry.

Felix kissed and touched Megan, making her gasp, then he said with a smile.

"We're getting married today. You'll have so much fun tonight."

"Oh, stop that!"

Megan shyly lay in Felix's arms.

She said, "For this wedding, all the prominent people in our family will come to attend. This is an unprecedented grand occasion."

"Yes, so I am very grateful to your family."

Felix sighed with a happy expression.

He had been secretly paying attention to the door and heard a faint breathing sound. He sneered inwardly, knowing that Frederick had always arranged people to monitor him.

Megan suddenly said.

"Felix, after we get married, we will stabilize the Red Umbrella Group, and then expand our influence in the Morris family. Maybe we will have a chance to seize the position of the head of the family..."

Before Megan could finish her words, Felix slapped her hard.

He said, "Megan, what did you just say? Mr. Frederick Morris is so good to us. How could you betray him? I am so disappointed.

"If you dare to say such things again, I will report it to Mr. Frederick Morris!"

Felix glared at Megan fiercely.

"Felix, calm down..."

Megan covered her swollen cheek and felt dizzy. The slap was hard.

Felix said, "Humph! Stop. I won't listen to you."

Felix waved his hands angrily and turned away.

"Felix, I don't mean to betray..."

Megan quickly hugged him tightly.

Felix said, "Why are you encouraging me to seize the position of the head of the family? I don't want to be in cahoots with you. Get out of here!"

Felix pushed her away coldly and ruthlessly.

Megan cried out and fell to the ground.

"Stop!"

Frederick's voice was heard outside.

Frederick walked in quickly with several senior members of the Morris family.

Felix quickly said to Megan, "You said nonsense. I will not agree to your betrayal."

Then he pretended to look at Frederick with confusion.

Frederick smiled meaningfully and said.

"Felix, I was right about you. Although you are ambitious, you are kind and grateful. I like young people who have a bright future. Our family is very happy to have you join us."

Megan stood up. It was obvious that the words she had just said were just a test to Felix.

"Felix, I promise you that I will make Alexander beg you for mercy!"

Frederick patted his chest confidently and made the promise.
