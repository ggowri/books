Chapter 1129 You've Been Tricked

Xavier blushed and said sheepishly, "It's nothing serious. In fact, I have some relationship problems."

Alexander couldn't help laughing. "I am surprised that a playboy like you will be troubled by love. Xavier, tell me, who made you so distressed?"

Xavier scratched his head and chuckled, "Alexander, when it comes to being a playboy, you are much better than me. There are so many stunning beauties around you. I will be overjoyed if I can date one of them."

Alexander laughed again. "Stop flattering me and get straight to the point."

After pondering for a while, Xavier said, "I met a woman and have been dating her for a month. I planned to propose to her. H-However, just a few days ago, she suddenly behave like a different person..."

"Alas, do you think this is retribution for me? I have deceived so many girls in the past that God punishes me."

Xavier drank another glass and put it heavily on the table.

"Oh? A month... Suddenly behave like a different person..."

Alexander frowned, thinking that there was something strange.

When Xavier was talking just now, Alexander quietly activated the Force Sense and discovered that there was a dark energy within Xavier, which was actually the same poison that Gavin had suffered from!

Maybe the woman approached Xavier to poison him.

Now, she made it and had to stay away from him.

Alexander remained calm. Instead of exposing it, he wondered who might attack Xavier.

Maybe it was to target the Ultimate Alliance, or maybe it was an enemy that Xavier had offended.

Alexander grinned, filled up Xavier's glass, and said, "Xavier, if you trust me, I will help you solve this matter!"

"Really? Boss, can you help me find Olivia?"

Xavier became excited, completely believing in Alexander.

"Xavier, you haven't even noticed that you've been tricked. If we hadn't met today, you would be in trouble..."

Alexander paused and didn't make it clear.

"Tricked? What do you mean?"

Xavier became anxious.

After he regained his composure, Alexander said firmly, "Someone targeted you and poisoned you. It might be someone else or your beloved woman, Olivia!"

"What? Poisoning? It's impossible..."

Xavier was dumbfounded.

If someone else had made such a statement, he would have already slapped them in the face.

But it was Alexander. Xavier had witnessed his power with his own eyes and had complete faith in his abilities.

"S-She won't hurt me. It must be someone else!"

In a panic, Xavier quivered uncontrollably.

Alexander smiled, "Don't worry. Fortunately, you were poisoned for a short time, and I can detoxicate you completely."

"Otherwise, you will be dead in a while! Think carefully. Besides Olivia, have you offended anyone recently, or are there any traitors around you?"

"Offending others? Traitors?"

Xavier frowned and racked his mind.

"Oh, I see. Half a month ago, I hired a male assistant who had graduated from a prestigious university. He is on good terms with Olivia."

"Now, on reflection, I think there's something wrong with him. He has a bright future. Why did he apply to be my assistant?"

"Although I offered him a decent salary, he could have a better career."

Alexander nodded and said, "Think carefully if there is anything unusual between him and Olivia."

"Yes, you've got the point. They seemed very familiar with each other the moment they met. I even wondered if they had known each other beforehand, but I thought it was an illusion at that time."

While speaking, Xavier sent a copy of the assistant's detailed information to Alexander.

"Tobias White... Twenty-five... From Risun? Wait - he must be connected to Yinson from the Sakra Society. That's it!" Alexander's eyes turned deadly as the pieces clicked together.

Yinson had been picking off Alexander's people one by one, and his cunning brutality was worse than anything Alexander had expected.
