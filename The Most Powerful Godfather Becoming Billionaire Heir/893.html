Chapter 893 Take Her Back

Vivian still said calmly, "Don't worry, Locke is the most experienced and powerful man in Mafia. I believe in his ability."

Although she was nervous, she would not say it out so as not to disrupt the morale of the others.

More than a dozen Mafia members looked nervous and tried to be focused.

If anything happened to Vivian, their whole family would suffer.

Just then, the heavy rain slowly stopped, and the gunfire ceased.

The bald man shouted, "Ms. Hawkins, it seems, it seems that someone is coming..."

Everyone stared ahead with eyes wide open.

Three figures appeared in the distance and ran towards them very quickly.

"Who! Is it Locke?"

The bald man quickly raised his gun and aimed in the direction of the people coming.

Vivian's body trembled, and her face turned pale.

She was Brandon's daughter and was also quite powerful, vaguely seeing who the people were.

"It's Alexander!"

"Impossible!"

All the Mafia members were stunned and terrified.

They watched Alexander coming in front of them with Shadow and Marvin, too shocked even to fire a shot.

Alexander leaped up like a bird and landed in front of Vivian.

Besides the wind, heavy breathing filled the air.

"Don't hurt Ms. Hawkins!"

The bald man raised his gun and was about to attack.

"Hey, why are you so emotional?"

Alexander raised his hand and swung the machete over.

It was like a flash of lightning that streaked through the void and easily pierced into the bald man's chest.

The considerable inertia carried the bald man up into the air and then nailed him to the wall.

The machete was deeply embedded in the concrete wall, which showed how powerful Alexander's casual attack was.

A large pool of blood gushed out, flowing on the ground and spreading...

The smell of blood was pungent in the fresh air after the rain.

There was dead silence. All the Mafia members dared not say a word or take any action. Vivian's face was pale as a ghost.

"If you don't want to die, get out of here!"

Marvin slapped them in the face one after another.

The few guys standing in front of Vivian fell out one after another, spitting blood.

"Piss off!"

Shadow also kicked several people one by one and sent them flying, and they all lay on the ground, gasping for breath.

After a battle that almost killed them, both of them had made significant progress in strength, which was a blessing in disguise.

Especially Marvin, who, at the most dangerous moment, broke through his potential and burst out with a powerful force beyond his own.

Although he couldn't maintain that burning state now, he could do it again since he had done it before.

Marvin's power would be fearsome once he could access his peak state at will.

The ability gained by breaking through one's potential was much more potent than the superpowers gained by taking power elixirs.

However, this time, it was forced by the situation and also a coincidence.

Even if Marvin was put in a death situation again, he might not be able to break out like he did just now.

"You are Brandon's daughter? Come here!"

Alexander hooked his finger at Vivian.

"What...what do you want to do?"

Vivian's expression was extremely dark. Although she had poison needles, a pistol, a dagger, and even dozens of ways to poison people instantly, she did not have any courage to attack Alexander.

Alexander's deep eyes seemed to be able to see through her heart, and the evil smile on the corner of his mouth made her shiver all over.

"Come over!"

Alexander ordered her again.

Vivian walked slowly and carefully in front of Alexander, lowered her head obediently, and didn't even dare to look at Alexander.

"Look me in the eyes!"

Alexander gave a stern order.

Vivian trembled, daring not to resist. She subconsciously raised her head and looked into Alexander's eyes.

In an instant, Vivian's eyes became dull and confused. Influenced by Alexander's Force Control, an anchor of submission was planted in her spiritual world.

Vivian suddenly felt that Alexander was not so scary anymore and even had a little admiration for him.

She panicked and stammered, "What... What did you do to me?"

Alexander ignored her and waved to Marvin. "Take her back!"

Vivian was Brandon's daughter, so she would be a useful pawn.

She excelled in poison and assassination, a cruel beauty, yet Alexander remained unfazed.
