"You planned to use these photos to blackmail Lynn, didn't you?" Alexander asked, his expression cold.

Victor couldn't even scream, only making strangled noises as a mix of saliva and blood dribbled from his mouth.

"If you want to live, you'll confess everything."

As Alexander's grip tightened, Victor's face turned red, his lungs screaming for air.

"I'll... I'll confess... Please, just let me live..."

Victor flailed wildly, struggling like a fish tossed onto dry land.

Thud!

"Answer my questions honestly, and you won't suffer more. Understand?"

"Y-yes, I understand," Victor nodded frantically like a chicken pecking at grain.

Victor hesitated before mumbling, "I... I wasn't planning on leaving her. I wanted to marry her. She's gorgeous, has an amazing body, and she's rich..."

Alexander's fist smashed into Victor's face.

"Ah!"

"I'll ask you one more time. When were you planning on ditching Lynn?"

Alexander's cold gaze pierced through him.

"You said that Lynn was beautiful, had a great body, and was wild. Are you really willing to ditch her?"

Every word of this exchange was captured by Timothy, who recorded it on his phone.

Just then, one of the men brought out a notebook they found in the room.

It was Victor's diary, detailing all the women he'd deceived over the years, complete with their contact information.

"What a scumbag," Alexander muttered, kicking Victor twice more, leaving him lying on the ground, trembling.

"Timothy, I don't want to see this piece of trash in Tijuanea again. You understand?"

Timothy nodded and ordered his men to tie Victor up, gag him, and throw him into the trunk.

"Don't worry, boss. I'll feed him to the sharks. He'll vanish without a trace."

"I'll transfer twenty million dollars to you. Split one million among the men here, and you and Arthur take the rest. Keep doing good work for me."
