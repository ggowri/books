Chapter 411 Argument

Simon shivered and stepped back.

With every step he took back, Alexander moved one step closer, his anger intensifying.

With a thud, Simon involuntarily slumped down in front of Alexander.

Even when Simon confronted Felix, he had never felt such deep fear.

"Daniel did it on his own, and he just loves to catch pretty girls. I'm his cousin, so I can only defend him for the sake of us being related..."

Simon blamed it on Daniel unexpectedly.

Daniel was shocked. Then, he growled angrily, "Simon, screw you! It was clearly you who wanted me to catch that woman and little girl!"

"How could it be me? You obviously wanted to get that woman!"

The two start arguing, and they hit each other in front of Alexander, looking awkward.

Jane was confused. She had mixed feelings watching Simon act so cowardly in front of Alexander.

Simon was the director of the Loscity Institute of the Red Umbrella Group, and he was an important person, but he was surprisingly so inferior in front of Alexander.

"You bastard, you hit me for that bitch!"

A deep sense of loss and resentment enveloped Jane's mind.

There was another tumultuous rush of footsteps outside. A crowd had obviously come, and it was interspersed with shouting and yelling.

"Come on, don't let that asshole ran away!"

"Protect Daniel!"

A dozen men rushed in with various weapons in their hands, protecting Daniel as soon as they entered.

These were the security guards of the water park, Daniel's subordinates.

Seeing that, Daniel was delighted and thought that he was going to survive in a desperate situation.

He instantly jumped up from the ground and pointed at Alexander, cursing, "Son of a bitch, how dare you hit me? Do you realize I'm the supervisor here?"

Alexander couldn't help but laugh, thinking, "You're just a supervisor at a water park. You are so short-sighted."

"How dare he laugh? Go ahead and break his hand first. If anything happens, I'll take the responsibility!"

Daniel arrogantly ordered his men.

"Get out of the way, all of you!" Alexander took the action.

Alexander had already moved around the crowd quickly without being noticed.

With a sound, the men's machetes, crowbars, and other weapons fell. The guys who had just rushed in fell to the ground with screams.

"Are you a devil?"

Daniel was horrified to death.

"I'm the one who will send you to hell!"

Alexander slapped Daniel on the forehead.

With a crack, a crisp sound of bone cracking rang out.

Daniel fell to the ground like a rotten log that had been cut down, and he wailed as he held his head, which was cracked open.

The others all turned pale and didn't dare to speak again. Alexander was ruthless, they didn't dare to provoke him.

"Why don't you get out of here?"

Alexander yelled, and the man who had fallen to the floor got up and ran outside.

In an instant, the room fell into silence again.

Simon was already sweating profusely, not daring to imagine what would happen to him.

Jane stared at Alexander blankly, and she couldn't remember how many times she'd been blamed by Alexander, and she'd become numb to it.

Daniel wailed miserably.

"I was wrong! It was truly my fault! Simon came to me today and urged me to do it... It was him! It was really him!"

Simon immediately started arguing.

"I didn't, I truly didn't do it. It's obviously you who likes pretty girls, and you tried to kidnap them!"

Simon looked angry as if he had been wronged by Daniel, and he was good at acting.

Alexander had long been staring at Simon, his All-Seeing Eyes catching Simon lying like a lie detector, and Simon's heartbeat and blood pressure shifted dramatically as he lied.

"You're lying. You're from the Red Umbrella Group, aren't you?"

Alexander asked Simon with a sneer.

Simon kept stepping back in fear, thinking, "How did this guy know that?"

Alexander became even more certain of his suspicions and took large steps towards Simon.

Every step he took was like a sledgehammer hitting Simon's heart so forcefully that it was as if a death knell had sounded in Simon's ears.

"Don't come any closer. You will not die if you touch me, and I am a senior executive from the Vanderbilt family, so you'd better not make any trouble!"

Simon completely panicked. He threatened Alexander as he stepped back nervously.

Alexander sneered contemptuously, his eyes filled with anger.
