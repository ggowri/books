Soon, the news that the Sakra Society had teamed up with the military to conduct human experiments and launch the Apocalypse Project in an attempt to dominate the world spread around the world.

The five empires held a global emergency meeting.

The president of the world's largest empire had been in office for four years and was about to run for re-election.

Unexpectedly, such a crisis concerning global security occurred, and the president immediately ordered a thorough investigation.

As long as he handled this matter well, he would continue to be the president.

The five empires issued the most severe warning in history, asking the governments of Risun to properly handle the crisis and severely punish those involved, otherwise, they would resort to war and wipe Risun off the map first.

In Kylaville.

"Well..."

The Prime Minister of Risun sighed deeply.

He didn't expect this matter to be exposed. Whether it was using living people for human experiments or creating a biochemical crisis around the world, it was unacceptable to all mankind.

The Prime Minister picked up the phone, called Salman, and ordered in an extremely stern tone.

"Notify Yinson to destroy all immortality potions and materials.

"I have warned you long ago that it must not be exposed, but Yinson has been too high-profile and is becoming increasingly out of control. You should let him know who is the real ruler of our country!

"If Yinson dares to resist, he will be killed without mercy."

After saying that, the Prime Minister hung up the phone, lay back in his chair with a blank expression, and sighed deeply.

In just over ten minutes, thousands of soldiers surrounded Sakra Society's headquarters and blocked off the entire surrounding block.

Teams of soldiers rushed in like wolves, and a gunfight soon broke out.

However, the military had more people and more power, and Sakra Society was no match for it.

After a while, a group of soldiers in white protective suits escorted a sealed box onto the anti-biological and chemical vehicle.

Then a dozen light armored vehicles escorted the chemical defense vehicles and sped away.

Yinson had a grim face.

"Fuck!"

He roared and smashed everything that could be smashed in the office.

"A bunch of cowards and losers! You want to destroy my hard work? It's not that easy..."

Yinson gave a ferocious smile.

He opened a secret compartment in the bookcase, revealing a small safe.

After entering the password and fingerprint, the safe opened, revealing a red potion and a small mobile hard drive.

That was the latest third-generation immortality potion, and the hard drive contained all the research data of the Apocalypse Project.

The scarlet liquid flowed in the glass tube, emitting a strange light.

This was a third-generation immortality potion that he had secretly hidden. With this potion and the information, he could continue to promote the Apocalypse Project when the storm calmed down.

But even Salman betrayed him, so he would not let anyone know this secret.

He had to wait until the day when the storm was calm and the people who were monitoring him left.

"Just wait! What the hell are the five empires? One day, I will let you know that I am the true ruler of the Earth!"

He gritted his teeth and looked like a ghost.
