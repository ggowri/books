Chapter 571 Danger Ahead

Barry stormed out with Diego, slamming the door behind them. Felix quickly followed.

Cole sneered. This was the territory of The Kaylon; an inescapable trap was already set, and neither Felix nor Barry would escape.

Alexander had been watching the scene unfold from the surveillance room. Observing Barry and Felix's reactions, he chuckled.

"Bring Joyce in. I have some questions for her," he ordered.

Soon, Joyce was handcuffed and personally escorted by Cole.

"What are you planning?" Joyce asked, puzzled.

Joyce looked at them in confusion, then turned her gaze to Finley and Jaylon.

Finley and Jaylon, the two who had "won," just smirked at her, shrugging and spreading their hands.

Joyce understood immediately. This was a trap set by The Kaylon, specifically aimed at her and Barry!

Cole threatened coldly, "Cooperate, and nothing will happen. But try anything funny, and I guarantee you'll regret it."

Joyce shivered, feeling a chill, as she was escorted toward the room where Alexander awaited.

"What do you plan to do with her?" Nancy asked Alexander.

Alexander chuckled, "Felix and Barry teaming up can't mean anything good. As for Joyce's fate—that'll depend on how smart she is."

"If she honestly spills Felix and Barry's plans, I might just let her live. I don't kill women unless I have to."

A hint of menace flashed in Alexander's gaze.

Although it was only a slight hint, it was enough to make Nancy's heart tremble.

The room fell silent, with only the faint sound of coffee brewing breaking the stillness.

Before long, Cole escorted Joyce into the room.

Alexander sat commandingly in the center of the sofa, with Nancy beside him.

As Joyce entered, her full attention was drawn to Alexander.

"The man is simply..." Joyce stared, momentarily at a loss for words.

At first glance, he looked like a young, handsome guy. With a second look, she felt an intense, commanding presence that seemed untouchable.

His eyes were the most striking. It was like terrifying whirlpools capable of swallowing a person's very soul.

Joyce stopped in her tracks, feeling as if her very soul had left her body.

Alexander gazed at her indifferently, showing no trace of emotion.

The moment they locked eyes, Alexander's All-seeing Eyes were already working to influence Joyce's mind.

Since Joyce wasn't an ordinary person, it would be best to subtly break her will without her noticing.

"Wh-Who are you?"

Joyce asked, dazed.

Alexander chuckled lightly, "Me? I'm the one Felix wants to take down, Alexander."

"What? You're Alexander?" Joyce's face paled in shock.

She looked horrified, as if jolted awake from a nightmare, and took several steps back.

Could it be that The Kaylon was already working with Alexander?

This would explain why Finley and Jaylon had set up a trap, deliberately provoking Barry and then winning her as a "prize." It was all to find out Barry and Felix's plan.

Joyce wasn't a fool; in an instant, she had guessed the truth of the situation.

By now, the casino was probably covered with inescapable traps, and leaving would be impossible.

The sad part was, Barry had no idea!

A wave of sympathy for Barry hit Joyce hard.

Alexander said calmly, "Lay out Felix and Barry's plan, in full detail. Since you're a woman, I might just spare your life."

Joyce's face grew darker. Her suspicions had been right all along.

Nancy looked at her coldly. "Go on, speak up. You're still young; you don't want to die, do you?"

"And you are...?" Joyce asked, still dazed by Alexander's influence.

Joyce's mind was foggy from Alexander's mental influence.

Nancy gave a sweet smile.

"Me? I'm Nancy, The Kaylon's leader."

Joyce froze in shock. The leader of The Kaylon herself was here.

And Nancy, leaning on Alexander like a lover, signified that he was The Kaylon leader's man.

This time, Barry was truly in danger!

The thought filled Joyce with dread.

Seeing Joyce looking lost and dazed, Alexander's eyes flashed, and he barked out.

"Speak up!"

It was as if thunder had suddenly cracked in the room.

"Ah!"

Joyce clutched her head and collapsed to the ground, her mental defenses shattering instantly...
