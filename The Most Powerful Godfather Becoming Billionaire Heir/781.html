Chapter 781 Can't Win

The important guests who came to the celebration were feeling uneasy.

They knew this celebration wouldn't be peaceful, and they regretted coming.

Yvonne was so powerful, and if Alexander really brought his people to fight, there would be a bloody battle.

They worried they would be caught in the middle.

Not only the guests but also Monica and Rock were anxious.

Rock looked at Yvonne coldly and asked, "Why are you so fast? Tell the truth, and maybe I'll let you die without too much pain."

Yvonne looked at him with disdain and didn't answer.

"How dare you?" Rock shouted and walked towards Yvonne.

His steps were steady, and his killing intent was strong.

Everyone watched in awe, holding their breath.

Rock was powerful, and his presence made everyone nervous.

Yvonne was calm and ready to defend herself.

She didn't plan to back down, but she looked serious, feeling the pressure from Rock.

It was like she was facing a wild beast.

"Yvonne, do you think that because you serve Alexander, you can be rude to me? Come on, use your strongest witchcraft to attack me," Rock said.

He stopped at a spot not far from her and looked down on her.

Yvonne snorted at his arrogance.

She was ambitious and strong, and Rock's words were an insult to her.

"Old fool, I don't care if you're a white wizard or a dark wizard. Die!" Yvonne shot back.

She lunged at Rock at top speed.

She threw dozens of poison needles at him. If even a tiny bit of skin was pricked, it would be fatal.

Everyone was shocked by her moves.

Now she was even faster than just now.

But Yvonne was facing Rock, a white wizard.

When Rock was still a dark wizard, he could block the attacks of Shadow, Charlize, and Rosalia together using the Ancient Grimoire.

Now that he was stronger. He wouldn't be easily defeated by Yvonne.

To everyone's surprise, when the poison needles reached Rock, they were blocked by a black mist and couldn't move forward.

"Is this all you've got?" Rock looked at Yvonne with a smirk.

Yvonne waved her hands quickly, and it looked like she had a thousand hands throwing countless poison needles at Rock.

There were also witchcraft insects mixed in.

The wizards watching were scared. Such a strong attack would be impossible to dodge for any of them.

Monica frowned slightly but still looked down on Yvonne.

Rock opened the Ancient Grimoire, and a black mist came out, forming a strong shield that blocked all the poison needles.

The witchcraft insects that touched the black mist fell to the ground, dead.

It looked like Yvonne was winning, but she couldn't hurt Rock at all.

"I'm in trouble!" Yvonne thought.

She knew she had to run.

Alexander had told her to run if she couldn't win.

Yvonne had overestimated her strength. She was still much weaker than Rock, and Rock hadn't even started his real attack.

"You're scared now, aren't you?" Rock laughed wickedly.

He flipped the Ancient Grimoire, and the black mist turned into a red light, pulling all the poison needles towards it like a magnet.

The needles formed a scary blue ball.

"Break!" Rock pointed at the needles.

The red light surrounded the needles, and with a bang, all the needles turned into dust.
