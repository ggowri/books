Chapter 672 Retreat

"Kill him!"

Paxton's eyes were bloodshot, which was scary.

Beverly's face turned pale. She was out of options.

She knew that the operation had failed and that the situation was very dangerous and that she had to escape.

As long as Alexander came to her, she would be surrounded by the Axe.

"Paxton, let's go. He is too strong. He may rush in within a minute. We still have time to retreat now!"

Beverly grabbed Paxton's arm and spoke anxiously.

"Retreat?"

Paxton glared at her with disdain and sneered.

"Where can we go? If we run, we will all be defeated, and the Axe will occupy the Mandale Town. Where else do you think we can go?"

Beverly said in a trembling voice.

"Even if they want to occupy Mandale Town, we still have the opportunity to escape to Goldford or go abroad. If we don't leave now, we will be dead..."

Paxton hesitated for a while.

"Okay! Follow me. Let's leave Mandale Town!"

In fact, Paxton knew that from the moment the six experts and Dereck died, they had already lost the battle.

There were still some people defending the headquarters. They could take shelter there temporarily and then gather manpower for a counterattack or withdraw from Mandale Town.

"Let's go!"

Beverly was delighted.

"Hurry up. Retreat. Let them hold on."

Paxton agreed and then shouted.

"For those who hold on to the end, I will reward you handsomely. Each of you will receive half a million dollars. I will keep my promise."

Paxton retreated into the crowd with Beverly and a few of his henchmen.

The wage of avarice was death.

Paxton announced a huge reward of half a million dollars per person, which immediately aroused the greed of many people.

They were so overwhelmed by desire that they even launched a fierce counterattack towards Alexander.

There were some smart people who, seeing Paxton start to retreat, realized that the situation was bad and immediately followed Paxton and ran outside.

Some people even ran away faster than Paxton.

"Hurry up! Don't let them get away first. Or we'll be killed."

Paxton stopped pretending and quickly pulled Beverly into the car.

The two leaders fled. And the coalition forces lost their fighting spirit. They would not get the bounty Paxton promised.

"Chase him!"

Stanley ordered his men to chase Paxton but was stopped by Alexander.

"Clean up the scene and stabilize the situation. Paxton and Beverly can't get away. Leave it to me."

Speaking, Alexander jumped into the car and chased after Paxton's convoy.

"Damn Paxton, you're in trouble for messing with Mr. Hawthorne."

Malone smiled.

From now on, Mandale Town would be ruled by the Axe.

"Lock up all captives, then seize all gem mines and warehouses. Let's go!"

The Axe was cheering.

"Stop! Stop!"

Beverly kept reminding the driver that they had arrived at the Weaver House.

Several armored off-road vehicles suddenly braked and stopped in front of the gate.

"Get out of the car!"

Beverly opened the car door and ran, looking back and screaming in fear.

Paxton turned his head and saw a black car following behind him. And it seemed that it was about to catch up with him.

"Quick! Pay attention. Kill the enemy!"

Beverly gave an order.

The sentry at the gate of the Weaver House immediately took action, aiming heavy machine guns and rocket launchers at the black car.

"Great, kill Alexander! Fire!"

Paxton looked happy.

Two heavy machine guns were placed in front of the Weaver House's door, using large-caliber ammunition with a maximum firing rate of up to three thousand rounds.

And there were rocket launchers and a dozen guards armed with guns.

Two heavy machine guns fired wildly. The shell hit the black car.

The next second, the rockets were launched and hit the car.

A loud bang!

The car exploded, with countless fragments and parts flying everywhere.
