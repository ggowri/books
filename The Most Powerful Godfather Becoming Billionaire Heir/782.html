Chapter 782 Sowing Discord

Yvonne spat out a mouthful of blood. Her mind and body were hurt.

Her mental power was connected to every poison needle. When all the needles were destroyed, her mind was badly shocked.

She quickly retreated, but no matter how fast she moved, Rock followed her like a shadow.

"Die!" Rock grinned and waved his hand.

A dark mist rolled toward Yvonne's head.

If it hit her, Yvonne would surely be killed.

Yvonne was thrown far away, but she was not hurt at all.

A light shield appeared around her and blocked the dark mist.

"You have an amulet?" Rock was both surprised and happy.

He turned to look at Monica. She was also shocked.

An amulet that could defend against witchcraft was a treasure that all wizards dreamed of.

Monica's secret weapon was an amulet she found in an ancient witch's tomb.

That amulet was very powerful. It had saved Monica's life many times and helped her become the ruler of the City of Sorcery.

"How does Yvonne have such an amulet?"

"Did Alexander give it to her? Is that why she became his servant willingly?"

"No matter where she got it, we can't let her escape. She can die, but the amulet must stay."

"Yes, the amulet must stay in the City of Sorcery."

The wizards talked among themselves. They looked at Yvonne with greed.

Rock's witchcraft was blocked, proving that Yvonne's amulet was powerful.

Monica glared at Rock, worried that he would take the amulet before she could.

Monica stepped forward and pointed at Yvonne.

She shouted, "Give me the amulet, and I will let you live."

The wizards gathered around, looking at Yvonne with greed.

Yvonne felt pressured, but the amulet gave her a sense of safety.

Alexander had said that with this amulet and her speed, no witch could stop her.

Rock saw that Monica wanted to take the amulet.

He quickly stepped forward and shouted at Yvonne, "Give me the amulet, and I will forgive you. I will also keep you safe."

Monica glared at Rock and shouted, "Yvonne, I am the ruler of the City of Sorcery. Only I can keep you safe if you give me the amulet.

"If you give me the amulet and admit your mistake, you can come back to the City of Sorcery. I will even make you the Deputy Ruler."

The wizards were shocked.

Rock glared back at Monica with a look that could kill.

Because of Yvonne's amulet, the two strongest wizards were now against each other.

"This is funny," Yvonne laughed. "Just as my master predicted, they are fighting over a small benefit."

Alexander planned to break the unity of the City of Sorcery. Now, both Rock and Monica wanted Yvonne's amulet for themselves.

"Rock, step aside. I will deal with this traitor myself." Monica suddenly attacked.

A strange black mist exploded and covered Yvonne.

In an instant, the air around them seemed to boil. The temperature rose quickly.

Everyone wondered what kind of witchcraft it was.

It was not only fast but also made the air so hot that no one could come near.

Yvonne reacted quickly, but she was already trapped by Monica's witchcraft.

There was a loud noise. Black flames burst on the light shield. Monica's attack was blocked by the amulet.

Yvonne groaned and quickly retreated.

Suddenly, she felt a chill on her back.

A dark mist hit her back hard. Rock was afraid that Monica would take the amulet, so he attacked Yvonne from behind.
