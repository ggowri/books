Chapter 304 The Beach

After venting his anger, Schroder waved his hand impatiently. "Get out of here!"

"Yes, General."

His subordinates quickly retreated.

Schroder sat there with a dark expression, silent for a long time.

"It must be Alexander causing trouble. That damn guy, I need that magic powder formula. He has to be eliminated."

He cursed Alexander, feeling an ominous premonition that more trouble was on the way...

The next day, the sun shone brightly, and countless cheerful tourists flocked to the beach.

This was the most famous beach in Loscity, bustling with visitors who either surfed in the waves or basked in the sun.

Many beautiful women paraded around in bikinis, their alluring figures drawing the attention of many, creating a scene of vibrant excitement.

Alexander, wearing sunglasses, appeared relaxed as he lounged on a beach chair.

He held a pair of binoculars, observing a nearby villa with rocky exterior walls.

According to his intel, that was Schroder's stronghold in Loscity, where Jane and her group were being held.

Alexander's physique was well-defined and muscular, perfectly balanced—not overly exaggerated like a bodybuilder, yet undeniably strong, with clearly defined abs.

His sunglasses complemented his handsome features, and his tall, perfect build attracted many women's gazes.

Some bold women not only stared but also flirtatiously tossed him coy glances.

While William discreetly protected Ava, Augusta, Marvin, and the recently released Logan from the hospital, along with thirty elite operatives trained in special forces, all concealed themselves among the crowd.

They weren't distracted by the throngs of bikini-clad women. Instead, they kept a watchful eye on the villa.

They surrounded Alexander, ensuring that any woman attempting to approach him was kept at bay.

"With that many bodyguards, he must be wealthy!" the onlookers remarked, their eyes on Alexander heating up.

"Wow, what stunning beauties! Here they come!"

Suddenly, a man shouted excitedly, pointing in another direction.

Everyone turned to see the stunning Betty and Charlize strutting towards them in their sexy bikinis, radiating allure.

They instantly became the focus of the beach.

Not only the men but even the women present were captivated by their beauty, feeling momentarily inadequate.

The two beauties approached Alexander, flanking him, and no other woman dared to interrupt.

At the same time, they drew envious and greedy looks from many men.

"What's everyone staring at? Get lost!"

Marvin shouted, brandishing his fist at the rude men, exuding an air of dominance.

Alexander chuckled wryly, realizing that allowing the two of them to come had created quite the stir, potentially alerting Schroder's men.

Sure enough, he sensed a slight aura of hostility and immediately warned, "Everyone be careful. Disperse quickly—we've been compromised!"

Augusta and the others discreetly signaled their subordinates to blend into the crowd.

Meanwhile, Alexander remained casually seated on his beach chair, his gaze sweeping around, quickly spotting several suspicious individuals approaching.

His remarkable hearing caught snippets of their hushed conversation.

"Sam, are you sure that's Alexander? You're not mistaken, are you?"

"Mr. Harper, I saw him clearly behind the general at the banquet."

"Is he here for a swim? Are we being too tense?"

"Regardless of whether he's swimming or not, this is a perfect opportunity to grab him and take him back. It would be a major coup, and the general will reward us handsomely."

Hearing their conversation, Alexander smirked.

He had been contemplating how to infiltrate the villa, but it seemed they had walked right into his trap. If he took out these guys, it would significantly weaken Schroder's stronghold.

"Heh heh..." Alexander chuckled to himself, scanning the area to confirm the number of targets.

Over twenty sneaky individuals were edging closer—a considerable number.
