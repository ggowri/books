Chapter 353 We Must Get the Formula

Alexander chuckled, "I am playing a long game! You go back first, and I'll follow them and see who is behind Arthur."

Brittany asked worriedly, "Are you going alone? Isn't it too dangerous?"

"Don't worry. They are just the Mafia, and they are no match for me."

Alexander waved his hand confidently.

Brittany blushed slightly. "Okay. I'll go back to the hotel and wait for you. Be careful!"

Alexander was stunned for a moment. Instead of going home, she decided to wait for him at the hotel. Her intention was self-evident.

"Take the room card. I'll be back soon."

Alexander handed over the room card without hesitation.

"I'll wait for you. Come back soon!"

Brittany gently touched Alexander's hand and gazed at him tenderly with her watery eyes.

After sending her to a taxi, he chased after Arthur and his men. They supported each other and staggered into several cars.

"Follow the cars ahead. Don't let them notice us."

Alexander hailed a taxi and gave the driver a few large bills.

The driver became excited and stalked them carefully.

Ten minutes later, Arthur and his men pulled up in front of a bar.

Alexander didn't walk in calmly until they all entered the bar.

It wasn't very big, only a few hundred square yards, but it was crowded. The air was mingled with the odor of alcohol and the distinctive aroma of the magic powder.​

Alexander glanced around and found that most of the customers were enjoying the pleasure brought by the magic powder.

He grinned. The more popular the magic powder became, the greater the profit he would make.

Arthur waved all his men away and then stepped into a quiet corridor.

Alexander waited for a while. After confirming that no one was paying attention to him, he secretly entered the corridor.

It was very long. He turned left and spotted a thick wooden door.

Alexander vaguely overheard the conversation within. Like a cat on the attack, he kept his every footstep as silent as possible.​

Inside, an unattractive middle-aged man, Timothy, lay on his back comfortably, with two young women in scanty bikinis massaging him.

Seeing Arthur enter the room dejectedly, he asked seriously, "Arthur, I heard that you were beaten up. What happened?"

Arthur replied with a dark face, "Timothy, it's Alexander Hawthorne who is wanted for 100 million dollars on the Dark Web. He attacked us."

"What! Alexander?"

Timothy immediately sat up and pushed the two women aside.

"Are you sure it's him? He's a man worth 100 million dollars, and he has the magic formula. We can't let him leave Tijuanea."

Arthur showed a fearful expression.

"Timothy, Alexander is too powerful. I brought more than a dozen of my robust men with me, but he defeated them all!"

Timothy calmed down in an instant.

Arthur was his most capable right-hand man, and he had many ruthless men under his command who had fought a lot, but they had all been beaten up by Alexander, so Timothy had to think hard.

"Arthur, this is our best chance to make a great contribution! A reward of 100 million dollars is nothing compared to the magic powder formula. We must get the formula!"

"Timothy, Alexander is formidable. We are really no match for him."

Arthur couldn't help shivering and was very scared.

Timothy snorted coldly, "Loser!"

"Don't be afraid. I'll take care of it this time. I've been fighting underground for twenty years and have won the black market boxing championship several times. Can't I beat a twenty-something brat?"

Arthur suddenly became thrilled.

"Timothy, if you lead the team, we will definitely take Alexander. After all, you have worked hard to get to your current position in the Mafia."

Timothy nodded with satisfaction.

"It's a pity that there are so many capable people in the Mafia that I can only be a mere two-star member."

Arthur thought of something and asked cautiously, "Timothy, I heard that our Godfather is going to deal with that old guy, right? Making great achievements in the operation will help you be promoted to a four-star or even a five-star member."

"Yeah!"

Timothy nodded vigorously in agreement.

"Our Godfather, Mr. Satan, and Reaper, also at the Godfather level, will have a final battle to decide who is the true king of the Underworld!"
