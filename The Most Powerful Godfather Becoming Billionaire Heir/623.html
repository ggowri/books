Chapter 623 Unbelievable

The grinding wheel spun rapidly, and the ore had just been ground for less than ten seconds, but everyone saw a dazzling green light appear and burst out from the crushed stone chips.

There was indeed a gem in there.

Unbelievable!

Everyone was stunned, staring at the ore with wide eyes.

"Oh my God! Such a bright green light! Could this be a top-grade emerald?"

"That's impossible. Such a large piece of top-grade emerald would cost hundreds of millions."

"Stop grinding it. I'll give you 30 million dollars. Just sell this ore to me."

"Thirty million dollars? I'm willing to buy it for fifty million dollars."

Many people became excited and started bidding to grab the ore.

"Sir, you'd better not cut it completely open. What if the gem inside is not top-grade, but just high-grade? Sell it to me for fifty million dollars." Someone said.

They were even more anxious than Alexander, and they couldn't wait to snatch the stone away.

Even though someone had offered a high price of fifty million dollars, Alexander ignored them.

The staff member ground it carefully, fearing that the grinder would damage the gem inside.

He was also very excited to be able to grind a top-grade gem.

Everyone gathered together, staring at it and talking excitedly.

Evan's face looked very ugly. He prayed in his heart, hoping that there was no high-grade gem.

Soon, the outer shell of the ore was worn away, leaving only a green stone the size of an egg inside.

Both its luster and texture showed that it was a natural emerald. After being polished, it would emit an even brighter light.

The staff member took out a flashlight and shone it on the emerald a few times.

The lush green glow radiated, making people intoxicated.

Everyone was silent. This was a top-grade emerald.

Evan fell to the ground with a thud.

He had thought he was sure to win, but he didn't expect to lose so badly. His body became limp uncontrollably, and he couldn't get up after struggling several times.

Everyone looked at Evan with pity. He lost the exchange.

The transaction commission of this underground exchange was over one million every month, which was very valuable.

More importantly, Rose Market was a good place to collect intelligence. Otherwise, the Mafia wouldn't have set their sights on this place.

Evan would be in trouble.

First, he spent thirty million dollars to buy a scrap mine, and now he lost the exchange. Mafia wouldn't forgive him.

A middle-aged jeweler said to Alexander excitedly, "Sir, please sell this gem to me. I'll give you sixty million dollars."

Alexander waved his hand and refused directly.

With this top-grade emerald, he only lacked orange and purple top-grade gems. With seven colors of gems in total, he could upgrade the Lightsabers to level two, greatly increasing its power.

So, no matter how much money others offered, he would not sell it.

At this time, Evan wanted to sneak away secretly.

Alexander grabbed him and said, "You want to run away?"

Evan's eyes were filled with despair and resentment, and he stared at Alexander viciously.

"Mr. Tate, you can't run away. The exchange is mine now. Sign it."

Alexander waved his hand.

A large group of men in black rushed in from outside, surrounding the entrance and exit.

Betty twisted her waist and handed an agreement to Alexander with both hands.

At the same time, she looked at Olivia a few times and smiled.

Seeing this scene, Olivia was very nervous. Her palms were sweaty, and she couldn't help trembling slightly.

Evan gnashed his teeth and said, "So you are from Ultimate Alliance."

Mafia members all looked pale. Faced with enemies with absolute superiority, they dared not resist.

"If you still want to live, just sign it."

Alexander threw the agreement in Evan's face.
