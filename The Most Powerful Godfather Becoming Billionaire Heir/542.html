Chapter 542 Seeking Death

"Kris, tell your father to inventory your family's property. Someone will take it within 24 hours, as well as your entire family's lives, as a warning to others!"

Alexander's voice was as cold as polar ice, and he intended to eliminate the Lauren family.

"Come on. Kill the lunatic!"

Kris waved his hand.

His men immediately swarmed forward, full of murderous intent.

With the Lauren family's support, they were not afraid of killing people at all.

Time seemed to stand still. The muscular man who had first rushed upon Alexander swung his machete and struck at him fiercely.​

He suddenly found himself motionless, as if his body was trapped in an invisible quagmire.

Despite his struggles and efforts, he couldn't move at all.

"You! What did you do to me?"

The man gazed at Alexander in horror. The pressure on his body increased, causing him to tremble uncontrollably. His bones creaked under the weight, and beads of sweat the size of beans streamed down his face.

"I can't move. Why is this happening?"

He clutched the machete with both hands, only a foot away from Alexander's head, but he couldn't cut it off.

Because he used so much force, his eyes were bloodshot and nearly popped out of their sockets.​

"What's going on? I can't move, either. Who caused this?"

"Could it be this guy?"

Other people had the same experience, feeling as if the air had turned into a quagmire or as if they were carrying a mountain on their backs. They were completely unable to move and were all terrified.

They were all the elite of the Lauren family. Conceited, they thought highly of their fighting skills.

But the dozens of people became powerless before getting close to Alexander.

"You! Are you a fucking devil?"

Kris tried hard to raise the gun in his hand with a ferocious expression.

But, despite his many attempts, he couldn't do it.

Upon entering, Kris felt superior and looked down upon others.

But now, unprecedented fear overwhelmed him.

"Since you are seeking death, I will send you to hell as you wish."

Alexander spoke of the murder as calmly as if he was chatting with an old friend.

In an instant, several soft white lights flashed across each other, and something horrible happened.

The muscular man in black standing in front of Alexander, along with the machete in his hand, all broke into pieces and collapsed to the floor.

Kris narrowed his eyes as a chill ran down his spine.

It was just a simple beginning.

The second muscular man also fell down to pieces, his limbs twitching and bleeding all over the floor.

One by one, the elite of the Lauren family were smashed to pieces, all lying in pools of blood, their bodies like cut cakes.

Blood flowed across the floor, carving out strange flowers, and the pungent smell filled the entire restaurant.

Kris flopped down on the floor, his face as pale as paper.

"A-Am I not dead? Am I not dead?"

He was surprised and overjoyed. Yet, his whole body went limp, and he couldn't even stand up in fright.

Hoson was extremely shocked by the scene.

He didn't expect Alexander's fighting skills to be so terrifying!

Alexander killed dozens of people without warning and cut them to pieces effortlessly.

He was incredibly formidable, his strength unfathomable.

Kris trembled and stuttered, "H-How dare you kill the elite of the Lauren family?"

Alexander cast an icy glance at Kris without even frowning.

"Remember what I just said. Go back and tell your father, Johnathan, to prepare your property. Someone will come to take your lives."

"Get away!"

Alexander waved his hand.

Kris felt a tremendous force strike his chest and, with a wail, flew out of the restaurant.​

"Ouch!"

Kris thought that at least several of his ribs were broken, for the pain was excruciating.

Fearing death, he desperately crawled out of the hotel, leaving a long trail of blood behind him.

The onlookers were all scared silly, gazing at him blankly.

Just now, he had rushed in with a large group of people, but now, he was the only one thrown out.

It was obvious that his men had been murdered!
