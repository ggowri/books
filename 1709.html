"Where are you?!" Osborne quickly typed a response to Hannah, his heart racing with anxiety.

He waited impatiently for a reply, but as the seconds ticked by with no response, his worry only deepened.

He urged, sending multiple voice messages, "Please, just tell me where you are!

"I need to know your location!

"Where are you?"

Osborne kept sending messages, hoping Hannah would respond. He couldn't let her be killed by the Paramount Supreme Clan's Bowman family. He had to save her.

But it seemed Hannah sensed his intentions to rescue her. Perhaps she was in the middle of a fight or didn't want to drag him into her troubles; whatever the reason, she remained silent.

Osborne's anxiety grew.

He bit his lip and spoke urgently into the device, "Hannah! I know you saw my messages! Answer me right now! Where are you? Don't you consider me a friend?

"I'll be honest. I killed Tobey!

"If you get caught by the Bowman family because of this, I'll never find peace! I swear, if you die, I will hunt down the Bowman family for revenge!

"I know you're trying to protect me, but if you die, I will have no choice but to go after them! I swear, because you're my friend and this is my fault, I'll go to the ends of the earth for you!"

After sending the message, Osborne waited anxiously. Finally, he received a reply from Hannah.

"Why are you so foolish? Why would you tell me you killed Tobey? Aren't you afraid I might betray you? Why would you even think of saving me? Or going after the Bowman family for my sake? Do you have any idea how powerful they are?

"Please, don't be reckless! If I die, then Tobey's story ends with me. I'll take the blame for killing him. You just need to live!"

Osborne shot back. "No! I won't let you die!

"I don't need you to take the blame! If you even think about doing that, I'll tell everyone it was me who killed Tobey! I don't care how powerful the Bowman family is! If you die, I will go after them without hesitation!

"So you'd better tell me where you are right now! I can save you! Trust me, we can both make it out alive!"

Hannah replied, "You can't save me! I'm already surrounded, and there's no way out! The ones surrounding me are peak Deity Overlords, dozens of them from the Bowman family's elite!

"I know you're strong, but you're no match for them right now! Please, don't be impulsive! Just let it go, okay? I'm begging you!"

Meanwhile, in a mining area not far from Wild Flame City, Hannah was staring at her terminal, tears streaming down her face.

Osborne's urgent desire to rescue her touched her deeply.

But she truly didn't want to drag him into this mess. The Paramount Supreme Clan's Bowman family was no small threat!

Dozens of peak Deity Overlords had surrounded the entire mining area, equipped with a powerful Spirit Tracer that had locked onto her position.

She knew it wouldn't be long before they found her.

Even with her skills in stealth, she couldn't hide for long against such formidable technology. Even though Hannah had advanced to the mid-level of Deity Overlord, facing dozens of peak Deity Overlords left her with no chance of survival.

She genuinely wished Osborne wouldn't come for her.

Besides, it was already too late!

To her, Osborne was still at the Divine Peak Nine base, miles away from Wild Flame City. Even with the Ultimate Malevolence Airship, it would take nearly two days to reach her.

How could he possibly save her from so far away?

Hannah's face was streaked with tears as she hurriedly replied to Osborne, "Osborne, thank you for wanting to save me, but it's really too late! They'll find me soon; I can't wait for you to arrive!

"Farewell... please don't take revenge for me!

"Live a good life; I'm begging you! This is my last request. Just grant me this, okay?"

After sending her message, Hannah prepared to shut down the terminal.

But just then, she received another message from Osborne. "Hannah, you need to tell me where you are right now! If you honestly believe I can't reach you in time, what do you have to lose by giving me your location? I promise you, if you tell me where you are, even if I can't save you in time, I won't go after the Bowman family!

"But if you don't tell me, I'll make sure to confront them directly!"

Hannah stared at Osborne's words, and her face filled with inner conflict.

After a long pause, she finally exhaled slowly.

"He won't make it in time, anyway. By the time he arrives, I'll probably already be gone. I might as well tell him where I am..."

With that thought, Hannah reluctantly sent her location to Osborne.

"Osborne, you'd better keep your word! Now that I've told you my location, you must promise you won't go after the Bowman family in revenge! I can't talk any longer; I need to prepare for battle! My chances of survival are slim, but I have to fight, right?"

With that, Hannah hung up the terminal and steeled herself for what was to come.
