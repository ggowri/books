"So, I could create an Omnipotent Divine Body, but I could also end up with a useless body?

That's a pretty huge risk!

Besides, the idea of an Omnipotent Divine Body is just a legend. The chances of pulling that off seem slim to none. If I end up with a worthless body, wouldn't it be safer to stick with the methods Isa provided and just forge a Level Seven Solar Divine Body?

Maybe I should play it safe?"

"To defeat him, we need to find a new path of cultivation. We need someone stronger than a Deity King to emerge!

"That's where hope comes in!

"Every new path of cultivation represents a chance for hope! It might lead you to a dead end, but it could also be the way to surpass the Deity Kings!

Osborne stood there, stunned. It finally clicked for him why Isa had been so insistent on pushing him to grow stronger, to evolve.

The seven Deity Kings had pushed the conventional paths to their limits and still couldn't handle Lord Malevolence.

"Goddess of Fate, are you still pretending to be asleep?"
