Over four thousand members of the Blood Demon soared through the air, perfectly aligned. Each warrior exuded an overwhelming aura, their combined strength so potent it felt as if they could tear the sky apart.

However, as they spotted Osborne, they immediately saluted in unison.

"Mr. Augustine!"

"Mr. Augustine!"

The thunderous chorus of their voices echoed across the landscape.

Osborne smiled slightly, a gesture that radiated calm authority.

"At ease, everyone."

With that command, the Blood Demon members straightened up, standing tall and proud like a row of spears, awaiting their leader's next order.

Cillian and his siblings stood frozen in wide eyes as they took in the scene.

This was the backup Osborne had mentioned!

Although they couldn't gauge Osborne's true strength, the sheer power emanating from the Blood Demon members was unmistakable—every single one was Class S.

Even with the current abundance of energy in the world, Class S warriors were still figures of awe and respect.

For an Ancient Family to produce even one Class S warrior was cause for a grand celebration.

Most Ancient Families didn't have a single Class S warrior!

Even among the superpowers, the number of Class S warriors was limited.

Each one held an esteemed position, revered and respected.

And here were over four thousand Class S warriors!

Cillian and his siblings had never seen even a fraction of that number of Class S in their lifetimes. The amount of Class A warriors they had encountered didn't even come close to this assembly.

What left them utterly dumbfounded was how these Class S warriors had just saluted Osborne with such respect and admiration.

They stared at him.

Suddenly, they recalled their earlier conversation with Osborne, where they had casually joked and urged him to leave this dangerous place for his own safety. Their faces flushed with embarrassment as they wished they could disappear into the ground.

They thought, "Who exactly is Osborne? What identity does he possess?"

"Os... Osborne..." Cillian stammered, struggling to find his words.

Osborne chuckled. "You're wondering about my identity, aren't you? I assure you. I'm not lying. My name is indeed Osborne! I'm the head of the Augustine family in Newport City and the current owner of the Draco Group."

As those words sank in, Cillian and the others' eyes went wide with disbelief.

They thought, "Wait, Osborne is the head of the Augustine family and the Draco Group?

That means he is..."

"The Godfather!" They gasped, their voices tinged with awe and incredulity.

They were merely from a wealthy family, completely out of their depth when it came to the likes of the Augustine family.

They had heard rumors that the former Godfather of Rosmandi had stepped back, but they had no idea who had taken his place.

And now, they knew Osborne was the new Godfather of Rosmandi!

Cillian and his siblings were utterly stunned, their minds racing to comprehend the implications.

But Osborne paid them little mind, his focus shifting back to the task at hand.

"If you need help from the Augustine family in Newport City, feel free to let me know later. But for now, I need you to wait here. I'll handle this emerging Beast Tide first."

He raised his hand, commanding, "Go! Eliminate all evolved Ferocious Beasts! Wipe out every last Dark Exotic Beast!"

"Yes, sir!" came the thunderous reply from the Blood Demon members.

With high speed and precision, the four thousand eight hundred Blood Demon warriors swiftly divided into 10 combat squadrons. Except for the elite Blood Demon Vanguard, a hundred strong, each squad had over 500 soldiers.

"Kill!"

They dispersed rapidly, encircling a sprawling expanse of forest that stretched over dozens of miles, partitioning it into 10 strategic sectors.

Simultaneously, they lunged into the dense woods from every direction, targeting the swarm of Ferocious Beasts.

A cacophony of terrible roars erupted.

Countless Ferocious Beasts surged forth, hurtling towards the Blood Demon with rabid ferocity, a horde that included numerous Class A, AA, and even AAA Dark Exotic Beasts.

Their numbers were huge!

Though the beasts' ranks paled in comparison to the Blood Demons' prowess, sheer numbers overwhelmed. Each squad found themselves besieged by tens of thousands of ravening beasts.

The Blood Demon warriors unleashed their full firepower, hewing and cutting. However, inexplicably, the beasts waged on, their numbers never dwindling, practically ballooning.

Mad with bloodlust, the beasts surged onward, heedless of death, launching themselves in suicidal frenzies against the Blood Demon ranks.

Shock froze Cillian and his trio like statues. Their eyes riveted on the battle unfolding like a nightmare, their bodies trembling uncontrollably.

Osborne could not help his own deepening frown.

"These Dark Exotic Beasts are smart! They're orchestrating these ordinary beasts. Without that, these beasts would've lost their nerve long ago, bolted in all directions.

"But this works too!

"If they're not running, then we simply wipe them out!

"It's time for us to join them!"
