"So, you're coming straight for us?

"And just look at that—exactly a thousand!

One of them was surely the traitor!

"Perhaps they were both involved?

Well, if I can't figure it out just yet, I'll wait for you to expose yourselves," he thought, shifting his focus back to Icy Six and his companions.

The thousand Supernal Deity Kings meant to protect them were already entangled in battle, leaving Osborne and his group feeling isolated and vulnerable.

They had to face the three Dark Sovereigns on their own.

Icy Six was an Ascension Six Supernal Deity King, while Icy Seven and Icy Eight were both Ascension Five. To anyone watching, their combined strength seemed more than enough to wipe the floor with Osborne and his crew.

But Osborne had anticipated this very moment.

Icy Six sneered, "Spatial power? What of it?

"This area is sealed by Ninefold Heaven's Tower!

At the same time, Icy Six and his companions reached the edge of the seal, closing in fast.

Even though Osborne possessed spatial power and could travel interdimensionally, his speed hardly outpaced theirs.

As Osborne and his group materialized, Icy Six was already hot on their heels.

Osborne's expression remained stoic as he continued to use interdimensional travel.

The three Dark Sovereigns exchanged glances, determined to keep up. While they could catch up to Osborne, holding him down proved to be a different story.

Osborne immediately felt the strain; using interdimensional travel became significantly harder.

He chuckled, deciding to abandon the interdimensional travel and instead stand his ground.

Casting a glance toward the God of Light, he saw her locked in a fierce battle with Icy One and the others. The clash was so intense that no one else could intervene.

The residual force alone could easily obliterate most Supernal Deity Kings.

Meanwhile, the Supernal Deity Kings on both sides were engaged in fierce combat, with no clear advantage emerging.

Icy Six burst into laughter, a look of utter disdain on his face. "Toying with us? A battle?

This was just a distraction to obscure the outside world's view!

Osborne had to elevate his own cultivation level to Ascension Two to effectively suppress Icy Six!

Each of these Blood Demon members had reached the pinnacle of the Deity King Level.

"Absolute forbidden zone!"
