Osborne's eyes turned red instantly.

Watching Alfreda killed by Naomi, he was so furious he nearly ground his teeth to dust.

Even though Alfreda could be resurrected after death, the pain of dying was not so easily endured.

At this moment, Osborne's urge to kill Naomi erupted like a volcano.

"You’re fucking asking for it. I’ll kill you!" Osborne growled through gritted teeth, instantly appearing in front of Naomi.

Naomi was utterly shocked.

"How is that possible? How did you get here so fast?"

She immediately wanted to flee. Although she was confident in her own strength, Osborne's battle record was terrifying. Back in Fairy Moon Sect, he had seriously injured Weston with a single arrow.

Weston was much stronger than her!

She knew she stood no chance against Osborne. She could only choose to run away.

But this time, Osborne wouldn’t let her escape.

In an instant, a massive pitch-black Stele appeared behind him and was quickly activated.

The power of the Domain Suppression Stele swiftly suppressed the energy flow within the entire Elf City, causing Naomi's movements to slow down instantly, her face showing utter shock.

"Domain Suppression Stele?!"

Domain Suppression Stele was exchanged by Osborne from the Royal Treasury using Kingdom Duel Scorepoints. As a princess, Naomi had entered the Royal Family's treasury more than once to select a suitable Supreme Sacred Artifact. Naturally, she had seen the Domain Suppression Stele before and remembered it vividly.

At the time, she found the function of the Domain Suppression Stele ridiculous, as it indiscriminately suppressed energy flow, leaving only physical strength usable.

To her, this thing was completely useless!

After all, as a member of the Human Community, her body was weak and couldn't compare to other races. Using the Domain Suppression Stele would essentially mean helping the enemy suppress her own strength.

Therefore, she believed no one would value the Domain Suppression Stele.

But now, she saw it in Osborne's hands.

The Domain Suppression Stele suppressed energy flow, leaving them only able to use physical strength.

At this moment, Naomi not only remained calm but even became excited.

The reason she feared Osborne was because of his strange bow and arrows, as well as his terrifying archery skills.

But now, with the Domain Suppression Stele suppressing energy flow, Osborne could no longer use such archery.

Thinking of this, Naomi stopped running.

She suddenly turned around and swiftly launched an attack on Osborne. Osborne was merely an Empyrean Sacredness Great Perfection, while she was a Supreme Proto-imperial Empyrean Sacredness.

Both being from the Human Community, she was confident her physical strength surpassed Osborne's!

"Osborne, go to hell!"

Naomi drew a Sacred Artifact Sword and fiercely stabbed it toward Osborne's chest.

Osborne's eyes were filled with anger.

Facing Naomi's sword thrust, he didn't dodge, letting the Sacred Artifact Sword strike his chest with a crisp clang.

The Sacred Artifact Sword didn't even pierce his skin!

Energy couldn't flow, and the power of the Sacred Artifact couldn't erupt. Relying solely on the sword's sharpness and Naomi's strength, breaking through Osborne's defense was simply impossible.

"How is this possible?!"

Naomi was stunned. Watching Osborne block her attack with his body, she suddenly panicked, a flash of terror in her eyes.

But Osborne made his move at this moment.

"Naomi, you deserve to die!"

He grabbed Naomi's Sacred Artifact Sword. With a slight force, Naomi lost control of the sword. She felt a terrifying power snatch the sword from her hands.

This power was terrifying.

Naomi finally understood why Osborne activated the Domain Suppression Stele.

Osborne's power far surpassed hers!

She thought, "I'm no match for Osborne! I must escape immediately!"

Naomi tried to escape again, but Osborne was faster. He thrust the Sacred Artifact Sword he had taken straight forward, piercing her thigh.

Naomi let out a painful scream. Her thigh was pierced, and escaping became a distant dream. Gritting her teeth, she drew another Sacred Artifact Sword, ready to fight Osborne to the death.

Unfortunately, a small gap in strength is called a desperate fight; a huge gap is just courting death!

At this moment, Naomi was practically courting death. Osborne effortlessly snatched the second Sacred Artifact Sword from her hands.

Then, he swiftly pierced her other leg.

Naomi let out another piercing scream.

She realized that relying solely on physical strength, she was as weak as a baby in front of Osborne.

And Osborne was simply a beast!

At this moment, Osborne seemed invincible to her. She couldn't comprehend how a Human Community could possess such a powerful physique. In her impression, not even the warriors of the Beast Clan could achieve this.

At this moment, Naomi felt a bit of despair.

She regretted following Isa and Clarissa's trail to this place.

But her regret was useless now.

Osborne's fury completely boiled over; he wouldn't let Naomi off easily. Not to mention the grudge he already had with Naomi, just the fact that she killed Alfreda was enough to drive Osborne mad.

Alfreda had just been resurrected!

Alfreda, who had just awakened from the darkness, was killed by Naomi again!

He must make Naomi pay!

Osborne held two Supreme Sacred Artifact Swords. Without energy flow, these swords were merely hard and sharp ordinary blades.

He kept a cold face, repeatedly driving the sword into Naomi's body, piercing hole after hole in her. He watched her blood gush out with indifference.

Osborne even worried she might die like this, using the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity to heal her wounds and help her recover quickly.

The sound of the sword piercing flesh, combined with Naomi's shrill screams, echoed through the entire Elf City, sending chills down the spine of everyone who heard it.

At this moment, Isa and Clarissa felt an immense sense of satisfaction.

Thinking of Alfreda's fate, their eyes were filled with hatred. Clarissa drew a blade, walked up to Naomi, and stabbed her directly.

"It's you who killed Alfreda!"

"Why did you hurt Alfreda?"

"You deserve to die!"

Clarissa was nearly insane as she kept torturing Naomi.

Naomi felt extreme pain and wished to die immediately.

Unfortunately, Osborne kept treating her, making it impossible for her to even die.

She began to plead, her voice extremely shrill. "Osborne, stop! I'm your sister!"

"Although we don't share the same mother, we have the same father!"

"Osborne, please let me go!"

Osborne looked at her coldly. "Sister? Ridiculous! Who acknowledged that? You didn’t say so before! Didn’t you call me a bastard, a bastard tainting the Royal Family's bloodline? Now you’re willing to admit my identity?"

"You are truly pathetic!"

"Besides, that was the past me; the present me has nothing to do with you!"

After speaking, Osborne thrust the sword into Naomi's mouth, shredding her tongue, leaving her unable to speak and only able to let out piercing screams.

"Osborne, stop for now!"

Isa shouted anxiously, "Hurry and revive Alfreda! There was already a major problem with her fusion with the Elven Mother Tree before she died..."

"I'm worried that even after reviving Alfreda, these problems won't be resolved!"
