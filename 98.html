“Sneak attack me? I’ve been waiting for you.”

Osborne had Satan’s Eye’s crisis warning and wasn’t afraid of sneak attacks at all. He suddenly kicked back heavily on his enemy’s fist.

Boom!

The next second, Osborne felt a tremendous force coming from the soles of his feet. He couldn’t help but rise into the air, landing and taking a few steps back before getting his body under control.

He couldn’t help but become more serious.

“What a strong power.”

Osborne was shocked and thought, “I used my feet, and he used his fist. But in a contest of strength, I actually lost? This enemy’s power is incomparably terrifying. Even the three werewolves combined are inferior to him.”

That man had a bearded face and an expressionless countenance.

But Osborne couldn’t help but laugh at his face with a look of contempt.

“Captain Paul, you look great in this look. You are much more handsome than you really are.”

When Osborne finished, the other man remained expressionless.

It wasn’t that he didn’t want to change his expression, but because he was wearing a human skin mask.

If it had been anyone else, they might not have recognized him as Paul.

But unfortunately, Osborne had Satan’s Eye.

He could see through everything.

Through the human skin mask, Osborne saw Paul’s expression become instantly grim.

He said in a hoarse voice, “I don’t know what you’re talking about. You’ve ruined things for the organization a few times. Now I’ll kill you.”

After that, Paul struck again.

Not only was his strength terrifying, but his speed was equally impressive. He reached Osborne in an instant and quickly sliced towards Osborne’s throat with his hand.

Osborne reacted exceptionally quickly and instantly raised his alloy dagger.

Bang!

Paul’s hand contacted Osborne’s alloy dagger. Osborne stepped back again while Paul grunted. The boxing gloves he wore on his hand had been sliced through an opening, and blood flowed out.

“What a sharp dagger.”

Paul’s eyes were filled with greed.

“Let’s strike together and kill him.”

At his command, the few remaining werewolves came together at once. They cooperated with Paul to surround Osborne, hitting him with deadly moves.

If it were two days ago, facing Paul with so many werewolves surrounding him, Osborne wouldn’t have been sure of surviving even with Clarissa’s healing.

But now, Osborne wasn’t the least bit flustered and grunted coldly.

“You will die.”

A fierce battle broke out.

As the leader of Clarissa’s bodyguard team, Paul was an extremely powerful Class C martial artist. Combined with a group of Class E or even Class D werewolves, even a Class C+ powerhouse might not be his match.

But Osborne, relying on Satan’s Eye with his Class B daggers, blocked their attacks completely.

He took the life of a werewolf with every counterattack.

In an instant, five more werewolves fell.

Seeing that he still couldn’t kill Osborne, Paul turned completely serious.

“Damn it!”

He suddenly broke away from the battlefield, no longer caring about Osborne but making a quick dash for Clarissa’s car.

Paul pulled on the car door. And when it didn’t pull open, he slammed his fist down hard on the window.

Bang!

The bulletproof glass that could withstand sniper bullets was smashed with countless cracks by Paul’s terrifying fist, and the entire car shook violently.

“Fuck!”

Paul blasted the car window with punch after punch. The glass shattered across the ground and was about to be smashed through.

Clarissa in the car turned pale at this point.

“Osborne, help me!”

Osborne’s expression changed. His ocular energy erupted violently, controlling the bodies of the last three werewolves. He stabbed his alloy dagger through their hearts instantly, ending their lives.

He turned his head and just saw Paul smash his fist through the car window.

“Don’t you dare,” Osborne shouted sternly, unable to care too much, and violently threw the alloy dagger.

Paul was about to reach for Clarissa in the car. He had to stop his movement and duck quickly.

The alloy dagger thrillingly flew past his ear, nearly cutting it off.

“You are courting death. You’ve completely angered me,” Paul shouted angrily as his body abruptly began to change, becoming taller and thinner. The nails of his fingers became longer, like blood-red daggers. Two long fangs appeared in his mouth.

At this time, he was like a legendary vampire.

“Osborne, watch out. He’s one of the Blood Clan.”

Clarissa exclaimed at the sight.

“Go to hell!”

Paul moved lightning-fast and was at Osborne’s side in an instant. Even though Osborne had Satan’s Eye, he could barely make out Paul’s movements.

Osborne’s left eye trembled wildly, sensing a powerful threat.

Without thinking, he jerked aside.

But it was still late.

Paul caught Osborne in the chest, his sharp nails nearly ripping Osborne’s body apart. Thanks to the Class B Defensive Soft Armor, Osborne wasn’t disemboweled.

But even so, Osborne still felt a terrifying force hit him, knocking his body fiercely out.

“Osborne!” Clarissa exclaimed at once, but there was nothing she could do.

Paul sneered and reappeared at Osborne’s side, clasping Osborne’s shoulders with his sharp claws.

“In front of the current me, you are simply as weak as an ant. You are simply feeble.

“Now, you can go die.”
