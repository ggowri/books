"Die now!"

Osborne regained his composure and immediately launched two punches at the two Tauren.

Deadly Force!

Bang, bang!

The two Tauren tried to stop, but it was too late. As they charged at Osborne, they were struck by his punches at the same moment.

The terrifying force penetrated their tough hides, entering their bodies and destroying their internal organs and brains.

Their bodies froze in place.

The life in their eyes quickly faded.

Thud!

Both Tauren fell to the ground.

They were dead!

Osborne was also sent flying from their impact. He coughed up more blood, feeling more of his ribs breaking. His body was now in a severely damaged state.

But seeing the three dead Tauren, he couldn't help but smile.

"Seven left!"

"Damn it!"

At this moment, Osborne finally relaxed. Although seven enemies remained, only five still had the strength to fight.

One Medusa and one Werewolf had already used their ultimate moves. They were now extremely weak and posed no threat to Osborne.

However, Osborne had paid a heavy price for this.

His injuries were severe. He had countless broken bones. His body was covered in wounds, with blood gushing out everywhere.

He was also extremely weak now.

But he wasn't worried.

He suddenly focused on the second Medusa.

"As long as I can force her to use her ultimate move, I'll win this battle!"

With that thought, Osborne dragged his battered body and quickly charged at Medusa.

Medusa's expression changed dramatically.

"Ghost Petrification!"

Her eyes emitted a bright light as she tried to petrify Osborne, hoping to slow him down.

Unfortunately, it had no effect.

This basic petrification skill was now useless against Osborne.

"Quick, stop him!"

Medusa shouted, hoping the remaining two Titans and two Werewolves could keep Osborne occupied so she could escape.

But Osborne had his sights set on her.

He didn't care about the damage from the others. He relentlessly attacked Medusa, his eyes bloodshot as if he was out for blood.

Medusa was terrified now.

Osborne was like a madman, clinging to her. She couldn't escape or create any distance.

In the end, she had no choice but to use her ultimate move.

"Ashes of Death!"

Instantly, Osborne's body was petrified again. Medusa immediately tried to use this chance to create some distance.

But Osborne didn't give her the chance.

"Light of Redemption!"

After Medusa used her ultimate move, Osborne quickly cast Light of Redemption on himself.

A beam of light descended from the sky and entered his body, instantly undoing the petrification and healing his injuries. Even his spent strength and energy were restored to their peak.

Meanwhile, Medusa was now extremely weak.

Given the situation, there was no way Osborne would miss this opportunity.

He aimed a powerful punch at Medusa's head.

Bang!

Medusa's head exploded.

At this point, the remaining Werewolves and Titan, who still had some fight in them, stopped. Seeing Osborne back at full strength, fear filled their eyes.

How could they continue the fight?

The ten of them, all Class AAA, had ganged up on Osborne. Yet in such a short time, four were dead, and two were too weakened to fight.

With only four of them left, how could they stand against Osborne?

Hesitation and fear began to creep into their eyes.

"Hmph! As expected, you're all worthless! Half-baked products can't be relied on."

"Unfortunately, today you still have to die! You can consider it an honor to die by my hand!"

Osborne snorted at his words.

"You're confident? You're just Class AAA. What are you so proud of? They weren't a match for me even when they teamed up, and you think you can kill me?"

Luca chuckled. "I told you, don't compare me to those worthless things! They're just half-finished products, at best Class AAA in name only.

"I'll take you down with ease!"

A massive axe radiating terrifying power.
