Chapter 222

Charles walked out of the room without looking back.

He had barely taken three steps when Mr. Belly hurried behind him.

“Hey man!”

He paused.

Mr. Belly came rushing at him. He stopped in front of Charles and immediately bowed deeply.

“I’m sorry,” he said, “I truly apologize for Raphael’s behavior.”

Charles blinked, then waved it off calmly.

“It’s nothing.” Charles gave a faint scoff. “We just have a little ruffle. He thinks I’m here for retaliation or some dirty scheme.”

Mr. Belly let out a tired breath. “Honestly,” he muttered, “I didn’t even take it personally. I understand how much of a troublemaker he can be.”

Charles nodded.

For a moment, Mr. Belly hesitated, as if swallowing something heavy in his chest. Then he finally spoke, voice softer.

“I’m really grateful for what you did.”

Charles glanced at him.

“Saving my daughter… that was not something anyone else dared to do. I will pay you handsomely for it.”

Charles was about to reject it but mr. Belly stepped closer, lowering his voice.

“But… if you do me a favor…I will pay you even more. Much more.”

Charles frowned, suspicion flickering.

“A favor?” he repeated. “You don’t even know me.”

He added.

“And you were the only one who didn’t accuse me of causing the fire. How can you trust me to ask for a favour”

Mr. Belly scoffed,“About the fire… I already know who did it.”

Charles’s eyes narrowed.

“You do?”

Mr. Belly nodded, firm.

“You shouldn't worry about that, the person will be horribly punished.”

Charles felt a strange twist in his stomach.

He knew who did it but was acting like he didn't all along?.

He steadied himself and asked,

“Then what is this favor about?”

Mr. Belly’s voice dropped even lower. “I want you,” he whispered, “to tail Gabriella’s fiancé.”

Charles’ brows pulled together. “Raphael?”

Mr. Belly nodded once.

Charles stared at him. “That one? Why would you want me to do that?”

Mr. Belly found a more quiet place for them and laid down everything.

The engagement between Gabriella and Raphael had never been about love.It had been arranged almost twenty years ago, long before Gabriella even understood what marriage meant.

Back then, Gabriella had been born crippled, and worse, with a heart condition so fragile that any moment could have been her last.

Doctors predicted she would die young.

That was when the Trisian family approached.At the time, they were nothing compared to him.

Mr. Belly was already wealthy beyond measure. Snyx World was already rising. The Trisian were nothing.

They claimed they had methods, treatments and resources that could keep Gabriella alive. They even swore they could one day make her walk.

But there was a price. They wanted sponsorship and a very big Investment plus Access to Synx World’s wealth and influence.

And more than that… They wanted Raphael to marry Gabriella. His only child, that would inherit everything.

Which meant that one day, Raphael wouldn’t just marry into the family. He would take over the company.

And with it, the Trisian family would own everything Mr. Belly had built.

Mr. Belly knew exactly what it meant.But when the choice was his daughter’s life or his empire, he chose her.

He accepted this.

And in truth, Gabriella didn’t die young.

She survived. But even now, the heart problem remains. The Trisian family always claimed they were treating it. Also, she wasn't even able to walk.

Mr. Belly’s face tightened. “I feel they are deceiving me and only after my money,” he said quietly. “So I want you to find that out.”

Charles scoffed at this. Mr. Belly continued.

He couldn’t just call off the engagement. He couldn’t break the contract either, not without proof. The Trisian family had wrapped everything in signed papers.

Without evidence, he was trapped. And worse…Gabriella was truly in love with Raphael.

But Mr. Belly had always suspected Raphael was sinister, that he was only playing the role of a perfect fiancé until the day he could take everything just as his family planned.

Charles listened, making sense of everything.

The Trisian family’s rise was all built on Mr. Belly’s wealth.That was why they were so powerful now and popular.

And suddenly, Charles understood. why Godfather wanted him close to Gabriella.

Godfather wanted the Trisian family destroyed at the root. If Mr. Belly stop sponsoring them, they will financially crumble.

Charles scoffed faintly. As usual, Godfather's mission always had reasons. He stayed silent, lost in thought.

Mr. Belly misunderstood the silence. He thought Charles was refusing.

So he added quickly, “I’m asking because Gabriella said you are also a Cultivator. Like them. You have power.”

His voice lowered.

“If you can help me find anything… anything at all…” He swallowed. “I will give you twenty billion dollars.”

Charles lifted his head sharply.
