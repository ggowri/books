Chapter 230

Gabriella shook her head violently after she was told everything, and also listened to the records.

“No. No. My Raphael couldn’t do this,” she said through tears. “He would never.”

Mr. Belly’s face was heavy with pain. “I was shocked too. But this isn’t speculation. These are recordings. Clear recordings. They back everything up.”

He paused, then added quietly, “They only want us for the money. And I’m sure they don’t care about your leg either. They can't do anything about it”

That broke her.

Gabriella covered her face and cried harder, her shoulders shaking as she sat in her wheelchair.

 “Raphael is the only person I’ve ever had,” she sobbed. “He takes care of me. He stayed with me.  He is the only one I have. How could he do this? I can’t believe it.”

Mr. Belly knelt in front of her. His voice trembled. “The Trisian family has always been like this. They never loved you. They only wanted to use you. They even said they would kill me after the marriage.”

A sharp knock sounded at the door.

Mr. Belly straightened. “Who is there?”

The door opened without an answer or permission.

Raphael walked in.

“It’s me,” he said with a grin.

Then his eyes landed on Charles.

The grin vanished.

His jaw tightened. “What are you doing here?” he snapped. “I told you never to come here again.”

Fear flickered beneath his anger. He thought Charles had told them about Serena. He had no idea he was even more than that.

Gabriella looked up, tears streaming down her face. “No,” she said weakly. “You tell me. What are you doing here?”

Raphael forced a smile and walked toward her. “To check on you of course.”

Gabriella cried harder. “Fuck you,” she shouted. “You acted sweet because you wanted our wealth. You played with my feelings.”

Raphael stepped closer. “Wait,  what happened? What has this fucker told you. Just listen to me.”

Charles moved and blocked his path.

Raphael’s eyes burned. “Move out of my way,” he growled. “Or I swear I will kill you this time.”

Mr. Belly stood up slowly. “You don’t have to pretend anymore.”

He reached for the device and played the recording to him. Which was unmistakably his voice and every horrible thing he had said that day. 

“Fuck”, He muttered under his breath. 

Raphael laughed harshly and shook his head.

“What the fuck is that?” he snapped. “How could you let this no-name man ruin our relationship? That wasn’t me.”

He scoffed and spread his hands. “What if it was AI? You know there are a lot of fakes these days.”

Charles tilted his head slightly. “Really?”

He stepped forward and held up another device. “Then what about this?”

He played the video of the footage. 

The room fell silent as the footage ran. Raphael’s voice.  Clear and Undeniable.

Raphael’s skin flushed. Heat rose up his neck. His jaw tightened so hard it looked like it might crack.

Gabriella let out a broken sob. “How could you be so horrible?”

Raphael rushed to her. “Baby, I love you. That was taken out of context.”

It was an obvious lie.

Gabriella looked at him through tears. Her voice trembled with hurt and anger. “Can you just leave? You disgust me.”

Mr. Belly stepped forward.

“With this evidence, and everything you have said,” he said coldly, “our relationship ends today. You breached the contract from your side. You have been deceiving us the whole time.”

He paused, his eyes steady. “I won’t sue. I won’t collect compensation. But I and the Snyx World will stop funding the Trisian family. We are cutting all ties.”

Raphael’s face twisted with rage.

That decision would destroy him. His father would almost kill him for this.

He sneered. “No way, old man. You aren’t stopping shit.”

He leaned forward, eyes dark. “Do that, and the Trisian family won’t hesitate to put you down.”

Gabriella stared at him in shock.

She had never seen him like this. The switch was instant. The sweetness is gone. What stood there now scared her.

Charles scoffed. “You are not going to do anything.”

That pushed Raphael over the edge.

“Fuck you!” Raphael snapped. “You caused all this shit. You caused it. And you are going to pay for it.”

Charles didn’t flinch. “All I did was expose you and your clan’s little secret.”

Raphael turned to Mr. Belly, forcing a cruel smile. “Look, this doesn’t change anything. The Trisian clan will always own you. Don’t even try that shit.”

Mr. Belly stepped forward, unshaken. “No. Fuck that. We made your existence matter. You were above the other clans because of our funding.”

His voice hardened. “Your clan was so stupid they never built a real income source after all these years. You squandered every cent we gave you. Now tell me, how will you survive without us.”

That did it.

Raphael snapped. His eyes burned as he turned back to Charles. “Look… I can’t wait to kill you for this.”

He smiled, cold and vicious. “The Cultivator Arena will be opened to us tomorrow. Not next week anymore.”

Raphael now confident he could beat Charles after their little fight.

He added, “I will embarrass you. In front of the whole Cultivator world”.

Charles scoffed and said, “okay, tomorrow”.

Raphael said, “why don't we make the bet more spicy, if I get to win. I will get that your bitch store, and also, the Belly Family stay with us, and keep funding us!”.

Charles scoffed, and said, “I'm not the owner Of the company, why would I bet it”.

Raphael stated with a smirk, “You seem like family to them already”, He faced Mr. Belly, “What do you think, if this your new son-in-law gets to beat me, we will never disturb you again, and break the deal peacefully. And if he doesn't, the deal continues”.
