Chapter 215

Everyone was amazed by how this was turning out.

Jimmy let the silence stretch, then dipped his hand into his inner pocket and pulled out a flash drive.

“And this,” he said, holding it up, “is the Pandora Box. Where every information we talked about contain”

A ripple ran through the room.

“I personally safeguarded it under Mr. Moretti’s direct instruction,” Jimmy continued. “Only one successor was authorized to receive it.”

He looked straight at Serena.

“I also confirm, without reservation, that Mr. Moretti authored the document,” he added calmly. “We drafted it together.”

Collins shook his head. “That’s impossible. You all lying, you are fabricating things”

Jimmy reached into his pocket again, this time pulling out his phone. “As a precaution, Mr. Moretti requested a video attestation while signing the document ”

He tapped the screen of his tablet and turned it toward the room.

The video played.

Mr. Moretti appeared on screen, seated, composed, unmistakably alive in that moment. His voice was steady, deliberate.

“I, Alessandro Moretti, in full mental capacity, appoint my daughter, Serena Moretti, as the next CEO of Moretti Enterprises and head of the Moretti family. This decision is final.”

The video ended, everyone went mute.

This kind of recording was known legally as a video testamentary attestation—a lawyer-witnessed video confirmation used to reinforce written directives. Nothing can dispute it.

Jimmy lowered the phone. “So,” he said evenly, “does anyone have anything else to say?”

Mr. Gerald and Collins stared at each other.

There was nothing left for them to say.

Jimmy stepped forward and placed the document into Serena’s hands. Then he set the flash drive gently on top of it.

“With this,” he said, “you are officially the CEO!”

Mr. Valentino straightened and then bowed for Serena also.

“I promised your father,” he said solemnly, “that I would serve and protect you, no matter the cost.”

Serena’s breath caught. She hadn’t seen this coming. Her eyes softened and she smiled as he collected the file.

“Thank you,” she said. “I truly appreciate it.”

Then her expression changed.

She turned to Mr. Gerald.

“Why are you still sitting there,” she asked coldly. “Stand up. Now.”

Mr. Gerald swallowed hard. The room watched as he rose, humiliation written all over him, and stepped aside.

Serena walked past him without a glance. She reached the head of the table and sat down majestically.

Mr. Gerald remained standing.

Serena looked around the room.

“As you’ve all witnessed,” she said calmly, “I am the rightful CEO and the head of the Moretti family.”

She rested her hands on the table.

“I am not taking this position for personal gain. I am here for the growth of this company and the future of this family. I will take it to the next horizon.”

Her gaze swept across every face.

“I expect your full cooperation,” she finished. “And I believe you will give it.”

The room stayed silent.

Serena leaned back slightly, her fingers resting against the arm of the chair.

“Well,” she said,“my first policy starts now.”

The room stiffened.

She looked around slowly. “I’m raising everyone’s salary by twenty percent.”

For a split second, no one breathed.

Then the murmuring exploded.

“Did she just say twenty percent?”

“Is she serious?”

“That’s… that’s huge.”

Serena raised her hand, and the room quieted.

Mr. Moretti paid every member of this family because he believed we were meant to build the company together. That is why there was no WILL for split Inheritance.

Serena continued “Yes. I will increase everyone salary”

Relief spread across several faces.

“But…That raise only applies if you actually contribute,” Serena said evenly. “You will hold your role. You will do the work that role requires and will earn what you’re paid.”

Groans rippled through the room.

Because everyone knew the truth.

When Mr. Moretti was alive, many of them barely showed up. They collected salaries, used influence, and contributed nothing.

Serena leaned forward. “Let me be clear. You don’t work, you don’t get paid. No contribution, no raise.”

The murmuring returned, darker this time.

“I knew it.”

“She’s already tightening the leash.”

“She’s going to make things unbearable.”

Serena scoffed softly, then she turned her head.

Her gaze locked onto Collins and Mr. Gerald

“And now,” she said, “ yes, you both have to face the consequences of your behaviour!”

The room went still again.

She faced Mr. Gerald

“You forced my father’s burial without informing me or consulting me,” Serena said coldly.

“You betrayed him. You tried to seize this family and this company while the investigation is going on! You don't deserve to be in this family, and for that, your income in this family will be seized for 6 months!!”

Mr. Gerald couldn't believe this, “What! Six months, how are you expecting me to survive?”.

Mr. Valentino chimed in, “Really? You still have the audacity to babble. She is even graceful, I would have sent you away from the family!”.

Mr. Gerald's lips hit each other. He was seriously fuming,but he did not utter any word as Serena gazed at him.

“You have anything to say?”, Serena asked him.

Mr. Gerald just stayed mute and didn't know what to say.

Then she looked back at Collins alone.

“You were banished from this family once before,” she said. “and you come back”.

.

“For you to be accepted back into this family,” she said calmly, “you will kneel. Right here. And you will apologize to me. for everything you have done.”

A stunned silence followed.

Collins let out a short, disbelieving laugh. “You want me to kneel for you? I'm your brother, remember?”

“Yes,” Serena replied without hesitation. “A brother that nearly killed me. That is nothing compared to what you did.”

Collins slowly stood up.

His face hardened.

“I would rather die,” he said coldly. “I will leave this family, just like you want.”

He paused, eyes locked on hers.

“But don’t think this is over. I won’t back down, I will do everything I can to drag you down. Whatever it takes. Even if it's meant killing you”

What!.

Did he just threaten to kill her?

She leaned back in her chair, utterly composed. “Is that a threat?”

Collins smiled darkly. “Take it however you want.” With that he turned around, grabbed Tracy's hand and stormed out of the room.
