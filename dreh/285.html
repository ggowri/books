Chapter 286

Charles scoffed again and shook his head slowly.

“So you are accusing me because of the clothes I’m wearing?”

The woman folded her arms and lifted her chin arrogantly.

“Anyway,” she said coldly, “someone dressed like a street beggar has no right touching me.”

The young man immediately stepped forward beside her, puffing out his chest like a hero arriving at the perfect moment.

“You heard her,” he said loudly. “Move away from him this instant. I will protect you all and teach this guy a lesson.”

Natasha was the first to respond.

She tilted her head slightly and looked at him with calm amusement.

“I don’t think he can do that. Touching someone inappropriately?. He can never do it!”.

The young man blinked.

“What?”

Natasha shrugged lightly. “I said I don’t think he can”

His pride flared instantly.

“Of course I can,” he snapped. “Just look at him.”

He pointed at Charles with open disgust.

“This guy will never get a woman in his entire life. No one would want to be with a useless, dirty, stinky pauper like him.”

A few people in the crowd nodded.

The young man continued confidently.

“So obviously he’s finding cheap ways to please himself.”

Lizzypie scoffed loudly.

“Really?”

“Yeah,” the young man replied smugly. “My ladies, that man is a loser. Unlike me, I treat women well.”

Sapphire had been quiet until then.

But now she stepped forward.

Her expression was calm, but her eyes held a dangerous sparkle.

“Really?” she said slowly. “Because he actually has women in his life.”

She paused.

“And I’m one of them.”

The young man frowned instantly.

“What… what are you saying?”

Before he could process it, Sapphire moved.

She stepped behind Charles and wrapped her arms around him from the back, hugging him comfortably like it was the most natural thing in the world.

“I said he has women in his life,” she said sweetly. “And I’m one of them.”

The young man’s eyes nearly popped out.

“That… that doesn’t make any sense!” he stammered.

He looked Sapphire up and down like his brain was trying to solve an impossible equation.

“A perfect… Aphrodite-like woman like you… with this pathetic loser?”

His voice cracked slightly.

“What does he even have on you?”

Sapphire scoffed.

“In fact,” she said confidently, stepping closer to Charles, “I’m crazy in love with him.”

The crowd erupted in whispers.

“What the hell?”

“Did she just say that?”

“How is someone that hot with that guy?”

“What’s going on here?”

The young man’s face twitched as his confidence started crumbling.

“Mmm… mmm…” he muttered, trying to regain control.

Then his expression hardened again.

“Anyway,” he snapped at Sapphire, “you are just a slut.”

He turned to the other women quickly.

“Look, don’t be like her—”

“What are you saying?” Lizzypie cut in sharply.

Then she walked straight to Charles, took his hand, and lifted it to her lips before kissing it softly.

“I’m his woman too.”

The young man froze.

“Uh… what… what is happening here?” he stammered.

Natasha smiled sweetly.

“Oh, didn’t you hear?”

She leaned her head on Charles’ shoulder.

“I’m his slut too.”

Gabriella raised her hand casually.

“Same here,” she said with a playful grin. “I’m also his.”

The entire beach seemed to gasp at once.

Whispers exploded through the crowd.

People in the crowd began whispering louder, the shock spreading like wildfire.

“What’s going on here?”

“All four of them… are his?”

“That’s impossible.”

“But why would they lie about something like that?”

Eyes moved back and forth between Charles and the four women clinging to him so naturally.

It didn’t look staged. It didn’t look forced. Natasha’s arms were still wrapped around him from behind, Sapphire stood confidently beside him, Lizzypie still held his hand, and Gabriella leaned casually close as if she belonged there.

The young man’s girlfriend gulped, clearly struggling to process it.

“You four… are sharing one man?” she asked, disbelief dripping from every word.

Her eyes moved across their bodies, then landed on Charles again.

“This… this stinky, dirty man? All of you are sharing him?” She shook her head repeatedly. “I can never believe this.”

The young man beside her nodded aggressively.

“Yeah,” he added, still trying to recover his confidence. “Make it make sense. This is insane.”

Natasha rolled her eyes.

“Hey, look,” she said calmly, “we really don’t care what you think.”

She tightened her arms slightly around Charles as she spoke.

“But yes. We are sharing one man.”

The statement landed heavily.

The young man gulped.

His eyes slowly moved across the scene again, noticing something that made his stomach twist with envy. Every single one of them was touching Charles in some way. A hand on his arm. A shoulder leaning against him. Fingers intertwined with his.

All four of them.

Damn.

For a brief moment, he wished he was Charles.

The realization made him even angrier.

“I… I don’t care what he has over you,” he said quickly, his voice rising again. “Maybe he’s blackmailing you. Maybe he has some leverage over you all.”

He pointed angrily at Charles.

“But I don’t care. He still has to kneel and apologize for what he did to my girlfriend.”

His girlfriend stepped forward immediately.

“Fuck yeah,” she snapped. “He has to apologize.”

Charles didn’t even blink.

“I won’t repeat myself again,” he said calmly. “I’m not doing that.”

He took a step forward and looked directly into the young man’s eyes.

“And if you dare touch me again, it won’t be only your fingers I will twist this time.”

Then Charles turned, clearly done with the entire situation. But the young man shouted behind him.

“Wait!”

Charles paused.

“You are dead today,” the young man said, his voice cold now. “I didn’t want to cause trouble before.”

His eyes burned with anger.

“But you made me.”

He pulled out his phone quickly and dialed a number.

When the call connected, he barked into it.

“Come right here. Now. Right now!”

People nearby could hear the rage in his voice.

He ended the call and slipped the phone back into his pocket, then looked at Charles with a sinister smile.

“You are finished,” he said. “I will show you what it means to mess with the mayor’s son.”

That was when the whispers in the crowd exploded again.

“Wait… did he say mayor’s son?”

“Yeah… fuck… that’s Johnson.”

“The mayor’s son?”

“Oh no…”

“The wicked mayor…”

“That man is finished.”

Some people even shook their heads sympathetically toward Charles.

“He should just apologize.”

“Him fighting the mayor’s son? That’s suicide.”

Hearing the murmurs made Charles pause.

The information sparked a small interest in him.

Instead of leaving, he slowly stopped walking.

Then he turned back and decided to stay.

He wanted to see what would happen next.
