chapter 131

Wilson and Norman roared at the same time, voices cracking.
“FATHER! Why are you bowing to him?”
“Father, get up! Are you mistaking him for someone else?”
But Mr. Eden and Mr. Jason didn’t lift their heads.
They bowed even lower.
Mr. Eden said, “Please… our sons are gullible and reckless. They don’t understand anything. Forgive them, Mr. Charles. We will compensate you for every damage done. Just… spare us.”
Mr. Jason repeated, “Please forgive them, Mr. Charles.”
Norman’s face twisted in disbelief. “Father… why are you doing this? This is CHARLES! He’s nothing. A low-life! He broke my hand! Why are you bowing to someone like him?”
Wilson stumbled forward. “Father… are you pranking us? Stand up. Stop embarrassing us. This is ridiculous!”
Their fathers didn’t move.
Then Mr. Eden snapped his head up and roared:
“YOU BOTH SHUT THE FUCK UP RIGHT NOW!”
The entire lobby shook from his voice.
“Get down on your knees,” he ordered, veins visible on his neck. “Kneel. And apologize to Mr. Charles. Right now.”
Norman froze.
Wilson’s eyes widened. “Father… I’m not kneeling for this nobody. This doesn’t make sense—”
But they had no idea.
Before coming here, Charles had used his final emergency request from the Godfather.
He asked the Godfather to notify Mr. Eden and Mr. Jason who he truly was.
They were instructed not to mention Charles’s true identity out loud.
Just to acknowledge … and obey him.
The moment they got the message, they rushed here before their sons could do something stupid.
And now they were kneeling for survival.
Mr. Jason looked at Norman, face red with rage. “If you refuse to kneel right now, I am disowning you on the spot. Don’t test me.”
Norman shook his head, trembling. “Father please, what are you saying—”
Mr. Eden turned to Wilson, voice low and murderous. “Wilson… I swear, if you don’t kneel to Mr. Charles this second, I will put a bullet in your leg myself and throw you out of this family.”
Wilson stepped back. “Father… what—what are you talking about…”
Mr. Eden marched toward them with the fury of a man who had swallowed humiliation for years. Before Wilson or Norman could react, he slapped both of them hard.
Smack!
Smack!
The sound echoed through the lobby.
“Do NOT question us!” he barked. “Do it. Now!”
Wilson and Norman froze.
Their fathers had never hit them in public before.
But Mr. Eden’s face was twisted with pure rage. Even the guards stepped back.
Norman whispered under his breath, “Charles must have gotten to our fathers… he must know one of their darkest secrets… he must be using it against them.”
Wilson nodded quickly. “That’s the only explanation. There’s no other reason they would bow to him.”
They were both shaking now — but they knew better than to argue. Humiliation burned through them as they slowly sank to their knees.
Charles watched quietly, not blinking.
Wilson and Norman never imagined there would be a day they would kneel for this man.
They thought he was bluffing when he said it before.
But here they were.
Broken pride.
Shaking hands.
Knees on the cold floor.
Surrounded by their own men watching them fall.
Mr. Eden shouted, “Now apologize to him! Loudly! Apologize for your stupidity!”
Wilson felt his stomach twist. Norman wanted to disappear into the floor. Their faces were red, their bodies trembling with humiliation.
Mr. Jason snapped, “Now!”
Both men panicked and shouted at the same time:
“W-We’re sorry!”
“We apologize for our stupidity!”
“Please forgive us, Mr. Charles!”
Their voices cracked, Everyone stared, and stunned.
The great Titan Heir and the Real Consortium Heir were kneeling and begging like schoolchildren.
Mr. Eden bowed again and said, “Please… we will punish them accordingly. Please spare us.”
Charles didn’t waste time. “I’m not here for drama or a show. I’m here for the money he owed. The damages he caused.”
Both fathers nodded immediately.
“Oh—yes, yes, of course,” Mr. Eden said quickly. “If that is all, please follow us. This way.”
Their politeness shocked everyone.
Even the sixty gunmen behind them exchanged confused looks.
The men who were ready to kill Charles minutes ago now parted to let him pass like he was royalty. 
 Wilson and Norman were still on their knees. After their father's has gone upstairs,
they slowly stood, their faces burning with humiliation.
Wilson clenched his fists. “I swear… I will kill Charles myself. He definitely has something on our fathers. That’s the only reason they bowed.”
Norman nodded. “Yeah. He thinks he’s cool now. Our fathers are just pretending. They want to play smart with him. They’ll get him later. Watch.”
They had no idea how wrong they were.
---
Upstairs, in the executive room
Mr. Eden and Mr. Jason acted like servants.
They gathered cash, even opened several safe vaults. In the end, they prepared a total of 10 million dollars and placed it on the table.
Mr. Eden’s hands trembled slightly. “Mr. Charles… is this enough? If not, we can add more. We can double it.”
Charles checked the amount and nodded. “It’s enough.”
Both men exhaled in relief.
Charles added, his voice cold, “Now warn your sons. If they cross me or my loved ones one more time… I won’t spare them.”
“Yes, yes, we understand,” Mr. Jason said instantly. “We understand… Godfather.”
Charles frowned. “I’m not the Godfather.”
Mr. Eden swallowed hard. “No… but you are his grandson. That alone means you are a Godfather by blood.”
Both men lowered their heads again.
