Chapter 294

Collins gulped, but quickly forced a confident smile back onto his face.

For a moment the tension dissolved as several people around the hall began praising him.

“Ohh… that’s impressive.”

“If he really landed a deal with Akeem of Dubai, that’s huge.”

“I should connect with him.”

A few businessmen even stepped closer, already imagining potential opportunities.

“That kind of partnership could change everything.”

Tracy’s face softened with pride as she looked at Collins.

Meanwhile Charles quietly stepped forward.

“But something confused me,” he said calmly.

The room slowly quieted again.

Charles looked directly at Collins.

“Yesterday I overheard you saying you were leaving the country by boat.”

He paused and added.

“More like you were running away.”

The words dropped like a stone in the room.

Immediately whispers spread again.

“Running away?”

“What is he talking about?”

“Why would someone with a massive Dubai deal run away?”

Tracy frowned.

She looked between the two men.

“Charles… what are you saying?” she asked slowly. “Running away?”

Charles nodded slightly.

“Yes. I heard it clearly.”

Tracy turned toward Collins.

“You’re running away?” she asked sharply. “Why?”

Collins’ face hardened instantly.

“Why the hell are you lying?” he snapped at Charles. “You must be jealous.”

Charles didn’t react.

Instead he asked calmly,

“Do you even know Akeem of Dubai at all?”

For a brief moment Collins’ heart skipped.

He knew immediately.

Charles knew.

To buy time, Collins discreetly pulled out his phone and typed a quick message under the table.

You can come in now.

Meanwhile the whispers grew louder.

“What is Charles implying?”

“Is he saying Collins is lying?”

Collins suddenly raised his voice confidently.

“Of course I know Akeem from Dubai!” he declared.

Charles tilted his head slightly.

“Really?”

Then he spread his hands casually.

“Why don’t you call him right now? In front of everyone.”

The room erupted with agreement.

“Yeah!”

“Call him!”

“Let’s see the truth.”

Collins felt sweat forming behind his neck.

Still, he forced a confident laugh and raised his phone.

“Fine,” he said loudly. “I’ll call him.”

He pointed at Charles.

“And when it turns out I’m telling the truth, you’ll kneel and apologize to me in front of everyone.”

Charles nodded without hesitation.

“Definitely.”

Now the entire hall had gone silent.

Everyone watched.

Collins pretended to dial a number and held the phone to his ear, praying the person he texted would arrive quickly.

Seconds passed.

The tension grew thicker.

Then suddenly—The main doors of the hall burst open.

“YES! That’s him! That’s him!”

Two girls rushed inside breathlessly.

They were pointing straight toward Charles.

Everyone turned to look at them in confusion.

The two girls rushed forward and stopped directly in front of Charles.

One of them pointed at him angrily.

“Why the hell did you run away without paying us?” she shouted.

The entire hall froze.

Charles stared at them, confused.

Then he recognized them.

They were the same two girls from the lounge the other night—the ones he had helped out of that messy situation before leaving quietly without saying much.

His eyebrows furrowed.

“What are you talking about?” Charles asked calmly.

One of the girls scoffed loudly.

“Oh please, don’t pretend now,” she said.

The second girl crossed her arms.

“You carried both of us, we satisfied you all night, and then you disappeared without paying us.”

A wave of shock spread through the hall.

“What?!”

People stared at Charles with disbelief.

Even the women beside him looked surprised.

Serena frowned.

Natasha’s eyes narrowed.

Sapphire looked at the girls carefully.

Charles himself looked genuinely confused.

“What?” he said again.

The first girl stepped closer and pointed at his chest.

“Don’t play dumb with us,” she snapped. “You picked us up as hookups and refused to pay us.”

Gasps spread across the crowd.

Several people began whispering.

“So that’s the kind of man he is? These girls are underage!”

“He fuck girls!”.

“He hires girls and runs away without paying?

“That a pedophilia act, I want to vomit right now”.

“That’s disgusting.”

Collins, who had been sweating moments ago, suddenly relaxed.

A slow smile crept across his face.

Well, well.

The situation had just flipped perfectly in his favor.

He folded his arms and watched the scene with amusement.

Tracy, however, looked stunned. She couldn't believe what she was hearing as she stepped forward. 

“Charles. What are they saying? You hire girls for hookups?”.

In fact, the ladies were surprised to hear it, because they knew Charles wouldn't do that. 

Natasha was the first to step forward, “Hey. You better know what you are saying. This is a big accusation!”.

Sapphire chimed In, “Charles can never do that. Girls?. That's crazy!”.

Charles finally stated. “I had no idea what you girls were saying, you know false accusations can land you in jail!”.
