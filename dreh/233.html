Chapter 233

The next day arrived quickly. Charles was already dressed, fully prepared to head to the arena.

Lizzypie and Sapphire insisted on following him. Even Gabriella added that she wanted to go as well. Charles immediately refused, telling her it was too dangerous and that she should stay behind for her own safety.

Gabriella shook her head. “No. I can’t just stay at home and wait.”

Lizzypie moved to her side, placing her hands on the wheelchair handles. “She will come with us,” she said calmly. “Don’t worry. I will protect her.”

Just then, Natasha walked in, dressed up as boldly as ever. She smirked and said, “Well then, let’s go watch that brat get his ass whooped.”

Charles scoffed. This fight was no joke, his women would be watching. His dignity depended on it.

Meanwhile, the arena was already packed. It was hidden deep underground beneath an ordinary store, built solely for secret duels.

Only cultivators or those with invitations could enter. The place buzzed with energy, voices overlapping, powerful figures filling the space. Big men arrived one after another.

When Raphael showed up, almost everyone rushed to greet him, bowing slightly, showing respect and awe. His father was there as well. Mr. Jacob, the owner of Trisian himself, an old man in his seventies, He walked with a supporting stick but imposing in presence.

Voices echoed around Raphael.

“I can’t wait to see you beat that man. I heard he disrespected you.”

“Break his legs,” another added.

“Break his bone, humiliate him!”.

Raphael listened calmly, his expression relaxed. He simply nodded in response promising he would do it all.

Just then, Charles arrived at the arena hall. Unlike Raphael, he showed up alone at first.

The crowd went quiet for a brief second, then murmurs broke out. Fingers pointed in his direction.

“I don’t even know him,” someone scoffed. “And he wants to duel Raphael? He’s dead already.”

Another laughed. “He’s a nobody. Look at him. He came alone.”

Before the mocking could continue, a calm voice rang out. “Who said he’s alone?”

Lizzypie stepped out behind Charles. As always, her presence drew instant attention. Conversations halted. Eyes widened.

“Wow…”

“She’s fucking beautiful.”

“Does he even know her?”

Before the whispers could settle, Sapphire appeared, then Natasha, and finally Gabriella in her wheelchair, all positioning themselves behind Charles.

The arena stirred violently.

“Wait… all these women are with him?”

“How is that even possible?”

“How did he bag them?”

Another voice dropped in disbelief. “Even Miss Gabriella? I thought she was Raphael’s fiancée.”

Raphael’s expression darkened instantly. He strode forward, fury rolling off him. He stopped in front of Charles and sneered.

“You fucking leech,” Raphael spat. “Living off women. Look at you. Not even one male supporter.” His eyes swept over the ladies behind Charles. “I’ll humiliate you in front of them. I’ll cripple you, make you useless, then I’ll take every single one of them.”

That did it.

Charles’ eyes hardened. He stepped forward slightly. “I’ll humiliate you in front of your hundred supporters,” he said coldly.

A heavy voice sounded from behind Raphael.

“I know that one day,” the voice said slowly, “you will step into a problem far bigger than you.”

They all turned toward the incoming speaker.

It was Norman.

He was being pushed forward by two handlers, his movements stiff but deliberate. The crowd stirred as he drew closer. Norman stopped beside Raphael and pointed straight at Charles.

“This is the bastard,” Norman said coldly, “and those are the bitches who ruined me.”

Raphael raised a brow. “Ohhh.”

Norman then faced Charles, his eyes burning. “I told you I would come back for you. Your day ends today because you mess with the wrong person!”

Raphael smiled faintly and said, “Don’t worry. I will make sure he ends up just like you.”

Charles scoffed. He looked directly at Norman. “I’m disappointed in myself,” he said calmly. “You can still walk. After I beat Raphael up, you are next.”

Raphael smirked. “Well, the fight will start soon. Let see if you can still keep on bluffing"

His gaze slid to Gabriella, lingering for a moment, before he turned and walked away.

Just then, another voice sounded.

“Well, we met again.”

Everyone turned to see Lord Hierem and his son, Jeremy. Lord Hierem stepped forward and offered Charles his hand. Charles took it.

“I can’t believe you challenged Raphael,” Lord Hierem said. “The best young cultivator of this generation. I can’t wait to see how this turns out.”

Charles said nothing. Lord Hierem smiled and walked off toward his seat, leaving Jeremy behind.

Jeremy’s eyes locked onto Lizzypie. He smirked. “I will make sure I get you after this dead-brain boyfriend of yours gets himself killed.”

Lizzypie scoffed. “Are you still hurting? Charles will defeat Raphael, and you will do nothing.”

Jeremy smiled thinly. “We shall see.” Then he turned and walked away.

At that moment—gong!

A bell rang loudly as a voice boomed across the arena.

“Now that the two contestants are here, the fight shall begin!”.

Charles took a slow breath and prepared himself.

Sapphire stepped forward first. “You will win.”

Natasha crossed her arms and smirked. “The victory is already yours.”

Lizzypie met his eyes and nodded. “Come back standing.”

Gabriella spoke last, her voice softer but firm. “If you see that you can’t hold up… don’t force it.”

At that moment, Mr. Belly entered.

“FATHER!” Gabriella excitedly said.

He smiled, greeted her and also greeted the women, then turned to Charles.

“You can do this,” Mr. Belly said. “But if Raphael starts winning, tap out. Don’t take unnecessary injuries.”

Charles smiled. “I will be okay.”

Then he stepped forward and entered the arena ring.

The space was enormous, wide enough for a full battle. Seats surrounded the ring on all sides, packed with spectators watching closely.

Charles stopped at the center. Raphael stood opposite him.

Raphael suddenly ripped off his clothes, revealing his muscular body. He flexed deliberately.

The crowd erupted.

“Beat him!”

“Don’t spare him!”

“Break his bones!”
