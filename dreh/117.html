Chapter 117

Charles hauled Serena into his arms instantly, screaming her name as she collapsed on the dusty floor.

“Serena!! Serena!!”

The bullet had hit her directly in the chest.

Blood poured out fast, soaking her shirt, spilling over Charles’ hands. She coughed violently, puffing out blood with each breath.

Charles shook her lightly, panicking.

“No, no… stay with me. Serena, look at me, you are going to be fine. You hear me?”

But Serena kept choking on blood, her body trembling weakly.

Behind them, the leader scoffed.

“Well. The bitch we wanted dead is already dead.”

Charles slowly lifted his head, his entire face stained with Serena’s blood, his eyes burning with rage.

He glared at the leader with pure hatred.

The leader smirked wider.

“Haha… what are you going to do? Nothing. You are finished. And now I will kill you too. You should have accepted my offer when I was being nice.”

He raised his hand and gave a sharp signal toward the corner.

“Sniper! Finish him!”

…

Nothing happened.

The leader frowned.He signaled again.Still nothing.

“Huh? Where the hell is that bastard?” he barked.

THUD!!

A body dropped from above and slammed onto the center of the room.

Everyone flinched backward.

It was the sniper.

Dead and his neck snapped.

Before anyone could react, someone moved at the entrance. Just then, a figure walked in calmly.

“Who the fuck are you?” one thug shouted.

No answer.

Suddenly, dozens of figures stepped in behind him.

The entire room froze.

The thugs turned around and saw that the entire building's front, back, side, staircases were now filled with heavily armed mercenaries. Each thug had at least two rifles pointed at his head.

They were wearing dark tactical gear, heavy boots, bulletproof plating, advanced weapons not normal guards.

More than a hundred of them.

The thugs were overwhelmed instantly.

The sound of multiple safeties clicking echoed loudly.

TK TK TK TK TK TK

The sound of a helicopter spinning its blades thundered from outside the compound.

The pressure in the room shifted, everyone now understood this wasn’t just an ambush.

This was a full-scale professional operation.

The first figure walked further inside.

Under the dim light, his face became clear.

It was Rain.

The leader’s smirk vanished instantly.

His face drained white.

Everyone recognized him. Rain, the right hand of Godfather. Because the Godfather never showed his face, Rain was the one everyone saw, the one they feared.

And in this city, there was not a single soul in the underworld who didn’t know his name.

The leader stammered, trembling:

“M–Mr… Mr. Rain… what—!?”

His men murmured in pure panic.

“Godfather’s men…? Here?”

“Why is he here—?”

“We are doomed… we are dead…”

Rain’s gaze swept across the room  before landing on Charles, who was kneeling on the dusty floor, holding Serena tightly in his arms as blood soaked through his clothes.

The leader instantly panicked.

He spun toward his men and shouted at the top of his lungs.

“DROP YOUR WEAPONS!! NOW!! YOU FOOLS!! DROP EVERYTHING!!”

Every thug obeyed instantly, guns clattering on the ground like rain.

Rain didn’t speak. He didn’t need to.

The fear in the room was enough that the thugs were commanding themselves.

The leader forced an awkward smile as he approached Rain with trembling legs.

“Sir… I–I’m Macho. Please, how can I be of help—”

BOOM!!

A gunshot exploded in the quiet room.

Blood sprayed.

A body hit the floor.

Rain had shot him clean in the forehead without even blinking.

Gasps filled the entire room, terror choking the air.

Just like that. With no warning.

All the other thugs froze, eyes widening in absolute horror. Including the other two.

A heartbeat later, they collapsed, every single one dropping to their knees at once, slamming their foreheads down so hard their skulls echoed.

"Mercy! Please, spare us!"

"Forgive our ignorance! However we have offended the Godfather, we will repay a thousand times over! Just let us live!"

They were shaking like leaves in a storm.

Rain didn’t even spare them a glance.

He walked straight toward Charles.

“Young Master,” he said, giving a slight, respectful bow. “Are you injured?”

The thugs' faces went sheet-white. Their jaws hung slack.

One of the kneeling men suddenly vomited onto the floor.

"Young Master... Godfather..." a thug stammered, the words dawning on him with horrific clarity. "Oh god... we didn't know! We're blind! We're fools!"

Charles looked up, he didn’t look at the thugs. His hands shaking as he held Serena’s unconscious body against his chest.

“Please… save her,” Charles breathed heavily. “Take her to the hospital.”

Rain knelt and quickly examined the wound.

“It’s a gunshot,” he said calmly. “Nothing serious. We have a potion that can close the wound and heal her fast.”

Charles blinked in shock. His grandfather had magic potion medicine too?

Rain signaled to two mercenaries, who immediately rushed forward.

“Carry her to the medical van. Don’t heal it fully, just reduce the bleeding and pain. Leave the wound visible, so she does not suspect supernatural interference.”

“Yes, sir,” they bowed and lifted Serena carefully.

As they rushed her out, Charles stood up 

When Serena said they were coming here, Charles already sensed something was off.

He didn’t trust the situation.

So he informed Lizzypie earlier, before they even entered the hideout that he might need the  Godfather help requests.

When they surrendered, guns pointed at them. Charles used his phone’s quick dial to send the emergency signal to Lizzypie. That was the reason Rain arrived in time.

Charles wiped Serena’s blood off his hands slowly, then lifted his head and stared at the trembling thugs still kneeling.

His voice was calm, deadly calm.

“You… two. Come forward.”

Two of the thugs that attacked him immediately scrambled up, practically tripping over themselves as they rushed forward and knelt right in front of him, shaking.

Charles pointed at the first one.

“Your name.”

The thug gulped hard.

“L–Liam, sir… but everyone calls me Reaper.”

Charles turned to the second.

“You.”

“Zander… but I’m called Hazard.”

Charles’s eyes narrowed.

“I’m going to ask only once…you won’t even see the next second.”

Both thugs nodded violently.

“Yes—yes sir! We will talk!”

“We will tell you everything!”

“I’m listening,” Charles said coldly.

Reaper swallowed and then shouted in fear,

“It was Mr. Damien!!”

Zander backed him up instantly, voice cracking,

“Yes!! It’s fucking Mr. Damien!! He paid us to kill her!!”

Charles’s face dropped into a deep frown.

Mr. Damien. He knew that name all too well.

Serena’s uncle.

Mr. Moretti’s blood brother.
