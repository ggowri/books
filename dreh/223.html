Chapter 223

Charles was slightly amused.

The way Mr. Belly said twenty billion like it was pocket change told him everything.

This man wasn’t just rich. He was strikingly rich.

The kind of wealth that could build empires… and also build monsters like the Trisian family.

Mr. Belly nodded. “I will even give you a bonus.”

Charles didn’t react much. The money was insane, but that wasn’t what pulled his attention. It was the scale of power behind it.

He kept his face calm and said, “So you want me to tail Raphael until I find evidence that proves they’re deceiving you.”

Mr. Belly answered immediately. “Yes.”

Charles nodded. “And what if I don’t see anything?”

Mr. Belly’s expression didn’t change. “You will.”

His voice was firm.

“I am sure.”

Then he added, “But this is only a thirty-day job. Thirty days. If there’s anything hidden, it will show.”

Charles scoffed lightly and nodded. “Alright. I will do it.”

Mr. Belly’s face lit up. “Really?”

“Yes,” Charles said simply. “I will.”

Mr. Belly quickly said, “I will pay half now—”

Charles shook his head. “No. We will talk about payment after I find something.”

Mr. Belly froze, genuinely surprised. Then his eyes softened.

“I’m… truly grateful,” he said. “Thank you so much.”

They exchanged contacts.

Charles told him he would inform him if anything came up. And with that, they split.

×××

After Charles reached home, he told Lizzypie everything.

Even she looked genuinely surprised that the Trisian was living under Mr. Belly.

Lizzypie exhaled. “If you find anything, the Trisian family will crumble financially. They only look big because they have money to maintain that image. Without it, they fall.”

Charles agreed. “Yeah.”

Then he added, still a little shocked, “That man said billions like it was nothing.”

Lizzypie shook her head. “I wonder how Mr. Belly even amassed that kind of wealth.”

Charles leaned back. “The issue now is how to do the tailing without being caught. Raphael is a high-level Cultivator. He might notice me.”

Lizzypie nodded. “You’ll have to get close to get anything useful. That’s the dangerous part.”

She paused and added. “I will do more digging to look for ways”

Charles scoffed lightly. “That’s why I love you.”

Lizzypie waved it off. “Nothing for you tonight, no matter how you flatter me.”

She already knew where his mind was going. Charles only smiled. The mission failed.

The next day, in the afternoon, Lizzypie came back with results.

Raphael had a pattern. He always went to a high-level bar in the city. Lizzypie checked the surrounding CCTV footage.

“He visits there every day,” she told Charles. “Every single day”

Charles’ eyes sharpened. “Then we might get something.”

Lizzypie nodded once. “Yeah… so let's get started”.

Charles was about to speak when a knock came at the door.

He paused.

“Uh… are we expecting someone?”

Lizzypie nodded. “Yeah.”

Charles frowned, still confused, and walked over to open it.

The door swung open to find Sapphire standing there.

She smiled brightly. “Hi, Charles.”

She looked even more striking than before. Her figure was sharp, confident, almost distracting.

Charles’ eyes lingered on her sexy hip, legs, tight to her big breast. Sapphire was truly endowed.

Sapphire raised an eyebrow, amused. “Instead of staring,” she teased, “you can just grab it. I won't mind”

Charles snapped back to himself. “Uh… wow. Sapphire.”

He stepped aside and let her in. “Sorry”.

Sapphire scoffed, “You shouldn't be, it all belongs to you”.

Charles gulped hearing this. He then asked carefully, “Why… are you here?”

Lizzypie answered instead.

“I called her.”

“She’s helping us with this mission. She will be the one to find a way to get close to Raphael.”

Sapphire smiled.

Lizzypie added bluntly, “Even if she has to throw herself at him.”

Charles’ eyes widened. “What?!”

Charles’ voice rose instantly.

“What are you saying? Get close to Raphael?”

Lizzypie nodded like it was obvious. “Yes. We need someone who can get near him without suspicion.”

Charles sneered. “Near him for what?”

“To tap him,” Lizzypie said simply. “To hear what he says, who he meets, what he’s planning. Only Sapphire can help us do that.”

Charles’ jaw tightened.

“You know how dangerous Raphael is,” he snapped. “What if he finds out?”

Sapphire smiled, calm and steady. “Actually, we already talked about this. I’m willing to help. I can do it.”

Charles shook his head immediately.

“No,” he said firmly. “You can’t.”
