Chapter 201

 

Charles gulped as he looked around, his grip tightening for a split second before he forced himself to relax. 

His eyes moved fast, scanning the circle of guns, the distance between each man, Natasha’s position, the leader’s breathing. He was calculating. 

He was just about to make his move when a loud voice cut through the tension.

“What the hell is happening here?”

The words hit like a command which made everyone freeze.

Even the men pointing their guns hesitated.

Charles turned his head.

Walking toward them was a young man, clearly younger than Charles, with messy curly hair and a face that still carried a trace of boyish innocence. 

He didn’t look tense. If anything, he looked annoyed, like someone interrupted mid-meal.

Everyone immediately bowed.

“Young Master Jeremy!”.

The man still trapped under Charles’s arm immediately shouted, “This fucker came here and started causing a ruckus!”

Jeremy stopped a few steps away and looked at Charles. They stared at each other for a moment.

Then the young man spoke, his tone casual.

“So… you can’t capture and kill him?”

He said it like it was nothing. With that, Charles knew he was just another bray. 

The leader gritted his teeth. “He seems to have super power.”

Hearing that, Jeremy scoffed softly. “Oh? Really?”

He stepped closer, eyes roaming over Charles with open curiosity.

“Tell me,” he said calmly, “what are you looking for here in the Hierem clan, showing off your power like this?”

Charles released the leader, who immediately coughed.  Then Charles stepped forward to the man. 

“I’m just here to meet my friend,” he said. “Lizzypie.”

Hearing the name, Jeremy’s expression shifted. It was brief but Charles didn’t miss it. Which confirm he knew lizzypie. 

He didn’t wait for a response.

“Call her out,” Charles said firmly. “I want to talk to her.”

Jeremy let out a short laugh. “And why should I do that?” he asked. “Who do you think you are?”

“I told you,” Charles replied. “Her friend.”

Jeremy scoffed and nodded in realization. “You must be that fool who didn't let her come here all those months ago.” His smile turned sharp. “You two must have been badly in love for you to come here for her. ”

Natasha was just observing things. 

Jeremy leaned forward slightly. “Sadly for you,” he continued, “she belongs to me now.”

Charles’s eyes hardened. “What the fuck do you think you are saying?”. Getting angrier. 

Jeremy’s smile faded. “Maybe I should remind you,” he said slowly, “you are in the Hierem clan.”

He took another step forward. “You won’t like what happens to you if you don’t tread carefully.”

“So what?” Charles shot back. “Hierem clan, or whatever the fuck you call yourselves. Call out Lizzypie.”

Jeremy raised a brow. “Or what?”

Charles didn’t hesitate. “I will force my way in.”

Jeremy burst into laughter. Several of the guards joined him, the sound sharp and mocking.

“Hahaha. Is that bravery,” Jeremy said, wiping his eyes, “or pure stupidity?”

He spread his arms slightly. “You won’t even be able to get past me. Talk less of my men out here, and the ones inside.”

“I don’t care,” Charles said. “I will fight my way in. You are all nothing.”

His way of speaking made Jeremy more mad.

Jeremy’s face twisted. “We are nothing?” he repeated slowly. “I think that little power you have is confusing you.”

His voice dropped, sharp and cruel. “By the time we’re done with you, you will be on your knees, begging for death.”

Charles smiled coldly. “Small power,” he said. “If that’s what you want to call it. But with it, I can beat you and all your men easily.”

He intentionally said that so he could  provoke him. 

It worked.

Jeremy’s eyes burned. He was not a man who tolerated his authority being challenged. Especially not in front of his people.

“You are bluffing!. And asking for death" Jeremy snapped. “If that’s what you think, why don’t we have a one-on-one spar?”

The courtyard went still for some moment. 

Charles didn’t even let the moment cool.

“Fine,” he said immediately. “Let’s do it. If I beat you, you call Lizzypie out for me.”

A murmur rippled through the guards.

“And if I lose,” Charles continued calmly, “I will leave this place without asking any questions.”

Jeremy laughed darkly. “Fuck you. You think you can just walk away after I whoop your ass?”

He leaned forward, voice dripping with malice. “If you lose, I will  have both your feet cut off. You will drag yourself out of here.”

Charles scoffed. “If that’s what you want,” he said, “then I will bet on that.”

Everyone couldn't believe he agreed to that. 

“What?”

The guards stared. Some of them whispered under their breath.

“Is he crazy?”

“Doesn’t he know who he’s challenging?”.

Even Jeremy hesitated for a brief moment, clearly caught off guard by Charles’s level of confidence.

Behind them, Natasha snapped.

“Charles, no!”

She rushed forward and grabbed his arm, pulling him back hard.
