Chapter 285

The man who caught her steadied her easily and pulled her closer, one arm still around her shoulders. He was young, probably in his mid-twenties, tall and lean with the kind of polished look that came from money and confidence.

His hair was styled perfectly, his sunglasses expensive, his beach shirt open just enough to show a sculpted chest. His face had that smug, bratty sharpness that made it clear he was used to getting his way.

He looked down at the woman clinging to him and then slowly lifted his eyes to Charles.

“Is that true?” he asked, voice already hard. “Did you really touch my girlfriend?”

Charles sighed.

“Your girlfriend here bumped into me,” Charles said calmly. “She was about to fall. I helped her. That’s all. She’s lying.”

The young man didn’t answer immediately. Instead he looked Charles up and down slowly, his gaze dragging over the damp rag on his body, the sand stuck to the fabric, the worn look of the cloth.

Then his lips curled.

“You?” he said with disbelief. “You, with those rag clothes? You’re stinky, dirty, completely out of place here and you dare touch my woman?”

His voice grew louder with every word.

“You freak. You pervert.”

Charles frowned slightly. “Calm down. Don’t talk to me like that.”

The young man laughed, but it wasn’t a pleasant sound.

“Look at you,” he continued, spreading his arms as if presenting Charles to the small crowd that had gathered. “What are you even doing here? This place is for real men. For people who belong here.”

He stepped closer and pointed at Charles’ chest.

“Don’t tell me you actually thought my girlfriend would fall for you. With that dirty, stinking rag you’re wearing?”

A few people in the crowd chuckled.

The young man shook his head with mock pity.

“I know your type. Cheap tricks. Pretending someone fell so you can grab a woman’s body.”

Charles’ jaw tightened. He said, “Your girlfriend is not even all that to go through such stress. she is ugly as fuck!”.

This made the woman convulse so hard.

The young man turned slightly toward the people watching and chuckled again.

“Look at him acting like he has some kind of woman waiting for him somewhere.”

Then he looked back at Charles with open ridicule.

“I bet you have never even seen a woman’s panties in your entire life,” he said cruelly. “Tell me, who would ever open their legs for someone as useless, dirty, and ragged as you?”

The words rippled through the crowd.

Charles took a step forward.

His eyes were calm, but there was steel in them now.

“The women I have,” he said slowly, “your girlfriend is absolutely nothing compared to them.”

He said it deliberately.

And it worked.

The young man burst out laughing loudly.

“Shut the hell up,” he sneered. “Stop bluffing. Women?... the only woman you will ever get in your life is ”

He shook his head, still laughing.

“Anyway, enough nonsense. I want you to kneel down and apologize to my girlfriend this instant.”

Charles looked at him without the slightest hint of submission.

“If that’s what you’re looking for,” he said evenly, “then you’re wasting your time. I’m never going to kneel and apologize for something I didn’t do.”

The words landed like a slap.

The young man’s face darkened instantly. His jaw tightened and the smug arrogance on his face twisted into anger.

“Hey,” he snapped, pointing a finger at Charles. “It seems like you don’t know who I—”

Charles cut him off mid-sentence.

“I don’t fucking care, man.”

The blunt interruption stunned him.

Charles continued calmly, his voice firm and flat.

“I’m not apologizing. In fact, if anyone should apologize here, it’s your pathetic wife for spilling my drinks.”

Then the young man exploded.

“You bastard!”

He lunged forward, fist flying toward Charles’ face in blind rage.

But Charles moved first.

The moment the punch came, Charles stepped aside and grabbed the man’s wrist in midair. His grip was firm, controlled, and before the young man even realized what was happening, Charles twisted.

CRACK!

The sound was sharp.

Charles bent his fingers backward at a painful angle.

“YAAAAAA!” the man screamed instantly.

The cry was loud enough that several people on the beach turned their heads.

“My finger! My finger!” he howled, dropping to one knee as Charles released him.

He clutched his hand like it was on fire.

“You’re dead today!” he shouted between gasps. “You hear me?! You’re dead!”

Charles simply stood there, calm, unmoved, his posture relaxed as if nothing serious had happened.

It was at that moment that four figures rushed toward the scene.

Lizzypie, Natasha, Sapphire and Gabriella.

They had seen the commotion from their spot near the water and hurried over immediately.

The moment the young man saw them approaching, something strange happened.

He stopped crying. Completely. As his eyes widened.

The pain in his face suddenly competed with pure astonishment.

These ladies were stunning.

Sexy as hell.

Each of them walked with confidence, their bikinis hugging their perfect bodies under the golden sunlight. Their skin glowed, their curves flawless, their presence impossible to ignore.

The young man gulped.

He had never seen such beauty gathered in one place.

Even his girlfriend noticed the way his eyes widened.

Instantly he straightened his posture, forcing himself to look composed despite his injured fingers.

He smoothed his hair, cleared his throat and put on a charming smile.

“Hello, beautiful ladies,” he said smoothly.

He gestured casually toward Charles.

“I’d advise you to stay away from that guy. He’s been trying to use cheap tricks to touch women’s bodies.”

He shook his head like he was doing them a favor.

“He’s a freak. A pervert.”

Lizzypie frowned slightly and glanced toward Charles.

Charles scoffed.

“I helped your girlfriend from falling down,” he said flatly. “And this is the story he’s telling.”

The moment Lizzypie heard that, she and the others immediately understood what had happened.

But they didn’t reveal they knew Charles.

Instead, they played along.

The woman stepped forward dramatically, placing a hand on her hip as she glared at Charles.

“It’s all a trick,” she said loudly. “He wanted to touch my perfect body. Just look at him.”
