Chapter 241

Ivan’s face darkened completely.

He rose slowly from his kneeling position, the ring box still in his hand. His polished image began to crack under the weight of humiliation.

“Why are you embarrassing me?” he sneered. “Why are you doing this to me?”

Caroline  didn’t flinch. “The one who caused this is you. Deal with it.”

With that, she turned to walk away.

But Ivan suddenly reached out and grabbed her wrist tightly.

Caroline  spun back toward him, anger flashing in her eyes. “What are you doing? Let go of my hand this instant.”

His grip tightened.

“Do you think you can just disgrace me like that and walk away?” he said through clenched teeth.

Caroline  tried to yank her hand free. “Just let me go.”

“No,” Ivan snapped. “Either you accept my proposal… or else.”

“Or else what?” she shot back.

His voice dropped dangerously low. “Or I tell my men to bundle you up and carry you with me.”

Caroline  froze for a split second.

This was Ivan. Entitled. Used to getting whatever he wanted. And he had a dark reputation. He had once being charged of rape before. 

Her heart started racing because she knew he might actually do it.

She began struggling harder, twisting his wrist. “Let me go! Let me go this instant! What are you doing?!”

He didn’t release her.

Caroline  turned desperately toward the crowd. “Help me! He’s going crazy because I rejected him!”

The crowd hesitated. Cameras were still recording. But no one moved.

Ivan’s pride had completely shattered now.

“That’s it,” he barked coldly. He pointed toward three of his men. “You. And you. Bundle her up.”

The three men in black suits stepped forward immediately.

As the three men stepped forward—

“Wait!”

A voice rang out.

Then—

Bleeeeeew.

A violent gust exploded across the scene.

Everyone squinted as thousands of roses lifted off the ground at once. The carefully arranged carpet shattered instantly, petals swirling wildly into the air like a red storm. Ribbons tore loose. Decorative stands toppled. Loose petals smacked against cars and suits and faces.

For nearly three full minutes, the wind howled.

It didn’t feel natural. It felt directed.

When everything finally settled and the last petals drifted down, the once-perfect rose carpet was completely ruined.

And standing at the center of the scattered mess—Was Charles.

He stood calmly amidst the chaos, one foot slightly forward. He had only used a leg sweep, but he had infused it with his power. Just enough to destroy everything.

The crowd erupted into shocked murmurs.

“What the hell—”

“Who is that guy?”

“What did he just do?”

“Did he just ruin the rose carpet?”

“Does he even know who he just offended?”

Ivan stared at the destroyed roses, fury rising visibly in his face. His jaw twitched.

“Who the fuck are you?” he barked. “How dare you ruin the roses?”

Charles replied evenly, “Because it was useless. The lady you did it for didn’t want it.”

Gasps spread again.

Ivan’s eyes burned. “Hey, fucker. This is none of your business. I will forgive you this once. Leave my presence. Right now.”

Charles didn’t move.

“I will leave,” he said calmly. “But not without her. Let her go.”

Ivan scoffed loudly. “What the hell are you saying? Let her go? Who do you think you are?”

Charles sighed faintly. “She’s cornered. It’s obvious she doesn’t want you. You should have been confident she would accept before pulling all these stunts. Now you are using the public to pressure her. Just let her go.”

“Shut the fuck up!” Ivan snapped. “What do you know? Who the hell are you to interfere?”

Charles opened his mouth to respond—

But Caroline  suddenly shouted,

“He’s my boyfriend!”

The crowd erupted in shock.

“Ms. Caroline?!”

“Her boyfriend?”

“Who is he?”

Whispers spread like wildfire. Ms. Caroline was known everywhere in the city. Beauty, elegance, status. For someone unknown to suddenly claim her like this—it stunned everyone.

Ivan blinked slowly, disbelief written all over his face. “Wait… what?” He pointed at Charles. “He is your boyfriend?”

Caroline didn’t hesitate. She rushed to Charles, grabbed his hand tightly, and leaned into him.

“Yes. Of course,” she said firmly. “He’s the one I love. So please, stop everything.”

Ivan shook his head. “No. No, I can’t believe this.”

Charles played along smoothly. He slipped an arm lightly around her waist. “Yeah. She’s my beloved girlfriend. Don’t disturb her again.”

The crowd buzzed louder.

Ivan’s eyes narrowed. “I will ask you again. Who the hell are you?”

“It doesn’t matter, just let her go man” Charles replied calmly.

“Yes, it does,” Ivan snapped. “I have done everything to capture her heart. I spent millions. Everything here costs at least fifty million dollars!”

Charles shrugged slightly. “Well, I spent no dime. You don’t have to buy love, you earn this. All this paparazzi can't get you one.”

A few people in the crowd murmured at that.

Ivan let out a cold smirk. “I know your type. This poverty mentality. ‘Money is nothing.’ only poor, useless, pauper says that!. Who is even your father? Where are you from?”

“I’m nobody,” Charles said plainly.

Ivan burst into loud, mocking laughter. “I knew it! A fucking nobody!” He gestured at the destroyed roses. “You have nothing. So how did you end up with Caroline? Are you threatening her with something?”

“For God’s sake, Ivan, stop!” Caroline snapped. “I’m not being threatened!”

Ivan’s expression hardened. “So you’re seriously telling me—you reject me? I give you fifty million dollars’ worth of surprises and you choose nobody?”

Charles stepped forward slightly, his gaze steady.

“This nobody,” he said quietly, “can do more than that.”

The confidence in his voice made several people fall silent.

Ivan stared at him, studying him more carefully now. He gazed at Charles and glanced at him from head to toe.

Then, he burst into laughter, “What the fuck is he saying!. No one, I mean, absolutely no one in this city can pull what I did. A 50 million dollar surprise!. But you… who can't even name his father claimed he can do more than that. Stop dreaming”.

Charles put on a smirk and stepped forward,  “Should we put that into test!”.
