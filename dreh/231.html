Chapter 231

Charles didn’t answer immediately.

He looked at Raphael, then at Mr. Belly, his jaw tight. “I’m not agreeing to that,” he said. “His wealth has nothing to do with me. I can’t bet something that isn’t mine.”

Raphael scoffed loudly. “Just as I thought.”

He turned to Mr. Belly with a mocking grin. “See that? Your new son-in-law is scared of losing. A coward.”

Mr. Belly laughed coldly. “No, he isn’t.”

He stepped forward, his voice firm. “And I’m sure he’s going to beat you. So, I will take the bet,”

The room froze.

Raphael’s eyes widened for a split second before excitement flashed across his face. “You really mean that?” he asked. “If he loses, our deal continues?”

Mr. Belly nodded without hesitation. “Yes. And if you lose, you leave us the fuck alone. Forever.”

Raphael smirked. To him, this was an easy win. “Good,” he said. “Then we will make it official. A formal document. Signed.”

Mr. Belly met his stare. “Fine. I will be expecting it.”

Raphael’s smile widened. He glanced at Charles one last time, slow and smug, then turned and walked out.

The door shut behind him.

Charles immediately faced Mr. Belly. “Why would you agree to that?” he asked. “That was reckless. That is a stupid bet”

Mr. Belly smiled calmly. “Because I believe in you.”

Charles frowned. “You don’t even know how capable I am. I could lose. I could be beaten.”

Mr. Belly shook his head. “You entered the fire for my daughter. I know you can do more than that.”

“You will lose everything, if I lose also. This is crazy”, Charles said.

“And I will easily claim my fortune back if you win”.

Charles let out a slow sigh.

He then looked at Gabriella. She gave a small shrug, her eyes steady despite everything. “I believe in you too,” she said softly.

Charles exhaled again.There was too much at stake now. He had to win.

Charles nodded slowly. “Okay. I’ll do the best I can,” he said. “But I can’t guarantee a win.”

Mr. Belly smiled gently. “Even if you don’t win, it’s fine. You have already done more than enough for me. You are worth far more than that.”

Charles looked at him for a moment. He could see it clearly now. Mr. Belly was a good man. Too good. And it was obvious why someone like him could be deceived so easily by the Trisian family.

Mr. Belly hesitated, then spoke again. “I’m sorry, but I want to make one more big request.”

Charles raised his eyes. “What is it?”

Mr. Belly took a breath. “Can you take my daughter with you. Let her live with you for the time being.”

“Huh?” Charles froze.

Gabriella looked up sharply. “Father—”

Mr. Belly lifted a hand to stop her. “I have my reasons,” he said firmly. “The fire was a direct attack on Gabriella. I know who did it, and I’m afraid they will ry again soon. The Trisian family might also make a move.”

His voice lowered. “She needs to be somewhere safe. And I can’t think of anyone safer than you.”

Charles swallowed hard. He turned and looked at Gabriella for a long moment, then said, “Well… that won’t be an issue, if she doesn’t mind.”

He met her eyes. “Do you mind?”

Gabriella shook her head quickly. “I don’t mind at all.”

Charles nodded once. “Okay. I will do it.”

Mr. Belly let out a relieved breath. Then he said, “Give me your account number. I will send you two hundred billion.”

Charles frowned immediately. “That won’t be necessary.”

Mr. Belly blinked. “What?”

“I don’t need your money,” Charles said calmly. “I’m doing this out of goodwill. That’s all.”

Gabriella stared at him, her heart skipping. How could a man be like this?, He was so perfect, and she instantly fell in love with him.

Even Mr. Belly was stunned.

He had just turned down two hundred billion!!!.

Mr. Belly gulped and said, “Please, sorry for asking, who are you really”. He believed that for someone to turn down 200 billion dollars easily, he must be worth more.

Well, that's true. But, Charles didn't disclose his identity, he smiled and said, “I'm just an orphan”.

Mr. Belly showed his condolences, and apologized for the questions.

After that, Gabriella prepared to leave with Charles.

Mr. Belly prepared everything she would need, her luggage and everything. They entered the car, with Charles carrying Gabriella in his arms and placed her gently on the seat. Her heart kept beating.

They bid their farewell, and drove away.

Charles, who was driving, keen his eyes on the road when Gabriella asked.

“Can I ask you… why are you willing to go all this length for me and my father. You appear in my life for the first time to save me from fire. Also, my father trusts you so easily”. She stated.

Charles scoffed and just said, “It… Fate”.

Gabriella just frowned and nodded to this, just then she asked, “Are you married?”.

This caught him off guard and said “uhn?”. He turned his head at her.

Turning his head was what made him see it, because from the side, was an upcoming van, driving directly at them at full speed. So fast.

And no doubt, It was targeting them, to hit them.
