Chapter 226

Raphael carried Serena off the floor.

Serena was still conscious but her body wouldn’t obey her. 

Her limbs felt heavy and numb, her vision blurred at the edges. Raphael’s grip tightened as he lifted her with ease, strength humming beneath his skin. He smirked, because he used his super power to make her like that.

“You came asking for help,” he said quietly. “Nothing is free.”

He laid her on the bed, slow and deliberate.

“You help me,” he continued, “and I help you.”

His hand moved to his jacket as he started unbuttoning himself.

CRASH!!!

The door didn’t just open. It tore off the hinges.

Three men flew into the room and slammed into the far wall, bodies twisting as they hit the ground hard. 

They only groaned, writhing in pain on the floor.

Raphael staggered back, shock flashing across his face.

It was Charles!. He stood in the doorway.

Raphael scoffed. “What the fuck are you doing here?”

Charles’ eyes were cold. “You disgusting pig!!!”

He wanted to storm at him.

More men rushed him at once. Charles met them head-on. One went down with a single strike. 

Another tried to grab him and got thrown across the room. A third charged and Charles dropped him before he could land a hit. This showed how very angry he was.

If he wasn't around, Serena would have got raped

On the bed, Serena forced herself upright. Her head throbbed but her mind was clearing. She slid off the mattress and steadied herself against the wall.

Raphael straightened slowly, rolling his shoulders as Charles glared at him. 

“Looks like we don’t need an arena anymore,” Raphael said with a grin and confidence. “We can do this right here.”

Serena stumbled forward and grabbed Charles’ arm.

“Let’s go,” she said urgently. “Please. Let’s go. I'm okay now”

Raphael laughed. “Your bitch is telling you to run. Will you chicken out?”

Charles’ jaw tightened, this made him more mad. His fists clenched.

He didn’t look away from Raphael as he told Serena.

“Get out. Now. I will meet you outside”

Serena shook her head, gripping his arm harder.

“No,” she said. “Charles, stop. You don’t have to fight him. I’m fine now. I swear.”

But Charles was determined. He wanted to use this opportunity to figure out how powerful Raphael was. Also, he was fed up with how he was acting like he owned the whole street. 

He finally turned to her. His voice softened but his expression didn’t.

“I will deal with him. Please. Go. Right now.”

Serena knew that look on Charles' face. There was no changing his mind.

She hesitated for one last second, then nodded and ran for the door. She decided to call the police 

Raphael laughed as she disappeared into the hallway.

“Deal with me?” he said mockingly. “You know what. I will beat you without using my super power.”

Charles scoffed. “Good. Let’s keep it that way.”

They charged at each other. 

Raphael swung first. Charles blocked it and drove a punch into his ribs. 

Raphael staggered back, surprised. He came again, faster this time, but Charles met him head-on.

Thier Fists collided. The room shook with the sound of impact.

Charles pushed him hard, slammed him into the wall, then followed with another hit. Raphael tried to counter and missed. Charles didn’t. He hit him again and again.

Raphael stumbled across the room and barely stayed on his feet. His smile was gone now. He couldn't even catch his breath as Charles was beating him.

Charles stepped forward, controlled and focused, pressing him without mercy. 

Every move Raphael made, Charles answered it. He knocked him down once. When Raphael got back up, Charles threw him aside like he weighed nothing.

Raphael stood and wiped his mouth that was seriously bleeding, his eyes burning with rage and humiliation. He didn't expect Charles to be this good. 

“This is impossible,” he growled. “I swear I will kill you”.

With that, he used his super power.  Then his body blurred. One second he was standing alone. The next, he split apart.

Four of him now stood in a wide circle around Charles.

Charles’ eyes narrowed and use his power also

[Eyes of Satan activated]

[X-ray vision activated]

Raphael smirked and said. “Like I said, I'm really going to kill you!”.

The Raphaels charged at him.  What was crazy was that each Raphael got a unique super power. 

One Raphael vanished in a blur, speed tearing the air apart.

 He struck from behind before Charles could fully turn. Charles blocked late and felt the force rattle his arms.

Another Raphael threw him a powerful punch.

A blast of raw power slammed into Charles’ side and sent him skidding across the floor.

Before he could recover, the other two lifted their hands and Charles found himself under control. He couldn't move. 

They were controlling him, using telekinesis.

Charles clenched his jaw and forced himself free, rolling just as another blast tore through the space where he had been. 

He managed to punch two of them, but the victory lasted half a second.

The speed-type Raphael hit him again. Then again. From the left. From above. From nowhere.

Charles tried to track him with his X-ray but the movements were too fast, too chaotic. His flash steps couldn’t create distance. 

A telekinetic strike caught him mid-air and slammed him down.

Thud!!!

He hit the floor hard.

Raphael’s laughter filled the room.

“Told you,” the voices said together. “You are not a match for me.”

Charles pushed himself up, breathing heavier now. He decided to go more crazy also.
