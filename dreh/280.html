Chapter 281

The hallway grew quiet after the man disappeared down the stairs.

Charles turned to the two girls. “Are you okay?”

They nodded quickly.

“Yeah,” the gum-chewing one said, though her voice shook slightly.

“We are fine, Thanks so much” the second added, rubbing her wrist.

“wow. You really deal with him, I'm so impressed. I can't thank you enough”

Charles studied them for a moment. He knew that they were underage. Their breathing was uneven, but they weren’t injured. He gave a short nod. “Okay then”

He turned to walk back toward his room.

A hand caught his sleeve.

“Please,” one of them said softly. “Help us.”

“please.  We need your assistance!”.

Charles stopped and looked at her. “Help you with what?”

They exchanged glances before the first spoke again. “We are not from this city. We came here for work. Someone tricked us. Took our money and duped us.”

The second nodded quickly. “We lost everything. Then that man tried to take advantage of us when we couldn’t pay for a room.”

Charles’ expression didn’t change.

“We just need somewhere safe to stay tonight,” the first continued. “Just one night.”

The other added, “You seem like a good person. You helped us. Please. We won’t cause trouble.”

Charles exhaled slowly. He can't just bring some strange girls into his room. 

“I’ll call the bartender,” he said. “He can lodge you into a room. I will pay for it”

Both of them shook their heads immediately.

“No,” one said quickly. “We… We have PTSD. We’re scared of being alone. After what just happened.”

“We don’t trust strangers,” the second added.

Charles raised an eyebrow. “You just met me today.”

They hesitated.

“Yeah,” the first said carefully. “But you look kind. And strong. You protected us.”

“Aren’t you a good person?” the other asked, almost pleading. “We just want somewhere safe.”

Charles stared at them in silence. He felt pity, he knew they were just underage girls. 

He sighed.

“Fine,” he said at last. “Just for tonight.”

Relief flooded their faces instantly as he opened the door wide. 

“Thank you.”

“Thank you so much.”

Charles stepped back and opened his door wide. “Come in.”

They entered quickly, murmuring gratitude as they passed him.

The door closed.

Outside the hotel, near the parking lot, the large man Charles had beaten was pacing furiously, clutching his swollen hand.

“What the hell is wrong with you?!” he shouted at Collins. “You didn’t tell me he was that powerful!”

Collins kept his voice calm. “I didn’t know.”

“Didn’t know?!” the man barked. “He broke my fingers like sticks!”

Collins glanced at the hand. “How bad is it?”

“Fuck you!”

The man kicked a nearby trash can, wincing from the pain. “I’m not doing this again. You better send me my balance”

“Yeah, I will,” Collins replied quietly.

 The man angrily left.

What Collins was planning was something very big that could drag Charles down. And that was accusing him of being a pedophile.

He sent the two girls, who were now on mission, to drink with Charles. Make him take off his clothes and they just have to snap themselves having fun with him.

With that, Charles would be done for. Because such crime is horrendous to society. 

Inside the room, the girls settled down.

Charles moved without fuss, pulling spare blankets from the wardrobe and handing one to each of them. 

He then walked to the small counter near the kettle and prepared two cups of coffee, placing them carefully on the table in front of the bed.

“Drink,” he said calmly. “It will help you relax.”

They thanked him and wrapped the blankets around their shoulders, fingers still trembling, though their eyes seemed more alert than before.

Charles leaned against the wall for a moment and studied them quietly. “What kind of work were you both doing in the city?”

The two girls exchanged a brief glance.

“Just some business stuff,” one replied vaguely.

“Yes, small business,” the other added with a forced shrug.

They scoffed lightly as if brushing the question aside.

Charles noticed the deflection but did not press further. If they were hiding something, it would surface on its own. He had no interest in interrogating them.

“You can sleep on the bed,” he said, gesturing toward it. “I will take the couch.”

Their expressions shifted again.

“Oh no, we can manage the floor,” one said quickly. “We do not want to inconvenience you.”

“Yes, you have already done too much,” the second added softly. “We do not want to make you uncomfortable.”

Charles shook his head. “It is fine. I was planning to sleep on the couch anyway.”

That part was true. 

“You are a very good person,” one of them said, her tone lowering slightly as she stepped a little closer. There was a subtle shift in her posture, a deliberate softness in her gaze. She was making an advance towards him. 

Charles caught it immediately.

He smiled politely and stepped back just as smoothly, creating distance without making a scene. “Get some rest,” he said evenly. “The sun will rise soon.”

There was finality in his voice.

He walked to the couch, lay down, and closed his eyes without another word.

Exhaustion swallowed him quickly. His breathing steadied within minutes.

The room grew silent. With that, he drifted to sleep.

After some hours of sleeping soundly. The girls sneaked out, and then whispered at each other.

“Let's do it quickly and leave before he wakes up”. With that, they began with their mischievous act.
