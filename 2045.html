"Not only do you possess the ability to resurrect after death, but you can also grant that same power to others?"

The God of Light turned to look at Osborne, her eyes gleaming with something akin to awe or perhaps calculation.

It was clear she wanted to say something, but each time her lips parted, words failed to come out. The idea seemed too monumental, too overwhelming to articulate easily.

Osborne smiled gently, sensing her excitement, "I know what you're thinking, God of Light. But the resurrection ability is limited. I can only bestow it upon a certain number of individuals. Plus, each resurrection requires a significant amount of energy, so..."

"I'll start gathering everyone from the Sanctum of Light right away! I'll also bring in some key figures from the Human Community, especially the young talents. I hope you can grant them the same resurrection ability."

"They must be people you can absolutely trust. If word about the resurrection ability gets out, I fear Lord Malevolence might take action against me."

Osborne aimed to enhance his cultivation level to influence the war's direction?

Such ambition!

Even she, in the grand scheme of things, felt like just a pawn.

But Osborne seemed intent on becoming the one pulling the strings.

That thought sent a shiver down her spine. The stakes were higher than ever, and the road ahead was fraught with danger.

The God of Light shook her head, unable to shake off her doubts about Osborne's ambitions. Despite his remarkable talent—one that rivaled even the God of Night and Lord Malevolence—his current cultivation level was simply too low.

The grand chessboard of this all-out war had only two players—the God of Night and Lord Malevolence.

For Osborne to rise to their level, he had a long, arduous journey ahead of him.

Even with his exceptional gifts, without a stroke of extraordinary luck, it would take him ages to reach such heights.

And time was not on their side.

A month was just around the corner, especially for someone like the Supernal Deity King.

No matter how hard he tried, he wouldn't see significant improvement in that short span.

This was another reason she doubted Osborne's chances.

But we also can't win too many, or the Paramount Supreme Clan might lose hope and decide to launch their war earlier than expected..."

"Hearing you lay it all out like that gives me hope! No matter what, I'll do my utmost to keep the tournament going for a full month!

Even if they fell in battle, he would have the means to bring them back.

The coming month would be his time to push himself to the limits and elevate his strength.

His destination? The deep space!
