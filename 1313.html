Terrence was brimming with confidence.

Osborne's tactics, in his eyes, were indeed perplexing. Were his opponents not of equal or higher standing, they would likely be powerless against Osborne.

Terrence even believed that Osborne could dispatch a Transcendent Deity warrior with greater ease than he could.

Tragically, for Osborne, Terrence was simply stronger.

Osborne's methods, though formidable, were manageable and even restrained by Terrence. The balance of power was decisively tipped in Terrence's favor.

In Terrence's view, any hope of Osborne overcoming him was nothing short of a fantasy!

Only a breakthrough to the Superior Deity realm stood a chance.

Osborne himself was muttering under his breath, "To defeat Terrence, it seems I must attain Superior Deity status.

"Terrence's strength far outstrips that of an ordinary Transcendent Deity warrior. My tactics, shattered and suppressed, offer no viable options against him. The only path left is a risky trade of injuries, relying on the regenerative, even revivifying prowess of the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity to outlast him."

Realizing this, Osborne shook his head.

Revealing the resurrection capabilities of the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity was a risk he was unwilling to take.

Despite his belief in Divine Shadow and God of Star's trustworthiness, the Cosmic Treasure's allure was simply too potent, even for Deity King warriors.

He dared not gamble with it.

Thus, Osborne's sole path to triumph over Terrence lay in reaching the Superior Deity realm!

For others, the leap from Intermediate Deity to Superior Deity was arduously slow, requiring immense time and energy painstakingly absorbed and refined from the environment.

But for Osborne, this was far simpler.

He didn't need to arduously draw energy from his surroundings; the energy of the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity would suffice.

With its advanced essence, Osborne required no further refinement. Simply absorbing the requisite energy from the Lotus would rightfully elevate him.

Nonetheless, even Osborne needed time to make this ascension.

Instantaneous progression was out of the question.

However, Osborne had another ace up his sleeve, a method to momentarily reach the Superior Deity level, if not Transcendent Deity.

A cold smile crept onto Osborne's face. "Terrence, you believe you've already won? You're counting your chickens before they hatch."

Terrence frowned at the provocation. "Do you have any tricks left? Lay them bare, but unless you can instantly ascend to Superior Deity, you stand no hope against me!"

Divine Shadow and God of Star, seeing the situation clearly, nodded in agreement.

Osborne was undeniably powerful, but the gap to overpower Terrence was evident.

Any victory would require Osborne's further enhancement.

After all, he only stood at the Intermediate Deity level.

"Instantaneous breakthrough to Superior Deity? That's impossible," Divine Shadow sighed, resigned.

Osborne merely smiled, "Instant Superior Deity, you say? Well, stranger things have happened.

"Watch closely, for the impossible shall now unfold.

"Superior Deity level, here I come! Break through!"

With his proclamation, Osborne's divine power surged violently. His energy aura rapidly intensified, akin to a volcanic eruption, growing stronger and more potent.

Everyone present could clearly sense the swelling power in Osborne, the rapid ascent from Intermediate Deity toward Superior Deity.

Terrence's eyes widened in shock.

So, too, were Divine Shadow and God of Star, their gazes locked upon Osborne in utter astonishment.

"Some kind of secret technique to unlock potential and boost power?"

Terrence's face was etched with grim determination.

He suspected that Osborne's rapid surge in power was due to a forbidden technique—one that tapped into, or even drained, potential for a temporary surge in strength.

Techniques like that usually came with a heavy price.

Terrence mused, "Is he really willing to go so far just to take me down? Tch, even if that's the case, there's no way I'm going down.

I have to survive!

As long as I live, the Deschanel Clan lives on. And as long as I live, there'll be a day for revenge!

If I get the chance, I need to capture him alive. If I hold him hostage, I can get out of here in one piece. Then, I'll vanish into Celestara!"

Unbeknownst to Terrence, the members of the Blood Demon squad surrounding him were swiftly losing their vigor.

They had all temporarily lent their strength to Osborne.

Each of them carried the roots of the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity embedded within their bodies—a curse that granted them immortality but at a cost. Through those same roots, Osborne could siphon a portion of their power.

With over four thousand eight hundred Blood Demon members contributing, even just fragments of their strength were enough to send Osborne's power skyrocketing.

Osborne's aura surged violently.

His power broke through the ceiling, shattering the boundaries of an Intermediate Deity.

Osborne was now temporarily a Superior Deity.
