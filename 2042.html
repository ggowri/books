"We're all in!

"Let's hope this works! If it doesn't, we'll have to brace ourselves for an all-out war. Who knows what will happen to the Human Community in Celestara then..."

The God of Light was a bundle of nerves, her heart racing with anxiety.

Yet, she maintained her composure, determined not to let Icy One and his allies catch wind of her distress.

But Icy One was not so easily fooled.

He smirked, sensing something was off. "God of Light, you seem a bit rattled. Ha! The Seven Young Deity Kings of the Human Community must be done for! Your panic is futile... there's no hope left for you!

"You know Icy Six's strength better than anyone. Even you—if Icy Six wanted to escape, you wouldn't be able to stop him! And as for those Seven Young Deity Kings?

"Sure, they've got talent, but they're still just Ascension One Supernal Deity Kings.

She retorted, "That's not a given! Osborne isn't just any ordinary genius! He has plenty of tricks up his sleeve.

"Maybe he's already met his end!"

Is it possible something unexpected happened?"

After all, Icy Six would never unleash this realm on his own.

It was Osborne who had created it.

The fact that the Eclipse Realm was still active meant one thing. Osborne was still alive.

Icy One's expression darkened in an instant.

"Damn that Icy Six! What a waste! He can't even handle a few mere Ascension One brats yet?" Icy One fumed internally.
