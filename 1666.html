Osborne's plan was straightforward. Using his spatial power and the Eclipsing Mantra, he intended to stealthily approach the seven peak Deity Overlord Deathborns alongside Diana and launch a surprise attack.

If everything went according to plan, he was confident he could take down one Deathborn, while Diana would likely be able to severely wound another.

That would leave them with just five peak Deity Overlord Deathborns. With Diana holding two of them at bay, Osborne believed he could dispatch the remaining three in short order.

If all went well, they could eliminate the seven Deathborns and quickly harvest the three Miracle Elixirs before Angelo and his team even had a chance to react.

But no plan survived contact with the enemy.

Osborne had thought that with his current spatial power and the Eclipsing Mantra to mask his energy signature, the peak Deity Overlord Deathborn wouldn't detect his approach.

Unfortunately, fate had other plans.

Just as he and Diana were closing in on the Deathborn, the humanoid stone figure suddenly trembled.

A surge of energy erupted from the stone, sweeping across the entire platform. Osborne was stunned to find that his spatial power had inexplicably failed.

What was supposed to be a stealthy ambush turned into a very public reveal as he and Diana materialized right in front of the Deathborn.

"What the hell is happening?"

Osborne gasped, staring in disbelief at the stone figure.

Until now, he had thought it was just an oddly shaped rock, completely unaware of its true nature. Who would have guessed that it could unleash such a force that disrupted his spatial power?

His space elemental essence was already at Level Seven, a feat typically reserved for Deity Kings.

Even other Deity Kings, without special means, wouldn't be able to force him out of parallel space.

Yet here was this strange stone, doing just that!

Osborne realized that as long as he was within a certain distance of the stone, he wouldn't be able to enter parallel space or perform interdimensional travel.

"This humanoid stone is trouble!"

"It's likely not just an ordinary rock but a terrifying divine artifact!"

The thought sparked a glimmer of realization in Osborne's mind.

But there was no time to ponder further. The seven kneeling Deity Overlord Deathborns, now aware of Osborne and Diana's presence, erupted in furious roars and charged at them.

"Diana, watch out!" Osborne called, his expression hardening.

With their ambush thwarted, they had no choice but to face the Deathborn head-on. There was no way he could abandon those three Miracle Elixirs, nor could he leave that mysterious stone behind.

He had to secure them, no matter what!

"Let's go!" Osborne shouted, unleashing his full power.

The air around him crackled with terrifying spatial energy as he prepared for the fight.

"Space Collapse!"

Osborne launched a punch infused with the power of Space Collapse, causing the very fabric of space to convulse violently in front of him.

The force was akin to a nuclear explosion, erupting from his fist and slamming into the chest of the fastest-charging Deathborn.

Even a peak Deity Overlord Deathborn couldn't withstand the full force of Osborne's strike.

According to Diana, he had reached the status of a Quasi-Deity King.

He might have still been lacking in the realm, but in terms of Divine Body and elemental essence, he had already met and even surpassed the Deity King's standards.

At that moment, Osborne was not just a Celestial Deity but a Quasi-Deity King.

With a single punch, Osborne obliterated one of the Deity Overlord peak Deathborn, reducing its body to nothingness.

But no sooner had he celebrated this moment of victory than four more Deathborns surged toward him, their faces twisted with rage, devoid of any fear.

They charged at him recklessly, like mindless killers consumed by their thirst for blood.

Surrounded and on the defensive, Osborne felt the pressure mounting as the four Deity Overlord peak Deathborns unleashed a relentless flurry of attacks.

Despite his skills, he struggled to keep up, dodging and blocking as best as he could.

After all, Osborne was only in the mid-stage of Celestial Deity and was momentarily overwhelmed.

On the other side, Diana was in a similarly precarious position.

Though she was in the late stage of Deity Overlord, taking on two peak-class Deity Overlords was pushing her limits. She was holding her ground, but the tide was not in her favor.

Meanwhile, the fierce battle at the center began to draw the attention of Angelo and his team.

Angelo's expression darkened as he sensed the commotion.

"What's happening? That direction... It's where the Miracle Elixirs are! How can there be a battle there? With such intense fluctuations, could it be that a third party is trying to snatch the Miracle Elixirs away from us?"

The thought sent a chill through him, and he scoffed in annoyance.

"Edison, I'll handle the Deathborn army here! I'll carve a path for you. You need to get your team across and grab those Miracle Elixirs immediately!"
