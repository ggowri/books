Among the crowd, Osborne spotted familiar faces, such as Lance, Owen, and Matthew, each adorned with the Heir's Emblems of the Augustine family. They were seated in the front row.

Next to them were two young figures, a man, likely in his forties, and a woman, around twenty-four or twenty-five, who appeared younger than Osborne.

They also had the Heir's Emblems on their chests.

Osborne recognized them as the other two prospective heirs to the Augustine family he had yet to meet.

They were Vincent and Emma, respectively, the best of masters and the number one genius of the younger generation.

These people seemed to be having a conversation, but unfortunately, Osborne had never learned lip reading and thus had no idea what they were talking about.

He was helpless.

"We are all prospective heirs to the Augustine family. They are attending Godfather's birthday party, seated in the most prominent spots closest to him, while I...

"I am locked up in a dungeon."

Osborne suddenly grew displeased.

He glared at Matthew, feeling an urge to beat him up. Nonetheless, he acknowledged a mutual appreciation between them, as he found Matthew's tactics against his rivals particularly cunning and shrewd.

However, he was the one being targeted.

"What a pity! They seem to be enjoying themselves so much. It would be nice if I could hear the voices from above," Osborne murmured.

He didn't notice a shadow deeper in the dungeon, which was the residual soul of the God of Star.

Upon hearing Osborne's wish, he immediately took action.

"He is Mr. Night's man. I still need Mr. Night to help me rebuild my divine body. His request must be met!"

With that, the God of Star waved his hand.

In an instant, Osborne realized the surrounding environment had changed. The once desolate space suddenly came to life, and he could sense the power once more.

At the same time, faint voices reached his ears.

He listened carefully and found that they were from the birthday party above.

He was stunned.

"What happened? Is there something wrong with the dungeon? Why did it suddenly return to normal? Can sounds pass through now?"

Osborne was puzzled. He checked the surroundings and discovered nothing unusual. Except for his cell, other places were still desolate and silent.

He raised his eyebrows. "Could it be Isa?

"She might be worried that I would feel uncomfortable here," Osborne murmured and no longer pondered about it.

The cell environment became normal, which was a good thing for him.

He focused on the sounds coming from the birthday party.

Meanwhile, in the front row of the venue, the five prospective heirs to the Augustine family, Vincent, Matthew, Owen, Emma, and Lance, were in the most conspicuous positions.

Many family members looked at them with envy.

Despite being competitors, the five of them seemed to get along well and were chatting with each other at that moment.

Vincent, the oldest among them, glanced at Matthew and smiled, "Matthew, I heard you used some clever tactics to lock up Osborne, another prospective heir to the Augustine family, in the dungeon.

"I don't quite understand why you do this."

After he finished speaking, Owen, Emma, and Lance all fixed their eyes on Matthew curiously.

Matthew chuckled, "I simply dislike him. He is too arrogant, so I want to teach him a lesson."

"Really?"

Vincent continued, "Based on my understanding of you, you would not do such a boring thing. Did he pose a threat to you, so you attacked him in advance? You have done such things many times."

Both Owen and Emma glared at Matthew with some dissatisfaction because Matthew had once targeted them before.

However, as they grew more powerful, Matthew found it increasingly difficult to scheme against them.

He was embarrassed.

But he replied calmly, "A threat? Attacking him in advance? Vincent, are you crazy? You are my biggest competitor. Why didn't I try to deal with you but go after a guy from a branch bloodline instead?

"While his strength is notable, he's still far behind you, and even more so compared to Owen and Emma. Most importantly, he doesn't gain a foothold in the Augustine family, and the strength of the branch bloodline from Dampston is negligible.

"In this situation, do you still think Osborne can pose a threat to me? Do you look down on me or yourself?"

Vincent fell silent, staring at Matthew as if trying to read something from his face.

Unfortunately, he couldn't discern anything.

He smiled, "So that's the case. Maybe I was mistaken. I haven't met Osborne yet, but I'm curious about him."

Owen replied, "There is nothing to be curious about. Osborne's strength is merely average. Not to mention you, Vincent, even I can easily defeat him! He doesn't stand a chance."

"Really?" Vincent grinned and finally stopped dwelling on it.

However, Matthew cast a meaningful glance at Owen with a smile, revealing deep disdain and contempt.

Emma, who had been silent, finally said, "You all can compete for the position of heir to the Augustine family. I won't contest that. But Godfather's successor is mine and mine alone!"

She was quite confident and proud.

The other four were furious.

Owen snorted coldly, "Emma, how dare you?"
