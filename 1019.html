Osborne barely used any effort to slice through the Ultra Diamond.

Though it was the top-tier super material developed by the Draco Group Materials Research Institute, it clearly paled compared to the material used in the Goddess of Grain's scythe.

In fact, Osborne found the Ultra Diamond rather unimpressive.

He could cut through others’ divine artifacts—including even top-grade ones—but only with full strength.

Without overwhelming force, the Goddess of Grain's scythe couldn't even sever an Ordinary divine artifact.

He looked at Antonio, puzzled.

"You said the hardness of Ultra Diamond surpasses that of most divine artifacts, right? But to me, it feels like they are poles apart."

Antonio was taken aback.

Then, he quickly jumped into the explanation.

"Mr. Augustine, that's not how we measure it! The reason divine artifacts feel harder to you is that they possess power! The creators of divine artifacts imbue them with strength, making them 'alive'!

"To destroy a divine artifact, you need to break not just the material itself but also the 'power' it possesses, which makes it much more challenging.

"And this piece of Ultra Diamond is just a regular material. Without any power on it, it's naturally flimsy.

"In fact, if any deity were to grant it power, Ultra Diamond would be on par with ordinary divine artifacts!"

Osborne rubbed his nose awkwardly, realizing he'd made a blunder.

"So, you're saying that as our technology advances, one day we could even make divine artifacts ourselves?"

Antonio said seriously, "Absolutely! As long as you, Mr. Augustine, reach the level of a deity and master divine power, you could endow them with strength and transform them into divine artifacts."

Osborne rubbed his nose again, feeling a bit sheepish. He wasn't even close to Class S, let alone a deity.

That felt so far out of reach!

But thanks to Antonio's explanations, he finally understood how divine artifacts were created.

"What about its hardness? Is it sufficient?"

Antonio's eyes lit up as he gazed at the Goddess of Grain's scythe, his excitement palpable.

"More than enough!

"It's definitely the hardest and sharpest divine artifact I've ever seen! It clearly hasn't been empowered by any deity, so it's not technically a divine artifact, yet it can effortlessly slice through Ultra Diamond.

"Although I haven't conducted a detailed test yet, its hardness is estimated at least ten times that of diamond—possibly even higher! I'm going to take it for testing and analysis right now.

Perhaps through this, we can indeed develop Hyper Diamond, taking our advanced materials to the next level."

With huge excitement, Antonio carefully took the Goddess of Grain's scythe and rushed to test it with the institute's cutting-edge technology.

Curious, Osborne lingered instead of leaving right away.

The testing results came in soon. When Antonio reviewed them, his eyes went wide with shock.

"What's the matter?" Osborne asked, intrigued.

Antonio took a deep breath and exclaimed,

"Mr. Augustine, it’s amazing! Our best instruments couldn't measure its exact hardness, meaning it exceeds the higher detection limits of the equipment. The limit was fifty times the hardness of a diamond!

"In our previous analyses of divine artifacts, the toughest one only matched up to ten times the hardness of a diamond! Most couldn't even reach twice the hardness!

"That's definitely the toughest super material I've ever seen!

"What's even more incredible is that we've managed to analyze 99% of its components! If we can try out based on these findings, even if we can't replicate its hardness exactly, we'll still make significant strides!

"I'm confident we can develop Hyper Diamond within three days! And who knows? In the near future, we could even create even more advanced materials like Mega Diamond and Tera Diamond!"

Osborne couldn't help but share in Antonio's enthusiasm.

"Then let's get to work! With the looming threat, we need more super energy guns and cutting-edge weaponry now more than ever!"

Antonio replied with determination written all over his face, "Absolutely! You can count on me, Mr. Augustine! I won't let you down!"

With that, he dove headfirst into his work.

Meanwhile, Kash from the Weapons Research Institute was buoyant. He had everything in place; once Hyper Diamond was successfully developed, he'd be ready to mass-produce a new line of weapons and gear that would outshine anything they had made before.

Osborne decided it was time to check in with the second-in-command at the Weapons Research Institute.

He summoned Giovanni Mirante, Antonio's brother, and another top-tier researcher.

While Antonio was focused on Hyper Diamond, Giovanni had been tasked with other critical projects.

Osborne asked, "Giovanni, have you finished analyzing the samples of ore that were sent over? Any new discoveries?"
