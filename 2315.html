Leroy left the team to find me? Why would she do that?

Could it be because I was gone for so long? Did she fear something bad had befallen me, so she risked going to the sixth layer to look for me, falling behind on token collection and getting kicked out by Jared?

Osborne guessed the likely cause, a wave of guilt washing over him.

He knew Leroy's search was out of a sense of gratitude, yet the fact remained that she'd left her team because of him.

And judging from Jared's words, Leroy might face punishment from the Azure Haven Academy after the state duel, paying a heavy price for her actions.

Punish Leroy? Over my dead body.

The Eight Great Academies are obsessed with cracking the top ten. They threw their weight behind Jared to make it happen, right? Well, I'll get Leroy there myself. No one in the Eight Great Academies will dare touch her then.

First things first, though. Gotta sort out this mess. Leroy and Clara are about to be toast.

Leroy was currently locked in a desperate battle with Jared, who, as the twenty-eighth ranked powerhouse, wielded two fully mastered Sacred Artifacts—one for tearing opponents apart, the other for keeping himself safe.

Despite her recent surge in power and complete mastery over the Inferno Drake Lance, Leroy was still outgunned. She was losing ground, her face paling with each parry, her hands shaking as she struggled to maintain her grip on the Lance.

She was on the ropes, about to go down!

Jared taunted, "Leroy, face it. You're outmatched."

With that, Jared went nuclear.

Leroy found herself staring down the barrel of a terrifying attack, her eyes widening in stark realization.

She knew she couldn't weather this storm; she was about to get wrecked.

Clara, caught in her own brawl nearby, was beside herself with worry. She was desperate to bail Leroy out, but she was already fighting tooth and nail just to stay alive.

Just as Leroy braced for impact, the unmistakable twang of a bowstring sliced through the air.

Leroy knew that sound.

It was Osborne's Bloodwrought Bow.

A split second later, a Sacred Power arrow, crackling with energy, blazed past Leroy, nailing Jared's attack dead-center.

The force of the impact was cataclysmic, obliterating Jared's assault instantly.

The Sacred Power arrow, showing no signs of slowing, continued its flight straight toward Jared.

"What the..."

It was Osborne's Sacred Power arrow!

She was in deep trouble.

The attackers around Clara weren't in Jared's league, and they didn't have the protection of a defensive Sacred Artifact. Osborne's Sacred Power arrows sliced through their defenses like a hot knife through butter, leaving them seriously wounded.

If Osborne had wanted them dead, they'd be pushing up daisies.

Even so, it was scary as hell.

With a single volley of three Sacred Power arrows, Osborne had critically injured three of them. Jared's eyes widened in disbelief, his face etched with shock.

He was well aware that his seven teammates were among the most powerful disciples in the Eight Great Academies. While not all were ranked in the top hundred, they were all formidable experts within the top two hundred, each capable of holding their own in a fight.

Yet here, Osborne had managed to wound three of them at once!

Glancing at his injured teammates, Jared knew they were out of the fight for the time being.

His face contorted with rage as he stared daggers at Osborne. "Osborne, you son of a bitch! How dare you harm my teammates?
