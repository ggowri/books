Chapter 2384 The Highest Odds-2

He carefully examined Clara's face and suddenly realized. "You're Clara from Zephyr State, right? Sorry, I didn't expect you to be the genius participating in the Zone Duel. Your Kingdom Duel Scorepoints can be exchanged for 791,000 Sacred Crystals!"

Clara nodded in confirmation and pointed at Osborne, saying, "All the Sacred Crystals I get from the pledge will be handed over to him to handle!"

At this moment, Leroy also took out her badge and said, "Then I'll pledge my Kingdom Duel Scorepoints too!"

Leroy's badge displayed 708,100 Kingdom Duel Scorepoints.

In the City Duel, she was injured due to the backlash from the Sacred Artifact and ultimately couldn't face Clara, securing only third place.

Therefore, her Kingdom Duel Scorepoints were exactly 1,000 points less than Clara's.

The middle-aged man said, "You're Leroy from Zephyr State? No problem! All your Kingdom Duel Scorepoints can be used as a pledge!"

Leroy nodded and replied, "The Sacred Crystal I pledge will also be handled by him."

Osborne felt extremely surprised at this moment.

It turned out that Kingdom Duel Scorepoints could actually be pledged at the Phantom Commerce Guild. This was essentially a form of borrowing. If repayment couldn't be made within a certain period, the Phantom Commerce Guild would directly acquire their Kingdom Duel Scorepoints.

Theoretically, Kingdom Duel Scorepoints and Sacred Crystal can be exchanged at a 1:1 ratio, but in reality, their value far exceeds this.

For many factions, Kingdom Duel Scorepoints are far more important than Sacred Crystal because they can be used to exchange for rare treasures in the Glorious Divine Kingdom's treasury.

These treasures are nearly impossible to buy, no matter how much money is spent outside.

Thinking of this, Osborne couldn't help but feel grateful to Leroy and Clara.

Then, he also took out his badge and said, "In that case, I'll pledge my Kingdom Duel Scorepoints too!"

Osborne's badge had over a million Kingdom Duel Scorepoints.

As the State Duel champion, he directly earned one million Scorepoints. The City Duel champion gets ten thousand points, while the Sect Tournament champion gets one hundred points.

This action completely shocked the middle-aged man.

Over a million Scorepoints meant Osborne was the State Duel champion. However, among the hundred states in Eastern Zone 3, he knew every champion but had no idea who Osborne was.

After carefully examining him, a look of surprise appeared on his face. "You're Osborne, the State Duel champion of Zephyr State? Sorry, I didn't recognize you earlier. It seems you've changed quite a bit since the State Duel."

Osborne gave a faint smile without offering much explanation. "Now, please place all the Sacred Crystals we three pledged, along with these 300,000 Sacred Crystals, all on me. I bet on myself to win the championship!"

As soon as Osborne finished speaking, the middle-aged man immediately widened his eyes.

Although many people would bet on themselves to win at the casino, like Valentino from Rose Rebels or Brenda from Skyearth Dominion, their bets were relatively small, usually not exceeding 100,000 Sacred Crystals.

They did this more to show confidence to the outside world.

However, Osborne actually placed a bet of nearly a million Sacred Crystals on himself.

Osborne, Leroy, and Clara's combined Kingdom Duel Scorepoints could be mortgaged for 2,427,300 Sacred Crystals, plus the 300,000 Sacred Crystals the three gathered, making Osborne's total bet over 2.7 million Sacred Crystals.

If Osborne ultimately wins the championship, with odds of 1 to 100, he will earn over 270 million Sacred Crystals.

This number is simply too massive.

For the Phantom Commerce Guild, two or three hundred million Sacred Crystals might not be much, but for the casino under their banner, it's still a huge fortune.

The middle-aged man looked serious but eventually nodded. "No problem! We are a casino under the Phantom Commerce Guild; no matter how much you bet, we can handle it!"

After speaking, he quickly got to work.

Soon, the bet was placed.

Osborne bet over 2.7 million Sacred Crystals, while Leroy and Clara each bet 20,000 Sacred Crystals.

After finishing all this, Osborne left the casino.

A determined expression appeared on his face.

That was a bet of over 2.7 million Sacred Crystals; he absolutely couldn't accept failure.

Therefore, he had to win the Zone Duel championship no matter what.
