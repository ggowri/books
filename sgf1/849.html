Chapter 849 Kill the Dark Deity

Mosley was utterly dumbfounded as she sensed the divine power radiating from Osborne's transformed leg bone.

"No way... it actually worked!"

Her disbelief was palpable. With her blood energy nearly depleted and Osborne's leg bone now a Divine Bone, all her hopes were dashed.

The transformation had rendered the amount of blood energy Osborne could absorb massive beyond measure. There was no way she could overwhelm him now.

Fury filled Mosley's eyes. "No! I refuse to accept this!

"After sleeping for countless years, waking up only to face a monster like you.

"I won't let you have it easy, even if it means my own death!"

Despair and madness flashed across Mosley's face as a terrifying force surged forth.

"Mental Collapse!"

Her voice thundered through the air.

Mosley's primary power was psychic power, and with her realization that death was imminent, she had no choice but to unleash her most powerful mental attack.

It was Mosley's trump card, a desperate move that would strike her own soul with immense backlash, regardless of whether it harmed her enemy.

She had held back this final gambit as long as possible, but now, she was forced to use it.

"Mental Collapse! Die, you wretch!"

In a heartbeat, Osborne sensed an overwhelming danger.

Without hesitation, he activated his Soul Barrier to shield his soul and mental strength.

In the next instant, it felt as if his brain had been hammered with a colossal force. He felt as if thunderbolts were crashing through his mind.

Osborne let out a blood-curdling scream, spat out a mouthful of blood, and was hurled backward.

Osborne's face turned ghostly pale, fear gripping him.

Despite his formidable soul and mental strength, and his mastery over psychic power and Spirit Realm, Mosley's power made his defenses seem insignificant.

Without the Soul Barrier, her final assault would have easily shattered his soul and crushed his mental strength pool.

Even if his body had survived, his soul would have been obliterated.

"Thank goodness! Thank god Isa taught me Soul Barrier. It deflected most of the attack aimed at my soul and mental strength, or I'd be dead already!"

With a shuddering breath, Osborne wiped the blood from his mouth and turned to look at Mosley.

Her body had withered.

The blood energy within her had been completely drained by Osborne, and her final desperate attack had inflicted severe damage on her own soul.

She was on the brink of death.

Mosley struggled to lift her head, her massive eyes filled with reluctance and fury.

But eventually, her enormous form collapsed with a resounding thud.

Dark Deity Mosley was dead.

Her life force dwindled rapidly, and she ceased all movement.

Osborne, seeing this, let out a deep sigh of relief.

He had finally won.

The battle had pushed him to use every trick in his arsenal, and it could be said that luck played a significant role in defeating Mosley.

But in the end, victory was his.

Exhausted, Osborne collapsed onto the ground, gasping for breath. It wasn't until some time had passed that he finally managed to stand and walk over to Mosley's massive corpse.

He thought, "She was a deity after all. Even in death, her body is incredibly powerful! Especially her mouthpart. It's as tough as a Premium divine artifact and can absorb blood energy!

Perhaps I could use it to create a divine artifact!

Her wings and those serrated, long legs are also excellent materials for crafting divine artifacts!"

Osborne pondered for a moment before he waved his hand, storing Mosley's corpse in the Dimensional Space.

Then, he moved over to Kendra and Yulissa. After checking their conditions, he let out a sigh of relief.

The two seemed to be poisoned and had not yet regained consciousness, but their physical state was stable. It wouldn't be long before they woke up.

Osborne then looked at his right leg.

Although it no longer glowed and appeared just like before, only Osborne knew that his right leg bone had now transformed into a Divine Bone, imbued with terrifying power.

He could feel the immense strength coursing through his right leg. It felt as if Osborne could kick a mountain out of his path without breaking a sweat.

In fact, he even believed his leg could take on a regular divine artifact head-on!

With that thought, Osborne took out the Rock Smasher.

He then delivered a powerful kick to it.

The force unleashed was immense, and Osborne noticed the Rock Smasher seemed to have bent slightly.

"Impressive!"
