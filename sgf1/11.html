Chapter 11 Get Away

Ophelia clenched her fists tightly, glaring at Osborne angrily. Meanwhile, she'd already made up her mind in her heart.

"Get out of here!" she snapped.

Justin's face instantly paled. "No way! Absolutely not gonna let him go!"

If Osborne left, there'd be no way for him to prove that the explosives on Osborne were fake.

Ophelia shot him a look of disdain. "Justin, don't forget who you are. I'm the heir of the Caldwell family now!"

Justin was furious but couldn't utter a single word.

"Justin, you'd better watch your own back! If I find out you ever poisoned me and had your personal bodyguard try to assassinate me, you know what will happen," Ophelia sneered.

Justin's face turned even grimmer.

He stared daggers at Osborne, his eyes blazing with murderous intent as if he wanted to devour Osborne alive.

Ophelia locked her cold eyes on Osborne. "Okay, Osborne, I'll own up. I'm beat today. But you better pray you never end up in my clutches. The Caldwell family's money? It's not some easy pickings.

"It'll burn your hands if you're not careful."

Osborne shrugged nonchalantly. "Don't you worry about that. Since I dared to take it, I'm not afraid of any consequences."

With that, Osborne didn't waste another word and headed out of the villa.

The members of the Caldwell family stepped aside, not daring to stop him.

"Ophelia, you just let that bastard go? That's 20 million dollars! How are you gonna explain this to the Elder Council?" Evelyn gritted her teeth, her eyes fixed on the direction where Osborne had disappeared.

Ophelia shook her head. "Mom, I couldn't take the risk. What if the explosives were real? It'd only play into Justin's hands. So I had to let Osborne go. As for the Elder Council, I'll handle it.

"The money of the Caldwell family isn't easy to take. I'll settle this score with him sooner or later," Ophelia said, her face still gloomy with anger.

She'd been extorted 20 million dollars in cash by the once-looked-down-upon loser, Osborne. It was like getting slapped in the face several times. How could she not be enraged?

This grudge had to be avenged.

She'd make sure Osborne got the money but couldn't spend it!

Evelyn opened her mouth but didn't say a word in the end. A crazy glint of murder flashed in her eyes.

On the other side, Osborne walked out of the Caldwell Estate.

Once he was far enough away, he started running like crazy.

"Yes! I did it! I actually pulled it off!"

Although Osborne had always appeared calm, only he knew how nervous he'd been.

Especially when Penelope exposed the fact that the explosives were fake, his heart almost jumped out of his chest.

Thankfully, he'd managed to outsmart Ophelia with his wits.

If Ophelia had been more decisive, he'd have been a goner.

Because, indeed, the explosives were fake.

He was just an ordinary civilian. How could he get his hands on high-powered explosives? He'd just used the fake ones to scare Ophelia.

Fortunately, everything had turned out okay.

"Time to claim my reward! I wonder what the Godfather will give me," Osborne murmured, his eyes filled with anticipation.

There were ten levels in the heir test. Each time he passed a level, he'd inherit a part of the Godfather's wealth and power.

But how could he contact the Godfather?

Just as Osborne was thinking about it, he saw a motorcycle speeding towards him.

On the cool-looking motorcycle sat a stunning woman wearing a red-and-black helmet.

Even though he couldn't see her face clearly, just the sight of her long legs, perky butt, and graceful curves told him she was a real beauty.

But for some reason, Osborne sensed a faint threat from this woman on the motorcycle.

His face changed, and he quickly tried to hide in the woods by the roadside.

Before he could get away, the motorcycle suddenly accelerated and did a cool drift right in front of him, coming to a stop with a screech!

The woman propped her long leg on the ground, took off her helmet with a fluid motion, and let her wavy hair flow freely, revealing a perfectly gorgeous face.

She wore a playful smile as she looked at Osborne teasingly.

"Why did you run when you saw me?

"Are you really that much of a coward? Weren't you quite brave back at the Caldwell Estate, scaring people with fake explosives?"

Osborne's face changed, and he eyed her warily. "Who are you?"

The woman said with a faint smile, "Don't worry. I'm not from the Caldwell family.

"My name is Isa Bowin. I'm a doctor, and people call me—The Reaper.

"Oh, I'm here to give you your reward as ordered by the Godfather."

The Reaper?

And here to give a reward?!

Hearing that she was here with a reward, Osborne perked up with excitement.

"What's the reward?"

Isa smiled gently. She puffed out her ample chest and pointed at her beautiful face. "The reward is me! So, how do you like this reward? You can do whatever you want to me."

Osborne was instantly stunned.

The Godfather had rewarded him with a woman?

He stared at Isa's face, truly amazed by her beauty.

She was really gorgeous and had an amazing figure. However, he couldn't figure out why a beautiful girl like her, who was also a doctor, had the nickname the Reaper.

But getting a woman as a reward just didn't seem worth it to him.

What Osborne needed most now was wealth, power, something that could change his current situation, not just a hot-bodied pretty woman he could have his way with in bed.

Thinking of this, Osborne let out a sigh.

But since the reward was a woman, he couldn't complain about anything. He began to size her up from head to toe.

Seeing this, Isa shot him a glare.

"Hey, you seem dissatisfied with me? Humph, you're not even qualified to have me as your prize yet!"

With that, Isa took out a smartwatch from her bag and casually tossed it to Osborne.

"Here, your real reward is in this!"
