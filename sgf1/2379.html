Chapter 2299 Out of Your Team-2

Leroy bit her lip, but in the end, she just let out a scoff. "Gonna pin this on me just because you're useless? Do whatever the hell you want. I'm outta here!"

With that, Leroy spun around and walked away without so much as a backward glance.

Jared bellowed at Leroy's disappearing figure, "Son of a bitch!

"Damn bitch, how dare she talk to me like that! Fine, if that's the way it is, from now on, we're enemies, no more friends! You better pray we never cross paths again, or I'll make you wish you'd never done this!"

With a snarl, Jared stomped off in a huff.

Jared didn't have the luxury of wasting time obsessing over Leroy. His sights were firmly set on breaking into the top ten. His team was composed of the most elite and genius disciples from the Eight Great Academies. If they couldn't even manage to secure a top-ten finish, it'd be a major embarrassment for him, and the entire Eight Great Academies would be humiliated.

Meanwhile, after splitting from the team, Leroy made a beeline for the sixth layer.

She had now fully gotten the hang of the Sacred Artifact, the Inferno Drake Lance. By tapping into her own Sacred Power and with the help of the Lance, she could manage to stay on the sixth layer for a short while.

However, each stint up there was limited to just twenty minutes.

Under normal circumstances, Leroy wouldn't even think about going to the sixth layer.

It was a perilous place. One false move, and she might not make it out alive.

Yet, here she was again, setting foot on the treacherous sixth layer.

This was her third attempt. The first two times Leroy braved this dangerous terrain, all her thoughts were on finding Osborne.

But the sixth layer was a massive and unforgiving place. Each of her brief forays, lasting just a measly ten-odd minutes, ended in disappointment. It was like searching for a needle in a haystack.

But Leroy's got grit. She was not about to throw in the towel.

Leroy knew she owed Osborne a huge debt. During the city duel, the Inferno Drake Lance made her lose her mind, its power overwhelming her. If Osborne hadn't knocked her out in time, she would have been finished.

Moreover, she'd never have managed to fully get a handle on the Inferno Drake Lance.

So, Leroy was in her debt up to her eyeballs. Now that Osborne might be in a real pickle on the sixth layer, she was determined to find him, dead or alive.

With that in mind, Leroy made her way back into the sixth layer.

Meanwhile, in a far-off nook of the sixth layer, a wild, unrestrained laugh rang out.

"Hahaha, finally! I've struck gold and found a sixth-layer token!"

Conrad's face was lit up like a Christmas tree with excitement.

"With this sixth-layer token, my score's hit 310 thousand! I've finally outdone that son of a gun, Osborne! And I'm in the top ten..." Conrad said, holding the token up close to his badge.

In a flash, the score on his badge jumped to 310 thousand, and his ranking bumped Osborne out of the way, landing him in fifth place.

Conrad was over the moon.

"Fifth place! It's just the first day of the state duel. I can't let it go to my head! But this is a great start. Most importantly, I've finally put that good-for-nothing Osborne in his place. I can finally relax a bit."

"Speaking of Osborne, that loser actually had the nerve to come to the sixth layer looking for tokens. And he was somehow lucky enough to find three right off the bat! Too bad he was all bark and no bite. Coming to the sixth layer was like signing his own death warrant!"

"By now, he's probably six feet under. His body's probably lying in some god-forsaken corner of the sixth layer, getting shredded by those brutal storms, ain't it?"

Thinking about it, Conrad let out a sneering smile.

"You dared to take me on, and this is your payback! Shame I couldn't do the deed myself. You're getting off easy, dying quietly like this!"

With that, Conrad put Osborne out of his mind and got back to scouring the sixth layer for tokens.

Meanwhile, on the fifth layer of the Arctic Maw Chasm, Gustave claimed a fifth-layer token, and his score shot up by 10 thousand points right away.

"Finally cracked the 100 thousand-point mark!"

Gustave peeked at the leaderboard. Upon seeing that Osborne still had a score of 300 thousand yet had tumbled to the sixth spot, he couldn't help but heave a sigh. "What a shame!"
