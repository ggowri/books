Chapter 660 Siege

Osborne's expression suddenly changed. This strange attack left him with no idea how to defend.

Swoosh!

Werewolf's claws tore through Osborne's martial art aura, leaving gashes on his body.

Bang!

Titan's massive fist slammed down in front of Osborne. He barely raised his arm to block but was sent flying.

He spat out a mouthful of blood. His face looked grim.

The blood he spat out was instantly drawn to Vampire in the distance, appearing in his hand.

He grasped Osborne's blood. A cold smile appeared on his lips.

A flame rose up, scorching Osborne's blood. At the same time, a searing flame enveloped Osborne, burning his body.

Osborne suddenly felt like his body was about to catch fire.

Scorch marks quickly appeared on the surface of his body.

Osborne gritted his teeth. He used the martial art aura to protect himself, trying to fend off the flames.

But it was useless.

He realized the flames were burning from within his blood.

"What kind of strange trick is this?"

Osborne was astonished.

"Controlling my blood? Attacking my blood can hurt my actual body?"

Osborne frowned.

Vampire's bizarre methods made Osborne's already tough situation even harder.

If this continued, even if Werewolf and Titan couldn't kill him, Vampire would burn him alive until his blood dried up.

"This can't go on!"

Osborne was sent flying again by Titan. His injuries worsened. Even with his recovery abilities, he couldn't keep up.

Osborne wondered whether he should use the Light of Redemption.

As long as he activated the Light of Redemption, all his negative statuses, injuries, and drained energy would instantly recover. He'd be back at his peak.

After using the Light of Redemption, he wouldn't be able to use it again for at least half an hour.

Osborne didn't dare to use it lightly.

"It seems I have no choice. I hope this works!"

Osborne glared at Vampire in the distance. He snorted coldly and activated the power of the Blood Cross.

"Vampires!

"Transform!

"You're Vampire? Perfect. I can transform into a Vampire, too! Do you want to control my blood? Let's see if you can handle this!"

Osborne muttered. His body quickly began to change.

In just the blink of an eye, Osborne completed his vampire transformation. His hair turned blood-red. Sharp teeth and claws appeared, along with a pale, chiseled face.

In that moment, Osborne felt a powerful surge of strength.

The strange flames inside his body were extinguished instantly. His previously agitated blood stabilized, no longer affected by Vampire.

Osborne even faintly sensed Vampire's blood.

That blood seemed delicious!

That blood seemed like it could be controlled, as long as he willed it.

Osborne glared at Vampire. A cold glint flashed in his eyes.

Vampire was stunned as he looked at Osborne's transformed state. His face showed a mix of shock, fear, and awe.

At that moment, he sensed the presence of a higher-level Vampire ancestor.

"No, this isn't possible!"

Vampire growled. He felt an urge to kneel. Only his higher cultivation level allowed him to resist.

At that moment, he was thrown into disarray.

He didn't even dare to attack Osborne again.

"Let's start by taking you out!"

Osborne glared at Vampire, his face icy. The blood in his body surged rapidly as if enraged that Vampire wasn't kneeling before him.

Osborne extended his cold hand and reached for Vampire.

Vampire let out a scream instantly.

The blood in his body gushed out like a fountain.

"Help me! Help me!"

Vampire felt the presence of death. He shouted in terror.

Medusa, Titan, and Werewolf heard his cries. Their expression changed dramatically.

They didn't understand why such a strange thing was happening. They could only attack Osborne desperately to stop him from harming Vampire.

Bang, bang, bang!

Osborne used the martial art aura to defend. It was under immense pressure, shaking wildly. However, it was still shattered, leaving more injuries on his body.

Medusa's petrification spread quickly, threatening to turn Osborne entirely to stone.

Osborne clenched his teeth.

He didn't retreat. This was his best chance to kill Vampire. If he missed it, Vampire would be on guard, and he might not get another chance.

"Get over here!"

Osborne roared angrily. Vampire's blood spurted out even more violently, forming a bloodline that Osborne grabbed.
