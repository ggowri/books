Chapter 2200 Self-detonation

The Ascending Sacred Sanctum, which looked as flimsy as a small cottage, effortlessly smashed the massive Malevolence Tower into pieces.

Lord Malevolence was completely caught off guard. The impact shattered his brief moment of hope and sent him plummeting to the ground, spitting out mouthfuls of blood.

The onlookers were so shocked that they stood still, their jaws dropping in utter disbelief.

It was like watching a puny little rabbit charge at and take down a mighty, muscular tiger in one fell swoop.

Who could have ever imagined such a turn of events?

All eyes were fixed on the ruins of the Malevolence Tower, and a stunned silence fell over the crowd.

Everyone knew the tower's reputation well.

If there was a list of the top ten Apex divine artifacts on Celestara Planet, the Malevolence Tower would undoubtedly top the list, with the Sanctum of Night coming in a close second.

Yet, this once-invincible artifact now lay in ruins, destroyed by Osborne's shabby-looking Ascending Sacred Sanctum.

"Whoa..." Isa gasped, her eyes widening as she had a sudden realization.

"There's definitely something special about Osborne's temple! Could it be... a quasi-Sacred Artifact?"

Clarissa and the others all nodded simultaneously, their faces filled with a sense of urgency. "It's definitely at least a quasi-Sacred Artifact! It might even be a full-fledged Sacred Artifact! I mean, something that can smash the Malevolence Tower to bits like that must be of a Sacred Artifact we've never even dreamed of."

As they talked, a cold shiver ran down their spines.

The thought of a Sacred Artifact in the form of a palace sent shockwaves through the group.

They understood the consequences only too well. Even though they'd never left Celestara Planet, they knew exactly what a Sacred Artifact palace meant. If the news got out, they'd be in deep trouble.

There'd be a tidal wave of chaos and greed, and it might even attract the attention of the powerful Empyrean Sacredness warriors.

"Quick! We have to keep this a secret!" Isa shouted.

But when they looked at the huge crowds of spectators, billions upon billions of them, a feeling of helplessness washed over them.

How could they possibly make all these people keep quiet?

"Are we seriously going to kill them all?" Clarissa said, her voice laced with disbelief.

The massacre was something no one could bring themselves to do, no matter how high the stakes.

"We can only take it one step at a time," Isa said, steeling herself.

Just then, the earth erupted, and Lord Malevolence violently emerged from the debris, his figure a pitiful sight.

His heart ached as he stared at the remains of Malevolence Tower, blood continuing to drip from his lips.

But it wasn't just heartbreak he felt; fear coursed through him. He fixed his wide eyes on the Ascending Sacred Sanctum, trembling with awe.

If Isa and Clarissa had pieced together the significance, Lord Malevolence had no trouble understanding.

Gritting his teeth, he exclaimed, "A Sacred Artifact?! You possess a Sacred Artifact palace?!"

Osborne remained silent, his brow furrowed in thought.

"Ah!" Lord Malevolence didn't need a confirmation from Osborne.

His smile turned sinister, a grim reality dawning upon him. "I never imagined you'd come into possession of a Sacred Artifact palace! Even in its dilapidated state, it still vastly outperforms my Malevolence Tower. It exceeds any mere quasi-Sacred Artifact! Hahaha, I guess I can't argue with fate!"

Despair flickered in his eyes as he took in the destruction.

He knew the truth—he was defeated. In the face of Osborne's Sacred Artifact palace, all his cunning strategies appeared foolish and futile.

He had lost!

Not only had Malevolence Tower fallen, but with it went any hope of escape. There was no retreat to safety, no chance to find a new beginning after this catastrophic defeat.

Lord Malevolence understood that Osborne wouldn't let him slip away this time.

His eyes blazed with madness as he snarled, "Osborne! God of Night! You're cursed! You've ruined everything for me! You'll never rest in peace!

"Even in death, I, Zayne, will make sure you suffer!"

In an instant, his body began to swell monstrously, unleashing a terrifying surge of power.

"I'll take the entire Celestara Planet with me to the grave!

"Even in death, I'll make you live forever in regret!

"Self-destruction!!"

The explosion roared to life, unfolding at a speed no one could anticipate.

Lord Malevolence's sudden decision to self-destruct left no time for anyone to react.

As an Ascension Ten Supernal Deity King, the sheer force of his self-destruction was apocalyptic. It could easily annihilate half of the Celestara Planet, turning it into a desolate wasteland.

People within a radius of tens of millions of miles would perish under the onslaught of his power.

The onlookers watched in horror as Lord Malevolence's body expanded uncontrollably, his power erupting like a volcanic explosion.

Faces turned pale, and terror filled every pair of eyes.

"No!"

"The esteemed Lord Malevolence, please don't!"

"My lord, we've always been loyal to you! Even if you've fallen, don't take us with you!"

"Stop! Please stop!"

"Make it end!"

Countless voices pleaded with him to halt the destruction, but Lord Malevolence ignored them, his twisted grin widening.

"Ha! You'll regret this! You'll live with the guilt of killing them!

"Let everyone burn with me!"

The ground trembled as his body exploded, unleashing a wave of devastating energy.

Osborne's face twisted in grim realization.

He hadn't anticipated this. Even he wasn't sure he could endure the full force of the blast without injury.

Everyone here was at risk. With a terrifying certainty, he realized that at least a significant number would suffer severe injuries, and most wouldn't survive.

This was no ordinary catastrophe.

The Human Alliance alone contained ten billion souls, while the Dark Deity Legion boasted nearly fifty billion. To make matters worse, just a stone's throw away lay Peaks City, one of the ten major cities in the West Zone.

Within those bustling streets lived over 100 billion innocent people from the Human Community! Even they could meet their end in Lord Malevolence's catastrophic explosion.

"Damn it!" Osborne's heart raced with urgency.

There was no time to back down or hide from this disaster. Without a moment's hesitation, he charged toward Lord Malevolence, heading straight for the explosive core.

He knew, deep down, that only he could stop this cataclysm.

Only he could save the countless around him!

With that resolve in his heart, Osborne surged forward, racing into the heart of the chaos.

"Osborne, have you lost your mind?" Isa shouted, her voice trembling with shock.

But Osborne didn't look back. With a fierce determination burning in his eyes, he pushed his speed to the limit, reaching the core of the blast in the blink of an eye.

The overwhelming energy seared his body, but he stood firm.

He bellowed, "Second form! Absolute defense!"

In a flash, his body transformed into his second form—a colossal crimson lotus.

Its massive petals enveloped the explosive force, containing the chaos.

Then, the shockwave from Lord Malevolence's self-detonation rippled outwards.
