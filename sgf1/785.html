Chapter 785 The Powerful Cuthbert Williamson

"What incredible power!"

Osborne was slightly surprised. Although the people challenging Cuthbert couldn't make it onto the Godson Rankings, there was still a significant gap between them and Cuthbert, ranked 36th.

However, it was shocking how easily Cuthbert defeated his opponents with just one move, barely exerting any effort.

They were all Class AAA. Why was Cuthbert so much stronger?

The crowd buzzed with discussion.

"Next!"

Cuthbert said calmly.

"I'll challenge you!"

Another challenger rushed onto the stage, about as strong as the previous ones. Cuthbert remained utterly calm and easily defeated him with one move.

The challenges continued.

Seven or eight challengers went up one after another, but not a single one could withstand even one of Cuthbert's moves. They were all instantly defeated.

The situation left everyone stunned.

"So powerful! No wonder he's confident about holding the Honor Quest Ceremony. With the strength he's showing, he might just achieve fifty consecutive victories!"

"Fifty straight wins is already amazing! In over two hundred years, the Honor Quest Ceremony has been held thousands of times, but only sixty-three people have achieved fifty consecutive victories!"

"I think Cuthbert has a good chance!"

The crowd was excitedly discussing, astonished by Cuthbert's strength. He was nearing ten consecutive victories but hadn't appeared to expend much energy at all.

He wasn't even trying yet. He hadn't shown his real abilities.

"Hmph, fifty straight wins?"

"Fifty wins won't be enough for Cuthbert! His goal is eighty consecutive wins, and he might even go for ninety, or even a hundred!"

"Just wait. When Cuthbert goes all out, he'll shock everyone!"

Someone sneered as they spoke.

The crowd turned and saw that it was someone from the Williamson family speaking. He was also a Godson, ranked in the 300s.

Seeing the pride on his face, the crowd frowned.

"Ninety wins? Even a hundred?

"Hmph! You folks are way too cocky! Are you looking down on the rest of Godsons? The challengers so far have been weaklings. Even I could beat them easily!

"When we step up, the real fight begins. He might talk about a hundred straight wins if he can still beat us easily!

"A hundred wins? In two hundred years, only nine people have ever done that!

"I don't believe Cuthbert can do it!"

A Godson ranked in the top 300 said coldly.

"Let's wait and see!"

Soon after, Cuthbert easily defeated two more opponents, bringing his total to ten consecutive wins.

He still looked full of energy, completely in peak condition, with hardly any signs of energy depletion.

"So powerful!"

Emma couldn't help but murmur, her expression serious.

"He is the highest-ranked among the younger generation on the Godson Rankings. With this kind of strength, it makes sense that he'd be confident enough to hold the Honor Quest Ceremony," Osborne said.

"I challenge you!"

"I challenge you!"

Just then, another figure rushed onto the stage. It was Satoru Byrd.

Seeing Satoru take the stage, many grew expectant. He had mastered a domain. While he had failed to break into the Godson Rankings the day before after being defeated by Samuel, his strength was not to be underestimated.

Compared to Cuthbert's earlier opponents, Satoru was much stronger.

But Satoru knew he wasn't a match for Cuthbert. His goal was simply to last as long as possible and drain Cuthbert's energy.

"Dark Realm!"

Buzz!

Without hesitation, Satoru activated his Dark Realm, enveloping Cuthbert in it.

"Cuthbert, use your Realm! Show me how strong it is! I want to see how wide the gap between us really is!"

Hearing this, Cuthbert smirked.

"This is finally getting interesting, but you're not even worthy of me using my domain! Your Dark Realm is full of holes. Watch me break it!"

With that, Cuthbert threw a punch.

Boom!

In an instant, Satoru's Dark Realm shattered like fragile glass. His face went pale as his mental strength suffered a backlash.

Satoru stared at Cuthbert in shock, but with just one more casual punch, Cuthbert sent him flying off the stage.

Defeated!

Despite having mastered the power of a Realm, Satoru only managed to last two moves against Cuthbert.

One punch shattered the Dark Realm.

Another punch sent him flying out of the ring.

A total defeat!

"Whoa!"

The crowd watching couldn't help but erupt into chatter, staring at Cuthbert in astonishment.

This was just too powerful!

If it weren't for the nature of a gauntlet match, who among the younger generation could possibly defeat him?

Could he really be aiming for one hundred consecutive victories?
