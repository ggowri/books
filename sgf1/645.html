Chapter 645 Medusa Mark

Osborne's face was grim as he clenched his teeth.

His back injury was slow to heal, and his spiritual energy had not yet recovered enough for interdimensional travel.

If Duke Medusa caught up to him, he would be in serious trouble.

"What should I do?"

Though he had managed to escape, Duke Medusa, at Class AAA, was faster. And the distance between them was quickly closing.

Her eyes glowed with a cold, petrifying light.

The stone skin around Osborne began to spread again. At this rate, he feared he would be completely petrified before his spiritual energy recovered.

"Am I going to die here?"

Osborne bit his lip, desperately searching for a solution.

Crack, crack!

Duke Medusa's petrification power was overwhelming. Osborne's martial art aura offered no resistance, and he felt his body gradually stiffening.

His speed gradually slowed down.

"Damn it! If I had known this would happen, I would have fused the dark and light elemental essence to raise my Devil's Light to level eight."

"That would have been enough to withstand Duke Medusa's petrification power. But it's too late now!"

Osborne clenched his teeth.

As the petrification reached his chest, he realized he had only seconds left before being completely immobilized.

Suddenly, a force erupted from his chest.

That was the power of Cain's Blood Cross.

Without his control, the Blood Cross surged, transforming him into a Vampire.

In an instant, he had long red hair, sharp teeth and nails, and a pale, angular face.

Osborne felt an extraordinary surge of power, halting the spread of petrification.

Though relieved, he knew the crisis was not over.

Duke Medusa was still closing in at an alarming speed.

"You can't escape! You lack the spiritual energy for spatial power, don't you? I'll enjoy torturing you until you become a perfect stone sculpture!"

Duke Medusa's chilling voice dripped with malice.

Osborne ignored it.

The transformation not only suppressed petrification but also increased his speed. While he still couldn't match Duke Medusa, he was now faster than before.

Using some of his spiritual energy, he activated his wind elemental power, propelling himself forward and pulling away from Duke Medusa.

Joy surged within Osborne.

His current speed had surpassed Duke Medusa.

If he continued to maintain this speed, he would be able to get rid of her even if he couldn't perform interdimensional travel.

However, his relief was short-lived as he noticed Duke Medusa's speed had increased again, surpassing him and slowly closing the distance.

Grimly, Osborne thought, "You don't give me a break. If I survive today, I'll make sure to beat you to a pulp when I reach Class AAA!"

Osborne became furious and ran away desperately.

As he fled, he focused on gathering the star power with Satan's Eyes to replenish his spiritual energy, which was finally stabilizing.

"Soon, I'll be able to perform interdimensional travel again, but even if I escape temporarily, I need to heal my back injury or risk being caught by Duke Medusa."

"She might have already contacted others from the Aberrants to set a trap for me. I must eliminate her to be safe!"

Osborne's expression grew serious.

The Aberrants were not limited to just Duke Medusa at Class AAA.

There were other powerful dukes, including werewolf, Titan, Tauren, and Vampire.

The strength of the Aberrants was extremely terrifying.

Having been entangled with Duke Medusa for too long, Osborne worried she had summoned reinforcements.

"I need to heal my wound quickly!"

At this moment, he missed Clarissa very much.

Clarissa's divine healing could save even those on the brink of death. If she were here, she could easily heal the injury on his back.

Suddenly, Osborne's eyes lit up and he thought of Aria Rosenthal, the sixth-ranked Godson of Yalynch.

She had once used a unique light elemental power, the Detoxify, during her battle with Emma.
