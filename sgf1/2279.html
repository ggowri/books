Chapter 2241 Sign Up-1

"This is your competition token!"

Earvin handed Osborne a small token, its surface displaying a screen with a single number—0.

"What's this?" Osborne asked, intrigued.

Earvin explained, "You should know that this Sect Tournament for Mystic Sirius Sect is essentially a qualifying round for the Glorious Divine Kingdom Duel. This token is called the Kingdom Duel Token!

"The number on it represents your Kingdom Duel Scorepoints. You can exchange these points for cultivation resources or various treasures. Just one Kingdom Duel Scorepoint can be traded for at least one Sacred Crystal!

"Of course, earning Kingdom Duel Scorepoints isn't easy! If you manage to stand out in Mystic Sirius Sect, your rank will determine how many points you earn—anywhere from ten to a hundred Kingdom Duel Scorepoints.

"Tenth place nets you ten points.

"Ninth place gets you twenty, and eighth place earns thirty!

"The champion of Mystic Sirius Sect? That's a whopping hundred points!

"If you can make it into the top ten, you'll represent Mystic Sirius Sect at the Tempest Fortress City's city duel. If you do well there, you could earn a thousand to ten thousand Kingdom Duel Scorepoints!

"The higher your rank, the more Scorepoints you'll rack up!

"If you can shine in the Zephyr State's city duel, you're looking at a hundred thousand to a million Scorepoints! And if you make it through the Eastern Zone 3 battle, that could skyrocket to ten million or even a hundred million points!

"When you finally reach the Glorious Divine Kingdom Duel, the rewards will be even greater! Those Kingdom Duel Scorepoints can be exchanged for items from the Glorious Divine Kingdom's treasury—everything from cultivation resources to divine artifacts and Sacred Artifacts!

"So, put in the effort!

"If you can land in the top ten of the Glorious Divine Kingdom Duel... the rewards you could exchange for with those Scorepoints would be unimaginable!"

As Earvin spoke, he shook his head, a hint of skepticism in his eyes.

Osborne, at Ascension Seven, standing out in Mystic Sirius Sect is already a stretch. Just avoiding embarrassment would be a win for him. The idea of competing in the city duel, the state duel, the zone duel, or even the Glorious Divine Kingdom Duel? That's just wishful thinking.

Yet, Osborne felt a spark of excitement igniting within him.
One Kingdom Duel Scorepoint can at least be exchanged for one Sacred Crystal?

"If I fight my way through and make it to the Glorious Divine Kingdom Duel, I could potentially earn millions, even hundreds of millions of Scorepoints?"

That's an incredible amount of Sacred Crystals!

He couldn't help but gasp, a wave of astonishment washing over him.

Back on Umbra Earth, he had worked hard to gather fifty thousand Sacred Crystals and thought that was a fortune.

But now, with a good performance in the Glorious Divine Kingdom Duel, he could be looking at over a hundred million Sacred Crystals!

That was an astounding prospect.

With that kind of wealth, he wouldn't have to worry about resources after breaking through to Empyrean Sacredness.

At that moment, Osborne's desire to enter the Glorious Divine Kingdom Duel and achieve a stellar performance grew even stronger.

But he also understood that such substantial rewards would attract talented warriors from across the entire Glorious Divine Kingdom, making it even harder to stand out.

"Thanks for the insight, Mr. Earvin Corenswet! I'm going to prepare seriously! I won't let Peak Seven down in the Sect Tournament in half a month!"

With that, Osborne took his leave.

Earvin watched him go, shaking his head in disbelief.

"Another dreamer! At Ascension Seven, he thinks he can earn Kingdom Duel Scorepoints? What a joke! Does he think our Ascension Ten core disciples are just decorations? He'll be lucky to avoid embarrassment, let alone crack the top ten and earn Scorepoints!"
