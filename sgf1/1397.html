Chapter 1396 The Divine Flame Rekindled

"Wait, is that... mid-grade Essence Crystals?"

Osborne's Satan's Eye detected a small vein of minerals nearby.

Although this vein was relatively modest and the density of Essence Crystals was lower than that of surrounding deposits, every crystal within it radiated a purer, more potent energy.

Unlike the dark brown Essence Crystals he had encountered before, these glimmered with a soft blue light.

"Could it really be mid-grade Essence Crystals?" Osborne pondered, uncertainty creeping in.

He had never seen mid-grade Essence Crystals before, but he was certain these were not low-grade.

"This vein might be small, but it still holds over 10 thousand crystals. With my Satan's Eye pinpointing their location and my earth elemental power to navigate the mine, I could extract them all in under an hour.

Ten thousand mid-grade Essence Crystals are equivalent to a million low-grade ones! For Harry, an Intermediate Deity, he only needs to submit a thousand low-grade Essence Crystals each month. A million would cover his contributions for eighty years!

That's enough mid-grade Essence Crystals to help dozens of Superior Deities break through to Transcendent Deity!"

Osborne's eyes sparkled with excitement.

Although his injuries hadn't fully healed yet and he didn't need Essence Crystals at the moment, he knew he would require a vast quantity in the future. Even if Osborne couldn't use them himself, they could be saved for Sebastian, Anastasia, Zoe, and even the Augustine family still on Blue Planet.

Moreover, Essence Crystals served as the currency in Celestara.

Everything in this place came at a cost, and once he recovered and left Rodriguez Village, he would undoubtedly find a use for them.

Low-grade Essence Crystals weren't worth his time to mine, but mid-grade ones? Those were a different story.

"Let me take some time to gather them all!" he decided.

Within two hours, Osborne had extracted all the mid-grade Essence Crystals from the vein, which totaled over 12 thousand.

He carefully stored them in his Dimensional Space.

"Now, to hunt down more Intermediate Deity Deathborn! I can feel my strength returning—I'm on the verge of regaining my Junior Deity status!" Osborne murmured to himself.

He stowed away the Eclipsing Mantra, and three translucent Divine Flames flickered above his head, representing his past achievement as a Superior Deity.

However, these flames were mere shadows of their former glory, dim and lifeless due to his severe injuries.

Yet, thanks to the recovery of the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity, one of the Divine Flames began to show signs of reigniting. Osborne estimated that if he could take down a few more Intermediate Deity Deathborns and let the Lotus absorb their high-grade death scent, the first Divine Flame would truly reignite.

Once that happened, he would reclaim his Junior Deity status.

With his skills and power, even at that level, he would be more than a match for an ordinary Superior Deity and might even come out on top.

"Keep pushing forward!" Osborne urged himself, determined to find more Intermediate Deity Deathborns.

With hope rekindled, he refused to waste a single moment, eager to restore his strength.

Before long, he encountered another Intermediate Deity Deathborn.

Then a second, and a third.

Three hours passed, and after delving deeper into the mine for over eight hours, Osborne had slain eleven Intermediate Deity Deathborns. He ensured that the high-grade death scent left behind by each was absorbed by the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity.

The vast network of roots from the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity surged back to life, pulsating with renewed vigor.

With their assistance, Osborne felt a significant recovery in his Divine Body. Among the three dim Divine Flames flickering above his head, one finally reignited with a resounding pop, bursting into brilliant flames once more.

A wave of relief washed over him as the familiar sensation of power surged back, almost intoxicating in its intensity.

"I've finally regained my Junior Deity status!

In Celestara, Junior Deity might be the lowest rank, but my version of it is different. I'm not your average Junior Deity; I can take on a Superior Deity without breaking a sweat!

Time to head back. If Harry and Amelia return and don't find me, they're going to be worried sick."

With that thought in mind, Osborne decided to return for now, planning to continue his hunt tomorrow.

He was eager to explore the deeper levels of the mine, where he could confront even stronger Superior Deity Deathborns. The high-grade death scent from those powerful foes would be purer and more potent, allowing the Lotus to recover even faster.

Everything was falling into a positive cycle.

As long as nothing went awry, he felt confident he could fully restore his strength in the coming days.

"Time to go!" Osborne said to himself, making his way back.

As he passed by the area where Harry and Amelia were mining, he was surprised to see that the siblings hadn't returned yet; they were still hard at work.

They were situated in a small vein of their own, where the density of Essence Crystals was surprisingly high.

Every so often, they unearthed a low-grade Essence Crystal, their faces lighting up with excitement each time.

Seeing their enthusiasm sparked a thought in Osborne's mind.
