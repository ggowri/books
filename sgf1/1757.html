Chapter 1756 Creator Deity

In that instant, a voice like thunder reverberated through Osborne's mind.

"An ant dares to gaze upon the Creator Deity?

"Ant, I will remember you. Should you ever ascend the divine steps, I will take your life! Now, begone!"

A deafening roar echoed in Osborne's head, causing blood to stream from his eyes, nose, mouth, and ears.

He let out a piercing scream, the agony overwhelming him.

After what felt like an eternity, Osborne finally managed to pull himself together, gasping for breath, terror, and confusion etched in his eyes.

He felt his body shudder violently, his eyes wide with shock.

"What in the world was that?! I merely exchanged glances, and I'm left with these grievous injuries? My divine power essence, elemental essence—they're nearly shattered, and my Divine Body is cracking! Even my Soul Current Ocean is on the verge of collapse..."

Osborne's face darkened with gravity.

The injuries he sustained were far worse than when he first arrived in Celestara.

Thankfully, the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity remained unaffected.

With its aid, his wounds began to heal rapidly, the Soul Current Ocean and Divine Body mending, while his divine power essence and elemental essence slowly restored themselves.

"If it weren't for the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity, I'd be done for! What was that colossal humanoid statue? Why was it so terrifying?

An ant dares to gaze upon the Creator Deity?

Could it be that he is the Creator Deity? An existence beyond the Deity King realm?

What did he mean by ascending the divine steps?

He said he'd remember me and that he'd come for me if I ever ascended..."

Osborne's expression was grave as he replayed the images in his mind—the countless roots entwining the massive Celestial Sword Peak at the center of Divine Peak Nine, and the ominous humanoid statue looming over it.

"That statue must be the Shadow Terror Fiend I encountered! He calls himself the Creator Deity—could he truly be a god of creation? Stronger than Lord Malevolence or the God of Night?

If that's the case, everything makes sense!

Lord Malevolence and the God of Night were both severely wounded by the Shadow Terror Fiend atop the divine peak!

And those roots... they resemble the root creatures I encountered in the deep space!

Were they fighting the Shadow Terror Fiend, or was there something else at play?

If those root beings can ensnare the Shadow Terror Fiend, could they also be of Creator Deity caliber?"

Osborne shook his head, frustration gnawing at him.

He yearned for another glimpse to uncover the connection between the root beings and the Shadow Terror Fiend.

But Osborne ultimately forced himself to suppress the impulse.

Biting his lip, he thought, "Just a single glance, and I'm gravely injured! If I continue to pry, I might provoke the Shadow Terror Fiend and end up dead!

If he kills me, I can't even be sure the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity could bring me back! I can't take that risk!

My understanding of this world is still too limited! Divine peak undoubtedly conceals great secrets—perhaps the greatest secrets of this world! But I need to reach the level of the God of Night or Lord Malevolence to explore the core secrets of the divine peak!

After all, both Lord Malevolence and the God of Night survived the Shadow Terror Fiend's pursuit. If I can reach their level of power, the Shadow Terror Fiend won't easily erase me, and then I could delve into the core mysteries of divine peak!"

With a deep breath, Osborne steadied himself, pushing aside the haunting memories he had just encountered.

He buried those images deep in his mind and redirected his focus to exploring the changes that had come with his breakthrough to Level Ten of Satan's Eye.

The enhancement of his vision was merely the tip of the iceberg.

Now, he didn't even need to consciously activate Satan's Eye to perceive the subtleties of his surroundings; it was as if a veil had lifted, revealing the world in sharper detail.

But the most significant transformation lay in his Infinite Devouring Eyes.

Upon reaching Level Ten, Satan's Eye had altered the nature of the Infinite Devouring Eyes entirely. Previously, they had merely been a shadowy presence, capable of consuming ambient energy to aid Osborne in his ascension.

Now, they had grown larger and more intricate, hovering above him like a colossal magical eye. The veins pulsing on its surface were vividly clear, a testament to its newfound power.

What struck Osborne most was the dramatic increase in its absorption capabilities.

No longer limited to merely devouring stray energy, the Infinite Devouring Eyes could now consume any material substance.

For instance, Essence Crystals!

Now, the Infinite Devouring Eyes could directly devour Essence Crystals, swiftly converting their energy into the divine power he desperately needed.

The speed of this process was staggering compared to before.

In mere moments, the Infinite Devouring Eyes could consume a Celestial Crystal, draining its immense energy and using it to elevate Osborne's power.

The efficiency was astounding.

Previously, to break through the Deity King realm, Osborne needed vast amounts of energy. In Hope Haven, his Infinite Devouring Eyes would take a month to gather enough power.

Now, with the ability to consume Celestial Crystals, he could achieve that in just half a day!
