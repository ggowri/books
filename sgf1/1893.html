Chapter 1892 Divine Ascendance Technique

Osborne chuckled. "Grandpa, I just wanted to clarify everything."

Jethro nodded, his expression serious. "That's fair enough!

"The Binding Twin Pearl works both ways. Once we're bound, if your energy signature starts to weaken, I'll know you're in trouble. I can sense it through the pearl and teleport to you immediately.

"And if you ever feel my energy signature fading, that means I'm in danger, too! But don't be foolish and try to teleport to me!

"Like I said, if I can't handle the threat, you showing up would just be throwing your life away! Remember, only I can use the Binding Twin Pearl to reach you. You absolutely cannot come to me!

"Of course, if you're in a dire situation and need to escape, that's a different story. Just know that using the Binding Twin Pearl to teleport takes a bit of time to guide, and for a true warrior, that time is often enough for the enemy to finish you off.

"You have spatial power at your disposal, so when it's time to run, use that instead! The Binding Twin Pearl should only be a last resort!

"For example, Trevor's Universe-Sealing Formation—that's his Divine Ascendance Technique!

"He's a Supernal Deity King, and you're still at mid-level Deity King. If you get caught in his Universe-Sealing Formation, you won't be able to use interdimensional travel.

"But with the Binding Twin Pearl, you can still teleport to me; the formation won't stop that!"

As Jethro finished speaking, Osborne's eyes lit up.

The Universe-Sealing Formation was indeed formidable, and he had no idea how to escape if he ever fell into such a trap.

But now, with the Binding Twin Pearl, he had a backup plan.

After a moment of thought, he asked, "Grandpa, you mentioned that the Universe-Sealing Formation is Trevor's Divine Ascendance Technique? What exactly is a Divine Ascendance Technique?"

Jethro chuckled. "I knew you'd ask that.

"A Divine Ascendance Technique is a power only a Supernal Deity King can master! It's incredibly strong, akin to a formation—it harnesses the very forces of heaven and earth! I can't explain it all right now, but you'll understand once you become a Supernal Deity King yourself.

"Just know that every Supernal Deity King has their own unique Divine Ascendance Technique! Trevor's Universe-Sealing Formation isn't all that impressive; it's just good at trapping people. Once you're caught, it's tough to break free from the inside, even if you're stronger than him.

"But it has its weaknesses, it lacks raw power and can be easily breached from the outside! In short, the Universe-Sealing Formation isn't worth worrying about! You'll encounter many Divine Ascendance Techniques far superior to it in the future!"

Osborne's eyes widened in disbelief.

Anastasia, Zoe, and the others were equally astonished.

What seemed like an insurmountable challenge to Osborne was, in Jethro's eyes, merely a minor obstacle among the vast array of techniques.

With a serious expression, Osborne nodded firmly.

"So, what's your Divine Ascendance Technique, Grandpa?" His curiosity got the better of him.

Jethro smiled knowingly, saying, "Even if you hadn't asked, I'd want to share it with you! Since you're heading out on your journey, I don't have much to offer you as a parting gift, so I'll give you my Divine Ascendance Technique instead."

With that, Jethro pulled out eight tokens.

These tokens shimmered with a smooth, polished surface, clearly crafted from the finest materials. Intricate patterns were etched all over them, radiating a unique energy that was hard to ignore.

As Jethro revealed the tokens, Zachary and the others stared in wide-eyed amazement, their expressions a mix of envy and awe.

Jethro declared, a proud smile on his face, "These are my Divine Ascendance Technique! Because of their immense power, activating a Divine Ascendance Technique usually requires a bit of time to prepare.

"But in the heat of battle, you often don't have that luxury!

"That's why most Supernal Deity Kings seal their techniques in advance. Take Trevor's Universe-Sealing Formation, for example—he had it sealed within those young men.

"The moment he thinks about it, the formation activates, saving him precious time. While it won't be as powerful as if he cast it himself, it still packs a solid punch—about sixty to seventy percent of its full strength.

"The tokens I'm giving you are sealed with my Divine Ascendance Technique—Gale of Netherworld! Each one can unleash the Gale of Netherworld once, and when it's activated, it carries about eighty percent of my full power!"

He paused for effect, his pride evident.

"Now, let me tell you, my Gale of Netherworld is in a league of its own, far beyond Trevor's Universe-Sealing Formation! Once activated, it can summon the chilling winds of the underworld to sweep away your enemies.

"Even a Supernal Deity King like Trevor would have to flee in the face of my Gale of Netherworld! If he hesitates for even a moment, he's as good as dead!

"Each of you will get one of these tokens as a parting gift from me!"
