Chapter 2456 Notary-1

After Brenda finished speaking, everyone burst into surprised chatter.

Why would Brenda be willing to use her Ice Scepter as a bet? That's a Legendary Sacred Artifact!

Although Osborne's Warfare Shield and Bloodwrought Bow looked impressive, they were at most Mid-grade Sacred Artifacts, not even considered Superior Sacred Artifacts.

Compared to the Ice Scepter, Osborne's Sacred Artifacts were far inferior.

On Racial Convergence Planet, the value gap between different grades of Sacred Artifacts is immense.

Normally, a basic Inferior Sacred Artifact is worth around one million Sacred Crystals, a Mid-grade Sacred Artifact is close to ten million Sacred Crystals, and a Superior Sacred Artifact is about one hundred million Sacred Crystals.

The value gap between each grade is roughly tenfold.

A basic Legendary Sacred Artifact is worth one billion Sacred Crystals, but due to its rarity, even the wealthy may not be able to purchase one.

The Ice Scepter is worth at least one billion Sacred Crystals, possibly even twenty to thirty billion Sacred Crystals, but what about the Bloodwrought Bow and the Warfare Shield?

The Warfare Shield is a Mid-grade Sacred Artifact. Since it is crafted by the Warfare Ancient Tree, its power rivals that of typical Superior Sacred Artifacts.

However, it is only worth one hundred million Sacred Crystals.

The Bloodwrought Bow is currently just an Inferior Sacred Artifact, worth only a few million Sacred Crystals.

So, the value of the Ice Scepter is at least dozens of times that of the Warfare Shield and the Bloodwrought Bow.

In such a situation, Brenda still agreed to Osborne's request, using her Ice Scepter as a wager.

This is simply a huge loss!

Thinking that Brenda is almost certain to win this bet while Osborne has no chance of winning, they feel very understanding.

"Osborne might regret it now!"

"He probably suggested using the Ice Scepter as a wager to scare Brenda off. He thought Brenda wouldn't dare to take the risk, but Brenda has already agreed."

"Well, Brenda will definitely win. If it were me, I’d agree too. Even if she bets her life, so what? After all, she won’t lose, only win!"

Everyone was talking about it.

At this moment, Brenda sneered and looked at Osborne.

She also thought Osborne suggested using the Ice Scepter as a wager to scare her off. Since she agreed, she believed Osborne already regretted it.

Unfortunately, she didn’t see a trace of regret on Osborne’s face.

Osborne smiled faintly, "I must thank you for willingly giving me a Legendary Sacred Artifact. However, words alone are not enough. Someone needs to notarize our bet to ensure that if you lose, you won’t go back on your word and refuse to hand over your Ice Scepter."

Hearing this, Brenda thought Osborne was still making excuses.

She thought Osborne would soon cancel the bet for failing to find a suitable notary.

Brenda sneered and immediately interrupted Osborne, "That's simple! Everyone present is a witness to our bet. If you're worried I'll go back on my word after losing, you can ask the referees. With them as our notaries, you can rest assured, right?"

After speaking, Brenda pointed to the chief referee Gerald and the others in the distance.

Osborne immediately began to ponder upon hearing this.

Brenda is a top-tier genius disciple of the First-rate Power—Skyearth Dominion, holding a high status. Although the chief referee Gerald and the others are quite strong, they might not be able to defeat Brenda, and they wouldn't dare to offend Skyearth Dominion easily.

If Brenda refused to hand over the Ice Scepter after losing, the chief referee Gerald and the others wouldn't dare to forcefully do anything to her.
