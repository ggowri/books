Chapter 1635 Diana's Breakthrough

"A Deathborn Relic?!" Osborne exclaimed, a spark of excitement lighting up his face.

He hadn't expected such a delightful surprise—this was a genuine Deathborn Relic.

Deathborn Relics were the crystalline eyes left behind by Deity Overlord-level Deathborn after their demise. These were no ordinary relics; they were mutated eyes, incredibly rare and valuable!

Each one contained a unique essence of Deathborn power. When activated, this essence could cloak the user, making them indistinguishable from other Deathborns, thus preventing attacks.

Only by defeating a mutated Deathborn could one hope to obtain a Deathborn Relic, which made them exceedingly scarce. The essence within was precious, diminishing with each use. In critical moments, it could mean the difference between life and death.

A standard Deathborn Relic in the Divine Peak Nine outpost could fetch more than a hundred Ultimate Essence Crystals.

The one before Osborne, harvested from a late-stage mutated Deathborn, was even more potent. Its Deathborn essence was richer, allowing for at least a dozen uses, and capable of deceiving even stronger Deathborn.

Its worth was several times that of a typical relic—potentially trading for over a thousand Ultimate Essence Crystals!

For Osborne, Ultimate Essence Crystals were not an issue.

During his foray deep beneath Divine Peak Nine, he had amassed a considerable haul—certainly in the hundreds of thousands.

To put that into perspective, Diana and Emmett, seasoned leaders among the miners, could only collect a couple of hundred crystals after a week of hard labor.

The entire workforce at Divine Peak Nine, numbering over 10 thousand, managed to scrape together only a few tens of thousands of Ultimate Essence Crystals each month.

Even that amount was insufficient for the Ninth Dark Deity Legion's internal needs, leaving little for ordinary folk.

Thus, the Ultimate Deathborn Relic before him, capable of trading for a thousand Ultimate Essence Crystals, was a treasure of immense value.

It wasn't just about having Essence Crystals; it was about being able to acquire such a relic.

"Put it away! This might come in handy later!" Osborne commanded, and his clone tucked the Deathborn Relic into Dimensional Space.

In the blink of an eye, the clone vanished.

Meanwhile, Osborne's main body had arrived at the spot where Diana had been moments before.

He looked ahead.

To Riley and the others, there was nothing there.

But to Osborne, Diana was visible, encased in an isolated space—a separate realm carved out by his clone using Spatial Cleaving.

Diana was deep in the process of breaking through her realm.

"She's almost there!" Osborne thought, feeling a rush of relief.

In that isolated space, Diana was completely safe; ordinary beings wouldn't even notice her presence, let alone pose a threat. Only someone with spatial power, like Osborne or someone significantly stronger, could sense something was amiss.

"But why did such a powerful Deathborn attack when Diana was about to break through to Deity Overlord? They seemed frantic to stop her!

There's something big going on with her—some kind of secret?

A curse?"

Osborne thought to himself, but he had no desire to pry deeper. Everyone had their secrets, and as long as Diana didn't pose a threat to him, that was enough.

Just as Diana hadn't delved into Osborne's secrets either.

Inside the isolated space, energy surged violently as Diana made her breakthrough, the commotion startling even Osborne.

"She's not fully through yet, but with those divine power fluctuations, she's hitting the equivalent of a mid-level Deity Overlord's full-force effort, right? Her speed in cultivation is impressive, and her strength is formidable! The way she easily defeated Emmett at peak Celestial Deity rank despite being a late-stage Celestial Deity was no fluke—that wasn't even her full power!

Now that she's broken through to the early stages of Deity Overlord, she's stronger than the average mid-level Deity Overlord!"

Osborne sighed, truly amazed, "This is what you call a super talent!

"No, a monster! Give her enough Ultimate Essence Crystals and a safe place to train, and she'll become one of the top warriors in the Human Community before long."

Even he felt a pang of envy for Diana's incredible gift.

Finally, after a thunderous explosion of divine power, Diana completed her breakthrough.

The intense energy surrounding her gradually simmered down, settling into a calm that radiated strength.

Osborne smiled. He knew she had succeeded!

With a thought, Osborne reformed the spatial division, bringing Diana back into the fabric of reality right in front of him.

As she opened her eyes, a flash of brilliance danced within them, and a powerful energy burst from her—then slowly receded.

"You back with us?" Osborne said with a grin.

Diana's face lit up in a warm smile, her eyes shining with gratitude.

"Yes! I'm awake! Thank you for saving me earlier! If it weren't for you, I'd be dead right now and wouldn't have been able to break through."
