Chapter 2165 Seven Helpers-2

Icy One's face turned ashen at the thought.

But there was nothing he could do.

Neither Tyrese nor the God of Time was someone he could take on.

After an intense exchange, Tyrese and the God of Time both retreated again, their faces looking a bit pale.

Tyrese said icily, "God of Time, I admit I underestimated you! Even when I used my Apex divine artifact battle-knife, I still couldn't take you down when you were bare-handed! If we had a fair fight, I'd probably lose to you."

"Humph!" God of Time snorted. "You got that right! If you're smart, just get out of here. I don't want to kill anyone today. I'm just waiting for God of Night."

Hearing this, Tyrese gave a faint smile. "Leave? No way! Zayne paid a price to have us come and back him up. How could we just leave like this? I may not be able to handle you, but you're not gonna make it out of here today!"

As soon as he said that, God of Time's face hardened abruptly.

"'We'?"

"Yeah!" Tyrese grinned faintly. "Zayne didn't just hire me to help. I thought I could deal with you on my own, but it turned out I underestimated you.

"So, no more hiding. Everyone, come out!

"Let's get rid of these guys quickly and head back to Racial Convergence Planet. The celestial energy here is way thinner compared to that on Racial Convergence Planet."

After saying that, Tyrese looked into the distance.

Someone laughed. "Tyrese, looks like you're not up to the task.

"You were bragging before that you could take out this little girl all by yourself. But after all this fighting, even with your Apex divine artifact battle-knife, you still couldn't beat her.

"Newbie, you better train more when we get back to Racial Convergence Planet!"

Tyrese's face darkened. "Humph! That's because this little girl is really strong.

"She came back from the cycle of reincarnation in just a thousand years, with the experience and memories of two lifetimes. Plus, she has incredible talent. It's normal that I couldn't handle her.

"At least, I'm not being stubborn. That's why I called you guys out, right?"

Tyrese's words only made the laughter even louder.

Then, a few figures suddenly appeared beside Tyrese, looking at God of Time with faint smiles.

Including Tyrese, there were seven figures in total.

Each of them had an energy signature no weaker than Tyrese's.

One of them, whose energy was as vast as the sea, was even more powerful than Tyrese.

God of Time's face instantly turned extremely pale, with a hint of panic in her eyes.

She gritted her teeth and said, "Six Ascension Seven Supernal Deity Kings?! One Ascension Eight?!

"To deal with me, Lord Malevolence really spared no expense! He even got you guys from Racial Convergence Planet. It seems Lord Malevolence is dead-set on wiping out the Human Community on Celestara Planet!"

Hearing this, Tyrese smiled, "The Human Community on Celestara Planet? Just a low-class race. Your existence has been getting in the way of Zayne's plan to rebuild the Malevolence Empire. So, of course, you all have to be wiped out!

"It's a pity God of Night isn't here. Otherwise, we'd really like to have a go at him and see how powerful this guy that Lord Malevolence fears actually is."

After saying that, the seven of them stared at the God of Time.

The leading Ascension Eight Supernal Deity King said to her indifferently, "Little girl, I'll give you a chance. Surrender now, and I won't kill you. From now on, you can be my maid and serve me well. I'll take you to Racial Convergence Planet, where you'll have better cultivation conditions and can reach a higher cultivation level.

"Otherwise, I'll have to be harsh and kill you. I wonder if you'll be lucky enough to come back from the cycle of reincarnation this time..."

As he spoke, there was a clear threat on his face.

At the same time, a terrifying aura pressed down hard on the God of Time, making her face turn ashen.
