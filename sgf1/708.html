Chapter 708 Cracking the Dark Realm

"Light elemental power! Dissolving Light!"

Osborne channeled his light elemental essence and unleashed Dissolving Light with the power of his essence.

A bright beam of light suddenly erupted within the Dark Realm, immediately dispelling the surrounding deathly darkness.

Buzz!

Dissolving Light, an intermediate light elemental power, was powerful and had a strong piercing force.

The Dissolving Light shot out, but it quickly felt as if it were sinking into a quagmire, growing weaker and smaller until it abruptly vanished after traveling one hundred yards.

This situation made Osborne's expression change.

Palmers laughed out loud. "Osborne, you think you can use a light elemental power to break my Dark Realm? How laughable!

"Light elemental power can indeed counter dark elemental power. But your Dissolving Light is only an intermediate skill! The Dark Realm, on the other hand, is an advanced dark elemental skill!

"If you want to break my Dark Realm with Dissolving Light, use it at least ten times!

"It's a pity that within the Dark Realm, there's no light elemental energy. Your light elemental essence won't get any replenishment, so how often can you use Dissolving Light?

"Once?

"Twice?

"Or maybe three times?"

Palmers sneered. "Even if your light elemental essence is powerful, it can at most let you use Dissolving Light three times! You're still far from breaking my Dark Realm!"

After saying this, Palmers looked pleased with himself.

Osborne raised an eyebrow.

"So, I need to use Dissolving Light ten times to break your Dark Realm? In that case, I'll just use it ten times!"

Osborne snorted coldly and attacked again.

Dissolving Light!

Another powerful beam of light shot out. This time, the Dissolving Light traveled over one hundred yards before the Dark Realm snuffed it out.

Osborne knew this meant the Dark Realm's power was weakening.

"It's working!

"Let's keep going!"

Osborne felt that his light elemental essence still held immense power. Without hesitation, he continued to unleash Dissolving Light.

Dissolving Light!

Dissolving Light!

Dissolving Light!

In an instant, Osborne unleashed Dissolving Light three more times. Each time, the beam shot further, depleting the Dark Realm's energy.

This situation stunned Palmers.

"How is this possible? How can you use Dissolving Light five times? How can your light elemental essence be so powerful?"

Palmers murmured, his face flushing red.

"Damn it! Osborne, you forced my hand. I wanted to take my time torturing you, but now I have to kill you quickly!

"Dark Tide!"

Palmers roared in fury, commanding the terrifying dark energy to surge toward Osborne like a tide, aiming to engulf him in darkness.

Osborne's expression changed, but he didn't panic.

He raised his martial art aura to protect himself while continuing to rapidly unleash Dissolving Light.

Dissolving Light!

Dissolving Light!

Dissolving Light!

Osborne's martial art aura was quickly draining, growing weaker by the second. It seemed like it could shatter at any moment.

At the same time, Palmers' Dark Realm was also rapidly losing power.

Palmers' expression grew more anxious.

He unleashed his most powerful attack, trying to shatter Osborne's martial art aura and kill him before the Dark Realm could be broken.

Osborne was also fighting desperately.

He could feel that Palmers' Dark Realm was on the verge of collapsing.

His expression was icy as he swiftly drew on the power of his light elemental essence.

Dissolving Light!

Dissolving Light!

Boom!

The moment Osborne's martial art aura shattered, Palmers' Dark Realm was pierced by Dissolving Light.

Immediately, Osborne felt the surrounding energies from the void. His recently shattered martial art aura was instantly replenished, shining brightly once again.

A faint blood-red light began to glow.

Palmers' face was filled with terror.

"How is this possible? How can your light elemental essence be this powerful? Did you manage to unleash Dissolving Light ten times without any energy replenishment?

"Even my grandfather, a Class S Supreme Overlord, couldn't do that!

"Impossible!"

Palmers' face turned pale, and blood trickled from the corner of his mouth.

The Dark Realm was powerful. The moment it was shattered, he suffered a severe energy backlash, leaving him with significant internal injuries.

Most importantly, he couldn't use the Dark Realm again for a while.

Osborne stared at him with a cold smile.

"Palmers! Your Dark Realm is nothing special! Now that it's gone, are you ready to face disappointment?

"Palmers, your head is mine!"

With that, Osborne's body flickered, and he appeared behind Palmers. The Goddess of Grain's scythe in his hand slashed toward Palmers' neck.
