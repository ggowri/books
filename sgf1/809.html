Chapter 809 Eityreia Organization Assassins

Osborne unleashed his power, shattering the combined attack of the four adversaries with a single punch.

"How did you find me?" Osborne asked, genuinely curious.

He had completely altered his appearance, changed his clothes from top to bottom, and even suppressed his aura.

One of the attackers smirked. "Axell, your disguise is impressive. You changed your entire look, and we were almost fooled! But you overlooked a few details. For instance, your shoes are too clean.

"If you were just a regular tourist who had been wandering around the mountains, your shoes would be caked with mud. But yours are spotless!

"Plus, this is the Williamson family's private grounds, not open to the public. So you're not escaping!"

Osborne felt a bit resigned; it was amazing that such a minor detail had given him away.

"Fine, then let's settle this!"

Osborne pulled out the Volt Hammer from Dimensional Space and struck out with it.

The air crackled with the sudden burst of lightning.

The four members of the Williamson family were taken aback.

They quickly braced themselves, attempting to fend off the attack. They had witnessed Osborne defeat Abner with a single blow using the Volt Hammer before.

Abner was ranked 320th on the Godson Rankings and wielded a divine artifact.

In contrast, the four of them, though elders of the Williamson family and all Class AAA, were no match for Abner, let alone Osborne.

They had only tried to stall him, hoping for reinforcements. Faced with Osborne's assault, they had no choice but to fight as a team.

The lightning struck them with devastating force, piercing through their defenses and heavily wounding them.

The air was soon filled with the faint smell of burnt flesh.

The four were hurled violently backward, crashing to the ground, struggling to rise.

They stared at Osborne in fear.

Osborne sneered, saying, "Is that all you've got? You dare to hunt me down with such weak skills? Time to end this!"

As he prepared to finish them off, his Satan's Eye suddenly throbbed violently, a deep sense of danger erupting from within him.

Without a moment to think, Osborne quickly dodged his previous position.

A dark dagger sliced through the air where he had just stood, a faint shadow materializing and vanishing almost immediately.

But Osborne had seen enough.

"The Eityreia Organization?!"

His face hardened. The attacker was someone he recognized from the Honor Quest Ceremony—Mose, one of the Eityreia Organization assassins, had been sitting next to the attacker then.

So, this was one of Mose's comrades.

"Die!"

Before he could react, another figure appeared behind him, moving without any energy fluctuations, a dagger speeding toward his heart.

But this time, Osborne was ready.

"Get lost!"

He spun around and delivered a powerful punch, sending the attacker flying through the air, blood spattering as the figure's eyes widened in shock.

A cold voice cut through the tension. "You've figured out our Stealth Arts!"

It was Mose, the top-tier assassin ranked 205th on the Godson Rankings.

Mose emerged from the shadows, flanked by five more dark figures, all cloaked in black robes, their faces obscured except for their eyes.

"Mose!"

Seeing Mose, Osborne felt a wave of relief instead of panic.

This meant that Mose hadn't gone after Emma and the others. He diverted the attention of the Eityreia Organization assassins, which would have made Emma and her group safer.

However, this only intensified the pressure on Osborne.

Mose sneered, saying, "Axell, I warned you. The moment you left the Williamson family's grounds, I'd come out here to take your head! Well, I'm here now. Is your head ready?"

Osborne smirked. "My head's right here. Come and take it if you can! But you'd better watch out, or you might end up leaving yours behind!"

Mose laughed. "You think just because you beat Cuthbert at the Honor Quest Ceremony, you're on my level? You just got lucky!

"Besides, in the wild, even Cuthbert would be no match for me.

"I'm coming for your head now!"

With that, Mose's body flickered and vanished into the void.

The other five Eityreia Organization assassins followed suit, disappearing from sight.

Osborne took a deep breath, focusing intently on the spatial fluctuations around him. The Class S Stealth Arts of the Eityreia Organization assassins caused subtle ripples in the space.

It didn't take long for Osborne to detect several spatial distortions.

He thought, "One! Two! Three! Four! Five!

Wait a minute!

There should be six Eityreia Organization assassins, but I only sense five fluctuations. Where's the sixth?"

Osborne's expression turned serious.
