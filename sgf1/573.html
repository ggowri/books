Chapter 573 Picking Up the Fight

The battle with Owen was unavoidable.

If Osborne didn't face him, everyone would think he was scared. His sister had been beaten by Owen, and if he remained silent and didn't seek revenge, it would be a clear sign of weakness.

As a fellow heir, Osborne would forever be overshadowed by Owen, and people would look down on him for it.

Osborne's anger was like a raging fire, almost impossible to control. He wanted nothing more than to march straight to Owen's door, challenge him to a duel on Challenge Peak, and smash his arrogance to pieces, all to avenge Yulissa.

But instead of acting impulsively, Osborne decided to wait a few more days.

His Satan's Eye was close to reaching level six. Just a couple more days of absorbing star power, and he'd be there.

With that added strength, he'd have a much better chance of winning.

But his fury needed an outlet. He was about to explode if he didn't do something.

Osborne's voice was icy as he asked, "Isaac, since Dampston won first place in Branch Bloodline Competition, we get to pick our residence first, right?"

Isaac was caught off guard by the question but nodded.

"That's right. As the champions, we have the first pick among the ten estates on Mount Seven. Traditionally, the winners choose First Estate at the top of the mountain."

Isaac frowned, sensing something was up. "You're not thinking of moving into First Estate, are you? That might be tricky. I don't think Yalynch will give it up easily.

"Even though we have the right to choose First Estate, their strength far exceeds ours. If they refuse, there's not much we can do."

Osborne's lips curled into a cold smile. "First Estate at the top? I'm not interested. What I want is Third Estate, the one Ransford is currently occupying."

"Third Estate?"

Isaac was astonished. "You're planning to go after Ransford? They're much stronger than us. I don't think they'll hand it over."

Osborne chuckled, a dangerous glint in his eyes. "Of course they won't. That's exactly what I'm counting on. If they refuse, it gives me the perfect excuse to make my move."

Isaac stared at him, stunned.

"You're seriously planning to start a fight with Ransford?"

Osborne's voice was laced with cold determination. "They had a hand in bullying Yulissa. Do you think I'm going to let them walk away unscathed? Go and tell Ransford that we want the Third Estate.

"They'd better pack up and leave by tomorrow morning, or they'll be facing the consequences."

After delivering his icy ultimatum, Osborne turned and left, while Isaac sucked in a sharp breath, his brow deeply furrowed.

But in the end, he nodded.

He knew that Osborne's anger had to be released. Compared to challenging Owen, going to war with Ransford seemed a better option.

It offered a slightly better chance of success.

"Alright, I'll notify Ransford immediately. This is our right, after all."

Osborne nodded slightly. "I'm off to cultivate."

After speaking, he reached out and gently ruffled Yulissa's hair. Her face was still wet with tears, but her eyes glistened with deep emotion and gratitude.

"Rest and recover. I'll make sure you get your revenge.

"First, Ransford, and then Owen. None of them will escape."

And with that, Osborne returned to his place.

He made his way to the rooftop, settling into a meditative pose as he looked up at the vast, starry sky.

Osborne activated his Satan's Eye, letting the energy of the stars flow into him.

This was the fifth day of his star absorption, and he was close to a breakthrough.

Just two more days and the sixth star would ignite in his vision, marking the evolution of his Satan's Eye to the next level.

When that happened, his power would surge, placing him among the strongest, right up there with Vincent, Matthew, Owen, and even Godfather himself.

With this newfound strength, Osborne was confident he could finally face Owen and crush him.

"Soon.

"Owen, once Godfather's birthday banquet is over, it'll be time for our battle.

"You dared to strike Yulissa. I'll make sure you pay for that a hundred times over."

As he whispered these words, Osborne's gaze sharpened, drawing in the star power from all around him. His Satan's Eye absorbed it all, and instantly, a layer of frost formed on his skin, encasing him in an icy shell.

But inside, his strength was growing rapidly, both his body and soul becoming more powerful.

Meanwhile, Isaac had reached Ransford's Third Estate, as Osborne had instructed.

He was met by the elder of Ransford, Larry Augustine, who looked at him with a cold, mocking smile.

"You want us to vacate Third Estate for you? And you expect us to do it by tomorrow morning? Are you out of your mind?"

Isaac remained unfazed. "We won first place in the competition. By the rules, we have the right to claim the Third Estate.

"You're supposed to move out!"

Larry's laughter echoed through the room.

"First place, huh? And you think that gives you the power to kick us out? We're not going anywhere.

"If you want Third Estate, you'll have to take it by force."

The rest of Ransford joined in, their eyes filled with ridicule as they looked at Isaac.
