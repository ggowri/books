Chapter 1065 Blood Demon Takes Action

Gerardo's face showed disdain. "If it were Sebastian using the Godfather's scepter, I might have been wary. But it's you, Wesson. You are not worthy of using it. So, just give it to me."

After saying this, Gerardo launched his attack.

Wesson's expression changed dramatically. Gerardo's speed was too fast. In the blink of an eye, he was in front of Wesson, reaching out to snatch the Godfather's scepter.

Fortunately, Wesson reacted quickly, immediately swinging the Godfather's scepter down against Gerardo's hand. Gerardo's palm exploded.

A terrifying force erupted from the Godfather's scepter, sending Gerardo flying backward.

Gerardo looked at his shattered hand, somewhat in disbelief, but then turned his gaze back to the Godfather's scepter in Wesson's hand, greed shining even more fiercely in his eyes.

"Hmph. I underestimated the Godfather's scepter. It seems I must kill you if I want to get it. In that case, I will kill you. Fusion!"

Gerardo shouted, and his body began to undergo drastic changes, soon taking on the form of the goddess Medusa.

His exploded hand fully restored.

A terrifying power erupted from Gerardo's body, causing everyone present to feel immense pressure.

Gerardo sneered, "Wesson, it's all over! Without your assistance, those worthless fools won't hold out for long. They will be killed off soon. And you will be next."

Wesson tensed up, his complexion turning ashen.

The situation was highly unfavorable to them.

Although the coalition forces had a significant advantage in fighting the Aberrants' army with combat drones and robots, they had suffered substantial losses yet were still on the path to victory.

However, with another one hundred Class S clones, the combat drones and robots suddenly felt more like decorations, their numbers rapidly dwindling.

The battle had entered a meat grinder mode. The casualties among the coalition forces were increasing, and there was no hope of victory in sight.

Even worse was the plight of the nearly fifty Class S warriors, who were now down to only forty.

Their opponents, over five hundred Class S clones, were launching a frenzied siege, with someone dying every moment.

And the only one with a chance to turn the tide, Wesson, was entangled with Gerardo.

"Are we really going to lose?" Wesson felt immense anguish.

"Let's roll!" But just then, a powerful shout suddenly rang out, accompanied by a terrifying battle intent that dispersed some of the dark clouds above.

Following that, a team that no one had noticed finally moved out. It was the Blood Demon Organization.

Nearly five thousand members of the Blood Demon Organization seemed quite insignificant compared to the nearly fifty thousand strong coalition forces.

They had remained hidden until the situation turned unfavorable, and they finally decided to take action.

Their target was not the Aberrants' army but rather the over five hundred Class S clones attacking Bryce and the others.

Members of the Blood Demon Vanguard, such as First Augustine and Ninety-third Augustine, charged to the forefront, raising their super energy guns and aiming at a group of Class S clones.

When a divine power bullet from the super energy gun hit a clone, it broke through their defenses and caused an explosion.

While it did not kill the clone, it seriously injured him, and subsequent divine power bullets shattered his body into pieces.

The power of these super energy guns was at least equal to that of the massive energy cannons but offered far more flexibility and precision.

At the same time, regular members of the Blood Demon Organization pulled their triggers, using ordinary energy guns to attack the clones.

These energy guns had undergone special modifications. While they were less potent than the super energy guns, they were still much stronger than standard energy weapons.

One energy gun could not breach the defenses of a Class S clone, but one hundred or a thousand energy guns striking the same Class S clone simultaneously could easily kill him.

Members of the Blood Demon Organization were well-trained, and with their sudden arrival, over twenty clones were quickly killed, greatly relieving the pressure on Bryce and the others.

The members continued to pull their triggers coldly.

One by one, clones fell under their fire, like chopped trees. Waves upon waves collapsing.

After several rounds of shooting, without any physical engagement, at least one hundred clones had been blasted apart.

With the addition of the Blood Demon Organization members, the tide of battle began to change once again.
