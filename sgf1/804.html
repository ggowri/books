Chapter 804 Leaving

Osborne shook his head. "I can't leave with you.

"My interdimensional travel isn't advanced enough to bring all of us to Rosmandi in one leap. Once the Williamson family realizes they've lost my trail, they might send a Class S Supreme Overlord to track me down.

"And then, we're all finished.

"But as long as I'm within their sight, they won't rush to act. Even a Class S Supreme Overlord, who values their prestige, won't attack me immediately.

"Then, I'll get my chance.

"After you've ensured your safety, I can use Dimensional Space to return to Rosmandi alone. That's our best option."

Osborne finished speaking, and Aleena hesitated.

He had only offended the Williamson family to help Rhett seek revenge.

Leaving Osborne alone to face danger was a burden Aleena couldn't bear, but she knew it was the best plan.

"Alright!" Aleena gritted her teeth. "Just be careful. If anything happens to you, I can't explain it to Mr. Caleb Augustine, nor can I face Godfather."

Osborne smiled lightly, "Don't worry. Killing me won't be that easy. Let's go now. While other superpowers are dispersing, the Williamson family shouldn't dare to pursue us openly."

Emma was anxious. "I'm not going anywhere! I'm staying to face this with you!"

Osborne shook his head. "It's out of the question!"

"Why? We came here together. I can't abandon you to danger."

Osborne remained firm. "I said no, and that's final. If the Williamson family fails to capture me, they might send a Class S Supreme Overlord to get me personally. Since you can't help me, staying would only slow me down.

"Plus, with you, my interdimensional travel would drain more energy, and it would take longer to escape. I have no certainty of escaping a Class S Supreme Overlord!"

Once he'd finished, Emma fell silent, biting her lip and looking at Osborne with worry.

Not giving Emma a chance to speak further, Osborne continued, "Enough dawdling. If other superpowers clear out, it'll be even tougher for us to leave safely!

"Go back to Rosmandi and wait for me. I'll be fine."

Emma's eyes were full of concern, but she finally nodded. "Okay, I'll go back. But you take this. Otherwise, I'm staying."

Suddenly, Emma produced a small shield.

Osborne was surprised. "Swallow Shield?"

He hadn't expected Emma to entrust him with the divine artifact, the Swallow Shield.

"Take it! If you ever face a Class S Supreme Overlord, the Swallow Shield might buy you a moment's time. If you don't take it, I'll stay by your side and face death with you."

Osborne hesitated but eventually nodded.

"Alright, I'll keep it safe for you. I'll return it to you when I reach Rosmandi."

Emma finally relaxed, although she knew that, with the Swallow Shield, if Osborne were ever pursued by a Class S Supreme Overlord, he would still be no match, buying only a few extra seconds of life.

Emma wrapped her arms around him, whispering in his ear, "Be careful, Osborne.

"If you die, I don't want to live. I'll descend into hell, become a demon, and when I've exacted vengeance on the Williamson family for you, I'll come to you. So, you must live!"

Osborne's body trembled, and he nodded firmly. "I will, for your sake."

As Osborne prepared to move, Emma suddenly activated Satan's Eye.

A peculiar energy surged from her Satan's Eye and flowed into Osborne's body.

"What is this?!" Osborne exclaimed, astonished.

Emma's cheeks flushed as she explained softly, "This is the energy that can enhance our cognition and improve our mental acuity. I can control it myself, but now I'm giving it all to you. You have to make it back alive."

With that, Emma released Osborne and took his hand. "Let's go!"

Osborne, still stunned, thought to himself, "Emma just gave me all the energy that could enhance our minds. What about her?"

Unfortunately, he had no way to return the energy to Emma.

All he could do was nod. "Alright, let's move!"

Osborne waved his hand, opening a Dimensional Space. "Hold hands. I'll take you out of here!"

In no time, he and the five others stepped into the dimensional rift.

Their figures vanished instantly.

Thirty miles out at sea, their silhouettes reappeared. Osborne's face was pale, clearly showing the strain.

Transporting five people through interdimensional travel had taken a severe toll on him. He could manage only two or three more jumps before his mental strength would be utterly depleted.

"Just one more time!"

Osborne gritted his teeth and tore through space once more, reappearing another 30 miles away.

"Alright, this is as far as I can take you! Stay safe, everyone!"
