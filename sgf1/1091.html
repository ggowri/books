Chapter 1090 Counterattack

With Yulissa's excited exclamation, everyone's attention snapped to Osborne hovering in the air.

Instantly, a wave of exhilaration swept through the Augustine family.

The Godfather had arrived!

However, that jubilation quickly gave way to a sobering reality. Osborne's presence, while inspiring, was just one person against an overwhelming tide.

They were still encircled by six hundred Class S warriors, with nearly a hundred hidden assassins lurking in the shadows, barely visible.

What could Osborne do to change the dire situation they found themselves in?

Even Wesson, Bryce, and Greyson held no illusions that Osborne's arrival would shift the balance. To them, it seemed like adding another body to the grave rather than a victory.

Despair hung heavily in the air, sinking deep into their hearts.

But with keen perception, Osborne suddenly fixed his gaze in one direction, his expression chilling in an instant.

"Eityreia Organization assassins! You're asking for death!"

In a blink, Osborne vanished, moving with the speed of thought itself.

Meanwhile, on the left flank of the Augustine family, an assassin emerged from the shadows, brandishing a divine artifact dagger aimed straight for the heart of a Class AAA warrior.

A cruel smile twisted his lips as he thought to himself, "This is my third kill today; it's going to be just as easy! I'll strike and vanish!"

With lethal precision, the dagger's point was just inches from its target. The Class AAA warrior seemed frozen, unable to react, overwhelmed by the superiority of this Class S assassin.

But before the assassin could savor his imminent victory, Osborne appeared before him.

Osborne's icy glare sent a ripple of dread through the assassin.

Yet, fueled by instinct, the assassin swiftly redirected his dagger and launched it at Osborne's chest.

Osborne laughed coldly.

"Fool! You're asking for it!"

In a flash, he seized the assassin's wrist, gripping it with a vice-like hold.

"Let go!" the assassin screamed, unleashing every ounce of strength in a desperate bid to escape.

But Osborne's grip was unyielding, grounded in a formidable power that rendered the assassin's struggles futile.

Panic crossed the assassin's face as he realized he was outmatched.

Desperately, he brandished another dagger and aimed it at his own wrist, intent on severing it to escape.

Osborne declared menacingly. "Bold move! But too bad for you, you won't be getting away! Die here!"

In the blink of an eye, Osborne launched a powerful kick directly into the assassin's chest.

Even the top-tier Class S warriors, Gerardo and Armstrong, couldn't withstand Osborne's might. How could this average Class S contender hope to fare any better?

The sheer force of the impact sent shockwaves through the assassin's body, shattering his lower half in a gruesome display.

"Ahhh!" The scream pierced the air but was quickly snuffed out, replaced by an eerie silence.

Just like that, a Class S Eityreia Organization assassin lay dead at Osborne's feet.

The shock was palpable among the onlookers; they stood frozen in disbelief.

Yet Osborne's didn't stop here. His mental strength was still finely tuned, sensing more assassins lurking nearby, one of whom was set on targeting Emma.

A wave of murderous intent washed over Osborne as he zeroed in on her. Without a second thought, Osborne vanished again, reappearing right before Emma just as another assassin was about to strike.

He kicked the hidden assassin hard, determined to keep her safe.

The assassin's body erupted in a gruesome display, half of it exploding into a mist of crimson as he was revealed from his cloaked position—already lifeless.

The realization hit the Augustine family like a thunderclap; they hadn't even noticed there was an assassin targeting Emma until now.

"Whoa!" A wave of hushed gasps rippled through the crowd, followed by a crescendo of loud murmurs.

"Did Osborne just take out another one? Another hidden Class S assassin? With another kick?"

Stunned expressions adorned the faces of the Augustine family members. Those who had initially thought that Osborne's arrival wouldn't change their dire predicament suddenly found a spark of hope igniting within them.

Their cheeks flushed with excitement as adrenaline surged through their veins.

"Eityreia Organization assassins, if you're here to meet your end, then I'm happy to oblige!" Osborne's voice dripped with menace.

With cold determination, he reached into the Dimensional Space, retrieving the Goddess of Grain's scythe.

He had relinquished most of his divine artifacts to the Blood Demon Vanguard, but he still clung to the Thundergod Gloves, the Volt Hammer, and this haunting scythe.

As he gripped the weapon, a vicious glint danced in his eyes. He swiftly scanned the surroundings, pinpointing the hidden assassins lurking in the shadows.

Then, with ruthless intent, he acted.

"Kill!"

Osborne's speed was nothing short of breathtaking; he closed the distance to one of the assassins in an instant. The assassin was still cloaked and utterly oblivious to the danger now stalking him.

By the time he sensed the threat, it was too late.

The Goddess of Grain's scythe sang through the air as Osborne swung it with deadly precision.

Time seemed to freeze for a heartbeat before the assassin's head sailed through the air, a fountain of blood spraying upwards, painting the scene in vivid scarlet.
