Chapter 1656 A Plan

Osborne reflected on the gap between himself and the Deity King warriors. "The biggest difference is my realm; I'm still stuck in the mid-stage of Celestial Deity. Even with my Level Seven spatial elemental essence and a Level Nine Divine Body, I can't unleash my full potential!

But as long as I keep leveling up, the distance between me and a Deity King warrior will shrink rapidly! Maybe once I reach the Deity Overlord stage, I'll be able to face an ordinary Deity King head-on!"

The thought ignited a fire within him, and he felt an urge to find a Deity King warrior right then and there to test his strength.

But he held back; now wasn't the time.

The abilities of a Deity King were unpredictable. Even though Osborne possessed Level Seven spatial elemental essence, which could keep him safe, he couldn't guarantee it would protect him from a Deity King's countermeasures.

If he couldn't escape using spatial power, he'd be in deep trouble.

He focused on his goal. "If I can obtain death scent liquid and fully restore the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity, I could challenge a Deity King!"

A sense of urgency washed over him.

He hadn't forgotten his primary objective for coming to Divine Peak Nine. To acquire the death scent liquid needed to completely revive the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity.

"Death scent liquid can only be obtained by defeating a Deity King-level Deathborn! I can't take one down on my own, but maybe I can leverage Angelo's strength. How can I get him to take out a Deity King-level Deathborn for me?"

With that thought, Osborne began to formulate a plan.

After a while, he suddenly stood up and gently roused Diana from her meditation.

"Diana, Angelo's team is approaching! It looks like they're on the same path as us. We should hide!"

Diana nodded, understanding the urgency.

Osborne took her hand, and they slipped into a parallel space, using the Eclipsing Mantra to mask their presence, positioning themselves at least six miles away from Angelo's group.

Activating his Satan's Eye, Osborne quickly spotted Angelo's team.

They were a formidable force, composed of over a hundred Deity Overlord warriors, exuding strength that allowed them to move confidently through Divine Peak Nine.

They knew there were no large Deathborn nests nearby, only a few village-level and town-level ones. Even if they were discovered, those lesser nests wouldn't pose a serious threat.

To challenge them, a city-level or national-level Deathborn would be necessary.

As they traveled, they would pass by two city-level Deathborn nests and one national-level nest.

If they could navigate around these without being detected by the patrols or surrounded by the Deathborn army, they could safely reach the Celestial Crystal mining area.

In essence, as long as they exercised enough caution, there was still hope.

From six miles away, Osborne watched Angelo, who appeared to be just an ordinary old man at the center of the group.

"Only within a national-level Deathborn nest will there be Deity King-level Deathborn! If I want that death scent liquid, I need to ensure that Angelo's team gets discovered by the Deathborn from the national-level nest. If that happens, there's a good chance he'll end up fighting a Deity King-level Deathborn! If he's strong enough to take it down... I could finally get my hands on that death scent liquid!"

Excitement sparked in Osborne's eyes.

Just then, he noticed Angelo glancing in his direction, a slight frown forming on Angelo's brow.

Osborne's heart skipped a beat, a chill running down his spine.

He thought, "Could he have spotted me? No way! I'm six miles away, and with this thick fog enveloping Divine Peak Nine, he shouldn't be able to detect me! Besides, I'm in a parallel space with the Eclipsing Mantra masking my energy signature!"

Taking a deep breath, he calmed himself.

"Maybe he just sensed someone was watching him."

With that in mind, he shifted his gaze.

Sure enough, Angelo's furrowed brow relaxed, though his expression remained somewhat dazed. He turned away, no longer focusing on Osborne's direction.

Osborne couldn't help but marvel at Angelo's perceptiveness.

"So, he can feel my watching gaze! The capabilities of a Deity King warrior are impressive. Even from such a distance, he could sense my gaze."

Becoming more cautious, Osborne decided to avoid eye contact with Angelo and began studying the others in the Deity King's entourage.

To his surprise, a familiar face caught his attention, and a look of disbelief spread across his features.

"Calvin?!"

Osborne was stunned.

Calvin had been rendered useless when Osborne shattered his divine power essence; he'd assumed Calvin's journey was over. Yet here he was, somehow brought to Divine Peak Nine by Athena.

"Could it be that Calvin hasn't lost hope? Is he banking on the chance of finding a Miracle Elixir here to restore his divine power essence?"

A cold smile crept onto Osborne's face.

At this moment, Angelo's team finally reached the spot where Algernon and the others had met their demise.
