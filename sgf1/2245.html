Chapter 2218 Take Action-2

"Set sail!"

"Set sail!"

With a soft hum, a hexagram formation materialized in the clearing.

It glowed brighter and brighter, engulfing the metal boxes.

It was a Spatial Translocator!

Osborne's heart sank.
So the Phantom Tide Demon Sect isn't keeping these slaves for themselves. They're shipping them off somewhere else. This just made things a whole lot more complicated!

Even if my parents were sold to the Phantom Tide Demon Sect over twenty years ago, finding them now is like looking for a needle in a haystack!

As the harsh reality dawned on him, his spirits plummeted.

Isa's voice cut through the tense atmosphere, laced with urgency. "Osborne, should we make our move now? If we don't act fast, those slaves will be teleported away! And their fate..."

Osborne's expression turned steely.

He took a deep breath and said, "Let's do it! Even though these slaves aren't our problem, we can't just stand by and watch now that we've found them!"

With that, he fixed his eyes on Vince, then turned to the imposing figure in heavy armor standing behind Vince.

That was an Ascension Nine Supernal Deity King—the most powerful being in the area.

Osborne used his Satan's Eye to see through the armor and recognized him.

It was Shelvin, one of the seven core leaders of the Phantom Tide Demon Sect!

"Isa, you deal with Vince! I'll take on Shelvin! We need to strike like lightning—don't give them a chance to send out an alarm!" Osborne commanded.

Isa nodded. "Roger that!"

"Let's rock and roll!"

In the blink of an eye, Osborne and Isa sprang into action. Just as the Spatial Translocator was about to kick into gear, Osborne materialized out of thin air.

He pointed a finger at the device, and poof! The Translocator ground to a sudden halt, and the glowing hexagram formation vanished in a flash.

Vince's excitement fizzled into confusion.

He stood there, rooted to the spot, totally bewildered about what had just gone down.

Shelvin was a lot quicker on the uptake. He roared, "Damn it! Mr. Vince Brunson, look out! We've got trouble! Someone's trying to mess with us! Contact Mr. Brunson right away!"

With that, he locked eyes with Osborne, his gaze blazing with homicidal rage.

"Take him out!" he bellowed and lunged at Osborne.

Osborne scoffed. "You're just at Ascension Nine level, and you think you can take me down? You're in for a rude awakening!"

Without a moment's hesitation, Osborne unleashed a thunderous palm strike at Shelvin.

Sacred Power raged as his hand connected with Shelvin, shattering the divine power defense in the blink of an eye. However, the thick armor Shelvin was wearing absorbed the impact.

"Wait, what the hell?!"

Osborne was caught off guard.

His attack, though not at full throttle, was no joke—no run-of-the-mill Ascension Nine warrior could take it. After all, he'd just reached Ascension Seven, way stronger than before.

Back when he was Ascension Four, he'd taken down an Ascension Ten warrior like Lord Malevolence.

Taking out an Ascension Nine should've been a piece of cake, but here he was, shocked that Shelvin had weathered his strike.

Osborne stared daggers at Shelvin's armor, his eyes icy cold.

That armor's got some serious defense! But you know what? It won't be your savior! Unless that armor's a Sacred Artifact, you're toast!

He launched another attack.

This time, a massive wave of Sacred Power coursed through him, forming a gigantic hand that came crashing down on Shelvin like a meteor.

Shelvin's eyes popped out in disbelief.

A deafening explosion shook the place.

Osborne's strike sent Shelvin flying through the air. His armor shattered into a million pieces under the impact.

Blood spurted from Shelvin's mouth as he hit the ground and lay there, unable to move a muscle.

Meanwhile, on the other side of the battlefield, Isa had also jumped into the fray. Enveloped in darkness, she darted towards Vince at breakneck speed.

The power of the darkness exploded all around her.

Vince, an Ascension Seven Supernal Deity King, didn't stand a ghost of a chance against her. Isa had all the strength of Osborne's past life and could go toe-to-toe with Ascension Ten foes like Lord Malevolence for quite a while.

Vince?

He didn't have a prayer!

Vince was thrown back, blood pouring from his lips as he crumpled to the ground, unconscious.
