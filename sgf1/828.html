Chapter 828 The Confrontation

"What did you just say?" Clarissa shouted.

Her voice was as cold as the arctic wind, and she clenched her fists tightly.

Ebony's smile was barely there. "What's this? Are you excited now? I said, my brother has taken a liking to you! I'm here to escort you to Marcelin. He's prepared a grand wedding in your honor.

"Let me enlighten you. Women vying to marry my brother could form a line from Rosmandi to Marcelin! Even princesses and daughters of superpowers dream of becoming his bride.

"It's quite the honor for you to be chosen by my brother."

After Ebony delivered her statement, her face glowed with unmistakable pride.

Clarissa turned as frigid as frost and let out a disdainful snort.

"Your name is Ebony, right? You must be delusional. Go find someone else who's interested in marrying your brother! Who he fancies is his business, not mine!

"I want nothing to do with your Thornberry family, and I have zero interest in marrying your brother!

"Now, please leave!"

Ebony was taken aback and raised her voice in disbelief, "You're refusing? Do you understand the gravity of your decision?"

Clarissa's reply was as cold as the winter frost, "I'll say it again. Leave, now!"

Ebony's face hardened.

From her being erupted a formidable and terrifying aura that instantly pressed down upon Clarissa with palpable intensity.

Clarissa gaped in shock.

"Class AAA?" she whispered.

Stepping in front of Donna and the others, she shielded them from Ebony's oppressive might.

Ebony haughtily declared, "You're so fortunate that my brother has feelings for you! How dare you refuse? You're courting death!

"As the heir of the Thornberry family, my brother has never been rejected! Not in Marcelin, and certainly not in Rosmandi!

"I invited you politely, and you refused. Then don't blame me for taking you by force!" Ebony declared.

Her hand signaled her subordinates.

"Take her! She's the Fatebearer, crucial for my brother's advancement to Class S. We must have her, no matter the cost. If she resists, subdue her!"

At her command, the men in black swiftly encircled Clarissa. Their auras flared, revealing their prowess. A wave of shock began to seep into Clarissa.

These men clad in black were no ordinary enforcers but masters; each one boasted strength above Class A, with two among them having ascended to the formidable ranks of Class AA.

These men, AA-class masters, were acting as mere bodyguards for Ebony? The Thornberry family's influence was staggering.

Clarissa was shocked. Yet, fear did not quell her resolve to refuse Ebony. Her heart belonged to Osborne alone, so she would never yield to another's affection.

"Aunt Donna, father, please go back!" Clarissa called out.

Her gaze locked onto Ebony with a cold determination. "You want me to go to Marcelin with you, right? Then try and make me! Let's see if you can drag my body there!"

After her declaration, Clarissa unleashed her own aura.

Ebony looked stern as she shouted, "How stubborn! You'd rather die than come with me to marry my brother? Very well! You've angered me!

"But with your mere AA-class strength, you're not even fit to die before me! I forgot to mention that I rank 398th on the Godson Rankings.

"Though my ranking isn't high, it's more than enough to defeat you!"

With those words, she flicked her wrist dismissively as she commanded, "Go on! Seize her!"

In an instant, over a dozen men in black launched their attack.

They were all masters, each ranking above Class A, with two among them even achieving the formidable Class AA. Together, they surged towards Clarissa, painting a fearsome spectacle that would chill anyone to the bone.

Yet, Clarissa remained unshaken.

Behind her, the Divine and Demonic Wings unfurled, encasing her in a protective cocoon. The onslaught of the men in black was met with the unyielding barrier of the wings.

The Divine and Demonic Wings flapped with a strength that sent over a dozen men in black flying, even the two AA-class masters unable to withstand the force. They were hurled away, blood painting the air.

Bang!

In mere moments, the men in black lay defeated.

Ebony's surprise was evident. "Impressive. You're only at Class AA, yet you wield such power. You are indeed worthy of my brother.

"But your strength, though commendable, is still not enough against me!"

Ebony sprang into action with a decisive swiftness.

"Solar Realm!"

Buzz!

The air around the Radcliffe Estate was suddenly charged with a terrifying force. Ten suns appeared in the sky. Their scorching breath was focused solely on Clarissa, causing her Divine and Demonic Wings to crackle ominously under the strain.

Clarissa's face paled.

"Realm!"

Ebony's smile was chilling. "Within my realm, I am omnipotent. You'd do well to submit."

With a wave of her hand, the ten suns blazed even terrifyingly brighter.

Bang!

The Divine and Demonic Wings shattered under the relentless assault, and the force slammed into Clarissa's body with the fury of a tempest.

Clarissa coughed up a mouthful of blood.
