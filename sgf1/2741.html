This won't do!

Necropolis City is full of dangers. If she knows nothing about it, she could easily be used by Frederick or even lose her life there."

She looked shocked, her eyes narrowing slightly.

Osborne replied, "Necropolis City is where the Twelve Celestial Overlords of the Dark Planet rose to power. At that time, the Overpowering Dynasty ruled the Racial Convergence Planet, and the beings there suffered under oppression. Until the Twelve Celestial Overlords of the Dark Planet suddenly emerged, the twelve supreme warriors defeated and destroyed the Overpowering Dynasty in one fell swoop, shaping the current structure of the Racial Convergence Planet."

"Few people know why the Twelve Celestial Overlords of the Dark Planet were so powerful. But I do! They gained their strength in Necropolis City.

"By then, he might quickly break through to Imperial Empyrean Sacredness.

"By then, can the Fairy Moon Sect withstand him?"

Once Emperor Frederick breaks through to Imperial Empyrean Sacredness, not only will the Fairy Moon Sect be unable to oppose him, but even the Evernight Court and Phantom Commerce Guild will face trouble.

Once Frederick breaks through to Imperial Empyrean Sacredness, his first move will undoubtedly be to force her. If she refuses to marry Frederick, he will mercilessly kill her and destroy the Fairy Moon Sect to eliminate any threat to the Glorious Divine Kingdom Royal Family.

Adriana quickly said, "Osborne, I have some important matters to handle. Stay in Nebula City, and contact me with the Star Token if anything comes up."

After saying that, she hurriedly left.

Obviously, she was going to prepare a countermeasure. In the past, although she knew Necropolis City was full of dangers and opportunities, she hadn't paid much attention to it.

But now, after learning the true origin of Necropolis City, she naturally needed to make more preparations.

This was precisely why Osborne told her the truth.

Osborne worried that relying on his strength alone, he couldn't stop Frederick's plan in Necropolis City, nor prevent him from breaking through to Imperial Empyrean Sacredness.

After all, the gap in strength between him and Frederick was too great.

With Adriana added, their chances of success would be much higher.

After all, even if Adriana's strength was slightly inferior to Frederick's, the gap wouldn't be too large. With Adriana's help at a critical moment, things would become much easier.

Osborne pondered seriously, "However, this still isn't secure enough.

Emperor Frederick, since he dared to bring powerful figures like Adriana into Necropolis City, must have not only intended to leverage their strength but also considered the possibility of them competing with him for opportunities. He must have made preparations..."

Osborne woke up Clara and Leroy; it was time to head to the treasury of the Glorious Divine Kingdom.
