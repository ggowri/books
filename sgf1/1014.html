Chapter 1014 Shocking News

Osborne blinked, momentarily stunned.

"Isa, what did you just say? It belongs to Anastasia herself? She cultivated them on her own? That's impossible! When I found her, she was just five years old!

"Ever since then, she's been with me!

"She never trained, not even before she fell ill, she didn't know the world had metahumans! How could she possibly cultivate such formidable energy?"

Isa gave a casual smile. "You don't trust your woman, do you?"

"Uh?"

Osborne froze.

Of course, he trusted Isa, but this time, her words were almost unbelievable.

Isa explained, "You misunderstood me! I said her body is special! She indeed didn't actively train, but as long as she's alive, she's essentially training!

"To be precise... she's not entirely human!"

"Not human?"

Osborne was dumbfounded again, finding Isa's words increasingly absurd.

He thought, "How could Anastasia not be human?"

Zoe, too, was left speechless, staring at Isa in disbelief, wondering if Isa had been drinking.

She mused, "Anastasia is not human?

Since I'm her twin sister, does that mean I'm not human either?"

Zoe looked down at her own body.

"I'm human! Definitely a human!"

Isa chuckled. "Let me be clear to avoid any wild speculations. What I mean is, she's not from this Blue Planet! Her body is different from Earthlings; she doesn't need to train. Her energy would rapidly increase just by being alive. By adulthood, she'll at least reach Class AAA!"

Hearing this, Osborne was even more stunned.

"Not an Earthling, so she's an alien?"

Isa shook her head, her expression turning serious.

"Alien? You could say that, but it's not accurate.

"If I'm not mistaken, she comes from a destroyed and sealed realm—Celestara! She's a human born of Celestara!"

Osborne was shaken to the core.

"Anastasia from Celestara? A human born of Celestara?"

Isa swiftly continued, "Remember what I told you before? A long, long time ago, Celestara was invaded by extraterrestrial Dark Deities. The powerful Lord Malevolence, leading countless Dark Deities, shattered Celestara, obliterating it!

"Though the Seven Deity Kings eventually injured him and sealed him, Celestara was destroyed, filled with a thick death scent, making it nearly impossible to give birth to intelligent life.

"So, the Seven Deity Kings joined forces to seal the immense Celestara, leaving only a small portion, cleansing it of the death scent to serve as a breeding ground for intelligent life to develop!

"This breeding ground is none other than Blue Planet!

"Blue Planet is a tiny fraction of Celestara. Even though Celestara is sealed, it doesn't mean it can't give birth to intelligent life.

"In fact, due to the harsh environment of Celestara, filled with deadly death scent, intelligent life born there is far more powerful, as the weak have been naturally selected out.

"Like her..."

Isa pointed at Anastasia, adding, "She's an intelligent life born of Celestara, a Celestara human! Her body structure is 99% identical to Earthlings, except for the extra crystal in her brain!

"This crystal, unique to Celestara humans, is called the Crystal of Life. Its function is to filter the death scent.

"With the Crystal of Life, they can survive in the death-scented environment of Celestara without being corroded or enchanted by the death scent.

"Celestara humans, once they reach adulthood, typically possess strength comparable to a Class A master, at the very least. Talented individuals might reach Class AA or even surpass that!

"Before, perhaps due to the environment of Blue Planet, constrained by its rules, her power didn't manifest. It only erupted now.

"You can feel her brain yourself; see if you can detect the Crystal of Life!"

Osborne was now utterly astonished.

He thought, "Isa claims Anastasia isn't an Earthling but a Celestara human?

That she has a crystal in her brain?

The Crystal of Life?

I don't believe it!"

He approached Anastasia, placing his hand on her head, and carefully sensed her mind.

Then, Osborne was left dumbfounded.

He really did feel a crimson crystal in Anastasia's mind, pulsating with immense vitality.

It was the Crystal of Life!

Osborne looked at Isa in shock.

Isa simply smiled, "Now you believe me, don't you? I once followed my teacher to Celestara and saw Celestara humans. So, I'm quite sure."

At this point, Isa turned her gaze to Zoe.

"Zoe, come here and let me check your body. Since you two are twin sisters, if she's from Celestara, so should you."
