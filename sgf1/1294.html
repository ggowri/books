Chapter 1293 Hilarious!

Terrence scoffed derisively at Osborne, his arrogance radiating off of him.

"A trump card? I'm curious to see what you've got that could possibly measure up to the twenty-nine Transcendent Deities of the Deschanel Clan!

"I hope your secret weapon packs a punch; we wouldn't want this to end too quickly, would we? That would just disappoint me."

With that, he wore an indifferent expression, as if the outcome was already decided.

The members of the Deschanel Clan looked on with mocking smiles at Divine Shadow and Osborne; their disdain was evident.

Divine Shadow's frustration boiled over. "Kid, are you going to show us your trump card or what?"

Osborne chuckled awkwardly. "Hold on, Grandma Divine Shadow. If they want to see my card, let's give them a show."

He raised his hand dramatically, and suddenly, the skies split open, creating a massive dimensional rift.

Figures began to emerge, stepping out one after another with purpose.

The Blood Demon members had arrived.

Fully armed and with steely expressions, they emerged from the rift, standing strong and unwavering in the face of the Deschanel Clan's twenty-nine Transcendent Deity warriors.

Their numbers grew larger—ten, then a hundred.

The members of the Deschanel Clan exchanged glances, their laughter echoing through the air as they took in the sight of Junior Deities emerging from the rift.

One of them couldn't help but chuckle.

"Is this really the ace up your sleeve, the Augustine family? A bunch of Junior Deities? You think they can take on our Transcendent Deity elders and leader?"

Laughter erupted around them. "This is priceless! Using Junior Deities against us? It's like throwing eggs against a rock—sheer madness!"

"Any of our Transcendent Deity elders or leader could take them down without breaking a sweat!"

What once might have been worry about Osborne's trump card was now replaced with condescension as the Deschanel Clan reveled in their perceived superiority.

Terrence grinned, mocking their display.

"Is this really your secret weapon? I'm deeply disappointed! Sure, it's impressive that each one boasts a Level Three or higher Divine Body, but at the end of the day, a Junior Deity is still just a Junior Deity, nothing more than insects in front of Transcendent Deity warriors."

His words provoked a chorus of laughter from the other warriors in the clan.

They were brimming with confidence and pride as they looked down on the Blood Demon members.

Terrence said, "Junior Deities?

"In our eyes, they're nothing but ants! We wouldn't even bother lifting a finger against them."

Terrence continued, "However, we've got to admit the Augustine family has a big secret. Each of their divine warriors having a Level Three Divine Body is something unbelievable! But do they really think that's enough to stand up to a full-on assault from the Deschanel Clan? They might as well be asking for a death wish!

"Even hundreds of Junior Deities stand no chance against just one Transcendent Deity. But this secret of granting at least a Level Three Divine Body to each warrior could be incredibly useful for us!

"If we could uncover that secret, the Deschanel Clan's power would skyrocket! So, I suppose waking up early wasn't such a bad idea after all."

Nods of agreement rippled through the members of the Deschanel Clan.

"Let's wipe out the Augustine family and seize that secret for ourselves!" one of the Deschanel Clan members exclaimed.

"Exactly! Crush them!" another shouted.

"Relying on a bunch of Junior Deities as their trump card is downright hilarious!"

As the scornful laughter rang out, Divine Shadow's anger churned within her like a storm.

She glared at Osborne, who stood beside her, brimming with confidence as if he held the ultimate secret weapon.

"This all you have?

"Osborne, are you pulling my leg? You actually think you can launch a counterattack against the Deschanel Clan and keep your cool in the face of twenty-nine Transcendent Deities with just these Junior Deities?"

Divine Shadow's voice trembled with rage as she gestured angrily toward the Blood Demon members.

At that moment, the group had swelled to over a hundred, and they kept coming.

Soon, their numbers would approach two hundred. But so what?

Even with two hundred Junior Deities, they'd barely manage to take down a few Superior Deities. To think they could pose a threat to the Transcendent Deities was nothing short of delusional!

"Easy there, Grandma Divine Shadow! No need to panic!" Osborne said, a trace of embarrassment creeping into his demeanor.

It was his first time establishing a two-way dimensional portal, with one end right there and the other hundreds of miles away at the Augustine Bastion.

This setup allowed him to teleport back to the bastion in a flash, while Blood Demon members could also quickly make their way to him.

Since it was his first attempt, Osborne wasn't quite adept yet, and the portal wasn't very wide.

Only ten Blood Demon members could pass through at once, which meant they were coming through at a snail's pace.

Fortunately, the Deschanel Clan hadn't launched an attack yet; they seemed to be biding their time, waiting to capture all of Osborne's cards in one go.

If they had decided to strike early, it would have posed quite a headache.

"Don't panic? How can I not? What's your real trump card? Don't tell me it's just these Junior Deities!"

Divine Shadow was nearly at her wit's end.

Osborne said with a grin, "Of course, there's more to it!

"Some of the Blood Demon members have already broken through to Intermediate Deity!"

"Intermediate Deity?!"

Divine Shadow's jaw dropped in disbelief.
