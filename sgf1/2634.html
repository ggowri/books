Chapter 2520 Illusionary Space Shattered-1

"I finally got the thousandth Demon Dragon's Breath!"

Osborne used the Heaven Arrow to kill the last Illusionary Heavenly Demon at Empyrean Sacredness Lesser Perfection, reached out to grab a Demon Dragon's Breath, and his expression was incredibly excited.

It's really too hard!

Fortunately, he finally did it. On the fifteenth day of entering the Phantomagic Realm, he collected a thousand Empyrean Sacredness Lesser Perfection Demon Dragon's Breaths.

A thousand Demon Dragon's Breaths can condense into a drop of Dragon Elixir.

At this moment, Osborne placed all the Demon Dragon's Breaths together. They quickly intertwined and fused, eventually forming a drop of Dragon Elixir radiating multicolored brilliance.

A word instantly flashed through his mind: perfect!

This is the Perfect Dragon Elixir, far stronger than the Legendary Dragon Elixir. Just a trace of its aura emanating made Osborne feel as if he saw countless stars twinkling, from the beginning of the Big Bang to their destruction...

This is Enlightenment State!

Osborne quickly stored the Perfect Dragon Elixir into the jadeflare vial and sealed it with Sacred Power. Only after ensuring no aura leaked out did he breathe a sigh of relief.

He thought, "Just a trace of its aura almost pushed me into Enlightenment State! The effect of this Perfect Dragon Elixir is too powerful! If I take it directly, I might need a long time to fully absorb it and wake up from the Enlightenment State."

Now is not the time for me to use it!

While there's still time, I need to collect more Perfect Dragon Elixirs! Such treasures are probably rare even in the deeper parts of the universe."

So, Osborne immediately took action again.

But what puzzled him was that after wandering around for a long time, he didn't encounter a single Illusionary Heavenly Demon. All the Illusionary Heavenly Demons in this independent illusionary space had vanished.

He thought, "Could it be that I've already killed them all?"

Osborne frowned deeply. He originally wanted to seize the time to collect more Perfect Dragon Elixirs. However, he couldn't find a single Illusionary Heavenly Demon.

At that moment, he suddenly felt the entire starry sky shaking violently.

Then, cracks began to appear in his vision, as if the heavens and earth were about to split apart!

Like glass shattering, more and more cracks appeared. Suddenly, at a certain moment, with a crisp sound, everything broke apart.

And Osborne found himself back in darkness.

At the same time, he sensed some distinct auras not far away.

This situation didn't make him nervous; instead, it filled him with joy.

Osborne thought, "That independent illusionary space has shattered!"

I’ve finally escaped from that independent illusionary space and returned to the Phantomagic Realm!”

Then, he looked into the distance.

There, Osborne sensed the presence of several warriors. At this moment, his Satan's Eye activated, instantly revealing those people clearly.

He felt surprised.

"It's actually them!"

At this moment, in Osborne's eyes, Azael and his elder were standing cautiously in the Phantomagic Realm, carefully observing their surroundings.

Osborne thought for a moment and let Flash take the form of a warhorse. After transforming back to his previous appearance, he mounted Flash and approached them.

"Who's there?"

Because Osborne didn't hide his presence, Azael and his elder sensed him as soon as he approached.

When they saw it was Osborne, they heaved a sigh of relief.

"So it's you, Mr. Augustine!"

Azael quickly greeted Osborne with utmost politeness.

Previously, Osborne single-handedly and effortlessly killed several advanced Empyrean Sacredness warriors from Cobalt Island. Azael had long been intimidated by his strength and dared not be impolite.
