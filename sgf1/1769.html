Chapter 1768 One Thousand Miracle Elixirs

Stellan, the super genius of Snow Fox Clan, declared with a fierce glint in his eyes, "I'll join the fight too!

"How dare the pathetic Human Community set up a challenging arena to confront the young talents of our clans? They're asking for trouble!

"Not only will I join the fight, but I'm sure every genius from our clans will step up. If we don't, those lowly Humans might actually think they're formidable.

"This battle is one we can't afford to lose, and we must win it decisively.

"We need to show those contemptible humans that their so-called talents are nothing but a joke in front of us!"

As Stellan's words hung in the air, nods of agreement rippled through the crowd.

"Exactly! The super geniuses of Raging Elephant Clan will also join the fray!" one of them shouted.

"And Fire Tiger Clan's top talents will be there too!" another added.

"Thunder Serpents Clan is in..." came the enthusiastic response.

"Then let's get moving! The arena is near Divine Peak Nine, and it's a staggering tens of millions of miles away. Even with the Ultimate Malevolence Airship, we'll need two days to get there."

"Let's set off right now!"

"Let's crush the Human Community's Seven Young Deity Kings!"

"Go!"

Within moments, the warriors from various clans who had been waiting in Wild Flame City for over a month were on the move, their destination set for Divine Peak Nine.

Not just the warriors but many geniuses who had been in seclusion were awakened by their elders and rushed to Divine Peak Nine to take part in this grand challenge.

"How dare the lowly Human Community challenge the young talents of our clans! You all head over there—even if you don't get a chance to fight, it's a great opportunity to learn!"

"Go and trample the pathetic Human Community underfoot!"

Meanwhile, within Divine Peak Nine, Osborne and Anastasia were swiftly navigating the terrain.

Osborne murmured to himself, "Up ahead is Celestial Sword Peak!

"There's a giant humanoid stone there that looks just like the one I saw before. It resembles the massive statue at the center of Divine Peak Nine—Shadow Terror Fiend—but it's not exactly the same.

"I wonder what the connection is!

"But that doesn't matter right now. That humanoid stone has five Miracle Elixirs growing on it, each radiating a different color. I can't let this opportunity slip away!"

His eyes sparkled with excitement as he gazed at the distant Celestial Sword Peak.

The effects of Miracle Elixirs far surpassed those of Celestial Crystals, and they were much rarer.

The red Miracle Elixir could rapidly boost one's cultivation level.

The yellow one enhanced the Divine Body's strength.

The blue Miracle Elixir improved elemental essence.

The white Miracle Elixir had powerful healing properties.

And the black Miracle Elixir increased soul power and comprehension.

In short, Miracle Elixirs were incredibly precious and only grew within divine peaks.

For most, obtaining a Miracle Elixir was a daunting task, given the dangers of the divine peaks. The thick mist obscured vision, making it challenging to locate the elixirs, not to mention the lurking threat of Deathborn creatures.

But for Osborne, it was a different story.

He could easily sense where the Miracle Elixirs were, whether they were guarded by Deathborn, and the strength of those Deathborn. He could effortlessly retrieve the Miracle Elixirs.

What happened next confirmed his confidence.

Although there were indeed Deathborn guarding Miracle Elixirs, they were rarely of Deity King caliber.

With Osborne and Anastasia's strength, dealing with a group of Deathborn beneath the Deity King level was a walk in the park.

Before long, they had dispatched all the Deathborn surrounding Miracle Elixirs.

Just like before, Osborne carefully moved the humanoid stone into the warehouse of Hope Haven, a satisfied smile on his face.

"Huh?" Suddenly, Osborne's face twisted in surprise.

"What's going on here? Where's that humanoid boulder I saw earlier? Why isn't it in the warehouse? Who took it?" His brow furrowed as he pondered.

After asking around, he couldn't get a straight answer. The boulder had simply vanished without a trace.

Osborne waved a hand dismissively, thinking, "Ah, forget it! There are plenty more of these humanoid boulders around Divine Peak Nine. Almost every Celestial Sword Peak has one.

Nothing special..."

He paused, scratching his head. "But this one... it feels different from the last. The previous one could suppress my spatial power, while this one seems... well, just a regular old rock."

Osborne couldn't wrap his head around it.

Little did he know, if it weren't for that very boulder, he would have perished during his Deity King Tribulation.

The truth was that unique boulder existed nowhere else in Divine Peak Nine.

Shaking off his confusion, Osborne thought, "Might as well keep searching for Miracle Elixirs."

With Anastasia in tow, he continued wandering through Divine Peak Nine. Armed with his Satan's Eye, he felt invincible, certain he wouldn't fall victim to any ambushes. Not a single threat crossed his path.

Together, they scaled one Celestial Sword Peak after another, collecting Miracle Elixirs with every step.

Before he knew it, three days had passed in the blink of an eye.

"Alright, let's head back and get ready for the tournament!"

Osborne looked around. He'd thoroughly scoured the outer reaches of Divine Peak Nine, racking up over a thousand Miracle Elixirs.

True, there were more to be found deeper within, but Osborne decided not to push his luck.

The Shadow Terror Fiends and root creatures lurking in those regions gave him the creeps.
