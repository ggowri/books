Chapter 1150 One Hundred Top-Tier Class S

Cillian and his siblings were feeling anxious. In Eidolon City, danger could strike at any moment, and they were itching to whisk Osborne away immediately.

But with Osborne's assurance, they had no choice but to wait.

However, their impatience quickly faded when Osborne waved his hand, tearing open a massive rift in space.

In the blink of an eye, he led them, along with over 48 hundred Blood Demon members, through interdimensional travel and straight into Newport City, right to the Augustine Estate's doorstep.

The sheer display of power left Cillian and his companions utterly speechless.

Osborne felt a sense of satisfaction.

His strength had surged, and his mastery over spatial power had reached new heights. If he were traveling solo, he could easily cover over 600 miles in one jump.

Even with the entire contingent of Blood Demons in tow, they could still traverse more than 200 miles in one go.

The travel took a lot of energy, though. But for Osborne, it was a drop in the bucket.

The energy in the planet was so dense now that his recovery rate had skyrocketed.

Osborne knew he would replenish any lost energy in no time.

"Wait here for a sec!" he called out, motioning for someone to take care of Cillian and his siblings while he returned to the training base with the Blood Demon members.

As he surveyed the 4,800 Blood Demons around him, Osborne was weighing choices.

Today, they had slaughtered a staggering 500,000 Ferocious Beasts, with over 50,000 of them being Class A or higher Dark Exotic Beasts.

His Blood Cross was now brimming with blood energy from the dead beasts, and he pondered how best to utilize it.

He could split this blood energy among all Blood Demon members, giving each of them a boost.

Or he could condense it into just a hundred drops and let the Blood Demon Vanguard absorb it, then they would become top-tier Class S warriors!

"Should I share it among everyone or focus on elevating the Vanguard to the peak of Class S?" Osborne couldn’t decide for a while.

In the end, he chose the latter. Boosting the Vanguard would provide a greater overall increase in the Blood Demon's fighting strength.

"Alright, Vanguard stay. The rest of you can head back and rest up," Osborne ordered, and the other members dispersed.

Next, Osborne concentrated the blood energy from the Blood Cross into a hundred drops, ready for the Vanguard to absorb.

As they consumed the energy, their power began to surge dramatically.

Class S warriors came in various strengths. A top-tier Class S could easily take down an ordinary Class S, or even five, six, or ten ordinary Class S warriors.

With the massive influx of blood energy, the hundred members of the Blood Demon Vanguard were rapidly breaking through to top-tier Class S.

Half an hour later, every single one of them had absorbed the energy and reached the limit of Class S!

Now, their next step was to forge their Divine Bones and Fleshes, honing their unique Divine Bodies to master divine power and ultimately transcend into true deities.

"One hundred top-tier Class S warriors!" Osborne thought, a broad smile spreading across his face.

Most of the Blood Demon members didn't have the best talents, certainly not when compared to the prodigies from superpowers.

However, since they had been transformed by Osborne using Blood Cross into Vampires, their progression had no bottlenecks, requiring only ample blood energy.

Consequently, the advancement rate of Blood Demon members far surpassed that of any so-called geniuses.

Now, a hundred top-tier Class S warriors had emerged.

Osborne mused, "Even the League of Divine Descent and the Eityreia Organization likely don't have many top-tier Class S warriors. It's too bad we can't take them out just yet.

First, let me ascend to godhood, quell the Beast Tide, and elevate all Blood Demon members to top-tier Class S, maybe even gods. Only then will we deal with them."

Osborne then prepared to head to Eidolon City.

He was particularly intrigued by the sudden rift spewing Dark Exotic Beasts.

"Let's move!"

Soon, Osborne led a hundred members of the Blood Demon Vanguard to meet with Cillian and his team.

The Vanguard members had toned down their auras to a mere Class AAA level.

After all, a hundred top-tier Class S warriors could wipe out most superpowers; there was no need to reveal their full strength too soon.

Cillian and his group were overjoyed, though they thought Osborne's choice to take only Anastasia, Zoe, and a hundred Class AAA warriors to Eidolon City was too modest.

Still, they dared not voice their concerns.

In a flash, Osborne tore through the space, embarking on an interdimensional journey that spanned over 200 miles, depositing them near the Carey Estate.

Emerging from the dimensional rift, Osborne activated Satan's Eye, focusing on a direction where intense combat raged.

It was a colossal canyon at the edge of the Lostridge Mountains within.
