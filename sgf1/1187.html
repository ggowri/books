Chapter 1186 Time Power

What new abilities would Level Eight Satan's Eye unlock? Osborne had no clue, nor did anyone else in the Augustine family.

No one had ever ascended their Satan's Eye to Level Eight, or even Level Seven for that matter.

The capabilities of Level Seven—Temporal Stasis—had been theorized by the family's ancestors after extensive research.

As for Level Eight, even speculation was beyond reach.

Osborne's curiosity was piqued.

"The Level Seven ability is Temporal Stasis, which freezes time within a 33-foot radius for a single second. While it's not very practical in battle, considering its limited range and duration, enhanced Temporal Stasis could be a game-changer.

When Ophelia pulled that stunt at Gerardo's lair, she used Temporal Stasis, and I was totally helpless!

My time was frozen; my brain couldn't think, and my body couldn't move while she attacked at her leisure.

That power is terrifying!

But she's the God of Time, so her mastery of time power is way beyond anything Satan's Eye could ever hope to achieve. If I could get even a tenth of that ability, it would dramatically boost my power."

As Osborne pondered, he couldn't help but feel a surge of anticipation.

His Satan's Eye, absorbing the core energy from the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity, underwent a dramatic transformation. Just as he had hoped, without any surprises, it broke through to Level Eight.

In that instant, eight luminous stars appeared in his left eye.

Previously, they had been a pale purple, but now they glowed with the same crimson hue as the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity.

The Level Eight mutant Satan's Eye!

A flood of information surged into Osborne's mind, revealing the new ability that accompanied the breakthrough—Temporal Dominator!

No longer just Temporal Stasis, but the ability to manipulate time—accelerate, decelerate, or freeze it—except for reversing it.

This revelation left Osborne stunned.

"Maneuvering time? Isn't that Ophelia's—the God of Time's—power? My Satan's Eye at Level Eight grants me control over time?"

Osborne was ecstatic.

Manipulating time was among the rarest and most potent powers in existence.

Even spatial powers paled in comparison.

To wield such power through his Satan's Eye marked a monumental shift in his abilities and combat.

Osborne sensed the effects his Level Eight Satan's Eye could exert on time.

"First, there's a time freeze.

Before, I could only halt time in a 30-feet radius for a mere second. Now, within a thousand yards, I can freeze time in any chosen area for an entire minute!

Any adversary caught within this radius is trapped in a time-frozen state for a full minute, unable to think or act until I release them. By the time they break free, it's too late for them..."

Osborne was thrilled.

The power of manipulating time was simply awe-inspiring. He could recall his battles with Ophelia, where he couldn't fight back, trapped under her relentless assault.

Had it not been for his robust defenses or Ophelia's reluctance to kill him, he wouldn't have survived.

And now, thanks to his Level Eight Satan's Eye, Osborne could do the very same things!

"Besides freezing time, I can also slow down or speed up time in any area within a thousand yards!

If an enemy is moving too fast, I can slow down time around them, making their relative speed crawl to a snail's pace.

I can slow time by ten times, and it'll last an entire hour!

The same goes for speeding up time—I can accelerate it by tenfold for an entire hour!

If I use it on myself, while others experience just one hour, I'll have lived through ten hours!

Imagine how much faster I can train and grow stronger?"

Osborne was ecstatic. The Level Eight Satan's Eye had granted him mastery over time, and if used wisely, it would undoubtedly skyrocket his power.

What mattered most was that he now wielded time power.

Next time he faced Ophelia, he wouldn't be reduced to a defenseless punching bag, unable to strike back.

"Perfect! With Satan's Eye breaking through to Level Eight, all of its abilities have been massively enhanced, including Memory Control! I should now be able to extract Cain's memories..."

Osborne's smile grew wider as he contemplated the possibilities.
