Chapter 10 Confrontation

Soon, the video call was connected, revealing Justin's handsome face.

Beside him stood a middle-aged man in his forties, with a bruised face and a terrified look.

Osborne frowned when he saw the middle-aged man.

This man was the owner of the store.

Justin looked calm. He smiled and said, "Please repeat what you just told me. What on earth did that good-for-nothing Osborne do in your store?

"Don't you dare lie, or you'll end up dead."

"I'll tell. I'll tell right away!" The middle-aged shop owner was extremely scared.

He started to describe in detail how Osborne had used his tools and scrap items in the store to make the fake explosives with his own hands.

His account was so detailed that it didn't seem fabricated at all.

After he finished, Justin looked at Ophelia with a smile.

"Ophelia, you were so careless. You got tricked by a good-for-nothing with fake explosives!

"If it weren't for me, you would have cost the family 20 million dollars. You've made a huge mistake. I'll report this to the Elder Council, and they'll decide your punishment."

After Justin said that, he looked extremely smug.

He always thought he was the most outstanding descendant of the Caldwell family. He was handsome, graduated from a prestigious university, and was well-liked by the Elder Council.

Justin was once the most likely candidate to become the heir of the Caldwell family. But in the end, he lost the competition to Ophelia.

Today, as he learned that Ophelia's ex-husband had extorted 20 million dollars in divorce compensation from the Caldwell family, he thought his chance to turn things around had come.

"Ophelia, I've found out the truth. Why don't you arrest that good-for-nothing right away?

"Do you really want the family to lose 20 million dollars?

"Are you even fit to be the heir?" Justin scolded harshly.

Ophelia's face darkened, looking extremely grim.

Justin wanted to report her to the Elder Council? And he dared to say she wasn't fit to be the heir?

He was indeed full of evil intentions!

Ophelia said coldly, "Justin, shut up! I know you're trying to kill me through Osborne. Don't think I'm stupid."

Hearing this, Justin was stunned.

Penelope quickly said, "Justin, Osborne said you were deliberately deceiving us! He said it was your plot to use the explosives on him to kill us all.

"Please tell everyone that's not true, right?"

Justin's face turned gloomy. Once he grasped the situation, he couldn't help but look at Osborne in shock.

"Punk, you're quite good at making excuses."

Osborne gave a faint smile. "Whether I'm making excuses or not, you can find out by coming here yourself. Since the explosives on me are fake, why don't you come and arrest me in person?"

Justin frowned, his face getting even grimmer.

But seeing Ophelia staring at him intently, he sneered, "You think I'm scared? Osborne, you know better than anyone that the explosives are fake. So stop putting on an act. Trying to extort money from the Caldwell family? You're a dead man!"

Osborne gave a smile. "Really? Then come here and give it a shot. Let's see if I won't drag you to hell with me!"

Looking at Osborne's smile, Justin suddenly felt a chill run down his spine.

This made him a bit unsure of himself.

He turned to the general-store owner, his face twisted with rage, and started beating the owner mercilessly.

"Tell me, are the explosives real or fake?"

The boss couldn't bear the beating and cried out, "Fake! They're really fake!

The shop owner couldn't bear the beating and cried out, "Fake! They're really fake! There's no gunpowder in there, just sand. I even brought the sand to him myself. Please stop..."

Osborne said with a faint smile, "Stop hitting him. Justin, don't blame me for looking down on you. You're a coward! Even if you beat him to death and confirm what he said is true, you still won't have the guts to face me!

"If you dare to come here, I'll blow you up!"

Justin stopped, fuming with anger.

"Damn it, what did you say? You, a good-for-nothing, dare to call me a coward?

"And you say I'm afraid to face you?"

Osborne ignored him and turned to Ophelia. "Ophelia, do you remember the time three months ago when I suddenly overturned the dining table?"

Ophelia was taken aback and nodded.

Three months ago, she and her stepmother Evelyn were about to have a meal when Osborne went crazy and flipped the table. She and Evelyn were furious and gave Osborne a good scolding.

Of course, she remembered it.

"Osborne, why are you bringing this up all of a sudden? You've done so many stupid things over the years. This one isn't anything special."

Osborne said indifferently, "Oh, you stupid woman. You should thank me! If I hadn't overturned your table, you'd have been poisoned to death by Justin!

"Justin bribed your maid to put poison in your food. Because if you died, there'd be no one to compete with him for the heir position. But I saw through his plan.

"Right, Justin?"

Ophelia frowned tightly, not believing a word.

But Justin's eyes widened in shock. So it was Osborne who had foiled his poisoning plan three months ago!

Justin hated Osborne to the core but didn't dare to show it. He roared, "Osborne, you good-for-nothing. How dare you frame me? Without any proof, do you think Ophelia is a fool? She's my cousin. Why would I want to poison her?

"Ophelia, he's just trying to drive a wedge between us. You're not going to fall for his tricks, are you?"

Ophelia's face was gloomy, and she said nothing.

Osborne smiled calmly and continued, "Whether I'm trying to drive a wedge or not, Ophelia, you can send someone to investigate. You can start with that maid."

After he said that, Justin's face paled.

Osborne added another blow. "And one month ago, Justin sent his personal bodyguards to kill you. But again, I found out about it in advance.

"I fought them in the dark, and you even scolded me for making noise and disturbing your rest."

Ophelia's face hardened again.

She looked at Osborne in disbelief. "What proof do you have for what you're saying? And why didn't you explain it back then?"

Osborne shrugged. "Do you believe me if I say I didn't want you to worry?"

Ophelia was stunned and bit her lip tightly.

"As for the proof, that night I injured their arms. If you check, you'll find out if any of Justin's personal bodyguards had arm injuries a month ago. Then you'll know the truth."

After Osborne finished speaking, Justin's face turned red with anger.

He roared, "Since it was a fight in the dark, how can you be sure they were my bodyguards? They couldn't see you clearly, so how could you recognize them? You're just slandering me!"

Osborne looked at Justin contemptuously and didn't bother to explain.

He just teased Ophelia, "Ophelia, what's your choice now? If you're so sure the explosives are fake, come and end me!"
