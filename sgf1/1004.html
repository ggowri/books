Chapter 1004 Counterattack

Crimson was a whirlwind of fury, unleashing her rage and bloodlust with each strike.

Her divine artifact dagger sliced through Osborne's defenses over and over, leaving a painful array of wounds across his body.

It filled her with twisted delight; each cut felt like retribution for her daughter's death, each thrust of the blade granting her a small taste of the vengeance she craved.

Though she found herself marveling at Osborne's resilient defenses—defenses that felt out of place for a mere Class AAA, even rivaling many Class S warriors—Crimson felt no fear.

On the contrary, the very act of slowly inflicting wounds upon him brought her a twisted satisfaction.

With each cut and gash, she felt her pleasure deepen, her mind swimming in visions of stripping Osborne's flesh away, layer by layer, until he was a withered skeleton.

Osborne didn’t mount a solid defense.

His injured left leg hindered his movements.

To Crimson, she thought everything was in her control and Osborne was nothing more than a toy for her to ravage at will.

Suddenly, Osborne's hand shot out, seizing her arm with an unexpected burst of strength.

The grip was strong enough to prevent her from pulling away, but Crimson remained unfazed.

She said with a sardonic smile,

"Look at you, hanging in there. You really think grabbing my arm changes anything? Even if you hold on for dear life, I can still carve you up, piece by piece!"

With a swift motion, she produced yet another divine artifact dagger, plunging it viciously toward Osborne's chest.

The blade met its mark, embedding deeply and providing multiple fresh gashes that oozed blood, painting Osborne red as if he were nothing more than a canvas for her rage.

Yet, he seemed unfazed, almost indifferent as if the daggers didn't even pierce his skin.

"Vampire Transformation! Breath of Celestial!"

Osborne tightened his grip on Crimson's arm, summoning the full force of his power as he channeled the strength from his right leg's Divine Bone. With a determined kick, he struck her hard.

Crimson's arm was seized by Osborne, leaving her with no escape.

Osborne struck her with a kick. This time, she was ready, bracing herself in anticipation. Still, the force sent her reeling, a spray of blood erupting from her mouth, though the wound was relatively minor.

But twice now, Osborne had drawn her blood—a provocation that ignited a fierce rage within her.

"I'll kill you for that!" Crimson screamed. Her voice was laced with madness as she lashed out with renewed vigor.

Her strikes grew even stronger, slicing into Osborne's flesh, leaving gaping wounds that exposed bone beneath.

But Osborne didn't flinch; he kept retaliating, his kicks finding their mark against her body.

As time ticked away, the Divine Bone energy drained, but Osborne wasn't finished. He unleashed Explosive Force time and time again, trying to give Crimson's internal injuries. They finally made Crimson bleed again.

Her pain was stacking up, but compared to Osborne, who now resembled a ragged cloth saturated in blood, it was merely a scratch.

His vision blurred, consciousness teetering on the edge of darkness as he felt his mind slipping away.

Crimson noticed his deteriorating state, a triumphant smirk dancing on her face.

"You think you can trade blows with me? How cute! I'm just going to get a bruise while you are on a fast track to the grave!"

But suddenly a flicker of a smile broke through his pain, a cold smile that held an edge of defiance.

"Is that so? Light of Redemption!"

Osborne unleashed the power of Light of Redemption upon himself when he realized that he was about to faint.

A radiant light descended from the sky, enveloping Osborne in its embrace. In an instant, every wound, every ounce of energy he had expended, began to heal.

Just a heartbeat ago, Osborne had been teetering on the brink of unconsciousness, but now, he surged back to his peak, revitalized and powerful.

Martial art aura erupted from him once again. With no trace of his previous injuries, he locked eyes with Crimson, his cold gaze brimming with lethal intent.

"I think it's going to be you who ends up dead!" Osborne declared.

Before Crimson could even process the shift in the battle, Osborne launched himself forward, and the divine power in his right leg surged back to life. He unleashed a devastating kick aimed directly at her chest.

"No!" Crimson gasped, her face draining of color as she saw his recovery.

She tried to draw back, but Osborne's grip on her arm was stopped her.

Osborne's right foot struck her chest like a hammer hitting an anvil.

The impact was catastrophic; the wounded Crimson had no chance of withstanding it. Her body folded inward as the sound of cracking bones filled the air.

But Osborne wasn't done. He knew that Crimson was a top-tier Class S warrior notorious for her tenacity.

With that understanding fueling him, he unleashed a torrent of speed and brutality unmatched in previous exchanges.

Each strike was merciless, Osborne pouring all his strength into every blow as he hammered down on Crimson. Blood and flesh splattered because of the blows.
