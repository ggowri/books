Chapter 891 Plant Realm

Scarlett smirked, her confidence radiating as she declared, "For me, it's easy to take you down in the wild, and I can do the same here in the arena. You don't stand a chance."

Her pride wasn't just arrogance; it stemmed from her undeniable strength and a track record that spoke for itself.

In her eyes, there was only one person among the younger generation who truly warranted her respect—Marlon.

Not even Cuthbert made the cut.

She believed her full power could easily place her in the top twenty of the Godson Rankings, and if the opportunity arose, she could even take out a Class S Supreme Overlord.

With that kind of confidence, how could she possibly take Osborne seriously?

Osborne simply smiled, unfazed, "Is that so? I've had plenty of challengers tell me the same thing before, but they only managed to block a couple of my moves. I wonder how many you can manage?"

Scarlett scoffed, her disdain evident. "Comparing me to those losers? You'll regret it!"

Shrugging, Osborne replied coolly, "Alright then, let's not waste any more time. If you really have what it takes, come at me."

Scarlett let out a cold huff. "As you wish! Prepare to witness my true power!"

With that, she sprang into action, a cruel smile on her face as she waved her hand.

The arena trembled violently, and towering trees erupted from thin air. Massive vines twisted and curled, transforming the once flat stage into a vibrant, primal jungle in an instant.

This was Scarlett's Realm!

The enormous vines intertwined, forming a throne-like structure that lifted Scarlett high above the ground, making her look like a queen of the forest.

Osborne stared in disbelief.

"A plant-controlling metahuman? A Plant Realm? And the strength of this Realm... Level Three?"

This was the first time he had encountered a plant-controlling metahuman.

Scarlett's Realm had turned the entire arena into a lush wilderness, with every tree and vine seemingly alive, under her command, ready to be wielded as weapons.

Most impressively, her Plant Realm was incredibly stable, the energies woven together like fabric. Combined with her top-tier AAA Class strength, even Osborne would find it difficult to shatter her Realm easily.

Seated on her vine throne, she looked down at Osborne, a smirk on her face.

"So, Osborne, what do you think of my Realm? Do you still believe you can defeat me and achieve your hundred wins?"

Osborne maintained his composure with a slight smile playing. "Nice Realm! But if you think this is enough to take me down, you're underestimating me."

"Let's put that to the test," Scarlett replied with a cold laugh, her hand slicing through the air.

Instantly, every tree and vine around Osborne sprang to life. The vines lashed out like whips, striking at him with the force of a raging storm, accompanied by a howling wind that carried a terrifying power.

The trees swayed gently, their leaves transforming into countless blades that surrounded Osborne.

In a heartbeat, the assault came crashing down.

Osborne's expression turned fierce as he activated his martial art aura, enveloping himself in a protective shield and darting away.

In an instant, all the attacks missed their mark.

"Your Spatial Domain is indeed tricky. I'll just have to shatter it!" Scarlett sneered.

Raising her hand, she commanded all the vines to strike, battering Osborne's Spatial Domain with a thunderous roar.

Though Osborne's Spatial Domain was incredibly close to Level Three, it still fell short. It could handle ordinary experts, but against Scarlett, it wasn't as solid as he had hoped.

The vines danced menacingly, and with just two synchronized strikes, they shattered Osborne's Spatial Domain into fragments.

Scarlett wore a smug smile. "Without your Spatial Domain, how do you plan to escape now? This is my world; I'm the deity here, and you're nothing more than my plaything!"

With that, the vines surged and extended wildly, lashing out at Osborne with terrifying force.

Osborne scoffed, no longer attempting to dodge. Without the spatial power, using interdimensional travel would drain his mental energy far too quickly.

Faced with the onslaught of countless vines barreling towards him, he made a bold decision—he would confront them head-on.

Osborne swung his fist forward.

His punch collided with the rushing vines, shattering them in an instant.

But as he did so, a tremendous force rushed back into him.

Staggering back two steps, Osborne was surprised. For the first time at the Honor Quest Ceremony, he found himself at a disadvantage, actually being pushed back by sheer strength.

Yet, the broken vines didn't stay defeated for long. They quickly reformed, reshaping themselves into even more numerous and densely packed tendrils that lunged at him again, stronger than before.

With determination, Osborne punched once more, but the impact threw him back another two steps.

After seeing Osborne’s setback, Scarlett's expression twisted with contempt and amusement.
