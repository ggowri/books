Chapter 1117 Mutation

Osborne knew he had to be resolute to firmly draw the line between himself and Ophelia once and for all.

He wrestled internally with the thought, "I can't possibly forgive Ophelia!

No simple apology can mend the wounds she has inflicted on me! Signing that bloody divorce agreement was my way of closing that chapter. There's no going back!"

"Why?!"

Ophelia's voice screeched through the air, but Osborne remained fixated on the five bodies before him, hardly sparing her a glance.

His silence ignited a spark of fury in her—the rage turned into madness, then into hatred.

She mused, "Why is Osborne being so cold toward me?

I've apologized, I've humbled myself, and I've been begging for forgiveness, yet he still refuses to accept it!

I can't accept it!"

The enormity of that thought was too much for her to bear.

Ophelia trembled violently, an overwhelming force bubbling up within her as she murmured to herself, as if seeking the answer deep inside.

"Why are you doing this to me?

"I've apologized! I know I was wrong!

"Why won't you forgive me? Why are you still so distant?

"Why!"

At that moment, her voice rose into a furious roar, and the air around Ophelia crackled with an intensity that shook the very room.

Even the five divine bodies trembled.

Powerful energy surged from Ophelia, a force that far exceeded what a Class AAA could harness.

Osborne turned to her, his expression one of shock.

"What on earth is happening? Is she breaking through to Class S?"

The commotion she was causing was unprecedented, completely unlike any typical breakthrough he'd ever witnessed.

And her emotional state was way out of control!

If this continued, her breakthrough could very well end in disaster!

"Ophelia, you need to calm down! You're on the verge of breaking into Class S, but you must steady yourself, or the process will fail!" he implored urgently.

"Stay out of it!" Ophelia screamed, her red eyes locked onto him.

The air around her crackled with energy, her clothes whipping wildly, her hair swirling as she floated, looking down at him with pure fury and hatred.

"This is none of your business!"

Right now, Ophelia was oblivious to the chaotic power rampaging through her; she was merely venting the torrent of rage and bitterness inside her.

"Why should I abase myself begging for his forgiveness only to be met with this?

Why, despite all my achievements, won't he even look at me? I can't accept this!"

She felt a chasm opening up within her, a void pulsing with boundless energy that sought to engulf her, pulling her deeper into its depths.

But she didn't fight it. Ophelia embraced that power, welcoming the plunge into the abyss!

"Ophelia, please, calm down!" Osborne shouted again, witnessing how dangerously far gone she had become.

Suddenly, Ophelia laughed, a chilling sound that sent shivers down his spine.

"Osborne, I swear! One day, you'll regret this!

"One day, you'll come to see what you lost by refusing my apologies! Just you wait!"

Osborne's brow furrowed tightly.

"Ophelia, this isn't the right mindset for a breakthrough. You really need to take a step back!"

With that, he readied himself for action.

He intended to knock Ophelia out—just temporarily—to let her regain her calm before moving forward with the breakthrough.

Just as Osborne approached Ophelia, a terrifying force erupted from her, blasting him backward.

Its sheer power was even stronger than Gerardo's full might.

Osborne's face paled, blood trickling from the corner of his mouth as he stared in shock at Ophelia.

A sense of abnormality about her breakthrough resurfaced in his mind.

Ophelia simply smiled, her eyes locking onto Osborne for one last profound gaze before she closed them, two crimson tears rolling down her cheeks.

In the next instant, her energy exploded wildly, causing the entire lair of the Aberrants to shake violently.

The force rippling from her was so intense that Osborne could only stagger back, his face awash with shock.

Even more astonishing was the sight of the five divine corpses, which disintegrated into beams of light, swiftly merging into Ophelia's body.

A formidable, sacred power then emanated from her, leaving Osborne utterly speechless.

"This... is..." Osborne stammered, his heart racing as he sensed the divine energy coursing through her.

"Divine power?!"

The force surging within Ophelia was unmistakably divine—pure, raw divine power.

Osborne was dumbfounded.

Then, abruptly, Osborne felt a violent throbbing in his left eye.

His Satan's Eye seemed on the brink of a breakthrough.
