Chapter 270 The Truth

"How can you prove it?"

Wesley asked coldly, "You chose a place without surveillance, after all. You are not stupid enough to attempt something like this where cameras could catch you."

Wesley looked at Osborne with disdain, clearly not believing he could prove his own innocence.

But Osborne simply smiled. "It is simple. I will have them tell the truth themselves."

Osborne pointed at Blake and Ava.

Wesley's scorn deepened. "Have them tell the truth? They have already said you tried to molest and rape her, and Blake caught you in the act, which is why you wanted to kill them."

Osborne shook his head. "What they said before might not be true. But I have a way to make sure what they say next is the truth."

Wesley sneered, "Why would what they said before be a lie, and what they say next be the truth? Are you planning to threaten them?"

"Of course not."

Osborne suddenly pulled out a bottle.

This was a bottle of truth serum.

Osborne had bought it for five million dollars a long time ago to deal with Davy of the Maelstrom and Raymond of the Aberrants.

There were still three doses left.

He displayed the bottle to the crowd. "For those who know, this is a product of the Draco Group—truth serum.

"Once taken, it induces a trance-like state where the person will truthfully answer any question asked without lying."

After Osborne finished speaking, Wesley turned uneasy.

The crowd gasped in surprise, and many of them looked at Osborne in disbelief.

"It is really truth serum!"

"I have heard of it. It is a product of the Draco Group's lab, rarely available on the market. Even professionally trained agents cannot resist its effects and will reveal all their secrets."

"If this is indeed the Draco Group's truth serum, it can definitely get the truth out of them."

Wesley looked even more distressed.

He angrily retorted, "How do we know it is truth serum and not poison? You might be trying to silence them! Once they are dead, no one can testify against you!

"They are my guests. I would not allow you to use drugs on them!"

With that, Wesley signaled his bodyguards, but Osborne laughed coldly. "Wesley, do you think I am asking for your permission? Who do you think you are?

"I am proving my innocence. If you have the guts, try to stop me."

Osborne approached Blake and Ava, who were pale with fear. They knew they could not afford to take the truth serum, as it would expose all their secrets and schemes.

They could only look at Wesley.

"Mr. Ashford, save me!"

"Mr. Ashford!"

Wesley's face was livid with anger as he gritted his teeth and said, "Osborne, you are too arrogant! Today is my wedding, this is my turf, and I would not allow you to act out!

"Stop him!"

"Let's see who dares!"

At that moment, Clarissa coldly shouted, "They slandered Osborne, and now he wants to prove his innocence. I dare anyone to stop him!

"Anyone who dares to stop him will be making an enemy of the Radcliffe family!"

After Clarissa finished speaking, several bodyguards from the Radcliffe family appeared, staring coldly at the Ashford family's bodyguards.

Instantly, the Ashford family's bodyguards felt a chill run down their spines and did not dare to move.

Wesley clenched his fists, feeling extremely frustrated.

Osborne ignored him and walked up to Blake, who was screaming in pain. Grabbing his jaw, Osborne forced the truth serum into his mouth.

He then did the same to Ava, making her swallow the truth serum as well.

Both of them were ordinary people and could not resist the powerful effects of the truth serum. Soon, their eyes became vacant and unfocused, like walking corpses.

Osborne smiled faintly at the crowd.

"Now, I will begin asking questions."

No one spoke.

Osborne turned to Ava. "Ava, tell everyone, did I try to molest you? Did I attempt to rape you?"

Ava looked dazed, but immediately replied, "No! Blake told me to seduce you. He promised to pay me a lot if I succeeded. I waited here to try and get you to sleep with me and take a video.

"But you called me ugly and pushed me away!"

At this, the crowd burst into laughter.

In fact, Ava was far from ugly. If she were, Blake would not have sent her to seduce Osborne. She was also quite attractive, with a voluptuous figure that many men found very enticing.

However, whether or not Ava was considered beautiful depended on who she was compared to.

If she was compared to Clarissa, Ava fell far short.

Since Osborne's girlfriend was Clarissa, it made sense that he would reject Ava.

Ava continued, "When I failed to seduce you, Blake changed the plan. He told me to tear my clothes and pretend you molested me, then scream rape to frame you!

"I did everything under Blake's orders."
