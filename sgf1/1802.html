Chapter 1801 An Unexpected Turn of Events

Caesar was dead!

As one of the Five Brilliant Scions of the Paramount Supreme Clan, a prodigy ranked among the top five in the younger generation, Caesar had met his end at the hands of Bryce, who had decapitated him on the arena floor.

But at that very moment, a terrifying force erupted from Caesar's lifeless body.

A shadowy figure materialized before Bryce in the blink of an eye.

"Watch out!"

Osborne shouted, his face paling as he quickly unleashed his spatial power, trying to pull Bryce to safety.

But it was too late.

The instant the figure appeared, it struck Bryce squarely in the chest with a swift, powerful blow.

The speed was so astonishing that Bryce, who had just savored the thrill of killing Caesar, didn't have a chance to react.

The impact echoed through the arena. Gasping, Bryce felt the force tear through his divine power defenses—the Supreme Malevolence Shield that had automatically activated and his Level Eight mid-stage Divine Body were no match for this overwhelming attack.

In an instant, all his defenses crumbled, leaving a gaping hole in his chest. His internal organs were obliterated, and even his Divine Body began to disintegrate rapidly.

Bryce was stunned, utterly speechless.

Anastasia, Kendra, and the others stood frozen, their expressions mirroring shock and disbelief. None of them had anticipated such a sudden turn of events. As they watched Bryce's body collapse under the onslaught of the shadowy figure, their hearts raced, feeling as if they were being squeezed in a vice. They opened their mouths to scream, but no sound escaped.

Fortunately, Osborne was still in motion.

A ripple of spatial energy surged beside Bryce, and in the next instant, his shattered body was safely at Osborne's side, who caught him just in time.

Osborne's face darkened as he assessed Bryce's condition. "Hang in there!"

Without a moment's hesitation, he tapped into the power of the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity, rushing to heal Bryce's wounds.

"Hannah! We need life energy! Hurry!" he shouted, urgency lacing his voice.

Hannah snapped back to reality, her face pale with shock. Upon hearing Osborne's command, she immediately summoned her life power, a torrent of vitality flooding into Bryce's body.

Osborne and Hannah poured their energies into Bryce, but even with their combined efforts, his condition remained dire.

His body continued to disintegrate, albeit at a slower pace.

Hannah exclaimed, her complexion growing even more ashen, "It's not working!

"What kind of power is this? It's weakening our healing!"

She cried, panic creeping into her voice.

Osborne's expression hardened, gritting his teeth. "This is Lord Malevolence's dark energy! I can't believe he left behind a fragment of it inside Caesar! Its potency is so high that it's dampening our healing effects! Keep the life energy flowing!"

With determination, Osborne fought to harness the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity's power to mend Bryce's injuries while simultaneously channeling dark power, attempting to purge Lord Malevolence's dark energy.

If he could expel this malevolent force, the healing properties of the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity would surely restore Bryce quickly.

But he was struggling.

At that moment, Osborne felt the crushing weight of the power disparity between himself and Lord Malevolence. Just a remnant of dark energy left behind by Lord Malevolence was enough to thwart him.

He realized with horror that his peak Level Eight dark elemental essence was utterly ineffective against this dark energy.

Despair washed over him as he grappled with the reality of their situation.

Bryce's injuries hadn't healed at all; they were still in a state of collapse and decay. If this kept up, his body would soon completely shut down, and he'd be dead.

At that moment, Kendra rushed over, her face a mix of tension and worry as she watched Bryce's body break down before her eyes, tears streaming down her cheeks.

"Kendra! Kendra, hang in there!

"Kendra, you'll be okay!

"Hold on, Osborne will save you! Kendra, you have to hold on!"

Kendra's cries echoed, but Bryce was already unconscious, and even in his sleep, the agony of his deteriorating body caused his brow to furrow in pain.

Osborne gritted his teeth and snapped, "This can't go on!

"Go get the White Miracle Elixir! All of it! Every single vial!"

Osborne's voice carried to Anastasia, who immediately nodded and dashed into Hope Haven.

She made a beeline for the central warehouse and emerged with every vial of White Miracle Elixir she could find.

Sixty-five in total!

In the next second, she was beside Osborne, handing them over in a rush.

Osborne swiftly crushed a white Miracle Elixir in his hand, the liquid shimmering with an otherworldly glow. Without hesitation, Osborne tilted Bryce's head back and poured the elixir into his mouth.

Instantly, the painful processes of collapse and decay started to slow down.

"It's working! Keep using the White Miracle Elixir on him!"
