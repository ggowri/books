Chapter 2240 Glorious Divine Kingdom Duel-1

Osborne stashed Angela's Peak Lord Token away and cast a wry smile around at the empty surroundings.

At last, he'd gotten his identity token from the Mystic Sirius Sect and officially become an inner disciple at Peak Seven.

As for Isa, Clarissa, and the others—where they'd ended up or what had befallen them—nobody knew, and truth be told, apart from Osborne and Angela, no one seemed to give a hoot.

Osborne made himself at home in his new abode halfway up Peak Seven. It was a snug and peaceful dwelling. The view from his courtyard was simply stunning, with a sea of clouds billowing below.

A protective barrier encircled the yard, and only his identity token could grant entry, guaranteeing he wouldn't be bothered while he trained.

He sat cross-legged in the courtyard, gazing out at the clouds, deep in thought about his next move.

"I gotta figure out a way to get to Nebula City, the capital of the Glorious Divine Kingdom. I should pay my respects to my mom from my past life. But she's buried in the Royal Family's Royal Sepulcher. With my current standing, there's no way I can get in. And trying to sneak in? That'd be a death wish!"

Gaining entry to the Royal Family's Royal Sepulcher is no walk in the park!

Osborne frowned as he pondered the matter.
For outsiders, there are only two ways to get in. The first is to become a Royal Family guard in the Glorious Divine Kingdom. You'd have to safeguard a member of the Royal Family when they go into the sepulcher.

But becoming a Royal Family guard is a real uphill battle. You've gotta be incredibly powerful and pass a strict background check.

"Even if I started prepping right this second, it could take years before I'd stand a chance."

Scrap that option!

The second way to get into the Royal Family's Royal Sepulcher is a bit more straightforward.

"The Glorious Divine Kingdom holds large-scale sacrificial ceremonies in the sepulcher during major festivals, and that's my golden opportunity! If I could score an invitation, I'd be in."

However, to get an invitation, you'd need to be at least the head of a Second-rate Power, like the sect master of the Mystic Sirius Sect...

Right now, that's just a pipe dream for me!

"Looks like my only shot is to get in as a performer."

Every ten years, the Glorious Divine Kingdom holds a national competition—the Glorious Divine Kingdom Duel! And the venue is right in the Royal Sepulcher...

"If I can take part in the Glorious Divine Kingdom Duel, I can get into the Royal Sepulcher and find an opportunity to pay my respects to my mom from my past life!"

With that in mind, Osborne felt a rush of determination.

He summoned Maddox to ask about the Glorious Divine Kingdom Duel.

"The Glorious Divine Kingdom Duel?"

Maddox paused for a moment, then responded, "Oh, it's gonna start pretty soon! The cities are already gearing up! The finals will be held in a year!"

Osborne said, "A year from now?! Give me the lowdown!"

Maddox nodded, itching to explain, "The Glorious Divine Kingdom is split into ten zones. Each zone's got a hundred states, and every state's home to a hundred cities! Only ten teams can enter the Glorious Divine Kingdom Duel!

"In other words, each zone can only send one team to Nebula City for the showdown. So, if you wanna take part, you gotta first rise above the rest in your sect, then in your city, after that in your state, and finally, dominate your zone to be the top dog.
