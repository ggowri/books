Chapter 1629 A Hundred Times

The Infinite Devouring Eyes had manifested during the breakthrough of Osborne's Satan's Eye to Level Nine. It appeared as a colossal eye—a mere illusion without any physical form.

This existence was both unique and enigmatic, even to Osborne himself.

With just a thought, he summoned the Infinite Devouring Eyes from the No. 9 mining area, and in an instant, it vanished from there, reappearing right beside him.

"It looks like it's grown bigger!" Osborne exclaimed, his eyes widening.

When the Infinite Devouring Eyes first appeared, it was roughly 3 feet in diameter, but now, to Osborne's amazement, it had expanded to at least three times that size—now surpassing 9 feet.

As soon as it manifested, the Infinite Devouring Eyes began to voraciously consume the dense energy surrounding it. The speed of absorption was astounding, leaving Osborne utterly astonished.

A small vortex started to form around the Infinite Devouring Eyes; no energy could escape its grasp, quickly being transformed into pure divine power to elevate Osborne's cultivation level.

At that moment, Osborne felt a rapid surge in his realm of power—a pace at least a hundred times faster than before!

"Whoa, this is impressive!" he marveled.

Although he had anticipated such a rate of improvement, feeling it firsthand left him almost speechless with wonder.

Osborne was already close to the mid-stage of Transcendent Deity, and in just a few minutes, he sensed his realm effortlessly shifting to the mid-stage of Transcendent Deity.

What was more, he could feel himself rapidly pushing toward the late stage.

This rate of enhancement was exponentially faster than what those prodigies experienced while cultivating with Ultimate Essence Crystals—perhaps even more!

Feeling the thrill of reaching a new level of power flooded him with excitement.

Osborne thought, "Here's the place. If I set the Infinite Devouring Eyes here, with this pace of improvement, I might reach the late stage of Transcendent Deity in just two days!

In five days, I could hit the peak, and within ten days, I should be able to break through to Celestial Deity!"

His spirits lifted at this prospect.

Resolutely, Osborne began to establish the Infinite Devouring Eyes.

As before, he placed them within the parallel space. This way, even if someone were to approach this area, they wouldn't detect the Infinite Devouring Eyes.

The most they'd notice is the bizarre disappearance of vast amounts of energy.

"Let's hope this all goes smoothly!" he murmured, preparing everything carefully.

After a moment of hesitation, Osborne returned to the surface from the depths of the earth.

He lifted his gaze to the sky.

Thick, swirling mist enveloped his surroundings, and even with his Satan's Eye active, he could only make out the massive form of Divine Peak Nine floating overhead.

Curious, he turned his gaze eastward, eager to see what secrets lay beyond.

But to his dismay, visibility dwindled to nothing!

The impenetrable mist obscured all sight, rendering anything beyond a few hundred miles invisible. He still had at least several hundred thousand miles to the central area—a daunting distance shrouded in mystery.

"Forget it! There's definitely a big secret inside, but it's not something I can explore right now. Whatever lurks within has even made the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity feel anxious, so it must be a terrifying presence on par with that massive root creature!

Perhaps the sudden appearance of the Celestara and these divine peaks is tied to that mystery! Even Lord Malevolence couldn't breach a single divine peak and ended up getting seriously injured—likely because of that enigmatic power!

With my current strength, recklessly approaching to investigate would be nothing short of suicide! If I want to explore, I'll need to wait until I reach the Deity King level.

For now, I should focus on my cultivation!

The Infinite Devouring Eyes will take care of my realm improvement, but I need to cultivate my elemental essence myself to truly grow!

Here, my comprehension will improve dozens of times over. I'll use these few days to see if I can push my space elemental essence to Level Seven!"

After settling on this plan, Osborne descended once more into the depths of the earth.

He sat cross-legged, but he hesitated to begin his cultivation right away. The thought of falling into an illusion worried him; after all, a boost in comprehension also meant a heightened danger.

Although his realm was low, his soul power was formidable enough to theoretically withstand the creation of illusions; he couldn't shake the fear of the unexpected.

So, Osborne reached out to Riley, asking him to wake him every ten minutes just to be safe. Once that was arranged, he finally began his cultivation.

As he entered the meditative state, Osborne was immediately struck by the exhilarating rush of heightened comprehension.

It felt like his mind had been cleared, as if ice had enveloped it, sharpening his clarity and allowing him to think through problems with lightning speed—he could even draw connections he hadn't seen before.

In this state, Osborne's understanding of spatial power deepened significantly.

He had been stuck at a bottleneck with his space elemental essence for quite some time, but now, with his newfound clarity, he felt it beginning to break through.

Osborne could distinctly sense his space elemental essence pushing past the barriers, advancing toward a whole new level.

Closer and closer.

The breakthrough was within his grasp.
