Chapter 2379 Strange Teeth-2

Osborne described his feelings in detail, which immediately piqued Primordial's interest. "Even your Satan's Eye can't see through it? And there's a special energy that's unperceivable? But staring at it for too long causes your Satan's Eye to feel pain? This is indeed quite interesting!"

Primordial became interested, started observing more carefully, and silently pondered.

Osborne did not disturb him.

Even if Primordial couldn't identify the origin of that tooth, Osborne still planned to buy it for future research.

After all, anything that even Satan's Eye can't see through is definitely a treasure.

Just by its shape, it looked like a massive dagger. Osborne thought it could be directly hung on the Warfare Ancient Tree to let the tree nurture it for a while.

Perhaps this way, he might truly obtain a decent Sacred Artifact.

"Have you taken a liking to anything?" the shop owner asked with a smile.

Osborne was ready to act.

He casually picked up a fake Empyrean Sacredness bone and calmly asked, "How much for this Empyrean Sacredness bone? I plan to take it back and have the elders of my sect refine it. It should reach the quasi-Sacred Artifact level."

"Quasi-Sacred Artifact?" The owner sneered, "My Empyrean Sacredness bone, combined with some other materials and casually refined, would at least become a Sacred Artifact! And you want to use it to make a quasi-Sacred Artifact? Isn't that a waste?"

Osborne gave a faint smile. "I lack the strength to handle a true Sacred Artifact. Besides, I don't have much money. If this Empyrean Sacredness bone is too expensive, then forget it."

After saying that, he got up and prepared to leave.

"Hey! Don't rush. I haven't given you a price yet!" The owner's eyes darted around.

The Empyrean Sacredness bone Osborne had his eye on was actually a fake, costing only a dozen Sacred Crystals. Although the owner wanted to quote a high price, he was afraid of scaring Osborne away.

Finally, he said, "We’re destined to meet, so I’ll sell it to you at a discount! This Empyrean Sacredness bone is top-notch! Thirty thousand Sacred Crystals, take it!"

Osborne frowned after hearing that. "Thirty thousand Sacred Crystals? Impossible! It's just an Empyrean Sacredness bone. I don't want it!"

After saying that, he pretended to leave again.

The owner quickly said, "Hey! What price would you accept? Name your price. If I can agree, I'll sell it to you. If not, then you can leave."

Osborne immediately put on a pained expression. "Five hundred Sacred Crystals!"

He knew in his heart that this fake Empyrean Sacredness bone was worth no more than ten Sacred Crystals. The reason he offered such a high price was to keep the owner from noticing his true intention.

"Five hundred Sacred Crystals? Impossible!" the owner shouted. "At least fifteen thousand Sacred Crystals!"

"No way, I'll offer at most six hundred Sacred Crystals!"

"No, twelve thousand Sacred Crystals."

After some haggling, they finally settled on twelve hundred Sacred Crystals. On the surface, both Osborne and the owner looked pained, as if they had suffered a loss.

But in reality, both were secretly overjoyed.

Osborne took out twelve hundred Sacred Crystals, just about to hand them to the owner, but suddenly pulled his hand back and said, "No! I feel like I'm losing out!"

"Throw in that pile of useless bones too! This is over a thousand Sacred Crystals, just for one Empyrean Sacredness bone. I can't explain this when I go back!"

"Give me this pile of useless bones, and I'll feel better! Otherwise, I won't buy it..."

The owner frowned immediately upon hearing this.

In the end, he still nodded. "Alright! I really can't do anything about you. I'll throw in this pile of useless bones for you!"

"Give me the Sacred Crystals!"

Osborne immediately said, "Deal!"
