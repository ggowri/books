Chapter 996 A Bloody Clash

Greyson, Roy, and the rest of the Lambert family stared at the edge of the dense, shadowy forest, their expressions a mix of confusion and uncertainty. The enemy was invisible, lurking just beyond their sight.

It wasn't until Osborne unleashed his fury that the reality of their situation became clear. In an instant, he took down several Eityreia Organization assassins, their bodies collapsing heavily to the ground as they broke free from their Stealth Art.

Greyson and Roy finally caught sight of their adversaries, and shock washed over their faces.

Osborne wielded his super energy pistol like a sniper rifle, picking off assassins hundreds of yards away with deadly precision. They were momentarily frozen, awestruck by the display of power.

It suddenly clicked for them how Osborne had managed to eliminate the fifty-odd Eityreia assassins that had previously surrounded the Lambert family so swiftly.

Roy murmured, shaking his head in disbelief, "Impressive strength!

"These young folks are terrifying!"

He glanced over at the younger generation of the Lambert family, a wry smile creeping onto his face.

It wasn't the youth that was frightening; it was Osborne.

At least the younger Lambert family fighters, even Rhett, were utterly useless in this situation.

Osborne continued to pull the trigger, systematically taking down one stealthy assassin after another.

In no time, he had eliminated ten Class AAA assassins.

But the remaining Eityreia Organization operatives finally caught on.

"Damn it! Our Stealth Art has been compromised!"

"Charge! Wipe out the Lambert family! If we fail, Mr. Kane and Mrs. Kane will have our heads!"

In an instant, nearly a hundred Eityreia Organization assassins surged forward, abandoning all caution.

They raced toward the Lambert family with their speed incredible; in just a few breaths, they closed the gap of hundreds of yards.

Osborne fired again, managing to take down two more assassins, but the rest were already within striking distance, crashing through the water walls and sending ripples across the surface.

"Fight!" Osborne roared, leading the charge as the Lambert family sprang into action, unleashing their attacks against the oncoming wave of assassins.

The night erupted into chaos as various attacks lit up the darkness, signaling the beginning of a bloody clash between the Eityreia Organization and the Lambert family.

Nearly a thousand Lambert family members formed a makeshift battle line, their backs against the rocky mountain, strong fighters at the front and the vulnerable behind them, ready to defend.

Osborne, Greyson, and Roy each took a flank, determined to minimize casualties among their kin. They opted for an aggressive strategy, aiming to push the assassins back before they could reach the heart of the Lambert family forces.

Osborne landed a powerful punch, obliterating the fastest Eityreia assassin who dared to charge at him.

But as one fell, more swarmed around him, eager to breach the line and attack the Lambert family.

With a snort of contempt, Osborne declared, "Is this all you've got? Just Class AAA assassins? You'll need a lot more than that to take us down! You're all going to die here!

"Thunder Realm!"

Osborne activated the Thunder Realm in an instant, enveloping all the approaching Eityreia Organization assassins within a radius of hundreds of yards.

At least forty of them found themselves trapped inside the swirling maelstrom of energy.

These weren't just any killers; they were Class AAA, and Osborne knew his Thunder Realm wouldn't hold up for long.

Without wasting a moment, he unleashed a devastating assault.

"Thunder of Judgment!"

The skies roared as countless bolts of lightning came crashing down, turning the Thunder Realm into a scene straight out of an apocalypse. In his hands, Osborne wielded the Volt Hammer, channeling even more powerful bursts of electricity to strike at the panicking assassins within the Realm.

Screams echoed through the chaos.

The smell of burnt flesh filled the air, and with just one strike, Osborne had taken down over twenty Class AAA Eityreia Organization killers.

However, he could feel his Thunder Realm trembling violently, on the brink of collapse.

"One more time!"

He pressed on, calling down another barrage of Thunder of Judgment.

At that moment, he truly felt the advantages of his soul of powers merging with his physical form—his speed in launching attacks was astonishing.

Most would have struggled to unleash a second wave in this situation, but Osborne wasn't just anyone.

As the Thunder Realm began to crack and shatter, terrifying bolts of lightning sailed down once more, finishing off half of the remaining assassins.

The few who were left were heavily wounded and reeling from the onslaught.

Without hesitation, Osborne drew his super energy pistol, methodically taking them down one by one, landing shot after shot with precision.

By now, he had racked up more than fifty kills.

Breathing a sigh of relief, Osborne quickly scanned his surroundings.

But then, his expression hardened as his eyes widened in alarm.

"Watch out!

"Mr. Lambert, there's a Class S Eityreia Organization assassin 30 feet to your left!

"Watch out!"

Osborne's voice boomed, urgency lacing every syllable. Greyson's face paled immediately. He had been just about to finish off a Class AAA killer, but without a moment's hesitation, he rolled to his right.

In the blink of an eye, a gash appeared on his back.

If he hadn't moved, that blade would have found its mark right on his neck.
