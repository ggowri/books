Chapter 2213 They Have Been Sold-1

At the news, Osborne's eyes lit up with a flicker of hope. The prospect of finding his parents, even as he stood on the brink of breaking through to Ascension Seven, made him suppress his advance.

But when he realized it was bad news, his expression shifted to one of tension.

He took a deep breath and asked, "What's going on? Just tell me—I can handle it!"

The worst-case scenario was that his parents had been dead for ages.

It was a bitter truth Osborne wasn't ready to accept, but he knew he could bear it.

Isa didn't hesitate, sensing his urgency. "Osborne, if our investigation hasn't gone awry, it seems your parents... they might have been sold!"

"Sold?" Osborne echoed, stunned.

They aren't dead; they have been sold?

What kind of situation is this?

Isa continued, "Based on your orders, the Empire of the Sun has been frantically searching for them. Various factions are eager to please you and have been putting in a lot of effort, but there's been no trace. It's as if your parents never set foot on Celestara Planet.

"Things remained a mystery until recently when the Mystic Feather Clan offered a clue. They suspect that over the past century, the Paramount Supreme Clan and the Demonic Dragon Clan have been secretly running a slave-trading operation. They've been snatching up talented warriors from the Human Community on Celestara Planet and selling them to the dark forces on other planets in exchange for resources.

"They think that right after your parents landed on Celestara Planet, they were probably captured by the Paramount Supreme Clan and the Demonic Dragon Clan and then sold off to other worlds!

"Based on this lead, we dug deeper and sure enough, found that the Demonic Dragon Clan and the Paramount Supreme Clan are indeed involved in this slave-trading racket. After some... 'encouragement', we managed to track down one of the guys who might have personally captured your parents and sold them..."

With a flick of her hand, Isa conjured up a member of the Demonic Dragon Clan.

He looked like a complete mess, weak as a kitten, clearly having gone through a lot of torture and interrogation.

When he saw Osborne, the man immediately pleaded, "Your Majesty! Please, have mercy on me!

"We were just following the Paramount Supreme Clan's orders to sell slaves from the Human Community. We had no idea your parents were among those we sold!

"Your Majesty, please!" The member of the Demonic Dragon Clan sobbed, his voice thick with desperation.

Osborne's face clouded over, and he ignored the man's pleas. Instead, he reached out, grabbed the man, and without a moment's hesitation, snapped his neck.

Then, activating his Satan's Eye, Osborne delved into the Demonic Dragon Clan member's memories and locked them deep in his own mind.

He closed his eyes and quickly scanned through the memories.

Moments later, a surge of emotion hit him, and he jolted.

His face brightened with excitement; he had indeed seen his parents in that memory. It was over twenty years ago, shortly after they'd arrived on Celestara Planet from Blue Planet.

Their luck had run out when they bumped into a hunting party from the Demonic Dragon Clan.

There was no way for them to escape, and they were captured!

Osborne's parents were then locked up in a secret place, surrounded by other talented slaves from the Human Community.

Once they'd rounded up enough captives, a Supreme Malevolence Airship showed up at the prison, all set to take them away.

They were going to be sold off to other planets!
