Up to one hundred thousand combat drones and battle robots erupted at once. The power of energy weapons in such a large-scale war was still terrifying.

The fighters of the Aberrants fell one after another. The casualties of the coalition forces were quickly brought under control.

With the help of countless battle robots and drones, the coalition forces surged with momentum and completely suppressed the Aberrants' army.

The Aberrants suffered massive casualties. If they were an ordinary army, they would have retreated long ago in such a disadvantageous situation. But they were merely a group of clones, clones who had thought but completely obeyed Gerardo.

Gerardo did not order their retreat, so even if they died in battle, they would absolutely not back down.

Gerardo snorted, "A bunch of soulless drones. Even optimized to their peak, this is all the power they possess. In the past, I indeed had no way to deal with these battle machines, which is why I never returned to Rosmandi. But now...

"Now they're just a pile of scrap machines. I can dismantle them directly."

After saying this dismissively, Gerardo waved his hand.

From among the eight hundred Class S clones beside him, one hundred stepped forward and quickly flew towards the swarms of combat drones in the sky.

The combat drones sensed the threat and rapidly changed their attack directions. Countless energy attacks instantly enveloped those one hundred Class S clones.

Unfortunately, their energy weapons were barely enough against Class AAA, let alone Class S.

The defenses of the Class S clones completely ignored the attacks from the drones. When energy attacks landed on their defenses, they merely stirred up some ripples, failing to cause any substantial damage.

"Death Claw."

"Ghost Petrification."

"Formidable Punch."

The one hundred Class S clones attacked at the same time, causing the combat drones to either explode or be petrified, losing their ability to fly and attack.

In an instant, large numbers of combat drones plummeted to the ground.

The materials used to manufacture the drones were the finest diamond from the materials research lab. Even a Class AAA warrior would find it challenging to wreck them.

However, in the face of Class S, they were far too fragile.

Each time the clones launched an attack, large quantities of drones and robots were destroyed, as if they were one hundred Titans crushing thousands of ants.

Wesson's expression changed dramatically. These weapons were the most cutting-edge technological products of the Draco Group. Each one cost dear.

He would not have minded losses in normal combat, but now they were being slaughtered—Wesson could not accept such losses.

"Retreat! Retreat! Combat drone units retreat, provide remote weapon support," Wesson ordered.

All the combat drones and battle robots began to withdraw, while atop the nine peaks of the Augustine Estate, dense energy artillery appeared.

Each peak had hundreds of energy cannons, totaling thousands.

Cannons on each peak locked onto a Class S clone and unleashed a torrent of violent energy that struck the Class S clone at a terrifying speed.

The horrendous energy attack broke through the defenses of the targeted clone, completely shattering its upper body.

The energy cannons on the other eight peaks also fired. Each targeted one clone and unleashed terrifying power, blasting clones apart.

After two simultaneous barrages, eighteen of the one hundred Class S clones disappeared.

Gerardo snorted coldly, "No wonder it's the Augustine family, no wonder it's the Draco Group. After driving me away, you ruled Rosmandi for thirty years. If you had no more other cards, I would actually feel disappointed.

"Energy cannons capable of killing Class S? If I couldn't mass-clone Class S Jackals, I might face defeat against these energy cannons.

"Is this your trump card? Well, it's still not enough. Today, you must face annihilation." After saying this, Gerardo issued another command.

Another one hundred Class S clones charged out, all of them Tauren Kings with formidable defenses, shaking the ground as they ran. Their target was the energy cannons on the nine peaks.

"Just a pile of immobile scrap. Knock them down," Gerardo commanded.
