Isa smiled, "I know! But until I take care of this guy from Racial Convergence Planet, Lord Malevolence won't show up.

"Let me show you what I'm really capable of! Divine Thunder Execution!"

Maddox brought the Hammer of Thor crashing down, unleashing a torrent of lightning that surged toward Isa like a raging river, moving at breakneck speed.

An otherworldly dread unfurled across the universe. The sheer, raw power was so overwhelming that it sent shivers down spines from a staggering distance of millions of miles. The world went deathly still. Amidst the enveloping darkness, there was but one sole beacon: a jagged bolt of lightning, a tempestuous ribbon of light.

Utterly petrifying! Brutally overpowering!

But can Isa really handle someone as formidable as Maddox?

Even the God of Night himself would struggle against him, wouldn't he?

If Isa were to fall, then the Human Community would be doomed!

Her legs bent slightly, then propelled her upward as she launched a kick straight at the oncoming lightning.

Isa's foot appeared almost insignificant compared to the massive, menacing lightning.

Anyone would think that if the lightning made contact, her foot would disintegrate into nothingness.

Maddox was left in shock.

His full-powered attack had just been obliterated by a single kick from Isa!

It was no different from when Isa had kicked Ricky away before!

As the disciple of the God of Night, Isa, no matter how strong, couldn't possibly surpass him, right?

Such power left everyone present astounded.

He clearly couldn't accept that he had been defeated in one blow by someone of his own rank.

Maddox was desperate to rush at Isa for a rematch.
