It wasn't worth much at all.

"Wait a minute. This Soul Resonance Fluid belonged to Tobey! How could someone from the Paramount Supreme Clan have such a thing? Could it be... Ultimate Soul Resonance Fluid?"

In Lachlan's memories, Ultimate Soul Resonance Fluid was a prized possession, worth millions of Essence Crystals per drop, and it was nearly impossible to buy even if one had the money.

The fluid's primary function was to attract high-level Deathborns.

If he were ever chased by powerful creatures, tossing out some of this fluid could draw their attention away, allowing him to escape.
