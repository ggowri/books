"Oh? Decisive enough. Then I'll personally test your strength!" Osborne thought, not explaining who he was.

He looked at the fastest person to act, an instructor from the training base and one of the three Class AA instructors. Without any probing, he directly transformed into a Vampire and punched Osborne with the proficiency level of Ninefold Tide Crash.

The terrifying force, like a tide, arrived in front of Osborne in the blink of an eye.

"Well done!" Osborne murmured and punched out as well.

The massive collision of forces was like two high-speed large passenger planes colliding in the air, making a deafening sound. The airwaves blew the surrounding trees to tremble wildly, almost uprooting them.

Both Osborne and the instructor were forced to take a few steps back.

The difference was that Osborne only took three steps back, while the instructor took more than ten steps back.

Even so, Osborne was amazed.

His current strength was mighty and terrifying, and even Sam, known for his strength, was no match for him regarding it.

Even Aresta, the 13th-ranked Godson of Yalynch, who had defeated Sam with a single punch, only had a little more strength than him.

And this instructor, after fighting him with a punch, only took a few steps back.

In Osborne's view, the instructor, who had fought with all his strength, was definitely not weaker than Sam.

"Interesting!" Osborne murmured, very satisfied.

But his opponent was extremely surprised, even shocked.

His full-force punch was actually repelled!

Most importantly, he could feel Osborne's energy fluctuation. Osborne was just Class A+!

"Be careful! He is very strong!"

He shouted loudly and immediately threw another punch. This time, several other Class A+ instructors also joined in. Realizing Osborne's formidable strength, they no longer fought him head-on but began to surround and attack him with tacit cooperation.

Osborne tried to attack a few times, but each move was resolved by their joint efforts. He had great power but couldn't touch these instructors.

"Even more interesting."

Osborne was even happier. He had never encountered such a situation. He was confident that he could defeat each of the six instructors fighting with him at this time, and it would be very easy.

But when the six of them joined hands, it was as if he had fallen into a quagmire.

Though his attacks were powerful, they couldn't break through the combined defense of these six instructors.

If he didn't employ more tricks, he would surely be worn down by these six instructors.

And there were only six instructors at the moment!

At this time, fourteen more instructors were on high alert not far away and not yet joining the battle.

"It seems I must bring out my true skills! Otherwise, I'm afraid they will look down on me even if I reveal my identity later."

With that thought, Osborne let out a cold snort.

"You can transform into Vampires, and so can I! Moreover, my Vampire Transformation is more complete!"

With that, he activated the power of the Blood Cross within his body and began to transform.

His short hair grew wildly, instantly turning into flowing long hair, each strand blood-red, like fresh blood.

Two sharp fangs emerged in his mouth.

His fingers grew long, sharp nails like daggers.

Even his face turned pale and more angular.

After the transformation, Osborne's speed, strength, defense, and recovery energy all became much stronger.

He stared at the six instructors, a smirk on his lips.

The six instructors in front of him, however, looked considerably changed. After Osborne's transformation into a Vampire, they clearly felt a kind of suppression and fear that came from their bloodline.

They suddenly stopped moving, their bodies shaking violently.

In addition to them, the instructors and students in the distance who were not fighting also looked horrible, staring at Osborne in shock.

Osborne noticed something was wrong.

"What's going on?"

He was puzzled, and the instructors and students were even more puzzled at this moment.

The leading instructor looked up at Osborne with difficulty and asked, "Who are you? Why do we feel a power in your body that makes us afraid and unable to resist!"

"A power in my body that makes you afraid and unable to resist?"

Osborne was even more surprised.

"Is it because of my Vampire Transformation? I'm at a higher level than them after I transformed into a Vampire, so I can suppress them and make them afraid?" thinking of this, Osborne quickly retracted his Vampire Transformation.

In an instant, the instructors and students, in fear, returned to normal, but their eyes on Osborne were still heavy.

They exchanged glances quickly.

Then, the leading instructor suddenly shouted, "Everyone, attack together! His Vampire Transformation can suppress us. That's a huge threat to us! No more holding back. Kill him now!"

With that, he rushed to Osborne. He launched an attack on Osborne madly, abandoning defense altogether, entangling him like a fierce beast, and not giving him a chance to transform.

Osborne was a bit speechless. He was about to reveal himself but was interrupted by the dense attacks.

The other nineteen instructors also rushed over at this time, their eyes full of crazy killing intent, launching a dense attack on him.

Everyone was attempting to tear Osborne apart.

At the same time, the students in the distance controlled countless combat drones and robots, instantly sealing the entire Training Base No. 6.

Each combat drone and robot had now activated its weapon system, locking onto Osborne.

In an instant, Osborne was in a desperate situation.
