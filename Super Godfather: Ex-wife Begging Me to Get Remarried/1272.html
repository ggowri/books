Chapter 1271 Deschanel Clan's Transcendent Deity

"Their reinforcement is here already?! That's fast!"

The God of Star's expression turned incredibly grave, but instead of halting, she accelerated her actions.

"Stop!"

At that moment, a thunderous roar echoed, rolling in like a storm. The Augustine family's Junior Deities felt their blood surge uncontrollably, their faces turning pale.

They all looked towards the southern sky, astonished.

In the next second, a figure sped towards them with terrifying velocity, aiming directly for Joseph and his companions.

"Hmph! Trying to save them from me?

"Dream on! I'm not stupid enough to let you rescue them, only to have them gang up on me again!

"Die!"

Without a moment's hesitation, the God of Star unleashed her power. Countless rays pierced through Joseph and his companions, instantly dissolving their bodies.

Joseph and his group, who had momentarily shown excitement and hope at the sight of the Deschanel Clan's Transcendent Deity warrior, were frozen in the next second.

Feeling their bodies dissolve rapidly, they tried to speak but found no chance.

Instantly, they were all dead.

With a grave expression, the God of Star faced the southern sky.

A figure finally stopped about half a mile away from the God of Star, his face gloomy and menacing as he looked at the completely dissolved bodies of the five.

"You're looking for death!

"Did you not hear me when I told you to stop?!"

The Transcendent Deity warrior of the Deschanel Clan glared at the God of Star. He was a middle-aged man, strikingly handsome but with a cold, fierce glint in his eyes.

The God of Star remained calm. "If I told you to stop, would you? So why should I?"

Eamon Deschanel barked, "Because I am Eamon Deschanel of the Deschanel Clan!

"How dare you kill five Superior Deity elders of my clan in front of me? I don't care who you are; you're as good as dead!"

Without another word, Eamon attacked.

The God of Star snorted, "Step back! A battle between Transcendent Deity warriors is not something you can interfere with. Even the aftershock of our fight could severely injure or kill you.

"Hurry and find Osborne!

"There might be more than one Transcendent Deity warrior in the Deschanel Clan. I can't hold him off for long! Get Osborne to figure something out, or not even I can save the Augustine family!"

With that, the God of Star charged forward.

A fierce battle between the two Transcendent Deity warriors erupted.

The God of Star transformed into a blinding light, while Eamon unleashed his divine power like a raging tide.

The sheer terror of the battle made it clear, even without the God of Star's warning, that Sebastian and Anastasia had to retreat.

They were Junior Deities, after all.

Facing a Superior Deity, they might hold their ground for a while, but against a Transcendent Deity?

Their Divine Bodies were too fragile!

"Grandpa, what do we do now? God of Star told us to find Osborne! But even if Osborne isn't in seclusion, what can he do?" Anastasia frowned deeply.

Though they had suspected the Deschanel Clan might have a power beyond Superior Deity, witnessing it firsthand was shocking.

This was a Transcendent Deity!

And God of Star had suggested there might be more than one in the Deschanel Clan. How could they possibly stand against such power?

Even if Osborne were here, what could he do?

In everyone's mind, he was only a Junior Deity, after all. In the face of a Transcendent Deity warrior, he was as weak as an ant. What hope did they have?

Sebastian watched the fierce battle between the God of Star and Eamon with bated breath, grinding his teeth as he barked orders, "Maybe it's time we prepare for a run for our lives!

"Everyone, get ready to bolt at a moment's notice!

"And send someone to inform Osborne; I'd rather not disturb him while he's in seclusion, but we're at the brink of extinction. We have no choice!"

The crowd nodded in unison.

Tensions were palpable as everyone swiftly prepared for the worst. The thought of God of Star faltering against the Deschanel Clan's onslaught sent shivers down their spines.

Luck seemed to be on their side, as Osborne possessed formidable spatial power.

If only they could rouse him from his meditative state, they might stand a chance of fleeing to some obscure corner the Deschanel Clan wouldn't easily scout.

However, that hope was abruptly shattered when a grim report reached them.

"What? A potent energy barrier surrounds Osborne's chamber? It's impenetrable and prevents us from even attempting to awaken him?"

Sebastian's face darkened with dread.

Without Osborne's spatial power, their escape route was effectively sealed.

The dire implications for the myriad Augustine family members hung heavily over them. How many of the tens of thousands could possibly survive such a cataclysm?

Sebastian's visage mirrored the collective despair.

But then, the skies to the south crackled with fresh waves of potent energy.

The Deschanel Clan's reinforcements had arrived. The already dire situation now spiraled beyond all hope.
