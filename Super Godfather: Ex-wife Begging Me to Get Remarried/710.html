Chapter 710 Killing

"Cough, cough!"

Emma was lifted by Osborne, and she suddenly coughed up a mouthful of blood.

After spitting out the blood, her complexion looked slightly better, but she was still extremely pale.

"You... you finally came!"

Emma looked at Osborne and said weakly, "If you hadn't come, I would've been dead! This time, I lost big. If you don't help me unlock Satan's Eye, I'll haunt you forever!"

Osborne was stunned by her words.

"Help you unlock Satan's Eye? What do you mean?"

Hearing this, Emma blushed.

She thought Osborne was playing dumb.

Then Emma glared at him. She tried to speak, but immediately spat out another mouthful of blood.

Osborne frowned and quickly said, "Don't talk anymore, just rest."

Osborne looked up at Clarissa.

Clarissa and Luca were still in a fierce battle, with no time to use Divine Healing on Emma.

Osborne had already used the Light of Redemption once before and couldn't use it again right now.

"Hang in there a little longer."

Osborne gently lifted Emma onto his back, instantly transforming into a vampire, using his blood-red hair to securely bind her to his back.

"I'll take you with me to fight!"

Osborne said, facing the remaining eleven Class AAA Evolved Human.

"Come on!"

Osborne gripped the Goddess of Grain's scythe, his eyes burning with killing intent.

"You're all going to die!"

After saying this, Osborne attacked.

Emma lay on Osborne's back, feeling his strong frame. Smelling his masculine scent, and hearing his words, she blushed.

"Bang!"

Osborne punched Titan, sending him flying, then turned and delivered another punch to Tauren's head, using Deadly Force to kill him instantly.

"Ten left!"

Osborne murmured, then suddenly pointed at Medusa, who was trying to use her ultimate move.

"Thunder of Judgment!"

Boom!

A terrifying thunderbolt descended instantly, interrupting Medusa's move.

She tried to dodge desperately but was still struck, causing half of her body to vanish instantly.

"Dissolving Light!"

A beam of light flashed quickly, piercing through Medusa's head, and she collapsed.

"Nine left!"

At this moment, Osborne transformed into a god of slaughter, disregarding the drain on his power, unleashing various ultimate moves and hidden techniques.

He waved his hand, tearing the space apart, creating countless dimensional rifts within a radius of hundreds of yards.

With Emma on his back, he walked into a dimensional rift.

The next second, they appeared behind Werewolf. The Werewolf reacted quickly, attempting to dodge.

Unfortunately, it was too late.

The sharp Goddess of Grain's scythe easily sliced off his head, leaving his body to lunge forward while his head stayed behind.

Blood sprayed wildly.

"Eight left!"

Osborne panted heavily, feeling the extreme drain on his energy.

He gritted his teeth and suddenly waved his hand.

Instantly, the thick blood mist on the battlefield began to converge toward Osborne, absorbed by the Blood Cross within him.

The immense blood energy seemed to revive the Blood Cross, sending waves of terrifying power coursing through it.

Osborne's exhausted body felt replenished.

"Again!"

Osborne entered another dimensional rift.

The remaining eight Class AAA Evolved Humans were frantically attacking at this moment, shattering all the dimensional rifts around them.

Osborne couldn't get close to them, so he appeared about one hundred yards behind Medusa.

Medusa didn't hesitate and immediately turned to run.

"Think you can escape?"

Osborne snorted coldly. "Spatial Prison!"

In an instant, the space around Medusa transformed into a solid prison, trapping her inside.

Medusa panicked, attacking frantically, trying to break free from the Spatial Prison.

But at that moment, another bolt of lightning struck down.

Thunder of Judgment!

Medusa, trapped in the Spatial Prison, had barely managed to break free but didn't have time to escape before the Thunder of Judgment struck her.

Her body instantly vanished in the purple lightning.

"Seven left!"

Osborne smirked coldly.

At this moment, he was like a god of slaughter, unleashing terrifying attacks that left Emma, clinging to his back, utterly stunned.

Originally, Emma thought her strength wasn't any weaker than Osborne's or Clarissa's.

But now, Emma realized she was wrong.

Even though they were all Class AA, both Clarissa and Osborne were significantly stronger than her.

At the very least, there was no way she could kill Class AAA Evolved Human like this.

Even with the Swallow Shield, she wouldn't be able to withstand Luca's attacks.

Since she was a child, Emma had been considered the greatest talent of the Augustine family, far surpassing even Vincent and Matthew.

But now, Emma realized that the true genius of the Augustine family wasn't her, but Osborne!
