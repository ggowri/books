Chapter 547 Time Difference

The ridicule of the crowd made Nathan, Queenie, and other people from the Dampston branch bloodline feel very uncomfortable.

But they had no way to refute it.

They could only clench their fists tightly and swear in their hearts that they must practice harder to make themselves stronger and never be ridiculed again.

If they were all Class A and could sign up for the competition, Osborne wouldn't fight alone, and other branch bloodlines wouldn't dare to mock them.

"I want to kill them!" Nathan gritted his teeth and whispered.

"Calm down."

Isaac comforted him, "This is a good thing for you. Now you see their arrogant faces. After you have the strength in the future, you can slap them in the face."

Nathan and others nodded.

At this time, Osborne was the first to jump out of the helicopter and jump into the dense jungle of the Lostridge Mountains.

However, the picture didn't disappear.

The Draco Group's drones followed closely behind Osborne and transmitted the images back.

"It's finally officially started."

"Osborne, come on!"

Nathan and others clenched their fists and stared at the screen closely, praying for Osborne in their hearts. They were also curious about what Osborne would do in such a desperate situation.

Osborne landed easily on the Lostridge Mountains.

Looking at the small drones not far away, he wondered whether to blow it up.

But he gave up.

These drones were used to broadcast the footage of the Branch Bloodline Competition. If they were blown up, he would probably violate the rules and be punished.

At this moment, Osborne's Draco Star Wristlet received a message from Caleb, which was the specific rules of the Branch Bloodline Competition. Osborne took a quick look and saw that there was indeed a rule that prohibited intentional damage to the broadcasting drones.

"Fine."

Osborne no longer cared about the drones but was thinking about what to do next.

Just as he was thinking, a figure suddenly fell from above his head.

It was Lance.

Lance looked at Osborne with a confident smile. "You haven't run away yet? You only have ten minutes. After ten minutes, they will set out to chase you, snatch your emblem, and eliminate you."

Lance had a playful look on his face.

Osborne glanced at him and said calmly, "Didn't you also not run away? Compared to me, you seem to be calmer."

Lance couldn't help laughing.

"I didn't run away because I know that no one dares to snatch my emblem. My strength makes me believe that I can keep the emblem.

"But you don't seem to have such ability."

Osborne shrugged. "Really? Maybe I will become the future heir in the end, and you will fail."

"Hahaha."

Lance couldn't help but burst into laughter, and his laughter was full of contempt and ridicule.

"It's impossible!

"You should be thankful for the rules. The rules don't allow me, who already has the emblem, to attack you. Otherwise, you would have lost it by now."

Osborne also smiled and said, "I want to say this to you as well. If the rules allowed it, you would have lost your emblem."

"You are arrogant!"

Lance sneered, "Then we'll see. If you can become the future heir, I will avoid you when I see you next time."

After saying that, Lance left.

He walked very slowly as if he was traveling without any sense of urgency at all.

Osborne snorted coldly and continued to think of a solution.

He couldn't escape.

The emblem on his chest would send out a signal every fifteen minutes, and everyone could locate him. No matter how far he ran or where he hid, he would be found.

With more than a hundred people chasing him, there was no way he could escape until the end of the competition.

Thus, he could only fight.

"In a few minutes, everyone except Lance will target me and try to snatch my emblem.

"I have to find a way to reduce their numbers first."

Osborne had a plan.

"The time difference.

"The emblem will only send out a signal every fifteen minutes. There are still ten minutes before the next signal is sent. In five minutes, everyone will leave the helicopter.

"The first thing they will do is to gather together."

"After they finish gathering, my emblem will send out a signal. After they locate me, they will chase me."

"What I need to do is to take advantage of the time difference and launch a sneak attack before they gather and locate me. In this way, I can reduce the people of their group."

Osborne sneered and began to get ready.

At this time, at the Arena Number Two on Challenge Peak.

Everyone was looking at the screen, which had now been divided into nine smaller screens, allowing everyone to see the different situations of the nine people.

But at this time, almost everyone was staring at Osborne's picture.

"That brat is so arrogant."

"He offended Sam and even dared to talk back to Lance. He is courting death."

"If it weren't for the rules, Lance would have killed him in one move."

"Arrogance without strength is stupidity."

"Why doesn't he just run away? Why is he still there?"

"What does he want to do?"
