Chapter 909 The Last Move

Half an hour had passed since Osborne last unleashed the Light of Redemption.

Now, as he called upon it again, a radiant glow enveloped him. In an instant, his wounds healed, his energy surged, and his mental strength was restored.

It was as if he had been reborn, standing at the peak of his power once more.

Feeling the rush of strength coursing through him, Osborne turned to Isa, his eyes blazing with renewed determination.

"Isa, let's try again!"

Isa smiled, "Bring everything you've got! If you can't take me down today, you might find it a lot harder to beat me in the future."

Osborne nodded but didn’t say anything.

Instead, he focused, summoning his strength. "Light Realm!"

He knew that going toe-to-toe with Isa in a battle of strength or skill was futile. He needed the advantage of his Realm's power.

As the Light Realm enveloped Isa, her expression shifted to one of surprise.

"Your light elemental essence has been unsealed? Who did that for you?"

Osborne hesitated for a moment but then replied in secret,

"Isa, how did you know my light elemental essence was sealed? It was the God of Star who helped me unseal it. He's an ancient deity."

"Really?" Isa nodded in understanding, her gaze piercing.

"I didn't expect you'd met him! If he's the one who helped you, that makes sense. But a Level Four Light Realm won't be enough to handle me."

Before Osborne could respond, Isa moved.

With a powerful kick, she struck the Light Realm, and Osborne's face paled as he felt the shockwave. A portion of his Level Four Realm shattered under her blow.

He was stunned.

This was a Level Four Realm, solid as iron, and even most Class S Supreme Overlords couldn't master it.

Breaking through the Level Four Realm was no easy feat.

The Level Four Light Realm was incredibly demanding in terms of energy, and Osborne couldn't maintain it for long.

Yet, in an instant, Isa had created a gaping hole in it!

Osborne's expression grew serious; he knew he couldn't hesitate any longer. It was time to unleash his ultimate moves.

"Destruction Orbs! Disintegration Rays!"

A dazzling array of light cascaded down like rain, surrounding Isa. Anything that touched those shimmering orbs would be obliterated.

Simultaneously, countless rays shot forth, piercing through anything in their path, all aimed directly at Isa.

Osborne's face lit up with anticipation.

The combination of Destruction Orbs and Disintegration Rays, enhanced by the Level Four Light Realm, was his most formidable attack.

He hoped to catch Isa off guard.

Isa, however, simply watched the onslaught approach with a smile.

"Not bad! Looks like he taught you some real skills."

With that, she moved again, her demeanor as casual as ever.

She simply struck a kicked, seems effortless yet powerful.

In an instant, all the Destruction Orbs and Disintegration Rays shattered before they even touched Isa, causing her no harm whatsoever.

Osborne was dumbfounded.

He'd thought that such a powerful attack, if it were aimed at him, would have left him battered and bruised, if not gravely injured. But Isa had erased it with such ease.

Despair truly gripped Osborne now.

The crowd below the arena had fallen into stunned silence, their mouths agape as they watched the two combatants. Even they could feel the weight of Osborne's hopelessness.

Isa was simply too strong, like an impenetrable fortress.

Osborne had already given his all, even unleashing his Level Four Light Realm, yet he hadn't even managed to push Isa back an inch.

Isa remained as nonchalant as ever, making the sense of despair almost suffocating.

Osborne felt like he was about to choke on his own frustration. He stared at Isa, who wore a smile on her face, and took a deep breath.

He said, "Isa, I got this one last move!

If this doesn't work, I'm out of options!"

Isa's smile widened. "Go ahead, then."

Osborne took another deep breath. "Then watch!

"Vampire Transformation!

"The Breath of Celestial!"

With a fierce shout, Osborne began to channel his remaining power. He activated the Divine Bone in his right leg, drawing on its divine power to deliver a devastating kick aimed at Isa.

"Explosive Force!"

This kick represented the full extent of Osborne's strength. If Isa could deflect this with ease, he would have no choice but to concede.

The kick unleashed a terrifying force, lighting Isa's eyes in surprise. But she said nothing. Instead, she raised her leg and gave a matching kick.

The shockwaves were immense.
