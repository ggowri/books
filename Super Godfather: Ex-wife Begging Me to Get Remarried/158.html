Chapter 158 Betrayal

Just as Clarissa approached and treated those who were lying on the ground, they suddenly stood up.

The ten people surrounded Clarissa in an instant.

Before Clarissa could react, one of them put a sharp alloy dagger on her neck.

At the same time, Harris, who was standing behind Donna, suddenly punched her hard on the back.

Harris' strength was far inferior to Donna's, but he was a Class D+ martial artist. The power of his punch was strong. Donna was caught off guard. How could her fragile body withstand such a hard attack?

Donna vomited blood and fell to the ground.

Her face was pale, and she was stunned.

It seemed that she had not come to her senses. She saw Harris taking his gun. He aimed at her head and prepared to pull the trigger.

"Go to hell, bitch!" Harris shouted ferociously.

"Damn it!" Barlyn finally reacted.

He watched Harris launch a surprise attack and try to shoot his mother, but his mother seemed dazed. He suddenly became angry.

He waved his hand, and an earth wall instantly appeared in front of Donna.

The bullet hit the wall, making a muffled sound.

The members of Clarissa's Personal Guards finally reacted.

They took their guns, aimed at Harris, and prepared to fight back.

Seeing this, Harris immediately flashed to Clarissa and pointed a gun at her head.

He shouted, "If you don't want her to die now, stop it!"

Clarissa's Personal Guards panicked. Looking at Clarissa, who was being controlled, they dared not shoot.

Donna came to her senses.

She stood up with difficulty and looked at Clarissa, who was being controlled. She was trembling all over, looking furious, and she clenched her fists.

Donna growled, "Harris, how dare you betray us!

"How dare you attack Clarissa? You are courting death!"

Harris sneered, "Donna, don't scare me. I'm timid. If I get scared, I may accidentally pull the trigger and blow up Ms. Radcliffe's head. You don't want to see this, right?"

Donna was stunned, and her eyes were full of worry.

Harris continued, "Betray you? Humph, how can you say that? I'm not a member of the Radcliffe family. I'm just an employer. You pay me, and I work for you. That's all.

"Someone has given me more money than I can spend in the rest of my life. I just made a right choice. It's not a betrayal."

Donna's face became even angrier when she heard what Harris said.

Harris said coldly, "Don't blame me!

"If you let me be the bodyguard captain, why would I do this? You actually let a newcomer be my superior and give me orders. What right does he have? He actually targeted me and didn't give me any opportunity to make money!

"In that case, I will make you pay the price!

"You even wanted to take action. I contacted the Aberrants and destroyed your plan. I didn't expect that they would even promise to give me a large sum of money. How can I refuse such a good deal?"

After Harris finished speaking, he looked arrogant.

"Asshole!" Barlyn could not help but curse. "If you dare to hurt Clarissa, I'll kill you. With the people of the Bodyguard Unit Two, you can't run away!"

Harris sneered but said nothing.

Just then, a man laughed. "You are too confident. Since we knew your plan, how could we not have made arrangements? You have no chance."

The next second, two people from the Bodyguard Unit Two stood out with sneers.

Seeing this, Donna was stunned. "Aren't you from the second Bodyguard Unit? You... You are metahumans!"

The man said, "Let me introduce myself. I'm Hammond Alvarado, a Class C+ plant-controlling metahuman. The one next to me is my brother, Harland Alvarado, a Class C+ martial artist.

"As for the others, they are all shape-shifting metahumans, werewolves."

After Hammond finished speaking, the eight men roared. Their bodies began to swell, and they were covered with black hair, transforming into werewolves.

Seeing this situation, Donna widened her eyes in surprise.

"A Class C+ plant-controlling metahuman and a Class C+ martial artist?" This situation was tough.

There was restraint between elemental powers. The earth elemental restrained the plant elemental.

Even though Donna was not injured, and her power was in peak condition, it would be difficult for her to defeat a plant-controlling metahuman of the same level, and she would probably even lose.

She was seriously injured.

Moreover, there was also a Class C+ martial artist.

How could she fight against them?

With Donna, Barlyn, the Personal Guards, and bodyguards, they were no match for Hammond and Harland at all. What was more, Harris controlled Clarissa, making them hesitant to act rashly.

"Kill Clarissa first!" Hammond suddenly said.
