It was soon ten o'clock in the evening.

At this time, at the headquarters in Dampston, all the members from the Blood Demon Organization had finished resting. Their power had become stronger. After the baptism of blood, they also became more like a true warrior.

Osborne stopped his cultivation and had everyone gather once again.

He projected a map, pointed at the three red dots on the map, and said calmly, "These are the three rare metal mines in Dampston that are our targets tonight. The Aberrants started this war, and I felt that there was something weird about it.

"Their real goal is not to occupy more areas of Rosmandi but to occupy these rare metal mines. I suspect that the Aberrants have assigned more warriors to guard these rare metal mines!

"Our actions tonight will be based on sneak attacks!

"I will be the first to set out to spy on these rare metal mines. All of you will wait for my order and prepare for the attack!

"As you command!"

Osborne nodded and waved his hand to rip open the dimensional rift. He took Clarissa's hand and stepped in.

The next second, his body disappeared and appeared thirty miles away.

After several interdimensional travels, the two arrived at the first rare metal mine area.

Osborne had been to this mine area once before.

At that time, it was guarded by only three Marquis Medusa who had reached Class AA, but he had easily killed them. Now the place was incredibly lively, with countless large machines running. They were mining like crazy even at night.

Osborne and Clarissa hid in the distance and observed.

Seeing such a situation, they were both very surprised.

"What kind of rare metals does this rare metal mine produce? The Aberrants seem to pay extra attention to it."
