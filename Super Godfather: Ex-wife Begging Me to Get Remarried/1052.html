Chapter 1051 Crazy Search

Gerardo barked orders, and the team sprang into action.

Some scoured the base, while others bolted outside, searching the surrounding area for any trace of the vanished Human Slaves.

Gerardo frowned in frustration, "How the hell did this happen?

"There's no sign of spatial fluctuations here. How could hundreds of people just disappear in an instant? Who has that kind of power?"

His mood darkened.

Gerardo felt an overwhelming urge to vent his rage on someone, anyone.

He fixed on the doctors, fury radiating from him.

"Though you had nothing to do with this, they vanished right under your watch! You useless fools should pay for this!"

Gerardo raised his hand. Bang!

In an instant, the doctors' bodies swelled, then exploded. Blood and flesh splattering across the nearby cultivation tanks.

They didn't even have a chance to plead for mercy before they were gone.

But Gerardo's anger didn’t fade.

Hundreds of Human Slaves were missing!

Gerardo didn't give a damn about the others. He could always create more later. He didn’t even care about the missing Lord.

It was Osborne’s missing that angered him most.

Osborne's level of enslavement had reached 92%. He was on the verge of becoming Gerardo's most powerful Human Slave weapon.

And now, he vanished!

"You can't count your chickens before they hatch?" Gerardo seethed, his anger boiling over.

He had poured countless resources into Osborne—top-tier culture solutions, rare herbs, even the heart's blood of deities.

Now, he couldn't even recreate another one of the top-tier culture solution!

But Osborne disappeared, taking all that precious energy with him!

"I have to get Osborne back!" Gerardo thought.

He was unable to bear the loss, nor losing control of physically enhanced Osborne, with a strength level of 200 and a potential to use that against him.

He thought, "I create a beast against me?

Damn it!

I need him back! I have to!"

His expression was gloomy as he waited for the news from his subordinates.

"Mr. Aniston, how could this happen?" Ophelia finally spoke up, her voice tinged with genuine astonishment.

She was truly confused.

She thought, "I just saved Osborne. Why did all the Human Slaves vanish? What on earth happened here after I left with him?

Who has the power to quietly whisk away hundreds of people?"

A chill ran down her spine as she thought of this.

It felt like there were secret, terrifying eyes watching her every move.

Gerardo scoffed as an answer.

He, too, was in the dark. At first, he had suspected Ophelia, thinking she might have used her time power to rescue Osborne.

But after considering her abilities, he quickly dismissed the possibility.

Ophelia's time power was indeed formidable, but at Class AAA, she wouldn't be able to secretly took hundreds of people away.

Not even if she broke through to Class S!

Gerardo thought, "So, who could it be?"

" Your Majesty: No traces found at headquarters!"

" Your Majesty: No traces around two miles!"

Gerardo's anger fueled as his subordinates delivered the news.

"That's unacceptable! Keep searching! Hundreds of people can't just vanish into thin air; there has to be a trace! You must find them!"

Once he fired off his orders, he couldn't hold back his rage any longer. Gerardo had to be out there searching himself—there was no way he would allow Osborne to slip away. Even if it meant killing Osborne, he could never let Osborne go.

If Class AAA Osborne, already boasting a strength of 200, broke through to Class S, what kind of monstrous power might he had then?

It could be more formidable than Gerardo when he fused with a god’s body!

While that didn't guarantee Osborne's strength would surpass his own, Osborne would undoubtedly become a big threat—an obstacle in Gerardo's quest for revenge against Sebastian and his plan to reclaim Rosmandi.

"Find him!" Gerardo yelled as he charged out the door, his mind racing as he scanned the surroundings for any clues.

Before long, he arrived at a hidden cave.

Ophelia, trailing behind him, felt her blood run cold as she realized where Gerardo was staring at the very cave where she had hidden Osborne.

Before she could say anything to stop, Gerardo stormed into the cave, and despair washed over her.

"Has he discovered everything?" Ophelia thought, biting down on her lip.

Ophelia steeled herself and followed him inside.
