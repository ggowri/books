Chapter 1314 Crushing Dominance

Osborne's breakthrough was complete; he now stood at the pinnacle of Superior Deity, with the third Divine Flame crowning his head.

The sight sent shock-waves through Terrence, Divine Shadow, and the God of Star.

Terrence's astonishment was palpable as his expression turned grave.

"Impossible! What kind of forbidden art could leap an entire realm?

To actually achieve Superior Deity!

This is bad news for me!"

Terrence knew he was in dire straits. Osborne's ascension to Superior Deity made him a true threat, negating any advantage Terrence once held.

But what truly shook him was Osborne's aura—it was still growing stronger!

"How can he keep getting stronger?!"

Terrence's composure wavered.

He couldn't fathom what secret technique Osborne had used to achieve such a colossal boost in power. "Doesn't he fear crippling his own potential?"

Osborne harbored no such fears.

He hadn't employed any forbidden technique—he'd simply borrowed strength through the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity from the Blood Demon members.

Already, he'd reached the mid-stage of Superior Deity, with each Blood Demon member providing roughly a third of their power.

"If all four thousand eight hundred Blood Demon members lent me their full strength, I might reach the peak of Superior Deity or even break through to Transcendent Deity.

But there's no need for that!

Superior Deity mid-stage is more than enough to deal with Terrence."

Osborne ceased absorbing power.

His divine power swelled as he coldly eyed Terrence.

"Terrence, let's resume our fight. This time, I'm curious to see how you plan to beat me!"

Face darkening, Terrence clenched his teeth. "Osborne, resorting to such a potent strength-enhancing technique, aren't you worried about your potential? You must really crave my demise, huh?

"But even if you've reached Superior Deity, it won't be enough. How long can this boost last anyway?"

A calm smile appeared on Osborne's face. "That's none of your concern. I think it won't be too difficult to defeat you now."

Terrence seethed. "Arrogant fool! Even as a Superior Deity, you're still leagues beneath me. What makes you think you can win? Not difficult, you say? This is a joke!"

Osborne's eyes narrowed, his body brimming with overwhelming power eager to unleash.

"Let's test that theory! Fight me!"

In an instant, Osborne struck.

Utilizing the same tactic, he halted time, appearing behind Terrence. This time, Terrence was ensnared for over a second, ample time for Osborne to amass his strength.

When Terrence snapped back, Osborne's boot collided with his back.

The force shattered Terrence's divine power defenses, cleaving his Divine Body with destructive impact.

Terrence was propelled forward, spewing blood, eyes wide with shock and fear.

His defenses had crumbled, and it had been effortless for Osborne.

Osborne was equally taken aback. He'd known his Superior Deity status would weaken Terrence's defenses but hadn't anticipated the ease of it.

Terrence's Divine Body was gravely injured.

Osborne's smile turned cold as he spoke. "Terrence, your time is up! You're the last of the Deschanel Clan. Save yourself the struggle and join your kin in oblivion. I can't afford to leave any loose ends."

Terrence wiped the blood from his lips, eyes burning with frenzy.

"No! I can't die!

"The Deschanel Clan can't be wiped out!

"I have to become a Deity King! We must rise to be the mightiest clan!

"I refuse to perish here!

"I will defeat you!"

In a fit of desperation, Terrence launched himself at Osborne, unleashing every ounce of his divine power with ferocious abandon. His attacks were wild and formidable.

Even Divine Shadow's expression hardened, and she quickly warned Osborne, "Osborne, be careful! He's going all out!"

But Osborne merely chuckled, "Going all out, are you? Too late! Maybe if I were still an Intermediate Deity, your reckless assault might work. But now, I'm a Superior Deity. You don't stand a chance! Prepare to fall!"

Choosing not to dodge, Osborne met Terrence's attack head-on.

After just three exchanges, Terrence lay bloodied on the ground.

Despite his all-out assault, he could only withstand three moves from this temporarily ascended Superior Deity before his Divine Body was nearly shattered.

He lay motionless, his eyes filled with despair and regret.

Divine Shadow and God of Star, watching from a distance, were dumbstruck. A glance shared between them reflected their shared shock and fear.

Osborne was overwhelmingly powerful.

Facing Osborne as he was now, their fate would likely be the same—or even worse than Terrence's!
