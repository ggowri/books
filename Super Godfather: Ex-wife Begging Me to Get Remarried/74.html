Chapter 74 New Ability at Level Three

“Money is just a number. But Satan’s Eye is related to my strength and whether I can complete more successor tests. I must improve it as soon as possible. As long as I become stronger, I can make more money in the future.”

Osborne took a deep breath and picked up the entire piece of Imperial Green diamond. He opened Satan’s Eye and placed the Imperial Green diamond tightly on his left eye.

In an instant, Osborne felt as if his left eye came alive and was twitching slightly. At the same time, a powerful suction force spread.

Osborne felt that a strange power surged out from the Imperial Green diamond. His left eye absorbed it. Suddenly, his left eye felt extremely clear and comfortable.

His eyes became brighter and brighter, as brilliant and profound as the Milky Way. Some changes that were difficult for science to explain were taking place.

Half an hour later, everything finally stopped.

At this time, a third silver star appeared in Osborne’s left eye. Although these three stars were tiny and could not be seen without careful observation, they were extremely bright.

“Satan’s Eye reached level three,” Osborne whispered. He could clearly feel that Satan’s Eye had become more powerful. Both his dynamic and static vision had been improved. His ability to see through things was also stronger.

But what made Osborne most happy was the new ability of Satan’s Eye in level three.

“Manipulating matter?” Osborne murmured. He wanted to try it immediately. But when he suddenly saw the diamond in his hand, he was surprised. “It’s not completely destroyed! It’s just that the quality has changed from Imperial Green diamond to ordinary diamond. It’s still a top-grade diamond. It is worth at least over 10 billion dollars!”

Osborne was thrilled.

He thought that this piece of Imperial Green diamond would be completely ruined and worthless. But it turned out that its quality was only reduced.

“I will give it to Alison later.” Osborne carefully put down the diamond and began to test the new ability of Satan’s Eye. According to what Godfather said, when Satan’s Eye reached the third level, Osborne could control matter.

Osborne opened Satan’s Eye and stared at Draco Model H mini-handgun. He whispered in his mind, “Take the gun out of the holster.”

The next second, something magical happened.

Osborne felt like he had an invisible pair of hands. He nimbly unhooked the holster, took out the pistol, aimed the muzzle at the distance, and pulled the trigger.

A slight gunshot was heard. A vase shattered in the distance.

Osborne was extremely surprised.

All these movements were easy and flexible for him. The invisible pair of hands were just like a part of his body. He could even do many more things than before.

“I don’t know my limit…” he said.

Osborne used the top-grade Imperial Green diamond to upgrade Satan’s Eye. In theory, this upgrade was perfect.

So, Satan’s Eye would be more robust than usual.

“Let me try how much weight I can control.”

Osborne left the room and came to the hospital’s backyard. Osborne used some huge lead blocks to test his strength.

The smallest lead block weighed only 20 pounds. The largest lead block weighed more than four thousand pounds.

Osborne first came to the smallest lead block and opened Satan’s Eye. His pupil light covered the lead block. The lead block instantly flew up and floated in front of Osborne.

“That’s easy.” Osborne was delighted. He came to the lead block that weighed 100 pounds. He lifted the lead block easily and moved it flexibly.

Then he came to the lead block that weighed 200 pounds.

This time, although he lifted the lead block, the speed of movement was greatly reduced. Osborne felt the difficulty for the first time.

Finally, he felt the limit of Satan’s Eye when he tried to lift the lead block, which was 800 pounds.

Osborne could barely lift that lead block and move it at all. He only held on for ten seconds. He was sweating, and his eyes were sore.

“It seems that my current limit is to control an 800-pounds object. However, as the level of Satan’s Eye increases, this limit will definitely increase. If I want to maintain the flexibility of manipulating objects, the weight cannot exceed 100 pounds and the distance cannot exceed 33 feet.”

Osborne roughly understood the capability of the third level.

Overall, he was very satisfied.

The ability to manipulate matter was similar to mind control. His combat methods would become more flexible.

If the situation at the Radcliffe Manor reappeared, Osborne wouldn’t have to brave the hail of bullets to get the riot shield. Now, with a glance, the riot shield would fly to his side.

“Can I use Satan’s Eye to control the bullets that are coming at me at high speed? If I can, I won’t be afraid of bullets shooting at me,” Osborne said.

A thought suddenly emerged in his mind. He became excited. “I am going to try it now.”
