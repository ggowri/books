Chapter 1391 The Deathborn Came

Over thirty Deathborn surged toward Harry, Vance, and the others, bursting forth from the rocks.

Their forms quickly solidified, revealing sharp claws, jagged teeth, and long, blood-red hair.

Without strategic intelligence, they had no plans for ambush or to observe the fight from the sidelines, ready to swoop in for an easy kill.

Instead, they rushed headlong into the fray, considering both sides as mere prey, targets for their insatiable hunger.

Their sudden appearance turned the tide of battle.

Harry and Amelia, as well as Vance and his team, all felt their hearts sink at the sight of the overwhelming horde charging toward them.

"Damn it! It's the Deathborn!

"What the hell?

"Why are there so many? Wasn't this secret passage supposed to be safe from Deathborn? This is madness! We need to hold them off!" Vance shouted, his face twisted in anger.

Everything was unfolding just as he had feared.

Amelia was already struggling against her assailants, and Harry was losing his grip on the situation. Just when victory seemed within reach, this monstrous wave of Deathborn crashed into their plans.

These creatures posed an existential threat; Vance realized he could no longer afford to focus on Harry.

Even if he had his grudge against Harry, these Deathborns had to be dealt with first. If he didn't eliminate them, they would all end up as food for the beasts.

The horror of facing the Deathborn was all too familiar to those born of Celestara. Once defeated, the only fate that awaited them was a gruesome end—having their Divine Bodies devoured without a sliver of hope left to escape.

"Kill them! Harry! We'll settle our scores later!

"We have to take out these Deathborn first!" Vance yelled as he engaged one of the beasts, hoping to rally Harry to his side.

Harry hesitated, an irritated snort escaping him. Rather than seizing the opportunity to strike at Vance, he sprinted to Amelia's side, ready to tackle the advancing Deathborn together.

He realized the overwhelming number of Deathborns made infighting a foolish risk; any infighting would only benefit their enemies.

Among the thirty Deathborns, one was an Intermediate Deity, eight were Junior Deities, and the rest were Class S.

Unfortunately for Harry, the lone Intermediate Deity Deathborn had set its sights on him.

The Deathborn might have appeared weak before Osborne, trembling under his oppressive aura, but in the face of Harry and his allies, they were terrifying.

The sheer power they wielded was daunting; their claws and teeth were weapons capable of piercing defenses that a human god of the same level would typically rely on. Their resilience was equally remarkable—wounds healed rapidly, making them formidable foes.

Harry found himself at a distinct disadvantage against the Intermediate Deity Deathborn.

At the same time, two Junior Deity Deathborns, flanked by a dozen Class S Deathborns, encircled Amelia. Herry and Amelia's situation was becoming increasingly dire.

In contrast, Vance and his team were holding their own against just six Junior Deity Deathborns and a handful of Class S foes.

With their strength, the battle felt manageable.

They quickly dispatched the Class S Deathborns, and the remaining Junior Deity threats were visibly weakening.

Watching this unfold only fueled Harry's anxiety.

Harry shouted, "Vance, are you going to help us or what? If these Deathborn kill us, you won't escape unscathed either!"

At his words, Vance burst into laughter, a loud, mocking sound that echoed through the chaos.

"Help you? Harry, have you lost your mind? Or do you think I'm the fool? This situation is exactly what I wanted to see! Don't worry; I won't let your sister die—not yet, anyway.

"Once she's weak from those Deathborn's attacks, I'll swoop in to save her. After all, she's quite the beauty, and I haven't had the pleasure of sampling her yet. I can't just let her perish like that!

"I'll make sure she experiences the wonders of being a woman! Hahaha! As for you, I'd rather see you fall to the Deathborn, so I don't have to get my hands dirty!"

Harry felt a surge of rage boil within him at Vance's cruel words.

"Vance, you won't get away with this! But don't forget—if this Intermediate Deity Deathborn kills me, you're next! There's no way you can escape!"

Vance just laughed, unfazed. "Who says I can't?

"An Intermediate Deity is nothing! Once I take down these six Junior Deity Deathborns, it will just leave that one Intermediate Deity. With all six of us together, we can definitely take it down.

"So you might as well just sit back and wait for your death!"

With a dismissive wave, Vance called to his followers, "Let's wrap this up! Take out these Junior Deity Deathborns, then we can sit back and watch the show as Harry gets devoured—skin and all! Hahaha!"

He was elated. Despite the unexpected arrival of the Deathborn, everything was still falling into place just as he had hoped.

Harry and Amelia were as good as finished!
