Chapter 1032 Well Done!

His fury boiled over as he shouted, "You damned Aberrants actually think you can capture me and make me a Human Slave? You wiped out everyone from Likreia Mountain! I'm going to take you down!

"A bunch of worthless clones, you all can go to hell!

"Come at me! I'll wipe the floor with you scum!"

In an explosive surge of power, Bryce unleashed an attack that engulfed over twenty Class S clones.

They were in the sea now, with water all around them, and Bryce's main discipline was water elemental power, which naturally gave him an edge in this environment.

The terrifying force from his attack sent the attacking clones staggering back.

"Some of you, go kill him!"

With that command, the over twenty Class S clones abandoned their assault on Osborne and charged at Bryce instead.

Bryce's face paled slightly.

"Crap, that looks like more than twenty now! Bring it on, I'm not scared!"

A wild gleam danced in his eyes.

"Osborne, I can't let you outshine me! There are fifty-one Class S clones in total—I'll take on twenty-six, and the rest are all yours!

"Let's see who's tougher!"

With that, he charged into the fray, madness written all over his face.

"Mr. Clarkson, well done!" Osborne exclaimed, excitement flooding him.

In his plan, as long as Bryce could hold off twenty enemies, he would stand a chance against the rest and escaped.

Now, with Bryce managing to take on twenty-six, that left Osborne facing just twenty-five!

His confidence surged.

Locking eyes with the twenty-five Class S clones, he growled,

"Did you enjoy that? Now it's my turn! You're all going to meet your end!"

As he charged in, twenty-five attacks converged on him.

But at that moment, Osborne felt absolutely unfazed.

Among the attackers were ten Medusa Queens, known for their petrifying powers but lacking in brute strength.

And that petrifying power?

It didn’t work on him now!

Osborne's martial art aura flared, absorbing all of their incoming attacks.

Though the ensuing attacks eventually broke through, they had lost much of its potency after the aura, barely leaving a mark on him.

Not to mention, Osborne still had the Crystal of Life replenishing his vitality, and he felt his wounds closing fast.

"Time to strike!"

With that battle cry, Osborne jumped into action, donning his Thundergod Gloves and gripping the Volt Hammer.

A tremendous burst of electricity crackled as he propelled himself into the thick of the fight, wielding the Volt Hammer like it was just any other hammer, bringing it down hard.

In a sound of thunder, he smashed the head of a Tauren King. The sound resonated with a resounding crack, and Osborne felt a jolt travel up his arm as the immense recoil flung him backward.

The Tauren King let out a tragic wail as it collapsed to the ground, its skull crushed.

Despite its formidable defenses and insurmountable tough skin, it was clear that Osborne's power was beyond anything that creature could hope to withstand.

"Damn it!"

Seeing one of them fall, the remaining Class S clones, consumed by rage, unleashed a barrage of attacks toward Osborne, breaking through his martial art aura and inflicting injuries upon him.

But it was futile!

For Osborne, he needed at least thirty Class S clones on him to feel any pressure.

But now, with only twenty-four left to face, it simply wasn't enough!

"Die, all of you!"

With a cold smirk, he transformed into a Vampire, drawing upon the blood of the last two Class S Vampire Kings in the crowd and swiftly dispatching them.

Then, with a roar, the Breath of Celestial erupted.

Osborne's power surged dramatically!

His strength multiplied again—at least eight times stronger than before.

No one could withstand his onslaught now.

With his martial art aura as a shield, Osborne barreled through the crowd, swinging the Volt Hammer down with relentless fury.

Each strike was lethal.

Whether it was a Tauren King, a Titan King, or a Wolf King, none could withstand his might; each fell to the ground with a single blow.

"Damn it!"

In the blink of an eye, ten Class S clones had died because of the power of his Volt Hammer.

The clumsy Titan Kings and Tauren Kings were dead like the Vampire Kings before them, leaving only ten Medusa Queens and five Wolf Kings standing.

Osborne moved like a god of war, annihilating them with ease.

The remaining fifteen Class S clones began to tremble, fear creeping into their eyes.

"Damn it!"

"Block him!"

They screamed in frantic desperation, but their attempts were utterly futile. One by one, they fell to Osborne's rampage, slaughtered like prey before the hunter.
