Chapter 1395 Rapid Recovery

With the last Intermediate Deity Deathborn defeated, an eerie silence enveloped the cavern.

Harry and Amelia collapsed to the ground, utterly exhausted. Vance and his five companions, along with over thirty Deathborns, lay lifeless around them.

The siblings exchanged a glance, and in each other's eyes, they saw the relief of having survived a harrowing ordeal.

They had won!

Against all odds, they had made it through this perilous situation and emerged as the last ones standing. It felt like a miracle—almost too good to be true.

Harry murmured, "When did our luck turn around like this?

"I thought we were always the unluckiest ones.

"Amelia, I still can't wrap my head around why those Deathborns suddenly stopped attacking us. Do you think it's really our father's spirit watching over us?"

Amelia remained silent.

Deep down, she thought, "Is it really just luck?

I don't buy it!

I'm starting to believe someone has been helping us from the shadows, but we just haven't found the proof yet."

Just then, her eyes narrowed as she noticed something astonishing.

After the Intermediate Deity Deathborn was slain, the high-grade death scent it left behind began to vanish rapidly, as if being absorbed by something unseen.

Her eyes widened in disbelief.

Amelia mused, "How is this possible? I've heard that even a Level Five Divine Body can't withstand the high-grade death scent; it would be easily corroded. What on earth is absorbing it?"

Amelia's mind raced with questions, but she kept her thoughts to herself.

As she scanned the area, she realized that the high-grade death scent left by the thirty-plus Deathborn was now completely gone.

"What's really going on here? Who's helping us from the shadows?" she wondered, biting her lip in confusion.

Meanwhile, Osborne was brimming with excitement. After the Intermediate Deity Deathborn fell, the high-grade death scent it left behind was far superior to that of the Junior Deity Deathborn.

The Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity began to absorb the high-grade death scent, and its roots surged with renewed vitality, quickly mending Osborne's injuries.

As he felt his strength returning, Osborne couldn't help but let out a joyful sound.

He murmured, "Finally, I'm feeling better!

"But it's still not enough!

"Right now, my power barely compares to a thousandth of what it was at my peak—just enough to match an ordinary Intermediate Deity!

"Taking on a regular Intermediate Deity would still be a challenge! But against the Intermediate Deity Deathborn? I should be fine! That means I can hunt more Deathborns and speed up the recovery of the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity!"

With that thought, Osborne grew impatient.

He glanced at Harry and Amelia.

"All the Deathborn within a twelve-mile radius have already been drawn to me. They should be safe for a while. I can leave them behind and venture deeper into the mine to regain my strength!"

Having made up his mind, Osborne set off.

Only with sufficient power could he survive in Celestara.

He had heard Vance's final threat: Patrick was Vance's sister's husband, and after Vance's death, Patrick would undoubtedly come looking for answers. It was very likely he would track down Harry and Amelia.

When that day came, it would spell a much greater crisis!

"Patrick is a Superior Deity and part of the Ninth Dark Deity Legion's reserves. This could get messy. I need to recover my strength quickly if I'm going to deal with him!" Osborne thought as he plunged deeper into the mine.

Here, he found an even greater abundance of Essence Crystals.

However, Osborne already had over 50 thousand low-grade Essence Crystals in his Dimensional Space, and until his injuries healed, those low-grade crystals were of no use to him. He didn't want to waste time collecting more.

If it were mid-grade or top-grade Essence Crystals, then he definitely couldn't miss out on them.

Thus, Osborne ignored the numerous Essence Crystals around him and headed straight for the deeper parts of the mine.

If his actions were known to others, they would probably scold him for being so extravagant.

It was extremely difficult for others to obtain Essence Crystals, yet Osborne turned a blind eye to them, even if they were right at his fingertips.

In this manner, Osborne quickly reached a point hundred miles deep into the mine.

Here, he finally picked up the trail of the Deathborn.

When not in action, the Deathborn existed in a completely soul-like state, fully integrated with their surroundings. Even with his Satan's Eye, Osborne found it difficult to detect them.

However, at this moment, he finally spotted an Intermediate Deity Deathborn nearby.

"This is it! Today, I must kill at least ten Intermediate Deity Deathborn and use their high-grade death scent to restore the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity!"

With this thought, Osborne immediately made his move.

Shortly after, Osborne successfully eliminated the Intermediate Deity Deathborn. After absorbing the high-grade death scent, the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity recovered significantly, and Osborne's injuries also healed somewhat, making him even stronger.

Osborne continued to search for and swiftly slaughter Intermediate Deity Deathborns, acquiring their high-grade death scent.

Soon, he had killed five of them, reaping a huge reward.

As he was preparing to search for the next one, his eyes suddenly lit up.
