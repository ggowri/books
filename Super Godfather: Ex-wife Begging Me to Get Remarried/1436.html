Chapter 1435 Ensnared

The panic in the crowd was palpable as the realization hit them.

Faces turned pale, and one by one, they fumbled for their communication devices, desperate to reach the outside world. But their attempts were futile.

Osborne watched them with a cold smile, making no move to stop their frantic efforts.

Their devices, far superior to any phone from Blue Planet, were designed to function in even the harshest environments, almost like divine artifacts capable of summoning help from anywhere.

Unfortunately for them, they were trapped within Osborne's Spatial Domain.

This Level Six Spatial Domain was intangible and profound, transforming their surroundings into an entirely different realm where all communication devices were rendered useless.

It was as if they were on Blue Planet, trying and failing to contact someone from Celestara—two worlds separated by an insurmountable divide.

Unless they could break through the barriers of these distinct spaces, they would remain cut off from one another.

And breaking Osborne's Level Six pinnacle Spatial Domain? Not even a combined effort from this group could achieve that.

Not even a Celestial Deity could manage it.

Only a Deity Overlord Warrior with divine power far exceeding Osborne's might stand a chance of forcibly breaking through.

But these people were neither Deity Overlords nor even Celestial Deities.

Aside from the already injured Jeffrey and Elroy, they had only four Transcendent Deities among them.

The rest were merely Superior Deities.

To them, Osborne's Spatial Domain was an unyielding prison. Even if he did nothing and allowed them to attack, they couldn't hope to chip away at his defenses.

They were already doomed; they just didn't know it yet.

Osborne chuckled, "Did you really think I'd come unprepared? None of you so-called 'noble' races are leaving this place unscathed today!"

The crowd exchanged worried glances, their expressions shifting from defiance to fear.

"What do you want from us, Lachlan? We don't know what trick you pulled to cut off our communication, but do you really think you're invincible? We're a united front, and you can't take us all on!

"Even if you think you can handle us together, if we split up, you won't catch any of us. If just one of us escapes, it's game over for you!"

Osborne's smile widened. "Escape? You really think you can get away?"

Their faces fell, dread creeping in.

In a sudden burst of determination, one of them charged off into the distance. Osborne merely watched, making no move to pursue.

The man slammed into an invisible barrier, bouncing back in shock. He didn't hesitate, immediately unleashing his divine power in a desperate attempt to shatter the unseen wall.

But it was all in vain.

The barrier was utterly indestructible; no amount of effort could even rattle it.

"What?!"

"Is this... Realm?"

"Why can't I sense any Realm fluctuations? Is this really a Level Six Realm?"

Shock rippled through the group as they processed the situation.

Osborne laughed, a sound rich with triumph. "Congratulations, you got it right!

"This is my Level Six Spatial Domain, and it's at its pinnacle! With your pathetic abilities, give yourselves a hundred years, and you still won't break through."

As Osborne finished speaking, the crowd stared at him, eyes wide with disbelief.

"Level Six pinnacle Realm? A Spatial Domain? No way! Lachlan only had Thunder Power! You're not Lachlan! You're an imposter! Who the hell are you?!"

Osborne smiled again, "Figured it out, did you? Too bad it's too late! Now, let's not waste any more time."

With that, he prepared to make his move.

"Don't you dare! No matter who you are, if you try to kill us, you're as good as dead!"

The crowd erupted in shouts.

Osborne sneered, saying, "Who said I was going to kill you? Keeping you alive is far more useful than ending your lives! From now on, you'll be my loyal subordinates!"

With a flick of his wrist, a terrifying surge of spatial power erupted around him.

It swirled like a prison, instantly binding everyone here. Superior Deities and Transcendent Deities alike found themselves utterly immobilized, unable to so much as twitch.

This was Osborne's Spatial Domain, where he reigned supreme. Here, his spatial power was magnified, rendering his captives helpless.

The group, even with their Transcendent Deities, stood no chance against the overwhelming force he commanded.

Every single one of them was ensnared, including Jeffrey, Elroy, and nearly a hundred members of the Wild Flame Army.

Fear shone in their eyes, but their bodies remained frozen, unable to respond.

Osborne murmured, "Welcome to your new fate—now you'll all become my Blood Clan progeny!"

With that, a drop of blood shot forth from his body, bursting into a dazzling spray that transformed into nearly a hundred beams of crimson light.

These lights surged into the bodies of the trapped individuals.

Osborne was forcibly converting them into Blood Clan members.

With his current power, he no longer needed their consent; he could transform them all against their will.
