Chapter 1440 One Point Five Billion

Osborne's mind raced with visions of the future, imagining if one day he could reach the Deity King realm, just how potent his Infinite Devouring Eyes would become. "Man, if I ever hit Deity King, these eyes will be like bottomless pits! Their devouring power—unreal! Maybe I could even clean up the whole Celestara, suck up all that death scent, and make the place normal again.

All the Celestara cultivators could ditch Essence Crystals and just soak up the ambient energy to level up."

His heart raced with excitement at the prospect.

Previously, Osborne had relied on extracting the core energy of the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity to expedite his progress, but it turned out even a Cosmic Treasure had finite resources. Once depleted, replenishment was a tall order.

In the deep space, had it not been for Osborne's excessive extraction, weakening the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity, their encounter with the root creature might have ended differently—better.

Neither he nor the Lotus would have been so gravely wounded.

Now, even if the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity fully recuperated, Osborne couldn't afford to sap its core energy again for quick gains.

This meant his advancement would slow down considerably—especially once he hit Transcendent Deity.

Each subsequent realm demanded immense energy, and relying solely on Essence Crystals would require a mountain of them.

Potentially years, even decades, just to inch forward.

Deity Overlord, even Deity King, could take centuries, maybe millennia.

Osborne pondered, feeling a knot of urgency, "Can Celestara's Human Community even hang on that long?

It should be tough!

I gotta hit Deity Overlord, even Deity King, asap if I wanna take down Lord Malevolence and support Isa and Celestara's homegrown folk.

If it takes centuries or millennia, they'll be toast by the time I get there!"

The advent of the Infinite Devouring Eyes finally eased some of this tension for Osborne.

"I'm gonna rocket to Deity Overlord, maybe Deity King, in no time. Before that, I need to quietly dismantle Lord Malevolence's Dark Deity Legion, re-wire them to be my Blood Clan, loyal only to me!"

With resolve renewed, Osborne sprang into action.

His next objective: stockpile as many top-grade Essence Crystals as possible.

While the Infinite Devouring Eyes ensured his ascent even without dedicated cultivation, those crystals would supercharge his progress.

Osborne was never one to turn down a speed boost in his journey.

So, he needed all the top-grade Essence Crystals he could lay his hands on.

At this very moment, Osborne activated his Satan's Eye in search of top-grade Essence Crystals, instantly feeling a stark improvement in its X-ray vision abilities.

Due to the formidable rock formations of Celestara, his previous vision had been heavily restricted, unable to peer too far, thus limiting his speed in locating Essence Crystals.

However, now, the efficacy of Satan's Eye's X-ray vision had soared—at least tenfold!

With a single sweeping glance, he could discern all the mid-grade and top-grade Essence Crystals lurking within the rock layers across a radius of six miles.

The revelation filled Osborne with elation.

If ordinary miners were to excavate such a six-mile expanse, it might take years, let alone in the deepest bowels of the mining area.

But for Osborne, it was merely a glance to spot every mid-grade and top-grade Essence Crystal within that range.

As for the low-grade ones, he had long since deemed them beneath his notice.

He promptly set into motion, employing spatial power to extract the identified crystals directly, depositing them into his Dimensional Space.

Thus, Osborne embarked on a whirlwind expedition, akin to a locust swarm, through the depths of the No. 9 mining area.

The only hitch was the paucity of top-grade Essence Crystals here; they were sparsely scattered.

Despite Satan's Eye's aid, three hours weren't enough to scour the entirety of the No. 9 mining area's deepest reaches.

But Osborne had to call it quits; the haul was already substantial.

Osborne had amassed over 50 thousand top-grade Essence Crystals and an astounding 10 million mid-grade ones.

He calculated the worth, drawing a sharp breath.

Fifty thousand top-grade crystals equated to 500 million low-grade ones, and 10 million mid-grade ones amounted to a billion low-grade crystals.

In total, that made for one point five billion low-grade worth—a staggering sum.

Lachlan's monthly military stipend was merely two thousand low-grade crystals.

The entire No. 9 mining area's monthly yield barely scraped 200 million, and the Wild Flame Army's fifty-hundred-thousand-strong contingent needed only 500 million monthly for expenses.
