Chapter 773 Defeating Emi Byrd

"If it's a fight you want, then let's do this!"

Osborne calmly stepped onto the stage.

Emi had been challenged several times before, easily defeating each opponent with her overwhelming strength.

She was ranked 499th on the Global Godson Rankings, making her the lowest-ranked Godson among the challengers.

Her ranking was precarious.

If a few more strong opponents emerged and were acknowledged by the Diviner League, she could be pushed off the Global Godson Rankings and lose her title as an Earth Godson.

Therefore, she had to keep challenging others to improve her rank.

Emi stared intensely at Osborne, her eyes filled with fierce battle intent.

"Osborne, you can still surrender now! Don't think that just because you beat Andrew with your Spatial Domain, you're on equal footing with us!

"Your Spatial Domain is useless against me."

Osborne raised an eyebrow. "Is that so? I'm looking forward to it."

"Hmph, you're asking for it!"

Seeing that Osborne had no intention of giving up, Emi instantly launched her attack, shrouding Osborne in her Dark Realm.

"Spatial Domain!"

Osborne didn't hesitate and immediately expanded his Spatial Domain.

The two domains collided in an instant.

Crack! Crack!

Osborne's Spatial Domain began to show signs of cracking under the pressure. He remained calm, using his powerful mental strength to hold it together.

"Hmph!

"Osborne, your mental strength is impressive. It compensates for the instability of your Spatial Domain! Andrew lost to you because he couldn't break through it!

"But I'm not like him! Do you think I'd challenge you without knowing how to break it?

"You're only at Class AA. Once I shatter your Spatial Domain, you'll be trash before me! Now, I'll crush it!"

Buzz!

"Let's destroy it together!"

Boom!

In an instant, Emi's Dark Realm exploded with tremendous power, slamming into Osborne's Spatial Domain. Even with his strong mental power, Osborne could no longer maintain the stability of his domain.

Pop!

Osborne's Spatial Domain shattered instantly.

The backlash from his domain's collapse caused Osborne's face to pale as his mental strength took a hit.

Fortunately, his mental strength was robust enough that the backlash wasn't enough to injure him.

In comparison, Emi was worse off.

She had intentionally shattered her own Dark Realm, causing both domains to collapse together. The mental backlash she suffered was far more severe than Osborne's.

Blood trickled from the corner of her mouth, her face as pale as a sheet.

Yet, her eyes gleamed with fierce determination.

She sneered. "Now I've lost my Dark Realm, and you've lost your Spatial Domain! But I'm Class AAA, and you're only AA!

"You've already lost!"

With that, Emi manipulated her powerful dark energy to attack Osborne. Even without her Dark Realm's amplification, her attacks were still terrifying.

But Osborne remained calm. Facing her dark energy, he responded with a single punch.

Boom!

The power from his punch shattered Emi's attack in an instant, sending the energy back at her, causing her face to pale as her body was forced backward.

"You?!"

Emi stared at Osborne in shock.

Osborne smiled faintly. "Why do you think that just because we've both lost our domains, you can definitely win? You underestimate Class AA?"

Emi's expression darkened.

"I don't believe it! Without your Spatial Domain, there's no way you can beat me! Come on, again!"

Emi became more serious.

She melded into the darkness, reappearing from Osborne's shadow, slashing at his neck with a sharp ancient artifact dagger.

Osborne had anticipated her move. There was no way she could catch him off guard. He dodged her strike and countered with a powerful punch, forcing her back.

Bang! Bang! Bang!

Boom!

The ensuing battle was intense. Even without their domains, both of them were top-tier fighters, unleashing wild bursts of energy.

Bam!

Finally, Osborne found an opening, breaking through Emi's defenses and sending her flying out of the ring.

Huff!

"Finally, I won!"

Osborne looked a bit exhausted.

The battle had been tough. Osborne, cautious of revealing his identity, had refrained from using many of his techniques, relying only on raw strength to fight Emi.

Though victorious in the end, he had used up a lot of energy.

"As expected, none of the fighters on the Global Godson Rankings are easy to deal with!"

With that thought, Osborne prepared to step off the stage.

But at that moment, another figure stood up, glaring coldly at Osborne.

"Axell, I'm challenging you next!"
