Chapter 1510 Training With Top-Grade Essence Crystal

Darren continued, "Currently, out of all the members of Spark of Blaze, only two hundred and fifty-five actually need Essence Crystals for cultivation. Even if everyone trains day and night, it's highly optimistic to expect that they could each consume 10 thousand mid-grade Essence Crystals per month. Mr. Lachlan Swift, this is indeed too much!"

Osborne chuckled at the remark. "In fact, it's not excessive at all. The mid-grade Essence Crystals in these bags are not for your training purposes but for emergencies.

"You see, as a squad leader of the Wild Flame Army, I'm soon to be promoted to centurion. Consequently, I'll be frequently away on missions and won't always be here.

"During my absences, other clans and powerful factions are bound to cause trouble. If the enemy's strength is overwhelming, you'll have to rely on these mid-grade Essence Crystals to activate the divine power shield for protection.

"The crystals are specifically reserved for this purpose. With nearly a million mid-grade Essence Crystals, even multiple Deity Overlord warriors attacking the shield simultaneously could be stalled for an extended period, long enough for me to return."

Betty and Darren exchanged a glance upon hearing this.

They nodded in understanding. "So that's the rationale behind it! Mr. Lachlan Swift, you have indeed thought of everything!"

Betty then added, "However, I think the members should still use mid-grade Essence Crystals for training. The speed at which one can train with mid-grade compared to low-grade Essence Crystals is vastly different."

Her voice carried a hint of pleading. "The quicker everyone improves, the stronger Spark of Blaze becomes, so..."

Osborne couldn't help but laugh, recognizing Betty's unintended misunderstanding.

He explained with a smile, "Why would they need to train with mid-grade Essence Crystals? I've already included 10 thousand top-grade Essence Crystals in the Dimensional Bag, which should suffice for a while, right? Given that we have top-grade Essence Crystals, why resort to mid-grade ones? You wouldn't think I'd have the members train with just low-grade crystals, would you? Ha! To be honest, I have plenty of mid-grade Essence Crystals, but not a single low-grade one."

"What?!"

"What?!"

Osborne's revelation stunned Betty and Darren, their eyes widening in disbelief as they stared at him.

Betty exclaimed, her voice filled with astonishment, "There are 10 thousand top-grade Essence Crystals in the Dimensional Bag?!"

"How on earth do you have top-grade Essence Crystals, Mr. Lachlan Swift?"

"Have you stumbled upon some ancient divine treasure trove?"

"In Wild Flame City, top-grade Essence Crystals are only within reach of the upper echelons living atop the floating mountain or the super-talented elites of dominant races."

Darren nodded in agreement. "Even in the East Zone, top-grade Essence Crystals are extremely rare. As a Deity Overlord warrior, I seldom had access to them."

"If our Spark of Blaze members, who are already highly talented, train with top-grade Essence Crystals, their growth rate will rival that of the super-talented elites of dominant races."

Darren sighed wistfully. "Sadly, my comrades' power essences have been destroyed, rendering them incapable of cultivation for life.

"Otherwise, their achievements would surely match those of the great races' elites."

Osborne smiled reassuringly. "Mr. Pollitt, there's no need to fret. I'll find a way to heal you all. Don't ask how—just trust me, I'm confident."

"It could be as short as three days or as long as a fortnight, but I promise to restore you all. By then, everyone can train with top-grade Essence Crystals."

Darren was taken aback.

"Is it truly possible to heal us?" he asked, his voice tinged with hope.

Osborne nodded firmly. "Absolutely, within at most two weeks. Just be patient. Believe me; I'll ensure every one of you recovers your strength. By then, no major faction in Wild Flame City will dare disrespect Spark of Blaze again!"

Osborne's eyes gleamed with unshakable confidence.

As long as he ventured into the wild and dispatched more Deathborns, the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity would devour enough high-grade death scent to fully recover.

Then, he could utilize the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity to heal Darren and the others completely.

Moreover, once the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity was fully restored, he could release the members of the Blood Demon from the Dimensional Microcosm to assist him!

Including Sebastian, Anastasia, Divine Shadow, and many more, they would all be freed from the confines of the Dimensional Microcosm!

After sending Betty and Darren on their way, Osborne began to contemplate.

"Spark Estate is protected by a divine power shield and bolstered by over a million mid-grade Essence Crystals. For the time being, its safety should not be an issue."

"My primary focus must now be on restoring the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity!"

"Tomorrow, I shall depart Wild Flame City and venture into the wild to hunt Deathborns, aiding the recovery of the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity. With my current strength, even encountering a Deity Overlord-level Deathborn poses no significant threat!"

"I must find the area teeming with the most Deathborns in order to expedite the recovery of the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity!"
