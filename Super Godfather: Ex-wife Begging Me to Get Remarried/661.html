Chapter 661 Killing Vampire

Osborne grabbed Vampire's bloodline. At that moment, he felt the immense power and life force within the blood.

It was as if he wasn't just holding blood but Vampire's life.

"So that's how it is."

Osborne's lips curled up.

He activated his Thunder Power. A streak of lightning shot into the blood, crackling loudly.

Vampire's body stiffened in the distance. He was surrounded by lightning, which scorched his entire body black.

But his life force was still incredibly strong.

"Looks like that's not enough!"

Osborne sneered and swiftly switched the lightning to flames.

Earlier, Vampire had used flames to burn Osborne's blood. Now, the tables had turned. It was Osborne's turn to burn Vampire.

"Ahh!!"

Vampire's body erupted in intense flames that couldn't be extinguished.

The only way to put out the flames was to extinguish the fire in Osborne's palm that was scorching his blood.

Unfortunately, Osborne's transformation into a Vampire made them feel he was facing the Blood Clan ancestor. He didn't even dare to look Osborne in the eye, let alone attack him.

"Ahhh!"

Vampire let out a horrified scream.

"Help me! Someone help me!"

He could only cry out for help.

"Hmph! Your life force is tough! Looks like lightning and fire won't kill you. Then try this!"

This time, Osborne activated the light elemental.

"Purify!"

The light elemental's power shone on Vampire's blood. Instantly, his body began to melt bizarrely, like ice under a scorching sun.

It melted from the inside out.

"No! No!"

Vampire roared in agony, but he couldn't stop his body from melting.

All his blood sprayed out, held in Osborne's hand. The light elemental purified the power within.

His body was also being purified.

His skin became dry and wrinkled. His eyes turned dull and lifeless. All his blood was purified, and his life force quickly faded.

Bang!

In an instant, Vampire's withered body collapsed.

Dead!

Osborne chuckled coldly.

Vampire's blood began to compress in Osborne's palm, quickly forming a beautiful blood pearl.

"You killed Vampire No. 8?

"How dare you!"

Medusa, Titan, and Werewolf watched helplessly as Vampire died. They were furious.

They exchanged glances.

"We can't hold back anymore! If we don't go all out, we won't just fail to kill him. We'll all die here!

"Let's use our ultimate moves!"

Medusa gritted her teeth.

Titan and Werewolf looked serious but nodded in agreement.

Tauren was dead!

Now, Vampire was dead, too!

Osborne was too unpredictable. If they didn't use their ultimate moves now, they'd be the next to die.

They stared at Osborne, their expressions grave.

Osborne took the opportunity to heal his injuries. He had endured too many attacks to kill Vampire, and his body was severely wounded.

He focused on the blood pearl in his palm. It was formed from Vampire's blood, containing all his power and energy.

After thinking for a moment, Osborne controlled his blood-red hair to pierce the blood pearl.

Instantly, the blood-red hair began to absorb the power of the blood pearl.

Osborne could feel the Blood Cross in his body frantically devouring the blood pearl's power, growing stronger and stronger.

Soon, the blood pearl was completely gone.

The power of the Blood Cross had become incredibly dense, as if it had overindulged. The overflow of power gave Osborne a significant boost, making his body feel immensely stronger.

Osborne felt a surge of joy.

At that moment, his Satan's Eye began to pulse wildly, sensing an immense danger.

"Osborne, die now!

"Forcing the three of us to use our ultimate moves to kill you, you should be proud!

"You'd die now!"

Medusa shrieked. Blood poured from her eyes as she shot a magma-like, scorching gaze directly at Osborne's body.

"Death, Ashes!" Medusa shouted.

Medusa roared furiously.

In an instant, Osborne felt his body freeze, as if he'd turned to stone. A searing force erupted from within him, threatening to turn him into molten rock and then ash.

Osborne's expression changed drastically.

"Death Ashes?

"This is Medusa's true ultimate move? The martial art aura can't defend against it at all!"

At that moment, Osborne's body was completely immobilized, with only his mind still active.

He felt like a stone burning from the inside, unable to stop until he turned to ash.
