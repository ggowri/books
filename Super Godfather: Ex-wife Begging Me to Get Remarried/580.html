Chapter 580 A Sense of Mutual Respect

Matthew gave a faint smile. "Why would you say that?

"Simply because Vincent, Owen, and Emma think you're no threat, while I see you as a major one. That's why I took action against you early.

"Is that reason enough for you?"

Osborne looked at Matthew in surprise, deeply eyeing him.

Matthew's frankness somehow made Osborne dislike him a little less.

"If I were Matthew, I would do the same," he thought to himself.

After all, using tactics against a rival was completely normal. It would be foolish to just watch a competitor grow stronger and eventually become a threat.

Such people weren't suited to be the head of the Augustine family.

"What kind of place is the dungeon? Why does everyone in the Augustine family fear it so much?"

Osborne suddenly asked.

Matthew laughed. "It seems you've accepted that I targeted you. That's good. Flexibility and resilience are true skills, and now I'm even more wary of you! If I don't completely break you now, you might become my biggest rival in the future."

Osborne rolled his eyes, ignoring him.

Matthew then continued lightly, "Do you know what solitary confinement is?"

Osborne nodded. "Of course."

Solitary confinement was a disciplinary measure within the military for errant soldiers.

Many soldiers were terrified of it.

Firstly, the conditions in solitary were harsh, often only 32 to 43 square feet, so cramped one couldn't stand upright or lie down flat, severely restricting movement.

Moreover, the intense feelings of hunger and profound loneliness were what made solitary confinement most dreadful. For humans, who were social animals, it was a severe mental torment.

Many have even suffered mental breakdowns because of it.

Matthew smiled faintly. "The Augustine family's dungeon is essentially a large solitary confinement cell! But don't think it's any less terrifying because it's bigger. In fact, our family's dungeon is even more horrifying than any solitary cell.

"Because it's a world of decay and silence! There, there is nothing but continuous psychological assaults!"

Osborne frowned upon hearing this.

"A world of decay and silence?

"Nothing but continuous psychological assaults?

"That does sound quite terrifying."

Matthew smiled faintly. "Once you experience it yourself, you'll understand. I hope you can withstand it. Just don't go mad. If that happens, the Augustine family would lose a talent."

"You're worried I can't handle it? Then why punish me for a whole month?"

Matthew shrugged, "Just want to test if you have any other tricks up your sleeve!

"If you can manage to get out early, you obviously have means. But if you're stuck there for a month, it means you're out of options. Even if you go mad, I won't be too upset."

Osborne snorted coldly. "You really do think of everything!"

"No choice. The Augustine family is a huge family. If I want to be the first heir and eventually lead the main family, I have to think ahead."

Matthew sighed. "Despite being Rosmandi's oldest family, the pressure on us is immense. Other nations and ancient families match or even exceed the Augustine family in strength.

"If I become the head of the Augustine family, I will make it the most powerful family in the world!"

With that, Matthew's eyes sparkled.

Osborne was surprised, having not expected Matthew to harbor such aspirations.

He suddenly felt that Matthew might indeed be the right person to lead the Augustine family.

He had the strength, vision, tactics, and aspirations!

Setting aside their rivalry, Osborne had to admit he respected Matthew.

Unfortunately, they were destined to be rivals.

Osborne had to become the first heir of the Augustine family and had to defeat Matthew and all the young members of the Augustine family.

"So, your ambitions are commendable," Osborne finally said, "but I must tell you, you won't become the first heir of the Augustine family!"

"Why not?"

Matthew asked in surprise, "Who do you think could become the first heir of the Augustine family?"

Osborne smiled faintly, pointing at himself.

"Me!"

Matthew looked even more surprised and then laughed.

"Not bad, you're as confident as I am. I was right to target you regardless of everything. It's definitely the right move!

"It's a pity you're too weak now. Otherwise, I'd really like to see you as my biggest threat and use more tactics against you. It would be so interesting."

Osborne rolled his eyes.

"Wait until I get out. I'll get my revenge! Whatever you do to me, I'll do right back at you!"

"I look forward to it!"

Matthew said with a smile, "A strong opponent makes me stronger, and only the strongest can lead the Augustine family to greater glory!

"I'll be waiting for you!"

With that, Matthew and Osborne high-fived as if making a pact, a sense of mutual respect in their expression.

"Let's go. We've reached the dungeon!

"First, experience the horrors of the dungeon! If you can't withstand the dungeon, you're not fit to be my rival."

With that, they reached a dark doorway.

Behind the door, Osborne sensed a dreadful presence.
