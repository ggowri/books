Chapter 1471 Thirty Million

Even Osborne was taken aback. "A starting bid of thirty million? What kind of slave is the auction house boasting about this time?

Could it really be a Deity Overlord warrior?

That seems unlikely!"

He quickly dismissed that thought. "If it were truly a Deity Overlord slave, the bidding would start higher than 30 million. So, what kind of slave could possibly justify such a staggering price?

Is it due to high status?

Exceptional beauty?

Outstanding talent?

Or something else entirely?"

Curiosity gnawed at him, especially since this slave was confirmed to have human blood.

"With a Human Community lineage, it might be worth taking the plunge just to see what kind of slave she is.

After all, Essence Crystals are just numbers to me. I wouldn't bat an eye at spending a few extra if it means satisfying my curiosity."

With that thought in mind, Osborne raised his paddle. "I bid 30 million!"

The moment he spoke, the once-noisy auction hall fell into an unsettling silence. Everyone turned to stare at him, shock plastered across their faces.

No one expected that anyone would actually bid on a slave with such limited information, especially at the absurd starting price of 30 million.

"Is he out of his mind?"

A few in the crowd stared at Osborne with mocking eyes.

"I think he's falling into a trap! That's what this slave market is banking on—luring in this clueless human. He's the type who impulsively buys any enslaved human, no matter the cost! The slave market knows that and has set this trap for him.

"Why else would they point out that this slave has half human blood?" one person murmured, analyzing the situation.

His words struck a chord, and murmurs of agreement rippled through the crowd. Osborne suddenly felt more like a target for their ridicule.

"Maybe it's not the slave market pulling the strings but rather the client trying to con this guy! You love bidding on human slaves, right? Rich, right? Well, here's a thirty-million-dollar human slave for you. What's your choice? Bid or lose her? If you don't buy her, she'll just be wiped out!"

Laughter erupted, echoing in the hall. "That actually makes sense! This slave might have been thrown in at the last moment, and surely only a Super VIP could pull that off."

"Too bad we're not Super VIPs! Otherwise, I'd be tempted to auction off a bunch of human slaves myself at a high price just to sell them to this guy!"

"Such a shame!"

The crowd continued to gossip, their whispers reaching Osborne's ears. Yet he remained unfazed.

While he, too, questioned whether this unexpected auction item was some kind of trick, it didn't bother him in the least. Thirty million Essence Crystals was a drop in the bucket for him.

Besides, this auction was about to close, and he hadn't spent a cent of his allotted 100 million Essence Crystals.

He wanted to secure his status as a Super VIP in the slave market, and buying this special hybrid slave was his golden ticket. In his mind, a reputable market wouldn't engage in such shady schemes.

So, with a calm expression, Osborne awaited the end of the auction, eager to find out what made this thirty-million-dollar hybrid human slave so extraordinary.

Finally, no one else stepped up to challenge his bid.

The auctioneer swiftly brought his hammer down. "Thirty million low-grade Essence Crystals, sold!"

Osborne turned to Tiana. "So, Ms. Tiana Hart, I guess I can call myself a Super VIP of the slave market now?"

Tiana nodded, then shook her head slightly. "You've spent an impressive total of 116 million Essence Crystals at our auction, which meets the criteria to become a Super VIP. Once the auction wraps up and the transactions are processed in the back, you'll officially receive your Super VIP badge."

Osborne inclined his head, rising from his seat. "In that case, let's head backstage. I'm curious to see just what kind of mysterious slave I bought for 30 million Essence Crystals."

Tiana smiled, "Oh, you won't be disappointed! But before we go, there's one last batch of items up for bid. This group is quite special—human slaves with a significant number involved, all commissioned by the Dark Deity Legion from the West Zone. Are you sure you don't want to take a look at this final offering before heading to the back?"

Osborne raised an eyebrow, intrigued.

"Human slaves? A large quantity? Commissioned by the Dark Deity Legion? Could it be..."

A thought began to form in his mind, and with a more serious expression, he sat back down.

"Alright, let's see what they have then!"

At that moment, the auctioneer cleared his throat, capturing the crowd's attention.

"Ladies and gentlemen, we've reached the final segment of our auction. Up next is the last batch of slaves for today—the stakes are high! These individuals are no ordinary slaves; they were once elite warriors from the human resistance in the West Zone, captured soldiers of the Dark Deity Legion. It took considerable effort to bring them in. However..."
