Chapter 761 Spatial Realm

Osborne's face went blank, caught off guard by Emma's bluntness.

He rubbed his nose awkwardly. "I'm not rejecting you."

Emma's eyes sparkled with mischief. "Great! Then have sex with me. I promise I'll leave you alone after that. Deal?"

She looked at him with a hopeful expression.

Osborne was stunned. In all his twenty-five years, he'd never met a woman who came on this strong.

Clarissa had always been shy and reserved.

Isa, on the other hand, was bold enough to initiate kisses and hugs, but if he ever tried to take it further, she'd knock him back in line.

She had her limits and made sure he knew them.

Then there was his ex-wife, Ophelia. She was way out of his league, wealthier, more powerful. She usually called the shots, except in the bedroom, where he took the lead.

As for Ophelia's mother, Evelyn, their one time together had been a weird mix of revenge and regret, and she'd just gone along with it, not really into it.

The only one who came close to Emma's level of boldness was Austin, who had been hell-bent on getting pregnant with his child.

She'd been relentless, chasing after him like her life depended on it.

But even Austin had never been this blunt.

Osborne couldn't deny that Emma's direct approach was having an effect on him.

He was a healthy, energetic guy, and here was a stunning woman practically throwing herself at him. It was hard to resist.

He swallowed, his voice hoarse. "Just once?"

Emma nodded enthusiastically, her face flushed. "Just once! You're way too much for me to handle more than that!"

Her words sent a jolt through him.

Without another word, he scooped her up and tossed her onto the bed.

"Emma, you're a real temptress, you know that?"

After speaking, Osborne leaned in, kissing Emma's neck and earlobes as his hands explored her delicate figure, his grip firm and insistent.

Emma's face flushed, her head tilting back as soft, melodic moans escaped her lips, driving Osborne's breath to quicken in response.

It didn't take long before both of them were stripped bare, facing each other.

Just as Osborne was about to take control, Emma suddenly flipped them over, pinning him beneath her.

Her face was flushed with a mix of excitement and mischief. "Let me take the lead this time," she whispered.

Before he could respond, she took charge, and soon the room was filled with the rhythm of their movements.

But thanks to Osborne's Spatial Prison, none of the sounds escaped the room.

Two hours later, Emma lay exhausted on Osborne's chest, breathing heavily.

Her fingers traced the lines of his strong chest, her eyes filled with a dreamy satisfaction.

"I think I should head back."

Osborne nodded, feeling drained, not physically but mentally, from maintaining the barrier to keep her sounds contained.

"Yeah, you should go."

Emma stood up, her eyes lingering on his well-built body as she playfully licked her lips. "You know, I've still got some energy left. How about another round?"

Osborne shot her a look, half amused, half warning.

"Okay, okay, I'm just teasing! It's not just about the sex. I really like you, Osborne. Anyway, keep up the good work."

She leaned down, kissed him on the forehead, then got dressed and left.

After she was gone, Osborne sat up and began to cultivate.

Strangely, after the sex with Emma, his mind felt sharper than ever, and his cultivation flowed effortlessly.

Those tricky concepts he'd struggled with before now seemed to unravel before him as if a light had been switched on.

"So that's how it works?" he murmured, suddenly sitting upright, his eyes shining with realization.

"Spatial Realm!" he whispered, and immediately, the space around him shifted, detaching from the main world as if he were in a Dimensional Space.

He could feel everything around him bending to his will. Energy from the void surged through him, making him feel invincible, like he could control everything with a mere thought.

With just a little effort, he found he could twist, stretch, and even collapse space at will.

In this spatial realm, Osborne felt like a god.

"So this is the power of a Spatial Realm? I've actually mastered this advanced technique!"

His eyes were filled with excitement.

The realm's power was beyond what he had imagined. Anyone who entered it would be at his mercy, their strength drained while his own surged.

He played with his newfound abilities, immensely satisfied. Although his domain wasn't yet unbreakable and could easily collapse when clashing with others, it still marked a significant leap in his strength.

But just as he was basking in his progress, he suddenly felt his previously sharp mind become sluggish.
