Chapter 1434 Your Pride

After all that, Osborne shot Jeffrey a cold smile.

In response, Jeffrey's expression hardened as he glared back, the air around him thickening with an intense divine power. He was ready for combat.

"Let's get this started!"

Excitement buzzed through the crowd.

"Mr. Alick, teach that arrogant human a lesson! Can you believe he thinks he can beat you with just one punch? That's laughable!"

Jeffrey huffed, "Oh, don't worry. He'll regret saying that."

With a sudden burst of speed, Jeffrey launched himself at Osborne.

"Lachlan, whatever gave you the audacity to act so high and mighty, you're about to pay the price! So get down!"

In the blink of an eye, Jeffrey was right in front of Osborne.

His fist, crackling with divine power, rocketed toward Osborne's chest.

The air trembled as a catastrophic force released between them.

This was the might of a Transcendent Deity warrior; back on Blue Planet, that punch would be enough to shatter a mountain and devastate half a city.

Here on Celestara, where the rocks were tougher—especially in the mines—everything began to quake as if the very tunnels would collapse under the strain.

The raw power sent waves of exhilaration through the onlookers.

"Lachlan is done for!"

"Mr. Alick clearly isn't holding back. He's not aiming for a fatal strike, but he's already giving it his all!"

"Lachlan's Malevolence Amulet probably won't survive this hit, or worse, it could leave him severely injured—like a dead dog lying there!"

The crowd buzzed with excitement at the thought.

But at that moment, Osborne was far from passive.

He stood there, unfazed by Jeffery's fierce attack. Even before his powers had fully returned to Superior Deity levels, he felt no fear.

To Osborne, the seemingly powerful Jeffrey was nothing to worry about.

Calmly, he raised his hand and punched back, matching Jeffrey's ferocity.

In an instant, their fists collided, producing a thunderous crash that echoed through the air, followed by the ominous sound of Jeffrey's Malevolence Amulet shattering. Then, a figure was sent reeling backward, blood spewing from his mouth as he came crashing down onto the rocks, collapsing and unable to get back up.

The surrounding crowd fell silent.

Those who had eagerly anticipated Osborne being knocked down by Jeffrey were left gape-mouthed, staring at Osborne, who stood completely still, utterly unfazed.

Then their eyes turned to Jeffrey, who lay on the ground, blood pooling around him.

They were stunned into silence.

It was unbelievable!

Jeffrey had been knocked away by Osborne's single punch!

At that moment, Jeffrey's centurion Malevolence Amulet had been pulverized in an instant, the powerful shield bursting apart, unable to protect its wielder from Osborne's devastating blow.

In just one punch, Osborne had decisively defeated Jeffrey.

A sharp intake of breath echoed through the crowd, shock and fear etched on every face.

Osborne, however, merely smiled, his expression calm. To him, injuring Jeffrey with a single punch was a trivial matter, hardly worth his time.

He said calmly, "I told you it would only take one punch. All that chatter was just a waste of my time. Now, do you believe me?"

His gaze swept over the onlookers, and an icy chill settled over them, a wave of dread washing through their hearts.

"Did you really think you could insult me, just because I'm from the so-called lowly human? You looked down on me, thinking you could trample my dignity without a second thought. How's that pride treating you now?"

With a glint of coldness in his eyes, Osborne took a step forward, his face a mask of disdain.

The crowd shrank back, their fear palpable.

One voice cried out in panic, "What do you think you're doing, Lachlan?!

"We're all soldiers of the Wild Flame Army—your comrades! Are you really going to attack us? You've already injured Mr. Alick, your direct superior! If the military discipline office finds out, you'll be locked up for sure!

"And let's not forget your lowly Human Community status—you could be executed on the spot!

"Stop right now!

"Otherwise, I'll report everything that happened here to the military discipline office! My uncle is a centurion there, and with just a flick of my finger, your life is over!

"Be smart and apologize. Maybe I'll let today's incident slide and keep it from the discipline office."

Osborne couldn't help but laugh at the threat.

"Report me to the discipline office? You think I'm afraid of that? Go ahead, try it."

His expression dripped with sarcasm, and the man's face shifted from pale to flushed with anger and fear.

"This is your doing! I'm contacting the discipline office right now!" he spat, his fingers flying to the communicator in his hand.

But the next moment, confusion and shock washed over his features.

"What's going on? Why can't I reach the discipline office?" he exclaimed, his voice trembling with panic.
