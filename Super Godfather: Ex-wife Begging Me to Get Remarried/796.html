Chapter 796 Fierce Battle

"That was just a warm-up. Did you really think you were that good?"

Cuthbert scoffed coldly.

"From now on, you won't get any chances. Think you can end my winning streak? You're not worthy.

"Come at me again!"

Cuthbert launched another attack but was still not using his Strength Realm. While Osborne's strength was considerable, it still fell short compared to his.

If not, it would've been Osborne stumbling back five steps, not him.

At this point, Cuthbert was confident he could still defeat Osborne without wasting too much energy.

He was still aiming for eighty consecutive wins.

Bang!

Osborne stood his ground. Though his power didn't match Cuthbert's, the gap wasn't huge. If Cuthbert didn't change his tactics, neither would he.

"If it's a head-to-head, so be it.

Bring it on!"

Bang!

Bam, bam, bam, bam!

On the stage, the two figures clashed fiercely. For every punch Osborne took, he returned one. He unleashed his Spiral Force, Ninefold Tide Crash, Point Blank Force, and even Deadly Force.

Osborne had mastered these advanced martial arts techniques to their extreme level.

Cuthbert wasn't far behind. Though his mastery of advanced techniques wasn't quite at Osborne's level, his superior strength kept Osborne on the defensive.

Osborne was forced to retreat continuously under his attacks.

But only Cuthbert knew how painful Osborne's strikes were. Each punch caused stinging pain throughout his body, almost unbearable.

"Damn it! His advanced techniques are even better than mine?

"And what is that move? His strength bypasses my defense and attacks my internal organs! If my body hadn't fused with that ancient artifact, I'd be gravely injured by now."

Cuthbert's expression darkened.

He had mastered techniques like Spiral Force, Ninefold Tide Crash, and Stretch Soft Force, all to proficiency level, but he hadn't learned Deadly Force.

Osborne's Deadly Force bypassed his defenses and directly struck his internal organs, causing him great discomfort.

"This kid's more troublesome than I thought. If this keeps up, I might be unable to beat him, and I'll waste too much strength. Even if I win, I'll lose the chance for eighty straight victories.

"I need to finish him off quickly!

"I can't waste any more time!"

With that in mind, Cuthbert was done waiting.

"Strength Realm!"

He unleashed his Strength Realm in full force.

Cuthbert's strength was amplified several times over inside his Strength Realm, while Osborne's was suppressed. With this shift, Osborne couldn't block his strikes.

Bang!

Their fists collided again, but this time Osborne felt like he had been hit by a freight train. He coughed up blood as his body flew backward.

Thud!

Osborne finally stopped at the edge of the stage, wiping the blood from his mouth. He looked at Cuthbert with a mocking grin.

"Couldn't hold back from using your Realm?

"I thought you were tougher than that. Couldn't resist using your Realm already?"

Cuthbert's face twisted in anger.

This was the first time he had used his Realm before his opponent. It was a sign of weakness, and hearing Osborne call it out infuriated him.

Hmph!

"I just didn't want to waste any more time with you! The sooner I beat you and get my fiftieth win, the sooner I can focus on tougher opponents.

"I'll hit sixty wins, then seventy, then eighty! I'll use that momentum to break through the AAA bottleneck and become an S-Class Supreme Overlord!

"And you're just a stepping stone! After today, you won't even be worthy to fight me again."

Cuthbert was brimming with confidence.

The power boost from his Realm made him feel invincible, as if no one could stand in his way.

"Go ahead, show me your Realm!

"I've heard you're one of the rare spatial metahumans. I've heard your Spatial Domain is tricky. In front of me, it's as fragile as glass!"

"I'll shatter it with one punch before you even get a chance to use it!"

Hearing this, Osborne smiled faintly.

"Is that so?

"Since you insist, I'll oblige! Do you think you can break my Realm with one punch? Give it a shot!"

With that, Osborne's thoughts shifted.

"Light Realm!"

Whoom!

In an instant, Osborne's body seemed to transform into a small sun, radiating an intense brightness. Wherever the light reached, it became part of his Realm.

Multicolored beams of light intertwined, creating a dazzling Light Realm that made everything feel like a vivid dream.

Light Realm!

Osborne knew his Spatial Domain wasn't stable enough yet and would be easily shattered by Cuthbert, making it useless in this fight.

That's why he chose to reveal his Light Realm instead.

"Let's fight!"
