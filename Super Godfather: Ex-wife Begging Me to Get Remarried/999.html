Chapter 999 Unbreakable

At that moment, even Greyson and Roy couldn't help but pause their attacks, their eyes wide with disbelief as they watched Osborne.

Nearly a thousand members of the Lambert family stood frozen in shock, witnessing the spectacle unfold before them.

They had seen Osborne obliterate over fifty Class AAA assassins from the Eityreia Organization with his Destruction Orbs and*Disintegration Rays, and now he had just driven his fist through the chest of a Class S assassin.

"What terrifying power!"

Everyone was awestruck by Osborne's sheer strength, and they began to ponder, "If he unleashes a punch on us, can we withstand it?"

Even Greyson and Roy had their doubts.

"Even the former Godfather would struggle to take down a Class S assassin in a single blow without a divine artifact, right?" Greyson murmured, his voice laced with disbelief.

Roy nodded in agreement, his expression mirroring Greyson's astonishment.

They looked at Osborne as if he were a monster, a young man barely twenty-five years old yet wielding power that seemed beyond comprehension.

Osborne was still just a Class AAA!

They couldn't fathom how formidable he would become once he broke through to Class S.

Perhaps he would be able to face off against the top Class S fighters on the Global Quasi-Divine Rankings head-on.

One thing was certain: Osborne's attack power would be nothing short of extraordinary!

Meanwhile, Osborne was contemplating how to test the defensive capabilities of his martial art aura.

In an instant, he stood beside Greyson and Roy, a cold smile appearing on his face as he focused on the two terrified Class S Eityreia assassins.

"Mr. Greyson Lambert, Sir Lambert, leave these two to me!"

The two exchanged glances, silently agreeing.

They had already gained the upper hand, and while they were confident they could defeat the two Class S assassins, it would take considerable time and likely result in injuries.

Since Osborne was stepping in, they wouldn't contest his decision.

With an air of calm confidence, Osborne began to stride toward the two Class S assassins, who were now visibly shaken.

"Osborne, are you really willing to make an enemy of the Eityreia Organization? I strongly advise you to back off. We serve Sin and Crimson; if you dare to kill us, they will not let you get away with it!"

Osborne's expression turned scornful at their words.

"I've already killed their daughter. Do you really think I'm afraid of taking out a couple of their underlings? Ridiculous!"

The two assassins exchanged a glance, determination flashing in their eyes as they prepared for what was to come.

"Osborne, we're not going down without a fight!"

"If you want to kill us, you'll pay the price!"

In an instant, they launched their attacks. Having witnessed Osborne's brutal efficiency in dispatching their comrade, they knew that defense alone wouldn't cut it.

They had to go on the offensive—fierce, relentless attacks were their only hope of buying time.

They could only hope Osborne's defenses weren't as insane as his attacks!

With their lives on the line, they poured everything they had into their assault, unleashing their most powerful techniques.

"Watch out, Godfather!"

"Get out of the way!"

Greyson and Roy shouted warnings to Osborne.

To them, he was still just a Class AAA, and while his offensive capabilities were mind-boggling, there was no way his defenses could match up.

The two Class S assassins were clearly going all out, and even Greyson and Roy felt the weight of their power. They believed Osborne must dodge.

But Osborne had other plans.

He was determined to test the limits of his martial art aura's defenses.

Standing his ground, he enveloped himself in the dark red aura, which now resembled a suit of armor.

"Let's see how strong this really is," Osborne muttered to himself.

In an instant, the two assassins launched their attacks at Osborne, striking against his martial art aura defenses.

A terrifying force erupted in an instant.

This was the ultimate move of two Class S Eityreia Organization assassins, a full-powered strike that even someone like Greyson or Roy would have to avoid or risk getting hurt.

Yet, Osborne simply absorbed it with his martial art aura.

The overwhelming force caused the aura to vibrate violently, but it didn't shatter. Osborne staggered back two steps, a slight pallor crossing his face, but he fully withstood their attacks.

Remarkably, his body showed no signs of injury whatsoever!

The sight left the two Class S Eityreia Organization assassins dumbfounded.

Not far away, Greyson and Roy were equally stunned.

They had been bracing themselves to rush in and save Osborne. Yet here he was, effortlessly blocking the attack without even a scratch.

Not even his martial art aura had broken!

The two sucked in a sharp breath, suddenly feeling like they had wasted decades of their lives. If Osborne could withstand the full force of two Class S Eityreia Organization assassins' most powerful attacks, then he could certainly handle whatever they threw at him.

This meant that if they didn't employ some special methods, they would likely struggle to break through Osborne's defenses!

"This is insane!"
