Chapter 687 Questioning

Osborne confirmed once again that there were no enemies in the entire mining area before he began to clean up the battlefield.

He began to gather the blood and energy on the battlefield.

During this period, the Blood Cross absorbed a large amount of blood and energy, becoming more and more powerful. Osborne felt that it seemed to be awakening.

However, this was a good thing for Osborne.

A large amount of blood power couldn't not only enhance the power of the Blood Cross, allowing him to gain more power when he transformed into a vampire, but the released power could also make Osborne's body stronger.

Osborne's soul of powers had long been integrated with his body, which meant that the stronger his body was, the stronger his supernatural powers would become.

In short, collecting a large amount of blood energy would bring him countless benefits.

After doing all this, Osborne looked at the two Marquis Titans.

They were not as huge as the Duke Titan but still more than 16 feet long. Even if their limbs were broken by Osborne, they still looked like a mountain of flesh when lying on the ground.

Osborne looked very small standing next to them.

They looked at Osborne with hatred in their eyes. One of them even detonated the power of his body, blowing his body into pieces. He was trying to commit suicide.

It seemed that he knew that if he didn't commit suicide, he would have to endure Osborne's crazy torture.

Unfortunately, his plan failed.

His physical injuries recovered quickly under Clarissa's divine healing.

Osborne looked at him with disdain.

"You're just a clone, but you're afraid of being tortured? You even want to commit suicide? Since you're afraid of being tortured, things will be easy!"

Osborne sneered and walked up to the other person.

Next, he began to torture the Marquis Titan with various methods. With Clarissa by his side, he was not worried that the Marquis Titan would die.

Every time his body was tortured beyond recognition by Osborne, Clarissa would perform divine healing and save him from the brink of death.

Then, he had to continue to endure the torture.

Under such circumstances, the Marquis Titan quickly collapsed.

"I say! I'll tell you whatever you want to know!"

"I just ask you to give me a quick death and stop torturing me!"

The Marquis Titan burst into tears.

Osborne smiled faintly, "Your willpower seemed not as strong as your body! I still have many ways to torture you, and you surrender?"

"In that case, I will let you go for now! But if your next answer does not satisfy me, or if I find you are lying, I promise to make you regret it!"

After saying that, Osborne began to ask questions.

"Tell me, what are you digging for in this mine? What is your real purpose?"

The Marquis Titan looked horrified, and a look of struggle appeared when he heard this.

"We... We just want the rare metals here. Various rare metals, such as lithium, beryllium, tantalum, niobium."

Osborne sneered when he heard this.

"It seems that you are not honest at all. Do you dare to lie to me? If so, then you can continue to endure the torture!"

After saying that, Osborne was ready to continue.

"No! I'm not lying! Everything I said is true! Let me go and don't torture me anymore. Everything I said is true."

The Marquis Titan pleaded loudly, extremely terrified.

Unfortunately, Osborne was not merciful at all.

He pointed at Clarissa and said, "You may not know, but my girlfriend has the power, to discern lies! She can tell at a glance whether you are lying or not."

"Clarissa, was he telling the truth just now?"

Clarissa shook her head, "He lied!"

Osborne nodded and sneered at the Marquis Titan, "Since you are not honest, continue to endure the torture!"

After saying this, he ignited the flames and set the Marquis Titan's body on fire.

The burning sensation made the Marquis Titan scream miserably and roll around on the ground madly, but he couldn't put out the flames on his body.

Osborne looked at the other Marquis Titan.

He smiled faintly and said, "Now, let me ask you a question. You'd better not lie! Otherwise, I guarantee that you will suffer endless torture like him."

The Marquis Titan looked horrified and nodded.

"I say!"

"Our real goal is not those ordinary rare metals. Our goal is something called Originium! This Originium currently only exists in a few provinces along the coast of Rosmandi."

"Our mission is to dig up as much Originium as possible in the shortest possible time, and then bring it back to the King of the Aberrants!"

After he finished speaking, Osborne looked at Clarissa.

Clarissa nodded, " Osborne, he didn't lie!"

"No lies?"

Osborne frowned. Since the Marquis Titan did not lie, their real purpose was the Originium.

What is Originium?

What's the use?

Osborne had never heard of it!

He thought for a moment and said, "Where is the Originium? Take me to see it!"
