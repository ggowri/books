Chapter 611 The Gambling Fight Continues

Hearing this, Everett glared at Godfather angrily.

"You're shady, Sebastian. Did you already know that his light elemental power was given by Ms. Night?"

Godfather smiled faintly, "I was just guessing.

"I believe she's the only one bold enough to disregard the League of Divine Descent's agreement."

Everett's expression shifted.

He snorted coldly and murmured, "Times have changed. Right now, we, the League of Divine Descent, are giving her face, but that may not last.

"We'll see!"

With that, Everett erupted in a burst of dazzling light, rapidly healing his injuries.

Godfather observed calmly, with a hint of a mocking smile.

Everett scoffed, "Sebastian, keep today's events to yourself. If you upset me, I'll ally with the Aberrants."

Godfather frowned but replied calmly, "Don't worry. I have no interest in sharing this, and Ms. Night prefers silence, too."

"That's better!"

Everett sighed in relief. "But don't get complacent. There's still no clear winner in this betting match. Even if I don't pursue that kid for blasphemy, your Augustine family might not prevail!

"Don't forget, the Rosenthal family still has two spots left to fight.

"You'll all be consumed.

"Get the divine artifacts ready!"

With that, Everett ignored Godfather and quickly left the sky.

Meanwhile, in Arena Number One, the Augustine family anxiously awaited Godfather's return, knowing the outcome of his battle with Everett would determine their future.

At that moment, both returned simultaneously, taking their seats.

The crowd eagerly looked to them for the results, but their expressions revealed nothing.

"Mr. Rosenthal..." a few from the Rosenthal family murmured.

Before they could finish, Everett coldly interrupted, "The betting fight continues. Sene lost the previous battle."

He glanced at Osborne, coldly dismissing the situation.

His declaration stunned the Rosenthal crowd.

"Mr. Rosenthal, are you not going to hold him accountable? He privately possesses light elemental power. It is blasphemy against the gods. How can you let him go?" someone said loudly.

The Augustine family members were equally shocked. The gambling fight continued? Randall would not pursue Osborne's crime of blasphemy against the gods? Would he not take Osborne to the Mount of Gods to be executed?

What's happening?

"Did Godfather and Everett strike a deal?" they speculated.

Everett snorted, "I just learned that the League of Divine Descent has canceled Divine Accord from decades ago. Light elemental power is no longer exclusive to the League.

"The Gods are benevolent. Everyone has the right to this power.

"Therefore, he is not guilty."

With difficulty, Everett closed his eyes.

The crowd was stunned. Divine Accord made decades ago was canceled? The light elemental power was no longer exclusive to the League of Divine Descent?

Everyone could possess it, and it wasn't considered blasphemy against the gods?

Was Osborne not guilty?

The Augustine family looked at Osborne, relieved by his unexpected fortune.

The League of Divine Descent wouldn't wage war against them over this.

Osborne was surprised. Was Divine Accord canceled? Was his possession of light elemental power now acceptable? Was he not guilty?

He frowned, sensing something was off.

Could such an important agreement be canceled just like that?

Moreover, even Everett didn't know the news in advance.

He thought, "Things can't be this coincidental. Did Godfather make a deal with the League of Divine Descent to protect me?"

Osborne bit his lip, but he had no time to ponder as the betting fight was about to resume.

Having defeated Sene, only two spots remained for the Rosenthal family.

If Osborne could defeat two more opponents, he could turn the tide.

Among the Rosenthal crowd, a woman stepped forward, glaring at Osborne. She couldn't accept that a lowly person like Osborne had light elemental power.

It should only be possessed by the noble Divine Descent like them.

"Osborne, don't get too comfortable.

"Even without Divine Accord, we, the Divine Descents, won't tolerate a lowly person like you possessing light elemental power. People like you are heretics and should be eradicated.

"Now, I will defeat you.

"Your light elemental power won't save you because only we, the Divine Descents, can wield true light power.

"Remember my name, Melanie Rosenthal.

"I'm ranked fifteenth among the Godsons of Yalynch, and I will be the one to defeat you!"

After saying that, Melanie knelt, raising her hands high.

With a devout expression, she called out, "Light, grant me strength!"
