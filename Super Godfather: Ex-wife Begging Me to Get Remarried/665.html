Chapter 665 Gathering Blood Energy

Medusa, who was frantically fleeing, suddenly felt an extreme danger. But she was too weak to dodge it.

She could only watch as the space around her shattered, collapsed inward, and unleashed a terrifying force that shattered her body, collapsing into a singularity and disappearing.

From start to finish, she didn't even have time to scream.

The force of the Spatial Collapse was so powerful that even at her peak, she would have died if she hadn't escaped the core area in time.

At the same time, the immense force of the Spatial Collapse restored the surrounding frozen space back to normal.

"One down!"

Osborne sneered as he looked in the direction of Titan.

In the next second, he waved his hand to open a dimensional rift.

"Clarissa, wait for me here!"

After saying this, Osborne stepped into the rift and appeared before Titan.

"Die!"

Osborne took a deep breath and drained the air within a 328-foot radius. He used the Breath of Celestial to increase the flow, then punched Titan.

Bang!

Titan, towering at 33 feet tall, was instantly shattered by Osborne's punch.

He was dead!

Finally, only the fastest runner, Werewolf, was left.

He sprinted away on all fours, fleeing rapidly. In no time, he had escaped several miles away, his speed leaving only a blur.

Unfortunately, Osborne had Satan's Eye to lock onto him and spatial power to catch up quickly.

"Do you think you can escape?"

Osborne tore open another rift with a cold laugh and stepped through it. In the next second, he appeared in front of Werewolf.

Werewolf was terrified and immediately turned to change direction.

"Just a futile struggle!"

Osborne intercepted him again by warping through dimensions. This time, Osborne had already launched his attack before Werewolf could turn.

"Die!"

Osborne punched Werewolf's waist, instantly breaking his spine. His body folded over, rolling on the ground several times before stopping.

Even so, Werewolf was still alive.

He coughed up blood violently and glared at Osborne with hatred. "Osborne, you won't have a good end! The King of The Aberrants will avenge us!

"I'll be waiting for you in hell!"

After saying this, Werewolf spat out blood mixed with bits of his organs. His head drooped forever.

He was dead!

Osborne let out a long breath and finally relaxed completely.

Tauren.

Vampire.

Medusa.

Titan.

Werewolf.

Five AAA-level powerhouses from the Aberrants had surrounded and tried to kill him. Now, they were all dead by his hand. No one would believe this if it were told.

He was just Class AA.

Even encountering one AAA-level enemy, surviving would have been a miracle, let alone counterattacking and killing them.

Osborne faced five AAA-level opponents. He not only survived but also killed all five. The battles were extremely difficult and dangerous. Several times, Osborne was on the verge of collapse and almost died.

If he hadn't learned the Deadly Force, which perfectly countered Tauren.

If he hadn't had the Blood Cross, he could have transformed into a Blood Clan member, perfectly countering Vampire.

If he hadn't had the Light of Redemption, which could cleanse negative effects, instantly heal his injuries and restore his strength.

If Clarissa hadn't shown up in time...

He would have been dead already.

Fortunately, the ending was good. After killing the five AAA-level powerhouses, Osborne had a clearer understanding of his own strength.

At this moment, Osborne glanced at Werewolf's corpse and suddenly had an idea.

He could feel the dense blood energy around him.

This blood energy contained immense power.

After transforming into Vampire, Osborne became incredibly sensitive to this blood energy. He hadn't paid much attention before. After fighting Vampire, he realized controlling blood energy was Vampire's greatest power.

As long as he controlled the enemy's blood, he could control their life.

Even if the enemy died, the immense energy within their blood could still be used by him.

Osborne waved his hand slightly, and countless blood streams sprayed from Werewolf's body. The blood and scattered blood around him formed into a blood sword that Osborne grasped in his palm.

Werewolf's body instantly withered.

Osborne looked at the swirling blood energy in his hand. With a slight clench of his fist, the blood energy compressed into a bright red blood pearl.

This blood pearl was filled with immense power.

Osborne didn't hesitate. His blood-red hair pierced the blood pearl. Within a few breaths, the blood pearl disappeared, and all its power was absorbed.

Osborne could feel the Blood Cross within his body becoming more active. It seemed to be slowly awakening more power, providing Osborne with greater strength.
