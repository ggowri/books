Chapter 965 Instant Kill of Six Class S Clones

Osborne knew he had only one shot to avoid being overwhelmed.

Once he struck, he had to make a quick getaway.

To maximize his impact on the Class S clones, he had to unleash his most potent attacks in the shortest time possible.

He mentally organized his arsenal, readying all the techniques that could pose a serious threat to the Class S clones.

As the moment to strike approached, he steeled himself.

"Here goes nothing!" Osborne thought, and then he unleashed his onslaught.

"Light Realm! Destruction Orbs! Disintegration Rays!"

In an instant, the vast expanse of the Light Realm enveloped not just the eight Class S clones but also thousands of ordinary clones.

The battlefield erupted in chaos as countless Destruction Orbs and Disintegration Rays burst forth.

While these radiant attacks wouldn't deal fatal damage to the Class S clones, they would certainly confuse them and swiftly eliminate regular clones.

But Osborne's true killing blow was yet to come.

He thought, "Goddess of Grain's Scythe!

Vampire Transformation!

Breath of Celestial!

Explosive Force!

Come on!"

With the clones disoriented, Osborne seized the moment. Armed with the Goddess of Grain's scythe, he materialized behind two Class S clones, swinging the scythe with all his might.

In a heartbeat, their heads flew off, their bodies collapsing before they even had time to react.

But he wasn't done yet. With a powerful kick, he unleashed the strength of his Divine Bone, propelling himself forward.

His leg connected with the back of another Class S clone, sending it crashing into its comrade, resulting in a thunderous explosion.

Two more Class S clones fell.

Just then, the remaining four Class S clones finally snapped to attention, their fury igniting as they charged at Osborne. Meanwhile, his Light Realm was under siege, crumbling under a barrage of attacks.

Without a moment's hesitation, Osborne faced the four approaching clones, a cold smile creeping across his face.

He recognized them: two were Tauren Kings, and two were Vampire Kings.

He had intentionally left these foes standing.

The Tauren Kings boasted formidable defenses, and Osborne knew he couldn't take them down in one swift move.

As for the Vampire Kings, he had a distinct advantage over them.

"Time to die!" Osborne shouted.

With a flick of his wrist, he manipulated the blood within the bodies of the two Vampire Kings. Before they even realized what was happening, their bodies exploded in a gruesome display.

At that moment, Osborne's Light Realm shattered completely.

Thousands of clones, filled with murderous intent, turned their attention to him, launching a relentless wave of attacks. Osborne felt his heart race as Satan's Eye pulsed wildly.

"I can't stay here any longer! I have to get out!" Osborne thought, not daring to hesitate even for a second.

He made a hasty retreat.

The very spot he had just vacated was instantly engulfed by a torrent of attacks.

Had he lingered even a moment longer, he would have either been killed or severely injured.

With a burst of speed, Osborne dashed into the ranks of the Blood Demon members.

Relief washed over him as he saw their faces light up with encouragement.

They cheered, their spirits lifting at his arrival. They quickly sprang into action, shielding him from the onslaught of attacks.

Finally, Osborne exhaled, a wave of relief flooding over him.

"I have made it back!"

Just then, Sebastian appeared at his side. Though his face was a bit pale, he wore a grin that spoke volumes.

"How many did you take down?" Sebastian asked.

Osborne, puffing out his chest with pride, waggled six fingers in front of Sebastian. "Six! I managed to take out six of them!"

Sebastian raised an eyebrow, clearly impressed.

Osborne was only Class AAA, yet he had just obliterated six Class S clones in an instant. These weren't run-of-the-mill fighters; although they lacked mastery of the Realm, their strength was still nothing to sneeze at.

Sebastian had anticipated that Osborne might take out one or two, but six? Now that was something else entirely.

"Impressive! If you keep growing like this, it won't be long before nobody dares to mess with the Augustine family!" Sebastian chuckled, pride swelling in his chest.

Feeling a bit cocky, Osborne then asked, "What about you, Grandpa? How many did you take down?"

Sebastian smirked. "Eight. Wiped them all out!"

"Eight?! With your injuries?" Osborne thought, momentarily stunned.

He had thought he'd done well with six, but compared to his grandfather, he still had a long way to go.

"Alright, time to turn the tables!" Sebastian announced.

They had just taken out fourteen out of sixteen Class S clones, leaving only the two Tauren Kings standing.

Not to mention, Osborne had also managed to wipe out several hundred ordinary clones in the chaos. The Aberrants' army was already starting to falter, their ranks disorganized and confused.

The momentum of the battle had shifted dramatically.

Osborne couldn't help but let a cold smile creep across his face. "Listen up, everyone in Blood Demon!

"Follow my lead!

"Let's wipe them out!"
