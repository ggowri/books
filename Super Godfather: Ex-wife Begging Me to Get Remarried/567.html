Chapter 567 It's Over!

The raging purple-gold lightning finally subsided, leaving behind a colossal crater where Rodney once stood.

The pit was charred black by the lightning, with streams of molten magma seeping out from its depths.

At the very bottom of the pit lay a tall, charred figure. Its body was in tatters and covered with ghastly, scorched wounds.

Yet, it still breathed!

"Mr. Rodney Augustine!"

The members of the branch bloodline from Norlend cried out in alarm and rushed over frantically, lifting Rodney, who had fainted and was barely breathing.

They glared at Osborne with fury. Their eyes were burning with a desire to devour him.

But at the same time, fear flickered within their gaze.

Osborne not only possessed mutated Thunder Power but had also mastered the intermediate application—Thunder Explosion. With a single move, he had gravely injured Rodney.

Had Rodney not prepared a fourth-level defensive ancient magic weapon and activated it in time, he would likely be dead now. His body might be blown to pieces by the horrific Thunder Explosion.

At this moment, everyone from Norlend loathed and feared Osborne.

Rodney had made extensive preparations for this Branch Bloodline Competition, aiming to stand out and become the prospective heir of the Augustine family.

If he succeeded, the entire branch bloodline from Norlend would be immensely honored.

But now, everything had been ruined by Osborne.

Rodney's injuries were so severe that he had fainted. He was eliminated and withdrew from the competition.

However, the competition continued.

At that moment, Osborne moved with a suddenness that caught everyone off guard. His blood-red hair surged forth like a tide, swiftly entangling a member from Norlend.

This person was holding the Forbidden Space Disk in his hand!

Concerned about Rodney's condition, the man never expected Osborne to launch a surprise attack, let alone that Osborne's assault would be so peculiar.

In the blink of an eye, Osborne employed his blood-red hair to ensnare the man tightly.

The Forbidden Space Disk in his hand was also wrapped in the blood-red hair and quickly returned to Osborne.

With the Forbidden Space Disk in his grasp, Osborne showed a hearty smile.

"It's over!"

Osborne murmured, "I declare that the battle for the heir-to-be is now concluded."

With that, Osborne dispelled the power on the Forbidden Space Disk.

In an instant, the frozen space returned to normal. Osborne could even sense the spatial fluctuations clearly.

Waving his hand, he suddenly felt dense space cracks appear around him.

Osborne casually tossed the Forbidden Space Disk into the Dimensional Space.

"Done!"

Witnessing this scene, the onlookers shared the same thought. The only device capable of restraining Osborne's spatial power was now beyond their reach.

The frozen space was restored, so Osborne could depart at will, unhindered.

Sam looked terrifyingly gloomy.

Sam clenched his fists. His gaze upon Osborne was one of a predator denied its prey.

Osborne met his gaze without flinching.

He smiled faintly and said, "Sam, do you still wish to continue this fight?"

Sam snorted but said nothing.

He thought, "Continue fighting?

How?

Osborne possessed spatial power. If victory eluded him, Osborne could escape at any moment. The most critical point was that even without his spatial prowess, Osborne was not a foe I could easily overcome.

His Thunder Explosion posed a grave threat.

If his target had been me instead of Rodney...

Even with my robust defenses, it was uncertain whether I could stand unscathed.

So, how could I fight under such circumstances?"

Sam's eyes reddened as he helplessly stared at the Heir's Emblem adorning Osborne's chest. Though Sam had allies at his side, they were rendered useless in the face of Osborne's prowess.

Sam closed his eyes, finally conceding.

"It seems you have no desire to continue, so I shall take my leave."

Osborne smiled faintly and walked out.

His departure was unimpeded by the seething crowd who dared not act without Sam's command. They parted reluctantly, allowing Osborne to depart step by step.

"Osborne!"

Just as Osborne was about to vanish, Sam's voice rang out.

Osborne turned, a faint smile playing on his lips.

"What? Do you regret your decision? Do you still wish to fight against me? I am unopposed. I stand ready to engage you at any time."

Sam felt ridiculous at these words.

He snorted, "Osborne, do not be too arrogant! Today, I can admit defeat, but this is not the end! You have stolen my position as the heir-to-be, so I will not rest until I reclaim what is rightfully mine!

"Moreover, you have killed David and gravely injured Rodney. The branch bloodline from Ransford and Norlend will revenge what you did!

"Most importantly, even if you ascend to the position of quasi-heir, what good will it do you? The strength of your branch in Dampston is too feeble! Even if you become the heir-to-be, you stand no chance against the other five contenders. In the end, your aspirations will come to nothing!

"If you are wise, you should renounce your claim to the heir-to-be and surrender the emblem to me! Only then can I promise to absolve you of your troubles!

"The branch bloodline of Marcelin possesses the power to make this promise a reality!"
