Once Anastasia and the others had dispersed, only Sebastian, Divine Shadow, and God of Star remained.

The three approached Osborne to congratulate him, their expressions warm with admiration.

Osborne couldn't help but chuckle as he watched Sebastian and Divine Shadow holding hands.

He found their affection amusing but chose to keep his thoughts to himself, turning his attention to the God of Star.

With a flicker of intent, he used the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity to send a message to Darren.

Osborne was well aware of Darren's feelings for God of Star. In fact, Darren had sought his approval before pursuing her.

Naturally, Osborne had been supportive.

However, seeing that Darren had yet to succeed made Osborne a bit anxious for him.

"So, God of Star, spending time alone these days? It must be tough for you, watching my grandpa and Grandma Divine Shadow flaunt their love every day," he said with a teasing smile.

God of Star rolled her eyes in response.

Sebastian and Divine Shadow exchanged exasperated glances, with Sebastian playfully scolding, "You little rascal, looking for trouble? You dare to tease your grandpa like that!"

Osborne laughed heartily. "Come on, Grandpa, it's not all bad! I actually hope you two have a few more kids! Hope Haven could use a little more chaos!"

At that, both Sebastian and Divine Shadow turned bright red.

Seizing the moment, God of Star chimed in with a grin, "I heard your grandpa and Divine Shadow were even discussing having another one! You might just end up with an uncle soon!"

Osborne's eyes widened in delight. "That would be amazing!"

Sebastian sighed in resignation, saying, "Enough of this! Divine Shadow, let's head back to our training!"

God of Star smirked. "Oh, you mean back to making babies? Just keep it down. There are plenty of kids around!"

The two adults were left speechless, but just then, Darren finally arrived.

Upon seeing him, God of Star instinctively turned to leave.

But Sebastian and Divine Shadow quickly stepped in front of her, blocking her path.

"Where do you think you're going, God of Star?" Divine Shadow asked playfully.

God of Star sighed, feeling trapped.

Darren approached, bowing respectfully to Osborne first. As a reformed member of the Blood Clan, Osborne was always his top priority.

Then, he turned his gentle gaze toward the God of Star. "Star, how have you been lately?"

God of Star bit her lip and nodded, but the words seemed to stick in her throat.

Darren stood there, looking like a deer caught in headlights, simply grinning at her like a fool.

Osborne shook his head, realizing that if he didn't intervene, Darren and God of Star would never find a way to connect.

"God of Star, I'm guessing your concern is about Darren's Blood Clan background, right? Well, I can sever my ties with him after a while!"

God of Star blinked, taken aback. "Sever your ties with Darren?"

Osborne nodded confidently.

He had already achieved an impressive level of mastery with the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity, completing eight layers of fusion. This mastery was crucial for managing the Blood Clan transformations.

Once he reached ten layers of fusion, he would essentially become one with the Primordial Crimson Lotus of Eternity, gaining full control over its powers.

At that point, he could easily sever the connection with Darren.

In fact, even reaching the ninth layer would suffice.

Although all the members of Blood Demon were transformed Blood Clan members, they held a different significance for Osborne.

He considered them friends and allies rather than mere subjects.

In the future, he planned to give Blood Demon members the option to choose whether they wanted to sever their ties with him, allowing them to regain their freedom.

"Since God of Star is concerned about Darren's Blood Clan status, I can simply restore Darren's freedom," Osborne thought.

But as soon as he mentioned it, Darren couldn't help but panic.

"My Lord..."

Osborne quickly cut him off. "Darren, hold on! Don't say a word! Transforming you into a Blood Clan member was a last resort. With my strength back then, there was no other way to save you and restore your damaged divine power essence!

"If you really like God of Star and want to be with her, I'm all in! Restoring your freedom has been on my mind for a while, and I won't let you refuse! Even after you regain your freedom, we'll still be friends and comrades, right?"

With a warm smile, he turned to the God of Star.

Her expression shifted as she processed his words. She was indeed struggling with Darren's Blood Clan identity, knowing just how much control Osborne had over the situation.

But the thought of Darren regaining his freedom changed everything.

Deep down, she had feelings for him, too.

With a determined glint in her eye, she asked, "Can you really sever the ties and let him be free?"

Osborne nodded, his smile unwavering. "Just give me a little more time, and it'll happen soon!"

God of Star's face brightened. "Alright! The day he regains his freedom, I... I will..."

Before she could finish her sentence, she turned and dashed away, leaving everyone in stunned silence.

But her intentions were clear.

Osborne chuckled, looking at Darren. "Congratulations, Darren! Just hang tight a bit longer. It won't be long now!"

With a stretch, he added, "Now, I need to get back to hunting for that Miracle Elixir!"
