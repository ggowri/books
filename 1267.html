"Kill him!"

Instead, he found himself besieged!

The Augustine family had fifty members who had all broken through to become deities, each boasting a Level Three Divine Body! The combined power of these fighters was overwhelming, and he was struggling to hold his ground.

Before long, his divine power defenses were shattered.

With fifty foes attacking Joseph simultaneously, his defenses were bound to falter. Despite his formidable abilities, he couldn't withstand the relentless onslaught forever.

Wounds began to mar his Divine Body, and panic started to creep in.

"Am I really going to die here?"

The thought sent a chill down his spine.

"Run! I have to get out of here! Even if I just bring back news of this battle, it'll still be a significant achievement! And if I can escape, I can call for help! He had abandoned any notion of claiming all the glory for himself; survival was his only priority now. Joseph fully focused on defense while desperately seeking an escape route.

"He's trying to get away! Stop him!"

Sebastian's face hardened. He poured every ounce of his strength into stopping Joseph from fleeing.

But even with fifty fighters, they were no match for Joseph's determination. If he was willing to risk injury to escape, they couldn't hold him back. Their coordination faltered, and Joseph quickly found a weakness in their formation, bursting through the encirclement, bloodied but relentless.

His speed was beyond their reach.

"Damn you, Augustine! I won't forget this! You'd better watch your backs, because I'll be back, and next time, it'll be your end!"

Joseph's furious voice echoed in the air, but his figure quickly vanished from sight.

"Don't chase him! It's pointless!"

More importantly, he had pieced together their secret!

The worst scenario had indeed unfolded—the arrival of not one but five Superior Deities.
