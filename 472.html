"They wanted to stop me from killing Alexander and even tried to snatch Clarissa's divine artifacts. I had to teach them a lesson, so yes, I suppose I have offended them."

"Osborne, aren't you worried?"

Compared to the uncertain timing of reprisal for offending Evelynn, Charlie, and others, Osborne felt that resolving issues with the Walker and Luther families and completing his fifth heir test was more urgent.

Offending Evelynn didn't matter then!

He might be her cousin!

As for Charlie and others like Larson, they were merely Evelynn's lackeys.

Osborne didn't care about Evelynn, nor would he care about them.

Thus, Osborne wasn't worried about Evelynn's vengeance; he was focused on quickly dealing with the Walker and Luther families, as well as other forces colluding with the Aberrants.

Isaac's expression grew extremely worried.

"Osborne, are you sure about this? If they join forces, their power is nothing the Paltrow family can match. What if something goes wrong..."

Clarissa also looked on worriedly at Osborne.

Osborne didn't respond, and his expression remained resolute.

He had already nearly completed his fifth heir test; all he needed to do now was to eliminate the high-level members of the four ancient families colluding with the Aberrants—the Walker family, the Luther family, the Marion family, and the Hawthorne family.

Osborne didn't want to waste any more time.

Despite the risks, he was determined to bait them into making a move, and then he would strike back.

"Don't worry, I know what I'm doing."

At this moment, within the Walker Estate...

Osborne was able to wipe out the Paltrow family today; tomorrow, he could come for the Walkers. The day after that, the Luthers and then your Marion and Hawthorne families!
