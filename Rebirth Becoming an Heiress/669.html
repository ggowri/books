Chapter 669 A Stalker

"Accusation? What are you talking about? I calculated the timing. She hadn't completely broken up with Elliott when she got pregnant with this child. So, there is a fifty percent chance that this child is Elliott's. Did I say something wrong?"

Laura must have been going crazy with the desire for a grandson during this period, as she actually blurted out such words in public.

In that moment, I felt humiliated from head to toe. I glared at her, about to speak, but Grey stood up abruptly.

"Laura, we are not like you. The Woods family would not easily accept a child of unknown origin, let alone create a scandalous situation. Madeline has no connection with you now. Please refrain from speaking nonsense in front of everyone, as it not only damages Madeline's reputation but also affects your son. I believe you understand this."

"Yes, Mrs. Laura Bryant. Some things should not be said casually. Today is a big day for your family, so you should go and entertain other guests first."

"Yeah, Mrs. Laura Bryant, you can't have a conflict today. Your blood pressure is already high, and if you get angry again, it could easily lead to a stroke."

***

As soon as they realized something was wrong, several ladies immediately stood up and, while persuading Laura, led her to another table.

Listening to their mocking words, I found it somewhat amusing. However, I couldn't laugh and had no appetite for the food in front of me.

When he saw that I wasn't very happy, Grey squeezed my hand and comforted me.

"Madeline, don't let her irrational words affect you. The Bryant family has been going through a lot lately. I think she's a bit unstable. Don't pay attention to her."

"I knew. Grey, I went to the bathroom."

I nodded and then stood up, wanting to go to the bathroom to wash my face and calm down. So, I headed straight toward the direction of the bathroom.

The place where the Bryant family hosted the banquet was a vacation hotel located not far from the cemetery at the foot of a mountain. Perhaps due to being contracted by the Bryant family, the hotel was quite deserted. The corridors were empty. A gust of cold and eerie wind passed by, causing me to shiver uncontrollably.

I subconsciously headed toward the direction of the bathroom. As I walked, I suddenly heard a series of footsteps following behind me.

The footsteps were hurried. I instinctively looked back, but I didn't see anyone's figure.

Was it probably the hotel waiter?

I didn't think much and continued toward the direction of the bathroom. However, just then, I heard the sound of heavy footsteps following closely once again.

The footsteps made me inexplicably feel a tingling sensation on my scalp. I thought it was another guest walking behind me, so I gathered my courage and looked back. However, as I turned around, the footsteps disappeared. There was still no trace of anyone behind me.

In that moment, I got goosebumps all over my body.

I stood still, looking at the somewhat dark restroom, and for a moment, I didn't know whether I should move forward or quickly return to the banquet.

And at that moment, there came the sound of rushing water from the bathroom.

Someone was washing their hands in the bathroom.

I immediately breathed a sigh of relief and quickly walked toward the direction of the restroom...
