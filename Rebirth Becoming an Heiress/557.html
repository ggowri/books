Chapter 557 Red Sports Car

Elliott suddenly spoke, but he didn't speak to me.

"Our baby..." His words were like thunder, hitting me hard.

The next second, he had already picked Giselle up and strode into the B-ultrasonic room.

I didn't look back, but I could imagine the image of him holding Giselle.

My heart which was gradually recovering was broken by his words and movements again.

I froze, and Paige cursed, "Bastard! His kids deserve to have problems!"

I couldn't bear it, so I quickly pulled Paige out of there.

When I returned to Fiona's office, I felt relieved.

Seeing that I looked strange, Fiona immediately poured me a glass of water, and then asked us in surprise, "What's wrong? What happened just now?"

"Elliott, that bastard, carried Giselle into the B-ultrasonic room in front of Madeline. He also said that their baby must be safe!"

Paige was so angry that she couldn't help but curse angrily.

"What? How could he do such a thing?"

When Fiona heard it, she was very angry.

I drank the water in one gulp and finally calmed down.

"That's his child after all, even if he doesn't care about Giselle, he must care about his children," I said and felt a pain in my heart again.

"But the child in your belly is the real one..."

Fiona was tempted to retort. However, she realized that it was inappropriate, so she stopped.

I looked at her and said firmly, "Fiona, don't mention this to anyone in the future. I hope you and Paige can keep it a secret for me. This child belongs only to me."

Realizing that she had said the wrong thing, Fiona immediately nodded and said, "Okay, I will be careful in the future. Paige, don't tell Philip about this, okay?"

"I won't mention it to him. Don't worry," Paige patted her chest and promised.

After the three of us chatted for a while, I sent Paige back to her residence.

On the way, we chatted. However, just as we were about to arrive at Paige's home, a red sports car drove towards us.

The sports car was a Ferrari convertible, which was very eye-catching from a distance. The driver was a woman wearing sunglasses.

The path to Paige's house was a bit narrow, so I slowed down when I saw a car ahead.

But the woman driving the sports car had no intention of slowing down at all. She sped towards us and passed by quickly. Then she turned a corner quickly and disappeared from our sight in an instant.

"Why is this woman so panicked? She's driving so fast. Isn't she afraid of an accident?"

Paige was so frightened that she clutched her chest and cursed.

"That's right, she seems to be arrogant while driving a Ferrari!" I was also frightened.

"It's strange. This is the road to my house. Why did she look like she had come out of my house?" Paige muttered.

"What? Are you sure this road only leads to your house?" I was stunned and asked quickly.

At the same time, I recalled that woman. Although she was wearing sunglasses, I felt like I had seen this woman before somewhere.
