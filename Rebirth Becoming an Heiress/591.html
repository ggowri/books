Chapter 591 Mixed Emotions

After Elliott heard her words, he finally lowered his head to take a closer look. When Elliott saw the pool of amniotic fluid between her legs, Elliott was immediately flustered.

"What are you standing there for? Quickly call an ambulance and get her to the hospital!" I finally snapped out of my daze, shouting.

Then, I swiftly pulled out my phone and dialed 911.

Elliott seemed to realize the urgency of the situation. He immediately stepped forward to support Giselle.

"Are you... okay?"

"Elliott, it hurts so much, really hurts, I... I can't stand anymore."

Giselle weakly collapsed into Elliott's arms, her legs trembling uncontrollably, sweat dampening her hair.

It didn't seem like an act.

Elliott had already lifted Giselle.

As we rushed out of the office door, Elliott glanced at me with guilt in his eyes.

However, faced with a life-threatening situation, he didn't hesitate. Elliott rushed into the VIP elevator.

As I reached the ground floor, Giselle had already been pushed onto the ambulance by doctors and nurses, with Elliott following suit.

I stood quietly in place, watching my former husband, who was now guarding a woman about to give birth to his child, feeling a mix of indescribable emotions.

The ambulance doors closed quickly. Elliott gave me a look full of guilt, but I didn't respond.

I quickly turned away, pretending not to care, and left the scene in one breath, then got into the car.

The ambulance sped away, leaving me with a whirlwind of complex emotions. I complained in our group chat with my two best friends: [You can't imagine what I just went through. Giselle is giving birth today.]

Fiona quickly replied: [What? Isn't it before her due date?]

I replied promptly: [She was stressed into premature labor, such a turbulent fate for this child...]

Fiona responded with a series of ellipses.

Just then, Paige suddenly sent a message: [Damn it, forget about her, I'm about to be stressed into premature labor too!]

Seeing Paige's message, I felt worried and quickly asked: [What's wrong?]

Paige promptly sent an angry emoji: [That jerk, Philip! He's been disappearing lately, and I seriously suspect he's cheating!]

Paige's words instantly brought back memories of everything I had seen on the cruise ship.

From Paige's words, I guessed she still didn't know the full extent of the situation and was just feeling something was off.

I thought, "Should I tell Paige the truth?"

I was at a loss for what to do when Fiona sent another message: [Damn it, that wretched woman has been brought to our hospital! Not only did she go into premature labor, but she also lost a lot of blood! I'm heading to the operating room to help out!]

As I suddenly heard how serious things had become, a foreboding feeling enveloped my heart.

At this moment, Paige, seemingly enjoying the drama, tagged me, saying: [Madeline, my stomach feels a bit off too. Come pick me up! Take me to the hospital for a check-up!]
