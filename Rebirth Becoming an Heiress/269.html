Chapter 269 So Smoothly

Byron just said his parents were out of the country. And I remembered that in my previous life, Patrick was taken abroad by his children. Was it such a coincidence?

"Your grandfather is a doctor. Well, I know. Come on. I'll take you home!"

I decided to give it a try and promptly walked back with Byron in my arms. Before long, we arrived at Patrick's doorstep.

To my surprise, there had been a lock on Patrick's door just moments ago, but now it was gone, indicating Patrick's return.

Byron recognized his home at once. He instantly struggled off me, excitedly slapping the door and shouting, "This is my home! Grandpa! Grandpa!"

"Byron, my baby, where have you been? I was so close to calling the police!"

A call came from inside. Immediately afterward, an elegant elderly man with short hair hurriedly ran out.

At the sight of Byron, he quickly hugged him and kept muttering, "Thank God!"

Although I had never seen Patrick before, the distinguished older man standing was indeed Patrick.

"Grandpa, she sent me home!" Byron said gleefully to Patrick, pointing at me.

He looked up at me gratefully and stood up with Byron in his arms. He said in a rush, "I really appreciate it. I just took him out to buy something, but I didn't realize that when I was paying for it, he ran far away on his own. It was raining heavily. I searched around twice but couldn't find her. It was driving me crazy!"

"You're welcome. You should be Doctor Cullen. Hoswich Medical University recommends that I come here to visit you. It's quite a coincidence to run into your grandson here!" I said quickly with a smile.

Upon hearing my words, Patrick immediately welcomed me into the house.

The old mansion had a classic four-square layout, appearing old yet well-kept. A smell of medicinal herbs wafted throughout the entire courtyard. In addition to that, there were many flowers and plants planted.

Patrick knew that since I had been recommended to him, there should be a purpose. So, after handing Byron over to his wife, he led me to his dispensary and gestured for me to sit down.

"If I'm guessing correctly, you came over to see me for an illness, right?" Patrick asked gently.

"Yes. Doctor Cullen, to tell you the truth, I found out about congenital endometriosis some time ago. Here is my diagnostic report. I heard from my friend that you are a great doctor, so I wanted to see if you have any potential remedies."

I quickly took out all my report and handed it to Patrick.

Unexpectedly, Patrick didn't even look at the report but said, "I don't read those reports. Show me your hand."

Patrick's order was a clear consent to treat me.

I couldn't help but feel ecstatic. Fiona had warned me that he might refuse to treat me even if I managed to locate him. I hadn't anticipated today to go so smoothly.

I quickly reached out my hand. Patrick began to diagnose me. He pressed my right hand and then my left hand. He closed his eyes and meditated for a while before slowly saying, "Madam, your situation is indeed a bit complicated. If you want to get pregnant, it is not so simple, but..."

Speaking of this, he suddenly paused.

I sensed the hope in his words, and instantly, my heart was in my throat. I hastened to ask, "Doctor Cullen, is there something you can do? I have heard that you are an authority in this field."
