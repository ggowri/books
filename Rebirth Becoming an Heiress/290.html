Chapter 290 Breakfast

At this time, Elliott gave me a call.

"Wash your face and brush your teeth. Breakfast will be ready soon."

"Okay."

I responded quickly to the phone. Then, I quickly got up from the bed, changed my clothes, washed up, and went downstairs.

Elliott was wearing a black housecoat and was busy making breakfast in the kitchen.

His figure was perfect. Although he was standing in the kitchen wearing ordinary home clothes, he was still eye-catching.

I felt itchy. I felt a sense of satisfaction overflowing from the bottom of my heart.

I walked forward, held his slender waist from behind, and gently pressed my face against his back.

In my imagination, this breakfast should be exquisite and delicious. But as soon as I got closer to him, I smelled a slight burning smell.

I was confused and subconsciously glanced at the trash can.

Only then did I realize that the trash can was already filled with half a basket of burnt eggs.

And the two on the dining table were indeed the two best-looking ones among the bunch.

I suppressed my smile.

I asked, "How long will it take for this breakfast to be ready?"

"It's almost finished. Wait at the dining table, I'm cooking spaghetti now."

Elliott turned around and held me in his arms. We couldn't help but kiss again. Then, he pointed to the dining table and asked me to wait there.

I didn't go over but leaned against the door frame and watched him cook.

Elliott started cooking spaghetti, but the order in which he cooked them was not right. Before the water boiled, he had already put the noodles into the pot.

He stared at the still-hard spaghetti in the pot with a frown on his face. He looked helpless.

It seemed that Elliott didn't have any talent for cooking.

"Why isn't this spaghetti soft?"

He didn't realize that I was still standing behind him and muttered.

"You put the spaghetti into the pot before the water boils. Of course, the spaghetti won't be soft. Let me do it."

I couldn't help laughing and said quickly behind him.

He turned around and glanced at me helplessly. He didn't look dominating like he did last night.

"Should I put spaghetti in it after the water was boiled? No wonder I tried several times but failed."

I covered my mouth and snickered.

"Of course. You'd better go and wait at the dining table and I'll do it."

After he left the kitchen, I started to make spaghetti. After a while, I took out two plates of spaghetti from the kitchen.

Elliott took a look at the two eggs that he had fried until they were mushy and then looked at the two plates of spaghetti that I cooked. He immediately gave me a thumbs-up.

"All right, Honey, you are great. "

"Try how it tastes."

His words made me sweat.

We must have consumed too much energy last night, so we both devoured the breakfast. Soon, we finished our respective spaghetti.

Like an ordinary couple, we chatted all day long. The sun shines into the room from the window and shines on us. Everything seemed so peaceful and happy.

I handed him a tissue and let him wipe his lips. He smiled slightly, took my hand, and naturally kissed it gently.

Our eyes met, showing our deep affection for each other. It seemed that we couldn't make out enough.

Elliott walked around the dining table, picked me up, and carried me straight from the dining table to the sofa.
