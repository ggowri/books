Chapter 69 Believe Me

All along, Quinn has always given me the impression of being a carefree, playboy type who treats life as a game. But I never expected that he actually had such a profound side.

"Quinn, I never realized before, but you actually have some culture."

I was extremely surprised. So, I couldn't help but blurt it out.

He slapped my head hard. Then, he said helplessly:

"Madeline, what do you mean by this? After all, I am a graduate of a top-tier institution, you know? In terms of education, I am not inferior to Elliott, whom you love."

Quinn? Graduated from a top university?

I stared wide-eyed in astonishment. I was somewhat incredulous:

"Is what you said true? How come I've never heard you mention it before?"

"What's there to boast about? I used to be an excellent student too. It's just that I wasn't as flashy as Elliott. I didn't enjoy doing business; I preferred living freely and unrestrained."

Quinn smiled slightly. He blinked his eyes at me again.

In the past, I only knew a few people who surrounded Elliott, and they were all wealthy second-generation individuals. But I never took the time to learn about their experiences.

If it weren't for my deep interaction with Quinn nowadays, I wouldn't have thought that he also has many unknown sides.

This suddenly enlightened me.

Originally, there were more than just Elliott as excellent men in this world.

And why should I, just like in the past, have such narrow-mindedness? Why should I only identify with one Elliott?

Quinn is right. As long as I do well on my own, I will definitely meet someone better and more deserving in the future. Perhaps I will even find the love that I desire and hope for.

"Quinn, although you can be quite unserious at times, I still appreciate your words today."

I looked at him and expressed my approval for him for the first time.

He burst into laughter. His smile became even more radiant, like the warm sun. He said:

"It's good if you can open up. Come on, take off your shoes, I'll take you to play in the water. Forget about all the unhappiness today, tomorrow will be a new day!"

Quinn was indeed very good at playing.

Under his instigation, I actually took off my shoes. Afterward, I ran to the seaside with him.

He poured seawater on me recklessly. I was not willing to accept it. So, I fought back desperately. We played like this by the seaside. Later, he took me to try beach motocross and surfing.

The dormant pleasure cells inside my body were easily activated by him.

Once upon a time, I also enjoyed extreme sports. Under Quinn's guidance, I once again experienced the joy of pushing my limits.

Once, I drowned in the sea. Therefore, I had a fear of the ocean deep within me. But that fear was completely dispelled by Quinn.

I felt as if I suddenly became young and energetic. And all the unhappiness in my heart was completely thrown into the sea.

We played crazily by the seaside like children. It was not until the sky gradually turned dark that we finally put away our playful nature and left the beach.

Quinn took me back to my parents' house.

He draped his only towel from his car over me. Just as I was getting out of the car, he called out to me. Suddenly, he said to me:

"Madeline, if this marriage truly made you so unhappy, then you should have divorced earlier. There will be better people waiting for you in the future. Trust me."
