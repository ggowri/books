Chapter 74 Thanksgiving

However, I quickly denied my own infatuation.

Perhaps, Giselle appeared. It was probably the time for him and Sylvia to come to an end.

"Sylvia, I'll be honest with you. Even without the rumors between you and Elliott, I wouldn't have considered you. However, since you're being sincere today, I can consider paying attention to a suitable role for you. I'll contact you when the time comes. How does that sound?"

I originally didn't want to give Sylvia any chance.

But when I thought about the story that Elliott and Giselle were about to experience next, I suddenly felt that instead of having one more enemy, it would be better to have one more ally.

Maybe one day, on the long and arduous journey of breaking up Elliott and Giselle, I might just need someone like this to cooperate with me.

When Sylvia heard me say this, she appeared very disappointed. Afterward, she stood up and sighed.

"Okay. But I really liked the female lead. This book is also my favorite among all the novels I have read. Ms. Mayers, if you change your mind, please feel free to contact me anytime."

"Okay."

I nodded. My conversation with Sylvia came to an end.

After Sylvia left, I opened the stack of materials that Adam had given me. Suddenly, among the various documents, I discovered the existence of a girl.

This girl is named Audrey Carter. She is 22 years old this year. According to the information, she graduated from a vocational school. She has been working as an extra in the film and television city. She has played some unknown small roles. However, she has a remarkable appearance. She has a small round face. When she smiles, her eyes curve like a crescent moon. She looks very innocent, but there is a sense of resilience in her eyes, as if she has experienced hardships.

The reason I noticed her was because I knew in the previous life, this girl would become popular all over the country two years later due to a TV drama. Since then, her career has been very successful. Afterward, she also played many popular roles.

At that time, this actress was still an unknown small actress. So, her information was placed at the bottom by Adam. But I became interested right away, and I immediately called Adam in.

"Madeline, did you look for me?"

After Adam came in, he quickly asked.

I extracted Audrey's information and handed it to him.

"You called and made an appointment for this girl to come for an audition tomorrow."

Adam took the documents. Then, he paused for a moment.

"Her? Madeline, are you sure? She's just an unknown actress. Besides her somewhat innocent appearance, she doesn't have any advantages. And she has a round face, she shouldn't be able to play the lead in this play, right?"

I shook my head.

"I had my plan. You should invite her for an audition first."

"Okay, no problem. I will arrange it right away."

Adam didn't say anything more. He immediately responded.

After Adam left, I busied myself for a while. Suddenly, my mom called me.

"Madeline, once you finish your work today, please go home early. Today is Thanksgiving."

"Ah? Is today Thanksgiving? It came so quickly--"

If my mom hadn't said anything, I wouldn't have known that Thanksgiving had already arrived. I looked at the calendar again, and indeed, today is Thanksgiving.

"Okay, Mom. I'll be right back."

I quickly responded.

Afterward, I hung up the phone. Immediately, I picked up my bag and left the office. I immediately drove to the mall.

In the past, I was an irresponsible daughter. During holidays and special occasions, I rarely went back home to accompany my parents. I never even thought about buying them any gifts.

In my previous life, I had already vowed to be a filial child. Just like other daughters do for their parents, I will also provide everything for them.

I ran to the jewelry counter in the mall and picked out a gemstone bracelet that my mom usually likes. Afterward, I also saw another bracelet made entirely of emeralds.

Since today was Thanksgiving, I bought a bracelet for my mom, so I decided to buy a gift for Laura too!

So, I directly asked the shop assistant to pack one for me. Afterward, I went to the imported premium cigar store and bought a box of top-quality cigars for my father and Jasper.

Later, I called the driver Toby to help me deliver the cigars and the gemstone bracelet to the Bryant Villa. Meanwhile, I carried the gifts back home myself.

When I went back, my dad was already in the kitchen cooking with my mom.
