Chapter 14 Shout Louder

But unexpectedly, at the very moment I stuck out my tongue, that damn bastard suddenly bit down hard on it.

The intense pain spread from the tip of my tongue to my whole body, and I recoiled in agony, letting out a scream that I couldn't hold back. I covered my mouth, writhing in pain, and rolled to the edge of the bed.

Elliott seized the opportunity and swiftly pulled the quilt away from under me. He quickly wrapped himself up in it, and at the same time, in the darkness, I distinctly heard his laughter.

"Madeline, keep shouting and shout louder. My parents will be happy to hear it."

"You are perverted and shameless. You are a bastard. You..."

Realizing that he had just been playing me and that his true intention was to take away the quilt, I couldn't help but angrily curse.

"This is the consequence of you provoking me."

At this moment, Elliott spoke with a hint of pride in his words. He wrapped himself in the quilt, turned his back to me, and actually fell asleep just like that.

Regardless of the season, I would habitually set the air conditioner temperature to the lowest and then cover myself with a thick quilt before sleeping.

At this moment, I was wearing extremely thin pajamas, and the air conditioning in this room was enough to make me shiver.

It was said that men tended to take care of women, but Elliott slept soundly in the quilt, soon emitting a steady breathing sound.

I wasn't the person he cared about after all. Even if I froze and caught a cold, he probably wouldn't have any sympathy for me, not even a bit.

I instinctively embraced my cold body and couldn't help but recall the chilly and gloomy feeling when I struggled in the vast sea in my previous life.

I was just now actually being toyed with by this heartless man to the point of almost losing my sanity.

I couldn't help but blame myself, and I really felt useless.

In my future life, I must find a few young and handsome guys to experience love, get more familiar with men's tricks, and try kissing a few times. Then I wouldn't be so unsettled like this.

Standing under the cold air, I blamed myself and felt helpless. Watching his back inexplicably angered me. I no longer wanted to go back to sleep on this bed that belonged to us.

I lay on the sofa in the dark, hugging a pillow. I fell asleep in a daze while feeling cold all over my body.

The next day.

When I opened my eyes, I felt warm. I looked closely and realized that someone had covered me with a quilt, but the big bed was already empty.

Was it him?

No. He never showed the slightest bit of concern for me.

I subconsciously glanced at my phone. Obviously, at this point in time, Elliott had already left home for the company.

I went downstairs. Sean brought over the breakfast that had been prepared for me a long time ago. He also handed me a few packets of medicine.

Sean said, "Mrs. Bryant, Mr. Jasper Bryant and Mrs. Laura Bryant have left in a hurry. Mrs. Laura Bryant specially brought the medicine to improve your health. I was instructed to remind you to take it."

"Another prescription to help me get pregnant?"

I looked at the medicine and asked, not knowing whether to cry or laugh.

"Yes. Mrs. Laura Bryant said that this medicine was very difficult to obtain. She had to wait in line for a long time to buy it. Mrs. Bryant, please hold on a little longer. As long as you get pregnant, everything will be fine."

Although Sean was sent by my in-laws to take care of us, he had always been kind to me since I got married. He also knew that I had tried various prescriptions to get pregnant in the past two years and had suffered a lot, so he comforted me.

"Okay, then I will bring the medicine to my parents' home later."

My in-laws had already left, so I didn't need to stay here and pretend to be affectionate with Elliott anymore. I planned to leave after finishing breakfast and packing a few clothes.

"Mrs. Bryant, are you going back to your parents' home again?"

Sean asked in astonishment, with a hint of sadness in his eyes.

He had always been perceptive. Perhaps he noticed before Elliott that I had changed a lot.

"Well, I am going to work. It will be more convenient for me to stay at my parents' place, so please take care of things here."

I nodded and quickly went upstairs to grab a few clothes after having a simple breakfast.

When I drove out, Sean hurriedly fetched me the medicine that I intentionally forgot to take and placed it on the passenger seat.

I said goodbye to him and drove away from Serenity Villa. As the car reached the corner, I turned my head and glanced at those several packets of medicine. After thinking for a moment, I picked them up and threw them all into the trash bin.

I would stop being a foolish, cheap, and unappreciated baby-making machine. I would never let myself be ruined by these inexplicable medicines and develop stomach cancer.

I shook my head with a bitter smile and drove back to my parents' house, where I practiced the piano all day.

Shortly after this period in my previous life, the Bryant Group held a grand mid-year banquet. They were originally supposed to invite a famous pianist, but the pianist was involved in a major car accident on the way and was absent from the banquet.

It was an extremely important banquet for the Bryant Group. The absence of the pianist disrupted the entire event. Just when everyone was at a loss, the unremarkable Giselle unexpectedly volunteered and claimed to have learned piano. She said she was willing to go on stage as a substitute.

It was Giselle's amazing performance that caught Elliott's attention.

That piano performance became the beginning of their intersection.

I thought of this and uncontrollably laughed bitterly.

When I was young, I used to be the girl hailed as a piano prodigy.

My parents spared no expense to cultivate my piano skills, hiring a renowned teacher to instruct me. And I did live up to their expectations. At the age of 11, I won an award in an international piano competition with my performance.

If I had continued on the original path, I would have become the most dazzling socialite in Hoswich City.

However, under the instigation of Jade Mayers, my cousin, I later became rebellious and unwilling to follow my parents' arrangements. I started skipping school and became obsessed with everything exciting, such as e-sports, racing, and billiards, which my father forbade me to get involved in.

The life that my father planned for me was all destroyed by me. If I had not fallen in love with Elliott later and studied hard to get into the university where he was, I might have completely neglected my studies.

I looked back at everything I did back then. They were so absurd. Even I myself felt regretful.

Fortunately, in this lifetime, it was not too late to correct my mistakes. Everything could be remedied in time.

I had made up my mind to divorce Elliott at any time, but I would never let Giselle shine brightly in front of Elliott so easily.

In my previous life, Giselle ended my life and took away everything from me. In this life, no matter what, I would never let her and Elliott be together again.

After making up my mind, I decided to practice the piano diligently from now on so that on the day of the mid-year banquet, I would be the one to save the day.

And now, the day of the mid-year banquet of the Bryant Group was getting closer and closer...
