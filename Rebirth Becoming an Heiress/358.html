Chapter 358 Jealousy

I didn't say anything since the beginning because I didn't want to reveal my purpose in front of Elliott.

Having finished the meeting with them, I accompanied Elliott to send Marcus downstairs.

Before Marcus left, I asked him for his contact and added him on WhatsApp, because we hadn't seen each other for years and there was so much we wanted to talk about. Then, I set up a date with Marcus in front of Elliott for the next time we could have dinner alone and catch up.

Elliott didn't say much. However, no sooner had Marcus left than Elliott looked grim directly. He glanced at me with a curious gaze and said, "I can't believe I didn't know before that you had such a dignified old friend."

His tone was full of jealousy.

Seeing this, I couldn't help but tease with a smile, "Well, why do I have a strange feeling? Is someone jealous again?"

I deliberately added the word "again". As expected, Elliott looked even angrier for a split second.

"You just had a nice chat and even set up a date for our next dinner together, but did you ask my permission?"

Elliott was unconsciously exerting his hegemony again. As soon as he finished speaking, he turned and walked into his office.

I rushed to follow him and said, "What's wrong? Am I not allowed to have dinner with an old friend?"

"You were so angry when someone arranged flowers in my office. What's wrong? Are you the only one who can do whatever you want?"

Unexpectedly, it was Elliott's turn to be sarcastic.

From his tone, he seemed to be defending himself from what had happened to Giselle that morning.

"So you're worried about your sweetheart, are you?"

I was instantly disgruntled and couldn't help but raise my fist in protest.

Elliott directly gripped my fist with his warm and strong hand and yanked so tightly that my whole body instantly fell into his arms.

He pinched my nose harshly.

"Sweetheart? What are you babbling about all day? If you ever casually seduce another man in front of me again, don't blame me for being rude to you."

I had fallen into his arms, so I had to reach up and wrap my arms around his neck. I blinked and said, "What do you mean? I'm socializing normally, and I'm not your captive pet. What's wrong? I'm not allowed to eat with people normally, right?"

"It seems you don't know chalk from cheese."

Elliott leaned down to look at me. His eyes had just been serious, but now they were filled with lust.

How could this happen?

We just had sex last night. It's daytime at work, but what's going on in his mind?

I realized something was wrong and struggled in panic, trying to get up from his arms.

But how could he let me go so easily?

He had already lowered his head and kissed me on the lips before I started to struggle. At the same time, he touched my waist directly with his strong hand through the hem of my suit.

A limp and numb feeling like a slight electric shock went through my entire body.

Oh, dear! He wouldn't want to have sex here, would he?

My face went flushed. I pressed his hand down in panic and said, "Elliott, it's working hours. What are you doing?"

But instead of stopping, he became intensified and bit my earlobe.

"What's wrong with working hours? It's my office, so I call the shots."

He was like a glacier in his abstinence, but he was like a flame when he was full of desire.

"No, the door is open. What if someone comes in?"

I couldn't help but exclaim in a whisper.
