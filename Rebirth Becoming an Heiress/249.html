Chapter 249 Encounter with Rowan

Fiona, knowing my personality well, didn't press further with her questions.

"Alright, then get back to me later. By the way, what about you and Alexis?" I asked with a curious smile.

"There's nothing between us. I'm still waiting for that jerk. Even though it's unlikely for us to be together, he recently mentioned he's planning to return."

Fiona sighed with a hint of resignation in her voice. It was evident she hadn't completely moved on from her previous relationship.

"He really is a jerk. I heard his private life was quite complicated during his studies abroad. My classmates even saw him with another girlfriend."

I felt empathetic towards Fiona's loyalty.

While discussing my love life, Fiona seemed to see through everything. Yet, when it came to her own relationships, she was deeply entangled. In truth, Alexis and Fiona were a good match, at least much better than her first love.

"I know, he must be lonely overseas. Once he's back, I'll have closure with him. After all, we haven't officially broken up yet," Fiona said rationally.

"You're too forgiving to accept his chaotic personal life," I said, tapping her head in disbelief.

"Well, I am a gynecologist. I've seen many peculiar things and people. Compared to them, my own love life seems insignificant," Fiona chuckled, displaying a broad-minded attitude.

"Alexis is truly a great match for you. Young achievers like him are rare. If you miss out on him, you might regret it in the future," I remarked, feeling a pang of regret for Fiona as I admired the tasteful decor of the office.

"He is indeed wonderful, but I can't dive into another relationship before ending one properly. That would be irresponsible of me."

Possibly influenced by her profession, Fiona stuck to her principles unwaveringly.

I didn't push further, and we found ourselves downstairs without realizing it. At that moment, Rowan suddenly approached us.

Seeing me, he first looked surprised, then exclaimed with delight, "Madeline, it's you! How come you're visiting our law firm?"

"Yeah, I happened to be nearby having lunch, so I thought I'd stop by," I replied with a smile, observing Rowan.

He seemed to have just returned from the hospital, still carrying the pink lunch box. I shot him a meaningful glance and instinctively asked, "What's this?"

Rowan chuckled awkwardly.

"My girlfriend was with her grandpa at the nearby hospital and didn't have time to eat, so I just dropped off some food for her."

Rowan still referred to Giselle as his girlfriend, indicating that their relationship hadn't reached a breaking point.

With a sudden idea in mind, I smiled and remarked, "You're really sweet to your girlfriend, Rowan."
