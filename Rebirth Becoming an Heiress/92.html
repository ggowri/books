Chapter 92 Plaster Statue

Elliott and I regained our senses instantly.

Elliott immediately let go of me, and I immediately stood up as if I had been electrocuted.

But it was already too late. At this moment, Barry and Giselle were already standing at the office door. Giselle was holding a large bouquet of flowers and looking at us in surprise.

"Wasn't my timing right when I came?"

Giselle had just graduated at that time, so she immediately blushed and became flustered upon seeing this scene.

"I'm sorry, Mr. Bryant. It was my fault."

Barry also blushed. After all, I used to argue with him when I came to see Elliott before. Then, I would leave after Elliott yelled at me.

Therefore, Barry did indeed see the scene where Elliott and I were getting along harmoniously for the first time.

"No problem. Giselle, why did you bring flowers today?"

Elliott, being very experienced, quickly sat down on the chair calmly and seriously.

"Today, a batch of flowers just arrived at the store. I thought these flowers would be perfect for your office, so I picked a few of the freshest ones and brought them over to you. I even brought a vase specifically for them."

Giselle placed the large bouquet of flowers in a white and artistic vase and placed it in a corner of Elliott's office.

In my previous life, around this time, I noticed that different flowers would appear in Elliott's office every day.

I thought, "Could it be true that I really had no way to prevent this from happening? Are they destined to become closer gradually?"

I suddenly became irritated.

Elliott walked around his desk and approached Giselle, taking a glance at the flowers on the table.

"The flowers are nice. The vase is also very unique. Giselle, thank you."

I felt sad for no reason.

"Mr. Bryant, I think this kind of flower suits your temperament very well. This vase also happens to match the decoration in your office. I also brought you a small gift."

Giselle suddenly took something out of her bag and handed it to Elliott.

That thing looked very familiar. It was the plaster statue of a man and a woman that I saw in Giselle's flower shop that day. In the past, Elliott always kept this sculpture in his office and cherished it greatly.

I didn't expect Giselle to give this plaster statue to Elliott now.

"What is this?"

Picking up the plaster statue, Elliott furrowed his brow in confusion after observing it.

"Mr. Bryant, you may have forgotten. Actually, during my childhood every summer vacation, Mr. Jasper Bryant used to pick me up and let me stay at the Bryant Villa for a short period of time. Do you remember me?"

Giselle looked very nervous. Elliott's eyes instantly lit up.

"I remember now! You were the crybaby who stayed at my house during those summer vacations a few years ago."

"Yes! I used to cry a lot back then. You always loved playing pranks on me and giving me nicknames like 'Crybaby'. Now that you remember, does this sculpture remind you of anything?"

Giselle became excited, and her eyes lit up. Elliott and Giselle talked and got lost in memories. I, standing in place, instantly became an outsider again.
