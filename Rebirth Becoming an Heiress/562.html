Chapter 562 Met Him Again

No woman in this world did not desire to be recognized, understood, and cherished.

It didn't matter if this recognition came from a lover, a relative, or a friend. We longed for the feeling of being valued.

Maybe I was affected by the pregnancy hormones or I was too moved, I cried.

Quinn didn't expect that his words would make me cry, so he hurriedly took out a tissue and wiped away my tears. Then he comforted me, "Why did you cry suddenly? I didn't realize you were so emotional before."

"It's because of you." I wiped my tears and said angrily, "Didn't you hate me before? Why are you so nice to me all of a sudden?"

I asked about the confusion in my heart, however, Quinn refused to tell the truth as before.

"I don't need a reason to be nice to you. Besides, you are already so miserable. If I don't treat you better, won't you be even more sad?"

"Don't try to fool me."

I knew he wouldn't tell me the truth, so I didn't ask any more questions.

The waiter brought our orders to the table at this time.

This store was very thoughtful and offered soup, many dishes, and desserts. All kinds of dishes were placed together, looking particularly exquisite, no less good than the food in a big store.

I looked at the dishes, took a photo, and posted it on social media. Then I ate with Quinn.

In this kind of weather, it was great to drink this tonic soup. Since I took the medicine prepared by Marcus for me, my pregnancy reactions gradually eased, and my appetite gradually improved. Before I knew it, I had finished two bowls of soup.

Pregnant women were prone to frequent urination.

Halfway through the meal, I hurried to the bathroom.

Since my divorce, no matter where I went, Elliott would follow me.

But this time, Sylvia became the woman next to him.

While Giselle was still in the hospital trying to preserve her fetus, he hooked up with his old lover again.

Seeing this scene, I suddenly understood that everything in the past was fake, and no one was irreplaceable for him.

I felt glad I had escaped from him.

My eyes lingered on them briefly, then quickly turned away. Then I walked past them without looking at them.

"Isn't she your ex-wife? Ms. Mayers, what a coincidence!"

Sylvia caused trouble on purpose. She was not afraid at all and took the initiative to call me.

I had no choice but to turn around and look at them.

"Ms. Mayers, are you coming here to eat alone tonight?"

Sylvia looked at me thoughtfully and proudly.

Despite our secret alliance, she still enjoyed the superiority of having her arm around Elliott. She was competing with me.

"It's none of your business, right?"

I finished speaking expressionlessly, turned around, and left.

"This store has just been reserved by Elliott. If you don't mind, you can stay and eat with us!" Sylvia said proudly.
