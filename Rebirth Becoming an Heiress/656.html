Chapter 656 Despair

"Is Cormac dying?" Giselle paused for a moment, then muttered to herself. Suddenly, as if realizing something, Giselle snorted coldly, "I don't care. He was already seriously ill. He was bound to die sooner or later, no wonder."

Now that Giselle, who used to disguise herself, had been exposed, she clearly didn't want to continue pretending. She actually said such cruel words in front of us.

Seeing her cold attitude, I couldn't help but recall how obedient, filial, kind, and sensible she used to be in front of Cormac.

Back then, Giselle was only pretending in order to achieve her goals. It was only at this moment that she revealed her true self.

"Giselle, didn't you respect Cormac a lot before? Now that the truth has been revealed, you don't want to continue pretending anymore?" Fiona couldn't help but mock. She was a very honest person and looked down upon people like Giselle who were good at pretending.

Giselle glanced at the four of us, and then, a very eerie smile appeared on her face.

This smile reminded me of my last life. When I was thrown into the icy sea by a few strong men on the deck of a yacht, Giselle also had the same sinister and malicious smile.

As soon as I thought about how she hurt me and replaced me in the previous life, and then saw her current desperate look like a bereaved dog, the hatred in my heart finally found its release.

"The truth has been revealed?" Giselle's expression was filled with intense resentment. She scanned all of us in the room. "Don't think I don't know who did it. Madeline, besides you, no one else would sabotage my good plan like this! It was you, right?"

Giselle suddenly cast a hateful gaze towards me. Her eyes were malicious, and that once beautiful face now appeared grotesque.

"You have the courage to do such a thing. Aren't you afraid of being discovered one day?"

I was worried that she might have had listening devices or something like that on her, so instead of directly saying it, I asked Giselle to answer.

She snorted and said, "As long as I become Elliott's wife, everything will be decided by me! So what if I am found out? In this world, the power of speech is in the hands of the winners. Tonight, I was supposed to have won, but unexpectedly, you ruined everything!"

After Giselle finished speaking, she suddenly pointed at me. Her expression was malicious, giving me a strong sense of discomfort.

"It was you who ruined my happiness, Giselle, please understand that. As for your own life, you destroyed it with your own hands. You had the opportunity to graduate from a prestigious school, work diligently, and then marry a good man. Isn't that good enough? I don't understand why you had to entangle yourself with Elliott! , Don't you feel ashamed of being a mistress and destroying someone else's family?" I said, looking at Giselle, who showed no signs of remorse.
