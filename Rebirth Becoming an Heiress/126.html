Chapter 126 My Heart Was Broken

I felt like my heart was forcefully cut open by him with a knife, instantly becoming extremely painful, and then blood flowed like a river.

I suddenly laughed.

Elliott, do you know? I can doubt any other woman, but Giselle, how could I not doubt her?

She was your dream lover, you know.

He started feeling again that everything I did was wrong, and that anything I said was also wrong.

My reason reminded me that I acted impulsively tonight and lost control. I asked questions I shouldn't have asked, and I shattered the peace I had been trying to maintain during this period.

I said things I shouldn't have said. However, I am just an ordinary person, and as long as I care about him, I cannot help but care if he likes other women.

"Elliott, how much have I liked you all these years? I liked you to the point where I knew what you loved to eat, I knew what you enjoyed doing, I was well aware of every stage of your life, and even a single glance or gesture from you, I knew what you were thinking and how you would act. So, how could I not know what kind of woman you liked?"

I covered the position of my heart, looked at him, and couldn't help but mutter to myself.

The moment I finished saying these words, my entire soul instantly plunged into complete disarray.

What was I doing?

Does someone who doesn't care about me show their true feelings?

Doing this, I wouldn't get his attention at all. It would only make him look down on me even more. What am I doing?

No, I found that my emotions once again walked on the edge of losing control.

And I, I absolutely couldn't go on like this anymore. If I continued to stay with Elliott, I was afraid I would say more things that I would regret, exposing more of my inner feelings.

Then, my whole being would be seen through by him again, then despised by him, and mercilessly abandoned by him.

As soon as I thought about these things, I, like a refugee fleeing in panic from a fire scene, swiftly pushed open the car door without any hesitation and jumped out of his car.

"Madeline, damn it! What were you doing?"

Elliott's roar came from inside the car, and at the same time, I heard the sound of the car door closing behind me.

I didn't look back, as soon as Ijumped out ofthe car, I ran forward like crazy.

The bag that contained all the heartbreaking memories of my past life seemed to have been torn open by someone, creating a huge hole.

I ran while those shattered memories fell, and tears blurred my eyes without me knowing when.

I don't know when I lost my high heels on my feet, and I ran forward desperately like a refugee escaping from death, regardless of everything.

The sound of the wind whistling behind could be heard.

I could feel that Elliott chased me for a while.

However, perhaps he thought I was too crazy, or perhaps he thought chasing after me was beneath his status, or perhaps, I simply wasn't that important to him in his heart.

He did not catch up with me anymore, behind me, only the fierce wind blew across this empty road.
