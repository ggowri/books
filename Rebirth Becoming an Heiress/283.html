Chapter 283 Affectionate

Elliott became insatiable, and I was incapable of resisting him at this point.

He pinned me to the chair, lavishing me with kisses and caresses, then moved me onto the table, where his kisses turned into fervent sucking.

By the end, I was breathless and desperately pleading, "Elliott, please stop..."

"Since you've sparked my desire, why not help me out with it?"

He was unwilling to let me go, and the more I begged him to stop, the more passionate he became.

His touch and kisses left me utterly powerless, like a pile of cotton.

When Paige and Philip returned, their eyes widened involuntarily.

I was straddling Elliott's lap with a string of red marks adorning my neck. Plus, I was completely limp in his embrace, unable to do anything else other than breathe.

The private room was saturated with a suggestive allure.

As adults, they all grasped the situation in an instant.

I narrowed my eyes at them.

Paige had never witnessed me in such a state. She recoiled into Philip's arms, patting her chest as she exclaimed, "Oh my, let's leave. We're being quite the disturbance."

Immediately, I noticed the blush on Paige's face and the distinct red marks on her neck.

Clearly, those two had abruptly vanished and went off somewhere to flirt with each other.

Philip and Elliott exchanged a glance, sharing a silent understanding.

"Well, we'll head back to the hotel. You two enjoy your evening..." Philip said subconsciously.

"We don't need your concern. Why don't you both leave now?"

Elliott shot Philip a displeased glance, evidently finding him bothersome.

Philip swiftly grasped his meaning, promptly escorting Paige out and even closing the door to the private room for us.

"Let's take a breather. I'm truly exhausted, and I give in, alright?"

With their departure, I hastily grabbed a napkin and waved it, conceding defeat.

Elliott gazed at me with a wicked smirk dancing on his lips.

"You're already tired? That was just foreplay. The real fun begins when we return to the apartment."

His grin grew even more devious.

I used to ponder how someone as serious and earnest as him became friends with the likes of Quinn and Philip, known for being playboys.

Now, I realized he was quite skilled in this area too, perhaps even more so than his companions.

"I'm hungry."

My stomach grumbled, reminding me that I hadn't eaten a thing all night.

I wanted to stand up, but Elliott showed no intention to let me go.

He picked up a sausage from the table, bringing it to my lips with a smirk. "Alright, I'll feed you. Eat up, so you'll have energy later."

"You..."

I was left speechless and glared at him.

Yet, hunger overpowered me. Sausages were irresistibly tempting in the late hours, prompting me to take a bite obediently.

"We're already married. What's wrong with saying that?" He seemed somewhat dismissive as he fed me the entire sausage, then took a glass of orange juice from the table, bringing it to my lips.

I was about to drink it, but he beat me to it and took a big gulp of the orange juice himself.
