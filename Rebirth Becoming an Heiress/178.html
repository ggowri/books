Chapter 178 Quietly Left

We slept on the hospital bed like this all night, and the next morning, Elliott's phone ring woke us up.

I opened my eyes and he had already gotten up, picked up his phone, and walked to the window to answer the call.

The first thing I heard Elliott say was, "Are you awake? Are you feeling uncomfortable?"

As soon as I heard Elliott's gentle voice, I knew it was Giselle calling.

I don't know what Giselle said on the phone, Elliott said in a low voice.

"Okay, I will come over in a while."

Elliott hung up the phone, turned around, and looked at me with a calm expression. Seeing that I was still looking at him, he asked me casually,

"I went to buy breakfast for you, what do you want to eat?"

Buy me breakfast... Shouldn't you be going to see Giselle?

I admired Elliott's composure, his eyes were clear as if there was nothing between him and Giselle.

"It's fine, Giselle woke up. You go and check on her first, I'm fine, not hungry."

I deliberately burst this matter to see what his reaction would be.

However, to my surprise, Elliott remained calm.

"Okay, I will go and have a look."

Elliott turned around and walked out of the ward. He hurriedly walked away, and from his hasty figure, I could vaguely sense his concern and worry for Giselle.

I half leaned on the pillow, the warmth of him still lingering in the bed. The room was filled with his presence, and I could feel that I was suffocating, I was unhappy, very unhappy.

I picked up my phone and called Fiona.

"Fiona, were you working?"

"Are you feeling better, my dear? I was planning to take a day off today and come see you."

"I wanted to transfer to your place, and I didn't want Elliott to know. Can you make it happen?"

I held the phone and asked urgently.

I am currently in a private hospital, where the conditions and environment are top-notch, but I feel that the atmosphere here is too oppressive. It is so oppressive that I can't wait to escape.

"Now? Alright, wait for me, I will handle it."

Fiona has always been decisive. She is not as nosy as Paige and doesn't love asking me a bunch of questions. Whenever I have any demands and approach her, she won't ask for the reasons, but only assess if she can accomplish it. If she can, she will immediately solve it for me.

Not long after Fiona hung up the phone, she had already arranged everything and called me to say that she could help me with the transfer, as long as I agreed.

So, not long after, Fiona's hospital ambulance drove directly to the hospital I was in. I quickly packed up all my belongings, and Fiona arranged for my transfer. I then left directly in that ambulance.

The whole process took only half an hour, and as the ambulance I was sitting in drove out of the hospital gate, I saw Elliott hurriedly walking in from outside the hospital. Behind him, Barry was holding two breakfasts in his hands, obviously one for me and one for Giselle.

"Isn't that Elliott? Is he buying breakfast for you?"

Fiona saw me staring in that direction, so she asked me.

"What I bought for others, I just benefited from it. Fiona, do you think I should be grateful that now, in his eyes, at least he is not ignoring me anymore, but treating me equally with other women?"

I laughed at myself, my heart had already become numb, and I didn't care about pain or not.

Anyway, that's it.

Fiona angrily hammered my shoulder.

"Do you need to be so humble? There are no three-legged frogs in the world, but men with two legs are everywhere. Is it necessary for you to degrade yourself and share a man with others?"
