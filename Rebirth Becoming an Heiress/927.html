Chapter 927 The Test of the BFF

"I hope so."

Elliott was still sincere, and his humble look surprised Orson.

"Elliott, didn't you say that you came to Korea to help me with school matters, in addition to a very important business negotiation?"

"It's fine, I can put it off."

"But that negotiation, if it succeeds, it's a contract of hundreds of millions of dollars!"

"It's not as important as accompanying your sister-in-law."

"Elliott, did you also say that you're going to set up a dinner party in Korea to maintain friendships with old clients?"

"Your sister-in-law and her friends are more important."

......

Orson and Elliott's conversation left all three of us looking dumbfounded.

Where had Paige ever seen Elliott look so humble, she was so shocked that the fried chicken in front of her didn't even smell good, she looked at Elliott in surprise:

"Elliott, you weren't like this before, you're not acting, are you?"

"No, I genuinely was. I was before and I am now."

Elliott responded honestly, his manner was respectful and his tone was humble, indeed, he was different from his former condescending demeanor.

"Elliott, it's not like we're forcing you, you asked for the opportunity. It's not that easy to be a bag boy and a tour guide, women are a pain in the ass to go out, if you regret it, it's not too late."

Fiona was still on her pedestal as she said with a straight face.

"It's okay, I can."

Elliott said as he lowered his head and tapped his phone a few times.

After a few moments, a text message sounded from his phone and he looked up, addressing the three of us:

"Why don't you guys check out of the hotel you booked before, I've booked you into the top of the line suite at the hotel I'm staying at, the suite has several separate rooms so that you can stay together without being disturbed by each other, and you'll have a special personal butler to attend to you."

"Are you talking about that top of the line hotel in Seoul? Rooms there are expensive, wouldn't a suite be ridiculously expensive?"

Paige's eyes widened in surprise, amazed at how rich Elliott was.

"So what if it's expensive, are we the ones who don't deserve it? He's willing to pay for it, so we can just stay there."

Fiona was calm the entire time, she gave Paige a direct blank stare, then she drifted a look towards me triumphantly.

"Elliott, would you be wasting your money like that?"

After all, I hadn't agreed to make up with him yet, so I said with some difficulty.

Elliott lowered his head, he was still looking at his phone, he looked like a pretty scatterbrained guy, but the next second, he said:

"I've got the itinerary planned for you guys for tomorrow's outing, I've just chartered a luxury motorhome, so you can have fun and rest."

"You're so thoughtful! Elliott, why didn't I realize before that you were still so considerate?"

Paige's mental defenses were always lowest, and when Elliott said that, she instantly got starry-eyed with excitement throughout.

Fiona was so angry that she twisted her arm fiercely and glared at her, "Paige, calm down, okay?"

"It's late today, why don't you guys eat and rest at the hotel I booked for you, and for your luggage, I'll just send someone to pick it up."

Elliott looked at the limited edition watch on his hand as he said.

"Okay, I'm a little sleepy, I can't keep my eyes open."

Paige yawned cooperatively.

I realized at once that she was actually already longing for the luxury suite Elliott had booked.

Although the three of us weren't too poorly off, and the hotel we stayed in during this visit to Korea wasn't too bad, it was still bad as for a rich man like Elliott.

He was notorious for being generous when it came to spending money.

"Let's pretty much break up then, I see Madeline is tired too."

Fiona could see that I was tired, so she said.

"Elliott, since you're so generous today, why don't you book one for me too? I don't need a top suite, I'll just have the same suite as you."

Orson saw this and he hurriedly asked Elliott.

"Get your ass back to school and sleep in your dormitory, I haven't talked to you yet for what you did in school."

Elliott faced Orson with a completely different attitude, he immediately put on his parental demeanor and said to Orson.

Orson flattened his mouth with an aggrieved look:

"Elliott, you don't value your brother! How can you be like this?"

"Cut the crap."

"Elliott, why don't I be your driver tomorrow? I'll do all the tiring work for you, how's that?"

"You go back to school and cut the crap!"

Elliott scowled, he was serious.

Orson looked at Elliott, he didn't want to go, yet he didn't seem to dare disobey Elliott, he stood up obediently.

"Elliott, why don't you let Orson come with us tomorrow, I don't mind having an extra bag carrier."

Paige suddenly spoke up for Orson.

Orson was instantly happy:

"Yes, Elliott, you serve your sister-in-law and I'll serve the other two sisters, we'll each do our own work, and that way I'll be able to take some of the work off of you too, won't I?"

Elliott gave him a look, probably thinking that his words made sense, so he said nothing more, as a sort of acquiescence.

We all got a little hungry in the next few moments, and each ate the food on the table in silence.

Afterward, we followed Elliott back to the same hotel he was in.

He had booked a room directly for the three of us, which saved me the embarrassment of having to be alone with him, and made me secretly relieved.

Thankfully, we held the line and didn't cross it.

Paige, Fiona and I went to the suite he booked, when we entered the door, we found that the suite is particularly large, moreover, the decoration style is extraordinarily luxurious and noble, at first glance, it is from the big designers.

Paige squealed in amazement as she picked out one of the rooms and ran in, yelling and screaming, and jumped right on the bed:

"Oh my god, this bed is so high class, I've seen it on a luxury website, the price of this bed is a million dollars, not including the mattress!"

"Paige, you're rich, can you stop looking like you've never seen it before?"

Fiona couldn't help but shake her head as she couldn't help but spit at Paige, however, in the next second, she suddenly let out a surprised yell:

"Oh my god, there's actually that 'Desire' by that most famous Korean painter, KING, in this room! Oh my god, is this for real? Can't it be? Such an expensive painting, aren't they afraid of other guests breaking it?"

Fiona immediately ran towards that painting, she stood beside it in awe, she didn't dare to touch it with her hands at all.

These two people ......

I looked at their exaggerated appearance, suddenly helpless, I stood still, quietly looking around this room.

Obviously this suite is more advanced and luxurious than Elliott's room, however, my heart is a bit empty, and even my body, it seems to be emptied.

As soon as I thought of everything that happened in Elliott's room, my thoughts drifted a little, and my body went a little soft ......
