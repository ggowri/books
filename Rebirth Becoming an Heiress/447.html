Chapter 447 Small World

Standing up hastily, I was about to walk in. And the moment I stood up, I clearly heard Giselle exclaim,

"Madeline? Madeline Bryant?"

Fiona asked me in a hurry, so I had no time to pay attention to Giselle. I followed Fiona into the B ultrasonic room without any hesitation.

Fiona had already told the doctor inside about my situation. After I entered the room, the doctor immediately asked me to lie down. And then, he began to examine me.

Feeling the cold probe on my lower abdomen, I couldn't help feeling worried.

"How is the baby going on?"

Both Fiona and the doctor showed serious expressions, so I became worried and nervous. Then, I couldn't help asking.

"You have just been pregnant not long ago. Now I can see the folk sac and the embryo bud, but the fetal heart is not obvious yet. It's strange. Theoretically, the embryo bud and the fetal heart should be detectable."

Crossing her arms in front of her chest, Fiona frowned at the screen. Obviously, she was unsatisfied with the current test results.

"Ah? What should I do? What will happen if the fetal heart cannot be detected?"

I felt panic instantly.

As a first-time pregnant woman, it was the first time I heard these medical terms. And I had no idea what it meant not to be able to detect fetal heart.

"Some embryos implant late. It just develops later. Don't worry. You have to come back for a checkup in a week."

Seeing my worried expression, Fiona held my hand and said softly.

"Yes. You are not alone. So, don't worry."

At this time, the doctor finished the examination. She handed me a tissue to wipe my belly. And then, she said to me.

Fiona helped me out of bed. Initially, I wanted to ask something else, but I suddenly saw Giselle's gloomy face appearing behind the small window on the door of the consulting room.

I was startled.

I thought she must be wondering why I was here, so she wanted to see what I was doing.

Fortunately, after Fiona and I came in just now, she locked the door.

I couldn't expose my pregnancy to Giselle yet.

Otherwise, I could predict that she would do something terrible for me with her character.

I quickly pulled the curtain to cover myself. Then, I whispered to Fiona,

"Fiona, I met Giselle in the waiting room just now. She would be bound to wonder why I received the B ultrasonic test. Maybe she will wait for me outside the door, so you hurry up to think of a way to cover it up."

Suddenly, Fiona was stunned.

"Why is that bitch here?"

"I just heard it that her attending doctor seems to work in this hospital. Fiona, is there any way to cover it up for me? "

"Lie down. I'll ask my colleague to examine your chest. Since she knows a doctor working here, I'll delete the examination record later."

Finishing speaking, Fiona immediately asked me to lie down again. Then, she asked the doctor to examine me again.

A few minutes later, I took the test results, walking out of the B ultrasonic room with Fiona.

After coming out, I saw Giselle pacing restlessly in the corridor as expected.

Hearing the door open, she immediately turned and looked at me with sharp and some inquiry on her face.

"Madeline, it's really you."

Looking at me intently, she shouted out in surprise.

"Small world. It's me. So, what do you want?"

I looked at her and asked coldly.

She couldn't help looking me up and down. And then, noticing the test results in my hand, she immediately rolled her eyes.

"Are you here to check why you can't get pregnant? Actually, you should save your breath. Doctor Louis said that you can hardly get pregnant. So, stop daydreaming!"

While speaking, she subconsciously stroked her belly. Her silent action was clearly to stimulate me.
