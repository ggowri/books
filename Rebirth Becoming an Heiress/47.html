Chapter 47 Beef Noodles

"It's so late, how about we have something to eat? What do you want to eat? I can go and make it for you."

I really wanted to get out of this embarrassing situation right now, and on top of that, I was feeling a bit hungry, so I said.

"Sure, then you cook spaghetti, and after eating, we'll go to the banquet together."

Elliott said in a low voice.

"Okay."

I quickly responded and went to the kitchen.

I took out spaghetti from the cupboard and then got some beef from the refrigerator.

I didn't know how to cook many dishes, but because I loved eating beef spaghetti since I was young, my mom forced me to learn how to cook beef spaghetti after I grew up.

My mother said that girls must learn a few signature dishes, not for the purpose of serving their future husbands and in-laws, but for themselves, so that they don't have to rely on others when they want to eat something.

In the past, I always disagreed with what my mom said, but now, after experiencing setbacks and making mistakes, I understand that many of the things said by those who have been through it actually have their own reasons.

I started preparing pasta, and in the living room, the sound of Elliott making a phone call came through.

Today is the mid-year banquet. Although he doesn't need to personally prepare for such an event with his position, some details still need to be consulted with him before making decisions.

Over here, a pleasant fragrance quickly filled the air, and in the living room, his voice occasionally echoed. This feeling was reminiscent of the typical scent of fireworks in an ordinary household.

And this kind of situation, since we got married two years ago, has happened for the first time in this house.

Sean came into the kitchen at this time and offered to help me. Seeing the rare harmony between Elliott and me, he jokingly said, "Well, well, what's going on here?"

"Mrs. Bryant, you and Mr. Bryant are starting to look more and more like a married couple. I've said it before, marriage is all about working things out together."

Did they look like a married couple?

I smiled slightly and subconsciously felt satisfied in my heart. But as soon as I thought about the upcoming Giselle, that little joy in my heart instantly disappeared.

If I hadn't experienced the previous life, I'm afraid that now, as long as Elliott is even a little bit kind to me, I wouldn't be able to resist falling, just like before, like a moth to a flame.

But now, I can't, I will never be able to again.

"Sean, it was just temporary, I wasn't the person he liked."

I shook my head and smiled helplessly.

"Mrs. Bryant, it doesn't matter whether you like living your life or not, what matters is compatibility. In my opinion, you and Mr. Bryant are a very suitable couple. Trust me, I won't be mistaken."

Sean comforted me again.

At this moment, I had already put the diced beef into the pan and stir-fried it for a while. Afterward, following my mother's previous method, I made beef spaghetti.

After everything was ready, I boiled the water and then put the spaghetti into the pot.

In just half an hour, I had prepared three plates of beef spaghetti, and one of them, I specifically made for Sean to eat.

In my previous life, the only person who remained kind and unchanged towards me was this simple-minded butler. However, I only regarded him as a butler in the past, and I never truly appreciated his care.

In this lifetime, I will do my best to make up for all the people who have sincerely cared for me in the past.

Sean was very surprised. He thanked me repeatedly and praised the delicious beef spaghetti I made. Then he picked up the plate of spaghetti and happily went to the small restaurant next to us to eat.

I called Elliott over and placed two plates of cooked beef spaghetti on the dining table.

He sat down and saw two plates of beef spaghetti in front of him, with colors, fragrance, and taste all complete. A slightly surprised expression appeared on his face.

"What did you do?"
