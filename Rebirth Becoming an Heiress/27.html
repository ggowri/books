Chapter 27 Jealousy

"I thought you were going to have fun tonight, but I didn't expect you to have such a plan. Madeline, you have indeed grown a lot."

His voice became even deeper than before, and it sounded like he was praising me.

But I knew this was not exactly a compliment. After all, how could he praise me when he despised me so much?

"Don't be like this. It's just a small script. Recently, there was also a good project called Allure. Instead of wasting money on a small script, the Bryant Group would rather invest their energy in creating a big production."

I tried to calm his inner anger, so I suggested to him.

"You have just been working for a few days, but you already know quite a lot."

He looked at me coldly, scanning my face.

"You used to always tell me to study hard, didn't you? I'm just following your words."

I deliberately drew circles on his chest with my hand.

Even though I didn't understand the art of seducing men, in my previous life, I watched so many romantic dramas that I eventually learned.

It turned out that a woman's act of being coquettish is somewhat effective, as the anger in his eyes disappeared.

"Oh? Are you being so obedient now?"

"Yes, honey."

I smiled slightly, lifted my head, and playfully looked into his eyes.

Today, I deliberately dressed up, with light and elegant makeup, and my eyes were accentuated with eyeshadow and eyeliner.

My eyes were very bright.

However, in the past, I was constantly heartbroken because of Elliott, which caused my eyes to lose their sparkle.

But now, I have moved on, and my eyes have regained their natural brightness.

At the moment when I locked eyes with Elliott, I could feel a flash of brightness swiftly pass through his eyes, and at the same time, his hand around my waist unconsciously tightened.

He tightly embraced my waist, creating a very ambiguous posture between us.

I suddenly realized that recently we seemed to have a lot more physical contact than in the past.

"I was determined to get that drama. You can break the contract with Jacob, and I will pay the penalty for you."

He seemed to have taken a step back but was still domineering.

He has always been like this. Whatever he set his sights on, he must get it. He would never give up if anyone took it away from him.

But I was also determined to win this drama.

Besides, I had already succeeded. I also couldn't compromise so easily.

"Darling, could you just let me have this project? Could you please stop fighting with me?"

I wanted to do it a different way.

In order to make him give up, I intentionally hooked my hand around his neck and imitated Sylvia's tone, starting to act coquettishly towards him.

Just at the moment when I touched his neck, I distinctly felt a slight tremor in his body.

He looked at me with a half-smile. He used to be so cold to me in the past, but I didn't know why he was unusually patient tonight.

"I can give you anything else, but I won't compromise on work. Moreover, what I have always detested the most is people scheming with me."

He clearly meant that he did not want to make any concessions.

I couldn't help but feel a little annoyed, so I withdrew my hand and pretended to be angry.

"The Bryant Group had so many projects. You didn't even need this small one. Your purpose in doing this was just for Sylvia, right?"

"So what if I am? Are you jealous?"

"Just because I know your intention, therefore, it is even more impossible for me to give you this project. Elliott, we have no need to discuss it!"
