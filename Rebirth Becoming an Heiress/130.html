Chapter 130 Heartache

I didn't expect Quinn to be so dominant.

He didn't treat his arm as an arm at all, but stuffed it into my mouth like a chicken leg.

The doctor disinfected the sole of my foot with iodine and then started to remove the fragments from my foot using tweezers.

I was in so much pain that I broke out in a cold sweat. At the most unbearable moment, I couldn't control myself anymore and bit directly into Quinn's arm.

He didn't make any sound, not even moving his arms, and let me bite like that.

I was covered in sweat, leaning against his waist, while his other hand was wrapped around my shoulder, giving me an inexplicable sense of security. With his support, I endured these long and painful minutes.

Fortunately, the doctor at the clinic quickly treated my wound and removed all the fragments from the sole of my foot. Afterward, the doctor bandaged me up and administered a tetanus shot.

"Doctor, how long do you think her foot will take to heal?"

Philip has been with us all along, and he asked with concern.

"You need to rest for about a week, and the wound should heal by then. I'll write a prescription, and when you go back, remember to help her change the dressing."

After the doctor finished speaking, he wrote me a prescription and explained in detail the method of changing the medication.

After the treatment was over, Quinn carried me back onto the off-road vehicle. It was only then that I noticed his arm, which had been bitten by me, had a bright red tooth mark. Moreover, his forehead was covered in fine sweat. Obviously, the process of being bitten by me must have been very painful for him.

"I'm really sorry, but could you go back and get another rabies shot?"

I felt a bit touched and guilty in my heart, with my head down, not knowing what to say.

I successfully made him laugh with my words. He pushed my head with his hand and said, "

"Did you admit that you were a puppy?"

I suddenly realized that I had said the wrong thing, and I immediately felt embarrassed, my face turning red. I was so angry that I punched him in the chest.

"What I mean is, what if I had any germs that I transmitted to you?"

Quinn laughed heartily, and then he asked me with confusion, 'Why didn't you come to the party last night?'

You haven't told us what exactly happened, why were you being chased by a few hooligans barefoot on the street so late?"

Quinn's question instantly made me feel down.

How should I answer him?

Did I tell him that I had a fight with Elliott and once again he left me on the road? - I felt too embarrassed to tell him, so I remained silent.

Quinn noticed that I didn't want to speak, so he said to Philip:

"Philip, there is still a distance to Madeline's house. You drive to your house first, and then I will drive her back."

Philip turned his head and glanced at Quinn with a meaningful look, his eyes filled with a lot of emotions.

However, he didn't say anything and just made an "OK" gesture.

"Okay, then I got off at the intersection ahead."

After Philip parked the car at the intersection ahead, he got out of the car.

Quinn sat in the driver's seat and drove towards my house.

I thought he would directly take me home, but to my surprise, after driving for a short distance, he stopped directly at the entrance of a tastefully decorated restaurant.

Hejumped out ofthe car, opened the backseat door, and asked me to climb onto his back.

"Come, let me take you for some late-night snacks."
