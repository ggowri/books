Chapter 338 Encounter in the Hospital

[What's going on with you? Go to the hospital quickly and ask Fiona to see you!]

After seeing Paige's message, I quickly replied to her.

[I don't dare to move at all. Damn Philip, I really want to beat him!]

Paige sent several angry emojis in succession, which showed her anger right now.

[Then you just wait for me. I'll come and take you to the hospital.]

I quickly replied. At the same time, I found Fiona didn't reply to my message, so I kept sending her more messages.

At this time, Fiona sent me a voice message. Her voice was hoarse and lazy. It seemed she was still sleeping.

"Sorry, I'm studying abroad, so I'm still sleeping now. Paige, you can go to my hospital. I will ask my colleague to see you. "

I rolled down the window and shouted to Elliott.

Seeing I was awake, Elliott quickly came over and opened the car door.

Under the reflection of the rising sun, he didn't look tired after all night's work. Instead, he looked energetic! He gave me a rare bright smile.

"Are you awake? Are you tired?"

I was so tired, but I couldn't say that.

I blushed with embarrassment and didn't dare to look into his eyes.

"Paige said she had a stomachache, but no one can't accompany her to the hospital. So I want to go with her. "

"Okay, I'll take you there," Elliott replied positively and simply.

"No, no, no, you just take me to my house. I can drive my own car. It's not very convenient for a man to get involved in women's matters," I said quickly.

Elliott looked at me with a simper.

"Are you sure you can drive by yourself?"

It was obvious that his question had another meaning. I was so ashamed that I wanted to find a crack in the ground and crawled into it.

"Of course, please drive quickly."

"Good."

Seeing my red face, he finally stopped teasing me. He quickly got into the cab and drove me away from the coastal road.

It was too late last night that I couldn't appreciate this beautiful scenery along the way. At this moment, I realized how beautiful the coastal road was.

I looked at this beautiful scenery through the car window and listened to the music played in the car. The slightly salty sea breeze blew my hair. So I raised my hand to remove them.

At this time, Elliott stretched out one hand and inadvertently clasped his fingers with mine. I turned my head subconsciously and found that both of us looked at each other and smiled.

"It will be Christmas in two weeks. At that time, let's go back to Bryant Villa together, " Elliott said.

His words reminded me of the passing days. How time flew! It turned out that Christmas would be celebrated in two weeks.

"Go to Bryant Villa? "Thinking of Laura's attitude, I was hesitant. "I'm afraid that your parents and grandfather would not be happy to see me."

"No, I will be with you there. "Elliott's tone was very firm.

"Okay, let's decide it later. I hope my dad can wake up before Christmas."

I nodded. I couldn't help but think of my father who was still lying in the hospital. My heart suddenly became heavy.

"It will definitely happen. Otherwise, you can transfer your father to another hospital. I will arrange for several authoritative experts to conduct a joint consultation on him to find out the causes of his disease as soon as possible."

Elliott had once again mentioned the idea of transferring my father to another hospital. I understood that if I refused him again, he would have other thoughts in mind.

Considering the hospital where Fiona worked had not cured my father so far, I pondered for a while and said, "Okay, I'll discuss this issue with my mother later. If she agrees, I will transfer my father to another hospital."

"Okay, I will talk with the Bryant Hospital about this. "

Receiving my agreement, Elliott's voice was full of excitement. He held my hand tighter.
