Chapter 686 Reveal the Truth

Quinn directly stuffed food into my mouth, while at the same time, he signaled me with his eyes not to speak the truth.

"What's wrong, Madeline? What do you want to say?"

Jessica asked in confusion.

Quinn's hand came around from under the table at this moment, gently squeezing my palm. He repeatedly signaled me with his eyes, urging me not to speak the truth.

I certainly understood his intention. He hoped that this matter would only be a secret between him and me, never to be told to anyone else.

However, I understand that the truth will come to light sooner or later. If I don't speak up now and wait until the child is born in the future, and their family has invested all their emotions, only to discover that it was a deception, then I would truly be betraying my conscience. Then, Giselle and I would have no difference at all.

Moreover, there is such a good girl by Quinn's side. We were not lovers, so why should I delay his lifelong happiness because of me.

As a close friend, even if Quinn is willing to sacrifice for me, I cannot just stand by and watch him do so.

So, I mustered up the courage. I cleared my throat and looked at Jessica.

"Jessica, the child in my belly, actually wasn't Quinn's."

My sudden remark stunned everyone, especially Carole. When she heard my words, she was so shocked that she almost sprayed the soup out of her mouth.

"Madeline, what nonsense are you talking about!"

I didn't expect that I would still speak the truth, and Quinn immediately pretended to scare me seriously.

However, I looked at him apologetically.

Then, in the face of the questioning gazes from everyone, I calmly said, "Many of you must have heard about my previous involvement with the Bryant family. During this time, I am very grateful to Quinn for always being by my side, accompanying me through this difficult journey in life. I know he did it out of kindness, wanting to protect me and be there for me, but I cannot delay his own life, and I cannot deceive your feelings. This child has no connection with Quinn. In fact, Quinn and I are just friends. I am very grateful for the care and nurturing your family has shown me during this time. I'm sorry, I lied to you."

I finished saying these words in one breath, then stood up and bowed deeply to Quinn's parents to express my apologies.

After experiencing the events of the Bryant family and getting to know Laura, I actually realized that Quinn's parents were very easygoing people. If I could become Quinn's wife, I would have a peaceful and happy life in the later years.

However, I had already lost the chance to be with Quinn. I didn't have such luck, so I could only go this far with Quinn.

My sudden confession caught everyone off guard. I saw Jessica's right hand still raised in mid-air. It was evident that she was genuinely surprised.

"Quinn, is this true? What on earth is going on with you guys? You've completely baffled me!"

Carole snapped out of it at this moment. She glanced at me, then at Quinn, and asked incredulously.

"Quinn, speak up. Is this true?"

The cheerful sound of Zara's chirping came over, but at this moment, it seemed a bit untimely.

Quinn's originally friendly face instantly turned cold.

He looked at me deeply, his eyes filled with a strong sense of helplessness.

Seeing that I had already confessed, he had no choice but to say, "It wasn't Madeline's intention to hide this matter, it was me who didn't want you to know. I chose to be with Madeline, so no matter what her situation is, I accept it and I will treat the child as my own. Dad, Mom, please don't blame Madeline. This has nothing to do with her."
