Chapter 437 Am I Pregnant?

We arrived at the hospital where Fiona worked just as the rain stopped.

Quinn pulled me along, not bothering to notify Fiona ahead of time, and we headed straight for her office.

Unexpectedly, she was on duty tonight.

Even more surprisingly, when we opened the door, we found Alexis in her office as well.

It was late at night, and almost no patients were around, leaving the corridors deserted. So, when we entered, Alexis and Fiona were in each other's arms.

They probably never expected someone to walk in at that moment. They quickly disentangled themselves, their faces a mix of panic and shock.

It wasn't until they saw it was Quinn and me that they somewhat regained their composure.

"What are you guys doing here? Why come so late?" Fiona asked.

Fiona wore a lab coat, I noticed her voice was hoarse, and her eyes were red as if she had just been crying.

"Madeline's not feeling well. She keeps throwing up lately and wants you to check on her."

Quinn watched Alexis and Fiona with a smirk as if he knew something but chose not to expose it, simply stating coolly.

"Now? That's incredibly rude. If you need to see a doctor, you should make an appointment first. How could you just barge into the doctor's office..."

By this point, Alexis had regained his composure. He glared at Quinn and couldn't help but retort.

"If we hadn't barged in, how would we have witnessed that spectacle? It's late. Why aren't you resting instead of sneaking into the doctor's office?"

Quinn, fully aware of the embarrassing situation, deliberately pointed it out, causing Fiona to blush instantly, while Alexis turned awkward.

"Mind your own business..."

Alexis lowered his head, at a loss for words, and muttered under his breath.

Fiona walked over and pulled me aside.

"Your face is so pale. Why do I smell alcohol on you? Did you drink tonight?"

"I had a little drink. I don't know why, but I just can't handle alcohol these days. I throw up as soon as I drink," I confessed honestly.

"She's been throwing up a lot lately. Fiona, could you check on her? She's not pregnant, is she?"

Quinn chimed in, his comment turning Fiona's face pale.

"No way? She couldn't possibly be..." Fiona's initial reaction was just like mine. However, she suddenly asked me, "Have you been taking the medication Dr. Cullen prescribed you on time?"

I nodded. "Yes, I've been following the prescription strictly."

Fiona then had a thought.

"That could be possible. Dr. Cullen is really skilled in this area. Madeline, most doctors are off now, so you should go get a blood test first."

After saying this, Fiona arranged for a blood test for me.

Skeptically, I went for the blood test, and afterward, I waited quietly in Fiona's office for the results.

Initially, I had no hopes regarding being pregnant.

Yet, Fiona's words inexplicably made my heart race.
