Chapter 122 Lost

"Madeline, your necklace is so beautiful. Is it a high-quality pearl necklace?"

She looked as if we already had a good relationship.

A girl who came from a small town and lost her father at a young age could instantly tell that this was a high-quality pearl. Her perception alone is enough to show that I cannot underestimate her.

And she deliberately acted intimate with me in front of Laura, which made me even more cautious. However, I didn't want to show it.

"When I was planning the jewelry event for someone else before, I had found out. It turns out it was Laura who gave it to you. Laura is really kind to you."

After hearing that it was Laura who gave it to me, there was a hint of disappointment in Giselle's eyes.

And I saw all of her subtle reactions. But I just smiled and didn't say much.

Laura smiled and said. Her meaning was that, in her mind, she saw Giselle and me the same way.

"How can that be possible? I cannot afford such expensive gifts. Besides, this necklace only looks good on Madeline. I am still too young and not worthy of such luxury."

Giselle quickly and obediently said.

Upon hearing her compliment, Laura turned her head and gave me a satisfied look, then nodded with a smile.

"Yes, this pearl necklace complements Madeline's skin even more delicate. She indeed looks beautiful."

"Mrs. Laura Bryant, did you give the medicine I brought to you last time to Mrs. Bryant? The doctor said that medicine has a very good effect. If Mrs. Bryant has finished drinking it, I will go and buy some more for her."

Nicole walked up to Laura at this moment and said,

Medicine?

I instantly recalled that Sean had indeed handed me a few packets of medicine last time, saying that Laura specifically instructed me to drink them. However, I had thrown those medicines into the trash can.

Originally, it turned out that Nicole had helped to buy that medicine.

I was instantly shocked.

In the previous life, under Laura's coercion, I drank medicine hundreds of times. However, until my dying moments, I still couldn't conceive Elliott's child.

Not only did I not have any children, but I also got stomach cancer at a young age.

However, no one in my family had ever heard of anyone dying from stomach cancer. Moreover, I was still so young, not even 30 years old at the time.

If Nicole bought the medicine, is it possible that they tampered with it?

Could it be that my infertility and stomach cancer in my previous life were related to this medicine?

Did they start planning from now on, or did they make their move later?

I directly kept this matter in my heart.

"Oh, I just finished drinking that medicine. If it's convenient, could you please help me buy some more? Laura really hopes that Elliott and I can have a child now. If I get pregnant, I will definitely thank you very much."

A wave of doubt surged in my heart, but on my face, I displayed a calm expression.

I had already thrown away the previous medicine, so I could let her buy some again and then ask Fiona to analyze the ingredients for me. In this way, I would be able to know if my suspicion was true or false.
