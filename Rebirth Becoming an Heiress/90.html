Chapter 90 Blushing

"What do you think I brought for you?"

I lifted the food bag in my hand. As soon as he saw the packaging bag, he immediately knew and showed a faint smile.

"Did you go to Listen to the Wind?"

"Yes. I remember you loved eating their foie gras, so I packed a portion for you and brought it over."

I walked over, opened the bag, took out the foie gras, and placed it in front of him.

Elliott saw this scene and his eyes softened. He didn't hesitate and gracefully put a piece of foie gras into his mouth, praising, "Still so delicious."

"Yeah, it's very tender. Have another piece."

I smiled and raised my eyebrows, indicating him to try again.

There was a layer of ice under the lunch box, so the foie gras was very fresh. Paired with blueberry sauce, the foie gras tasted unique and tender. Because I used to love Elliott, I fell in love with the food he liked.

Elliott tried another piece of foie gras. It was evident that foie gras suited his taste very well. His smile became even brighter.

Listen to the Wind's food portion was not large but exquisite. Before long, Elliott finished eating the foie gras.

He wiped his mouth satisfactorily with a tissue, then he smiled and asked me, "Why did you suddenly bring me my favorite food today?"

I chuckled and said, "I just had dinner with Paige today. Thinking that you love foie gras, I packed it up and brought it over to you."

"Did you see Paige?"

Elliott leaned back in his office chair. He wasn't very busy and started chatting with me.

"Yes. We hadn't seen each other for a long time, so we had a meal together. By the way, you were looking for a long-term flower supplier before. Paige's flower plantation is excellent. I have visited it. There are many types of flowers, and she also has a professional flower arrangement team."

I deliberately brought up this topic, wanting to know how Elliott would explain it.

When Elliott heard me say that, his eyebrows twitched slightly, and there was a hint of mischief in his gaze.

He stared at me meaningfully. He was clever enough to understand my intention of trying to please him.

"I knew you wouldn't be kind to me for no reason."

Upon hearing his words, I walked behind him and massaged his shoulders.

"The Purchasing Department has already compared. Paige's flowers are not only fresh and diverse in variety, but also available for long-term supply. Moreover, her prices are reasonable. The Bryant Group is a large enterprise that requires a large quantity of flowers and plants. Can you help support Paige's business?"

My icy fingers touched Elliott's neck as I gently massaged him. He felt comfortable and didn't push me away. Instead, he held onto my hands, causing me to lean and press against his back.

We were too close. I was unguarded and blushed.

"I considered Paige. You know Giselle helped me last time, and I owe her a favor. Besides, my father also owes a favor to their family. So, I plan to have Giselle's mother supply the flowers, which can be considered as support to her family."

Elliott gently caressed my hand. His voice was soft. Compared to his previous indifference towards me, he had become gentle.

At least, we were no longer hostile towards each other. In this moment, we were like a couple discussing matters.
