Chapter 462 Acting

"Elliott, Madeline loves me now. You two are not a match, so you should get divorced as soon as possible."

Before I could speak, Quinn suddenly held my hand, looked at Elliott, and said with a serious look.

Elliott stood there. At that moment, he looked gloomy and sounded extremely cold.

"What did you say?"

"Can't you feel it? Madeline doesn't love you anymore. Because she loves me now."

Quinn emphasized again. And his words made me shocked.

I was stunned for a few seconds, and then I quickly understood Quinn did this to let Elliott let me go.

"Do you think I will believe you?"

Elliott's body was slightly stiff, and his eyes were getting colder. However, he suddenly smiled coldly, and then raised his head.

Elliott was too proud to believe that a woman who loved him so much would suddenly fall in love with someone else.

I looked at Elliott and then at Quinn. At that moment, the arrogance in Elliott's look broke my defenses.

I admitted that he was like my inner demon in both my previous life and this life. I still couldn't resist his charm and would fall in love with him easily again.

But now, I didn't want to let myself fall in love with him again.

I liked being sober and independent, and I didn't want to fall into a relationship that had gone bad again. I didn't want to bet everything on a meaningless journey.

Since he didn't believe it, I should take some actions to make him believe it.

So, I made up my mind. Before Quinn could react, I stood in front of Elliott, hooked Quinn's neck, and kissed his face without hesitation.

Quinn's body visibly stiffened for a moment.

"Elliott, do you believe it now? "

After kissing Quinn's face, I pretended to be unsatisfied and leaned on Quinn's arms, staring coldly at Elliott and waiting for his reaction.

He gradually lost hope and stared at me. There seemed to be complex emotions in his look, which made me tremble slightly for no reason.

Fortunately, Quinn knew my intentions.

He quickly came to his senses. He held my waist firmly. To make Elliott desperate, Quinn deliberately said,

"You should have felt it, Madeline is obviously happier when she stays with me. Elliott, why don't you let Madeline stay with me?"

After Quinn finished his words, Elliott's eyes turned red.

Clearly, he was enraged. His expression became more and more severe, and his eyes became more and more gloomy.

He directly punched hard towards the corner of Quinn's eye!

I exclaimed and subconsciously wanted to stop him, but it was too late.

Quinn didn't dodge, but he just smiled and said nothing.

"You make me sick!"

Elliott growled, his hoarse voice showing that he was in pain. His voice echoed in the empty corridor as if the whole world had become quiet.

As if being greatly stimulated, Elliott clutched his chest tightly and walked away with particularly heavy steps.

He walked quickly, his heavy footsteps echoing in the corridor.

My heart was beating hard. The moment I saw him disappearing from the corner of the corridor, I seemed to have removed all my defenses, slumped against the wall weakly, and gasped for air.

I just realized that my back was covered in cold sweat. The moment I leaned against the wall, I felt extremely cold on my back.

Quinn then let go of his hand. He looked at me and said distressedly,

"You can't go on like this. Madeline, would you blame me for making arbitrary decisions? "
