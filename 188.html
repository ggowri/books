How could Donna not want Clarissa to be happy?

How could Donna not know Apollo was not a good man?

Clarissa was the Fatebearer of the Radcliffe family. Her fate had long been determined, and it was not something Donna could change, nor was it something Clarissa and Osborne could change.

"Why can't we bear it? Osborne..."
