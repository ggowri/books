As they reached the deck, they found that Osborne had already arrived.

The Alisa Ship would definitely be severely damaged!

This was the reason why the Augustine family's ships had an accident before.

No matter how careful the escort team was, it was difficult to avoid a group of pirates who wanted nothing more than to blow up the ship.

But Osborne discovered this group of pirates in advance.

Not only did he detect them, but he also effortlessly dealt with the pirates, eliminating them two miles away, without giving them any opportunity to attack the Alisa Ship.

The enemies were all dead two miles away!

Ordinary Class A masters could not do this.

Even the two Class A- elders from the Augustine family in the dark looked at each other with shock in their eyes.

Because they couldn't kill the enemy from two miles away.
