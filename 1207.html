He had found it in Cain's memory, and it was one of the most powerful techniques out there.

If Bryce harnessed the power of Phoenix Ascension, he could boost his Divine Body by two levels, potentially reaching a Level Four or even a Level Five Divine Body!

They'd be operating on entirely different planes!

There was even a chance he could reach the ultimate Level Eight Galactic Divine Body!

Many Deity Kings could only claim a Level Seven Solar Divine Body.

If Bryce secured a Level Five Divine Body at this stage, he'd have a clear path to the Deity King realm, assuming he stayed alive.

The incredible power of Phoenix Ascension was now sinking in for the group, and they were left utterly stunned.

Their eyes gleamed with fervor and ambition.
