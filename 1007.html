Osborne was still reeling from the backlash of self-destructing three Realms. Blood poured from his mouth, his face pale as a ghost.

He barely had time to process his pain before his eyes landed on the still-standing Plant Realm, shocked.

"Why? With close to a thousand people attacking, alongside dozens of Realm self-detonations, we haven't managed to shatter Sin's Plant Realm?

It's impossible!"

Osborne's gaze locked onto Sin with an intensity that could burn.

Suddenly, a peal of laughter rang out.

Osborne turned to see Crimson laughed in Sin’s arms despite blood at her lips. Her vitality was extremely low, yet her eyes glinted with hatred as they bore into him.

She taunted, her voice dripping with malice, "Osborne, are you feeling desperate?

"You think your attacks hit my husband's Plant Realm? Think again! I used my Dark Realm to deflect all of them!

"Now, it's your turn to face hell!

"Sin! Finish them off!"

Crimson shouted, her eyes seething with fire as they fixed on Osborne.

Her words pierced through the haze of confusion in Osborne's mind.

His expression turned grim.

Who would have thought that despite her wounds, Crimson was willing to risk her life to protect Sin with her Realm?

With Sin's Plant Realm still intact, their situation had grown dire.

They couldn't mount another assault like that again.

"Don't worry, my love. I swear I'll wipe them out—revenge for you and our daughter!

"Go to hell! All of you!" Sin growled.

In the next moment, from within that relentless Plant Realm, more vines and roots shot out like gigantic whips, blocking out the sky as they came crashing down upon everyone.

Osborne, Greyson, and Roy—targeted by Sin's fury—were sent crashing through the air, their bodies battered as they spewed blood.

Dozens of Lambert family members exploded inside bodies.

Osborne's face twisted in anguish. If they didn't come up with a plan soon, they would all die here.

"The power gap is just too vast!" Osborne thought, his fists clenched tight.

He'd already used too much strength, and now, facing a new round of attacks, he found even self-preservation to be a struggle.

He failed to fight against Sin!

Compared to a top-tier Class S like Sin, his Class AAA status felt laughable. No matter how hard he fought, he was outmatched.

"Am I really going to die here?"

Osborne felt the despair creeping in as he was flung away again by Sin's relentless strikes. Frustration gnawed at him.

"If I could just break through to Class S, even if I couldn't take Sin down, I'd never find myself in a situation like this! I wouldn't let him kill my people so easily!"

The hunger for power gripped him, a desperate longing to ascend to Class S.

Osborne knew he was strong—strong enough to take out many Class S opponents with ease—but it still wasn't enough! He had to evolve further to live fearlessly in this world.

However, transforming to Class S from Class AAA wasn't an easy feat; it was a colossal leap, a transformative journey that couldn't be forced.

Time and time again, Osborne felt Sin's attacks hurl him away, leaving him battered and broken.

As for the Lambert family, they were beginning to falter under the relentless assault.

Each strike from Sin resulted in a multitude of their members exploding, and now only about six hundreds of them remained.

The once formidable Class S duo of Greyson and Roy now lay grievously wounded.

Under the relentless onslaught, it seemed their extinction was imminent.

All around, the Lambert family faced utter despair as they beheld Sin's monstrous Plant Realm, a canopy of colossal roots and vines that seemed to blot out the sky.

"Is this the end for us?" they murmured. They couldn’t defend Sin's attacks anymore.

But just as all hope seemed lost, a deafening rumble erupted from within Sin's Plant Realm. The realm shook violently, as if struck by an external force of unimaginable power.

Within moments, the Plant Realm's structure fractured and, shattered.

Sin, taken by surprise, spat out a mouthful of blood and looked up, shocked.

There, in the skies above, he spotted a swarm of helicopters approaching with urgency.

Before they could even touch down, figures began leaping out. There were 500 of them, most of whom are Class AAA warriors.

It was this force that had breached and shattered Sin's Plant Realm from the outside.

They were members of the Blood Demon.

Osborne, witnessing the descent of the Blood Demon warriors, swiftly wiped the blood from his lips, a cold, vindictive smile spreading across his face.

"Finally!" Osborne muttered, glaring at the now visibly nervous Sin.

"Had your fun, didn't you? Well, now it's my turn.

"All Blood Demon members, listen up!

"Kill him!"
