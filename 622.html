Training Base No. 6 was nestled in a canyon.

Osborne felt someone gaze at him as he approached within three miles of the area.

He activated his Satan's Eye to scan the surroundings.

He quickly discovered that at least a dozen individuals were lurking in hiding, well-disguised. Without the immense power of his Satan's Eye, Osborne would have struggled to detect them.

Their powers were at Class B+ or A-.

"Seems like they're all students from Training Base No. 6! Should I test their strength first?"

With this thought, Osborne couldn't help but chuckle.

With his current strength, dealing with Class B+ or A- metahumans was akin to handling ordinary people. Even facing a regular Class A metahuman, he could defeat them with ease.

But he was curious about their techniques.

"If I come across a few stronger ones, I might as well give it a try!"

Osborne murmured, and his body vanished in an instant.

He utilized the dark elemental power to move through the shadows of the forest swiftly.

Back when David used the dark elemental power to sneak around, Osborne had a hard time detecting him even with his Satan's Eye active.

Now, as he used this power to move stealthily, the sentries outside Training Base No. 6 naturally had no chance of noticing him.

Osborne swiftly passed through their defense perimeter and arrived at Training Base No. 6.

At this moment, the canyon was bustling with activity. Twenty instructors and five hundred students—excluding those on guard duty—were all present.

Osborne instantly zeroed in on the twenty instructors.

Then, a look of pleasant surprise appeared on his face.

"Not bad. Three Class AA and eight Class A+ metahumans! Although not all are Class AA, it's still quite impressive. They will be a tremendous force if I utilize them well."

Osborne hid in the shadows, observing their training.

Their training essentially involved sparring, enhancing their abilities, and gaining more combat experience through real battles.

After watching for a while, Osborne couldn't help but be amazed.

He found that all the instructors and students at Training Base No. 6 were skilled martial artists.

Each of them had mastered numerous advanced martial arts techniques, such as Spiral Force, Ninefold Tide Crash, Stretch Soft Force, and even Point Blank Force.

Every one of the hundreds of students had at least one advanced martial art technique; some had mastered several, and all had reached a basic level of proficiency, with some even achieving proficiency level.

The instructors were even more formidable.

Each had mastered multiple advanced martial arts techniques, all at least at the proficiency level.

Osborne even saw an instructor who had taken Stretch Soft Force to the extreme level. He was astonished.

After all, even Osborne hadn't taken Stretch Soft Force to the extreme. He had only reached the extreme level with Spiral Force and Ninefold Tide Crash.

"Impressive!"

Osborne was delighted.

The instructors and students worked together with perfect coordination, like actual soldiers.

In his eyes, they were a group of mighty warriors who had undergone rigorous training. Their individual strength might not be extreme, but their combined strength would be formidable.

They could unleash formidable power on the battlefield at any time.

What truly surprised Osborne was not this but the fact that each of them possessed the same shape-shifting power—Vampire Transformation.

All twenty instructors and five hundred students could transform into Vampires, enhancing their speed, strength, defense, and recovery capabilities.

However, their appearance after transformation was slightly different from Osborne's Vampire form.

They didn't have blood-red long hair. Their fangs were shorter, and their claws were also shorter.

In short, compared to Osborne's Vampire Transformation, their transformation seemed to be a weakened version, but it still greatly enhanced their strength.

Most importantly, since so many people possessed Vampire Transformation, it must not have been naturally activated but caused by some kind of genetic potion.

That was to say, Training Base No. 6 had the means to enable ordinary people to activate the power of Vampire Transformation.

Feeling their Vampire Transformation, Osborne suddenly found that the Blood Cross in his body seemed particularly excited, with waves of powerful energy surging.

"Strange, why is it reacting like this? Could it be that these people's Vampire Transformation is related to my Blood Cross?" Osborne thought quickly.

"Who's there? Who is it?!"

Suddenly, a loud shout rang out, and then the twenty instructors and nearly five hundred students of Training Base No. 6 moved in an instant, surrounding the place where Osborne was hiding in the blink of an eye.

Osborne was surprised. He had been discovered.

Perhaps it was because the Blood Cross's abnormal reaction had leaked a bit of energy fluctuation.

However, the fact that such a slight fluctuation exposed him showed that these instructors and students were indeed well-trained and capable.

Thinking of this, Osborne smiled, withdrawing the dark elemental power.

His body appeared slowly into their view.

Everyone had a hostile look at his appearance. It was unacceptable for them that someone had infiltrated the training base.

"We got an intruder! Kill him, no matter who he is!"

A stern shout rang out, and a group of instructors and students immediately took action.
