As Osborne had predicted, he had ranked number one in the ranking list once again.

Moreover, people who ranked second were nowhere near him.

Duke Medusa, Duke Titan, and Duke Werewolf, who had reached Class AAA, brought him three thousand points.

A total of sixteen masters who reached Class AA brought him another sixteen hundred points.

Then, one hundred and eighty-four people who reached Class A and Class A+ brought him a total of two thousand four hundred points.

Finally, they snatched the headquarters in Dampston back, so Osborne got a thousand points.

In this battle, Osborne received eight thousand points.

With the three thousand points he had before, his total points had broken through ten thousand, reaching eleven thousand points.

At this moment, the person who ranked second was still Vincent, who had already withdrawn from the heir selection.

He only had three thousand five hundred points, seven thousand five hundred less than Osborne.

Emma came in third place today, and her score was three thousand three hundred points.

Matthew came in fourth with three thousand two hundred points.

Then, Owen came in fourth place with two thousand nine hundred points.

Finally, Lance's score hadn't grown in a long time, being negative two thousand points or so.

In truth, Lance had already quit the heir selection.

Vincent had also quit the heir selection due to a serious injury.

The competition between Emma, Matthew and Owen was unbelievable.

"Who will get second place?" Osborne murmured.

"Will it be Emma, the most gifted of the Augustine family's younger generation? Or Matthew, the most resourceful and popular of the younger generation? Or Owen?"

Looking at Owen's points, Osborne didn't feel right. He just couldn't imagine how Owen had gotten so many points, not losing out to Emma and Vincent.

"Could that be because of Lance?"

"Lance's score hasn't grown in a long time. Could it be that Lance gave up his chance to help Owen?"

Osborne stroked his chin, thinking that this was the only possible explanation for Owen's unusual points.

However, Osborne soon didn't care.

With the power he had now, Owen and Lance were way out of his league. Even if they teamed up, they couldn't touch him."Keep up the counterattack! By the time we've completely destroyed the Aberrants in Dampston, I should have enough points to ensure I get first place! As for who comes in second place, it's none of my business!"

Thinking about it, Osborne began to cultivate.

At this moment, he didn't know that his several competitors were all silent when they saw the scoreboard, their eyes filled with despair.

They took a chance on their plan of attack and ended up winning. Their points were close to Osborne's, and they had expected to overtake them soon.

Osborne had scored eight thousand points in just one battle, bringing the gap between them once again to a level that made them almost despair.

It had been almost half a month since the Aberrants had started this battle, and they had gone through more than a dozen battles with a lot of losses.

But they got far less than eight thousand total points as well.

Under the circumstances, even if Emma had absolute confidence, she couldn't help but begin to doubt herself.

"How the hell did he do that?"

Emma frowned tightly and turned to an older man behind her, "Grandpa, Caleb's with Osborne, right? He must know the situation. You and Caleb have a good relationship, so why don't you ask him for me? I'm really curious."

The old man nodded when he heard that.

He was Emma's grandfather, James Augustine, the fourth elder of the main bloodline from the Augustine family.

Since Osborne had gotten eight thousand points, not only was Emma curious, he was also inquisitive.

"I'll contact Caleb, but I can't guarantee he'll tell me."

With that, James began to make a phone call.

Emma waited patiently.

After a while, James finally hung up the phone, his face full of surprise.

"Grandpa, what's going on?"

Emma asked in a hurry, "Is it true that Osborne has found a powerful helper?"

James nodded.

"Caleb told me that they counterattacked the headquarters in Dampston, killing three members from the Aberrants who had reached Class AAA and a dozen warriors who had reached Class AA."

"Three Class AAA? A dozen Class AA? That’s sick!"

Emma murmured, "With Caleb, Janet, and Osborne, they have a way to do it."

James shook his head. "That wasn't the case. Caleb told me none of them had even attacked, not even Osborne."

"The attacker was Fatebearer from the Radcliffe family!"

"She killed all three warriors of the Aberrants who reached Class AAA all on her own! Also, it only took her a short time."

Emma was stunned when James finished speaking.

"Fatebearer from the Radcliffe family? How is that possible? I've heard of her, but I remember she's not even twenty years old. How could she have such power?"

James said seriously, "She's not an ordinary woman! She makes me think of someone."

"Who?"

"Vivienne!"

"Vivienne?"

Emma became incredibly surprised. Although she had never met Vivienne, she had heard her name countless times.

Vivienne had reached Class AAA when she was less than twenty years old!

She married Godfather's son.

Vivienne was also Emma's idol.

Emma took a deep breath. "So that's it! We can explain that. Why is she helping Osborne though?"

James said, "She's Osborne's girlfriend."

Emma looked shocked when she heard it.
