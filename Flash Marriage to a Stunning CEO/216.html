At that moment, she didn't even seem human. She looked like a ghost that had crawled out of a horror movie to claim a soul.

Once he got out, he'd make her pay—strip her of everything, ship her off to some godforsaken place, and leave her begging for death for the rest of her miserable life.

Just keep her calm. Say what she wants to hear. Stay alive.
