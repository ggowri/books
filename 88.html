Justin’s words caused Osborne, who was eavesdropping downstairs, to suddenly reveal a murderous glint in his eyes.

“Justin, you’re courting death.”

In recent days, Osborne had been busy completing the Godfather’s heir test, so he hadn’t sought trouble with Justin yet. He didn’t expect that Justin would actually want to hire a hitman to kill him and even get caught by him in the act.

Given this, Osborne could only strike first.

Osborne didn’t rush to act. He wanted to hear more of their conversation.

“Mr. Caldwell, are you overthinking this? Milo is a Class C assassin in the organization with powerful psychic gifted. The person you want to kill is just a waste, right? Could he really turn the tables on Milo?”

Davy sneered, “Milo must have been delayed by something. Just wait for the news. The organization has more important matters to attend to in Mayby City right now and can’t spare the manpower.”

Justin frowned and raised his voice. “Mr. Jones, I paid a ten million dollars, but the person I want dead is still alive. I suspect you’re not really trying to help me.”

Davy was getting impatient. “Alright, alright, stop shouting. Give me the information on the person you want dead, and I’ll arrange for someone to take care of it. Happy now?”

Justin smiled. “The person I want dead is named Osborne.”

“Osborne? Why does that name sound familiar?”

Davy was surprised, then suddenly exclaimed, “You said Osborne? Could it be the Osborne who just joined the Radcliffe family as Clarissa’s bodyguard?”

Justin was also surprised. “You’ve heard of him?”

Davy’s expression grew serious.

“Heard of him? This guy has messed up big plans for the organization. If it’s him, then Milo might indeed be in trouble. Even our mole in the Radcliffe family is wary of this guy.”

Hearing that, Justin grew even more anxious. “What should I do then?”

“That bastard Osborne has been at my family for three years. I always thought he was a useless fool, but who knew he was hiding so deeply?

“If it weren’t for him, I would have killed that bitch Ophelia long ago and become the Caldwell family’s heir. Only as the Caldwell family heir can I fully support the organization. Now that Milo is in trouble, that bastard will definitely guess it’s me and come to kill me.”

Justin was getting agitated.

Davy, however, smiled faintly and said, “No need to worry. That guy might have some skills, but he won’t live much longer. Tomorrow night, the organization has a major operation against the Radcliffe family. He’s as good as dead. Just wait for the good news, Mr. Caldwell.

“If you’re really scared, stay here for a few days, and enjoy yourself. It’s absolutely safe here. No one can harm you.”

Hearing this, Justin happily nodded.

“Then I’ll stay here for a few days. Once that waste Osborne is completely dead, I’ll go back. I won’t bother you, Mr. Jones. I’ll just go downstairs to look around.”

Davy nodded and had someone escort Justin downstairs.

Downstairs, Osborne’s eyes were filled with joy.

He didn’t expect to gather so much useful information from Davy and Justin’s conversation.

First, Davy was indeed connected to the Aberrants, possibly as their liaison.

Second, the Aberrants had a mole in the Radcliffe family, most likely Paul.

Third, the Aberrants were likely scheming against the major families, and Justin had long sided with them.

Fourth, and most crucially, tomorrow night, the Aberrants would launch a major operation against Clarissa.

Osborne’s eyes turned cold.

Seeing Justin come down to watch the fight by the octagon, Osborne’s eyes filled with murderous intent.

He swore that Justin must die.

Moreover, he needed more precise information from Davy. The information he had now was mostly conjecture and wouldn’t easily earn the Radcliffe family’s trust.

With this in mind, Osborne decided to act.

Using his Satan’s Eye, he released a burst of ocular energy, enveloping a member of Maelstrom.

This Maelstrom member suddenly felt his body freeze, bound by a tremendous force controlling his arm to draw his gun from its holster.

Then, the gun swiftly aimed at Justin’s head.

In the next second, a gunshot echoed through the fight club. Justin’s head exploded like a watermelon, blood splattering onto the faces of the surrounding guests.

Justin didn’t even know what happened before he died.

Chaos erupted instantly in the fight club.

The crowd screamed in terror, frantically fleeing and trampling over each other. The Maelstrom member who fired the shot was dumbfounded.

“No, it wasn’t me,” he shouted, but no one listened. His arm, still uncontrollably, raised the gun and fired at other Maelstrom members.

He fired three shots in quick succession!

Immediately, two Maelstrom members fell to the ground, hit by bullets.

More Maelstrom members finally reacted, quickly taking cover and returning fire. The sound of gunfire filled the fight club.

It was utter chaos.

Many Maelstrom members tried to regain control, but bullets from their members’ guns kept them all on edge, not daring to trust anyone.

At this moment, Osborne took advantage of the confusion to rush upstairs.
