They hadn't seen each other for over a year, but Isa's habits remained unchanged.

Even if Isa were a hundred times stronger, she would still have to beg for mercy in front of him.

Soon, Isa collapsed weakly onto Osborne's chest, resting her face against it, her expression full of contentment.

"Honey, you're even more amazing!" At this moment, Isa gazed seductively at Osborne's face, her eyes seemingly unable to hold anything else.

Osborne stroked her smooth back.

For Osborne, the pleasure of breaking through the cultivation level was intense, even surpassing that of physical intimacy. However, Osborne preferred the ecstasy of being with Isa.

Because it was not just a physical connection but a fusion of souls. This emotional exchange was something he could never experience when breaking through the cultivation level.

Osborne could feel Clarissa's immense excitement as she eagerly responded to him.

Soon after, Clarissa also begged for mercy.

After "subduing" five women, Osborne then woke up Anastasia, Zoe, Mila, Kendra, Bryce, Hannah, and the others.

Adriana waved her hand. "No need to be so formal. Otherwise, Osborne might blame me for not treating you well enough."

However, Anastasia widened her eyes and said, "Ms. Alba, what did you just say? Did you say Osborne's strength is about the same as yours? How is that possible?"

"You are the strongest warrior on Racial Convergence Planet. Except for the three who have reached Sovereign Proto-imperial Empyrean Sacredness, no one can match you."

Anastasia clearly didn't believe Adriana's words, and neither did the others.

Because Osborne used the Sublime Concealment Orb to hide his cultivation level and energy, they still don't know his true cultivation level.

At this moment, Osborne smiled and said, "Ms. Alba's words are a bit exaggerated. My current strength is still quite far from Ms. Alba's."

Immediately, Osborne withdrew the effect of the Sublime Concealment Orb.

Instantly, the energy of Empyrean Sacredness Great Perfection spread out, leaving everyone stunned.

She thought, "No wonder I felt Osborne was stronger than before. Turns out, Osborne's cultivation level has surpassed mine by so much."

Isa took just over a year to break through from Ascension Nine Supernal Deity King to advanced Empyrean Sacredness, so she originally thought her cultivation level had already surpassed Osborne's.

However, Osborne gave them a huge shock.

At this moment, everyone felt a bit envious.

"Although your cultivation level is only at Empyrean Sacredness Great Perfection, which is lower than mine, your actual strength is almost the same as mine. On the entire Racial Convergence Planet, among those at the same cultivation level as you, no one could be your match, right?"

They each get thirty emperor fruits? Are these emperor fruits enough to elevate them to Proto-imperial Empyrean Sacredness, or even Supreme Proto-imperial Empyrean Sacredness or Sovereign Proto-imperial Empyrean Sacredness?

Osborne felt a bit helpless.

He smiled and said, "Ms. Alba, you should be close to reaching Imperial Empyrean Sacredness, right? Can these hundred emperor fruits help you achieve it?"
