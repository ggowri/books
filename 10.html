Osborne was calm now.

He looked at Ophelia and said coolly, “What do you want me to prove?”

“Compared to the Caldwell family’s billions, what does a sister matter? Would you turn down ten billion dollars if it meant losing a sibling?”

Osborne’s words left Ophelia visibly shaken.

Penelope’s jaw dropped, completely stunned.

Doubt flickered across her pretty face.

Could Justin really be using Osborne’s bomb to kill Ophelia? Would he even sacrifice his sister in the process?

“No, no. Something’s off. You must be lying. The explosives on you must be fake,” Penelope insisted.

Osborne’s eyes flashed coldly as he snapped, “Penelope, stop being naïve. If Justin is so sure the explosives are fake, why doesn’t he come here and expose me himself? Why send you?”

“Because he’s scared!”

“He’s afraid I’ll detonate the bomb and kill us all.” Osborne’s words left everyone in silence.

They bowed their heads, deep in thought, realizing Osborne might be right.

Doubt began to spread. They started to suspect Justin.

Even if the bomb was fake, they’d just kill Osborne and get back their $20 million.

But what if it was real?

They’d all die!

The gamble wasn’t worth it.

Penelope, her face red with anger, said, “You’re lying. Justin would never harm me. I’ll video call him right now, and you can confront him. You’re about to get exposed.”

Then, she whipped out her phone.

In no time, a video call connected, revealing Justin’s handsome face.

Beside him was a man in his forties, bruised and terrified.

Osborne frowned. It was the grocery store owner.

Justin, calm and collected, yanked the owner’s hair and smiled, “Tell Osborne here what you just told me. What did that loser do in your store? No lies, or you’ll regret it.”

The owner, trembling, nodded frantically. “I’ll tell, I’ll tell.”

He then recounted how Osborne used his tools and scrap materials to make fake explosives.

His story was so detailed that it couldn’t be made up.

Justin sneered, “Hmph, let’s see what you have to say now.”

Penelope quickly explained, “Bro, Osborne said you tricked us. He claims it’s your plot to use his explosives to kill us all.”

“Bro, tell me it’s not true, right?”

Justin’s face darkened instantly. “You sneaky loser. No matter how you twist it, I saw through your lies.”

“Are you sure the explosives are fake? Why don’t you try them yourself?” Osborne smirked.

“You think I won’t?”

“Go ahead, I’m waiting,” Osborne replied coldly.

“Ophelia is my ex-wife. No matter what, we had feelings. I don’t want to drag her to hell unless I have to.”

“But you?”

“Why don’t you try, asshole?”

Osborne’s eyes gleamed with a manic grin.
