Not only did he successfully toy with Lionel and seize Lionel's Supreme Sacred Artifact, the Void Sword, but he also shifted the trouble onto Lionel, allowing himself to escape.

As for other aspects, there's basically no room for improvement.

Just in time to use this period to search for my parents. They were sold as slaves to the Stellar Exploration Organization, Frenetic Storm Warband. Within this half year, I must find the Frenetic Storm Warband and rescue my parents!"

This path leading to the depths of the universe is still full of dangers for Osborne at this moment. Since no one is chasing him now, it's best for him to stay away from them.

"The Sacred Path of the Empyrean Sovereign leads to the depths of the universe.

I'm doomed!
