The two Class S assassins, sent flying by Osborne's kick, were left reeling. The force of that blow, powered by his Divine Bone, was enough to shatter bones and send them spewing blood in a gruesome display.

While such injuries weren't necessarily fatal for Class S fighters, they certainly left the assassins looking pale and shaken.

As they stared at the imposing figure of Osborne, a chill ran down their spines.

It finally clicked for them why he had dispatched Scarlett so quickly. They had rushed to her aid the moment they received her distress signal, but it had only taken a matter of seconds for Osborne to end her life.

Scarlett was no slouch; she was among the top-tier geniuses, someone who could hold her own against ordinary Class S fighters.

Yet, in the face of Osborne, she hadn't lasted even a few seconds before meeting her demise.

Now, they understood why Osborne topped the Godson Rankings.

"Such incredible power!"

They exchanged a glance, their eyes burning with determination.

Despite Osborne's overwhelming strength, they were resolute in their mission to avenge Scarlett.

They shouted in unison, their expressions hardening, "Alright! Osborne, you've sealed your fate by killing Scarlett. You've earned the wrath of the Eityreia Organization, and everyone connected to you will pay the price with blood!

"But you won't live to see it! We'll kill you first!

"Realm, unleash!"

In an instant, both assassins activated their Realms.

One enveloped himself in a Dark Realm, while the other summoned an Earth Realm. Though both were only Level Three, their Class S prowess made them formidable, perhaps even surpassing Osborne's Light Realm.

Osborne's expression turned serious as he realized the gravity of the situation.

These two assassins were leagues above the Class S clones he had faced before.

He had been able to take down numerous Class S clones with his Class AAA strength because those clones lacked a Realm.

They were merely at the Class S power level without the true essence that came with a Realm.

"Kill him!"

With renewed vigor, the two launched their assault.

The Dark Realm unleashed a torrent of destructive energy, while the Earth Realm provided an unyielding defense, their teamwork flawless.

"Destruction Orbs! Disintegration Rays!"

Osborne wasted no time, countering with his most powerful techniques—attacks taught to him by the God of Star. As his mastery grew, so did the potency of these attacks.

Blinding bursts of light and searing rays erupted, but they were met with the Earth Realm's formidable defenses, unable to inflict any real damage.

However, the attacks from both of them had no effect on Osborne.

The back-and-forth battle raged on, neither side gaining the upper hand.

Osborne maintained his calm demeanor, while the assassins' faces betrayed their growing frustration.

Time dragged on, and the tension mounted.

They knew they had to finish this quickly. The Lambert family was close by, and the ruckus would surely draw their attention.

With two Class S fighters among them, any reinforcements would spell doom for the assassins.

They exchanged knowing glances.

"We need to shatter his Realm! As long as he has that, our strengths in stealth and assassination are useless. If we can break it, we can take him down fast!"

Without hesitation, they sprang into action.

Their Realms began to tremble violently, and Osborne's face shifted into one of alarm.

"Not another Realm self-detonation!"

Osborne furrowed his brow, realization dawning upon him.

No matter how powerful or stable his Level Four Light Realm was, it couldn't withstand the backlash of two Class S warriors self-destructing their Level Three Realms.

In an instant, all three of their Realms shattered, and they simultaneously experienced a backlash to their mental strength.

The difference lay in the severity: the two who had chosen to self-destruct faced a more violent recoil, blood spurting from their mouths as their faces drained of color.

Meanwhile, Osborne bore only a slight trickle of blood from the corner of his mouth, his demeanor still composed despite the chaos.

The Class S assassins of the Eityreia Organization didn't linger on their injuries. The moment their Realms imploded, they activated their Stealth Arts, vanishing from sight like whispers in the wind.

"Damn it!" Osborne scoffed, his body taut with tension, every muscle poised for action.

Suddenly, he felt a pulse from Satan's Eye, a warning that sent a chill down his spine.

"Thunder Realm!" Osborne shouted decisively.

The moment he did, the two assassins, who had been stealthily inching closer for a surprise attack, were once again caught in his energy field.

"Crap!" they both cursed under their breath, infuriated by Osborne's unexpected agility.

He had already unleashed the Spatial Domain and the Light Realm, and now he was calling forth a third Realm!

With their positions exposed, they lost any chance of ambushing him.

Osborne's Thunder Realm, with its Level Two peak status, proved insufficient to withstand their combined onslaught. They unleashed their full power, and in a flash, his Thunder Realm crumbled.

Realizing they needed to regroup, the assassins slipped once more into stealth, planning their next strike.

Yet before they could strike, Osborne raised his hand.

"Spirit Realm!"
