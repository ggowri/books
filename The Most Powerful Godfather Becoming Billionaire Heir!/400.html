Chapter 400 Calling the Police

Alexander walked calmly toward Eugene.

He wore a smile and strolled casually, yet to Eugene, Alexander looked like a terrifying demon.

Standing over Eugene, Alexander looked down on him like a god gazing upon an ant.

"You're done for!"

Alexander slapped his palm firmly against Eugene's forehead.

At that moment, with a loud bang, someone kicked open the front door from the outside.

A dozen police officers burst into the room.

"Freeze!"

"Hands up!"

Seeing the police, Eugene let out a long sigh of relief, feeling he was finally safe.

Alexander turned to see the police, with Melin in the lead.

Her uniform hugged her athletic figure, radiating a powerful, untouchable aura like that of an ice queen.

Melin looked at Alexander in shock; she hadn't expected to meet him in this kind of situation.

"Mr. Hawthorne, are you alright?" she asked.

Everyone froze. Did Alexander look like he was in trouble? Clearly, they were the ones in disarray.

The officers began to gather around, but Melin held them back.

She waved them off. "It's alright; Mr. Hawthorne isn't the bad guy here."

The officers hesitated, unsure what to do.

"Who called in the report saying there was a killer here?" Melin asked, scanning the room.

She noticed that everyone present was a gang member and let out a dry laugh.

No one answered; heads hung low, not daring to admit it. Reporting to the police was just too humiliating.

"You are wasting public resources! Take them all in!" Melin ordered.

At Melin's command, the officers were momentarily stunned.

The officers stood confused, wondering what was up with Melin.

Seeing no one move, Melin raised an eyebrow and shouted, "Are you deaf? Take them all in!"

Only then did Eugene and the others get cuffed and loaded into the police vehicles.

Eugene felt a surge of relief, thinking he had narrowly escaped death.

Alexander gave a cold smile, knowing both Wyatt and Eugene had been affected by Force Annihilation and would likely die once they reached the police station, leaving him blameless.

"Mr. Hawthorne, when you have time, I'd like to discuss this case with you," Melin said, blushing slightly.

Alexander gave her an amused smile. He knew she wasn't interested in the case details. She was interested in getting a bit more personal.

"Sure, I'll call you later."

With Alexander's confirmation, Melin's spirits lifted, and she led Eugene and his crew away with a satisfied smile.

The room was left with just Alexander and Mary.

Mary was still stunned, never expecting events to turn out like this.

"Alexander, thank you!" she exclaimed.

Filled with emotion, Mary threw herself into Alexander's arms, pressing her full figure against him, her perfume filling the air around them.

"Mary, it seems like you've gotten even more... bouncy," Alexander joked with a playful smile.

"Do you like it? If you do, kiss me harder!"

Mary's breathing grew rapid as she held Alexander's hand tightly against her.

"I've missed you so much these days. Right here, love me, give it to me!"

"Right here?" Alexander felt a thrill.

Alexander's heart raced.

With her mature beauty and allure, Mary possessed a seductive charm, unlike any other woman.

Sparks flew!

They embraced, falling onto the conference table together.

Mary pressed close to Alexander, savoring his kisses, and wrapped her hand around his powerful form.

The moment she touched him, her heart skipped a beat.

"Oh my god, you've gotten even bigger!" she gasped.

Without hesitation, she tugged down his zipper, and he sprang free.

Mary's eyes widened in disbelief. It was even more impressive than she'd imagined.

She sighed in awe. "You... you're destined to be the downfall of any woman."

Alexander chuckled. "You like it?"

"Of course I do. I just don't know if I can handle it alone."

Mary felt both anticipation and fear.

She could only imagine the incredible experience Alexander would give her today.

Alexander pinned Mary down and leaned over her.

Her soft, enticing moans echoed through the room, utterly electrifying.
