Chapter 892 Kill One by One

The bullet, imbued with supernatural power, saw its might be magnified several-fold.

The invisible Force on Alexander's body suddenly surged, and the speed of the bullet dropped sharply.

Then Alexander dodged the bullet and shot Locke at the same time.

The Force made the bullet as fast as lightning, instantly hitting Locke's right shoulder.

The powerful impact sent Locke flying several feet away, and he fell to the ground, with blood gushing out of his injured shoulder.

In the meantime, Marvin and Shadow supported each other and hid in the woods to avoid becoming a burden to Alexander.

"Ah!"

The woman suddenly screamed, her whole body trembled, and blood flowed from her nostrils.

She tried to conquer Alexander but suffered a backlash.

"I can't restrain him at all. He just glanced at me just now... And I felt like I was struck by lightning..."

Locke covered the wound on his shoulder and muttered, "Where did he go? Be careful of his sneak attack!"

Alexander had thrown away his sniper rifle and restrained his breath so that no one could discover his location.

Marvin and Shadow sat on the ground, gasping for breath, with their backs against a tree, both covered in blood.

"Boss, I'm sorry. We got you into trouble!"

Alexander suddenly appeared, and Marvin and Shadow struggled to stand up.

"Don't move. Take a rest!"

Alexander put his hands on the shoulders of the two and began to use the Force to heal them.

Soon, Shadow and Marvin felt much better and had regained most of their strength.

"Guard the surroundings well and don't let Locke and that woman get away. Leave the rest to me."

Alexander then disappeared into the darkness again...

"Crack!"

Locke was knocked into a tree by a powerful force before he could react.

Alexander pursued Locke, colliding with him like a speeding truck, and sent him tumbling deep into the woods again.

With loud crackling sounds, big trees kept falling to the ground.

Locke, bloodied and enraged, ordered a mass of cobras to intertwine into a colossal snake chain after Alexander's beating.

Alexander's renewed assault prompted the massive snake chain to strike at him.

Although it was dark in the woods, Alexander could see through things and could see everything clearly, which gave him a natural advantage.

"Squeak!"

The snake chain wrapped around Alexander frantically, and countless snakes bit Alexander's body fiercely.

Locke couldn't help but be overjoyed. Even if it were an elephant, it would definitely die if so many snakes bit it.

However, he was disappointed. There was a shield formed by the Force on Alexander's body, which prevented the poisonous snakes from hurting Alexander.

"Bang!"

The snake chain exploded, blood rained down all over the sky, and countless poisonous snake fragments fell and burned wildly.

Meanwhile, the surrounding temperature rose rapidly.

The originally dark forest became as bright as day under the coverage of the burning Force.

"Oops!"

Locke was completely frightened and turned to run.

Alexander appeared behind him like a ghost and thrust his palm like a knife into Locke's back.

Locke's face was ashen, and he looked terrified.

"Die!"

Alexander shouted softly. With the Force vibrating, Locke's heart broke into pieces.

"No..."

Locke screamed and wanted to resist, but his body was limp, and he couldn't muster any strength.

He slowly lowered his head and saw that the left side of his chest had collapsed entirely, leaving only a mass of rotten flesh.

And that woman was also killed by Marvin and Shadow.

At this time, there was unrest in the branch. The Mafia members sensed something was wrong and prepared to escape in the heavy rain.

However, they had already been surrounded, and anyone who went out was shot dead.

Deep inside the branch, there was a beautiful woman in her twenties, looking at the surveillance screen with a severe expression.

Behind her, a bald man led more than a dozen fully armed men, each of whom was holding a weapon tightly, with an anxious expression, and looked towards the door from time to time.

"Ms. Hawkins, we'll escort you out. It will be too late if you don't leave now!" the bald man said urgently.

This woman was Vivian Hawkins, the only daughter of Mafia Godfather, Brandon. She was just visiting Kylaville, but she didn't expect to be attacked by Alexander.
