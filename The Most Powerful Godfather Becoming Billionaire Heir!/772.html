Chapter 772 Be My Servant

In Yvonne's mind, Alexander was supposed to be on the verge of agony, begging on his knees for mercy.

She had full confidence in her poison—it had never failed her before.

But Alexander showed no signs of pain. Instead, he took a step forward and commanded her sharply, "Kneel!"

The word boomed with the Force, like a thunderstorm exploding in Yvonne's ears.

Boom!

It was as though a fierce tempest had torn through her mind.

Yvonne screamed, clutching her head as her legs weakened, stumbling backward in disarray.

Her confidence shattered; there was no trace left of her earlier pride.

"You... How are you not affected by the poison?" she stammered, stunned that Alexander appeared completely unharmed.

Panic gripped her.

She spun around, trying to escape, but it was too late.

Alexander appeared in front of her, his gaze locking onto hers.

The Force surged through him, with his All-seeing Eyes and Force Control, holding her mind captive.

Yvonne gaped at him, her heart flooding with awe and fear, as if he were an invincible angel, standing above her with an aura of absolute power.

A sense of awe and reverence instantly rose within her heart.

"Kneel!"

Alexander pointed at her as though commanding a disobedient child.

The very air seemed to freeze. Yvonne shook her head, desperate to keep her thoughts clear, but her mind felt thick and sluggish, incapable of coherent thought.

"Alexander... You bastard, I won't submit to you!"

Yvonne's face twisted in defiance, her last remnants of resistance flaring.

She summoned every ounce of her strength, desperately fighting to stay conscious. With a final burst of energy, Yvonne unleashed three rounds of witchcraft against Alexander, even releasing her witchcraft insects.

But nothing worked—each attempt was effortlessly blocked by Alexander as though she were a mere jester.

"I said, kneel!"

Alexander placed his hand on Yvonne's shoulder, forcing her to her knees.

She let out a pained scream.

"You dare make me kneel? I'll kill you!" she spat venomously, still struggling against him. Her body trembled, curves accentuated in her thrashing, a desperate allure in her rebellion.

But Alexander barely glanced at her, entirely uninterested.

He had no time for games.

His only focus now was to fully subdue her with his Force Control.

Yet this was uncharted territory. Controlling a witch was far more difficult than any Warrior—her mental strength was formidable, even by his standards.

Breaking Yvonne's willpower would require both persistence and a considerable amount of the Force.

Had his abilities not evolved so dramatically, he wouldn't even have dared attempt it.

"If you won't submit, then let me show you real pain," he said, channeling the Force into her body.

The energy drilled through her like a relentless blade, burrowing deep into her nerves.

Yvonne crumpled, writhing on the ground, her screams echoing through the space as her body convulsed.

Alexander had no desire to kill Yvonne. Her ambition served as a stark reminder of her potential usefulness.

If he eliminated Monica and allowed Yvonne to take over as the head of the City of Sorcery, it could work in his favor—provided he maintained absolute control over her.

The City of Sorcery was like a malignant growth; if not kept in check, the sorcerers would continue to cause chaos, unleashing spells and witchcraft insects that could harm countless innocents.

The recent plague in Loscity was proof enough of the danger.

Even the Axe had been dealt a heavy blow, with Alyssa's kidnapping nearly severing Alexander's crucial support in Mandale Town.

Alexander watched as she lay motionless, barely breathing, her body twitching in spasms.

Only then did he speak, his tone cold, "So, how does that compare to your so-called poison? Do you want to die a painful death, or will you submit and be my servant?"

"Please... I'll do anything... anything you ask... I'll be yours... serve you... even be your woman, but please, not a slave..."

She pleaded, clutching at his legs desperately.

Her face twisted with pain, yet she forced a seductive smile, trying to tempt him.

She tugged at her crimson robes, revealing her ample breasts, flaunting them with a practiced touch as she knelt before him, imploring for his mercy.
