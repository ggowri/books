Chapter 194 Home Field

Undoubtedly, the Federer family was out in full force.

Gary was indeed cunning; after failing to kidnap Ava, he quickly gathered men for an assault—true villain behavior.

A fierce light flashed in Alexander's eyes as he clenched his fists.

Sure enough, the convoy stopped about 300 feet away.

Over a hundred people jumped out of the cars into the rain, armed with various weapons.

The rain pelted their faces and drenched them, yet no one sought shelter or made a fuss.

Clearly, these people were well-trained elite forces.

They quickly formed a line and advanced toward the villa.

This was a wealthy area, and there should be tight security, but there was no warning—clearly, the Federer family had taken care of it.

However, every move they made, even with the cover of darkness and rain, was clearly visible to Alexander.

A freckled-faced guy, Chris Federer, in the front said excitedly, "It's the villa right ahead! Ava sent it to Alexander. According to our intel, she came here after running away. If we capture her and Alexander, there must be a huge bonus from the master!"

Another guy with a hooked nose and tanned skin looked serious.

"Chris, the master wanted us to be careful. Even the old butler lost to Alexander; that guy shouldn't be underestimated. If anything goes wrong, we retreat immediately."

The freckled-faced rolled his eyes.

"Edward, you're just too timid. We have over a hundred people, plus you, the Federer family's top fighter. How can we fail?"

Edward Federer stroked his hooked nose and replied, "In normal cases, we're well-prepared for a sudden attack, so Alexander and Ava definitely won't escape.

"Today, the Fraternity just clashed with the Ultimate Alliance. After we capture Alexander and Ava, everyone will think it was the Fraternity."

Chris' freckled face was almost twisted with excitement as he nodded repeatedly.

"Exactly! The master has a brilliant plan. Our action will surely succeed! Even if Alexander is strong, we have so many people to defeat him!"

But Edward's expression changed, and he hesitated.

He looked around anxiously, and had an ominous feeling.

But in the storm, everything was pitch black, and he couldn't see anything clearly.

Maybe he was just overthinking.

Edward forced a smile, then patted his chest to bolster his spirits.

"Pass the orders: anyone except Ava and Alexander must be killed without mercy—no witnesses."

He issued the command ruthlessly, his tone devoid of any human emotion.

However, Edward didn't realize that Alexander heard their entire conversation loud and clear.

Edward could never have imagined that there was something like Legendary Warrior in this world.

Although Alexander was just a Novice Warrior, the power of The Force was beyond Edward's imagination.

Every detail captured by Alexander, and he showed a wicked smile.

"Since you're here, then stay here forever."

He commanded Augusta that once he made a move, they needed to surround the scene and not let anyone escape.

If they were going to act, they had to root out every last one!

Ever since Alexander learned he was the Godfather's heir and became a Warrior, he was no longer the weak man he was in the past.

He vowed to protect his family and become powerful, looking down on all.

Gary must die, and the Federer family must fall apart.

To protect family ties, love, and friendship, and for all the brothers of the Ultimate Alliance, this massacre today is inevitable.

Today's heavy rain has come just in time; with Alexander's incredible perception, the pitch-black rainy night is his perfect stage.

Alexander, like a ghost, quietly faded into the darkness.

"Boom!"

Thunder roared, and torrential rain poured down from the sky.

More than a hundred members of the Federer family charged forward aggressively, led by elite members, Chris and Edward.
