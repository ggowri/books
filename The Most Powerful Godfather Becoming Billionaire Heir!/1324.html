Chapter 1324 Determination

Two priestly soldiers stepped forward, helped Sellers up, and took him to the back hall.

At this moment, the middle-aged priest shouted, "Sellers Schneier, you've passed!"

"Sellers is the heir of the Schneier family, and I heard that he is already at the peak of a level-two warrior, yet he is seriously injured! I am afraid that we..."

"Yes, the Schneier Spear Technique is unpredictable and unmatched. They command the world's respect!"

Seeing Sellers in such a miserable state, everyone seemed to have taken a step back.

"Next!" the middle-aged priest shouted.

"Me!" A young man in green clothes entered the vermilion doorway.

In the Dominion Church Outer Court, a servant walked straight to Fermin and whispered, "Congratulations, Mr. Schneier. Mr. Sellers Schneier has passed the assessment."

"Good!" Fermin stroked his beard and smiled.

"Next! Alexander Hawthorne." A priestly soldier held the registration book and called the name.

Alexander hurriedly entered the vermilion doorway.

The same green corridor, brightly lit lights, and Thunderclap Bronze Man. Its fierce and violent vibe rushed towards Alexander.

Alexander twitched his eyebrows. He was calm. He urged the date-shaped Force vortex in his martial core, and strands of spiral Force roamed around.

His muscles rippled, rising like mountain ridges. It was as unyielding as iron yet soft as cotton when relaxed.

If Alexander truly mastered the level-two warrior stage, his muscles wouldn't just soften like cotton. They would flow like water.

Alexander lunged toward the Bronze Man's flank.

The Thunderclap Bronze Man roared and rose up. It flickered and slammed its giant golden fist straight at Alexander's head with the sound of thunder.

The Bronze Man was so fast that it was too late to dodge. Alexander could only muster the Force of his whole body to meet the blow head-on.

"Bang!" Like a hammer hitting a nail, Alexander, the nail, flew out more than 30 feet. While still in the air, he felt his ribs tighten before a mouthful of blood burst from his lips.

The Thunderclap Bronze Man didn't chase after Alexander but stood in place. Its pair of eyes were inlaid with black crystals. It stared at Alexander, and a hint of mockery seemed to flash across its eyes.

Frustration welled up inside Alexander. As expected, the Bronze Man was as powerful as a level-three warrior. If not for his rapid progress over the past two months, he might have ended up knocked out cold on the spot like last time.

But now, Alexander had no way out. If he remained stuck just half a step away from a level-two warrior, there was no chance he could pass the trial. He didn't know what to do.

"What's wrong? Are you afraid? Do you think martial arts is just about learning a few fancy moves? That it's a path to wealth and glory? A shortcut to power and prestige? No! True martial arts exist to protect the nation and to vanquish all evil!" The words of the middle-aged priest echoed in Alexander's mind.

"True martial arts are about cutting down evils, carving a path through thorns, and enduring countless hardships. Only then can you truly rise above!"

Before participating in the assessment, Alexander merely wanted to live well in this world, to find out whether the experience of another world in his mind was true or a dream.

But Alexander was deeply touched after listening to the middle-aged priest's words. He no longer wanted to live an ordinary, timid life. He thought, "I can also live a vigorous life, leaving a glorious name for eternity. This Thunderclap Bronze Man can't stop me."

A surge of excitement rushed through Alexander as sudden clarity washed over him. "Water is colorless and formless. In a square, it takes the shape of a square. In a circle, it flows as a circle. It moves without resistance..."

He took on the stance of Harmonic Fist and performed its 24-posture sequence. The universe was always in motion. Balance came from opposing forces, sky and land, firm and yielding, solid and void.

Then, Alexander performed the Vardaanian yoga that included serpent pose, twisting pose, wide-angle pose, and shoulder stand.

Gradually, Alexander felt his entire body soften as if his bones had vanished. He became as light as cotton, as fine as a strand of hair, as fluid as water.

Alexander clenched his fists, laughed heartily, and finally entered the level of a level-two warrior.

A level-two warrior mastered both hardness and softness. They could move swiftly and leap nimbly.

Fifteen minutes had passed. There was no time to waste.

Alexander took off his coarse linen clothes, baring his upper body. He urged the Force vortex, and the spiral Force filled his body.

The muscles of his body no longer bulged but were as usual. Under the flattened muscles, there was endless power.

Alexander gave a soft shout, exerted force under his feet, and stepped down continuously with three consecutive "Bang" sounds.

Alexander was like a shooting star, flying towards the Thunderclap Bronze Man.

The Bronze Man seemed stunned, as if it didn't expect Alexander to suddenly become so fast and agile, unable to grasp the trajectory of his running.

And now, Alexander was about to slip past it.
