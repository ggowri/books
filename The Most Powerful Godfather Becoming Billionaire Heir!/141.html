Chapter 141 Black Snakes

Lara felt her hope fading, even her thoughts of Alexander fading as her senses blurred.

Worse, Drake began to look like Alexander to her, and her gaze grew hazy.

Drake's eyes lit up with excitement as he reached to unbuckle his pants and pressed down on her.

At the last second, a shout rang out.

"Stop!" Alexander came charging forward, carrying Jimbo like a sack of potatoes.

"So, you're Alexander! How'd you find me? Damn, Jimbo must've betrayed me."

Drake quickly pulled up his pants, startled by Alexander's intense killing intent.

He knew immediately that Alexander was no ordinary opponent. His aura was too menacing.

But he didn't attack immediately because he saw Jimbo.

As a powerful gray wizard, Jimbo now lay helpless, his limbs useless, proving that Alexander wasn't to be underestimated.

"Alexander, what did you do to my disciple?" Drake asked.

Alexander scoffed. "Can't you see? I've taken care of him. Now it's your turn. Surrender, and I might let you live."

"Big words. I'm not like Jimbo." Drake glared at Alexander, not daring to blink.

In a duel between skilled fighters, a single blink could mean death.

"Alexander, die!" Drake kicked a rock at Alexander.

The rock flew like a cannonball, hurtling straight at Alexander.

"Idiot!" Alexander used Jimbo as a shield.

Jimbo screamed as the rock smashed into his chest, shattering his bones.

"Some master you are, sacrificing your disciple." Alexander taunted him.

Furious, Drake picked up a large branch and swung it at Alexander.

From Drake's perspective, he could see it hitting Alexander.

But in reality, Alexander moved way too fast for Drake's perception. The branch hit nothing but a shadow of him.

Drake felt a chill from behind and immediately swung a branch behind him. However, Alexander caught it effortlessly.

Crack! The branch shattered as Alexander's palm struck Drake's chest, sending him flying and coughing up blood.

"Die!" Drake suddenly pulled out a gun and fired repeatedly at Alexander.

The gunfire tore up the bushes, but Alexander stood untouched.

Alexander looked at Drake emotionlessly.

"Is that all? Aren't you a dark wizard? Show me your strongest tricks."

"Hmph!" Drake tossed the gun aside and pulled out a black pouch.

"That was just a warm-up. Now you will face real terror!"

Suddenly, he opened the pouch.

"Hiss!" Dozens of black snakes slithered out.

Those snakes had triangular heads, ominous patterns, and sharp fangs.

The snakes formed an arc, slowly advancing toward Alexander.

Alexander grabbed a handful of stones and threw them at the snakes with the Force.

The stones hit the snakes with deadly precision, as powerful as bullets.

"Hisss!"

Several snakes let out shrill hisses as they bled.

Drake paled, spitting blood.

The snakes were linked to him, so their injuries affected him.

He screeched, commanding the snakes to spray venom at Alexander.

The black venom filled the air, falling like raindrops.
