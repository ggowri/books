Chapter 1079 Targeted

Alexander smiled softly, "I'm interested in the technology your husband developed. Of course, I'll offer a generous fee for the technology transfer."

"Really?" Emma's eyes lit up with hope. "Mr. Hawthorne, if you can rescue my husband, I'll convince him to give you the all-purpose bulletproof vest technology free of charge."

Alexander merely nodded, saying nothing more.

When Maxine heard that Alexander was only after the technology, she looked a little upset.

Was he not interested in her at all?

Emma immediately noticed her daughter's strange reaction, and anxiety began to creep in.

She had known that Alexander wasn't Maxine's boyfriend.

If that were the case, would Alexander truly help their family wholeheartedly?

Emma took a deep breath and pondered carefully.

Alexander drove them to a five-star hotel in Newbrook.

The hotel had tight security measures—while Alexander went to rescue Alvin, Maxine and Emma would be safer here.

Just as Alexander parked in the garage, his instincts told him something was off. It felt like they were being watched.

Could it be Silver Shield Security?

Meanwhile, Emma and Maxine hadn't noticed anything unusual. In fact, they felt particularly at ease under Alexander's protection.

Alexander's gaze swept the surroundings before fixing on two black Buicks parked nearby.

Something wasn't right!

"Be careful!" Alexander warned urgently. "We're being stalked!"

"What? Has Silver Shield Security caught up with us?" Emma trembled in fear, her boobs shaking.

Despite her middle age, Emma maintained an elegant vibe, her skin still smooth. Her mature, graceful figure carried a natural allure that surpassed even Maxine's.

No wonder Lanny wanted her.

Suddenly, car doors flew open on two vehicles.

Five masked men burst out, lunging like wolves toward Emma and Maxine.

Two of them immediately seized Emma, who had been the first to exit her car. They grabbed her arms while another assailant moved to force a black hood over her head.

"Help! Somebody help!" Emma snapped out of her daze and began desperately struggling, screaming for help.

Maxine was also being dragged toward the black car by two masked men. Terrified, she fought against their grip while crying and calling for her mother.

The remaining masked thug charged at Alexander, trying to stop him.

But Alexander had already jumped back into his car. He started the engine and slammed down on the accelerator.

The unfortunate thug rushing toward him was sent flying with a tremendous impact, crashing through one of the black car's windows and landing inside the passenger compartment.

The other black car had barely started moving when Alexander's car slammed into it with brutal force.

The car skidded a dozen feet across the ground with a loud bang.

Just as the kidnapper behind the wheel regained consciousness and reached to restart the engine, there was a deafening crash—the locked car door was suddenly ripped clean off its hinges.

The driver trembled violently in fear. Before he could even process what was happening, a shadow flashed before his eyes, and Alexander appeared before him like the Grim Reaper himself.

"I'll kill you, you bastard!" the driver cursed, frantically releasing the steering wheel to reach for his gun.

In the next instant, searing pain shot through his throat. The world spun as Alexander seized him by the neck, yanked him straight out of the driver's seat, and slammed him brutally onto the ground.

The driver let out an agonized cry and tried to fight back. But as he lifted his head, all he saw was a black shoe sole rapidly filling his vision before it crashed down onto his face.

"Ahh!" The driver collapsed to the ground, his face a bloody mess.

At that moment, the masked men abandoned Emma and Maxine. Some drew their guns while others brandished knives, charging at Alexander with wild shouts.

They fired several shots in quick succession, but Alexander used their own companions' bodies as shields.

Three shots, three dead accomplices. The shooter stood there, stunned.

Before he could react, Alexander hurled one of the corpses at him with crushing force. His spine shattered on impact, and his body crumpled.

The last kidnapper, realizing he was dealing with someone lethal, turned to flee in terror.

Alexander didn't bother giving chase. Instead, he kicked one of the fallen blades on the ground.

The knife streaked through the air like a shooting star, piercing through the final kidnapper's chest and pinning him to the ground.
