Chapter 683 Forced Retreat

Felix, clad in designer attire and exuding confidence, walked with a commanding presence beside the white-haired elder.

The elder held a crown crafted from white bone and placed it reverently on Felix's head.

The moment Felix donned the bone crown, something astonishing happened. The entire Demon Summoning Array trembled, and even Treasure Island itself seemed to shake.

A sinister, otherworldly aura emanated from Felix, spreading outward.

His presence became overwhelmingly powerful, both psychologically and physically.

Augusta and William, hiding in the shadows, nearly lost their cover due to the force of the energy.

Even through the screen of his phone, Alexander could sense Felix's amplified strength.

And this Demon Summoning Array wasn't even fully operational yet. Once when it was done, it would provide Felix with a formidable power boost.

Alexander's expression turned serious, his gaze fixed on the unfolding scene.

The elder then produced a black tome, chanting a solemn incantation.

A strange melody began to echo through the air. Though there was no visible source, everyone could hear it.

It was a melody that seemed to come from another world.

And an unbearable stench filled the air, forcing everyone on the site to cover their noses.

Augusta and William were no exception, the combination of the eerie music and the foul odor causing a deep unease.

"This array really does look powerful."

Meanwhile, another figure in a black cloak watched the proceedings from the shadows. It was Shadow.

Eyes wide, Shadow looked pale and shocked.

"That old fool Rock... he's actually capable of summoning demonic power? Has Anthony Hawthorne lost his mind, making a pact with demons? This is madness..."

Suddenly, the clear sky darkened as thunderclouds gathered above, filling the air with the sound of rolling thunder.

Lightning flashed, and a storm seemed imminent.

What was the most terrifying was that the dark clouds and lightning were confined to the area above the array while the rest of the sky remained perfectly clear.

It was as if some kind of miracle had occurred.

Within one or two minutes, the storm abruptly vanished, and the sky returned to its previous sunny state.

Felix, visibly thrilled, asked, "Mr. Rossetti, why did you stop? I felt an endless surge of strength throughout my body."

The elder chuckled darkly. "No rush. The array isn't complete yet. I merely wanted to test the effectiveness of the Ancient Grimoire.

"It's a pity that the Ancestral Sorcerer's Staff and the Spirit Bead Bracelet are still in Alexander's possession. Otherwise, with the grimoire, I could summon an even greater power."

Felix grinned wickedly. "Even that brief surge was strong enough to kill Alexander. By the way, who originally owned this grimoire?"

Rock cast him a meaningful look, his tone flat. "Never ask again. The more exalted the source, the less the name should be spoken."

Felix gave an awkward smile. "Of course, my apologies for overstepping."

With that, the two of them entered the central elevator shaft and disappeared from sight.

"Augusta, William, leave the area immediately. Do not return until I've arrived on Treasure Island!"

Alexander's voice came through the phone, tense and commanding.

"Why?"

Augusta protested, reluctant to withdraw. "We could take out those workers and prevent them from completing the array."

Alexander's voice turned steely. "That's an order! Even if the array isn't complete, Felix's current strength is more than enough to kill you both, not to mention that elder.

"I suspect that elder is none other than Rock, the Grand Elder of the City of Sorcery.

"This Demon Summoning Array is no ordinary formation. Within its range, their power is constantly amplified!

"So until I arrive, you're to lay low and focus on defense. Do you understand?"

"Yes, Boss!"

Though Augusta and William felt frustrated, they didn't dare defy Alexander's command.

Especially William, who had just emerged from a period of closed training, having activated his rare bloodline. He had hoped to strike down the Red Umbrella Group and unleash his full strength.

But realizing that Felix's power was significant enough to warrant Alexander's concern, he reluctantly withdrew.
