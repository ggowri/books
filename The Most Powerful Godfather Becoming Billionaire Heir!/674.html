Chapter 674 Upgrade

Beverly sank down on the couch with a deep sigh, feeling that she was safe at last.

She thought, "He must have thought I ran away. He can't find me anyway. I'll hide for a few days and then get a chance to slip away."

Beverly was secretly proud that she was the only one who survived.

Snap!

She turned on the lights. Dozens of incandescent lamps lit up in the spacious basement.

The display cases were filled with antiques and jewelry, as well as a large number of gold bars.

The pile of brand-new cash was six feet long and wide and more than three feet high. These were hundreds of millions of dollars at least.

Beverly ignored all this wealth. She took off her clothes and prepared to take a shower, then have a sleep and watch TV to relax and calm her tense mood.

Suddenly, Beverly's face changed drastically.

"Why are you here? How did you get in?"

Alexander was actually lying on the bed in the bedroom with a playful smile.

He said, "Why can't I come in? You can't hide from me."

He stood up and looked at the display cases.

Some of them were valuable antiques.

These jewels were also made by famous people and were worth a lot of money.

"Your collection is quite good. It should be worth a lot of money!

Alexander put the bracelet into his pocket, then looked at each display case one by one, talking. Beverly trembled with surprise.

"You have very good eyes... I'll give you all these things. Please let me go!"

Beverly begged for mercy.

Alexander was in a very good mood now.

Beverly asked excitedly.

Alexander was stunned. She looked like she really had it.

Beverly seemed to be afraid that Alexander would go back on his word, so she quickly opened a drawer of a display cabinet and took out a small black wooden box.

She opened it. There were two orange gems in it, one large and one small.

Alexander was surprised. They were indeed orange top-grade gems.

He immediately grabbed the wooden box and examined it carefully.

"Yes, very good!"

After careful confirmation, Alexander couldn't wait to integrate the gems into the core of the Lightsaber.

"Please... I have already given you the gemstones. And everything here is yours... Please don't kill me. I am willing to obey you!"

Beverly straightened her plump breasts. She revealed a seductive look and walked towards Alexander.

She was the most beautiful woman in Mandale Town. She had a hot body and was very seductive.

The atmosphere became ambiguous.

Beverly was very confident in her beauty and figure and believed that the powerful man would not refuse.

She thought he would get excited.

Beverly was not only afraid of Alexander, but full of admiration for him.

Alexander smiled and pressed on Beverly's carotid artery.

"Ah!"

Beverly fell down.

Alexander took out the two gemstones and prepared to integrate them into the Lightsaber core. He couldn't wait.

The Lightsaber was finally successfully upgraded. And its power more than doubled.

The previous level-one Lightsaber could cut through the armor of a main battle tank. And the Lightsaber could easily cut through an entire tank now.

This would make the Lightsaber even more powerful!
