Chapter 673 Secret Room

"Is that guy dead?"

Paxton asked expectantly.

When the smoke cleared, Alexander appeared and walked towards them calmly.

Beverly's face changed drastically. "Is this guy a monster? He hasn't been killed yet!"

"Ah!"

A scream was heard. And the head of the shooter operating the heavy machine gun exploded. Blood spilled on the floor.

Then there was a second scream. And another machine gunner was shot in the head.

Everyone was trembling with fear. They only saw Alexander waving his hands, but there was no gunfire. They didn't know how he did it.

Beverly hid behind the SUV, howling in shock and anger.

"Fire now. Fire!"

Someone wanted to replace the machine gunner, but before he could touch the gun, his head exploded.

This time, everyone saw that it was a small stone that pierced the gunman's head.

Alexander kept waving his hands. And Beverly's men were shot in the head one after another.

"Hide!"

Some people screamed in fear and quickly hid behind sandbags.

The scene was chaotic. No one cared to stop Alexander. They all followed suit and hid.

Beverly ran inside desperately.

"Wait!"

Paxton hurriedly followed her.

"Ah! My legs..."

Paxton, who had only run a few steps, let out a scream and collapsed to the ground.

He had a gaping hole in his right leg, bleeding profusely.

"Help me! Help!"

Paxton shouted desperately, but Beverly just turned her head and looked at him, then ran into the inner courtyard at a faster speed.

"Bitch, stop. Help me!"

Paxton wailed and could only watch Beverly run away.

He was unwilling to wait for death, so he crawled on the ground, leaving a long trail of blood.

"Ah!"

Then Paxton screamed again. Another stone pierced his left leg. He was disabled.

The positions where the two stones hit were symmetrical.

In less than a minute, the heavily guarded scene was reduced to a pile of corpses like hell.

Paxton was terrified. He was crying and crawling on the ground with difficulty.

His body was covered in blood and dirt. And it was hard to imagine that he was the head of the Mafia in Mandale Town.

Not long ago, Paxton was like the king of Mandale Town, but now he had become a useless person crawling on the ground.

There were slight footsteps. Alexander walked up to Paxton elegantly, looking down at him, and said, "You regret it now, don't you?"

Paxton burst into tears.

"Please don't kill me! Please, don't kill me... I can do anything. I can serve you, really!"

Paxton begged Alexander. Now he only wanted to stay alive, and he was willing to give up his dignity and moral integrity.

"What?"

Alexander smiled and responded calmly.

"It will be hard to be my servant. Forget it."

Saying, Alexander waved his hand.

A stone flew out and hit Paxton's head hard. Blood flowed on the ground.

Alexander would not let him go.

There was silence in the Weaver House. And everyone had already run away.

Alexander sneered. He could let everyone else go, but not Beverly.

He had already locked onto Beverly with his All-seeing Eyes. She was cunning. She did not escape like the others but ran into a room.

Beverly pressed a hidden button in the corner of the wall.

The floor in the center of the room cracked, revealing a staircase leading underground.

Beverly entered the secret passage, and the floor closed automatically. There was no visible flaw from the outside.

"Damn it!"

Beverly cursed and looked relieved.

This secret basement contained most of the Weaver family's wealth, as well as enough food and water for her to survive for a month.

There was even a bedroom and a bathroom inside with all the necessary furniture and appliances.
