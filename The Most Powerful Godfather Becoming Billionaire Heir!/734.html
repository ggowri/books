Chapter 734 We Are Saved

Two hours later, Alexander happily walked out of the room.

Ava and Lynn were so exhausted that they cuddled up to each other, and Alexander was just too energetic.

"Charlize, you will stay here. I'll take Rosalia to Loscity Hospital to get some of the patients to test it out," Alexander ordered.

"Yes, Mr. Hawthorne!"

Rosalia agreed excitedly.

Charlize curled her lips in displeasure, and she missed another opportunity.

Loscity Hospital had become the eye of the storm for this disaster.

Loscity used to be a beautiful and charming place. The streets that used to be clean and tidy were now a mess everywhere, and there were emergency vehicles speeding by every now and then.

People fell down on the side of the road from time to time and never got up again.

As soon as people saw someone collapsing, they immediately fled in panic.

Everyone was wearing protective masks, but there were still many people coughing, who were obviously infected.

The piercing sound of sirens echoed through the city, mixing with cries for help, as if they had come to hell.

Since Loscity Hospital was the hardest hit area, half of the hospital staff had been infected and were unable to protect themselves.

This plague was so horrible that the mortality rate was as high as ninety percent or more, and even those who managed to survive would become vegetative.

It was the most horrific nightmare!

Melin was still guarding the hospital gate diligently and responsibly to maintain order in the hospital.

Watching the dying patients, each one looking wiped out, coughing terribly, and someone collapsing every now and then, made her very anxious and uneasy.

It wasn't just because of the terrible plague, but Melin hadn't seen Jeff in hours.

"Where did he go? Why can't I get in touch..."

Melin muttered worriedly, thinking, "Did something happen to Jeff?"

Thinking about it, Melin was wary in her mind.

"Ms. Ward, we still haven't found Jeff."

Several team members came over to report, and they all looked haggard, obviously all reaching extreme fatigue.

"Well..."

Melin sighed deeply, revealing an extremely reluctant smile.

"You guys should go get some rest, and Jeff will be fine."

"Melin, so there you are!"

At that moment, Blaze walked over quickly, waving and exclaiming excitedly at Melin.

Melin frowned, and a hint of veiled disgust showed in her eyes.

"Look, I brought you something delicious."

Blaze walked over holding a pizza.

The outside of the pizza box was wrapped in several layers of plastic wrap.

Under the effect of this terrible plague, any food was under strict seal to prevent being infected.

Seeing Melin, who was pretty and charming, Blaze kept glancing at Melin's plump boobs with greedy eyes.

Melin got even more skinny from exhaustion, looking incredibly sexy with her unusually plump boobs and slim waist.

"Hurry up and eat it. I got this with great difficulty, and I couldn't bear to enjoy it all by myself, so I brought it over to eat with you."

Blaze handed the pizza over flatteringly, wanting to take the opportunity to touch Melin's hand.

Melin retracted her hand quickly and smiled.

"Dr. Bates, thanks. I'm not hungry at all right now, and I can't eat anything. You can stay and eat yourself."

Melin rejected it coldly and bluntly, and Blaze was instantly so embarrassed that he turned pale.

He suppressed his anger and forced a smile.

"I know that you're upset about Jeff's disappearance, but have you ever thought that maybe Jeff isn't missing?"

Melin asked suspiciously, "What do you mean?"

Blaze rolled his eyes, came over to her, and said with a smile.

"Maybe Jeff was too scared to stay in a high-risk place like a hospital, so he snuck away..."

"Shut up!"

Melin chortled angrily, interrupting Blaze.

"Blaze, look, don't slander Jeff. If he wanted to run, he would have absolutely done it a long time ago, and he didn't have to wait until now."

She glared at Blaze angrily, her tone quite harsh.

Blaze hadn't seen Melin get angry yet, and he subconsciously took a step back.

"Don't be angry. I was wrong. I was just saying, but why are you so upset?"

Blaze smiled helplessly and gave himself an out.

"Good news! Good news! The expert group and the rescue team are here, and there are so many renowned experts coming. We're saved."

A doctor in a protective suit came flying in, shouting excitedly as he ran.
