Chapter 741 He Is a Madman

"This guy is quite good at pretending, and I think he is at his wits' end."

"Alexander invented the magic powder at such a young age, and why would he want to destroy his reputation and future?"

These experts were discussing the matter, but no one was optimistic about Alexander.

"Save me! Mr. Hawthorne, please save me... I am willing to pay 50 million dollars... No, 100 million dollars. I will pay 100 million dollars. Please save me..." Archer stammered and begged, and he was about to suffocate.

He suddenly screamed, rolled his eyes, curled up into a ball, and twitched continuously.

"Oh no! Save him quickly!"

Even Melin couldn't bear it anymore, and Eddy rushed over to rescue him.

"Don't touch him!" Alexander suddenly shouted.

The powerful voice exploded in everyone's ears like thunder.

Everyone covered their ears and took a few steps back in fear.

The area around the bed was cleared in an instant. Everyone stayed away and stared at Alexander in shock, not knowing what was wrong with him.

Alexander used the Force to increase the frequency of sound vibration, which was also a technique inherited from the Ultimate Legacy.

However, this kind of sonic attack could only be used by one with the strength of a peak of the Intermediate Warrior.

During this period, Alexander's Force had improved a lot.

"Now is the best time to treat Mr. Crawford. Please don't interfere with me. If I can't cure him, you will cause trouble to me."

Alexander was full of confidence, and his tone was filled with invisible majesty, which stunned everyone.

"Mr. Crawford, you said it yourself and give me 100 million dollars. Life is the most precious thing. I can save your life, so 100 million dollars is not expensive."

Alexander started the treatment while talking to himself.

He opened Archer's mouth, stuffed in a small piece of antidote, and then used the Force to heal him.

The experts watched Alexander's actions intently, all showing contemptuous expressions.

"Is this how he treats the plague? It's crazy!"

"I knew this guy was a liar. Hurry up and get him arrested. This is ridiculous."

Everyone was talking about it.

Elaine sneered, "Will he cure Mr. Crawford? Stop kidding, and people from Loscity are rubbish!"

"Shut up!" Alexander shouted and asked everyone to be quiet.

"What nonsense are you talking about? Isn't Mr. Crawford out of danger?"

Everyone was startled and looked at Archer lying on the hospital bed.

As a result, he kept his eyes closed and did not move, showing no sign of being out of danger.

"Madman! Is this guy a madman?"

"He is really good at pretending, but we are not blind. What's so good about Mr. Crawford?"

"This is full of nonsense, Mr. Blunt. If you don't severely punish this liar, the reputation of Loscity will be completely ruined."

Experts attacked Alexander with language one after another.

Even Gideon and Melin began to lose confidence, and Anderson's face turned gloomy. He was ready to order the arrest of Alexander.

"A bunch of idiots!"

Alexander's mocking voice was clearly heard by everyone and aroused the common hatred of all experts.

"Mr. Blunt, arrest this liar!" Elaine said loudly.

However, Anderson was stunned, staring at the bed stupidly as if he had seen something unbelievable, his expression full of surprise.

Elaine and the others turned their heads curiously, and suddenly, everyone's expression changed, as if their bodies were frozen in place, their bodies stiff.

Archer, who was on the verge of death just now, actually sat up by himself at this moment.

His eyes were wide open, and his face was confused, as if he hadn't figured out the situation yet.

"Am I dead? Where is this? The hospital?"

Archer stared and looked around in confusion.

After a while, his dull eyes became bright, and his expression was full of excitement.

"Is this Loscity Hospital? Am I not dead? Am I not dead?"

Archer seemed to have had a nightmare. After coming to his senses, he actually wanted to get out of the hospital bed.

He held onto the edge of the bed and slowly moved his body.

Amid everyone's shocked gaze, Archer successfully stood up.

Although his legs were still shaking and his body was trembling, Archer, who was on the verge of death, actually stood up!
