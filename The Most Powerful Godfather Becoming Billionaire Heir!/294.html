Chapter 294 Hiding Strength

Melin's vision went dark, and she was frightened.

Although she knew this was a psychological trick, she felt helpless.

"Don't move!" Rosen shouted.

He pulled out a dagger and placed it on Melin's neck.

Melin felt the cold blade and tensed up.

She couldn't control her body from shaking.

"Now I ask you a question, you answer one question. If you don't answer, I will strip you naked and expose you to everyone. Understand?" Rosen threatened.

"Don't touch me, ask!" Melin shouted.

"Are you from the Loscity Police Department? What's your name?"

"Yes, I'm police officer Melin."

"Did you come alone? Where are the others?"

Melin hesitated before answering. "I came alone because my boss was afraid to get involved."

Rosen snorted and placed the blade on Melin's bra strap, suddenly cutting it.

Melin cried out, and her full chest was exposed.

The room was filled with Rosen and his men's wild laughter.

"Officer, now it's time for our welcome party." Rosen ripped off the black cloth covering her eyes.

With a wicked smile, he started to unbuckle his belt.

"What are you going to do? I'm a police officer. Don't come near me!" Melin was terrified.

She knew her looks and figure were top-notch, and many men pursued her, both at work and in life.

She had already preserved her chastity.

But now, facing these thugs, she felt hopeless.

Rosen walked up to Melin, smiling wickedly.

Melin looked up at him fiercely. "You've probably done this to other women before, right?"

"Oh, you're quite fierce!" Rosen grabbed Melin's chin and said, "Officer, it's too late now. If you serve me well, maybe I'll spare your life."

"If you touch me, I'll kill you," Melin warned.

Alexander, who was secretly watching, suddenly realized that Melin's demeanor was completely different from before. He wondered if she had hidden her strength.

"Heh, you're trying to scare me?" Rosen laughed.

He unzipped his pants and pushed Melin's head down.

Melin suddenly sprang up and headbutted Rosen in the groin.

Rosen screamed in pain and fell to the ground.

Melin's arms twisted, moving from behind her back to the front, but the handcuffs still bound her wrists.

All this happened in a flash. When everyone came to their senses, she had already picked up the dagger and was holding it to Rosen's throat.

"Tell your men to back off, or I'll kill you!" she threatened.

The room fell silent.

Alexander couldn't help but cheer, not expecting Melin to pretend to be weak and then strike at the right moment.

Unfortunately, Melin underestimated these men's fighting spirit.

Someone pointed a gun at Melin.

The others held guns or knives to the Fairfax family members.

Rosen laughed, "Capturing me is useless. Everyone, listen up! Kill one person every ten seconds until she lets me go."

Melin was at a loss. In this situation, even killing Rosen wouldn't help.

Rosen sneered, "Aren't you a police officer? Don't you want to save them? Now their lives are in your hands."

Soon, screams filled the air.

A Fairfax family member was stabbed in the thigh and fell to the ground, writhing in pain.

"Stop!" Melin shouted.

She stabbed Rosen's thigh with the dagger.
