Chapter 93 Disgusting

He was no longer the naive and innocent man he once was; he had long seen through Jane's true colors.

"Alexander, you bastard, stop right there!" Jane screamed in frustration.

Andrew said, leading the Fairfax family to swarm around Alexander, "Jane, I told you this trash doesn't know what's good for him. Look, he's slapped you in the face again.

"I've already notified Mr. Sebastian Tate. He'll be here with his men in a few minutes.

"Alexander, you're not going anywhere today!" Andrew gloated.

Five million dollars and Sebastian's favor, it was a two-for-one deal.

Alexander couldn't help but sneer, saying, "Jane, so this was a trap, something you had planned from the beginning."

Jane's face was filled with fury. "Alexander, this is all your fault. You brought it on yourself. I begged you for forgiveness, but you were unmoved."

She looked like she had suffered a great injustice, which made Alexander both angry and amused.

"You think this trash can stop me?" Alexander asked disdainfully.

Andrew smiled and said, "Alexander, no matter how powerful you are, can you beat my gun?"

He drew his pistol, aiming the dark muzzle at Alexander.

"If you dare to run, I'll turn you into a colander!"

Jane asked again, "Alexander, will you remarry me? If you don't agree, I'll leave you to your own devices."

Andrew was furious. "Jane, are you crazy? Have you been hit with Alexander's witchcraft?

"Why do you insist on pleasing him? You hated him before!"

But Jane insisted, "Alexander, please forgive me. I was wrong. Let's remarry!"

Alexander was speechless. On the one hand, they were ambushing him with the Fairfax family, and on the other, she was demanding forgiveness with such conviction.

He was so sick of looking at Jane that he felt nauseated thinking about the humiliation he had suffered in the Fairfax family, Jane's betrayal, and her forcing him into a divorce.

Alexander just wanted to hurry back and explain to Ava before Rebecca could cause any misunderstandings.

"Move if you want to live!" Alexander ignored Andrew's gun and strode forward.

Andrew, who was in the front line, was startled and quickly pressed his gun to Alexander's side, shouting, "Do you want to die?"

Alexander sneered and swiped his hand.

Andrew spun around, and the pistol ended up in Alexander's hand.

"Those who stand in my way, die!"

Alexander held Andrew in his left hand and pressed the gun to Andrew's forehead, marching forward.

The Fairfax family members, who had never seen such a scene, were immediately terrified and backed away.

Jane, stimulated, screamed, "Alexander, you bastard, let go of my dad!"

Alexander ignored her and continued forward.

Andrew stuttered, threatening, "Alexander, your arrogance will end soon. When Mr. Sebastian Tate arrives, you're as good as dead..."

Alexander smashed the gun's butt into his face.

With a scream, Andrew fell to the ground, his face swollen like a mound of earth.

Suddenly, the sound of car engines roared, quickly approaching from a distance.

Seven or eight black cars sped over and came to a screeching halt.

The door of the black Rolls-Royce in the middle opened, and Sebastian, supported by several men, stumbled out.

"Mr. Sebastian Tate, Alexander is here! I'm Andrew, the one who contacted you. Come and get him!" Andrew exclaimed excitedly.

Sebastian saw that it was indeed Alexander and was overjoyed.

"Quick! Help me over there..."

Andrew was still trying to take credit. "Mr. Sebastian Tate, the Fairfax family has gone through a lot to capture Alexander. Look at my face—he's bruised it badly."

Sebastian ignored Andrew, his mind racing as he struggled with the thought of publicly begging Alexander for mercy.

Seeing Sebastian's distressed and almost inhuman expression, Alexander felt a surge of amusement.

It was clear Sebastian was buckling under the pressure from the Force and had come here to beg for his life.

As Sebastian pondered how to plead with Alexander, Andrew, eager to take credit, began directing the Fairfax family members to aim their guns at Alexander.

Andrew barked, "Alexander, apologize to Mr. Sebastian Tate now, or I'll blow you to pieces!"
