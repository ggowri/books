Chapter 980 Another Gamble

"Ms. Lawson, this is my birthday gift to you. I hope you have a wonderful birthday," Alexander said with a smile.

"Thank you, Alexander. I really appreciate it!" Grace replied, blushing as she accepted the necklace and signaled for Alexander to help her put it on.

Alexander obliged, fastening the necklace around her neck.

Their closeness appeared quite intimate.

The room fell silent as onlookers cast pitying glances at Asher.

He had previously boasted about being Grace's fiancé, only to embarrass himself.

Ave's expression darkened. As a mother, she understood that a woman allowing a man to put a necklace on her in public likely had feelings for him.

"Son of a bitch!" Asher shouted in anger, throwing the gift to the ground with a ferocious expression on his face.

"What happened? What are you guys doing?" Gavin and Gale stepped out.

Behind him was a group of imposing figures, including the governor, the mayor, several generals, and diplomats. Each of them is a high-ranking powerhouse with an imposing presence.

"Mr. Hawthorne, you're here," Gavin said, greeting Alexander with a warm smile and a firm handshake.

Asher's eyes brightened. He thought, "If I could heal Mr. Gavin Lawson, I'll regain control."

He said, "Mr. Gavin Lawson, Alexander is a fraud. Let me treat your injuries first, and then I'll deal with him."

Gavin frowned, aware that Alexander was formidable and Asher's arrogance was misplaced.

Alexander replied calmly, "Do you think you can heal Mr. Gavin Lawson? You're a fraud."

Furious, Asher pointed at Alexander, shouting, "You're the fraud! That necklace is fake, too. I'll expose you today.

"Let's raise the stakes. If I can cure Mr. Gavin Lawson, you leave Loscity and give me the magic powder formula.

"If I lose, I'll leave Loscity immediately and never return."

Asher had made up his mind to turn the tables.

Everyone knew Gavin's injury was notoriously difficult to treat. Now, Asher wanted to increase the stakes with Alexander. It would be a good show.

Alexander laughed derisively. "You lost before, but I'll give you a chance. Let's have a competition. If you lose, you will not only have to leave Loscity. Do you dare to bet?"

"Okay, I'll bet!" Asher replied confidently, convinced he had the advantage.

After the two made the bet, the atmosphere on the scene became tense again.

Grace felt increasingly anxious, fearing Alexander might lose.

After all, Asher was recognized by everyone as a genius with excellent medical skills.

"Asher, are you really sure?" Gavin asked, concerned.

Hearing Gavin's words, Asher answered respectfully, "Don't worry, Mr. Gavin Lawson. I'm prepared and can cure your injuries. However, I hope to marry Grace. Our families..."

Before he could finish his words, Gavin interrupted him. "If you can truly heal my injuries, then we can discuss it. Otherwise, it's off the table."

Grace's anxiety peaked, her palms sweating.

"Alexander, you must not lose!" she silently prayed, hoping God would bless Alexander.

Asher turned to Grace with a smile. "Grace, I'll definitely cure your grandfather. Just wait to be my wife."

Ave said, "Asher, I know you truly care for Grace. Regardless of the outcome, she should be with you."

"Mom, what are you saying? I won't agree," Grace firmly refused, "My marriage is my own decision, and I don't believe he can cure Grandpa's injury."
