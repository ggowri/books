Chapter 669 Demon Puppet

Where was the wizard hiding? There was nothing!

Alexander still stared at the shadow. With his All-seeing Eyes and powerful perception, he had already discovered the wizard.

It was not the first time he had come into contact with wizards from the City of Sorcery.

However, the wizard's aura was the strongest among all the wizards Alexander had ever met, even stronger than that of Guzman. He must be a big shot in the City of Sorcery.

Alexander's judgment was correct. Beverly's trump card was Dereck, a senior elder there.

Dereck was almost ninety years old. He grew up in the City of Sorcery and was the most famous dark wizard besides Rock.

Alexander kept approaching the shadow aggressively.

Realizing that he couldn't escape, Dereck suddenly emerged from the shadow and waved his hand to send out several puffs of black smoke, which looked like several ferocious black poisonous snakes and enveloped Alexander in a frenzy.​

Everyone was shocked. Witchcraft was a horrible taboo in their minds.

Alexander snorted disdainfully, his All-seeing Eyes catching everything very clearly.

Those puffs of black poisonous smoke were just a disguise on the surface. Dereck's real killer was the mysterious witchcraft insects hidden inside.

Although it was unknown how terrifying the witchcraft insects were, they were definitely fatal.

"Go to hell!"

Alexander swung the Lightsabers quickly, and an impenetrable circular light curtain suddenly formed in front of him.

The black smoke hit the light curtain, just like boiling water poured onto a raging fire, causing bursts of green smoke, which then evaporated and disappeared.

Countless witchcraft insects hidden in the black smoke were also destroyed by the Lightsabers and turned into ashes.

"Damn it! How dare you kill my little babies?"

Dereck suddenly appeared and glared at Alexander fiercely.

He was a skinny man with a few thin white hairs on his head, dressed in a tattered black linen robe.​

His exposed parts seemed to be mere bones, with wrinkled skin clinging to them, and there was not a vestige of flesh. His whole body was shriveled.​

Moreover, his body was covered with all sorts of strange, colorful tattoos, even a white skull tattooed on his neck.​

As Alexander eliminated all the witchcraft insects, a stream of blood flowed from the corner of Dereck's mouth, and he was obviously injured.

"Is this all your strength?" Alexander sneered, walking towards him step by step.

"You are seeking death!"

Dereck, glaring resentfully at Alexander, took from his bosom a tattered weird puppet, waved it at Alexander, and then muttered some incantation.​

"What is this?"

After taking a closer look, Alexander finally realized what that weird puppet was and immediately flew into a rage.

It turned out to be a shriveled fetus the size of a palm that had been refined into a mummy-like appearance. It was completely black and exuded an extremely grotesque aura.

It was a vicious witchcraft, using a fetus to make a demon puppet capable of killing!

The demon puppet was taken from a fetus that had not been born at full term, soaked in special materials such as oil boiled from corpses, and then refined using witchcraft.​

Because it wasn't a full-term baby and was killed along with their mothers, the demon puppet was quite resentful, evil, and ferocious and would take people's lives without hesitation.

The more people it murdered, the greater its power grew. One day, it would turn against Dereck and kill him, the man who refined it, thus becoming a true demon.

People of all faiths, including Christians and Catholic priests, were very afraid of something so evil.​

But there were still a few wizards who were willing to take the risk of being killed by the demon puppets in the end because they were incredibly powerful. As a result of the demon puppet, Dereck became a senior elder in the City of Sorcery, only second to Rock in strength.

Now, Dereck felt the threat from Alexander and used his trump card, the demon puppet!

Alexander sensed a sinister and strange energy rushing towards him.

Suddenly, he felt a chill along his back, as if something were riding on his back, and at the same time, a pair of cold, black hands closed around his throat.

Alexander couldn't breathe, and an acute lack of oxygen made him dizzy.

Everything was doubled. He couldn't see clearly at all, and his body was growing heavier and heavier.
