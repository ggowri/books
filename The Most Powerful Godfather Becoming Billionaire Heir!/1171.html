Chapter 1171 Terrifying Power

"Alexander, you needed to be careful. They had several people."

Lynn, ignoring the danger, desperately reminded Alexander.

"Don't be afraid. No matter how many of them there are, I will get you out safely."

Alexander reassured Lynn, his cold gaze firmly locked on the man in black. As long as that man dared to make a move, Alexander would unleash the Force and take him down with one strike.

If it weren't for worrying about Lynn getting hurt, that man would have already been dead.

Alexander calmly said, "I took Stephen down with a single blow. Do you want to end up like him? Release Lynn and I can spare your life."

"Impossible. Although Stephen was Test Subject Number One and had many imperfections, he could never be taken down by you in one hit."

The man in black scoffed at Alexander's ability.

"Alexander, I know you have some skill, but why did you choose to oppose the Sakra Society?

"Our president said he could give you one last chance. As long as you hand over the magic powder formula and join the Sakra Society, not only will we let you go, but we will also guarantee you eternal life and riches."

Alexander couldn't help but laugh.

"It seems my magic powder played an important role in your experiments, didn't it? Test Subject Number One? Quite interesting."

Although Alexander was laughing, he felt a weight in his heart.

The Sakra Society's research on the Immortality Serum had become increasingly in-depth. They had already begun to mass-produce strong individuals like Stephen, which was a terrifying power.

The man in black knew he wouldn't easily obtain the magic powder formula today.

He tightened his grip on the sword and coldly said, "Hand over the magic powder formula, or I will kill this woman."

As the man in black threatened, he pretended to swing the saber towards Lynn.

In that instant, Alexander made his move.

The Force, which he had stored for a long time, erupted, propelling him forward with an uncanny pace until he stood right in front of the man in black.

The man in black screamed in fear. He never expected Alexander to be so fast.

He couldn't see or keep up with Alexander's movements as if Alexander had always been standing right in front of him.

The man in black tried to escape, but Alexander swiftly followed.

"Damn it!" The man in black shouted in terror, wildly swinging his saber.

In an instant, soft lightsabers ignited and collided with the saber.

The man in black's sword broke in half like paper, and he quickly rolled to the ground in panic.

Blood flowed from his back. He ultimately failed to evade Alexander's strike completely.

However, the man in black miraculously escaped death. With a large chunk of flesh missing from his back and blood soaking him, he trembled all over, his previous confidence completely gone.

He knew he had to fight desperately now, so he shouted with a twisted expression, "Number Three, Number Four, Number Five, why aren't you coming out to fight?"

Several dark shadows moved outside the cave. They were all test subjects from the Sakra Society.

This scene filled Lynn with fear, and her face turned pale.

"Do you even have a chance to fight?"

A fierce glint flashed in Alexander's eyes. He saw very clearly that one of the shadows was carrying a rocket launcher, intending to collapse the cave and bury Alexander alive.

"Looking for death." Alexander threw his lightsabers.

Brilliant white light flashed, and the unfortunate one with the rocket launcher couldn't react in time before both he and the launcher were cut in half.

Then, Alexander kicked the ground, sending a flurry of stones flying. Those stones, propelled by the Force, shot toward the remaining two dark figures like cannonballs.

There was a sound of continuous piercing through flesh. The two men in black were riddled with holes, and before they could attack Alexander, they collapsed to the ground.

The man in black inside the cave spat out a mouthful of blood, his face turning pale.

He charged toward Lynn like a madman. He knew that controlling Lynn was his only chance of survival.

He never expected Alexander to be so powerful, truly capable of taking them down in an instant, especially since each of them had strength comparable to or exceeding Stephen's.

But in front of Alexander, they felt like insignificant trash.
