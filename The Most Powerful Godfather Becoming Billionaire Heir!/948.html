Chapter 948 On High Alert

"You think you can push me around? You really believe we don't have any tricks up our sleeves? Anyone who dares come near us, I'll blow this entire building sky-high!"

Cynthia screamed, slamming her finger onto a red button on a remote control, her finger poised to detonate at a moment's notice.

At the same time, Jaxon let out a guttural roar, his hands raised with a lethal intensity, his body brimming with murderous energy.

Hundreds of dark, poisoned needles shot toward Alexander in an instant, covering him in a storm of deadly projectiles.

But Alexander was like a shadow, his movements so fast they were barely perceptible.

"Ah!" Jaxon howled in agony, his arms twisted in unnatural angles as Alexander wrenched them apart.

The hidden mechanism in his sleeve, which had been firing the poison needles, was destroyed in one swift motion.

Dozens of poisoned needles buried themselves in Jaxon's arms, causing excruciating pain.

As Jaxon fumbled for the antidote, Alexander grinned coldly, a flick of his wrist sending the vial crashing to the ground, shattering it into pieces.

Hundreds of black pills scattered across the floor.

"Alexander, you're a monster!"

Jaxon snarled, his head spinning, darkness clouding his vision.

He tried to grab the antidote from the floor, but it was as if his body was made of lead, collapsing like a fallen tree.

Alexander stood over him, watching impassively as he convulsed on the ground, foaming at the mouth.

Cynthia's face turned ashen, her hand still clutching the detonator, trembling violently as she screamed at Alexander.

"Give him the antidote! Now, or I'll blow everything up! The whole Island will go up in flames!"

Alexander glanced at her coldly, and before she could react, he moved. A blinding flash of light shot out.

Cynthia's hand, still holding the remote, fell to the floor, severed in an instant.

Blood poured from her stump, and she screamed in agony.

Cloe quickly grabbed the detonator, her heart racing.

She couldn't believe that the Ghost had secretly planted explosives in the building.

Alexander gestured coldly. "Take them both away. Don't let them die. Interrogate them thoroughly."

The others snapped out of their stupor, rushing over to drag the two of them out of the room.

Alexander grabbed the detonator from Cloe's hand, his face dark.

"The Ghost, huh? You really want to cross me? Fine, don't say I didn't warn you."

The threats from the Mafia and Sakra Society were still looming, and with Yinson ready to trigger a global crisis at any moment, Alexander couldn't afford to relax.

Now, with the even more dangerous Ghost involved, he needed to be on high alert.

He ordered Cloe to organize an emergency meeting to speed up the defense construction on Treasure and Devil Island.

Top members of the Brilliant Group, including Shadow, William, and Marvin, were all called in.

*****

The next morning, Alexander hurried back to Loscity.

By then, Cloe, who had been working all night, was lying in bed, sound asleep, her body still bearing the marks of his affection.

Norma had already delivered several hundred pounds of iridium to Loscity.

Along with the new batch of rare gems that Alyssa and Beverly had collected, Alexander's first priority was to upgrade the Lightsabers from Level 2 to Level 3.

Power was the foundation of his strength. Only with that could he protect his loved ones.

Alexander didn't care about being a hero, but he couldn't stand the thought of losing the people closest to him, especially not his daughter.

When Ashley had been admitted to the ICU, he had sworn to never let anything hurt her again.

Alexander walked into the villa in Loscity.

"You're finally home..."

Ava rushed into his arms, her emotions spilling over. The worry, fear, and anxiety she had been holding in for days all came crashing down.

Tears streamed down her face like pearls, her body trembling uncontrollably as she clung to him.

"Those monsters... I was so scared... it felt like a nightmare! I love you so much... I don't want to live without you..."

Rosalia watched from the side, her eyes filled with jealousy.

Charlize had just picked up Ashley from kindergarten.

"Daddy!"

Ashley ran straight toward them, and the three of them embraced tightly.

Charlize's eyes shifted, filled with a mix of envy and excitement, her gaze no different from Rosalia's.

On his way back, Alexander had already discussed the situation thoroughly with Augusta.
