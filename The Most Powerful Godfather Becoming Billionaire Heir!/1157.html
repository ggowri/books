Chapter 1157 Just Kidding

Behind the villa, in the garden, there was a standard outdoor swimming pool surrounded by lush flowers, creating a picturesque scene.

The weather was perfect, with clear skies, white clouds, and bright sunshine. It was a beautiful day.

Alexander sank into a lounge chair, sipping juice and humming a cheerful tune, completely relaxed.

When Ava and Lynn changed into their bikinis, Alexander's eyes widened in surprise, his body stiffening with sudden tension.

The two beauties had incredibly seductive figures. Their flawless, voluptuous bodies were curvaceous, and their perfect legs made heads spin.

Under Alexander's intense gaze, Ava and Lynn walked hand-in-hand into the pool.

They looked like two beautiful mermaids from a fairytale, gliding through the crystal-clear water.

They were in high spirits and started a water fight. Water splashed everywhere as they giggled and laughed, their joy adding to the already stunning scenery. Alexander's hormones surged in response, and he couldn't help but feel stirred.

"Water fight? I want to join in."

Soon after, Rebecca also jumped into the pool. The three stunning beauties splashed around playfully, turning the pool into a chaotic scene of laughter and fun.

Alexander felt thirsty and took large gulps of juice, his eyes never leaving them.

After a while, the three beauties grew tired of their play. Ava and Lynn left the pool.

Ava waved at Alexander, saying, "Mom and I are going to prepare lunch. You guys play."

Alexander asked, "Didn't you say other guests would be arriving?"

Ava smiled and gave him a teasing look, "Are you that gullible? I told you there would be other guests, but actually, it's just the four of us today. We were just messing with you."

Alexander was speechless. Had he known this earlier, he would have jumped into the pool and had some fun with them.

Ava and Lynn walked away hand in hand. Lynn leaned in to whisper something in Ava's ear, causing Ava's face to turn bright red.

At that moment, Rebecca made a funny face at Alexander, slightly embarrassed.

She, of course, understood that Ava leaving meant they were being given some private time together.

Alexander grinned at Rebecca and then patted the lounge chair beside him.

Seeing that Ava and Lynn had gone, Rebecca left the pool and sat down beside Alexander.

His eyes roamed over her alluring figure, growing even more excited.

The bikini clung to Rebecca's fiery figure, droplets of water dotting her smooth, pale skin.

The water sparkled under the sunlight, tracing the curves of her body, flowing from top to bottom, accentuating her shapely backside and long legs, making her irresistibly seductive.

"Have you missed me recently?" Alexander asked with a playful smile, already feeling a surge of excitement within.

"It's you who must have missed me," Rebecca shot him a teasing glance while reaching for a towel to dry off.

The movements of her body as she wiped herself down only drew more attention, making Alexander struggle to control himself.

Seeing Alexander's intense gaze, Rebecca couldn't help but feel a sense of delight.

There was nothing more satisfying than seeing her beloved man so captivated by her.

"So, what gift did you prepare for me?" Rebecca's eyes sparkled, locked onto him with an unreadable expression.

"A gift? Ahem, sorry..." Alexander cleared his throat awkwardly, trying to cover up his embarrassment.

"But I promise you. Today is your big day, the day you move into your new home. You're the most important. Whatever gift you want, as long as I have it, it's yours."

"Really? I can have anything I want?" Rebecca's eyes gleamed mischievously, full of playful intent.

"Well... I suppose that's fine. You can tell me what you want," Alexander hesitated for a moment, gathering the courage to respond.

"I want you to marry me. How about that?" Rebecca leaned in closer to Alexander, her expression one of coy amusement, like a sly little fox.

Upon hearing this, Alexander was completely stunned. He scratched his head, unsure of how to respond.

Rebecca leaned in even closer, her delicate fragrance overwhelming him. Her flawless body was so near that Alexander could almost feel the warmth of her skin.

He swallowed hard, forcing a smile as he stammered, "Rebecca, you're just joking with me, right? I'm not thinking about marriage right now. How about you pick something else?"

"Ha! You can't handle it. You said I could have anything I want, but I knew that you can't let go of Ava."

Rebecca gave Alexander a playful but forceful pinch before rolling her eyes at him. Pouting, she angrily sat back down on the lounge chair.
