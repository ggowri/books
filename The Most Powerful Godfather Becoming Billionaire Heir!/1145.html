Chapter 1145 I'll Deal With Him

With the power of Sunrise Group behind him, Karson held significant influence at the port.

Joaquin had initially planned to poison Karson, but his security was too tight, and after exhausting all his drugs, he failed miserably.

"Karson? I'll take care of him!" Stephen scoffed derisively.

*****

Meanwhile, Alexander was on his way to Louvian when his phone rang—it was a call from Quentin.

"Alex, I've just received word that Stephen from Sakra Society has arrived in Louvian. They're planning to attack my friend Karson. Can you help him out for my sake?" Quentin asked, his tone more of a plea than a demand.

Alexander's interest was piqued. He had been worried about locating someone from Sakra Society, and since Quentin was Brittany's grandfather and had supported him greatly, he felt inclined to help.

"Don't worry. I will definitely help him!" he replied.

"Great! I'll have Karson reach out to you," Quentin said, hanging up with a sense of relief.

Before long, two black Cadillacs pulled up, hazard lights flashing, leading the way.

Alexander smiled, "Karson's people are here already. That was quick."

With the Chapman family's vehicles in the lead, Alexander's BMW sailed through intersections, ignoring red lights.

The journey that usually took over three hours was cut down to just two as they arrived at Karson's residence.

Karson had constructed an impressive ancient castle by the sea, a lavish imitation of royal architecture, showcasing great wealth and power.

The heavy security surrounding the castle made it clear that the owner held an extraordinary status. Armed guards patrolled the area, and there were booths equipped with heavy weaponry.

Alexander recognized immediately that these guards were mercenaries seasoned from battle, each with piercing eyes that radiated a dangerous aura.

An old man with gray hair approached with a hearty laugh. "Hey, Alex! You're finally here. I've been looking forward to your arrival! Come in and take a seat."

His familiarity instantly brought him close to Alexander.

Louvian was not far from Loscity, and Karson was understandably cautious about Ultimate Alliance's might, especially since Alexander was invited by Quentin for assistance.

"Alex, thank you for coming!" Karson greeted warmly, shaking Alexander's hand.

"Mr. Chapman, it's my pleasure," Alexander replied with a smile as he followed Karson into the castle and settled into a spacious, opulent living room.

Alongside Karson and Alexander sat three others.

A quick glance revealed they were all formidable individuals, one of whom was a metahuman with powers.

Their presence was palpable; their breathing was slow yet powerful, distinctive enough for Alexander to discern immediately.

"Alex, please take a seat here," Karson said, motioning for Alexander to take the head seat.

Without hesitation, Alexander sat down.

Cesar Dussel, dressed in black, shot a challenging look at Alexander. "Hey, kid, that's pretty presumptuous of you!"

Alexander asked coldly, "What do you mean?"

Cesar frowned. "Don't play dumb. The people in front of you are all notable figures. Do you really think you belong in the most important chair?"

Alexander was speechless. Fighting over a seat seemed rather petty to him.

Despite Cesar's fiery temperament, his intense gaze and heavily calloused fists hinted at a dangerous capability that made people scared.

"Youngsters today really lack respect for elders," another man chimed in, targeting Alexander.

It was Dariel Garvey, a towering figure over 6.3 feet tall, broad-shouldered with big hands and thick fingers.

"Everyone..." Karson began to explain, but Alexander interjected.

He said calmly, "There's no point in wasting our breath on them. When Stephen arrives, we'll let our actions speak for themselves."

Karson nodded awkwardly, opting to remain silent.

Dariel narrowed his eyes at Alexander, scoffing, "Actions? I could take down Stephen on my own. I don't see why Mr. Chapman would give you a shot."
