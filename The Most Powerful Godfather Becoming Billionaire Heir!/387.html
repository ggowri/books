Chapter 387 Naïve

"Hahaha!"

Alexander couldn't help but burst out laughing.

"What are you laughing at?" Eddie asked, his heart pounding.

Alexander gestured. "Bring that guy over."

Marvin immediately brought Calvin forward.

"Calvin, you useless coward! Weren't you supposed to be The Cripples' top fighter? Pathetic!" Eddie cursed.

Calvin said nothing, walking straight to Eddie. He took the dagger Logan handed him and drove it straight into Eddie's chest.

"Ahh!"

Eddie collapsed to the ground, clutching his bleeding chest and staring in terror at Calvin.

Calvin's expression twisted with fury.

"Boss, sorry, but Mr. Hawthorne said if I kill you and hand over the list of The Cripples' members, he'll spare me."

Eddie's face twisted as he fell heavily to the floor, unwilling to accept his fate.

Calvin fell to his knees before Alexander.

"Mr. Hawthorne, can you let me go now?" Calvin asked.

Alexander looked at him with a cold stare.

"Bernard was in on this kidnapping too, wasn't he? Call him. Tell him to come over, or you'll be joining him in hell to meet Satan."

Calvin shuddered. Alexander was ruthless, forcing Bernard to walk right into his trap.

But Calvin knew he had no way out and had to follow Alexander's orders; this was his only shot at survival.

Calvin didn't even have the courage to try bargaining. He hurriedly pulled out his phone and called Bernard.

Soon, the call connected.

"Mr. Clifford, we've captured Alexander, yes... my boss requests you come over... yes, at the villa. Alright, I'll be waiting!"

After hanging up, Calvin was gasping for air.

Alexander shot him a scornful glance. No matter what Calvin did, Alexander planned to silence him in the end.

All the members of The Cripples had been disarmed, cuffed, and taken down to the basement.

Calvin served Alexander tea and water like a loyal servant.

Marvin and Logan led over a hundred members of the Ultimate Alliance, standing around imposing and ready.

Each one stood tall and straight, their gazes cold and filled with murderous intent.

Eddie wasn't completely dead yet, lying on the floor as blood continued to pour from his wounds.

He made several weak attempts to call for help, but Alexander ignored him, calmly sipping his coffee as if he hadn't heard.

Calvin occasionally glanced over at Eddie, his heart filled with indescribable fear.

Soon, the sound of hurried footsteps could be heard from outside.

"Boss, I really appreciate you avenging my son this time!"

Before he even entered, Bernard's excited voice echoed through the hall.

Bernard walked in with a broad smile, taking big strides.

Immediately, someone blocked the door, and the hall filled with men in black, their intimidating presence unmistakable.

Bernard froze, his face paling as he saw Eddie lying motionless on the ground and blood everywhere.

"Bernard, do you want revenge for your son?" Alexander asked with a smile.

Bernard whipped around, finally noticing Alexander sitting confidently on the sofa.

"You! What are you doing here? You weren't captured?" Bernard stammered, his face twisting in shock.

His face turned pale.

"Alexander, I'm head of the Clifford family! You touch a single hair on my head, and the Cliffords won't forgive you."

"Shut up!"

Before he could say another word, Marvin grabbed Bernard and slapped him several times.

Marvin was always ruthless with enemies, and he hit Bernard so hard that he spat blood, his face swelling up like a pig's.

"Kneel to my boss!"

Logan kicked Bernard hard in the knee.

Bernard screamed, dropping heavily to his knees, cold sweat pouring down his face from the pain.

Relying on the power of the Clifford family, he had always been arrogant, never taking others seriously. Now, he finally felt fear.

Alexander stood up, hands clasped behind his back, and calmly walked over to Bernard.

"Bernard, your son did countless evil deeds; all I did was ruin his hands.

"You harassed Ava, and I still let you go.

"Instead of showing any gratitude, you dared to conspire with The Cripples to kidnap me. Tell me, don't you deserve to die?!"

Alexander's cold gaze radiated pure murderous intent.

"W-What are you planning? Murder is against the law. You'll go to prison..."

Bernard was completely panicked, childishly trying to rely on the law to save him.
