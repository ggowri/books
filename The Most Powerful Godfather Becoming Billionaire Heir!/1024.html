Chapter 1024 Break the Poison

Ava's smile faded, and her expression turned cold.

"What's wrong? Is your family really the kind to repay kindness with ingratitude?"

Malakai's face twisted in distress, and he quickly explained, "Ms. Blackburn, I'm sorry. That's not what I meant! This is a very difficult decision. I need to consult with my mother."

Ava frowned. It seemed that Malakai really had his reasons.

Everyone knew Malakai was a dutiful son, and his mother was a business genius in her own right.

Without her, the Nexura Group wouldn't be where it was today.

Malakai stepped into the hallway, dialing his phone and pacing nervously.

"Yes, yes... you're right..."

He nodded while rubbing his eyes.

Ava, perplexed, couldn't help but wonder why Malakai was on the verge of tears.

When the call ended, Malakai's face was clouded with a feeling of deep sorrow.

Then he turned to Ava, his voice low and filled with regret.

"Ms. Blackburn, I just spoke to my mother. We will continue our cooperation with you, and we'll supply materials at even better prices."

"Really? Not only will you resume the supply, but at a discounted rate?"

Ava's face lit up with joy. The situation had taken a complete turn, and she was finally at ease.

Malakai bowed deeply.

"I apologize for the disturbance, Ms. Blackburn. Please extend our family's respects to Godfather. If possible, we would like to meet him in person to express our gratitude."

Ava didn't know how to answer. After all, she couldn't make decisions regarding Godfather on her own. She would have to consult with Alexander first.

Seeing Ava remain silent, Malakai thought she was still unhappy.

With determination, he said, "From now on, the Nexura Group will provide materials at a 20% discount."

"A 20% discount?" Ava was stunned by this good news.

Her excitement was barely contained. After all, the money saved would be a fortune.

"Mr. Valdez, it's too much. After all, the Nexura Group needs to make a profit, too. As for your visit to the Godfather, I'll definitely report that."

Relieved by her response, Malakai exhaled but still looked troubled.

Ramona suddenly burst into tears, wailing uncontrollably.

"Malakai, if we go against their orders, what will happen to our son?"

"If we don't follow the Vanderbilt family's instructions, our son will die!"

"My poor son... he has just turned five... What will we do?

"Doctor said I can't have another child... If anything happens to our son, I won't be able to live with myself!"

Ramona clung to Malakai, sobbing desperately.

"Ms. Blackburn, I'm sorry to let you see this. The Vanderbilt family in Capital Region is threatening me. They want me to stop supplying materials. They want revenge on you. If I don't comply, my son's poison will trigger."

Malakai sighed and his gaze was heavy with sorrow.

Ava finally understood. It was Felix's family behind this, threatening Malakai and Ramona from the shadows.

They were forcing them to stop supplying materials, or their son might die.

Ava's expression softened. "I can find someone to help your son. The poison won't be a problem."

Malakai's eyes widened with hope.

"Ms. Blackburn, who are you talking about?"

Malakai suddenly bowed to Ava.

"Mr. Valdez, please! I'll definitely help you!"

Ava hurried to stop him.

Ramona bowed to her as well, crying as she begged,

"Ms. Blackburn, please help us... We've tried everything. We even went to City of Sorcery, but nothing worked. We had no choice but to comply with the Vanderbilt family's demands.

"Skyla said if I don't stop the supply, she won't save our son... It's all the Vanderbilt family's doing."

Ramona sobbed as she pleaded, "If you can save our son, we will follow your lead. The Nexura Group will do anything you ask."

Ava quickly helped them to their feet and reassured them, "Don't worry, Alexander will help you."

Malakai's face lit up with joy. "Can Mr. Hawthorne really cure the poison?"

"Of course. Even Ms. Quinn in City of Sorcery is defeated by him!"

Ava was fully confident in Alexander's abilities.

With Ava's assurance, Malakai and Ramona were overjoyed.
