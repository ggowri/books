Chapter 1130 Undercover

Alexander pulled out his cell phone and dialed Elaine's number.

The ringtone sounded for a long time, but no one answered.

Alexander couldn't help but frown, realizing that it was impossible to find Yinson through Elaine.

Ever since his departure from Risun, he had lost contact with her.

Controlled by the Force, she worked undercover under Yinson.

To Alexander's surprise, she disappeared before he could make good use of her, and she was probably killed.

As he remained silent, Xavier hurriedly filled Alexander's glass with beer.

"Alexander, I think Tobias is quite sensible and hardworking. Maybe he's not Yinson's man?"

Alexander smirked. "Xavier, I bet this Tobias is with Yinson. He planted him to get to Ultimate Alliance through you."

Xavier's face darkened in an instant.

"If so, why did he need to poison me?"

Alexander explained calmly, "Yinson relied on the Immortality Serum to develop a strengthening potion that is harmful to normal people."

"You are poisoned with that potion, or you are infected. If nothing goes wrong, when the poison takes effect, Tobias will threaten you."

"They must have drugs that can delay the onset of the poison. Because of your desire to survive, you will have no choice but to be at their disposal."

Xavier jumped up in shock and asked in a trembling voice, "Boss, will I become a living dead?"

Alexander patted his arm and said, "Don't worry. I will treat you right now."

He injected a powerful Force into Xavier's body.

Xavier shuddered and felt burning hot and energetic.

After a while, Alexander released him and said, "Okay. I have cleared all the poison within you."

"Really? That's great. Boss, you are really awesome."

Xavier was overjoyed and really felt a great deal more at ease.​

Alexander tapped his arm and urged, "Eat it. After breakfast, I'll go to Phoenix Gemstone Establishment to meet Tobias. It will be great if we can find clues about Yinson from him."

"Okay. If Tobias is really an undercover agent of the Sakra Society, I'm going to skin him alive!"

Xavier had a serious and murderous look.

Alexander nodded secretly. Xavier used to be timid. In order to collect the top-grade gems for Alexander, he traveled to all the gem-producing areas around the world.

Having encountered various people and many dangers, he was no longer the same as before but had become resolute and decisive.

"Alexander, I'm full. Let's go!"

Xavier finished his spaghetti in no time and couldn't sit still any longer.

Alexander smiled and shook his head, knowing that Xavier really wanted to settle the score with Tobias. They got into the car together hurriedly.

Coming to Gemstone Street again, Alexander found that it had changed a lot and became more prosperous. More and more customers came in an endless stream to buy gems.

"Xavier, you manage it well!"

Alexander nodded with satisfaction.

Xavier smiled, "It's all thanks to my mother. I am out on errands. Mainly because of the support of the Ultimate Alliance, no one on this street dares to disobey."

Two security guards were standing at the door of Phoenix Gemstone Establishment. At the sight of Xavier, they hurriedly came up to him with grins.

"Mr. Ulysses, good morning!"

Xavier nodded. Alexander winked at him, and they walked straight in.

The two security guards did not know Alexander, but seeing that Xavier was very respectful to him, they treated him with great courtesy.​

"Is Tobias here?" Xavier asked the receptionist casually.

The receptionist smiled gently, "Tobias just went in. Mr. Ulysses, do you need me to call him over?"

"No."

Xavier waved his hand and then looked at Alexander. They entered quietly into the office.

With a ding, the elevator doors opened, and they stepped out.

The building belonged to the Ulysses family. The front was a shop. The upper floors were used as offices, and the underground space was a warehouse.

Xavier and Alexander reached the floor, only to find that the office was empty.

"Hey! Where's Tobias?"

Xavier was puzzled.
