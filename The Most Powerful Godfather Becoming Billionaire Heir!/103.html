Chapter 103 Negotiation

"Alexander!"

The moment Ava spotted him, she rushed over with enthusiasm.

Today, she was dressed to impress in a sleek black Chanel suit paired with a crisp white shirt and just a hint of makeup that exuded both elegance and allure.

Memories of last night's candlelit dinner flooded her mind, and she felt her cheeks flush as she recalled waking up bare in bed.

"I'm here to pick you up. Let's go!"

Alexander grinned. "Are you planning another candlelit dinner?"

As he spoke, he wrapped his arm around Ava's waist and planted a kiss on her cheek.

"What are you thinking?"

Ava pushed him away playfully, feeling a mix of shyness and excitement.

Once they were in the car, Ava filled him in on the latest.

Rebecca would be bringing the Hansen family to negotiate, and they were gearing up to seize the initiative at tomorrow's conference.

Along with Rebecca, the heiress of the Hansen family, there was Orion Hansen, Rebecca's cousin and the one currently holding the reins of power.

Ava continued, her tone serious, "According to reliable intel, the real mastermind behind the magic powder will be there tomorrow.

"Sebastian is definitely going to try to snatch that formula. No matter what, we can't let him succeed!"

As she spoke, she adjusted Alexander's collar, her hands deftly smoothing out any wrinkles.

"Don't worry, he won't get the formula," Alexander replied confidently, a smile spreading across his face.

Ava raised an eyebrow, saying, "It's great that you're confident, but Rebecca is coming to negotiate mainly because of me.

"So, please, don't start any arguments with them."

Alexander nodded, his expression turning serious.

Before long, they arrived at the Saint Group.

Hand in hand, they walked into the meeting room, where they immediately spotted a burly man in a sharp suit lounging on the sofa.

His intense gaze was fixed on Alexander and Ava.

Alexander's eyes first landed on Rebecca, who huffed and turned away.

Then his attention shifted to the man, who exuded a calm, powerful presence—clearly no slouch in the strength department, likely on par with Joseph.

"Mr. Hansen, this is my boyfriend, Alexander," Ava introduced warmly.

Orion remained seated, showing no intention of standing up to shake hands.

"Ms. Blackburn, I've heard your boyfriend's quite the fighter, but looking at him, he seems a bit... underwhelming."

As he spoke, he flexed his massive biceps, clearly trying to intimidate.

In response to Orion's provocation, Alexander simply smiled and said nothing.

Ava felt her irritation rise; Orion was being downright rude.

Just then, to her surprise, Alexander extended his hand toward Orion. "Mr. Hansen, it's a pleasure to meet you."

Orion paused, clearly caught off guard by Alexander's boldness.

As their hands clasped, Orion squeezed tightly, hoping to embarrass Alexander in front of Ava.

With two decades of boxing and strength training behind him, he was confident this pretty boy would wince in pain.

But in the next moment, Orion was stunned. No matter how hard he gripped, it was as if his strength had vanished into thin air.

Alexander remained calm, even managing a smirk that suggested he wasn't fazed at all.

"Mr. Hansen, why the death grip? This is my hand, not a gym weight." Alexander chuckled, casually shaking off Orion's hold.

Suddenly, a surge of Force emanated from Alexander, sending Orion crashing to the ground.

"Are you okay?"

Rebecca rushed over to help Orion.

She asked, her voice laced with anger, "Alexander, what's the meaning of this? Were you trying to ambush him?"

Orion's face turned crimson. He had been the one to provoke, yet here he was, humiliated.

"Rebecca, it's fine. This guy actually has some strength. Maybe we can talk about a collaboration," Orion said.

Rebecca blinked in surprise. Orion was known for his arrogance, and she hadn't expected him to acknowledge Alexander's abilities.

Orion continued, his tone shifting, "However, I have a small request. If Ms. Blackburn wants to partner with the Hansen family, well..."

Ava sensed trouble brewing and cautiously asked, "Mr. Hansen, what do you mean?"

Orion straightened his suit, adopting a faux polite demeanor.

"Ms. Blackburn, I've always admired you. Given this opportunity for our collaboration, would you consider being my girlfriend?"
