Chapter 215 Villain

Alexander smiled coldly and did not comment.

He was not naive at all. He came here today just to see what Aiden was up to.

Alexander had a plan. If he could support Julia and Xavier to control the Ulysses family, it would be very helpful for him to collect gems in the future.

"Mr. Hawthorne, I want you to represent our family in a fight with the Carter family to keep the control of Gemstone Street."

Aiden said with a smile.

Alexander suddenly realized that Aiden wanted to use him. No wonder Aiden was so attentive, inviting Alexander to dinner and giving him a gem.

Alexander said calmly,

"Since you are so sincere, I will help you. But please give me the gemstone you promised first."

Aiden immediately took out an exquisite gift box and pushed it to Alexander reluctantly.

Alexander opened it. It was indeed a top-quality ruby. Although it was not big, it was valuable.

Angelica taunted, "Since you've accepted our gem, you have to win. If you can't, you'll have to pay double the compensation!"

Alexander gave her a cold look and said, "Okay, I will."

"I wish us a happy cooperation!"

Aiden laughed and applauded.

"Aiden, Angelica, I'm back!"

At this time, an excited male voice sounded.

Everyone turned around and saw a handsome man in his twenties striding in, looking exhausted.

"Kevin, why are you back?"

As soon as Angelica saw him, she rushed over excitedly.

"Kevin, weren't you learning boxing? Why did you come back?"

Kevin patted her ass and said with a smile.

"My studies are finished. I heard that the Carter family was going to have a fight with us, I rushed back immediately."

Aiden asked, "Kevin, really?"

Aiden stood up excitedly. Kevin's skills must be very good.

Aiden said, "But the person the Carter family hired is very powerful and difficult to deal with."

Kevin laughed contemptuously and said, "Don't worry, Aiden. My teacher said that with my skills, I can rank among the top three in Loscity."

After saying this, he pressed his palm on the solid wood table.

A clear palm print actually appeared on the hard table top, which showed how powerful and terrifying this palm print was.

Aiden was suddenly inexplicably excited.

"Kevin, did your teacher really say that?"

Kevin said, "Of course. Don't worry. No matter who the Carter family hires, they are no match for me."

Kevin patted his chest and made the promise confidently.

"That's great! You have worked hard for twenty years and finally made it. With you, our family will be better."

Aiden patted Kevin's shoulder hard.

Angelica pointed at Alexander and shouted arrogantly.

"Leave the gem here and get out of here. Kevin is back. We don't need a piece of trash like you to help us!"

Alexander looked at Aiden coldly.

Aiden said with a fake smile.

"Mr. Hawthorne, do you hear what Angelica said? Put the gem down and apologize to my daughter before you can leave."

Alexander couldn't help but laugh. This family was so similar.

Aiden was a hateful villain.

Kevin asked, "Aiden, who is he? What does he do?"

Kevin looked at Alexander in confusion.

Aiden said with a gloomy face, "This guy bullied Angelica and was going to take our family's top-quality gemstone. He's abominable."

Kevin was furious. He strode up to Alexander and sternly ordered.

"Apologize to Angelica. Otherwise, I will teach you a lesson!"

"You are the ones who should apologize."

Alexander answered simply.

Kevin looked Alexander up and down and sneered.

"You are weak but very brave. Aren't you afraid I'll hit you?"

Alexander looked down on it and left with the gem.

"This is your reward for changing your mind. As long as you can stop me, it'll be fine. Don't fight with me. I'm warning you."

Kevin got angry was about to take action, but he was stopped by Xavier.

"Aiden, you were the ones who invited us to dinner and gave us the gem. Now you regret it. You've gone too far."

Xavier said angrily.
