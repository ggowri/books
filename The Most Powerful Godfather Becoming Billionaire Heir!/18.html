Chapter 18 Pleading

The car door swung open, and a group of men charged out aggressively.

Alexander immediately pulled Ava behind him.

"Ms. Blackburn, don't be afraid. I'll protect you."

Ava felt a surge of warmth in her heart. Despite facing eight or nine men, Alexander showed no fear.

Ava chuckled and said, "Just a bunch of trash. I'm not scared."

As the CEO of the Saint Group, Ava had seen plenty of fights and never bothered with small-time thugs like these.

"Boss, that's Alexander!"

The bald man eagerly pointed at Alexander.

Billy sneered, threatening.

"Kid, come with us to the hospital to see Mr. Hawkins, or we'll kill you."

Alexander calmly replied, "Ryan started it. He deserved what he got."

Billy, infuriated, shouted, "Still talking back? In this world, might makes right. Right now, we've got the numbers, so it's your bad luck to be caught!"

Alexander looked at him with pity. "Are you sure numbers matter?"

Billy's gang burst into laughter.

"Boss, this kid's an idiot. Let me knock some sense into him."

The bald man drew a shiny knife and walked toward Alexander with a sinister grin.

Ava immediately stepped out from behind Alexander, glaring at the bald man.

"If anyone dares touch him, I'll wipe out your entire family!"

"I'll handle this."

Alexander said, trying to shield Ava again, but she gently pushed him aside.

Seeing Ava, Billy froze, showing a flicker of recognition. He thought, "She looked familiar, but I couldn't quite place where I'd seen her before."

The bald man didn't recognize Ava, but he was instantly captivated by her beauty.

"Hey, bitch, watch your mouth! Or are you asking for sex?"

He leered at Ava as he moved closer.

Ava, enraged, shouted.

"Billy, do you have a death wish?"

At that moment, Ava's demeanor was no longer tender and flirtatious towards Alexander. She radiated the commanding presence of a leader.

Her stunning face, combined with that demeanor, sent a chill through everyone.

"Bitch, you dare threaten our boss? Boys, let's take her down and have some fun!"

The bald man sneered as his group got ready to pounce.

Ava stared at Billy, speaking each word slowly.

"Do you really not recognize who I am?"

Billy slapped his forehead, suddenly remembering.

He shuddered violently, cold sweat breaking out on his forehead, his back drenched.

The bald man, eager to please, leaned in.

"Boss, let's take this bitch too. You can have her first..."

"Shut up, you idiot!"

Billy roared, swinging his arm and landing a hard slap on the bald man's face.

"If you want to die, don't drag me with you!"

The bald man held his burning cheek, stunned by the sudden turn of events.

The others were equally shocked, unsure of what was happening.

Under the astonished gaze of his men, Billy ran over to Ava and bowed deeply with a fawning smile.

"Ms. Blackburn, I truly didn't recognize you just now. Please forgive me."

Ava's expression was icy. Being insulted by the bald man in front of so many people had put her in a foul mood.

"Billy, your Wolf Gang is really something now. It won't be long before you have Loscity at your feet."

Ava's murderous intent was palpable, sending a shiver down everyone's spine.

Billy's face turned pale with fear. If the Saint Group decided to act, not only would the Wolf Gang be wiped out overnight, but his entire family would meet a terrible fate.

"Ms. Blackburn, you're joking, right? In front of you, I'm just an ant on the ground. You could crush me with a single step."

"I see your Wolf Gang has grown quite bold!"

Ava's voice was full of anger as she slapped Billy across the face.

"Still thinking about kidnapping Alexander? Still planning to rape me?"

Billy fell to his knees with a thud, slapping himself hard across the face and pleading.

"Ms. Blackburn, please spare me! I had no idea Mr. Hawthorne was your friend, or I wouldn't have dared come.

"It's all his fault! He was disrespectful to Ms. Blackburn. I will teach him a harsh lesson."
