Chapter 890 Venomous Snake

"A true top-tier assassin adapts to the environment and chooses the most effective way to eliminate their target.

"The master taught you everything you need, but he told me one extra thing... A real assassin doesn't waste words."

Shadow's eyes burned with rage, and he wanted nothing more than to tear this bastard to pieces.

He quickly searched Flame's body and found the antidote, swallowing it immediately.

Swish!

Shadow sliced off Flame's head, grabbed it, and ran toward Marvin.

"Huh, that useless Flame got himself killed."

A flash of lightning illuminated Shadow as he emerged from the darkness, bleeding heavily and holding Flame's severed head.

Flame's lifeless eyes were wide open, filled with disbelief.

Locke's expression turned grim, his gaze fierce and cruel.

Marvin was overjoyed and quickly ran to join Shadow.

Seeing Shadow's limp right arm with blood still flowing freely, Marvin frowned. It was clear Shadow had lost most of his fighting strength.

"You're too badly hurt..."

Relieved to see Marvin unharmed, Shadow's tightly strung nerves eased a little.

"Don't worry, the boss will be here soon..."

Suddenly, Marvin pressed an assault rifle against Shadow's head.

Shadow was shocked and furious. "What are you doing?"

Marvin's face was filled with pain and struggle, his strong body trembling violently as though resisting an invisible force.

Marvin stammered, "It's not me... They're trying to force me to pull the trigger..."

As he explained, he fought to move the barrel away.

Shadow quickly dodged, putting some distance between them.

In the shadows, the woman had closed her eyes, her hands outstretched toward Marvin as if manipulating him from afar.

"Mr. Welsh, that man is too strong. I can't fully control him or hold him for long. Hurry and finish this..."

Locke nodded. "Your binding ability has improved—very impressive!"

He turned to Marvin and Shadow, his fierce gaze brimming with murderous intent.

Shadow roared, "Marvin! Fight off her control and unleash your full strength. Hurry, you can do it!

"I order you to kill him!"

The woman's sharp voice pierced the air, screeching like an owl.

Blood streamed from Marvin's eyes, yet he refused to give in. His face contorted in pain as he struggled to break free of her control.

"I command you to shoot!"

The woman continued her furious shouting, unwilling to give up.

Marvin's body trembled violently, blood now dripping from his eyes, nose, and mouth. Yet, he still refused to pull the trigger on Shadow.

Locke chuckled coldly.

"Impressively stubborn. Looks like I'll have to handle this myself."

He suddenly vanished into the shadows, followed by the eerie sound of a whistle.

A rustling sound filled the air as countless venomous snakes slithered out of the forest, closing in on Marvin and Shadow under the whistle's command.

The mass of snakes flicked their tongues, all of them king cobras.

Shadow and Marvin now understood why there were no guards on their way here.

With so many cobras around, anyone foolish enough to intrude would surely be bitten to death.

Hiss!

The cobras lunged at them, some with their hoods flared, spraying venom in their direction.

The snakes and their venom surrounded them, leaving Shadow and Marvin with no escape.

Shadow's face turned pale. Already gravely injured, he was powerless against such a dire situation.

Death was closing in!

In that moment, Marvin's vision blurred, and everything around him seemed to freeze.

The sound of rain falling surrounded him, the cobras closing in, venom already raining down from above.

"No! I can't die! Even if I die, I won't let Shadow die!"

Marvin gritted his teeth, determined to break free from the powerful force holding him back.

The attempt to force him to kill Shadow was unforgivable.

"Kill!"

Marvin roared furiously, his heartbeat surging, blood pumping faster than ever.

A medical exam at that moment would reveal Marvin's adrenaline levels skyrocketing to dozens of times the normal amount.

Adrenaline surged through his body, unleashing unimaginable strength.
