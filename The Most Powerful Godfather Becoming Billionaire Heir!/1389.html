Chapter 1389 Shameless

Logan's face was glowing with excitement as he eagerly reported his progress.

"Alexander, I've already secured several dozen acres of land. I've built glass-tiled greenhouses and bought seedlings and equipment from Hall of Lives. I plan to start with fleeceflower root."

Alexander patted him on the shoulder. "Not bad!"

Alexander paused, his expression turning serious as he spoke slowly, "I gathered you all here today to tell you—I'm joining the imperial army on the campaign to Xhantao! While I'm gone, you must stay on high alert, especially around Josh's territory. Vegeta, I want you stationed at Sacred Spirit Gang. I have a feeling something big will happen there."

Everyone nodded in agreement.

After the others had left, Logan looked anxious. "Alexander, are you really enlisting?"

Alexander's eyes flashed coldly. His tone was firm and unwavering. "To break through the limits of martial arts, there is no turning back!

"Logan, be careful. If anything happens, retreat to Dominion Church."

"Don't worry, Alexander. I'm a Hall of Lives client now. No one would dare touch me!"

Two days later, the trade fair was jointly hosted by the Outer Court of Dominion Church and the Hrodvik Empire. Participants included Dragon Sect, Elite School, Iron Vanguard, Four Seas Guild, Pacific Trading Coalition, and many more...

The entire arena was packed with people, shoulder to shoulder. There were Outer Court disciples, imperial officers ranked captain and above, as well as representatives from various trade guilds, martial halls, and escort agencies, all gathered to enlist.

The vast square was filled with densely packed stalls and fluttering banners.

"Huh? Why are there Risunese here at the trade fair? Aren't we about to go to war with them?"

"You don't know? The Pacific Trading Coalition, which is Risunese is putting up both money and resources, publicly condemning Risun for attacking a vassal state of the empire."

"Wouldn't that make them traitors to their own country? Hahaha!"

A crowd gathered around the Pacific Trading Coalition stall. The Risunese merchants there showed no shame—in fact, they seemed rather proud of themselves.

Alexander sighed inwardly. If the Hrodvik Empire were truly strong, there would be no traitors. No one would look to foreign powers with admiration.

As Alexander wandered through the stalls, he quickly got a sense of things. The best goods came from Dominion Church, followed by those from the imperial court.

The rest would be considered high-quality anywhere else, but here, they were second-rate.

He decided to check out what the Outer Court's marketplace had to offer. At the center of the arena stood several long wooden platforms.

Multi-colored protective energy barriers shimmered above the platforms, displaying a variety of goods inside. A large crowd had gathered to browse.

Nearby, two arched gateways—one large, one small—shimmered like rippling water. Several priest guards stood watch, while a middle-aged scholar sat behind a large nanmu wood table, accompanied by a few attendants.

The scholar had a powerful aura. He was at least a level-four warrior.

Alexander was stunned. A fighter of this caliber was working as a gatekeeper? Curious, he walked over and asked, "What's this entrance for?"

Seeing Alexander's impressive demeanor, the scholar didn't dare be disrespectful. He stood up and bowed.

"This is an auction jointly hosted by the Hrodvik Business Alliance and the Hrodvik Treasury. Entry requires a silver waist badge from the Dominion Church's Outer Court or a military rank of lieutenant or higher, along with a 1,000 silver coin fee."

A thousand silver coins wasn't much, but right now, he only had half a chest of jewels...

Alexander made up his mind and asked the scholar, "Can I use jewelry and jade as payment instead of silver?"

The scholar chuckled. "Of course, we accept jewelry and jade, but there will be a slight depreciation."

Alexander calculated quickly—probably about a 10% markdown. That wasn't a big deal.

He reached into his Dimensional pouch, pulled out his silver waist badge, and placed a large nanmu wood chest on the table. When he lifted the lid, a dazzling display of gems and gold shone brightly.

The scholar's eyes lit up when he saw the Dimensional pouch, but when the chest was opened, he was momentarily stunned.

"Sir... this... we'll need a moment to appraise this properly..."

"No rush. Take your time," Alexander said with a casual smile.

He smirked to himself. Money really does make the world go round—even my title just changed to 'sir.'

The scholar bowed slightly. "If you trust us, sir, you may enter the auction first. Within half an hour, your silver notes will be delivered to you."

Alexander nodded. There was no reason to doubt any transaction within Dominion Church.

The Hrodvik Trade Alliance and Hrodvik Treasury had business spread across the entire Sahasia Continent. His wealth was just a drop in the ocean—it wasn't worth them risking their reputation.

The scholar quickly instructed one of the attendants, "Escort the gentleman to the VIP section."

The attendant immediately bowed and respectfully led Alexander through the larger of the two shimmering archways.
