Chapter 1365 Limited to Ten Moves

"Rhodes Monroe, just you wait. If I, Alexander, survive today, I will avenge this!"

Alexander pulled out a bottle of Docpirin from his robe and downed the last two pills in one go. With a slight push off his toes, he shot up onto the stage.

Below the stage, Julius twisted his face into a savage grin and burst into laughter. "Hahaha! He's done for!"

Rhodes smiled at Alexander and clapped his hands.

"Alexander, you're truly a young hero. I admire you!"

Alexander's eyes burned with fury, while Rhodes' smile looked as sinister as a snake's.

His expression remained icy as he silently gathered all of the Force within him.

"Relax! Since you're only a level-three warrior, I won't take advantage of you. I won't use Dragon Sword!"

Playing the part of a gracious opponent, Rhodes tossed Dragon Sword aside, letting it land far away on the stage.

"Throwing away your sword doesn't mean you're no longer a level-four warrior!"

Below the stage, Logan shouted, drawing everyone's attention.

Rhodes' face flickered with anger and embarrassment. He was about to end Alexander in a single move.

Suddenly, a middle-aged priest leaped onto the stage. Alexander and Rhodes quickly bowed in respect.

The priest returned the gesture and said, "By decree, I have come to question Rhodes Monroe!"

Rhodes immediately straightened up, bowed, and said respectfully, "I am ready to listen!"

"Rhodes Monroe, when did you enter the Outer Court?"

"I have been in the Outer Court for eleven years."

"Very well. You have been in the Outer Court for eleven years, while Alexander has only been here for six months.

"Rhodes Monroe, what is your current level in martial arts?"

"I am a level-four warrior, at the peak of my mastery. I have already comprehended the true essence of martial arts."

The priest nodded in admiration. "Impressive! Rhodes Monroe, you truly are a martial arts prodigy!"

Hearing this, Rhodes trembled slightly, overwhelmed with excitement!

The priest turned to Alexander. "Alexander, what is your current level?"

Alexander respectfully answered, "I am a level-three warrior, at the peak of my mastery."

The priest fixed Rhodes with a sharp gaze. "Rhodes Monroe, can you win against Alexander in a sparring match?"

Rhodes' face turned red, his chest rising and falling rapidly. He lowered his head. "I... I can win the match..."

At this moment, Rhodes hesitated. He had already accepted the gold and the estate. He couldn't return them now. He needed them too much...

Gritting his teeth, he bowed deeply. "I... I will limit our match to ten moves!"

The priest was silent for a moment, then nodded slightly. "Very well. A fellow disciple may issue a challenge, so ten moves it is." With that, he left the stage and walked toward the judges' platform.

Rhodes burned with rage. This Alexander had caused the master to indirectly reprimand him. He would make him pay for that.

Trying to appear magnanimous, Rhodes shouted, "Alexander and I hit it off instantly, so our match will be limited to ten moves!"

Alexander smirked inwardly. "Ten moves, huh?

Did he think I am made of paper? With my golden energy, my muscles and bones are as strong as steel. I won't end up paralyzed like I was against Arturo."

His eyes flickered as he took a step forward and cupped his hands toward Rhodes.

"Since you think so highly of me, why don't we add a little wager?"

"Oh? What kind of wager?" Rhodes asked, intrigued.

"I have 200,000 coins. If you can knock me off the stage or make me unable to stand within ten moves, the money is yours!"

Rhodes burst into wild laughter. Had Alexander lost his mind? He was already half a step into becoming a level-five warrior... ten moves? One would be enough!

He readily agreed. "Alright! I have 10,000 gold coins and a prime estate in the southern part of the city, valued at 200,000 coins. Let's make it fair!"

"Hahaha..."

The two of them laughed heartily, each with their own schemes in mind. Below the stage, officials from the Ministry of Revenue stepped up to collect their wagers.

The officials below the stage shouted excitedly, "Place your bets! Hurry up! A level-four warrior versus a level-three warrior..."

Julius was ecstatic. This was practically free money!

He grabbed one of the officials and roared, "Here's a 200,000-coin banknote! I'm betting on Rhodes Monroe to win within ten moves!"

The crowd surged forward like a swarm, with nine out of ten people placing their bets on Rhodes winning within ten moves.

Logan, the Valdez brothers, Vegeta, and Nova pooled together 10,000 coins to bet that Alexander would last all ten moves.

"Alexander is at 1-to-10 odds! Let's go all in! Alexander, you got this!"

Logan and his group were anxious, murmuring among themselves.

On the judges' platform, Fermin bowed to Fay Joel of the Ministry of Revenue.

"Mr. Joel, why are you even allowing this bet? Alexander is just a level-three warrior. Rhodes Monroe is guaranteed to win within ten moves!"
