Chapter 550 Let's Compete

"Go to hell!"

Clay was furious.

He hated it most when people called him a country bumpkin. That was what Nancy called him, and he would always remember that humiliation.

Clay hated Alexander. The realization that Alexander must have slept with Nancy annoyed him even more.

Alexander chuckled, "I'm here to treat Ms. Elaina Gomez today, not to fight with you. If you have the ability, let's compete to see who can cure her."

Clay sneered, "You aren't worthy enough to compete with me."

He clenched his fists and grew fierce. In the eyes of Orlando and his men, Clay seemed to have grown taller, and his aura was overwhelmingly terrifying.

Alexander remained unfazed.

"Clay, you don't have to scare me. If you don't dare to compete, I advise you to leave Kingsdon as soon as possible and stop approaching Nancy. Otherwise, you will suffer the consequences."

There was a dead silence, and the atmosphere was tense.

Clay took a deep breath, his knuckles crackling.

"Well, you want to compete with me, right? Okay. Whoever of us can cure Ms. Elaina Gomez will win.

"The loser should apologize for three days and three nights and then get out of Kingsdon forever. Do you dare?"

Clay stared at Alexander like a tiger staring at a lamb.

"Aren't you afraid that I will win?"

Alexander showed a half-smile.

Clay sneered, "You can only win if the sun rises from the west."

"Well, it's settled!" Alexander agreed immediately.

Clay waved his hand. "Mr. Gomez, please send someone to guard the door to prevent this guy from running away. He must apologize to me later."

"Don't worry, Mr. Burch. Leave everything to me."

Orlando made a gesture, and countless bodyguards immediately surrounded the door.

"I will let you go first, so you won't blame me for bullying you."

Clay pointed at Alexander with disdain.

"No, you go first. Otherwise, you will have no chance once I make a move. Then, you might complain shamelessly that I won't give you a chance."

Alexander's refusal was so reasonable that it almost pissed Clay off to death.

"Since you don't want the chance, I will go first."

Clay walked to the bedside confidently and pretended to treat Elaina.

Alexander sneered secretly. He had long discovered that she was under witchcraft and that it must be Clay who was behind it.

That was why Clay was sure to win.

Alexander had used the Force to isolate Elaina's body, allowing him to destroy the evil energy within her at any time.

At the same time, before lifting the witchcraft, Clay must first eliminate the influence of the Force. It depended on his ability.

Everyone widened their eyes and stared at him nervously.

Orlando didn't even dare to blink for fear of missing something.

"Keep your eyes wide open and watch how I heal Ms. Elaina Gomez!"

While taunting Alexander, Clay pulled out a black dragon head stick.

Alexander smiled contemptuously and prepared to watch Clay's embarrassment.

Clay was muttering something. After a while, a faint light appeared on the black dragon head stick, revealing a strange aura.

The temperature in the room seemed to have dropped by several degrees. Except for Alexander, everyone felt a chill on their back.

A doctor asked curiously, "Does witchcraft work?"

Another doctor said with displeasure, "If it works, what do we doctors do?"

Orlando was angry. "Shut up! You quacks, don't bother Mr. Burch."

In an instant, everyone fell silent, and no one dared to speak.

Clay confidently placed the dragon head stick on Elaina and moved it smoothly and swiftly to draw several pentagram formations. The series of movements was dazzling.

Soon, a ball of black energy appeared in her chest, surging and rolling.

Everyone gasped, wondering what it was.

Orlando gazed at it intently in shock.

"What is this? Could it be that Ms. Elaina Gomez has really been bewitched?"

"Really?"

Everyone was very curious. They stood on tiptoe to watch, afraid of missing any details.

Orlando was very excited, thinking, "My daughter is finally saved!"

Only Alexander snorted coldly with a playful smile.
