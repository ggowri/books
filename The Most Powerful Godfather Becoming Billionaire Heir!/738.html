Chapter 738 Defamation

"What? What did you just say? Do you have a special medicine to eliminate the plague?"

The soldiers present were all shocked.

Ambrose was stunned at first, then burst into laughter. "It turns out that he's a lunatic!"

The soldiers were talking about it.

"Could it be an expert who arrived late?"

"How is that possible? This guy must be here to defraud money."

"I think he has bad intentions. He must be arrested and interrogated strictly!"

Ambrose became impatient and pointed his gun at Alexander.

"I'll give you one last chance, hold your head, and squat down!"

"Get out of the way. I don't have time to waste on you."

Alexander waved casually.

Amid-st the screams, Ambrose and several soldiers seemed to have been blown down by a raging hurricane.

Not only did they fly up, but even the guns fell away.

"Open..."

Before Ambrose could even utter the word "fire", his pupils suddenly contracted, and he stared at Alexander, who appeared in front of him.

He really wanted to order his men to shoot, but Alexander had already picked him up and blocked him in front of him.

If the soldiers opened fire, the first person to die would definitely be Ambrose.

He was dumbfounded and didn't expect that he would become the other party's hostage so soon. It was really embarrassing.

"If you don't want him to die, get out of my way!"

Alexander carried Ambrose and strode forward.

The soldiers standing in front felt a terrifying force crushing them. They could only retreat and did not dare to shoot.

They were in a panic, and Alexander took the opportunity to stride into the hospital.

Ambrose was terrified and looked pale.

Rosalia, who had been staying in the car, had a very interesting expression on her face. Alexander was becoming more and more powerful, and his aura alone could intimidate many people.

"Mr. Hawthorne, come on, quickly eliminate the plague!"

She prayed and was full of expectations for him.

At this time, the pathological anatomy room of the hospital was temporarily set up as a critical care center, accommodating dozens of critically ill patients.

Moreover, the identities of these patients were not ordinary. They were all well-known figures in Loscity, either rich or noble.

The plague was so contagious that even though the wealthy areas had strict epidemic prevention measures, many people were eventually infected.

Eddy took Elaine and the expert group on a tour.

The doctors and nurses from Loscity Hospital followed obediently behind, not daring to say a word.

The most famous among these experts was Elaine, followed by Steve McLean, a representative of the medical community in Loscity.

The ancestors of the McLean family came to Loscity more than 100 years ago and had been working as doctors ever since. It was a recognized medical family in Loscity.

Steve was the representative figure of the McLean family. He was very famous in the medical community in Loscity and had his own unique insights into medicine.

At this time, several experts had begun to express their opinions and views.

Everyone agreed that the plague was terrible and there was no particularly effective treatment.

Apart from city lockdown, quarantine, and symptomatic treatment, they had not come up with any useful solutions.

Everyone frowned, and the atmosphere was very solemn.

No one noticed that Alexander had appeared silently behind them and was listening to their analysis of the situation with great interest.

As a result, he became more and more disappointed the more he listened. He didn't expect that the so-called expert group had only such limited ability.

If people placed their hopes on these people, perhaps all of them in Loscity would die.

At this moment, someone asked, "Dr. McLean, the McLean family is a local medical family in Loscity. Don't you have any good ideas?"

Steve frowned and smiled awkwardly, "Just because the McLean family is from Loscity, does that mean we can come up with a good solution? What kind of bandit logic is this?"

A brown-haired man sneered, "I knew there were no outstanding people in Loscity. The so-called medical family is nothing but a fraud who deceives the world and steals the reputation. Maybe this plague was caused by something bad done by the people of Loscity."

Alexander originally wanted to listen more, but he was very upset because this guy discriminated against Loscity.

He stood up and rebuked wryly.

"What qualifications do you have to slander Loscity?"

Alexander's powerful and magnetic voice echoed, immediately attracting everyone's attention.
