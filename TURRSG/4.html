Chapter 4 Who Are You

Evander let out a roar as he lunged forward, his left hand clamping down on Denny's wrist that held the knife while his right fist slammed into his face with a whoosh of air.

CRACK!

Denny slammed into the glass coffee table, shattering the expensive crystal decorations. Blood instantly streamed from his nose.

Without pausing to clean himself, Evander grabbed Denny by the hair and slammed his head against the table's edge repeatedly.

THUD! THUD! THUD!

Each impact left Denny's forehead bloodier. As consciousness slipped away, the knife clattered to the floor.

"Don't kill him..."

Iris's breathless voice came from behind as she approached.

Evander immediately stopped.

Denny slumped to the ground like a rag doll, blood trickling from his mouth, completely unable to fight back.

Just then, the living room door burst open.

Nadine's friends Naomi and Kimberly Nelson charged in, wielding clubs.

Evander shielded Iris's escape, taking a hit to the back of his head from Naomi's club. He crumpled to the floor as darkness took him.

*****

He'd lost track of time.

SPLASH

Evander jolted awake as ice-cold water drenched his body.

Water dripped from his hair and face onto the rust-stained concrete floor, forming small puddles that reflected his disheveled appearance.

His wrists throbbed from the rough rope binding them, and the clank of chains against metal racks echoed through the vast, empty warehouse.

"Awake now?"

Denny sneered, blood still caked on his face.

He shook the empty bucket. "Looks like that water worked."

Evander lifted his heavy head, his vision blurring through the haze of water, focusing on the menacing faces before him.

Denny's mouth curved in a cruel smile as he raised his club and brought it down hard.

Searing pain shot through Evander's body. Having just regained consciousness, he felt himself slipping toward the darkness again.

Behind Denny stood Naomoi and Kimberly, their carefully applied makeup distorted by malice.

"Denny, you're injured—rest up and let me handle this!"

Naomoi raised the whip in her hand.

CRACK!

The lash struck Evander's body.

"Evander, Nad was right. You're nothing but a pathetic loser. I'm going to kill you for Nad today!"

Kimberly sneered from the side. "I heard you're the founder of Summit Group? Nelson Group and Ellery Group could bankrupt Summit Group in minutes!"

"Bring it on!" Evander's lips twisted into a bloody smile, the metallic taste spreading through his mouth. "If you've got the guts, kill me today. Otherwise, I'll make you all pay for this!"

"You're asking for it!"

Enraged by those words, Denny snatched the whip from Naomoi and lashed it toward Evander.

CRACK!

The sharp sound exploded through the warehouse as burning pain shot up his spine to the top of his head.

Evander clenched his teeth, refusing to make a sound. He only stared at Denny with ice-cold eyes, his gaze filled with hatred sharp enough to cut glass.

"Still glaring? I'll fucking whip you to death right now!"

Denny screamed, the whip in his hand raining down on Evander like hail.

The sound of tearing flesh filled the air, mixed with Naomoi and Kimberly's shrill curses.

The pain was unbearable, making Evander dizzy. His blood-soaked shirt clung to his back, and each breath sent sharp agony through his wounds. His vision kept fading to black.

But he never begged for mercy!

Just then, a brilliant light pierced through the dim warehouse.

Several mercenaries in black tactical gear burst in with raised weapons, their laser sights dancing across the walls with deadly intent.

"Who are you?"

Denny asked in panicked terror, his whip clattering to the ground.

"It must be people my father hired—they're here to deal with Evander!" Naomoi pulled Denny and Kimberly aside.

"Just wait. Tomorrow his body will turn up in some slum gutter!"

The mercenaries said nothing. Moving with fluid precision, they hoisted Evander up and carried him away.

*****

He didn't know how much time had passed.

Evander woke again.

The sharp smell of disinfectant replaced the warehouse's rusty odor.

He found himself lying on a cold marble floor, his torn clothes stained with dried blood, completely out of place in the luxurious surroundings.

Men in black suits lined both sides of the grand hall, their faces expressionless.

Ahead, on a raised platform covered with dark red carpet, a massive chair loomed. In front of it stood an old man with his back to him.

His silver hair was perfectly groomed, and his suit was embroidered with dark gold patterns. His silhouette radiated unquestionable authority.

"Who are you?"

Evander summoned all his strength to prop himself up.

"Kid, we're the ones who saved you!"

The old man called out, slowly turning around.

When that face came into view, Evander's breath caught in his throat.
