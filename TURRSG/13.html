Chapter13 A Sudden Kiss

"Can't sleep..."

Evander bit his tongue, the sharp pain barely keeping him awake.

He knew this drug was brutal. Sleep wouldn't help—it'd only make things worse.

His eyes swept across the room before landing on the bathroom.

Cold water. That was the only thing that might kill this fire.

He stumbled into the bathroom, and the sound of rushing water filled the space.

The bathtub faucet was cranked all the way up, icy water crashing against the porcelain and sending up tiny droplets.

He stood by the tub, watching the water rise as his breath grew more ragged and his vision blurred.

No. He needed to cool down first.

He staggered to the shower and yanked the cold water valve open.

The freezing stream hit him like a slap, soaking straight through his clothes.

He shivered hard, but for just a second, his foggy mind cleared.

He grabbed his soaked shirt and ripped it off. Buttons scattered across the floor with sharp little clicks.

Then, bam—the bathroom door flew open.

Iris stood in the doorway, her cheeks burning red, eyes glazed and steamy. Her usually clear gaze now held nothing but raw desire.

Her suit jacket was already gone. Her silk blouse, damp with sweat, clung to her and showed off her delicate shoulders.

"Iris?"

Evander froze, reaching for the faucet, but she threw herself into his arms.

Her body was burning hot, like a flame against his ice-cold skin.

Before he could even react, her lips crashed into his—desperate, breathless, reckless. The impact left his mouth tingling.

"Mmm..."

Evander's mind went completely blank. Cold water kept pouring down on them, but the fire inside him ignited at her sudden kiss, spreading like wildfire.

He tried to push her away. His brain was screaming at him to stop. But his arms seemed to have a mind of their own, wrapping around her waist.

Iris kissed him deeper, broken sobs escaping her throat—like she was cursing that damn drug, or finally letting out emotions she'd been holding back way too long.

Her fingers clawed desperately at his back, nails almost breaking skin.

"Iris..."

Evander's voice came out hoarse and broken.

Iris lifted her tear-blurred face, nose brushing his jaw.

"Evander, I want you. Right now..."

Evander kissed her back, done fighting the desire in his heart. He pulled her tight against him, like he was trying to melt their bodies together.

Cold water dripped from their hair, mixing with burning sweat and pooling on the tiles.

When they were both gasping for air, Evander finally loosened his grip and scooped her up, carrying her toward the bathtub.

Warm water had filled it completely, tiny bubbles floating on the surface.

He carefully lowered Iris into the tub. Water splashed up, soaking his bare chest.

"Don't move."

Evander's voice trembled just slightly as he reached for her clothes.

When his fingertips touched her burning skin, Iris shivered but didn't resist. She just bit her lip and watched him, the fire in her eyes almost scorching.

Her bra slipped away, revealing her delicate collarbone and full breasts.

Evander's breathing hitched. His Adam's apple bobbed as he looked away, but his hands kept moving, finding her skirt's zipper.

The black dress floated on the water like a blooming dark flower.

Suddenly, Iris grabbed him and yanked him into the tub.

With a splash, water sprayed everywhere.

Evander tumbled in, landing right on top of her.

Their faces were inches apart, noses touching, breath mixing in the humid heat.

"You—"

Before he could finish, Iris kissed him again.

This kiss was deeper, softer with the water's warmth, yet burning with passion.

Her arms wound tight around his neck, her body pressing closer, as if trying to merge into his very bones.

Evander couldn't hold back anymore. He lifted a hand to stroke her hair, deepening the kiss.

Warm water surrounded them both, washing away the cold but doing nothing to put out the flames inside.

His hand slid gently down her back, making Iris shiver.

Her fingers started their own restless journey—from his chest to his back, then to his firm abs, finally wrapping around his thick, burning length.

Every touch sent electric thrills through them, stirring shivers wave after wave.

"Evander..." Iris whispered in his ear, her voice honey-sweet. "Please... I just need you..."

His lips trailed down to her heaving chest, capturing her rosy peak.

Her breathing got more frantic, almost suffocating, until she felt his thick heat push into her. Only then did she let out a long, shuddering breath.

Water rippled outward, bubbles scattering in the tub.

He held her tight, like he was trying to merge her into his very soul.

Outside, the waves crashed in the distance, blending with the sound of the bathroom water and their heavy breathing.

Time blurred. Eventually, the water went still.

Iris lay against Evander's chest, her cheek pressed against him.

Her breathing slowed down, and her eyes cleared up from that hazy look, though her cheeks still glowed pink.

He lifted her carefully from the tub and wrapped her in a towel, then grabbed another to dry himself off.

As he carried her back to the bedroom, the air conditioning still hummed away, though the air seemed to hold traces of their heat.

He laid Iris gently on the bed and covered her with the blanket.

Sitting beside her, he watched her eyelashes flutter—long and trembling like a frightened butterfly.

"I'll get you some water."

He stood to leave, but Iris caught his hand.

"Don't go."

She looked at him with something almost vulnerable in her eyes. "Stay with me for a while."
