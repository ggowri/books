Chapter 92 Accuse

"Yes." Evander nodded confidently, his voice firm.

"What's more, once we finalize our cooperation, Vanguard Tech will send a top-tier technical team to help Royal Tech integrate these technologies into your humanoid robots.

"In return, all we ask for are the core data of Royal Tech's power systems and joint-drive technologies for robots. These will be crucial for our new energy projects at Vanguard Tech, and they won't affect Royal Tech's leadership in the European humanoid-robot market."

Chad paused, clearly considering the offer. He knew this could be a major win for Royal Tech, but sharing core technologies wasn't something he could take lightly.

He spoke carefully, "Mr. Bexley, your offer is very tempting. But, I can't make this decision alone. It'll have to go through the Royal Council. I'll present your proposal to them and get back to you as soon as possible."

Evander smiled, nodding understandingly. "I understand, Your Grace. I'm happy to wait for the Council's decision. I'm confident this cooperation will benefit both of us. Vanguard Tech and Royal Tech working together will make a real splash in the tech world."

Lois chimed in, "Uncle, Mr. Bexley is trustworthy, and Vanguard Tech has solid technology. We can't let this opportunity slip away. We should act quickly, or we'll fall behind."

Chad glanced at Lois, his smile a little exasperated. "You're always in such a rush."

He turned back to Evander, "Alright, I'll set up the council meeting and aim to give you an answer in the next three days."

Just then, the door to the reception room swung open.

A secretary hurried in and whispered something to Chad. His expression immediately darkened.

He looked at Evander seriously. "Mr. Bexley, Mr. White from the Scientific Paranormal Research has arrived with his team. He says it's urgent and that he needs to speak with you."

Evander's expression hardened.

It seemed Fred had tracked him down. News of Felix's death had spread quickly.

Evander stood up calmly. "I see. Your Grace, I apologize for the disruption. I'll go speak with Mr. White."

Lois looked at him with concern. "Mr. Bexley, do you want me to come with you? Fred and his people are always up to something."

"No, I can handle it," Evander replied, gently patting Lois' hand. "I'll be fine. Stay here."

He followed the secretary out of the reception room, his mind already turning to what Fred might want.

In the hallway, Evander spotted Fred standing with two special-forces soldiers. Fred's face was tight, his glare hard as steel.

As Evander approached, Fred stepped forward, his voice cold and hostile. "Mr. Bexley, Felix is dead. You know that, right?"

Evander feigned shock, raising his eyebrows. "Felix died? That's... unexpected. I saw him yesterday, and he seemed fine."

"Cut the act!" Fred snapped, his voice sharp.

"You must've killed him! You've got superpowers—you could've taken him out without anyone noticing. Come with me to the Scientific Paranormal Research headquarters for an investigation!"

Evander couldn't help but sneer, his tone dripping with mockery. "Mr. White, you're going to need more than just a hunch to make accusations like that. Where's the evidence that I killed Felix?

"Besides, I've been with Princess Lois here at Royal Tech since this morning. Plenty of people can vouch for me. You can't just drag me away without proof—that's a bit much, don't you think?"

Fred froze for a moment, caught off guard. He didn't have solid proof—just his gut feeling. But he wasn't about to back down now.

His teeth clenched as he scowled, his words laced with frustration.

"I don't care about proof right now. Felix's death is linked to you, and that's enough! You're coming with me to cooperate with our investigation!"

"What if I don't?" Evander's eyes grew cold, and an imposing presence enveloped him. Fred and the two soldiers couldn't help but take an involuntary step back.

Just at that moment, Chad and Lois walked out of the room.

Chad frowned and said sternly, "Mr. White, this is Royal Tech, under the royal family's purview. It's not the place for you guys from the Scientific Paranormal Research to cause a ruckus.

"Mr. Bexley is our guest. Without solid proof, you can't just take him away. Leave right away, or I'll summon the royal guards!"

Fred could sense that Chad was deadly serious. He knew he couldn't create a scene here.

Royal Tech had the royal family's support, and he didn't want to get on their bad side.

He just gave Evander a threatening look and growled, "Evander, you're in deep trouble! I'll dig up the evidence to prove you killed Felix and make you pay the price!"

Saying that, Fred led the two soldiers out, clearly disgruntled.

As Fred left, Evander let out a sneer.

In fact, Felix had been taken to Helheim by Hela and was being detained there for three days. He wasn't dead at all.

Fred was just wasting his time looking for evidence.

Evander was actually looking forward to the chaos that would ensue when Felix "came back to life" in three days.

Chad turned to Evander, a touch of curiosity in his voice. "Mr. Bexley, what's the story between you, the Scientific Paranormal Research, and Felix?"

Evander glanced at Lois and replied casually, "It's nothing serious. Just some minor spats on the cruise ship before.

"I never thought Felix would die suddenly, and Mr. White just has his doubts about me. But don't worry, Your Grace. I'm innocent, and the truth will surface soon."

Chad nodded and didn't probe further.

"Since there's no problem, let's get back to the cooperation. I've contacted the royal council members. We'll have a meeting tomorrow to discuss your proposal."

Evander was overjoyed and quickly expressed his gratitude. "Thank you, Your Grace! I'm confident our partnership will be a huge success."

Lois let out a sigh of relief and smiled, "Great! Once the council approves, our two companies can start collaborating."

Afterwards, Lois snuggled up to Evander, hooking her arm with his and leading him off.

They looked like a love-struck couple deeply infatuated with each other.

Chad caught sight of this scene and couldn't resist shaking his head.

After all, Lois was engaged to Felix.

Could Evander be somehow involved in Felix's death?

Fred's thoughts raced. He realized that he needed to seriously rethink the entire cooperation plan between Royal Tech and Vanguard Tech.
