Clara and Leroy would never have expected that, faced with such a huge harvest, Osborne was not happy but rather a bit dissatisfied.

"Continuing to hunt native creatures like this won't yield much more! After all, it's easy to take them down, but dealing with the corpses afterward... Clara and Leroy are already struggling to handle the current pile of prey corpses."

"No matter how many creatures I kill, if they can't handle the corpses, our harvest won't increase."

The first is Abyssal Snake Slough.

Even though they only shed their skin once they reach a certain level of strength, the number of Abyssal Snake Sloughs in the Abyssal is still considerable.

Osborne previously found an Abyssal Snake nest and obtained 620 Abyssal Snake Sloughs, worth 31 million Kingdom Duel Scorepoints.

This is far more cost-effective than hunting Abyssal Snakes.

The second is Eaglebeak Moth!

The wings of the Eaglebeak Moth are the easiest to handle and can be removed effortlessly.

An Eaglebeak Moth has four wings worth 12,000 Kingdom Duel Scorepoints.

The most important thing is that there are plenty of Eaglebeak Moths.

Yesterday, Osborne killed a total of 4,800 Eaglebeak Moths. The total value of their wings was nearly 60 million Kingdom Duel Scorepoints, accounting for almost half of the harvest.

Osborne made the Eaglebeak Moth his second target.

He thought, "Bloody Ivy can be given up. Although Bloody Ivy is quite valuable, it's somewhat scarce and very hard to handle, a waste of time!"

Let Brenda and her team of contestants handle the Bloody Ivy! As for the Abyssal Snake... I'll only look for the Abyssal Snake's nest. If it's not in the nest, I won't bother with it anymore.

Tigerstripe Bee is also quite valuable, but there are too few of them!

"Give up!"

Osborne quickly calculated in his mind, thinking, "However, if we can find the Tigerstripe Bee's nest, that would be great! Tigerstripe Bee honey is highly valuable. Every drop of Tigerstripe Bee honey can be exchanged for 100 Kingdom Duel Scorepoints."

The honey in a Tigerstripe Bee's nest can be exchanged for at least several million Kingdom Duel Scorepoints, or even tens of millions of Kingdom Duel Scorepoints!

Additionally, Brenda's target is the northern glacier region! In the northern glacier region, there's a unique native creature that usually doesn't leave the area, called the Ice Beetle!

These Ice Beetles aren't very valuable but require no processing! One Ice Beetle can be exchanged for 2,000 Kingdom Duel Scorepoints, but they appear in large numbers, always in swarms!

A nest of Ice Beetles can easily have tens of thousands, even hundreds of thousands! As long as we kill one nest of Ice Beetles, we can exchange it for tens of millions, or even over a hundred million Kingdom Duel Scorepoints..."

Osborne excitedly thought, "So my next targets are the Abyssal Snake Slough, Eaglebeak Moth, Tigerstripe Bee's nest, and Ice Beetle!"

He made a decision in his heart and shared his thoughts with Clara and Leroy.

Clara and Leroy exchanged glances. Only then did they realize that Osborne was dissatisfied with the previous day's gains and wanted to earn even more points.

They said, "We'll follow your lead!"

"Whatever you ask us to do, we'll do it! All we can do is try our best not to hold you back and help you handle the prey's corpses."

"You handle the corpses first. Once you're done, have Flash take you to catch up with Brenda's team! I'll check around for any Abyssal Snake or Tigerstripe Bee nests, and also Eaglebeak Moth..."

His goal was clear. He would directly ignore low-value or hard-to-handle creatures like Abyssal Snake and Bloody Ivy, but he wouldn't pass up high-value, easy-to-handle ones like Eaglebeak Moth.

In such circumstances, his harvest rate indeed increased significantly.

"Eaglebeak Moth, a total of 7,600!"

"Tigerstripe Bee nests, three!"

"Tigerstripe Bee, 2,100!"

"Abyssal Snake poison glands, 2,000!"

"If converted into Kingdom Duel Scorepoints, the total is..."

Ice Beetles usually hide deep within the glacier, making them impossible for ordinary people to find! When they are in a dormant state, their aura is completely concealed, blending entirely with the glacier...

It's normal for someone to die. He could clearly feel that the number of native creatures swarming to attack was increasing, and the pressure on Brenda's team was bound to grow heavier.

Even if they are united, casualties are inevitable.

"Luckily, we'll enter the glacier region tomorrow!"

Now he needs to gather as many resources as possible to exchange for Kingdom Duel Scorepoints.
