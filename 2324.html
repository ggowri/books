Clara and Leroy glanced at each other and realized they had no other option but to put their faith in his call.

After all, the high scores they'd racked up were thanks to Osborne leading the way.

How can he be so certain? Agatha, Horace, Broderick, and Benson are all in the same league. It's not gonna be a total wipeout, is it?

Hold on a second! Agatha was conscious the whole time? She knew I saved her? And she heard everything I said to Primordial? Even the part about me taking nine drops of her Phoenix Blood...

Thank God. From what Clara and Leroy said, Agatha doesn't seem bent on revenge.

That's a good sign!

After all, I did save her life. Sure, taking her Phoenix Blood was a bit underhanded, but compared to the debt of saving her life, she shouldn't hold it against me, right?

Still, I've got to be on my guard.

If she has a volatile temper, I could end up with a powerful enemy. And let's not forget, she's from the Ember Phoenix Clan. According to Primordial, her Dark Phoenix bloodline has awakened, making her the one-of-a-kind in this cosmos.

Her status in the Ember Phoenix Clan is bound to be off the charts. If she decides to come after me I'm in deep trouble!

Osborne's expression turned grave, a flash of regret crossing his face for not having taken care of Agatha when he had the opportunity.

Forget it! I'll wait for a chance to talk to Agatha and figure out what's really on her mind. If she resents me for taking her Phoenix Blood and overlooks the fact that I saved her life, then I'll find a way to get rid of her!

Before she becomes a big shot in the Ember Phoenix Clan, I need to eliminate the threat before it grows.

It was Conrad and his teammates!
