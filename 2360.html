After collecting the Warfare Ancient Tree, the surrounding Warfare Mist dissipated. Within their field of vision, they could no longer see anything of value.

At that time, Osborne still wanted to continue exploring.

The Ascending Sacred Sanctum was also being slowly repaired.

"Warfare Ancient Tree is truly something amazing," Osborne sighed once again.

If he hadn't been unexpectedly trapped by the Chrono-Spatial Turbulence, he wouldn't have come here, nor would he have obtained the Warfare Ancient Tree.

In that case, he would hardly be able to repair the Ascending Sacred Sanctum.

After all, the various materials needed to repair the Ascending Sacred Sanctum alone were enough to overwhelm him. Even if he spent a lot of time, he might not be able to gather them all.

But the Warfare Ancient Tree only needed enough Sacred Power to complete all the repairs.

Standing on the warship's deck, Osborne observed the outside, hoping to come across some more good things.

This warship was also a mid-grade Sacred Artifact. It wasn't as high-level as the Ascending Sacred Sanctum, but it was intact. After Osborne refined it, its power was comparable to the Ascending Sacred Sanctum and even stronger in some aspects.

However, growing a Sacred Artifact would consume a significant amount of the Warfare Ancient Tree's power and time. But since I can't find another one, I have no choice but to do this. A close-combat Sacred Artifact crafted by the Warfare Ancient Tree according to my wishes will definitely suit me better."

He first decided on the weapon form he wanted.

Dagger, longsword, saber, spear, or gauntlet?

Various weapon forms flashed quickly through Osborne's mind, but he disliked them all. Finally, a weapon shape suddenly appeared in Osborne's mind. "Scythe!"

A divine artifact appeared in his hand, the Goddess of Grain's scythe, which he had used for a long time on the Blue Planet.

The Goddess of Grain's scythe was already too ordinary for Osborne now. After all, it was merely a top-grade divine artifact. Osborne no longer needed it.

But Osborne had deep feelings for the Goddess of Grain's scythe.

After all, when he was still weak, the sharp and sturdy Goddess of Grain's scythe had helped him defeat strong enemies time and again, even helping him survive.

So, even now, Osborne still carried this scythe.

Osborne instantly became excited, thinking, "In that case, I can have the Warfare Ancient Tree grow a new scythe-shaped Sacred Artifact based on the form of the Goddess of Grain's scythe. Or, I can directly place it on the Warfare Ancient Tree and let it help enhance, modify, and upgrade it. This might save some time."

He shared his idea with the Warfare Ancient Tree. The Warfare Ancient Tree immediately understood and agreed to help Osborne enhance and upgrade the Goddess of Grain's scythe, adding the abilities Osborne desired.

Osborne was instantly thrilled.

He placed the Goddess of Grain's scythe on the trunk of the Warfare Ancient Tree. Instantly, he felt the Warfare Ancient Tree channel most of its power into it.

Osborne could clearly feel the quality and level of the Goddess of Grain's scythe rapidly improving.
