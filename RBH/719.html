I sat in the center of the conference room, holding my hands and admiring her fluent presentation. Although her appearance was not outstanding, at that moment of her explanation, the radiance emanating from her whole body was truly dazzling and irresistibly attractive. "Ms. Mayers, I have finished explaining. First, I would like to share my opinion. I believe that this project is feasible. I have visited Norch City several times a few years ago during business trips, and the unique climate there is indeed suitable for cultivating many scarce medicinal herbs. After Jane finished her explanation, she directly expressed her own opinion.

I actually think that going to Norch City at this time is a good choice."

Even workaholics like Isaac felt it was acceptable, and everyone immediately started expressing their own opinions one after another.

"I also think it's possible. Because of the flu, I didn't go out much during the Spring Festival before. It's a good opportunity to go and relax."

"I agreed!"

Since the establishment of this new team, the initial period of adjustment in our work has already passed, and now it is indeed time to deepen our relationships.

We went on a team building trip to Norch City... I hadn't thought about it before, but everyone thought it was a good idea, so I immediately agreed happily.

After the meeting, I returned to my office and Marcus was still sitting there leisurely drinking coffee. At the same time, he was also writing something on a white paper.

Curiously, I approached and discovered that it was his beautiful handwriting with a fountain pen, and what he had written was the poem that I had loved the most during my high school years.

Is it?

Yeah, back then the love I longed for was clearly a relationship of mutual dependence and independence, but later on, how did I end up losing myself so much in my love for Elliott?

"I remember. But Marcus, you reminded me once again today," I said.

I felt more and more like I had found my original self. I looked at Marcus and smiled with satisfaction, then shook the project proposal in my hand. "Marcus, we have unanimously approved this plan and plan to go on a team-building trip to Norch City in the near future. Do you have any recommendations?"
