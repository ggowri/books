Elliott said in a frustrated tone, but he didn't provide any explanation, leaving me puzzled about his motives for doing so.

"Are you sure?"

I didn't want to get involved with Laura anymore. Since Elliott said I could leave, I couldn't ask for more. After I finished speaking, I wanted to push open the car door and get out.

Elliott suddenly said, "Find a time to renew the contracts of Glory Group and Bryant Group. The prices will remain the same as before, I have already spoken to them, and there will be no price increase."

"No need, I have already found a new partner. The collaboration between Glory Group and Bryant Group can be terminated by the end of this month."

I was stunned, and then decisively refused.

My words shocked Elliott. He became angry.

"Are you saying that you were with Benjamin and Grey today, discussing a collaboration? Benjamin's company has developed a new motor, but its performance is currently unstable. Aren't you afraid of the consequences of using a new product without caution?"

It was obvious that Elliott was very dissatisfied with me cutting off cooperation with them, so he deliberately provoked me.

"The market has always been about updates and replacements. If the old ones don't go, the new ones won't come. People are like this, and so are products. I appreciate your kindness, goodbye, Mr. Bryant!"

I looked at Elliott, calmly smiled slightly, and then, without hesitation, pushed open the car door.

A gust of cold wind blew into the car at that moment, and at the same time, I heard Elliott's voice behind me, filled with an inexplicable sadness.

"Are you now determined to cut off all ties with me in every aspect?"

"Yes."

I hesitated for a few seconds, but I didn't turn back. Instead, I quickly got out of the car and walked towards the entrance of Glory Group with determined steps.

After the termination of our cooperation with Bryant Group, we successfully signed a three-year contract with Benjamin's company, Infinite Precision. Meanwhile, Fiona also gave me the paternity test report that she had secretly done.

Originally, there was such a big secret hidden behind the child.

Giselle was so bold. Wasn't she afraid of being discovered and embarrassing herself?

I found the whole thing unbelievable. And just at that moment, the news of the Bryant family hosting a grand baby shower and engagement banquet started to circulate in high society.

However, I had expected it because my grand gift had already been prepared. I was just waiting for their engagement day to give them a pleasant surprise!
