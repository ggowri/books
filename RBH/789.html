My eyes are round and bright, full of life. Hers, however, are long and narrow, reminiscent of a fox's, with a slight upward tilt.

Thus, her smile gave me a fox-like vibe.

The hue of this wine is a vibrant orange-yellow, resembling a glass of fresh juice. It's housed in an aesthetically pleasing bottle, exuding an air of sophistication and class.

"Happy Birthday!"

Quinn truly didn't want to hide his feelings for me, not even a bit.

Given my previous tolerance, this amount of alcohol should've been a piece of cake.

Could it be that my health has deteriorated due to recently giving birth?

So, there's something off about the wine Lisa brought?

I blinked, my gaze instinctively shifting towards Lisa. Yet, sipping on the same drink, Lisa seemed utterly unaffected. She was as composed as ever, deeply engrossed in a lively conversation with a group of girls.

Must be my poor tolerance for alcohol, I thought. I decided to get up and splash some water on my face in the bathroom to sober up.

Yet, the moment I rose to my feet, I found them oddly shaky. It was as if a flicker of flame was surging upwards within me, igniting a sudden thrill in my nerves.

Seeing Quinn there, I felt an overwhelming urge to just throw caution to the wind and rush to him...
