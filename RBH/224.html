Elliott took me to our car without talking to Giselle anymore.

However, as soon as we got to the car, I immediately pushed his hand away. There was silence again in the car.

I still felt suffocated. The thought of going back with Elliott to Serenity Villa made me feel suffocated even more.

Fortunately, Paige sent me a message at this time.

[Sweetie, we are having a party again tonight, but you are in Louis City and can't come.]

As if seeing hope, I immediately replied: [I'm in Hoswich. Where are you? I'll go and find you.]

Paige was pleasantly surprised and replied at once: [What? You're back? We are having drinks. Come here.]

I quickly replied: [You call me now. I'm with Elliott. Hurry up.]

Paige understood what I meant right away. As soon as I sent the message, she called me.

On the phone, she deliberately invited me to come see her in a loud voice and said she had something important to tell me. Elliott sat beside me, and I was sure he must have heard it clearly.

After hanging up the phone, I said calmly, "Paige and Fiona haven't seen me for a long time and asked me to go over and accompany them. You can send me there."

Elliott turned to look at me. I could tell he seemed a little unhappy. Still, he said in a deep voice, "Okay, but you have to come back early."

At this moment, I just wanted to stay away from him and didn't want to argue with him anymore. I responded briefly, "Okay."

I told him the address that Paige had sent to me. He drove me there not long after.

I immediately got out of the car and quickly walked into the club. When I pushed open the room door and saw Paige, Fiona, Quinn, and Philip, I felt like a great weight had been lifted.

Paige rushed over to hug me and joked, "Look who's here! It's Madeline, a new businesswoman. It's been half a month since I saw you last time. You're getting cooler and cooler! "

"You live a happier life. You can drink together every day. I envy you so much." I smiled.

"Yes. Paige is the freest among the three of us. She has fun with men every day. I could only hang out at night."

Fiona came up to me and hugged me.

She said, "Madeline, you are back. You have to have drinks later. I asked the waiter to bring over the bottle of red wine that I've kept secretly here. You three run away tonight. Philip and I will have a drinking contest with you. What do you say?"

Quinn carelessly sat on the sofa, his hands lazily resting on the handle.

He smiled, "The three of us competing against the two of you? Come on. Not a problem for me."

Paige got competitive immediately.

She readily said, "Okay. You said that!"

Quinn stood up and walked out to ask the waiter to get the wine.

Seeing their happy faces, I felt a lot more relaxed. I hadn't enjoyed drinking for a long time. So, I quickly joined them.

The five of us started playing a dice game. I didn't know if it was because I was in no mood, but I had bad luck and lost easily.

Before I knew it, I had drunk several glasses of red wine and became dizzy.

Realizing that I could no longer drink, I asked them to continue. Then, I played a song and sang.
