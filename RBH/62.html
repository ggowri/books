In a daze, I seemed to hear Quinn cursing. Afterward, he struggled to pick up my phone while carrying me on his back.

I faintly heard him shouting:

"Elliott, do you fucking remember that you have a wife? If you want to be with Madeline, then be wholeheartedly devoted to her! If you don't want to be with her, there are plenty of people who do!"

Hmm... It seems like this is the first time in so many years that someone has had my back.

I didn't know before, but Quinn actually had such a handsome side.

Why did he suddenly become so nice to me?

"Quinn, you... you were still good to me."

I lay on his shoulder and couldn't help but mutter.

Another wave of drunkenness swept over me. I felt my entire head buzzing. I was lying on top of him. By this time, I was completely unconscious. I had no idea where I was.

The next day, when I woke up, I found myself not at home, but on a large bed in a hotel.

The room was quiet and there was a faint scent. My clothes had been changed into pajamas. And on the bedside table, there was a brand new set of sportswear, a note, and a brand new phone.

How did I end up here?

I sat up subconsciously. I rubbed my sore head. Then, I vaguely recalled what happened last night.

Did Quinn arrange for me to be here?

I hesitantly picked up the note. On the note were a few lines of casually written large characters.

"The clothes were changed by the waiter at my request. The sportswear was specially bought for you by me. Your phone's screen was broken last night, so I took it to get repaired for you. You can use this new phone for now. I have already changed the SIM card for you. - Quinn"

He was probably afraid that I would misunderstand. So he explained a lot on the note.

I sat up from the bed. Then, I picked up the pink sportswear set that was placed by the bed. After I walked into the bathroom and changed into this sportswear set, I found that the size of this sportswear set fit me well.

However, this soft and pink color was something I had never tried before.

Was Quinn quite careful?

However, how did he know what size I wore?

I couldn't help but "mutter" in my heart. And soon, I also noticed that he not only prepared clothes and a mobile phone for me. Moreover, he specially prepared a hangover pill and a bottle of mineral water.

I haven't drunk alcohol for a long time. The feeling of a hangover indeed made me feel uncomfortable.

So, I grabbed that hangover pill and swallowed it with water. Then, I opened my phone.

The phone was brand new. The SIM card was still the same one I had before. I logged into WhatsApp on my phone. I found that I had several unread messages, including ones from Quinn and Elliott.

Quinn sent a picture. In the picture, I was hunched over the toilet, looking extremely disheveled as I vomited.

Along with the picture, there is a sentence "You shouldn't drink so much alcohol in the future."

What Elliott sent was only a few words: "How dare you stay out all night?"

I glanced at the time when Elliott sent the message. It was around midnight last night.

From his words, it seems that he had already returned to Serenity Villa.

Shouldn't he have been with Giselle at the hospital last night?

Would he be willing to go back when they were having such a great time chatting?

I don't know why, but whenever I think of Elliott and Giselle, my heart becomes complicated. And just at that moment, Elliott seemed to have a telepathic connection and suddenly dialed my phone.

He rarely called me before.

Even the number of times he sent me messages was very few, very few.

He has never replied or liked any of my WhatsApp messages. In fact, he might have never even seen them.

And his WhatsApp, it was always a constant blank. He rarely posted any updates.

In her memory, over the years, he had only posted one update on WhatsApp. That was when he was forced by the organizers to share a tournament message after winning a basketball game.

I frowned as I looked at the flashing name "Elliott" on the screen. I hesitated for a moment, but in the end, I answered the call.

"Hello?"
