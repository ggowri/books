Fiona had reminded me that the later the pregnancy, the more I should not take it lightly, especially when such phenomena occur, I must pay more attention.

This child came very difficultly, so no matter what, I must definitely give birth to it. Therefore, I cannot take even the slightest risk.

Realizing that if I continued to stand for much longer, I might not be able to hold on, and having just confessed to Quinn, it was impossible for me to go back and ask him to see me off.

So, I thought for a few seconds and ultimately chose to get in Elliott's car.

After getting on the car, there was a moment of silence in the carriage.

The driver skillfully maneuvered the steering wheel. I noticed that the driver had been changed, and it was no longer Toby.

Elliott coughed lightly and said, "At this time, you should have gone to the Woods villa for dinner, right?"

"Yes."

"Why didn't Quinn come out to see you off? Did you have an unpleasant meal?"

Elliott's deep eyebrows slightly arched, and a hint of a smile appeared on his face.

"Yes."

"Did you guys have a fight? I guess his parents didn't agree, right? The Woods family is known for having high expectations."

Elliott furrowed his brow and let out a light chuckle. This laughter was unexpectedly tinged with a hint of smugness.

"Are you done or not?"

I heard the sarcasm in his tone and retorted angrily.

"I just find it strange that Quinn claimed to treat you well. You rarely go to his house for a meal, but he didn't even offer to drive you there! He is indeed untrustworthy..."

Elliott had a slight smile on his face, and his gaze occasionally swept over me.

I didn't look at him, but I could feel the burning gaze in his eyes.

I coldly responded, "He was busy. Besides, what happened between him and me doesn't seem to concern you, Elliott."

"I just find it strange," a faint hint of mockery flashed in Elliott's eyes. He suddenly leaned closer to me, "Could it be that you guys are pretending?"

He was still testing me.

Even though I repeatedly proved in front of him that Quinn and I were in a romantic relationship.

But he seemed to have seen through it. He leaned in close to my ear. His breath brushed against my ear, causing my heart to instinctively flutter.

I truly regretted.

I should not have gotten into his car just now.

I instinctively dodged to the other side and said, "Can't we have an argument? Arguing can actually strengthen the relationship between a man and a woman. Don't you understand? Who says that two people must always be together when they are in a relationship?"

Elliott laughed even more mischievously.

"Did he still argue with you even when you were pregnant? So, he claims to be a good man like this? It seems that this child has nothing to do with him, that's why he doesn't care."

Elliott was not so noisy before.

Why has he become so talkative and gossip-loving now? Does he have to pry into other people's privacy?

"What do you want to say?" I got a little angry. "Stop at the intersection ahead. I want to get off!"

"Madeline, come back to me. I wouldn't treat you with such inconsistency. Quinn is really not right for you."

Elliott, however, seemed completely indifferent to what I said. He leaned in again, lifting my chin, his voice seductive.
