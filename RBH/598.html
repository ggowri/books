It was Jezebel who appeared unexpectedly.

It seemed she had come to visit upon hearing the news of Giselle's childbirth.

Wearing sunglasses, Jezebel deliberately put on a haughty demeanor, with her assistant carrying a large bouquet of flowers and a gift bag following closely behind. Despite the bustling crowd at the hospital, hardly anyone recognized her, yet she pretended to be afraid of being photographed, swaying her hips as she walked towards the hospital entrance.

Naturally, the image of that night on the yacht between her and Philip surfaced in my mind.

I felt a wave of disgust wash over me.

Just then, Paige whispered in my ear, "Is that the celebrity named Jezebel?"

Paige's words caught me off guard.

I thought, "Could it be that she already knows about Jezebel and Philip?"

I turned to her and asked, "She's not that famous. How do you know her?"

Paige replied disdainfully, "Because Philip brought home a few magazines with her on the cover, each photo intentionally revealing her figure. I was curious, so I looked her up. That's how I know she's Jezebel Carlton."

That is outrageous!"

"You need to keep Philip away from these improper women. This woman has had a lot of scandals, rumored to have had sexual relationships with many people in the entertainment industry. My voice wasn't loud, but to my surprise, Jezebel heard it all.

I never had a good impression of Jezebel. Seeing her behave so rudely only fueled my disdain.

Paige couldn't help but fiercely retort against Jezebel, "Don't talk nonsense! Madeline broke up with that unfaithful man on her own initiative. Jezebel, you're a celebrity. Is it appropriate to argue with us in public like this?"
