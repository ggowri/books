Giselle's child would forever become the wall between me and Elliott.

It had been confirmed Elliott was the biological father of that child. No matter how Giselle did it, it was a dead issue.

Nothing could be changed, and there was no room for maneuvering.

"You make me, right?" Elliott showed distress on his face. He weakly and bitterly smiled. Suddenly, he approached me, saying with a little hoarse voice, "How could you be so indifferent all of a sudden? How is that possible?"

It was bustling with people here. We were also downstairs at Glory Group, so there were a lot of employees passing by.

Being pressed against the car by him in public, I felt embarrassed and awkward. So, I attempted to push him away, but he didn't let me go. Instead, he held me tighter.

He leaned forward to press me against the car. I could clearly feel my lower abdomen being squeezed.

If it had been before, I would definitely be oblivious. But now, I have a baby in my belly, so I was afraid that something would go wrong.

I became so anxious that I couldn't help shouting,

"Elliott, that's it. Can you let me go? I have made it very clear! "

Pushing him as hard as I could, I struggled out from under him and walked forward subconsciously.

Unexpectedly, he suddenly grabbed me and locked me tightly in his arms.

"Madeline, I told you that I couldn't let go of you. You can't use other's mistakes to punish me. This is not fair! "

Elliott's breath sprayed in my ear. I could feel his strong heartbeat through his clothes.

He gripped my waist tightly with his hands, and his lips came closer to my earlobe. Then, he begged with a hoarse voice,

"Let's not get divorced, okay? Madeline, if you don't divorce me, I can agree to whatever you want. "

I felt sour after hearing his words.

I had never seen him look so groveling.

If he had begged me in this way before, I would have shaken involuntarily. No matter how strong a wall I built in my mind, he could easily push it down.

But now, I instantly become hard-hearted after thinking about my child.

I would rather choose to divorce starting a new life with my children than be trapped in this triangle relationship.

"I have decided. Elliott, we have no chance anymore as long as that child is alive."

I said firmly.

At the same time, I forcefully pulled his arms away from my waist. He resisted with all his might but in vain. Finally, he had to let go of me.

I tried to walk forward firmly because I didn't want him to see my weakness.

Adam stood still. Seeing me get rid of Elliott, he immediately ran forward and helped me. Then, he asked in a low voice,

"Madeline, are you OK? "

I shook my head. With Adam's help, I quickly got into his car.

Adam took me away quickly. While Adam turned around, I saw Elliott standing still motionlessly through the rearview mirror. He seemed to be shrouded in huge loneliness.

I didn't know what he was thinking at that moment, but I didn't care.

Because I suddenly felt that the air around me was getting thinner and thinner. I had a blackout for no reason. Soon, I lost consciousness.
