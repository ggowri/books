The atmosphere at the beginning of the meal was tense, but it gradually became cozy. Unconsciously, I ate a lot, even finishing a whole plate of spaghetti.

"You're also dining here! We could have shared a table."

A familiar voice suddenly chimed in as we were nearing the end of our meal.

I hadn't expected Fiona to be here.

Not only was Fiona present, but Alexis was standing beside her.

Clearly, they had just finished their meal upstairs and had coincidentally spotted us at the staircase, so they came over.

Fiona had clearly put effort into her appearance today, wearing a shirt dress paired with a cream-colored trench coat. Her figure was alluring, and her bobbed hair and shell earrings gave her a simple yet professional look. Her makeup was minimal, giving her a fresh and elegant appearance.

Standing next to her, Alexis was slightly taller. He was still dressed in a suit, sporting gold-rimmed glasses, which added to his scholarly charm. Together, they looked especially well-matched.

"Are you two on a date? If we shared a table, you wouldn't be able to have your private conversations," I teased, intentionally lightening the mood.

"No, we just happened to run into each other and decided to grab a meal together. I'm not on a date with him, don't tease me, Madeline."

Fiona's face immediately blushed as she playfully pinched my cheek.

Alexis inquired with a smile, "Elliott, you seem have a good mood today, dining here with Mrs. Bryant?"

He was a gentleman, so my impression of him was still favorable.

"We went to visit Cormac in the hospital this morning, and this place is close by. Alexis, I heard your law firm has been doing well lately?"

Elliott wiped his lips with a napkin, stood up, and greeted them with a warm smile.

"I've taken on several major international and domestic cases and won them all. My law firm is nearby. Would you like to come over for coffee?" Alexis suggested with a nod and a smile.

Elliott instinctively looked at me.

"I have some free time this afternoon, just need to check if Honey here is available."

Elliott rarely addressed me so affectionately in front of them, and his sudden respect for me left both Alexis and Fiona visibly surprised.

"I'm in. I've never been to Alexis's law firm," I said to Fiona deliberately.

In truth, I had no desire to go, but for Fiona's sake, I had to go and assess Alexis' capabilities.

So, the four of us settled the bill and headed directly towards Alexis's law firm on foot.
