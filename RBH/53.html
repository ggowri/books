Elliott still said it in a bad mood, and his "wife" really surprised me.

The man who never treated me as his "wife" in the past, today, surprisingly, called me "wife"?

Hmm, I must say it really surprised me.

Elliott paid no attention to my surprise and immediately pulled me toward the center. Afterward, he introduced several heavyweight guests to me one by one.

It was my first time accompanying Elliott to entertain guests, and at first, I was a little uncomfortable with this role. However, since I had seen the world from a young age, I quickly adapted and soon I was moving around the room with ease.

Elliott gave me face, and I also gave him face. It was our first time thanking the guests as a couple, but it seemed like there was a special understanding between us. We coordinated seamlessly and complemented each other perfectly.

However, after a long time of fake smiling, it did become a bit tiring.

Did the process go wrong?

Wasn't that the thing about the pianist?

Just as expected, it was the same as the previous life...

Carli Patterson, a renowned mixed-race pianist, was the highlight of tonight's performance. Many VIP guests in the audience were fans of Carli. Prior to the event, the Bryant Group had also emphasized the promotion of Carli's piano performance. However, her sudden absence undoubtedly diminished the entire show.

Elliott was an extremely perfectionistic person, and any flaw in detail was unforgivable to him.

Upon hearing Amy's words, Elliott furrowed his eyebrows and scolded her in a low voice.

"Weren't you supposed to ensure that nothing goes wrong? How could such an accident happen?"

"It was a sudden situation when a large truck suddenly rear-ended their car, and we certainly didn't expect it."

"Mr. Bryant!"

Just at that moment, a clear and pleasant female voice suddenly rang out in the crowd. Immediately after, Giselle, who had been silent in the crowd, suddenly stood up and shouted.

This sound startled me, and my blood was about to freeze.

Has that long-awaited historic moment arrived?

Elliott was about to meet the woman he had been longing for and obsessing over in the future.

What kind of look would he have when he saw her for the first time?
