Everything matched up in an instant, that thing from the previous life happened today, there is no mistake.

Giselle's careful and diligent work ensured the timely elimination of potential hazards for this exhibition. The construction team ensured the quality of the project, preventing any accidents on the day of the exhibition and saving Bryant Group from possible significant losses.

In my second life, up until now, I still remember Elliott's cold and ruthless appearance that day.

His extremely cruel words, like a knife, deeply pierced into my heart, and when he admitted in front of me that Giselle was the love of his life, I couldn't even stand, and I fell to the ground directly, stumbling.

He was so cruel back then, his eyes only had Giselle in them. In his eyes, I was just a burden, something not even worth mentioning.

He would even rather play with a dog than say a word to me.

Even, he openly declared to everyone in the Bryant Group that I would be prohibited from entering the Bryant Building in the future.

The things that happened in the past, which I now recall, still feel like a heart-wrenching pain.
