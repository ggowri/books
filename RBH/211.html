A couple who started climbing the mountain almost at the same time as us caught up with us. Seeing us taking photos, they immediately came forward and invited me to take a group photo for them.

I readily agreed. They stood where we had just stood in a very intimate pose, and I took a few photos of them.

"It's rare to meet a couple while climbing a mountain. Let us take group photos for you."

This couple was particularly enthusiastic and said to us.

"Huh? Thank you, but we just took solo photos..."

Elliott and I had never taken a group photo. I was a little shy to take photos with him.

Unexpectedly, Elliott nodded, walked to that position, and waved to me.

"Come here, let's take photos."

Elliott had already said that. I couldn't refuse anymore, so I walked over with a smile.

Elliott naturally put his arm around my shoulders and motioned for me to put my arm around his waist. Then, the couple quickly took a few photos of us. We exchanged contact information and sent photos to each other through WhatsApp. After thanking them, we continued climbing to the top.

Travel could not only broaden people's minds but also eliminate the barriers and grudges of the past.

I found that when we weren't in Hoswich, my relationship with Elliott had become more harmonious than ever before, and he could even drink a bottle of water with me naturally.

Could we be so intimate?

I had thought I could never cross the gap between me and Elliott.

But after spending the two days together, I began to doubt this.

We stood on the top of Flora Mountain. The wind blew. I watched him standing on a boulder not far away. His tall figure looked more majestic against the blue sky.

The attachment to him deep in my heart suddenly surged again.

I told myself to be rational and keep myself away from him, and I thought I could finally let go of the past... But I had to admit that as long as Elliotts showed tenderness to me, I could not bear to refuse him. And I would only become more addicted to him uncontrollably.

Was I really willing to give him to Giselle?

A strong sense of unwillingness rose in my chest. I clicked Instagram. When I found that Elliott posted a group of photos of the scenery on Flora Moutain, I posted the group photo of him and me on Instagram. This time, it was still only opened to Giselle.

In my previous life, she often used this trick to force me to see reality.

Then, in this life, I could use her method to let her see the reality and recognize her status.

I felt more relaxed inside after posting the photo. I walked toward the boulder where Elliott was standing. Elliott looked down at me and pulled me up.

He helped me to stand on the boulder. The uneven surface almost made me lose my balance. Fortunately, he put his hand on my waist and steadied me.

Elliott hugged me, and I looked up at him. The moment our eyes met, I felt like there was an electric current flowing between us. He leaned over and kissed me...
