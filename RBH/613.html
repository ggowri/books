I snorted, "Of course. Don't you want to know why Quinn is so good to me? It's because we have been in love for a long time. Everything I did for you was just to make you fall in love with me. Then, you would know how it feels to not win one's heart. Besides, I don't want you to be with Giselle. If someone else wore my clothes, I would feel disgusted, let alone a man."

I stared at him, thinking, "I probably don't love him anymore, so when I say these words, I don't care about his dignity. I just want to vent my anger."

Elliott was angry, and he squeezed my neck even harder.

"Madeline, these better be just empty words from you. Otherwise, you know the consequences of provoking me."

"Elliott, stop! Let go of her!"

When I was out of breath, Quinn rushed over. He punched Elliott in the face. Then, he quickly pulled me behind him.

Blood dripped from the corner of Elliott's mouth as he stood still, wiping the blood off his mouth. His gaze towards me and Quinn was filled with humiliation and pain.

"Good. During this period, I have been reflecting on myself. I didn't expect that I would be a fool. Both of you betrayed me."

I knew that he had already believed my words.

Quinn probably didn't hear our conversation.

Upon hearing Elliott's words, he asked, "Elliott, what do you mean? Madeline and I..."

Quinn wanted to argue, but I quickly pinched him on the back. Quinn immediately understood and stopped.

He said, "Madeline and I had been in love for a long time. From now on, I will protect her. Since you two have already divorced, please stop bothering her."

Quinn's words confirmed what I had said. After hearing Quinn's words, Elliott became even more indifferent.

"Humph. My best friend and my ex-wife..."

Elliott let out a helpless and desolate laugh. At that moment, I could feel the brokenness in his heart.

For my child, I had to be cruel. Only in this way, the Bryant family wouldn't take away my child.

"Elliott, have you finished speaking? If you have, you can leave," I said coldly.

Elliott stopped his self-deprecating laugh. He looked at me and Quinn with indifference.

He said coldly, "Tell Paige that as long as she spares Jezebel, I can agree with her any demand."

Elliott immediately regained a calm tone.

However, his words were like a knife stabbing deeply into my heart.

From the moment he appeared in front of me, I felt that he must have been sent by Giselle to plead with Paige.

He just said so much. His intention was simply to plead for Jezebel.

This time, I couldn't help but silently let out a self-mocking laugh.

He was so affectionate just now, and I almost believed him.

If it were in the past, perhaps I would have already believed him.

I didn't expect that this was his ultimate goal.

How ironic!

"Impossible. Paige would not let Jezebel go. If you immediately send Giselle and her son abroad and never allow them to return, I might help you persuade Paige..."

I knew Elliott wouldn't agree, but I couldn't help but want to make things difficult for him.

He once said that he would send them abroad after Giselle's child was born.

I wanted to see if Elliott could walk the talk. Or, he deceived me once again...
