The woman, by all means, with her status and power, shouldn't have been able to sneak onto this yacht.

One glance at her attire, which matched the evening's welcoming style, instantly revealed the truth to me.

"You step aside for now. I have matters to attend to."

Philip impatiently pushed her away, gesturing for her to move aside.

Meanwhile, he approached me with a pleading tone.

"Madeline, I know I messed up. Please, I beg you, don't tell Paige about this. She's pregnant now, and her emotions are already unstable. If she finds out, it'll surely lead to a big argument between us."

"If you know she'll be upset, why do it in the first place? Philip, I thought you chose Paige, a turning point for you. But I never expect you to still do such a thing. I have to tell Paige. She still has a choice, and the child hasn't been born yet. If I let you continue deceiving her, her life might be ruined forever!"

I looked at Philip, feeling utterly disappointed.

Regardless, as Paige's best friend, I had to inform her of this matter and let her make her own decision.

Otherwise, if she found out after the child was born that Philip wasn't a good person, it would be too late!

Once again, I pulled out my phone and dialed Paige's number directly.

Philip grew increasingly frantic, rushing over, attempting to snatch the phone from my hand.

I held the phone high, dodging desperately, doing everything to prevent Philip from grabbing it. Unbeknownst to me, in the struggle, we had edged closer to the railing of the deck.

"Madeline, don't make that call! You can't say it, if you do, Paige will forever hate me!"

"Philip, am I supposed to watch you deceive my best friend? I can't let her life be ruined by you!"

"Madeline, are you out of your mind? This is between Paige and me, please don't interfere."

"Sorry, I can't do that. Since I've seen it, I must intervene! I..."

Both of us fought fiercely for the phone, but how could I, a woman, match the strength of a 6-foot-tall man?

In the struggle, Philip pushed me towards the edge of the railing, his sole focus on seizing my phone.

As he heard Paige's voice clearly on the phone, he panicked, suddenly shoving me forcefully outwards.

I was caught off guard. By the time I reacted, I was plunging into the icy sea!

A massive wave engulfed me, submerging me completely. I struggled desperately, but memories of drowning in a past life flooded back like a nightmare.

In the water, I heard distant shouts of my name from the deck.

I told myself inwardly, "I can't die! I can't! Not like in the past life!

I have a child inside me. I can't just lose my life like this!"

Driven by a fierce will to survive, I held my breath, slowly resurfacing from the water.

Fortunately, in this life, learning from past mistakes, I had taken private swimming lessons. No longer the Madeline completely clueless in water, I managed to keep myself afloat.

People on the deck continued calling out my name.

The sea was freezing. My body stiffened from the cold. I thought, "On this massive vessel with so many people, someone will surely come to rescue me!"

I kept mentally reinforcing my resolve. As I was nearing my breaking point, a small boat gradually approached me.
