As soon as I entered the office, I noticed that there were several potted plants in my office. There was also a humidifier on the desk, and a cute teddy bear cushion on my chair. Additionally, there was a paper cup placed on my desk.

These thoughtful arrangements, needless to say, must have been done by Adam.

"Adam, what did you bring me today?"

I looked at these things and couldn't help but feel happy, so I shouted loudly outside.

Adam immediately responded and came in, smiling brightly as soon as he entered.

"Madeline, the weather has been quite dry lately, so I prepared a humidifier for you. This latte was freshly bought this morning, so it is still warm. Hurry up and drink it."

"Okay, thank you." I smiled at him.

"Madeline, have you heard about the things regarding Giselle in the past few days?" Adam couldn't help but gossip with me when he saw that I was in a good mood.

"Did you mention the thing about her and Elliott? I heard about it."

I responded lightly, not telling him that I was the mastermind behind the whole thing.

"No, everyone knew about that thing a long time ago. After that incident, Mr. Cormac Bryant passed away. I heard that Elliott was particularly angry and demanded that Giselle pay for his grandfather's death. So, guess what happened?"

Adam immediately said.

In the past few days, I have been staying at home with Austin and Lily, hardly looking at my phone or paying attention to the news. So, Adam's words caught me off guard.

"What happened?"

"Giselle left a suicide note and jumped into the sea. Her family reported it to the police. The authorities only found a pair of high heels she had worn at the spot where she jumped into the sea."

Adam spoke slowly.

"What did you say?"

I felt somewhat incredulous. I started to become somewhat excited.

I never imagined that this woman, who caused chaos in both my past and current lives, would die like this.

Although I wished more than anyone for her to pay the price, to feel all my pain and receive the deserved retribution, I still felt so sudden when I heard this news.

"That night, there was a strong storm. The police determined through surveillance footage that she had jumped into the sea, but they searched for a long time and couldn't find her body. To be honest, I felt quite uneasy upon hearing this news."

Adam spoke again, his tone somewhat heavy.

I wanted to say something, but I didn't know what to say.

In this world, as long as you have done a little harm to others, villains would wish for you to die, but kind-hearted people always choose forgiveness and mercy.

I hated Giselle, but I never thought about making her die.

I didn't expect that she would ultimately choose to end her life by jumping into the sea. It was really sudden.

"Madeline, the Bryant family has sent a notice and hopes that you can attend the funeral of Mr. Cormac Bryant. Take a look..."

Adam then took out another letter and handed it to me.
