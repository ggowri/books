I patted Elliott shyly in his arms. After all, there were many people around, but he didn't let me go.

He kissed me deeply for almost a minute before letting me go with a smile. Seeing my blushing face, he chuckled and gently rubbed my nose.

"Madeline, we are a legitimate couple, but your action makes me feel as if I am molesting you."

Yes, we were a couple...

But why did I always feel that he was as far away as the moon in the sky? Only at this moment did I feel that he was my husband.

I snuggled into his arms and said nothing, but I could not help smiling.

The wind was still blowing, and there were many climbers on the top of the mountain. We hugged each other and enjoyed the wind for a while.

After a while, Elliott whispered to me, "It's time for us to get down, or it'll get cold."

"Okay," I responded gently.

We held hands and started walking down the mountain.

I was a little hungry. Fortunately, there were shops along the mountain road. Some vendors sold a local specialty snack. It was made of flour, wrapped with fresh meat and shrimp, and fried into pancakes. It smelled delicious.

Elliott bought one for me, but he didn't eat it. I took it and took a bite. It tasted very good, so I put it to his lips.

"Try it, it's really delicious."

"I never eat fried foods." Elliott frowned. His face filled with resistance.

"Just take a bite, okay?" I said coquettishly.

Elliott had no choice but to lower his head and take a bite in the place where I had just bitten.

This was the intimacy that couples should have, right?

But Elliott and I had just reached this step today.

I held the pancake that he had taken a bite of and ate it slowly as if I tasted happiness. This feeling of happiness and satisfaction made me feel in a trance.

Elliott held my hand all the time. Even though our palms were sweating a little, he didn't let go of my hand.

We walked down silently, and when we were halfway down, Elliott's phone rang.

Elliott answered the phone. I didn't know what the person said on the other end of the phone, but I found his expression instantly turned solemn.

"Okay, I'll rush back now. You must let the best doctor treat Cormac and make sure that nothing will happen to him, or you'll have to face the consequences."

After saying that, Elliott hung up the phone. He turned to look at me, and his face looked particularly solemn.

"What's wrong? What happened to Cormac?"

Upon hearing what Elliott said, I felt something was wrong, and I asked him uneasily.

"Cormac suddenly fell down at home, and he is now in the hospital. Madeline, we have to return to Hoswich as soon as possible," "Elliott said to me with a serious expression.

Then, he quickened his pace and went down the mountain, and I quickly caught up with him.

I knew very well that Cormac was important to Elliott. He was worried after learning that Cormac had a fall.

"Okay, let's go back quickly."

We didn't talk much and went down the mountain at a trotting pace. After we arrived at the parking lot and got in the car, Elliott drove onto the highway.

He drove at a high speed all the way to Hoswich. I grasped the handle without saying anything.
