When I woke up again, I found myself in a hotel.

I had a splitting headache, and I tried to sit up, only to find that I had no strength at all.

In front of me, a camera was pointing at me, and two muscular men wearing bathrobes were standing at the edge of the bed.

"You go first, or I go first?"

"You take off her clothes first, and I'll take the picture."

"Alright, let's enjoy her tonight."

After the two men had agreed with each other, extremely evil smiles appeared on their faces.

"What were you going to do?"

I looked at the scene in front of me, and I was so shocked that I broke out in a cold sweat. I instinctively blurted out, but soon, a strong man stuffed a towel into my mouth.

"Little beauty, don't worry. We will definitely serve you right tonight."

The strong man looked at me evilly and couldn't wait to pounce on me, tearing my clothes apart.

Meanwhile, the other man had already positioned himself behind the camera. He turned it on, pointed it at my angle, and started shooting.

I really wanted to struggle, but no matter how hard I tried, my hands felt useless. I couldn't exert any strength at all.

The strong man had already started unbuttoning me. The immense feeling of humiliation forced me to shake my head desperately. I couldn't help but shed tears.

For so many years, Elliott never slept with me, and until now, I was still a virgin.

But I never thought that I would be inexplicably kidnapped and about to be raped tonight.

Who exactly wanted to harm me?

Was it sent by Elliott?

No matter how ruthless he was, he wouldn't go so far as to harm me, right? After all, I was still his nominal wife.

I was lost in thinking when I saw Jade.

That day on the rooftop, I clearly heard Tom and Jade's conversation. They discussed and planned to target me.

So, undoubtedly it was the most vicious move to have someone rape me, take pictures of me, and ruin my reputation, enough to make me infamous.

Realizing this, I broke out in a cold sweat.

I tried to struggle, but the water that the driver handed me must have been spiked with something. At this moment, I felt like a helpless lamb being slaughtered, completely powerless to fight back.

Looking at the ugly man in front of me, who was pouncing on me like a vicious wolf, I broke down and started crying uncontrollably as he was about to tear my clothes apart.

Just at that moment, a noise suddenly came from the door.

The two strong men heard the noise and quickly turned their heads, shouting loudly, "Who is it?"

"You bastard! You dare to do this on my turf. Are you looking for death?"

A man's roar came from the room, and immediately after, several bodyguards rushed in and effortlessly pinned the two strong men tightly against the wall.

"Madeline, are you okay?"

The familiar sound rang in my ears, and I opened my eyes to find Quinn appearing before me like a heavenly soldier.

How did he suddenly appear here?
