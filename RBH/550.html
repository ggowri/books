Fiona finished speaking, and the camera shook before quickly focusing on something not far in front of her.

Sylvia, with her cascading waves of hair, donned a revealing off-the-shoulder black dress, her diamond tassel earrings gleaming. She still maintained her seductive appearance from before, with heavy makeup, forming a sharp contrast with Giselle, who was dressed in pajamas.

Perhaps due to pregnancy, Giselle, who used to have a slender figure, now appeared clumsy and swollen.

She had probably rushed over from home when Sean mentioned it. So, she wore no makeup, and a headband holding her hair, resembling an ordinary housewife.

Clearly, she hadn't expected Elliott to be sitting with such an alluring woman, and even if she saw Giselle, there was no sign of avoiding suspicion from the other party.

The argument between the two reverberated through the phone, and I listened with relish. "Move aside, you bitch! Who do you think you are? Elliott is my husband, get out of here now!" Giselle said.

"I... I'm carrying Elliott's child! If you don't move, I won't let you off!" Giselle threatened.

"Elliott specifically invited me tonight. I won't move. Since you're pregnant, you shouldn't be running around, especially not in a bar. It's bad for the baby!" Sylvia replied.

Clearly provoked, Giselle couldn't bear it anymore. Seeing Sylvia sitting so provocatively next to Elliott, with Elliott drunk and slumped on her shoulder, Giselle snapped and grabbed Sylvia's long hair!

The screams of the women, accompanied by the sound of a bottle smashing on the ground, echoed through the VIP room.

Alexis tried to intervene, but the two women were already grappling with each other.

The room descended into chaos in an instant, and soon, Giselle's blood-curdling screams came through the video call. Hearing that voice, I couldn't help but feel a chill down my spine.

"Let go! My stomach hurts! I'm bleeding! I'm bleeding!" Giselle cried out.

"Sylvia, let go! She's really bleeding! She's pregnant, and if anything happens to her, you'll be responsible. Sylvia, don't act rashly!" Alexis pleaded.

Seeing Giselle bleeding, Alexis's voice rang out in the VIP room.

Sylvia finally realized the seriousness of the situation and let go of Giselle's hair. At this moment, Fiona, being a doctor, sensed trouble and quickly said to the phone, "I'll go check on the situation. It'll be disastrous if something happens! I'll hang up now; call you later if there's any news!"

After briefing me, Fiona promptly ended the call.

In an instant, my world returned to silence, but the chaotic scene from moments ago continued to replay in my mind.
