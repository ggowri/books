"Don't just focus on me, have some yourself. Eat more shrimp. Fiona mentioned that it's great for men's health."

I quickly loaded his plate with plenty of shrimp.

Elliott caught the underlying meaning in my words and, across the table, playfully flicked my nose.

I thought, "Could it be that I have misjudged his feelings for Giselle?

Elliott seemed to grasp my subtle hint, looking at me with a suggestive gaze and a touch of romance in his words.

Blushing, I shook my head hastily.

"Don't worry, I never travel with someone of the opposite sex. Barry and a few executives are going with me," Elliott reassured me, seemingly understanding my concerns.

I relaxed, knowing that he wouldn't have any interaction with Giselle while he was away.

My heart raced as I lowered my head to enjoy the food on my plate.
