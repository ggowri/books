I kept my eyes open and looked at the darkness in front of me.

He remained silent, a long silence, and I, too, did not say anything more.

There is a voice in my heart that told me, "My love for him, it cannot go back, it can never go back, unless he is willing to let go of all other women and love me unconditionally."

However, I am more aware than anyone that this is undoubtedly impossible, it cannot happen.

Elliott obviously realized that I had lost my feelings, and for someone as proud as him, forcefully possessing me would be meaningless.

"Okay, I could let you go, but marriage, it couldn't be ended."

I don't know how long it had been, but he said in a low voice in the darkness.

"It was so difficult to maintain like this, and it seems... it doesn't have any meaning, don't you think?"

I responded weakly.

To be honest, I had wanted to leave a long time ago, and not just once, I had entertained thoughts of divorce.

If it weren't for not wanting Giselle to be with him so soon, even if it meant resorting to legal means, I would also want a divorce.

"In a while, Cormac was still ill, and I didn't want to upset him."

Elliott whispered.

It seems that he didn't want to divorce, not because he couldn't bear to leave me, but because he was concerned about Cormac's health.

"Okay, from today on, we won't interfere with each other, alright?"

I understood and then said lightly.

He had been silent for a long time, and then, after a while, he suddenly turned over. In the darkness, he suddenly reached out a hand and pulled my frail body into his arms.

I was stunned.

I thought we had an agreement, but what is this sudden action he is taking again?

"Or, let's try a different approach. You always say that I don't care about you, that I don't treat you like a wife. What if I tried to change that starting from today?"

Elliott rested his chin on top of my head, his tone gentle yet determined.

I was startled and trembled for a moment.

If I didn't just hear wrong and didn't misunderstand, is Elliott compromising with me?

No, this is not possible...

Didn't he and Giselle already start interacting frequently?

Did he have a good impression of Giselle already?

"Are you saying...?"

Various speculations surged in my heart, but I still dared not believe it, so I asked in confusion.

"Over the past few years, those rumors outside may not necessarily be true, it's just that I didn't want to explain. I also didn't have the desire to meet any true love like you thought. I feel that we can try to start over."

After Elliott finished speaking, he held me even tighter.

His embrace was indeed spacious, and, without any obstacles, he held me tightly, our bodies pressed against each other. I could fully feel the warmth of his chest and the strong beating of his heart inside his chest.

Such an embrace was very genuine, and the genuine feeling often made one's heart flutter.

The high wall that I had built in my heart suddenly loosened a bit, because I knew that Elliott, being so proud, rarely wanted to make such a compromise.

It seems that, compared to the past, he has started to care about me... and this is definitely a good sign.

Didn't I once hope to break apart the love between him and Giselle?

Since he compromised willingly and now we cannot divorce, why should I not agree?

"Okay, but how do we start over?"

I laughed without realizing it, and at that moment, I instinctively reached out and wrapped my arms around his waist.

My actions made him tremble for a moment, and he suddenly lowered his head, and then planted a light kiss on my forehead.

"I would change, it's getting late, let's go to sleep."

He resumed his usual aloofness, unwilling to say too much about anything, summarizing his attitude in just a few words.
