As I thought about it, the hand holding the phone trembled nervously.

The voice of the woman on the phone suddenly became eerie.

"Prepare ten million dollars and come to Ruini to redeem him. You must bring the money within one day, remember, you can only come alone. If I find out that you have called the police or brought someone with you, I will kill him."

What?

Was Quinn really kidnapped?

My whole body's blood froze in an instant, and my first thought was, this is impossible.

Quinn, who has always been accustomed to traveling alone around the world, had a wealth of experience and a wide social circle, with friends all over the world. How could someone like him possibly be kidnapped?

My first thought was not to believe her.

I immediately said, "I don't believe it! I heard that there are many scammers in the border area of Norch City. You wouldn't be one of those scamming gangs trying to deceive me, would you?"

"Don't you believe it?" the voice on the phone laughed wickedly, "You can add me on WhatsApp and see him with your own eyes!"

She hung up the phone after she finished speaking, and soon after, she added me on WhatsApp.

The sleepiness that I still had lingering moments ago has completely dissipated.

I stared at the phone screen intently, praying that all of this wouldn't be true. But soon enough, she actually sent a video, and just as I had opened it, I was almost scared enough by the horrifying scene inside to throw away the phone.

In the video, a man was locked in a water dungeon made entirely of wooden circles. Except for his head, all other parts of his body were immersed in extremely dirty water. I could clearly see snakes, leeches, and grasshoppers in the water... In short, it was very filthy and terrifying.

The man's face had been beaten to a pulp, and through the video, I couldn't see his face clearly at all, nor could I identify who he was. However, I did notice the wristwatch on his bound arm.

That watch indeed belonged to Quinn.

It was an extremely valuable wristwatch, passed down from the ancestors of the Woods family. Quinn had proudly told me that this watch was one of a kind in the world, a unique piece, so he always wore it wherever he went, even when he slept, he never took it off.

Is the man who was locked in the water dungeon really Quinn?

My heart instantly rose to my throat, and I instinctively covered my chest, unbelievably watching everything in the video.

And at that moment, the hoarse female voice in the video reappeared.

"Quinn, your crush doesn't believe that I kidnapped you. I have no choice but to show her your miserable state. Whether you can survive or not depends on whether she has a conscience toward you..."

As soon as the woman finished speaking, a long whip suddenly fell down and fiercely struck the man's hand exposed outside the water dungeon.

The man let out an extremely painful scream instantly, desperately struggling in the water dungeon, with his facial expression twisted in extreme agony. I watched the video repeatedly, trying to see clearly who he was, but I still couldn't confirm if he was Quinn until he suddenly shouted in extreme pain.

"Madeline, please don't come!"

His voice was extremely weak, yet it seemed to have used up all his strength. Despite his hoarse voice, it was indeed Quinn's tone and intonation!

I felt as if my heart was instantly split open by a lightning bolt, causing immense pain.

I immediately dialed the woman's phone number. As soon as the call was connected, I couldn't suppress the excitement in my heart and started shouting at her crazily.

"Who the fuck are you? What did you do to Quinn? Why did you treat him like this? What do you want?"
