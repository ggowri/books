"What's going on? Where did this water come from? Madeline, what trick are you playing again?"

"Ms. Mayers' water broke! She is about to give birth to a baby soon! Hurry and take her to the hospital! Regardless of any resentment you may have with Ms. Mayers, the baby in her belly is innocent!"
