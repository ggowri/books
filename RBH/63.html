"Where were you?"

"I was at the hotel."

"Who were you with at the hotel?" his voice suddenly raised.

"That was my freedom. You had no right to interfere."

I didn't want to say anything more to him. So, I hung up the phone directly.

Afterward, my mood inexplicably became irritable.

Why do I still care so much about his every move?

I could completely ignore and easily handle everything he did before.

But why, as soon as Giselle appeared, did I feel like I was back in that state of being overly concerned about Elliott, just like before?

What exactly was I still caring about?

Shouldn't I have already let go of all this a long time ago?

Did Giselle's appearance suddenly expose my true feelings for him? Am I really that vulnerable?

I was lost in my thoughts in my head. However, the more I thought, the more my head started to ache. I shook my head and stopped thinking. I opened the door and was about to walk out. I didn't expect Quinn to appear at the door.

"Hey, you woke up, huh?"

After Quinn saw me, he paused for a moment and then he laughed heartily.

"Um. Thank you for last night. How much were the phone and clothes? I'll transfer the money to you."

I said hastily.

"With our relationship, it seems strange for you to say that. Do I really need you to transfer this small amount of money to me?"

Quinn blinked at me.

"What's our relationship, Mr. Woods? If I remember correctly, you used to dislike me a lot."

I was amused by Quinn's remarks. So, I couldn't help but tease back.

"I said, people change. Haven't you changed too?" Quinn laughed again. He reached out and affectionately patted my head, "After you got drunk last night, you said a lot of nonsense. Do you remember?"

I shook my head.

It is evident that I drank too much last night. This caused me to completely forget what happened afterward. I only remember that I and him drank a lot of alcohol at his club. I remember that we both played dice. I remember him carrying me... I can no longer remember what happened afterward.

"What did I say? Quinn, did I say something I shouldn't have?" I asked skeptically.

He chuckled.

"You said it. You said quite a lot. I even recorded it. You better treat me better in the future. Otherwise, I will spread all the things you said!"

"What did I say? Where is the recording? Let me listen to it-"

After I heard his words, I immediately became anxious. Subconsciously, I reached out my hand, wanting to snatch his phone.

He held the phone high above his head. Quinn and Elliott were about the same height. They were both tall, standing at over 6ft. Even if I tiptoed or jumped to reach, I still couldn't reach the phone in his hand.

Quinn was clearly teasing me on purpose. I tried to grab him a few times, but he skillfully dodged each attempt. This made me so angry that I couldn't help but angrily slap his chest. And he just laughed heartily in response. His infectious laughter echoed throughout the entire hallway.

"Quinn, hurry up and let me listen to what you recorded?"

"Wait until you snatch the phone, then I'll let you listen. You little dwarf."

"Quinn, you were just a shorty. Your whole family was shorties. I was freaking 5.6ft. I was not short at all, okay?"

"You are not short, how could you not reach my phone? Are you too clumsy?"

"Quinn!"

Quinn's continuous teasing made me suddenly lose all interest. I shouted out his name loudly.

Afterward, I stood still in the same spot. My emotions plummeted to the bottom. I no longer had any intention to continue fooling around with him.

He probably sensed my emotions all of a sudden. So, he also instantly became serious. Then, he opened the empty recording software on his phone and said to me:

"I lied to you. I didn't record anything. You didn't say anything you shouldn't have last night, so don't worry."

Since he didn't have a recording, why did he just lie to me?

I couldn't help but glare at him fiercely. Then, I took the lead and stepped into the elevator. But to my surprise, at the moment when the elevator door was about to close, he actually squeezed in forcefully.

"Actually, you said you didn't care about Elliott, but you still cared about him."

In the cramped elevator, Quinn suddenly said these words. It was as if he had already seen through all of my thoughts.
