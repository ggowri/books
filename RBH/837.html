Just then, the driver Adam had sent was already waiting in our company's business car not far away. Without hesitation, I headed straight for the car and quickly climbed in.

Arrived home.

The moment I pushed open the door and dropped my luggage, I immediately dashed towards the nursery, my heart pounding with excitement.

I had barely made it to the second floor when Lily rushed in. She held a bottle of alcohol disinfectant spray in her hand, swiftly misting it all over me from head to toe.

"Mom, what are you up to?" I asked, furrowing my brows.

"There's all sorts of flu going around out there. How can I let you near my little one without disinfecting you first?" Lily said, her brows furrowed in concern.

She used to dote on me so much, but now, with Noah around, she barely pays me any attention. Her heart and mind are all for Noah now.

"Is your darling now Noah, not me?"

"Of course, you've got to see this, Noah has grown so much these past few days. His little face is all chubby and absolutely adorable. Your dad and I wish we could stay awake 24/7, just to watch him. He's just too cute."

The moment Lily mentioned Noah, she instantly became animated, words pouring out of her in a torrent.

She yanked my hand, immediately leading me into the nursery.

In my haste, I was about to rush over to see Noah, but to my surprise, I found Quinn and Austin standing by the crib, playfully entertaining the little baby inside.

"Noah, do you like the little sand hammer Daddy bought for you? Blink if you do."

"Noah, give daddy a smile. If you do, I'll buy you anything you want in the future—"

……

Quinn was facing away from me, amusing Noah with laughter, while Austin stood nearby, chuckling.

Quinn's so-called "dad" had me taken aback.

Clearly, he's fully embraced his role as Noah's dad, otherwise, he wouldn't speak so naturally.

Yet here I am, forced to stick by Elliott's side, feeling a pang of guilt.

"Quinn has been coming over almost every day lately. Each time, he either brings toys for Noah or something for us. He really adores Noah. A couple of days ago, when the nanny was off and your dad and I were too nervous to bathe Noah, it was Quinn who stepped in. He was incredibly attentive."

Lily leaned in close, whispering softly into my ear.

Hearing her say that, I was deeply moved.

"Quinn, I'm back. Is Noah awake?" I approached with a smile, asking casually.

"Madeline!" Quinn turned, a look of surprise and delight on his face. "The little guy just woke up a bit ago. You two really do have a knack for timing. He's been sleeping all this while, and the moment you return, he wakes up. Come, give him a cuddle."

It seemed as though Noah truly knew I was back, as he suddenly burst into tears.
