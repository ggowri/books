A more suitable supplier?

Is this matter related to Giselle?

I became alert and calmly asked, "When did they tell you they had found other suppliers?"

"They contacted me in the past few days. I had prepared the flowers for them, thinking of delivering them after signing the contract. I don't know what happened, but they suddenly informed me that they won't be cooperating with me," Paige said angrily.

Later, she couldn't help but blame Elliott.

Paige and Fiona have always belittled Elliott in front of me due to his indifferent attitude towards me over the years. I have become accustomed to this.

"After we finished eating, I went to ask Elliott. Don't worry," I said seriously.

I recommended Paige because I didn't want Giselle to have the opportunity to meet Elliott every day by sending flowers. So, I had to prevent this from happening.

After having dinner with Paige and Fiona, I packed a portion of foie gras and went to the office building of the Bryant Group.

I knew Elliott's preferences. The foie gras at this restaurant was one of his favorite foods.

So, he was also a regular here. That's why I used to come here often with my friends before. I hope to have the chance to meet him more often.

I arrived at the Bryant Group around 4 o'clock in the afternoon.

When I was riding the elevator to the floor where Elliott's office was located, Barry, his assistant, happened to be standing at the elevator door.

Barry saw me and was still trembling with fear.

"Mrs. Bryant, Mr. Bryant was in a meeting."

Maybe because I used to cry and make a fuss, I left a bad impression on Barry. Every time he saw me, he would habitually say, "Elliott is not here."

I laughed and handed the coffee I packed to Barry.

"Barry, I remember you loved drinking coffee, so I specially packed a cup for you from Listen to the Wind. Is Mr. Bryant in the office? Don't worry. I'm here to deliver food to him, not to cause trouble."

Barry took the coffee with surprise. Before he could react, I walked towards Elliott's office.

"Mrs. Bryant, wait..."

Barry hurriedly caught up with me, but he couldn't stop me. I pushed open the door to Elliott's office, and as soon as I entered, I immediately revealed a sweet smile.

"Elliott, were you busy? I didn't disturb you, did I?"

I rarely spoke to him so gently.

Elliott sat behind a large desk. Upon hearing my voice, he instinctively looked up. When he saw me, a hint of confusion flickered in his deep eyes. He forced a smile and waved his hand, gesturing for Barry to leave. Then, he asked, "What are you doing here?"
