I opened the door and standing at the entrance was a delivery person.

However, I did not order takeout.

"Hello, may I ask if you are Ms. Mayers? Someone asked me to deliver this to you."

I was feeling confused when the delivery person handed me a bag of something.

I was stunned. It seemed that someone had sent it to me.

I took the black bag from the delivery person's hand, closed the door, and curiously opened the bag.

However, when I opened the bag, the things inside almost scared me to death!

I screamed in shock and instinctively threw the bag away!

I never expected that someone would deliberately play a prank on me by putting a live snake in that bag!

I have always been afraid of these long creatures since I was little. I would tremble all over when I was scared, and it would even cause a slight pain in my lower abdomen!

I opened the door as if I had gone crazy, desperately calling for help. Fortunately, the waiter quickly came over and asked me what had happened.

As soon as I said there was a snake in the room, they immediately called the security and cleared out the black bag.

I was scared to the point of turning pale, my legs went weak, and I still couldn't calm down. Seeing me in this state, the waiter quickly helped me to their lounge and let me rest for a while.

Although I experienced abdominal pain due to being startled, it didn't take long for the pain in my stomach to gradually disappear.

I leaned on the sofa, carefully thinking about this matter, and increasingly felt that the whole thing was very strange, and it felt very wrong.

I just saw in that video that Quinn was surrounded by water snakes, and immediately a delivery person brought a snake... It seems that these two things must have a huge connection.

And, most importantly, that woman actually knew my room number so accurately and acted so quickly.

Could it be true that I offended someone by investing in Dalton's movie, just like Elliott said, and now someone wants to harm me?

I suddenly felt a chilly sensation down my spine, and at that moment, Adam and Jane hurriedly appeared in the hotel lobby.

When they saw me leaning on the sofa, they immediately ran over, squatting on either side of me, and grabbed my hands.

"Ms. Mayers, are you okay?" Jane asked with concern.

It was probably because she was in such a hurry to come back that I noticed sweat on her face.

"It's nothing, I just got a little scared," I said calmly.

"Madeline, it's a relief that you're okay. We were really scared when we heard the news! I had said at that time that I wouldn't go to the snowy mountain and would stay here with you, but you didn't agree. But now, you have indeed encountered a problem."

Adam carefully looked me up and down, criticizing me with concern.

Time was running out, and I couldn't afford to exchange pleasantries with them. I quickly glanced around and then, lowering my voice, I said to them.

"I was fine, but Quinn got into trouble, we must go and save him!"

Although Jane had not been working in our company for a long time, she often saw Quinn coming in and out of my office, so she also knew who Quinn was.

Hearing me say this, both Jane and Adam's faces slightly changed.

Adam quickly asked me in a low voice, "Madeline, what happened to Quinn? Where is he now?"

The hotel lobby was constantly bustling with people coming and going, making it an unsuitable place for conversation.

I moved with them to Jane's room, and I had already developed a great psychological shadow over my own room, and I dared not step in there again.
