A yawn escaped me as I prepared for bed, but my drowsiness was shattered by the insistent ringing of the doorbell downstairs.

Who could be at our door at this hour?

With a puzzled frown, I left the study and flicked on the lights in the living room. Peering through the peephole, I found the area outside devoid of any presence.

At this moment, fear pricked at me.

Who would play such a prank late at night?

I stood and waited in the silent hallway for some time, then seeing that there were no further movements out there, decided to return to my bedroom upstairs.

Yet, as I turned, the doorbell rang once more, this time urgently.

Startled, I rushed back to the door and checked the peephole again, but there was still no one in sight.

What was happening?

A shiver ran down my spine as I called out, "Who's there?"

But there was only the whistle of the wind in response, with no sign of anyone outside.

I turned away again, but the doorbell rang again, each time more urgently than the last.

Panic surged through me. Crouching, I checked the peephole again, but the view remained empty.

What was happening?

Was someone deliberately toying with me?

My instinct urged me to open the door and investigate further, but I hesitated, realizing I was alone and vulnerable. If there was indeed danger at my doorstep, I wouldn't be able to do anything to resist.

The alarm on my phone monitoring system sounded, and I quickly checked the surveillance feed. Simultaneously, the doorbell rang again.

Beneath the feed, I saw a figure dressed in black at the doorstep. His face was obscured by a hood, but it seemed to be a man.

But with his face hidden and his fully black attire, I couldn't discern his identity.

The incessant ringing intensified my unease. Trembling, I watched the figure on the surveillance feed.

As the person outside realized I wasn't opening the door, he began forcefully pounding on it, the repeated thuds echoing through the midnight air, intensifying the terror of the moment.

My body broke out in a cold sweat, and just as suddenly as it began, the pounding ceased. But as I thought the person had left, a sinister silhouette appeared at the living room window.

Then came the sound of shattering glass as the intruder, unable to breach the door, attempted to force entry through the window!

Watching the shadow at the window and hearing the frantic crashing of the window, I felt a chill run down my spine. Hastily, I reached for my phone to call the police.

However, at that moment, the roar of a car engine filled the air outside my home.

Soon after, the engine quieted.

Glancing at the surveillance footage, I spotted a white Porsche Cayenne parked in my yard.

And just like that, the figure who moments ago tried to break into my home vanished...
