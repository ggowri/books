I thought Paige wanted to calm herself down and have a good sleep. I sat beside her silently but heard her faint sobbing after a while.

Realizing something was wrong, I quickly threw the blanket away and hugged her.

"Paige, what's wrong? Why are you crying?"

"Madeline, my baby ... my baby is gone. Yesterday, he still kicked incessantly in my belly. He must hate me for not bringing him to the world. Madeline, I'm so devastated..."

Paige finally stopped pretending to be strong. She collapsed in my arms and cried uncontrollably.

My heart ached.

"Paige, your baby has left. Don't miss him anymore. It's not your fault. He will hate Philip and Jezebel instead of you. He should not have been a sacrifice in your relationship. Stop blaming yourself. Don't cry."

"I miss him so much. Madeline, I can't fall asleep. Every time I close my eyes, I can't help but think of him. I feel so sad. I have never been sad and hated someone as much as I do now. Why did Philip betray me? I gave him everything I had. Why?"

Paige cried her heart out, and her agonizing accusations made me sad.

"He didn't deserve your love for him. You shouldn't punish yourself for his mistakes. Don't cry, Paige..."

I kept stroking her head and back to comfort her. At that moment, any words seemed useless.

Paige cried for a long time and finally fell asleep with my comfort. Looking at her tear-stained face, I was sad.

I had experienced the pain of being betrayed, and I hoped my friends would never go through it and wished for them to be in a long-lasting relationship. Unfortunately, there were too many jerks in the world.

That night, I did not leave. Afraid Paige would be upset, I stayed by her bedside the whole time.

She had not yet told her parents about the baby, so her family was unaware. Fiona and I were the closest to her.

Fiona had to work the night shift and would occasionally come to see Paige and me. Worried about me catching a cold, she arranged a bed for me, bringing me a blanket and asking me to rest next to Paige.

That night, because of worries about Paige, it wasn't until almost dawn that I finally fell asleep.

However, not long after, I heard someone shouting outside, "She wants to jump off the roof! She's committing suicide!"
