I was devastated when I heard that the child no longer had a fetal heart. I angrily punched Philip a few times. However, no matter what we did, it was already meaningless.

About an hour later, the door of the emergency room opened. Then the attending physician and several nurses pushed Paige out.

I immediately rushed forward, instinctively looking at Paige's belly. When I saw that the previously swollen belly had flattened, my heart felt a sharp pain. I couldn't help but hold Paige's hand, tears streaming down my face.

"Paige..."

Looking at Paige's pale face, I felt heartbroken. I couldn't speak, tears streaming down my face, not knowing what to say.

"Madeline, I gave up the child... It's a boy, and I don't know if he will blame me. But, I don't want him to have such a dirty father, and I don't want to bring him into this world to suffer," Paige murmured.

When she finished speaking, tears slowly slid down from the corners of her eyes.

Every mother loved her child. I knew Paige was sadder than anyone else.

Looking at the tears on her face, I didn't know what to say. In this situation, comforting words were useless.

"Paige, I know I was wrong. I shouldn't have betrayed you. I am a jerk. But, that was the child we had been expecting for so long! How could you give up on him? You can punish me, but how could you abandon our child?"

Philip kept apologizing to Paige. I knew he didn't want to lose that child. He covered his face with both hands, tears streaming down.

Paige slowly turned her head and looked at Philip. There was no longer any love in her eyes, only infinite hatred.

She said coldly, "Philip, it was you who ended the life of our child, not me. I told you that if you betrayed me during my pregnancy, I would not hesitate to abort our child and break up with you. Now, I have kept my word. From now on, we have nothing to do with each other. Get lost!"

Paige squeezed out the last few words with resentment.

Philip was shocked. Paige's words made him almost lose his balance. He grabbed Paige's hand.

"Honey, don't do this to me. I know I was wrong. I promise to be loyal to you in the future, and such things will never happen again."

Philip's eyes were filled with panic, indicating that he cared deeply about Paige.

Humph! Men were all like this.

I couldn't help but secretly sneer. At this moment, Paige shook off Philip's hand and turned her head, no longer looking at Philip.

"That's enough. Go deceive other women with your nonsense. I'm tired. If you feel sorry for me, don't show up in front of me again," Paige said coldly.

I couldn't see her love for Philip in her eyes.

At this moment, Quinn hurriedly came over. He couldn't wait and asked, "How is Paige? Is the child okay?"
