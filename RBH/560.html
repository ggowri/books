Time passed quickly, and it was time to get off work.

I packed my bag and walked downstairs to the company. I did not expect that Quinn would be waiting for me at the entrance of our company with his cool off-road vehicle.

He was still dressed in black. The weather was very cold, but he was only wearing a black leather jacket with a black T-shirt and seemed extraordinarily free and casual.

"Why are you here?"

I saw the bright smile on his face from afar, so I greeted him with a smile.

As soon as I got close to him, I punched him in the shoulder. He grabbed my hand and magically pulled out a box of yogurt from his pocket.

"I'm here to pick up my son from work. This will give you some nutrition."

I took the box of yogurt from his hand and found that the outer packaging of the yogurt was still warm. And it took me a few seconds to realize that the son he was talking about was the baby in my belly.

"How do you know the baby is a boy?" I asked, dumbfounded.

He opened the car door and helped me get into the car. Then, he smiled mysteriously.

"I can feel it."

Quinn got in the car. Seeing that I was holding the yogurt but not opening it, he took it over, inserted a straw in it, handed it to me, and then said with a smile, "Call your mother and tell her that you won't go back for dinner tonight. There is a restaurant that has just opened in Hoswich. I heard that it is pretty good. I'll take you to try it."

Quinn had even arranged the dinner for me.

I never thought that I would be cared for by others when I was pregnant after a divorce.

Unexpectedly, Quinn said he would be the father of the child in my belly, and he meant it.

I was moved by his gentleness.

I called my mother and told her that I wouldn't go back for dinner. Not long afterward, Quinn took me to an alley.

He parked the car in the parking lot and got out of the car with me.

It was still very cold in Hoswich in February. Wearing a woolen coat, I was shivering with cold as soon as I got out of the car.

Seeing this, Quinn took out a thick down jacket from the trunk, put it on my shoulders, and said to me, "Try it on. I bought this specially for you two days ago. It is very warm. You must not catch a cold again during this special period."

I thought he bought this jacket for himself, but I found it fit me perfectly, then I realized that he had bought it for me.

"Quinn, you..."

I was so flattered that I didn't know what to say.

Through my eyes, he seemed to understand what I wanted to express. He put the hat of this down jacket on me and then said with a smile, "Don't be moved so soon. This is nothing. There will be things that move you even more."

"You don't have to be so nice to me. You owned me nothing. You don't have to be like this, Quinn."

His relaxed tone made me feel relaxed. Then, I deliberately expressed my thoughts in a teasing tone.

"Don't I owe you something? I probably owed you something in my previous life, so after my rebirth, I am willing to pay for you."

He smiled and put his arm around my shoulders, leading me into the alley.

If Quinn hadn't brought me here, I wouldn't have known there was an old street hidden in the downtown area of Hoswich.
