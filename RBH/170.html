Quinn grabbed a large apple and skillfully began to peel it with a fruit knife. Watching his extremely smooth movements, especially the continuous peeling of the apple skin in circles, I couldn't help but exclaim, "Quinn, you know how to do this too, I didn't expect that."

"I knew how to do a lot of things. This one is so simple. Sometimes the club gets too busy and the bartender can't keep up, so I personally made the fruit platters." Quinn became even more proud and pleased when he heard me praising him like that.

I laughed all of a sudden and said, "Really? Then one day I will go to your club and you can personally make a fruit platter for me to see if you're bragging or not."

"You come, don't mention the fruit platter, I can also make you a cocktail. I recently learned a new cocktail called Tequila Sunrise, it tastes really good and is very suitable for you girls to drink. After you recover from your injury, I will make it for you then."

"Sure, by the way, let me ask you, have Paige and Philip been getting close lately?" I suddenly remembered what Paige said that day, so I quickly asked.

Quinn, upon hearing my question, immediately had a gossipy expression on his face. He mysteriously said, "I don't know how close you two are, but lately, you've been hanging out together quite often. Philip himself is a playboy, and it seems like your best friend is quite similar. They seem to make a good match. Oh, by the way, Alexis seems to be helping your other friend with a case related to academic copyright, and they have been in frequent contact."

Another best friend?

Is it Fiona?

I hadn't heard them mention this matter before, so I quickly asked, "Is Philip a playboy? A man who often goes out should have many women, right? Are you talking about Fiona? She didn't tell me she's been involved in a lawsuit recently. I must have been out of touch."

"Philip? Well, how should I put it, he was a bit of a player, but I guess it's because he never really met a woman he genuinely liked, right? Your best friend, I think she's quite flirtatious too, isn't she?" Quinn and I had a discuss.

"She was just superficially flirtatious, but in reality, she didn't fool around. She just loved going to nightclubs. I don't care, if Philip is unreliable, you keep an eye on them and make sure they don't get too close, so as not to hurt my best friend later on." I was afraid that if they got too close, there would be problems, so I warned Quinn in advance.

"They currently seem to only enjoy playing together, I can't really sense anything else. But Alexis and Fiona, on the other hand, I do feel like they are quite compatible. One is a lawyer and the other is a doctor, it feels just right, haha..." Quinn said, suddenly becoming excited.

And as he said that, I instantly recalled the scene from that evening in my mind.

Alexis's gentle and refined face and Fiona's somewhat aloof face flashed before me. Indeed, both of them, in terms of appearance and profession, were indeed a perfect match.

"Alexis wouldn't mess up, right?" I quickly inquired for the sake of my best friend's future.

"He lived a disciplined life, every day he would either go to work or exercise, a typical academic man."

"That did indeed match well with Fiona-"

"Cough, cough..."

We were joking about gossip between friends, and no one noticed that Elliott had turned around at some point, with a completely downcast expression on his face.
