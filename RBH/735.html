Jane smiled faintly and said, "Not only have I served in the military, but I also served in a very difficult place before, in a highland area. During my three years in the military, I encountered many situations even more dangerous than this, so don't be afraid. No matter what happens, I will definitely protect you."

"This is indeed a frightening issue that we are facing, but for now, we have no other choice but to wait for Dehan's update."

What Dehan said on the phone made my heart instantly tense to the extreme.

As soon as I heard what he said, I realized that it had nothing to do with me at all, so I asked him in confusion.

"Think carefully, have you offended anyone recently?" Dehan asked me on the phone.

The first person that came to my mind was Giselle, but then I realized she had already jumped into the sea, so it was impossible for her to do these things.

I thought and thought, and I felt that this must be related to the agreement I signed with Dalton. However, I had just signed the agreement, and they acted so quickly.

And even if they wanted to retaliate, they should retaliate against me, but why would they do this to Quinn?

No matter how much I thought about it, I couldn't come up with a reason, but I still told Dehan about the situation.

After hearing my words, Dehan pondered for a few seconds and then said, "It is highly likely that it is related to your investment in this film. If that's the case, you better contact the director and find out exactly which Mexican force they have offended, whether it's the 731 Group. This will also facilitate our intervention to rescue Quinn."

Upon hearing Dehan's words, I immediately said into the phone, "Alright, I will contact Dalton right away."
