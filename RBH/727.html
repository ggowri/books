The more hysterical I became, the more that woman laughed with increasing joy.

"Haha, so you do feel sorry for him. It seems like you still care about him. I'll spare his life for now, but if I don't see the money at the time and place I specified tomorrow, then I'm afraid this Mr. Woods will either lose his limbs or his organs."

I didn't know who this crazy woman was, she was torturing Quinn so cruelly, and what's terrifying is that I knew absolutely nothing about her.

"Do you know that what you did is illegal? This is kidnapping and extortion. Are you really not afraid of me calling the police?"

I tried hard to control my panic-stricken emotions and wanted to scare her a little.

But I didn't expect that she would laugh even more recklessly.

"Call the police? Hahaha... Well, you can give it a try. I guarantee that if you call the police, not only will you not be able to save him, but you will also make his situation a hundred times worse than it is now. Besides, I am currently at the border, less than 6 miles away from Mexico. If you want Quinn to never come back, then of course, you can call the police!"

I gasped in shock. I didn't expect her to be afraid of anything.

"Okay, I can refrain from calling the police, but I am currently here in Norch City for tourism, and I cannot come up with ten million dollars in cash right now. I have an idea, though. If you provide me with an account, as long as you safely hand over Quinn to me, I promise to transfer all the money to your account."

Realizing that calling the police here might be futile, I started engaging with her, trying to gather as much information as possible.

However, she laughed mockingly on the other end of the phone.

"Madeline, I am not discussing a deal with you right now, but giving you a final warning. You have no room for negotiation, and you must do everything according to my instructions. Remember, if you deviate even slightly from my demands, I will immediately subject him to torture. I will continue to send you videos. Now, I have prepared one hundred water snakes for him to experience the taste of a water snake spa!"

After the woman finished speaking, she let out a chilling laughter, and soon, the man's hysterical shouts came from the other end of the phone.

I held the phone, trembling all over. Quinn's agonizing screams made my heart feel as if it were being stabbed with a knife.

I shouted into the phone in a hurry, "Quinn! Quinn! Don't be afraid! No matter what, I will definitely save you! Hold on, you must hold on!"

However, at that moment, she cruelly hung up the phone, and in an instant, my surroundings returned to a dead silence.

With a last glimmer of hope, I kept trying to dial Quinn's phone number, but the voice on the other end kept reminding me that his phone was already turned off.

Before, Quinn's phone was always on 24/7.

If that's the case, his three-day disappearance is so bizarre, and then the man in the water dungeon is likely to be him.

Quinn was brought up in a privileged manner since childhood, and he had never experienced such hardship.

Just thinking about that dark and terrifying water dungeon, and the one hundred water snakes lurking around him, I felt my whole body trembling. For a moment, I was so anxious that I paced around the room in circles!

And at that moment, someone suddenly knocked on my door...
