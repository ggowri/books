Quinn and I stopped and turned around in unison.

Elliott was standing behind us with a gloomy face. He was wearing a silver suit, and his cold look revealed great hostility.

"Madeline was tired, so I just brought her here to rest. "

Quinn explained calmly.

"I want to talk to Madeline alone."

Elliott looked at Quinn with clear intention.

Quinn's hand was originally on the doorknob. When he heard Elliott's words, he immediately retracted his hand and shrugged.

"Okay, the lounge is quiet, you can just talk here. However, I hope you can chat calmly. Don't get angry."

"This has nothing to do with you."

Elliott looked disdainful.

"We have nothing to talk about. Let's meet at the Courthouse tomorrow."

I looked at Elliott's eyes, feeling very disgusted.

Laura already brought Giselle to attend such an event. If I hadn't embarrassed them with my strength tonight, I would have been the one who was humiliated.

I had nothing to talk with him now.

Even if we did talk, it was just nonsense.

Sensing my strong resistance, Elliott stepped forward and pushed me into the lounge without words. Immediately, he locked the door to the lounge.

"I know everything about what happened tonight. I admit that what my mother did was wrong because she shouldn't have brought Giselle here. "

He pressed me against the door of the lounge and apologized with a low voice.

The lounge was dark, with only the windows reflecting the light from outside. Elliott didn't intend to turn on the light, so I couldn't see his expression but only his eyes.

"There's nothing wrong with that. Giselle has the power to control your mother. I can only blame myself for being incapable."

I smiled to myself and sounded exhausted.

To be honest, I was exhausted after a night of socializing, and I had a bad appetite, so I almost didn't eat.

"I will give Giselle a sum of compensation to let her have an abortion. Madeline, don't divorce me, okay?"

Elliott took a deep breath as if he had made a great decision, and suddenly said in a deep voice.

Would he let Giselle have an abortion?

I raised my head and looked at him in disbelief. He should know that Giselle's baby was important to the Bryant family.

If he did this, Cormac and his parents might not forgive him, and they would blame it on me.

Giselle had been pregnant for more than five months, and the fetus had taken shape. It would be too cruel to ask her to have an abortion.

"No need. This is what the Bryant family is looking forward to most. I don't want to be your family's sinner forever."

I thought about it and shook my head firmly.

Elliott was a little anxious, so he held my chin.

"Madeline, then what do you want me to do? "
