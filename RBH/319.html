These despicable individuals! They dared to target my mother, a woman nearing fifty, tore her clothes to shreds, gagged her with a towel.

Among them, one was busy taking pictures of my mother from different angles.

It was clear that Jade knew I wouldn't back down anymore, so she intended to use humiliating photos of my mother to make me relent.

Thankfully, I had installed surveillance cameras at home. Otherwise, I wouldn't know what these bastards might have done to my mother!

"Mom!" My heart shattered when I saw my dignified mother, now disheveled and bound to a chair, her clothes in tatters.

Before the thug about to leap on me could react, the figure delivered a fierce punch to his face.

I finally got a good look at his face.

I had thought it was Quinn who had come to help. But to my surprise, the man who held me protectively was Elliott.

I was both shocked and puzzled.

How did he know something had happened at my home?
