Giselle had been striving to become a member of the Bryant family.

I thought Elliott might not agree with her. He gave me a strange glance.

His voice suddenly became gentle, and he said to Giselle, "Alright. If you come down now, I promise you."

This was the answer I expected. However, when I heard Elliott say these words, my heart ached for a moment.

Giselle sensed something from Elliott's tone. In front of me, she gently said to Elliott, "Elliott, I feel so cold. I have no strength in my body and my legs are weak. Come and hold me down."

Her delicate voice gave me goosebumps all over my body.

I instinctively looked at Elliott. At that moment, he focused on Giselle.

I didn't know what he was thinking. He was very indifferent to Giselle just now. After I appeared, he seemed like a different person. He walked towards Giselle.

In front of the onlookers, he took off his coat and wrapped Giselle in it. Afterward, he held her in his arms.

"You have just given birth to a child. Don't be angry. And don't punish yourself with your own health."

Under the gaze of the crowd, Elliott performed his tenderness towards Giselle. His indulgent tone was familiar and heartbreaking to me.

Did he do this to stimulate me?

I stood still, trying to appear indifferent. However, I didn't know why my body was trembling. Chills ran from the soles of my feet to my forehead.

"Don't send me and the child abroad, okay? Elliott, we just want to be by your side. I promise I will be a good wife. I won't make you angry."

Giselle nestled in Elliott's arms. She appeared gentle and submissive. The photographer beside them kept pressing the shutter, capturing the scene of their affectionate gaze.

"Okay. Let's talk about it when we get back," Elliott replied softly. Then, he slowly lifted his head and looked at me standing in the distance.

At that moment, he tightly pursed his lips. I couldn't see his emotions from his deep eyes. He held Giselle and walked towards me step by step, brushing past me, and heading towards the exit behind me.

At the moment when we passed by each other, I felt a proud gaze cast upon me.

I didn't look up, but I knew whose gaze it was.

However, I wouldn't let her be proud for too long... I silently swore.

After Elliott took Giselle away, the onlookers quickly dispersed. Soon, the rooftop returned to tranquility.

Fiona walked over and patted my shoulder.

She angrily said, "Damn it! Elliott is a jerk. How could he be so intimate with her in front of you?"

"She gave birth to a child for him. It is normal that they are close. It's fine. I don't mind."

I pretended to be indifferent and shrugged my shoulders.

Fiona saw through me and said to me, "Really? If you didn't care, you wouldn't be standing here alone in a haze. Madeline, maybe you can deceive others, but you can't deceive me. I know you very well."

"I no longer cared about him. I just wanted to get back at him."
