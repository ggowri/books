"So late and calling us for a meeting, I wonder what it's about?"

No matter how good Jade's relationship was with them usually, as long as it touched their interests, they would all turn against her.

Jade, this malignant tumor, was finally removed from Glory Group.

If any abnormal situation occurred, the surveillance system would alert me immediately.

It seemed that Jade had started to target my mom!
