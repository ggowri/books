It was likely that Laura exerted pressure on me and should have communicated something to Elliott as well. It didn't take long before he sent another message.

[Ignore whatever my mother said. We can decide our own affairs.]

His attitude made me feel that he didn't desire a divorce, which left me both relieved and sorrowful.

I stared blankly at the chat window, still not replying to his message.

I thought I needed some time to digest and be alone.

Leaning lazily on the seat, I sat quietly in the service area for a long time. Finally, I decided to call Fiona.

In my previous life, in order to get pregnant, I sought Fiona's advice and explored various methods. I faintly recalled her mentioning a retired doctor in Louis City who had a distinctive approach to treating female infertility. However, this doctor was selective and believed in fate. He only met those destined to cross paths with him.

Back then, Fiona took me to find him, but at that time, the doctor happened to be taken abroad by his children and had not returned yet.

Later, when the doctor returned to the country, I had already divorced Elliott and been driven out of the Bryant Villa.

In this life, Elliott and I had taken such a rare turn for the worse. I was reluctant to be evicted by the Bryant family again. Regardless of the method, I had to try again.

I dialed Fiona's phone number. She asked on the other end of the phone, "Madeline, what's wrong?"

"Fiona, I remember you saying that there was an older doctor in Louis City who had a special prescription for female infertility. Isn't that right?"

"I've heard my mentor talk about this expert. His last name is Cullen. He lives in a historic mansion in the old area of Louis City, but Dr. Cullen is hard to get an appointment with. Madeline, would you like to visit him?" Fiona asked.

"Yes. Could you help me get his contact information? I happen to be in Louis City in the next few days, and I'd like to see if I can have that chance to meet Dr. Cullen."

"Alright. I'll look into it. I will send it to you after getting the details," Fiona readily agreed.

I hung up and took a deep breath. Then, I drove out of the service area and went straight to Louis City.

Upon arriving there, I went back to the flat Elliott had offered me.

The flat was the same as it was before he and I left. The black and white pair of couple slippers at the door were the ones we had bought when shopping in the supermarket.

Without Elliott's presence, the large flat seemed extraordinarily empty.

I casually cooked some spaghetti in the kitchen, but without much of an appetite, I ate a few bites of it randomly and threw it all away. Alone, I turned on my computer and began to sort through the emails and messages I had received over the past few days.

The filming of Do Not Love was in its final stages, and it was estimated that all the scenes would be wrapped up in half a month.

Luckily, the director and the cast chosen this time were more reliable, so all the filming went smoothly, and the costs were always within reasonable control.

​I organized and filed a summary of all the work into a PowerPoint and emailed it directly to my dad as a conclusion to my first project with the Glory Group.​
