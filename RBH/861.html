Elliott eyed my casual attire, a slight frown creasing his forehead as he said:

"Why are you in sportswear today?"

"It's the weekend, isn't it? I find sportswear quite comfy. Don't you?" I asked, already knowing the answer.

"I adore, adore you. No matter what you wear, I simply adore it."

Elliott Bryant, who used to dictate what I wore and what jewelry I adorned, now doesn't dare to voice a single opinion.

I hopped into the car, and he quickly followed suit.

The driver whisked us off in no time, heading straight for the Bryant Villa, a place I hadn't set foot in for several years.

Elliott led me out of the car.

Laura, who never used to greet us at the door, was surprisingly waiting for us there today.

Upon seeing us, Laura immediately came over, greeting us with a warm smile.

"Elliott, you're finally back. Your dad and I have been prepping lunch since morning. If you'd been any later, the food would've gone cold."

Laura was genuinely thrilled to see Elliott, but her gaze kept dodging mine, as if she was afraid to meet my eyes.

I was clueless as to why she suddenly invited Elliott and me over for dinner.

But now, I rarely pay mind to their thoughts. Whatever her intentions may be, I simply don't care.

"Mom, did you and dad prepare today's lunch?"

Elliott was taken aback as well.

"Yeah, it's not often you and Madeline come home. Your dad and I definitely have to show off our culinary skills! Madeline, come on in, make yourself at home."

Finally, Laura mustered up the courage to face me. She looked at me, her face beaming with a smile.

Her attitude was a stark contrast to yesterday's. I was on the verge of calling her out, but considering Elliott's condition, I held back.

Who doesn't know how to act?

She wants to play a part, I'll play along.

"Really? After all these years of marriage with Elliott, I've always been the one serving you guys, never tasted a meal made by you. Today, I'm definitely going to savor it."

I teased her with a grin. And just like that, we strolled into the living room together.

Jasper was already seated at the dining table, waiting for us.

In the past, he always wore a stern expression whenever he saw me. But today, he surprisingly flashed a rare smile and even took the initiative to greet me:

"Madeline's here, grab a seat."

This attitude, it really takes me by surprise.

Is it because of Laura's drama yesterday?

Why did they suddenly become so friendly towards me overnight?

"Thanks."

I murmured a thank you. Despite mentally preparing myself to play along with their charade, the sight of the couple and the all-too-familiar layout of the living room brought back a flood of unpleasant memories. So, I couldn't bring myself to utter the words "Mom and Dad".

I can't overcome the deep-seated aversion within me.

"The spread today is truly lavish. Mom, it's just the four of us for dinner, why go all out like this?"

Elliott pulled me into a seat, facing a spread of sumptuous dishes. He couldn't help but ask.

"Today's meal, your dad and I prepared it especially for Madeline. On reflection, we haven't done the best job in the past, we owe Madeline an apology..."

Laura, who used to love bossing me around from across the table, surprisingly sat next to me today.

Suddenly, she grabbed my hand, speaking with utmost sincerity.

This took me by surprise. I was stunned, the shock clearly etched on my face...
