Jezebel... If I remembered correctly, this person would become popular in the film and television industry in the next two years. Just like Audrey, both of them would become A-list stars.

But Jezebel was unknown at that time. How could he suddenly decide to use Jezebel?

"I see. Which TV station are they going to premiere on?"

A vague anger suddenly rose in my heart. Elliott did actually set up such a big scheme without letting out any information.

"Just like us. They are negotiating with Netflix about the prime time. I guess they plan to broadcast their drama right after our show..." Adam said.

I interrupted him directly before he finished his words. "It is obvious that they want to compete with us. We must disrupt their plan!"

Adam was startled by my decision. He looked at me with hesitation and finally, he asked, "But isn't Elliott your husband? Do you mean you want to have a war with him in business? "

"In business, we must be well prepared to fight with anyone. Since they plan to compete with us, we should compete with them too," I watched Adam and said solemnly.

"Okay, I get it! Mrs. Bryant! Don't worry, I wouldn't let their dream come true. " Adam immediately understood how to lay out the plan. He took my order and left my office.

Elliott's call came at this time. The name "Elliott" on the screen made me feel angry.

I picked up the phone angrily and heard a very magnetic and gentle voice. "Honey, are you still busy? "

My heart would melt when he called me like this in normal times. But now I just was angry. "I'm not busy now. What else do you want to do?"

He immediately noticed my mood from my words. "What's wrong? Why do you treat me like this?"

"Cut through to the point," I said coldly.

"I am waiting for you downstairs in the Glory Group. Lily said the meal was ready and asked me to take you back to eat," he said in a deep voice without any emotion in his tone.

"Oh." I suddenly remembered that my mother went back home to cook dishes. So I carried my bag and went downstairs. As I walked out of the door, I saw Elliott putting one of his hands in the pocket and leaning on his car.

His handsome and elegant appearance coupled with the white Porsche behind him indeed made him paralleled with any popular youngsters in the entertainment industry today. But at this moment, I had no time to appreciate him.

He initiatively opened the car door for me, and I got into the car angrily.

He noticed my angry mood. So he immediately reached over his hands to pinch my face after I got into the car. "What's wrong? Why are you so angry all of a sudden?"

I turned to look at him. As he looked innocent, I tried my best to suppress my inner anger and pretended to be calm and asked him, "Is there anything you have lied to me?"
