I could feel through his eyes that he had no ulterior motives at all. He genuinely wished from the bottom of his heart for me to live a better life.

His words cleared the fog for me and made me think clearly about many things. I instantly freed myself from tonight's emotional turmoil.

I suddenly realized that I was so out of control tonight because I started caring about Elliott again.

Because I cared, I said those words that I now regret.

Originally, I had already managed to control my emotions and feelings well in front of him, but tonight, I almost ruined it.

Quinn was right, indifference is the best weapon to retaliate against someone.

Since I had already sworn that I would never fall in love with Elliott again in this lifetime, I must strengthen my inner self and not let anything break my determination, returning to the hysterical state I was in before because of him.

"You're right. So now I just want to focus on my work. I have already filed for divorce with him, but he doesn't want to divorce me at the moment."

Because I was touched by his sincerity, I also truthfully expressed my thoughts.

"Why do you think he didn't agree?" he asked me.

"Of course, I never naively thought that he couldn't live without me. He didn't agree mainly because of the family's reputation."

I gave a bitter smile and then said.

Quinn suddenly laughed and opened another bottle of beer. He tapped the glass in my hand and said, "Cheers!"

"Madeline, I am relieved that you can think this way. Don't ask me why, as his best friend, I am on your side instead of his. I cannot tell you the reason right now. But what I want to tell you is that a girl must love herself first before loving a man. A woman who doesn't love herself is not attractive in a man's eyes. Keep going in the way you are now, and make yourself better and better, so that one day he will regret losing you. That's what you should do!"

Quinn's eyes held encouragement, and the depth in his words made me almost believe that he had also been reborn. Otherwise, how could he have undergone such a significant transformation?

I almost couldn't help but want to ask him this question, but then I thought that being reborn is undoubtedly something that happens in novels in anyone's eyes.

If it weren't for the protagonist, if it were me instead, I would never believe it, and how could Quinn also coincidentally be reborn?

It should not be.

With that thought, I didn't ask any further, but Quinn's words completely woke me up.

"I knew what to do. You will take me back to my parents' house later, and from now on, I won't be impulsive like tonight."

I nodded and smiled at him.

To be honest, ever since I was reborn, I haven't been able to share many of my inner thoughts with anyone because no one has had such an experience, so no one would understand.

But from Quinn's words, I have felt more than once that he understood me, he would always appear in my world at the right time, giving me strength.

"That's good," Quinn smiled with relief, he affectionately patted my head, "If my silly friend ever truly cares about you, and falls in love with you, I will bless you both. But if he continues to not appreciate you, I will also help you beat him up! Don't worry, I will always be by your side!"

Quinn said sincerely.

I was deeply moved when I heard his words. The darkness in my heart gradually dissipated under the comfort of his words, and in its place, a stronger determination emerged than before.

I admit that his words did indeed heal me.

And just then, my phone suddenly rang, and when I saw "Elliott" displayed on the screen, I hesitated whether to answer. But unexpectedly, Quinn snatched my phone and answered the call.
