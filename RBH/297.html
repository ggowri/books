He looked disappointed. It was obvious that he cared about this.

However, I was still distraught. Because my father was still in danger, I didn't have time to care about these details at the moment, so I said a little impatiently.

"Is this important, Elliott? The most important thing now is that my dad can wake up. I don't care anything else."

However, Elliott didn't know the hidden worries in my heart. He seemed even more disappointed when I said this.

"My wife thought of someone else instead of me when something happened to her father, isn't this important?"

"So now what do you want me to do?"

I asked, looking at him helplessly. I didn't expect Elliott would care so much about such trivial things.

"It seems that you don't need me to come here. You haven't even called me. If I didn't call you, you were not going to tell me about this, right?"

Elliott suddenly asked again.

I was silent.

I was confused again by the question.

Indeed, from the time my dad had the accident until the rescue was over, I never thought of calling him... This was a fact.

Seeing that I was silent, he pursed his lips silently. His gloomy face made me anxious.

I wanted to explain, but I didn't know what to say. He waited for a while, looked at the people present, and suddenly said in a low voice, "You told Quinn immediately, and you even told your best friend Paige and Fiona. But I was the last one to know this."

At this moment, he looked like a neglected wife.

And I had never seen or imagined him being like this.

He was arrogant and even refused my request to meet him when I was dying.

How could I have imagined that such a person would be so upset because I didn't notify him immediately when my father had an accident?

I remained silent, and then subconsciously tugged on his hand.

However, he threw away my hand angrily. He turned around and walked in the direction of Lily. I thought he was leaving. Unexpectedly, he walked up to Lily and said, " What about transferring Austin to the Bryant hospital? The medical capabilities there are better than here, and the doctors are more experienced. "

He was angry, but he didn't vent his anger in front of everyone. He still maintained his usual cold and arrogant image.

Lily immediately raised her head after hearing this. She was a little tempted, but she still said with some concerns, "I don't want to bother you again..."

"It's nothing,"

Elliott said.

I originally thought his proposal was a good one. But then I thought that Cormac was recuperating in the Bryant family's hospital. Now he wanted me to divorce Elliott. Would I cause trouble if I sent my dad to Bryant Hospital? It might be better to let my dad recuperate quietly here.

With this thought in mind, I immediately blurted out, "No need, the doctor said my dad will have to stay in the intensive care unit for observation for a few more days. He can just stay here for the time being, and it's not good to let him move around at this time."

As soon as I said this, Elliott looked at me immediately. His expression and eyes instantly became serious and gloomy...
