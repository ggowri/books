I still pretended not to care about Elliott. Then, I pulled Fiona downstairs with me.

I was planning to go back to Paige's ward, but as soon as I reached the door of Paige's ward, I saw Philip standing outside, with strawberries and flowers scattered on the floor at the entrance of the ward.

These things were brought by Philip, but Paige threw them all out.

Philip glanced at me wearily and said, "Madeline, help me persuade Paige. She still refuses to talk to me. I brought her favorite strawberries and her beloved flowers, but she threw them all away."

"You should leave. She won't forgive you. Philip, I have warned you, but you didn't pay attention to my words," I said coldly.

"I know I was wrong. In the past, I didn't care enough about her, but I will change in the future."

After saying this to me, Philip suddenly raised his voice and shouted in the direction of the ward, "Paige, believe me. I will never let something like this happen again. This incident made me realize that you are the person I care about!"

As soon as Philip finished speaking, there was an immediate "bang" sound coming from the ward.

Paige smashed something in the ward again.

"If you care about her, let her have a few days to calm down and rest. She just lost her child and her emotions are still unstable. Please don't make her feel sad, okay?" I said coldly to Philip.

Afterward, I quickly pushed open the door of the ward.

As I pushed open the door, I saw glass shards all over the floor. Paige had just smashed the glass vase that was placed on the bedside table.

"Madeline, tell him to get lost immediately! I don't want to see him!"

Seeing me, Paige held her head and angrily pounded on it.

I hurriedly stepped forward and grabbed her hand.

"Paige, don't do this. If you are angry, you should punish him, instead of yourself."

"I hated him, but I hated myself even more. The moment he appeared, I thought of my child. Do you know how much pain I felt when I gave him up? Madeline, I can't close my eyes. Whenever I close my eyes, I feel the pain. I keep wondering if I made the wrong decision. I will never forgive Philip. Philip, go away..." Paige cried out loudly towards the door.

She became more and more emotional and more painful as she spoke. Her eyes were crying red.

"I'm leaving right away. Don't be angry, Paige. I was wrong."

Philip's voice came from outside the door. Despite his sincere apology, Paige could no longer forgive him.

I held Paige. Seeing her so upset, I couldn't help but cry too.

At that moment, I suddenly received a message on my phone. I picked up the phone and, after seeing the message sent by Giselle, I felt despair...
