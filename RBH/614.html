"Remember what you said." Elliott looked cold.

He turned around and walked away, hurried.

After Elliott left, Quinn put his arm around my shoulder and whispered, "You provoke him so cruelly? I'm afraid he can't stand it."

"You underestimated his powers of endurance."

Feeling a sharp pain in my heart, I tried to make my tone sound relaxed.

A woman could be deceived by a man, and she could also be the cruelest person in the world for the sake of her beloved child.

I wished Elliott was disappointed in me. That way, he would stay away from my child.

"Alright. No matter what you want to do, I'll help you," Quinn said in a deep voice.

He pinched my shoulder as if he were giving me more strength.

Only he and I knew it was only mutual support between friends.

Unexpectedly, Elliott suddenly turned around and glanced at Quinn and me.

He mistakenly believed that Quinn was intimately embracing my shoulders.

At that moment, I saw his gaze turn even colder. He immediately turned his head, and soon, his tall figure disappeared at the end of the corridor.

"He misunderstood us, but it was what I wanted."

Quinn withdrew his hand and shrugged helplessly.

"You're right," I said in a low voice. "Forget it. Let's go spend some time with Paige."

I no longer thought about Elliott, shaking my head at Quinn and walking into the ward with him.

When I pushed open the door, the scene surprised both Quinn and me.

Philip stood guiltily next to Paige, while Paige angrily turned her head aside, refusing to look at Philip.

Quinn walked over and kicked Philip.

"What are you doing? Are you begging for Paige's forgiveness?"

"Yes. If Paige doesn't forgive me, I will just stand here."

Philip seemed to be under a spell. He would rather stand there forever than break up with Paige.

Paige snorted and said, "Philip, don't pretend to be poor. I had a miscarriage, and I won't make up with you. I was foolish before, deceived by you into bed. I won't be a fool forever. Just go away."

"Honey, I know I was wrong. Give me a chance to make it up to you. I can do anything for you. I promise I won't do such a thing again. Trust me one last time."

Philip walked to the other side of the sickbed. However, Paige turned her head away, unwilling to make eye contact with him.

"Philip, seeing you, I'll feel myself stupid. Put away your hypocrisy and leave quickly. Don't bother me. I want a rest."

With that, Paige covered her ears, closed her eyes, and refused to talk with Philip.

Philip would like to say more, yet Quinn pulled him out and closed the door to the ward.

Paige covered her head with a blanket.
