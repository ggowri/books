"My dad was in a car accident and just came out of the emergency room."

I tried to adjust my mood and said into the phone.

Elliott's voice immediately became serious.

"Which hospital is Austin in now? Don't worry. I'll be here right away. "

Elliott didn't show indifference but was concerned. So, I immediately told him the hospital I was in.

About twenty minutes passed, and Elliott came in a hurry.

He was obviously at work just now. He was now followed by his assistant Barry. Wearing a gray suit, he looked dominating. However, he was probably in a hurry, as there was sweat on his forehead.

As soon as he saw me, he immediately stepped forward and held my shoulders.

"How is Austin now? Is he okay? "

"The operation was successful, but he is still in the intensive care unit. The doctor said he is still in a coma."

He now looked worried, different from being indifferent when he was in the Bryant hospital.

For a moment, I was a little confused.

He immediately looked around. Seeing Lily crying in sadness, he comforted her with a few more words.

Quinn, Fiona and Paige were all still in place. Quinn saw this, patted his shoulder, and said.

"It's okay. Fortunately, the operation was successful. Your father should be able to wake up soon according to the doctors' words."

Elliott looked up suddenly as if he just realized Quinn was also there. He looked at Quinn in disbelief, immediately frowned, and asked,

"Why are you here?"

"I..."

Quinn didn't know how to answer.

I quickly said subconsciously,

"It was me who called Quinn. I was afraid that my mother would be too emotional, so I asked him to pick her up and bring her to the hospital. "

I didn't realize there was anything wrong with that. However, Elliott's eyes widened even more when he heard this. He looked at me in disbelief,

"So, when something happens to your dad, you give Quinn a call instead of me? "

"I..."

I was speechless and didn't know how to answer.

I remember he asked me to leave indifferently in the Bryant hospital.

Moreover, Cormac was also in danger. If I called him rashly, I didn't know if he will be angry.

The atmosphere at the scene suddenly became awkward.

Fortunately, at this time, Fiona interjected.

"This is normal. You are so busy and not as idle as Quinn. Madeline was worried about disturbing you, so she contacted Quinn."

Fiona's words did not let Elliott's expression soften. He still looked at me with a frown and then pulled me aside without saying a word.

"Did Quinn mean more in your heart than me? When something happened to Austin, you should think of me first, right? "

He obviously couldn't accept it.

"Cormac was seriously ill, so I am afraid that you will be too busy. Besides, I was not sure if you were with your family. If my phone call irritates your family again, I will feel guilty. "

I raised my head to meet his eyes, and calmly explained myself.

"This is not an acceptable reason. It means that subconsciously, you trust Quinn more than me."
