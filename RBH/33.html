I was very confused, and at that moment, he had already taken the towel out of my mouth. Afterward, he sat down and helped me up from the bed. Before I could say anything, he quickly checked my body and, after confirming that I was not injured, he breathed a sigh of relief.

Afterward, he considerately pulled a towel and covered it over my chest.

"Are you not injured? Fortunately, fortunately, we arrived just in time. It's really fortunate!"

Compared to my previous life, Quinn has truly changed completely.

At this moment, his words of concern and caring gaze seemed so genuine, to the extent that I could feel his body slightly trembling due to excessive nervousness about my safety.

"I was fine, how come you appeared here?"

I weakly said, my head still feeling a bit dizzy. The heat inside me made me unable to resist reaching out for Quinn's arm, and my gaze became somewhat hazy.

He was startled and quickly realized that something was not right with me.

"Did they drug you? Come on, I'll take you to the hospital! Are you wondering why I'm here? Last night, Paige and I were drinking together, and she told me about it."

Quinn explained while quickly grabbing me and heading out.

"You guys keep an eye on these two individuals! You must make them confess the mastermind behind the scenes. I won't spare them!"

Quinn turned his head and shouted angrily at the several bodyguards in the room.

"What happened to Madeline? Is she okay? Did anything happen to her?"

Paige's voice came from the hallway at this moment, she was out of breath and it was obvious that she had been running all the way.

"Fortunately, I arrived just in time. If I had been a step later, something bad would have happened! Let's not talk about it now, let's take her to the hospital first, she's not feeling well!"

Quinn hurriedly said to Paige.

"Okay, Fiona was already at the hospital, let's hurry!"

At this moment, the effects of the medicine inside me had fully kicked in. I was extremely uncomfortable, and my body was convulsing uncontrollably. I couldn't restrain myself and desperately squeezed into Quinn's embrace, unable to resist pulling at his clothes.

I, who was uncontrollable, was difficult to control.

Quinn held onto me tightly, quickly carrying me into the elevator, stuffing me into his car, and together with Paige, rushed me to the hospital.
