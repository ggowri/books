Holding the plaster statue for a while, Elliott seemed to remember something.

Elliott said, "One year, my father deliberately made me live in the village, so I stayed at your house and accompanied you and Toby to the fields with your mother. We were playing and fooling around in the fields, and ended up covered in mud. Later on, I made this trinket as a gift for you. Right?"

Elliott suddenly recalled the joyful childhood he had spent with Giselle.

Giselle nodded with a bright smile and shining eyes.

"Yes! After that, I never saw you again. But I have always cherished this ornament. Mr. Bryant, can I still call you Elliott?"

"Of course! If you were free, I could take you to my house for dinner. My parents would be very happy to see you."

Elliott smiled.

Watching Elliott and Giselle sincerely lock eyes, I even felt happy for them.

I finally understood why Elliott had always loved Giselle.

Giselle and Elliott used to grow up together and had such wonderful memories.

"I haven't seen your parents for a long time either! I was able to go to college and pursue my interests thanks to the help of your family. My mom has always hoped for a chance to visit your home too!"

Giselle's words were sincere and genuine. However, as a fellow girl, I could keenly perceive her undeniable admiration for Elliott.

Since Giselle has treasured this little trinket for so many years, perhaps for her, Elliott was the star she couldn't have. And Rowan was an ordinary boyfriend she reluctantly chose.

As long as Elliott showed even a hint of appreciation or sympathy towards her, I believe Giselle would go to great lengths to get closer to Elliott.

Unfortunately, they met a little late. After all, I had already married Elliott.

I couldn't let them reminisce so intimately anymore! If sparks were to fly between them, it would be difficult to put out the fire.

I immediately walked up and shook Elliott's hand.

I smiled and said, "Giselle, I didn't realize you spent your childhood with my husband. Let us know if you need our help in the future."

I declared my sovereignty. Giselle instantly became sober and nodded.

Giselle withdrew her gaze and said, "Okay. Thank you, Mr. and Mrs. Bryant. Mr. Bryant's agreement to collaborate with our family's flower shop has already been a great help to me."

"Don't mention it."

Elliott noticed that Giselle became reserved.

Elliott said, "No need to be polite."

I agreed with Elliott's words, but in my mind, I cursed, "If I don't stop you guys, this bitch will eventually throw me into the sea again. Damn it!"

I was very angry, but Giselle had already started giving flowers to Elliott, so I had to think of another way.

After Giselle left Elliott's office, I also left because I was not in a good mood.

I walked to the elevator and found Giselle standing there, waiting for the elevator. So, I entered the elevator with her.
