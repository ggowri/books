"Giselle, remember to go downstairs later and don't stay in the room for too long."

Nicole reminded her in a hurry while Giselle was quickly running upstairs.

Suddenly, only Elliott, Nicole, and I were at the dining table. Nicole looked at us and smiled awkwardly.

"I don't know what's wrong with Giselle. She suddenly had a stomachache. Just help yourself."

"Okay, we're eating now. Nicole, how about Giselle's relationship with her boyfriend now? "

I asked with a smile on purpose.

Hearing my question, Nicole was a bit embarrassed.

She said, "I don't know much about young people. Giselle said they had broken up, but she didn't do anything with him. I was very strict with her in this regard. I told her that girls must not get too close to men casually."

Nicole said it in a very detailed way, as if she was trying to clarify something.

I glanced at Elliott, and he seemed cold without any reaction.

Then he said, "I heard that his family didn't want to buy a house at Hoswich, so GiselleI felt insecure. Nicole, what do you think of the house I talked about last time?"

Elliott mentioned the house again. Nicole quickly responded, "Of course that house is good. It's even too good for my family, but..."

She was too embarrassed to continue talking, but Elliott had already understood what she wanted to express.

He said directly, "You don't have to worry about money. If you're satisfied with that house, I'll deal with the other things."

"How much does that house cost?"

His generosity surprised me, and I asked immediately.

"Its current market price is about 5 million dollars," Elliott said in a deep voice.

Five million dollars! I couldn't believe that he would agree to buy it for them. His generosity was the same as that of his in my previous life.

I doubted whether Elliott was not interested in Giselle at all.

Suddenly, I wasn't very sure about this.

As I became a little anxious, I put down my fork and blurted out, "Why do you want to buy them such an expensive house? What if I disagree?"

As soon as I said this, Elliott darkened his face.

He probably didn't expect that I would object. After all, we had always kept our finances separate.

I had never had the right to interfere with what he wanted to do or buy, and I was not qualified to raise objections.

"Giselle saved me before and saved Cormac this time. What's more, her father lost his life because of saving my father. Madeline, I have made the decision to buy them a house."

In front of Nicole, Elliott's tone was very firm. It could be seen that he had made up his mind to buy this house.

I looked into his calm eyes and felt extremely sad at that moment.

In this life, I wouldn't have any objection if he gave a house or anything else to anyone.

However, he insisted on giving it to Giselle, the woman who had ruined my marriage and hurt me deeply in my previous life.

I couldn't accept the fact that Elliott treated her differently.

I was unable to continue to eat. I stared at Elliott and felt very uncomfortable.

I asked in a calm voice, "So you mean I have no right to interfere in your decision, right?"

He probably felt my sadness. His lips moved as if he wanted to say something, but he didn't speak out.

He just reached over, grabbed my wrist, and whispered, "If you have anything to say, can we talk about it when we go back?"

He didn't lose his temper in public because of my objection, which was a huge change for him.

But with a grudge against Giselle, I couldn't help but push his hand away. I turned my head and was overwhelmed by my grievance.
