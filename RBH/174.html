The phone rang once but was not answered.

Could it be that if I call them now, I will disturb their good time?

Elliott would fight with Giselle's boyfriend for her, which goes to show that Giselle is still quite important to him, right?

I underestimated Giselle after all. It turns out that even though I set up obstacles time and time again, even though I took away her script in advance, she still managed to become Elliott's one and only dream lover.

And this, is something I can never reach.

Love is truly something that makes people feel uncomfortable.

I dialed Elliott's phone number once again, and this time, Elliott finally answered the call.

Elliott's side sounded very quiet, like being in a closed compartment. However, Elliott's voice was a bit tense and accompanied by a slight panting sound.

"Hello, I was busy just now and didn't hear the phone ring."

The quiet atmosphere, the faint breathing sound, and the ambiguous phrase "just busy", instantly painted a picture in my mind that only adults understand.

My heart felt like it was suddenly lifted up, a wave of powerless dull pain spreading from my heart to my whole body.

"Oh, what were you busy with? Didn't you say you would be back in a while?"

"The company just had a meeting, and there happened to be some urgent matters. You rest first, I will be there in a while."

Elliott's voice still carried a slight panting, and I didn't know what he was doing or why he seemed a bit struggling.

The astonishing imagination made my brain confused, and just at this moment, what made me suffocated even more was that I faintly heard a woman's weak and feeble voice.

"Elliott..."

The sound was very weak, very weak, but on the other end of the phone, it was so quiet, so quiet that the sound seemed exceptionally clear.

I wanted to listen carefully again, however, at that moment, there came a burst of sound from the other end of the phone, as if two people had collided violently, the sound sometimes becoming noisy, sometimes very chaotic.

After about ten seconds, finally, the other end of the phone became quiet again. Surprisingly, there was a hint of nervousness in Elliott's tone.

"Sorry, there was a little situation just now, but I will be back soon. Don't worry."

Before I could say anything, Elliott had already hung up the phone in a hurry.

The busy tone "beep beep" came from the other end of the phone. I held the phone in my hand, which was shaking uncontrollably, and my palms, along with my back, were covered in sweat.

I checked the call time, it was only two minutes.

However, these two minutes were more agonizing for me than any other time.

A drunken and powerless woman, and a strong and powerful young man, both of them in the same compartment. Who would believe that nothing would happen?

Moreover, just now Elliott's tone, including his voice, sounded completely different from usual, and the sound emitted by Giselle, clearly sounded like...

I dared not continue to think further, for if I continued, I would not be able to resist getting up from the sickbed and going to Elliott's car to find out what was going on.

My heart still ached.

Although I kept telling myself over and over again that I no longer cared about Elliott, I was still, after all, his lawful wife.

I found that I simply couldn't accept how well Elliott treated Giselle, let alone how close he was to her.
