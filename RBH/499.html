I returned to my seat, and Dalton still sat next to me. Seeing me walking in alone, he asked me curiously, "Ms. Mayers, are you okay? Is the man just now your friend?"

I shook my head and said angrily, "No, he's not in his right mind."

After listening to my words, Dalton was shocked. However, he didn't say much and just smiled. Then, he cast his gaze on the stage again.

Not long after, I saw Audrey, an actor from my company, was invited to the stage by the host. She was awarded the year's Best New Talent Award.

Although it was her first time to win an award in the film and television industry, her performance was surprising. Wearing a simple white tube top dress and a simple bun, Audrey looked like a rose that had just bloomed.

Looking at her calm speech and decent performance, I couldn't help but smile. I believed that my investment in her was correct.

When I was glad for her, Elliott returned to his seat and sat down next to me again.

Instinctively, I felt a little heavy in my heart. In order to divert my attention, I talked to Dalton again.

Elliott didn't say anything. Throughout the second half of the ceremony, he sat there quietly, like a thousand-year-old iceberg, motionless.

Meanwhile, I had been talking to Dalton all the time. Yet, the massive pressure from the right side still made the right side of my body feel stiff.

The moment the ceremony came to an end, I couldn't wait to stand up and leave quickly from the other side.

Just as I walked out of the venue, Audrey suddenly called and stopped me. She came forward, lowered her head, and said, "Ms. Mayers, I have never found a suitable opportunity to thank you. Thank you for giving me the opportunity to act in Do Not Love."

Audrey stood in front of me and smiled sweetly.

Although I hired her as an actor in Glory Group, I had never been too close to her. I didn't want to put pressure on her. On the other hand, I also wanted to see if she could rely on her talents to perform in that complex acting field and stand firm in the circle.

Therefore, to her, I was her boss behind the scenes. She had always kept a distance from me and rarely came to me to say anything.

It was the first time she expressed her gratitude to me in person.

I smiled and patted her shoulder, then said gladfully, "You're welcome. There will be many more opportunities like this in the future. I hope you will continue to work hard and perform well."

"Ms. Mayers, thanks for the opportunity again. Don't worry, I will definitely continue to work hard!" Audrey nodded and expressed her gratitude to me again. From her bright and dazzling eyes, I could feel that she was really grateful to me from the bottom of her heart.

Only if a person knew how to be grateful could she go further.

I believed she had a promising future, but I didn't say much. After chatting with her for a few while, I turned and walked toward the entrance of the venue.

Outside the venue, the wind was biting cold. I instinctively wrapped my shawl tightly around myself. Just as I was about to get into the car, someone suddenly called my name from behind.

"Madeline."

That sharp voice sounded so familiar that I subconsciously stopped and turned to look over.
