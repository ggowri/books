Is someone tailing us?

So, this car is heading in the same direction as us, isn't it?

I came up with a plan. Spotting a rest area up ahead, I steered the car into it, parking neatly in one of the available spots.

"Adam, let's get out of the car."

I nudged the sleeping Adam, signaling him to get out of the car with me.

Ever since that incident in Norch City, I've been on high alert every time I return to this place, constantly wary of any potential mishaps.

After all, even though Jade and her crew were reined in by the military last time, there might still be others keeping a close eye on me.

Adam caught my wary look and instantly realized something was off. Without missing a beat, he joined me in exiting the car.

No sooner had we stepped out of the car, than I spotted the black sedan trailing behind us into the service area. What's more, it smoothly slid into a vacant spot not far behind us.

Adam caught me staring at the car, he asked me with a puzzled look:

"Madeline, what's up? Something going on?"

"Hmm, that car has been tailing us for a while now. Let's hang back here and see. I'm curious about who exactly is in that vehicle."

I whispered to Adam.

Adam gave a nod. He pulled out a cigarette from his pocket and lit it up, then he started to saunter, deliberately heading towards the direction of the black sedan.

He was a smart one. He feigned a simple trip to the corner store for a bottle of water. But in reality, as he passed the front of that car, he made it a point to sneak a peek inside.

I made a quick trip to the restroom and then I was back.

Adam was standing by the car, waiting for me. As soon as he saw me return, he promptly handed me a bottle of mineral water:

"Madeline, hop in first."

I grabbed the bottled water and hopped into the car, immediately questioning him, "Did you get a good look? Who's in there?"

Adam nodded, "There are three men in black suits sitting inside, all of them look pretty burly. But due to the tinted windows, I can't make out their faces."

Three burly men?

My heart instantly leaped into my throat. Instinctively, I dialed Quinn, briefing him on the situation and our current location.

Originally, I thought, just in case something happened to us, he could promptly get his friends in Norch City to lend a hand.

To my surprise, after hearing what I had to say, he just burst into laughter:

"Don't fret. Those three individuals were specifically assigned by me to covertly guard you. I didn't tell you because I feared you might think I'm overreacting."

"Quinn, why... why didn't you tell me sooner? You had me on pins and needles this whole time!"

At her words, I instantly felt a wave of relief wash over me. Simultaneously, I was deeply moved, my heart swelling with emotion.

Quinn, he's truly wonderful. He always puts me first, silently giving his all for me.

We're hitting the road soon.

At dusk, we rolled up to the base where Marcus and Jane were stationed.

They've leased a large plot of land at the foot of a mountain in Edulet City, using it as a base for their medicinal herb cultivation. They've also specifically built a quaint three-story white building to serve as their company's office and a place to hang their hats.

The little white building housed numerous rooms. By the time we arrived, they had already prepared two rooms for our stay. The interior decor was identical to that of a typical budget hotel, impeccably clean.

Marcus and Jane have been here for a while now. It's clear to see, they've developed quite the seamless partnership.
