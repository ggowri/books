I raised my head, looked him in the eyes, and said, "I want to divorce you."

"Divorce..." Elliott's tone suddenly became excited and urgent. "Why can you say this word so easily? Does it mean that I am not important to you?"

We were so close that I could feel his heart beating rapidly. I couldn't see the expression on his face clearly, but I could feel the pain in his voice when he said these words.

"I said, I once loved you. But now, I want to let you go."

I said slowly, without any emotion in my tone.

In the last life, we seemed to be getting divorced by this time.

However, the difference was that the person who begged not to divorce was him now.

"Let me go?" Elliott sneered. He held my chin tightly, which made me feel extremely painful. "Don't you care about all the happy times we spent together? Madeline, I do not believe!"

He still seemed to have great confidence in himself. I didn't find it strange. After all, I had been very humble in this relationship and even made him mistakenly think that I would die if I left him.

Men always had inexplicable confidence in the women who once loved them.

"If you don't believe me, just wait and see. Elliott, I'm tired. I only want to divorce you now, the sooner the better."

It was like a beautiful dream was completely shattered. I faced Elliott calmly and hoped that he could let me go.

Elliott was getting angrier. He stared at me and suddenly said grimly,

"You won my body and heart step by step, but now you want me to leave by just saying one sentence?"

I held my breath. To be honest, after I stopped loving him, I was afraid that he would pester me. What's more, I didn't have that much time to waste on him now.

I subconsciously thought of my child. If they noticed that I was pregnant, it might be difficult for me to get a divorce.

According to Elliott's suspicious personality, he would find out something, and by then, everything would only become more complicated.

I tried to regulate my breathing and calm down.

"Elliott, let's face it, okay? You should accept Giselle's child and let me go. You also know that even if we continue our marriage, there will not be a happy ending between us."

"How is it possible?" Unexpectedly, Elliott insisted and said, "You should know how happy we were when we only cared about each other."

"We only cared about each other back then!" I became angry and said, "Giselle's baby will be born soon. How can we only focus on each other with that child around?"

I was really tired. I thought there was no point in arguing over such trivial matters with Elliott.

After I yelled, before he could say anything else, I opened the door and prepared to leave.

I opened the door, but I didn't expect that Quinn had been standing at the door all the time.

Because of my behavior, he lost his balance, and we bumped into each other. We were all taken aback.

"You..."

I gave him a surprised look and wanted to ask him why he was eavesdropping outside the door, but I held back.

Quinn stood firm, but his hands were still on my waist. Elliott saw this and flushed with anger.

He suddenly said, "Madeline, we should tell Elliott the truth."

Truth?

What did he mean?

I was confused and raised my head to glance at him. At that moment, his sly look made me seem to understand what he was going to say. I was suddenly shocked.
