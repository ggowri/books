At that moment, he showed a nervous expression. He immediately walked over quickly and picked up his phone from the table, then swiftly opened the message.

Later, I noticed that his mood seemed to improve. He quickly tapped on the screen and replied to something.

I thought to myself, "So, even a message from Giselle could make him so nervous?"

"Perhaps only Giselle could make him care so much,"

As I looked at the pure white moonlight, I felt even more sorrowful.

I stood up from the chair and pretended to yawn, saying tiredly, "I am a bit tired, I want to go back and sleep. Or, would you like to sit for a while longer?"

"No, let's go downstairs together."

He put away his phone and placed it in his pocket, then he said to me in a casual tone.

I yawned again, watching as he put his phone into his pocket. At that moment, I didn't know why I said inexplicably, "I heard that Giselle already had a boyfriend."

I didn't know why I said this sentence, maybe I just wanted to see how Elliott would react, after all, he was such a possessive person.

"I knew. But they weren't a good match, that man wasn't suitable for her."

To my surprise, he immediately responded to me. From his words, I could tell that he had already gained enough understanding of Giselle's life, and I could even faintly sense the confidence in his speech.

His performance left me speechless.

"How do you know that they were not suitable? Are you suitable, then?"

I didn't mean to say it like this, but I couldn't help but ask.

Elliott seemed to only realize the irony in my words at that moment. He suddenly laughed and flicked my forehead with his hand.

"Are you serious? Are you jealous of a little girl?"

I thought to myself, "Little girl?"

Elliott, she was the person you loved the most.

Do you know that in the near future, you will fall uncontrollably in love with him?

I smiled at him and continued, "I wasn't jealous now. Even if you started dating her tomorrow, I wouldn't care."

I tried my best to show that I didn't care. But when Elliott heard my words, the smile on his face immediately disappeared.

"I was not a playboy, I was not liked by every woman."

I thought to myself, "I hope you can remember the words you said tonight."

I couldn't help but mutter in my heart, but I said, "Hmm, I hope not."

"Alright, stop arguing with me. Let's go, we're going downstairs!"

He reached out and grabbed my shoulder, wanting to take me downstairs.

And I quickly bent over, bypassing his hand and agilely slipping behind him. He glared at me angrily, and I smiled, explaining, "This staircase is too narrow, we really can't walk side by side."

He knew I was making excuses, so he coolly put his hands in his pockets and went downstairs first.

I followed him downstairs, but he unexpectedly went into the master bedroom again. Moreover, as soon as he entered the room, he directly took off his shirt and the white T-shirt inside.
