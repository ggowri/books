I don't know how long it took, but the driver brought us to the hospital.

Elliott asked the driver to bring us here, not a public hospital, but a private clinic.

Elliott assisted me out of the car, and as soon as my aching right foot touched the ground, it immediately hurt so much that I couldn't help but let out a gasp.

"Come up here, I'll carry you."

After Elliott paid the driver, he squatted directly in front of me, carried me on his back, and walked resolutely in the direction of the clinic.

At the entrance of the clinic, a young male doctor with a refined appearance, a white lab coat, and gold-framed glasses had long been standing at the door waiting.

As soon as he saw us appear, the male doctor's face immediately showed a friendly smile, and he came forward and quickly talked to Elliott in Korean.

My Korean is very poor, so I could barely make out that they were exchanging pleasantries and greetings, and that they seemed to know each other from the past.

Afterwards, the male doctor and Elliott helped me into his office.

The male doctor greeted me in English:

"Hello, Mrs. Bryant, I'm Elliott's college alumni, my name is Mike Arnold, you can also call me Mike."

He addressed me directly as "Mrs. Bryant", obviously thinking of me as Elliott's wife.

I was a little embarrassed, and subconsciously glanced at Elliott, who smiled with his eyebrows and didn't make any special explanation.

Moreover, looking at him, he seemed to enjoy this feeling of being misunderstood.

So, I had to shake his hand as well:

"Hello, my name is Madeline. please do my checkup, I have a little fever, and then the soles of my feet hurt a lot."

Mike quickly talked to me in English.

He first asked me about the cause of my illness, when I started to have fever, and then what kind of food I had eaten in the past few days. After that, he took my temperature and prescribed a fever-reducing medicine for me to take first.

After that, he gave me a urine test to see if the gout in my feet was caused by high uric acid.

After I took the fever-reducing medicine, my body soon sweated and my temperature slowly came down.

Only, my whole body was weak, plus my feet hurt so much that I had no strength to walk at all.

Elliott became my wheelchair and carried me on his back, ignoring the stares of the public, and accompanied me to do all kinds of checkups.

I was lying on his back, smelling his familiar scent in a daze, and although my body was in pain, I felt a peace of mind that I had never experienced before.

In the past, how could I have dared to think that one day I would be able to let Elliott do this for me without complaint?

But now, this scene was truly happening in my life.

When he took my bottle with the urine sample from my hand without hesitation and sent it to the place for examination without the slightest bit of disdain, I couldn't help but feel a hint of sweetness like cotton candy swirling in my heart as I sat on the bench and watched his back.

He really has changed a lot.

Before, he was a deity to me, someone I needed to look up to and admire.

But now, he made me so truly feel his thoughtfulness and love for me.

After he finished delivering the sample, he went to the restroom and washed his hands, after which he returned to my side, holding my hand and accompanying me as I quietly waited for the results of the lab tests.

"Are you still having a hard time? Do you want some water?"

I leaned helplessly against his shoulder, and when he saw that I hadn't spoken for a long time, he asked me softly.

"I'm a little better, it's just the soles of my feet that still hurt."

I said softly.

"Bear with it a little longer, the lab results will be out in a little while, if it's confirmed that it's foot gout, take the medication for gout and you'll see results soon."

Elliott softly reassured me.

"Elliott, I feel ...... you are completely different from before."

I murmured in a low voice, but I felt his body visibly shake slightly, and then I heard a soft chuckle from him:

"That's because, before, you didn't actually know me. I'm not as powerful as you think in your diary, in fact, I'm just an ordinary man, when I used to go to school abroad, I used to cook for myself, clean for myself, and do everything by myself."

I had never heard him mention any of this in the past.

Once, in my imagination, he should always have that glamorous image, wherever he went, he was embraced by many people, and there were many things that didn't even need him to do, there would naturally be people to help.

"Is that so? I've never heard you talk about it. According to your character, even if you went abroad to study, shouldn't you have hired a houseful of servants to help take care of things?"

I couldn't help but smile because I knew that at the time he told me this, it meant that he had completely unloaded his defenses on me and was willing to show the truest version of him to me.

"Not really, I've been very independent since I was a kid, and I do a lot of things on my own. If you don't believe me, you can ask Mike later, I used to study abroad and he lived two doors down."

Elliott smiled back, he couldn't help but hold my hand a little tighter.

"Looks like you're finally willing to bring me into your circle of friends?"

It was then that I finally responded to why he hadn't chosen to bring me to the hospital, but to this clinic.

That's because, Mike is not only the owner of this clinic, but also, a former friend of his, and he probably wanted to use this opportunity to introduce us.

"Didn't you always used to be curious about my life, wanting to enter my circle and know everything about me? Now, I can open up to you as much as I want, no matter which part of my life you want to know about me once. As long as, you still have this heart for me ......."

Elliott suddenly cupped my chin as he looked at me with sincerity and tenderness in his eyes.

He was now truly willing to open his heart to me on all fronts ...... I was slightly stunned, and my heart felt like it had been scratched by a cat's claw.

"Elliott, you don't really have to be like this. I ...... haven't thought about getting back together, and I think that right now, it's fine."

I subconsciously panicked a little, and I hastily avoided his gaze, trying to bring that inner part of me back to its senses.

"I know what you think," he held my shoulders and picked my chin up again, forcing me to look at him, "and I'm not asking right now to say that you have to get back together with me, I'm just asking you to give me another chance. I'm willing to be unreserved to you, to be the man your heart once truly longed for me to be, and you, all along, have had the choice to want me or not want me."

It's amazing how he's suddenly so open-minded.

Instead of trying to desperately push me or use a ruse on me like before, he started to actually think about me, not centering himself anymore.

I was stunned, I never thought that Elliott would be so open with me.

My lips trembled as I stared at Elliott in disbelief, "You ......"

I didn't know what to say.

Elliott, however, seemed like he knew exactly how I felt, he slowly came closer and a tender kiss gently landed on my forehead.

His voice was low, and his hand gently stroked the hair on the back of my head, "You don't need to think about anything more, you just need to quietly experience and feel my love, okay?"
