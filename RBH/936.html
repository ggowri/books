What I didn't expect was that Quinn didn't make a move, he walked over and patted Elliott's shoulder.

His tone was calm:

"Elliott, I'm here."

Elliott turned and looked at Quinn with a lowered brow, "I heard you.Quinn, what do you want to talk about?"

Elliott's tone was equally calm.

It was just that there seemed to be an infinite tension in the calmness of the two men, a sense of oppression.

"Is this where we're going to talk?"

Quinn looked around the room, a hint of mocking laughter in his tone.

After all, having a room is somewhat of an ambiguous thing in anyone's eyes, and what's more, he must have felt that Elliott had taken me to this room for a purpose.

"It's quiet in here, have a seat.Madeline, you sit too."

Elliott took a deep breath as he stubbed out the cigarette directly in his hand, then he gestured towards the couch not far away, signaling for us all to sit down.

"Good."

I nodded, taking the lead and sitting over.

Quinn and Elliott both sat down in succession.

Quinn sat across from me, and the moment he sat down, I sensed that he seemed to have lost a lot of weight lately, and his whole body looked tired.

"Quinn, haven't you eaten anything yet? There's still bread here, you eat first, and I'll accompany you out for breakfast when it gets light."

I panicked and asked with concern.

"It's okay, I haven't had an appetite lately," Quinn politely declined, "Madeline, you can start by saying your innermost thoughts. I also know that this stalemate between the three of us is not the way to solve the problem."

Quinn's eyes kept falling on me, and I could sense that he seemed to have a lot to say inside.

"That's what I'm feeling too, so I wanted to talk to all of you. I think it's good to be alone like this at the moment, and I really don't want to waste any more of my energy on relationships, and I don't want you guys to continue to live around me like this anymore, and I don't have to take on any of the burdens that come from you guys, so isn't it good that it's easy for everyone?"

I took a deep breath and spoke honestly about what I was really thinking.

I admitted that my scales had tipped in favor of someone, but if making a choice meant hurting the other side, then at this stage, I'd rather not make any choices.

"I don't feel any burden, everything is of my own free will.Madeline, in fact, if there is no Quinn, you will definitely choose to get back together with me, I can feel that you are really happy with me these days. You should stop lying to yourself, why don't we make it clear with Quinn together, how about it?"

Elliott looked at me sharply, his eyes were steely as a knife and seemed to go right through my flesh and into my heart.

He always talks like this, his tone is not high, but it carries a powerful aura that pierces through all of you in an instant.

My heart thumped, obviously my reason desperately wanted to deny these words he said, but my heart was like being sucked in by that heart of his, simply uncontrollably wanting to go to agree with his statement ......

I had to admit that these days together, all the thoughtfulness he had shown me did make me feel happy.

I also have to admit that being close to him, my heart is easily stirred by him.

I can't deny that what he has done for me, and this gesture of his nowadays, does make me feel that he is really completely different from before, and makes me feel the respect as well as the importance that he has for me.

It's just that alone, it's not even close to getting back together.

I was silent, and in Quinn's eyes, my silence was undoubtedly tantamount to acquiescence to what Elliott had just said.

With a hidden pain in his eyes, he looked over at me:

"It seems that you have made your choice. In fact, the moment I heard Elliott's voice on the phone, I already knew the answer. I also thought about it a lot on the way here.Madeline, I just need you to tell me what you think now.If you really want to get back together with him, I ...... am still willing to be your friend again and wish you all the best."

As Quinn finished those words, he subconsciously covered his chest.

It was obvious to him that it took courage for him to say those words.

I was heartbroken to hear it, and I subconsciously reached out and pressed my hand to his shoulder, my tone choking up for a moment:

"But Quinn you've done so much for me, I really ......"

"And that's what I did voluntarily. You don't ask for something in return for something you do voluntarily. What's more, if your feelings for me are just out of touch or wanting to return the favor, that's not what I want either."

Quinn shook his head as he looked at me and forced a small smile.

I opened my mouth in some surprise, and for a moment I didn't know what to say.

Turns out, he actually knew it all in his heart.

He knew that I had never been attracted to him, and he also knew that all my feelings for him were because of touching.

Without Elliott as a comparison, I originally could not tell the difference between being touched and love.

But nowadays, I could clearly feel that love could really make one's heart stir, while being touched could not at all.

"Quinn, I know you've done a lot for Madeline, and I thank you for being there for her during the time I brought Madeline pain. It's just that we both know that the person she likes is me."

Elliott said slowly at that point.

"Who said the person I like is you Elliott, you ......"

I didn't expect him to put it so bluntly, and for a split second, I scrambled to argue.

However, before I could finish my sentence, he directly interrupted me, and those sharp eyes looked like they pierced through me:

"Madeline, stop denying it and bravely face your heart, okay?"

Elliott's words directly shook me into silence as soon as they came out.

Because, I had to admit, what he said was true ......

"Then I have nothing to say, feelings are meant to be by the heart.Elliott, I can quit, I will never bother Madeline's life in the future, but you must promise me that you will treat Madeline better than I treat her."

Quinn said slowly looking at Elliott.

I looked at Quinn in a daze and my eyes reddened the moment I heard him say he was quitting.

I knew that he was the person in the world who wanted me to live a happy life the most besides my parents.

However, such a nice person, I don't know why I can't get my heart to move on to him and hurt him over and over again.

Though he seemed calm, I could feel the waves inside him by the hand he repeatedly covered his chest with.

"I promise," Elliott sighed in relief as he tightened his grip on my hand in front of Quinn, "Thank you for making us whole, I will give Madeline the happiness she desires. And you don't have to quit, in fact, I would prefer you to be Madeline's brother, to be my brother. Love isn't the only thing that can guard her, affection can too, can't it?"
