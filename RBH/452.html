Laura, who was sitting beside Giselle, made her hair in a vintage style and was wearing a dark red dress, precious neckline, ring, and bracelet with diamonds.

Obviously, Laura specially brought Giselle here, otherwise, she would not have been able to get a ticket for this annual banquet with her identity.

I didn't expect that before I officially divorced Elliott, Laura had already taken Giselle to attend the annual banquet.

Seeing this, I deliberately took a few steps closer, standing behind the pillars to listen to their conversation.

"Mrs. Laura Bryant, you haven't attended the annual banquet for several years, right? I feel like I haven't seen you for a long time."

"Yes, since Bryant Group was handed over to my son, Jasper and I have retired and enjoyed life. We happened to be in Hoswich recently, so we wanted to have some fun here and meet you."

"Laura, who is this girl next to you? I don't think I've seen her before. "

"Oh, this is Elliott's wife Giselle. Giselle, let me introduce to you, this is Ms. London from Miracle Group, this is Ms. Lee from Jason Group and this..."

It seemed that Laura wanted to bring Giselle into the circle of noble ladies, so she was introduced the background of each noble lady enthusiastically.

Although Elliott and I had been married for more than three years, except for our wedding day, Elliott rarely took me to such occasions, and I had little interaction with these rich ladies.

Therefore, they were not familiar with me. Laura's introduction easily made them mistake Giselle as Elliott's wife.

I stood there and curled my lips.

Laura especially attended this annual banquet tonight to prepare for the child in Giselle's belly.

She took Giselle to attend such an occasion so that she could announce the child's identity in the future.

They met Giselle first, and because they were unfamiliar with me, they must have thought that Giselle was Elliott's wife. In this way, if Elliott and I divorced in the future, Giselle would naturally become a member of the Bryant family.

Laura made a great plan, but it was a pity that I happened to be here tonight.

How could I endure the Bryant family silently replacing me with Giselle and erasing my existence?

"Laura, what a coincidence. You never attended this annual banquet before, why are you interested in it today?"

As Laura was about to let everyone focus on Giselle, I walked directly towards her, held Laura's hand affectionately in front of all the ladies, and said with a smile.

My sudden appearance made Laura stunned. The moment I held her arm, I felt like she was about to jump.

For a moment, she couldn't hide her surprised expression and exclaimed.

"Why are you here?"

"Laura, who is this lady? "

A wealthy lady in the crowd immediately raised a question. Everyone instantly focused on me and Giselle.

I saw Giselle, who was smiling happily just now, looked gloomy now.
