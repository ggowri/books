Because I knew, you know.

Because I had experienced everything bit by bit in my previous life.

Because I knew it even clearer than you, Elliott, I am not the type you like at all. I have seen how crazy you were in love with someone a long time ago.

My heart was filled with countless sorrows, but I couldn't say anything. After quickly putting on my clothes, I picked up my suitcase and turned around. I glanced at him and bid farewell calmly, "I'm leaving, Elliott."

He looked at me with a melancholic expression in his eyes. He sat up and reached out, attempting to grab my hand, but perhaps my prickly appearance, like that of a hedgehog, made him give up. In the end, he nodded.

It's strange, this is clearly my room, and all the items clearly belong to me.

But now, it seems like everything has been taken over by Elliott, I have no attachment at all, so I just opened the door and left.

However, as soon as I reached the staircase, I thought for a moment and decided to turn back and put the diary back in the drawer, locked it securely, and then closed the door to the room.

When I went downstairs, Lily was still arranging flowers in the living room.

Seeing me carrying luggage and Elliott not following, she was surprised and quickly approached me to ask, "Madeline, what are you doing? Where is Elliott?"

"Lily, he fell asleep. I need to hurry to Louis City. When Austin wakes up, can you help me talk to him?" I looked at Lily and said with a smile.

Lily stared deeply at my neck for a moment, and a meaningful smile immediately appeared on her face.

I suddenly realized that Elliott must have left another hickey on my neck. Embarrassment instantly spread across my cheeks, and without saying much to Lily, I immediately rushed out of the house, stepped on the gas pedal, and drove away.

I drove directly to the headquarters of Glory Group, intending to go to the office to retrieve some documents. By the way, I wanted to have a chat with Katrina, the financial director of Glory Group.

Katrina was the daughter of Austin's distant cousin, and back then, she was also nurtured and promoted by Austin from a low-level accountant.

Over the years, all the financial data and secrets of Glory Group were in her hands, and according to Austin, she was a very diligent person. Austin trusted her.

However, I distinctly remember in the previous life when Austin was kicked out by the entire board, various loopholes in Glory Group's accounts became one of his stains. If she was truly a trustworthy person, why were there so many loopholes in the accounts at that time?

In this life, I had to find a way to understand the accounting issues of Glory Group as soon as possible. Only by knowing oneself and the enemy, I could have enough leverage in dealing with Katrina and her father in the future.

I specially brought a box of handmade pastries made by Lily for Katrina. In my impression, this middle-aged sister in her forties loves to eat snacks.

Sure enough, Lily loved the pastries she made, and combined with the fact that she and Austin were distant relatives, I quickly became close to her.

"Katrina, what about Austin? He expressed an interest in letting me know more about our company's situation. I would like to start with the financial aspect. Could you please make a copy of the financial statements from the past year for me? I will study them when I have free time, as a form of learning. Is that possible?" Feeling that I had almost wrapped things up, I immediately asked directly about the main topic.

Katrina seemed somewhat surprised and hesitantly said, "But financial statements are highly confidential data, and without authorization from Austin and the other directors of the board, if I were to give them to you hastily, I would be held responsible if something goes wrong."

Her response was within my expectations, so I leaned in close to her ear and whispered, "Katrina, I heard that your daughter is about to start high school, but her grades are just a little below the best local high school. I know the principal, maybe I can put in a good word for you. Plus, think about it, I am Austin's only daughter, how could I casually leak financial reports to others? Since I dare to ask you for it, it naturally represents Austin's intention, don't you understand?"
