And Elliott, being the kind of person he was, would never bow down and grovel to a small copyright holder.

"Hello, I am honored to have the opportunity to invite you for dinner tonight. Please come, please have a seat."

"Mr. James, I am Madeline, Elliott's wife. Nice to meet you."

"Oh... So you are Mrs. Bryant. Nice to meet you. Please have a seat as well."

"Mr. James, this is my husband, Elliott. However, he was usually serious and not very talkative, so please don't mind."

Jacob appeared to be very reserved.

It was obvious that Sylvia kept calling him or sending him messages.

"Darling, why didn't you eat?"

"I used to eat less for dinner, you don't have to worry about me."

"He was like this, serious even at home, except for the rumors, he didn't seem like a young person in other aspects."

I deliberately teased Elliott.

Elliott's face immediately grew darker, and he warned me.

"Madeline!"
