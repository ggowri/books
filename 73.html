Among the uncut diamond, there was the top-quality diamond that Osborne needed.

It was not glass type, but Imperial Green.

Osborne was very excited. He sped up the car and returned to the Institute of Human Research.

After moving the uncut diamond back to the room, Osborne began cutting it. He didn’t have a cutting machine. So, he could only use an alloy dagger to peel off the diamond skin bit by bit.

Osborne was very strong. It only took him half an hour to peel off all the diamond skin. It revealed an Imperial Green diamond. The size was like a volleyball.

Brilliant green filled the entire room.

More than twenty pounds of top-grade Imperial Green diamond was worth at least tens of billions of dollars, or even hundreds of billions of dollars. But Osborne wanted to use it to enhance his supernatural power, Satan’s Eye.

At this moment, Osborne couldn’t help but feel a little distressed.

If he used this to enhance Satan’s Eye, the Imperial Green diamond would be ruined. If he used it to exchange for money, it would be enough for him to live a luxurious life for ten lifetimes in Mayby City. Would someone else be willing to use something worth tens of billions to increase the strength of his supernatural powers? In fact, Satan’s Eye could be improved by using cheaper types of diamonds.

Osborne was hesitant. But in the end, he made up his mind.
