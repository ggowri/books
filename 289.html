"Barlyn is right. The future husband I have chosen for Clarissa is Alexander. After learning about Clarissa's situation and seeing her photos, he is very satisfied with her and strongly demands that the wedding be held soon.

"Boy, do you think you're not a waste? Do you think you can compare to Alexander?"
