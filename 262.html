With Blake's shout, the surroundings erupted into chaos.

Many guests on the first floor, having heard the commotion from the second floor, followed Wesley up to check, including several heads of wealthy families and their heirs.

Upon hearing Blake's loud accusations, their expressions darkened.

"How dare you commit such despicable acts at Mr. Ashford's wedding."

"Mr. Ashford, do not let him off!"

"Today is your wedding. If you do not want to handle this, leave it to me. I guarantee I will make him disappear cleanly, so he would not harm anyone else."

Wesley waved his hand to signal everyone to be quiet.

Today was his wedding with Ophelia. To ensure her safety, aside from the security personnel at Rose Castle, only Ashford and Caldwell families' bodyguards were allowed to carry guns.

Other guests had to go through security checks and were prohibited from bringing guns inside.

With a dozen guns pointed at Osborne's head, Wesley did not believe Osborne could turn the situation around.

Even if he knew Osborne was powerful, he doubted he could withstand a dozen guns.

Furthermore, Wesley could call in more bodyguards at any time.

No matter how strong Osborne was, he was doomed today!

Wesley's face was smug as he looked disdainfully at Osborne. "Osborne, kneel down quickly! After causing such a scene, I will deal with you slowly after the wedding and give all the guests an explanation!"

Osborne's lips curled into a smile as he looked at Wesley calmly.

Even with a dozen guns pointed at his head, he showed no signs of panic.

"Wesley, quite a method."

Upon learning that Osborne had tried to assault and rape Ava, and after attacking Blake and getting caught in the act by Wesley, they were all enraged.

"Humph, such things happened in broad daylight, truly reckless!"

"Mr. Ashford, do not let him off!"

"Yes, he definitely should not be spared!"

"Osborne, you are such a disappointment!

"Humph, do not even think about making excuses!"
