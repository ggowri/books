"Tobey mentioned that many within Paramount Supreme Clan are plotting to completely wipe out the Human Community. This decision has already been tacitly approved by the esteemed Lord Malevolence.

West Zone was the last hope for Human Community. Various resistance groups had united there to hold back Lord Malevolence's Dark Deity Legion.

Isa, a disciple of the God of Night, was one of the leaders of Human Community resistance. She was definitely in West Zone.
