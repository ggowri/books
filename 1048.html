"Current physical strength is 199, almost breaking through 200!"

"Improvement speed: +3 per hour!"

"Pain level—18!"

"Enslavement rate: 92%!"

"Remaining energy in nutrient fluid—25%!"

"It looks like we're about to make it!"

A dozen white-clad doctors, their faces alight with excitement, eagerly relayed Osborne's latest status to Gerardo.

Over the span of three grueling days, Osborne's physical strength had skyrocketed from a mere 50 to an astonishing 199. The 17th level of pain had not shattered his will but had instead bolstered it, propelling his upgrade speed five hours ago from +2 per hour to +3.

17th level of pain turned into 18th, yet his consciousness was still holding.

"His sensory nerves must have gone numb."

The doctors were astounded.

"Hmph! An 18th level of pain doesn't just numb the sensory nerves; it strikes directly at the soul!" Gerardo retorted sharply, his heart churning with a mix of awe and frustration.

Here was Osborne, enduring pain at the 18th level for a solid five hours, showing no sign of a mental collapse.

"Nothing in the future could bring him down. He wouldn't even flinch if you crushed his heart," Gerardo marveled.

But what truly shocked him was that Osborne's physical strength was about to reach 200.

At 200, Osborne's level would surpass even the top-tier Class S warriors, who stand at 100. Osborne would be twice as strong as them. Even Gerardo himself only had 180.

Gerardo thought, "Terrifying!

But thankfully, the enslavement rate has finally reached 92%! In just ten more hours, his body will be fully enslaved.

He'll become my Human Slave then, my ultimate weapon. He will do whatever I told him to do."

Gerardo's face lit up with excitement at the prospect.

A Human Slave with a physical strength exceeding 200? This was unprecedented.

Osborne, currently at Class AAA, would see more physical strength once he broke into Class S.

A mere doubling would bring it to 400, and tripling it would land him at 600.

Gerardo's eyes gleamed with a mix of envy and admiration.

Gerardo mused, "Such prodigious talent. If it were mine, I'd have already been invincible. Even fused with a god's body, my physical strength tops out at 500.

And he could reach 600 just by breaking into Class S.

But this masterpiece, this marvel, is of my creation.

Without my Human Slave culture fluid, his strength would never have reached such heights. Such power is the price of enslavement."

Gerardo's pride swelled.

"Keep a close eye on him! Those final ten hours are crucial!"

With that, he exited the Human Slave incubation chamber.

Over last three days, to replenish the nutrient solution's energy, he had twice extracted the heart blood from gods’ body, even from his fused Goddess Medusa.

The extraction made him feel diminished.

"But the feeling won’t last. I'll regain my peak in no time. A brief period of weakness for a flawless Human Slave? It's a price worth paying."

Gerardo arrived at Ophelia's training chamber.

Strange, he thought, as he recalled how, over the past three days, Ophelia had seized every opportunity to pepper him with questions about her training. She showed an urgent desire to break through.

Despite his fatigue, Gerardo decided to offer her some guidance before resting.

After all, Ophelia was one of his prized disciples, and her growth was important for his future plans.

A menacing glint flickered in his eyes at the thought, which was quickly masked as he entered the rrom.

"Ophelia, I'm here to answer your questions."

Ophelia looked at Gerardo, a calculating gleam in her eyes.

"Mr. Aniston, what have you been so busy with lately? You seem exhausted. Are you sure you're alright? Maybe you should take a break?"

Gerardo waved her concern aside. "It's nothing serious, almost over now. Don't worry. I know my own limits. I'll bounce back in a few days."

"Oh?" Ophelia nodded and didn’t ask more.

She cast her gaze down, as if deep in thought, to hide the gleam in her eyes

Gerardo was vulnerable right now.

This moment she’s been waiting was now. Without hesitation, she struck.

"Freeze!"

In an instant, time around them ground to a halt. Everything—everyone—became motionless, including Gerardo.

With no delay, Ophelia darted forward.

"Three minutes!"
