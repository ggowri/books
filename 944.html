As Osborne unleashed the power of his right leg's Divine Bone, he sent the second Class S Titan King crashing to the ground. But at that moment, his Light Realm finally shattered under the relentless assault.

The intense drain on his mental strength forced him to spit out a mouthful of blood, his face twisting in pain and frustration.

His Light Realm had barely held up for ten seconds against the onslaught of several Class S clones.

It wasn't that his Level Four Light Realm was weak; it was just that Osborne's own level was too low.

At only Class AAA, he was far from being able to withstand such overwhelming power. If he had been Class S himself, even eighty Class S clones wouldn't have been able to break through in such a short time.

Osborne murmured, "Damn it!"

Medusa Queen's triumphant smirk faltered as she realized that Osborne had just taken down two Titan Kings. Anger and murderous intent flickered across her face.

They had sent ten Class S clones into battle, and in a matter of moments, Osborne had already dispatched five of them.

Now, only five remained.

Fortunately, Osborne had lost his Light Realm.

Even with just five of them left, they were confident they could finish him off.

"Kill him!" The five Class S clones surged forward in unison.

The two Medusa Queens' eyes ignited with power as they unleashed their petrification abilities on Osborne. Instantly, his body was engulfed in a thick layer of stone skin.

Osborne's expression shifted to one of alarm; he could feel his body seizing up.

One Medusa Queen's petrification power was manageable, only slowing him down a bit, but with both of them attacking together, he found himself completely immobilized.

The Tauren King seized the opportunity, charging at Osborne with reckless abandon.

Meanwhile, the two Wolf Kings unleashed a flurry of razor-sharp claws, surrounding him completely.

Danger loomed over him!

With his body trapped, Osborne could only tap into his spatial power, desperate to teleport away.

But just as a rift began to tear open, it was shattered by the Wolf Kings' claws.

A torrent of devastating attacks rained down on Osborne.

Despite his formidable defenses, he couldn't withstand the onslaught.

Osborne's body was riddled with countless wounds, and the brutal impact from the Tauren King left his chest caved in, bones splintering as he was sent flying.

Before he even hit the ground, hundreds of Class AA and Class AAA clones closed in, unleashing a relentless barrage of attacks that overwhelmed him entirely.

At that moment, Osborne's martial art aura crumbled, leaving him utterly defenseless.

"Light of Redemption!"

Without a moment's hesitation, Osborne activated his Light of Redemption, restoring himself to peak condition and summoning his martial art aura for protection.

Unfortunately, the hundreds of attacks crashed down once more, shattering his martial art aura again.

And just when he thought he might catch a break, the five Class S clones were already closing in.

Osborne's expression was grim; he was cornered, surrounded by an overwhelming number of foes.

The Aberrants had deployed two thousand clones, and while most were busy attacking Serena and the others, over a hundred were now focused on him alongside those five Class S clones.

In such a dire situation, Osborne couldn't hope to defend himself, let alone escape.

Serena and the others were in no position to come to his aid.

He was on his own.

Medusa Queen cackled, relishing the sight of Osborne's despair.

"Osborne, I'm giving you a chance. Surrender and swear your loyalty to the great King of the Aberrants. We can spare your life! You have potential, and as long as you're willing to be a loyal dog to the King, you can escape death!"

Osborne's face hardened even further at her words.

Fighting off the relentless onslaught, he retorted coldly, "Medusa Queen, you're a twisted creature. You're willing to be a dog for Gerardo—does that mean everyone else should be like you?

"If you'd just lick my boots, I might actually consider letting you live."

Medusa Queen's fury ignited at his words, her face twisting in rage.

"Since you can't see the writing on the wall, then you're better off dead! Guys, kill him!"

With that, the five Class S clones surged forward. Their attacks became even more frenzied with over a hundred Class AA and Class AAA clones' powers. Osborne felt like a tiny boat lost in a stormy sea, on the verge of capsizing.

His recently recovered body was soon covered in fresh wounds, his complexion growing paler by the second.

"I can't take much more of this!"

Osborne gritted his teeth.

He thought, "Is this how it ends? Am I really going to die here?

I haven't found my parents... I haven't rescued Grandpa.

There's so much left to do... I can't just die like this!"

In an explosive blast, Osborne was launched backward, blood spraying from his mouth, as if his very body was coming apart at the seams.

More attacks rained down on him, and once again, he was engulfed by the chaos.
