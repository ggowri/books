Satoru's plan to wear down Cuthbert failed.

However, thinking it over, it made sense.

Since Cuthbert had the confidence to host the Honor Quest Ceremony, he must have anticipated facing many challengers, each trying to exhaust him. Without a way to deal with such fatigue, he'd run out of energy after a few battles or so.

At that point, anyone could easily defeat him.

To achieve a streak of victories at the Honor Quest Ceremony, you didn't need overwhelming strength but strong recovery abilities and a way to deal with opponents swiftly.

Cuthbert's approach was sheer brute force!

His terrifying strength easily shattered Satoru's Dark Realm, giving him no chance to wear him down in a long fight.

Achieving this showed that Cuthbert's strength was far beyond Satoru's.

Only such immense power could crush the Dark Realm.

Osborne looked serious.

At least he had to admit, for now, he didn't have the strength to break Satoru's Dark Realm by force.

"How is his power so strong?"

Osborne muttered.

"His body is unique!"

Emma quickly responded, "When Cuthbert was born, strange phenomena occurred. The earth split open, and a divine artifact in the form of a pearl appeared and merged with his body.

"Since then, he's had strength far beyond ordinary people. He hit Class AAA at 28, and now at 36, he's at the peak of AAA. He's already approaching Class S.

"The Williamson family hosted the Honor Quest Ceremony, hoping the intense battles would put enough pressure on him to find the key to breaking into Class S.

"In short, his body is fused with a divine artifact, which is why his strength is so terrifying."

After Emma finished, Osborne raised an eyebrow.

"That explains it!"

In the Honor Quest Ceremony, they weren't allowed to use divine artifacts, only their own power. After all, the power of some artifacts is just too overwhelming. If they were allowed, there wouldn't be any point in hosting the event.

However, since Cuthbert's body had fused completely with a divine artifact, that power now counted as his own.

In a way, Cuthbert had an advantage.

It didn't mean Cuthbert could easily breeze through the Honor Quest Ceremony, as his situation wasn't unique.

Many people ranked on the Godson Rankings had also fused with divine artifacts.

Osborne, for example, was one of them.

"Fusing with a divine artifact, huh?"

Osborne smirked. His body was also fused with the Blood Cross, though the boost it gave him wasn't as dramatic. He could only double his power after transforming into a Vampire.

Compared to Cuthbert, it was still far less.

But that was because the Blood Cross hadn't fully awakened its powers yet.

Osborne believed that once the Blood Cross fully awakened, the benefits he'd gain wouldn't be any less than what Cuthbert had.

The fight continued!

After Satoru was defeated, Clay took the stage. He also activated his realm, hoping to wear Cuthbert down, but his fate was no different from Satoru's.

Cuthbert only used two moves.

One shattered Clay's Light Realm, and the second sent him flying off the stage.

The challenges continued.

Cuthbert's winning streak continued. Though his challengers were all skilled fighters hoping to crack the Godson Rankings, none could put up any resistance against him.

Two punches!

No matter who stepped onto the stage, they were always defeated in two punches, no exceptions.

At this point, the challengers weren't even thinking of exhausting him anymore. They just wanted to force him to use his Ground Punch.

Unfortunately, no one had managed to do it.

Soon, Cuthbert had secured twenty consecutive wins, which was already quite an achievement.

Yet Cuthbert still seemed to be at his peak, showing no signs of fatigue.

"He's so strong!"

Osborne could sense that Emma, standing beside him, was itching to take the stage.

Cuthbert's immense strength had ignited her fighting spirit.

Before Emma could act, Rhett stood up, ready to challenge Cuthbert.

"Good luck, Rhett!"

"You can definitely force him to use a third move!"

Both Axell and Vivian cheered for Rhett.

Rhett nodded and leaped onto the stage. "Cuthbert, I'm here to challenge you!"

Cuthbert appeared calm, but a flash of excitement flickered in his eyes.

In that moment, Osborne sensed something was off.

He noticed that the way Cuthbert looked at Rhett was different from how he had viewed the other challengers.

"This isn't good!"

Osborne's expression changed, and he shouted, "Rhett, watch out!"
