"Ah!" Crimson let out a piercing scream, a sound reminiscent of a wounded beast.

"Die!" Osborne roared, fury coursing through him.

Osborne knew he was racing against time—Sin was nearby, and once he realized what was happening, he would be there to help Crimson in a flash.

Sin's strength surpassed Crimson's, and with all these injuries, Osborne was in no shape to go toe-to-toe with him.

Osborne had to kill Crimson quickly and join Greyson, Roy, and the others to stand a chance against Sin.

At that moment, Osborne unleashed everything he had, pouring all his energy into the assault on Crimson.

But he had underestimated her resilience.

Despite her severe injuries and falling into Osborne's trap, Crimson refused to go down easily.

Her life force was tenacious, almost defying belief.

As Osborne delivered a brutal kick to her chest, something snapped within her. A surge of terrifying power erupted from Crimson, slamming into Osborne's chest with bone-crushing force.

"Ugh!" He spat out a mouthful of blood, his grip on her arm slipping as he was sent flying backward, crashing hard into the ground and creating a massive crater.

Crimson was no better off; her chest was caved in, and countless internal injuries throbbed painfully within her. Yet, against all odds, she still clung to life.

She hit the ground with a thud, struggling to rise once more.

Osborne stared in shock.

"With injuries like that, how can she still stand? Is she even a human?"

Fortunately for him, Crimson couldn't fully get back up. She shot Osborne a furious glance before collapsing again, blood pouring from her mouth, mingled with fragments of organs.

"Darling!" Sin finally sensed something was wrong.

When he saw Crimson's nearly dying, his expression turned to one of horror. He charged forward, a deadly intent radiating from him.

"Damn it, you’re dead to me!"

Sin's figure materialized right in front of Osborne. His divine artifact dagger aimed straight for Osborne's heart.

But Osborne had expected this move. A dimensional rift opened up, swallowing him whole just as Sin's dagger struck, embedding itself into a nearby rock.

The impact caused the stone to explode, sending shards flying like bullets in all directions.

In the next instant, Osborne appeared hundreds of yards away, standing beside Greyson.

"Ugh!" He coughed up more blood, watching as Sin scooped up Crimson. A pity that she didn’t die.

" I still couldn't kill her. Still, I've dealt her a heavy blow. She's in no shape of fighting now!"

Osborne breathed a sigh of relief.

Meanwhile, Greyson, Roy, and the others were utterly stunned.

They thought, "Osborne has severely injured Crimson, nearly killing her?

Crimson is a top-tier Class S warrior! In the Eityreia Organization, among thousands of assassins, Sin ranks second, with Crimson close behind at third. While she isn't quite as strong as Sin, their differences are negligible.

Sin has nearly driven us to despair, yet here is Osborne, a Class AAA, almost putting Crimson down for good?"

"Whoa!" Greyson gasped, completely taken aback.

The Lambert family members stared at Osborne in disbelief.

"I'm going to kill you!"

Sin, assessing Crimson's condition, felt a surge of rage as he took in her shattered body.

He fixed his gaze on Osborne in fury.

In the blink of an eye, Sin vanished, but the terrifying murderous intent still hung in the air, suffocating everyone around.

"Watch out!"

Greyson's face filled with alarm as he noticed Sin slipping into a stealth state once again. Dense tension in the air; no one knew from where Sin would strike next.

Only Osborne wore a mocking grin.

He said, "That Stealth Art of yours may work on others, but not on me!"

He then spun on his heel, saying, "Everyone, follow me!"

In an instant, Osborne drew the Volt Hammer, unleashing a powerful surge of lightning aimed at a seemingly empty spot 164 feet away.

But the moment the lightning struck, two figures materialized, revealing Sin clutching Crimson in his arms.

The lightning crackled viciously as it struck them, yet it barely seemed to faze Sin and Crimson.

However, the Lambert family's forces weren't about to miss their chance; hundreds of attacks whipped through the air, converging on the exposed duo in a torrent of power.

Sin's eyes widened in shock as he realized the impending doom.

Caught off guard, he dodged frantically but couldn't escape the onslaught.

The combined force of hundreds of strikes hit him like a freight train, sending his body crashing against a nearby tree.

But the barrage didn't relent; more attacks were hurtling toward him.
