Osborne's expression became extremely serious.

He thought, "Are the Beast Soul Flowers here really planted by some powerful creature? Is the entire volcanic area that creature's herb field?"

"Am I stealing Beast Soul Flowers from someone else's herb field? If this is true, once that creature discovers what I've done, it will definitely stop at nothing to kill me. So, should I leave here immediately?"

Osborne was very unwilling.

A single Beast Soul Flower can be exchanged for one million Kingdom Duel Scorepoints, equivalent to the value of a regular low-grade Sacred Artifact. In just half a day here, he had already found sixty-five Beast Soul Flowers.

This wasn't much because he hadn't discovered the planting pattern of the Beast Soul Flowers before.

Now, he had discovered the pattern and could easily find Beast Soul Flowers. At the center of the volcanic area, there was one Beast Soul Flower for every hundred volcanoes. Next, he could directly follow the pattern to search. This way, his efficiency could increase by dozens of times.

Under such circumstances, Osborne didn't want to leave.

He said seriously, "I can't leave now. We've been searching here for half a day, and no creatures have appeared to stop me. So, even if these Beast Soul Flowers were planted by some powerful being, it must either not be here for now or be in a deep sleep."

"I've already picked sixty-five Beast Soul Flowers. Even if I leave now, that powerful being won't let me go. After all, I can't replant them."

The moment the Beast Soul Flower is picked, it loses its vitality.

Osborne continued, "So leaving is the worst choice. I should pick as many Beast Soul Flowers as possible before being discovered. After all, the Beast Soul Flowers here follow a pattern, and I can harvest them very quickly."

"If the pattern we discovered is correct, I can pick at least a few hundred Beast Soul Flowers in the central area within a day. I can leave then."

"Most importantly, since we can discover the pattern of the Beast Soul Flowers, others will too. Valentino, Brenda, Dustin, and the others must have already arrived in the volcanic area and are searching for Beast Soul Flowers."

"If they discover the pattern of the Beast Soul Flowers, they'll be able to find a large number of them in a short time. If I leave now, it would be like handing these Beast Soul Flowers over to them."

"After the zone duel ends, the resources and treasures I find will definitely not compare to theirs. By then, I won't stand a chance of winning the championship."

Osborne's eyes were filled with determination. He continued, "So, I must pick all the Beast Soul Flowers in the volcanic center area before leaving."

Primordial frowned tightly and said, "You're really not afraid of death. Let me be honest with you, the powerful creature that planted these Beast Soul Flowers is absolutely not something you can defeat right now. If you're discovered, I'm afraid you won't even be able to escape."

"Moreover, I have spatial power and numerous methods. Even if that powerful creature is at mid Empyrean Sacredness or even advanced Empyrean Sacredness, it can't kill me in a short time. Besides, on Abyssal Planet, apart from Gerald, the chief judge, there are at least a hundred other judges."

"As long as I can buy some time, the judges will definitely come and slay that powerful creature."

Osborne nodded.

He didn't waste any more time. Since he didn't know when that powerful creature would return or wake up, he needed to gather as many Beast Soul Flowers as possible, as quickly as possible.

Osborne took action immediately.

According to the pattern, at the center of the volcanic area, there would be one Beast Soul Flower for every hundred volcanoes. Osborne had been searching one volcano at a time before, afraid of missing any.

"We'll soon know if the pattern we discovered is correct," Osborne murmured.

He thought, "There really is a Beast Soul Flower here. Primordial's guess is likely true. I can't waste time; I need to pick it quickly and leave."

He even started using interdimensional travel just to make himself faster.

In this situation, his speed of picking Beast Soul Flowers increased dozens of times.

Where is this powerful creature now? When will it appear?

"I've already picked about one-tenth of the entire volcanic core area. In just a dozen more hours at most, I can harvest all the Beast Soul Flowers in the core area. There are at least three thousand Beast Soul Flowers here!"

In at most two more hours, he could finish harvesting all the Beast Soul Flowers in the core area.

Osborne had no idea what creature made this roar. But it was incredibly piercing and sharp, instantly causing his eardrums to bleed. He felt a wave of dizziness, stumbled, and almost fell from mid-air.
