"Take care of the elders from the Augustine family! Leave Osborne to me!" Everett declared.

With that, he launched his attack.

A casual wave of his hand unleashed a terrifying force that erupted in front of Osborne.

There was no way for Osborne to dodge.

He barely had time to summon his martial art aura for protection before the onslaught hit him.

Osborne was sent flying, the martial art aura flickering dimly around him as his face drained of color.

"Damn, he's strong!"

This was Osborne's first real encounter with a top-tier Class S fighter, and he quickly realized that the power he faced was far beyond what he could handle at the moment.

Sure, Osborne could unleash his trump cards to defeat or even kill an average Class S, but against someone like Everett, he was outmatched.

Everett, seeing that Osborne had managed to block his attack without sustaining injuries, raised an eyebrow in surprise.

He sneered, saying, "Looks like you've got some skills after all.

"In that case, I'll just have to put in a little more effort to take you down."

With that, Everett charged at him again.

Osborne's eyes flashed with determination. "You want to kill me? You'll pay a heavy price for that!"

In a heartbeat, he vanished from sight, reappearing right behind Everett.

Channeling the power of his Divine Bone, he delivered a fierce kick aimed at Everett's back.

Everett could have dodged, but he didn't even flinch.

His expression was one of icy confidence; he didn't see Osborne's attack as a threat. After all, Osborne was just a Class AAA—a mere youth. How could he possibly pose a danger?

With a casual punch, Everett met Osborne's attack head-on.

The impact sent Osborne sprawling, but Everett's expression hardened as he felt a tremendous force surge through him, threatening to explode from within.

Everett staggered back, a surprised grunt escaping him as a trickle of blood appeared at the corner of his mouth.

"Did you just hurt me?"

Shock washed over Everett's face as he stared at Osborne, who was now reeling from the blow.

His eyes turned cold, locking onto Osborne. "Not bad. I underestimated you. If you can hurt me as a Class AAA, I can only imagine the chaos you'd wreak if you ever broke into Class S!

"Prepare to die!"

Fury ignited within Everett, and his wariness of Osborne reached a boiling point. If Osborne survived and continued to grow, the entire League of Divine Descent would have sleepless nights.

"Die!"

With that, Everett's attacks turned ferocious. He treated Osborne as a worthy adversary. He unleashed his full power, intent on crushing Osborne into submission.

Under the relentless assault, Osborne found himself unable to defend, let alone counterattack.

He could only focus on survival, but even that was proving difficult.

Blood spewed from his mouth as he struggled to hold on.

Desperate, Osborne activated Light of Redemption, but even that was just a temporary reprieve.

It wouldn't change the tide of battle; it merely extended his time in the fight.

"Ha! Time to go down!"

Everett's eyes glinted with determination as he landed another brutal punch on Osborne, aiming to finish him off for good.

Osborne was on the brink of collapse, barely holding himself together.

But just then, a massive gray bear apparition suddenly materialized in front of him, blocking Everett's strike.

"Osborne, I'm here to help!"

Emma's figure appeared, wielding the Swallow Shield, ready to absorb the brunt of Everett's attack for Osborne.

Osborne's expression tightened, anxiety washing over him.

"Emma! Stay back! It's too dangerous here!"

Everett was far too powerful.

Emma, though a top-tier fighter among the younger generation—stronger than most of the Augustine family elders—was still no match for a peak Class S like Everett.

Even with her formidable Swallow Shield, she wouldn't be able to withstand his onslaught.

Sure enough, when Everett unleashed his attack, it struck the Swallow Shield, causing Emma to gasp as her face paled dramatically. Blood trickled from the corner of her mouth.

"No, I won't leave! I'm here to help you!"

Emma wiped the blood away and stood firm; her Swallow Shield raised in front of Osborne.

At that moment, he looked utterly defeated.

Emma knew she couldn't hope to beat Everett, but she couldn't stand by and watch as he slaughtered Osborne because Osborne was her man.

"Come on!" Emma shouted defiantly, directing her fury at Everett.

A cold smile crept onto Everett's face. "A volunteer for death? Ha! Due to certain... reasons, I can't kill Osborne myself, but I'm more than happy to leave him heavily injured. As for you? No mercy! Die!"

With a swift motion, Everett struck again. The sheer gap in their strengths made it effortless for him to break through the Swallow Shield's defenses. His overwhelming power crashed down on Emma.

She coughed violently, blood spraying from her lips.

Osborne's eyes widened in horror.
