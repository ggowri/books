"Finally, I completed the task!"

Osborne was over the moon, as this proved that his guess was correct. Only by announcing in public that he and Clarissa were lovers could the fourth test be completed.

"So tough!"

Osborne thought the fourth successor test was more challenging than the first three put together. If he weren’t lucky, he might have failed it.

No wonder even Godfather advised him to give up before he started.

To complete the fourth test, he made a lot of effort and killed many people, including, Apollo Chevalier, Orion Lark, and Derek Chevalier.

Each of them were once Osborne's distant idols, but now they were all killed by him, serving as the stepping stones for his test completion.

Also, because of the fourth test, Osborne felt that he had indeed completed his transformation, which came from the inside out.

He was now full of confidence. Even if he was not Godfather's grandson, he was already an absolute big shot in Skyefall.

No matter who he faced from Skyefall, he could hold his head high.

Even when facing an ancient family, he was fearless.

Most importantly, the fourth test led Osborne to his soulmate--Clarissa.

Thinking of Clarissa, Osborne showed a smile.

He quickly opened the Draco Star Wristlet to check the rewards for completing the fourth test.

First of all, it was still about Satan's Eye.

The method to upgrade Satan's Eye from level four to level five had now appeared.

"Class B mental power?" Osborne murmured.

The prerequisite for Satan's Eye to reach the fifth level was for him to possess Class B mental power. Godfather gave him many ways to quickly improve his mental power, such as taking medicine, injecting genetic drugs, and so on.

However, Osborne did not need these methods.

If nothing unexpected happened, his mental power would have already reached Class B.

Isa told him that mental power was related to physical fitness.

The higher the physical fitness was, the higher the mental power would be.

Therefore, Isa, a martial artist, could be Zoe's master.

Zoe was a psychic.

As Isa trained Zoe's body, Zoe's mental power would naturally increase rapidly. At least before her mental power reached Class B+, it would be enough just to train her body.

According to Isa, Zoe's improvement was rapid, and her mental power had already reached Class B.

Osborne's mental power was initially not as good as Zoe's, but ever since he absorbed the unique energy of the blood diamonds, his physical fitness had skyrocketed and reached Class B+.

And his mental power also surged to Class B+.

If he wanted to improve his mental power, simply training his body wouldn’t be enough. It required more complex training. However, Osborne was not a psychic, so the mental strength of Class B+ was enough for him.

"I didn't expect that the conditions for upgrading Satan's Eye to level five could be achieved so easily," Osborne murmured.

Maybe even Godfather didn't expect that his physical fitness could improve so quickly.

A month ago, Osborne was unaware of martial arts, and even the most talented could only reach Class D in a month.

But Osborne reached Class B+.

He didn't need to spend much time enhancing his fitness and mental power and could promptly upgrade Satan's Eye to level five.

Satan's Eye of level five was considered above average even in the Augustine family in Newport City.

Except for Godfather and some evildoers, few people could upgrade Satan's Eye to level six.

"Satan's Eye of level five! My strength is going to increase again!"

Osborne's eyes lit up.

He didn't upgrade Satan's Eye right away despite meeting the conditions, as the process was risky and required thorough preparation without any distractions.

Osborne continued to check other rewards.

The second reward was still about money-A full 20 billion dollars in cash.

For Osborne, although 20 billion dollars was a lot, he no longer cared about it because money was just a number to him now.

He owned Luna Jewelry, Skyefall Security, and a diamond mine.

He already had more money than he could spend. He remained unfazed in front of the cash.

But no matter what, Osborne was thrilled to have received 20 billion dollars, which he could allocate to charity or save orphans, making a meaningful impact.

He then checked the last reward.

Generally speaking, Godfather would reward him with some tangible assets in the end.

For example, after completing the second test, Godfather rewarded him with the Luna Jewelry.

After the third test completion, he rewarded him with Skyefall Security.

Luna Jewelry was worth one billion dollars.

And Skyefall Security was worth tens of billions of dollars.

Therefore, Osborne was quite looking forward to the scale of the industry that Godfather would reward this time.

Could it be a giant company worth hundreds of billions?

Osborne took a deep breath and finally clicked on the last reward.

Then, he was stunned.
