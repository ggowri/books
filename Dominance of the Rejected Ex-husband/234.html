Chapter 234

Just then, a man stood up in the arena, with a loud microphone, enough to cut through the excited murmurs.

“Ladies and gentlemen of the Cultivator world!, The fight will begin now,” he announced. “Listen carefully. The rules are simple. Either opponent can tap out at any time. If someone taps out, they accept defeat, and the fight ends there. If no one taps out, the battle will continue until one falls or dies. That is all!!!.”

Charles’ throat went dry. He looked around the arena. The crowd was on edge

“Kill him.”

“Set him as an example.”

They all shrieked.

Raphael smirked, taking a step closer. “Are you scared already? You can still back out, Charles,” he taunted.

Charles scoffed, his voice steady. “Just shut up and bring it on.”

The announcer raised his hands and yelled, “Let the fight begin!”

Raphael, as usual, moved first. He hasn't used his powers yet. He advanced with fists alone, a storm of punches aimed at Charles’ chest and face.

Each swing was fast, sharp, meant to overwhelm. Charles dodged, stepping back and weaving, letting some punches graze him. He didn't even get to lay hands on Charles.

Then he countered. Charles fists moved clean, precise, hitting Raphael’s ribs, jaw, and stomach. Each strike landed with a thud and snap.

The crowd gasped. Charles’ strikes were powerful, and heavy.

Raphael stumbled slightly, gritting his teeth, a faint line of blood forming on his nose.

Lizzypie, Sapphire, Natasha, and Gabriella were cheering.

“Yes! Yes! Go Charles!”

“Beat his ass up!”.

Raphael realized Charles had the edge in raw fist combat. He can never defeat him on that. He wiped the blood away from his nose.

His eyes glinted with fury. Then, he inhaled and of cousre, use his power. He split himself into ten identical versions, each circling Charles, their movements synchronized, and they were fast as lightning.

Charles gulped. Ten Raphaels now, surrounding him from all sides.

“You are fucking dead!”.

They attacked together. Right punch, left kick, high strike, low sweep. One moved so fast it was almost invisible.

Another lifted one of the tables nearby telekinetically, hurling it toward Charles. Others combined punches with bursts of wind and energy blasts.

Thud!.

Boom!

Charles tried his Flash Movement, attempting to evade them in a blink, but the attacks were too many, and too fast.

He was knocked back, staggering, absorbing blows from multiple angles. Which made his nose bleed also.

Then he stepped back and closed his eyes for a fraction of a second

[Soul Sight activated].

He could see the next thirty seconds of attacks from the ten Raphaels. Every punch, kick, telekinetic throw, speed burst—he predicted them all.

During this process, the pain was intense. Every strike hit him, while Raphaels kept attacking him. Everyone started praising him.

When the thirty seconds of prediction ended, Charles struck. He suddenly grabbed the raphael fist, then used it to lift him up and throw him at others.

His movements were smooth, almost effortless now. Each attack he predicted, he countered.

First, a left punch to the fast-moving Raphael doubled him over.

Then, a precise kick sent the telekinetic attacker stumbling back.

Another blow cracked ribs, dropping a third clone. One by one, he hit them, every strike placed perfectly as he anticipated their next move.

Lizzypie and the others chanted again, their voices loud and clear.

“Yes! Yes! That’s it!”

Raphael’s expression shifted from smug confidence to alarm. Each clone fell with a thud, and the real Raphael staggered.

Then, Raphael stood back up and shouted, “Wow, I'm impressed, but enough of this childplay!”.

In an instant, he split again—this time into fifty identical versions, each circling Charles like a swarm of predators.

They started closing in on Charles. From left, right, up and every angle.

The thirty seconds of Soul Sight had ended, and now there was no prediction.

Charles’ eyes widened slightly, impressed by Raphael power. It astonishing!. he see reason why he was the powerful young cultivators

They moved like thugs, battering him from every side, they managed to pin Charles’ down, and started climbing over him.

“Charles! Tap out! They are going to crush you!”, Gabriella echoed, fear lacing her voice. “Just tap out! You can’t take all of them!”

Even lizzypie started getting scared.

Charles’ body was under immense pressure. Over fifty Raphaels pressed on him, pinning him down!.

It felt like he was being pulled apart. Gabriella was right—if he stayed like this, he could suffocate or be killed. But he knew what he was doing. He wanted to gather them for the perfect moment.

Just then, Charles’ eyes blazed. He slammed his fists together, gritting his teeth. A roar erupted from his throat.

“Haaaaaaaaa!!!!”

A burning, white-hot light exploded from his eyes. The Hell Beam Fire.

The energy cut through the swarm instantly. Every one of Raphael’s illusions screeched, their forms squishing under the force of the beam, distorting, burning and melting away.

The first line of clones evaporated under the intense fire.

The second wave was hit next, crushed against each other like paper dolls under a magnifying flame. Sparks flew around.

Charles’ vision blurred, his energy flaring as he pushed harder. The Hell Beam Fire surged in intensity, sweeping over the fifty attackers.

The real Raphael, seeing this, immediately looks for an escape.

The crowd was shocked.

“Wow, that was so intense. What kind of power is that!”

“He is not normal also.”

At that moment, the hell fire beam had reached its limit, which made Charles stop. He fell on the floor and was heaving heavily.

Raphael who managed to escape the intense moment was at first, shocked by the level of the power.

Then, after seeing Charles on the floor he smirked.

“Look like you have hit your limit, You nearly scare me”, Raphael stated with a smirk.

Then he added, “I will advise you to tap out now, please, I'm ready to kill you. Because, newsflash!. You are not the only one that knows how to use fire!”

With that, Raphael started splitting again. There were so many this time. Like 100. What made it crazy was that, in each person's hand, was a blazing fireball.

Raphael can use fire also.

Gabriella who was now so panicked said, “What! That's like almost 100 of Raphaels each with a fireball, one attack will burn Chalres to ash!”.

Natahsa also added, “Charles, tap out now. Please tap out.”

While lizzypie just stayed put, watching how things would turn out.
