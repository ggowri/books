Chapter 229

Morning light leaked through the curtains.

Charles groaned as he shifted, his body feeling heavy, drained, and sore in a way that reminded him too clearly of how wild the night had been. His eyes fluttered open.

Serena was beside him. Curled into his side. Peaceful and asleep.

For a moment, he just stared at her. Her breathing was slow and steady, her face calm in a way he wasn’t used to seeing after everything she’d been through lately.

He tried to sit up.

A dull ache rolled through his body and he hissed, dropping back onto the bed. “Damn…”

Then—The air in front of him flickered.

A translucent interface snapped into place, floating just above his vision.

[Level 5 Unlocked]

[Ability Name: Moment Lock]

[Effect: Temporarily halts all movement within your surroundings]

Charles blinked and recalled that he already upgraded.

“…You are serious?” he muttered.

He stared at the words, his mind still foggy. “You mean I can stop time?”

The interface pulsed softly.

[Correction: Not time]

[Moment Lock halts movement, not time flow]

Charles exhaled slowly. “But… everything freezes. People. Objects. Motion.”

[Confirmed, Except time]

New lines appeared beneath it.

[Power Capacity: 40%]

[Effective Radius: 10 meters]

[Duration Limit: 60 seconds]

Charles clicked his tongue. “One minute?”

That was way too short, too short, even though the power was so cool.

He rubbed his face, already calculating. Still… if used right, a single minute was more than enough.

Raphael’s face surfaced in his mind. A slow smile tugged at Charles’s lips. “Yeah… I can work with that.”

He turned his head toward Serena.

“Let's give it a test,” he whispered.

[Moment Lock: Activated]

The room changed instantly and with that, Serena didn’t move, didn’t even breathe.

She looked like a paused image, frozen mid-sleep. Charles looked at her beautiful face and kissed her gently.

“…That’s insane,” he breathed. After a minute, it stopped and everything went back to normal. 

Charles stood, grabbed his phone from the bedside table, and headed for the bathroom. 

Charles stayed longer  in the bathroom, and made a call.

After that, he washed his hands.

Outside, Serena’s phone rang.

The sharp sound cut through the quiet room and jolted her awake.

 She groaned softly and rolled onto her back, one hand pressing her forehead as the ringing continued. Squinting, she reached for the phone on the bedside table.

She frowned when she saw the caller ID.

“My assistant?”

She answered quickly. “Hello. Is everything okay?”

The voice on the other end was practically shaking with excitement.

“Ma’am! Ma’am, you have to hear this. ?”

Serena pushed herself upright. “What is it? Talk to me.”

“You know the new  Mining and Refinery project in the city? The one people say belongs to the Godfather’s son?”

Serena blinked. “Yes. What about it?”

There was a sharp intake of breath.

“He sent three billion dollars today.”

Serena froze.

“What?” she screamed, her voice echoing across the room.

Serena shot to her feet. “Three billion dollars?” she repeated, almost laughing in disbelief. 

“That’s a lot!”

“I was shocked too,” the assistant said quickly. “But we have confirmed it. He wants to invest in us and form a partnership. We have already received the draft agreement.”

Serena’s hand trembled around the phone. “Wow…this… this changes everything.”

“Yes, ma’am. Completely. I’m heading to the company now to prepare. You should come as soon as possible.”

“I will,” Serena said, still stunned. “I will be there shortly.”

The call ended.

She stood there for a few seconds.

First Sapphire’s partnership had come almost effortlessly. Now this. It felt unreal. Like the world was favoring her.

“What's that?”, Charles asked. 

She turned to him, eyes bright, excitement spilling out of her. “Charles, you won’t believe this. The Godfather’s son wants to partner with us. He sent three billion dollars today.”

He raised an eyebrow and gave a low whistle. “That’s huge.”

“I know.” She laughed, still half in shock. “This can save the company. No, more than that. This can change everything.”

Charles smiled and nodded. “Congratulations, Serena. You deserve it.” He said it like this was the first time hearing it, even though he was the one that made it happen. 

Serena, still excited, immediately washed up, and kissed Charles first, before she made his way to the company.

Charles also dressed up, then immediately went straight to Mr. Belly's house. When he arrived there, he was greeted by Mr. Belly himself who maneuvered him into his room.

“Do you find anything please?", He demanded.

Charles sighed and said, “I found a lot”.
