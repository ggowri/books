Chapter 137

“It’s been going well,” Colloms said smoothly, voice almost casual, “in fact, the Dubai man—Akeem—we already concluded that he would give me the… Global Tech Expansion Contract.”

Tracy’s face lit up. “Wow, that’s great then!”

But Collins’ expression shifted instantly, tight and sharp. Tracy noticed the change and frowned, concern creeping into her voice. “Mmm… why? What’s wrong?”

Collins exhaled, shaking his head slightly, as if holding back irritation. “The thing is… they don’t trust me. They feel I didn't have a company with enough potential that they can see. They are linking the contract to a company with performance, I had none, so they’re not confident in me. So… they might want to reconsider.”

Tracy’s brows furrowed. “Ohh…” she murmured, unsure what to say.

Collins’ voice dropped, low, tinged with bitterness. “If only that bitch Serena hadn’t collected the company from him. I was talking to them before they took it! This is mad money… and we are just going to let it slip away.”

Tracy felt a pang of sympathy. She bit her lip, unsure what to do. “If only there was something I could do to help…” she whispered.

Collins leaned closer, letting his tone drip with both charm and manipulation. “There is.”

Tracy frowned, confused. “What?”

He smiled, slow and deliberate “Your company. If you sign over ownership to me, I can show them that. That’s all they need to see.”

The room went quiet. Tracy’s mind raced. She hadn’t expected it to come to this… her own company, the one she had built with sweat and sacrifice, being used as a bargaining chip.

Collins could see her hesitation, and immediately added. “I'm just suggesting, you don't have to do it. I will understand perfectly”.

Tracy took a deep breath, steadying herself. In her mind, the numbers spun wildly—twenty billion dollars across 2 years.

Ten billion in a single year. With that, her company could soar, expand, thrive beyond anything she had imagined.

And then there was Collins. He had saved her, rescued her company when it was on the brink, put in a lot of investment and effort she could barely comprehend. (Which Charles did but she misunderstand it to Collins).

They were engaged.

He had also done so much for her already. Why hesitate now? She could help him, and in return, the contract—and the money—would eventually benefit her again. She could always reclaim the company once the deal was closed.

It made sense. It was worth it.

Her gaze met his, steady and resolute. “Okay,” she said softly but firmly. “Let’s do that.”

Collins’ eyes practically popped out of his head. “Really? You’re going to give me your company?”

Tracy scoffed, shaking her head lightly, a faint smile playing on her lips. “Since it’s for our benefit… we’re talking twenty billion dollars here. Just for you to get the deal. I’ll give it.”

“Wow,” Collins breathed, genuinely astonished. “I’m very grateful… thanks so much, babe. How can I show my gratitude?”

“It’s nothing,” Tracy said softly, her gaze distant for a moment. She thought of everything he had done to keep her company afloat when it was on the brink of bankruptcy. he had saved it before, and now she was just repaying It

Collins didn’t have idea of what she was saying. He simply put on a polished smile, nodding. “Thanks so much.”

“So… when are you signing it over?” he asked,.

“I have a few procedures to complete,” Tracy replied. “Give me five days. Then I will hand over the ownership file. You present it, and the deal is yours.”

Collins leaned back, letting out a low, satisfied hum. “Thanks. If we get this, I swear… Serena and Charles won’t even be able to stand beside us. We’ll surpass them and shock them!”

Tracy nodded, a sly glint in her eye. This wasn’t just about money—it was a way to intimidate Charles too. “We will be bigger than them,” she said quietly, her tone measured.

Collins reached for her, pulling her into a hug. “Thanks for staying with me,” he whispered, voice warm.

But behind that smile, a smirk tugged at the corner of his lips. His plan was already forming. Once he had the company in his hands… he would sell it and vanish.
