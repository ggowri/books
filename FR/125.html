Celeste collapsed onto the floor, her entire body shaking violently.

Daniel stopped alongside her, confusion flashing in his eyes as he quietly asked, "What's wrong, Ms. Elvador?"

Daniel stuck to Aurora like glue, following right behind, ready to step in if she needed backup.

What truly devastated him was that his beloved daughter, Bertha, had ended up in jail.

The embroidery Bertha had trashed was worth 20 million dollars, and they'd have to pay every penny of that back.

But just covering the damage wouldn't cut it.

Since he knew Julian's methods, he realized that pulling Bertha out of jail would require a hefty sum for greasing palms up and down the chain.

Sure, they had 50 million dollars, but that money was supposed to be their ticket to living it up abroad—their passport to luxury. The thought of blowing all cash before they left the country made his skin crawl.

Joshua could only transfer his personal assets and company holdings. He couldn't touch a single cent from Celeste.

Celeste still held a decent chunk of company shares, along with her personal savings and a luxurious mansion in her name from Elvador family. Raising 50 million dollars would be a breeze for her.

He could kill two birds with one stone—get Bertha out of jail and make Celeste suffer and pay a hefty price for everything.

If Celeste had actually loved him, she would've married him with Jerome Group as her gift, letting him walk in with his head held high. But no, she made him marry into her family with nothing, swallowing his pride daily.

If he hadn't been smart enough to take out that stubborn old man early on, he probably would've spent his life with his head down, getting stomped on by everyone.

His resentment blazed through him like wildfire, completely out of control.

All these years, he'd treated Celeste like a queen, giving her everything she could possibly want.

Now his precious daughter was behind bars, and it was finally Celeste's turn to pay him back.

Any time he looked this worried, she'd comfort him.

That setup would have given him the perfect opening to drop the news about Bertha's arrest.

Bertha couldn't handle it? But Aurora could?

Why was she using Bertha's full name like that? This bitch breaks her leg and suddenly grows an attitude problem. Hmph!

Once I've squeezed last cent out of you, you'll be completely useless to me.

After being a housewife for 20 years, no house, no savings, a son in prison, and a daughter who hates your guts. Let's see how high and mighty you act then.

You'll end up homeless on the streets, begging for scraps. A homeless woman is a plaything for men anyway. Hah!

She kept screaming while swinging wildly at him with both hands. She looked ready to rip him apart to satisfy the pure hatred burning inside her.
