Lily narrowed her eyes slightly.

She turned swiftly and walked directly into the internet café next to the bar.

Inside, the dim yellow lights barely illuminated the rows of computer screens flickering in the darkness.

Her gaze quickly scanned the room before she settled in front of an unoccupied computer.

Fingers flying over the keyboard, she typed at an astonishing speed.

Lines of code streamed across the screen, the monitor's bluish glow casting a sharp light on her face, making her appear even more austere.

A few moments later, she stopped.

All surveillance cameras within a half-mile radius were rendered useless.

Their signals were jammed, freezing the footage on a single frame as if time had momentarily stalled.

The code she had just input would erase itself in an hour.

By then, the cameras would return to normal, but that gave her more than enough time to carry out her plan.

Lily stood up and left the café.

Meanwhile, Wilson and Lizeth had already rushed into their car. The engine roared to life, and within seconds, they disappeared.

That left Zoey standing alone at the bar's entrance, her face brimming with excitement and triumph.

Zoey's manic laughter pierced the silent night.

Lily's gaze turned cold as she observed the woman reveling in her newfound wealth.

Zoey sneered, "Unbelievable. All I had to do was rough up some useless woman in prison, and I got paid this much. This is way faster than making money through business.

"Back when I was hustling, I couldn't even make 10 thousand dollars a month. But now? A million dollars, just like that. I'd have to scrimp and save for at least ten years to earn that kind of money. Who knew a five-year prison sentence would turn out to be so profitable?"

The more she spoke, the more arrogant she became.

A sinister grin twisted her face.

"Too bad that useless woman got out before me. Otherwise, I could've picked another fight, landed back inside, and squeezed even more cash out of them.

"But one million dollars should last me a long while. And when it runs out, I can always go back for more."

"You seem quite pleased with yourself." Lily's voice rang out suddenly, low and carrying an undeniable force.

Zoey flinched at the unexpected sound behind her, and her laughter cut off abruptly.

She spun around, her eyes sharp with caution, locking onto Lily with an intense glare.

"What the hell is wrong with you? Trying to get yourself killed?" she snapped.

Lily met her gaze with a chilling calmness, completely unshaken as if she were watching a pathetic clown perform.

Zoey shifted uncomfortably under her stare, feeling inexplicably irked.

Her expression darkened as she scowled.

"What the hell are you looking at? Keep staring, and I'll gouge your damn eyes out!"

An eerie smile spread across Lily's face.

In the dim light, that smile carried an unsettling chill, sending a shiver down Zoey's spine.

She had never encountered someone like Lily before.

Before prison, Zoey had always been a troublemaker.

Inside, she had thrived in the chaos, becoming one of the most feared inmates.

People either avoided her or cowered before her.

Never had anyone dared to meet her gaze like this, let alone speak to her this way.

"You're really asking for it." Zoey gritted her teeth, her face twisting with fury.

She lifted her hand, aiming a vicious slap straight at Lily's face.

Zoey's raised hand carried enough force to tear Lily's face apart.

Yet, Lily remained completely still, amusement flickering in her eyes.

The moment Zoey's palm was about to land, Lily swiftly pulled a knife from her pocket, the sharp tip aimed directly at Zoey's hand.

With a sickening pierce, the blade sliced clean through Zoey's palm.

Blood gushed out instantly while Zoey let out a blood-curdling scream.

Her arrogance evaporated in an instant, replaced by sheer agony.

Lily watched her with a smirk, casually twisting the knife still embedded in Zoey's palm.

Zoey's screams grew more desperate, her entire body collapsing to the ground as she trembled violently.

Lily crouched down, bringing her face close to Zoey's.

"Hurting others must feel good, doesn't it? What do you think?"

Zoey's face turned ghostly pale.

Terror filled her wide, pleading eyes.

She shook her head frantically.

"Please... let me go... I was wrong..."

Lily blinked, feigning innocence.

The contrast made her all the more terrifying.

"Let you go? Why should I? When you were torturing others in prison, did you ever let them go?

"Now you're asking me to let you go. This is wrong and very hypocritical."

Without warning, she yanked the knife out.

Blood spurted from Zoey's palm.

Zoey's body spasmed violently, on the verge of passing out.

Lily seized her by the hair and dragged her toward the dark alley nearby.

Zoey's entire body shook like a leaf in the wind.

Her eyes were filled with fear, and all traces of her earlier arrogance and smugness had completely vanished.

She could feel fear after all.

How ironic.

Just moments ago, she had been boasting about the way she tortured Aurora in prison.

Now, the tables had turned.

She was the prey, utterly powerless.

Lily scoffed.

She had expected Zoey to be stronger than this.

It turned out that she was nothing more than a coward.

Tears streamed down Zoey's face as she stammered, "What... what do you want?"

"Nothing. Just going to gouge out your eyes, cut off your tongue, deafen your ears, and sever your tendons. That's all."

Zoey's body went rigid.

She shook her head violently.

"No, no, please! I don't want that! We have no real grudge! Why are you doing this?"

Lily frowned slightly with an innocent expression, "Who says I need a grudge to do this?"

Zoey sobbed, "Please... let me go. I'll give you money..."

Lily ignored her pleas, letting out an amused laugh.

"Money? I don't need money. I just want you ruined."

Zoey collapsed, her entire body convulsing as tears and snot mixed together on her face.

"I was wrong! I know I was wrong! Please spare me!"

Lily twirled the knife between her fingers, the crisp metallic sound echoing in the silent alley.

Zoey's eyes widened in terror.

She scrambled backward, desperate to escape. But Lily grabbed her by the ankle.

"What are you doing? No! No!" She kicked wildly, but Lily's grip was like iron.

She couldn't break free at all.

Lily gripped the knife tightly, her gaze remaining cold.

"You wanted to gouge out my eyes, didn't you? Now, I'll let you experience what that feels like."

With those words, she thrust the knife toward Zoey's left eye swiftly and mercilessly.

A piercing scream tore through the night, sharp enough to shatter the silence.

Lily didn't hesitate. The knife twisted within Zoey's eye before she yanked it out and drove it straight into her right eye. Blood gushed down Zoey's face, a crimson stream painting her pale skin.

Zoey writhed on the ground in agony, clutching her ruined eyes, her screams filling the alley.

"Now, it's your tongue's turn."

Lily pried open Zoey's mouth, shoving the knife inside.

With a swift flick of her wrist, the sharp edge sliced through flesh, severing Zoey's tongue in an instant.

A garbled, choking wail escaped Zoey's throat as blood spurted wildly, splattering across the pavement.

Then, Lily picked up a jagged stone and stepped toward her.

"Tonight, I'll make you taste every bit of the torment you inflicted on Aurora in prison."

She raised the stone high and brought it down on Zoey's ear.

A sickening crunch echoed in the darkness as Zoey's ear was reduced to a mangled mess of blood and flesh.

The world around her fell into silence, her hearing shattered beyond repair.

Zoey's body convulsed before she passed out from the pain.

But Lily wasn't done.

She crouched beside her, the knife in her hand gleaming under the dim light.

With precise force, she severed Zoey's tendons, both in her hands and feet.

Zoey's body jerked violently, the fresh agony dragging her back to consciousness. A weak, broken moan escaped her lips.

In the darkness of the alley, her wails echoed like the cries of a tormented soul, lost and doomed to eternal suffering.
