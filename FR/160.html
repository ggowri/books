Dating a girl is much better than messing around with those unreliable friends.

Lily stood at the foot of the teaching building, waiting for a long time, but the phone call had gone silent.

"Look behind you."

"How does she even dare to come to school? If it were me, I'd hide at home."

"Having a sugar daddy is great, huh? She can make money just by lying down. Don't you think?"

Lily put down her plate and turned to leave.

Her face was covered in curry and salad dressing.

Aurora was pure and kind, but Lily was the complete opposite. Lily was ruthless.

They knew Lily's true nature better than anyone.

Anyone who provokes Lily will face a terrible fate.

If it weren't for the fact that Lily had dirt on him, he would have kicked her out long ago.

"Lily, don't worry. I know what to do."
