So this is what it feels like—to be believed in, to be backed up, completely and unconditionally.

She bullied me in the last life, and now she's trying it again in this one? Fine. We'll settle both lifetimes' debts right now.

I won't let this go."

Mom, do you even hear yourself right now? I am not the one getting hit.
