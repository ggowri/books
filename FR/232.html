Ungrateful?

"Wait a sec! I remember now! She... she's that brainiac from the Putnam's Barron family! She got into MIT when she was just five, graduated by nine, and her ground-breaking research made waves all over the globe."

Aurora was his idol.

Shit! Shit! Shit!

The genius girl's been kidnapped!

A once-in-a-blue-moon talent like her, if something goes wrong, it'll be a disaster for the country!

The police were shaken when they got the call.

Before Celeste could do a thing, Darren had already spilled the beans to the cops.

The police were all shocked when they got the call.

Aurora was like a national treasure under protection.

Her contributions to the country were off the charts.

A scientific whiz like her should've been the belle of the ball in Orley, yet here she was, snatched away.

If something went south, it'd be a total nightmare for Orley.

Unconscious, she lay on a frigid operating table, while on another table lay Bertha.

Isabella tenderly stroked Bertha's head and cooed, "Don't you worry, honey. I'll start the surgery soon. Once we pop this little bitch's kidney into you, you'll be alright."

Bertha managed a feeble smile and turned her head to eye Aurora, her eyes blazing with envy.

So what if that shameless Celeste is crazy about you?

And what if the Barron family spoils you rotten? You're still gonna fork over that kidney to me.
