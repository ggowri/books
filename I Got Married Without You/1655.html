Chapter 1655 You don't have even a tiny place

Eva looked up and stared at Paul, who pretended to be clueless, with a cold smile.

"Just pretend that I don't love you that much."

She didn't love him that much...

So she didn't want to marry him.

More than his mother's opposition, this hurt Paul the most, but...

"I know you don't love me that much, but as long as I have a tiny place in your heart, it's enough."

Kate witnessed her son, Paul, begging Eva in a pitiful manner and suddenly felt sorry for him.

She had always thought that Eva was the one clinging onto Paul, but as James had said, it was her son who couldn't let go.

And Eva, facing her son's plea, not only was not moved, but also looked expressionless, staring at Paul, who was almost kneeling.

"Paul, you don't have even a tiny place in my heart."

Paul didn't believe her words at all.

"If I don't have even a tiny place, then why did you... that night?"

"I was drunk!"

Eva coldly interrupted him.

"That night, even if it wasn't you, it could have been any other man."

When these cold and heartless words struck, it felt like being stabbed by a sword, making Paul turn pale.

"Eva, when you say such things, have you ever considered my feelings?"

"I don't love you at all, so why should I consider your feelings?"

This counter-question caused Paul's clear eyes to gradually turn red.

He took a step forward, grabbed Eva's wrist, but she reacted strongly and shook him off.

This repeated resistance suddenly infuriated Paul-

He disregarded everything and held Eva's shoulders, pushing her against the wall, and then pulling her into his arms.

While suppressing the urge to vomit, Eva struggled desperately, but he held her hands with one hand and used his legs to trap her legs.

After making her unable to move, Paul pinched her chin, making her look up, staring angrily into her evasive eyes.

"Eva, look into my eyes and say it again, do you really not love me?"

He wanted to take it slowly, but this stubborn Eva made it impossible for him to do so!

Forced to meet his gaze, Eva, with anger in Paul's eyes, indifferently spoke.

"I don't love you."

These few words extinguished all of Paul's anger, leaving only cold blood flowing through his body.

He stared at Eva, trying to see any trace of a lie in her eyes, but all he saw was disgust and disdain.

Looking at Eva like this, Paul stared blankly for a while and then weakly let her go.

Eva, able to breathe again, turned and ran, as if she stayed for another moment, that 50% of love would make her stay.

Seeing the two argue, Kate quickly walked to Paul's side, raised her pale hand, and patted his shoulder.

"When I talked to Ms. Miller earlier, I asked her if she loved you. Her answer was no, and she shouldn't have lied to you about that."

Paul, whose heart was already bleeding, smiled coldly at these words.

"If she doesn't love me, then there's no need for anyone to beg her!"

Leaving these words behind, Paul turned around and walked in the opposite direction of Eva.

As he passed the tables and chairs placed in the hallway, he raised his leg and kicked them hard.

With this resentful action, his toenails inside his shoes flipped over...

Paul, who used to find even a gentle bump painful, now felt nothing.

Standing in the hallway, Kate couldn't describe the feeling inside her.

This was the result she wanted, but seeing Eva truly reject Paul, she felt a bit sorry for him.

With a complicated mood, she glanced at Eva's back and then at Paul's back before catching up with her son's footsteps.

After returning to Ryan's place, Eva rolled up her sleeves and went to the kitchen to prepare dinner.

Not long after, the smell of fish came from the pot. Eva, who should have found it fragrant and delicious, suddenly felt nauseous.

She thought the fish had gone bad, so she quickly lifted the lid and tasted it with a spoon. The urge to vomit almost made her throw up.

Fortunately, she only dry heaved and didn't actually vomit. She didn't pay much attention to it, put down the spoon, and poured the fish into the trash can...
