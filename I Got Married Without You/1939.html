Chapter 1939 Let me kiss you first

James recovered faster than Amelia, and most of the time he took care of her. When Amelia opened her eyes, she saw a tall and handsome man holding a hot towel, gently wiping her legs.

His movements were skilled and gentle, afraid of waking her up or making her uncomfortable.

Sensing her burning gaze, James looked back with a tender and tired look.

"The doctor said that if you're in a coma for too long, the blood circulation in your legs will be affected. Wiping them with a hot towel every day will help," he explained. He then turned his body and changed the towel, first applying it to her lower legs and then gently wiping them.

What she saw was the deep and affectionate kiss between the man and woman under the warm sun.

Eva also raised her hand to cover the big eyes of the baby, who was staring at what his parents were doing.

Meanwhile, Paul, who was leading the way, shamelessly burst into laughter. "You guys continue, we'll go outside and watch."
