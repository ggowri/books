Chapter 1893 An apple

James didn't argue anymore and gestured for her to continue.

Amelia then said, "I promised before that when Ryan stood up, I would give him a big gift..."

Afraid that James would misunderstand, Amelia added, "Johnny also saved my life, so I have to give him a gift, and Neil and George, who helped take care of Grace, also deserve one, so..."

Before she could finish explaining, James interrupted her gently, "Babe, if you want to give gifts, go ahead. You don't need my permission."

Upon hearing this, Amelia cupped James' handsome face and kissed him deeply, saying, "Babe, you're so good."

After praising him, Amelia continued, "I'll go pick out a few gifts now and have Frank send them over."

Before she could get up, James said, "Tell me what you want to give, and I'll make the arrangements."

He didn't want her to exert herself.

Amelia felt sweet and content with such a good husband.

She smiled and told James what she wanted to give, and he went to make the arrangements.

After everything was arranged, James accompanied Amelia back to their room.

He didn't want her to take a shower alone, so he helped her.

After they finished, he put a bathrobe on her and carried her to bed.

After doing all this, James went to the bathroom and doused himself with cold water, extinguishing his desires.

He tilted his chin up, facing the cold water, letting it pour over his head and face.

Amelia was in his arms, and James swallowed hard several times, finally suppressing all his desires and closed his eyes to sleep.

The next morning, Grace went to school, Frank went to deliver the gifts, and James and Amelia boarded the private jet again.

When they arrived in Barcelona, it was noon, the sky was blue, the sun was shining, and the temperature was just right.

James brought the gift for Johnny and handed it to him when they met.

"Even though you don't want it, I still have to thank you for saving my life."

Johnny glanced at Amelia and then at the gift box, showing a somewhat unwilling expression.

It seemed like after receiving the gift, they would draw a clear line between them, but it was Amelia's intention, and it wouldn't be right not to accept it.

Johnny reluctantly accepted it, feeling happy inside because it was from Amelia.

"I didn't expect you to come all the way here just to see Zenith."

Johnny put the gift in the passenger seat and turned to signal them to get in the car.

He had been by Gigi's side and was already familiar with the routes in Barcelona.

He didn't bring a driver since he heard they were coming and drove to the airport himself.

Peter had arranged the car, so James and Amelia got into Johnny's car.

They thought the atmosphere in the car would be tense, but as soon as they sat down, they ran into an old acquaintance, Herb.

He happened to come out of the airport and ran into their group, raising his arm and waving it in the air with a big smile on his face, basking in the sunlight.

James felt uncomfortable with his bright smile and quickly shifted his gaze to Johnny, saying, "Aren't you going to drive?"

The man trying to start the car tried a few times but failed, furrowing his brow, "I don't know how to drive this foreign car."

As soon as he finished speaking, Herb opened the passenger door and sat on top of the gift box.

Seeing the gift box being squashed by his butt, Johnny's heart skipped a beat, and then he exploded in anger, "Get up!"

Herb, who was still sitting on his butt, took out the gift box and, trembling, said, "I didn't mean to."

With a cold expression, Johnny snatched the gift box from Herb's hand and carefully opened it.

When he saw the metallic red apple inside, he couldn't help but turn his head and look at Amelia.

Amelia smiled and nodded, as if to say, "Remember the time you saved my life with this gift."

Johnny didn't say anything, just glanced at her and pretended not to care, handing the apple to Herb.

"I thought it was something valuable, but it's just a toy."

His actions and words were all a cover-up for his previous panic.

The people in the car, except for James, thought he was being disdainful.

Especially Herb, who held the red apple, looked at it from left to right, unable to see anything special about it, and simply threw it away...

Fortunately, he only threw it in the car.

Otherwise, Johnny would have kicked Herb out of the car by now.

Herb refused to get out of the car and turned back to look at James, "I heard from my dad that you're my Aunt Ashley's son. Is that true?"

James, regretting getting into the car, flipped his eyes upward without saying a word, showing his impatience.

But Herb didn't notice and continued excitedly, "It seems to be true. My dad didn't lie to me. But oh my god, you're actually my cousin. I can't believe it!"

No wonder when James slapped him earlier, he felt a sense of familiarity, as if the person slapping him was his relative.

He didn't expect it to be true!
