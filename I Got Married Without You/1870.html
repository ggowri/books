Chapter 1870 The final trump card

Looking at the chaotic crowd in the passage, James slowly stopped in his tracks. "Holden, it's up to you now."

Holden immediately waved his hand and led his members to quickly catch up.

The black-clad men who stayed behind to cover their retreat ran and fired their guns backwards.

Holden shouted loudly. "We only kill the manipulators, we won't kill anyone else who works for Erebus. Clear? Move aside!"

In a life-or-death situation, everyone chooses to protect their own lives, especially the black-clad men who were only working for Erebus.

When the black-clad men saw Holden pass by them and head straight for the manipulators at the front, they slowly stopped shooting.

Seeing the black-clad men switch sides, Lewis, who couldn't run anymore, suddenly stopped and turned around to confront the members of S.

Lewis drew his gun and aimed directly at Holden's forehead, but his speed was no match for Holden's.

His massive body fell to the ground, and the other nine manipulators, seeing Lewis dead, also stopped running.

"Everyone, open fire!"

The manipulators of Erebus were also well-trained.

As soon as Lewis died, 1-7 took command.

Following 1-7's orders, the nine manipulators raised their guns and opened fire on the members of S.

At the moment Lewis fell, they were already prepared to die together, but Holden had no intention of dying with them.

"Save yourselves, kill them!" With Holden's command, the members of S also went crazy, aiming at their opponents and firing desperately.

The intense battle between the two sides lasted for about five minutes before all nine manipulators were finally defeated.

After they fell, James walked over the bodies of the fallen manipulators.

10 down. 39 more to go.

James looked towards the escape passage that led to Zone B. "Let's go to Zone B--"

The monitoring of the escape passage in Zone A was not destroyed, so the manipulators in Zone B could naturally monitor the situation here.

Upon hearing that James and his group were coming to Zone B, the manipulators prepared themselves for the worst.

"Be ready, as soon as they enter the blast range, detonate!"

1-8, the main person in charge of Zone B, planned to blow up all the members of S in the escape passage.

However, James suddenly looked up.

With his eyes filled with killing intent, he looked at the monitor, and the screen instantly went blank.

In the blink of an eye, all the monitors were shut off!

After cursing in anger, 1-8 gave the order again. "Detonate, block their path!"

Unfortunately, just as they were about to detonate, James and his group had already returned along the same route.

Before long, 1-8 saw James' figure appear on the monitor at the entrance of Zone B.

Unlike Lewis' determination to die together, 1-8 clearly valued his own life more.

Before the battle even began, he sought help from Matty. "Boss, Zone A has fallen, and James has also reached Zone B. What should we do now?"

Matty, who could see all the monitors, was well aware that Zone A had fallen, but he chose not to say anything.

Clearly, in the current situation, even if the manipulators in Zones B and C were to die together with the members of S, it would only buy them some time.

To minimize casualties, there were only two options: either break through the enemy's hackers and blow up James' head, or reveal their trump card and negotiate.

However, the first option would clearly result in a prolonged stalemate.

Matty no longer had time to waste on the detonation program. He could only...

Although he had previously claimed to use Amelia as the final trump card to silence all the manipulators, Matty didn't actually have that intention.

But now...

Unable to watch himself be defeated, Matty left with a final remark of "Hold on, I'll be right back," and quickly headed to the escape room below the biochemistry area.

At the same time, James adopted a different approach.

He commanded Holden and Emelyn to enter Zones B and C respectively, while he himself headed to the biochemistry area.

Because he knew that there was an escape room in the biochemistry area that no one could break into.

If he were Matty, he would definitely place his final trump card there.

That's why, after James entered, he didn't immediately go to find Amelia.
