Chapter 1897 Zenith and Gigi’s story

Later, Zenith followed his heart and willingly became a substitute for someone else, as entrusted in his dream.

He became Zack, liked the things Zack liked, and fully embraced Zack's identity.

However, there were many times when he questioned himself whether he was Zack or Zenith.

Years later, Gigi, who had already given birth to two children with Zenith, suddenly realized that he was not Zack.

Like encountering a ferocious beast, she fled from him and refused to see him again.

Zenith no longer questioned his identity as Zack or Zenith, but knelt before Gigi, repeatedly telling her that he was Zack, a Zack who had lost his memory, and begged her not to leave him.

At that moment, Gigi had already recognized who the person in front of her truly was.

However, she had unknowingly fallen in love with Zenith, someone other than Zack.

It was a terrifying realization.

She felt guilty towards Zack, as she had promised to love only him for the rest of her life.

How could she easily fall in love with someone else and even have children with them? What should she do about her Zack?

Gigi was tormented by guilt and felt that her life was not happy.

However, having Zenith by her side in a different form of Zack brought her a sense of warmth.

Life was always complicated and full of entanglements, but one must continue living.

Gigi still prayed in front of the church, hoping to meet Zack again in the next life, promising not to mistreat him this time.

Gigi's life remained at this point.

At that time, she still saw Zenith as Zack, and Zenith, because of that dream, loved Gigi openly.

They were still in a sweet and loving relationship.

When James and Amelia saw them, Zenith was adding a little honey to the honey water and stirring it with a spoon.

He handed it to Gigi and said, "Pay attention to your diet these days."

Gigi took the glass made of glass and placed it to her lips.

After taking a small sip, she smiled at Zenith and said, "Zack, honey water made by you is always delicious."

For Zenith, every time Gigi called him Zack, his heart would tremble uncomfortably.

However, he accepted it willingly, saying, "If you like it, then next time you feel unwell, come to me and I'll make it for you."

Gigi smiled, stood up, leaned over the bamboo table, and kissed Zenith's cheek.

Zenith lowered his gaze and continued to brew tea, saying, "If I didn't have a religious heart, I wouldn't have met you. If I hadn't met you..."

"Then I wouldn't have to be Zack for my whole life." Zenith didn't say those words, but he smiled bitterly.

He covered the teapot and reached out to touch Gigi's face, saying, "Since I met you, you must not escape from me in the future."

Gigi casually replied, "I've finally found you. How could I run away? I will stay by your side and cling to you for the rest of my life. We'll have two more children, and then you will never leave me again..."

Zenith knew that she wanted to have children for Zack.

He couldn't help but smile bitterly, but he agreed.

Just as he finished speaking, he saw two people standing outside the door.

The man was handsome and noble, and the woman was beautiful.

It could be said that they were the most attractive people Zenith had ever seen.

Especially the man, with his noble appearance and elegant temperament, even when standing casually, he became the most dazzling scenery in the winter.

Looking at the man, Zenith remembered what Johnny had said, that Zack sacrificed himself to protect someone.

Although he didn't know the reason, Zenith could guess from bits and pieces of information that the person Zack protected must be someone very important.

And the man standing outside the door must be the person Zack willingly sacrificed himself for, the person Gigi resented and hated.

Although Gigi resented that person, Zenith couldn't ignore their presence.

He let go of his hand that was caressing Gigi's cheek and looked up, asking, "Are you here to find me or to find Gigi?"
