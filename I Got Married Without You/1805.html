Chapter 1805 Gigi attached Amelia

Amelia looked at Eva and noticed the same expression in her eyes.

She slowly lowered her gaze and said, "Let's talk about it when he comes back."

Paul caught on to the implied meaning and quickly smiled, "Amelia, you're so kind. Let me get you some more soup."

Paul served the soup and placed it in front of Amelia.

Before she could take a sip, Johnny walked in. "I ran into the Lawrence family's three brothers downstairs. Are they here to deliver the DNA test results?"

Amelia confirmed, and Johnny wondered how the elder Mr. Lawrence knew that she resembled his first love.

Amelia explained that Mr. Lawrence had seen her when she was substituting for her sister on a project, and he had remarked on her resemblance to his first love.

She then handed the test report to Eva and added more details for Johnny.

Johnny was still puzzled, as Dylan's explanation seemed flawless and he couldn't find any discrepancies.

He asked Amelia, "Have you decided to return to the Lawrence family?"

Just as Amelia was about to answer, Gigi burst into the room. "Amelia!"

"You're awake?"

Seeing Gigi, Johnny instinctively furrowed his brow.

He hadn't informed her, but she had come to the hospital on her own.

During this time, Gigi had also lost weight and looked even paler.

She had wanted to rest at home, but she couldn't stop worrying about Zack's life or death, so she had come to check if Amelia had woken up.

"She's awake. Why didn't anyone tell me?"

She looked around at the people sitting in the chairs, feeling abandoned.

"I didn't inform you because I saw you weren't feeling well," Johnny explained, and without saying more, he tried to lead her away.

Gigi, however, shook off his hand and walked up to Amelia, her eyes red and filled with tears.

Seeing Gigi in this state, Amelia thought of the image of Zack being swallowed by a snake, feeling both scared and heartbroken.

Gigi had already sensed something from Amelia's expression, but she still asked hopefully, "Amelia, tell me, is Zack alive like James?"

Amelia didn't know how to say it, and her eyes, filled with bloodshot veins, couldn't even look at Gigi.

Seeing that Amelia was hesitant to speak, Gigi quickly raised her pale hand and placed it on Amelia's shoulder.

"Don't be afraid. Just tell me what you know. I won't be angry, and I won't blame you." Gigi had a faint smile on her face, appearing very stable emotionally.

Just as Amelia was about to speak, Johnny stepped forward and once again grabbed Gigi's arm.

"Her answer is the same as mine. Don't pressure her. Let me take you back to rest."

It's unclear which words upset Gigi, but she angrily shook off Johnny's hand.

"I just came to ask her. Why are you pressuring her?"

"I know you didn't pressure her. I'm worried about your health. I..."

Before Johnny could finish his sentence, Gigi, who had suddenly erupted with emotion, angrily shouted at Amelia.

"I'm asking you a question. Why aren't you saying anything?"

Amelia had known before leaving that Gigi was emotionally unstable and had a nervous disorder, but she was still worried that Gigi wouldn't be able to handle it.

However, if she didn't provide a clear answer, Gigi's heart would never be at peace. So... it's better to get it over with.

Amelia clenched her fists and, with a pained expression, looked at Gigi. "Zack is dead." Upon hearing this answer, Gigi calmed down and didn't look as hostile as before.

After a while, she suddenly let out a bitter laugh. "James is still alive, but he's dead. Is James' life more valuable than Zack's?"

These words pierced Amelia's heart, causing her discomfort.

After all, Zack had died trying to save James.

"I'm sorry," Amelia said, not knowing what else to say.

"What's the use of an apology?" Gigi, standing by the bedside, gradually lost her clear expression and became clouded with resentment.

"Zack died because of your husband!"

She was stating a fact, and Amelia couldn't argue with her. She thought that Gigi would calm down after a few accusations.

However, as Amelia lowered her gaze, the scalding hot soup suddenly splashed onto her face.

It hurt so much...

Amelia couldn't even speak.

As her vision blurred from the splattered soup, she saw Gigi pick up the porridge from the table and pour it over her.
