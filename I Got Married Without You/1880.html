Chapter 1880 The darkness is end

After everyone had left, the bombed and charred forest was filled with the smell of burnt wood.

Only Holden and Emelyn remained, along with two helicopters.

"You should go too," Holden said. Upon hearing his words, Emelyn stood frozen in place.

After a long time, she finally found her lost voice.

"Brother, you said that if you didn't come back safely from Erebus, I should find someone else to marry.

Now that you're here, is that still what you want?" In the past, Emelyn had always spoken in a tentative tone, afraid to confess her feelings directly.

But in this moment, she seemed to have let go of many reservations and confessed her love for Holden.

Holden, instead of avoiding the topic, sighed helplessly. "I am Bernard's descendant. Once Matty finds out, he won't let me go."

According to Master Davies' inhumane methods, he would definitely reveal Holden's identity once Matty came knocking.

If Emelyn followed him, she would have a bleak future.

As Holden thought this, Emelyn reached out with her fair and delicate hand, grabbing Holden's wrist and looking at him earnestly.

"Brother, don't worry about me. I willingly chose to follow you. No matter what kind of days await us, I will accept them."

She had liked Holden for many years, liked him to the point where she was willing to sacrifice her life if he took a step forward.

Looking at the unabashed love in Emelyn's eyes, Holden, despite his hesitation, felt it. It was just his concerns that made him hesitate.

"Brother, have you thought it through? If you agree, we can take one of the helicopters. If you don't, I'll turn around and marry someone else."

She pointed at the two helicopters facing different directions, as if indicating two paths for Holden to choose.

Holden stared at the two helicopters, hesitating for a long time before forcefully pushing away Emelyn's hand.

"Go and marry someone else."

In that instant, Emelyn, who had been pushed away, felt a wave of disappointment.

Her heart sank, descending into hell, as she took a step back. With each step she retreated, she felt disappointed in Holden's indifference.

Finally... she reached the position of the helicopter, but Holden didn't take a step forward. Her disappointment grew even stronger.

She was the kind of person who could love and let go.

After being rejected, she simply turned around and left.

She grabbed the helicopter door, leapt inside, and started it up without any hesitation or pause.

As the helicopter was about to take off, the cabin suddenly sank, and a tall figure sat down.

The disappointment in Emelyn's heart disappeared in an instant, replaced by a hint of excitement.

"Didn't you tell me to go marry someone else? Why did you follow me?"

Holden raised his gaze and looked at Emelyn's face, slowly lowering his head.

"I... I'm afraid... of you marrying someone else."

He didn't know if this was love, but whenever he thought of Emelyn marrying someone else, he felt very sad.

Emelyn was somewhat shocked, as if she rarely heard such words from Holden's mouth.

"If you hadn't come up just now, I would have made you regret it for the rest of your life."

Holden knew that Emelyn could say and do what she promised. He couldn't help but feel fortunate that he had followed his instincts earlier.

Emelyn came out of the cockpit and boldly sat next to Holden, then pouted. "Kiss me, as an invitation for you to be my boyfriend."

Upon hearing this blunt request, Holden's eyes darted around, avoiding direct eye contact with Emelyn's red lips.

He looked all around the cabin but couldn't bring himself to meet her gaze. After waiting for a long time, Emelyn frowned.

"I'm not shy, why are you?" Holden said directly, "We're too familiar with each other."

"Three seconds," Emelyn counted down, and Holden, feeling awkward, leaned forward and lightly kissed Emelyn's lips.

Emelyn then smiled and hooked her lips, hugging Holden's waist and nestling into his embrace, tilting her chin up to ask him, "How does it feel?"

"Too fast, I didn't feel anything."

"I'll go find someone else then," Holden grabbed Emelyn, who was about to get up, and approached her with some embarrassment.

"Let's try again..." Five seconds later, Emelyn asked again, "Do you feel anything now?"

"No."

The first love couple in the helicopter wasn't very happy, but the other helicopter quickly arrived at the hospital.

Paul, who had received the notification, realized that the detonation program hadn't been activated and quickly rushed to the hospital with the remaining people.

The meeting of the two groups at the hospital represented the end of all the darkness, and what followed was a bright future.
