Chapter 1197 Amelia, you are really lucky to have escaped (2)

Just a sideways glance from Gloria was enough to make Amelia's heart tremble uncontrollably. Her gaze was so poisonous.

Amelia had never seen such a terrifying and vicious gaze before. Its impact was so strong that she instinctively hid behind James.

If she hadn't died back then, would she have fallen into Gloria's hands and been tortured to death like James's childhood friend?

Because of her small movement, Gloria noticed her and his blood-red gaze slowly moved to the side.

When Gloria saw Amelia's face, the hatred in his eyes gradually turned into evil as he smirked and said, "You're not dead?"

Gloria thought she had died a long time ago, but she never expected to see Amelia again one day, and even holding hands with James to come see her.

Gloria stared at her and sneered, "You're really lucky. You faked your death and escaped, otherwise I would have made you taste the pain of being tortured to death."

She guessed right. Gloria did plan to do that, but why did he have to destroy the person James cared about?

Amelia was just accompanying James, and she couldn't speak even if she wanted to. She just held onto his arm and stood obediently on the side.

Seeing that she didn't speak, Gloria's vicious gaze shifted back to James. When she saw the wedding ring on his left ring finger, her expression turned cold.

"You're married?"

The man who used to hide Amelia away now held her hand in the open and showed Gloria their wedding rings.

"Yes, I didn't do as you wished. In the end, I married her."

Gloria's gloomy eyes slowly filled with anger as he sought an accurate answer.

She threw the book in her hand and raised her dry, burnt hand, pointing fiercely at Amelia.

"You promised your brother that you would marry Emily, how could you go against his dying wish and marry her?!"

The voice, so angry that it was deafening, came through the window...

Not only did the volume not decrease, but it also carried a cruel desire for them all to go to hell.

"Have you been bewitched by this woman and completely forgotten how good your brother was to you?!"

Gloria looked like she had gone mad, glaring with her sunken eyes, and hysterically shouting at James.

"If you have any conscience left, divorce her and marry Emily, otherwise your brother will never rest in peace!!!"
