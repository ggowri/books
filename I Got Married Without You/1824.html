Chapter 1824 Extreme love and hatred

Amelia looked at Matty in this state, feeling a chill in her heart.

His hatred had transformed from seeking personal revenge to seeking revenge against the general public.

This was why Erebus offered a variety of deadly experiences, to satisfy his bloodthirst. Amelia was well aware that Matty's mind, twisted by hatred, now saw human lives as insignificant.

She remained silent, simply observing the old man with white hair and bloodshot eyes, slowly coming to understand a truth: sometimes loving someone too much is not a good thing.

"So, Amelia, do you think I'm wrong?" Matty put away his memories of the past and looked calmly at the face in front of him, so similar to Sophia's.

"You shouldn't sacrifice so many lives for the sake of revenge," Amelia paused for a moment, then continued to explain, "You should find the person who killed Sophia and seek revenge, rather than involve innocent people."

"I can't find them!" Matty's voice rose with agitation. "Do you know how that feels? I want revenge, but I can't find my enemies. This helplessness is driving me crazy!"

"Will you disband Erebus once you find your enemies?" This question calmed Matty down, and he stared at Amelia, hesitated for a moment, and then nodded gently. "No one wants to live in hatred forever."

Once he had taken his revenge, he could follow Sophia and be free forever.

Previously, Holden had mentioned not knowing if the person behind Erebus had a grudge against James or Master Davies.

Now that James had joined Erebus and become the mastermind, it meant that the person behind Erebus didn't have a grudge against James.

This left only Master Davies, but since Master Davies was of the same generation as her mother, how could he have been involved in Sophia's murder?

She felt that the problem lay with the S organization, so she subtly probed Matty for information about the S organization.

"I heard that the S organization was not established for a long time, and Sophia's death happened many years ago."

As she asked this question, Amelia was not only inquiring about Erebus, but also trying to ascertain if Matty knew about James's identity as S.

"How do you know the time when the S organization was established?" Matty asked, and Amelia breathed a sigh of relief, not detecting any falsehood in his words.

"A friend who entered with me is the vice president of the Eurasian Union Chamber of Commerce, and he knows a little bit about it."

"Is that so?" Matty smiled and nodded. "I know him, Johnny. He's a good kid, willing to sacrifice his life for you."

Hearing about the bloody events in Erebus, Amelia's anger was difficult to contain. "You should be grateful that someone saved him, otherwise I wouldn't be sitting here now."

Matty spread his hands in apology. "If I had known earlier that you were my granddaughter, you and your friend wouldn't have had any trouble in Erebus."

Amelia ignored him and quickly returned to the previous topic. "The timeline of the S organization's establishment doesn't match up. The killer of Sophia should not be from the S organization."

"That's impossible," Matty denied outright. "The original S was called 'darkness' and has been around for a long time. Later, for some reason, all the members of darkness were sacrificed, and that's when it was renamed to S."

Hearing this, Amelia was stunned.

James had said that Master Davies established S for a woman, who was James's biological mother, Ashley, the illegitimate daughter of The Clark family.

If this was true, then Master Davies had been deceiving James all along. If Master Davies had been deceiving James since childhood, then Sophia's death might be related to Master Davies.

Even if he wasn't directly involved, it was likely connected to the previous organization. The question was, what was the reason behind it?
