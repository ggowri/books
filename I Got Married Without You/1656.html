Chapter 1656 Only 30% of her love

When Kate saw the bloodstains on Paul's socks at the hotel, she was startled and immediately called for a doctor. Paul, who showed no reaction no matter how the doctor treated him, curled up on the couch, closed his eyes, and sulked alone. Seeing her son in such a state, Kate naturally felt heartbroken. She sat down beside him and tried to comfort him in a gentle voice.

"Paul, if she doesn't want to marry you, just let it go. Don't ruin your health over a woman like her."

Paul didn't want to listen. He turned away, facing the couch.

Looking at his tall figure from behind, Kate sighed heavily.

"If she loved you, she wouldn't have hurt you so recklessly."

Paul got a little angry and clenched his fists.

"What do you know?"

Eva did love him, just not as much. Maybe only 30% of her love, which was why she didn't hesitate to hurt him.

"I may not understand, but if it were me, I would never go back to Eva."

Paul felt a bit wronged and reached for a pillow, hugging it tightly.

"Mom, I don't want to go back to her either, but I love her so much and can't let go."

Since he became aware of his love for Eva, he couldn't control his emotions anymore.

He missed the old Eva, who would forgive him no matter what he did wrong or said if he begged her.

He also missed when she used to curl up in his arms like a little kitten, asking him if he had feelings for her.

He missed that Eva and wanted her back.

But he had been begging her for two years, and she still didn't want to come back...

Paul's nose tingled, and he instinctively covered his eyes.

"Two years ago, when she asked for a breakup, I shouldn't have agreed."

If he hadn't agreed, that Eva from back then would never have left.

Hearing the choked voice, Kate's heart sank subconsciously. Did he love Eva this much?

Paul didn't give an answer, but his actions showed her that he loved Eva to the extreme.

Seeing Paul lying in the hotel, refusing to go back to the country and refusing to eat, Kate also felt sad.

"Paul, it's pointless for you to act like this. You can't win Ms. Miller back, so why torture yourself?"

He was causing a scene here, but she couldn't see it. Why torment himself?

Paul ignored her, looking outside when his eyes were open and sleeping when they were closed, like a walking corpse.

Looking at Paul, who couldn't eat or drink, Kate's heart grew even more resentful towards Eva.

But deep down, her subconscious told her that it was because of her interference that Paul and Eva ended up like this.

If it weren't for her, Paul might have already married smoothly, so why would he lie in a foreign country waiting to die?

Feeling increasingly guilty, Kate asked the bodyguard to watch over Paul and went to the hospital to find Eva with her bag.

Coincidentally, Eva had just come out of the ward when she met Kate. She originally wanted to avoid her, but Kate directly stopped her.

"Ms. Miller, would you have time to have a cup of coffee?"

Compared to their first meeting, Kate was much more polite this time, but Eva still refused.

"I need to buy a wheelchair. I don't have time."

Kate took a step forward and blocked her way.

"It's just a few words. You don't have to resist so much."

Eva stopped and raised her cold gaze, looking at the wealthy woman in front of her.

"Mrs. Evans, I rejected your son. You should take this opportunity to cut off his feelings instead of coming to chat with me."

Compared to their first meeting when she was humble, Eva was more sharp-tongued this time.

She really didn't want to marry Paul, so she no longer pretended to be polite. Was she still playing hard to get?

Kate had reservations about this, but her heart leaned more towards the former.

"Ms. Miller, my initial intention was for you to reject my son, not to ruin his life."
