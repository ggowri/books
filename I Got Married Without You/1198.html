Chapter 1198 I'll kill Amelia as soon as I get out of prison

At this moment, Amelia finally realized how difficult it was for James, who was forced to marry Emily, back then.

She looked at his profile and saw that he didn't react at all, just coldly watching Gloria go crazy, so she didn't say much.

Stella had said that Gloria had a mental illness, and a little stimulation would make her behavior abnormal. What was happening now was probably a symptom of her illness.

Gloria cursed for a long time until James raised his hand and the prison guard came in and gave her an injection in her arm. Her emotions gradually calmed down.

But she still refused to give up and accused James with weak and vicious words:

"You knew that your brother's wish was to marry Emily, but you didn't help him fulfill it."

James stood outside the door, expressionless, looking through the clean glass at the woman who was tied to a wheelchair and had no strength to struggle.

"If my brother knew that I had feelings for someone else, he would never have forced me to marry Emily. All of this is just you binding me and shackling me with moral constraints."

Gloria sneered, and as she laughed, her eyelids, which were wrinkled and drooping, suddenly lifted, revealing eyes that were red with bloodshot veins.

Amelia saw those eyes under the eyelids, which were so red that they were filled with bloodshot veins, and her heart trembled with fear again.

James's mother was so terrifying with just one look, and she didn't know how terrifying her methods of torturing people could be.

Sensing that Amelia was a little scared, James lowered his head and comforted her, "Don't be afraid, I'm here. She can't hurt you for the rest of her life..."

Amelia nodded slightly, tightened her grip on his arm again, as if getting closer to him would give her a sense of security.

The sweet and harmonious scene of the two people, in Gloria's eyes, was like a needle and a thorn, making her uncomfortable and wanting to tear apart the arms of the two people holding each other!

Why is it that her son died, while Ashley's son can have everything - the position of heir, beloved person, and a happy and fulfilling life? Why is it that her son is lying in the cold hell?!!!

Gloria gritted her teeth and glared fiercely at James, "Just wait, when I get out of prison, I will definitely tear your wife's body into pieces!"

It was this fear of being stalked by a hungry wolf that made Amelia scared, as if Gloria would break through the glass and come out to strangle her in the next second.
