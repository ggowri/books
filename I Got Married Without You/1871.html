Chapter 1871 Revenge for Zack

James's footsteps came to a slow halt as he intended to confront Matty directly in the biohazard area for negotiations.

However, he did not expect to find one of the manipulated individuals who had previously tampered with the seventh round game hiding there.

In an instant, James's cold, snow-like eyes locked onto the person's head.

After the person's skull was opened, James realized that someone had intentionally manipulated the seventh round game.

Zack, who could have survived, was sacrificed to test him.

James was determined to personally avenge this blood feud.

With his gloved hand, James holstered his firearm and slowly pulled out a golden knife.

In a swift motion, he charged towards the manipulated individual designated as 1-9 in Sector B.

Caught off guard, 1-9 didn't even have time to recognize the approaching man before his throat was swiftly slit.

Clutching his bleeding throat, 1-9 stared with eyes filled with unwillingness, fixated on James, who had already sheathed his knife.

At first, 1-9 couldn't understand why James only killed him, but in his final moments, he realized it was because he had killed Zack.

After 1-9 fell, James calmly crouched down, and Johnny, who had followed him into the biohazard area, mistakenly thought James was going to strike again.

However, James simply used the clothes of the deceased to wipe the blood off his knife.

Johnny rolled his eyes in disbelief and then raised his gun, shooting and killing the person who attempted to shoot James.

"I saved your life. How do you plan to thank me?"

James, still calmly wiping the blood off his knife, heard Johnny's voice but didn't bother to look up, lightly parting his lips. "If you don't mind, once I rescue your sister-in-law, let's have a meal together as a couple."

"I shouldn't have saved you!" Johnny gritted his teeth, holstered his gun, and angrily retreated with his men.

However, halfway through the retreat, he stopped.

Amelia hadn't been rescued yet, how could he just leave like this?

With that thought in mind, Johnny suppressed his anger and returned to the battlefield.

Holden and Emelyn, who had finished off the last manipulated individuals, quickly arrived in the biohazard area to join James. "Mr. Shawn, all 49 manipulated individuals have been eliminated."

James slowly stood up, sheathing his golden knife. "What about the others?"

"We have them locked up."

After putting away his knife, James raised his cold and solemn gaze, looking towards the remaining surveillance in the biohazard area.

"Matty, release Amelia, and I will release your people."

Matty, who had just reached the escape room, slowed his pace upon hearing the conversation through the surveillance.

Through the thick glass window, Matty looked at Amelia sitting on the bed, hugging her knees.

As his gaze fell upon her face, which bore a striking resemblance to Sophia's, Matty's expression gradually became complex.

However, this complexity only lasted for a moment before he immediately composed himself, changed his direction, and walked towards the control panel outside the escape room.

He pressed the button for the upper area's broadcast and responded to James. "The conditions are not enough."

Matty, who no longer valued the lives of the other manipulated individuals, only wanted to know who the founder of S was.

"What if I add the founder of S? Will that be enough?"
