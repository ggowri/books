Chapter 1875 Where is Master Davies

James nodded slightly. Bernard was just an uncultured street thug, how could he compete with the powerful Matty?

Even if Bernard united many thugs and formed Darkness, it would still take a long time to gather enough power to kill him.

"But Bernard was killed by Darkness' second-in-command before he had a chance to seek revenge."

Matty understood, but he didn't realize that he had been living in Bernard's plan all along.

Bernard had killed Zelene early on, so Matty wouldn't suspect a stranger's death.

He then manipulated all the evidence of Sophia's betrayal and directed it towards the Wilson family's other enemies.

Then, using Matty's time spent investigating, Bernard expanded the Darkness organization and used its power to seek revenge.

It was a good plan, but unfortunately, Bernard's life was cut short by his own friend.

Matty felt some relief thinking about Bernard's fate. But what about himself?

He was involved in all of this.

No, he caused all of this.

If he hadn't killed Daley, Zelene wouldn't have had the opportunity to frame him, and Bernard wouldn't have taken his anger out on Sophia, let alone in that way.

Matty found it difficult to accept and took a step back. If it weren't for the console supporting him, his old body would have collapsed.

"It's impossible!"

Matty still refused to believe that Sophia's death was because of him.

If that were true, then what face did he have to continue living?

"If you hadn't killed Daley, how could you have killed Sophia?"

In Matty's cold-blooded heart for decades, guilt suddenly filled him.

"Daley tried to kill me first, that's why I..."

If it weren't for Daley almost killing him with that shot, Matty wouldn't have killed him in return.

Amelia, who had been silently listening, interjected when she heard this.

"If you hadn't interfered between Sophia and Daley, why would Daley want to kill you?"

Matty raised his bloodshot eyes and looked at the woman standing behind the bulletproof glass.

Her serene and beautiful face was almost identical to Sophia's when she was young.

Looking at that face, Matty thought of his first encounter with Sophia, when he fell in love at first sight.

"When I fell for Sophia, Daley was just her childhood friend..."

Matty's eyes, now wrinkled with age, filled with tears.

"It was Daley who noticed my interest in Sophia and confessed to her first..."

"By the time I knew what love was, Daley had already taken the lead..."

Matty choked up, feeling aggrieved. "I was just one step too late."

He was one step too late, missing out on Sophia's first love.

He was one step too late, causing misunderstandings between them.

Matty thought about all the grievances and pain caused by these events, and trembled with the inability to accept them.

He suppressed his guilt, discomfort, regret, and longing, and slowly shifted his gaze to James across from him.

"What would you do if you were in my place?"

In matters of the heart, they should be the same kind of person.

But James didn't respond, he just looked at him indifferently.

Because for the former James, besides taking Amelia away, there was also the desire to make her happy.

"I'm not at fault." James didn't answer, Matty answered for himself, affirming that he wasn't at fault.

He wiped away the tears in his eyes and stared at Amelia with determination and unwavering strength.

"It was Daley's jealousy that drove him to snatch Sophia away from me!"

"If it weren't for him, I wouldn't have let Sophia follow him, I would have fought to get her back!"

Amelia looked at Matty, who suddenly learned the truth and became frantic, and for a moment, she didn't know whether to feel pity or resentment.

Like James, she fell into silence and watched as Matty spoke to himself, denying any wrongdoing and regaining composure.

"Who told you all of this?" Finally getting to the point, James gradually regained his composure.

"The founder of the S organization."

"Who is he?"

James glanced at Matty with indifference, his voice cold and devoid of warmth. "He is Zelene's nephew, Master Davies."

Master Davies...

Matty's face became serious, and James' voice, devoid of any warmth, echoed in his ears.

"Master Davies' father was also involved in Sophia’s death."

"Where is he?"
