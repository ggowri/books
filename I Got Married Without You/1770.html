Chapter 1770 How could you spare her?

Amelia pushed away Brandon's hand in disgust. "Thanks to you, she's dead too."

Sumasa starved to death on the streets of England, Skender ended up in the slums, and Amelia became an orphan, all because of Brandon.

He caused the separation of the three of them, and they each went down a miserable path. Meanwhile, Brandon raised Emily to be a socialite and paved the way for her.

After recovering from his surprise, Brandon reached for Amelia's hand again. "How could she be dead? What happened?"

Amelia pushed him away again, "Stop pretending to care about them and answer my question. Why did you help Sarah back then?"

She knew how Sumasa was killed by Brandon, but she didn't know why he helped Sumasa.

Did he know from the beginning that Sarah's identity was Sumasa, and that she had a huge inheritance, which was why he did it?

Despite Amelia's attitude, Brandon didn't get angry. Instead, he calmly studied Amelia's face. "I saw your mother walking the streets of England with a completely different face, and I felt sorry for her. So I brought her home, fed her, gave her a place to stay, and encouraged her to keep going."

"Later, when my company was on the verge of bankruptcy, I met her at the airport. She had plastic surgery and I didn't even recognize her. It was only when she told me that I realized she was the person I had helped."

"To thank me, she gave me money to save my company, the Moore Group. I'm grateful to her, so I married her, had a child with her, and wanted to live a good life with her."

Amelia believed the first two, but not the last one. Because Sumasa had said in a video that Brandon only married her for her money, and that the person he loved was his childhood friend Eliza. He had a family with her while also having a family with Eliza outside.

Brandon used her mother's assets and abilities to make the Moore Group a giant, and then kicked Sumasa out, who had helped him, to the curb. He even bought a big ship and tricked her into taking a trip with her child.

Then, on the ship, he pushed the three of them off. He didn't need Sumasa anymore, and that was when Brandon tore off his mask of hypocrisy.

Brandon didn't try to defend himself too much. He just stared at Amelia's face and couldn't help but reach out to touch her. "Your mother's original face was so beautiful..."

Sumasa had kept her past, identity, and appearance a secret from Brandon. Otherwise, when Amelia first entered the Moore Group, he would have recognized her.

Amelia stepped back to avoid Brandon's touch, and the disgust and hatred in her eyes were clear.

Brandon saw it, but didn't get angry. He just smiled. "By the way, I named you Elvira."

He walked up to Amelia and, as a father, patted her shoulder. "I wanted you to grow up in warmth, so I named you Elvira. But then..."

"Later, you got blinded by greed and selfishness, and you destroyed me," Amelia cut him off coldly, leaving Brandon speechless.

And he was unable to argue with the truth Amelia had spoken.

As Brandon silently stared at Amelia's face, his phone in his pocket suddenly vibrated. It was Emily calling.

Brandon saw the incoming call on the screen, avoided Amelia's gaze, and turned to answer the phone.

Emily immediately asked, "Dad, did you kill Amelia?"

Brandon turned back to look at Amelia, hesitated, and said, "Emily, she hasn't done anything to harm you. Maybe we shouldn't kill her..."

"Dad, what are you saying?" Emily was furious, standing up from the sofa.

"If it wasn't for her, James would have married me a long time ago. She stole my sweetheart and even caused me to have plastic surgery. How can you spare her?"
