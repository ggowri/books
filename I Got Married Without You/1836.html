Chapter 1836: Brief Reunion

Once Matty finished speaking, he left.

His departure activated a countdown in James' brain from the implanted chip, serving as a warning.

The countdown, along with Amelia's potential pregnancy, was a threat - if James broke their agreement, Matty would take drastic action.

Regardless of James' hints or Amelia's suspicions, Matty's goal was their permanent separation.

Matty could easily terminate Amelia's pregnancy at any time, and the chip kept James trapped in Erebus, unable to protect Amelia or their unborn child.

So, his priority was to escape and confront Matty. Until then, their reunion was destined to be brief.

Overwhelmed, James hid his pain, yanking off his mask.

Amelia, seated at the center, did the same.

As their eyes met, a blush crept into their eyes.

They remained silent until the countdown broke it.

James, shadowing his face, walked over to her.

"You...," he began, voice tight, "You said we'd never see each other again. I believed you."

Amelia glanced at him, his figure hidden by the light.

"Don't we all say things we don't mean when we argue? Are you still upset?"

James nearly teared up at her words but held it back.

"Why would I be upset?" Amelia shot back, her neck rigid.

Then, she gestured for him to sit, pulling out a chair. James steadied himself and sat down.

As he complied, Amelia said softly, "Cameras are watching. Let's watch our words. I'll fill you in, alright?"

Her voice was comforting, like a gentle stream, unlocking his emotions.

James gazed at Amelia, consenting with a nod. He leaned in closer to her, feeling her warm breath tickle his ear, a reminder of his humanity.

Amelia, unaware of his thoughts, whispered, "I won't tell how I met Matty. Let's cut to chase. You know Matty, I've sent Erebus's location to Andrew. You're free to leave Erebus as 1-1. Rejoin Andrew, do what you want. I'll stay here, with access to all areas. If you return, I'll let you in."

Amelia's plan was impeccable, leaving James impressed. Her eyes sparkled.

But she likely didn't know Matty was watching everything.

Would cunning Matty let her expose Erebus? He'd likely intercept her message.

Still, Amelia was brave and loved him deeply, yet he was about to disappoint her again.

"Why are you silent?" Amelia asked, stepping back and studying his handsome face.

James listened to the countdown, his expression shifting.

"Amelia, don't fret over this."

"But... if he's controlling you, how can you leave without my help?" Amelia asked, puzzled.

"He's not controlling me. I... can come and go from Erebus," James replied, gripping his lap, his softer tone still leaving Amelia cold.

Shocked, she looked at James, feeling a sudden distance between them.
