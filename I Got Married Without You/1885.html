Chapter 1885 Lucky Eva

When their private jet arrived at the hospital, Eva had already come out of the delivery room.

Paul and Eva's relatives had rushed over to congratulate them, one wave after another.

When James and Amelia entered the hospital room, it was filled with flowers and gift boxes, most of which were sent by the Evans family.

Eva was lying on the delivery bed, eating porridge that Paul had fed her, while staring at the baby Rebecca was holding.

Rebecca was holding the girl, while Kate was holding the boy, and Eric was sitting next to them.

Dominic was also there, sitting across from Eric.

They were so focused that they didn't notice the two of them entering, until Eva noticed and called out, causing everyone to turn around.

"Amelia!" Seeing Amelia, Eva immediately smiled and waved at her.

"Come over here!"

Amelia smiled back, holding a small gift box in her hand, and walked over slowly.

Perhaps because Amelia was pregnant, Dominic had nothing to criticize.

When Amelia called him "grandpa," he responded.

His demeanor was neither cold nor indifferent, as if he didn't care, but much better than before when he strongly opposed it.

After dealing with a few elders, Amelia greeted Rebecca and received a gracious response, allowing her to go and talk to Eva first.

Amelia knew that Rebecca didn't care about such formalities, so she nodded with a smile, turned around, and quickly walked towards Eva lying on the delivery bed.

"Eva, how are you? Are you okay? Is there anything uncomfortable?"

Amelia's first concern was Eva's well-being, while everyone else was focused on the baby.

Especially Dominic, who initially didn't plan to come when he heard that Eva had given birth to a girl.

He only came after a few minutes when the boy was born.

Although they had already discovered during the prenatal check-ups that there were two babies, James didn't tell the couple because of his recent issues and Amelia's disappearance.

Now that Amelia had safely returned and showed such concern, Eva felt as if her heart was filled with honey, sweet and warm.

"I'm fine, nothing at all. And you?"

"I'm fine too, don't worry."

After Amelia responded with a smile, she reached out and held Eva's hand, using her other hand to tuck Eva's slightly messy hair behind her ear.

"Eva, you've worked hard."

In the blink of an eye, Eva had become the mother of two children, which was great and she had finally overcome the hardships.

Seeing the emotions in Amelia's eyes, Eva knew that she was feeling sorry for her, and couldn't help but feel a surge of emotion, her nose tingling and tears welling up. "Marrying the right person and having children is a natural thing, there's nothing hard about it."

In her life, she was actually quite happy.

She had a husband who loved her, doted on her, and was willing to change for her.

Although she was the owner of an entertainment venue and didn't lack money, she had also married into a wealthy family.

From a class perspective, she herself and the two children she gave birth to had also crossed boundaries.

To be honest, for someone with a low level of education like her, to live a life like this was already very good.

Amelia knew that Eva was currently sailing smoothly and content, so she was happy for her and squeezed her hand tightly, changing the subject.

"I bought some small gifts for the two children, wishing them a safe and healthy growth."

Amelia opened the gift box and took out two pieces of jewelry made of gold, handing them to Eva.
