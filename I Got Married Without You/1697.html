Chapter 1677 The new rule

Herb glanced at James, who seemed uninterested, with a hint of malicious intent in his eyes.

"Alright, let the Clark family surrender this round first, but whoever reaches this stage must finish the final round, with new rules for winning or losing. How about that?"

Before James could respond, Paul immediately slammed the table.

"I agree, that's settled then!"

No matter what, they had to protect his ship and the two robots!

James didn't have much of a reaction, he simply reached out and pulled Amelia closer to him, then lifted his cold gaze towards Johnny.

"Shall we continue?"

Johnny's gaze briefly flickered over the hand placed on his waist before quickly looking away.

"There is no winner yet, of course we should continue."

James moved his hand from Amelia's waist up to the back of her head and gently pressed, causing her to nestle into his arms.

"Then let's continue."

Although it was a bit embarrassing to embrace each other in front of so many people, Amelia obediently cooperated with James.

She nestled in his arms, looking up at him, which Johnny found inexplicably irritating.

He clenched his fist, averted his gaze, and stopped looking at Amelia.

After all, James had warned him multiple times of his sovereignty, and it wouldn't be good to keep looking.

But he couldn't control himself from looking at her, he certainly couldn't gouge out his own eyeballs.

Herb couldn't see the hidden tension between James and Johnny, he was only focused on the ongoing gambling game.

"Mr. Evans, for the final round, let's raise the difficulty level, shall we?"

Eva frowned disapprovingly at Herb.

"What kind of tricky idea do you have now?"

Herb drew two jokers from the deck of cards.

"Mr. Evans, you and I will draw cards first. Whoever draws the big joker gets to set the rules for winning and losing, and the rewards and punishments can be announced after the game ends."

After speaking, Herb raised an eyebrow at James. "What do you say, Mr. Evans, do you dare to gamble with me?"

Eva felt that things were getting a bit excessive. "Mr. Clark, with the rules you're setting, won't the loser end up losing everything?"

Herb looked at Eva. "Amelia's sister, don't worry, the stakes won't exceed ten million, I know my limits."

Pausing for a moment, Herb's gaze shifted to James, who was as steady as a rock. "Besides, with both sides having a 50% chance of winning, how do you know Mr. Evans will lose?"

This statement left Eva speechless. James had an overwhelming presence and luck, who knows, maybe he could draw the big joker and she wouldn't have to say anything anymore.

The others who had been eliminated felt a mix of excitement and worry, but James remained calm and raised his head. "Let's start."

Seeing James enter the game, Herb smiled and hurriedly collected the two cards on the table, wanting to shuffle them himself, but Paul snatched them away. "Let me do it."

Herb was taken aback. "Let me do it instead."

Paul sneered, "I noticed something off about you earlier. Are you cheating?"

Herb became angry. "I didn't shuffle the cards just now, so how could I be cheating?"

Paul countered, "Even if you didn't just now, you're eager to shuffle the cards now, isn't that a clear sign of cheating?"

Herb's hidden intentions were exposed, but he still insisted, "I'll shuffle them behind my back..."

Paul interrupted, "It doesn't matter where you shuffle them, it's useless."

Herb retorted, "You..."

"Both of you, shut up!"

Johnny impatiently shouted, "Give it to the waiter."

The waiter standing by was startled, "I..."

He was about to say "I dare not," when Paul forcefully handed him the cards.

Trembling, the waiter took the cards from Paul, turned around, and continuously swapped their positions before placing them back on the table.
