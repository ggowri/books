Chapter 1895 A sense of hope for life

Johnny's face turned pale with anger. "Get out of here!" he shouted.

Herb tightened his seatbelt, rebellious look on his face. "I won't!"

Just as Johnny was about to kick him, James in the backseat glanced at the front.

"Your sister-in-law is pregnant, please focus on driving and stop arguing."

The two almost quarreling brats immediately quieted down.

Johnny slowed down the car and suppressed the urge to kick Herb, driving in silence to the church.

Gigi was at the church, attending the afternoon service.

Every day, she would come to read the Bible, thinking of Zack.

Gradually, her heavy heart began to calm down. But every night in her dreams, she would be awakened by Zack in her dreams.

Losing a loved one was a painful experience that couldn't be easily forgotten in just a day or two.

Many times, Gigi would sit by the window, gazing at the night sky and the bright moon, sitting there all night.

She stared at the stars, starting to believe that people turned into stars after death.

She believed that the stars she saw with her naked eyes must be the transformation of Zack, always entering her sight and following her.

With this deception, she comforted herself and waited for Zack.

And sure enough, Zack came as promised.

Gigi remembered kneeling in front of the church, closing her eyes and praying for Zack, when a clear and pure voice, untainted by the world, sounded in her ears.

The person asked the priest, "Why am I restless day and night lately?"

The priest replied, "There must be something you desire but cannot obtain."

The person asked again, "Is there a way to resolve it?"

The priest said, "It all depends on you to resolve it."

As the person spoke, he noticed Gigi, with tears streaming down her face, looking at him with her tearful and aggrieved eyes, piercing his heart.

In an instant, the priest's voice disappeared, his ears fell silent, and even his eyes were devoid of any color.

All he saw was a woman kneeling on the ground, silently shedding tears.

Until she shook her frail body, took difficult steps, and walked in front of him, trembling fingers caressing his face, did he snap out of it.

The person took a step back, but Gigi, thinking it was Zack who had returned, hugged him without hesitation.

Zenith vaguely looked at Gigi, feeling somewhat familiar, but it was just a fleeting moment.

He pushed her away and told her she had mistaken him for someone else. Gigi looked at the face in front of her, identical to Zack's, and couldn't believe it.

She approached Zenith several times, grabbing his arm and telling him that he had said if she prayed in the church for a year and still hadn't forgotten him, he would come to her in a different form.

Now that she hadn't forgotten and he had come in this form, how could it be wrong?

Gigi asked Zenith to wait for her, she went back to get her phone to show him the evidence he had left behind.

But by the time she returned with her phone, Zenith had already left the church.

Gigi stood in the vast church, looking around in confusion, but couldn't find Zack's figure anymore.

Her eyes suddenly stung, and tears poured down as she squatted down, hugging her knees.

She cried and muttered to herself, "Zack, is it because I didn't protect our baby well that you appeared and left again? But..."

She paused, not making any more excuses for her mistakes.

She just murmured over and over again, softly whispering, "Zack, come back, let's have another child, okay?"

It rarely rained in Barcelona, and when it did, it came suddenly, without waiting for the clouds to darken.

The dry rain fell, and Gigi crouched under the steps, with no shelter from the rain.

The rain soaked her entire body.

When her hair was wet, a transparent white umbrella appeared above her head. Zack, in Gigi's eyes, stood tall in front of her, like a life-saving straw, blocking the rain in time, giving her a sense of hope for life.
