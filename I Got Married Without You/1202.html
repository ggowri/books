Chapter 1202 Gloria, you’re so pathetic

After all doubts in James' mind were cleared, he began to ask his second question: "Who switched the medicine that Simon took before he died?"

Upon hearing this, the malice in Gloria's eyes instantly disappeared, and even her complexion became dull and lifeless.

It seemed that this matter had touched upon the most difficult part of her past, causing her to collapse in her chair, feeling weak all over.

After a long while, she slowly spoke: "It was me..."

Even James, who was ruthless and cunning, never thought that she was the one who switched the medicine.

He was stunned for a moment before he regained his composure and said incredulously, "You were so cruel as to harm your own son?"

Gloria's eyes turned red as she shook her head frantically and said, "No, that's not it. I switched the medicine for you, but somehow Simon took it instead. I clearly switched your medicine, how could he have taken it? How could this happen..."

Upon hearing this, Amelia felt a bit sorry for James and held onto his arm tightly, saying, "So she wanted to kill you..."

"Nonsense!" Gloria roared, pointing at him and saying, "Who said I wanted to kill him? That medicine would only lower his intelligence, not kill him. He's my toy to play with, I want to torture him for the rest of his life. How could I possibly want to kill him..."

Her son, who had finally taken the position of power, fell ill at a young age due to overwork and developed a brain disease.

James, who had always been regarded as the smartest child by Dominic, was secretly groomed by him to be a backup for the position of power in the future.

How could Gloria be willing to let the children of that couple take away what belonged to her son? So, when James fell ill, she switched his medicine.

Who could have thought that the medicine that would accelerate the deterioration of the brain would be eaten by her own son!

James finally understood why Gloria wanted him dead but didn't directly kill.

It turned out that she wanted to keep him alive and torture him for a lifetime...

Her intention extinguished the last bit of morality in his heart.

He held Amelia's hand, took a step forward, and looked coldly at the painful Gloria through the glass: "You are really pathetic."

How could Gloria have the face to live in this world when she wanted to harm him but ended up killing her own son with her own hands?
