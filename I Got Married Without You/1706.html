Chapter 1706 An old acquaintance

Amelia walked upstairs and approached Dylan. Dylan smiled politely at her and opened the door to the study, inviting her in.

"Miss Clark, please come in."

Amelia stepped inside and was greeted by a clean and tidy study, with wooden floors and furniture. The room was filled with ample sunlight, creating a warm atmosphere.

In this setting, there stood an old man in a white suit with silver hair. He had his back to the door, standing by the window and admiring the beautiful view outside.

"Grandfather, Miss Clark is here," Dylan said.

After inviting Amelia in, Dylan respectfully called out to the old man.

Matty turned around, his eyes deep and powerful, carrying the weight of experience and wisdom, as he examined Amelia.

She also took a moment to observe Matty. Despite his age, he still had the same tall and strong figure as in the downstairs photo, with a radiant and lively appearance.

He didn't look like someone in his twilight years; instead, he exuded a mature, dignified, elegant, and composed aura, like that of a middle-aged man.

"Mr. Lawrence, hello," Amelia greeted proactively. Matty then shifted his gaze away from her and looked at Dylan.

"You can leave now. I'll talk to Miss Clark alone."

Upon hearing this, Dylan gave Amelia a meaningful look before turning and leaving the study.

Once Dylan was gone, Matty's inquisitive gaze returned to Amelia.

"Miss Clark, what flavor of coffee would you like?"

After examining her for a moment, he turned around and walked towards the coffee machine, taking out two clean cups from the side.

Amelia expected him to offer her a seat on the sofa first, but instead, he went straight to asking her about her coffee preferences.

"A mocha would be great, thank you."

As she held the cup, she hesitated for a moment, but Matty turned around and glanced at her once again.

Feeling the silver-haired handsome gentleman's gaze on her, Amelia couldn't help but feel a bit nervous. Did she say something wrong?

As she thought this, Matty's stern face and tense sculpted lines gradually softened.

"I had an acquaintance who also liked mocha, just like you."

Amelia didn't respond to his words and simply watched Matty as he turned around to make the coffee.

"You can sit anywhere you like."

Although Matty seemed a bit stern, Amelia felt that he wasn't too difficult to get along with. On the contrary, he seemed quite friendly. She relaxed and chose a small sofa to sit on.

After making the coffee, Matty saw Amelia sitting on the small sofa, with her hands on her knees, looking adorable and obedient. He suddenly became lost in thought for a moment.

Noticing his puzzled expression, Amelia instinctively glanced at the sofa she was sitting on.

"Is it not allowed to sit here?"

Holding her notebook, she was about to get up when Matty spoke first.

"I don't have so many rules here. You can sit wherever you want."

Then, Matty walked over with the coffee and handed it to her.

"Miss Clark, may I ask where you're from?"

After standing up to receive the coffee, Amelia politely thanked him and looked at Matty, who was elegantly sipping his own coffee by the desk.

"I'm American."

"Are there other people in your family?"

"I have a younger sister."

"A sister?"

Matty narrowed his dark eyes slightly.

"Which sister?"

This question made Amelia pause. She felt like he had looked into Skender's background and knew that Skender was part of the Clark family.

However, she wasn't sure if he knew everything about Sumasa, so she couldn't answer confidently.

"As far as I know, there are only four children in the Clark family. Besides Johnny, there's Herb, who is a boy, and then there's Gigi, the only girl."

Matty's probing gaze became tinged with suspicion.

"Miss Clark, you wouldn't happen to not even know the name of your own cousin, would you?"

Amelia's heart skipped a beat. Matty had indeed investigated Skender's background. But even though he knew that the Clark family only had Gigi as a girl, why did he ask her which sister?

Does this mean that Matty had long known about Sumasa's situation, knew that Skender wasn't part of the Clark family, and knew that Skender had a sister?

But if that's the case, why did Matty only search for Skender and never look for Amelia? Could it be that he also doesn't know who Skender's sister is?
