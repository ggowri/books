Chapter 1699 Let's play another round

Johnny's mind was filled with all sorts of thoughts, but could he really kiss Amelia?

Even if Amelia wasn't married, Johnny wouldn't dare to make a move on a woman he just liked through a game.

He wanted mutual affection, but from the moment he fell for Amelia, he knew that was just wishful thinking. It could never happen in this lifetime.

Johnny suppressed his feelings, raised his foot, and kicked Herb hard. "What kind of stupid idea was that? Am I the kind of person who can kiss anyone?"

After saying that, he quickly glanced at Amelia, controlled his emotions, and left. "We've already delivered Eva's wedding gift. Goodbye."

Herb was reluctant to leave without getting to James, but when Johnny shot him a fierce look, Herb immediately backed down and followed him.

"Stop!" A chilling voice came from behind. Herb instinctively shrank his neck. Could it be... James wasn't going to let him off the hook?

"Mr. Clark, come over here." Herb swallowed nervously and slowly turned around.

The man sitting on the couch stared at him with a murderous gaze.

Seeing the murderous intent in James' eyes, Herb didn't dare to go near. "Mr. Evans, I..."

"Let's play another round. The rules will be announced after the game."

Herb thought James would kill him directly, but he didn't expect James to suggest playing another round.

Herb didn't understand what James was planning. He had a feeling that James would find a way to get rid of him.

He looked to Johnny for help, but Johnny didn't even glance at him and left the cabin.

Johnny left, and James didn't stop him. It meant that James only had eyes for him...

Without waiting for Herb's agreement, Paul placed two shuffled cards in front of him.

"There are two jokers here. If you draw the big joker, you can leave. If not..." Paul paused for a moment, then turned to show Herb the murderous intent in James' eyes.

Everyone in the Evans Family respected Amelia, except for Sir Evans. Even his brothers didn't dare to look at Amelia too much, just to show their respect for her.

Herb, this little troublemaker, dared to embarrass Amelia on the Evans Family's territory.

Herb looked at James, thought for a moment, and considering his luck earlier, maybe he could draw the big joker this time.

He hesitantly reached out his trembling hand and picked the card on the right side...

Paul gave him a cold glance and revealed the two cards. "The one on the right is the small joker, and the one on the left is the big joker. You're doomed."

Herb's heart skipped a beat, and he subconsciously looked at James, who was enveloped in a murderous aura. "What are the rules for winning or losing?"

James didn't answer him. He just took out a pair of white gloves and calmly put them on.

After putting them on, the tall and imposing man stood up from the couch and walked towards him with steady steps.

The man naturally emanated a sense of dominance, coupled with the intimidation from being in a high position for a long time, he felt like a mountain pressing down on Herb.

"Are you going to apologize on your knees, or should I slap you a few times?" Although James wasn't wearing his studded gloves this time, his palm strikes were still powerful.

After taking a few slaps, Herb's face would surely swell for days. But in front of the Evans Family, if he kneeled and apologized, he would lose all his dignity.

"My patience is limited." A cold voice mixed with anger echoed in Herb's ears, and he felt an uncomfortable pressure from his older brother.

"I choose the slaps." He would rather be beaten than apologize to his enemy. This was the Clark family's dignity!

James raised his gaze, glanced at the defiant Herb, and without any hesitation, slapped him hard a few times.

After receiving four consecutive slaps, Herb covered his swollen face and looked pitifully at James, who was ready to continue hitting him.

"I was wrong. Can't that be enough?"

But James only paused for a moment before raising his hand to slap him again.

Herb quickly leaned back to avoid a fatal blow. After stabilizing himself with the help of his waist, he begged James for mercy. "I'll never joke about Amelia again. Please let me go..."
