Chapter 1855: Finding Paul to Crack the Code

After six long months, Frank was finally looking at a living, breathing James again. His aged face was instantly streaked with tears of relief.

"Mr. James Evans, you're finally back. I thought..."

"I'm fine."

With a gentle pat on Frank's shoulder, James reassured the old man and headed straight to his study.

Seeing that James looked busy, Frank didn't dare to disturb him. He instructed the chef to prepare a feast and went to pick up Grace from school.

Ever since James and Amelia left, Grace had been missing them. The adults had been through dangerous times.

Frank told her they were on a business trip to ease her worries, but Grace, smarter than most kids, didn't believe that people on a business trip wouldn't even call or video chat.

With James back, Frank breathed a sigh of relief, finally having something to tell Grace.

James had a chip in his head, a fact he hadn't disclosed to Andrew. He frowned slightly when he saw Andrew following him into the study.

"Andrew, I'm safe now. You can go back to your family."

During James' absence, Andrew had become a father. His newborn child and recovering wife needed him, but Andrew refused to leave.

"Mr. Evans, you'll need to plan a lot to take the S team back. Count me in, let me help."

James thought for a moment, then picked up a pen and wrote down the name of the previous S organization, darkness, and Sophia's name. He handed the note to Andrew.

"Find out why this organization wants to kill Sophia. Find out everything."

Andrew, now with a task, took the note and left the study.

Watching Andrew leave, James leaned back into the sofa, his body relaxing for the first time in half a year.

But it was only a moment of relaxation. He quickly moved to his computer, typing in the chip program recorded in his head.

James knew Matty had altered the program. The code he was typing out now wouldn't do much, but he could guess the altered program based on the original one.

James was smart and had a photographic memory, but when it came to programming, he couldn't outsmart Matty. Seeing the word "encrypted" appearing on his screen over and over, he had to call Paul.

Paul was a computer genius and a renowned hacker. With his help, they might be able to rewrite the program.

Paul was massaging Eva's legs when he received James' call. Recognizing James' unique voice, he jumped up excitedly, "James, you're back! I'll come right away, wait for me!"

Before James could even tell him why he was needed, Paul had hung up in his excitement...

"My dear, are you feeling better? If not, I'll finish massaging you before going to James..." Paul looked at Eva.

"I'm much better, go find James. Don't worry about me."

"Take care. Call me if you feel uncomfortable." With a kiss on Eva's forehead, Paul rushed out to his car. He drove at top speed to Blue Bay Island and stormed into James' study.

"James!"

Seeing James safe and sound, sitting in front of his computer just like before, Paul's emotions were on a roller coaster ride.

"I knew you were fine!"

The man who had always believed James would return safe and sound was finally able to release all his worry and fear.

He opened his arms and walked over to give James a bear hug, but James pushed him away with a stern look.

"Enough talk, come and work."

James pointed at the screen, where lines of code were popping up one by one.

"This is a chip program that can listen in, locate, and detonate. Can you disable it?"

Paul, who had wanted to ask about James' experience in Erebus, swallowed his questions when he saw how serious and urgent James looked.

Paul leaned in to examine the lines of code, then took James' computer and sat down beside him.
