Chapter 1876 You must commit suicide

Matty's sudden raised voice, filled with endless resentment, seemed to want to crush Master Davies immediately.

James, with his calm and cold voice, casually revealed Master Davies' location. "He may be in London or Washington."

These two places were where Master Davies often went, as for whether he was there now, it was not James' concern.

After informing, James moved his sharp and proud gaze away from Matty and directed it through the bulletproof glass towards Amelia.

"Release her."

Matty, who had obtained the location of his enemy, followed James' gaze and glanced at Amelia.

The murderous intent that filled his eyes gradually subsided when it touched the face that resembled Sophia's.

He lowered his eyelashes and stared at the control panel.

With a gentle press of the open door button, Amelia and James could leave this place.

He actually wanted Amelia to stay by his side, to accompany him through the last journey of his life.

But now, knowing the cause of Sophia's death was because of himself, it seemed meaningless.

His hands were stained with blood, yet he never felt guilty, except when it came to Sophia, who could easily break through his psychological defenses.

Hatred supported him, enduring decades of torment.

Now that this grudge had fallen on him, it should come to an end.

It was just a bit regretful that he had gone through so much trouble to trap Amelia, claiming it was to make up for her, but only ended up hurting her.

The harm he caused to Amelia had already been done and couldn't be undone, but he could still minimize it in time.

Having come to this realization, Matty withdrew his gaze, raised his finger, and pressed it against the control panel.

"Boss." Just as his fingertip was about to touch the open door button, a gun was pressed against the back of Matty's head.

"What about our enemies, now that you've found yours and want to let them go?"

Upon hearing the voice, Matty slightly turned his gaze and looked at 2-5, who was responsible for guarding the biohazard area.

"Their enemies have nothing to do with my granddaughter."

"It may have nothing to do with your granddaughter, but we need to use her."

Matty's eyes turned cold. "What? Are you going against my orders and choosing to oppose me?"

2-5, holding the gun, sneered coldly.

"What do you think we, your partners, are for? Are we just tools for your revenge?"

"Matty, ever since you ordered Dylan to kill Nick, we no longer intend to be your tools!"

After 2-5 finished speaking, he ignored Matty and his gaze crossed over Matty's white hair, scanning the man opposite him.

"Shall we discuss a condition?"

James, with his indifferent gaze, stared at the other person without saying a word, appearing like a contemptuous king.

"I can release your wife, but you must pay a price."

James raised his defiant eyes and coldly stared at the other person.

"What price?"

"You must commit suicide as an apology."

Upon hearing these words, Amelia, trapped in the escape room, felt her heart skip a beat and her face lost its color.

James seemed to sense her fear and gave her a reassuring look with a slight tilt of his eyes.

Amelia, using some strength, pressed her fingers against the glass door, suppressing all her worries, and nodded gently.

As James withdrew his gaze, his slender fingers slowly moved to his waist. 2-5's eyes were fixed on James' moving hand and he couldn't help but sneer.

"Mr. Evans, the red dot on your body is now under my control. I advise you not to make any rash moves."

"Is that so?"

In the moment James' thin lips curled up with a faint smile, he quickly drew his gun from his waist.

James took a step back, aiming directly at 2-5's forehead before the sniper could shoot, and pulled the trigger.

In just a second, the bullet grazed Matty's ear and accurately blew off 2-5's head!

At the same time, Matty quickly turned his body and modified the trajectory on the control panel.

Matty managed to modify the direction just a second before the infrared beam erupted, allowing James to escape unharmed.

As the wall was riddled with countless holes from the infrared beam, James silently shifted his gaze towards Matty.

The two locked eyes, neither of them spoke, but they had the same understanding, which was to release Amelia.

However, in the instant the bulletproof glass door opened, the escape pod's hatch opened first, and two men in black rushed towards Amelia.
