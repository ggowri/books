Chapter 1884 Eva gave birth to twins

He suppressed his restless heart and stared at Amelia intently, like a fierce beast. "Honey, after you give birth and recover, I may not let you go."

Amelia raised her hazy eyes and looked at James's eyes filled with desire.

She suddenly remembered the scenes of being taken that night and couldn't help but shiver. "Can I not do it?"

Upon hearing this, James deliberately stopped and raised his slender fingers to hold her chin. "Do you want it or not?"

Amelia, already aroused with desire, felt a bit embarrassed and stubbornly said she didn't want it.

But James continued a few more times, making Amelia's face turn red and even more flushed.

"Honey, say it again, that you don't want it."

Amelia was about to speak when the tall and upright man leaned down and kissed a very sensitive spot.

"Mmm..."

"I won't say it, please let me go..."

James moved up to her ear and nuzzled her neck. "Honey, I want to hear you say that you want me."

"I won't say it."

"Are you sure?"

"I'm sure!"

"Alright..."

Not long after, Amelia gave in. She hugged James's waist and lowered her head. "I want you..."

James curled his lips, revealing a doting smile. "Good..."

Amelia was exhausted, and James held her and placed her on another clean bed before she fell asleep in a daze.

James didn't sleep, he just held the woman nestled in his arms and gently patted her back, soothing her to sleep.

It wasn't that he didn't want to sleep, but he couldn't sleep, afraid that this moment would be too short-lived.

The happier James felt, the more prone he was to worry.

He was always afraid of what would happen to Amelia and the child if he were to leave first.

He shifted his gaze and quietly looked out the window.

The bright moonlight shone on the windowsill, emitting a shimmering radiance, but it couldn't penetrate the man's heart.

The next day at noon, Paul called. Amelia had just woken up and looked at the man answering the phone with her drowsy eyes. "What?"

"Eva is going into labor?"

Amelia quickly signaled for James to put it on speakerphone, and then she heard Paul's panicked voice.

"Yes, her water broke, and she was taken to the delivery room. What should I do? What can I do?"

"I don't know."

James replied, and Paul instantly stopped panicking.

He had never seen someone so indifferent in his life!

"Paul, don't panic for now."

Compared to James's indifference, Amelia was much more nervous.

"Notify your family to go help first, and James and I will come back immediately."

Paul, who was comforted by Amelia, felt a moment of warmth and peace.

"My parents are already on their way, Rebecca is coming too, and my sister..."

Paul was clearly in a state of panic and kept mentioning names.

Amelia interrupted him a few times, but he didn't stop.

Amelia felt a bit helpless, and James directly extended his slender fingers and pressed the hang-up button.

Then he lifted the blanket, fetched clothes, and helped her put them on.

"The Evans family won't let anything happen to Eva, don't worry."

Paul was unreliable, but Rebecca was reliable. With her around, Eva would definitely be safe.

As James had said, they received a call from Paul with good news as soon as they boarded the plane.

"James, Amelia, Eva has given birth!"

"What? So fast?"

Amelia and James were very surprised. It had only been forty minutes from the hotel to the airport.

"The doctor said Eva is in good health and gave birth quickly."

"What about Eva? Is she okay?"

While Amelia was happy for Eva, she also immediately worried about her physical condition.

"Don't worry, she's fine. She'll come out soon."

Amelia breathed a sigh of relief upon hearing that Eva was fine.

"And is it a boy or a girl?"

Once it was confirmed that the mother was fine, the most important topic was the gender of the child.

"She gave birth to twins, a boy and a girl!"
