Chapter 329 My Love Is TRUE Love

When he saw her familiar face, he said firmly, "No, my love is true love. I love Amelia, so I should possess her!"If he loved her, but gave up her and blessed her, that kind of love didn't belong to him. Love that was overbearing and possessive could help tie her to him, even if she didn't love him!
