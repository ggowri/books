Chapter 1896 Someone else’s understudy

If Zenith had not appeared, Gigi could have suppressed her longing for a year.

But since Zenith appeared, it was like a raindrop falling into calm waters, causing ripples.

Despite everyone's advice that he was not Zack, Gigi was convinced that he was Zack, just that Zack didn't remember her.

This made it different, as she firmly believed that Zack had come back to her in a different form.

Zenith approached Gigi, who was still kneeling in front of the church, thanking God for returning Zack to her.

However, before she could fulfill her vow, her chin was held up by a slender finger.

Gigi looked up into Zenith's eyes and saw the mole in them, but she overlooked it.

She heard Zenith ask her, "You have moved my heart, but you constantly think of someone else. I don't want to be a substitute for someone else. If you have even the slightest place for me in your heart, then I will stay. If not, then I will leave."

Gigi's response was, "Zack, you are my fiancé and the father of my deceased baby. How could you be a substitute for someone else?"

She saw the sparkle in Zenith's eyes fade as she spoke, and silently, he left for a while.

Gigi went crazy looking for him but couldn't find him, so she cried and begged Johnny for help.

Johnny didn't want to see Gigi go crazy. After all, Zenith was not Zack.

But because of Zenith's appearance, Gigi had the motivation to keep going, and he couldn't help but be selfish.

Johnny investigated Zenith and confirmed their blood relationship through DNA testing.

Based on their appearance, Johnny speculated that they were twins.

Zenith's stepmother, fearing the influence of having twins, bribed the doctor on the night of the difficult birth of the main wife.

They exchanged a stillborn baby for the live one and sent the live baby away on a ship, drifting towards his own destiny.

If Zack hadn't encountered Master Davies on the ship, he would have starved to death as an infant.

If he hadn't met James, he would have died during training.

His seemingly bright but tumultuous life was all thanks to the help of others.

That's why Zack was willing to dedicate his wandering life to those who had helped him.

Johnny found Zenith and personally went to see him.

He happened to see Zenith in the church, holding a watering can and sprinkling water on flower petals.

He bathed in the sunlight, with a lowered gaze, just like Zack, except that Zack's hands were stained with blood, while Zenith's hands were untainted by the world.

Johnny approached Zenith and told him about Zack's existence, asking if he was willing to replace Zack and love Gigi.

Zenith was a kind-hearted person.

After learning about his brother's lonely life and tragic death, he was on the verge of tears but didn't want to replace his brother's love for Gigi.

Johnny didn't force him and left. In the following days, Johnny comforted Gigi.

After listening, Gigi didn't say anything, but when she fell asleep, she leaned against the door frame and asked, "Brother, am I not worthy of happiness?"

Johnny also thought the same way. It was painful to love someone you couldn't have.

But later, he thought that his parents had lived a sinful life and it would eventually be someone's responsibility to pay for it.

He just hoped that he would be the one to pay, rather than his sister, who had lost both her loved one and her baby.

Gigi didn't insist on Zenith anymore.

She continued to kneel in front of the church, reading the Bible every day.

She believed that as long as she focused, Zack would surely come back to her.

It was during a flower parade that Zenith happened to see Gigi in the bustling crowd.

That one glance felt like a first encounter, and it struck a chord in his heart, causing an irresistible attraction.

Unable to sleep, Zenith looked up at the night stars.

That night, something happened.

Zenith dreamt of a man who looked exactly like him.

After waking up, Zenith couldn't remember the details of the dream, but he knew that the person had said many things to him.

The most memorable was the request, "Zenith, please take care of my fiancée in my place."
