Chapter 1218 Jonny’s excuse sucks (2)

Jonny, who was about the same height as James, looked up and met his gaze. He was struck by the clear, watery eyes that seemed to hold a stream of emotions. Jonny's heart skipped a beat, and he instinctively looked away.

"I just met her. She brought a lot of people with her and said they were going to your house to take the child..."

"Then I have to go back and check!"

Amelia pretended to be nervous and quickly got up, but James held her waist.

"Eva needs to see you urgently. You go see her, and I'll go back and check."

James probably couldn't stand Jonny's lame excuse, so he made up one himself to get rid of him.

Suppressing the urge to laugh, Amelia took James' hand and pretended to be worried. "If it weren't for the urgent matter, I would have gone with you."

James patted her hand and reassured her, "It's the same for me to go alone. Besides, with me there, Jocelyn won't dare to cause trouble in our house."

Seeing Amelia nodding without any trace of acting, James picked up his coat and got up.

Jonny didn't expect to fool James so easily. The corners of his mouth slowly curved upwards.

Before he could even raise his eyebrows and show a victorious smile, James, who had walked halfway, suddenly turned back.

"You're quite familiar with Jocelyn. Why don't you come with me?"

Jonny frowned. "Why should I follow you, I am an outsider, to deal with your business with Jocelyn?"

James put his coat in the crook of his arm, put his hands in his pockets, and stared at Jonny with a half-smile. "Who knows if you're deliberately trying to get rid of me and then kidnap my wife?"
