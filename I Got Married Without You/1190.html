Chapter 1190 Turns out, it really does affect him

Hearing this, her heart sank bit by bit. "What... do you mean?"

Can't be together, does he want to divorce her?

Just because he had a conversation with his grandfather, does he want to divorce her?

She hugged J's waist with some fear and said, "You promised me forever, don't leave me."

James didn't know that she was falling more and more in love with him. If he left her, she might not be able to go on living...
