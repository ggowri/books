Chapter 1668 Ryan's surgery was successful

Eva couldn't believe she was pregnant. She went to the doctor for another check-up, and the results confirmed that she was indeed pregnant.

Sitting on the hospital bed, Eva held the report and examined it repeatedly, slowly accepting the reality of her pregnancy...

That night, they didn't use protection. The next day, when Eva woke up, she was caught by Amelia, and then called away by Kate.

She hadn't taken emergency contraception within 72 hours, so it was no surprise that she was pregnant.

But this child came at the wrong time...

Seeing Eva's sad and anxious expression, Ryan furrowed his brows slightly.

"Eva, is the child Paul's?" he asked.

Eva felt embarrassed when he figured it out, but she didn't deny it and nodded directly at Ryan.

"Then why aren't you happy?" he asked.

Wasn't she ready to accept Paul's proposal? Why was she still so troubled?

Eva raised her hand and touched her belly. She couldn't feel the rhythm of life yet, but it still weighed heavily on her.

"I don't know..." she said, tears rolling down her cheeks as she looked at Ryan.

"Maybe I've become more sensitive after getting pregnant."

She wiped away her tears and smiled at Ryan.

"Ryan, you go back to your place first. I'll calm down here and then come find you, okay?"

Ryan stared at her, unsure if it was due to pregnancy sensitivity or emotional distress.

He felt that Eva had a lot on her mind, but didn't know how to express it, so he chose to keep quiet.

He took out a square handkerchief and wiped away the tears that kept falling from her face.

"I'll wait for you at home, but please don't cry."

A gentle smile appeared on Ryan's warm face, like the winter sun, always able to warm people's hearts.

"Okay, I won't cry anymore."

Seeing her obediently nod, Ryan put away the handkerchief, pushed the wheelchair, and left the hospital room, leaving Eva alone.

Watching his receding figure in the wheelchair, Eva thought about Ryan and Amelia's missed opportunity, as well as Erin and Stella's fate.

She wondered, did it really matter whether Paul slept with Elsie or not?

Eva, maybe it's better for you to be a little confused and pretend that nothing happened, as long as he is willing to change for you in the future.

Startled by her own thoughts, Eva was suddenly shocked. Did she like Paul that much?

Was she really considering being with him in this confused manner?

But wasn’t this the root of all her problems?

Whether Elsie's words were true or false, she didn't care at all.

The only thing she cared about was whether or not to let go of everything and unconditionally trust that Paul would never do such a thing again.

Just like his promise during the proposal, from now on, no more drinking, and no more contact with any other women.

As long as he could do these two things, whether Paul had a relationship with Elsie or not, she was willing to forget about that night.

Just... consider it as him being drunk and unconsciously making a mistake.

After figuring out what mattered most to her, Eva lifted the covers, got out of bed, and stood up.

Just as Ryan said, if she failed, she could start over. What was there to be afraid of?

Moreover, she was already pregnant with his baby, the arrival of a new life, and the arrival of the family she longed for.

Filled with hope, Eva returned to her place, helped Zoey pack up, and then accompanied Ryan on the flight back to Arcadia.

After the plane landed in Imperial Capital and Ryan was settled, Eva expressed her desire to return to Arcadia.

Ryan suggested that she rest for a few days before leaving, but Eva refused, saying, "I need to go back and take care of some things."

She was in a hurry to see Paul, which made Ryan smile knowingly. "What about your pregnancy..."

Eva pulled the handle of her suitcase, revealing a determined smile, and smiled at Ryan. "I'll tell him myself."

Ryan nodded and waved goodbye to her. After they bid farewell, Eva hurriedly returned to Arcadia, while Ryan took out Edith's business card.

He stared at the name on it, hesitated for a few seconds, then asked Zoey to bring him his phone and saved the number from the card, then sent a text message.

[Miss Evans, I have completed the surgery and need rehabilitation treatment. When are you available?]

Edith, who was conducting a meeting in Imperial Capital, saw the message and slowly curved her lips, then lowered her head to type.

[Pay the treatment fee of $500,000 in advance.]

When Ryan saw the bank account numbers in the message, he didn't think too much and directly gave them to Zoey to make the payment.

Upon receiving the bank account notification, Edith thought Ryan was quite good, obediently doing what she asked, like a well-behaved child.

[Tomorrow, I'll visit your clinic for treatment.]

After sending the message, Edith added another condition.

[You'll cover all meals except breakfast.]

Ryan read it and simply replied 'okay', then threw his phone aside and took out the book, continuing to stare at the words on the page, lost in thought.
