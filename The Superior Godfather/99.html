Chapter 99 Partnership

In a VIP private room at the Kempinski Hotel, Thomas and Eric were staring at the anonymous invitation letter in their hands, both clearly puzzled.

The letter had promised to help them take full control of the Trillion Group and solve their current problems.

But now, after showing up at the agreed time and place, no one was around.

Eric looked unsure. "Thomas, we've been waiting here for a while with no sign of anyone. Do you think this could be a trick?"

Before Thomas could answer, the door to the private room suddenly swung open.

To their surprise, the hotel manager entered, escorting a young man with white hair and an older man.

It was none other than Denzel and Victor.

Denzel walked in confidently and took a seat at the head of the table, offering an apologetic smile to Thomas and Eric.

"My apologies for the delay."

"No problem," Thomas replied, his sharp eyes narrowing as he studied the men. "Why did you call us here today, and how should we address you?"

Denzel was no ordinary person, and his calm demeanor only confirmed this to Thomas, who had met many influential figures in his time.

Victor, standing quietly behind Denzel, spoke up, "Allow me to introduce ourselves. This is Denzel Vaughn. I'm Victor Collins and I work for him."

"Victor, one of the top eight experts in Azuriath State?"

"Denzel, one of the five heirs of the Vaughn family?"

Thomas and Eric exchanged shocked looks.

The Vaughn family was the undisputed ruler of Azuriath State, a century-old family that towered over even the wealthiest elites.

They controlled the entire region's economy and industries, essentially running the state.

Denzel, one of their five heirs, was a man of incredible influence.

The fact that someone of Denzel's stature would be reaching out to them left Thomas and Eric in awe.

They quickly stood up, eager to greet him.

"It's an honor to meet you, Mr. Vaughn and Mr. Collins."

"No need for formalities," Denzel said with a smile. "Please, sit down."

He wasted no time and got straight to the point. "The reason I'm here is that I'd like to discuss a potential partnership."

"A partnership?"

Thomas and Eric exchanged a brief glance before their faces lit up with excitement. "What kind of partnership are you proposing? How can we be of service to you?"

If they could partner with Denzel and gain the Vaughn family's support, it would mean a leap to the top for them.

Denzel's tone shifted to something more serious. "For certain reasons, I can't act directly, so I need your help to take down the Trillion Group."

He couldn't let Anastasia be with Brian, nor could he allow Brian to double the value of the Trillion Group and regain his position as the heir of the Godfather.

Thomas and Eric exchanged a look, a resigned smile spreading across their faces.

"Mr. Vaughn, the Trillion Group is doing well, booming even. Taking it down won't be a walk in the park... I doubt we have the means to pull it off."

Denzel smiled slightly, unfazed.

"Mr. Thomas Lee, you're being too modest. We all know you're one of the pillars of the Trillion Group, and you hold a lot of sway inside. Plus, I'll have Victor use the Vaughn family's resources to back you in the shadows...

"If you manage to take care of this, you'll be lifelong allies of the Vaughn family. I'll ensure you both live a life of luxury and ease, with wealth and power at your fingertips."

The words stirred a deep excitement in Thomas and Eric.

They immediately nodded with determination. "Mr. Vaughn, don't worry, we'll do whatever it takes to bring down the Trillion Group!"

Denzel took a leisurely sip of his wine, clearly entertained. "So, what's your plan from here?"

Thomas' face hardened as he responded, "Mr. Vaughn, I have access to key resources and information within the Trillion Group. I also have strong ties with many of the top executives and shareholders. With your support, I can persuade them to work for you, dismantle the company, and start something new.

"Once we've done that, the Group will be nothing more than an empty shell. With a little more effort, we can take it down completely."

Denzel gave a satisfied smile.

"Good. Let's go with your plan!"

Thomas hesitated for a moment. "But Mr. Vaughn, setting up a new company will require a lot of funds. We're a bit tight at the moment..."

Denzel waved it off, tossing a bank card onto the table with a casual motion. "Here's 10 billion dollars. Use it for now. If you need more, just ask Victor."

Thomas and Eric were stunned. Gratefully, they bowed their heads. "Thank you, Mr. Vaughn! You can count on us. We won't let you down."

Denzel waved them away. "Go ahead and get started. I expect good news in a month."

"We won't disappoint you," Thomas promised, and with that, they quickly departed.

As they left, Denzel turned to Victor with a mischievous smile. "Victor, what do you think? Do you think they'll pull it off?"

Victor's reply was confident. "With you behind them, they should have no trouble."

"By the way," Denzel said, "how's the assassination going?"

Victor's expression grew confident as he spoke, "The contract's been picked up by two of the best assassins from the Peerless Sect, Cole and Derek. They're well-known internationally. There shouldn't be any issues with them on the job..."

Before Victor could finish, his phone rang. After taking the call, his face darkened.

"Mr. Vaughn, the assassination failed!"
