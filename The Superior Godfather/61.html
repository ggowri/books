Chapter 61 Shareholders Shocked

"You're the chairman we are waiting for?

"Hahaha! Are you kidding me?"

Eric couldn't help laughing as if he had heard a huge joke.

Damian also had a look of mockery and amusement on his face.

He said, "This kid is really good at bragging!"

A sneer appeared on Thomas' lips at this moment.

He was anxious about how to confront Celeste, not anticipating that her attractive companion would show up and create trouble.

Despite Celeste clarifying her relationship with Brian at the investment promotion conference, many still viewed their connection as questionable.

Many believed Brian's partnership with Trillion Group was solely due to Celeste's manipulation of the chairman.

At that moment, Thomas gave Eric a look.

Eric understood instantly and suddenly fixed his gaze on Celeste and asked sternly, "Celeste, your man is really brave. How dare he pretend to be our chairman?

"What if it reaches the actual chairman?"

Celeste raised her red lips slightly, and a playful smile appeared.

She stretched out her slender hands and admired the freshly done manicure with interest.

She sneered, "What will be the consequences? I don't know. Why don't you call the chairman and ask?"

"You!"

Being looked down upon by Celeste, Eric was so angry.

He said, "Do you think I don't dare to call? If you have the guts, give me the chairman's phone number."

Celeste smiled slightly and handed the phone over.

Eric opened the call record and dialed the phone number marked "Mr. Edwards".

Soon, the cell phone in Brian's pocket rang.

The sudden ring startled everyone at the scene, and they all looked at Brian in confusion.

Eric was clearly calling the chairman, but why was Brian's phone ringing?

Thomas raised his eyebrows and narrowed his eyes. "Could this be a coincidence?"

Damian also frowned at this moment.

Eric didn't notice these but waited for the call to be connected and then spoke quickly, "Mr. Edwards, when will you arrive at the company? Celeste brought a man who is pretending to be you."

He realized something was wrong before he could finish his words and stood in a daze because Brian pressed the speakerphone button.

His voice came out clearly from the phone, echoing in everyone's ears.

"How is this possible?"

"Is he really the mysterious chairman of our company?"

Seeing Brian holding the cell phone and looking calm, people at the scene were shocked.

Damian's expression shifted.

Thomas narrowed his eyes slightly, and his expression became more solemn than ever before at this moment.

He thought, "If he is the new chairman, then his methods and schemes are too scary.

In this way, the investment promotion conference became a big drama directed and performed by him and a means to reorganize the company.

Otherwise, Celeste alone would not have the courage to touch Kevin.

But there's one thing I can't figure out.

Since Brian is the new chairman of the company, what's the point of letting the Transcendent Construction Group he founded become a partner of Trillion Group?"

Frozen in disbelief, Eric confronted Brian, murmuring, "You...you are Mr. Edwards, the new chairman of the company Celeste mentioned?"

Brian casually played with his phone, enjoying the moment. "What? Is there a problem?"

"It's impossible! You are the boss of Transcendent Construction Group. How can you suddenly become the new chairman of the company?"

Eric shook his head, unable to accept it.

"It's fake. All this is fake!

"This must be a show directed and performed by you and Celeste to deceive and fool us."

Without waiting for Brian to answer, Eric continued, "A real chairman would never be so bored as to start a new construction company and then do something stupid like handing over the Weston project of Trillion Group to this shell company!"

Everyone at the scene echoed him.

"That's right. If he's really the chairman, he would not do that. It doesn't make sense at all."

"This guy must be fake. He teamed up with Celeste to deceive us!"

Thomas and Damian stared at Celeste and Brian with serious eyes.

Celeste sneered and threw a document directly from the drawer in front of Eric and the others.

She said, "Do I have nothing better to do than lie to you?

"This is the company's equity transfer document. Please open your eyes and read it carefully to see if Brian is the new chairman of the company?"
