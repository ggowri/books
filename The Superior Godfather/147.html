Chapter 147 Sorry

"What?"

The whole room erupted in an uproar.

Everyone looked at Aria in disbelief.

Not only had she not spent the two-billion-dollar funds, but she had also reached long-term cooperation with the Trillion Group and won all of their company's engineering projects.

Most importantly, the Trillion Group had transferred five billion dollars to the account of the Queen's Construction Group.

The shareholders thought, "But isn't the Trillion Group on the verge of going bankrupt due to a broken capital chain?

How could they have so much money?"

At this moment, both the senior executives and the shareholders were stunned, and overwhelmed by this series of news.

Tanner stared at Aria with wide eyes, unable to believe her words.

Then, having thought of something, he burst into laughter.

He said, "Aria, you're lying.

"Everyone knows that the Trillion Group's capital chain is broken and it's about to go bankrupt. It's impossible for them to transfer five billion dollars to our group.

"You said the Trillion Group transferred five billion dollars to us. Where's the money? Show it to everyone!"

The shareholders also snapped out of their shock and stared at Aria, waiting for her to explain.

After all, what Aria had said just now was just one-sided.

They needed facts.

Aria sneered, "The rumor that the Trillion Group's capital chain is broken and it's about to go bankrupt is just a false one. As for the whereabouts of the five-billion-dollar transfer, why don't you ask the finance department to check our company's account?"

"Quick! Go check it right away!" With Noah's roar, the chief financial officer opened the company's bank account in public and started the query.

Soon, the five-billion-dollar transfer in the account was presented in front of everyone, shocking them all.

"There really is five billion dollars!"

"My god, the Trillion Group really did transfer five billion dollars to us!"

Seeing this, the shareholders led by Noah were excited and couldn't help exclaiming.

Nothing was more persuasive than money and facts.

At this moment, they completely believed Aria's words.

"How is this possible?"

Tanner was shocked, his mind went blank, and he couldn't believe that everything was really as Aria had said.

Thinking about his accusations against Aria, Tanner turned pale and felt extremely embarrassed.

After a while, everyone gradually recovered from their shock and looked at Aria with admiration, apology, and flattery.

"Ms. Jenkins, I'm sorry I wrongly accused you. Please forgive us!"

"Ms. Jenkins, you're a hero of our company. We will do our best to meet any request you may have."

The Trillion Group was very important in the real estate industry. With its support, the Queen's Construction Group had an infinite future.

Aria looked up at Noah and asked, "Any request?"

Noah answered without hesitation, "Of course. As long as we can do it, we will meet your requirements!"

Aria said, "Good, it's a deal."

Aria's expression was cold as she suddenly pointed at Tanner and said, "I want you to remove him from his position and fire him!"

Aria launched a counter-attack against Tanner.

Tanner's face changed drastically.

The shareholders looked at each other and then directly announced to Tanner, "Tanner, you're fired. Pack your things and get out of here right away!"

Although Tanner's wife was quite attractive, Noah was almost tired of her.

Hearing this, Tanner turned pale and sat down on the ground directly.

He had intended to use this opportunity to kick Aria out of the company, but he never expected that it would be himself who was fired in the end.

As if thinking of something, he pleaded with Aria, "Ms. Jenkins, I'm sorry. I won't do this again. Please give me a chance..."

However, Aria looked at him coldly and didn't say a word.

"I..."

Tanner was about to say something more when Noah interrupted him, "You guys, throw him out of here right now!"
