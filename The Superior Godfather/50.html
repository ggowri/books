Chapter 50 Herbert's Shock

The treatment was completed quickly.

Brian reluctantly pulled his hand away from Eliza's chest.

"How are you feeling now, Ms. Gray?"

Eliza stretched casually, revealing her perfect figure. She smiled, "I feel great. Thank you, Mr. Edwards!"

"No problem!"

Brian flashed a casual smile and led her out of the guest room.

"How are you doing, Eliza?" Anastasia asked, looking up from the TV where she was sitting on the couch.

Eliza replied with a smile, "Ms. Vaughn, thank you for your concern. After Mr. Edwards' treatment, I'm almost fully recovered. His elemental energy is truly amazing."

Hearing Eliza's praise for Brian, Anastasia felt an inexplicable sense of pride.

After all, Brian was the man she loved.

"You two rest for a bit. I'll get dinner started."

Brian tied on an apron and headed into the kitchen.

Eliza couldn't help but ask curiously, "Ms. Vaughn, does Brian really know how to cook?"

Anastasia nodded with a smile. "Absolutely. And his food is even better than what you'd get from a five-star hotel chef. You're in for a treat today!"

Eliza looked surprised, her eyes filled with doubt. "Really?"

Anastasia smiled enigmatically.

"You'll see!"

"Time to eat!"

An hour later, Brian emerged from the kitchen carrying a tray of delicious dishes, filling the dining table with an array of mouth-watering meals.

There were foie gras, prawns, spicy roast chicken, prime ribs, and pork chops...

The aroma of spicy and savory flavors filled the dining room, making Eliza's appetite soar.

"Wow, that smells amazing! Brian, you're incredible! Did you make all this by yourself?"

Eliza looked at the table in awe, her mouth watering.

Anastasia, however, picked up her fork and said calmly, "This is just his basic skill. Go ahead. Taste it."

She encouraged Eliza to try a piece of the spicy roasted chicken first.

Eliza took a bite, savoring the flavors curiously.

The next second, she let out a satisfied gasp.

"Wow! This is so delicious! I love the spiciness and the tenderness of the chicken. I can't stop eating!"

Eliza went on to try the other dishes.

At that moment, she finally understood why Anastasia had come here to enjoy a meal.

Brian's cooking was really exceptional!

Watching Eliza's ravenous expression, Anastasia chuckled.

Then, she picked up her fork and began to eat as well.

The next moment, she let out a contented sigh.

Brian's dishes were as delicious as ever, whetting her appetite.

Since many of the dishes were spicy, they both started to sweat. They even unbuttoned their clothes a bit, revealing large areas of enticing skin.

The sight made Brian's mouth go dry.

"Oh, that was so satisfying!"

An hour later, Eliza and Anastasia finally finished all the delicious food.

They slumped back in their chairs, their beautiful faces filled with contentment.

Eliza looked at Brian admiringly and said, "Brian, you are by far the best chef I've ever met!"

Brian felt proud of the high praise.

He replied with a smile, "Thank you. It's an honor to receive such a compliment from you!"

Eliza's phone rang just as she was about to speak.

"Ms. Vaughn, it's a call from your father," she said.

Anastasia said casually, "Go ahead and answer it."

Eliza nodded slightly and stepped out onto the balcony to take the call.

Herbert's voice came through the phone, authoritative and oppressive.

"Eliza, how did it go?"

Eliza replied respectfully, "Mr. Vaughn, I've tested Brian as you instructed."

Herbert asked gruffly, "Did he withstand how many of your moves?"

Eliza's face turned red with embarrassment. She hesitated for a moment and whispered, "Four."

"Four? So Brian is indeed a useless guy. Tell Anastasia to come back right away."

Eliza quickly said, "Mr. Vaughn, you've got it all wrong! I mean I failed to last four moves against him... And he even let me have three more moves first. Otherwise, I wouldn't have been able to withstand even one of his moves."
