Chapter 82 Cerberus Crew in Poskon

Three days had passed since Sophia had laid Ryan to rest, and the weight of the tragedy had aged her beyond her years.

After much hesitation, she decided to visit Ethan, Ryan's father, in prison, accompanied by Anne.

"Sophia! What are you doing here alone? Where's Ryan? Has he finally joined the War Deities and caught the Master's attention? That's why he couldn't come to see me?"

Ethan's face was a mask of urgency as he spoke, his excitement palpable.

For days, the mere thought of his son joining the War Deities had kept him awake at night, buzzing with anticipation.

Sophia felt a pang of discomfort at Ethan's hopeful gaze, unsure of how to break the news.

Anne sighed deeply, her voice steady but somber. "Ethan, I'm here to tell you something very important. You need to brace yourself."

Ethan's brow furrowed in confusion. "What could possibly require preparation?"

Taking a deep breath, Anne delivered the devastating blow. "Ryan... he's dead."

Ethan's face drained of color as he shot to his feet, disbelief washing over him.

"What? Ryan is dead?

"That can't be! You're joking, right?"

"Mr. Ethan Carter, we're not joking... Ryan is truly gone." Sophia, her heart heavy, held out a gold watch. "This is his belonging. Please take it."

Ethan's eyes widened as he recognized the familiar watch.

It was as if the ground had been pulled from beneath him; his face turned ghostly pale.

Ethan had been waiting in prison for Ryan to join the War Deities and take him home, never imagining the news he would receive would be this horrific.

At that moment, he could no longer contain his grief.

He collapsed into sobs. "Ryan! My Ryan... oh, no!"

"Mr. Ethan Carter, please take care of yourself. I'm so sorry for your loss," Sophia said softly, her own heart aching.

As she turned to leave, Ethan suddenly called out, "Sophia, tell me the truth. How did Ryan die?"

"I saw the police footage. He was under some external pressure, lost his grip, and jumped down the building..."

Sophia's words hung heavy in the air, and Ethan paused, confusion etched on his face.

"Ryan has always been so strong-willed. What could have possibly pushed him to that point?"

"The footage wasn't clear or complete, so I can't say for sure what triggered it... but there's no doubt he took his own life." Sophia's voice was laced with bitterness and helplessness.

Inside, she too was grappling with the question of what had driven Ryan to such despair.

Ethan fell into a long, heavy silence, the weight of her words sinking in like a stone.

Suddenly, Ethan looked up at Sophia with fierce determination. "Sophia, for Ryan's sake, I need you to do me a favor."

Sophia nodded. "Mr. Ethan Carter, whatever it is, just say the word."

Ethan's eyes flickered with hatred as he spoke through gritted teeth, "I need you to find Ryan's mother and inform her of his death.

"She needs to investigate the circumstances surrounding his passing and help seek vengeance!"

Sophia was taken aback. "But didn't Ryan's mother die shortly after he was born?"

Ethan confessed, "No. That was a lie I told him. The truth is, my union with his mother was purely accidental. And her identity is far too significant; she is Luna Sinclair, the leader of the Cerberus Crew in Poskon."

"What?"

Anne's eyes widened in shock, disbelief etched across her face.

Sophia felt the tumult of emotions raging within her.

The Cerberus Crew was infamous, a powerful gang ruling the underground world of Poskon with an iron fist, boasting hundreds of thousands of members.

The revelation that Ryan's mother was Luna, the head of such a notorious group, was staggering.

In the wake of her initial shock, excitement coursed through her veins.

If she could win Luna's support, the tides would surely turn in their favor.

Ethan continued, "I have a private safe at Switz Bank that contains a token Luna left behind. Take it to the Cerberus Crew and find her; she'll do everything in her power to assist you.

"Sophia, I'm entrusting this mission to you."

Without hesitation, Sophia replied, "Mr. Ethan Carter, you can count on me. I'll do everything I can to fulfill this mission and avenge Ryan."
