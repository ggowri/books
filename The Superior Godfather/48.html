Chapter 48 Eliza's Challenge

An hour later, Anne finally left.

Brian was about to head out to buy groceries when Celeste called. "Mr. Edwards, there's trouble."

"Thomas found out about Kevin's arrest and called an emergency shareholders' meeting at the company. Everyone's there, demanding answers from you.

"They say if you don't show up today and give them a satisfactory explanation, they'll pull their investments and the entire management team will resign and jump ship. Should I tell them you're on your way?"

Brian's brow furrowed.

He hadn't expected Thomas to react so fiercely.

He had just taken control of Trillion Group, and the three beauty products were still in development.

If the shareholders pulled out and the executives resigned en masse, Trillion Group would be thrown into chaos and panic, with disastrous consequences.

Brian thought for a moment and said, "Alright. Tell them to hold tight. I'll be there as soon as I wrap up what I'm doing."

After hanging up, Brian grabbed his groceries and headed to the café, where Anastasia was sitting, working on her laptop.

Eliza, dressed in a black leather jacket and looking every bit the tough bodyguard, stood respectfully behind her.

Brian walked up and smiled, "Rachael, sorry to keep you waiting."

"No problem. I was just finishing up some work," Anastasia replied with a charming smile.

She closed her laptop and handed it to Eliza.

She then glanced curiously at the pile of groceries in Brian's hands.

Actually, she was here not only to test Brian as per Herbert's instruction but also to enjoy Brian's meal.

"What did you get?"

"You'll see soon enough," Brian said with a mysterious grin, then turned his attention to Eliza.

"Rachael, who's this?" he asked.

Eliza extended her hand. "Mr. Edwards, I'm Eliza Gray, Ms. Vaughn's bodyguard."

Before Brian could respond, Eliza continued, "Ms. Vaughn says you're quite the fighter. How about a little sparring match?"

Her eyes were intense as she waited for his answer.

She had been tasked by Herbert to test Brian's skills. That was why she had this request.

The Vaughn family's internal conflicts were heating up, and Herbert didn't want Anastasia wasting time on a loser.

If Brian couldn't pass Eliza's test, Herbert would have Anastasia break off the engagement and marry another noble heir.

Anastasia was well aware of this, which was why she didn't stop Eliza from issuing the challenge.

Brian, however, remained calm and collected.

"If you're up for it, I would not turn you down!"

They moved to the nearby lawn, and Brian, the perfect gentleman, offered to let Eliza have the first three moves.

"Are you sure?" Eliza asked, surprised.

She was a formidable Meta and well-known for her fighting prowess in the Vaughn family.

Brian nodded and said in a good manner, "Absolutely. Don't hold back. Give it your all."

Eliza felt a flicker of irritation at what she perceived as Brian's arrogance.

"If that's how you want it, don't say I didn't warn you."

With that, she lunged forward like a cheetah, leaping into the air and bringing her right leg down on Brian with the force of a battle-axe.

Her speed was incredible.

Brian was impressed. He thought, "She is truly something."

He had just dodged the powerful strike when Eliza followed up with a kick to his face.

She was so fast she left afterimages in the air.

"That's some speed!"

Brian's eyes widened as he grabbed Eliza's leg just in time.

Her blow was blocked!

Eliza's face flickered with surprise.

She had given it her all, after all.

Immediately, she channeled her dark elemental energy, broke free from Brian's grip, and launched a punch at his chest.

Brian crossed his arms in front of him, blocking the blow.

A dull thud echoed as they both were pushed back by the force of the impact.

Brian steadied himself and stared at Eliza with a sharp gaze.

"Ms. Gray, that's three moves. It's my turn now," he said, his voice low and intense.
