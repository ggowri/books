Chapter 53 Karen's Apology

Brian looked at Karen with a calm expression.

"I said I'm here to talk to Aria about a partnership. I'm not here to apply for a job!"

Karen clearly didn't believe a word Brian said. She let out a derisive laugh and looked at him with a teasing smile.

"Don't even try to put on airs at a time like this. Do you really think I'll let security throw you out right now?"

Brian ignored her and took out his phone, calling Aria.

"Ms. Jenkins, I'm downstairs at your company. Someone's blocking my way. Could you come down, please?"

After hanging up, Brian turned to Karen.

"I've called Ms. Jenkins. She'll be right down to meet me. I suggest you don't make trouble for yourself. Go do your job and avoid losing this great position!"

In the past, it was always Karen who scolded Brian. Now, she was on the receiving end of his reprimand. Karen couldn't take it anymore.

"Brian, you son of a gun! Do you really think making a phone call is going to scare me? Dream on! I'm going to teach you a lesson today! Where's security? Throw this fraudster out!"

At her command, more than ten security guards, armed with batons, rushed from all directions and surrounded Brian.

"You've got some nerve coming to our company to scam us! Guys, beat him up!"

Just as the security captain, David Johnson, was about to move, a cold and authoritative female voice rang out sharply.

"Stop!"

The sudden voice startled everyone.

Under the stunned gazes of the crowd, a tall and elegant woman walked over, accompanied by her secretary.

She was Aria, the founder of Queen's Construction Group.

"Ms. Jenkins!"

"Ms. Jenkins!"

"Good afternoon, Ms. Jenkins!"

Everyone greeted Aria respectfully.

The security guards, led by David, also bowed their heads.

Karen's face was filled with shock and disbelief.

She thought, "Why would Ms. Jenkins come down at a time like this? Could it be that she's really here to see Brian? No way! There's no way a loser like Brian could know someone as important as Ms. Jenkins. This has to be a coincidence."

With that thought, Karen quickly said, "David, don't just stand there! Get rid of this waste of space right now! Don't let him disturb Ms. Jenkins..."

Aria slapped her across the face, cutting her off mid-sentence.

"Watch your mouth!"

"Ms. Jenkins," Karen said, covering her cheek in shock and confusion as she looked at Aria.

But Aria didn't even glance at her. She quickly walked up to Brian and bowed her head in apology.

"I'm sorry, Mr. Edwards. I've failed to keep my staff in line, and they've disrespected you. Please accept my apologies!"

Aria was extremely respectful towards Brian.

After all, he had won the Trillion Group's project from Ryan and Ethan.

His capabilities were undeniable.

For someone like him, Aria could only befriend him. She absolutely couldn't afford to offend Brian or become his enemy.

Otherwise, she might end up like Ethan and Ryan.

Seeing Aria's respectful attitude towards Brian, everyone was stunned.

The receptionist's mouth dropped open in shock.

She thought, "He was telling the truth! He really knows Ms. Jenkins!"

David and the other security guards were frozen in place.

Thinking about how they had almost beaten up and thrown out Brian, they felt a chill run down their spines.

They knew how arrogant Aria could be. Even when high-ranking officials visited the company, she hadn't shown them this much respect.

Karen's face turned pale.

Brian hadn't been putting on airs in front of her. He had genuinely called Aria.

She thought, "He's just a loser! How could he know Aria? Why is she treating him with such respect?"

At that moment, Karen's mind was filled with too many questions.

"Ms. Jenkins, there's no need for such formality. I should have informed you in advance," Brian said.

Aria smiled, "Mr. Edwards, rest assured. I will give you a satisfactory resolution for what happened today."

Then, Aria coldly scanned Karen and David among others.

She barked, "Stop standing there like statues! Apologize to Mr. Edwards right now!"
