Chapter 63 Warning to Others

Brian scanned the room and asked, "What do you think of my decision to transfer Eric to the publicity department?"

Celeste was quick to respond, "Mr. Edwards is brilliant."

Others quickly joined in and praised Brian.

"Mr. Edwards, you know how to use people wisely. I admire you!"

"The Publicity Department needs someone like Mr. Eric Lee. We believe he will excel there."

"That's right! Mr. Eric Lee has exceptional skills. He'll strengthen the department."

"Congratulations on your promotion, Mr. Eric Lee! You have to treat us to a drink later."

Eric's face fell as he absorbed the crowd's praise. He looked as if he had lost his soul and looked extremely depressed.

He knew that his career was completely over.

Thomas felt helpless, too.

After Kevin's arrest, he had hoped to support Eric's ascent to vice president.

But the opposite happened.

Eric didn't even get the job of vice president and was even demoted to a powerless role in the publicity department.

Brian's strategy had outmaneuvered him.

Thomas knew very well that he had lost this confrontation.

And he had lost completely.

He underestimated Brian.

Brian said, "Since there are no objections, it's decided.

"Eric, report to the publicity department tomorrow."

Brian dismissed Eric's pale demeanor and took his place at the conference table.

The room fell silent under his commanding gaze.

Calmly, Brian addressed the group, "I'm sure you're all wondering why I started Transcendent Construction Group for the Weston project."

Hearing this, everyone nodded curiously.

Even Thomas and Eric looked at him in confusion.

Without explanation, Brian distributed the signed partnership agreement with Aria. "Here's the cooperation agreement. You'll understand once you read it."

Everyone looked at the contents of the cooperation agreement with curiosity.

When they finished reading it, shock and realization spread through the room.

They finally grasped Brian's strategy, which had been in motion since Kevin's arrest.

The investment promotion conference was a calculated move.

One was to eliminate internal threats, clean up the workplace, and give everyone a warning.

The second reason was to save the company's costs to a greater extent and secure fifty percent of the project's profits for free.

If Trillion Group directly took this project out, no matter which company took it over, he wouldn't get the fifty percent profit.

Brian's plan was a masterstroke.

While they were shocked and impressed by Brian's methods and layout, they also felt a little more awe for him.

Even Thomas and Eric were impressed by his methods.

He was even more terrifying than they imagined.

Damian resolved to align himself with Brian, the formidable new chairman.

Unbeknownst to them, Brian had another motive.

He wanted to take revenge on Sophia and Ryan and make them regret it.

Though Celeste was aware of Brian's plan, she remained impressed.

She had underestimated him for too long.

Just as she prepared to praise him, the shareholders and executives beat her to it.

They looked at Brian with eyes full of admiration and worship and spoke attentively.

"Mr. Edwards, you're truly wise and intelligent."

"Your strategy is impressive. I admire you very much!"

"Yes, I believe Trillion Group will thrive under your leadership."

Seeing this scene, Thomas' expression looked extremely complicated.

He gathered these shareholders and senior executives to deal with Brian and put pressure on him.

But now they had become his supporters.

This time, he really lost miserably.

Brian smiled, stretched out his hands, and pressed them down slightly, and the scene suddenly became quiet.

He sipped his coffee leisurely, smiling at the crowd. "I heard Celeste mention that you're all eager to find me something. Let's hear it."

At his words, the crowd looked at each other, not knowing how to respond for a moment.

After all, they had all been summoned by Thomas.

Damian turned to Thomas and smiled, "Thomas, since you've called everyone here, why not share what's on your mind?"

As soon as these words were spoken, everyone's eyes fell on Thomas.
