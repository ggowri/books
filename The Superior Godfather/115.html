Chapter 115 Breaking Up

"Dad, I didn't do anything wrong. Why should I apologize to them?" Patrick protested with defiance.

Eleanor chimed in. "Mr. Smith, this Brian is just worthless trash from the bottom of society, a broke loser... And that woman's just a college student looking for a sugar daddy. Why should we apologize to people like them?"

"That's right, Dad. If anyone should apologize, it should be them apologizing to us."

Seeing their complete lack of judgment, Paul was livid.

He slapped Eleanor hard across the face and kicked Patrick.

"Worthless trash from the bottom of society? A broke loser?" he roared.

"And Ms. Hawthorne is looking for a sugar daddy? Which idiot filled your heads with this nonsense?"

As he spoke, Paul pulled off his belt and lashed out at Eleanor and Patrick, leaving welts on their skin.

"Listen up, you stupid fools! Mr. Edwards is a partner at the Trillion Group, and Ms. Hawthorne is the current CEO of Trillion Group!"

His words hit Patrick and Eleanor like lightning bolts, and they stared at Brian and Celeste in disbelief.

"What? Brian is a partner at Trillion Group!

"That woman is the CEO of Trillion Group?"

Olivia's eyes went wide at this moment, shock spreading across her heavily made-up face.

At that moment, she finally understood why Paul had hit her and gotten so angry.

These two people were completely out of their league to offend.

Seeing Patrick and Eleanor frozen in place, she shouted sharply.

"You two idiots, what are you standing there for? Get on your knees and apologize to Mr. Edwards and Ms. Hawthorne right now!"

As she spoke, she was the first to drop to her knees in front of Brian and Celeste.

"Mr. Edwards, Ms. Hawthorne... this is all our fault. We failed to teach them proper manners, which led to this misunderstanding. I sincerely apologize to both of you and hope you can forgive our offensive and ignorant behavior."

After speaking, she knocked her head against the ground three times in a deep bow.

Even Patrick, who was dense, could tell he had messed with the wrong people today, so he quickly apologized.

"Mr. Edwards, Ms. Hawthorne, I'm so sorry!"

Eleanor's face was bitter with regret. Though it was hard for her to accept the reality, she still knelt before Brian and forced out the words through her humiliation.

"Mr. Edwards, Ms. Hawthorne, I'm sorry."

"Celeste, let's go," Brian said coldly.

He gave them one last icy glance before turning to leave with Celeste.

They were nothing but ants to him, and they were not worth wasting any more time on.

Eleanor's heart grew more bitter at being ignored by Brian.

Once upon a time, she had seen Brian as nothing but a worthless loser, a poor devil she could bully and push around at will.

She never imagined this bastard would transform into a partner at the Trillion Group after divorcing Sophia.

This dramatic change in status was beyond her comprehension and acceptance.

Paul and Olivia breathed a sigh of relief when they saw that Brian and Celeste had not pursued the matter.

They then turned their attention to Patrick, shouting, "You good-for-nothing! Do you realize you almost got our entire Smith family killed today?

"Tell us what happened. Now."

Patrick hesitated before speaking quietly, "I... I don't know what happened. Eleanor said they bullied her, so I wanted to help her get revenge, and then..."

Paul and Olivia's eyes flashed coldly as they turned to look at Eleanor.

Just as Eleanor was about to speak, the manager of the Balenciaga store walked over.

"Mr. Smith, here's what happened. Mr. Edwards was shopping here with Ms. Hawthorne when Eleanor deliberately came over to harass and provoke them..."

Paul's face darkened as he demanded, "Is this true?"

"Mr. Smith, I... I..."

Before Eleanor could finish her sentence, Olivia cut her off with a sharp slap.

"You clueless idiot! You almost got our whole Smith family killed... Son, you know how to deal with stupid women like this, don't you?"

Patrick was shocked that Eleanor had started this whole mess. His face darkened with anger.

"Patrick, I..."

"Eleanor, we're done!" Patrick cut her off before she could explain.

He had always thought Eleanor was a kind and gentle woman, but now he saw her true colors - a bully who stirred up trouble.

Today, she had nearly caused the complete downfall of the Smith family.

Keeping a woman like this by his side would only hold him back.
