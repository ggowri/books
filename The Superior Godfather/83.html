Chapter 83 Karen's Request for Help

As Sophia stepped out of the prison, a wave of exhilaration washed over her, dispelling the dark clouds that had lingered in her heart.

She couldn't believe that Ryan's mother was none other than Luna, the leader of the Cerberus Crew.

Sophia thought to herself, "If I can find Luna with that token, as Ryan's fiancée, she'll surely trust and support me. With her backing, I can rise again and reclaim my place.

After all, the Cerberus Crew is the most powerful force in Poskon, with endless wealth and influence in the shadows."

Eager to set her plan in motion, she rushed to Switz Bank with Anne by her side to access Ethan's private safe.

Inside, she discovered a uniquely crafted dagger, its blade etched with Luna's name.

"Mr. Ethan Carter wasn't lying after all!" Sophia exclaimed, her heart racing with excitement as she admired the dagger.

After a moment of contemplation, she turned to Anne. "Anne, go back and tell my parents. I'm heading to Poskon to find Luna!

"With her support, the Evans family will rise again and reclaim its former glory.

Anne hesitated for a moment before nodding. "Just be careful out there!"

"Don't worry; I've got plenty of friends in Poskon. I'll be fine," Sophia said with a bright smile, clutching the dagger tightly as she set off.

Meanwhile, Anne returned to the Evans Villa to share the news with Robert and Mary.

"What? Ryan's mother is Luna, the leader of the Cerberus Crew?" Robert exclaimed, his eyes wide with disbelief.

"Ha! This is fantastic! If Sophia can win Luna's support, we will be back on top in no time. I can't wait to see those ungrateful leeches crawling back to us, begging for forgiveness!"

As Anne watched Robert and Mary's excitement, she felt a nagging unease.

Deep down, she sensed that Ryan's death might be connected to Brian.

If that were true, and Luna found out, Brian would be in serious trouble.

After all, the Cerberus Crew was no joke.

That night, Anne decided to confront Brian at Riverside Villa No. 9.

She needed to know if he had anything to do with Ryan's death.

Brian wasn't surprised by her arrival.

With a playful smile, he asked, "So, did you wrap up Ryan's affairs?"

Anne's tone turned serious. "I booked you a flight out of here. Why didn't you leave?"

Brian shrugged, unfazed. "Why would I leave? Just because of Ryan? I'm not about to uproot my life for him. Besides, wouldn't you miss me?"

As he spoke, Brian playfully lifted Anne's chin, his gaze lingering on her beautiful face.

"You..."

Anne felt her cheeks flush, turning her head away. "I need you to be honest with me. Is Ryan's death connected to you?"

Brian didn't shy away from the question. "Yeah, it's definitely connected to me."

Even though Anne had suspected it, hearing him confirm it left Anne momentarily stunned, her mind racing with the implications.

Anne turned to Brian, her brow furrowed in confusion. "What did you do to Ryan?

"Why did he end up losing his mind and jumping off the building?"

Brian's gaze slowly roamed over Anne's figure, a teasing glint in his eyes. "Honestly, I didn't do much.

"I just revealed my true identity to him. Turns out, he couldn't handle the shock and cracked under pressure. He lost it and took the plunge."

Anne gasped, her mind racing.

"What on earth is your true identity that could push Ryan to such a breaking point?"

"I thought I mentioned it before? I'm the Master of the War Deities. The whole thing with summoning Ryan? That was my doing, so..."

Before he could finish, Anne burst into laughter. "Brian, come on! Are you serious?

"You've been with the Evans family for years. If you were really the Master of the War Deities, I would've known!

"Besides, how could someone of that stature stoop to being a house husband in our family?

"Let's be real—your presence at Ryan's death scene was just a coincidence, right?"

Seeing Anne's disbelief, Brian shrugged, clearly uninterested in convincing her. "Believe what you want. I couldn't care less."

Relief washed over Anne as she thought, "Ryan's death has nothing to do with Brian.

He just happened to be there.

Even if Luna and the Cerberus Crew come looking for revenge, Brian won't be implicated."

Just then, Anne's phone buzzed, breaking the tension.

It was Karen calling.

As soon as she picked up, Karen's voice came through, choked with tears. "Anne... I'm in deep trouble.

"The loan sharks are at my door, and I can't pay them back. I'm scared to go home...

"Please, you've got to help me! I really don't know what to do!" Karen sobbed, desperation lacing her words.
