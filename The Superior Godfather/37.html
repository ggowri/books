Chapter 37 An Awkward Encounter

At 7:40 PM, business tycoons, wealthy families, and socialites from all sectors began arriving at the Plato International Hotel for Trillion Group's investment conference, creating a glamorous red carpet affair.

When Anastasia stepped onto the red carpet on Brian's arm, they immediately became the center of attention, causing quite a stir.

The media photographers went crazy, snapping pictures of them non-stop.

"Wow, who's that gorgeous woman? She's stunning. Her looks, figure, and that presence... she's top-tier!"

"That guy is super handsome, too. You can tell he's some big shot from a wealthy family."

"Hey, does anyone know who they are? I don't think I've ever seen them before."

"No idea, but just look at their appearance and aura... they must be someone important!"

Sophia and Ryan had just arrived when they overheard these comments.

Their faces darkened when they spotted Brian and Anastasia on the red carpet.

Jealousy flashed in Sophia's eyes as she spat out angrily, "What's wrong with your eyes? Brian, a big shot? Let me tell you the truth! He's just a loser I dumped."

Her outburst immediately drew many curious glances from the crowd.

"Is that the loser Brian you divorced recently, Ms. Evans?" asked someone who knew Sophia, their face filled with shock.

Sophia nodded. "That's him. God knows what's wrong with that woman to be interested in someone like him."

Aria Jenkins, CEO of Queen's Construction Group, frowned slightly, giving Sophia a skeptical look.

"Sophia, are you sure you haven't mistaken him for someone else? The man I saw had both looks and presence. He's definitely not a loser."

Ryan cut in irritably. "Even if Sophia could be wrong, you think I'd make the same mistake?

"That loser is Brian! He just got lucky enough to hook up with some rich beauty who bought him fancy cars and a mansion. She even started a company for him. Now he's talking big about competing with me to become Trillion Group's partner. It's ridiculous!"

Everyone was startled by Ryan's presence.

Their faces quickly broke into flattering smiles.

They all knew about the connection between Ryan's father, Ethan, and Kevin, the VP of Trillion Group.

Ryan was already Trillion Group's chosen partner-to-be.

"Come on, Mr. Carter, you're joking! Everyone knows the Carter Group is locked in as Trillion Group's partner. What chance does that gold-digger have against you?"

"That's right, Mr. Carter. Let me be the first to congratulate you on becoming Trillion Group's partner!"

"Mr. Carter, congratulations! Don't forget about your old friends when you make it big!"

"Don't worry, everyone. There'll be plenty to go around..."

Ryan beamed at their flattery, feeling downright smug.

A proud smile spread across Sophia's face.

It was the man she had chosen.

How could a loser like Brian, who relied on women, even compare to him?

Seeing this, Aria shook her head and turned away, her mature and alluring figure swaying as she walked toward the inner conference hall.

Having met countless people, she knew Brian's air of nobility and refinement wasn't an act, nor did she believe he was just a man living off women.

If anything, Ryan seemed shallow. He constantly showed off his family connections, put on a fake front, and chased empty fame—not the type who would achieve anything significant.

*****

As the investment conference was about to begin, people started filing in to find their seats.

Brian and Anastasia had just settled into their second-row seats when Ryan and Sophia sat beside them.

Both Ryan and Sophia shot Brian mocking smiles.

"Well, look who dared to show up," Ryan sneered.

"If you two can be here, why can't I?" Brian shot back.

Ryan's smirk grew even more condescending. "Don't tell me you think you can steal the Trillion Group deal from me just because you have Celeste backing you?"

Seeing their smug expressions, Brian couldn't help but smile. It was his showtime.

He'd let this pathetic couple enjoy their moment before crushing their spirits and leaving them in despair.

So, he feigned surprise as he addressed Ryan and Sophia.

"How did you know I was acquainted with Celeste?"

"Oh, we know more than that. We know you beat up Kevin to get a deal with the Trillion Group. You forced him to sign that cooperation agreement," Ryan sneered.

"Don't tell me a broke nobody like you thought that contract would hold up?" Sophia said with a sneer.
