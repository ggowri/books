Chapter 64 Recognizing Brian

Thomas' expression darkened.

He hadn't expected Damian to cut ties with him so quickly and choose to stand with Brian so quickly.

Apart from Eric, he had made the most noise moments ago.

Feeling the weight of the crowd's gaze, Thomas forced a smile and said, "It's nothing serious, but Kevin's accident has significantly impacted the company.

"I wanted everyone to meet with the chairman to discuss a solution to mitigate the negative effects.

"I wonder what the chairman thinks about this matter?"

He then looked at Brian, eager to see how he would respond.

He wanted to see what he was capable of.

Brian narrowed his eyes, sensing Thomas' intent to undermine him publicly.

However, he was prepared.

He turned to Celeste and said calmly, "Celeste, I recall discussing how to handle this. Please present your proposal."

"Yes, Mr. Edwards," Celeste replied, nodding as she distributed a prepared plan.

The proposal was straightforward, which was to issue a notice to the public and apologize.

The content roughly openly acknowledged a series of facts, including Kevin's corruption and bribery, and repeatedly emphasized that Trillion Group had been actively carrying out internal rectification for better development.

The Kevin incident was the attitude and result of the internal rectification of Trillion Group.

Now that this cancer had been completely eradicated, the company would no longer have to worry and would fully embrace a new future and challenge new heights.

As the crowd read the proposal, shock spread among them.

The announcement not only acknowledged Kevin's wrongdoing but also demonstrated Trillion Group's accountability.

At the same time, it also strengthened Trillion Group's future development and attitude, letting everyone outside know that Trillion Group was an excellent enterprise worthy of reliance and trust.

They could foresee that once the announcement was made, all negative impacts on Trillion Group would disappear, and the falling stocks would rebound.

Admiration for Brian swelled among the attendees.

Damian exclaimed, "Mr. Edwards, your public relations strategy is brilliant! It not only salvages our image but elevates it."

Others echoed his sentiment.

"Yes, Mr. Edwards, we're impressed."

"We're fortunate to have you as our chairman."

"With your leadership, Trillion Group is set to soar."

Many shareholders expressed their support for Brian.

"Mr. Edwards, we will no longer be involved in any of the company's affairs. You can do whatever you want."

"That's right, Mr. Edwards. Just go ahead and do it. We will unconditionally support any decision you make."

The purpose of everyone investing in a company was to make profits.

They recognized his strength and skills, believing he would bring greater benefits as the head of Trillion Group.

Watching the enthusiastic shareholders and executives, Eric's expression darkened.

He hadn't anticipated their swift change of heart in favor of Brian.

Thomas, meanwhile, felt a mix of frustration and disappointment.

He originally wanted to take this opportunity to give Brian a difficult problem and embarrass him.

He never anticipated that he would not only devise a perfect solution but also earn the shareholders' high praise.

From this moment on, Brian had firmly established his foothold in the company.

"Thank you for your trust. I will not disappoint you," Brian said, standing with a satisfied smile.

"I'm sure you're all tired after a long day.

"I've booked a banquet and rooms at the Paradise Hotel, so let's go have a drink and relax."

Upon hearing this, everyone showed surprise and astonishment on their faces.

They hadn't expected such an arrangement.

Brian's interpersonal skills were impressive.

Celeste gazed at him, her eyes sparkling with admiration.

She had never expected that Brian had such an arrangement.

This gesture would dissipate the resentment of those who had waited for him.

They were amazed by Brian's strategy.

Damian smiled, increasingly impressed by Brian's capabilities.

Eric clenched his fists, glaring at Brian in indignation. "This guy knows how to win people over."

Thomas looked at Brian with a mix of emotions, feeling an unfamiliar sense of fear toward someone so young.

What was frightening wasn't he showed off his talents but his ability to adapt and conceal his mistakes.
