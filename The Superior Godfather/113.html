Chapter 113 My Dad's Coming!

Patrick had trained in martial arts since childhood, specializing in kickboxing and fighting techniques. His combat skills were impressive.

He'd made a name for himself in underground fight clubs, earning the title "Boxing King of Riverwood" with an incredible streak of eighty-six wins.

His lightning-fast punch shot straight for Brian's face, but Brian blocked it with just one finger.

"How is this possible?"

Patrick's eyes widened as he stared in shock at the single index finger that had stopped his punch.

Drawing on his extensive fighting experience, he quickly shook off his surprise and prepared to change tactics, but Brian flicked his finger.

"Argh!"

The energy sent Patrick flying backward like a cannonball. He crashed to the ground, clutching his shattered fist and screaming in agony.

"Baby!

"Baby, are you okay?"

Eleanor rushed to his side, her face filled with concern.

Patrick didn't respond. He just clutched his right hand in pain while glaring at Brian with pure venom in his eyes.

He never imagined this guy would be so mighty.

"Let's go!"

Brian gave them a cold glance before turning to leave with Celeste, not wanting any further entanglement.

Patrick's face turned ashen with rage, feeling deeply insulted. He roared at his bodyguards.

"What the hell are you standing around for? Stop these two!

"My dad will be here any minute. Then I'll make this bastard pay!"

The bodyguards hesitated, then forced themselves to block Brian's path despite their injuries.

Brian stopped in his tracks and turned to Patrick. "Looking for another beating?"

"Screw you, you piece of shit! Just wait till my dad gets here. Then you'll see how I'm gonna end you!"

Patrick glared at Brian with venomous eyes, his teeth clenched in anger.

A mocking smile played across Brian's lips, his eyes filled with contempt.

He remembered something about Patrick's father, Paul.

At the business conference, Paul had initially kissed Ryan and Sophia. Still, when Brian became a partner at Trillion Group, he'd done a complete one-eighty and started groveling for forgiveness.

Brian had zero respect for such a two-faced opportunist.

With a cold smirk, he said, "You think daddy's gonna come save you?

"Want to bet he'll be on his knees begging for forgiveness when he sees me?"

Patrick couldn't help but laugh at Brian's words.

"You're full of shit. Who the hell do you think you are?" he sneered.

Eleanor chimed in with a mocking smile, "Brian, you worthless piece of trash, you're so full of yourself. Do you even know who Patrick's father is?

"Let me tell you! He is Paul Smith, and he's got connections everywhere. He's a big shot in Riverwood who can make a nobody like you disappear without breaking a sweat.

"You better get on your knees and apologize right now, or you'll be sorry when he arrives."

Brian and Celeste exchanged amused glances before responding.

"Oh really? We'd love to see what he can do to us."

Just then, a commotion erupted from the crowd.

To everyone's astonishment, Paul appeared with his wife, Olivia Smith, accompanied by hundreds of intimidating men in black suits marching toward them.

Their overwhelming presence and powerful aura had everyone backing away, creating a path through the crowd.

"Dad! Mom!" Patrick called out excitedly.

"Mr. and Mrs. Smith," Eleanor greeted them as she and Patrick rushed to meet them.

Seeing Patrick's miserable condition, Paul's face darkened, while Olivia asked with anger and heartbreak.

"Patrick, Eleanor... which bastard did this to you?"

"Mom, it was those two pieces of trash... you have to get justice for us!"

Patrick complained to Olivia about Brian and Celeste with a tearful face, spewing insults.

After hearing his story, Paul's face turned dark as thunder. In contrast, Olivia's face flushed red with rage as she pointed at Brian and Celeste and shouted.

"You worthless pieces of garbage, you dare to mess with my son?

"What are you all standing around for? Take these two out and dump them in the river for the fish!"
