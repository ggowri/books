Chapter 110 His Ex-wife's Best Friend

The sudden female voice startled them both.

Brian looked up and saw a young woman with heavy makeup and a pretty face walking in with an expensive bag.

Brian knew this woman. Her name was Eleanor Brooks, and she was Sophia's best friend. It was said that her boyfriend was a top rich man.

When Brian was with Sophia before, Eleanor often belittled him and made sarcastic remarks.

After hearing her words, the salesgirl who was responsible for receiving Brian hesitated for a moment and said apologetically, "Ma'am, I'm really sorry. This dress and stockings have been reserved by this gentleman."

Hearing this, Eleanor frowned. Her eyes fell on Brian, and a hint of surprise flashed in her eyes. She just felt that he was a little familiar.

The next moment, as if she thought of something, she smiled coldly. She looked at Brian with a playful look.

"Brian, it's you, loser. You're dressed so well that I almost didn't recognize you.

"You're something. You got a new woman so soon after being dumped by Sophia. She's young and pretty. I guess she's a poor college student who hasn't seen the world.

"But the dress and stockings she's wearing are not something a poor bastard like you can afford.

"If you know what's good for you, ask her to take off the dress immediately and leave with her. Otherwise, you'll embarrass yourself here."

Brian glanced at Eleanor coldly and ignored her.

He turned his head and said to the salesgirl, "Don't worry about her. Please continue to help my friend choose."

"Okay, sir!"

"You..."

Seeing Brian dare to ignore her like this, Eleanor felt greatly insulted.

She crossed her arms in front of her chest, looked down at Brian, and sneered.

"Tsk! You are still pretending in front of me, loser? Continue to choose? I'll wait and see if you can afford them in the end."

After saying that, she actually sat aside and waited to see Brian make a fool of himself.

Brian ignored her. He was interested in watching Celeste put on various styles of clothes and stockings, which made him happy.

Eleanor had to admit that Celeste, who was wearing various styles of clothes and stockings, was really amazing and beautiful.

She was like a completely different person.

If Brian were with her, she would wear a different outfit and stockings every day, just like changing a girlfriend every day.

It was simply a different experience.

It made Eleanor feel ashamed.

She wondered where Brian, the poor bastard, had tricked such a beautiful poor college girl.

Brian was very satisfied with the clothes and stockings Celeste tried and said to the salesgirl without hesitation, "Pack up all the clothes and stockings she tried on."

As soon as these words came out, the salesgirl was stunned.

Because Celeste tried more than 30 sets of clothes and stockings of different styles.

Each piece was a luxury item, and all of them cost a lot of money.

Eleanor couldn't help laughing and looked at Brian as if she were looking at an idiot.

"Pack up everything? Brian, do you think you are buying cabbage in the market?

"Do you know how much these clothes and stockings cost in total?

"Let's not talk about other things. Just one pair of stockings costs about four thousand dollars. Can a poor bastard like you afford it?"

"Can't afford it? Are you talking about yourself?"

Brian looked at Eleanor with disdain. He took out his bank card and handed it to the salesgirl.

"Swipe the card!"

"Brian, are you still pretending? Do you think I don't know your financial strength?"

Eleanor sneered, "Swipe the card? Let me see how long you can pretend!"

She believed that Brian's bank card could not have so much money.

However, the next second, he paid successfully.

Under her stunned gaze, the salesgirl handed the bank card and a brand new platinum membership card to Brian with her managers.

"Hello, Mr. Edwards. Your total spending this time is 9.86 million dollars. Here is your receipt. In addition, our company has issued a platinum membership card for you, a premium customer, for free, which you can enjoy a variety of benefits. Please keep it."

As Eleanor saw Brian surrounded by the managers and listened to their conversation, her mind went blank, and she stood frozen in place.

"Brian's bank card can actually pay 9.86 million dollars at one time?"

This fact had brought her a huge shock.

It overturned her understanding and made it difficult for her to understand and believe.

She didn't understand where Brian got so much money.

The group of salesgirls in the store opposite had been paying attention to their movements, ready to see Brian make a fool of himself.

But at this moment, they all widened their eyes and looked incredible.

Especially the salesgirl who mocked Brian for not being able to afford them. Her expression was like seeing a ghost.

In fact, their stockings could also be tried on. It was just that she thought Brian was a poor bastard and couldn't afford them. So she said that on purpose.

But she never expected that Brian was actually a hidden rich man who could spend nearly 10 million dollars at a time.

She regretted it very much.

Because she missed a top guest in her career, a top order in her life, and a great opportunity to get rich.
