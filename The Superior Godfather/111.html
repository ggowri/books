Chapter 111 Arrogant Rich Guy

"Mr. Edwards, Ms. Hawthorne. You bought too many things, and it's inconvenient to carry them. Our company provides door-to-door delivery service. Please leave an address. We will deliver it to your door later!"

After hearing the manager's words, Brian and Celeste nodded, left their addresses, and turned to leave.

But they were stopped by Eleanor before they took a few steps.

She stared at Brian arrogantly, pointed at his nose, and asked, "Brian, tell the truth. Where did you get all this money? Did you secretly transfer Sophia's property?"

In Eleanor's opinion, Brian was a poor bastard at the bottom of society, and it was impossible for him to have so much money.

Brian's eyes flashed, and he looked at Eleanor with impatience.

"Why should I tell you where my money came from? Who are you to me? What does this have to do with you?"

"I..."

Eleanor was speechless. She was about to speak when she was interrupted by Brian.

"A good dog doesn't block the road. Please get out of the way!"

Eleanor was shaking with anger, staring at Brian with anger.

"You... You called me a dog?"

Brian said nonchalantly, "I didn't say that. You admitted it yourself!"

"Brian, you're asking for death!"

Eleanor had never been treated like this before. She raised her hand and wanted to slap Brian in the face.

Celeste's eyes flashed coldly. She stood in front of Brian and grabbed her wrist.

Eleanor looked ugly and cursed. "You bitch, let me go!"

Celeste's face darkened, and she slapped Eleanor in the face.

She had been unhappy with Eleanor for a while.

"Ah..."

Eleanor screamed and fell heavily to the ground.

She covered her face and looked at Celeste with undisguised anger.

"You... You bitch. You hit me? I'll kill you!"

After saying that, she suddenly stretched out her hands with sharp nails and pounced on Celeste like a ghost.

Eleanor wanted to tear Celeste's face and completely disfigure her.

Unfortunately, she was kicked away before she rushed to Celeste.

Long legs were an advantage.

And Celeste's movements were so cool, which made Brian's eyes light up.

He didn't expect Celeste to be a martial artist.

Eleanor fell to the ground and covered her stomach in pain. She stared at Brian and Celeste with cold eyes. Her eyes were filled with undisguised anger and resentment.

"You bastards! How dare you do this to me? Do you know who my boyfriend is?"

Hearing this, Brian smiled. Celeste asked with interest, "Oh? Tell me. Who is your boyfriend?"

"You should have heard of the Paul Group, right?"

Eleanor's eyes flashed with pride, and she stared at Brian and Celeste with a cold face.

"My boyfriend is Patrick Smith from the Paul Group. If you know what's good for you, kneel and apologize to me immediately. Maybe I will be kind enough to spare your lives! Otherwise, when my boyfriend comes, he will throw you into the river to feed the fish!"

Hearing this, the faces of the people watching around changed. They subconsciously shrank their necks, and a trace of fear flashed in their eyes.

The Paul Group was one of the top ten well-known groups in Riverwood.

Patrick was notoriously arrogant and domineering. And he was very good at fighting. He was not someone that ordinary people could afford to offend.

But Brian's and Celeste's expressions did not change.

Not to mention Patrick, even Paul, the boss of the Paul Group, was nothing to them.

Seeing that the two of them didn't say anything, Eleanor thought they were scared and sneered.

"What? Are you scared? If you are scared, get on your knees immediately... Ah..."

However, before Eleanor finished her words, Celeste slapped her in the face again.

Eleanor covered her face and looked at Celeste in disbelief. "You bitch. How dare you hit me?"

"Even if your boyfriend came, I would still hit you!"

Celeste's eyes flashed coldly, and when she was about to slap her again, a cold voice sounded.

"Really? Do you have the guts to touch my woman again?"
