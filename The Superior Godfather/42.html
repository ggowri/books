Chapter 42 Unbelievable

The scene erupted into chaos once again.

Everyone stared at Celeste in disbelief.

Could this woman be having an affair with Brian, using her position to boost his career?

If that was true, she had some severe nerve!

Ryan and Sophia smiled slyly as they spoke up loudly.

"Now everyone can see what's happening here!

"I never betrayed or abandoned Brian. He cheated first by hooking up with Celeste, then used their dirty relationship to secure the Trillion Group deal! They're nothing but shameless adulterers!

"We ask Mr. Wright, the Trillion Group executives, and everyone present to help us clear our names and serve justice."

Paul and Mark watched Aria and Anastasia with interest.

"Ladies, you haven't won that bet after all!"

They stood up and addressed Kevin directly.

"Mr. Wright, please help clear Mr. Carter and his associates' names!"

The supporters of Ryan and Sophia all stood up and joined in.

"Mr. Wright, please serve justice for Mr. Carter and his team!"

Seeing this, it was clear they had to cozy up to Ryan! He was sure to become a partner at the Trillion Group!

Aria frowned, her face darkening as she remained silent.

Anastasia watched the scene with amusement, utterly unconcerned about Brian's situation.

While others might not know Brian's true identity, she knew who he was.

Brian's face turned cold instantly at the scene before him.

He couldn't believe Sophia's nerves and her man's, brazenly twisting the truth and trying to play the victim.

But this played right into his hands.

He wondered what kind of mess this pathetic couple would stir up.

Celeste fixed an icy stare at Kevin and the Trillion Group executives.

If they were eager to dig their graves, she'd happily help them.

She spoke with a sneer on her face, "You accuse me of having an affair with Brian and abusing my power to put him in position? That's ridiculous."

"Celeste, the evidence is right in front of us. How dare you deny it?" Kevin stepped forward and shouted, pointing at her.

"Step down as president of Trillion Group immediately, or we'll hold you accountable for all the damage you've done to the company!"

Kevin was thrilled inside. Once Celeste stepped down, he would become the new president of Trillion Group.

"Make me step down? You don't have that authority!"

Celeste let out a cold laugh and spoke calmly, "You all chose Ryan and the Carter Group, but our new chairman, our company's largest shareholder, overruled that decision!

"Brian and Transcendent Construction Group became our partners not because I abused my power but because our new chairman personally selected them!

"My relationship with Brian is entirely professional. I've only been following the chairman's orders!

"I never imagined you would twist the facts like this... It's despicable!"

The room fell silent.

Everyone stared at Brian in disbelief.

This man was the partnership candidate personally chosen by Trillion Group's mysterious new chairman!

Kevin was stunned!

Ethan was frozen in shock!

Mark and Paul stood there dumbfounded.

The Trillion Group executives and Ryan's supporters stared in disbelief.

"What the hell?" someone muttered.

"How does that loser know the new chairman of Trillion Group?"

Sophia's and Ryan's eyes widened as they gaped at Brian in utter disbelief.

Even Kevin didn't know the new chairman! How could a nobody like Brian have such connections?

It was entirely beyond their comprehension.

"Interesting. It seems my judgment wasn't wrong after all," Aria said with a slight smirk, her eyes fixed intently on Brian.

At this moment, Brian's charisma was genuinely dazzling.

Anastasia smiled charmingly and said, "That guy knows how to play his cards. I bet Sophia is kicking herself right now."

After their initial shock, Ryan and Sophia finally returned to reality.

Unable to accept what they were seeing, they started screaming like lunatics.

"This is impossible... absolutely impossible!

"Brian is nothing but a worthless bum! There's no way he could know the new chairman of Trillion Group!

"This must all be a lie! Celeste is trying to fool everyone!"
