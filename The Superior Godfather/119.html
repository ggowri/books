Chapter 119 Damian's Decision

As Damian walked out of Paradise Hotel, his expression was unusually grave. His mind was wrestling with uncertainty and hesitation.

He couldn't decide whether to tell Brian about this.

Then, there was the question of whether he should join Thomas' side.

After all, Thomas had aligned himself with Denzel, one of the heirs to the Vaughn family.

With Denzel's status, the Vaughn family's terrifying influence, and Thomas' strategic mind, he could hardly imagine how Brian could lead Trillion Group through this crisis.

Should he withdraw his investment?

Should he buy shares in Quadrillion Group?

At this moment, Damian's mind was a tangled mess of thoughts.

He sat on the steps by the street, lit a cigarette, and deeply contemplated.

He finally began to see a glimmer of clarity after finishing the entire pack.

He had a gut feeling that Brian wasn't as simple as Thomas' investigation had suggested.

Brian's performance at the shareholders' meeting showed a sophistication that no one from the bottom of society could possess.

He decided to go to George Reynolds, the former chairman and first shareholder of Trillion Group, to find out what was going on.

He mainly wanted to know why George had handed over his shares and chairman position to Brian for free.

Even though George had retired and settled in his hometown over three hundred kilometers from Riverwood, that wouldn't stop him from visiting late.

He jumped in his car and headed straight for George's hometown without hesitation.

After all, his following decisions would shape his future.

It would never hurt to be cautious.

At 1 AM, Damian arrived at George's home and rang the doorbell of the villa.

A young, attractive housekeeper opened the door, giving Damian a puzzled look.

"Who are you looking for?"

"Hi, I'm Damian, the third-largest shareholder of Trillion Group and a friend of Mr. Reynolds... I need to speak with him about something."

"Mr. Reynolds is already asleep. Why don't you come back tomorrow?"

Before Damian could respond, the housekeeper closed the door.

"I can't wait until tomorrow..."

Seeing this, Damian pushed past the housekeeper and rushed into the villa, shouting.

"Mr. Reynolds, it's Damian!"

As the guards moved to stop him, George's elderly but powerful voice came from upstairs. "Bring him up."

Soon, the housekeeper led Damian to the upstairs bedroom, where Mr. Reynolds was waiting.

Despite his advanced age, Mr. Reynolds appeared sharp and alert, especially with his eagle-like eyes, which seemed to pierce right through people and command respect.

"Mr. Reynolds..."

Damian bowed respectfully to the elderly man.

George gave Damian a long, meaningful look. "Well, what brings a big shot like you to my humble home?"

"Mr. Reynolds, to be honest... I need your guidance on something."

After hesitating, Damian explained Thomas' plan to withdraw investments and target the Trillion Group.

He concluded sincerely, "I need your advice on this, Mr. Reynolds."

"I always knew Thomas was ambitious, but I never expected him to make such bold moves after my retirement, especially cozying up to the Vaughn family..."

George sighed and added, "But I'm retired now. I won't get involved in your affairs and can't offer any advice. You should head back."

Damian protested, his face full of frustration. "But Mr. Reynolds, the Trillion Group is your life's work. How can you watch it get destroyed?"

"You're mistaken. The Trillion Group was never mine. I was just managing it. Now it's simply returning to its rightful owners."

As Damian tried to speak again, George waved his hand dismissively. "I've said all I need to say. It's time for you to go.

"Abby, show him out!"

"But..."

Damian looked at George with a bitter expression, who had already laid back down. He gave one final bow and turned to leave.

After Damian left, a contemptuous sneer crossed George's face.

"Those idiots following Thomas think they can break away just because they've got the Vaughn family in their corner? What a joke!

"Since this involves Mr. Edwards, I should report it to Godfather."

He pulled out his phone and dialed a mysterious number.

A deep, intimidating voice soon came through the line.

"Hey George, you're supposed to be retired and enjoying your golden years... Why are you calling me in the middle of the night?"

"Godfather, I have something important to report..."

George then proceeded to explain everything that had happened.

Upon hearing this, a slight smile appeared on the mysterious and authoritative Godfather's face.

"Well, things are finally getting interesting and challenging. Let's see if Brian can pass this test.

"If he can't handle even this much, there's no point in keeping him around.

"I'm looking for the strongest successor, not some worthless kid who wastes time and opportunities."
