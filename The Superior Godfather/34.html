Chapter 34 Temptation From Anastasia

It was five in the afternoon at Riverside Villas.

Brian spotted Anastasia waiting by the entrance as he approached the complex.

Her flowing hair framed her exquisite features, while her perfect figure looked stunning in a white custom Calvin Klein t-shirt and fitted jeans.

With her elegant and calm demeanor, she stood out like a beautiful painting against the backdrop of the residential complex.

"Sorry to keep you waiting, Rachael," Brian said as he hurried over with a smile.

Anastasia looked at him and replied with mock annoyance, "You think a simple 'sorry' cuts it? You owe me dinner, at least."

"No problem," Brian agreed readily. "What are you in the mood for? Steak or pizza?"

"I'm sick of eating out all the time. Why don't you cook instead?" Anastasia suggested, trying to sound casual.

She had deliberately chosen this time to visit, hoping to score another dinner at Brian's place.

After all, the delicious meal he'd cooked yesterday was still fresh in her memory.

"Alright, take a seat while I make dinner. After we eat, I'll help treat your condition," Brian said.

He showed Anastasia into the house.

Brian poured her a cup of coffee, turned on the TV, and went to the kitchen. There, he pulled ingredients from the fridge and got to work.

Lying on the couch, Anastasia sipped her coffee while watching his busy figure in the kitchen. A soft smile played across her beautiful lips.

For some reason, she loved this atmosphere and feeling.

It made her feel relaxed, comfortable, and at peace.

"Dinner's ready!"

After about an hour and a half, Brian called out steaming plates of delicious food from the kitchen.

He had prepared pepperoni pizza, steak, Buffalo wings, grilled pork ribs, pork chops, salad, and apple pie.

The rich aromas made Anastasia's mouth water uncontrollably.

She had never considered herself a foodie but couldn't resist Brian's cooking.

"Here, Rachael, try this and tell me if it's to your taste," Brian smiled as he placed a piece of grilled pork ribs on her plate.

The tender, spicy, and flavorful meat melted in her mouth when she took a bite. The sensation was so incredible that Anastasia crossed her legs involuntarily, feeling like she was floating on cloud nine.

The experience was so impressive that she couldn't help but let out a soft, satisfied moan.

"Mmm..."

That sound was incredibly seductive, and when it reached Brian's ears, it sent his heart racing.

Brian couldn't help but swallow hard as his eyes traced Anastasia's alluring lips and elegant figure.

"Brian, why do you keep staring at me? You should eat too," Anastasia said with a smile, noticing his gaze.

"I never understood the phrase 'feast for the eyes' until now... Just looking at your beauty is enough to fill me up," Brian replied.

Anastasia's lips curved into a smile, her heart melting like honey at his words.

She looked at him and teased, "Are you serious or just trying to make me happy?"

"I swear to God, you're the most beautiful woman I've ever seen," Brian answered without hesitation.

Anastasia thought momentarily before asking, "How do I compare to your ex? I heard you loved her so much you even took a bullet for her..."

Brian's expression darkened at her words and he fell into a long silence.

His marriage to Sophia wasn't just a stain on his life. It was his biggest mistake.

Sometimes, it made him feel like a complete failure.

"Brian, I'm sorry. I shouldn't have brought up your ex-wife," Anastasia said apologetically, seeing his reaction.

"Rachael, you don't need to apologize. It's just a past I'd rather forget. I admit I loved her once, but now she's nothing more than a passerby in my life.

"A woman like her isn't worth my dedication... I felt regretful about how much I gave to that relationship."

Brian let out a self-deprecating laugh as he picked up his glass and downed the red wine in one go.

Anastasia playfully winked at Brian. "So, am I worth your dedication?"

Brian thought momentarily before thoughtfully responding, "I don't know you well enough to answer that, but I believe you are someone worth trusting and befriending. I hope we can be friends."

Anastasia brushed her bangs aside and leaned forward, deliberately revealing the tempting expanse of her pale chest. She gazed at Brian with interest.

"Just friends? Don't you want something more?"

"Gulp!"

Brian couldn't help but swallow hard as he stared at Anastasia's alluring figure and the glimpse of her pale, full breasts. His gaze grew heated.

Was she trying to hint at something?
