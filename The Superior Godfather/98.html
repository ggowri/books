Chapter 98 The Employer

"Mr. Carter?"

Both Derek and Cole were stunned when they saw Sam.

They never expected Brian would have called in Sam with just a phone call.

Sam, equally shocked, had no idea that the people Brian wanted him to bring were Derek and Cole.

His face darkened as he asked, "What the hell are you two doing here?"

Brian, deciding to play it up, spoke coldly.

"You're asking why they're here? Half an hour ago, they tried to assassinate me, and I caught them. Now tell me. Was this your plan all along? Did you secretly send them after me, trying to seize power?"

"They tried to kill you?" Sam's face went pale, and a cold sweat broke out on his forehead.

He had only sent Derek and Cole to meet Brian, thinking it was just a formal introduction. He never imagined they'd try to kill him.

In a panic, he quickly bowed deeply to Brian.

"Mr. Edwards, I swear I had no idea! I wouldn't dare try to harm you!"

"Mr. Carter..." Derek and Cole were utterly stunned.

Before they could say anything, Sam slapped them both hard.

"You two are out of your minds! I told you to come here and meet the Master of the War Deities, and you think it's a good idea to try to kill him? Are you trying to get yourselves killed?"

Derek and Cole stared at Brian in disbelief.

"He... He's the Master of the War Deities?"

Sam shot them an irritated look. "What did you think? Who else would he be?"

The realization hit them like a wave of cold water. They immediately froze in fear, their spines tingling with the horror of what they had just tried to do.

They both lowered their heads in terror. "Master, this was a huge misunderstanding!"

"We're blind and stupid. Please forgive us!"

Brian didn't even spare them a glance, simply waving his hand dismissively.

"They're your people. Take them and handle it yourself." Then, he turned and walked toward the villa.

Inside, Anastasia, who had been watching from the window, felt even more curious about Brian.

The two assassins were clearly skilled, and it was clear they had some serious backing.

Their failure wasn't surprising, but the appearance of Sam only added to the mystery.

"Who is this man?

Why is he so respectful to Brian?

And those two assassins... are they his subordinates?"

She wondered but couldn't make sense of it all.

"Rachael, are you okay? Did I scare you?"

Brian's voice brought her back to reality.

She smiled, "With you around, what could possibly happen?"

She paused before asking, "By the way, did you find out who hired those assassins?"

Brian shrugged with a smile. "They're from the Dark Web. They were hired to kill me. Looks like my life's worth a lot. Millions of dollars, apparently."

Anastasia frowned. "Did you manage to learn who their employer is?"

Brian shook his head with a wry smile. "The job was posted as a bounty on the Dark Web. They didn't even know who their real employer was."

Anastasia's eyes narrowed, a cold edge in her gaze.

Not long ago, Denzel had asked her about her relationship with Brian, and now he was being targeted by Dark Web assassins.

It seemed like this was more than just a coincidence.

It was likely a message, both a test and a warning about her relationship with Brian.

Her thoughts grew darker as the weight of it all set in.

Brian noticed her change in demeanor and smiled reassuringly, "Don't worry about it. I've got it under control. The food's almost ready. Let's eat."

But Anastasia stood up, grabbing her bag. "I've got to take care of something. I'll come back another time..."

With that, she hurried out the door.

She needed to confront Denzel. If he was behind this, she would make sure he answered for it.
