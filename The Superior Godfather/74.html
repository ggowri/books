Chapter 74 I'm Master

"What? Ryan is about to join the War Deities and be highly valued by Master!"

This news was indeed shocking to Anne.

It was even more surprising than the news that Brian became a partner of Trillion Group.

Because the War Deities was once the most powerful organization in the world.

The Master stood at the pinnacle of power, only slightly less influential than the Godfather.

If Ryan truly joined the War Deities and gained Master's favor, he would undoubtedly soar to fame and make a stunning debut.

Anne took a deep breath and asked seriously, "Dad, you're not kidding me, right?"

"Anne, do you think we're joking?" Sophia smiled faintly and continued.

"Anne, Ryan has a cousin named Sam who has been working abroad. He was once the Unparalleled Blade, one of the Four Lords and Twelve Blades of the War Deities.

"Not long ago, he received a summons from the Master to reorganize the War Deities, so he returned to Riverwood. And just now, he recommended Ryan to the Master.

"And the Master has scheduled a meeting with Ryan for tomorrow noon. With Sam's support and Ryan's talent, he will undoubtedly rise in ranks. Then, avenging ourselves on Brian will be a piece of cake.

"Anne, just wait for that bastard Brian to beg for mercy after we crush him underfoot."

Hearing this, Anne stood there in a daze; her mood seemed particularly complicated.

She was happy for Sophia and Ryan, but worried about Brian's situation.

After all, if Ryan truly gained the recognition and favor of the Master, then what awaited Brian would be Ryan's relentless revenge.

By then, Brian would have no chance of escape.

Sophia frowned and asked, "Anne, what's with that look? Aren't you happy for Ryan and me?"

Anne quickly snapped out of it and said, "What are you talking about? Of course, I'm happy for you."

Sophia took Anne's hand and said with a smile, "Let's go out and celebrate tonight!"

By the time they finished celebrating, it was already past midnight.

Anne didn't go home with Sophia and the others. Instead, she went to Brian's villa and rang the doorbell.

After spending time with Brian, she'd developed an inexplicable feeling for him.

She didn't want to see Brian get into trouble, so she planned to tell him the news and urge him to leave as soon as possible.

Otherwise, if Ryan successfully joined the War Deities tomorrow, it would be the end of Brian.

"What are you doing here so late?" Seeing Anne standing at the door, Brian was surprised.

Anne's simple yet pleasing attire caught Brian's attention.

Her long hair was tied up, revealing her charming face. Her mature and perfect figure, accentuated by a tight white top and light-colored jeans, highlighted her curves, exuding youth, energy, and a touch of sensuality.

Her tightly stretched white shirt added a subtle yet undeniable allure.

She clenched her hands and lightly bit her lip, her gaze towards Brian filled with mixed emotions.

"I have something important to tell you."

Brian hesitated for a moment before inviting Anne inside. "Go ahead, tell me what's on your mind."

Anne hesitated before speaking, "I want you to leave Riverwood."

Brian looked at Anne in surprise. "Leave Riverwood? Why?"

"No reason, just go quickly."

As she tried to leave, Brian pulled her back, holding her mature and curvaceous body in his arms.

He lifted her chin, his gaze cold and assertive. "If you want me to leave Riverwood, you'd better give me a good reason."

Feeling his intense masculine presence, Anne's heart raced.

She hesitated, then spoke, "Ryan has a cousin who is a member of the War Deities, and he's one of the Four Lords and Twelve Blades.

"He recommended Ryan to Master, and Master has scheduled a meeting with him tomorrow. If he gains the Master's approval and joins the War Deities...

"Then dealing with you will be a piece of cake for him, so you need to leave now."

Brian was slightly stunned. Then, he couldn't help but laugh.

He thought it was something serious, but it turned out to be just this.

However, he never expected that the cousin Sam recommended would be Ryan.

Anne asked in confusion, "What are you laughing at?"

Brian smiled and said, "Don't worry, it's impossible for Ryan to get the approval of the Master and join the War Deities."

Anne asked, "Why?"

Brian looked intently at Anne and said seriously, "Because I am the Master you are talking about!"
