Chapter 55 The Accidental Kiss

"It's at least a 36E, I think," Brian thought to himself.

He hadn't expected Aria to have such an impressive figure.

The stunning view made his heart race.

He quickly shifted his gaze to avoid any possible misunderstanding from Aria.

Soon, Aria brought the coffee over to Brian.

"Mr. Edwards, here you go," she said.

"Thanks, Ms. Jenkins!" Brian replied with a smile.

Aria sat down on the sofa opposite him, crossing her beautiful legs encased in black stockings.

She looked at Brian with great interest. She was fascinated by this man who had managed to snatch the Trillion Group's project from Ryan and Ethan.

Her intuition told her that Brian was no ordinary person.

"Mr. Edwards, what brings you here?" she asked.

"Actually, I'm here to talk business with you. Ms. Jenkins, are you interested in the development of the Weston?" Brian replied.

"I don't think anyone would be uninterested in that project," Aria said with a charming smile.

She stretched casually, showing her perfect figure, which left Brian feeling a bit parched.

Aria was truly a rare beauty.

Every move she made exuded a uniquely feminine charm.

Regaining his composure, Brian asked again, "Ms. Jenkins, would you be interested in partnering with me to develop this project?"

"Partner with you?"

Aria was taken aback, her eyes wide with surprise.

Brian was actually planning to share such a lucrative opportunity with her.

Brian nodded with a smile. "That's right. I'm a bit lazy and don't want to get involved in it.

"I've investigated your company—you have strong capabilities, and I admire your character. I'd like to befriend you, which is why I'm willing to share this highly profitable project with you."

"Mr. Edwards, are you serious?" Aria asked.

Brian nodded again with a smile. "Of course."

"Mr. Edwards, you're not kidding, are you?"

"Ms. Jenkins, if you don't believe me, we can draft the contract right now!"

Seeing how serious Brian was, Aria became excited.

Her company, though strong, had been facing business shortages due to her refusal to compromise with certain capitalists.

If she could partner with Brian on the Weston project, it would not only solve her current predicament but also elevate her company to new heights.

It was incredibly significant for Aria.

However, she knew there was no such thing as a free lunch.

"Mr. Edwards, is there nothing else you want in return?" she asked.

"Else?" Brian frowned in confusion. "What else?"

Aria's face flushed slightly, her heart filled with worry.

She said in a low voice, "Like asking me to sleep with you or be your mistress..."

In her past business dealings with powerful figures, Aria had encountered such demands, and even worse.

Her refusal to comply had led to her company's less-than-ideal performance.

She feared Brian might be the same.

Brian burst into laughter, spilling some coffee from his mouth.

"Ms. Jenkins, what kind of person do you take me for? Do you really think I'm that despicable?"

Aria was taken aback, a mix of surprise and delight welling up inside her.

She quickly explained, "Mr. Edwards, don't get me wrong. I didn't mean it like that. I've just had too many experiences like this... But Mr. Edwards, do you really have no any other demands?"

Seeing Aria's lingering doubt, Brian let out a wry smile.

He thought, "Is it so hard these days to be an upright person?"

With a straight face, he said, "My only demand is that you help me make the Weston project a success."

Aria looked at him earnestly. "Really?"

Brian nodded solemnly. "Absolutely!"

With this affirmation, Aria could no longer contain her excitement and joy. She threw herself into Brian's arms, hugging him tightly.

"Mr. Edwards, thank you so much! Mr. Edwards, I love you!"

Brian was knocked backward onto the sofa by Aria's sudden embrace.

Aria lost her balance as well and landed heavily on top of him.

Their bodies were pressed tightly together at that moment.

"Aria..." Brian began, but Aria's lips met his, causing his eyes to widen in surprise.

Aria was also stunned. The warmth and softness of the kiss left her mind completely blank.

She had only intended to give Brian a hug to express her gratitude. She hadn't expected this awkward turn of events.

They stared into each other's eyes, time seemingly frozen at that moment.
