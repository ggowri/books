Chapter 12 A Moment of Stunned Silence

The members of Shadow Fang were all left dumbfounded.

This was the first time they had ever seen Zoe lower herself like this.

Normally, even when dealing with government officials or the mayor of Riverwood, Zoe had always remained composed, showing only the bare minimum of politeness.

But now, her actions had completely caught them off guard.

Even the usually composed Anastasia was stunned and taken aback.

Though she had learned from Brian earlier that he knew Zoe, she hadn't expected Zoe to treat Brian with such deep respect.

And then there was the fact that Zoe had called him "Master".

Anastasia didn't fully understand what that title meant, but it was clear it implied a unique and highly respected position.

Zoe's deference to Brian made it obvious that this wasn't just a casual acquaintance.

At that moment, Anastasia suddenly realized how little she truly knew about her fiancé.

A sense of curiosity about this man began to well up within her.

"Brian, how many secrets are you hiding?" she wondered.

Brian looked at Zoe, feeling a bit exasperated.

He helplessly reached out, pulling Zoe back to her feet, and with a mock scold, he said, "You... didn't I tell you before? I like to keep a low profile. I don't like all these formalities. You're really giving me a headache."

Hearing Brian's familiar words, Zoe couldn't help but let out a small, amused laugh.

Then, in front of the astonished onlookers, she opened her arms wide and pulled Brian into a tight embrace, burying his face into her ample chest.

"Master, I've missed you so much!"

Brian was immediately surrounded by softness. His face was smushed between her breasts, and it became incredibly hard to breathe.

He thought, "This woman... is as crazy as ever."

"Let go. I can't breathe..."

It wasn't until Brian's voice broke through that Zoe realized her mistake. She quickly released him, stepping back with a guilty look.

Brian gasped for air, greedily inhaling the fresh oxygen, then shot Zoe a look of displeasure.

"You've got to be kidding me... It's been years, and you're still the same. Can't you change?"

"Master, I didn't mean to. Are you okay? Are you breathing alright?"

Zoe's face flushed as she reached out, gently rubbing his chest, trying to help him calm down.

Brian glared at her, still catching his breath, and grumbled, "I nearly suffocated! Do you think I'm okay?"

"Master, I was just so happy to see you! I'm so sorry!"

Zoe blushed deeply, her eyes downcast as she nervously fiddled with her fingers like a little girl who had just been scolded.

Her shy, sheepish demeanor was so out of character that it left everyone in Shadow Fang completely stunned.

After all, Zoe had always been cold and domineering in front of them, ready to kill anyone at the slightest provocation.

Anastasia raised her brows, noticing the subtle tension between Brian and Zoe. She felt a sudden, unfamiliar unease welling up inside her.

After all, she was supposed to be Brian's fiancée.

Just as she was about to get angry, she realized she had kept her own identity hidden from Brian, and she quickly forced herself to calm down.

After a brief exchange with Brian, Zoe turned her cold gaze toward Raven.

"What are you standing there for? Hurry up and apologize to Master!"

Raven snapped out of his daze and rushed toward Brian.

He frantically began apologizing while repeatedly slapping himself across the face.

"Mr. Edwards, this was all Ryan's doing. He paid me two million to kill you...

"If I had known you knew Ms. Guzman, I never would've dared disrespect you.

"Mr. Edwards, I really know I was wrong. Please, forgive me..."
