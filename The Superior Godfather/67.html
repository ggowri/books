Chapter 67 Kevin's Shock

Meanwhile, at Lee Villa, Thomas and Eric sat silently on the sofa after leaving the Paradise Hotel.

At this moment, the uncle and nephew were sitting on the sofa with grim expressions, not saying a word.

Thinking of today's experience, Eric's face turned grim, and his fists clenched in frustration.

Unable to contain his anger, he slammed a cup on the floor.

The cup shattered, and Eric's angry roar rang out in the living room.

"Brian, that jerk, transferred me to the publicity department. What's the difference between this and asking me to go home?" Eric shouted.

Thomas raised an eyebrow and said coldly, "Those who achieve great things don't care about trivial matters. It's just a transfer to the publicity department. Can't you calm down?"

"Calm down? You've seen how arrogant Brian was. How can I stay calm? Today, we have completely lost face!"

Eric's fists tightened, his eyes filled with fury and resentment.

He could imagine how others would look at him with mockery and ridicule when he arrived at the company tomorrow.

Eric added, "Thomas, have we ever faced such insults? Are you really going to let him take charge?"

Thomas replied calmly, a glint of wisdom in his eyes, "He is no fool. He only showed a bit of skill and managed to impress Damian. Even I feel uneasy around him.

"Now that he's gained momentum, we must wait for the right moment to strike.

"The Trillion Group is mine, and no one will take it from me."

As he spoke, a cold light flickered in Thomas' eyes, and he crushed the cup in his hand.

As if he had thought of something, he continued, "Also, please help me go to the prison and explain the situation to Kevin. Ask him to wait patiently for a while. I will think of other ways to get him out."

"Don't worry, I'll see Kevin right away," Eric replied, leaving with a serious expression.

Soon after, he arrived at the prison with his lawyer to meet Kevin.

In just a few days, Kevin had changed drastically.

He wore a prison uniform, looking pale and bruised, as if he had been beaten and drained of energy.

When he saw Eric, he felt like he had seen a savior. He quickly grabbed his hand and spoke excitedly, "Eric, you're finally here! Can I get out of this hellhole?"

Eric said, "Kevin, it's complicated. I'm afraid you'll have to stay here a bit longer."

Eric's words hit Kevin like a shock, and fear flashed in his eyes.

He didn't want to stay here any longer and be raped again by those damn prisoners in the middle of the night.

The next moment, his expression suddenly became extremely excited, and he yelled at Eric, "Why? Didn't you say you would come and get me out?"

"Calm down, don't get excited. It's a long story," Eric said, his face tense as he explained the incident.

"To put it simply, the investment conference was a trap set up by that bastard Brian. In fact, he is the new chairman of our company."

Kevin was shocked, and he looked as if he had seen a ghost, letting out an inappropriate sound. "What did you say? He is the new chairman of our company?"

"Yes, and he's even had me transferred to the publicity department," Eric confirmed. "But don't worry, Thomas is determined to take down Brian to help you. Just hold on a little longer."

"But..." Kevin started, but Eric interrupted, placing a reassuring hand on his shoulder.

"Kevin, you have to endure for now. Trust me and Thomas. We'll find a way to get you out."

With that, Eric turned and left with his lawyer.
