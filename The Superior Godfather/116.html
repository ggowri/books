Chapter 116 Can You Lend Me Some Money?

"Breaking up?"

Eleanor's face turned ghostly pale as if struck by lightning.

Patrick nodded firmly. "That's right. We're done!"

"Patrick, I..."

Before Eleanor could explain, Patrick turned and walked away with Olivia and Paul.

"Patrick, I know I was wrong. I'll never do it again!

"Patrick, I swear I'll change. I'll be a good wife and mother..."

Eleanor's face crumpled as she rushed after him, desperately grabbing his hand and begging.

"I told you, we're done!"

Patrick's face turned cold as he yanked his arm away, sending Eleanor tumbling.

Patrick didn't even glance at her as he walked away.

"Patrick... no... no..."

Eleanor watched their retreating figures and burst into uncontrollable sobs.

She had worked so hard to win Patrick's heart and gain Paul and Olivia's approval to marry into the wealthy Smith family.

They were supposed to get engaged next month.

Her dream had been within reach. Still, she never imagined her mockery of Brian would lead to such a devastating outcome.

At that moment, her dreams of marrying into high society shattered utterly, and years of careful planning went down the drain.

"It is all Brian's fault!

"That good-for-nothing Brian ruined everything. None of this would have happened if it weren't for him."

Eleanor's face twisted with hatred as she blamed everything on Brian.

She was utterly blind to the fact that she had brought this all upon herself.

After a moment, she pulled out her phone and called her best friend, Sophia.

She needed to find out what the deal was with that damn Brian.

The call connected quickly, and Sophia's voice came through.

"Hey Eleanor, what made you call suddenly?"

Eleanor's face was bitter. She hesitated before saying, "Sophia, I... Patrick broke up with me."

The news shocked Sophia, who was hiding out in a slum somewhere in Poskon.

"What happened? Weren't you two getting engaged next month?"

She had just arrived in Poskon when a friend scammed her, and then she got robbed. Now, she was completely broke and living in misery.

"It's all because of that bastard Brian..."

Eleanor explained everything that had happened, ending with, "Sophia, you have to help me get justice."

Hearing this, Sophia's face turned bitter. She could barely care for herself, let alone go up against Brian.

Unless she managed to meet with Luna Sinclair, the leader of Cerberus Crew.

"Don't worry," she said firmly. "When I return from Poskon, I'll ensure that bastard Brian gets what he deserves."

"Poskon?" Eleanor was startled. "What are you doing there?"

"The Carter family is finished. Brian, that piece of trash, set Ryan up and got him killed. I'm in Poskon to find Ryan's mother and avenge him."

Eleanor's face turned pale with shock. "What did you say? The Carter family is finished, and Brian killed Ryan?"

"Yes."

Sophia nodded and gave her a quick rundown of what had happened.

"Listen, Ryan's mother runs the Cerberus Crew in Poskon. I'll be back on top if I can meet her and get her approval. Then I'll crush that bastard Brian under my heel..."

Eleanor was stunned to learn that Ryan's mother was the head of the Poskon Cerberus Crew!

If Sophia could win her support, her status would skyrocket.

Without hesitation, she said, "I believe in you, Sophia. If you need anything, just ask."

Sophia's eyes lit up, and she spoke after hesitating, "Eleanor, I got robbed recently, and I'm in desperate need of money. Could you lend me some if it's not too much trouble?"

"How much do you need?"

After a brief pause, Sophia replied, "Ten million dollars!"

"I don't have that much on hand right now," Eleanor said without missing a beat. "But hang on, I'll figure out how to get it!

"Don't worry, I'll wire it to you by three o'clock this afternoon."

"Great, I'll be waiting!"

With Eleanor's guarantee, Sophia let out a long breath of relief.

With this money, she could triumphantly overcome her immediate crisis, find Luna, and return home.

When that time came, she would make Brian pay double for all the misfortune she had endured.
