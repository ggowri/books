They had already lost their belongings and memories; now he was turning their precious villa into ashes.

And this was only the beginning of his revenge.

At the upcoming Trillion Group investment conference, he would make Ryan and Sophia feel the sting of true despair and regret.

Anastasia's eyes sparkled with intrigue as she replied, "I want to explore my options before deciding. You can head back now."

Once Kale departed, Anastasia floored it, steering her car straight toward Brian, who stood waiting by the roadside.

Brian caught sight of the approaching vehicle, and as it raced toward him, alert washed over him.

Is this an assassin sent to finish me off?

In a split-second decision, he leaped onto the car's hood, shattering the reinforced bulletproof glass with a swift kick.

He quickly climbed into the vehicle, a knife pressed against Anastasia's throat.

"Who sent you?" Brian demanded, his voice low and menacing.

Anastasia paled, fear flashing in her eyes.

I just wanted to stage a car accident to bump into Brian! Didn't think he'd mistake me for an assassin.

But hey, that was kind of cool—he kicked through that bulletproof glass like it was made of paper.

But... didn't he lose his energy?

She was a whirlwind of confusion.

Seeing the icy determination in Brian's eyes, Anastasia hastily raised her hands in surrender.

"Sir, you've got it all wrong. My driver had an unexpected emergency, so I had to drive myself. Unfortunately, I'm not very skilled behind the wheel, and the car got out of control and crashed into you. I sincerely apologize for the trouble and am more than willing to make it right. Please forgive me!"

As Brian listened to Anastasia's explanation, he couldn't help but notice her angelic appearance and elegant attire. He furrowed his brow, lost in thought.

She didn't strike him as an assassin at all.

Instead, she resembled a high-powered CEO, beautiful and poised, with a cold yet sexy allure that was undeniably captivating.

Real assassins carried a certain coldness, a menacing air about them. Even the best at disguising it couldn't hide the cues he had learned to spot easily.

Could it be that I've jumped to conclusions?

Realizing this, he quickly shifted his tone. "I'm sorry, beautiful lady. I think I might have misunderstood everything...

"I accidentally damaged your car in my anxiety just now. Let me fix it for you and offer proper compensation. Please, forgive me."

Anastasia was taken aback by his response. A flicker of surprise flashed in her eyes.

In situations like this, most people would likely take advantage of her, making excessive demands on her as a woman.

Yet here was Brian, owning up to it like a true gentleman.

This unexpected humility only piqued her interest in him further.

She decided to keep her true identity under wraps and get to know Brian better.

With a warm smile, she replied, "How could this possibly be your fault? It's entirely because of my poor driving skills that this misunderstanding happened. I should be the one compensating you..."

As Anastasia spoke, she pulled out a checkbook and pen, offering them to him. "Just write down whatever amount you think is fair!"

She wanted to see if Brian was easily swayed by money.

But faced with such an easy out, Brian simply smiled and shook his head.

"Since it's just a misunderstanding, there's really no need for compensation. Besides, I'm the one who caused the damage to your car..."

Anastasia's smile broadened, and she extended her hand in greeting. "I'm Rachael Vaughn. It's a pleasure to meet you."

"Ms. Vaughn, you're even more beautiful than your name suggests. I'm Brian Edwards!" he replied with a slight smile, taking her hand warmly.

A soft warmth surged through him at their contact, making his heart race, but Brian pulled away quickly, not wanting to linger.

This gesture of politeness sparked a flicker of surprise and admiration in Anastasia's eyes.

"Mr. Edwards, to make up for the inconvenience, how about I buy you a drink?" she offered, her tone inviting.

"Sure!" Brian replied, eager to accept the invitation from this stunning beauty.

They soon arrived at a nearby bar, settling in at a cozy corner table.

Anastasia ordered a cocktail while Brian opted for a glass of whiskey.

With her angelic features and alluring figure, Anastasia quickly became the center of attention in the bar.

Some patrons gawked at her, eyes filled with lust.

Others whistled, trying to catch her attention.

At that moment, a middle-aged bald man adorned with a thick gold chain sauntered over, flanked by two thuggish companions.

He said with a sleazy smirk, "Yo, babe! You're a total knockout. You just scream 'up for some wild times'!

"I'm full of energy and can really blow your mind. What say you we go get a drink, huh?"

The bald guy, Tyrone Duffy, was eyeing Anastasia like a hungry wolf, his eyes ablaze with lust as he let his lecherous gaze wander all over her.

His sister's husband was Raven Knox, a big-shot in the local and most powerful gang, the Shadow Fang. Riding on Raven's coattails, he'd been a real pain in the neck, causing trouble and harassing both men and women.

God knew how many women had fallen victim to Tyrone's cruelty and become his playthings.

Now, he'd set his sights on Anastasia's beauty and was itching to have some "fun" with her.

Anastasia frowned slightly, a flash of disgust crossing her eyes. But instead of saying a word, she decided to sit back and see how Brian would handle the situation.

Brian was about to speak up when Tyrone slapped his hand on Brian's shoulder, scowling menacingly.

"Your girl's silence means she's on board. I'm sure you wouldn't be a spoilsport, would you?"

As he spoke, Tyrone whipped out a dagger and pressed it against Brian's side.
