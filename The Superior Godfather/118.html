Chapter 118 Crisis of the Trillion Group

"Pulling out your investment?"

The announcement sent shockwaves through the room.

Everyone stared at Thomas in disbelief.

Was he planning to withdraw from Trillion Group?

Damian's expression changed at his words.

Was Thomas serious about this?

If Thomas pulled his investment, Trillion Group's stock would nosedive quickly.

Zac couldn't help but ask, "Mr. Lee, are you serious? You're withdrawing from Trillion Group?"

Thomas nodded firmly. "That's right."

As soon as he finished speaking, someone eagerly asked, "What are your plans after withdrawing, Mr. Lee?"

They needed to prepare for what would come if Thomas pulled out.

After a moment, Thomas replied, "I plan to start my own company. I've already obtained the business license. It's called Quadrillion Group, and its initial capital is fifteen billion dollars."

With that, Thomas held up the new company's business license for everyone to see.

"This..."

Everyone stared at the business license in shock, their jaws dropping.

None of them expected Thomas to move so quickly.

Damian narrowed his eyes and asked in a serious tone, "A registered capital of fifteen billion dollars? Thomas, as far as I know, all your money is tied up in the stock market. Where did you get such a huge sum?"

Everyone turned to Thomas with curious expressions.

Thomas smiled slightly and explained, "I got five billion dollars through bank loans. The other ten billion dollars came from an investor named Denzel."

"Denzel?"

"You mean Denzel Vaughn, one of the five heirs of the Vaughn family from Azuriath State?"

Their eyes widened as they stared at Thomas in disbelief.

No one knew that Thomas was connected to someone as influential as Denzel.

Everyone knew that the Vaughn family was among the elite of Azuriath State, with countless businesses nationwide.

Denzel was one of the five heirs to the Vaughn family and the CEO of Glory Group. He was a man of extraordinary status and powerful connections.

Damian's face showed pure amazement.

He couldn't believe Thomas had convinced Denzel to invest and partner with him.

With Denzel's backing, it seemed only a matter of time before this new corporation would overtake the Trillion Group.

Watching everyone's shocked expressions, Thomas felt smug as his smile grew wider.

"That's right, it's Mr. Denzel Vaughn! Mr. Vaughn prefers to keep a low profile, so I hope everyone can keep this under wraps."

After Thomas' confirmation, the crowd couldn't contain their excitement and started speaking up one after another.

"Mr. Lee, is the Quadrillion Group still looking for shareholders and investors?"

"Mr. Lee, you promised to help us make money together. Can we buy some shares?"

"Yeah, Mr. Lee, give us a chance to get in on this!"

Thomas smiled at their reactions and said, "Everyone, calm down. Mr. Vaughn and I believe in sharing the wealth, so we plan to expand our shares for financing. If you're interested in the Quadrillion Group, you can register for investment through Eric.

"Also, the Quadrillion Group is currently recruiting talent. We welcome all of you to bring your teams and join us for better opportunities."

As soon as he finished speaking, the scene erupted into chaos.

"Count me in!"

"Me too!"

"Don't forget about me!"

"Mr. Lee, we're following your lead from now on!"

People rushed forward eagerly to register, turning the whole place into a complete frenzy.

Witnessing this scene, Damian was stunned, unable to hold back a nervous gulp.

He never imagined Thomas would play such a ruthless game.

Not only was he withdrawing investments from the Trillion Group, but he also planned to gut it from the inside out, eradicating it.

He could already picture the mighty Trillion Group crumbling overnight.

At this moment, he felt his loyalty begin to waver.

Should he stick with Brian or join Thomas in the rebellion?

Thomas noticed his hesitation and walked over, patting Damian's shoulder with a smirk.

He asked, "So Damian, interested in joining my team?"

Damian took a deep breath and looked up at Thomas to steady his nerves.

"Look, Thomas, I'm always careful about taking risks. Can you give me one night to think it over?"

"Time is of the essence here, but for old times' sake, I'll give you until nine tomorrow morning to decide."

Thomas paused before adding, "And one more thing... everything discussed at tonight's meeting stays between us, understood?"

"Don't worry, my lips are sealed."

Damian nodded and took his leave.

As they watched him walk away, Eric couldn't help but ask.

"Thomas, why'd you let him go? Aren't you worried he'll run straight to Brian and Celeste?"

Thomas let out a sneer, and his eyes flashed with contempt.

"The fall of Trillion Group is already set in stone. What difference does it make if they know?"
