Osborne had faced the Aberrants' Class S clones before, having personally taken down a dozen or so. He knew all too well the kind of power they wielded.

Without the Realm, these clones were undoubtedly the weakest among Class S.

But even the weakest Class S was still formidable.

A typical Class AAA wouldn't stand a chance against them.

What if the latest clones were even stronger?

A thousand Class S clones, even if they were all the weakest of the bunch, were still a terrifying prospect.

With Osborne's current abilities, if he were surrounded by just fifty of those clones, he'd be dead in the water.

Even thirty would put his life in serious jeopardy.

Osborne frowned, deep in thought about his next move.

"The only ace up my sleeve is the Godfather's scepter. Using that scepter two miles around Augustine Estate would supercharge my strength!

With that, I wouldn't even flinch in front of top-tier Class S opponents!

If I could boost my own rank to Class S while wielding the scepter, I might even take down the strongest Class S in one hit! But even then, I can’t defeat Gerardo and those thousand Class S clones.

Plus, the Eityreia Organization and the League of Divine Descent will surely back the Aberrants. The enemies I'll face will be even stronger and more terrifying!

So, not only do I need to break through to Class S as quickly as possible, but I also need to gather more allies and resources. The only option I have left is the Blood Demon Organization!

Blood Cross has absorbed enough blood energy recently to elevate the members of Blood Demon once again!"

This time, it should be enough for all of them to reach Class AAA!

Close to five thousand Class AAA members might still fall short against the Aberrants' nearly a thousand of Class S clones, but at least we wouldn't be completely defenseless!

Plus, in battle, I can take down enemies and absorb their blood energy to further boost the Blood Demon members. Our strength will only grow with each fight; there's a chance we could turn the tide!

And I can also assemble the special operations team!

We can't just sit and wait at the Augustine Estate; we need to have the ability to strike back! Why should the Aberrants be the only ones on the offensive?

I'll swing by the Materials Research Institute and the Weapons Research Institute later. If I can solve the material issues for the super energy guns and mass-produce them, the Blood Demon's power will increase significantly."

With that thought in mind, Osborne stepped outside.

He instructed Zoe to keep a close eye on Anastasia and to contact him immediately if anything happened. Then he made his way out of Mount Celestial Reach and headed to the Blood Demon's base.

The Blood Demon base was located at Mount Three of the Augustine Estate.

Most members were currently out on patrol, so Osborne called them all back, ready to channel blood energy for their enhancement.

In no time, four thousand eight hundred Blood Demon members gathered, eagerly absorbing the blood energy he provided.

The vibrations of their breakthroughs filled the air, a relentless wave of power that surged through the group. Just as Osborne had hoped, all four thousand eight hundred members broke through to Class AAA.

As Osborne looked at the nearly five thousand Class AAA members of the Blood Demon organization, he was excited.

But the thought of the Aberrants quickly sobered him up.

With over a thousand Class S clones in the Aberrants' ranks, the Blood Demon members still had a long way to go.

None of them had yet broken through to Class S.

This wasn't their fault; some members like First and Second could already make that leap.

The only thing holding them back was Osborne's own rank—still stuck at Class AAA.

Their power could never exceed his. They can’t make breakthroughs if Osborne doesn’t.

"It's clear now; I have to break through to Class S as soon as possible! Otherwise, my own limitations will become a roadblock for the Blood Demon members," Osborne thought, scanning members around him.

These warriors were his backbone in the fight against the Aberrants, but right now, they weren't strong enough.

"First!" Osborne called out.

"Mr. Augustine!" First immediately stepped forward, ready for orders.

Osborne said, "I want to form a special operations team with just one hundred members for now. I need you to arrange a competition for everyone to identify the top one hundred with the best strength. You'll lead this special team!

"This unit will be called the Blood Demon Vanguard!

"Members of the Blood Demon Vanguard will be the first to gain access to divine artifacts. They'll get priority for super energy guns and advanced defensive armor. They will receive priority blood energy supplies and opportunities to break through!

"Of course, this team will also take on the most dangerous missions!

"Bottom line, I'm giving you one day to select the strongest and bravest members. Assemble the Blood Demon Vanguard!"
