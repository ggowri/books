Chapter 4

That crystal necklace—I've always kept it locked away in my safe.

Yet Mark had the audacity to use it as a tool to win favor and show affection, hanging it around his mistress's neck.

How dare he!

I could hardly believe my eyes, feeling so disgusted I wanted to vomit.

As the world spun around me, all I wanted was to rush up there and slap him hard across the face.

At that moment, the host smiled and said, "Now, let's have Mr. Smith share his feelings about returning to his alma mater after ten years to present scholarships to the underclassmen."

Mark couldn't hide his smile as he took the microphone, his gaze sweeping across the audience below before finally settling on Emily.

He said, "Returning to my alma mater after ten years, I'm delighted to see so many outstanding young people—each one represents hope for the future.

"Especially Emily, who has been interning at my company.

"Her proposals show exceptional talent, full of the boldness and innovation that young people possess.

"So let me say this from the heart—she's the most outstanding student I've ever encountered, and I'm personally adding another hundred thousand dollars as a reward for her to stay with the company!"

Good-natured laughter and applause erupted from the audience, teasing Mark for using this opportunity to recruit talent for his company.

I sneered inwardly, digging my nails deep into my palms.

Then the host suddenly said, "As I understand it, Mr. Smith's wife, Ms. Gilbert, is also here today.

"As Mr. Smith's partner and business associate, let's invite her up to say a few words as well!"

The cameras and countless eyes turned toward me as applause filled the air once more.

Mark had never mentioned this part beforehand, and now he simply stood on stage, smiling and waving me up.

I slowly walked onto the stage, wearing an appropriate smile as I switched places with him.

Standing up there, I realized I could see everyone's movements clearly below.

Mark and Emily stood together, their hands tightly clasped beneath their loose graduation gowns.

He could still look at me calmly, his smile expectant.

I knew what he was expecting.

That thirty-second deepfake video was supposed to play on the big screen behind me according to his plan.

However, I simply spoke calmly into the microphone: "Standing here today, besides congratulating everyone, I have an important announcement to make.

"Due to Emily's academic misconduct, I hereby declare that we are withdrawing her scholarship in the name of our company."

The audience erupted in shock as Mark stared at me in disbelief.

He grabbed the host's microphone, trying to say something.

The next second, however, he saw the images on the big screen. The microphone fell from his hands, crashing to the ground with a piercing screech.
