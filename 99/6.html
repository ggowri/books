Chapter 6

The crowd below erupted into chaos, with many people raising their phones to record the screen.

Mark's face went pale as he shouted angrily, "No filming! Turn it off, shut down the screen!"

After the photos finished playing through once, the screen slowly went dark.

All graduation ceremony proceedings had ended, and the faculty and students in attendance were evacuated early.

Mark shielded Emily, protecting her from any outside scrutiny.

Before leaving, he turned back to glare at me viciously, his eyes filled with venomous hatred.

He threatened, "Just you wait. I'll make you pay for what you've done today!"

I chuckled softly, "You never regretted your affair, so why would I regret exposing the truth?"

After the venue cleared out, I returned home alone.

The moment I pushed open the door, Natalia's call came through.

She said angrily, "Go check the news right now! That shameless Mark is twisting the facts and slandering you!"

The situation had already blown up online, with public opinion spiraling out of control.

Originally, Natalia and I had prepared a press release in advance, ready to publish it immediately to completely cut off any possibility of Mark fighting back.

But if he wanted to scheme against me, he wasn't unprepared either.

The graduation ceremony had been livestreamed online simultaneously. Though it was quickly cut off, plenty of videos and photos still made it out.

Initially, public opinion was still under our control, with the public condemning Mark's extramarital affair.

But Mark had clearly mobilized powerful PR resources. Within just one hour, massive numbers of internet trolls flooded in, manipulating the direction of comments.

Several pages of fabricated chat records twisted Emily's academic fraud into "her proactively seeking guidance from a senior on her thesis."

Meanwhile, I was portrayed as a jealous senior who exploited a junior's trust and deliberately set her up.

Internet users used two blurry comparison photos of crystal necklaces, insisting that Emily was wearing a knockoff worth only thirty dollars.

Surveillance footage of me meeting Emily at the company, combined with several anonymous employees' "insider revelations," instantly painted me as a malicious boss who abused her power and suppressed newcomers.

Emily even started a livestream, holding up a depression diagnosis, crying into the camera.

She said, "I didn't want to say these things, but Elena has gone too far.

"She uses her position as Mr. Smith's wife to run wild in the company. Who doesn't know that Mr. Smith does whatever she says?

"She won't allow any woman near Mr. Smith and has had it out for me for a long time.

"I wanted to endure it in silence, but she actually fabricated so much evidence to slander me and destroy the research results I worked so hard for!"

She became the innocent, weak victim who suffered bullying, while Mark was the caring supervisor troubled by his wife.

And I became the jealous, incompetent villain who climbed to power through her husband.

What pushed the entire incident to its climax was that video of me with the strange man.

Mark had the source file. Though he failed to play it successfully at the graduation ceremony, it still spread widely online.

Suddenly, I had another label attached to me: cheating and promiscuous.

Countless internet users flooded my social media accounts, attacking me with the most vicious language.

[I almost got fooled, thank goodness Mr. Smith clarified in time. Marrying you was truly his misfortune!]

[Her photo editing skills are really impressive. Why doesn't she edit herself a funeral photo as a keepsake?]

[Her character is corrupt to this extent. I suggest thoroughly investigating all of Elena's academic achievements - they're definitely all fake!]

Public opinion spiraled completely out of control, and the company began experiencing internal turmoil.

Mark posted a termination notice and a hiring notice on his social media account.

The former was for me, the latter for Emily.

[In fact, my wife has been working passively for three months, occupying a position in the technical department without making any contributions. After unanimous decision by the board of directors, we've decided to terminate Elena's position while welcoming Emily to officially join our team and inject fresh vitality into the company!]

The comments section was full of praise, commending Mark for his clear judgment and comforting Emily not to mind the slander and defamation, that justice would always stand behind them.

Natalia cursed for ten straight minutes, wanting to produce evidence to expose them.

I looked down, saving the evidence: "No rush. Let public opinion ferment a bit longer."
