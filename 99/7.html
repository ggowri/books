Chapter 7

When the controversy was at its peak, I had Natalia's law firm send out legal notices on my behalf.

I filed charges against Mark and Emily for theft and defamation.

I also sued several netizens with the highest repost counts for defamation.

At the same time, I formally submitted a divorce petition to the court.

Public opinion remained one-sided. Most people watched coldly, mocking and sneering, saying I was just making a desperate last stand.

Before long, Mark called me.

His voice was ice-cold, his tone condescending: "What tricks are you trying to pull now? You think filing for divorce will get you off the hook? I'll make sure you leave with nothing!

"If you drop the charges, I can give you an extra fifty thousand dollars."

I said nothing and hung up directly, blocking his number.

The next day, I went to Mark's company.

Emily had just walked out of Mark's office, deliberately disheveling her collar to reveal red marks on her collarbone.

She sneered: "Look who's here? Don't tell me you've come to beg me for mercy?"

I tossed my employee badge onto the desk: "I'm here to take back what's mine."

Just then, Mark rushed over with a group of executives. He nervously positioned himself in front of Emily, confronting me.

He said: "You've already been fired, and you still dare come here? Don't even think about hurting Emily in front of me!"

I looked at him coldly: "You want me to leave the company? Fine. But don't think you can keep what belongs to me.

"The latest project proposals and several technical patents all belong to me personally. You have no right to use them anymore."

His face changed dramatically, and he almost roared: "Elena, you should be begging me now! What gives you the right to act so high and mighty!"

I timed it perfectly and raised my phone: "Really? But now, it seems like you're the one who should be begging."

As soon as I finished speaking, his phone started buzzing frantically, and the phones of his secretaries and subordinates began vibrating too.

Seeing the messages, Mark instantly froze in place, his face turning deathly pale as he shouted at me: "Elena, what have you done?"

I shrugged: "Isn't it obvious? I've released all the evidence and completely destroyed you. You should thank me for putting mosaics over your naked photos."

His face turned bright red, pointing at me, too angry to speak.

After a night of preparation, I had used an even bigger sensation to break through public opinion, forcefully presenting the evidence.

I clearly laid out Emily's academic misconduct, Mark's affair and theft of my jewelry, along with their thousand intimate photos.

At the same time, I produced evidence of Mark's embezzlement and announced loudly: "Everyone, Mark has been embezzling company funds to satisfy his personal desires, causing huge losses to the company. Do you still dare to stay at such a company?"

The surroundings erupted in chaos, with no one in the mood to work, anxiety filling the air.

I looked around and declared loudly: "Not just the key personnel from the tech department—anyone from other departments who wants to come with me is welcome. I'll handle the resignation paperwork."

Mark finally completely lost control: "Elena, what gives you the right! Do you really think you can take everyone with just one sentence?"

I couldn't be bothered with him and turned to walk out.

An executive from the board stopped me, looking troubled: "Elena, we've worked together for so many years. Over a small conflict, there's no need to make this irreparable. Please come back."

I replied indifferently: "No need. When you all decided to fire me yesterday, you didn't consider our years of working relationship."
