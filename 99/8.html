Chapter 8

As I left the company, Mark was still making threats: "You're going to regret this!"

But as it turned out, he was the one who truly regretted it.

When I made the complete, solid chain of evidence public, public opinion reversed overnight.

The netizens who had been deceived began apologizing to me one after another, then turned around and started cursing Mark and Emily.

【So it was all an act? They really fooled me.】

【So disgusting! These two scumbags deserve each other!】

【Stop hurting other people and leave the wife alone. Cheating husband, stolen research, and then getting blamed for it.】

【Elena is a true independent woman. Look at her credentials—how could she possibly be jealous of a thief?】

Mark and Emily instantly became public enemies. They even deleted their social media accounts, but thoughtful netizens had already made backups.

Meanwhile, Mark's company was suddenly investigated, and authorities discovered tax evasion amounting to tens of millions of dollars.

Once the news broke, panic spread throughout the company, and capable employees began submitting their resignations.

Most of the key personnel in the tech department had already declared their intention to continue working with me.

A major project the company was working on also fell through as a result.

Mark might have been able to salvage the situation, but unfortunately, in his rush to make quick money, he had signed a gambling agreement.

This time, he lost the entire company.

When debt collectors stormed into his office and cleaned it out, Mark broke down and called me.

His voice trembling with regret: "Honey, I was wrong. I shouldn't have betrayed your trust. Please come back, okay? Give me a chance to make it up to you. We can get through this tough time like we used to, and I promise I'll listen to you about everything from now on."

"Too late." After saying that, I hung up without hesitation.

Just like that, Mark's company collapsed at a visibly rapid pace each day, teetering on the edge of bankruptcy.

And Emily delivered the final blow.

She secretly transferred the company's remaining funds and tried to buy a plane ticket to flee the country overnight.

However, she was recognized at the airport and surrounded by crowds.

In the end, Emily naturally didn't make it onto the plane and slunk back home.

After this incident, netizens seemed to find entertainment in tracking Emily down. Whenever they spotted her anywhere in public, they'd post about it, saying they couldn't let a criminal escape legal consequences.

When I saw Mark again on the court date, he looked like a completely different person.

He appeared utterly haggard, with bloodshot eyes and a terrible complexion.

Upon seeing me, he immediately put on a pleading expression.

His voice choked with emotion: "If this continues, I'll really go bankrupt. Aren't you over your anger yet?"

I looked at him with surprise: "What right do you have to make me angry now? After today's hearing, we'll never see each other again."

He instantly turned vicious, shouting at me with malice: "I'll never agree to the divorce! Don't expect to have it easy!"

But whether we divorced or not wasn't his decision to make.

The first piece of evidence I submitted was the record of him secretly transferring marital assets.
