The battlefield fell into stunned silence as Osborne effortlessly annihilated over thirty Superior Deities from Lord League in a single attack.

Every witness was left in shock, unable to comprehend what they had just witnessed.

Even Apocalypse Harbinger, with all his power, could not achieve such a level of destruction.

In the face of a coordinated assault from over thirty Superior Deities, a typical Transcendent Deity warrior would be forced to retreat, and even the mightiest among them would find it nearly impossible to eliminate such a formidable group.

To wipe out so many with a single strike?

It was unthinkable—unless they weren't defending themselves at all.

Yet, Osborne had done just that, shattering their defenses and transforming their bodies into a gruesome mist of blood in an instant.

The sheer display of power was terrifying, leaving everyone dumbfounded.

Osborne himself remained eerily calm.

If they only knew that he hadn't even unleashed his full strength yet, their astonishment would only grow deeper.

"He must die!" Apocalypse Harbinger's voice sliced through the air, cold and resolute.

Until now, he had viewed the battle with a detached air, a high-and-mighty spectator.

But the situation had shifted, and urgency crept into his demeanor.

With less than twenty Transcendent Deity warriors left in Lord League, the remaining forces clearly lacked the firepower needed to take Osborne down.

Fear had taken root in the hearts of the remaining warriors, leaving them paralyzed.

Moreover, if Osborne found himself in real danger, the ten or so warriors from the Prophet Alliance would surely intervene.

Realizing this, Apocalypse Harbinger turned his gaze to Emperor Dare.

"Emperor Dare, your people excel in assassination. I want you to send your best to launch a decisive strike. We need him alive—there's no doubt he possesses secrets that could be worth their weight in gold. I want him captured so we can extract whatever knowledge he has."

Emperor Dare nodded solemnly, understanding the gravity of the situation.

"Rest assured, Esteemed Apocalypse Harbinger. I will take care of it personally, leading my people to capture him alive! He may be strong, but he cannot hope to match the Eityreia Organization. Soon, he will bow before you!"

With a determined glint in his eye, Emperor Dare sprang into action.

Alongside him were nearly a hundred assassins from the Eityreia Organization, all vanishing from sight in an instant, slipping into stealth mode. They closed in on Osborne from all directions, poised to deliver a lethal blow.

"Osborne, watch out! The Eityreia Organization has launched a surprise attack!" Eddie shouted, snapping back to reality.

He was as astonished as the others by Osborne's devastating display, but he hadn't lost sight of their enemies, Apocalypse Harbinger, Nelson, and Emperor Dare.

Seeing Emperor Dare and the killers disappear from view, Eddie instantly realized they were going into stealth to ambush Osborne. He rushed to warn Osborne.

Osborne merely smiled, "Thanks for the heads-up; I've got it."

With that, he charged toward the remaining warriors of Lord League, seeming completely unconcerned about the impending ambush.

This bold move sent Eddie into a tizzy.

He wanted to help Osborne, but he also had to be wary of the lurking assassins from the Eityreia Organization.

"Defend!"

The warriors from the Prophet Alliance gathered anxiously, their senses heightened.

But their efforts were ultimately futile.

The assassins from the Eityreia Organization had no interest in targeting them; all their focus was on Osborne.

While Osborne was keeping the remaining Transcendent Deities of Lord League on the defensive, chaos reigned among them.

Terrified by the sheer might of his abilities, they had abandoned any thoughts of a counteroffensive. Instead, they huddled together, nervously attempting to shield themselves.

It was as if Osborne was single-handedly laying siege to the remaining forces of Lord League.

Yet, curiously, their strategy of collective defense seemed to be having some effect.

At least for now, it seemed Osborne couldn't easily dispatch them. A dozen Transcendent Deities banded together was no trifling matter, even for someone as formidable as Osborne.

More importantly, he couldn't afford to act rashly.

Though it looked like he was focusing on attacking the assembled Transcendent Deities of the Lord League, his primary concern was actually the assassins from the Eityreia Organization.

Their Stealth Arts were formidable.

But Osborne could see through them, and he felt the Eityreia Organization's leader, Emperor Dare, stealthily creeping within 164 feet of him.

This proximity gave Osborne the confidence to instantly wipe out Emperor Dare, but he didn't strike.

He was waiting for more assassins to close in.

Emperor Dare, too, refrained from launching his attack. The prowess Osborne demonstrated made him wary of an immediate assault—he needed more of his assassins within striking distance.

Emperor Dare glared at Osborne, his eyes blazing with murderous intent.

He mused, "Our Stealth Arts are peerless, even in the Age of Gods! Your cultivation can't possibly surpass mine, so you can't see through me! Boy, your fate is sealed!"

Sensing that enough of his assassins were in position, Emperor Dare signaled the attack using a special technique.

In an instant, he unleashed his fury.
