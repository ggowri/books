Osborne's calm words struck Ava Mariposa's ears like a thunderclap.

She raised her head and looked at Osborne in shock.

"How are you not affected? How dare you call me ugly?"

Ava's pretty face was full of anger.

Osborne smirked, "You are ugly. So, before I get mad, get off me. Do not make me use force."

Ava grew even angrier.

"You bastard, you deserve to die!"

Her eyes blazed with fury, ready to act, but then she paused, seemingly listening to commands through an earpiece.

In the next second, she calmed down, gave Osborne a strange smile full of contempt, and began tearing her dress and hair into a mess. She clung to Osborne's arm.

"Help!

"Assault!

"Someone is trying to rape me!

"Help!"

Ava's desperate cries echoed far, even reaching the first floor.

Osborne's expression changed.

He snorted coldly and gave Ava a light push, making her fall to the ground. Seeing her disheveled state, Osborne frowned.

But before Osborne could react, hurried footsteps approached.

A dozen figures appeared nearby, looking at the shouting Ava lying on the ground with gloomy faces.

"Hey, you dare touch my woman and hit her? You are dead!" shouted a young man at the front, his face dark with anger.

It was Blake Knight, the second leader of Mayby City's biggest gang, the Scarlet Gang.

"Surround him!" Blake ordered. Instantly, over ten Scarlet Gang members encircled Osborne, each with a cold, murderous look.

Osborne looked at them and laughed inwardly.

"So, this is the game. Use a seductive woman to lure me in, record it if I take the bait, and then use it against me.

"If I do not, Ava screams assault, and I am smeared. With no witnesses, people would believe I did something to her."

Having figured it out, he let out a cold laugh; now that he knew the enemy's plot, dealing with it would be much easier.

"So, what is the plan now? You dared touch my woman; you must pay the price!" Blake sneered.

Osborne smiled faintly, "What do you suggest?"

Thinking Osborne had surrendered, Blake scoffed, "Ava is mine. Touching her is touching me. But since you did not actually succeed, I would not kill you.

"Kneel, apologize, and let me break your legs. Then this ends. Otherwise, you are dead.

"If I do not make you pay dearly, how can I continue to thrive in the world? How can I remain the second leader of the Scarlet Gang?"

After Blake finished speaking, his expression was cold and cruel.

"Second leader of the Scarlet Gang?"

Osborne realized Blake's identity, and his expression instantly turned cold and fierce.

He said coldly, "So you are from the Scarlet Gang. If I am not mistaken, the Scarlet Gang is involved in the adult entertainment business, right? Most of the adult venues in Mayby City are controlled by the Scarlet Gang, aren't they?

"You even force homeless women, and even kidnap respectable women, controlling their bodies and forcing them to sell themselves for your profit!"

Upon hearing this, Blake not only did not get angry, but instead looked quite proud.

"That is right! Most entertainment spots here are ours. Knowing who I am, you should know what to do.

"Kneel and apologize, let me break your legs," Blake said sternly.

Osborne laughed icily, "Scum like you want me to apologize? I hate gangs that sell drugs in slums and force women into prostitution.

"You are unlucky to meet me today.

"None of you will leave!"

Osborne coldly said, then stepped towards Blake.

Blake's face darkened as he gritted his teeth and said, "You are asking for death! Everyone, attack together! First, break his arms and legs, then expose his dirty secrets.

"Today, no matter what, we're going to make him a disgrace to Mayby City!"

With that, Blake moved swiftly.

His nickname 'Black Knife' came from his skill with a pitch-black short knife, which he had used to stab countless disobedient women.

Now, he drew out his black knife and thrust it fiercely towards Osborne's chest.

"Go to hell!"

Blake snarled.

At the same time, his subordinates were not idle; they drew their weapons and rushed towards Osborne with shouts.

Various weapons were suddenly thrust into Osborne's face.

Osborne appeared stunned, standing still and motionless, letting the weapons fall upon him.
