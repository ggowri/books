Chapter 556 It Hurt

The blonde bombshell spoke, her ice-blue eyes flashing dangerously.

"Take it easy. Even if he's making some noise now, he can't escape my grasp!"

Roger spoke up, seemingly reluctant to let the blonde beauty take action herself.

She furrowed her perfect brows, determination settling across her stunning features. "No, I must eliminate him myself!"

With that, she turned and strode out of the command center.

Roger watched her leave without trying to stop her.

*****

Marcus remained oblivious to all of this.

In just one night, he had perfectly mastered one of the Rune from the Heavenly Circle Diagram.

Time flew by until 7:30 AM when Lila finally stirred from her slumber.

Though she'd slept deeply and peacefully, barely dreaming at all,

Lila still felt like she'd been put through the wringer, with every joint in her body aching.

She shot Marcus a glare and said, "Mr. Fitzgerald, this is all your fault! You were so rough with me yesterday. Now everything hurts!"

Marcus chuckled, "I admit I was pretty intense, but judging by your reaction, it seemed like you wanted me to go even harder."

With the power of the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire behind him, no woman could resist the overwhelming waves of pleasure it brought.

Even someone as reserved as Lila, who usually held onto her composure, couldn't withstand that euphoria. She had given in completely, demanding more without restraint.

It was also the main reason why every joint in Lila's body ached now.

Hearing Marcus' words, a blush immediately crept across Lila's delicate face.

She glanced at the clock—it was already 7:40 AM.

The performance was scheduled for 11:00 AM, so she began getting up to wash and do her makeup. "Mr. Fitzgerald, turn around! You're not allowed to look at me."

Despite the long night they'd spent together, Lila still seemed shy.

Marcus smirked and teased, "I've already seen every inch of you except your insides. What's the harm in looking a little longer?"

Fuming, Lila bit her lip in frustration. "No! Turn around right now! I can't handle it!"

Seeing her adamant insistence, Marcus sighed and reluctantly turned his back to her.

Lila stood up and wrapped herself in a bath towel, then quickly made her way to the bathroom.

The sound of running water filled the air, steam rising into the space.

From Marcus' position, he could make out Lila's silhouette through the mist.

Marcus had always been drawn to beauty since he was young.

He remembered watching period dramas as a child, always captivated by the elegant actresses.

Now that he had found someone special, his appreciation only grew deeper.

When Lila emerged, she was wearing a new set of light-colored clothing.

Her smooth complexion made Marcus' heart skip a beat.

Just then, Marcus' phone buzzed.

It was a message from Josie.

Marcus' brow furrowed as he opened it.

The message contained an image and some text.

In the photo, Josie was bound with rope and gagged with tape, forced to kneel on the ground. Two men stood beside her, pointing heavy weapons at her head.

Even from just a photo, Marcus could see the terror in Josie's expression.

[If you want your woman to live, come to the Black Hawk Legion headquarters in Acoton within three days. If not, you'll face the consequences!]

Marcus' eyes turned icy as he read the text message.

He'd just put Josie on a flight to Acoton a few days ago.

He never expected the Black Hawk Legion would kidnap her!

Marcus hadn't wanted to get involved with the Black Hawk Legion.

But now they'd dared to touch his one vulnerability. If that were how they wanted to play it, he'd make sure this organization disappeared from the face of the earth!

"I need to take care of something. I'll be back in about two days," Marcus told Lila.

Seeing Marcus' grave expression, Lila sensed something was off.

But she was tactful enough not to pry, merely giving Marcus a slight nod.

Marcus left the room and quickly pulled up the coordinate map in his mind.

He immediately located the headquarters of the Black Hawk Legion.

Marcus dragged the coordinates to a desolate mountain about 6 miles away from the Black Hawk Legion headquarters.

Using teleport, he teleported to that mountainous location.

Taking about an hour to restore his depleted Numinous Energy to its peak state, Marcus then headed straight for the Black Hawk Legion headquarters.

As a world-renowned mercenary organization, the Black Hawk Legion wielded immense power.

Their headquarters was nothing short of a military base.

It was heavily guarded by countless fully armed personnel.

Beyond mercenaries recruited from every corner of the globe, the Black Hawk Legion possessed an arsenal of heavy equipment and cyborgs.

They even had everything from aircraft to artillery to tanks and all the weapons typically reserved for full-scale warfare!
