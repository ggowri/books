Chapter 296 Hospitalization

Allan drove with a smug look on his face.

He wasn't a bad guy, and he just enjoyed the feeling of being flattered by others.

So Allan said, "Lisa's right. That doctor is my friend, and we're pretty close. I just need to tell him that he will definitely treat Nick."

Nick and Aria immediately smiled with satisfaction upon hearing that, "That's great. Allan is awesome!"

Lisa felt even more proud to be praised by her parents. "Of course. You should praise your daughter's great taste in getting a man like Allan."

"Yeah, my daughter has been the pride of the Fitzgerald family since she came of age, and we've never been disappointed."

Nick nodded in a hurry. Lisa was not only his pride but also the pride of their village.

Even though Lisa hadn't been to college, she was one of the few young people in their village who could work in the big city and earn a good living.

Nick's eyes fell on Marcus without him realizing it, and he couldn't help but sigh.

He wanted to say something, but he swallowed it back.

Marcus made Richard and his family live in a big villa, but Nick always thought it was shameful to marry into a woman's family.

He thought Marcus should be sorry for his family.

Moreover, Marcus was nothing compared to Lisa.

Nick had told Richard this a long time ago, but Richard wouldn't listen to him, so Nick ignored him.

"Marcus, when your dad and I were hospitalized last time, the doctor you got for us was the director, right? Also, he was so polite to you and gave us a VIP ward. How about you give Nick a VIP ward?"

It was as if Melinda was thinking about the last time they were hospitalized after being kidnapped.

Melinda was being kind and didn't mean to show off in any way.

She was completely trying to get Nick better treatment.

After all, Nick was the brother of Melinda's husband. They had so many years of kinship, so of course, she wished Nick well.

"Right, Marcus. You can contact the hospital and arrange a VIP ward for Nick. The normal ward is so messy that it will bother him to rest," Nick chimed in.

However, Allan and Lisa clearly didn't believe them.

Lisa said, "As far as I know, Solomia Hospital is one of the top ten hospitals in Uswad. Not to mention VIP wards, even general wards are in short supply.

"So many patients have to wait in line to go to the doctor who needs to be hospitalized. Uncle Richard, Aunt Melinda, I know you want Marcus to be well, but you have to be realistic in what you say."

Lisa had tried to put it as politely as possible, and she just didn't want to hurt the relationship between the relatives.

"Lisa is right. Solomia Hospital is so popular that patients from three surrounding states come here for medical treatment.

"My friend is a doctor, but he can't even get a VIP ward. Even if we pay extra, we can't even register, or else I would like to get Nick a VIP ward. After all, it's a better environment for him to get better rest," Allan also said the same.

Seeing Allan and Lisa not believing her, Melinda wanted to continue explaining, but she saw Marcus waving his hand at her.

They had already decided what they thought, and it was useless for Melinda to explain it any further.

If Melinda explained more, they would think she was lying instead, using the story to cover up the lies she had told earlier.

Soon, Allan parked his Sienna commercial vehicle in the parking lot of Solomia Hospital.

He had to wait in line to get into the parking lot because there were so many patients to see the doctor.

Besides, he had been in line for at least half an hour or more.

"Melinda, I remember the last time Marcus took us to the doctor, and we went straight into the underground parking lot without waiting in line, so what happened today?"

Richard asked in disbelief.

"I saw that parking lot that said VIP ward exclusive, so we didn't have to wait in line," Melinda said.

Allan and Lisa smiled without saying anything.

They thought, "Uncle Richard and Aunt Melinda are so good at acting, and they even know that the VIP ward has exclusive parking spaces. How did they learn that?

Did Marcus teach them in advance?

It's quite possible!"

When Allan finally got a parking space, he stopped the car and wasted over forty minutes.

As they got out of the car, they all stood still and took deep breaths, just feeling out of breath.

Nick felt even more dizzy and almost fainted.

"Dad, are you okay?"

Lisa helped Nick immediately.

Nick looked pale. "I feel a bit dizzy. Maybe I'd been in the car for too long."

"I've contacted my friend. Hurry up and take Nick inside," Allan said.

They rushed to help Nick into the hospital.

Unexpectedly, they had to wait in line to register, and they were also going to the specialist clinic in line.
