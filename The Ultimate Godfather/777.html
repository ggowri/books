Chapter 773 Hidden State-2

His strength grew once more, and the absorbed energy even accelerated his recovery.

Emboldened by the boost, Marcus launched another relentless assault on his next target.

He fought with a reckless strategy, trading blows to ensure he could eliminate each opponent one at a time.

The process was brutal. One slip could cost him his life, yet Marcus gritted his teeth and endured.

By the time he struck down the final figure, his strength had soared to an entirely new level.

Without hesitation, he stepped into the eighth challenge.

As expected, this time, sixteen figures awaited him.

But with the experience he had gained in the seventh trial, Marcus navigated the battle with relative ease. Though the process was grueling, he managed to pass unscathed.

When the light of the ninth trial ignited, Ada and Raphael, standing outside the massive castle, were overcome with excitement.

Before this, they had thought reaching the sixth challenge was already a monumental feat. After all, it had broken the records of those before him.

They hadn't expected Marcus to have made it to the ninth trial.

If he succeeded, he would become the first person to conquer all nine challenges since the castle's creation!

Marcus fought with unstoppable momentum.

The previous eight challenges had reshaped him completely.

Even when faced with eighteen versions of himself, he showed no fear, unleashing a level of power that defied imagination.

It took him less than five minutes to kill the first figure.

With the surge of strength that followed, his confidence soared.

Over the next ten minutes, he dismantled the remaining figures one by one, absorbing their energy into himself.

When the final opponent fell, Marcus' power reached its peak.

He let out a deep breath, feeling as though his entire body was undergoing a transformation.

His Numinous Energy surged uncontrollably, while his energy channels and bones underwent a profound metamorphosis. He felt as though he was on the brink of a breakthrough.

"What's going on? I haven't even claimed the Return Essence Crystals, and I'm already advancing to the Return Essence Stage?" he thought.

Marcus' face was filled with disbelief as the sensation of an impending breakthrough grew stronger by the second.

Not wanting to waste the opportunity, he sat down cross-legged and began channeling the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire, absorbing the ambient energy in the space with abandon.

Under the grimoire's guidance, he felt himself stepping into an entirely new state.

This state transcended the Ancient Martial Stage Nine, yet fell short of the Return Essence Stage. It was a state between the two.

Marcus' eyes widened in shock.

He had always believed the Foundation Establishment Stage had only nine levels. It was a truth confirmed by countless practitioners before him.

Conventionally, the next step after the ninth level was to use the Return Essence Crystals to ascend to the Return Essence Stage.

But now, he had discovered a hidden state between the two. It was something entirely unfamiliar.

"Human, congratulations. You are the first to conquer the castle's trials.

"Additionally, through your relentless combat experience and personal breakthroughs, you've unlocked the Hidden State between the Foundation Establishment Stage and the Return Essence Stage.

"This will provide you with a more solid foundation and an advantage in battle against others of the same stage. You can even fight across stages now."

As Marcus marveled at this newfound state, the ethereal voice returned.

"Hidden State?"

The term was entirely foreign to Marcus.

Even the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire hadn't mentioned it.

From what the voice said, achieving this state seemed nearly impossible.

Curious, Marcus asked, "So, my current cultivation level is in the Hidden State between Foundation Establishment and Return Essence. But what does that mean for my combat strength? Can you explain?"
