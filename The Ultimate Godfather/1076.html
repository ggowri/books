Chapter 970 Moral Coercion-2

Morgana's face twisted with anger, but hesitation flashed in her eyes. Marcus, meanwhile, watched silently—unmoved.

Marcus raised an eyebrow.

"Seriously?"

This was classic moral coercion.

So, Marcus smirked and said, "Weren't you just scolding me for entering the girls' dorm? Now you want me to help?"

Morgana's face flushed red, and she suddenly felt like the other side of her face was burning just as much as the one that got slapped.
