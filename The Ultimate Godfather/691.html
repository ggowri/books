Chapter 690 Bloodthirsty Cult

A frigid voice rang out from not too far away.

The leader of the Bloodthirsty Cult looked in that direction.

He witnessed a glimmer of light shining. The light expanded and swiftly coalesced into a figure.

The shape of a young man appeared.

The Bloodthirsty Cult leader froze and inquired, "Who are you?"

Whoever could appear silently within the main hall of the Bloodthirsty Cult must be extraordinary.

"The man who's going to kill you."

As Marcus spoke, he took rapid strides forward.

A terrifying light converged in the palm of his hand, and he immediately launched a Fist Ray towards the Bloodthirsty Cult leader.

The Bloodthirsty Cult leader's eyes were cold.

He hadn't anticipated that Marcus would attack him so soon.

In Southvaia, no one dared to be so audacious towards him.

Unprepared, he was caught off guard. He hastily launched a counter-attack to defend himself but was jolted back continuously.

"Boy, why are you so arrogant?"

The Bloodthirsty Cult leader was surprised.

Then, he sneered. He was pretty sure he could kill Marcus.

Marcus scoffed, "You're too weak."

He took another step forward and released the Fist Ray.

The Bloodthirsty Cult leader also entered a fighting state.

A red light radiated all over his body, and terrifying witchcraft was unleashed.

He quickly launched two destructive attacks on Marcus.

The attacks of a Curse Weaver were powerful.

It was as if two ferocious beasts, filled with tox, were charging madly towards Marcus.

Before Marcus could make a move, the two beasts spat out the green liquid. The liquid emitted heatwaves and was highly corrosive.

Anyone who touched the liquid would break out in ulcers all over their body, regardless of their cultivation level!

"You're the first person who has dared to attack my cult alone. You'll become my food today!"

Speaking, the Bloodthirsty Cult leader looked at Marcus with the will to kill.

He had already sensed that Marcus' cultivation had reached the peak of Ancient Martial Stage Eight.

While he was surprised, he had confidence in his own abilities.

The Bloodthirsty Cult leader could tell that Marcus had only recently advanced to this realm, while he had been dormant in this realm for many years. His strength was greater.

Marcus sneered, ignoring the attacks of the two beasts.

He directly retrieved the golden scimitar from his Spatial Ring and slashed it out.

The Blade Ray contained power of Rune with patterns lingering above it.

In an instant, it collided with the two ferocious beasts.

Rumbling sounds resounded.

The attack of the beasts was chopped to pieces by the Blade Ray and shattered.

However, the power of the Blade Ray remained undiminished. And it directly severed the heads of the two beasts. Green blood spurted out, giving off a foul stench.

The Bloodthirsty Cult leader was shocked. He hadn't expected that Marcus was not only proficient in swordsmanship but also integrated the power of Rune into the Blade Ray.

"Boy, our strengths are on par. If we continue to fight, both sides will suffer. What's your connection with the three guardians of mine who went to the Celestial Arc Order?"

The Bloodthirsty Cult leader asked.

After witnessing the power of Marcus' attack, he no longer wished to engage in a fight with Marcus.

Even though he was still confident that he could win the battle, he was wary of losses.

"I'm dating Ada," Marcus replied calmly.

The leader of the Bloodthirsty Cult burst into laughter. "You're strong enough to date all kinds of girls. Why would you fight me over one woman?

"If you want women, I have an abundance of them."

As he spoke, the leader of Bloodthirsty Cult clapped his hands.

Then, hundreds of women in sexy bikinis emerged from the rear of the hall.

They were perfect in appearance and figure.

And each had her own unique charm.

These women were from Oriana Land, Weston Land, and many more from Southvaia.

The scene was dizzying.

Even though Marcus had seen many women, he couldn't help but be momentarily stunned.

"You can take all these women with you. Each of them has been meticulously selected, and they're all virgins. You can pick any woman you desire!"

The leader of the Bloodthirsty Cult said and tried to tempt Marcus.

Marcus sneered, "If I kill you, all these women will belong to me anyway!"
