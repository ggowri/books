As he spoke, his body seemed to transform into a streak of purple light, flickering unpredictably in the air before unleashing another sudden palm strike.

The strike descended from above, carrying an oppressive force as terrifying as a mountain crashing down.

Marcus felt as though he were being crushed under a mountain.

He wanted to dodge, but his body refused to move. Left with no choice, he swung a punch skyward in defiance.

A deafening explosion reverberated through the air.

Marcus's body shook violently.

The oppressive force surged through him, pinning him as though he bore the weight of a mountain. His feet sank into the ground under the strain.

"Still not dead?!" Ricardo snarled, his expression wild with fury.

Ricardo's face twisted with madness as he poured more Numinous Energy into the palm strike.

Crack!

Marcus's bones made a disjointed crack as the pressure forced him to collapse to the ground.

He knew the gap in power between himself and Ricardo was immense—he was at a clear disadvantage in direct combat.

More importantly, Ricardo's strength was far beyond what Marcus had anticipated.

Seeing Marcus on the ground, Ricardo launched another devastating strike, intent on ending his life.

Marcus rolled away just in time, dodging several meters to the side as the strike landed, leaving a deep crater where he had been.

The power of a peak Ancient Martial Stage Three warrior was terrifying. There was no exaggeration in saying Ricardo was the strongest opponent Marcus had ever faced.

All his previous enemies combined wouldn't amount to even a fifth of Ricardo's strength!

"For someone at the peak of Ancient Martial Stage Two, you've held out surprisingly long," Ricardo said, a hint of admiration in his voice. "But you still won't leave here alive!"

Ricardo nodded slightly, now understanding why the subordinates he had sent before had all been killed.

The strength Marcus displayed now could even rival that of someone at the early stages of Ancient Martial Stage Three.

Before Ricardo could attack again, flames ignited in Marcus's palm, and he unleashed a Fireball.

The searing flames shot toward Ricardo like a lightning bolt.

Ricardo's face registered shock as he exclaimed, "You're a Wizard?!"

Still, he showed no fear. With a casual punch, he shattered Marcus's Fireball.

Marcus didn't let up, following with a burst of Lightning.

A terrifying bolt of lightning tore through the void, striking directly above Ricardo.

However, Ricardo's body merely trembled slightly, showing no significant damage.

"Lightning? Ha, interesting. I wonder how many tricks you're hiding," Ricardo sneered.

He advanced like an unstoppable giant toward Marcus.

With every step, he launched another attack.

Marcus didn't dare face them head-on and dodged left and right.

Taking advantage of Ricardo's momentary distraction, Marcus retrieved several magic crystals from his Spatial Ring.

Chanting under his breath, the magic crystals unleashed dazzling magical attacks, enveloping Ricardo completely.

A barrage of magical attacks rained down, and for the first time, a serious expression appeared on Ricardo's face.

He hadn't expected Marcus to possess so many magic crystals.

Magic crystals were typically consumables, capable of unleashing only a single attack.

The power of these attacks far exceeded what an ordinary Wizard could produce.

The crystals Marcus used carried the strength of a Wizard at Ancient Martial Stage Three unleashing their full power.

The simultaneous onslaught of magical attacks posed a significant challenge for Ricardo.

Ricardo's body trembled violently as he quickly released Numinous Energy to counter the attacks.

Thunderous booms echoed continuously.

Magic clashed with Numinous Energy.

Ricardo managed to completely neutralize the barrage of magical attacks through sheer force.

Marcus's expression darkened slightly. Ricardo's strength far exceeded anything he had imagined.

After all, these magic crystals were one of Marcus's last resorts for survival.

He had used them in hopes of quickly gaining the upper hand.

Yet, they had failed to inflict the damage he had anticipated.

Ricardo looked a bit disheveled but had lost none of his combat strength.

In fact, the attacks had only fueled his rage.

Empowered by his fury, Ricardo's combat strength seemed to grow even more formidable.

Each strike carried enough force to pose a fatal threat to Marcus.

In no time, Marcus was forced to retreat, sustaining severe injuries. Blood trickled continuously from the corner of his mouth.

"Give it up, kid! Against absolute power, all your tricks are useless!" Ricardo sneered coldly.

Ricardo's chilling laughter accompanied an ominous and terrifying aura radiating from him.

He struck again, his palm print descending in front of Marcus. Marcus countered using the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire combined with his Fire Attribute Power.

Yet, the impact sent Marcus flying, blood spurting violently from his mouth.

Ricardo refused to give Marcus any chance to recover, stepping forward again.

In the blink of an eye, he appeared before Marcus, channeling all his energy into his palm.

Ricardo's palm radiated a terrifying light. The concentrated Numinous Energy within carried overwhelming destructive power as it hurtled toward Marcus.

"Let's see how you handle this!" Ricardo shouted, his face alight with madness.

This attack was Ricardo's trump card, containing everything he had—it was meant to obliterate Marcus completely.

As the destructive force loomed closer, Marcus's expression remained surprisingly calm.

Just as the attack was about to land, a faint, enigmatic smile appeared on Marcus's face.

"Die!" Ricardo roared, throwing all his strength into the strike aimed to destroy Marcus.
