The crowd did not expect that Marcus actually dared to challenge. They thought, "Could he really drink five bottles of Everclear in one go? How is that possible?"

A normal person, if they consumed five bottles of Everclear, would either be dead or in the ICU.

Five bottles of Everclear were just a piece of cake for Nolan. If Marcus drank five bottles, he would surely lose his ability to fight, even if he didn't end up in the hospital.

Thus, there was still a considerable gap in their strengths.

Once Marcus got drunk by Nolan, Nolan's purpose would be achieved.

Thinking about this, the crowd unintentionally glanced at Melina's perfect figure.

"Why not?"

Nolan summoned the waiter into the private room and instructed, "Bring two larger glasses."

The waiter apologetically replied, "Sorry, Mr. Watson. These are the largest wine glasses we have."

Before the waiter could finish, Nolan interrupted, "Then bring beer mugs instead."

Under normal circumstances, a beer mug has a capacity of about five hundred milliliters.

This means that a beer mug can almost hold an entire bottle of Everclear.

The audience had never heard of anyone drinking Everclear from a beer mug. It was simply outrageous.

The waiter was also taken aback, somewhat shocked.

Marcus remained expressionless and said, "That's about right."

Saying this, he took out a bottle of Everclear, opened the cap, and poured the entire bottle into the beer mug.

This scene made everyone's scalp tingle.

They struggled to down 150 milliliters of Everclear. Marcus and Nolan were actually going to drink Everclear from 500-milliliter beer mugs.

Then he lifted the beer mug and poured it into his mouth.

The crowd only heard the continuous gulping sounds.

As Nolan's throat moved, he finished a full 500 milliliters of Everclear just like that.

Nolan showed no reaction and licked his lips in satisfaction, eyeing Marcus, "Kid, it's your turn."

Marcus glanced at Nolan, then quietly activated his X-ray vision.

Nolan was capable of this due to the cultivation method he practiced.

There were waves of sword energy coursing through his body, emitting a sharp aura.

The cultivation method he practiced was a type of Inhalation method, extremely mysterious.

The alcohol vaporized and lingered in the air, which was precisely why there was such a strong scent of alcohol in the room.

After figuring out Nolan's trick, Marcus did not hesitate and downed a glass of Everclear in one go.

Even though he had not practiced Nolan's extraordinary method, as a powerful Return Essence stage warrior, he could freely release Numinous Energy, thus easily refining the alcohol entering his body.

This was the domineering aspect of having high-level strength. Although Marcus had not practiced a method similar to Nolan's, he could still use the powerful Numinous Energy in his body to refine and break down the alcohol.

Therefore, after finishing a glass, Marcus felt completely unburdened, and even seemed more composed than Nolan, managing to drink alcohol as if it were water.

This scene left everyone present dumbfounded.

They never expected Marcus to have such a good alcohol tolerance.

However, they did not think too highly of Marcus. After all, this was just the beginning.

Some ordinary people with good alcohol tolerance could also down a bottle of Everclear if they went all out.

There were still four bottles of Everclear waiting for Marcus.

This time, just like before, he quickly vaporized and decomposed the Everclear using his method, keeping himself completely sober.
