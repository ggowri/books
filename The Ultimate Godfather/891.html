Chapter 860 Devouring an Acquired State Two expert-1

"This is impossible! How could you possess such powerful Devouring Power? What kind of monster are you?"

The middle-aged man could feel his Numinous Energy rapidly draining away as he struggled desperately against the invisible force, but his efforts proved futile.

His eyes widened with terror as he stared at Marcus.

Marcus mockingly smiled as he sneered, "None of that matters now! You're nothing more than fuel for my powers!"

Marcus intensified his Devouring power as he spoke, causing the middle-aged man's body to convulse even more violently.

As an expert at Acquired State Two, the man possessed more Numinous Energy than Marcus' previous victims combined.

The Devouring process lasted a full ten minutes before it finally came to an end.

The middle-aged man's resistance transformed from desperate struggling to hopeless screaming until he could only submit to Marcus' harvesting of his power.

He helplessly watched as Marcus drained away the Numinous Energy he had spent centuries cultivating within his body.

His eyes grew increasingly unfocused until they became utterly lifeless, while his skin slowly shriveled, leaving nothing but a desiccated corpse.

Sensing the surging Numinous Energy within his body, Marcus nodded with satisfaction.

He was confident that absorbing this Numinous Energy would take his cultivation to the next level.

Without hesitation, he collected the Spatial Rings from the two middle-aged men and unleashed his Fire Attribute Power, reducing their bodies to ashes.

A sea breeze swept through, carrying away their remains as if they had never existed.

"With my current strength, even an Acquired State Six expert would struggle against me if I went all out. Once I absorb this guy's Numinous Energy and advance my cultivation further, I'll become even more formidable.

When I meet Alison again, if she hasn't advanced her cultivation, I'll have enough power to take her on!"

Marcus thought to himself, a satisfied smile spreading across his face.

He recalled the events of two months ago when Alison descended from the skies above Solomia and overwhelmed him with sheer power.

Marcus felt angry as he remembered how helpless he would have been without Diana's intervention.

Back then, he couldn't put up any resistance against Alison.

At that time, he was just a Foundation Establishment Stage warrior, no different from an ant facing Alison, a Level Seven Demon Fighter.

Yet, merely two months later, Marcus was about to become an Acquired State expert capable of competing with Alison.

He wondered what Alison would think if she knew about his rapid progress.

Marcus snapped back to reality and returned to the patrol car.

Since all three of them were Acquired State experts, a real-world battle would have torn the fabric of reality apart.

That was why they had set up a Rune to contain their battlefield before the fight began.

The area appeared utterly peaceful to any observer as if nothing had happened.

No one would have guessed that an epic battle between Acquired State experts had just occurred there.

If word got out, Marcus' single-handed defeat of two Acquired State Two experts would send shockwaves through the world of the Ancient Martial Art of Uswad.

Thanks to the Rune, even Maxine remained utterly unaware of the battle.

When Marcus returned, Maxine asked, "Did you handle the situation?"

"Yeah, I managed to chase those guys off," Marcus replied casually.

He deliberately kept the true nature of events from Maxine.

Given Maxine's current understanding, she wasn't ready to accept the existence of Return Essence stage warriors, so Marcus chose to tell a white lie.
