Chapter 210 Changed the Situation

"How is this possible? How could you deflect my attack?"

The middle-aged man collapsed helplessly to the ground. If it weren't for the protective armor that had absorbed much of the impact, that attack would have obliterated him.

Even so, his bones were shattered, and he was completely crippled.

Thanks to Celestial Reversal, Marcus was completely unharmed, standing as calmly as ever. "You're not qualified to know."

Malcolm, who was still under Marcus' control, had already been scared out of his wits.

He knew exactly how terrifying the middle-aged man's power was.

In the presence of someone like him, even the most powerful firearms would be useless. Nothing could harm him.

Yet, right now, that same person seemed utterly powerless in front of Marcus, completely shattering everything Malcolm had thought he knew.

"Good! Very good! You've crippled my cultivation, but the Astral Serpent Order will never forgive you for this!"

The middle-aged man screamed in agony, blood trickling from the corners of his mouth as he threatened Marcus.

The Astral Serpent Order was one of Uswad's three great Ancient Martial Art Sects, with a deep foundation and extraordinary power.

It was said that the sect leader had reached Ancient Martial Stage Six, and his single strike could flatten a mountain.

By crippling one of their members, Marcus had surely drawn the wrath of the Astral Serpent Order.

However, Marcus merely sneered and didn't even spare the man another glance.

Before this, he had already killed Brandon of the Golden Group, a man once considered the strongest in Solomia, who was also from the Astral Serpent Order.

Marcus had already made enemies with the Astral Serpent Order, so he was not afraid of eliminating another one of their members.

The scene left everyone in stunned silence.

The once mysterious and powerful Astral Serpent Order warrior, who everyone had thought invincible, had been reduced to a pitiful state in front of Marcus, unable to even break through his defense.

They were all speculating just how powerful Marcus truly was and what kind of identity he held.

Many in the room couldn't help but wonder, and their gazes toward Marcus had completely changed.

Even Maxine's heart trembled in disbelief.

The power Marcus displayed was even more terrifying than when he had defeated Gibson in the International Invitational. It was almost beyond human limits, something almost godlike.

"Why haven't you put down your guns yet? Are you waiting for me to kill you all?"

Marcus' gaze swept over the black-clad men holding firearms, his eyes flashing with an intense and murderous intent.

The men were visibly shaken and instinctively took a step back, fear radiating from their eyes as they stared at Marcus.

"Don't be afraid of him. No matter how strong he is, can he be stronger than a bullet?"

At that moment, one of the black-clad men, eager to prove himself, spoke coldly.

Before he could finish, he moved to pull the trigger, and the two men beside him followed suit, aiming their guns at Marcus.

However, before the three of them could fire, three invisible streams of force shot from Marcus' fingertips.

In an instant, the force pierced straight through the center of their foreheads.

The next moment, loud splashing sounds filled the air as the three men's heads exploded, their blood gushing out in torrents. They fell to the floor, lifeless.

Seeing this gruesome scene, everyone present gasped, their faces filled with horror.

The black-clad men holding guns were now completely paralyzed with fear, sweat pouring from their foreheads.

Finally, under intense pressure, one of them couldn't take it anymore and dropped his gun.

Upon seeing this, the others followed suit, surrendering their weapons and raising their hands in submission.

The entire ordeal, which could have upended the power of the Burnett family, was resolved in less than three minutes, all by Marcus' actions alone.

Malcolm, Ruth, and Arnold's faces turned a ghastly pale. They never could have imagined that Marcus would be the one to change the course of the situation.

"Apologize to Mr. Owen Burnett!"

Marcus' gaze locked onto Malcolm as he spoke in a voice like thunder.

Malcolm, trembling in fear, his knees buckling, immediately collapsed in front of Owen.

Owen glanced at Marcus with gratitude, then looked down at Malcolm.

Malcolm's face alternated between blue and white, and in such a dire situation, he had no choice but to bow his head to Owen. "Owen, I was wrong. I was blinded by ambition. Please forgive me for this one time. I'll never dare again!"

Owen's voice was cold and stern. "Malcolm, you've truly disappointed me."

"Owen, Malcolm only rebelled because of Shaun's slander. Everything is Shaun's fault. We have nothing to do with this!"

Ruth suddenly dropped to her knees before Owen, shifting the blame entirely onto Shaun.
