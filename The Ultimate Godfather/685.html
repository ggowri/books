Nevertheless, the power of Marcus' Fist Ray remained undiminished in the slightest. In the twinkling of an eye, it reached the area right in front of the guardian.

The guardian's face was filled with horror. It had never crossed his mind that Marcus' punch could be this terrifying.

He made an attempt to leap backwards to avoid it, yet he felt that his body was ensnared by an intangible Binding Force, rendering him completely immobile.

He could only watch helplessly as Marcus' Fist Ray landed on him.

"No!"

Accompanied by his despairing cry, the guardian's body was sent hurtling through the air.

His body flew backwards like a cannonball for hundreds of yards. Before falling to the ground, it exploded, shattering into fragments that rained down all over. The air was thick with a ghastly stench of blood, creating a scene of utter wretchedness.

The scene immediately dumbfounded the other two guardians and Ada.

With one punch, Marcus effortlessly killed a guardian who was a Level Seven Curse Weaver.

His strength was simply formidable.

Moreover, judging by Marcus' demeanor, it was evident that he hadn't exerted his full might.

Merely a casual punch was sufficient to overwhelm the guardian and claim his life!

Clearly, a significant gulf in strength existed between them.

Marcus remained calm, glanced at the other two guardians, and asked with a sneer, "Who will be the next one?"

The faces of the two guardians turned deathly pale.

After exchanging a quick glance, they instantly reached a tacit agreement. Without uttering a single word, they simultaneously launched a fearsome assault on Marcus.

When they joined forces, their combined strength was, without a doubt, far greater than that of the guardian who had been instantly slain previously.

In the blink of an eye, the entire space was shrouded in endless blood-red light. It seemed as if a colossal Blood Curse materialized in the sky, rapidly spreading to enshroud Marcus.

He remained completely unperturbed. Waves of Runes surged forth from his body, encircling and shielding him.

The extremely terrifying power of the Runes radiated outwards, as if isolating the space from the rest of the world.

After descending, the Blood Curse was directly blocked by the power of the Runes, accompanied by a thunderous rumble.

The Blood Curse shattered into pieces, but Marcus remained composed and stood motionless.

The scene once again stunned everyone. They had never anticipated that he was actually a Rune Crafter.

Moreover, the way he arranged the Runes was a testament to his having achieved a level of perfection.

With a simple thought, the light of the Runes would cocoon him, and no form of attack would manage to break through the barrier.

His strength was even far beyond the reach of the top-notch Rune Crafters in the world of Ancient Martial Art in Uswad.

Almost at the same time, Marcus roared, "Go to hell!"

He fixed his eyes on one of the guardians and punched him casually.

The Fist Ray appeared light, but in the eyes of the guardian, it was like a death warrant.

Having witnessed what had befallen his companion, he didn't dare engage Marcus in a direct confrontation. Instead, he could only make a desperate attempt to dodge as swiftly as possible.

However, an incredibly powerful Binding Force descended from the void. It was like an invisible fetter that held the guardian firmly in position.

With an ashen face, he could only try to resist.

A deafening rumble of the explosion echoed out. In comparison to Marcus' Fist Ray, his counterattack was as flimsy as a piece of paper.

His attacks became useless in a short time, but Marcus' fist was an unstoppable force, hurtling towards him with lightning speed.

Gradually, it enlarged before his eyes!

Next, a thunderous bang resounded. Marcus' punch directly smashed the guardian's head.

The guardian figure struggled a few more times before collapsing to the ground.

Ada couldn't help but gasp.

The fight showed that in just a few days, Marcus' strength seemed to have undergone a drastic change.

It was much stronger than it had been a few days before.

These Curse Weavers from Southvaia were just like ants in front of Marcus.

He employed nothing but the most basic form of attack. Even faced with his casually launched Fist Ray, they were utterly powerless to resist.

He completely overpowered them!

Seeing this horrifying scene, the guardian, a Curse Weaver at the peak of Level Seven, didn't have the courage to stay.

Holding a yellow talisman in his hand, he attempted to crush it to make an escape.

Marcus showed no mercy. The instant the talisman was crushed, his large hand shot out and seized the guardian's body. It was as easy as an eagle snatching a chick, and he held the guardian firmly in his grasp.

"Sir, please spare my life! I'm ready to hand over all that I possess. But if you insist on taking my life, get ready to face the fury of the Bloodthirsty Cult!" the guardian pleaded, his face contorted with fear.

At that moment, he no longer had any power to fight back in Marcus' grasp.

"You can go to hell now!"

Without giving the guardian any chance to speak, Marcus just exerted the full strength of his large hand.

There was a rapid succession of crackling sounds of bones breaking.

The guardian's body was reduced to pieces, and blood flowed through the gaps between Marcus' fingers.

The guardian's eyes were wide open, brimming with unyielding resentment. He never dreamed that his life would end in this way...
