Because the Great Epoch was approaching.

By then, the world's rules would change.

The rulers of various continents were restless, trying to seize the advantage and claim more resources before the Great Epoch arrived.

And after the world's rules change, all existing order would be disrupted.

Even the barriers between major continents would weaken with the rule changes.

Moreover, as the Great Epoch arrived, countless opportunities would emerge across all continents.

By then, many destinies would be rewritten, and a new group of kings would rise, making their mark across the continents.

They may even replace the continent's former rulers, holding the power of life and death over it.

After the rules changed, the world within the Ice Wall, once seen as a cage by them, was also likely to give birth to many new opportunities.

Therefore, not only on the Lemuriat, but people from other continents also began to stir.

They captured young girls in the Solomia not because of an order from Roderick.

Instead, it was because of a vanguard called River Banks who came to this world.

According to the elder's notes, Rivers cultivated a sinister technique that consumed the blood of young women to enhance his cultivation.

Therefore, after arriving in this world, Rivers ordered his subordinates to capture some young women for his cultivation.

This led to the recent cases of young women disappearing in Solomia.

"The Great Epoch. The world's rules will change."

Looking at the contents of the elder's notes, Marcus seemed lost in thought.

If what he said is true, then more and more invaders will arrive in this world.

All the previous tranquility and peace will also be shattered after these invaders arrive.

Thinking of this, Marcus couldn't help but feel a sense of urgency.

He knew he must grow fully before the Great Epoch arrives.

Only in this way could he protect himself after the world's rules changed.

At the same time, he could better protect those around him.

The notes don't tell the exact time when the Great Epoch will arrive, but the sudden appearance of the Ophidian in this world suggests the time is near. I must quickly enhance my personal strength!

Marcus thought to himself.

Gertie said, "For thousands of years, people in our world have been endlessly longing for the world beyond the Ice Wall."

"Many even risked their lives to explore the world beyond the Ice Wall."

"Who would have thought that after the arrival of the Great Epoch, the barriers between worlds would completely weaken, and what was unattainable might soon be easily accessible."

Marcus nodded and said, "In theory, that's true, but the sudden weakening of the barriers might not be a good thing for the people of this world. They've grown accustomed to this peaceful and stable life."

"Moreover, the world beyond the Ice Wall is certainly harsher than this one, with countless unknown dangers. If they venture there recklessly, they will pay a heavy cost."

Gertie agreed with Marcus. She said calmly, "This Great Epoch is beyond our control. By the way, does the notebook mention where those girls are being held?"

Marcus nodded and said, "Yes, but we don't know the situation on the other side. There might be danger. I'll go rescue those girls alone. Wait for my good news."

Marcus didn't want Gertie to take risks with him, as he had no idea how many people were on the other side or what level of strength each of them possessed.

If he rashly took Gertie along, it could very likely put her in danger.

However, as soon as Marcus finished speaking, Gertie hurriedly said, "No, I want to go with you. No matter how dangerous it is, I must go. You can't leave me behind!"
