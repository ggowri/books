Gertie looked a bit confused and asked, "What are you laughing at? Why are you laughing at me?"

Marcus forcibly suppressed his laughter and said, "I'm laughing because you're both naive and adorable."

"To be honest with you, even if you didn't kiss me, I wouldn't ignore these cases."

"You..."

Gertie immediately felt tricked and had the urge to beat up the guy in front of her.

"Tell me, why are you so bad?"

Gertie's chest heaved with anger, and the two peaks on her chest seemed as if they were about to break free from her clothes.

"Haven't you heard the saying, 'Women love bad boys'?"

Marcus suddenly looked at Gertie seriously, leaning in so close that he almost filled her entire view, and asked, "Be honest with me, have you fallen in love with me?"

Gertie's face flushed red upon hearing this, and her heart inexplicably started to race as she looked at Marcus's impeccably handsome face.

She almost couldn't help but nod, but her inner rationality suppressed it as she said, "Don't talk nonsense, I haven't..."

Although she still denied it, Marcus had already gotten a definite answer from Gertie's expression.

"Ah! Marcus, look, those people you killed all looked different!"

At that moment, Gertie seemed to see something terrifying, grabbing Marcus's body tightly and screaming.

Marcus followed Gertie's gaze and saw that the corpses on the ground had indeed changed.

Previously, they still looked human, but now they had turned into human bodies with snake heads.

The snake heads were of various types, resembling known species like pythons, cobras, black mambas, rattlesnakes, and so on.

Combined with human bodies, they looked extremely grotesque and terrifying, giving off an unsettling feeling.

This made Marcus's brows furrow.

He asked Gertie, "They come from the world beyond the Ice Wall. Do you know of any continent beyond the Ice Wall where such creatures exist?"

Gertie pondered for a moment. As if recalling something, her eyes lit up and she said, "I remember now. I think I once heard the elders in my family mention that beyond the Ice Wall, on a continent called Lemuria, there are creatures that are half-human, half-snake living there They are called Ophidian."

"Could it be that these monsters come from Lemuria?"

Marcus nodded lightly, "It's very possible. I'll first open their Spatial Rings to see if I can find any clues inside."

Next, Marcus used the Heavenly Circle Diagram to shield this space, isolating everything within from the outside world.

Immediately after, he unlocked all the Ophidians's Spatial Rings and took out the contents one by one.

During this time, Marcus discovered that, besides common items like pills, weapons, and magical treasures, there were also many strange and unusual items.

These strange and unusual items were things he had never seen in this world and were likely exclusive to the world where the Ophidian lived.

Among them, a map drawn on animal hide successfully caught Marcus's attention.

He unfolded the map and saw a sea area, with an extremely vast continent located at the center.

The continent was surrounded by numerous islands.

The map clearly marked the major cities on the continent, the connecting roads between them, and even detailed down to towns and villages.

But the name of the continent was not marked.

"Lemuria Continent!"

Just as Marcus was guessing the name of the continent, Gertie let out a gasp.

"Can you be sure this is Lemuria?"

Marcus asked.

"Yes I can. I once saw a wooden plaque in my family's library. That plaque had the detailed outline and name of this continent engraved on it—Lemuria. It left a very deep impression on me!" Gertie nodded and said.

"Then that means the Ophidian really comes from this continent."

Marcus said in surprise.

Lemuria was already the second continent he had encountered beyond the Ice Wall.

It seems, just as that remnant soul said, there are many continents in the world beyond the Ice Wall, each possibly much larger than those within the Ice Wall.

There lies the truly vast world, filled with countless unexpected wonders.

"Since their Spatial Rings contain this map, we can confirm that they come from this continent."

Gertie took a deep breath and said.

Next, Marcus continued searching and, besides some cultivation resources, he also found a manual in the old man's Spatial Ring.

The manual recorded many events the old man had experienced, including the reason for coming to the world within the Ice Wall this time.

After some carefully reading, Marcus discovered that they came to this world under the assignment of someone named Roderick Clifford.

This person should be the master they mentioned.
