Chapter 869 Inauguration Ceremony-1

Joel held his wine glass with poise, exchanging pleasantries with members of Acoton's elite social circle and exuding an air of triumphant confidence.

For years, he had schemed to secure his position as the family patriarch, and today, his dreams were finally within reach!

From this day forward, McHale Pharmaceuticals Group would truly belong to him, and no one in the family would dare to defy him again!

He would reign supreme over the family, his word becoming absolute law!

As for that girl Cecilia...

How dare anyone compare her to him?

She was nothing more than a mere slip of a girl in her early twenties!

He had built many of the family's enterprises from the ground up while Cecilia lived off the profits her father had left behind!

Joel felt no remorse about kidnapping his father, as he was determined to fulfill his lifelong ambition.

Like sharks smelling blood in the water, everyone quickly joined the winning side.

After Joel gained control of the majority stake in McHale Pharmaceuticals Group, numerous family members began shifting their allegiance to his side.

Without a doubt, Joel's position as the new head of the family was now set in stone.

No one could stand in his way now.

The attention now turned to how Joel would deal with Cecilia and Rhett.

Joel wasn't standing alone. He had two companions, a man and a woman of contrasting ages, by his side.

The man cut an imposing figure in his tailored suit and glasses, appearing in his early fifties.

His name was Tobias Perez, the current CEO of McHale Pharmaceuticals Group and one of Joel's most loyal supporters.

Next to Tobias stood a tall, attractive young woman with golden hair and bright blue eyes.

This young lady was none other than Luna, whom Marcus had previously encountered at Solomia, and surprisingly, Albert was also present behind her.

Their faces beamed with confidence and satisfaction.

They couldn't help but sneer when their eyes landed on Rhett, who occupied the main seat.

"Ms. Cecilia McHale has arrived!"

A voice suddenly echoed through the banquet hall.

All eyes in the hall turned toward the entrance.

Everyone watched Cecilia and Marcus walk in, followed by several men in black suits.

The pair appeared perfectly composed in formal attire and seemed to move freely.

However, keen observers would notice the cold gun barrels concealed in the men's sleeves in black.

All pointed directly at Cecilia and Marcus!

Luna gazed at Cecilia with a complex expression that quickly turned resolute.

The two girls had grown up together, and Tobias was the CEO of McHale Pharmaceuticals Group.

Although Luna's family wasn't part of the McHales, they had made remarkable contributions to McHale Pharmaceuticals Group.

By all accounts, Luna should have commanded the same respect and status as anyone else in their circle.

Yet in Cecilia's presence, Luna had always felt like a servant, her self-confidence stripped away since childhood.

The incident at Solomia was the last straw when Cecilia slapped her across the face in public!

That slap had become Luna's wake-up call.

From that moment on, Luna realized that no matter how hard she tried, Cecilia would always treat her like a servant.

From that moment on, Luna finally understood that no matter how hard she tried, she would always remain nothing more than a servant in Cecilia's eyes.

What was the point of being the CEO's daughter if it meant nothing?

This realization had planted a seed of hatred deep within Luna's heart.

After returning to Acoton, Luna recounted her entire encounter with her father, Tobias.

She naturally embellished the story with extra details to make it more dramatic.
