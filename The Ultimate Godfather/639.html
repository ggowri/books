Chapter 638 Millet's Death

Millet was sent flying back like a cannonball until his body crashed into a rock on the seaside.

The rock shattered into fragments, scattering across the ground.

His bones were shattered, and his life was taken.

Ada watched in shock and anger.

She was devastated to witness the death of their leader.

Though Millet appeared to be a teenager, he was much older than her.

They had both lived through a cycle of samsara.

At one point, they had even harbored feelings for each other. However, due to their respective duties, they had never expressed their emotions.

According to the rules of Celestial Arc Order, both the leader and the Holy Maiden were forbidden from engaging in any romantic relationships.

They had both adhered to these rules.

But now, seeing the man she had once admired being slain so ruthlessly, she could no longer hold back her emotions.

Tears streamed down her face, and her body trembled uncontrollably.

Clenching her fists, she shouted at Marcus, "Why did you kill him?"

Marcus sneered, "He brought this upon himself. Didn't you see it?"

Ada was silenced by his retort.

It was true.

Marcus had spared Millet's life.

Millet had attacked Marcus again and ended up dead.

He brought it on himself.

"If you wish to avenge him, I'm ready whenever you are," Marcus said, turning to his car. He glanced at Ada one last time and drove off toward Bayside Villa.

Ada watched him leave with mixed feelings.

She didn't know whether she should hate or thank him.

Marcus had killed many of her fellow disciples and taken her chastity, but she didn't know if she hated him.

It didn't seem as intense as she thought.

Given his strength, he could have easily killed her, yet he had spared her life twice.

He had let her go and spared Millet's life for her sake.

A bad person wouldn't have done those things.

Ada was confused. For a time, she was shrouded in complex emotions and left in a state of disorientation.

With Millet gone, the Celestial Arc Order would inevitably decline.

But she would not allow that to happen.

Unknown to her thoughts, Marcus arrived at Bayside Villa.

He instructed Diana to acquire Bayside Petroleum Company in the name of Death.

After the deaths of Lonnie and his wife, Bayside Petroleum Company had fallen into chaos with stock prices plummeting. It was the perfect opportunity to buy in.

Under Diana's operation, Marcus successfully acquired all of Bayside Petroleum Company's shares for 150 billion dollars, becoming the real owner of the company.

This move once again caused a huge stir in the business world.

Death had been inactive for two months.

The last time he acted, he acquired four emerging industry groups in Kingston at 350 billion dollars.

In less than two months, the market value of those four groups had skyrocketed, approaching a trillion dollars.

This time, it was Bayside Petroleum Company.

With the company in disarray and stock prices dropping, Marcus seized the opportunity to acquire it entirely.

His precision and speed in executing these acquisitions were as sharp as a surgeon's scalpel.

It quickly became the talk of the town.

Marcus' reason for acquiring Bayside Petroleum Company was simple.

To truly grow and build his power, sectors like transportation and energy were critical.

He had already set up his logistics company through Darian, gradually expanding his influence in transportation.

Now, by acquiring Bayside Petroleum Company, he had resolved the issue of energy supply.

Though Marcus had not yet fully engaged in this area, he knew that one day, he would build his superpower.

For now, acquiring a petroleum company as a backup was a wise decision.

At Bayside Villa, Marcus immersed himself in his cultivation.

He only emerged the next morning.

Meanwhile, in Golden Group's Pharmaceutical Industrial Park, a different scene was unfolding.

In a luxurious conference room, Yvonne and other senior members of Golden Group sat at one side of the table.

Their counterparts were people from the Weston Land.

The person leading them was none other than Luna, whom Marcus had met the day before.

And surprisingly, Albert was there as well.

"McHale Pharmaceutical Group is offering this price as a courtesy to you. Otherwise, given our position in the global pharmaceutical industry, a simple sanction would be enough to bankrupt your new company," Luna said arrogantly to Yvonne.

Yvonne was not happy to hear that.
