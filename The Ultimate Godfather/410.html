Chapter 409 Heavenly Punishment Blood Curse

Meanwhile, the Curse Weavers who had been gnawing on human limbs froze mid-bite, their red eyes narrowing as they fixed their gaze on Marcus.

They watched him like predators eyeing their next meal.

One of the Curse Weavers licked his lips, a wicked grin spreading across his face. "This Uswadian's energy is brimming with power. A taste of him would definitely give us a major boost!"

The others chuckled darkly, their eyes flashing with hunger.

"Yeah, you're right. This kid's got a pure, sun-like body. His flesh alone would be a feast, and with his cultivation techniques, if we split him up... we'll definitely level up!

"Stay here. I'll take care of him!"

The High Pries' eyes glinted with greedy desire as he looked at Marcus.

As he spoke, he let out a powerful, dark aura.

Curse Weavers were akin to wizards or warriors, their power defined by mastery over dark magic.

The High Priest, Eden Child, however, was a Peak Fifth-stage Curse Weaver—his power was at least half a tier above Marcus.

As he spoke, a surge of curses erupted from him.

A blood-red light flickered in front of him, and with a snap of his fingers, a massive, glowing handprint shot straight toward Marcus.

The handprint carried an overwhelming wave of malicious energy, as if it were a deadly totem coming to life right before Marcus' eyes.

"Blood Curse!"

Marcus' mind raced as he immediately understood what the High Priest was doing. He was trying to plant a blood curse inside him! This was pure evil.

Without hesitation, Marcus activated Shadow Step, narrowly dodging the deadly strike.

The curse split into three, and one of the smaller fragments slammed into Marcus' body.

He instantly felt a rush of corrupting energy spreading inside him.

The blood curse dug deeper into his body, making his whole being tremble, as though it was draining his very life force.

The curse started feeding on his blood and energy, and within an instant, Marcus felt himself losing consciousness.

"You've been cursed by our High Priest! You're done for! Hahaha!"

One of the Curse Weavers laughed manically, gloating over his victory.

"Snap!"

But before he could finish, Marcus' scimitar flashed, and he slashed the Curse Weaver's throat in one clean stroke.

The Curse Weaver's face twisted in horror as he clutched his neck, but blood poured out uncontrollably.

Despite being cursed, Marcus' blade was swift and deadly.

The Curse Weaver tried to say something, but no words came. His body collapsed to the ground, lifeless.

"Kid, you actually dare to kill one of my men in front of me? You've really signed your death warrant!"

Eden's voice was seething with venom.

Without another word, he raised his hand, and it transformed into a monstrous claw.

The claw tore through the air, breaking through everything in its path as it aimed to trap Marcus completely.

"You're cursed, you're as good as dead. Stop struggling, it's pointless!"

Eden's face twisted into an expression of smug superiority.

As he swung the claw, he began chanting a dark incantation.

The blood curse inside Marcus began to surge with renewed power, draining more of his blood and energy.

As Marcus' life force drained away, Eden's aura grew stronger.

It was his way of feeding off his curse's effects.

Seeing the mangled limbs and the tortured souls locked in cages around him, Marcus' anger flared.

He now understood how these Curse Weavers cultivated their power, and disgust bubbled up inside him.

Despite the curse's relentless toll, Marcus refused to give in.

With a fierce roar, he activated the Timeless Numinous Energy, pushing his body to its absolute limit.

He launched a Thunderfire Punch, crashing it into Eden's claw.

The clash sounded like a thunderous explosion, shaking the very ground beneath them.

Eden, using his advantage with the blood curse, managed to push Marcus back, forcing him to stagger.

"Still not dead?!"

Eden's eyes gleamed with madness as more cursed attacks shot toward Marcus, each one dripping with evil intent.

He wasn't about to let Marcus catch his breath.

Marcus was pushed back again and again, but he refused to give up.

Drawing on all his strength, he unleashed his Timeless Numinous Energy and magic, striking back fiercely.

Even under the weight of the curse, Marcus kept fighting, refusing to submit.

Eden's brows furrowed in surprise, clearly not expecting Marcus to be so resilient.

Meanwhile, the other Curse Weavers didn't sit idly by. They launched their own attacks to back up Eden, surrounding Marcus with a relentless barrage.

But Marcus, in the midst of counterattacking Eden, managed to cut down another Curse Weaver!

"Looks like I'm going to have to pull out all the stops to finish you off!"

Eden's blood-red eyes flashed with malice.

"Heavenly Punishment Blood Curse!"

Almost as soon as the words left his lips, a terrifying new blood curse totem materialized before him, dark and ominous.
