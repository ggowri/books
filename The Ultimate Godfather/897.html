Cody spoke with an air of arrogance, appearing determined to follow through with this fight no matter what.

"Cody, I strongly advise you not to push your luck! Marcus has defeated an Eldorian cyborg," Maxine warned.

"Even if this guy did catch onto Josiah and Abel's situation, he shouldn't be running his mouth like this! Talk about having a massive ego!"

He was scared of losing, plain and simple!
