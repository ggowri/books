"I've heard about a hidden port of smuggling in Solariae. Not only do they smuggle guns there, but they even sell heavy weapons like grenades and mortars," Maxine said.

"Alright. Sure enough, the police know more."

Marcus nodded with a smile.

What he said sounded like a mockery anyway, and Maxine immediately rolled her eyes.

Marcus hung up the phone and was going to go buy some guns at the so-called port in Solariae in the next few days.

He had just hung up his phone when it rang again.

It was Richard calling.

"Marcus, your uncle came to Solomia to see a doctor. Can you come over and help him?"

Upon hearing this, a bearded and sloppy middle-aged man came to Marcus' mind.

This man was his uncle, Nick Fitzgerald.

Nick was also Richard's biological younger brother.

Since Nick was living in a different state, Marcus had only met him a few times.

Marcus hadn't seen Nick since he grew up.

He didn't know why Nick suddenly came to Solomia to see a doctor.

Yet Richard had already told Marcus so, and he had no reason to refuse.

Marcus drove straight to the Cedars Villas.

As Marcus walked into the villa, there was another middle-aged couple and a girl in her twenties in the house, in addition to Richard and Melinda.

The middle-aged couple was none other than Nick and his wife, Aria Fitzgerald.

The young woman was Marcus' cousin, Lisa Fitzgerald.

Marcus and Lisa only met twice when they were young, and now they hadn't seen each other for almost ten years.

Even though Lisa looked the same as before, she was dressed more fashionably. With her delicate and heavy makeup, she looked like a fashion model.

Based on Lisa's outfit, she was probably in the middle class as well.

Nick, on the other hand, got even older, his hair turned gray, and there were more wrinkles on his face.

Aria still looked simple, as all honest people in the rural areas did.

"Marcus, come and greet Nick, Aria and Lisa!"

Seeing Marcus come in, Nick immediately stood up.

Marcus greeted Nick, Aria, and Lisa respectively.

Nick stood up and held Marcus' hand. "Marcus and I haven't seen each other for over ten years, and he's grown up. Even though he's not a descendant of the Fitzgerald family, he was so nice to both of you.

"Look at the grand villa Marcus gave you. It's so luxurious!"

Aria had checked every room in the house since she entered, and she couldn't stop praising it.

It was much more luxurious than the two-story house they had in their hometown. When Lisa and her boyfriend got married, they would move to Solomia as well.

Maybe they'd live in a better place than Richard's house by then.

"Dad, don't you know Marcus lives at his wife's house? He married into a rich and powerful family in Solomia, so this villa was definitely bought by his wife," Lisa reminded in a low voice.

While they had been living in another state, the economy there was not as strong as in Solomia.

Besides, Lisa had studied in a secondary technical school in Solomia, so she worked in Solomia all along.

Lisa had rented a stall in a clothing wholesale market in Solomia and started a business, and she could earn two to three hundred thousand dollars a year.

Lisa's boyfriend was the deputy manager of the clothing wholesale market, and he came from a middle-class family.

Therefore, Lisa was considered pretty successful in the eyes of the ordinary people.

She had got engaged to her boyfriend, and they had bought an apartment of about two thousand square feet as a matrimonial home in one of the luxury communities in Solomia.

The apartment was paid off by both of them together.

This made Lisa feel quite proud of herself.

However, seeing Richard and his family living in a well-known villa in Solomia, Lisa was a bit jealous.

Later, Lisa heard that Marcus had married into one of Solomia's rich and powerful families, which was why she realized who had bought the villa.

"Oh, I have forgot that Marcus lives at his wife's house, but anyway, Marcus is quite capable."

Nick nodded. Regarding the matter of Marcus getting married, Nick despised Richard in his mind.

He called Richard several times to stop him, but Richard kept refusing him by respecting Marcus' choice.

This made Nick have a problem with Richard.

In Nick's opinion, even if the Fitzgerald family were poor, they wouldn't live in their wives' houses, and it would be a disgrace for their family.

"Nick, Lisa, Marcus got divorced a long time ago, and he bought this villa with his own money," Melinda reminded in a hurry.
