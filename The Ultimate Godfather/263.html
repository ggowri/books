Marcus and Cooke kept watching the gambling scene at Grand Fortune for a while.

Marcus found that, apart from some onlookers, most gamblers, unless they had certain professional knowledge, usually hired an experienced master.

These masters were mainly responsible for selecting raw materials for the players.

With the help of these masters, they have a higher chance to pick a good one.

However, the fees for hiring experienced masters were indeed quite high.

Hiring the top masters in the Southern Market for a day could cost over ten billion.

Even Marcus thought such pricing was outrageous.

In the ordinary world, one could buy a listed company with that money.

Yet in the Southern Market, exclusive to warriors, it could only hire these top gambling masters for a day.

Even at such high prices, these masters still considered the identity of the hirer before deciding whether to take the job.

There were only a few such top masters in the entire Southern Market, and they were always in high demand.

Marcus and Cooke had been there for a short while when someone opened a raw material that could shock the entire Southern Market.

The crystal produced from this stone was not only massive but also of very high grade.

Just this single crystal was worth over 50 billion.

After deducting the 10 billion for hiring the master, the person netted 40 billion in just half an hour.

This made many people present envious.

Such incidents happened frequently in the Southern Market, which was the real reason why these gambling masters were in such high demand.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, do you want to try your luck? But gambling on stones is risky. We can start with some cheaper materials to test our luck.

"Or we can hire a gambling master. That way, we'll have a better chance," Cooke said to Marcus.

"Let's wait a bit longer." Marcus wasn't in a hurry.

At that moment, the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire in his body quietly activated, and his X-ray vision opened, continuously searching for good materials.

Under the exploration of X-ray vision, all the stones on the site became transparent, and Marcus could easily see the internal structure of the stones.

Even the gambling masters couldn't compete with his ability.

"Look, that's Truman Dupont from the Astral Serpent Order. He's actually here at Grand Fortune, and he's hired Giles Hansen. It seems he's going to gamble big today."

At that moment, a shout came from the crowd.

Many people turned to look towards the entrance of Grand Fortune, where a young man in a white suit and with an extraordinary demeanor walked in first.

Behind him were several attendants, and next to him was an old man with white hair and beard.

The young man was Truman Dupont, the son of the grand elder of the Astral Serpent Order.

As one of the three major martial arts sects in Uswad, the Astral Serpent Order held a supreme position in the martial arts world.

The grand elder of the Astral Serpent Order was the most influential person after the sect leader.

His son, Truman, was highly regarded. Wherever Truman went, he was the focus of everyone's attention.

Truman had just turned 25 this year, and his cultivation had reached the peak of Ancient Martial Stage Three, just one step away from Stage Four.

His talent was among the best in the younger generation.

The Giles he hired was also the most famous gambling master in the Southern Market.

With Giles' professional selection, the success rate of gambling could reach over 80%.

Therefore, many people were willing to spend a lot of money to hire Giles, though ordinary people had no chance to even meet someone like Giles.

Seeing Truman arrive, the manager of Grand Fortune personally came forward to greet him.

Truman was in high spirits as he said to the crowd, "Everyone, please ignore me. Enjoy yourselves. I'm just here to have some fun today."

At this time, Camila and Jennifer also arrived at Grand Fortune.

"Camy, Mr. Dupont is over there. Let's go and take a look," Jennifer said with excitement.

They had heard that Truman was at Grand Fortune and had followed him here to catch a glimpse of this young prodigy.

Marcus also noticed Truman, but he didn't pay much attention.

He was currently selecting raw materials in the largest stone area.

Truman and Giles also came to this area.

Since Marcus was selecting, they waited nearby.

Soon, Marcus locked onto a raw material that was uneven and bumpy, about the size of a watermelon, and looked very unremarkable.

According to its appearance, this raw material was one of the few with extremely poor quality in the entire venue.

Marcus skipped over the raw materials that looked good and pointed to the one he had chosen, asking, "How much is this stone?"

Everyone looked puzzled and stared at Marcus as if looking at a fool.

Camila and Jennifer, who followed along, were full of ridicule.
