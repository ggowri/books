Chapter 382 Battle God Oliver

This bloody scene made everyone feel fear, and some women even gasped.

They thought those people from the underground world were indeed ruthless, severing their subordinates' ligaments of hands and legs in public.

They thought no wonder these subordinates were so afraid of the gang's head.

And it was all because of this young man in front of them.

It was hard to imagine how respected his status was.

After doing all this, Tony again smiled flatteringly at Marcus and said, "Mr. Fitzgerald, it was my poor management. When I get back to Solariae, I'll definitely clean up the Black Tiger Gang and won't let this riff-raff hold important positions anymore!"

Marcus waved his hand to Tony and said, "If you have other things to do, go ahead. I'll handle things here myself."

Tony's eyes flickered as he glanced at Randy not far away.

He immediately understood Marcus' meaning. Marcus' influence was mainly in Solariae, and Randy was the mayor's son in Solariae.

Marcus was doing this to protect their Black Tiger Gang, which made Tony feel some gratitude towards Marcus.

After bowing deeply once more, he dragged Nicholas, whose ligaments in his hands and feet had been severed, away from the scene.

Randy and his group trembled with fear. Despite their prestigious statuses as billion-dollar heirs, witnessing the severing of tendons was something they had only seen in movies and TV shows.

Seeing it with their own eyes was far more shocking than any on-screen depiction.

"Tell me, you blocked me and smashed my car. How are you going to settle this?"

Marcus asked Randy.

Randy's face darkened. Without Nicholas and the others to back him up, he felt uneasy.

It looked like he had no choice but to call his cousin for help!

Gritting his teeth, Randy snarled at Marcus. "Don't get cocky. So what if I smashed your car? Just wait. I'll call my cousin right now, and he'll come take care of you!

"I'll show you what Battle God Oliver is like. Just don't come crawling to me on your knees!"

Marcus chuckled coldly, "Oh, go ahead and call him. I'm really curious to see what Battle God Oliver is all about."

To everyone's surprise, Marcus sat down on a public bench by the roadside, lit a cigarette, and crossed his legs, waiting patiently.

"Battle God Oliver? This guy is actually his cousin. Looks like he's part of the Deleon family too!"

"Damn, what a legend. The Deleon family is known as the top family in Uswad. I never expected this young man to be part of the Deleon family. This is going to be interesting!"

"Yeah, I thought it was impressive that he brought in members of the Black Tiger Gang, but I didn't realize he was involved in Battle God Oliver!"

"Even though this guy is someone Tony respects, compared to Battle God Oliver, he's nothing!"

"Comparing him to Battle God Oliver is just an insult to the Battle God himself!"

Soon, various opinions filled the area.

More and more people stopped to watch.

Word spread that Battle God Oliver was about to arrive, and some people even called their friends to come see the spectacle.

After some back-and-forth, Randy quickly hung up the phone.

"If you have the guts, don't run. My cousin is on his way. You're just hanging out with a few gangsters at best. When my cousin arrives, I'll make sure he beats you so badly your own mother won't recognize you!"

Randy glared at Marcus, his eyes full of hatred.

Marcus ignored him.

As he had suspected earlier, Randy and Oliver were both members of the Deleon family, yet there was such a huge difference between them.

One was a rebellious and arrogant heir, while the other was a revered Battle God. The contrast was stark.

"Randy, I bet this guy is really nervous, but he's still pretending to be calm. Once your cousin arrives, he'll probably be scared out of his mind..."

*****

Within ten minutes, sirens sounded from the other end of the street.

Several SWAT vehicles sped towards them and quickly parked in front of Marcus and his group.

"They're here. Battle God Oliver has really arrived. It looks like this young man is truly a member of the Deleon family. I want to see how he handles this."

"What else can he do? Kneel and beg for mercy!"

Many people discussed this loudly.

The doors of the police cars opened, and Battle God Oliver, along with a dozen other SWAT officers, jumped out.

Marcus recognized them from previous operations, especially those involved in the capture of Abel and Josiah.

Now, they were all here with Battle God Oliver.

Oliver had one arm wrapped in bandages, but his presence was still powerful.

As soon as he arrived, countless people around them gasped in awe.

"This is Uswad's Battle God. As expected, he's truly impressive!"
