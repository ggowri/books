As the barrier continued to weaken, more and more outsiders would pour in.

And this was just the beginning.

If that was true, then in the long run, this world might face much killing and turmoil.

"Looks like you really have a death wish!"

The elder's tone suddenly turned icy.

As they spoke, their extremely eerie aura was giving off a deeply sinister feeling.

Several people quickly surrounded Marcus in the center. A tall, thin man with a sallow complexion wore a sinister smile, his gaze at Marcus revealing a hint of greed.

He said, "His body is so strong. I want to capture him, torture him to death bit by bit, and then drain all the energy from his body for my use!"

A strange smile also appeared on the faces of the others.

The old man said, "You've always liked men, and this kid's physique does look good. He's all yours!"

As he spoke, the smile on the sallow-faced man's face instantly disappeared.

He reached out a large hand and fiercely grabbed toward Marcus, just like catching a chick!

"Courting death!"

Marcus let out a cold laugh and casually threw a punch.

A blinding Fist Ray descended.

In the next moment, a terrifying rumble echoed, and an incredibly destructive force spread in all directions.

The attacks of the two collided in the blink of an eye.

The sallow-faced man initially wore an extremely smug smile.

But after sensing the terrifying destructive power within Marcus's Fist Ray, his smile vanished instantly, replaced by a look of shock.

"How is this possible? You are just a prisoner in a cage! How can be so powerful?"

However, before he could finish speaking, under the force of that destructive power, his body exploded into pieces, killed with a single punch!

This scene made everyone gasp in shock.

If Marcus's first kill was a surprise, then now, it's absolutely a display of strength!

"This prisoner's strength is extraordinary. We must not be careless. Attack together and kill him!" The elder said.

The others immediately nodded in agreement, preparing to attack Marcus together.

"Whoosh!"

However, just as the old man's words fell, a sound of something piercing through the air echoed from the void.

The next moment, a destructive streak of light descended like thunder, reaching the old man's body in the blink of an eye.

The old man's expression turned horrified as he instantly sensed the terrifying power contained within the destructive light.

He tried to dodge with all his might, but it was already too late!

The destructive light was not only incredibly fast but also contained an immensely powerful Binding Force, directly holding his body in place, unable to move an inch!

"No, I don't want to die!"

Watching the destructive light grow larger in his pupils, the elder let out a desperate cry.

He tried to use the last bit of potential within his body to break free from the binding force, but it was futile.

The next moment, everyone heard a terrifying explosion. Under the impact of the destructive light, the old man's head was blasted apart on the spot, leaving him a headless corpse!

The others gasped in shock, never expecting this young man, who looked barely in his twenties, to be so powerful.

He was completely different from the prisoners they knew in the cage. He was so terrifying.

Even the prodigies from their homeland might not be any stronger!

"Run!"

After the elder was killed by Marcus in one strike, everyone's first thought was to flee, and they immediately prepared to scatter.

But how could Marcus give them a chance?

He raised his hand, and several deadly lightning tribulations descended from the sky.

Purple lightning flickered with endless destructive light, striking down on the remaining individuals.

With several explosive sounds, their bodies were shattered by the power of the lightning, turning into clouds of blood mist that scattered with the wind!

This scene left Gertie trembling with fear in the distance.

Although she had already knew that Marcus was terrifying, it had never been as direct as this moment.

It’s worth noting that her cultivation had significantly improved compared to the last time she met Marcus.

Her personal strength was no longer what it used to be, yet when facing these people, she could only defend without the ability to fight back.

She was even about to die at the hands of these people.

But these people were no different from ants when facing Marcus.

Marcus casually waved his hand to launch an attack, and these people could hardly resist, and were squashed in an instant.

Such power was beyond words to describe.

In Gertie's eyes, Marcus was simply not human, no different from a monster.
