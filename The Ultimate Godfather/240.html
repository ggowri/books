They didn't have time to think much.

In response, they both unleashed fireball spells to counter Marcus.

A terrifying, thunderous roar filled the air as their fireballs collided, sending shockwaves that spread in all directions.

Since Marcus struck first, he gained the upper hand right away.

The two Wizards were knocked back, their bodies trembling as their internal energy churned.

Marcus didn't give them a chance to recover. Suddenly, two bolts of lightning came crashing down.

Purple lightning lit up the sky, striking with terrifying force.

The two Wizards, shocked, desperately released spells to resist, but still, the lightning hit them squarely.

Both let out muffled groans, blood trickling from their mouths.

Seeing this, the elder's smile faded, and a dangerous glint appeared in his ancient eyes. "It seems I underestimated you."

With that, the elder moved.

A simple step, and his body seemed to blur, creating countless afterimages.

The aura of an Ancient Martial Art Stage Four expert exploded in full force.

He threw a punch, and the punch's energy split into several devastating phantom strikes in the air.

Each one was powerful enough to obliterate an average Ancient Martial Art Stage Three practitioner.

"I didn't want to move so quickly, but you've forced me. You should be proud to die by my hand!"

The elder's voice was low but resonated with a shockwave powerful enough to shatter surrounding stones.

Despite the terrifying display of power, Marcus remained unfazed, not even attempting to dodge.

Having practiced the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire, his personal strength far surpassed that of his current opponents.

Facing the elder's deadly strikes, Marcus simply invoked Timeless Warrior's Grimoire.

His aura shifted dramatically, growing even more formidable.

Simultaneously, Thunderfire Power surged, coalescing around his own punch.

He struck.

A massive surge of destructive Thunderfire Power shot out.

A deafening crash echoed as their energies collided, unleashing waves of destruction in every direction.

The two Wizards were caught in the heart of the storm, their bodies being pushed back again and again as their chests heaved.

The elder smirked, pumping even more destructive force into his punch, hoping to strike Marcus down.

However, as the collision intensified, the elder suddenly sensed something was wrong.

From Marcus' punch erupted a terrifying surge of Thunderfire Power.

The power surged up his arm, burning with incredible force.

The elder screamed in agony as he tried to extinguish the Thunderfire Power, but it was futile.

The destructive force was overwhelming.

A burning smell filled the air as his arm turned to ash.

He staggered back, crying out in disbelief, "How is this possible? You were supposed to be at Ancient Martial Art Stage Three! How could you be a Stage Four expert? And you're mixing magic with martial arts? What is going on?"

Marcus smirked coldly, refusing to answer.

With a single step, he moved forward, striking with a terrifying palm aimed at the elder.

However, just as he neared, he felt a cold light flash like a lightning bolt.

Marcus instinctively dodged, but still felt a sharp pain in his arm.

If he hadn't reacted in time, he would have lost his arm.

Frowning, he saw the elder had drawn a golden curved blade, still stained with blood.

The whole area seemed to be shrouded in a deadly aura emanating from the blade.

"Kid, your life is mine!"

The elder's eyes flashed with murderous intent as he swung the blade toward Marcus.

This strike was more powerful than anything before.

At the same time, the two Wizards moved as well, channeling their most powerful magic to assault Marcus.

Together, their combined assault was overwhelming.

Marcus' eyes gleamed with determination. He wasn't about to waste any more time. Instead of retreating, he charged directly at the elder's blade.

The elder sneered, thinking Marcus was seeking death.

But in that instant, as the blade was about to strike, Marcus unleashed Celestial Reversal!

In the blink of an eye, the deadly blade was deflected by the Celestial Reversal, flying straight back toward the elder.
