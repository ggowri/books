To ensure his loved ones could protect themselves.

Marcus decided to pass down the ancient Magic Codex to them.

He first approached Charles and Yvonne's parents, then Michelle, Catherine, Jane, and Natalia.

He shared the Magic Codex with each of them, teaching them how to harness their powers.

These people had been ordinary civilians before, completely unfamiliar with what it meant to be a Wizard.

Yet they all showed keen interest and cooperated fully with Marcus, promising to master the Magic Codex within two weeks.

Next, Marcus visited his adoptive parents and sister, Nicole, sharing the Magic Codex with them in the same way.

Being older, Medeline and Richard weren't as quick to grasp new concepts as the younger folks. At first, they were skeptical about the very existence of witchcraft.

Only after Marcus demonstrated his powers did they fully believe and agree to start training.

He gave them a more generous timeframe of one month.

With everything arranged, he finally headed back to Bayside Villa, exhaustion weighing heavy on his shoulders.

However, just as his car turned onto Bayside Avenue, two figures suddenly blocked his path.

A man and a woman stood before him, both radiating immense power. They were the peak of Ancient Martial Stage Two.

Their auras clashed, one ice-cold, one blazing hot. When combined, these opposing forces created a devastating power far greater than the sum of its parts!

Without wasting words, the duo launched their attack. Their combined ice and fire powers transformed into a destructive force, crashing down on the Alphard.

A loud explosion rocked the air as the Alphard flipped violently before erupting into flames!

But Marcus was ready. He'd already leaped clear before the attack could connect.

The pair's cold eyes narrowed. They hadn't expected Marcus to be so quick. They immediately launched another fierce assault in his direction!

"Kid, you messed with the Kingston Browns. Time to die!"

The man's voice dripped with menace, his sunken eyes giving him a nightmarish appearance.

His flame attacks were potent, merging with the woman's ice aura to create an overwhelming force that bore down on Marcus like an avalanche.

Instead of meeting it head-on, Marcus dodged with a swift movement, barely avoiding the dual assault.

In one fluid motion, he stepped forward and unleashed three rapid punches at the woman!

Her eyes widened in shock.

She realized Marcus had targeted all her weak points with frightening precision.

The fight had barely begun when Marcus instantly spotted her weakness. This young man was clearly more formidable than expected.

Little did she know that Marcus possessed X-ray vision, allowing him to instantly identify vital weak points in his opponents with just a glance.

Those three strikes had left the woman reeling, barely managing to deflect them, even with her male partner's support.

"You've got some nerve, kid, taking on both of us like this," she snarled.

A murderous intent flashed in her eyes as she unleashed a devastating palm strike at Marcus.

Marcus stood his ground, meeting her attack head-on with a thunderous punch.

The collision of the fist and palm created an explosive impact.

The woman's face radiated confidence and arrogance.

She'd foolishly assumed her strike alone would be enough to match Marcus.

But she hadn't counted on Marcus's punch not only packing tremendous power but also erupting with terrifying flames in its wake.

The flames engulfed her like a tidal wave, consuming her entire body in an instant.

Horror swept across her face as searing pain shot through her body.

Realizing her dire situation, she desperately summoned her strongest ice powers to extinguish the flames.

But she was already a mess, her skin badly scorched and blistered.

"You're dead!" the man roared.

Enraged, he launched his fiery assault on Marcus.

Marcus remained unfazed, unleashing another devastating punch to meet his opponent's attack head-on.

Through his grueling training regime, Marcus remained at the peak of Ancient Martial Stage Two, but his combat prowess had reached new heights entirely.

His single punch sent his opponent staggering backward, and even the man's prized flame abilities proved useless against Marcus's overwhelming strength.

The man's face darkened as he attempted another attack on Marcus.

But at that precise moment, a terrifying bolt of lightning struck from above without warning!

The lightning, packing over ten thousand volts, possessed devastating power and struck with unimaginable speed.

Caught completely off guard, the man had no time to react before the lightning blast hit him head-on!

With a loud crack, the man's body convulsed violently. His eyes widened in shock as he stammered, "But... but you're supposed to be just a warrior... how can you use witchcraft...?"

Those were his final words before he collapsed, struck dead by Marcus's Lightning spell!
