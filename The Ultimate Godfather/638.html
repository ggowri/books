Millet was terrified, and his gaze changed completely.

He never imagined that the young man before him to be so powerful.

The strike he had just launched was something that even others of the same realm would find difficult to withstand, yet Marcus took it without batting an eyelid.

The strike left him severely injured, yet Marcus remained unscathed.

The disparity in their power was plain to see.

Millet finally understood why Ada had desperately tried to stop him from facing off against Marcus.

"How can you be so powerful?" Millet muttered in disbelief.

Just moments ago, he had considered the possibility that Marcus was someone who had lived through a cycle of samsara.

But after examining him closely, he realized that Marcus was only in his early twenties.

Marcus was not like them. He had lived through a cycle of samsara.

To possess such power at such a young age was hard to imagine.

"You don't need to know," Marcus sneered, activating Shadow Step.

In the blink of an eye, he appeared right in front of Millet.

Millet panicked, trying to dodge.

However, it was too late.

Marcus' hand gripped his throat. Millet struggled to break free, but it was hopeless.

He was the leader of Celestial Arc Order and had reached the minor completion stage of Ancient Martial Stage Seven.

He was respected in the world of Ancient Martial Art.

Yet now, he was being held by a young man like a helpless chick.

The humiliation he felt was overwhelming.

"With your meager strength, how dare you provoke me?" Marcus scoffed.

In the world of Ancient Martial Art, the higher the realm, the slower the rate of advancement, and the greater the gap between the two realms.

He was only one realm below Marcus, but the difference in their strength was enormous.

To Marcus, Millet was no different from an ant. He could easily kill Millet with a casual movement.

"Please, spare him." A melodious voice suddenly echoed from the void.

Both Millet and Marcus looked over. Ada stepped out from a dense forest.

She wore a white dress, and her long hair flowed freely in the wind. Her light makeup enhanced her ethereal beauty.

She looked like an angel who had fallen from the heavens.

Even after seeing her again, Marcus was still amazed by her beauty.

No wonder she had been chosen as the Holy Maiden of Celestial Arc Order.

Her talent, strength, and appearance were all at the pinnacle.

Millet was surprised, but his anger grew even stronger.

He had sought vengeance for his sect, fighting a bitter battle with the enemy.

But after being defeated, he now had to rely on Ada to plead for his life. This was a humiliation beyond measure.

"Why should I listen to you?" Marcus sneered.

Ada bit her lip, seemingly making a difficult decision. "Because I'm your woman. Is that enough?"

Millet was shocked. "What? You and he..."

He suddenly realized what had happened, and the humiliation in his heart deepened.

"If you want to live, then stop talking," Ada shouted at him.

In the face of overwhelming power, if people wished to survive, they had to abandon certain things like their pride.

Ada had already been violated by Marcus and had no pride left.

She had only been concealing her humiliation to maintain a semblance of face.

"If that's the case, I'll spare his life," Marcus said, loosening his grip and tossing Millet aside.

The sense of humiliation had reached a breaking point.

The moment Marcus let go of him, Millet suddenly gathered his strength.

Then, he attacked Marcus with all his power.

"Go die!" he shouted.

Clearly, he could not bear the humiliation anymore. The death of Colby and the blasphemy against Ada was too much.

Even if it meant death, he was determined to take Marcus down with him.

However, Marcus immediately sensed the attack and threw a punch at Millet.

The force was overwhelming.

"No!" Ada cried out in alarm. But it was too late.

Marcus' fist collided with Millet's palm.

With a thunderous explosion, Millet was repelled.
