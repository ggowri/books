Marcus said calmly, "Does a department head not need the director's approval to fire a doctor?"

Jayden immediately broke out in a cold sweat at those words.

Even though he was a highly paid expert hired by Solomia Hospital, he didn't have that kind of authority.

He had acted this way earlier because he wanted to dismiss Jade without Carter's knowledge.

Carter asked, "Doctors are the backbone of this hospital. Who dares to dismiss one without approval?"

Marcus raised his hand and pointed at Jayden. "It's this highly paid expert your hospital hired. He was just boasting about firing my friend!"

Jayden's face turned ashen as he hurriedly tried to explain, "Mr. Lake, don't listen to his nonsense! I didn't do that!"

However, before he could finish, Marcus presented the dismissal letter Jayden had given to Jade earlier.

Carter's expression darkened immediately as he quickly pieced everything together. "Jayden, you've only just started working here, and you already dared to dismiss a doctor without authorization? Do you think you're above the law?"

Jayden, visibly flustered, tried to explain. "Mr. Lake, it's all a misunderstanding. I thought she was being insubordinate and disrespectful, and I lost my temper. That's the only reason I wanted to fire her!"

"Insubordinate? You mean she didn't follow the rules you made up yourself?" Marcus sneered.

Marcus laughed coldly and said to Carter, "Jade just successfully performed a major surgery on a patient with severely fractured legs from a car accident. Once the patient recovers, they won't just walk again; they'll be able to run and jump too.

"Doctors of her caliber are highly sought after by hospitals worldwide, yet this man almost got her fired just now. Isn't that a huge loss for the hospital?"

Hearing this, Carter grew even angrier. He glared at Jayden and demanded, "Jayden, explain yourself!"

Jayden, pale-faced, argued, "Mr. Lake, don't just listen to his side of the story. He's deliberately trying to undermine me!"

Carter chuckled coldly. "Jayden, do you really think someone with Marcus' medical expertise needs to scheme to take you down?

"Let me tell you the truth. The person who cured that late-stage cancer patient in our hospital recently was Marcus. A master healer like him is not someone you can belittle so easily.

"Besides, the surgery that Jade performed is something you probably couldn't even handle.

"So as far as I'm concerned, you're no longer fit to serve as the head of surgery!

"I've decided that Dr. Kelly will take over as the head of surgery!"

Upon hearing this, Jayden felt as if the sky had fallen. His head spun as the weight of the situation hit him.

Despite his prominence in the field of surgery, being dismissed just a month into his tenure would destroy his reputation and severely hinder his chances of finding work at another hospital.

At the same time, Jayden, Freya, and the surrounding doctors and nurses were all deeply shocked.

None of them had ever imagined that the young man before them was the very master healer who had recently cured a late-stage cancer patient!

Although Jayden had only been working there for a month, he had heard of the master healer's reputation.

He never thought the healer would be standing right before him.

Thinking back to the mocking remarks he had made earlier, he felt his face burn with embarrassment.

He also finally understood why Jade's surgery had been so successful.

It must have been because of this healer's guidance!

Jade's eyes sparkled, and a look of excitement appeared on her face. She couldn't believe it—even in her wildest dreams—that she had been directly promoted to department head.

Before Marcus arrived, she had been constantly harassed and sidelined by Jayden and Freya.

She had almost been forced out of the department!

But ever since Marcus showed up, everything had taken a dramatic turn.

Not only had she successfully completed a nearly impossible surgery, but she had also been promoted to department head.

This position was now equal to her father's rank!

For Jade, this felt utterly surreal, like she was dreaming.

"Mr. Lake, please give me another chance! I won't dare to do this again!" Jayden pleaded desperately.

Jayden begged Carter, doing everything he could to salvage what he had lost.

Carter waved him off. "There's no need to say more. Your problem isn't just about dismissing a doctor without permission. By offending Marcus, you've also offended me. Even if I wanted to keep you, the other higher-ups in the hospital wouldn't agree. Do you understand now?"

Jayden's face was filled with utter despair.

His heart was filled with regret. He shouldn't have been so dismissive of others.

He especially shouldn't have targeted Jade.

Recognizing his own narrow-mindedness, he realized he had ruined his future in medicine.

Freya's face was just as pale. She didn't dare utter a single word.

But Marcus wasn't planning to let her off the hook. He turned to her and said, "According to the bet, you should resign immediately!"
