Chapter 985 The Missing Girl Case-2

He planned to visit Serpent's Coil again the next day to distribute all the cultivation resources he no longer needed.

Additionally, Diana recently succeeded in modifying nearly two thousand Energy Crystal guns.

He decided to give 1,500 to Kingston's Axe Gang, while the remaining 500 would be distributed to people he knew.

This way, it would not only boost the overall strength of the Axe Gang but also ensure the people around him had enough self-defense capability.

Besides that, Marcus also planned to keep collecting cultivation resources, striving to strengthen everyone around him simultaneously.

Only this way could he handle his own affairs with more peace of mind.

After all, he already had many enemies. Even without this incident, too many people wanted to deal with him, including his two brothers whom he had never met.

Only by continuously enhancing his strength and the strength of those around him could they have the ability to protect themselves when a crisis arises.

"Buzz, buzz..."

At this moment, a wave of energy rippled through the void.

Marcus instantly unleashed his spiritual powers, taking in everything around him.

He discovered several figures fiercely battling in a mountain range in the northern part of Solomia.

Their cultivation was roughly equivalent to Ancient Martial Stage Nine, with several of them besieging a stunning young girl in a white gauze dress.

These people were not only powerful but also emitted an aura distinctly different from warriors.

This aura was extremely eerie, exuding a faint sense of evil, making Marcus feel deeply unfamiliar.

He recalled everyone he had encountered before, none of whom had ever emitted such an aura.

It did not belong to warriors, Wizards, or metahumans.

As for practitioners of other professions, Marcus had encountered them more or less, but their aura was fundamentally different from these people.

He continued to amplify his spiritual powers and discovered that the stunning young girl under siege was actually Ada's cousin from the Celestial Arc Order, Gertie.

After multiple Dual Cultivations with Marcus, Gertie's cultivation level had successfully advanced to Ancient Martial Stage Nine, greatly enhancing her strength.

However, under the siege of this group of enemies, she still struggled to cope. A trace of blood stained the corner of her mouth, indicating that she was clearly injured.

Seeing this, Marcus couldn't help but frown.

He stopped the car without hesitation in a parking spot by the roadside.

The parking attendant was just about to issue a ticket to collect the parking fee, but his figure had already vanished in an instant.

The attendant asked in confusion, "Where did he go?"

Marcus had already teleported to the distant mountains.

The bearded man blatantly sized up Gertie and said to the elder beside him, "The women in this cage are really something, all so beautiful. The ones we caught these past two days were already great, but I didn't expect today's catch to be even more stunning! If we take her back, the boss will definitely love her!"

The elder nodded with a smile and agreed, "This is the forgotten world. I didn't expect its allure to be no less than our continent, at least when it comes to women!"

"Indeed, over the past few days, I've already Dual Cultivated with several women. My cultivation has advanced significantly. It seems the master was absolutely right to send us here. Although I don't know what exactly is his purpose, we can certainly enjoy ourselves!" The bearded man smirked wickedly, exuding an inexplicable sense of superiority.

As he spoke, he suddenly struck a palm toward Gertie.

Surrounded and attacked by so many, Gertie was already seriously injured.

Facing the bearded man's Hand Strike, she found it hard to resist.

However, just as she was about to desperately resist, a figure silently appeared in front of her, casually striking out a palm and effortlessly blocking the bearded man's Hand Strike.

In the next moment, a thunderous boom echoed through the valley.

The bearded man's gaze tightened slightly. He hadn't expected someone to come to Gertie's rescue.

"Who are you? How dare you meddle? Since you're here, prepare to die!" he said coldly.

At the same time, he suddenly infused terrifying power into his Hand Strike, attempting to completely suppress Marcus here.

However, as their attacks clashed more intensely, his expression suddenly turned utterly horrified.

He felt an irresistible force surging wildly into his body, almost tearing him apart!
