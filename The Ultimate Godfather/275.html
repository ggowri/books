Marcus soon got accustomed to the inflation in the Southern market and also understood why money depreciated so much here.

It was very simple. Warriors persuaded superior power, and once they achieved their goal, money would naturally flow to them.

For those powerful ones, making money was a piece of cake.

However, resources to boost their power, including energy crystals, were valued more than money.

Cooke, for instance, handled the Red River Mines with abundant wealth but couldn't even get a pill to improve himself due to his weakness.

An ordinary energy crystal would be a great treasure for him.

So, gradually, Marcus no longer felt confused about the different views on money between the Southern Market and the Mundane world.

Seven days later, Marcus successfully completed the work of a raw crystal appraiser.

Vanessa also fulfilled her promise. The total turnover of the Grand Fortune this week reached 6.3 trillion dollars.

According to the forty percent share ratio, Marcus could get a total of 2.52 trillion dollars, which would be a huge sum of money both in the mundane world and the world of Ancient Martial Art!

Instead of taking the reward in cash, he asked Vanessa to exchange the money for high-quality energy crystals.

Ans Vanessa agreed without hesitation.

According to Marcus' requirements, the quality of the energy crystals couldn't be lower than that of the crystals that he used to bet on.

2.52 trillion dollars almost hollowed out her storage of superior energy crystals.

Marcus roughly estimated that the energy crystals he now owned could update at least one thousand and six hundred submachine guns.

Once it finished, he could form an army of one thousand and six hundred soldiers with advanced energy crystal submachine guns.

It would be a horrifying force!

By then, he would even compete with top domestic Ancient Martial Art organizations.

Thinking of this, Marcus felt a sense of accomplishment filled his heart.

Although the trip to southeast Uswad was full of ups and downs, he finally achieved what he wanted.

He collected enough energy crystals, mastered the Red River Mines, and also made Cooke his partner.

And he even got the knowledge of beastmasters from Cooke.

As long as he studied hard, Marcus would probably become one of them in the future.

This was definitely a good thing for him to improve his power.

As he had collected enough energy crystals, Marcus said goodbye to Vanessa and left the Southern Market with Cooke.

In fact, Vanessa was a little reluctant to let him go. After all, the turnover of the Grand Fortune doubled with Marcus' skill.

So, he promised her that he would come back to purchase more energy crystals from time to time.

Only one thousand and six hundred energy crystal submachine guns were not his ultimate goal.

The two brothers he had never met must have handled horrifying power.

If he couldn't develop himself soon enough, maybe he would be eliminated by them before inheriting the position of Godfather.

They came back to the Red River Mines.

Marcus ordered Cooke to build roads to connect the mines with the outside world better and paid for legitimate quarrymen.

Cooke followed his order and immediately hired a local construction company to build roads. Meanwhile, he also put up wanted posters for quarry men.

In that case, the Red River Mines would be able to resume the work within a month.

Although it only produced decorative crystals, the annual turnover would also surprise everyone with enhanced management.

Before leaving the mines, Marcus gave Cooke some pills and some books of Ancient Martial Art to facilitate his cultivation.

Cooke was very grateful to him.

He took the pills Marcus gave him before and broke through the Ancient Martial Stage Two a few days ago.

Something he didn't dare to dream about in the past actually came true after meeting Marcus.

In addition, Cooke became more loyal to Marcus after witnessing his deterring power and surpassing talent for stone gambling.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, can I go to the mainland with you? I want to take care of you from now on."

A stunning young lady ran to Marcus as he just got into Cooke's SUV. It was Cooke's daughter, Pearl Ford.

The pearl at the moment, big dark eyes are staring at Tang Long with anticipation.
