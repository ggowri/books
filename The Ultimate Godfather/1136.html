Chapter 1002 The New Path of Conquest-1

After all, Ryan was Marcus's biological father. As his son, watching Ryan nearing death, even though they had rarely interacted, Marcus's feelings were quite complicated.

"Marcus, don't be sad. This day was bound to come sooner or later, I just didn't expect it to come so quickly."

Ryan's voice was very weak as he looked at Marcus lovingly.

He said, "I had already chosen you as my heir before. My words still stand now.

"After I pass away, you will be the Godfather of this world.

"But honestly, when I first met you, I didn't think highly of you because the gap between you and your two brothers was just too big.

"I didn't expect you to grow so quickly. Now, you are already an Acquired State Nine expert!"

"This way, I can rest assured passing the position of Godfather to you."

At the same time, Ryan looked at Marcus with a hint of expectation in his eyes.

"Father, I know the healing techniques. I should be able to cure your illness. Why not let me try?" Marcus suggested.

His Timeless Warrior's Grimoire possesses extremely powerful healing effects.

Ryan shook his head and said, "It's useless. I'm not sick; my lifespan has simply run out. This year marks my 5000th year in this world, yet I still haven't stepped into that State. This means I've lost my final chance to extend my life."

Marcus was deeply shocked.

He never expected that Ryan was already 5000 years old. In other words, when the world within the Ice Wall was still in the primitive era, he had already been born.

Ryan said, "Are you curious about my identity? Before I die, let me explain it to you. I hope it will help you on your future path."

Marcus nodded. He was naturally very curious about Ryan's background.

"Actually, I am not from the world within the Ice Wall. I come from Atrandis Continent, the son of the king of that land.

"Because of the fight for the crown prince position, I was betrayed and imprisoned on this continent.

"At that time, this continent was still in its primitive stage. After I arrived here, I was despondent for a long time before pulling myself together.

"Then, I became the most powerful man in this world.

"But I know that even if I rule this world, I still don't belong here. I am the prince of Atrandis Continent.

"There are things that belong to me there. For many years, my only goal has been to return.

"But obviously, I failed. I hope you can carry on my will, return to Atrandis Continent one day, and reclaim everything that belongs to me!" Ryan continued, his tone filled with excitement and unwillingness.

Marcus was deeply shocked.

He had always thought Ryan was a native of this world.

To his surprise, Ryan also came from a world beyond the Ice Wall, and he was the prince of the legendary Atrandis Continent.

"Father, I really want to know, what state are you in now?"

Ryan took a deep breath and replied, "The peak of Innate State Nine. If I could break through that state, I could return to Atrandis Continent and reclaim everything that belongs to me. Unfortunately..."

Marcus was astonished that this seemingly frail old man's cultivation had actually reached the peak of Innate State Nine.

With his state, he could easily defeat everyone within the world inside the Ice Wall.

No wonder he’s so powerful. Everyone in this world treats him with utmost respect!

In the end, strength means everything.

Yet even so, Ryan still lacks the strength to return to Atrandis Continent. This alone proves how powerful the royal family of Atrandis Continent must be!

"Marcus, my time is running out. Now, I will pass on all my lifelong knowledge to you. I hope you can inherit my skills and reclaim everything that belongs to me!" Ryan said slowly.

At the same time, he waved his palm. An invisible force burst forth from his hand, instantly enveloping Marcus's body.

Immediately after, Marcus sensed a terrifying power surging from Ryan's palm, rapidly pouring into his body.

In an instant, Marcus felt his body overflowing with energy, and the blood in his veins seemed to become extraordinarily active.

That power was clearly boosting his cultivation.

Before long, his cultivation reached the peak of Acquired State Nine.

Meanwhile, Ryan quickly aged.

Marcus could feel Ryan's power rapidly diminishing.

If this continues, he will soon die!

"Keep going!"

However, at this moment, Ryan completely ignored his condition and kept forcefully transferring power into Marcus.

Finally, at a certain moment, Marcus felt his cultivation rapidly break through the barrier, reaching a brand-new level, the Innate State!

Under Ryan's powerful infusion, Marcus's cultivation directly reached the Innate State.

The transition from Acquired State to Innate State is a transformation.

This State is far more powerful than Marcus's previous one. He only felt his body filled with endless strength.

Whether it was his physical body, spiritual senses, or other abilities around him, all were elevated to a brand-new level.

However, when Marcus's gaze fell on Ryan, he was startled.

At this moment, Ryan's eyes were wide open and vacant. He seemed to have aged many years compared to before. Forcing out his last bit of strength, he said, "Marcus, I have given you every last ounce of my power."
