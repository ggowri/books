"That's for sure! And this guy even dares to barge into our Axe Gang. He's not getting out of there alive!"

The thugs whispered among themselves.

Having been in the underworld for years, it was the first time they had heard of someone who dared to walk into the lion's den after attacking a member of the Axe Gang!

Sam also burst into mocking laughter.

In less than half an hour, the car stopped in front of a compound on the outskirts of the city.

This must be the headquarters of the Axe Gang.

At that moment, Blake was in the courtyard, drinking and chatting with several association leaders, Baron among them.

This was also the main reason why he could dominate the Kingston underworld and establish the Axe Gang.

Blake, Baron, and the others looked over.

"Mr. Fitzgerald..."

Blake said disdainfully, "What does his old friend have to do with me? He's your master, not mine. This guy has already offended the Axe Gang. If I don't teach him a lesson, how can I continue to hold my ground in the Kingston underworld?"
