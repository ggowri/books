Marcus's fist, like a death warrant, slammed fiercely onto his body.

Blasting his body into pieces, turning it into shattered flesh scattered all over the ground!

This scene made the faces of the other Ophidians involuntarily show a trace of horror.

No one expected the young man's strength to be so terrifying.

The Titan Python Snake Man said to his companions, "This guy seems ready to fight us to the death. Be careful, everyone. Attack him together and wipe him out completely!"

The other Ophidians nodded one after another, then collectively unleashed their aura and launched a terrifying attack toward Marcus.

Their cultivation was actually above the Acquired State, and each could even punch above their weight class.

Combined, the strength of dozens of them was not to be underestimated!

But they were up against Marcus, this freak, so it was their bad luck.

Next, they began unleashing terrifying attacks on Marcus.

Within the cave, various destructive forces erupted, enveloping Marcus's figure from all directions.

Gertie watched with her heart pounding in fear.

Her cultivation had only reached Ancient Martial Stage Nine, with a chasm still to cross before reaching the Acquired State realm.

Therefore, she was not qualified to partake in battles exclusive to Acquired State realm experts.

Any one of these Ophidians could easily kill her in an instant.

Therefore, Gertie could only watch from a distance and pray in her heart that Marcus would be safe.

Almost at the same time, one of the black mamba Ophidian's attacks swiftly descended in front of Marcus. He was surrounded by black mist, eerie and terrifying.

Marcus neither dodged nor avoided, delivering a sweeping kick that instantly broke through everything and struck the black mamba Ophidian's head.

With a dull thud, the creature didn't even have time to scream before its head was directly kicked apart by Marcus, leaving behind a headless corpse.

"Go to hell!"

Almost at the same time, a rattlesnake Ophidian appeared behind Marcus, holding a crimson dagger in his hand.

The dagger emitted a pungent smell, likely coated with some extremely potent venom.

Before the words had faded, the rattlesnake Ophidian raised the dagger and stabbed directly toward Marcus's back.

This attack was not only extremely swift but also quite abrupt.

If it were someone else, they might not even have time to react before being gutted by the opponent.

However, Marcus was no ordinary person.

While fighting, he also unleashed all his spiritual powers.

In an instant, he completely enveloped the space, and nothing within it escaped his eyes.

Everything was under his control.

Before the opponent's dagger could strike, Marcus swung a punch backward.

The Fist Ray pierced through the heavens and descended.

The next moment, a thunderous explosion sounded as the rattlesnake Ophidian's upper body was blasted to pieces, dead beyond doubt!

Next, more and more attacks descended, each with unimaginably immense power.

Especially seeing their companions continuously die at Marcus's hands, these Ophidians grew even angrier.

They almost unleashed all their potential, something others might truly be unable to withstand!

However, Marcus didn't care at all.

Facing these attacks, he swiftly activated the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire.

Strands of Timeless Numinous Energy swirled around him, accompanied by the release of Thunderfire Power.

Three Ophidians launched attacks at him together, but Marcus casually waved his hand.

In his palm, terrifying Thunderfire Power bloomed together with Timeless Numinous Energy.

It transformed into an overwhelming force of destruction descending.

Terrifying explosions echoed continuously as the two forces clashed.

The unleashed power was earth-shatteringly strong.

The three Ophidians thought joining forces would be enough to rival Marcus.

However, when the attacks truly collided, they realized just how naive they had been.

Marcus's attack was unstoppable, far beyond what the three of them could withstand.

In the blink of an eye, the Thunderfire Power engulfed their bodies and completely destroyed them!

"Die!"

Almost at the same time, the Titan Python Ophidian's attack arrived.

He was the strongest among them, with cultivation reaching the peak of Acquired State Three.

Moreover, he possessed terrifying power and the power of this attack was at least equivalent to that from a human at Ancient Martial Stage Five!

However, even though the strength of this Ophidian was beyond ordinary, in front of Marcus, it was still not enough. The terrifying Thunderfire Hand Strike slammed wildly toward the opponent's position.

In just the blink of an eye, it collided with the opponent's attack.

Everyone only heard an astonishing explosion sound.

Under Marcus's Thunderfire Hand Strike, the Titan Ophidian's attack was like paper, instantly shattered and destroyed.

Marcus's Thunderfire Hand Strike was extremely domineering, fiercely landing on his body!
