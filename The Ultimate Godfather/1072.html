Yet now, Marcus was proof that they could coexist in a single individual. It was terrifying.

Thorne's face twisted with despair.

This was supposed to be a simple dinner. Yet, by the end of it, he had lost everything. His job was gone. His father was ruined. He had dug his own grave.

A single night had completely destroyed his life. He felt nothing but overwhelming regret. He wished he could turn back time, take back every smug remark, every condescending glance.

But it was too late.

Marcus, meanwhile, had already lost interest in him.

He turned to Chloe and said, "This dinner is beyond boring. Let's get out of here."

Chloe nodded mechanically, still overwhelmed by everything that had happened.

Without another word, Marcus took her hand and led her out of the room.

Chloe followed him without hesitation.

Yet inside, her thoughts were in chaos.

Every time she thought she had finally grasped the extent of Marcus' power, he shattered her expectations yet again. Every time she thought she had finally understood him, he revealed a whole new layer of mystery.

He wasn't just the head of a billion-dollar tech empire—

He had deep ties to the military, too.

Who exactly was Marcus Fitzgerald?

She couldn't help but feel like he was... invincible. Like there was nothing in this world he couldn't control.

Sitting inside Marcus' Aston Martin, Chloe looked at him apologetically and murmured, "Marcus, I'm really sorry. I feel like I've caused you so much trouble tonight."

Marcus simply chuckled.

"How many times do I have to tell you?" he said. "It's not trouble. It was just a minor inconvenience—dealing with a nobody like him was nothing."

Meanwhile, back in the banquet hall, Eulalia and Isolde finally snapped out of their daze.

For the first time that night, they felt a deep sense of guilt toward Chloe.

As her immediate family, they had spent the entire evening mocking her, belittling her, and siding against her.

Now, after witnessing Marcus' absolute dominance, they realized just how foolish they had been.

They had treated a man worth trillions like he was some lowly extra on a film set.

Thinking back, it was laughable.

Thorne, on the other hand, was completely broken.

His mind had snapped under the weight of what had happened.

Even after regaining some sense, he began muttering nonsense—his words disjointed and irrational, like a man on the verge of a complete mental breakdown.

The shock had been too much for him.

Not wanting to take any risks, Eulalia and Isolde immediately called Thorne's family, telling them to come pick him up.

What happened afterward was no longer their concern. But one thing was certain—tonight's events had left an irreversible mark on everyone involved. And as for Marcus and Chloe? Their relationship had just taken another step forward.

When they returned to Sunridge Estates, Marcus wasted no time.

With burning desire, he pinned Chloe beneath him, claiming her without restraint.

Chloe gave herself to him completely, no longer holding back.

That night, they made love six times.

Each time lasted over an hour, their bodies entwined in endless passion.

By the end, Chloe had passed out from sheer exhaustion—her body completely spent from the pleasure.

Yet Marcus? He still wasn't done.

He immediately activated Timeless Warrior's Grimoire, beginning a rapid transformation of his body. With the aid of several pills, Marcus' cultivation became increasingly stable, steadily pushing forward.

Finally, just before dawn, he successfully broke through to Acquired State Six!

Upon achieving this cultivation breakthrough, Marcus' strength skyrocketed once again, reaching an entirely new level.
