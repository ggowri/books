The Black Hawk Legion has always been a force to be reckoned with.

Their overall strength rivals that of some small nations.

Though they may not have the most significant numbers or the most equipment, they make up for it in quality.

Both their gear and individual combat capabilities are incredibly formidable.

At that moment, in a courtyard at the Black Hawk Legion headquarters.

Josie was tied to a pillar, her delicate features contorted with fear.

Several men in combat fatigues, all in their fifties, emerged into the courtyard.

At their head was none other than Black Hawk Legion Commander Aquila Hoskins.

The others were all high-ranking figures within the Black Hawk Legion.

"That Uswadian has repeatedly challenged the Black Hawk Legion's authority and even killed two of our leaders, Josiah and Abel . When he comes for this woman, we'll tear him limb from limb!"

Aquila snarled through gritted teeth.

Last time, they'd received orders from Acoton's higher-ups to steal classified data from the Solomia database.

Everything had been going according to plan.

Even though Uswad had deployed elite special police units from various regions for protection, it had seemed futile.

Even the legendary Battle God Oliver almost met his end at the hands of Josiah and Abel.

But everything changed when Marcus showed up.

What should have been a successful plan failed, with Josiah and Abel meeting their deaths in Uswad.

They also lost dozens of 4th generation cyborgs.

For the Black Hawk Legion, this was the ultimate humiliation.

Through their investigation, they discovered that the previous aircraft hijacking failure was also connected to a man called Marcus.

That was why they had been secretly investigating Marcus lately.

They had thoroughly mapped out all of Marcus' relationships.

When Josie went to Acoton, they seized the opportunity to kidnap her.

They planned to use her as bait to lure Marcus into their trap.

"Sir, don't worry, we're fully prepared. If Marcus dares to show up, we'll make sure he never leaves alive!"

One of the leaders spoke with a cold smirk.

"If you've got the guts, face my husband head-on instead of using these dirty tricks. It's absolutely disgusting!"

Hearing the conversation between Aquila and the others, Josie trembled with rage as she spat out the words through gritted teeth.

Before she could finish speaking, one of the Black Hawk Legion's leaders struck her across the face.

Blood trickled from the corner of Josie's mouth!

"Shut your mouth, bitch. You've got no right to speak here. Say another word, and I'll tear your lips off!"

The leader threatened, his eyes blazing with murderous intent.

"Marcus is no ordinary man! His power is extraordinary. We can't afford to underestimate him. All of you, go prepare immediately!"

Aquila instructed, being his typically cautious self who never gave in to arrogance.

"Marcus is in Uswad. It takes over 10 hours to fly to Acoton. Even if he's coming, he won't be here until tomorrow. There's no need to panic, Sir."

One of the leaders smugly remarked as if everything was under his control.

"BOOM!"

But just as he finished speaking, an explosive sound thundered in the distance!

A massive mushroom cloud appeared on the horizon, followed by a burst of flames and continuous gunfire.

"What's going on?"

One of the leaders furrowed his brow and immediately contacted his subordinates through the radio.

"We've got trouble! That Uswadian just showed up out of nowhere and forced his way into Black Hawk Legion headquarters!"

The voice crackled through the radio.

At these words, Aquila and the other leaders froze, shock spreading across their faces.

They had just sent Marcus a message less than three hours ago, yet he had already arrived! His speed was beyond anything they had imagined.

"Quick, mobilize our elite forces and heavy equipment! Surround and eliminate Marcus! If necessary, deploy the 5th-generation cyborgs!"

Aquila barked orders to his men.

Since Marcus had come, he would make sure the man never left alive.

The leaders immediately acknowledged the order and began mobilizing their forces to encircle Marcus.

Meanwhile, Marcus was storming the Black Hawk Legion headquarters, armed with the submachine gun Diana had modified.

The massive explosion from earlier had been caused when he hit their fuel depot.

The Black Hawk Legion responded with lightning speed. As soon as the alarm sounded, troops began pouring in from all directions.

Their heavy machine guns opened up, spraying a wall of bullets at Marcus.

The sheer volume of gunfire was beyond imagination.

Marcus showed no fear, executing his Shadow Step to weave through the hail of bullets.

His compact submachine gun roared like a mortar in his palm as he squeezed the trigger relentlessly.

Each destructive beam that erupted from his weapon cut down swaths of enemies.

The Black Hawk Legion headquarters erupted into an all-out war zone.

Like the Grim Reaper himself, Marcus cut an unstoppable path straight into the heart of the Black Hawk Legion's stronghold!
