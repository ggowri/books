In the following days, Marcus didn't leave his house.

He focused on practicing the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire in his villa, and his cultivation successfully reached the minor completion stage of Ancient Martial Stage Two.

At the same time, he also looked through the Magic Codex.

He gained a preliminary understanding of the training methods for Wizards.

And he could cast some basic spells, such as Telekinesis, Fireball, and Lightning.

However, Marcus did not become a true Wizard, and his spells basically had no offensive power and could only be used for performances.

But Marcus was not discouraged. With this Magic Codex, as long as he practiced diligently, he would one day become a true Wizard.

By then, he could be both a Wizard and a warrior, and his strength would inevitably be stronger.

Soon, it was Wednesday when the Solomia Land Exchange held the land auction.

He received a call from Michelle early in the morning.

Marcus prepared five billion dollars in funds, and he was determined to get the land on Bayside Avenue.

Driving an Alphard business van, Marcus picked up Michelle, and they arrived at the land exchange.

Perhaps because the location of this land was superior, there were many people who came to bid, including many business groups from other cities.

Among the many bidders, Marcus saw a familiar figure— Haley.

Sitting next to Haley was his father, Jared.

Five days ago, Haley was cut off his hands by Bob, and it was unexpected that he came out to be active so soon.

His hands were wrapped in thick bandages, and it seemed that they were successfully reconnected.

Haley naturally also noticed Marcus and the hatred in his eyes was undisguised.

At nine o'clock, many bidders sat down, and the auction officially started.

"Dad, that Marcus seems to want to bid for this land. We can't let him succeed," Haley said to Jared.

Jared glanced at Marcus not far away and then withdrew his gaze and said, "Bidding this land is an order from the Brown family in Kingston. With their support, I am not afraid of him."

In fact, the Brown family in Kingston only gave him three billion dollars, and the excess part needs to be paid by TreatMed Pharmaceuticals.

But in order to vent for Haley, even if he can't bid successfully, he can't let Marcus get it easily.

"The bidding officially starts. Today we are auctioning a piece of land on Bayside Avenue, west of Meadows Road, covering 123.5 acres, with a starting price of one billion dollars. Ladies and gentlemen, you can bid now!" On the stage, a woman with a good image and temperament smiled and said to everyone.

Someone said, "One billion dollars."

Another said, "One point one billion dollars!"

Yet another said, "One point five billion dollars!"

A man said, "One point eight billion dollars!"

Many people started to bid.

This piece of land was unique in value, and everyone wanted to swallow this piece of cake.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, should we bid?" Michelle asked Marcus.

Marcus replied lightly, "It's not the right time. Wait a moment."

Michelle nodded.

Soon, the price was raised to three billion dollars, which exceeded the budget of most people on the scene, and no one bid for a while.

"Three point one billion dollars." Marcus knew it was time for him to enter.

He felt such a good piece of land it was impossible to be photographed for a dozen or two billion dollars; these people were simply wasting time before!

"Three point two billion dollars!" However, as soon as Marcus's voice fell, Jared, not far away, said.

Golden Group and TreatMed Pharmaceuticals were the two giants in Solomia.

Recently, the news that the two companies have terminated their cooperation has caused a lot of shock in the industry.

The two companies were competing for land.

The people present realized that the real competition was about to start.

"Three point five billion dollars!" Marcus looked confident.

Before participating in the auction, Diana had collected all the details of all the companies participating in the auction this time.

Besides TreatMed Pharmaceuticals, she also learned about the relationship between Jared and the Brown family in Kingston and even the maximum amount of money Jared could offer.

Marcus never fought battles he wasn't sure of winning. For this auction, he had already made ample preparations.

Seeing Marcus so confident, Jared felt a little uncertain.

But he still shouted out the price of four billion dollars with a stiff head.

"Four point five billion dollars!" Marcus spoke without hesitation; in order to enter the pharmaceutical industry, he was also desperate.

"Five billion dollars!" Jared seems to be determined to fight against Marcus.

"Five point five billion dollars!" The price had already exceeded Marcus' previous budget, but it was still within his acceptable range.

"Six billion dollars!" Jared said.

After a few rounds of bidding, Jared saw that Marcus had a strong desire for this land.

He suddenly had an idea. In the next few rounds, no matter how much Marcus offered, he would raise it by five hundred million dollars.

Finally, the price came to nine billion dollars.

"Nine point five billion dollars!" Jared bid again without hesitation.

"Mr. Jared Brown, you are indeed generous. This land is yours," Marcus said.

Jared thought that Marcus would continue to raise the price. In this way, his scheme to deliberately raise the price would succeed.

Unexpectedly, Marcus directly gave up the auction.

"Nine point five billion dollars once.

"Nine point five billion dollars twice.

"Nine point five billion dollars three times.

"Deal.

"Congratulations to Mr. Jared Brown for the successful auction," the hostess said.
