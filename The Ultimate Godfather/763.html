Chapter 761 Kill Them All-2

Upon realizing that, the remaining warriors of the Shark Tribe quickly prepared to flee.

But how could Marcus possibly give them that chance?

The power of Rune, Lightning spell, Fireball spell, and all kinds of destructive attacks erupted wildly.

The power of each attack was beyond imagination, relentlessly falling upon the members of the Shark Tribe.

They all screamed in terror and agony. Against Marcus' attack, they had no means of resistance.

They could only submit to the slaughter.

In an instant, the entire space was filled with various destructive powers, as if a hellish battlefield had appeared in the depths of the sea.

The waters turned crimson with blood, exuding a thick, bloody stench.

Even the Nuer Tribe members watching from a distance felt their hearts shake violently, their scalps tingling with dread.

It was a massacre, an undeniably cruel massacre.

Marcus' power was utterly terrifying, to a degree beyond anyone's imagination.

A tribe as powerful as the Shark tribe was instantly wiped out by his attacks.

After all, for the past 1,000 years, the Shark Tribe had always kept the Nuer Tribe firmly suppressed, leaving them no room to breathe.

Yet, even such a savage, bloodthirsty tribe with overwhelming power was nothing in the face of Marcus.

That level of power completely overturned everyone's understanding.

In less than five minutes, all the members of the Shark Tribe were slaughtered by Marcus alone.

Yet, his expression remained calm and indifferent. He slowly withdrew his Timeless Numinous Energy, not the slightest bit affected.

His body slowly descended in front of the Nuer Tribe members.

It seemed that the murderous intent surrounding Marcus was too overwhelming, causing the Nuer Tribe members to instinctively step back, fear creeping into their expressions.

Raphael took a deep breath, then lowered his head respectfully to Marcus, saying, "Mr. Fitzgerald, not only are your medical skills extraordinary, but you are also so powerful. I deeply admire you. Moreover, I am grateful that you lent your help in our tribe's time of crisis. Please allow me to show my respect!"

As he spoke, Raphael lowered himself in a bow, his face filled with reverence.

Seeing that, the other members of the Nuer Tribe followed suit, bowing before Marcus.

Even Ashton and his son, who had previously opposed Marcus the most, could only slightly lower their heads.

They had always thought of Marcus as nothing more than a useless human.

Even if he mastered incredible medical skills, they believed it would not threaten their status in the tribe.

But after witnessing Marcus' terrifying strength, the father and son no longer dared to speak.

They even realized that their previous actions against him had been incredibly foolish.

With Marcus' immense strength, not only the father and son but even the entire Nuer Tribe stood no chance against him.

If he wanted to, he could wipe them all out with a single wave of his hand.

And yet, they foolishly kept claiming they would capture him.

It was practically just digging their own graves.

Fortunately, Marcus was not the kind of person who enjoyed killing. Otherwise, the father and son would have already become corpses.

Under such circumstances, how could they still dare to act recklessly in front of him?

Nadia and Susan also lowered their heads in respect to Marcus.

In their beautiful eyes, there was not only respect but also a deep admiration.

As young women in their twenties, they were naturally drawn to exceptional men.

A man like Marcus, with such extraordinary power, was naturally someone they could not help but be attracted to.

Especially when they thought about the passionate moments they had shared with him, they couldn't help but blush.
