Chapter 635 The Death of the Jenkins Couple

As Marcus changed positions, Jade felt her deepest organs being continuously impacted.

The sensation was so intense that she momentarily forgot herself, and her mind went blank.

She reveled in Marcus' fiery passion.

Marcus channeled the force of Dual Cultivation to its peak, thrusting while absorbing the benefits it brought to his body.

Soon, Jade reached her fourth climax.

Marcus couldn't hold back any longer and began a frenzied series of thrusts, reaching his own peak and exploding in ecstasy.

After this intense session, Jade lost consciousness for a while, trembling as she lay in the chair.

It took her a long time to wake up.

"You jerk. You're always so rough with me," Jade grumbled.

Marcus laughed. "You told me to go harder. Don't you remember?"

Jade blushed. "Don't say that. You're so crude. It's embarrassing."

Next, Marcus dropped Jade off at the staff dormitory of Solomia Hospital.

He then drove his Aston Martin to the headquarters of Bayside Petroleum Company, founded by Lonnie.

Lonnie was in his office, reviewing documents.

A sudden knock on the door interrupted him.

The door opened, and several men carried in Joan, who was covered in blood.

Lonnie was shocked. "Who did this?"

One of the men replied, "It was the guy who attacked Mrs. Jenkins at the jewelry store today."

Lonnie fumed with rage. "I sent you to deal with him. How did this happen?"

The man explained the series of events involving Bob and his crew.

Lonnie frowned. "I can't believe that bastard is connected to Serpent's Coil."

"Honey, kill that guy. You have to kill him!" Joan, now awake, muttered incoherently.

"Too late for that." A sudden voice suddenly echoed from the doorway.

Everyone turned to see a young man casually walking in.

Lonnie scowled. "Who are you? How dare you barge into my office?"

Joan trembled. "Honey, it's him. He beat me up. I can't believe he showed up here. Don't let him get away!"

Before she could finish, Marcus shot her in the forehead.

Her eyes widened with horror, and she collapsed to the floor.

The room fell silent in shock. No one expected Marcus to kill so brazenly in their territory.

They reached for their weapons, but Marcus was faster.

He fired quickly, taking out everyone around Lonnie.

Lonnie trembled, sweat pouring down his face. "What do you want?"

Marcus sneered, "Your wife brought so many guys trying to kill me. Isn't it obvious what I want?"

Lonnie backed away, terrified. "I apologize for any offense. Whatever you want, I'll give it to you."

"Too late. You can go to meet your maker now," Marcus said, shooting Lonnie in the temple.

Lonnie fell to the floor, lifeless.

From then on, the chairman of Bayside Petroleum Company and his wife were no more.

Marcus didn't linger. He leaped from the high-rise.

After reaching Ancient Martial Stage Eight, he acquired flight abilities.

Though he couldn't fly, he could jump great distances.

Dropping from such a height was no problem for him.

Thanks to the cover of the night, no one noticed his departure.

By the time the employees discovered the bodies, Marcus was long gone.

The police arrived quickly and pulled up the surveillance footage.

Strangely, all the cameras in the area had mysteriously malfunctioned during the incident.

There was no footage of the killer.

This case bore a striking resemblance to other recent murders in the country.
