The little girl being scolded was Josie's daughter, Leia.

Audrey Torres, who had a landlord-like appearance, pulled away from Josie's grasp, placing her hands on her hips. "This little brat knocked my daughter over today. What's wrong with making her apologize?"

Beside Audrey stood another little girl.

This girl was chubby and looked like a miniature version of her mother!

Even at five or six years old, she was dressed head-to-toe in designer brands, looking every bit like a nouveau riche child.

"If my daughter really did knock over yours, she can apologize. But you, as an adult, screaming at a child like this in public? That's unacceptable!" Josie retorted firmly.

Leia had grown up without a father, which left her withdrawn and deeply insecure.

Josie knew her daughter well—Leia would never intentionally hurt someone.

Even if Leia had accidentally knocked over the chubby girl, it wouldn't have been on purpose.

Besides, kids playing together at kindergarten often bumped into each other—it was perfectly normal.

Audrey was clearly making a mountain out of a molehill.

Hearing Josie's response, Audrey snarled, "Then make your little brat apologize! If she messed up, she needs to kneel and apologize to my daughter. Right here, right now!"

Josie's face turned bright red with anger. She shot back, "What you're doing is illegal! I can sue you for slander and violating personal dignity!"

Audrey let out a cold laugh. "Ha! You little tramp, don't think I don't know who you are. Aren't you Josie, the woman from BioFusion Pharmaceuticals?

"Sue me for slander? Keep dreaming. Do you know who my husband is? Just mentioning his name would scare you!" she boasted smugly.

Then, with a triumphant smirk, she added, "Oh, that's right—you don't even have a husband. Your kid must be the result of some fling with a random man, huh? Hahaha!"

Leia raised her tear-streaked face to Josie and asked, "Mom, am I really what the fat lady says I am—a bastard?"

Seeing Leia's swollen cheek, Josie's heart broke. With tears in her eyes, she said, "Leia has a dad. He's just in another world now. Leia is not a bastard."

Leia's words were like a spark to a powder keg, enraging Audrey. "You little brat, who are you calling fat? You're kneeling today and apologizing to my daughter!" she shouted furiously.

She grabbed Leia's hair, trying to force her to the ground.

Josie tried to intervene, but Audrey was too strong. She shoved Josie aside, nearly knocking her over!

"That's enough!"

Just as Audrey was about to hurt Leia, a tall, commanding figure stepped in and blocked her.

It was Marcus!

"Marcus..." Leia cried.

Seeing Marcus appear, Leia's emotions overwhelmed her, and she burst into tears.

Looking at the pitiful little girl before him, Marcus felt a wave of tenderness. He picked her up and gently wiped her tears. "Don't cry, Leia. I'm here, no one will bully you!"

At that moment, Josie noticed Marcus as well.

She felt like she was seeing a mirage.

The man she had dreamt about countless nights had actually come...

Countless nights, Josie had woken from her dreams, her mind filled with Marcus's tall and imposing figure.

There had been times when Josie had an overwhelming urge to go to Solomia to find Marcus, but she had always suppressed it.

Josie was a self-aware woman. She had always believed she wasn't good enough for Marcus and had no right to intrude on his life.

And so, she had endured her pain and longing in silence.

She never imagined that in her most helpless moment, that man would appear!

At that moment, Marcus's figure seemed larger than life in Josie's eyes.

"Ms. Shelman, are you alright?" Marcus asked gently.

Marcus stepped forward, helping Josie to her feet and speaking with warmth.

For reasons she couldn't explain, seeing Marcus again brought tears to Josie's eyes. "I'm fine. I'm just... so happy to see you again, Mr. Fitzgerald," she said, her voice trembling.

"Oh, look, the secret lover has arrived! Everyone, check it out—this is Ms. Shelman from BioFusion Pharmaceuticals, the famous businesswoman of Kingston!" Audrey mocked loudly.

"Turns out, she's just a slut sneaking around with random men!"
