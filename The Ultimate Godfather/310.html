Jade found it unbelievable and rushed to examine the middle-aged woman's body.

Jade found that the blood pressure, heart rate, and breathing rate of the severely shocked and dying patient had miraculously returned to normal.

As the deputy chief physician of surgery in Solariae Hospital, Jade had rich clinical experience.

She had been exposed to too many patients with severe appendicitis.

Even if these patients had their appendix removed, it would take several days for them to recover.

The middle-aged woman had been in critical condition, dying.

And now, she had miraculously recovered!

This made Jade even think she was dreaming.

She asked in a trembling voice, looking at Marcus, "Excuse me, which hospital are you from? What's your name?"

Marcus glanced at Jade and said, "Don't you know it's rude to ask people for their personal information?"

Then, Marcus returned to his seat.

Looking at Marcus' back, Jade didn't leave immediately. Instead, she said to the middle-aged woman and the boy, "Although you feel good now, I suggest you go to the hospital for further examination. I am the deputy chief physician of surgery at Solariae Hospital. Come with me later."

The middle-aged woman and her son immediately nodded.

She was dying just now and would have to be checked out to avoid her worries.

Jade also returned to her seat.

She looked at Marcus from time to time, but Marcus kept his eyes closed and paid no attention to her.

"I was wrong about you just now. I apologize. Please answer the question I asked just now. Thank you!"

Jade couldn't help it, apologized, and asked again.

She was quite persistent.

Marcus didn't open his eyes but simply replied, "I don't care whether you apologize or not, because I have nothing to do with you. As for your questions, I will answer them now. I have no medical qualifications, and I don't belong to any hospital. "

Jade was angry and thought he was vindictive.

She thought Marcus must be angry because she had wrongly blamed him.

Jade didn't believe he was not a doctor.

Now, the medical system was so mature that it was difficult for ordinary people to teach themselves medicine.

He was even less likely to treat a severely shocked patient with appendicitis perforation.

It was difficult for even some specialists to treat such patients without the help of any medical equipment.

But it only took Marcus five minutes to get all the indexes of the patients back to normal.

Jade didn't believe his words.

Marcus got out of the car alone and got into a taxi to find a hotel.

Jade contacted the hospital vehicle in advance and went directly to Solariae Hospital with the middle-aged woman and the boy.

She quickly launched a series of examinations for the woman.

After all, with the current medical means, the treatment of appendicitis perforation could only be surgery. There was no other way.

"Jade, are you mistaken? There is nothing wrong with the patient's appendix. She's fine!"

The doctor said so.

Jade asked, her eyes wide in surprise, "No way. I took a train with this patient. She was in severe shock in the carriage. At that time, I was sure that she was caused by appendicitis perforation. How could there be no problem with her appendix?"

She said, "Why? Her appendix has completely recovered. What means did he use? "

Looking at the screen, Jade had a lot on her mind.

She was worried that the woman would not want to come to the hospital for a check-up, so Jade came with them.

If the middle-aged woman's appendicitis was not cured, she would be in danger again soon.

However, her appendix miraculously recovered.

Jade knew that this was most likely done by Marcus!
