With a deafening roar, four bolts of lightning struck down.

To Marcus' surprise, the four cyborgs trembled and swayed but managed to withstand the onslaught!

It took Marcus' breath away.

After all, his Lightning spell was on par with that of a level three Wizard. Even ordinary Ancient Martial Stage Three experts might not be able to withstand a casual strike from him.

Yet, these cyborgs remained unscathed. Their defensive capabilities were incredibly formidable!

The cyborg with the Gatling gun on its chest unleashed another furious barrage at Marcus.

Though the boulder was massive, it couldn't withstand such an intense hail of bullets for long.

Before long, it began to crack, at risk of collapsing at any moment.

Marcus moved swiftly, pushing the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire to its limits, with flames and thunder rapidly coalescing in his hands.

The two forces merged through a unique technique, and he unleashed a frenzied assault on the cyborg!

These cyborgs had formidable offensive and defensive capabilities but lacked mobility.

Faced with Marcus' attack, the cyborg intended to dodge but was too slow.

In the next moment, with a thunderous shock, the Gatling gun exploded under the combined might of Thunderfire Power.

Terrifying Thunderfire Power enveloped the cyborg's body.

The cyborg's body convulsed, attempting to extinguish the Thunderfire Power.

But the Thunderfire Power, which not only combined magic but also the power of the two crystals within Marcus' body—crystals of flame and thunder—was unstoppable.

With the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire, the power became even more formidable.

In an instant, the cyborg's body was enveloped entirely, his skin melting at a visible rate.

Beneath his skin, mechanical structures began to emerge.

Marcus strode forward, seizing the opportunity to unleash a powerful punch at the cyborg's body.

The punch transformed into claws upon contact, piercing the cyborg's body!

The cyborg let out a shrill roar and struggled desperately.

But Marcus didn't give him a chance, directly extracting the mechanical structures from within!

"This damn guy is much stronger than rumored. We need to be careful!"

Witnessing this, another cyborg warned the remaining two companions.

After the mechanical structures were removed, the cyborg lost all combat effectiveness, collapsing limply to the ground, lifeless.

"Go to hell!"

Almost simultaneously, the other two cyborgs attacked Marcus from both sides!

Their bodies had undergone various transformations, their internal mechanical structures unleashing terrifying power.

Their palms seemed to turn into meat grinders, converging on Marcus to grind him down.

Marcus sneered and dodged to the side swiftly.

He had already found the weak points of these cyborgs.

The Thunderfire Power erupted once more, combining magic and the power contained within the thunder and fire crystals.

At the same time, he pushed the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire to its peak, with the Thunderfire Power now entwined with powerful Numinous Energy.

It materialized in the void as an incredibly dreadful palm print, descending upon the two cyborgs in the blink of an eye!

The earth-shattering boom resounded through the heavens and the earth, and the bodies of the two cyborgs were sent flying by the impact.

Thunderfire Power spread rapidly, enveloping their bodies.

The next moment, a series of crackling sparks erupted.

Under the terrifying embrace of Thunderfire Power, the two cyborgs convulsed and struggled desperately, but it was to no avail.

The Thunderfire Power, now infused with Numinous Energy, became even more dreadful.

Before the Thunderfire Power could fully manifest its actual effects, their bodies were already shaken and injured by the Numinous Energy, leaving them at the mercy of the Thunderfire Power as it gradually burned and destroyed their bodies!

In less than two minutes, Marcus had already taken down three of the four cyborgs.

The remaining one, his face pale, looked at Marcus with a hint of fear for the first time.

He quickly retreated, trying to escape while Marcus was distracted.

Marcus didn't give him the chance, wildly slapping out a palm carrying Thunderfire Power.

It swiftly enveloped the space in front of the cyborg.

The cyborg let out a desperate growl as its body was directly blown apart!

The scattered limbs, minced flesh, and damaged mechanical components littered the ground, a gruesome and terrifying sight.
