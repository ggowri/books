Chapter 289 The Devouring Power Showing Its Might!

Marcus remained silent, unwilling to reveal anything to Asher.

After repelling the attack, the restraints of the Four Symbols Circle were shattered.

Without hesitation, Marcus unleashed his Lightning and Fireball magic, launching relentless long-range assaults on Asher.

While these magical attacks couldn't directly threaten Asher's life, they effectively sapped his energy and gradually wore him down.

Asher's expression soured as he dodged the magical attacks and exclaimed, "I never imagined your magical prowess had reached such heights. I clearly underestimated you."

Thunder and flames filled the air as he continued to evade Marcus' onslaught, inching closer.

"There are too many things you don't know. Don't talk nonsense with me. Come and destroy me if you can!" Marcus taunted, sensing Asher's injuries.

"Do you really think I can't handle you?" Asher scoffed, swiftly retrieving a pill from his Spatial Ring and swallowing it.

In an instant, a surge of power radiated from him and restored his aura to its peak even enhancing it slightly!

He seemed to grow taller and excluded a terrifying pressure that filled Marcus with despair.

Burning Blood!

Marcus realized Asher was using a technique to overdraw his potential for a temporary boost.

Asher used this pill to return to his peak temporarily.

Though this came at a significant personal cost, Asher was determined to kill Marcus.

Dong!

With a thunderous step, the ground shook, sending shockwaves through Marcus.

How did he know this move?!

Marcus frowned.

With Celestial Reversal, he thought Asher was seriously injured and he could have a break.

It never occurred to him that Asher's willingness to overdraw his strength to kill him.

Now Asher was even stronger than before.

Once again, Marcus was at an absolute disadvantage and didn't know how to deal with the next crazy and fierce offensive.

"You're the first to make me kill at the expense of burning blood!" Asher declared coldly, lunging forward with a powerful palm strike.

Caught off guard, Marcus could only muster a feeble defense that sent him flying and spitting blood.

If not for the healing properties of the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire, he would have surely perished.

"I'll tear you apart!" Asher threatened, closing in on Marcus and grabbing him by the neck.

Despair washed over Marcus.

He thought he was about to die here.

Before Asher could act further, a supreme Devouring Power erupted from Marcus, enveloping Asher.

Asher froze, unable to react as the terrifying energy invaded his body, rapidly consuming his internal power.

His energy surged into Marcus, filling his meridians to the brink and igniting an intense burning sensation.

Asher's face paled as he trembled, desperately running his cultivation manuals to break free.

He gritted his teeth and raised his left hand to strike Marcus, but he was powerless.

The Devouring Power intensified, greedily siphoning his energy and even his bloodline into Marcus.

"What kind of evil energy is this?" Asher demanded, frustration evident on his face.

Marcus trembled, surprised that the Devouring Power he had just acquired was now acting on its own.
