Chapter 946 Most Entertainment Stars-1

"Why are you following me?"

At the look of admiration in Chloe's eyes, Marcus asked.

"Can you take me home? You were the one who took me home last time."

Chloe's eyes sparkled, and there was a hint of expectation on her face as she spoke.

Marcus frowned and was a little speechless.

Before this, he could clearly feel that Chloe had always been indifferent toward him.

He never expected her to suddenly act so clingy.

"What, you don't want to? If you don't want to, I can just take a cab home."

At that, Marcus was silent. Chloe bit her lip, and disappointment flashed in her eyes.

"It's not that I don't want to. It's just that you have changed so much."

Marcus replied with a smile.

"I have changed so much? How?"

Chloe blinked, a bit surprised by his comment.

"Before, you definitely wouldn't have asked me to take you home," Marcus said.

Chloe blushed slightly and responded, "That's because you were Lila's boyfriend, so I... I just..."

Marcus froze for a moment, then looked at Chloe with an odd expression. "But I'm still Lila's boyfriend. That hasn't changed, so it seems like you're the one who's changed."

"I..."

Chloe was taken aback by Marcus' sharp response and couldn't figure out how to respond at that moment.

"Don't be nervous. I'm just kidding. Of course, I can take you home. It's no big deal. Let's get in the car!"

By the time he finished speaking, they had already arrived at the underground parking lot. Marcus opened the door to his Aston Martin and sat in the driver's seat.

Chloe, biting her lip, opened the passenger door.

It was her first time sitting in Marcus' car.

Looking at the luxurious interior, Chloe couldn't help but feel impressed. She asked him, "I notice that rich second-generation kids usually like Ferraris or Lamborghinis. Why did you buy this less popular brand? I think it shows your good taste."

"I liked watching the 007 serires movies when I was a kid. James Bond was my idol. Buying this car is like fulfilling a childhood dream."

Marcus explained calmly.

Another reason was that he didn't like flashy cars, and the Aston Martin fit his personality perfectly.

As they spoke, Marcus started the car, and the V8 engine let out a thrilling roar.

The sound echoed in the underground garage, becoming even more intense.

Then Marcus drove the car out of the garage.

"So, your passenger seat has been occupied by a lot of women, right?"

On the way, Chloe asked Marcus another question.

Marcus smiled, "If I told you that aside from Lila, you're the first, would you believe me?"

Chloe immediately pouted. "You're such a liar. A man as impressive as you couldn't possibly only have one woman around him.

"I've heard from Lila before that you're not just with her."

"Uh..."

Marcus felt speechless. It seemed like women loved sharing everything, especially private matters.

The next time he saw Lila, Marcus was definitely going to have a talk with her.

"So, your passenger seat must have been occupied by a lot of beautiful women."

Chloe said, looking at him with her beautiful eyes.

Marcus didn't explain. He knew that would only make things worse.

Chloe lived in Sunridge Estates, a mid-range neighborhood in Solomia.

What confused Marcus was that although Chloe was one of the top 12 rising stars and had just released a hit song, Midnight Serenade, she still gave him the impression of someone poor.

For a star at her level, it was strange that she didn't even own a private car.
