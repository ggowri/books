Chapter 372 Waiting Behind

Many people were stunned and didn't expect Marcus would appear at this time!

Before, Marcus suddenly disappeared, and most people had forgotten him.

Some people who found it didn't take it seriously.

After all, in their view, Marcus was a guy who liked to brag and was useless.

At least in military operations, Marcus was a layman.

He always said something exaggerated and even doubted Oliver in public.

Later, some people discovered that what Marcus said before actually came true one by one!

At this time, Marcus, who disappeared for an hour, reappeared in the sight of people at their most critical moment.

Abel and Josiah were stunned.

They could naturally feel the invisible strength that Marcus had just released.

That was not the power that ordinary people could control at all, because only people who reached Ancient Martial Stage Two could control the energy manifestation.

This showed that at present, this young man was at least in Ancient Martial Stage Two.

However, they didn't care about anything.

For this action, they made a careful plan but also dispatched the best semi-robots in the organization.

They didn't take someone who just reached Ancient Martial Stage Two seriously.

"You two are finally hooked!"

In the eyes of all people, Marcus walked to the central position of the data center and looked at Abel and Josiah.

He suddenly understood that the two of them were so crazy that they even transformed themselves into semi-Roberts to improve their ability.

It was extremely painful to transform ordinary people into semi-robots, and it was necessary to sacrifice much happiness that ordinary people could enjoy.

Moreover, this required great financial support to do it.

One could imagine how crazy Abel and Josiah were!

"Uswadian people, you are the one who took the bait, are you still unaware of it?"

"Those people who were killed by you before were all baits that we deliberately arranged to let all of you take the bait. We will keep you here and have a good slaughter!"

"I didn't expect you to be so stupid that you all took the bait. The so-called God of War in Uswad has nothing special!"

Abel sneered at Marcus and looked disdainful.

"Are you still counting on the support of the elite force in the rear?"

Marcus looked at Abel and suddenly spoke.

"Elite force is just our successor. I thought that you attached so much importance to arresting us that your team must be very powerful, so we prepared a large number of elite forces for backup."

"But now it seems that you are not good at fighting. Even your elite force is useless, and our existing people can easily wipe you out!"

Josiah sneered.

"You should always be well prepared."

"I killed all your elite force. Now, it's your turn!"

Marcus sneered and walked forward step by step.

"Don't go there, you are no match for them!" Oliver spoke.

Seeing that Marcus wanted to fight Abel and Josiah alone, Oliver looked very anxious.

Even Oliver couldn't handle them, so he thought Marcus was not a match for them.

Marcus directly ignored Oliver's words but continued to move forward.

"This guy is so ignorant, even ignoring the reminder of the Lord of War. I want to see how he died!"

This scene immediately made the rest of the SWAT officers dissatisfied and denounce Marcus.

Oliver also looked gloomy.

Not knowing why, when he first saw Marcus, he felt that Marcus was somewhat mysterious.

"Kill him!"

Abel took the lead in the attack and punched Marcus, with a sharp steel thorn on the fist.

Oliver completely lost his fighting capacity because of the blow.

Marcus, however, didn't take Abel's attack seriously but casually reached out and grabbed it, then buckled Abel's wrist.

Abel was stunned and wanted to use another hand to attack Marcus.

But Marcus just broke off casually, and the sound of bone and metal fracture was heard.

Abel's arm was abruptly broken off!

Abel screamed, and blood gushed from the broken wound, and more there were more mechanical sparking.

Seeing this scene, Josiah's arm instantly turned into a Gatling machine gun and fired wildly at Marcus.

Marcus used Shadow Step and easily dodged the bullets.

In an instant, it came to Josiah's body.

Josiah looked terrible, and his mechanical arm slammed into Marcus.

Marcus' speed was faster than Josiah's, and Marcus punched Josiah.

The fist fell directly on Josiah's head. And a bang was heard.

Josiah's head exploded immediately!
