Marcus' moving speed was quite fast.

Soon, we arrived at the first wooden house.

Two guards, hiding in the bushes next door, tried to shoot him.

Marcus cast a Fireball, instantly turning the two into ashes!

At the same time, he kicked open the door of the wooden house, and found a storage room inside.

In addition to some tools, there were two submachine guns and several magazines.

Marcus picked up a submachine gun and loaded the magazine.

He put the muzzle through the window, pulled the trigger and fired!

Gunfire continued to ring out.

Three assassins died immediately.

At the same time, Marcus' spiritual consciousness was released.

It directly covered the area within a radius of dozens of yards.

He could observe the situation around him.

There was a sniper hidden on a coconut tree on Marcus' left.

The sniper was waiting for an opportunity. And as soon as Marcus showed his head, he would be shot to death!

Marcus turned the muzzle of his gun directly to that direction and completed the shooting in just one second!

His reaction speed could not be matched even by the world's most powerful sniper.

The bullet broke through everything and shot the sniper's forehead!

Without the threat of the sniper, Marcus rolled out of the wooden house, used a coconut tree as a cover, and shot at another firing point.

This time, a more powerful submachine gun was used, and several enemies were instantly killed!

Marcus shot and moved forward at the same time. He was unstoppable!

The alarms were getting more urgent.

In a two-story wooden house in the Red River Mines, several middle-aged men were alarmed.

Judging from their clothes, they should be high-ranking officials here.

Especially the tall middle-aged man in the lead was a powerful Ancient Martial Art expert at the Stage One!

His skin was dark, and his body was full of muscles.

In addition to his cultivation, he had a ferocious beast tied to his side with an iron chain.

The ferocious beast looked like a tiger and like a lion. It seemed like a hybrid of the two creatures.

But it was fundamentally different from the ligers.

More importantly, the ferocious beast released spiritual power.

It was a spiritual beast.

In other words, this middle-aged man, in addition to possessing the skills of a warrior, was also a legendary beastmaster!

"Boss, the mine seems to have been attacked. There are so many gunshots outside."

A man said coldly.

The middle-aged man at the head was angry.

"This person must be crazy!"

As he said this, he led the beast out of the cabin.

From a distance, he saw Marcus slaughtering those armed killers.

The man waved at the killers, signaling them to stop.

In an instant, the gunshots and alarms all stopped, and the space returned to peace.

"Boy, we have no grudge against you. Why are you making such a fuss? If we have offended you, I will apologize to you. How about we end this fight?"

Obviously, the middle-aged man saw through Marcus' identity as a warrior.

However, he could not see through Marcus' specific cultivation level.

This was scary.

This meant Marcus had a higher revision than the middle-aged man.

Therefore, the middle-aged man was unsure and had to ask for peace.

Marcus glanced at the man and sneered, "I have no grudge against you, but your men invited me here. And what you did was shameful, so I won't stop!"

The middle-aged man looked unhappy and asked tentatively, "What do you want?"

Marcus sneered, "Give the order to release all the slaves that were trafficked here, and dissolve the Red River Mines. Otherwise, you and everyone around you will die!"

He had a domineering attitude.

"I cannot agree to your request!"

However, the middle-aged man could not accept it.

The Red River Mines was the result of his years of hard work.

And it was also the foundation of his cultivation. This industry had provided him with the cultivation to this state.

Once he did what Marcus said, he would not be able to continue practicing Ancient Martial Art.

"In that case, just don't regret it."

Marcus said with a smile.

"Please don't go too far. I have some abilities."

The middle-aged man's eyes became sharper.

The reason why he asked for peace was not entirely because he was afraid of Marcus, but because he did not want to suffer losses.

Since Marcus was showing no mercy, the man had no choice but to give it a try!
