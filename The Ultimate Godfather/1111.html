Seeing this, the other Ophidian was uttery petrified. He never imagined that the young man before him would be this powerful—so powerful that he didn't even dare to resist.

However, before he could run back to the cave, Marcus's large hand directly clamped onto his shoulder.

He was horrified as he desperately tried to break free, but it was completely futile.

Marcus's large hand squeezed, and a crisp sound of bone breaking echoed.

The bones in his shoulders were completely crushed.

He let out a piercing scream, unleashing all the power within his body in desperation.

He turned his hand and struck a palm toward Marcus.

This palm contained all the power of the Ophidians, and it was upon Marcus in the blink of an eye.

After all, this Ophidian's cultivation had reached the Acquired State, and the power of a full-force attack was not to be underestimated.

Gertie, watching from the side, felt her heart race with fear.

However, Marcus didn't care at all, casually releasing an attack that easily intercepted the opponent's strike.

A terrifying rumble echoed as Marcus's attack swept through like a hot knife through butter, completely destroying the opponent's strike in the blink of an eye.

At the same time, it swiftly broke through the opponent's defense and landed on HIS chest.

The next moment, the Ophidian's face was filled with terror as he watched Marcus's attack swiftly pierce through his chest.

It shattered his internal organs into pieces and he was completely reduced to a lifeless corpse!

All of this sounded complex, but it actually happened in an instant.

The two at Acquired State were all killed by Marcus alone.

He was clean and swift, without any hesitation, leaving Gertie on the side utterly shocked.

"Let's go, we're heading in!" Marcus said to Gertie.

Gertie then came to her senses and entered the cavewith Marcus.

After the two were killed, a rapid alarm sounded from inside the cave.

Obviously, these creatures had already noticed the arrival of intruders.

Therefore, as soon as Marcus and Gertie entered the cave, many gathered.

This cave was even more spacious than Marcus had imagined.

The farther they went in, the larger the space became.

He and Gertie had only advanced a little over a hundred meters when the openness ahead exceeded his imagination, with a diameter of at least several thousand meters.

It looked like an entirely different world within the cliff walls.

"Who are you? How dare you invade our territory?"

Among the gathered Ophidian, a tall and muscular figure with the head of a Titan python stepped out from the crowd.

The aura of this person was at least above the third level of Acquired State Three, likely a leader-like figure.

"What do you mean by your territory? This is the world within the Ice Wall, and we are the true masters here!"

Marcus sneered as he spoke.

Even surrounded by dozens of enemies, Marcus remained utterly unshaken.

The Ophidians stared at him in disbelief.

One of them with only one eye sneered and said, "You've got some guts for a prisoner in a cage, daring to break in here and even killing two of our guards."

"To tell you the truth, it won't be long before this world is under our control, and you prisoners will only serve as our slaves!"

After saying this, he burst into wild laughter, his laughter filled with arrogance.

However, before he could laugh for long, a terrifying First Ray had already descended!

Sensing that terrifying aura descending, the creature realized something was wrong.

But it was already too late.
