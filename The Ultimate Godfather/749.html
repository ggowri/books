In the area where spiritual powers couldn't penetrate, Marcus sensed waves of energy pulsing through the water.

The energy signature was compelling, seemingly contained by some constraining force.

"Could those energy waves be coming from the space-time gate?" Marcus wondered to himself.

Instinctively, he swam toward the source of the energy waves.

As he descended more profoundly, the water grew darker until he could barely navigate without his spiritual powers.

The increasing water pressure at these depths began taking its toll.

Even his Aqua Sphere's protective barrier started showing cracks, threatening to collapse at any moment.

Marcus quickly channeled his Timeless Numinous Energy to reinforce the barrier.

Only then did the protective shield stabilize.

He continued his descent until he reached 3,000 meters.

At this depth, no natural light penetrated the darkness. The abyss below gaped like the maw of some ancient sea monster, ready to swallow everything in its path.

Only the rarest deep-sea creatures ventured this far down.

As he pushed more profoundly, the energy waves grew more vigorous.

At the same time, an overwhelming, constraining force seemed to envelop his body.

He felt himself being pulled down faster, as if some unseen force had latched onto him.

Marcus wondered silently, "This suction force is potent... what could be lurking down here?"

He quickly activated the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire, channeling Timeless Numinous Energy to shield his body from the various energy currents assaulting him in the deep.

A bone-chilling howl echoed through the depths.

As he descended, the water carried a terrifying sound wave.

Though not particularly loud, the sound resembled a dragon's roar, somehow penetrating directly into his eardrums through some mysterious medium.

It made his heart tremble uncontrollably.

At 5,000 meters, the dim light from his Aqua Sphere revealed fine sand and coral reefs below.

This indicated he had reached the seafloor in this area.

However, when he finally touched down, he discovered an enormous, dark fissure stretching across the ocean floor.

The crack was impossibly long—even with his spiritual powers, Marcus couldn't detect where it ended.

The energy waves he sensed emanated deep within this crevice.

An even stronger suction force burst forth from the fissure, instantly enveloping Marcus.

His body was being dragged inexorably toward the crack's dark maw.

Marcus could easily break free from the mysterious pull with his strength.

But something held him back. A gut feeling that the depths of this crevice held secrets he needed to uncover.

The crack stretched endlessly downward, but it was narrow, barely wide enough for an adult to squeeze through.

Marcus continued his descent through the crevice, diving for half an hour, unable to gauge how deep he'd gone.

As he pushed through the endless darkness, a faint glimmer of light suddenly appeared below.

He picked up his pace, and finally, his body emerged from the confining crack.

Below him stretched a vast underwater realm.

The water here was surprisingly bright.

He could clearly make out every detail of this hidden domain.

A massive underwater mountain rose from the seafloor, its peak continuously spewing dense black smoke mixed with glowing red magma.

The volcanic activity had warmed the surrounding waters significantly, and Marcus could feel the pleasant change in temperature.

An incredibly vast submarine city lay at the base of this colossal underwater mountain range.

The city was filled with medieval Weston Land architecture, with moss-covered castles and crumbling walls that whispered tales of bygone eras.

As Marcus' body materialized in this underwater city, his heart trembled uncontrollably.

He had never imagined finding such a massive underwater city beneath the mysterious Morvalis Ice Wall in his wildest dreams!

Though abandoned, Marcus could still spot countless traces of human habitation throughout the submerged metropolis.

Everything pointed to this underwater city's once-thriving past.

He struggled to imagine what kind of people could have called this place home.

He'd heard legends about underwater cities before.

Back then, Marcus had dismissed them as mere fiction.

After all, humans were land-dwellers. The idea of underwater civilization seemed like pure fantasy.

But now, facing this enormous city, Marcus realized those legends weren't just tall tales.

"ROAR, ROAR..."

Suddenly, a violent roar echoed through the void nearby.

The sound rippled through the water like a terrifying shockwave.

The massive underwater city shuddered, and several ancient structures, already worn by time, crumbled under the force of the blast!

Marcus frowned as his eyes swept across the surrounding area.

He quickly spotted several massive shapes gliding through the water towards his position.

Their movement stirred up powerful undercurrents in their wake.

These creatures were about 330 feet long, shark-like in appearance, but covered in thick, heavy scales.

Their gaping maws revealed rows of razor-sharp teeth, and their blood-red eyes radiated an unmistakable bloodthirst.

Marcus felt his scalp tingle with unease.

He never expected such terrifying sea monsters lurking in these deep-sea crevices!

These creatures weren't just enormous compared to blue whales or megalodon sharks! They emanated an overwhelming aura of power.

Those razor-sharp teeth looked capable of crushing a mountain!

He knew without a doubt that these sea monsters were targeting him.

Not knowing their true strength, Marcus decided to retreat.

Even underwater, his movement speed remained unaffected.

He tried to dash to safety but was caught in some invisible force field.

The Binding Force felt like an invisible prison, completely trapping Marcus within the area.

The sea monsters let out thunderous roars, their eyes gleaming with savage hunger as they stared at Marcus.

One of the beasts opened its massive jaws and lunged at him with frightening speed.

Despite its enormous size, the creature moved with shocking speed.

In the blink of an eye, it was upon Marcus, who barely managed to dodge to the side.

He had just narrowly escaped the monster's deadly attack.

At that moment, another sea monster charged toward him jaws gaping.

Its massive maw generated a potent suction force.

The force was so strong it manipulated the water currents, pulling Marcus' body helplessly toward its waiting jaws!
