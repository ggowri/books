Chapter 361 Easily Defeated

Grace had no idea that Marcus possessed extraordinary abilities.

She thought, "These Crankhurstians are utterly shameless.

Their side claims to be 9th-dan black belt champions in Karate, yet they want to challenge the Uswad side in martial arts.

This is completely unfair.

If we do not accept the challenge, these Crankhurstians will definitely mock us mercilessly!"

"Ms. Mitchell, are you saying that Mr. Fitzgerald is afraid?"

Terry raised an eyebrow, pausing before continuing, "If Mr. Fitzgerald is afraid, then he should apologize for wasting our time earlier!

"If the apology satisfies us, we can call off this match."

These words made Grace even angrier.

"Mr. Fitzgerald does not even know you, why should he apologize? Do you Crankhurstians not understand reason?"

Zane responded, "Reason is built on strength. Those without strength do not deserve to speak of it!"

Seeing the right moment, Easton stood up and fixed his gaze on Marcus. "Mr. Fitzgerald, do not worry. We are only sparring. I will try not to hurt you. We Crankhurstian are always reasonable. I hope you make the right choice."

Grace gritted her teeth in anger.

But Marcus waved to her, glanced at Zane and Easton, and said with a smile. "My Uswadic Martial Arts never fight the weak, but since you want to spar, I will indulge you."

With that, he stood up from his seat, walked to the center of the living room, and beckoned to Easton.

Easton frowned. He had not expected Marcus to be so fearless. "The space here is too small. Mayor Benjamin, please arrange for a proper combat ring so I can show my skills."

Benjamin was about to speak, but Marcus interrupted, "To fight you, there is no need for a ring. We can do it right here."

The faces of the Griffin family turned dark.

Charlotte sneered at Marcus. "Easton is a 9th-dan black belt champion in Karate. You will regret this."

Before she could finish, Easton stood up angrily and walked toward Marcus.

"Hold on."

Marcus waved his hand at him.

Easton smirked. "Are you backing out? If so, apologize to our Griffin family right now. Kneel and bow your head, and I might forgive you."

Marcus sneered, "You misunderstood me. You alone are not enough. Bring your Uncle Zane too. I think he is also a martial artist."

This statement shocked everyone.

No one expected Marcus to challenge two people at once.

Grace's eyes widened.

Though she was worried for Marcus, she had to admit that his fearless demeanor was undeniably impressive.

"Are you sure you want to take on two people?"

Zane asked, his brow furrowed.

"I do not want to repeat myself, and I am not wasting time with people like you. Come at me now. If I cannot take both of you down in three seconds, I will admit defeat!"

Marcus' words were full of provocation, completely enraging Zane and Easton.

They rushed toward him. Easton took the first strike, launching a powerful punch at Marcus. The punch was not only strong but also tricky in angle. With its suddenness, a normal person would have difficulty dodging it and would likely be knocked down.

But Marcus simply sidestepped and swiftly dodged the punch.

At the same time, he struck Easton's neck with his arm. A loud thud echoed.

Easton screamed in pain and collapsed to the ground, writhing in agony.

In just one move, he was completely incapacitated.

Zane yelled and performed a spinning kick toward Marcus. Marcus did not even bother to dodge.

As the kick came toward him, he landed a punch on Zane's thigh.

Another dull thud sounded. Zane screamed and fell to the ground, knocking him back down as he tried to get up.

Both Easton and Zane had only taken one hit each, but the pain was unbearable. They rolled on the floor in agony, looking utterly helpless.

The entire process, though it seemed complex, happened in less than two seconds.

Two 9th-dan black belt Karate masters from Crankhurst were knocked to the ground by Marcus.

Marcus only made two moves and quickly defeated Zane and Easton.

The onlookers were stunned.

Especially Terry and Charlotte, who looked on in complete shock.
