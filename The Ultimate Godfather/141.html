Marcus came to the Aston Martin dealership and chose a new model, the Aston Martin DBS.

It had a long history and outstanding performance in the entire Aston Martin series.

Marcus particularly liked its low-key, elegant tone.

Unlike Lamborghini, Ferrari, and McLaren, Aston Martin was not familiar to most people.

It was very suitable for Marcus.

He paid the bill and left. Including the optional accessories, the total cost was more than six million dollars.

It was just a drop in the bucket for him now.

Marcus got into the latest Aston Martin DBS and stepped on the accelerator as the 4.0T V8 engine made a charming roar.

Tens of thousands of accessories worked together to serve him alone, and the car shot out like an arrow.

Compared to his Ferrari, the interior of the Aston Martin was more luxurious and much quieter.

It gave a feeling of elegance and calmness.

Marcus drove on the streets of Solomia on a rainy night and attracted many people's attention.

It was already six o'clock in the evening after he bought the car.

Catherine told Marcus her home address on WhatsApp.

He pulled up to her house in his Aston Martin.​

It was an old house built in the 1990s and allocated to Laura by the government.

Until now, their family of three still lived here.

In fact, Laura had already completed divorce proceedings with Philip Wilson, Catherine's father.

But he refused to leave, continued to drink and gamble, and squandered all the family's property, leaving only this old house that was barely habitable.

Marcus arrived at the courtyard and immediately found it old-fashioned.

It was overgrown with weeds, and the ground was muddy because it was raining.

The walls in the corridor were peeling and leaking, and rats even occasionally came into view.

Marcus bought some fruit and supplements at the convenience store downstairs and then knocked on the door of Catherine's house.

She opened the door in her apron and smiled sweetly, "Mr. Fitzgerald, come in."

Marcus put the gifts on the floor and noticed that Thomas was there, too, watching TV on the sofa in the living room.

"Hey, Dr. Fitzgerald, you are here, too," Thomas immediately sneered sarcastically.

Marcus wasn't surprised that Catherine invited Thomas.

After all, when Laura was hospitalized, Thomas helped Catherine a lot. He even drove them home personally, so it was natural that he was invited to the meal.

"Dr. Fitzgerald, have you brought the car?" Thomas asked tentatively with a malicious smile.

Marcus nodded lightly.

Thomas continued, "Aston Martin?"

Marcus made no reply and just threw the car key in front of Thomas.

Thomas was stunned. The sight of the two wings on the car keys instantly embarrassed him.

It was truly the Aston Martin logo.

Could it be that he really bought an Aston Martin?

It was impossible.

It was absolutely impossible!

The car key must be fake.

It was very likely that he got a fake product on Amazon just to show off!

Thomas was lost in thought.

At that moment, there were already various dishes on the dining table.

Catherine spent an afternoon in the kitchen and finally made a hearty meal.

"Marcus, Thomas, let's have dinner," Laura said.

Marcus put away the car key and sat down.

Thomas was upset. While thinking about how to make a fool of Marcus later, he sat down, too.

"Marcus, Thomas, help yourselves and take it easy. Don't be polite.

"Catherine has been preparing the dishes in the kitchen all afternoon to thank you," Laura smiled.

She looked at Marcus and said, "Marcus, without you, I would have died a long time ago."

Marcus grinned and waved his hand, saying, "You're welcome, Laura. Catherine and I are friends. It's just a small favor."

At the same time, Catherine had finished her work and sat down to eat.

Thomas complained, "Laura, Marcus' medical skills are really excellent, and he can even cure cancer.

"However, he is a bit of a boaster. But no one is perfect. It's normal that everyone has some shortcomings."

After hearing what Thomas said, Catherine knew that he was trying to stir up trouble again. Just as she was about to speak, Laura asked curiously, "Why is Marcus a boaster? I think he is pretty good, low-key, and honest. If Catherine can have a boyfriend like him, I will definitely be very satisfied."

Laura's praise for Marcus made Thomas' heart sink.

However, he suppressed his anger and explained, "Laura, you forgot what happened during the day. Marcus' car broke down. It was a Volkswagen Bora worth 100 thousand dollars.

"But he kept saying that he would buy a luxury car worth millions of dollars. Just now, he used a fake car key to fool me. How hypocritical!"
