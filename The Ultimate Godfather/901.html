Chapter 865 Breakthrough to Acquired State Three!-1

Waves of Numinous Energy coursed through Marcus' energy channels, eventually converging in the energy sea within his Spirit Core.

A tiny point of light, no larger than a bean, continued to hover inside his Spirit Core, emitting a faint glow.

This fragment of soul belonged to someone from the Ancient Land of Anak.

It had temporarily taken refuge within Marcus' Spirit Core.

Marcus had confirmed the stranger's words through careful observation over time. The soul fragment did not threaten his body.

It merely occupied a minuscule space within his Spirit Core.

Focusing his mind, Marcus began channeling the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire to refine the absorbed Numinous Energy.

Thanks to his previous experience, the refinement process came naturally to him.

The abundance of Numinous Energy in his opponent's body presented a unique challenge.

The refinement process stretched on for five grueling hours before reaching completion.

After fully processing the Numinous Energy, Marcus achieved a breakthrough, advancing to Acquired State Three!

His power had ascended to an entirely new level!

"Whew..."

Marcus exhaled deeply, gradually releasing his hold on the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire.

Marcus' complexion glowed with vitality as his aura surged like a boundless ocean.

After absorbing their Numinous Energy, Marcus had reached new heights in his Wizard abilities.

He had now attained the rank of a Level Seven Wizard.

However, this magical achievement still paled in comparison to his warrior prowess.

Yet even so, he stood among the elite spellcasters in the magical realm.

With that taken care of, Marcus emptied the Spatial Rings he'd claimed from the two Return Essence stage warriors.

He methodically cataloged each item he found.

The rings contained various pills, crystals, amulets, weapons, treasures, and currency.

While searching through one of the Spatial Rings, he discovered a journal.

The journal contained detailed records of its owner's recent movements and activities.

The owner was identified as Damien Caldwell, an ancient figure in the world of Uswad Ancient Martial Arts.

According to the journal entries, this man had walked the earth for over a thousand years.

That equated to ten complete cycles of samsara.

The journal meticulously documented Damien's journey of cultivation and advancement.

It chronicled his progression from novice warrior through the Foundation Establishment Stage until he reached the Return Essence stage.

He eventually rose to fame as a Stellar Lord in the world of Ancient Martial Arts in Uswad.

The records documented every detail meticulously, making it essentially a biographical chronicle.

According to the notes, Damien had made three attempts to explore the Morvalis Ice Wall region.

He had tried to uncover the mysteries of the Morvalis Ice Wall and venture into the world beyond it.

However, all three attempts failed.

Additionally, the chronicles recorded an incident where the space-time gate at the Morvalis Ice Wall lost control.

This phenomenon occurred at Damien's training ground about 1,000 years ago and lasted several decades.

During these decades, people from the outside world constantly invaded the known world.

These invaders acted without restraint, wreaking havoc through murder and destruction across the known world.

The known world endured decades of turmoil because of their actions.

Godfather eventually quelled the upheaval by gathering experts from around the known world.

He then sealed off the space-time gate at Morvalis Ice Wall.

From that moment on, Godfather rose to the pinnacle of the known world, becoming a figure whom all living beings looked up to with admiration.

As Marcus continued to flip through the pages, he uncovered more of Damien's secrets.

Surprisingly, he discovered Damien had even documented this operation in his notes.

Damien's objectives for this mission were straightforward.
