Yet Marcus remained unfazed by the middle-aged man's oppressive aura.

He held considerable influence within that martial arts family.

Though both were at Ancient Martial Stage Six, his mastery was far more stable than Marcus'.

His aura alone made other Ancient Martial Stage Six masters uneasy.

Yet Marcus showed no reaction whatsoever. It was truly astounding.

The middle-aged man's gaze froze, studying Marcus intently.

It felt like millions of pounds of pressure, bearing down on Marcus with overwhelming might.

Marcus stood unaffected, as if separated from this space by an invisible barrier. No matter how terrifying the other's coercion was, it couldn't touch him.

"Is that all you've got?"

His aura surged rapidly, quickly reaching a new power level.

"Though he's only mastered the third level of the Heavenstrike, it should be more than enough to deal with this youth!"

In his mind, this strike would end Marcus' life.

The punch appeared weak and flimsy, with no Numinous Energy surrounding it.

Yet here was Marcus, appearing so arrogant as to face the Heavenstrike with such an essential move.

The crowd shot him mockingly, eager to see him crushed under the opponent's strike.

BOOM!

He couldn't believe Marcus dared to counter him with such a basic Fist Ray, and his face showed absolute confidence in his victory.

Even in death, his eyes remained wide open.

Never in his wildest dreams had he imagined he would meet his end today atop Divine Mount.

All because of his arrogance, his fatal mistake of looking down on others.

In his mind, Marcus had been nothing but a worthless junior.

Even though their cultivation levels were equal, he believed Marcus couldn't possibly compare to him.

He'd assumed he could lord over Marcus, who would have no choice but to submit to his questioning.

Instead, he'd kicked a steel plate and paid for it with his life!

Before this, they thought the middle-aged man had unleashed his ultimate technique, and Marcus would surely pay the price for his actions.

With just a casual Fist Ray powered by pure physical strength, Marcus instantly ended his opponent!

Everyone trembled at such overwhelming power. They never dreamed this seemingly ordinary young man could possess such terrifying combat ability.

A deathly stillness fell over the space, no one daring to speak.

Especially those who had mocked Marcus earlier, who kept their mouths firmly shut.

Such was the way of this world.

The strong devoured the weak, who endured endless bullying. Only with formidable power could one earn others' respect.
