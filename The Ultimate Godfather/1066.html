Chapter 965 Something Wrong with His Brain-2

Hearing this, Eulalia and Isolde immediately showed expressions of astonishment on their faces.

They knew Thorne was wealthy but never imagined he would have so much money!

And Thorne had claimed that the eight or nine hundred million was only his savings, not including his parents' savings.

His parents were high-ranking government officials, and how many officials were honest and clean-handed these days?

It meant that his family's wealth was was likely an astronomical number.

Looking at Thorne's smug face, Chloe rolled her eyes.

Eight or nine hundred million dollars, and he had the nerve to show off?

Chloe knew Marcus had spent hundreds of billions of dollars to acquire Royal Entertainment.

Plus, he owned many other publicly traded companies, each with a market value exceeding hundreds of billions of dollars.

In other words, Thorne was nothing but a clown compared to Marcus.

Yet he was completely oblivious, showing off in front of Marcus. It was ridiculous!

"So what do you say, kid? Name your price. As long as it's not too much, I can make it happen. With my money, you'll be set for life!" Thorne continued, his entire demeanor excessively arrogant.

Marcus raised an eyebrow slightly and asked Thorne, "Isn't your annual salary as Sales Director at SiliconWave Technologies only 10 million dollars? Where did you get all that money from?"

It was what Marcus wanted to know. After all, SiliconWave Technologies was also one of his industries.

If he could discover some parasites within the group at this time and clean them out promptly, why not?

Thorne had no idea that the young man standing before him was actually the secret owner of SiliconWave Technologies.

From his perspective, Marcus was clearly from the bottom rungs of society, so even if he revealed his money-making schemes, the guy couldn't do anything about it.

So Thorne said, "You seem to have a screw loose. No wonder you're stuck at the bottom of society forever. Salaries are fixed, but people are flexible."

He continued, "My position as sales director isn't just for show. You honestly think I only make that 10 million dollars each year? Let me tell you the truth! My influence at SiliconWave Technologies is beyond your imagination. I can easily skim a little off the top, and boom, there's my money. Do you see any rich person making a fortune from their base salary? If you dig into it carefully, every wealthy person has gotten their hands dirty. Don't you believe me or not?"

Hearing Thorne's words, both Eulalia and Isolde couldn't help but give him a thumbs up.

Especially Isolde, she would love to embezzle, too. Still, since Cecilia truly took the helm of McHale Pharmaceuticals Group, all the company policies had become extremely strict.

She had no opportunity to embezzle anymore and could only earn her basic salary.

Because of this, Isolde was incredibly envious of Thorne.

"Oh, I get it now. So all your money comes from embezzling from SiliconWave Technologies, right?" Marcus said with a smile.

Thorne was about to nod when he quickly realized something, glaring fiercely at Marcus. "Kid, why do I feel like you're fishing for information? Tell me, what the fuck are you trying to do?"

No sooner had Thorne finished speaking than Isolde chimed in with a mocking expression. "Thorne, he's just an extra, bottom of the social ladder. Even if he knew some secrets, what could he possibly do? You're overthinking this!"

Thorne laughed loudly. "Haha, right! I was just thinking better safe than sorry." His gaze toward Marcus was filled with mockery.

"Talking to you is a waste of breath. Just name your price already. If you keep stalling, you won't get a single penny!"

Marcus looked at Thorne and said, "What I want isn't your money. It's your job."

Hearing Marcus' words, Thorne's expression froze momentarily before a hint of intrigue crossed his face. "Kid, did I hear you right? You want my job? How the hell do you think you're going to get that? Are you talking nonsense? I think you've got mental issues!"

Eulalia and Isolde couldn't help but laugh, their gaze toward Marcus brimming with derision.

Marcus ignored their reactions and said to Thorne, "Taking your job is actually quite simple—I'll just have you fired."

Marcus' shocking statement once again left Eulalia, Isolde, and Thorne stunned, their eyes filled with disbelief as they stared at him.

Isolde crossed her arms over her chest and said with a mocking smile, "This guy really does have something wrong with his brain! Chloe, I can't believe you found yourself such an idiot for a boyfriend. You should break up with him right away. Look at him spouting nonsense—I'm genuinely worried he might snap one day and do something crazy to you!"
