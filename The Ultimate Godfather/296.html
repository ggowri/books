Chapter 295 Regret

"Lisa, I bought this car. I had an Alphard before, but it was scrapped, so I drove this. How about this? I'll get a few cabs now," Marcus explained without hiding anything, and he just told the truth.

But Marcus was just bragging in Lisa's eyes.

She rolled her eyes helplessly and told Aria, "Mom, look at him. Even if we came to Uncle Richard's house, we'd need a cab to the hospital, so what's the point of us coming here? Are we here to watch Marcus show off his wealth?"

After Lisa finished speaking, Aria lowered her head and began to regret it in her mind.

"Why don't you have Allan come over and pick us up?"

"Okay!"

Lisa nodded and told everyone, "There are so many people here, so I asked my boyfriend Allan to come and pick us up. He's attending a wedding nearby, so he'll be here soon."

With that, Lisa dialed Allan's number.

Allan was nearby, not far from them.

Also, the wedding was almost over, so Allan agreed to Lisa.

After a while, a white Sienna commercial vehicle was driven into the Bayside Villa.

A tall man with a buzz cut and a suit got out of the car.

He was none other than Lisa's boyfriend, Allan Ames.

Allan was a native of Solomia.

His parents were both public officials who earned high and stable incomes.

He didn't do well when he was a child, and he didn't apply to college, so he went to work at the wholesale market. With his connections, Allan rose to the position of deputy manager.

With a salary of over two hundred thousand dollars a year, Allan was now at the elite level in Solomia.

More importantly, as a local native and with his parents' connections, Allan knew a lot of people, so he had friends in many different industries.

Hearing Lisa say he needed to pick up several people, Allan drove his friend's Toyota Sienna over.

"Allan, you're here!"

In truth, Nick and Aria were even close to Allan as if he were their son.

Seeing Allan, their eyes were filled with excitement.

"Yeah, why didn't you tell me in advance when you came to Solomia? I could have sent a company car to pick you up at the train station."

Allan nodded at Nick and Aria with a smile.

"We came over to Richard's house. We weren't going to bother you, and you were busy with your usual work, so we took a cab here," Aria said with a smile.

"Allan, this is Richard, Melinda, and Marcus."

Nick immediately introduced them to Allan.

Allan glanced at the grand villa in front of him before greeting Richard, Melinda, and Marcus respectively.

"The houses in Cedars Villas are expensive, right?"

Allan said enviously.

"Yeah. It's worth eight million dollars, but I have sort of forgotten the exact price," Marcus replied calmly.

Allan, Richard and Melinda couldn't help but take a deep breath.

Eight million dollars was the money an ordinary person would never earn in a lifetime.

Richard and his family were now living in a villa worth eight million dollars.

"Honey, is this villa truly worth so much money?"

Lisa asked Allan in a whisper, feeling even more jealous inside.

Allan nodded. "Richard is so rich!"

As the deputy manager of the wholesale market, Allan had seen the world.

There were even tens of millions of dollars in Cedars Villas.

Looking at the location of this villa, eight million dollars was considered a bargain.

"Are they rich? I told you before that Marcus lived at his wife's house, so the villa was definitely bought by his rich wife. How could their family afford it?"

Lisa said.

"Oh, alright."

Allan immediately realized what Lisa meant.

He couldn't help but look at Marcus deeply.

Allan thought, "Why was this guy so lucky to be loved by a rich girl?

If I could get a rich girl like that, I'd do anything!"

"Richard, Melinda, Marcus, hurry up and get in the car. Nick's illness can't be delayed any longer," Allan said after he came back to his senses.

Allan opened the side sliding doors on both sides of the car as he walked up to the main driver's seat.

They got in their cars as Allan started the car and drove towards Solomia Hospital.

"Marcus, you've married into such a rich family, so you know better doctors, right? Get Nick in touch with a doctor, or he won't live longer," Aria told Marcus in the car.

No sooner had Marcus said anything than Lisa got a bit impatient. "Mom, I've told you before that Allan has a lot of connections, and he knows a doctor in the cardiovascular department at Solomia Hospital, so we'll get him when the time comes. Marcus just got a nice marriage, but how could he possibly know a powerful doctor?"
