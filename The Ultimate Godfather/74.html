As Marcus left the manor with Natalia on his back, he returned to Kingston and took her on a plane back to Solomia.

Marcus felt relieved only after settling Natalia in the Bayside Villa, where Diana was protecting her.

"We must build up our own power as soon as possible." He secretly made up his mind.

Now, he had too many enemies, and if he was not careful, he would easily make mistakes.

Marcus did not care, but he did not want to see the people around him implicated.

Now that he had killed Leo, the Brown family in Kingston would definitely not let it go.

Therefore, Marcus prepared to go to Kingston as soon as possible to purchase a production line from a pharmaceutical company. As he returned to Solomia, he began to build up his power.

Returning to Kingston, Marcus went directly to BioFusion Pharmaceuticals, according to the address.

The person who received him was a young woman with an elegant temperament and beautiful face.

And this young woman was Josie Shelman, the president of BioFusion Pharmaceuticals.

Marcus had heard of her name before. She was a figure on the list of the richest people in Uswad.

Josie was a well-known female private entrepreneur in Kingston.

In just five years, BioFusion Pharmaceuticals, which she founded, became a leading manufacturer of medical equipment in Uswad.

Its products were not only popular in the country but also sold well overseas.

As he saw him today, Marcus was surprised.

Before this, he always thought that Josie would be an old woman, but he didn't expect her to be so young and beautiful.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, I heard that you are a senior executive of the Solomia Golden Group. Today I see that you are indeed young, promising and talented." She was tall and looked very capable in her professional attire.

Her brown and smooth hair was curled into waves, making her even more beautiful and charming. Her every move exuded the unique charm of a working woman.

"Ms. Shelman, thank you for your compliment. To make it short, I came here mainly to buy some medical production equipment." Marcus went straight to the point.

Josie showed a charming smile and said, "Almost everyone who comes to me has the same purpose, and you are no exception, Mr. Fitzgerald."

"May I ask what type of machine you would like to order, Mr. Fitzgerald? We have many options here, and you can choose one first." She handed a product brochure to him.

Marcus had previously made an in-depth study of BioFusion Pharmaceuticals' products.

After just a quick glance, he had a plan in mind. "I want your most advanced production lines, a total of ten, to be delivered within three months. I wonder if your company can meet this requirement."

A hint of surprise appeared on Josie's delicate face. "Mr. Fitzgerald, our company's top-end production line is not cheap. One production line costs about 1 billion dollars, and 10 production lines cost 10 billion dollars...In terms of funding..."

Marcus said firmly, "Ms. Shelman, don't worry about the funds. I can pay the 10 billion dollars in cash in one lump sum."

Josie looked at Marcus deeply. Most of the people who came to her to order production lines thought that the production lines in other countries were too expensive.

The price of her top-of-the-line production line was almost the same as those production lines in Weston Land.

She never thought that this young man would spend a huge amount of money to order a top-of-the-line production line from her. It really made Josie look at him with admiration.

Thinking of this, she reminded him, "In fact, our company can provide personalized optional services. In layman's terms, unimportant links in the production line can be replaced with low-priced machines, which can greatly save costs. Mr. Fitzgerald, if you are willing, I can make a detailed plan for you, which can save at least more than 2 billion dollars."

Josie's actions also left a very good impression on Marcus.

She could obviously make more money, but she only cared about the customers. No wonder BioFusion Pharmaceuticals was able to break the foreign monopoly in a short period of time and become the domestic leader. It was indeed different from others.

Later, Josie prepared to customize a plan for Marcus.

At this time, a secretary rushed in and said breathlessly, "Ms. Shelman, it's bad. Mr. Vincent Anderson has had a heart attack and is in the hospital."
