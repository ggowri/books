Marcus tried to practice Frostbind and Stormcall, which were found in the Spatial Ring of Wizard before.

With the previous foundation, it was not as difficult to cultivate other magic for him.

It only took him two hours to master the essentials of these two kinds of magic.

He believed these two kinds of magic could have a certain lethality soon.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, the first batch of guns has been reformed. Do you want to come back and have a look?"

Early in the morning, Marcus received Diana's message.

He had been looking forward to the transformation of guns.

Therefore, after receiving this news, he immediately said goodbye to Melina and rushed to Bayside Villa.

However, when Marcus drove to the Bayside highway, he felt that there were several gods sweeping through the sky.

These gods were so powerful that they enveloped this space and aimed his car.

Marcus frowned. They were up no good.

"You waste my son's training. You can't escape today. Stop the car and prepare to die!" A cold voice sounded.

It seemed to spread in a specific space. For others, this space was still quiet and peaceful and they could hear nothing.

Marcus was the only one who could hear this voice, which made his eardrum slightly numb.

Asher!

A name suddenly came to Marcus' mind.

The reason was very simple. He had just abolished Truman's cultivation in the Southern Market.

And Truman was his son.

To Marcus' surprise, Asher found him so fast.

Marcus pulled over.

Then he dodged into a mountain valley, which was surrounded by towering cliffs. Few people came to this place

Meanwhile, three voices sounded in the void.

Then three figures emerged and released a terrifying aura.

Two people on the side who looked about in their forties both reached Ancient Martial Stage Four.

The leader in the middle was an old man with a child's face and grey hair.

He seemed to be over sixty, but he was full of energy.

He was the leader of the Astral Serpent Order.

The expert of the peak of Ancient Martial Stage Five!

Marcus couldn't help but gasp when he sensed the cultivation of Asher.

Through the practice last night, he had just reached the minor completion stage of Ancient Martial Stage Four.

He never expected that he would meet Asher.

Asher was much more powerful than Marcus and he brought two experts with him.

How could Marcus beat Asher?

"Did you waste my son's training?"

Asher's eyes rested on Marcus, and his eyes were full of resentment.

"So what?"

Marcus didn't deny it.

Even in the face of Asher, Marcus didn't have any fear.

He was a tough man, so no matter what happened, he would never admit defeat.

"At a young age, you have no fear in the face of me. I didn't expect you to be a courageous person.

"However, I can't forgive you for what you did to my son, so you must die today!" Asher said with a cold voice.

He waved his hand.

There were bursts of lines in the palm of his hand.

In an instant, an array was formed in the void and covered the space.

With a strange force, the scene around him changed.

The seaside highway, as well as the mountain where he was both disappeared and turned into a deserted wilderness.

The wilderness was endless, and there was nothing as if he was in hell!

"This is the Rune of Illusion!"

Marcus called out.

To not affect the mundane world, some experts of Ancient Martial Art used this method to shield the battlefield when fighting.

This Rune was similar to the Rune of the Blind in Southern Market, but they were still different.

This was an important reason why ancient fighters were so mysterious in the eyes of ordinary people.

Warriors, especially some powerful people who had achieved a certain level of accomplishment, were invisible most of the time.

Sometimes, from the perspective of ordinary people, many scenes were visible to the naked eye, but they were not.

It was very likely that something was happening in that area that subverted the cognition of ordinary people.

What ordinary people could see was less than one ten-thousandth of the universe.

It was just like this moment.

Asher led people to try to kill Marcus. Before taking action, he shielded the battlefield.

But in the eyes of ordinary people, it was still the mountain before, and there was no difference.
