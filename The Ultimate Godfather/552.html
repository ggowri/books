Chapter 551 The Same Hometown

They got into the BMW 3 Series.

Marcus took his place in the driver's seat and asked Lila, "Where do you live? I'll drive you home."

"Urban Apartments."

Lila answered softly, stealing glances at Marcus' chiseled profile. She felt a strange flutter in her chest as if she still held onto specific hopes.

Marcus started the car and drove the BMW 3 Series to Urban Apartments.

He noticed that the complex definitely showed its age.

From the looks of it, the buildings had to be at least twenty years old.

After asking, Marcus learned that Lila was renting her place.

She hadn't bought a property in Kingston yet.

That made sense, really.

Kingston was a pricey place to live, every square inch costing a fortune.

Lila's fame had skyrocketed, sure, but it was a recent phenomenon. In reality, she hadn't raked in that much dough yet, certainly not enough to buy a place in Kingston.

They climbed the stairs together. The stairwell was old and narrow, crammed with electrical wires and pipes, reeking of moldy.

Lila lived on the top floor.

When they reached her floor, they found a drunk young man slumped in front of Lila's door.

As soon as he saw Lila, he stumbled to his feet, his eyes darting between her and Marcus with barely concealed hatred.

"Lila, why haven't you been answering my calls? Where have you been these past few days?"

Lila's face twisted with disgust. "I was performing. Where else would I be?"

The young man shot back, "Why didn't you tell me? Is this how you treat me?"

His eyes locked onto Marcus again. "Oh, I get it now. You weren't performing at all! You were fooling around with this guy!

"I've been pursuing you for years, taking care of your every need. When you first came to Kingston, I helped you so much, but you never gave me a chance. Now you're running around with some random guy? How could you do this to me, Lila?"

The young man grew increasingly agitated, his eyes burning with resentment.

His name was Shawn Wayne, and he came from the same hometown as Lila.

They'd both moved to Kingston a year ago to chase their dreams.

They met by chance and, being from the same hometown, naturally grew closer.

They would help each other out from time to time.

Since Lila was a young woman alone in the city, Shawn often helped her with various things.

And Lila's stunning beauty had captured Shawn's heart ultimately.

He'd been pursuing her relentlessly ever since.

Although Lila was grateful to Shawn, she had no romantic feelings for him.

But Shawn kept pursuing her relentlessly, and seeing his genuine feelings and their shared hometown connection that made the conversation flow easily, Lila started to consider giving him a chance.

However, the night before she planned to say yes, Shawn couldn't handle being alone and went to see a prostitute.

He even brought the girl back to his place, where Lila happened to catch them.

Lila felt sick to her stomach, and that tiny spark that had started to grow was instantly extinguished.

Years flew by, and Lila became a top social media influencer with millions of followers, her songs becoming hits across the country.

Meanwhile, Shawn remained unmotivated, working as a delivery driver and drinking himself into oblivion almost every day.

He still kept harassing Lila.

Today, clearly drunk again, he was waiting outside her door to ambush her.

"Who I hang out with and where I go is none of your business, Shawn. How many times do I have to tell you to leave me alone? Why won't you listen?

"I've held back from calling the police because we're from the same hometown, but if you keep this up, I won't be so nice anymore!"

Lila said with disgust written all over her face.

She used to have a decent impression of Shawn, but ever since that time he went down a destructive path and got caught with a prostitute, any positive feelings she had vanished, replaced by pure revulsion.

"Lila, I never thought you were such a cheap slut. Acting all pure and innocent with me, when really you're just trash that anyone can use. What a fool I was ever to like you! I must have been blind!

"Go ahead then, if you've got the guts, call the cops on me! I haven't done anything illegal! They can't touch me!"

Shawn sneered, looking at Lila with utter contempt.

The thought that Lila had rejected him only to be ridden by this man filled him with hatred.

Before Shawn could say another word, Lila's hand cracked across his face in fury.

When they first met, she'd thought Shawn was actually a decent person, reasonable, at least.

But now, whatever had happened to him, he'd turned into such a brute.

He was the one who'd sunk into depravity first, yet here he was, spewing vicious lies about Lila.

Lila couldn't take it anymore.

"You bitch, how dare you hit me! I'll kill you!"
