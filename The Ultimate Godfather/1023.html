Chapter 936 A House Visit-1

Natalia's face turned even redder when she heard this, and she quickly picked up her underwear and hid it behind her back.

It caused Diana and Pearl to give Marcus strange looks, making him feel completely uncomfortable.

Since Natalia was on vacation, Diana prepared a table full of delicious food that evening to welcome her back.

Marcus enjoyed a feast with the four women, then returned to his room to begin cultivation.

His cultivation had already broken through to the peak of Acquired State Four.

With the help of pills that enhanced his cultivation and the miraculous effects of the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire, it only took Marcus two hours to raise his cultivation to Acquired State Five.

After advancing to the Return Essence stage, each breakthrough in cultivation brought earth-shattering improvements to his abilities.

Although he had only advanced one State, Marcus' power had increased significantly again.

Now, he even had confidence in his ability to match up against an Innate Return Essence Realm Stage Two expert.

However, this remained merely an estimate, which only existed in theory.

Having never encountered an Innate Return Essence Realm expert before, Marcus had no idea what level of power such masters truly possessed.

After his breakthrough, Marcus fully stabilized his cultivation an hour before retrieving the Spatial Rings from the three Tucker family elders. One by one, he cracked their seals and inventoried their contents.

As experts who had cultivated for countless years at the Return Essence stage, these three possessed terrifying wealth in their Spatial Rings.

Even though Marcus had mentally prepared himself, he couldn't help being shocked by what he found.

The money alone was staggering, but what surprised Marcus was the sheer abundance of pills, treasures, and weapons that left him speechless.

Each Spatial Ring carried more value than everything he currently owned!

Those three Spatial Rings together nearly tripled Marcus' wealth!

His trip to confront the Tucker family hadn't been in vain. At the very least, he'd gained an enormous supplement of cultivation resources, laying a solid foundation for his advancement to higher States.

Now, he could casually pop pills like candy to break through to the next cultivation level.

Not because he could refine them himself but because he'd killed so many enemies and claimed their possessions.

This surplus of cultivation resources was precisely why Marcus had to share the excess with his important subordinates.

Besides all that, Marcus also conducted detailed research on the Rune Disk of the Dragon's Wrath Formation.

He discovered that this Dragon's Wrath Formation Rune Disk was far more intricate than when Brett had used it.

As he had previously suspected, Brett's control of the Dragon's Wrath Formation had barely utilized twenty percent of its true power.

All the energy of the Rune was contained within this Rune Disk.

However, the Rune Disk seemed to have an internal barrier, with all its power waiting to be unlocked.

Marcus didn't rush the process; instead, he took time to familiarize himself with the mastery of Rune Disk.

After just three hours, Marcus had mastered how to use the Dragon's Wrath Formation Rune Disk by applying his knowledge of the Heavenly Circle Diagram.

He knew that once he uncovered all the potential of the Dragon's Wrath Formation, it would become a terrifying weapon of mass destruction!

Marcus had just gotten up early the next morning and entered the courtyard to stretch his body and breathe fresh air.

Suddenly, someone knocked on the front door of the villa.

Marcus raised an eyebrow slightly, walked over, and opened the door.

A man and a woman stood outside.

Both were dressed professionally and were in their late twenties.
