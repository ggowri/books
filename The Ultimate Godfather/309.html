Chapter 308 Wake Her Up

Everyone in the car looked at Marcus in puzzlement.

He wanted to try.

People wondered if he was a doctor, too.

"I told you. The patient is in danger and needs to rest. You'd better not make trouble. Otherwise, it will probably lead to the patient's illness getting worse!"

Jade refused to give way to Marcus and still treated the middle-aged woman in her own way.

Marcus said, "I said you will only accelerate the perforation of the appendix. Since you can't cure her, why don't you let me try?"

He frowned, feeling more and more that Jade was too full of herself.

Jade said, "I am a surgeon at Solariae Hospital. And this is my work permit. Please don't challenge my expertise.

"Which hospital are you from? Which medical school did you graduate from? Show us your work permit! "

Jade showed her work permit directly.

She was from Solariae Hospital.

Many people looked at Jade respectfully.

Solariae Hospital could be ranked in the top five large general hospitals in Uswad.

The doctors who worked there were usually the cream of the medical profession.

Jade was only in her twenties and was already the associate chief physician at Solariae Hospital.

She was a medical genius.

But the man didn't look like a doctor at all.

He wanted to treat patients in the midst of acute appendicitis. Overreach!

Someone said, "Right. He must show us his work permit, or we will not allow him to fool around!"

"Yes, he's not even qualified to practice medicine and shouldn't treat a patient who is seriously ill!"

The other people in the carriage were all in favor of Jade's decision.

At this time, the middle-aged woman had fainted in pain and was in a state of severe shock!

This showed that her appendix had been completely perforated.

If someone didn't take the necessary measures within ten minutes, the woman would probably not make it to the moment of getting off the train.

"Mom, wake up. Mom!"

The boy was full of pain and kept shaking his mother.

But the middle-aged woman didn't respond at all.

Jade's face turned gloomy, and she quickly took a series of rescue measures for the middle-aged woman.

But none of these worked.

"Get out of the way!"

Marcus could not stand it anymore, pushed Jade away, and put his palm directly against the woman's right lower abdomen.

The gentle Timeless Numinous Energy was released in his palm and penetrated into her abdominal cavity along the flesh of the middle-aged woman.

Timeless Numinous Energy directly wrapped around her perforated and purulent appendix.

Under the powerful healing effect of the Timeless Numinous Energy, the appendix, which had already festered and perforated, healed and recovered little by little!

In less than five minutes, the middle-aged woman's appendix returned to normal. She was even healthier than she was before her appendicitis!

It brought her face back to life, and her breathing gradually calmed down.

Then, the middle-aged woman who had already entered a state of severe shock miraculously woke up!

"Baby, where is this? Am I dead?"

After the woman woke up, she held her son's hand for the first time.

Seeing his mother awake, the boy became excited.

He said, "Mom, you didn't die. This gentleman saved you!"

The middle-aged woman looked at Marcus and said with gratitude, "Thank you for saving my life, sir!"

She bowed and thanked Marcus.

Marcus said, "Although your appendicitis has been cured, you are still very weak and need to recuperate."

This magical scene shocked everyone in the place!

Jade froze and looked at Marcus in surprise.

She said, "He really knows medical skills and woke up a patient with appendicitis perforation and severe shock in less than five minutes. Incredible!"

"Yes, the doctor has been questioning his medical qualification before. Without him, the woman may be in danger!"

"It's a miracle. My brother was also in severe shock due to appendicitis perforation. Our family lived in a mountainous area, so he didn't get timely treatment. He died before he was thirty. If we met this young man at that time, my brother would be saved!"

The crowd in the train car talked and looked at Marcus with admiration.
