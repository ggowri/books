Chapter 171 A Cigarette

Marcus felt utterly relaxed.

Conquering Yvonne had been his long-held desire, and today, it was finally fulfilled.

"Ah..."

Marcus received a WhatsApp message from Maxine: [Do you have time tomorrow night? I'd like to invite you to dinner.]

So Marcus agreed, setting the time for 8:00 PM the following evening at Solomia's finest restaurant.

After all, he was at Yvonne's house, and he didn't want to end up in the same situation as last time when Emily caught him.

However, Marcus estimated that it would still take some time before he could break through.

Additionally, his Fireball and Lightning had also improved significantly. He could now release attacks that were almost as powerful as those of a Stage Three Wizard.

For Marcus, this was a powerful trump card.

On the surface, he appeared to be just a warrior with no trace of magical energy, but he could unleash terrifying attacks that rivaled a Stage Three Wizard's power, catching his enemies completely off guard.

His Energy Manifestation, Celestial Reversal, and X-ray vision were also progressing.
