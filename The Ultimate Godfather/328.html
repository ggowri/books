Chapter 327 Emma's Arrogance

After freshening up, Marcus booked a train ticket, preparing to head downstairs to catch a taxi to the station for his return to Solomia.

However, halfway through the ride, his taxi was abruptly blocked by a convoy of vehicles.

Marcus frowned slightly and stepped out of the car.

The taxi driver, not wanting to get involved, sped off immediately.

Standing in front of Marcus were Emma and several Rivera Construction Group personnel.

"Well, well, Marcus. I bet you didn't expect to see me again!"

Emma sneered, her gaze filled with arrogance and satisfaction.

"What do you want to do?"

Marcus narrowed his eyes slightly, surprised that it was Emma who had intercepted him.

"What do I want? You put my uncle in prison, so I'm here for your life. Once you're dead, we'll be even!"

Emma's gaze suddenly became sharp.

While speaking, she waved her hand to the men at a distance.

Tony, leading a group of Black Tiger Gang members wielding machetes, stepped forward.

"Tony, break his limbs, grind his body into pulp, and feed him to the fish! Only then will I be satisfied!"

Emma gritted her teeth and barked the order at Tony.

Tony smirked, "Ms. Rivera, don't worry. You pay, and I deliver. This guy's a dead man today!"

He waved to his men beside him while talking.

Understanding his order, the Black Tiger Gang lackeys prepared to charge at Marcus.

But just as they moved, one of the tattooed gang members hurriedly stepped forward to block them.

"Mr. Vito, we can't do this!"

Tony was stunned and asked impatiently, "What the hell is wrong with you? Why are you hesitating?"

"Mr. Vito, look at this man. This guy... I think he's Mr. Fitzgerald..."

The tattooed man stammered.

Tony's body froze as if struck by lightning.

Finally, he got a good look at Marcus' face.

"Tony, what are you waiting for? Kill this guy already!"

Emma naturally didn't know the relationship between Tony and Marcus. She was annoyed at the delay.

Without hesitation, Tony delivered a resounding slap across Emma's face.

It split her lip and left her stunned. Emma shouted in disbelief, "Tony! Are you insane? Why did you hit me?"

"Shut up, you stupid woman! If you speak again, I'll kill you myself!"

Tony roared before turning to Marcus and bowing to him. "Mr. Fitzgerald, we meet again so soon..."

Tony trembled with fear when he said this.

He had seen Marcus' terrifying strength personally. Even if given ten thousand chances, Tony wouldn't dare provoke him.

He thought, "Mark, that old fool, had sent me to kill Marcus. It's practically pushing me into a fiery pit of death!

And this little wretch standing here is no better."

"What a coincidence. We meet again so soon," Marcus said, his face calm and unreadable.

It was this very expression that sent chills through Tony's spine.

He vividly remembered Marcus wearing the same look when he killed that Ancient Martial Stage Two master at the port.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, I didn't know it was you. Otherwise, I wouldn't dare to bother you even if I had a hundred lives. Don't be angry!"

Tony pleaded and bowed in front of him, constantly slapping himself.

The scene left Emma utterly stunned.

Tony, the notorious Black Tiger Gang boss, the ruler of the region's underground world, was groveling at Marcus' feet, utterly humiliated.

What shocked her even more was that she had previously investigated Marcus.

He was primarily based in Solomia and rarely appeared in Solariae.

How could she have known his reach already extended here?

"Forget it. You didn't know. Get up," Marcus said dismissively, waving a hand.

Tony exhaled deeply as though receiving a divine pardon. He immediately stood up and asked Marcus, "Mr. Fitzgerald, what should I do with this bitch? Just say the word, and I'll handle it however you want."

Marcus replied casually, "It's up to you!"

Tony's face lit up with a sinister gleam of understanding.

Emma was undeniably beautiful, and Tony had long harbored thoughts about her but had never dared act on them.

He really wanted to act on them. Still, a flicker of doubt lingered. Emma was Mark Rivera's daughter, after all.

If he acted, Mark would undoubtedly retaliate.

"Rest assured. Rivera Construction Group will cease to exist tomorrow!"

Marcus gave him a gaze to reassure him.

If anyone else had made such a statement, Tony would have dismissed it as boastful nonsense. But Marcus had spoken, and Tony believed it without question.

His gaze fell on Emma, lingering with unabashed greed. Tony grabbed Emma forcefully and dragged her toward the nearby business van.
