Chapter 861 The Senior Military Advisor-2

Maxine grew impatient when she noticed Marcus' strange expression and prolonged silence.

She had always been a firecracker, having little patience for people who dragged their feet.

"What if I told you that I am the Special Advisor? Would you believe that?"

Marcus asked Maxine directly.

Maxine rolled her eyes at Marcus and said, "Come on, Marcus, cut the jokes. I'm trying to have a serious conversation here! Opportunities like this don't come around daily!"

It was clear that Maxine didn't believe a word Marcus was saying.

Even though Marcus had always impressed her with his capabilities, Maxine couldn't convince herself that Marcus was the one who had saved Julian.

"Alright, I'll go with you then."

Seeing Maxine's disbelief, Marcus decided against further explanation.

He knew how stubborn she could be. Once she made up her mind about something, no explanation would change it.

Only when she discovered the truth for herself would she change her perspective.

It was like when they were pursuing the Black Hawk Legion's leader, Marcus had shared his strategy from the start.

But Maxine had dismissed his ideas ultimately, accusing him of slacking off.

It wasn't until Marcus turned the situation around and proved himself that Maxine finally recognized her mistake.

"When Cody gets here, I'll tell him so I can take you along with him.

"Commander Ashford values this Special Advisor. Even though you hold a high position in Solomia yourself, they're also from Solomia, so building a good relationship with them can only benefit you. I hope you take this seriously."

Maxine said with motherly concern.

She was worried that Marcus wouldn't take this matter seriously.

"Don't worry about it."

Marcus nodded with a smile.

Just then, several military vehicles drove up the mountain road and quickly pulled up in front of the police car, where Marcus and Maxine were waiting.

Several uniformed soldiers stepped down from the military vehicles.

Leading them was a tall, imposing young military officer.

He appeared to be around Marcus' age, in his early twenties. Yet, he already wore the rank of lieutenant colonel with two bars and two stars on his shoulders.

For someone in their early twenties, the rank of lieutenant colonel was quite impressive.

"They're here."

Maxine said to Marcus as she spotted them arriving.

Marcus had been dozing off, and when Maxine called, he jolted awake with a drowsy expression.

"Let's get out. Cody and his team have arrived."

Maxine reminded him again, shooting him another disapproving look when she saw he had fallen asleep.

She couldn't seem to understand his behavior at all.

She had informed Marcus about the upcoming meeting with the Special Advisor. By any logical reasoning, he shouldn't have been sleeping.

He should have used this limited time to prepare thoroughly and present himself to meet such an essential figure in the best possible state.

Instead, he had chosen to take a long nap.

After all, opportunities like this were rare.

If Maxine hadn't begged Cody for this favor, she wouldn't have had the chance to meet someone of this caliber.

It made Maxine feel like Marcus wasn't taking this matter seriously.

She was at a loss for words about Marcus' behavior.

Maxine stepped out of the car first, followed by Marcus from the driver's seat.

The young Lieutenant Colonel was none other than Cody, the son of the Rowane District Commander and a senior staff officer of the Rowane Military District.

Despite being only in his early twenties with the rank of Lieutenant Colonel, Cody commanded an inexplicable yet powerful presence just by standing there.

He was a decorated graduate of the International Military Academy, and his capabilities were genuine.

This commanding presence stemmed from his self-confidence and absolute faith in his abilities.

When Cody saw Marcus and Maxine getting out of the patrol car together, his brows furrowed slightly.

He addressed Maxine, "Officer Burnett, who might this be?"

Maxine responded, "This is my boyfriend, Marcus."

Given the intimate nature of their relationship, it was natural for her to refer to him as her boyfriend.

Though she felt somewhat awkward saying those words, Maxine was trying to adapt to the new situation.

Upon hearing this, Cody's forehead creased with concern as he remarked, "Since when did Officer Burnett get a boyfriend? The Burnett family told me you were single!"
