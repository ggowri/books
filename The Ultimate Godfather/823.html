He had no desire to waste any more time on her.

Although Kenia had behaved terribly before, Grace simply couldn't bring herself to be harsh.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, I truly apologize. I'm so sorry for disrupting our meal because of me."

After all, the owner had worked hard for his business, and money was just a number to him.
