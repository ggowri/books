Feeling the murderous aura from Marcus, Mike trembled with fear.

His body involuntarily retreated and took advantage of Marcus' inattention.

Mike pulled out a pistol at his waist and tried to shoot Marcus!

Marcus was ready. He grasped the gun body and jammed the pistol trigger.

No matter how hard Mike pulled it, the pistol remained unresponsive.

Marcus grabbed Mike by the collar and turned him around.

Mike was terrified and struggled desperately.

"Tell me, was it your idea to attack me?" Marcus asked.

From the first time he saw Mike, Marcus felt that he was definitely not good.

Unexpectedly, Marcus still underestimated Mike.

"Mike is truly a wolf in sheep's clothing," Marcus thought.

"It's not me, not me. It's all my grandfather who wants to kill you, and it has nothing to do with me!"

Mike shook his head desperately. His cancer had just been cured, and he didn't want to be killed like this!

"Haha, your grandfather is dead, and you still want to frame him. Scum like you don't deserve to live in this world!" Marcus sneered.

He had suspected that the previous murder was led by Mike.

Yet he didn't expect that Mike wouldn't dare to admit it.

"If you dare to kill me, the Cole family will fight with you!"

Mike mentioned the Cole family again.

Marcus rolled his eyes, wondering what it meant that the two men always regarded the Cole family as their patron.

No more nonsense with Mike; Marcus directly broke his neck.

Mike looked desperate, instantly widened his eyes, and soon became a cold corpse!

After that, Marcus didn't stay and left the hotel directly.

He hated people who bit the hand that fed them.

Jordon and Mike could have received his free treatment, and they could have lived well in Uswad or Acoton in the future.

Unfortunately, they ruined a golden opportunity with their own hands.

At that moment, they both died in the hotel.

The next day, the news of the murder of Jordon and Mike immediately spread throughout Solariae.

It caused a huge stir.

After all, Jordon was a world-renowned figure in the medical field.

His visit to Uswad this time hadn't even started yet, but it had already attracted the attention of all parties.

To everyone's shock, they were shot dead in the hotel that night!

Robert and his daughter, Jade, received the news.

They were shocked and also speculated about the identity of the murderer.

Marcus ignored all kinds of rumors from outside.

Now, he came to a slum in the south of Solariae.

Sitting in the outdoor noodle restaurant in the slum, he was eating pasta.

Through a series of previous understandings, he knew that in Solariae, the slum was the gathering place for people in the underground world.

It was quite chaotic here, and there were often fights.

Some stall owners were miserable.

They were all small traders but had to pay high protection fees to those in the underground world.

The pasta restaurant where Marcus was eating was run by a couple in their thirties.

Their child was only three or four years old, but to make money, they took their child with them.

The child was dressed in rags and looked dirty, but he was very polite.

As long as guests came, he would take the initiative to greet them and move chairs for them.

"The people from Black Tiger Gang are coming. Pack up, or it will be too late!" A voice came from not far away.

The young couple, Theo and Mary, immediately looked worried and hurriedly began packing up their things.

They knew that if they didn't run, they would not only have to pay huge protection fees but also be beaten up!

"Bro, we have to close the stall, and you should go quickly. You can't leave when Black Tiger Gang comes. This plate of pasta is on me!"

The man, Theo, said to Marcus.

Marcus' eyes flashed, and he asked, "Is Black Tiger Gang the biggest gang around here?"

While packing, Theo replied, "No, but Black Tiger Gang is a famous gang in the south."

"Why do you ask so many questions? These people in the underground world are ruthless. It's no use knowing so much. Hurry, or you will be beaten!"

Theo reminded again.

Before Theo could finish, the table not far away was kicked to the ground by a gangster with red hair!
