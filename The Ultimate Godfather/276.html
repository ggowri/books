Marcus didn't seem to have any reason to refuse, so he agreed.

However, Pearl was very well-behaved. When he didn't talk to her, she was as quiet as a mouse.

Moreover, she was nothing like a spoiled rich girl. She was more like a maid.

She took care of Marcus with meticulous attention and was incredibly perceptive.

Even a glance from Marcus was enough for Pearl to know what he needed.

Marcus planned to settle Pearl in Bayside Villa. When he returned to the villa, Pearl could take care of his daily needs.

He didn't need Pearl to follow him around when he was out.

After all, Pearl's massage skills were really impressive.

Jane chimed in, "Indeed. But I have to say, Mr. Burnett always has a good eye for beauty. This girl is really pretty and has an exotic charm."

Marcus raised an eyebrow, feeling like their comments were a bit sarcastic.

So, he explained, "Her name is Pearl, the daughter of a new subordinate, Cooke. From now on, she'll be taking care of my daily needs. She'll be living in Bayside Villa."

Jane said with a sly grin, her arms crossed over her chest, which made her ample bosom look even more pronounced.

Pearl lowered her head shyly, even appearing a bit timid.

Cooke had hired a private tutor for Pearl, which meant she had hardly ever set foot in a school and didn't know how to interact with people.

Marcus smiled mysteriously and pulled out all the energy crystals from his Spatial Ring.

Hundreds of brick-sized energy crystals poured out onto the ground, forming a small mountain.

Even Jane, who knew nothing about energy crystals, was taken aback.

"Mr. Burnett, did you get all these energy crystals?"

"Who else could it be?" Marcus replied with a grin.

She couldn't fathom how Marcus could have acquired so many energy crystals.

He didn't spend any money!

She couldn't believe he got so many energy crystals without spending a penny.

But Diana knew Marcus never lied.

"About sixteen hundred," Marcus said.

Marcus was pleasantly surprised. "Two thousand? That many?"

Diana nodded. "The higher the quality of the energy crystal, the less quantity is needed. I conservatively estimate these energy crystals can modify two thousand guns."

"I see."

Marcus finally understood the principle.

Next, he settled Pearl in.
