Chapter 117 Satisfy

Marcus nodded in satisfaction. "Now that's more like it."

As he spoke, he playfully smacked Josie on her firm backside.

Josie shivered and glared at Marcus. "You're impossible..."

Marcus chuckled wickedly. "Didn't you just enjoy it a moment ago?"

Josie covered Marcus's mouth. "Stop it! You're embarrassing me..."

"Hahaha!"

Seeing Josie's bashful expression, Marcus laughed heartily before pinning her beneath him again.

"Marcus... I've had enough... I really can't... oh... ah..."

Once again, the room was filled with passionate sounds.

Marcus went at it three more times until Josie repeatedly begged for mercy. Only then did he stop.

Exhausted, Josie collapsed onto the bed and soon fell into a deep, peaceful sleep.

Marcus took a shower and was just about to lie down when he sensed a powerful telepathy envelop the entire building.

In an instant, everything in the building seemed to fall under its control.

Marcus frowned, immediately realizing that this telepathy came from a highly skilled warrior.

Telepathy was akin to a heightened sixth sense.

When a warrior reached Stage Three, they would develop their own telepathy.

This telepathy could extend beyond the body, covering vast areas and perceiving everything within.

It was like when Marcus first became a warrior and felt his senses sharpen exponentially.

But the telepathy of a warrior was far more powerful than ordinary senses.

Telepathy could cover an area extensively but couldn't replicate the function of X-ray vision.

The telepathy emanating now was terrifying, brimming with aggressive energy.

Marcus instantly understood that a formidable enemy was approaching.

He could even make an educated guess about the enemy's identity.

Wasting no time, Marcus quietly dressed. Seeing Josie still fast asleep, he didn't disturb her, locking the room behind him as he left.

Marcus didn't linger and hailed a cab, heading toward the outskirts of Kingston.

During the ride, the telepathy remained, precisely tracking his every move.

Arriving at a desolate mountain, Marcus stepped out of the cab and stood his ground. He shouted to the sky, "Show yourself!"

"Buzz..."

As soon as he finished speaking, a strange sound echoed from somewhere in the mountains.

Moments later, a figure in a purple suit flickered into view, landing in front of Marcus.

It was none other than Ricardo Brown, the head of the Brown family in Kingston!

"Hahaha, as expected of the Godfather's third son—keen and fearless!" Ricardo laughed.

Ricardo's gaze swept over Marcus, his laughter filled with approval.

Marcus had killed his son, igniting Ricardo's wrath.

Now, with the backing, Ricardo was even more determined to take Marcus's life.

Marcus's eyes narrowed, and he looked slightly surprised. "How do you know I'm the Godfather's son?"

"Your brother sent me to kill you. Of course, I know!" Ricardo said without hesitation.

Ricardo continued, "You killed Jared and Leo. You're an enemy of the Brown family, and with me involved, you won't escape!"

Marcus suddenly understood—it was his estranged second brother.

However, he didn't seem to care.

He had come to Kingston specifically to settle the score with Ricardo.

The current situation was exactly what Marcus had planned for.

Resolving the matter in the desolate mountains meant no one else would be caught in the crossfire.

"If you willingly accept death, I'll leave your body intact!" Ricardo said arrogantly.

His gaze brimmed with innate confidence as he spoke.

His cultivation had reached the peak of Ancient Martial Stage Three, and to him, Marcus was just an insignificant insect.

He was certain that given the chance, he could kill Marcus with ease.

"Do you really think you've got me beat?" Marcus said, locking eyes with Ricardo.

Marcus never fought battles he wasn't confident of winning. He had already studied Ricardo's capabilities before coming to Kingston.

"Haha, you seriously think killing a few of my men qualifies you to go up against me?" Ricardo sneered.

Ricardo burst into arrogant laughter. "Let me show you what the peak of Ancient Martial Stage Three can do!"

Before the words had even faded, Ricardo took a step forward, his body transforming into an eerie blur.

The aura of a Stage Three peak martial artist erupted fully.

With a single strike, a massive palm shadow loomed over the space, sending sand and stones flying as it descended upon Marcus.

Marcus roared, unleashing the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire, and countered with a full-force punch.

Boom!

A terrifying shockwave reverberated, and Marcus felt an overwhelming force bearing down on him.

Though Marcus's punch carried immense power, it was no match for Ricardo's palm strike and was utterly destroyed!

The force sent Marcus staggering backward. His chest heaved, and blood surged up his throat, though he stubbornly swallowed it back down.

"This is the strength you're so proud of?" Ricardo mocked, standing still as a rock.

Ricardo remained motionless, delivering his taunt to Marcus.
