Chapter 984 Faith's Misery-2

However, they never expected that this young man was someone even Serpent's Coil respected.

With his status, Aidan couldn't easily provoke him. This time, he's in serious trouble.

Hearing this, Aidan and Faith's faces instantly turned pale.

They never expected Marcus to have such a prominent status.

Especially Faith, she could never have imagined that this man held such a prestigious position.

If she had known earlier, she wouldn't have dared to provoke Marcus.

Looking at those aggressive Serpent's Coil members, Faith's mental defenses completely collapsed.

Her gaze turned to Morgana, and she began to plead softly, "Morgana, my dear daughter, I was wrong before, I shouldn't have treated you that way. Please forgive me this time. I apologize to you. I'm begging you!"

At this moment, Faith quickly shifted from her previous arrogance to a pitiful appearance.

Faith was not only shameless but also a great actress.

People who don't know the truth can easily be deceived by her.

However, Morgana was kind-hearted, and seeing Faith's pleading look, she couldn't help but waver a little inside.

But she didn't decide to forgive Faith on her own; instead, she turned her gaze to Marcus.

Marcus clearly understood Morgana's thoughts, but he calmly said, "She's treated you like this since childhood, and this time, if I didn't have enough strength to suppress them, do you think she would have let you go so easily?"

His words left Morgana in silence, not knowing how to refute for a moment.

Marcus said, "So, someone like her doesn't deserve forgiveness!"

After speaking, he didn't even wait for Morgana to respond and simply waved lightly at Jalen.

Jalen immediately turned and gave orders to his subordinates.

These people were all ruthless, and upon receiving Jalen's orders, they immediately started beating Aidan and Faith.

Aidan and Faith cried out in pain, struggling desperately, but it was useless.

In just a moment, both were beaten bloody.

Seeing this, fear appeared on the faces of the onlookers.

Aidan begged for mercy, "Mr. Fitzgerald, I was wrong, truly wrong! Please forgive me this time, I won't dare again. It's all Faith's fault, she made me misunderstand and offend you..."

Before he finished speaking, he unexpectedly broke free and rushed to Faith, hitting her fiercely.

The already severely injured Faith became even more miserable.

Jalen said, "You offended Mr. Fitzgerald, and you still have the nerve to plead with me?"

Jalen was a smart man and naturally understood that Marcus didn't want to forgive Aidan.

He immediately ordered, "Put this guy in a sack and throw him into the sea!"

The subordinates quickly obeyed, fetched a huge sack from the business car, and directly covered Aidan with it.

Aidan pleaded, "Mr. Zafer, please spare me, I really won't dare again."

Aidan was scared out of his wits and struggled desperately. He knew what being put into a sack meant.

However, with his strength alone, it was impossible to fight against so many people.

Soon, he was stuffed into the sack and thrown into the car.

Seeing this, Aidan's subordinates didn't dare to speak, fearing that Jalen's next target would be them.

Morgana was also frightened by their brutal methods, her face turning pale.

She whispered to Marcus, "Are they really going to throw Aidan into the sea?"

Marcus nodded calmly. "Of course, they wouldn't joke about something like this."

Hearing this, Morgana's body trembled slightly, and her gaze toward Marcus grew more complicated.

She hadn't expected that to vent Marcus's anger, the people of Serpent's Coil would deal with Aidan so ruthlessly.

This was enough to prove that Marcus held an extraordinary position in their eyes.

At this moment, Jalen stepped forward to Marcus, bowed respectfully, and cautiously asked, "Mr. Fitzgerald, do you have any other instructions?"

Marcus waved his hand casually. "No. Go take care of your own business."

Then, Jalen finally dared to leave with his people.

This scene shocked everyone once again.

Everyone knew Jalen was the hall master of Serpent's Coil, a prominent figure in Solomia's underworld.

But when facing this young man, he acted so humble, as if meeting the president of Serpent's Coil.

Everyone was guessing who Marcus really was.
