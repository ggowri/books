Chapter 937 Luminara Awards-2

"What's your annual salary?" Marcus inquired.

Callista seemed to realize something and replied, "My annual salary is half a million dollars, and with some company benefits, my total income each year is around 800 thousand dollars."

Marcus nodded casually and said, "Then I'll raise your annual salary to eight million dollars. When I'm not around, you'll assist Evander in managing Royal Entertainment's daily affairs."

This statement caught Callista off guard.

She had previously thought Marcus might give her a raise but never imagined he would increase it to eight million dollars in one go!

Although eight million dollars couldn't compare to Evander's 50 million dollars, their positions differed.

Her original salary was only half a million dollars, and increasing it to eight million dollars meant it had multiplied more than ten times!

It brought a pleasant surprise to Callista's pretty face. She had never dreamed she could receive such a substantial salary increase.

Perhaps because happiness came too suddenly, Callista didn't know what to say for a moment.

After a brief pause, she came to her senses, stood up, and bowed to Marcus. "Thank you, Mr. Fitzgerald. From now on, I will definitely support Mr. Bishop well and properly manage Royal Entertainment's daily affairs."

"Alright, that's it then. I have other matters to attend to, so you two should go now," said Marcus, dismissing them. He then rose from his seat and slowly walked into the villa.

Watching Marcus' retreating figure, Callista quickly asked, "Mr. Fitzgerald, will you be attending the Luminara Awards tomorrow?"

"Since you two keep insisting, I suppose I can drop by," Marcus replied without turning around.

Seeing that Marcus had agreed, Callista and Evander exchanged glances, their faces lighting up with joy.

Callista called out to Marcus, "Then I'll send a car to pick you up tomorrow, Mr. Fitzgerald."

Marcus shook his head. "No need. Just text me the address, and I'll go there."

Having received a definite answer, Callista and Evander left Bayside Villa.

The night passed uneventfully as Marcus became completely absorbed in his meditation practice.

Since the Luminara Awards ceremony was scheduled for 3:00 PM, Marcus remained immersed in meditation until 1:00 PM, when he finally emerged from his trance-like state.

He immediately drove toward the venue of the Luminara Awards, the Golden Hotel.

Perhaps because the Golden Hotel was hosting the Luminara Awards today, Marcus encountered heavy traffic, and his journey was constantly interrupted by starts and stops.

When he reached the Golden Hotel, it was already 2:30 PM, leaving only half an hour before the Luminara Awards ceremony began.

Marcus intended to arrive early.

However, before leaving the parking garage, he faintly heard a voice coming from nearby.

"You little bitch, you're going to give up that Most Popular Female Singer award this time, understand? If you dare talk back to me again, I swear I'll deal with you here and now. You think I won't?"

Marcus followed the sound and spotted a group of men dressed in black surrounding a stunningly beautiful young woman in an elegant evening gown in the corner of the parking lot.

The young woman was exquisitely dressed, her entire presence radiating refinement and grace.

Marcus immediately recognized her as Chloe Sinclair, one of Uswad Entertainment's "Twelve Rising Stars" and Lila's best friend.

Lately, Chloe's latest hit single, "Midnight Serenade," from her new album, has taken the country by storm.

It had become the hottest track in all of Uswad in recent months.

The familiar melody echoed through streets and alleyways, becoming one of the year's biggest hits.

With this song, Chloe had once again skyrocketed to fame.

This year, the Luminara Awards gathered numerous celebrities from the film, television, and music industries.

They would be presenting awards for Best Female Singer, Best Male Singer, Best Actor, and Best Actress, among other categories.

Under normal circumstances, given Chloe's explosive popularity this year, the Best Female Singer award should have been hers without question.

Yet just before the Luminara Awards ceremony was about to begin, Chloe found herself being threatened by these men.
