"Keep your voice down!"

The man whispered a warning to the girl.

The way he looked at Marcus was like looking at prey.

"How much commission can I get?"

The girl asked expectantly.

The man said, "That depends on how much wealth he has and how well he works at the mine!"

The girl said, "He looks quite tall and strong, so he should be able to work for a year or two. If he gets sick, we'll just kill him."

The two chatted in low voices.

Listening to the conversation between the two, Marcus sneered.

He realized that this seemingly simple girl was actually a devil in disguise.

They should be active in the trading market all year round, looking for prey that was both wealthy and capable of working.

They would lure their prey into the specific area, rob them, and then send them to the mines as slaves.

Before this, Marcus had only seen such things in movies.

He never thought he would one day actually encounter such a thing.

However, he did not expose them directly.

Instead, Marcus wanted to take a look at their mineral veins and wait for an opportunity to act.

"Hi, you want to buy energy crystals, right?"

The man in the red vest walked over and asked.

Marcus nodded.

"Energy crystals are not common, and they are definitely not traded in an ordinary market. I will take you to a place where I guarantee you can buy high-quality energy crystals. Get in the car!"

As he spoke, the man in the red vest took the initiative to open the car door for Marcus.

Marcus didn't hesitate and sat on it directly.

Besides him, there were two other men in the car.

The two men had sharp eyes and didn't say anything.

Soon, the car started and drove away.

They passed through a series of alleys and were soon out of the city.

They first drove for more than two hours on the highway, and then it was all hilly.

Another hour passed. And they entered a lush tropical rainforest.

This rainforest was sparsely populated, and the roads were becoming increasingly difficult to navigate.

As if to divert Marcus' attention, the man in the red vest chatted with Marcus while driving.

He acted very friendly.

The other two men never spoke.

Finally, at two o'clock in the afternoon, the car stopped in an open area in the middle of the rainforest.

There was a lake here with a diameter of about eleven hundred yards.

There were many Southvaia-style wooden houses built around the lake.

Behind the wooden houses was a vast rainforest, which was full of tropical trees.

On one side of the rainforest, there was a muddy road, with trucks carrying ore coming in and out from time to time.

"We are here. Get off the car."

The man in the red vest reminded Marcus.

"Can I buy energy crystals here?"

Marcus asked casually, took out his cell phone and saw that there was no signal.

However, his memory was far beyond that of ordinary people.

Although the car had traveled hundreds of miles, Marcus could still clearly recall the route.

He guessed that he should be on the border of the country.

The area was inconvenient to transport, covered by vast rainforests, and full of rampant criminals.

All kinds of illegal trade thrived in this area.

If someone got killed here, the cops couldn't find the killer.

And the place where Marcus was should be the mine office area mentioned by the man in the red vest.

The ore carried by the trucks was dug out from the veins.

"You'll know. Why are you talking so much?"

A bearded man who had been silent couldn't help but speak.

His voice was hoarse and low, which made people feel creepy.

Then, another man pushed Marcus under the car.

Marcus still pretended to be innocent and said to the man in the red vest, "Take me to buy energy crystals now. I have something else to do and am anxious to go home."

"Boy, you can't go home..."

The man in the red vest finally revealed his hideous face that had been hidden for a long time.

Meanwhile, the two men behind him had taken out their pistols and pointed them at Marcus' temples, saying, "Take out all the valuables on you, or we'll kill you!"

Marcus pretended to be terrified and asked, "Where am I? Why are you doing this to me?"
