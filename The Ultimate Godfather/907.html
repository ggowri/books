The windowless room made escape impossible for any ordinary person.

For Marcus, however, this was hardly an obstacle.

He could easily blast the iron door to pieces with a casual display of his power.

Yet he chose to remain quietly in the room instead.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, I'm truly sorry for dragging you into this mess," Cecilia said with genuine remorse.

She felt awful seeing Marcus imprisoned alongside her.

Her face was etched with guilt as she spoke.

"Please don't think that way, Ms. McHale, and don't blame yourself," Marcus replied reassuringly.

"I allowed them to bring me here of my own accord. Trust me, no one could keep me here if I didn't want to stay.

"Don't worry, as long as I'm around, your uncle won't get his hands on McHale Pharmaceuticals Group. You'll remain at the helm where you belong," Marcus said with a confident smile.

Cecilia felt touched by Marcus' optimistic attitude.

Her beautiful eyes flickered with emotion before a shadow of sadness crossed her delicate features. "Please don't try to comfort me, Mr. Fitzgerald. I know what lies ahead.

"As long as my uncle doesn't harm my grandfather, I can accept not being the head of the family."

From Cecilia's perspective, Marcus tried to console her with empty words.

Joel would never have dared to send Haywood to Uswad to make arrests unless he was confident about taking over the Helmsman's position.

Given his typically cautious nature, Joel would never have taken such a bold step without absolute certainty.

Moreover, Joel wielded far more power and influence than she hoped to match.

For example, Haywood wasn't someone an ordinary person could mess with.

He was a fifth-generation cyborg for crying out loud!

And his enhancements were top-notch!

Although Marcus possessed exceptional medical skills, he had even managed to cure her congenital heart condition.

Still, Cecilia couldn't imagine Marcus having enough strength to go toe-to-toe with Joel.

Even dealing with someone like Haywood would be way out of Marcus' league.

"The only thing bothering me right now is dragging you into this mess. If I had known this would happen, I never would have met with you. It is all my fault!"

Cecilia broke down in tears, her grief pouring out uncontrollably.

Marcus rushed to comfort her, saying, "Ms. McHale, I swear everything I've told you is accurate. You have to believe me!

"I arranged for them to bring us here specifically so I could help you reclaim what rightfully belongs to you tomorrow."

Marcus' words only made Cecilia cry harder.

Marcus' claims seemed far-fetched to her, given that her uncle was so powerful.

Seeing how deeply distressed she was, Marcus found himself at a loss for words.

He could only try his best to offer what comfort he could.

Unconsciously, Cecilia found herself leaning into Marcus' embrace.

Marcus gently comforted her, trying his best to help her calm down.

Time quickly moved forward to the second day.

Haywood walked in with several men in black suits and coldly addressed Cecilia, "Ms. McHale, Mr. McHale's inauguration ceremony is about to begin. I suggest you behave yourself, or not only will your grandfather not make it, but this young man won't survive either!"

Cecilia's face turned slightly pale at his words.

It became clear that Joel had kept Rhett alive only to ensure she would surrender her position as the family leader.

They would kill Rhett without hesitation if she refused to give up her position!

Under Haywood's direction, Marcus and Cecilia were forcefully escorted to the inauguration ceremony.

The event was held in McHale Pharmaceuticals Group's grand ballroom.

Today marked the inauguration of McHale Pharmaceuticals Group's new leader.

Many prominent figures from Acoton's high society had already gathered there.

The guest list even included notable personalities from beyond Acoton.

The McHale family patriarch, Rhett, sat in the main seat, still maintaining his dignified appearance.

However, a closer look would reveal the time bomb that had been planted on his body.

If he didn't follow their orders, the bomb could be detonated at any moment!
