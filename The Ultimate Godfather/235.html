Looking at the red phantom in the rearview mirror, John was in disbelief.

He envied Edison's driving skills. Even though Edison had retired for many years, he couldn't compare with Edison.

Just now he was sitting in the passenger seat, marveling at Edison's amazing driving skills.

However, Edison drove as if he were strolling in the garden and easily left the large group behind.

In John's opinion, this kind of technology was still the best in the country, and no one in Uswad could surpass Edison.

However, at this moment, the red Ferrari was much faster than them.

Edison's eyes fixed, and he subconsciously looked at the rearview mirror.

He smiled and said, "That's interesting. It seems I have met a master!"

As he spoke, he also stepped on the accelerator. Pagani was one of the world's three top sports car brands.

The car's price was several times that of a Ferrari 720s.

The acceleration performance was naturally stronger.

With full-throttle acceleration, the speed quickly exceeded 300 miles per hour.

This speed was on par with a Ferrari 720s, but there was a series of big bends ahead.

Edison had no choice but to slow down when turning.

With his skillful driving skills, his car kept drifting, drawing beautiful arcs on the track.

If this kind of cornering technique were broadcast live, it would surely cause the audience to exclaim in surprise.

However, the red Ferrari driven by Marcus seemed to have no intention of slowing down.

Even when faced with such a curve, it still drifted through the curve at an average speed of over 260 miles per hour.

Finally, at a bend, he overtook the Pagani sports car driven by Edison!

"You surpassed! Mr. Fitzgerald, you surpassed Edison, you are amazing!"

Melina, who was sitting in the passenger seat, was so excited that she couldn't speak.

Before this, when John proposed the conditions, she thought she could never achieve it.

At this moment, Marcus relied on his almost abnormal driving skills to directly surpass the three-time Dakar Rally champion Edison!

More importantly, in the first half of the race, Marcus had been getting familiar with the driving feel of the car.

Therefore, he was always at the end of all the cars.

But even so, he could still overtake in adversity, and the gap was getting wider and wider!

After surpassing Edison, Marcus' car was like a wild horse that had broken free from its reins, and no one could restrain him anymore.

After drifting through several turns, the straight sprint stage was ahead.

Marcus sprinted at full throttle, his speed once soaring to over 400 miles per hour, and he successfully crossed the finish line first without any suspense!

Edison won second place, and his charging time was 20 seconds later than Marcus!

This made John look gloomy, and his heart was filled with shock.

When the last car finished the race, everyone got out of the car.

They stared at Marcus blankly in disbelief.

Edison took a step forward and said to Marcus respectfully, "I didn't know you were so excellent. Please don't be offended. I have benefited a lot from this match. It is an honor for me to lose to a master like you!"

As he spoke, Edison bowed deeply to Marcus, behaving extremely humbly.

Marcus waved his hand at Edison and said, "Mr. Park, there is no need to be like this. I am not a professional driver. I am just driving a lap casually."

Marcus was telling the truth, but in the eyes of everyone, such words were like a slap in the face.

Especially John, whose eyes towards Marcus changed completely.

"So you are an expert. I accept my loss with all my heart!"

John looked at Melina and said, "I lost this time. If you want to quit, I won't stop you. I didn't expect you to find such a master. I wish you all the best!"

Having said that, John turned around and walked into his Pagani sports car.

Edison saluted Marcus again and drove away in his Suzuki Hayabusa.

The others surrounded Marcus with admiration and asked for his autograph.

They finally remembered Marcus said that he would let them see the power of the contemporary car god.

At that time, they all thought that Marcus was bragging.

It seemed that all this was true!

With such driving skills, if he were to participate in the Dakar Rally, he would win the championship with a huge lead.

No wonder Marcus said before that these people were not worthy to be his opponents.

It seemed that he was not bragging. His driving skills were simply abnormally strong, which exceeded the scope of human control!
