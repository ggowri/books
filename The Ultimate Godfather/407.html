The crimson light streaked through the air, moving with blinding speed and utter silence.

Marcus had no time to react before it descended upon Yvonne's body.

His eyes narrowed sharply, instinctively zeroing in on a particular spot.

There, he saw a face materialize, formed from swirling, bizarre patterns.

The face was unnervingly sinister, with blood-red eyes and a smile that was almost mocking, as if daring Marcus to challenge it.

Yvonne's body trembled violently as the red light rapidly morphed into a strange totem, which then sank into her skin.

In an instant, Yvonne seemed entirely consumed by the red glow.

Her complexion turned ashen, and she collapsed, losing consciousness completely.

Marcus' brows furrowed deeply. Without wasting a second, he scooped her up into his arms.

A surge of dark, malevolent energy rolled off her, making the hair on the back of his neck stand on end.

Without hesitation, he activated his X-ray vision, only to discover the totem had fully attached itself to her, draining her vitality with brutal force.

Her life force was rapidly slipping away, and Marcus could see her blood energy draining in real-time.

Acting swiftly, Marcus summoned his Timeless Numinous Energy, channeling it into her body in a desperate attempt to stabilize her.

The immense power he controlled usually had remarkable healing properties—often able to repair injuries almost instantly.

But Yvonne showed no signs of improvement. The totem continued to drain her as though nothing could stop it.

His energy was only able to prevent her from slipping further into the abyss, keeping her on the edge of life.

"Who's behind this?" Marcus thought, his eyes hardening with fury.

He knew this attack wasn't meant for Yvonne—it was aimed at him.

Just then, Michelle, Charles, and the others emerged from the hotel.

When they saw Yvonne unconscious, her face pale as a ghost, Charles panicked. "Yvonne! What's happening? Don't scare me, sweetheart!"

He immediately turned to Marcus. "Please, Mr. Fitzgerald, you have to save her! She's my only daughter! I can't lose her!"

As a skilled doctor, Charles immediately recognized that Yvonne's life was hanging by a thread. It felt like a knife in his heart.

Marcus' face darkened further as he met Charles' gaze. "Mr. Miller, don't worry. This happened because of me, and I'll make sure she's okay. I give you my word."

With a sharp turn, Marcus made to leave.

They'd tried to kill him, but dragging Yvonne into this—that was the final straw. His anger flared, hot and sharp.

Whoever was behind this would regret it, and he'd find a way to save her, no matter the cost!

"Mr. Fitzgerald, wait!"

But before he could leave, Diana, wearing a sleek leather dress, stepped in front of him.

"By the looks of it, she's been cursed with a blood spell, a particularly twisted form of dark magic from Southvaia. It's usually created through sacrifices—most likely involving dozens of souls.

"Once the curse is in the body, it starts draining a person's life force until they're nothing but a dry husk.

"The person who cast this curse is probably a very powerful Curse Weaver, and they've targeted you, Mr. Fitzgerald. If you go in blindly, you'll be walking right into their trap. Let me come with you instead."

"Curse Weaver..."

Marcus had heard of them, but they were a rare and mysterious group he'd never encountered personally.

Even during his time in the southwest of Uswad, he hadn't run into any.

To think they were now targeting him!

his attack had to be orchestrated by someone else.

Diana's expression remained serious. "To break the blood curse, you'll need the blood of the Curse Weaver who cast it. Be extra cautious, Mr. Fitzgerald."

Marcus gave her a brief nod, his resolve like steel.

He turned on his heel and moved toward the direction where the sinister face had vanished.

"Take her somewhere quiet. I'll keep her alive," Diana instructed Charles and the others.

Charles nodded and quickly carried Yvonne to the car, heading toward their villa.

Meanwhile, Marcus stretched his spiritual powers, scanning the area around him for any signs of movement.

After a few moments, he detected several energy fluctuations similar to the curse that had affected Yvonne.

Without wasting time, Marcus hailed a taxi and rushed off in pursuit of the source.
