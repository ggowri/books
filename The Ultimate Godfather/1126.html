Chapter 997 Menopause-1

"Are you afraid I'll get hurt? He's just a young man in his early twenties. How could he possibly hurt me? Are you joking with me?" Minerva said disdainfully.

She let out a cold laugh, her eyes filled with sarcasm, then said to Marcus, "Trespassing into Emotionless Gorge is a capital offense. You also deceived Gertie, causing her to lose her chastity. These two crimes alone are enough for me to kill you. But I disdain to lay a hand on you—take your own life."

Seeing Minerva acting like a mad dog, Marcus sneered. "Hold on, don't say all that yet. I have a question for you."

Minerva asked, "What question?"

Marcus asked, "Is it because you're getting older, so you've hit menopause?"

Minerva's face turned bright red upon hearing this, and a trace of icy killing intent flashed in her eyes. "What are you talking about? It seems you really have a death wish."

Immediately, Minerva's energy erupted completely. A supreme destructive force surrounded her, making the entire space incredibly oppressive.

Although Minerva's cultivation was only at the peak of Ancient Martial Stage Nine, she possessed the ability to challenge higher levels.

Marcus estimated that Minerva should be able to contend with someone who had just reached the Return Essence stage.

"Minerva, don't act impulsively. Stop it!" Seeing Minerva about to attack Marcus, Gertie hurriedly stepped forward to stop her.

"Gertie, didn't you hear what he said to me? I must kill him with my own hands today," Minerva said coldly.

"Minerva, please don't act impulsively. You're no match for him," Gertie finally admitted the truth.

She knew Minerva was a very prideful person, especially when facing men.

If Minerva was told she was no match for Marcus, she would be furious. But Gertie had no choice but to tell the truth to prevent Minerva from getting hurt.

Minerva said helplessly, "Gertie, what are you talking about? He's just a young man. How could I not be his match?"

"You don't need to lie to me anymore. I know you're saying this to protect him. But it's useless. He's already crossed my line, so no matter what you say, he must die today."

As Gertie expected, Minerva didn’t believe her at all.

Immediately, energy burst out from Minerva, streams of destructive force gathering in her palm. She then swiftly struck a destructive handprint toward Marcus.

Its power was incredibly terrifying. In a short time, the void trembled wherever the Hand Strike passed. It was clear that Minerva wanted to kill Marcus.

However, Marcus remained calm, quietly watching Minerva's Hand Strike, without even attempting to dodge.

"You’re not dodging at all. Looks like you’re truly scared stiff. But, you do have some self-awareness, because even if you tried to dodge, I’d still kill you in the end." Minerva sneered as she saw Marcus remain still.

At the same time, she infused an even stronger destructive force into her Hand Strike, attempting to kill Marcus with a single attack.

With a boom, Minerva's Hand Strike slammed into Marcus.

However, despite the immense power of her Hand Strike, it didn’t affect Marcus’s body at all.

Marcus remained calmly standing in place. If you looked closely, you’d notice a faint, almost imperceptible film covering the surface of his body. It was this film that blocked Minerva’s attack.

Minerva widened her eyes, a hint of surprise in her gaze toward Marcus, then couldn’t help but say, “How is this possible? How can you ignore my attack? Tell me, what treasure do you have on you?”
