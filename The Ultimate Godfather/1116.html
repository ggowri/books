Chapter 991 Am I not strong in bed?-2

His pupils dilated, his skin gradually lost its luster, and eventually, he withered rapidly, turning into a mummified corpse!

This scene left Gertie, not far away, stunned.

Her big, watery eyes were filled with horror. She never imagined it would end like this, even in her dreams.

She clearly knew that Rivers was an Acquired State Seven expert.

His combat power was almost equivalent to an Ancient Martial Stage Nine expert.

But even so, he couldn't withstand even three moves from Marcus!

Moreover, Gertie could tell that Marcus had the ability to kill the opponent within the first two moves.

The reason he kept dodging was merely to toy with Rivers!

In other words, Marcus now possesses the strength to instantly kill an Acquired State Nine expert.

His strength is simply overwhelming!

Gertie even believed that even if the nine ancestors of her family attacked together, they might not be a match for Marcus.

More importantly, Marcus killed Rivers with the extremely terrifying Devouring Power.

A Devouring Power that can instantly drain a person into a dried corpse!

Although Gertie had heard before that some people had mastered this evil power.

But those people, when using such evil power, not only had to pay a price but also couldn’t wield it as effortlessly as Marcus.

They usually need to burn their own essence blood and might even suffer backlash!

Marcus, however, could effortlessly perform such cultivation manuals.

Even someone as powerful as Rivers still couldn’t escape the fate of being drained into a dried corpse by Marcus!

Gertie wondered if she was imagining things.

After Marcus absorbed Rivers' energy, she felt that his aura seemed to have changed, becoming even stronger than before.

In fact, just as Gertie had guessed, after obtaining Rivers' energy, Marcus's cultivation skyrocketed instantly, rising from Acquired State Six to Acquired State Seven. His combat power surged significantly once again!

"Is he... dead?"

Gertie tiptoed up to Marcus, unable to look him in the eye.

"Yes, I said I'd kill him within three moves, so I naturally wouldn't waste time on him," Marcus nodded and said with a smile.

Feeling the changes in his cultivation, he was pleasantly surprised.

"Marcus, can you tell me what cultivation manuals you just used? Why are they so powerful?" Gertie asked curiously, blinking her bright, sparkling eyes.

Unknowingly, she seemed to be filled with admiration for the man in front of her.

"It's a type of devouring cultivation manual, absorbing others' energy to enhance my own cultivation," Marcus replied calmly.

Gertie's eyes lit up, and she exclaimed, "So powerful!"

She was not wrong.

After devouring Rivers' cultivation, Marcus indeed improved his own.

"Of course, I'm strong. You should know that well, especially in bed. Am I not strong?"

Marcus gave Gertie a meaningful look.

"You're so annoying..."

Gertie instantly understood what Marcus meant, and her face turned red at that moment.

"Let's go save those girls quickly." Marcus said with a smile, looking at Gertie's blushing cheeks.

Gertie nodded gently.

Immediately, the two of them headed deeper into the cave together.

After killing Rivers, there were still many Ophidians guarding the cave.

But their skill levels were not high, only at Acquired State Two or Acquired State Three.

For Marcus, they posed no threat at all.

Marcus casually unleashed attacks and killed them all.

As expected, the girls were imprisoned in the deepest cage of the cave.

There were hundreds of girls, none older than 25.

Some were even younger, looking only sixteen or seventeen, and each of them was very beautiful.

Apart from the girls imprisoned in the cages, human organs and the corpses of young girls were also scattered on the ground.

These people were likely killed by those Ophidians.

Seeing Marcus and Gertie, the girls, as if seeing their saviors, began shouting even louder.

"Help... help us... please save us, we're about to be killed by those monsters!"

Seeing these pitiful girls, Gertie felt heartbroken.

She quickly said, "Don't worry, we're here to save you. Those Ophidians are all dead, you don't need to be afraid."
