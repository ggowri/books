"Is this true?"

Robert once again confirmed. After all, it was far too shocking.

"Would I joke about my grandson's life?"

Jordon's expression darkened slightly.

"If you can save my grandson's life, I promise to help your daughter advance to Halvard Medical School in Acoton!"

Robert's eyes flickered. After thinking for a while, he said to Jordon, "Well, I can take you to Mr. Fitzgerald and show your request. As for whether Mr. Fitzgerald is willing to rescue him, I'm not sure."

Hearing this, Jordon nodded immediately.

In his view, given his status, Marcus had no reason to refuse.

Robert paused what he was doing and went to find Marcus at his hotel with Jordon.

Since Marcus had told Robert the address before, Robert and Jordon soon came to the door of Marcus' room and rang the doorbell cautiously.

At the moment, Marcus, who just finished counting the spoils, was surprised to see Jordon and Robert coming.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, I'm really sorry to bother you so late."

Robert apologized sincerely.

If it weren't for his desire to send Jade to study in Acoton, he wouldn't have interfered.

"What can I do for you?"

He had little fondness for this professor from Acoton.

"My grandson's pancreatic cancer has spread, and I am here to ask you to help!"

Jordon came straight to the point.

However, even while pleading, he struggled to let go of his academic pride, his tone lacking the humility the situation called for.

"If he doesn't drink, he can last at least a month. But you didn't take my advice seriously," Marcus remarked.

Jordon frowned, faint regret flickering in his heart.

However, he refused to show it. His tone still carried the air of superiority as he said, "What's done is done. My grandson's condition is critical now. Please save him immediately!"

This sentence was even more commanding.

It made Robert's heart sink.

On the way here, he told Jordon many times to approach Marcus with humility.

Jordon, however, had a feeling of seniority.

He still thought he was superior to Marcus.

He thought he was a world-renowned professor and Marcus should treat his grandson.

Marcus' brow furrowed instantly, "What does your grandson's illness have to do with me? Why should I treat him?"

Jordon looked stunned and immediately said, "I am a professor at Halvard Medical School. If you can cure my grandson, you will have a bright future and even become famous for curing my grandson. I hope you can cherish this opportunity!"

Jordon was getting more and more ridiculous.

The more Jordon spoke, the more outrageous his claims became.

Hearing this, Robert broke into a cold sweat.

He knew that Mike was beyond saving now unless the Cole family paid a price they couldn't imagine.

"Sorry, I don't need this opportunity. Mr. Cooper, take him away."

Marcus smiled sarcastically.

He didn't expect this old man to be so confused about the situation.

Jordon looked pale and asked quickly, "Then what do you want in exchange for saving my grandson?"

Marcus said, "It's very simple. Pay me, and I'll save him. That way, we'll owe each other nothing."

Jordon asked, "How much do you want?"

His confidence was restored the moment money was mentioned.

The Cole family wasn't just known for academics.

Their pharmaceutical empire dominated Acoton, and their wealth was vast.

To him, money could solve anything.

Marcus didn't speak, stretched out a finger.

Jordon asked, "Do you want 10 million dollars?"

Marcus shook his head.

Jordon frowned. "Is that 100 million?"

Marcus shook his head again.

Now visibly irritated, Jordon said, "You want 1 billion? That's outright extortion!"

Seeing that Jordon was slow to guess, Marcus smiled, "You guessed it wrong. It's 100 billion!"

The words hit like a thunderclap. Jordon froze, speechless. Even Robert's jaw dropped in disbelief.

One hundred billion dollars!

According to the current exchange rate, it was equivalent to 700 billion Uswadian coins!

Seven hundred billion Uswadian coins was an astronomical sum, even for the mighty Cole family.

Robert never dreamed that Marcus wanted so much. It was daylight robbery!
