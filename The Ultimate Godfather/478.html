Jade spoke calmly.

She was a strong-willed woman who didn't want to spend her life sheltered by her father.

What bothered her more was that while in Solariae, Robert kept insisting she study abroad.

While studying abroad was a coveted opportunity for most doctors, Jade was deeply resistant to the idea.

Bang!

As they reached an intersection, a sudden gunshot rang out.

The windshield of Marcus' Aston Martin was struck, the bullet piercing through both the front and rear glass.

The attack happened so quickly that the bullet almost hit Jade in the head!

Jade screamed in terror, her face pale as she curled up in her seat, trembling uncontrollably.

Marcus frowned, his eyes sharp with fury. He hadn't expected someone to open fire on his car in the middle of the city.

It was obvious they were targeting him.

Without hesitation, he turned the car and sped toward another road.

Bang! Bang! Bang!

Several more gunshots followed, bullets coming from all directions and aimed squarely at Marcus' car.

With Jade in the car, Marcus had no better option but to drive out of the crowded area as quickly as possible.

It was the only way to protect Jade and the innocent people in the city.

The sound of gunfire sent the crowd into a panic, scattering in every direction.

"Marcus...what's going on? Are we being robbed?" Jade asked, her voice trembling.

Curled up in her seat, Jade's voice shook as she spoke.

Though she appeared strong on the surface, it was clear that she was easily frightened.

At this moment, she was utterly terrified, her face drained of color as she showed signs of severe shock.

"This isn't a robbery. It's an assassination attempt against me!" Marcus said flatly.

Marcus replied calmly as he expertly maneuvered the car. In under ten minutes, the Aston Martin was out of Solomia's city limits.

They reached a highway leading into the mountains, surrounded by lush green foliage.

The distant mountain ranges were a breathtaking sight.

"Stay in the car for a bit. I'm going to take care of them," Marcus instructed.

He gave Jade a quick command, and she nodded, her body trembling. "Come back soon...I'm so scared..."

Marcus stepped out of the car and used the Heavenly Circle Diagram to set up a simple rune on the vehicle.

This would effectively shield the car from incoming bullets.

Bang! Bang! Bang!

From a short distance, more gunshots rang out, bullets speeding toward Marcus.

It was evident that as Marcus drove out of Solomia, his would-be assassins had followed closely behind.

With Jade momentarily secure, Marcus could now focus entirely on the fight.

He looked into the distance and saw six or seven tall men with Weston features stepping out of a white van.

Each of them carried heavy weaponry.

Marcus noticed that among them were three fourth-generation cyborgs and two metahumans.

The fourth-generation cyborgs were far more advanced than those of the Black Hawk Legion.

The metahumans were at Stage Four, equivalent to Ancient Martial Stage Four experts!

Such a lineup would be a formidable force anywhere in the world.

Yet, here they were in Solomia, aiming to kill Marcus.

Without Marcus' quick reflexes, either he would have been shot in the head, or Jade would have been caught in the crossfire.

"Pathetic Uswadian, prepare to die!" one of the metahumans shouted.

The leading metahuman had heterochromatic eyes—one icy blue and the other fiery red—giving him an eerie, menacing presence.

In broken Uswadic, he hurled a taunt at Marcus.

"Who sent you?" Marcus asked.

He needed to know who was behind this underhanded attack.

"Since you're about to die, it won't hurt to tell you. A few days ago, you killed Professor Cole. The Cole family was furious and paid a hefty sum to have us come to Uswad and take your life!"

The heterochromatic metahuman sneered as he spoke to Marcus.

A silver-haired female metahuman beside him looked at Marcus with disdain. "Honestly, I think the Cole family is overreacting. Judging by his aura, he's only at Ancient Martial Stage Two. Sending so many of us to kill him is such a waste of resources!

"But that's not our concern. The Cole family is filthy rich. As long as they pay, we'll kill whoever they want," she added with a smirk.

A cyborg nearby chuckled coldly.

To them, Marcus' Ancient Martial Stage Two seemed laughably weak. Any one of them could easily kill him.
