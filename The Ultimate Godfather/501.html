Chapter 500 The Peak of Stage Five Ninja

"Don't you like it?" Marcus asked.

"I don't like it!"

Shannon deliberately shook her head.

"Then why did you just say you felt so comfortable and liked it so much? Which one is the truth?" Marcus asked.

Shannon's fair face turned red again. "Mr. Fitzgerald, please stop! It's so embarrassing!

"By the way, Mr. Fitzgerald, where exactly are we now?" Shannon asked curiously, glancing around at the strange surroundings.

"Another world," Marcus said with a smile.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, do you know magic? How did you bring me to another world?" Shannon asked, her voice filled with astonishment.

She had never imagined she would find herself in another world with a man doing such things.

"Sort of," Marcus said, nodding lightly.

Shannon quickly put on her clothes, tidied her hair, and touched up her makeup, returning to her previous youthful and radiant appearance.

In the next moment, Marcus raised his hand.

An invisible force erupted.

Immediately, Shannon felt the space in front of her become hazy again.

Then, she found herself back on the familiar path near Solomia University.

"Wow, that's amazing! Mr. Fitzgerald, I want to learn magic too! Can you teach me?"

Shannon looked at Marcus with pleading eyes.

"Of course, no problem," Marcus replied calmly.

Before he finished speaking, he pulled a magic tome from his Spatial Ring and handed it to Shannon.

"Practice according to the methods in this book. Once you master these magic skills, I'll teach you more powerful magic."

Marcus gave her a brief instruction.

Shannon took the magic tome with a bright smile. She had never dreamed she would one day be able to study magic.

Then, Marcus escorted Shannon to the dormitory building.

Her gaze lingered on him, filled with affection.

The deep connection they had just shared had made her fall completely in love with him.

Reluctantly, she watched him walk away before finally heading back to the dorm.

By now, it was already past 10 PM.

The streets were becoming quieter, with fewer people walking by.

Marcus, in his Aston Martin, was on his way back to Bayside Villa.

Just as he parked the car, a cold light flickered out of the void.

It shot toward him at high speed, aiming to strike!

In the night, Marcus could clearly see the flying darts were the signature weapons of the Crankhurst Ninja.

These weapons were not only incredibly fast but also extremely stealthy.

Their power rivaled that of any bullet!

Although Marcus had reached the Ancient Martial Stage Six in his cultivation, his physical body was still unable to withstand bullet impacts.

So, when facing the ninja's attack, he could only dodge quickly.

He stepped forward, and his body instantly appeared outside the villa.

While moving, he extended his spiritual sense.

He soon detected several figures chasing after him.

These figures were dressed in black ninja uniforms, with ninja swords strapped to their backs.

In the dim light, they looked like black phantoms!

Almost simultaneously, a cold flash of light appeared not far away. It quickly formed into a Blade Ray, cutting towards Marcus in a deadly arc!

Marcus' eyes sharpened. Instead of dodging, he pulled out his golden scimitar from his Spatial Ring.

With a swift motion, he released his own Blade Ray to meet the attack.

The next moment, a dull thud echoed through the air.

Blood splattered from the void!

A ninja clad in black cried out as he fell from the air!

"If you want to kill me, why not show yourselves?"

Marcus, holding the golden scimitar, stood proudly in the darkness, speaking loudly.

In the next instant, several figures descended from the void.

Each of them exuded a powerful aura.

The leader was a ninja at the peak of Stage Five.

"Uswadian man," the ninja said with an air of arrogance, "I didn't expect you to have the guts to face us alone!"

According to their earlier intelligence, Marcus' cultivation was roughly around Ancient Martial Stage Four, but it was absolutely impossible for him to exceed Ancient Martial Stage Five.

Yet, the ninjas they had sent now were all at least Stage Four, comparable to Ancient Martial Stage Four experts.

The leader himself had reached the peak of Stage Five.

With such a lineup, taking down Marcus was more than enough!

He felt Marcus was essentially walking to his death by waiting for them here!

"Are you from the Crankhurst Black Dragon Society?" Marcus asked calmly, his expression unchanged.

He immediately thought of the Stage Three ninja he had killed, the one who was Easton's master.

That man had claimed to be from the Black Dragon Society in Crankhurst.

"You're smart, but it won't help you. You offended the Black Dragon Society, and now you must die!"

The peak of Stage Five ninja sneered.

As he spoke, he waved his hand, and the other ninjas quickly arranged themselves into formation, surrounding Marcus from all directions.

Their ninja blades gleamed with a chilling light.

Two of the ninjas took the lead, attacking with their blades. Their Blade Rays sliced through the air, leaving trails of cold light behind them!
