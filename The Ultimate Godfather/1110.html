Seeing her determination, Marcus had no choice but to agree.

According to the elder's notes, those young girls were imprisoned in a cave deep within this mountain range.

Without any hesitation, Marcus directly took Gertie's hand and swiftly flew toward the cave area.

Feeling the powerful aura emanating from Marcus, Gertie couldn't help but feel amazed.

Because she was very close to Marcus, at this moment, she could clearly feel the terrifying aura emanating from him, which was even stronger than she had imagined.

The mountain is quite vast, and this place is over a hundred kilometers from its depths.

Yet, relying on his strength, Marcus took less than 10 minutes to arrive at the depths of the mountain range.

Here, towering mountains rose steeply, rarely visited by people.

Ahead lay a valley, with dense forests growing within.

The entire forest looked quite mysterious, as if shrouded in mist. From where Marcus was standing, the interior of the forest was completely obscured.

Even his spiritual sense to probe was not helpful.

"What's going on? That forest seems strange."

Gertie also noticed something was off and asked Marcus.

"There should be an Illusion Rune there to confuse us. I'll break it right now!" Marcus spoke calmly.

As he spoke, Gertie saw Marcus wave his hand, with array patterns surging in his palm.

These people seemed to communicate with heaven and earth, containing extremely powerful Rune power.

"Go!"

As Marcus uttered the word, he waved his hand forward, and those Currents unexpectedly detached from his palm, quickly merging with the space ahead.

"Buzz, buzz..."

The next moment, strange sounds echoed, and after those Currents merged, the dense forest began to blur.

Eventually, it completely vanished, replaced by a brand-new reflection.

A towering cliff, sharp as if carved by a blade, soared straight into the clouds.

On the cliff, a cave was being carved, appearing bottomless from the outside.

Two figures were guarding the entrance of the cave.

These two had no disguise, with snake heads and human bodies, exuding an eerie and sinister vibe.

"Marcus, that must be where they are keeping the girls. Let's go check it out now."

Gertie said with a bright look in her eyes.

Marcus nodded, and the two of them went to the entrance together.

The two immediately noticed Marcus and Gertie and asked, "Who are you? Don't you know this is forbidden ground? If you don't want to die, you'd better get out of here now!"

Almost at the same time, the gaze of the other one fell on Gertie.

He saw how young and beautiful she was and a trace of greed immediately appeared in his eyes.

He couldn't help but say, "The man can leave, but the woman must stay!"

However, these two couldn't discern Marcus's true cultivation level at all.

They exchanged a glance, then a mocking smile curled at the corners of their mouths.

One of the Ophidians said, "You are just a mere captive. I really don't know where you get the courage to speak to us like this?"

"Seems like you have a death wish. Go to hell!"

As he spoke, the snake man extended his large hand directly, grabbing toward Marcus as if an eagle snatching a chick!

Marcus remained calm in place. Before the his palm could land, he directly grabbed the other's wrist.

Then he forcefully twisted it.

A cracking sound of a bone breaking was heard.

The opponent's wrist was directly broken, with ghastly white bones exposed, looking extremely horrifying!

"Ah!"

The creature let out a piercing scream and gritted his teeth, saying, "Prisoner, you’ve ruined my arm. I will kill you!"

As he spoke, an extremely terrifying aura erupted from him, and he launched a desperate attack toward Marcus.

"Seeking death!"

Marcus raised his eyebrow slightly and directly unleashed a Fist Ray, colliding with the opponent's attack.

A terrifying rumble echoed as an incredibly destructive force spread in all directions.

The creature felt an irresistible force descend, enveloping his body in the blink of an eye.

He let out a miserable scream, unable to withstand it any longer.

His body was directly blasted away, his internal organs shattered, and he became a lifeless corpse!
