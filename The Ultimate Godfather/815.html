Marcus was full of longing for the Return Essence stage.

He had unknowingly cultivated the Hidden State, which had given him a solid foundation far beyond ordinary people.

If he reached the Return Essence stage, his strength would certainly be much greater than an average person at that stage.

After a while, Grace woke up from her faint.

Her face was still flushed, but her eyes had returned to their clear state.

She said, "Mr. Fitzgerald, I'm so hungry. Let's go out and eat something. I heard there are many delicious things in Kingston."

Her first words after waking up were about food.

Marcus suddenly thought that she was quite the foodie.

He replied, "Okay, let's go eat something good now."

Marcus had no reason to refuse.

Grace suddenly jumped off the bed and into Marcus' arms.

Marcus caught her, but her round, firm chest pressed directly against his mouth.

For a moment, he felt his breath become a bit difficult.

"Before food, you want to invite me to eat your breasts?" he asked with a sly smile.

Grace gave him an embarrassed glare and said, "You're so bad. I won't talk to you anymore."

She got out of his arms and ran into the bathroom.

After a quick wash, Grace returned to her pure and charming state.

She took 20 minutes to apply light makeup and then went out with Marcus.

By now, it was already past 8 p.m., the busiest time in Kingston.

Workers from all over the country had finished work, and restaurants, bars, night markets, bathhouses, and other entertainment venues were starting to fill up.

Marcus and Grace stepped out of the Ritz-Carlton Hotel.

A military vehicle sped over and stopped right in front of them.

Several Uswad soldiers in military uniforms got out of the vehicle.

The leader was a woman, who appeared to be under 30 years old but already had the rank of lieutenant colonel.

The woman was beautiful, about 5'7'' tall, with a curvy and fit figure. In her military uniform, she looked heroic.

Her large, spirited eyes made her unforgettable after just one glance.

After the Uswad soldiers got out of the vehicle, they all saluted Marcus in a standard military way, their faces filled with respect.

The woman at the front spoke in a clear, powerful voice, "Lydia, staff officer of the 105th Command of the Uswad military, presents her greetings to Mr. Fitzgerald."

Marcus narrowed his eyes and returned a not-so-standard military salute. He asked, "What's the matter? Why are you looking for me?"

Apart from Oliver and Jareth, the deputy commander of Solace Military Division, Marcus had never really interacted with the Uswad military.

He didn't know why Lydia was looking for him.

She replied, "Mr. Fitzgerald, after you became a special advisor to the military, Commander Ashford personally set up a Special Advisor Department for you in the Uswad military. We are here on his orders to work in the Special Advisor Department.

"From today on, I am your secretary."

Lydia looked Marcus up and down.

She had heard of Marcus' heroic deeds of rescuing Julian.

In person, he was even more handsome than she had imagined.

This made her feel a bit dazzled.

"Special Advisor Department? But didn't we agree I wouldn't need to work for the Uswad military?" Marcus asked, surprised.

Lydia smiled and replied, "Yes, the Special Advisor Department is set up for you, Mr. Fitzgerald.

"As per the previous agreement, you still won't need to go to the military to work.

"On normal days, we will handle your work for you.

"And if you encounter difficult issues, we can use the military's power to help you solve them."

Marcus relaxed upon hearing this.

He didn't care whether the Special Advisor Department was set up, as long as he didn't need to go to the military.

From Lydia's explanation, it seemed that the department was created to make things more convenient for him, without causing any other issues.

He wouldn't refuse a department that could be helpful at times, especially when military power could solve many problems.

Marcus tentatively asked, "By the way, I'd like to know, what rank do I have as a special advisor, and what rights do I have? Could you explain that to me?"

Lydia's eyes sparkled. She hadn't expected that Marcus didn't even know how high his position was.

Lydia was puzzled.

It seemed he didn't care about fame at all.

She thought for a moment and then explained to Marcus, "The special advisor position was recently established by the Uswad military. Although it carries the rank of a major general, the position itself is equivalent to a military region commander.

"In theory, as long as you're willing, you could command the forces of an entire military region."

Marcus was surprised.

He had thought that Julian's offer was just a formality.

He never expected it to be such a powerful position, almost equivalent to a military region commander.

People in this position were usually considered military giants.

They were real big shots. From a rank perspective, Marcus now surpassed Benjamin, Grace's father.

Grace, who had been quietly listening, was also shocked.

She never imagined that Marcus' position held such great power.

Benjamin had worked for years in politics to become the mayor of Solomia. That was already an impressive achievement.

But now, this position had completely overshadowed Benjamin's.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, did we interrupt your travel plans? If so, we can leave now. Please feel free to contact me anytime. As your secretary, I'm available 24/7," Lydia added, remembering this part.

She added Marcus on WhatsApp and exchanged phone numbers with him.

Afterward, she left with those soldiers in military uniforms.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, you're amazing!" Grace said.

She looked at Marcus with admiration.

Marcus waved his hand and said, "It's just an empty title. Nothing to boast about."

Then the two of them took a taxi to a famous restaurant in Kingston.

The restaurant was very popular and well-known online.

Many celebrities visited to try the food and take photos.

Girls like Grace loved visiting trending spots.

She had wanted to come here for a long time, and she was thrilled to be here with Marcus.

But when they arrived, the restaurant was packed, and there were no available seats.

They had to find a place to wait for a table.

"Grace, is that you?" Just then, a familiar voice came from nearby.

Grace instinctively turned around and saw a man and a woman walking toward the restaurant.
