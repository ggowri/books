Chapter 864 Max Durant-1

The sudden appearance of the group left everyone stunned.

Every person present, including Cody, wore expressions of absolute shock on their faces.

The reason was straightforward—the man leading the group, wearing two wheat-stalk stars, was Commander Max Durant of the Rowane Military District, Cody's father.

Even Maxine found herself taken aback by his presence.

Someone of Max's rank and stature rarely appeared in the public eye.

She hadn't seen this decorated war hero and Commander of the Rowane Military District in many years.

"Good afternoon, Sir!"

As Max approached, the uniformed personnel immediately snapped to attention, executing a perfect military salute.

Max returned their salute with a brief gesture before fixing his gaze on Cody, his face dark with displeasure.

"Dad, what brings you here? I thought you had a meeting to attend," Cody asked, meeting his father's stern look.

"If I hadn't come when I did, you would have caused an absolute disaster!" Max replied in an ice-cold tone.

Cody looked stunned and asked Max, "Dad, what do you mean? I don't quite understand."

Max replied coldly, "Do you know who you were about to hit?"

Cody looked bewildered again and said, "Isn't he just Maxine's boyfriend? Just an ordinary guy from Solomia who supposedly has some military achievements, though I'm not sure if that's even true."

"How dare you!"

Before Cody could finish, Max snapped at him, "Listen carefully! This is Mr. Marcus Fitzgerald, the one who rescued Commander Ashford in Solomia!"

BOOM!

The news hit everyone like a thunderbolt.

Everyone stood frozen in shock, thinking they must have misheard.

Maxine, especially, felt her body tremble.

She suddenly remembered how Marcus had told her he was Special Advisor before Cody and the others arrived.

She'd dismissed it as a joke then, not taking it seriously.

But now, Max was confirming that Marcus was indeed Special Advisor!

How could there be any doubt?

For a moment, Maxine felt her mind spinning.

She stared at Marcus with shock and disbelief.

Cody trembled involuntarily. Staring at Max in disbelief, he said, "Dad, there must be some mistake. How could he possibly be Special Advisor?"

Max cut his son off sharply and said in an icy tone, "You ungrateful child, don't say another word. I couldn't possibly be mistaken about this. Apologize to Mr. Fitzgerald right now, or I won't be able to protect you if you upset him!"

His words hung heavy in the air.

Cody's expression crumbled utterly.

He felt like he was trapped in some bizarre dream.

Could this guy standing before him be the legendary Special Advisor who had single-handedly taken down dozens of criminals?

"Did you not hear what I said? Go apologize right now!"

Cody stood there in complete shock, unable to process the situation.

Max's stern reminder cut through his daze once again.

Finally, Cody had no choice but to accept the reality of the situation.

He couldn't wrap his head around the fact that Maxine had managed to date the Special Advisor!

The irony wasn't lost on him! He had just planned to arrange a meeting between Maxine and the Special Advisor.

Even more ridiculous was how he had refused to let Maxine bring Marcus along.

And now it turned out that Marcus was none other than the very Special Advisor they had been so eager to meet.

Looking back, he couldn't believe how foolish his previous behavior had been.

"I sincerely apologize, Mr. Fitzgerald. I had no idea who you were earlier. Please forgive my disrespectful behavior!"

Although Cody felt defiant inside, he had no choice but to swallow his pride, given the reality of the situation.
