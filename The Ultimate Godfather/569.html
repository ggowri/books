Chapter 568 Thanks

Marcus didn't explain but just took out his submachine gun and pulled the trigger at a rockery not far away.

The light burst in the muzzle of the gun.

The rockery was shot.

With a loud noise, the rockery directly collapsed and broke into rubble scattered on the ground.

This scene surprised Blake and Baron.

It took a long time before they spoke.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, what kind of gun is this? So powerful."

"This gun has the power of a mortar."

The two trembled and were surprised that the small submachine gun had such great power.

"Do you want it?"

Marcus asked with a smile.

Blake and Baron nodded with fanaticism in their eyes.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, are you really going to give us the gun?"

They were a little incredulous.

The powerful gun must be expensive.

And it probably wouldn't be mass-produced.

Marcus smiled, "Of course. I'll give you a hundred of these guns first. They'll arrive in about three days."

Then, he called Diana and told her the specific address of Axe Gang.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, thank you so much. If we had a hundred of these guns, no one in Kingston could defeat us!"

Blake said with confidence.

"Well, I hope so."

Marcus nodded and said to Blake, "I'm busy, so I'll leave the aftermath to you guys."

Blake agreed happily.

Marcus left the Axe Gang and went to see Lila.

After all, before he went to Acoton, he had promised her that he would return in two days.

After a while, he received a call from Lila.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, are you back? I missed you."

Lila's voice was soft and gentle and touched Marcus.

"I'm back and just about to go to see you," Marcus replied calmly.

"Liar! You said you'd come to see me in two days, but now 48 hours and 53 minutes have passed," Lila said with anger.

Marcus was surprised and helpless.

"My stepmother has come to Kingston and is now at my sister's house. I'll have dinner with them and my sister's boyfriend. Will you come with me?"

Lila pleaded.

Marcus asked in surprise, "What?"

Lila took a deep breath and said, "My mother died when I was three years old. And my father remarried. My stepmother gave birth to my sister that year.

"I was taken care of by my stepmother since I was a child.

"Although she is narrow-minded and snobby, she is pretty good to me. At least she never abused me since I was a child.

"I have always regarded her as my family. Even though my father is dead, I go home to visit her every year."

Marcus understood.

Lila grew up in a reorganized family.

But she was relatively lucky.

At least her stepmother was nice to her.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, what do you think?"

Lila asked with anticipation.

"Of course."

Marcus nodded lightly.

They were in a relationship now, after all, and Marcus wouldn't say no to Lila's request.

Soon, Marcus and Lila met at Lila's home.

Lila put on her makeup and clothes before she went downstairs.

They had dinner at a famous restaurant.

Lila's stepmother, Jacqueline Sassoon, sister, Liz Sassoon, and Liz's boyfriend, Abraham Toynbee, had already arrived.

"Lila, we missed you so much!"

Jacqueline said with a smile.

Although she and Lila had no blood relationship, they had a good relationship.

Liz said sarcastically with a smile.

"Mom, Lila is a star now and very busy. It's not easy for us to see her."

"It's a good thing. I hope you will be better in the future."

Jacqueline smiled.

Speaking, Jacqueline and Liz involuntarily looked at Marcus, who was standing next to Lila.

Liz asked, "Lila, who is this?"
