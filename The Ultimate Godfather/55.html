After the dinner ended, Haley left Charles' house.

He muttered, "That damn Marcus! Not only did he humiliate me today, but he also caused TreatMed Pharmaceuticals to suffer massive losses. I'll make him pay dearly!"

Sitting in the back seat of his Rolls-Royce Phantom, Haley dialed a number. "Hello, Bob? It's me."

A gruff voice answered on the other end, "Mr. Brown, you're a busy man. What brings you to me today?"

Haley's eyes gleamed with malice. "I need you to cripple someone's arms. Name your price."

Bob chuckled darkly, "For you, Mr. Brown, my old client, I'll give you a discount. One million dollars."

Hiring someone for such a job started at half a million dollars.

But Haley's targets were always prominent figures, so the prices were steep.

"Done. He shouldn't have gone far. Move out now, and I'll wire the money when it's done!" Haley agreed without hesitation, ten million dollars was nothing to him.

Only by cutting off Marcus's arm could he assuage his hatred.

Then, he would properly humiliate Yvonne!

As the heir to TreatMed Pharmaceuticals, Haley had many eyes and ears in Solomia.

Soon, he pinpointed Marcus's location and ordered his driver to follow. At the same time, he told Bob about it.

Marcus, driving his Alphard minivan, found his path blocked by Haley's convoy in a secluded street.

"Hey, bumpkin! Get out of the car!" Haley stepped out of his Rolls-Royce, a bloodthirsty grin on his face as he stared down Marcus.

Calmly, Marcus got out of his car and swept his gaze over Haley's group. "What's the meaning of this?"

"Teaching you a lesson," Haley sneered.

Marcus chuckled, unfazed, "You think you're capable of that?"

"You think I'll dirty my hands?" Haley shot back, stepping aside.

Several black SUVs pulled up, and Bob, accompanied by his men, stepped out. His intimidating presence filled the alley as he addressed Haley. "Mr. Brown, who's the target?"

Haley pointed at Marcus. "Him! Cut off both his arms and make sure he regrets being alive!"

"No problem. Anyone who crosses Mr. Brown doesn't walk away unscathed!" As he spoke, Bob's gaze naturally landed on Marcus. "You're unlucky to have crossed paths with me!"

After saying that, he waved his hand at the thugs behind him.

"Bob, long time no see. Looks like you've developed some temper?" However, before the thugs could make a move, Bob heard a familiar voice.

He squinted his eyes and immediately signaled the thugs to stop.

The street was dim, and Bob couldn't clearly see Marcus' face at first.

Upon closer inspection, Bob was utterly astounded. "Mr. Fitzgerald, how could it be you?"

The sight of Marcus' face sent a buzzing through Bob's head, nearly causing him to faint on the spot!

He vividly recalled the day when Marcus had single-handedly stormed the Cyan Serpent Lodge and slain Tory!

This memory instilled in Bob an ineradicable sense of dread towards Marcus.

In Marcus's presence, even without a word, Bob couldn't help but tremble.

"Are you here to help him chop off my hands?" Marcus asked with an intrigued tone.

Bob's expression collapsed entirely. "I dare not. Mr. Fitzgerald, I would never dare to offend you!"

As he spoke, Bob began to slap himself fiercely in front of everyone, uttering various apologies as he did so.

Haley's gaze intensified, his face filled with disbelief. He immediately stepped forward and asked, "Bob, what are you doing? Hurry up and chop off this guy's hands."

Bob backhanded Haley across the face, furiously declaring, "You son of a bitch, are you trying to get me killed? I'll kill you first!"

Haley, holding his face in bewilderment, said, "Bob, did you hit the wrong person? The one you should be hitting is that hick over there!"

Bob was seething with rage, and seeing Haley's expression fueled his anger. "Shut your damn mouth. It's you I'm hitting!"

As he spoke, Bob unleashed a barrage of punches and kicks on Haley.

Haley, merely a pampered rich kid, was no match for Bob. He quickly found himself on the ground, wailing in pain.

His entourage, terrified, also suffered, getting brutally beaten by the members of Serpent's Coil!

"Bob, please don't. Let's talk this through. Let's talk this through..." Haley whimpered, pleading for mercy.

Bob stopped when he was exhausted, then turned back to Marcus with a respectful demeanor, asking, "Mr. Fitzgerald, what should we do with this guy?"
