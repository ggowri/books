Chapter 164 Professor Cooper

Maxine's gaze towards Marcus had changed.

She never expected that Marcus would actually dare to sever Pete's arm in front of everyone!

And Maxine felt that Marcus' move wasn't just satisfying; it was also quite dominant, even a bit cool!

She had never imagined that one day, she would have such a dramatic shift in her perception of the man standing before her.

Thinking back to their first meeting at Kingston Airport, Maxine had once regarded Marcus as just another opportunistic lecher.

Now, reflecting on it, she suddenly realized how rash and hasty she had been back then.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, you've done our Uswad nation proud! On behalf of the Solomia government, I wanna thank you!"

Benjamin walked over, extending his hand to Marcus.

Before, he had seen Marcus as nothing more than a brave but naive young man, but now, his attitude towards Marcus had shifted to that of an equal.

"Mayor Benjamin, you're too kind. It was the least I could do."

Marcus waved dismissively.

Though he wasn't a diehard patriot, as a Uswad citizen, he couldn't stand Pete's arrogant attitude.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, by cutting off Pete's arm, the Hudson family from Eldoria won't let this slide easily. You should be cautious moving forward."

Benjamin warned Marcus.

The Hudson family not only held immense power in Eldoria, but they also had significant influence worldwide.

They couldn't possibly just let this go.

Marcus didn't say much, only nodding in acknowledgment.

He had heard of the Hudson family, and if they dared come after him, he would ensure they would regret it.

"How is Edward?"

Benjamin asked, concerned about Edward's injuries.

"I just called Professor Cooper. He should be here soon."

Brenden replied.

Benjamin raised an eyebrow. "Professor Cooper? Do you mean Robert Cooper, the chief surgeon at Solariae Hospital?"

Brenden nodded. "Yes, that Robert. He's also from Solomia and has been on a break here recently."

"Edward's injuries are serious. I don't trust the local surgeons, so I had to bring in Professor Cooper."

At this, Benjamin, Maxine, and the others felt a sense of relief.

Robert was not only a well-known figure in Solariae's surgical field, but he was also highly respected in the entire Uswad medical community.

Especially in bone fracture surgeries, he had extensive clinical experience.

With Brenden bringing in Robert, it seemed Edward had a chance at recovery.

Although it might take time, at least he should be able to live independently again.

Soon, several doctors in white coats rushed into the venue, and the crowd instinctively parted to make way.

Leading them was none other than Robert, the chief surgeon from Solariae Hospital!

"Professor Cooper, I'm sorry to bother you while you're on vacation. I owe you one, big time!"

Brenden greeted him warmly.

Robert waved it off. "Brenden, no need for formalities. Just buy me a drink next time. And remember, I want that special 1958 vintage. Don't be stingy!"

Brenden chuckled. "No problem! As long as you can save Edward, I'll buy you the whole cellar if you want!"

With that, Brenden motioned towards Benjamin. "Oh, Mayor Benjamin's here too."

Robert followed Brenden's gesture and quickly spotted Marcus beside Benjamin. His expression froze for a moment.

"Marcus, what a surprise! You're also here!"

The scene from a month ago, at the matchmaking dinner for Yvonne and Haley, flashed through his mind.

At that time, Robert had thought of Marcus as nothing more than a poor farmer's son, someone unworthy of Yvonne.

During the dinner, Robert had made several snide remarks about Marcus, even insulting him.

But then Marcus had stunned everyone with his extravagant gift, silencing everyone.

Due to that unpleasant incident earlier, Robert remained unaware of Marcus' exact identity until the end of the banquet.

He hadn't expected to run into him again here.

"Professor Cooper, do you know Marcus?" Brenden asked, surprised.

"Yes. We had attended the same banquet together a month ago."

Robert nodded and stepped forward to greet Benjamin, Marcus, Maxine, and the others.

After exchanging pleasantries with Benjamin, Robert turned to Marcus. "Mr. Fitzgerald, we meet again."
