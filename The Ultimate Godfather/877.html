Jade was still asleep and hadn't woken up.

Marcus knew she was truly exhausted.

Not wanting to disturb her, he took advantage of her rest to visit Josie and bid her farewell.

He had been in Kingston for several days now, and it was time to return to Solomia.

He had also contacted Grace via WhatsApp and learned that Benjamin had been released from prison.

They had returned to Solomia the day before. With Benjamin's release, this trip to Kingston had been a complete success.

When Marcus returned to the hotel, he found that Jade was still sleeping.

It wasn't until 4:00 in the afternoon that she finally woke up.

"How did you sleep? Should we head back to Solomia?"

Marcus asked, suddenly feeling an eagerness to return home.

Jade rubbed her sleepy eyes, glanced at the sky outside, and said, "Let's take the evening flight back to Solomia then."

Marcus nodded slightly and immediately booked the tickets.

As soon as the plane landed, Jade received a call from the Solomia Hospital.

Although she had already been appointed as the deputy director, she had not yet officially assumed the position and was still responsible for her duties as the head of surgery.

A patient with severe trauma from a fall had just arrived at the hospital and required immediate surgery.

The moment she got off the plane, Jade hurriedly took a taxi straight to Solomia Hospital.

Marcus, on the other hand, returned to Bayside Villa alone.

However, just as he was about to enter the courtyard, a shadow flickered in the void.

This shadow had no physical form, only an energy fluctuation.

Anyone else would have been completely unable to detect its presence.

But Marcus, as an Acquired Return Essence Realm Stage Two expert, had cultivated the Hidden State, making his strength comparable to that of a Stage Four expert.

With the additional enhancement of various cultivation manuals, his combat prowess even surpassed most Stage Four experts.

From the very beginning of his cultivation, Marcus had placed great emphasis on spiritual powers.

As a result, his spiritual power was stronger than those of others with the same combat level.

By his estimation, his spiritual power was already on par with an Acquired Return Essence Realm Stage Six expert.

So, even though the shadow was extremely well-concealed, Marcus detected it instantly.

"Who's there?"

Marcus locked onto the figure's presence with his piercing gaze, swiftly unleashing his spiritual power to restrain the intruder.

Yet, the shadow remained silent, flickering ceaselessly.

"If you don't speak, I won't hold back."

His eyes turned cold. The Timeless Warrior's Grimoire began to operate at full force, and strands of Timeless Numinous Energy gathered in his palm, ready to strike.

Hearing Marcus' warning, the figure finally ceased its movement, hovering motionlessly in the air.

Then, the shadow gradually became solid.

Only then did Marcus get a clear look at the stranger.

It was a cloaked figure, emanating a mysterious glow. A wide-brimmed hat covered its head, obscuring its face in a blur.

Despite the gentle aura surrounding it, there was an inexplicable depth to its presence.

"Who are you, and why are you lingering here?" Marcus demanded.

Little did he know that this figure had appeared before, after one of his previous battles.

It seemed that this being had always existed in this world, simply unnoticed by most.

"Calm yourself, I mean no harm," the figure finally spoke, its voice strange and indistinguishable in gender. "I arrived in this world by accident and have been unable to return."

"You mean... you don't belong to this world?"
