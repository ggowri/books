Chapter 742 Ada's Arrival

Marcus swept his gaze across the crowd, sneering, "If anyone else has a problem with my presence here, step right up. I'll gladly deal with each one of you."

The crowd exchanged nervous glances, and those at Marcus' power level didn't dare speak up.

As for those at higher levels, like the silver-haired youth, they merely gave Marcus a dismissive glance before looking away.

Perhaps in their eyes, even though Marcus had defeated the middle-aged man, he still posed no threat to their status.

His current show of arrogance was nothing but self-satisfaction to them.

"You must be Mr. Fitzgerald?"

Just then, a voice called out from the distance.

Marcus looked toward the source and saw a man in his fifties emerging from the layers of white mist.

The middle-aged man exuded an overwhelming aura. Each casual step he took gave off an awe-inspiring presence.

He carried an ancient sword with a faint sword aura swirling around him.

It was none other than the Swords Sect patriarch, Liam Sky!

"You must be Patriarch Liam of the Swords Sect?" Marcus asked coolly.

Liam nodded with a warm smile. "Indeed, that would be me!"

Marcus gave Liam a respectful bow. "It's an honor to meet you!"

"Mr. Fitzgerald, you should have told me you were coming. I would have sent someone down the mountain to escort you," Liam said pleasantly.

Marcus replied, "The place wasn't too hard to find, so I didn't want to trouble you."

The onlookers watched this exchange with surprise.

None of them had expected Liam to treat Marcus with such courtesy.

This further confirmed that Marcus was no ordinary person.

Just then, everyone noticed the silver-haired youth rise from the boulder and walk toward them.

He addressed Liam, "Patriarch, may I ask who this is?"

Liam smiled as he introduced Marcus. "Nathan, this is Mr. Marcus Fitzgerald, a rising star in the Ancient Martial Art world of Uswad. His talent is extraordinary, and his power formidable. I specifically invited him to join us in exploring the Morvalis Ice Wall space-time gate."

Nathan Caster gave Marcus a dismissive look before addressing Liam. "You've invited several people below Ancient Martial Stage Seven. In my opinion, those at that level cannot safely approach the space-time gate's core. Without power, at least at Stage Seven, it's practically suicide. So regardless of his talent, he doesn't belong here."

Nathan took another contemptuous glance at Marcus as he spoke these words, not trying to hide his disdain.

Liam's expression darkened at Nathan's bluntness, and he tried to catch his eye with a warning look.

Nathan ignored Liam's signal and was about to turn away when a voice rang out nearby.

"Lady Ada has arrived!"

As those words echoed, eyes lit up across the crowd as everyone turned toward the entrance of the Illusion Rune.

A burst of light suddenly appeared there.

In that brilliant glow, an enchanting figure emerged into everyone's view.

She moved with ethereal grace, radiating an otherworldly purity that seemed untouched by mortal concerns.

A single glance left an unforgettable impression.

It was none other than Ada, renowned as the most beautiful woman in the World of Ancient Martial Arts!

"So Ada came too. She really is as stunning as they say."

"God, she's gorgeous. I've never seen anyone this beautiful before. One chance to get close to her would make my whole life worthwhile!"

"Regular folks like us shouldn't get our hopes up. A beauty like that is way out of our league!"

Ada's arrival set off a wave of excited chatter among the crowd.

Everyone gazed at her with undisguised admiration.

"Ancient Martial Stage Seven! Ada has advanced to Ancient Martial Stage Seven! That's incredible!"

A voice suddenly rang out from the crowd.

Only then did people notice that Ada had indeed advanced her cultivation to the Ancient Martial Stage Seven.

In the World of Ancient Martial Arts, reaching Ancient Martial Stage Seven meant joining the ranks of true elite masters.

Not only was Ada stunningly beautiful, but she had also reached the pinnacle of martial prowess.

She was the definition of perfection. Who wouldn't be drawn to a woman like that?

Nathan's eyes brightened at Ada's arrival.

Marcus caught the look of admiration and desire in his gaze.

Clearly, Nathan had unique feelings for Ada.

Ada glided forward with graceful steps, walking quickly toward Marcus and his group.

Nathan's face lit up with joy as he greeted her.

But Ada didn't even glance his way, instead walking straight to Marcus in front of everyone.

Her stunning face bore a rare smile as she said, "I didn't expect you to arrive so quickly. Had I known, I would have hurried here myself."

Her words left everyone stunned.

No one had expected Ada to know Marcus.

And from her behavior, it seemed she was already quite familiar with him.

She'd even ignored Nathan to greet Marcus first.

It made Nathan's brows furrow deeply.

"I just arrived myself," Marcus said coolly.

He hadn't expected such warmth from Ada this time.

After all, she'd always been hostile toward him before.

The change in her attitude was evident to anyone watching.

"Perfect timing then. Let's team up when we head to the Morvalis Ice Wall space-time gate," Ada suggested to Marcus.

Since their last parting, she'd had time to think about many things, including her relationship with Marcus.

Though Marcus had once forcefully claimed her, he had rescued her from dire situations more than once.

Marcus had fought against the three guardians of the Bloodthirsty Cult to save her, helping her break free from the Blood Curse.

After that battle, Ada's opinion of Marcus changed dramatically.

The more she interacted with Marcus, the more Ada realized he wasn't the villain she'd initially thought him to be.

Instead, she discovered he was a man who took responsibility and stood by his principles.

More importantly, his power was extraordinary.

Though Ada had always been strong-willed and independent, an actual alpha female type.

Deep down, all women were attracted to strength.

Ada couldn't help but feel something special for a powerful, responsible, and dependable man like Marcus,

Moreover, during their time apart, Ada thought of Marcus every night.

Her body would flush with heat whenever she recalled their passionate encounters, and an overwhelming desire would stir.

It made her question whether she had always been this passionate by nature.
