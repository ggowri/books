"Introduce to my sister? Who is this guy?" Edwin asked curiously.

Cora entered the house with the man in his thirties.

Cora smiled and said, "Let me introduce you. This is Atlas Cruz, the sales manager at Bayside Petroleum Company."

As soon as Edwin heard this, his eyes lit up, and he asked, "Bayside Petroleum Company is the biggest oil company in our East Uswad region, right? Being the sales manager there must make a lot of money!"

Cora replied with a hint of pride, "Of course. Atlas earns two to three million dollars a year. Besides his salary, he also owns several gas stations here in Solomia. Together, they make at least 30 to 40 million dollars in profit each year!"

At these words, Payne, Edwin, and Mia all looked shocked.

Earning 30 to 40 million dollars a year was an astronomical figure for them!

Payne quickly turned to Cora and asked, "Cora, are you introducing Atlas to Natalie?"

Cora nodded with a smile. "Yes, I often hang out with his mother, and we became quite familiar.

"Atlas has been divorced for two years and is still single. His mother is anxious and wants to find him a new wife. I thought of Natalie," Cora explained.

Then, Atlas greeted Payne and his family. When he smiled, his large yellow teeth were exposed.

Although not particularly handsome, Atlas was very confident.

Marcus noticed that, when greeting the Vance family, Atlas maintained a look of arrogance.

When Atlas saw Natalie, his eyes immediately brightened with greed.

It wasn't surprising that Atlas was attracted to Natalie. She had been the class beauty when she was in school, and now, as an adult, she was even more beautiful.

What man wouldn't want to take a second glance at such a beauty?

Payne tentatively asked Cora, "Has Atlas been divorced? Does he have children?"

Cora smiled, "One son and one daughter. He has a complete family.

"Don't worry, Payne. Atlas' kids are well taken care of by nannies. Natalie won't need to do anything once she marries him. She can just enjoy being a wealthy woman. I also heard Natalie say before that she doesn't want to have children.

"This works out perfectly. She doesn't need to bear children or take care of them. And with Atlas being so wealthy, I think it's a great match, so I brought him here. What do you think, Payne, Mia?"

Mia didn't speak.

Although Atlas' conditions were indeed very impressive, she liked Marcus more.

After all, Marcus had saved her life, and she could tell that her daughter genuinely liked him.

Payne laughed heartily, "Not bad. I think it's great. A man like Atlas is hard to find. Cora, you're really good to Natalie!"

Cora patted her chest and smiled, "Of course, I'm Natalie's aunt! Who else should I look out for if not her?"

Edwin turned to Cora and asked, "Aunt, how much of a wedding gift can he give us? My dad said that unless it's at least five million dollars, no one can marry my sister."

Upon hearing Edwin's words, Atlas immediately showed a hint of disdain.

His eyes swept over Natalie again, and he said, "Five million dollars? That's nothing. As long as Natalie is willing to marry me, I'll give you 20 million dollars as a wedding gift!"

This statement shocked Payne, Edwin, and Mia again.

A 20-million-dollar wedding gift.

If Natalie really married him, their family would be set for life!

"Atlas, is this true? If my sister agrees to marry you, you'll give us 20 million dollars as a wedding gift?"

Edwin took a deep breath, his voice filled with excitement.

Atlas nodded. "Sure, but I have one condition. Your sister must sleep with me today!"

At these words, Natalie's brows immediately furrowed.

Before she could say anything, Payne greedily spoke, "You two are getting married anyway. It will happen sooner or later. If you want it, there's no problem. I can make it happen!"

Natalie could no longer hold back. She turned to Payne and said, "Dad, I don't even know this person. How can you just agree to something like that? Also, I already have a boyfriend. How can you promise my marriage to someone without consulting me?"

Natalie knew her aunt was only pretending to introduce a wealthy man.

In reality, Cora would never be so kind-hearted.

The previous men she introduced had been the same.

She was either to please them or to ask for favors.

In short, Cora had always used Natalie as a pawn to gain favor with others.

As for whether the men were a good match for Natalie or could make her happy, Cora didn't care at all.

She only cared about her own interests and achieving her own goals.
