"That's great. We've succeeded in the auction. You country bumpkin, daring to compete with my dad, you're still too green," Haley couldn't help but laugh out loud, and it felt as though half of the pent-up frustration in his chest had been released. He felt a wave of relief.

"The Brown family in Kingston only gave me three billion dollars, and the remaining six and a half billion dollars need to be borne by us.

Jared had intended to deliberately drive up the price, making Marcus spend much more buying the land.

If determined to be maliciously raising the price and disrupting the auction order, they would face a huge fine of five billion dollars.

If they refused to pay, the auction had the right to seize all the company's bank accounts.

Jared, of course, was aware of this. He just didn't expect to be outplayed by Marcus!

Michelle glanced at Marcus's profile, her eyes filled with admiration.

Michelle truly admired Marcus from the bottom of her heart.

If they didn't care, he was completely out of options.
