Stanley nodded and said, "Yes."

In an instant, he threw a punch at Stanley in the face.

There was a loud bang. Before Stanley could react, Marcus knocked him down to the floor. His eyeballs were bruised, and blood oozed out...

"How dare you punch me! I will kill you today!"

His burly men picked up stools and smashed them at Marcus without hesitation.

Marcus remained calm and soon defeated the burly men, leaving them crying out in pain.

"Hey, you have guts and can fight well, but since you hit me, your life will definitely be taken tonight. Wait for me here, and I will bring men over to kill you right away!" Stanley said through gritted teeth, covering his stomach.

"Get lost! Don't let me see you again, or I won't show mercy!" Marcus warned coldly.

He didn't bother to pay any attention to these scoundrels.

Stanley glared at Marcus and then left with his men in fright.

Philip gazed admiringly at Marcus, amazed by his excellent fighting skills.

Catherine immediately stepped forward and exclaimed, "Mr. Fitzgerald, thank you. Otherwise, my dad would definitely have suffered today!"

Marcus waved his hand and said, "You are welcome."

"Do you believe you are handsome and powerful?"

It was Stanley. How arrogant!

Thomas' face darkened. He never dreamed that Toby would come so soon.

He didn't want to be implicated, but it seemed impossible for him to leave.

He was thinking about how to protect himself.

"Mr. Fitzgerald, please leave through the back door. There is a fire escape. Don't worry about us. He is looking for you. He will not harm us," Catherine urged, unwilling to involve Marcus in her family affairs.

Catherine was worried about him. She asked Laura to wait upstairs, and she and Philip chased after Marcus.

Fearing that the people from the Shadow Syndicate would go upstairs to catch him, Thomas had to follow them downstairs.
