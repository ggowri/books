Maxine frowned and said, "It's normal. They don't know since I never discuss my life with my family."

Cody gave Marcus a thorough once-over, his eyes filled with scrutiny.

As a Kingston native, Cody had known Maxine for many years, and their families had always maintained good relations.

Back in their teenage years, Cody had harbored feelings for Maxine.

However, Maxine had consistently turned down his advances.

Their contact gradually diminished after he left to study at Acoton Military Academy.

It wasn't until this year when he returned and became a senior staff officer in the Rowane Military District, that they began reconnecting.

Upon his return home, Cody couldn't help but notice that Maxine had grown even more beautiful than in her teenage years.

She had blossomed into a more feminine woman, developing a confident air that wasn't present in her younger days.

These changes had reignited Cody's romantic interest in Maxine.

He had been hoping to use this opportunity, and at the same time, Maxine asked for his help to take their relationship to the next level.

He hadn't expected Maxine to reveal she had a boyfriend suddenly.

Moreover, judging from their demeanor, they had been together in the patrol car earlier.

A twinge of jealousy shot through Cody's heart as he pieced together what might have happened in that patrol car.

"I'm pretty curious about this boyfriend you're hiding from your family. What does he do that makes him so unique to you?" Cody said with a seemingly playful smile, though his eyes fixed on Marcus with an icy stare.

"He's a local from Solomia with his own business in an industry we're unfamiliar with, so there's no need to discuss it further."

Maxine replied to Cody, deliberately keeping Marcus' details private.

She knew Marcus had always been discreet and never revealed his identity to outsiders.

Before Cody could respond, Maxine continued, "Why don't we bring my boyfriend along to meet Special Advisor? He's really into combat techniques and has even defeated one of Eldoria's cyborgs."

"They could exchange some valuable insights," she added.

Cody's eyes narrowed at this revelation, and he pressed further about Marcus, saying, "Eldoria's cyborg technology leads the world. Your boyfriend must be quite the expert."

His tone suddenly shifted as he continued, "However, the Special Advisor isn't someone anyone can meet. Even I had to go through my father's connections and get approval from Ms. Gray. It was hard enough getting permission for you to come along—I'm afraid adding your boyfriend to the list won't be possible."

At this point, Cody had come to see Marcus as nothing but a thorn in his side, so he naturally didn't want to bring him along.

Maxine frowned and said, "Can't you make an exception just this once?"

She didn't want Marcus to miss meeting with the Special Advisor.

"Look, I've already told you it's impossible to arrange. Even for me, meeting someone of Special Advisor's status is a rare privilege, let alone your boyfriend.

"Besides, someone like him meeting Special Advisor? That would be beneath Special Advisor's social standing."

Cody shook his head, his words barely concealing his contempt and disdain for Marcus.

In his mind, everything Maxine told him earlier was a lie.

He had dealt with Eldorian cyborgs during his time at Acoton.

While those cyborgs looked human on the surface, they were essentially robots enhanced through advanced technology.

The power these cyborgs wielded was far beyond anything a human could match.

This guy standing before him looked utterly ordinary. How could he be a match for those cyborgs?
