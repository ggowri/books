Back at Bayside Villa, Marcus went straight to his room and entered his cultivation state.

He began practicing the two kinds of magic, Fireball and Lightning.

Through this period of cultivation, the power of these two spells had further improved.

Marcus estimated that, at his current level, unleashing the full force of these spells could pose a threat even to an Ancient Martial Stage Two expert.

In addition, he further practiced the "Celestial Reversal" technique.

Although this technique was a bonus reward for cultivating the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire, it still required skill and finesse.

Only through continuous practice could he genuinely master it.

For three consecutive days, Marcus dedicated himself to honing these three techniques.

By the third day, his Fireball and Lightning had shown significant progress, capable of quickly obliterating even the peak of Ancient Martial Stage Two expert!

He had also gained proficiency in Celestial Reversal. From initially deflecting ping pong balls, he progressed to deflecting cold weapons and, eventually, even attacks many times stronger than his own!

Of course, achieving this level of deflection came at a significant cost to his Numinous Energy.

After a single maximum-strength deflection, Marcus estimated he would be completely drained, losing all combat effectiveness.

Therefore, this ultimate deflection was a last resort, a life-saving technique. Using it carelessly could leave him vulnerable to a counterattack.

On the third day, Marcus arrived at Serpent's Coil.

He was there to evaluate Jalen's progress with the Magic Codex.

Perhaps due to what went down at Yolanda Village, Jalen's fear of Marcus had reached new heights.

His motivation to perform had skyrocketed accordingly.

Now, Jalen could recite the Magic Codex backward and forwards and had even mastered essential control over fire and lightning elements.

Though these skills were only for show at this point, it proved he had the potential to learn more advanced magic.

"Well done!"

Marcus praised Jalen and ordered him to share his knowledge of the Magic Codex with the other Serpent's Coil members.

Marcus understood the balance of carrot and stick. It was how you built loyalty among your followers.

Energized by the praise, Jalen threw himself into teaching the Magic Codex day and night.

The knowledge spread like wildfire through their ranks!

In just a few days, every member of Serpent's Coil could recite the Magic Codex from memory.

Some of the more gifted ones could even perform one or two witchcrafts.

Since Jalen had mastered the basics so thoroughly, Marcus rewarded him by teaching him Lightning and Fireball spells.

Overjoyed, Jalen dove into practicing with renewed intensity.

Marcus noticed that while Jalen usually focused on combat, he showed remarkable aptitude when he applied himself to learning something new.

By his estimation, Jalen would master Lightning and Fireball within a month.

When that happened, Marcus would have his first Wizard subordinate.

As time went on, more Wizards would join their ranks, potentially forming an entire magic battalion!

Marcus was thrilled at the prospect of building this magical army.

At the Kingston Brown Manor, patriarch Ricardo was seething with rage upon receiving news of Jared's death.

"Those bastards dare to kill a member of the Brown family!" Ricardo roared.

His anger exploded like a volcano.

After comparing the security footage from Jared's villa with that from the Brown Manor in Kingston, Ricardo made a shocking discovery.

The person who killed his son Leo was the same one who murdered Jared!

Ricardo's fury intensified, regretting that he hadn't eliminated Marcus when he had the chance, which led to these dire consequences.

"Buzz..."

Suddenly, a screen in Ricardo's secret chamber began flickering wildly before displaying the image of a tall man with golden hair and blue eyes.

Ricardo's eyes narrowed before widening in surprise. "Mr. Roger Gellar, is that you?"

He recognized the man's identity and immediately became deferential.

"It's me." the man on the screen nodded.

Even through the display, his aura of authority was unmistakable.

"What can I do for you, Mr. Gellar?" Ricardo asked.

"Kill my brother. Do this to my satisfaction, and I'll elevate the Brown family to Uswad's most powerful dynasty," the man on screen declared.

"Your brother... would that be Marcus, the one who killed my son?" Ricardo ventured.

Ricardo's mind raced with a bold theory.

His instincts had given him the answer, and his heart began to pound.

"You're sharp. Since he killed your son, you'll seek revenge anyway. Complete this quickly, and the rewards for the Brown family will last generations," the man stated, his ice-blue eyes glinting with deadly intent.

"Don't worry, Mr. Gellar, Marcus won't be breathing for long!" Ricardo promised.

Roger nodded once, then the display flickered, and his image vanished utterly.

Roger Gellar was Godfather's second son!

Even for a power player like Ricardo, this was his first glimpse of Roger's true face.

He'd only heard of him through whispers and rumors.

Never in his wildest dreams did Ricardo expect Roger to reach out to him.

If he could fulfill Roger's request, the Brown family would claim the throne as Uswad's most powerful dynasty.

The prospect made Ricardo's heart race with ambition.

More importantly, he didn't doubt the promise for a second.

After all, this was the Godfather's son, a man whose father was the most influential figure on the planet, someone who could change countless lives with a single word.

Even though the Brown family commanded respect in Uswad, they were nothing compared to the Godfather's influence.
