The Leah family could have easily eliminated all obstacles with Marcus's power and become the absolute ruler of the Kinton Mountains.

But it was their arrogance and outdated thinking that made them miss this golden opportunity.

And such an opportunity seems to never come again.

Afterward, ignoring the Leah family, Pedro led numerous Henderson family experts aboard the flying battleship and left Emotionless Gorge.

Their strengths were evenly matched.

Now, Kyson is dead, and nine Grandmasters of the Leah family are injured. However, Pedro alone cannot defeat them.

If they continue to fight, they will only end up in a lose-lose situation, benefiting other families.

In this situation, stopping the fight is the wisest choice.

Therefore, watching Pedro and the others leave, the Leah family did not stop them. The conflict ended there.

At this moment, Trevor said, "Minerva, come here."

Minerva asked, "What are your orders?"

Trevor asked, "Tell me, how far has Gertie and Marcus's relationship progressed?"

The others couldn't help but perk up their ears, equally eager to know the answer.

In response to Trevor's question, Minerva dared not hide anything and answered truthfully, "They have already been intimate."

"That's wonderful. They're already so close. Truly a blessing for the Leah family!"

As soon as Minerva finished speaking, Trevor burst into a fit of wild and joyful laughter.

The nine Grandmasters of their family were usually extremely dignified and never smiled casually.

At this moment, Trevor was clearly a bit out of control.

"Why are you laughing?" Minerva asked, somewhat puzzled by his meaning.

"I'm happy that Gertie and he got involved."

"Although he is unwilling to deal with the Leah family now, I believe over time, he will definitely help us for Gertie's sake."

"He might even elevate the Leah family to become the top family in the Kinton Mountains!"

Trevor's weathered eyes sparkled with anticipation.

The others nodded along, thinking his words made some sense.

Minerva immediately understood.

She could see that Marcus truly cared for Gertie.

Otherwise, he wouldn't have killed Kyson for Gertie.

As someone who had lived for centuries, Minerva had naturally heard of the legends about the world beyond the Ice Wall.

That was the vastest land, hiding numerous top experts.

Compared to the world within the Ice Wall, Lemuria's power was naturally stronger.

Yet Marcus, for Gertie, didn't hesitate to kill Roderick's subordinate.

This was enough to prove how much he valued her.

"Listen, if you meet him again in the future, you must treat him with respect and show him the utmost regard.

"If anyone disrespects him, you don't need to report to me. You can enforce the Leah family's highest level of punishment and execute them," Trevor continued to instruct.

Everyone squinted their eyes and nodded one after another.

They also realized the hidden opportunities of the Leah family.

If they could win Marcus's favor, the Leah family could rise in the Kinton Mountains.

Marcus used teleport and took Gertie away from Emotionless Gorge.

But they didn't go far and instead arrived at an open area outside the Leah Manor.

Gertie looked at Marcus with admiration.

Earlier, his battle to slay Kyson was absolutely breathtaking.

It was the strongest battle Gertie had ever seen.

She truly began to admire Marcus.

He smiled and said, "You're my woman. No need to be so polite with me."

"Besides me, the entire Leah family also needs to thank you. Without you, the Leah family might have been destroyed by Kyson!" Gertie said.

"I only did it to save you. This has nothing to do with the Leah family. If it weren't for you, given what they've done to me, I would have killed them all long ago."

Marcus continued, "Alright. You've just returned to the family, so you probably have other things to handle. I'll take my leave now."

Seeing him about to leave, Gertie suddenly felt a bit reluctant.

However, she didn't say much and just nodded gently.

Immediately after, Marcus quickly used teleport and disappeared from Gertie's sight.

Watching his departing figure, she felt as if she had just woken from a long dream.

Everything that happened in the dream felt so real. She seemed to want to continue this dream.

However, the other protagonist in the dream had already left her.

After leaving Kinton Mountains, Marcus returned to Bayside Villa.

For the next few days, he stayed in the villa to train.

With the help of various pills and the cultivation resources he found in Rivers and Kyson's Spatial Rings, Marcus successfully advanced his cultivation to Acquired State Eight.

Then, through Dual Cultivation with Diana, Jane, Pearl, and other women, he further enhanced his cultivation.

Half a month later, Marcus's cultivation had reached Acquired State Nine, elevating his strength to a whole new level.
