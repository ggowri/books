Chapter 749 Slaying the Sea Monster!

Marcus frowned as he activated Shadow Step, quickly dodging to one side while pulling out a golden scimitar from his Spatial Ring.

He brought the blade down swiftly toward one of the sea monsters.

The sea monster let out an earth-shattering roar.

As the golden Blade Ray descended, it sliced clean through the shark fin.

Blood burst forth, turning the surrounding waters crimson.

Enraged, the sea monster lunged at Marcus with its open jaws.

In its fury, the beast moved twice as fast as usual.

The sea monster's teeth caught Marcus' clothes in that split second.

With a loud ripping sound, it tore a gash in Marcus' clothing.

The flesh beneath was torn and bleeding!

Marcus grimaced as searing pain shot through his body.

Fortunately, these sea monsters were low-level beasts.

Yet their overwhelming offensive power, destructive capability, and defense made them comparable to human warriors at Ancient Martial Stage Nine!

There were five sea monsters in total.

It meant Marcus was facing an assault from five Ancient Martial Stage Nine warriors simultaneously!

Moreover, these creatures had thick hides, immense strength, and attack power that surpassed those at the Foundation Establishment Stage.

A single bite from these sea monsters would instantly tear anyone below the Return Essence stage to shreds!

Marcus channeled the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire to its limit, wreathing himself in Timeless Numinous Energy.

Using Shadow Step repeatedly, his movements remained incredibly swift even in these deep ocean waters.

When another sea monster attacked, he gathered his energy and unleashed a devastating Fist Ray instead of dodging.

This punch contained powerful Timeless Numinous Energy and terrifying Rune magic.

Streams of Rune patterns materialized along the Fist Ray, intertwined with Thunderfire Power.

The power of this single punch was beyond what even an Ancient Martial Stage Nine warrior could withstand.

In the blink of an eye, the Fist Ray struck the sea monster's body.

A thunderous boom echoed through the waters.

The impact sent the sea monster flying backward, crashing into a nearby castle.

The resulting shockwave caused the castle to collapse with a deafening rumble.

The monster roared, somehow managing to withstand Marcus' Fist Ray attack.

Marcus was shocked. The sea monster's defense was more potent than anticipated.

He took another step forward, instantly appearing before the sea monster.

The creature was still reeling from the previous Fist Ray's impact.

It snapped at Marcus with frightening power.

But Marcus easily avoided it using Shadow Step.

Marcus unleashed another devastating Fist Ray, even more terrifying than before, crashing it into the sea monster's skull.

The impact thundered through the water with a deafening boom.

The beast's head took the full force of the blow, and its massive body lost balance and plummeted downward.

It crashed through a section of buildings, demolishing them.

Almost simultaneously, the other two sea monsters lunged at Marcus with snapping jaws. Despite their simple minds.

Their attack came with deadly stealth, showing the predatory instincts of highly evolved hunters.

If not for Marcus' mighty spiritual powers, he would've been torn to shreds by these marine behemoths.

Thanks to his extraordinary predictive abilities, Marcus dodged both monsters' attacks.

Marcus figured out these sea monsters' attack patterns after these few exchanges.

His battle strategy became crystal clear.

Take them down one by one!

So, after evading the two sea monsters' attacks, Marcus ignored them completely.

Instead, he darted toward the beast he'd already hammered with successive blows earlier.

That monster, its head still reeling from Marcus' Fist Ray, was careening out of control through the underwater city, demolishing buildings in its wake.

Seizing the moment, Marcus launched two more consecutive Fist Rays!

The Fist Rays cut through the water like lightning, their earth-shattering power accompanied by rolling thunderous booms.

The monster let out an agonized cry as the shockwave hit, blood seeping from its crimson eyes.

Its internal tissues were damaged from the impact.

The monster lunged at Marcus with its massive jaws. Still, Marcus' golden curved blade slashed horizontally, cleaving through several of the beast's teeth.

He followed up with a devastating punch straight to the monster's forehead!

A sickening crack echoed through the air.

That single blow had shattered the sea beast's skull!

It let out one final cry of agony before its massive body crashed down, demolishing a building as it fell lifeless.

Seeing their companion slain, the remaining four sea monsters launched an even more ferocious assault on Marcus.

Now that Marcus had found their weakness, he employed a divide-and-conquer strategy.

Using his superior speed and agility, he focused his attacks on one beast while dodging strikes from the others.

Though he sustained some injuries, his plan was working smoothly.

BOOM!

Another thunderous shockwave tore through the air.

Under Marcus' Fist Ray, the skull of the final sea beast caved in. Its massive body crashed to the ground, joining the others in death.

And so, all five sea beasts fell by Marcus' hand alone.

"Whew..." Marcus exhaled heavily, wiping sweat from his forehead.

He'd killed five leviathans, yet he could still detect faint energy pulses emanating from their bodies.

Intrigued, Marcus approached one of the massive corpses.

He discovered a glowing orb inside the creature's body using his X-ray vision.

The energy waves were radiating from this mysterious orb.

According to the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire, this sphere was the beast's core.

Beast cores were the wellspring of power within magical creatures.

The higher the creature's rank, the more energy its core contained.

While these leviathans weren't particularly high-ranked, their sheer size made up for it.

Moving such enormous bodies required tremendous energy reserves.

As a result, their cores held substantial power.

The Timeless Warrior's Grimoire listed numerous uses for beast cores.

It could be used for cultivation, Rune crafting, amulet making, and many other purposes.

Without hesitation, Marcus used his golden scimitar to slice open one leviathan's belly.

He precisely located the core's position.

The moment Marcus held the core in his palm, he felt its immense power surging through him.

"This core's quality far exceeds my expectations. Using it for cultivation will give my powers a massive boost!"
