Chapter 444 Scared?

"You monster! Guess I was wrong all these years, thinking of you as my real sister."

Tracy gritted her teeth, her gaze shifting to Tristan, and she pleaded, "Tristan, what are we going to do? Please, think of something!"

Tristan's face darkened. Riley was the most influential person he knew, but even he couldn't help him now.

What was worse, he was treating Marcus with all due respect.

But Tristan still had one last card to play—his parents.

His parents were executives at Eastcomm Group, and he was certain they'd find a way out.

With that thought, Tristan threw Marcus a defiant look and said, "Brat, don't think just because you know people in the Ganston SWAT team, you're all that. My parents are high-level execs at Eastcomm Group. I'll call them right now, get them to call the big shots in Kingston, and order the SWAT team to release us!"

As he spoke, Tristan pulled out his phone and started dialing.

"Your parents are executives at Eastcomm Group?"

Marcus raised an eyebrow, slightly intrigued.

He couldn't quite figure out why he kept running into people from his company everywhere he went.

Was it because his empire was just that vast? Maybe...

"What's wrong, scared?" Tristan continued with a mocking smile.

"Eastcomm Group is the leading company in Kingston's emerging industries, and they're famous nationwide. My parents know a lot of influential people. Dealing with some Solomia guy like you will be a piece of cake! If you're scared, tell them to release us right now and apologize to me. Maybe I'll let you off the hook. Otherwise, get ready for my wrath!"

Tristan sneered, thinking Marcus was intimidated by the mention of his parents. He was sure that Marcus was about to back down.

Marcus' voice sounded.

"You believe me or not, one phone call and I can have your parents fired."

Tristan scoffed, and the Dowson family laughed too, their gazes filled with mockery.

Tracy scoffed, "You think you can make things happen just because you know a few SWAT officers? You probably haven't even heard of Eastcomm Group. Getting them fired? That's laughable!"

"Tracy, stop wasting time with him. I'm dying to see how one phone call can get Tristan's parents fired," Lucas chimed in, his voice laced with sarcasm.

"Go ahead and make that call. You have five minutes. If it doesn't work, you'll regret it!" Tristan shot back, his voice dripping with challenge.

"One minute will do," Marcus said with a smile.

Without another word, he dialed the president of Eastcomm Group.

As soon as the president heard Marcus' name, he became extremely polite.

Marcus immediately located a couple named Harrold, two managers in the company, and ordered the president to fire them both.

The president immediately set the wheels in motion.

After hanging up, Marcus was met with Tristan's mocking voice once again.

"Well, I've got to give it to you. You play the role well. You really think you're some big shot who knows the president of Eastcomm Group? I even suspect that your connections with these SWAT officers are just part of the act!"

Just as Tristan finished his taunt, his phone rang.

It was his father.

Tristan quickly answered the call, only to hear his father's dejected voice. "Tristan, your mom and I just got fired. You have to keep the BioFusion Pharmaceuticals deal going. This is our last chance at income!"

Tristan's world shattered.

His hands trembled, and he stammered, "Dad, what... what did you say? I didn't catch that. Say it again!"

His father repeated what he'd just said.

In that instant, Tristan felt his world spinning. He almost collapsed.

"Tristan, what's going on? What happened?" Tracy asked, worry lining her face.

"My... my parents... they've been fired!" Tristan said, his voice full of despair.

The room fell silent, all eyes wide with disbelief.

No one had expected Marcus to be right.

One phone call, not even a minute, and Tristan's parents were out of a job.

How could this be? How was it possible?

How?!

Tristan's parents were high-level executives at Eastcomm Group, earning millions of dollars a year.

How could they be fired so easily just because of one phone call?

The entire Dowson family was shocked, their gazes turning toward Marcus with newfound respect.

Even Miles and Riley, who had been watching from a distance, exchanged a look of surprise.

Until now, they'd thought Marcus was just an unstoppable force in battle—he'd single-handedly wiped out the Black Hawk Legion's cyborgs and earned his legendary status.

But now, they were seeing that his connections were just as powerful.
