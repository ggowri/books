Chapter 282 You're Dead Meat!

Marcus got up from his seat, stretched, and then said to Andre, "You're the secretary-general of the Municipal Government in Solomia?"

Andre was somewhat surprised by his attitude.

No one had talked to him like this for a long time. "Yeah, you got a problem with it?"

"Then you must know Adam, right?" Marcus asked.

Adam was the director of the Architecture Department.

When Marcus attended Nicole's parents' meeting, he had a conflict with Adam's wife.

But Adam was seized by the Department of Justice and under investigation of receiving a bribe of 10 million dollars from Golden Group.

According to Michelle, Adam had a close relationship with Andre.

Andre took advantage of the change in his position and helped Adam.

The two were involved in corruption, which was mentioned in the survey results of the Department of Justice.

However, the case was still in the final approval process, and the convict had not been announced for the time being.

So Andre only knew Adam was caught, but he didn't realize that the danger was approaching him.

Hearing his words, Andre frowned, "Adam is a corrupt criminal. Why did you mention him?

"And I'm warning you. I'm not familiar with him, so don't frame me, or I'll order someone to arrest you now!"

Andre acted all worked up.

So it was a little taboo to mention Adam in front of him.

After all, his people were caught and he was frightened.

Marcus didn't say anything.

Instead, he took out his phone and checked his chat record with Amelia.

Amelia had been reporting to Marcus about some specific details of Adam's case investigation these days.

Several of them involved Andre.

So he picked out one and said, "On May 28, 2024, Adam received a bribe of eight million dollars from the construction company and gave you three million.

"On June 29, 2024, Adam withheld construction workers' wages and profited four million dollars. And you made 1.5 million dollars out of it.

"On July 23, 2024, Adam made a profit of six million dollars from kickbacks on building materials and gave you two million dollars.

"Don't tell me that you don't remember all these things." Marcus looked at Andre.

His voice was so loud that everyone present could hear him.

Many were stunned in place, especially Andre, Emma, and the rest of Rivera Construction.

Their faces looked livid.

"What are you talking about? I have never received money from Adam. If you say it again, believe me, I will have the police arrest you right now!" Andre panicked.

Because everything Marcus said was true.

He didn't know who this young man was and why he knew so much confidential information.

"Who are you to frame my uncle? Oh, I see. You must be dissatisfied with Rivera Construction winning the bid, so you are trying to sabotage us, aren't you?" Emma's voice came with hatred.

"Just ask him whether I had framed him. I will say no more." Marcus shrugged and sneered.

Then Amelia messaged him again and told him that Adam's case had been thoroughly investigated.

Besides Adam, several other big shots were involved, including Andre, the secretary general of the municipal government.

Andre had protected Adam for years and allowed his corruption and bribery.

At the same time, he received huge amounts of property.

The Department of Justice had obtained sufficient evidence and would announce it to the public soon.

"Guards. Arrest this filthy liar and send him to the police!"

Andre didn't want to listen to Marcus' nonsense anymore.

Every word Marcus said made him tremble and he panicked.

With that, several municipal government workers wanted to catch Marcus.

"Mr. Fitzgerald!"

Melina looked nervous.

Although she knew Marcus was not an ordinary person, Andre was the secretary-general of the municipal government, who they couldn't offend.

But Marcus would offend such a person for her. Melina was both guilty and worried.

Marcus gave Melina a reassuring look. "Andre, you're at death's door and yet you don't know it. Within one minute, you will receive a call from the municipal government."

Andre's heart sank. However, he thought Marcus was just trying to scare him.

This man was too young to know everything.
