Chapter 1002 The New Path of Conquest-5

Since all energy crystal mines are controlled by the Atrandis Empire, the energy crystals they obtain are of very high quality.

A modified gun is almost equivalent to the power of an Ancient Martial Stage Nine expert.

In this way, when an ordinary soldier gets a gun, theoretically, he can possess the terrifying attack power of an Ancient Martial Stage Nine expert.

The entire Atrandis Empire has tens of millions of soldiers.

When everyone is equipped with such guns, they are equivalent to an army of tens of millions of Ancient Martial Stage Nine experts!

Additionally, Marcus also recruited numerous beastmasters to raise magical beasts, enhancing the overall strength of the Atrandis Empire.

After years of effort, the Atrandis Empire has been transformed by him into a truly indestructible fortress, even capable of expansion, striking fear into its enemies!

However, Marcus did not allow his subordinates to invade any continent beyond the Ice Wall.

On this day, within the palace of the Atrandis Empire, the sky darkened, and fierce winds swept through. A supremely powerful aura descended from the heavens.

The beam of light pierced straight into the clouds, seemingly connecting heaven and earth, dazzling beyond compare.

Marcus sat on a seat inside the palace, bathed in the beam of light.

With the aid of several top-tier pills, he was breaking through to a state beyond Innate State, the Martial Exalted Realm!

That was the state Ryan had pursued his entire life but failed to break through.

In just five years of cultivation, Marcus transformed from an ordinary person into a Martial Exalted Realm expert. His talent for cultivation was astonishing. This was unprecedented!

At the same time, this was also thanks to the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire he cultivated.

This type of cultivation manual not only allows Dual Cultivation but is also all-encompassing. Especially in terms of cultivation speed, it's far more effective than any magical cultivation manual commonly found on the market.

It was precisely the Timeless Warrior's Grimoire that made Marcus who he is today.

He remembered the old man who once gave him the pendant.

That old man was the one who changed his destiny. Marcus missed him deeply and longed to see him again.

Once, Marcus spent a lot of time searching for clues.

But as his cultivation and state grew, he realized that someone who could casually give him a pendant containing such powerful cultivation manuals couldn't be an ordinary person.

That old man's state might have already surpassed the level of this world.

According to the logic in some web novels, such people are likely already immortals.

The world they live in is called fairyland.

Before Marcus's cultivation and state reached that level, he had almost no chance to see that old man again, unless that old man took the initiative to find him.

This breakthrough, though quite slow, was also very smooth.

Several hours later, Marcus's cultivation reached the Martial Exalted Realm!

His strength made another qualitative leap, countless times stronger compared to his previous Innate State.

Marcus took a few days to stabilize his state.

At the same time, he knew the time had come.

It was time for him to head to Atrandis Continent and reclaim what belonged to Ryan.

So, in the following days, Marcus didn't continue cultivating but spent time with his family and women.

They enjoyed delicious food together, went out to have fun, and cherished the rare peaceful moments.

Half a month later, Marcus informed everyone about his upcoming journey to Atrandis Continent.

Although they were reluctant, they all supported his decision.

As Ryan's son, Marcus naturally had to honor Ryan's dying wishes.

What's more, having inherited Ryan's position, he was even more obliged to fulfill his wishes.

Thus, on this day, Marcus, escorted by his women and family, arrived at Morvalis's Ice Wall.

Maxine's eyes were filled with tears. She gently touched her already swollen belly and said reluctantly, "Marcus, you must come back soon. We're all waiting for you at home!"

In her belly was Marcus's child.

He kissed her forehead tenderly and promised, "Don't worry. I will definitely come back. Once I completely conquer the Atrandis Continent, I'll bring all of you there to live."

"Marcus, you must keep your word. We're all waiting for you here. Don't you dare lie to us!"

Natalia also walked over. Over the years, she had grown much more mature, yet she was still incredibly beautiful.

Marcus pulled her into his arms, gently stroking her hair, and said, "Of course. I won't lie to you."

"My dear, remember to write to us often when you get there. We'll all miss you."

Jane and Josie also walked over, their eyes filled with tears.

Marcus comforted them.

Yvonne, Michelle, Grace, Natalie, and the other girls also looked reluctant, holding back their tears.

But at the thought of parting with Marcus, they couldn't help but sob softly.

Richard said, "Marcus, don't worry. Your mom, Nicole, and I will always support you. I know you're outstanding and will surely fulfill Godfather's last wish in the future."

Although he was only Marcus's foster father, he always treated Marcus as his own son.

Marcus said, "Dad, I got it. Don't worry. You and Mom take good care of yourselves. And you, Nicole, you're not young anymore. Hurry up and find a boyfriend. I want to be an uncle."

Finally, his gaze fell on Nicole again.

Now, she had already graduated from college and was a beauty.

After Marcus became the king of the Atrandis Empire, Nicole naturally became a princess, admired and revered by all.

Hearing his words, her pretty face blushed. She said, "Marcus, stop worrying about me. I definitely won't let you down. When you come back, you'll have a nephew!"

He laughed and said, "Alright, I’ll remember that."

Marcus's gaze swept over the family and women before him, those who shared passion and hardships with him day and night.

Though reluctant, he knew a greater mission awaited him, and he had to give it his all.

After hugging each of them one by one, he took Diana, Alison, Ada, and Gertie with him into the space-time gate of Morvalis's Ice Wall.

The reason was simple. These four women were the strongest among those around him.

If they stayed within the world of the Ice Wall, it might hinder their personal growth.

Marcus brought them to the Atrandis Continent to broaden their horizons and help them grow quickly.

The Ice Wall's space-time gate shone brightly. The power of time and space enveloped their five bodies. Then, they vanished from everyone's sight.

Marcus thus embarked on a new path of conquest. He was destined to forge a brilliant life!
