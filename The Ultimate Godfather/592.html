Their orders had increased dramatically, and the entire logistics company seemed to be on the right track as soon as they opened it.

It was the day that the logistics company was open for business tomorrow, and Marcus was ready to visit it.

"Darian, don't you look in the mirror? You're just a truck driver, but you also want to organize a logistics company, huh? You are unbelievable!"

"Let me tell you, with our Rising Logistics Company around, you can't run your company!"

He saw hundreds of people in logistics overalls with weapons in hand, surrounding Darian, Ramona, Kiera, and the newly hired senior figures of the logistics company.
