Osborne had hoped for reinforcements, but as he glanced at the other battlefields, despair washed over him.

Both Everett and Rumen, top-tier Class S fighters, were overwhelming the Augustine family. Their strength was simply too much; the family stood no chance against such power.

Realization hit him like a ton of bricks: without help, they would soon be wiped out.

If they fell, it would mean the end of the Augustine family.

And the Draco Group would follow suit.

His eyes blazed with fury, a fierce determination igniting within him.

Osborne wouldn't allow that to happen!

Sebastian had entrusted him with the mantle of head of the Augustine family and the reins of the Draco Group. There was no way he would let them be annihilated.

"It looks like I have no choice but to go all out!"

Osborne gritted his teeth, his gaze icy as he fixed it on the two Class S Medusa Queens, along with Scarlett, Bradley, and Marlon.

"Damn you all!"

As the words left his lips, Osborne underwent a dramatic transformation. He morphed into a Vampire, his speed, strength, and defenses amplifying exponentially.

But that wasn't enough. He activated the Breath of Celestial, pushing his power to new heights—now at the fifth level, it doubled his strength once again.

At that moment, Osborne felt invincible.

The stone-like skin that had encased him shattered, falling away as the Medusa Queens' petrification powers lost their grip on him.

Scarlett and the others exchanged alarmed glances, their faces draining of color.

"Die!"

Without giving them a moment to react, Osborne lunged forward, appearing before Scarlett, Bradley, and Marlon in the blink of an eye.

He channeled the power of his right leg's Divine Bone, making his attack even more terrifying as he kicked out at the trio.

Their eyes widened in horror as they sensed the impending danger. They pooled their strength, desperately trying to fend off Osborne's assault.

But the Osborne they faced now was a far cry from what they had anticipated.

A collective scream echoed as the force of his kick shattered their arm bones, sending a jolt of agony through their bodies.

Blood sprayed from their mouths as they were sent crashing to the ground, unable to rise.

In a single move, Osborne had left all three of them gravely injured.

They stared at him in sheer terror, unable to comprehend how a full-powered Osborne could be so overwhelmingly powerful. They had joined forces, yet not even a single blow could they withstand.

"How is this possible?"

Fear gripped them as they watched Osborne advance, intent on finishing them off.

The two Medusa Queens, seeing the dire situation, unleashed their petrification powers in a desperate attempt to stop him, but it was futile.

With gritted teeth, they positioned themselves in front of Osborne, trying to shield their allies.

"Get lost!"

Osborne's face remained impassive as he unleashed another kick, sending the two Medusa Queens flying, their bodies nearly exploding from the impact, their fighting spirit crushed.

Scarlett, Marlon, and Bradley were now paralyzed with fear.

Marlon shouted, desperation seeping into his voice, "Are you crazy?

"If you kill me, my father will hunt you down! He's not far from here, and if you take me out, you're as good as dead!"

Osborne chuckled. "If I don't kill you, do you think he'll let me go?"

Marlon's expression hardened.

His voice turned venomous. "At least if you spare me, there's a glimmer of hope! If you kill me, it won't just be you who pays the price—everyone in the Augustine family will go down with you! The Thornberry family is not something the Augustine family can contend with! My father is a top-tier Class S, and my great-grandfather is the strongest in Blue Planet!

"If you don't want to die, you'd better back off right now!"

Osborne's eyes blazed with fury. "You dare threaten me at a time like this? What's the Thornberry family to me? What's the League of Divine Descent? What's the strongest on Blue Planet? If you lay a hand on me or the Augustine family, you'll pay the price! Die!"

With a cold glare, Osborne charged at the three adversaries, intent on taking them out and rushing to support Caleb and Serena.

But just as he moved, a wave of primal danger washed over him.

Without a second thought, he dodged backward.

A devastating attack slammed into the ground right where he had been standing. If Osborne hadn't reacted in time, it would have crushed him.

In the next instant, a tall figure stepped in front of Marlon and the others.

"Mr. Everett Rosenthal!"

Marlon's eyes lit up with hope. "You came to save us! Hurry, take him down! That bastard wants to kill me!"

Everett nodded, determination etched on his face. "Don't worry, I'll handle him."

With that, an overwhelming power radiated from Everett, enveloping Osborne in a fierce aura.

His gaze turned icy as he stared Osborne down. "Osborne, I underestimated you. I didn't expect that even these three working together wouldn't be a match for you. You nearly killed them!

"If you keep growing stronger, the entire League of Divine Descent is going to have sleepless nights!

"So, let's cut to the chase—you need to die!"
