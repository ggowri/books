"Super Energy Pistol?"

Osborne stared at the disheveled man in surprise. "Are you the head of the Weapons Research Institute?"

The man nodded quickly.

"Mr. Augustine, I am indeed the head of the Weapons Research Institute. My name is Kash Hopkins, and I've been in charge of energy weapon research. The energy pistol is my creation."

Osborne looked at Kash with astonishment.

He hadn't expected that this man was the creator of the energy pistol.

His interest piqued immediately. "What did you just say about a super energy pistol? Tell me more."

Upon hearing this, Kash became even more excited.

"Mr. Augustine, the super energy pistol is an upgraded version of the energy pistol. Due to the limitations of Energex Materials, the power of the energy pistol has a cap. At most, it can pose a threat to an ordinary Class A master, but it's practically ineffective against anyone stronger.

"I've been contemplating what kind of Energex Materials could make the energy pistol more powerful. It is something that could pose a threat to Class AA or even Class AAA individuals. My ultimate goal is for it to be a viable threat to a Class S Supreme Overlord!

"Unfortunately, I could never find the right Energex Materials until a few days ago. Godfather sent over a material similar to pyrite called the Divine Bone Crystal.

"The Divine Bone Crystal contains a terrifying energy. Once I used it as an Energex Material, the experiment was a swift success! Now, the super energy pistol has been developed. Its power far exceeds that of a regular energy pistol."

As Kash finished speaking, Osborne was even more astonished.

He thought to himself, "The Divine Bone Crystal?

If Kash used the Divine Bone Crystal as an Energex Material to create the super energy pistol, how powerful could it be?"

Deeply intrigued by the weapon, Osborne quickly said, "Show me."

"Of course, Mr. Augustine. Please, follow me!"

Kash, brimming with excitement, led Osborne to the Weapons Research Institute.

The institute was located on the twenty-eighth underground level—the lowest floor. The walls around them were made of the strongest alloys, reinforced to an unbelievable degree.

It was said that even a nuclear explosion couldn't damage these walls.

This place was not only the Weapons Research Institute but also an impenetrable safe house.

"Mr. Augustine, this way, please."

Kash guided Osborne into a room and carefully retrieved a handgun with a bronze sheen from a vault.

The handgun looked identical to a regular energy pistol, with the only difference being the magazine area, where a square Divine Bone Crystal was embedded.

Osborne took the pistol.

"Mr. Augustine, the activation of the super energy pistol differs from that of a regular energy pistol. The user needs to inject a certain amount of energy to activate the unique energy within the Divine Bone Crystal.

"I've already tested it. Any Class A master or higher possesses sufficient energy to activate the super energy pistol. The stronger the energy, the greater the power unleashed from the Divine Bone Crystal.

"I speculate that if the energy within a single Divine Bone Crystal were fully activated at once, even a Class S Supreme Overlord would be forced to retreat!"

As Kash finished, his body trembled with excitement. His eyes were fixed on the super energy pistol, gazing at it as if it were a lover, filled with pride.

Osborne, however, felt a wave of shock.

He thought, "Even a Class S Supreme Overlord would have to retreat?"

Osborne suddenly recalled the recent incident when Godfather activated a Divine Bone Crystal. The energy blast from the explosion had been so intense that even Godfather was pushed back.

The energy from that Divine Bone Crystal explosion had dispersed in all directions, and Godfather had only faced a portion of the shockwave.

Osborne thought that if all the power from a Divine Bone Crystal exploded in a single direction, it might indeed harm a Class S Supreme Overlord like Godfather.

His anticipation grew.

"I want to give it a try."

Osborne had always had a passion for firearms, but with his current level of strength, the power of any regular firearm was too weak to excite him.

To him, only divine artifacts were worthy of his interest.

If the super energy pistol was as powerful as Kash claimed, it could very well be on par with typical divine artifacts.

"Mr. Augustine, please feel free to try it out. The walls here are made of the strongest alloys, composed of many materials that don't exist on Earth, extracted from extraterrestrial meteorites. Even a Class S Supreme Overlord would find it hard to destroy this place.

"Over there are humanoid targets of various levels, each built to reflect the average defensive strength of warriors from Class A to Class S using special materials."

Osborne nodded, gripping the super energy pistol tightly.

He began injecting energy into the gun and immediately felt it devour a significant amount of his energy as if it were a bottomless pit.

Osborne couldn't help but feel astonished.

No wonder Kash said that to activate the super energy pistol, one needed to be at least at the level of a Class A master.
