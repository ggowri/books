In the blink of an eye, Osborne felt his powers sealed, leaving him utterly powerless.

His face paled as he stared at Gerardo, shock etched across his features.

At that moment, he felt like an ordinary person—stronger than the average human, perhaps, but devoid of any trace of his once-mighty abilities.

Even his Satan's Eye was rendered useless!

"What the hell is happening?" Osborne wondered, panic creeping into his thoughts.

Gerardo smirked, an air of superiority radiating from him. "Don't worry, I'm not going to kill you—at least not yet. I've simply used a divine artifact—the God Binding Rope—to suppress your powers. This thing can even restrain the strength of deities.

"My goal is Sebastian. Once I take him out, if you still refuse to bow to me, I'll annihilate your soul and turn your body into one of my puppet warriors.

"After all, it'd be a waste to kill someone with your talent."

Osborne's shock deepened. "A divine artifact? The God Binding Rope?"

Gerardo's smile widened as he led Osborne down to a small island below. He had seemingly retracted all his power, appearing as just an ordinary old man strolling casually alongside Osborne.

As they walked, Gerardo asked, "So, kid, do you know where we are?"

Osborne had no idea and remained silent.

Gerardo continued, "This little island doesn't have a name! But I call it 'Shame Island' because decades ago, it was here that Sebastian defeated me, and I lost control of the underground world of Rosmandi.

"For years, I've been plotting my revenge, dreaming of killing Sebastian and reclaiming what's rightfully mine. I want to return to Rosmandi and take back my throne in its underworld.

"After all this time, I'm finally close to achieving that goal! Once I kill Sebastian, I can reclaim my place in Rosmandi, becoming its ruler once more!

"I've waited far too long for this day, and I won't wait any longer. I brought you here to force Sebastian's hand.

"Decades ago, he humiliated me here. Now, I'll wash away that shame with his blood!"

As Gerardo spoke, a terrifying aura emanated from him, filling the air with dread.

Osborne bit his lip, a defiant glint in his eyes. "Maybe you won't wash away your shame but end up in an even more disgraceful position!"

Gerardo merely chuckled, the menace in his aura dissipating.

He regarded Osborne calmly. "You have no idea how powerful I am now! But soon enough, you'll see. When Sebastian arrives, I'll kill him right in front of you."

By then, they had reached the center of the island, standing before a small cabin.

A young man stood there, waiting respectfully.

As Gerardo and Osborne approached, the young man quickly said, "Mr. Aniston."

Gerardo waved him off casually, his gaze fixed on the cabin. "Every year, I come here for a bit, just to remind myself of the shame. After I kill Sebastian in a few days, I won't have to return here ever again."

With that, he handed Osborne over to the young man. "George, keep a close eye on him! He's got serious potential, and I'll need him later. Don't let him die! Also, use our intel network to spread the word.

"I don't care where Sebastian is hiding; he needs to know that Osborne is in my hands. If he wants to keep Osborne alive, he better show up here on Shame Island to meet me!

"I'm giving him just five days!"

As Gerardo finished speaking, he cast a lingering glance at Osborne with a cold smile.

"Yes, Mr. Aniston!" George replied respectfully to Gerardo before turning to Osborne with a mocking grin.

With a firm grip on Osborne's shoulder, he forcefully dragged Osborne into a small room.

Inside, there stood a massive iron cage.

Without any means to resist, Osborne was quickly confined within its bars, the binding ropes sealing away his power.

Osborne glared at George, fury blazing in his eyes.

But George merely chuckled, his expression unfazed. "What's that? Who do you think you are to glare at me? Mr. Aniston only ordered me not to kill you—he didn't say I couldn't have a little fun!"

With a wicked spark in his eyes, George unleashed a burst of flames that struck Osborne's body.

Instantly, the fire began to sear his skin.

Osborne's face contorted in pain, but he clenched his teeth and glowered at George even more intensely.

"You're George, right? I'll remember you. Once I get out of here, I'm going to make you pay!"

George threw his head back and laughed. "Get out? You actually think you can escape? Do you really believe Sebastian will come to your rescue? Hilarious!

"If Sebastian dares to show his face, he'll be dead for sure! This time, Mr. Aniston will finish him off for good! And by then, you won't mean a thing. Your fate will be far worse than death!"

With that, George shot another volley of flames toward Osborne, reveling in the torment.

Osborne bit down hard, keeping silent, but inside, his murderous intent was boiling over.
