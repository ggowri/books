Eric shook his head lightly and said, "Dylan, I hope you understand, sending you to prison is my way of trying to save you."

"Even after all the crap you've done, Mom still says she doesn't want you dead."

He leaned closer to Dylan, whispering, "Dylan, this is the only way to save you. If you take this information to Queenie, you'll be killed. Just like Frank. If you insist on being stubborn, I won't help you anymore."

Dylan trembled all over. He couldn't believe that the Queenie they had adored since childhood could be so cruel.

Eric looked at the officer and said, "Officer, please take him away. I'll provide the surveillance footage."

The officer said, "Sir, please cooperate with our investigation."

Eric said, "Fine!"

Dylan suddenly shoved past an officer and bolted outside. He refused to believe Queenie would kill him.

He had to find Queenie. He couldn't go to jail. He couldn't live with the label of a thief.

He thought, "Eric is such a bastard. Why did he call the cops? We could've handled this privately. He just wants to ruin me."

Dylan ran. It was the last thing anyone expected.

Eric followed the cops, chasing after him.

Dylan jumped on his bike. Being a skilled rider, he quickly disappeared into the city streets.

The cops pulled up street footage. Dylan was heading toward Queenie's place.

Dylan banged on Queenie's door, hard.

Queenie and Mario had been together many times tonight. Feeling great, Queenie went to bed early with Mario.

Suddenly woken by the banging, Queenie sat up, annoyed.

She thought, "Who's knocking this late? Psycho."

Mario woke up too. Hearing the frantic knocking, he instantly knew something was wrong.

He glanced at Queenie, noticing the marks he'd left on her. A smug satisfaction spread across his face. "Queenie, put some clothes on first. Something's probably up. I'll go with you to check."

Queenie thought about having Dylan steal Paula's company secrets.

She thought, "Did he get caught? Idiot. He can't even handle a simple task."

She also planned to pin all the blame on him, but she didn't expect George's situation to escalate so quickly.

It caught her off guard, leaving her no choice but to send Dylan to steal Paula's company secrets first.

Queenie got dressed and went to the door with Mario.

The door opened, and there stood a flustered Dylan. Both of them frowned slightly.

Dylan stared at the two, their disheveled appearance making him freeze for a moment. Then he shouted, "Queenie, how could you? George just got caught, and you're already jumping into Mario's arms?"

Dylan might be selfish, but Queenie's actions still left him genuinely shocked.

He was fully aware of the relationship between the two.

Queenie adjusted her clothes and snapped, "Dylan, this is my business, not yours. Why are you here so late? What do you want?"

Dylan remembered Eric's words and deliberately said, "Queenie, didn't you ask me to steal the company's secrets? I've got them. They're on my phone."

Queenie exchanged a glance with Mario. She smiled and said, "Dylan, thank you. Send me the files, will you?"

Dylan said, "Alright! I'll send them to you in a bit. What do you want me to do next?"

Queenie smiled faintly, saying nothing. She walked up to Dylan and slapped him across the face.

Dylan froze, stumbling back a step in embarrassment.

He smirked and asked, "Queenie, I got you everything you wanted. Why'd you slap me?"

Queenie said, "Idiot, I was just using you. Did you really think I saw you as my brother?"

"If it weren't for Diana ruining my plan, the four of you would've been dead already. My plan was flawless, and yet I lost to some nobody. You all deserve to die. Your existence is a reminder of my failure."

"Now I'll have someone send you off to deep-sea fishing. In six months, you could make around half a million dollars. If you're lucky, you'll survive. If not, well, stay out there."

Dylan had only been testing Queenie. But hearing her words at this moment, he still found it hard to believe.

He asked sadly, "Queenie, do you really want me dead?"

Even at this moment, Dylan still clung to hope.

Queenie let out a cold laugh. Watching his foolish expression, she leaned in slightly, her eyes fixed on him. "Dylan, out of the four of you, you're the dumbest and the most selfish. When I framed Diana, you even helped me. She's your own sister, yet you still sided with me to bring her down."

"Back then, I knew it. As long as I dangled money in front of you, you wouldn't even spare your own mother. I tempted you with cash, and look, didn't you just steal those business secrets for me?"

"Dylan, don't be sad. After all, you're just too selfish. Someone as selfish as you would ruin me if you ever betrayed me in the future."

"So, you'll just have to join Frank in the ocean."

Dylan was startled by the venomous look on Queenie's face.

He laughed and cried at the same time, feeling like Queenie was a demon baring her fangs. It sent shivers down his spine.

He said, "No, no, it's not like this. Queenie, I spoiled you so much, even hurt my own family for you. How could you do this to me?"

"Queenie, you're the girl Mom cared for so tenderly. How can you be so ungrateful?"

Dylan finally believed what Eric had said.

Only the truth could truly teach him.

Queenie openly admitted it, smiling smugly. "Dylan, so what if I'm ungrateful? And not just that, I'm going to take everything Paula has. It all belongs to me."

Her expression grew more twisted by the second. In that moment, Dylan truly understood what it meant to feel heart-wrenching pain.

He was furious, but there was nothing he could do.

Thinking of all the pain Diana had endured, he said bitterly, "Queenie, you're going to hell. I'll go to prison, but I'll watch you die with my own eyes. Killing you is pointless—living will hurt you more."

Queenie looked a bit surprised and asked, "Dylan, what's that supposed to mean?"
