Diana felt like her head was about to explode when she heard that.

She closed her eyes, trying to suppress the fury inside.

"Go check who's been to my office recently," she ordered.

Luna's eyes flickered as she replied, "Only your brother, Gale, was there."

Diana stormed out, remembering something.

Her design drafts had been destroyed by Gale, but not before he had taken photos of them.

Luna followed behind, her tone icy. "I reviewed the surveillance footage. He took photos before tearing up the designs."

Diana's eyes reddened. For the past two years, she had handed over the company's matters to Nolan while taking care of Steven.

If she hadn't wasted those two years, her company would have thrived by now.

Men really only slowed her down from making money, she thought bitterly.

Luna drove Diana to find Gale.

Gale was at the garage today, inspecting his race car.

He looked up to see an angry Diana approaching.

His eyes flashed with disgust. "Diana, what are you doing here? I haven't even confronted you about framing Dad last night," he said.

Diana strode over in fury. "Smack..."

Diana's eyes turned red again. She couldn't help it. Her eyes always welled up when she was wronged.

Damn it, she didn't want to cry, but the tears came anyway.

Her voice was filled with thunderous anger. "Gale, I always thought you were lazy and irresponsible, but I never imagined your character could be this rotten.

"You stole my designs and gave them to Steven's company, ruining all my hard work. Gale, you bastard!

"Do you have any idea how important those ten designs were to me? They were supposed to determine my company's future, iconic pieces I spent three days and nights creating. And you secretly photographed them and sent them to Steven's designer.

"Gale, you're worse than an animal."

Gale froze, disbelief written all over his face. "Diana, are you out of your mind? Why are you accusing me? I may have taken photos, but I didn't send them to anyone!

"Don't try to frame me. Your dirty tricks won't work on me."

Diana pulled out her phone and showed him a video. "This is Steven's design department's new product launch, and it's exactly those ten designs you photographed."

She had been caught up with Cecil's affairs and had put this matter aside.

But it had given them enough time to launch the new products.

"And you're the only one in my office and taking photos.

"I have evidence, Gale. How could you stoop so low?"

Gale saw himself in the video. He had indeed taken the photos, but only because he thought the designs were good and wanted to show them to Queenie.

But he had gotten busy at home and forgotten all about it.

"Diana, I didn't do this. Don't accuse me. I may have taken the photos, but I didn't send them to anyone. I only meant to show them to Queenie, but I never got the chance. Check my phone if you don't believe me."

Gale pulled out his phone and opened the gallery.

As soon as he tapped it open, he was dumbfounded. The photos he had taken were gone.

"What... I didn't delete them. Where did the pictures go?"
