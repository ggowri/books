"You can leave now. Don't come to my office again."

Steven's expression was cold, and Queenie's eyes widened in shock. He had just told her to "get out." Panic filled her heart. This was the same look he used to give Diana, but now he was giving it to her.

If he ever had to deal with a woman like that, he figured she'd take away his everything, leaving him nothing but his bones.

The two of them seemed to really hit it off.

Uh...!!!

Diana was right. The players and manipulative women deserve each other.

If it were him in that situation, he'd fight back with everything he had.

To put it bluntly, Steven didn't know what love was.

Suddenly, the air became thick with danger.
