Diana's cheeks turned bright red as she playfully smacked his chest. "Alright, enough with the sweet talk. Keep going like this and I'll pass out again."

Diana felt her face burn. "No, go out."

Dr. Davidson smiled and explained, "Mr. Gellar, this is your baby's heartbeat."

Steven's emotions were all over the place right now, his eyes filled with anticipation and excitement. This was his child's heartbeat - so strong. This was incredible. Finally, Steven would have a complete family of his own.

Dr. Davidson was pleased. "Of course, Mr. Gellar."

"Diana, do you think these clothes will be enough?"

Diana's beautiful eyes sparkled with unmistakable happiness. "Okay."

Copper Bishop's eyes filled with tears as he held his daughter close. "Diana, you're so understanding. Dad promises, from now on, no one will ever hurt you again."

Diana beamed, linking arms with both her father and Steven. "Dad, Steven's amazing. He's been so good to me."

Leopold chuckled from nearby. "Alright, enough standing around out here. Let's go inside. Diana needs to see her real home."

"Welcome home, Miss Diana," they said in unison.

Diana felt like she was living in a fairy tale. This was really her life now?

"Diana," Copper Bishop said softly, "this is where you belong. This is your heritage."

Copper Bishop pulled her into another embrace. "My sweet girl. We have so much time to make up for."
