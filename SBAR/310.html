Diana stood still, lost in thought. When had that boy appeared?

After work, Diana went downstairs and ran into Frank waiting at the entrance.

As Diana got closer, she saw Frank, and her eyes suddenly filled with hatred and disgust.

Seeing the loathing in her eyes, Frank's heart sank. "Diana, I..."

"Get lost," Diana said, her tone dismissive.

Frank frowned. "Diana, you're so harsh. No wonder you can't win Steven's heart."

"Why would I want to win over a shameless scumbag? To disgust myself?"

Right now, she just wanted to stay far away from Steven.

Wanting to win his heart? She'd have to be out of her mind.

Frank was taken aback. Diana had truly changed. Now, she was defiant and bold, no longer meek and compliant.

As Diana tried to leave, Frank quickly blocked her path. "Diana, for the sake of being my sister, I'll give you some money. Just leave here. Everyone says you're just a country bumpkin. What do you know about design? Stop embarrassing the Marsh family."

Diana frowned, glaring at him. "What did you say? What have I done? How have I embarrassed the Marsh family?"

"Isn't it you who's the disgrace of the Marsh family? Aren't you the one who's malicious and selfish, Frank?"

Frank was speechless, his expression pale. He pulled out his phone and opened a news article. "Take a look. People say you don't deserve the design director position. They think you slept your way up. And didn't you drop out of high school?"

Diana stared blankly at him for a moment, not expecting anything more.

But hearing such words from a blood-related brother still made her heart ache.

She glanced at his phone and saw the internet filled with insults against her.

Diana laughed angrily. "Frank, are you out of your mind? What right do you have to come here and dictate my life?

"And stop looking down on me with that superior attitude. I'm someone you can't even reach."

Nolan's warning from the other day hadn't sunk in for him.

"Also, do you think slandering me will make your life any easier? Frank, your days are numbered. I won't bother with you anymore."

With that, Diana turned and walked away.

Frank was furious. Why did she always say such things, making him have nightmares every night?

"Diana, why do you keep cursing me to die?"

Diana looked at him and said, "You won't die, but you will be paralyzed. Is that realy any better?"

In her past life, Frank had fallen into the water, and everyone thought he was dead. He was later found paralyzed from the waist down, but it didn't stop his scheming. He was just born rotten.

At that time, she had even thought about helping him recover, but he didn't trust her.

Her previous words were meant to scare Frank, to instill fear, but her words carried little weight, and he never believed her.

"Diana, you just can't stand to see me do well, can you?" Frank's gaze was icy as he stared at her.

Diana smiled. "Aren't you already down? Congratulations, you got played by your beloved Queenie, and your father snatched the deal from you. Doesn't that feel awful?"

Frank's eyes flickered, but he said nothing.

Diana continued, "Frank, you're lucky. You weren't the one taken away. I was. I'm the one who lost the Marsh family's honor. Do I deserve death for that?"
