I can't afford to make a wrong bet. Paula truly loves me; letting her go is tearing me apart."

But let's not rush into it; we can plan for spring next year. For now, just go back and spend some time with Mom. I have things to take care of."

The news of Jeff filing for divorce just after Paula's company went bankrupt exploded online in an instant.

She gently urged him to sit down and poured him a glass of water.

Each comment stings worse than the last. I know I messed up, but when a couple is in crisis, they look out for themselves.

She owes so much money; how am I supposed to pay that off?"

"Who the hell leaked my divorce news?"

If he's broke, what's the point of being with him?"
