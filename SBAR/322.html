In two years, she knew their baby products would become a global sensation.

The shelf life is also quite short, so I only got a small amount for now. After the baby is born, I'll buy larger cans."

Two big spider shells would be enough for a full meal.

Althea knew Diana had come for diving.

"But give me a moment. I'll start the rice cooker. When we come back, we can boil the crabs and have dinner right away."

She placed the rice in the steamer, knowing it would stay warm after it was done cooking.

Althea explained that they were fishing out at sea.

Diana put on her diving mask, grabbed her net, and dove into the water, while Althea stood at the shore, taking pictures of her.

Last time, she had a coach with her. This time, she was alone.
