Luna sneered and felt anger bobbling in her heart as her gaze swept over Eric. She thought, "Was he truly attempting to flaunt his sympathy in front of Diana?"

What a wickedly ingenious plan! It seemed that Diana and her assistant were aware of Queenie and Jeff's scheme, yet I was kept in the dark, used by Jeff and Queenie to harm those I held dear.

Queenie, Jeff, you turned my own family against each other. Don't think for a second you would find any peace from now on." Eric's eyes flashed with a murderous intent.

Queenie wouldn't profit a cent from any TV series she filmed.

He would make her regret ever being born.

He was curious to see how Queenie planned to kill him.

Queenie had returned today, and Eric intended to go home first to confront Jeff.

If Diana truly came from the countryside, how did she become a design director? How did her racing skills surpass Elroy's?

There was only one possibility. Diana was exceptional, but her brilliance was overshadowed by their suppression.

Queenie was a cowardly wretch. The moment Steven faced trouble, she fled abroad. The Marsh family clearly sought to cling to Steven's wealth yet blamed Diana for taking him away.

Diana married Steven for the sake of the Marsh family.

Yet, they showed no gratitude, wrongly accusing Diana.

Had Eric not learned the truth, he would have continued in his error.

Eric was in agony. Since his youth, he had been preoccupied with training and racing, neglecting his studies, leaving him unable to secure a decent job.

To claim Paula's estate and wealth, Jeff had ruthlessly molded his sons into useless beings.

His greed and selfishness, driven by a lust for riches, sacrificed his children's futures.

To aid his mother, Eric feigned the same doting demeanor as before. "Oh! Our little princess has returned?"
