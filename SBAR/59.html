"He's in the car. I've already sent the video to Cecil. If that fool doesn't believe it after seeing the video, I'll find a way to deal with him," Luna's voice was icy.

"Steven, you're finally here. You have to help me. I haven't done anything against the Farley family," Queenie pleaded.

Steven gently patted her back but couldn't help glancing at Diana. Her indifferent expression, devoid of any emotion, sparked an inexplicable anger within him.

"Why are you framing Queenie, Diana?" Jeff demanded.

Paula, who had initially felt a twinge of guilt, quickly lost it upon hearing the accusation against Diana.

"Why are you so malicious, Diana? I regret giving birth to you. If I had known you'd turn out this way, I should have strangled you at birth," Paula said, looking at Diana with disappointment.
