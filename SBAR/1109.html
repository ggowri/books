Chloe's pitiful expression froze instantly.

She had done her research recently and found that wealthy men adored women who were pure and kind. With her sweet looks, her tearful appearance was the most captivating.

She began to cry softly, "Mr. Gellar, I really know I was wrong. I promise I'll do better from now on."

Failing to seduce Steven didn't discourage Chloe. In her mind, if she could make Mario give up everything for her, she could do the same with Steven. She didn't believe Diana could ever outmatch her.

Chloe slowly cleaned up the coffee on the floor, her eyes constantly drifting toward the med room. She thought, "Diana, you have everything. So, I'll take your husband."

In the med room, Diana looked up as Steven walked in, a bit surprised.

Noticing the coffee stain on his white shirt and the faint smell of coffee, she asked, "Did Tom make you coffee?"

Tom loved coffee. She didn't. She only drank white coffee, heavy on the milk, light on the coffee. Too much caffeine kept her up at night.

Steven finally glanced at the coffee stain on his white shirt. "Diana, I'm here with you. Don't worry, I won't bother you."

Diana shot him a quick glance. Seeing he wasn't planning to leave, she pointed to a chair nearby. "Go sit over there. Don't disturb me."

Steven said, "Alright!"

Diana went back to work.

Steven glanced around the lab. It was huge, filled with cutting-edge machines from around the world.

Steven turned to look at Diana, focused on her work. A slight smile crossed his face. He sat down on the chair and went back to checking his emails.
