Diana was just about to hop in the shower when she got a video from Cecil.

The video revealed Queenie being trapped by two men.

Before leaving, Diana overheard Steven talking to Queenie on the phone. It turned out that Queenie had not been lying this time – she was truly in trouble.

Surveying the surroundings, Diana squinted as she recognized it was near Steven's villa.

Cecil's message popped up: [Diana, Queenie is much more cunning than we've ever known. She even killed Callie to win the champ.]

Diana was unaware that Queenie had won the championship in the piano competition because of Callie.

In her mind, Queenie was always a skilled pianist.

She responded to Cecil: [Stay alert. She's been training assassins in secret. Queenie is resourceful and excellent in everything.]

Cecil replied: [Diana, you think too highly of her. How can a ruthless woman like her be deemed excellent? She's just good at disguise and seduction.]

Diana knew that he had underestimated Queenie.

She replied: [Cecil, I'm serious. Consider the incidents in your company and think about why your overseas father met with a car accident. These aren't easy.

[Queenie wouldn't have done those things without meticulous planning. There are unseen forces backing her. Her current focus is on Steven, a member of Capitole's most powerful family. Marrying him would improve her status, helping her move up.]

Cecil replied: [Yeah, you have a point. She's so scary.]

Diana replied: [Never underestimate women.]

Cecil replied: [I saw her trembling, and I thought she was an easy target.]

Diana replied: [Alright. Good night.]

Diana then tossed her phone aside and went to wash up, prepared for bed.

Frustrated by Steven's sudden decision not to divorce, she just wanted to be alone.

With Leopold and Tobias absent, the villa was quiet.

As Diana lay down, she quickly fell asleep.

She had another nightmare, where she dreamed that in a past life, she was bound in an unfinished building.

She witnessed helplessly how those people hurt her.

A wave of pain contorted her face, and the axe smashed down on her fingers, causing her to be overcome with sheer terror.

The most excruciating moment came when Steven pushed her down the stairs.

She exclaimed, "No, Steven, please, save me. Save our child..."

Yet, Steven disregarded her plea. Suddenly, blood splattered on the ground, and she saw herself reduced to a mass of flesh and blood.

"No..." Diana jolted awake, drenched in sweat.

As sunlight streamed through the gap in the curtains, she realized it was just a nightmare.

Diana gasped for air.

The pain was so intense that she curled up in agony.

Her phone kept ringing. Diana reached out to get it. Without checking the caller ID, she answered, "Hello."

"Diana, how dare you? You actually hired men to kidnap Queenie. Are you trying to get yourself killed?" came a man's scolding.
