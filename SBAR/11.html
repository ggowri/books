Diana's beautiful eyes were filled with tears. "In order to marry Steven, I seldom contacted you. It hurt Grandpa's heart.

"Julius completely ignored me. Hubery hated me and went abroad. Only you stayed by my side regardless of the past.

"Tobias, I will never be stupid again. I won't love Steven anymore. I swear.

"If..."

Tobias took Diana back to Breeze Villa.

Breeze Villa was a manor villa covering an area of 37,673 square feet. The walls were made of white jade, with ten-meter-high floor-to-ceiling windows. It had a vast space and was gorgeous everywhere.

It was her favorite style of decor.

Leopold lived on the first floor, and she lived on the second floor. Tobias lived on the third floor, and Julius and Hubery lived on the fourth floor.

Each floor contained their favorite entertainment venues. She lived on the second floor, where there was a 1,291-square-foot library and a gym next door.

She grew up here. When the Marsh family found her, she had been training in the countryside. So, the Marsh family had always thought she was a country girl who was not presentable.

She had fallen in love with Steven at first sight and chosen to cut off contact with Leopold to marry him.

Now, she thought she was such a fool.

Leopold looked at Diana's injured foot and asked, "You walked out and came back lying down. What's wrong with you? Did you encounter a ghost?"

Diana was sad. "I indeed encountered a ghost."

It was a person scarier than a ghost.

Diana instantly raised his head excitedly and said loudly, "Grandpa, my life is in my own hands. If the god wants to destroy me, I will destroy him."

Diana shrank her neck.

Leopold widened his wise eyes and said, "Diana, being happy is the most important thing. Don't make the god angry. I... I'm going to sleep. It's thundering. I'm afraid of thunder."

He thought, "I just want to stay away from this doomsayer."

Leopold jumped away, just like a runaway child.

Diana was speechless.

Tobias was overjoyed. With Diana at home, Leopold always avoided her.

"Diana, come on. I'll take you back to your room. Think positively. We need to look forward. If we don't miss out on those crappy things, how can we know what is the best?"

Tobias stood up and carried Diana on his back.

Diana felt that Tobias' words were right. It was difficult to change fate. But she could change herself. "Tobias, I see. I want to be strong."
