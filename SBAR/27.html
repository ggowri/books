Jeff grew even more anxious, thinking, "This foolish Diana wants revenge instead of cooperation. How could I have such a stupid daughter?"

"Diana, although you saved me, your request is too much," Rita stated, not expecting such an extreme demand.

"Is it too much?" Diana coolly smiled. "Madam Farley, let me remind you that if I hadn't rescued you from the swimming pool that day, those who plotted against you would have targeted the entire Farley family. By saving the entire Farley family, cutting off your grandson's hands shouldn't be considered excessive, right? Your grandson cyberbullied, mocked, threatened, and physically assaulted me."

Diana pointed angrily at Steven and Queenie, continuing, "I was framed and insulted by them. And isn't it because Queenie stole my credit?"

Steven stepped forward and said, "Diana, breaking Mr. Farley's hands is too much. We misunderstood you. We apologize to you..."

Diana interrupted him, "Steven, shut up. You don't even have the right to apologize. You have been the main culprit. You tried everything just to secure a contract with the Farley family."

Steven narrowed his eyes and looked at her, asking, "What do you mean?"

Diana glanced at Rita. "It's up to the Farley family to investigate. It has nothing to do with me."

"Do you not believe me, Diana?" Steven asked coldly.

Diana sneered, "You have never believed in me, so why should I believe in you?"

Steven was left speechless, shocked by her arrogant and wanton demeanor.

She was right. Since Queenie had returned, conflicts had arisen, and he had never believed in Diana, who had always been by his side during the most difficult times.

Steven was suddenly overcome with guilt.

Diana looked arrogantly at Rita. "Madam Farley, if you have no objections, then I will take action."

Rita acquiesced.

While Cecil was stunned, Diana delivered a resounding slap across his face, causing him immense pain.

Anger surged within him, but before he could speak, another hard slap landed on his face.

Ten slaps later, Diana stopped.

Immediately afterwards, Cecil, who was dizzy after being beaten, suddenly felt severe pain in his wrist.

A scream echoed throughout the banquet hall.

Rita looked on in shock, her mouth agape.

Suddenly, another scream followed as Diana violently broke off Cecil's hands.

Everyone watched in disbelief.

Cecil curled up in embarrassment, writhing on the floor and glaring at Diana with intense hatred.

The pain was unbearable.

As a pampered rich man, he had never experienced such pain.

The room fell into complete silence.

The sound of Diana's high heels reverberated, breaking the stillness.

Diana looked at Cecil, who was in pain. "Cecil, I know you are rich and arrogant. I have endured so much abuse from your words. Do you understand the consequences when you said you would make my life a living hell?

"You can be arrogant with your money, but don't bully others or even think about taking their lives."

In her previous life, she had suffered numerous persecutions, all because of Cecil. Influenced by Queenie, he had frequently sent people to cause trouble for her.

In this life, she would not tolerate it any longer.

After speaking, Diana knelt down and held Cecil's injured hands.

Unable to bear it any longer, Rita hurriedly intervened, her voice trembling. "Diana, you have already taken your revenge. What else do you want to do?"
