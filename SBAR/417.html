Could it be that many things from her previous life had changed because she was reborn?

The events with Gale had followed the script from her past life, but Frank had been saved by her.

Steven's status as the wealthiest man had also been revealed prematurely.

In this life, would many things also change accordingly?

Evan had seen it, too. "Mr. Gellar, the title of Capitole's richest man is undoubtedly yours."

Steven glanced at Diana, but she showed no reaction. In the past, every time he closed a deal, she would be happy and cook him a delicious meal.

Now that he had become the wealthiest, she didn't react at all.

He responded with a nonchalant tone.

Diana was not interested in Steven being the wealthiest man.

All she wanted was to live.

Queenie, who was still on the island, also saw the news of Steven becoming the wealthiest. She was very excited as if she were already the heir to the wealthiest man.

She immediately sent a message to Diana.

[Diana, Steven is now the richest man in Capitole. You no longer match him. If you know what's good for you, leave the country quickly. Don't embarrass Steven.]

Diana looked at the message and laughed in anger.

If she didn't match, could Queenie, the mistress, match instead?

She replied: [It's Steven who doesn't agree to the divorce. If you try harder to have his child, he will definitely divorce. Use the methods you used on Jace on Steven. Maybe tomorrow I can give you my place.]

After that, she handed the phone to Steven. "Look, your lover can't wait to drive me away from you. You should see the message I replied to her. I think this method suits you two. As long as Queenie has your child, you two can be together without any obstacles."

Steven looked at the message and frowned slightly.

Diana mocked, "You don't believe it? You think this message is also synthesized by me?"

Steven frowned, his deep and angry gaze fixed on her. "Do you really hope that I have a child with another woman?"

"I really, really hope so. I'll be free after the divorce. Shouldn't I hope for it?"

Diana laughed happily. "Mr. Gellar, have you heard a saying?"

Steven asked, "What saying?"

"Staying away from a jerk is cherishing life!"

"Diana, you..."

Steven suddenly saw an unfamiliar message pop up.

[Diana, are you home? Be sure to message me when you get home. I will worry about you. Diana, tonight's dinner was delicious. I love it. Thank you for cooking for me.]

Steven's grip on the phone gradually tightened. She had cooked for Joseph tonight.

He slightly turned his head to look at Diana, gritting his teeth, his deep eyes surging with anger.

He questioned loudly, "Diana, can't you live without a man?"
