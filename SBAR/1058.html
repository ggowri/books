Queenie went along with his words, "Maybe. Paula feels guilty toward Diana, and she might have been threatened by her too."

Dylan was just that easy to fool. Whatever Queenie said, he would take it at face value.

She just enjoyed watching Diana's life turn into a complete mess.

Besides, Queenie was also using this time to work on her romantic pursuits.

Why bother with forever?
