Queenie's birthday was October 1st, the same day as Diana's.

So when Steven opened this shopping mall, he named it October Mall.

Seeing Olivia's happy look, she didn't say anything. Besides, she had completely given up on Steven. Now when she mentioned this matter again, her mood didn't fluctuate much.

In this life, she would never give Steven the chance to hurt her again.

"Okay, let's go. "

Diana drove there herself. There was no traffic jam at night, and she arrived at his destination in half an hour.

In the past, Diana rarely had time to go shopping at night.

After finishing the day's work, she still had to prepare dinner for Steven and clean the house. Now that she thought these past few years had been frustrating.

She lived a carefree life now. After getting out of the car, she looked at the buildings in the distance. The neon lights were flashing, and the colorful lights intertwined to create a strong sense of prosperity.

She took a deep breath. She worked hard for her life, but Steven turned a blind eye.

Now, she no longer had to work hard for their future, it was so relaxing.

Seeing Olivia getting out of the car, Diana calmed down. She walked over and took Olivia's hand, saying, "Come on, Olivia, let's go in."

Olivia looked at the name of the mall and was confused. "Diana, why is this place called October Mall?"

Diana smiled and said, "Because this is a mall owned by Steven, and Queenie was born on October 1st, so it's called October Mall."

Olivia stopped abruptly. She said apologetically, "Diana, I'm sorry. We shouldn't have come here. Let's go to another one."

Diana looked at her with amusement and said, "Why? We are here to spend money, not to do anything else. No need to change."

"I'm afraid you'll be sad," Olivia said frankly.

Diana shook her head and said, "I won't be sad, Olivia. People who are confused by love are fools, and fools are hopeless. So, I don't want to be a fool. Now I know what I want, and I am not living in confusion. I am now working hard to make myself richer."

Olivia understood what she meant, and she pulled her inside. "Great, if you can't hold the sand, you might as well throw it away."

"Yeah!" Diana smiled with relief, and the two took the elevator up.

But they didn't notice that there was a tall figure following them.
