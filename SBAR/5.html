Diana smiled, thinking about how her grandfather was heartbroken after she died in her previous life and passed away a year later.

She was such a terrible granddaughter!

The more Diana thought about it, the sadder she became, and finally, she burst into tears again.

The handsome man driving the car gripped the steering wheel tightly, wishing he could cut that bastard Steven into pieces.

Diana cried out of breath, "Grandpa, Tobias, I'm crying because I know how stupid I have been!"

Leopold's eldest grandson, Tobias Bishop, and his two brothers grew up with Diana together.

She must protect them and rewrite their fate.

She would tear off Queenie's mask and then do whatever she wanted.

His booming voice startled both Diana and Tobias.

She was ready to live her life fully and for herself.
