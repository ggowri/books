The task was tough, especially since Lillian's medicine was downright terrifying.

Cecil would lose his mind the moment he caught a whiff of that medicine.

Diana said, "Cecil, your family might be targeted again. Lillian won’t give up. I’m taking a break these days, make you something to keep you from falling."

Cecil, who had been a little scared, felt relieved after hearing her words.

As long as he could stay rational, Lillian couldn’t do anything to him.

The priority now is to trick Paula out of all her wealth and avenge Noah and my mom.

Selfish, cold, and only cared about money.
