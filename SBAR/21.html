The news of Diana pushing Rita quickly spread across the internet.

People expressed their worries and opinions online.

[Diana is the adopted daughter of the Marsh family. How could she be so bold as to push Rita?]

[Diana is wicked.]

[She is so awful. Madam Farley is almost 70 years old, and Diana actually pushed her into the swimming pool.]

[Hmph! I've seen Diana. She's just a simp. All the Marsh family members hate her, including the four brothers. I can't stand her timid and cautious demeanor. I didn't expect that she would do such a terrible thing.]

[When this kind of person explodes, it'll be scary. Why would she want to harm an old man?]

[I heard that it was Queenie who jumped into the swimming pool to save Madam Farley. They are a family. Why are they so different?]

[Yes. Queenie is gentle, kind, pretty, and elegant. She's a talented pianist and a good dancer. She is excellent in everything. Why did the Marsh family adopt that useless Diana?]

The news was filled with insults directed at Diana, making her the target of public criticism.

However, Queenie, who had planned the incident, felt excited and couldn't sleep.

She intended to use Diana as her foil.

She would expose Diana for imitating her and even stealing her man, thus making everyone hate Diana.

In less than half an hour, the news quickly gained traction on social media.

At this time, someone else surfaced another piece of news.

[Word on the street is that Mr. Farley will hold a special thank-you party tomorrow night to express gratitude to Ms. Marsh for saving Madam Farley.]

[Mr. Farley is so great. I like his face. He's so hot.]

[Wow! But it's a shame that I can't attend the party. I heard that only those with invitation letters can enter.]

[Don't worry. It will be live-streamed. Just wait for the fun.]

At Olivia's place, Diana and Olivia sat together, enjoying barbecue and beer while watching the online news and discussions.

Diana quirked and said, "Is that all Queenie has got?"

In her previous life, Diana had been a hopeless romantic who was willing to endure Queenie's bullying for the sake of a little family affection.

Sadly, she didn't feel any warmth from her family until her death.

Everyone compared her with Queenie, and because she outshone Queenie, her four brothers became jealous of her.

Diana cursed herself for being foolish in her previous life.

While enjoying their meal, Olivia frowned and stared at Diana. "You're just watching these people insult you and allowing Queenie to set you up behind your back?"

Olivia genuinely felt sorry for Diana.

Diana smiled mysteriously and replied, "Olivia, let them be. Tomorrow, they will have eggs on their faces. Don't worry. I have prepared for the party tomorrow night and won't let myself be bullied. "
