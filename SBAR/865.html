Before, he believed whatever she said.

"Steven, please, just trust me for once, okay? Why would I hurt him? And why would I kill him?"

Steven looked at the bodyguard behind him. "Throw her into the sea."

Two bodyguards came over and held Queenie on the left and right.

She was shivering from the cold and had no strength to struggle.

At this moment, Queenie felt helpless and scared.

She shook her head in fear. "Steven, no, I saved your life before, and please forgive me this time. I really didn't catch Evan."

He didn't say anything but looked at the bodyguard. "What are you standing there for? Throw her down."

Queenie instantly forgot to cry or struggle and just stared blankly.

But soon, she was thrown into the sea.

The freezing cold sea water made her feel like she was on the verge of death.

She spat out a mouthful of salty seawater.

The cold seawater left her with no strength to struggle.

Looking at Steven's ruthless eyes, Queenie was afraid. So, she cried and shouted, "Steven, I...I asked someone to release Evan, and you should ask them to pull me out."

Steven asked indifferently, "Are you sure you want to do it right away without any nonsense?"

Queenie didn't expect him to be so ruthless. "Okay! I won't talk nonsense. Save me, and I'll call them right away and ask them to release Evan."

Steven did this because he had evidence in his hands.

She couldn't understand Steven. Before, she seemed to have complete control over him.

However, apart from getting money, she seemed to get nothing.

Steven was a very cunning person.

Queenie had been working very hard and being very careful in order not to reveal her true colors.

Steven asked the bodyguard to go down. "Pull her out."

The two bodyguards did not go down but took out the shrinking steel pipe in the car, stretched it, and put it into the sea.

The bodyguard said, "Got it."

Queenie quickly grabbed the steel pipe, but she was so scared and nervous that her hands hit the pipe so hard that she almost failed to catch it.

However, her desire to survive made her forget the pain.

The impact of the sea water was very strong, but Queenie still held on to the steel pipe tightly.

The two bodyguards worked together to pull her up.

Queenie had never been treated like this since she was a child.

At this moment, she was in an extremely miserable state.

"Speak up!"

Steven's indifferent voice came from above.

She shook violently, and it was the first time she saw such a terrifying Steven.

"I...I want to call...Mario. He knows the other party's phone number."

Steven handed her the phone and warned her, "Queenie, you'd better tell me the truth. If Evan loses a hair, I will destroy the Marsh family tomorrow."

Queenie didn't dare anymore. She was very afraid of Steven.

He was a man who even dared to beat his own father. His childhood was extremely miserable, and she had seen him at his most miserable.

Queenie took the phone and dialed Mario's number with trembling hands.

"Hello! Mr. Gellar..."

"Mario, it's me. Tell them to release Evan," Queenie said, crying.

"Ms. Marsh, what's wrong with you? Did Steven bully you?" Mario's voice was very anxious. "Ms. Marsh, where are you? Why can't I find you?"

"Mario, I'm fine. Hurry up and have Evan released, quickly!"

As Mario heard this, he knew that Steven had discovered what they had done. He was very nervous and scared.

Steven was ruthless, and his methods were extremely cruel.

"Ms. Marsh, I'll call them and check out first."

Queenie was praying that Evan had not been thrown into the sea yet.

But a few minutes passed, and there was still no news from Mario. She was very anxious. Without any news, Evan was most likely thrown into the sea.

...

The beach was 0.6 miles away.

Evan, who was knocked unconscious, was thrown into the sea by two bodyguards.
