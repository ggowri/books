Diana heard the voice and found it somewhat familiar. Was it Queenie?

She suddenly thought of Cecil and turned to Alexander, saying, "Alexander, let's go check it out."

There was a crowd gathered outside the lounge on the second floor.

When Diana and Alexander arrived, they saw Queenie barely clothed, with Cecil on top of her, kissing her.

Steven and Jace were each trying to pull Cecil up.

Cecil was dazed and disoriented, his eyes unfocused.

Steven clenched his fist and punched Cecil in the face.

Diana rushed in and saw Cecil's veins bulging. With his current state of poisoning, he couldn't withstand Steven's punch; his veins would burst, and he'd die for sure.

Diana ran over as fast as she could and caught Cecil in her arms.

Suddenly, a sharp pain hit the back of her head.

"Ah..." Diana cried out in pain.

Steven couldn't believe it. Seeing Diana rush out like that, was she really risking her life for Cecil?

No, if Diana was here, then...

Steven looked at Queenie and realized she and Diana were wearing almost identical dresses.

They weren't exactly the same, but the hairstyle and dress style were similar.

He had mistaken Diana for Queenie when Cecil assaulted her, which was why he attacked Cecil.

"Diana, you rushed in like this! You're crazy!" Steven shouted.

Diana ignored the pain and paid no attention to Steven, focusing only on Cecil, who was tugging at her clothes.

She slapped Cecil's face forcefully. "Cecil, snap out of it."

Cecil's grip was strong, and Diana's clothes were almost torn apart.

Cecil's body was burning hot, and he was completely out of it.

The crowd around them was merely watching, with no one willing to help. Alexander approached and asked, "Diana, what should we do?"

Diana's eyes filled with tears as she watched Cecil, realizing he had been drugged. "Alexander, he's been poisoned. We need to get him to a hospital immediately. The poisoning is severe; he could die any moment."

Diana was furious. She had warned him to be careful and not get set up.

Her medicine was in the car, but Queenie wouldn't let them leave easily. She had only brought a clutch when she came in, leaving the medicine behind.

Alexander said, "I have an antidote for this kind of situation."

Alexander pulled a small pill from his bag and fed it to Cecil.

At that moment, Rita arrived.

Upon seeing Queenie in the room, Rita understood everything. She pointed at Queenie and yelled, "You shameless woman! Last time, you tried to kill me by tampering with the pool. I let you go once, but you repay kindness with evil. Now you drugged my grandson to seduce him! Can't you live without a man?"

Queenie's sobbing came to an abrupt stop. She was about to claim that Cecil had tried to assault her, but how dare this woman?

Jace's eyes narrowed as he sharply addressed Rita. "Madam Farley, when we opened the door, it was your precious grandson on top of Queenie."

Jace squinted, his eyes full of dishonest intent. He wanted the Farley business, and his interests aligned with Queenie's.

Rita, being experienced, saw right through Jace's cunning intentions.

"Jace, everyone here saw you holding Queenie. Are you having an affair with her too?" she retorted.
