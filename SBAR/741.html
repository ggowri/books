"All this work needs to lead to something real. You have to be sure Mark truly loves you before you decide to get a divorce."

Amelie nodded, her eyes shining with a hint of happiness. "Mark and I have been together for over twenty years, and our bond runs deep. Right now, he's organizing his assets. He's ending things with Lily, and soon, I'll be his only love. I've always been the one he truly cares about.

Amelie didn't want Queenie burdened by this child at this point.

Queenie felt confident that Amelie would cover her tracks and leave no evidence behind.
