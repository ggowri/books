Cecil smiled playfully and asked, "What?"

Diana said, "You are good with words."

Cecil touched his nose.

He wanted to hear, "You are very handsome."

But he didn't hear it.

Still, he blushed when Diana said he was good with words.

Diana didn't notice Cecil's reaction. Alexander sent her a message asking if she would go to the banquet that evening.

Diana replied, [Okay.]

Jace liked that kind of woman.

Queenie made him so angry that he wanted to curse.

That woman was shameless.
