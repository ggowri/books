[That's good. This minimally invasive surgery won't cause much harm to your grandma's body, and it's not likely to recur. Take good care of your grandma. If you need any help, feel free to call me.]

In the afternoon, she got off work and just walked out of the door when she met Frank and Elsie, who came to see her.

Frank handed a wedding invitation to Diana. "Diana, my wedding is coming soon. After all, you are my biological sister..."

Diana's cold voice interrupted Frank.
