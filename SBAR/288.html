Before Paige came, she had learned about Aria Group and knew that it had an excellent designer.

But she didn't know who the designer was.

The sale of the clothes produced by Aria Group was subject to a reduction every season. In the past two years, something happened to the designer, and the company gradually declined.

But Paige and her father had always remained optimistic about Aria Group.

Paige nodded happily. "Okay, Diana. I will tell my dad when I get home tonight. He will definitely agree. My dad also intended to cooperate with Mr. Watson before, but Mr. Watson never gave him a chance. With you this time, I'm sure the cooperation will be successful."

Diana knew about this. Back then, she had put all her attention into taking good care of Steven, and she had never produced any design.

Now that she was back, and the company was thriving.

Just then, Frank said sarcastically, "Diana, you are just a designer. What right do you have to decide on Aria Group's affairs? Mr. Watson hasn't decided yet.

"Humph, designer? You came from the countryside. What do you know about design? You are much inferior to Elsie."

Diana ignored his mocking words, thinking that he was annoying. She squinted at Frank. "Why are you still here?"

Frank paused and stared at Diana. "I've been here all the time. Diana, you vicious woman. Did you say something to Paige? She won't attend my wedding."

Diana thought Frank was unreasonable.

Paige was angry. "You jerk! Frank, why do you always suspect your sister? Diana is vicious? Humph, the real vicious ones are you and your family.

"You always think you are superior to others? Wake up! Do you know that what you said is like salt on Diana's wounds?

"Diana is a designer. Although she came from the countryside, she is better than you, a graduate of a diploma mill. Your ability is inferior to hers. Diana is the design director, and her design impressed Mr. Powell. She managed to make a cooperation with Mr. Powell. You are the last one who is qualified to criticize Diana.

"You enjoy a wealthy life, but you don't care about your own sister."

After saying that, Paige pointed at Elsie and said, "Thanks to Elsie, her shameful life made me find out that you two are the same type.

"Elsie, do you think no one knows your dirty deeds? I have already found out, so you'd better not offend me, or I will give you a big surprise at your wedding."

Elsie panicked. "Paige, you are framing me for not being able to get the love you want. Yes, I have a terrible life. My parents didn't love me. But you are a wealthy lady and can get whatever you want easily. Everything I have now was earned by myself. What qualifications do you have to criticize me?"

As Elsie spoke, she started crying and leaned in Frank's arms, feeling aggrieved.

Diana rolled her eyes at Elsie.

Paige was surprised by Elsie's impudent accusation, thinking Elsie was really cheeky.

Paige sneered, "Well, Elsie. Since you said this, I should really do something. I won't let you down. At your wedding, I will give you a surprise. Just wait."
