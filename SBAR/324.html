Diana smiled and said, "Althea, I once saw the biggest jellyfish crab in the world. They are incredibly large, and at that time, I was almost scared to death. Deep-sea diving is dangerous. Steven's mood improved. Without him, she was living better and much happier.

Diana cleaned a grouper fish and prepared it for steaming.
