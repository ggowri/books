Steven whispered in a hoarse voice, "Diana, I didn't know the design drawings were yours. They have already been stolen and released. How about selling them to our company?"

Diana was ruthless. She did not cause trouble to Steven in this matter because she knew that he did not need to plot against her over the design drawings.

Queenie clenched her fists and stared at Diana's cold face. She was very furious. What should she do?

After waking up at her birthday party, Diana had really changed.

No matter how stupid she was, it finally dawned on her that she was being used by Queenie.

Though outstanding, she ruined herself for those petty gains.
