Steven's eyes were deep and cold, with a hint of anger in them. "Diana, do you dislike me so much? No matter how I treated you before, you were always calm and gentle. But now, you seem to have become a different person."

Diana sneered when she heard this, "So, Steven, I should not fight back when you hit me, nor should I retort to you when you scold me. When you bully me, should I endure it like before?

"I was nice to you before because I saved your life. But now, I don't want to be nice to you anymore. You love Queenie, and I won't stop you from being with her. Humph, how dare you still have the nerve to say that?"

Diana didn't want to talk about this, as they had said it many times before.

Her eyes were cold, without any love in them. Steven looked at Diana and suddenly felt heavy in his heart.

"Mr. Gellar, I think I have already made it very clear." After Diana finished speaking, she stood up and went to rest.

Steven looked at her back and murmured, "Diana, I haven't had dinner yet. I have a stomachache."

Diana stopped and turned to look at him, feeling very angry. "You are not a child, but you can't even take care of yourself. How can you take care of me?"

Steven looked at her, his stomach aching slightly. "Sorry. I'm too busy today and don't have time to eat."

Diana closed her eyes and thought that if Steven were sick, Helen would call her again.

Then, the last warmth would sooner or later be exhausted by Steven.

Diana finally turned and went to the kitchen. She cursed herself as she walked, "Damn it! I deserve it. That bastard had left me on the highway this morning and almost caused me to die.

Now he has come to my house and is asking for food. He is so cheeky."

Diana went to the kitchen, and Steven stood up to help. Seeing that she could only move one hand, he felt guilty again. "What happened today..."

"Don't mention that again. I'm afraid I won't be able to control myself and kick you out later," Diana interrupted Steven coldly. She was not so generous as to forgive a man who betrayed and hurt her.

She said coldly, "Don't feel sorry. Your apology can't change the fact that I'm injured.

"I cook for you because of Helen. She's old, and now she has to worry about you. I feel sorry for her for being so hard all her life. So don't overthink it.

"I'm doing this because of Helen's kindness. You have used up my patience towards you."

Steven frowned. Diana's patience towards him had been used up.

The anger suddenly surged in his chest again. Steven stared at Diana with intense possessiveness in his eyes, and he didn't even realize it.

Steven said, "Diana, you..."

"Do you want to eat?" Diana interrupted him calmly.

Steven shut up and watched her cook with one hand.

Diana made soup. She took out a pot, put all the cut ingredients into it, and asked Steven to insert the plug.

Then, she opened the refrigerator, took out a cabbage, and made boiled cabbage.

Steven liked light food. He enjoyed eating cabbage without adding any season to it.

Steven took the cabbage and washed it. Diana asked him to break the cabbage into small pieces and then put them in the pot to boil.
