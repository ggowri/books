Queenie shook her head aggrievedly. "Gale, how can I threaten you? I am doing this for the sake of your health.

"Didn't you just say you didn't even have the strength to lift your hands or sign your name? You are too weak to check your phone, aren't you?

"Gale, you don't love me at all." She felt more and more upset.

"Giving it to you is a token of my love for you. Otherwise, it means I don't care about you. Queenie, you clearly know that the 5% of the shares is my only income. Are you so kind that you are going to take this away?"

Gale closed his eyes.

If he hadn't been lying down and unable to move, he would have gone to find Paula himself.

"Dad, I'll give you the shares. During the time I'm away for treatment, please take care of Penelope and our child. I'll get my legs healed and come back to look after them as soon as possible."

Gale picked up the pen with difficulty and signed his name.

Jeff wouldn't ignore his own grandchild, right?

Queenie and Jeff breathed a sigh of relief.

He was very excited. "Gale, take a good rest. I will go to deal with Diana. She must pay the price."
