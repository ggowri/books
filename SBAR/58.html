She didn't even glance at Steven.

Steven's hands clenched at his sides. "I'm just calling it as it is. I'm not bullying her."

She had already made up her mind.

Unable to intimidate Olivia, Steven clenched his fists in anger.

Olivia felt a mighty chill sweep over everything around them like a storm.

She frowned but was not afraid.

At that moment, Diana had already changed her clothes.

"Olivia, let's go," Diana said as she went over to pick up her suitcase.

Olivia could tell she was in a bad state. She took the suitcase from Diana's hand, and they went downstairs together.

Steven's gaze was icy. "I think she's just scared!"
