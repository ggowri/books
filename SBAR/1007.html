Jeff spent the entire day and night at the office, personally reviewing every collaboration proposal. But today, there were no calls, and that unsettled him. The calls from his partners, which he'd been expecting, weren't getting through.

Unable to wait any longer, he called his assistant, Jordan Hamilton.

"Hello, Jordan. What's going on? Why can't I reach our partners today?"

"I was just about to call you, Mr. Jeff Marsh. All of our business partners have pulled out. The company that acquired the Foliot family's business is under investigation for illegal activities. It's been shut down."

"What?" Jeff couldn't believe his ears. The unease that had been building inside him now hit with full force.

Diana hadn't lied to him. The entire situation was Lily's trap.

"No, that's impossible! What are you doing? Stop them! Get the authorities to back off—don't let them investigate my company!"

Jeff was furious. It had all come so suddenly. With only one day, he hadn't had enough time to prepare.

Mark's company had a few questionable dealings, sure, but he'd cleaned them up. There was no way it should be falling apart now.

Jordan's voice was strained. "Mr. Jeff Marsh, I've done everything I can. I couldn't stop the authorities from shutting us down. And it's worse than that—the bank we borrowed from just called, demanding we repay the loan immediately.

Jeff's phone slipped from his hand, clattering to the floor.

When Jeff saw Amelie, his eyes blazed with fury. If it weren't for this woman, he never would have ended up in this mess.

His gaze was full of hatred as he looked at her, and in a flash, he seized her by the neck with both hands, his grip tightening.

"Let go?" Jeff's voice was cold, filled with malice. He wanted nothing more than to strangle her. There was no way he was going to let her go.

His eyes, like an endless abyss, sent a chill down Amelie's spine. She could see nothing but the darkness within him.

Jeff's rage boiled over, his voice a growl, like a lion about to rip apart its prey. "It's all because of you. All because of you, I've lost everything. Paula loved me so much, but I betrayed her and ended up with someone like you—a useless trophy. You've ruined my life.

"You're the real villain here. I'll kill you."

His eyes were filled with bloodlust as if he were a demon from hell.

Amelie was terrified. She pounded on Jeff's arm in a desperate attempt to break free, but the pain was too much. She couldn't even get a word out.

"What was happening to him? Why was he acting like this?" she thought.

She gasped for air, her body wracked with pain, feeling as if death was closing in. Panic set in, and she could only think of one thing—she didn't want to die.

She had so much life ahead of her—she couldn't die like this, at the hands of this man.

"Help... help..." she choked, her voice barely a whisper.

All she could do was strike weakly at his arm, her world narrowing to the suffocating grip around her throat.

Amelie's neck was trapped in Jeff's grip, his hand like a vise, the veins bulging as he squeezed harder. It felt as if an iron clamp had locked around her throat, and she couldn't move an inch.

The suffocating pressure overwhelmed her like a tidal wave, making it almost impossible for her to breathe. Her face turned bright red from the lack of oxygen, and her eyes rolled back as she fought to stay conscious, the sight of her desperate struggle chilling.

"Jeff, if you kill her, you'll go to prison."

Paula looked down at her with a cold, triumphant gaze. "Not bad. If I hadn't caught on to Queenie's scheme, your fate would have been mine. But in this world, there are no 'ifs'.

Paula had been waiting for this day—for a long time.

Next, it was Queenie's turn.

The hate in Paula's eyes was unmistakable.

If it weren't for this family of three—Jeff, Amelie, and Queenie—Diana would never have left her.

Suddenly, Jeff dropped to his knees before Paula, gripping her hand with desperation, tears streaming down his face as he begged for her forgiveness. "Paula, I was wrong. Everything that happened was my fault. Please, let's get back together!"
