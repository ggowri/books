Meanwhile, Diana and Steven found Paige, who had fainted from the cold, in the innermost room of the basement.

Paige's down jacket had been removed, leaving her in a white wool sweater underneath. She lay lifeless on the freezing floor.

"Paige..." Diana ran over and hugged her.

Paige's body was icy, and her face was purple. She lost consciousness with her eyes shut.

Diana quickly took off her down jacket and gave it to Paige.

Steven stopped her. "Diana, put on your down jacket, and I will give her my coat."

"No. My down jacket can warm her up faster. Please call an ambulance. I want to take her to the hospital immediately."

Diana was very anxious, but she tried her best to calm herself down and looked at Paige's lifeless face.

She had never seen Paige like this before.

Paige had always been lively. Though slightly chubby, she was always optimistic.

In the pursuit of Frank, indeed, she was not very confident and simply followed him about.​

But he disliked her, so she kept her distance.

Paige was excellent. It was Frank who didn't deserve her.

After losing his memory, he was in the hospital for treatment. His aorta was injured. He was rescued but nearly disabled.​

Steven watched Diana quickly put her down jacket on Paige. Frowning slightly, he quickly dialed the emergency number.

He hung up and then called Evan.

But no one answered.

Steven was confused. Why didn't Evan answer the phone?

He called Evan three more times, but still no one answered.

Steven's eyes were chilly. "What's going on?"

Unable to get through to Evan, he put his phone back into his pocket.

Diana was rubbing Paige's hands.

With tears in her eyes, she encouraged Paige. "Paige, you must hold on. We can go to the hospital soon. You must hold on, okay?"

Paige's body was cold, and her eyelids didn't even move. Diana didn't know if she could hear her. At that moment, she just wanted Paige to hold on.

Diana put Paige's hands down, searched in her bag, and took out a pill, a very precious life-saving medicine.

Without any hesitation, she fed it to Paige.

Steven found the pill very familiar. It suddenly occurred to him that when he was seriously ill, Diana took out such pills and fed him.

She whispered softly in his ear, "Steven, after taking the medicine, you will be able to survive. You must persevere and try your best."

Steven still remembered that the doctor had said that such pills were very expensive and precious life-saving medicines and that they could not be bought even with money.​

Seeing Diana give the pill to Paige without hesitation, he stared at Diana deeply.

She would do her best to sincerely repay those who were nice to her.

Steven used to be the most important person in Diana's life.

He felt a deep regret. Even though it was too late, he would not give up.

"Diana, help her up. I'll carry her upstairs. There's heating upstairs."

"Well, okay."

Diana's voice trembled. She did not hesitate. At least with Steven's help, Paige could get treatment quickly.

Paige had been frozen unconscious. Diana was really worried about her.

Steven and Diana helped her up. He squatted down slightly, picked her up on his back, and walked towards the elevator.

Diana was too anxious to mind the cold. She followed him, praying that Paige would make it.​

When they arrived at the first floor of the hotel, there was heating.

In just a few minutes, Diana shivered from the chill.

Her hands shook, and she tried to hold her phone steadily and called Luna.

But no one answered.
