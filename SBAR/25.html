Before entering the venue, Diana had asked Olivia to find Leopold and stay with him, assuring her that she could handle things alone here.

Even Steven's status would rise due to her, making him treat her even better in the future.

It felt as if she was conveying the message, "Stay away from me."

Everyone knew that Rita held a significant amount of power through her shares.

Queenie pleaded loudly for Diana, which earned the favor of everyone present.

"Queenie, that's enough. I have already made my decision," Cecil responded before turning his angry gaze towards Diana. "Diana, apologize immediately. Don't waste Ms. Marsh's time."

Queenie looked at Diana, pretending to be concerned, and was about to speak when a cold voice suddenly came from the door, "Who said Queenie saved me?"
