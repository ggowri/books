Diana walked slowly to sit opposite Paula. Then, she asked, "Mrs. Marsh, have you gone for a paternity test?"

Paula said angrily, "Yes. But things are not as you said. Queenie is not your father's daughter."

Diana looked at Paula and felt that Paula was a failure as a person, living in deception of others all her life.

Anyway, whether Paula believed it or not, it had nothing to do with Diana.

This family had no feelings for me at all, and I was the only one who was stupid enough to give up on the people who loved me the most.

In this life, I will protect those who love me.

What do their lives have to do with me?"

Diana stood up and said in a cold voice, "There are three large medical institutions in Capitole, one of which belongs to the Gellar family and the Marsh family. They all know you. Once you go for a paternity test, the people in these two families will realize it soon. Do you think you can get a real paternity test report?

"It doesn't matter whether you believe it or not, because you and I have nothing to do with each other anymore. I don't want a mother like you, because you chose your adopted daughter over your biological daughter, and you like raising a daughter for others. As long as you are happy, I will not prevent your tragic fate."

Diana finished speaking and felt relieved all over.

"After a while, your tragic fate will officially begin, not only for you, but also for your four sons and your parents."

Diana felt very sad. What her mother, who she had once looked forward to the most, had given her was despair.

Now, she would have nothing to look forward to.

It was enough for her to have her grandfather and three brothers.

Paula was trembling with anger. She could no longer suppress her anger and roared at Diana's back, "Diana, you pushed Madam Farley into the water. Cecil is looking for you. He will not let you go."

Queenie is the daughter I raised. Queenie is excellent and is my pride.

Diana has a vicious heart and has attacked Queenie many times. I said nothing about her. Instead, she said she didn't want me as her mom.

Every time the evidence of her bullying Queenie is laid out in front of her, Diana denies it and says Queenie framed her.

She really refuses to repent.

Queenie has explained what happened at her birthday party. Why can't Diana let it go?

She has framed Queenie many times. And Queenie only framed her once. Why can't she be more sensible?"

Paula was so angry that her heart hurt. She went out after Diana in anger. But when she reached the elevator, she saw two men in suits taking Diana away.

Paula saw her daughter being taken away. Even if she had no feelings for Diana, Diana was still her biological daughter.

She trotted over and saw Diana being taken into the elevator.

Paula was very anxious. Diana was her daughter. How could she not be worried?

"Diana." Paula chased after them. But the elevator door had already closed.
