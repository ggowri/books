But she didn't dare ask—one wrong question, and she'd expose herself.

First impressions stuck. To the Marsh family, to Queenie herself, Diana was just an uncultured woman who'd never belong in their world.

Just who had been teaching her all this time?

And more importantly, who the hell was that old man?

And the way things were going, he and Yanson were next on Queenie's hit list.
