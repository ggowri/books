Nolan said, "Diana, I'm with the police right now. We're close to Luna's location. Don't worry, I'll bring her back."

"Alright! Make sure Luna is safe," Diana urged as she hung up. She looked ahead and saw that traffic was at a standstill, which made her anxious.

Her grip tightened on the steering wheel. Why the sudden traffic jam?

Diana was growing increasingly agitated, and the honking from behind only made her more irritable.

Steven was also watching the road ahead. "Diana, don't worry. There are often dump trucks on this road. It's likely there was an accident," he said.

Diana was anxious, but her car was stuck in the middle and couldn't move. She ignored Steven.

Her only hope was now with Nolan.

"Luna must be safe," Diana thought. Suddenly, an idea struck her.

She opened the computer in the car. If Luna had managed to capture the evidence, she would have uploaded it to the cloud.

If discovered, Luna could delete it directly without putting herself in danger.

Diana located the date of the file and clicked on it. There really was a file.

She opened it and found a video showing a male waiter approaching Cecil. While Cecil was chatting, the waiter dropped a pill into his drink.

A friend came over for a toast, and Cecil, unsuspecting, raised his glass and drank it all in one go.

Then, a figure with a similar silhouette to Diana walked by. Cecil stood up and followed. "Diana, wait for me. I don't feel well. Could you check on me?" he said.

It only took a few seconds to get upstairs from that spot.

While climbing the stairs, Cecil's condition worsened. As he pushed open the door, Steven, Jace, and others arrived, followed by Queenie's ear-splitting scream.

So, that's how it was. Queenie's scheme was indeed cunning.

Diana glanced at Steven, unsure of his intentions.

"Mr. Gellar, bringing so many people for a setup like this. You're impressive. Are you after the Farley family, too?" Diana asked bluntly.

Steven looked at her inscrutably. He knew that no matter how he explained, she wouldn't believe him.

"Diana, the Farley family are wealthy, and their stock value is high. As a businessman, of course, I want it," Steven replied.

Diana sent the video to Rita and said firmly, "But I won't let you and Queenie get what you want."

"Diana, I'm curious to see your strength," Steven said, not offering further explanation. He knew that his past actions had indeed wronged her.

Diana didn't respond. Strangely, she didn't feel like saying anything to him.

Rita quickly replied.

[Diana, thank you so much. I sent someone to check the surveillance room, but the footage had already been destroyed.]

Diana: [Grandma, Luna recorded the footage, but she might have been taken by Mr. Foliot's people. I'm on my way to find her. Be careful around him.]
