"Hello?" The voice on the other end was low, but Diana recognized Althea's voice." Althea, are you alright?"

Althea laughed, "I'm fine. I'm on vacation on a small island. I heard you've also left the Marsh family."

Diana narrowed her eyes slightly, "Yes, I've completely severed ties with them."

"It's a wise choice. If we can't fight the evil, at least we can avoid them." Althea's voice carried a hint of jest.

"Althea, we're both framed..."

"Psh, not a big deal. Don't take it to heart. Gale is obsessed with such a bitch. We've done all we could to dissuade him from dating her."

"Let it be. I'll have nothing to do with him anymore."

Diana knew these were words spoken in anger. Althea loved Gale very much.

Even after he was abandoned, she couldn't bear to let him die.

Now that she was pregnant, it was best to let her focus on her pregnancy.

"Althea, never hesitate to ask me for help. I've severed ties with the Marsh family, but we're still good friends."

After returning to the Marsh family, she had struck a friendship with Althea.

"Diana, I'm fine. Don't worry about me. I was more worried about you when I couldn't reach you then. Now that we're in touch, I feel relieved."

"Alright, I'll call you again when I have time."

"Okay! Diana, the Marsh family are all wolves in sheep's clothing, while the Gellar family is no different. Be careful. I'm not in Capitole, and can't help you much."

"I'm fine. Don't worry about me."

Diana hung up and drove away.

She had done her best to change what she could from her previous life.

"Who on earth tampered with Gale's car?

Could it have been Penelope?

But she wants to live a good life, so there is no reason for her to do that.

Could it have been Jeff's another son?

He has the strongest motive."

Diana pondered for a moment. "I'd never met Jeff's another son in my previous life.

Jeff protected him quite well.

And even sent him abroad to study. He was more capable than his four brothers.

I used to hear that my soul was bound to Steven, thus unable to go elsewhere.

There was only one time I didn't follow him. That day, I didn't know where Steven went. Yet when he came back, he..."

Diana stopped thinking about it and focused on driving.

Her grandfather and older brother had already retired to their rooms when she returned. Diana reported her safe return to her family on Line and went to bed.

After washing up, she lay in bed, remembering that in half a month, Paula would be sent to a mental hospital.

She had warned her already. If Paula failed to protect herself, she could do nothing to help.

Lost in thought, Diana heard her phone ring.

Who could be calling her at this hour?

"Hello?" Diana's voice turned soft because she was tired and sleepy.

"Ms... Ms. Bishop, it's me. Mr. Gellar's gastritis has flared up. What... What shall we do?" There came Evan's anxious voice from the other side.
