Isn't Paula still alive?

"Every one is despicable and downright vicious."

Diana really see all of us as despicable?

Indeed, there were things they had done to help Queenie scheme against Diana.

But Queenie had grown up with them, and all their love had been given to her.

Then Diana suddenly came back and started picking on Queenie at every turn.

Of course, they didn’t want to waste their time pleasing Diana anymore.

"Take a look. See how your dear father and sister forced c to divorce, and how your grandmother insulted her."

In it, Jeff shoved Paula into the snow, while Queenie slapped her across the face, spitting out the most venomous words imaginable.

"If Ms. Barnes hadn’t figured out Queenie’s plan, this bastard would’ve been dead already. Probably on some foreign street corner."

She knew Dylan was dumb, but she never thought he could be this dumb.

No, no way. Queenie wouldn’t do that to me.
