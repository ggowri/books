Why?

Amelie shouted, "Queenie, your father's company has gone bankrupt. Lily and Paula planned all of this. Her voice weary and choked with sobs, she felt distressed and said, "Amelie, why did you let me lose? I've staked everything on you, betting that I would have a happy and perfect family. How can you lose? How?"

The cold wind outside the window ruffled her hair, and the tears streamed ceaselessly down on her face.

She shouted, "Why is your love based solely on money? Without money, there is no love. Why are you like this?

"You are my biological mother. How could you do this to me? I schemed against Paula, who had raised me for 20 years, just so that you could become Mrs. Marsh open and aboveboard.

She planned everything for the sake of Amelie at the very beginning.

She lost, totally.

And Diana was at the height of her elation.

Noah shouted, "Mom."

Her son was over as well.
