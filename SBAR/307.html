Yes, she wanted Jeff and Queenie to voluntarily leave this house.

After she drove Frank out, Queenie no longer needed to deal with him.

He actually doctored photos to slander his own sister.

Now that she had woken up, she realized just how vile her son's actions were.

The doctor said you're fine aside from hypertension, diabetes, and high cholesterol.

"Not true? I heard everything from the doorway. Didn't I transfer $100,000 to your account? I've been busy and asked you to give it to your parents, but you didn't?

He was supposed to give it to his parents, but he forgot and gave it to Queenie's mother instead.

Tracey suddenly felt that her son wasn't as good to her as he used to be.

"Jeff, I've gotten used to that money. Without it, it's like my blood is being drained. You must give it to me tomorrow," she said.

Jeff was always good at creating conflict, wasn't he?

So, she'd use his own tactics against him.

Tracey valued money more than life, and Jeff's tough days were just beginning.

"I'll be staying at the office for now. You can take care of your parents yourself. And by the way, your company just lost a few projects. In business, you need to leave something for others, or you'll find yourself targeted," Paula warned.
