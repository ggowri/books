"What's happening? Amelie, what's wrong?"

"Jeff, please, you've got to help me. They're about to beat me up at the club on the sixth floor of Kinklin Mall."

"She's screaming that I'm his mistress, and she's totally going off on me. Please, you've got to rescue me. I'm in agony here!"

Jeff's anxiety spiked. "How can I save you? If I come, won't it all come out?"

"Jeff, you're a coward. Who am I doing this for? You won't even save me? What good are you? I might as well be with Mark. At least he's not as spineless as you. He's trying his best to protect me."

Amelie was livid. She knew Jeff was a coward, but she didn't expect this heartlessness.

Amelie hung up. Her once impeccably styled hair was now a disheveled mess, yanked and tangled, leaving her scalp throbbing with a numbing pain.

The shouting and cursing outside the door terrified her.

But she believed Jeff would save her. He needed her exceptional children for his future, didn't he?

"Amelie, you bitch, you seduced my husband. Get out here!"

Lily's furious roar echoed from outside.

Amelie was petrified. She didn't want to be exposed as a mistress.

She'd been with Mark for years. How did they suddenly get caught?

Their relationship had been so discreet.

The sound of the door being kicked sent shivers down Amelie's spine.

Beyond the velvet ropes of the club, Lily's friends had assembled, ready to confront the supposed mistress.

Not far away, Paula watched with a smile in her eyes.

Linda sneered, "I didn't expect Amelie to be so capable. She's been with Jace's nephew for years."

She had only learned this yesterday.

Today, knowing Amelie and Mark were meeting, Linda and Paula tipped off Lily, leading to today's drama.

Paula felt a profound sadness. The thought that she shared a husband with this kind of woman filled her with an overwhelming sense of disgust.

Linda frowned. "Will Jeff show up?"

Paula shook her head. "He won't come because he won't expose her until his plan works. He didn't dare show when I caught him at Jace's banquet last time."
