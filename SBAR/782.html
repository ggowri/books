Diana smiled at the tall young man beside her and asked playfully, "Hey there, can we dance together?"

"Sure!" He had an impressive height and a charming demeanor, with striking features and warm, inviting eyes. He was the quintessential sunny boy, and his smile was uplifting.

"Oh!" Laughter erupted around them, accompanied by playful whistles.

As the music began, the boy grinned and asked, "Is this song okay?"

"Absolutely, it's perfect!" Diana replied, excitement bubbling within her.

As the music played, Diana unleashed an incredible burst of energy. She pushed off the ground, soaring into the air and executing a beautiful spin.

Upon landing, she quickly crouched down, using one hand to support herself while her legs swept across the floor like a whirlwind, tearing through the air around her.

The boy mirrored her movements, their coordination seamless.

Facing each other, they began to sway their shoulders rapidly, embodying the rhythm.

Diana's head bobbed in sync with the beat, her hair flowing freely in the breeze.

Suddenly, the boy slid forward, leaning down to touch the ground. His legs traced a graceful arc in the air before he landed smoothly, reaching out to grab Diana's hand.

Together, they launched into a dazzling series of floor moves, their hands moving swiftly, bodies gliding like serpents.

As he sprang up, the boy twisted his waist with precision, each turn perfectly timed to the music.

Diana matched his energy, her feet dancing in a rapid exchange. Their eyes met, and an unspoken understanding passed between them.

After an intense series of moves, they executed a challenging aerial turn, leaving the audience in awe and prompting cheers.

Diana paused, looking at the boy with a smile.

"Wow, you dance amazingly well!" he said, beaming.

"Thanks! You're great too," Diana replied. "Can I have your contact info?" the boy asked.

Just as she hesitated, a familiar figure stepped in front of her. "Sorry, but my wife doesn't exchange contact information with strangers."

Diana turned to see Steven, who was radiating an air of authority that commanded respect.

The boy looked surprised at Diana's youthful and radiant appearance. "How can she be married? She looks so young!" he thought.

"Hey, dude, you're being a bit controlling here. We clicked. It's just a dance partner thing. What's wrong with exchanging numbers? Are you going to keep your wife from meeting other guys?"

Calling Steven "dude" darkened his expression.

The boy, seemingly around eighteen, stood in stark contrast to Steven's mature demeanor, making Steven feel a bit out of place.

The boy raised his eyebrows and looked at Steven.

"Do you really want to exchange numbers with him?" Steven asked Diana, turning to her with a serious expression.

Diana was confused. "I just wanted to dance! We were having a normal conversation. It's not what you think. If we exchange numbers, we can dance again next time. We have great chemistry!"

She realized she hadn't even noticed Steven lurking nearby.

She didn't see him here just now.

Steven's face hardened. Her mention of their chemistry only fueled his anger. In a low voice, he said, "Let's go."

He took her hand and started to walk away.

The boy watched them leave, a hint of regret in his eyes. "What a shame! She's such a talented dancer. If she joined our team, we'd definitely win the competition at the end of the year."

"Hey, it's no big deal. With your skills, you could easily find out who she is. That girl had a great figure and danced beautifully. She's genuinely talented. She's married, and we wouldn't do anything inappropriate. But that guy looked at us like we were criminals."
