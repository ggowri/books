Diana took a deep breath, filled with immense regret for wasting these past few years.

No matter how fast her company grew, it couldn't help the Farley family.

Diana bit her lower lip lightly as she turned to look for Luna, but saw Steven standing behind her. Under the dim light, Steven looked unusually relaxed, with a hint of coldness in his eyes.

He smiled and said, "Diana, if I told you I was set up tonight too, would you believe me?"

Seeing his familiar, mischievous smile, Diana was surprised. This side of Steven was rare.

Diana's fists tightened instinctively as she eyed him warily. "Steven, you're in cahoots with Queenie and Mr. Foliot. Do you think I'd believe you?

"Don't bother with sarcastic remarks. I couldn't care less how 'in love' you two are. Shameless as you are, you even matched your outfits. What a clever design. You three play your games well. Don't think you can be as slick in bed," Diana said.

With that, Diana turned and walked away, her back straight but icy.

Steven let out a helpless laugh. Now, he could finally understand how Diana must have felt back then. They all believed Queenie but never her.

So this was what it felt like to be disbelieved. It was truly hurt.

His heart burned with frustration, but there was nowhere to vent it.

The fire in his chest raged uncontrollably, making him feel unbearable.

Wait, did Diana just say something about... the bedroom?

"Diana, what kind of person do you take me for?" Steven gritted his teeth.

"Mr. Gellar, are we going home or heading to the police station to bail someone out?" Evan asked as he ran over.

He was trying to see if the boss was finally over his "love-struck" mindset.

Steven asked him, "Did you find out anything?"

Evan chuckled awkwardly. "Mr. Gellar, I was on the first floor watching the commotion. Paula caught Jeff cheating.

"Jeff slandered Ms. Bishop, so she called the police and got him arrested.

"Then I heard Queenie screaming upstairs, so I followed the noise to see what was happening, and it looked like you were all in it together!"

Steven's brows furrowed. If Evan thought that, no wonder Diana didn't believe him.

Steven growled, "I'm not in league with Queenie. Find out who drugged Cecil."

"Mr. Gellar, is there any need to investigate? Of course, it's your childhood sweetheart, but there's no evidence. Mr. Foliot is helping her, making it hard to find proof," Evan replied.

Evan had seen everything clearly from outside earlier.

Queenie loved to be the center of such chaos.

Now that Jace was protecting her, she'd make things as dramatic as possible. Even if people suspected her ulterior motives, they wouldn't intervene unless they were directly affected.

Steven pressed his lips together, watching Diana's distant figure as she hurried away.

Steven instructed, "Go investigate, and call me once you find something."

With that, he went after Diana.
