Dalton replied: [Got it, Mrs. Gellar. I'll take care of everything on my end.]

A glimmer of hope flickered in Diana's eyes. Dalton reaching out to her meant something significant.

Diana responded: [Please, don't call me Mrs. Gellar at the office. Just use my name.]

Dalton acknowledged her request: [Yes, Ms. Bishop.]

Diana put her phone away and powered her wheelchair into the elevator.

Nick Portman and Barry Donald were the bodyguards Leopold had arranged for her. They always kept a watchful eye from a distance.

Diana straightened her posture, revealing her confidence. "Queenie, what gives you the right? Is it your theatrics, your tears, or your attempts to play the damsel in distress to catch a man's attention?

"Sorry, but I'm a woman too, and your crocodile tears won't work on me."

"Diana, you..."
