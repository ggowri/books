Diana was enjoying her steak when Queenie's call came through.

She glanced at the caller ID and smirked, letting it go to voicemail.

It looked like the police arrived at Queenie's office.

She continued cutting into her steak as Queenie's second call came in.

The third and fourth calls went unanswered as Diana elegantly finished her breakfast.

Unable to reach Diana, a panicked Queenie had no choice but to be escorted out by the police.

As they reached the lobby, reporters caught the scene of Queenie being led away. "How could this happen?

That bitch Diana actually called the cops on me."

Noticing the reporters' shocked expressions, Queenie forced a smile. "It is just a misunderstanding. I'll be out soon."

But the crowd's suspicious looks said it all.

After all, the police wouldn't take away someone innocent.

Within moments, news of Queenie's arrest spread like wildfire online.

Zion saw the news and was floored. But the signing hadn't started yet, and he wanted to see if Steven would bail Queenie out.

Whether she was guilty or not didn't matter much. A little stint in jail wouldn't tarnish her reputation. He could easily whitewash her image.

Zion also rushed to the police station. He had to know for sure if Steven would come through for Queenie.

Less than half an hour later, Queenie was playing the victim online while Diana had deliberately exposed her lies about the fake pregnancy, causing the news to spread like wildfire.

Diana had even gotten Nolan to fan the flames, making sure the story went viral.

Having lived through this once before, Diana wouldn't let Queenie get away with slandering her reputation anymore, whether openly or behind her back.

Queenie would have to face the consequences of her evil actions.

Queenie sent Mario to find Diana.

But Mario had no idea where to look for her.

The only thing he could think of was to go to Steven's company and ask him about it.

Being fiercely loyal to Queenie, Mario drove there, ignored the receptionist's protests, and barged straight into Steven's office using the elevator.

Steven had just seen the news himself and smirked, pleased that Diana had handled things on her own.

The solution was crude but effective - unless Queenie had actual proof.

But framing Diana had become such a habit for Queenie that she never bothered to gather proper evidence.

When she saw Mario barge in, his handsome face was cold. "Don't you know how to knock? Did you feed your manners to the dogs?"

An arctic chill filled the office.

Mario was taken aback but pressed on anyway. "Mr. Gellar, where's that bitch Diana?"

Steven grabbed a book from his desk and hurled it at Mario's chest, and then it hit the floor with another loud thud.

"Mario, you son of a bitch, that's my wife you're talking about. This is your last warning! If I ever hear you disrespect her again, I'll cut out your tongue. Now get the hell out!"

Mario flinched, his chest stinging where the book had hit him. He was stunned that Steven had actually acknowledged Diana as his wife.

"Mr. Gellar, don't forget that Queenie is the one you love. She's the only one worthy of you."

Mario clenched his fists, glaring at Steven in anger.

Steven's resolve was clearly wavering, and Mario could sense it. Yet he couldn't find the right moment to hypnotize Steven as planned.

With the holidays approaching, even Queenie couldn't get a hold of Steven.

Steven's gaze darkened as he took a deep breath. So this was what everyone around him thought... no wonder Diana had walked away for good.
