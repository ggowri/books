Queenie clenched her fists, feeling a wave of discomfort washing over her.

Her stomach even ached slightly.

She couldn't let all her hard work become a laughingstock, especially when everyone believed she was Steven's favorite.

Closing his eyes, he felt an inexplicable irritation at the thought of Diana's indifferent demeanor, as if he meant nothing to her.
