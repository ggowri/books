Diana looked at Steven and asked, "Mr. Gellar, which university do you want to send me to for further studies?"

Steven replied, "Gaffa Design University. You have a great talent for design. If you continue your studies, your future value will be immeasurable."

Diana sneered, "Why don't you call the principal yourself and see if he's willing to accept me? If he is, I promise I'll go there."

Steven was delighted. "Okay! I'll do it in a moment. I happen to know the principal."

Diana said, "Mmm. Grandma, let's go upstairs for acupuncture."

Mrs. Helen Gellar asked Steven to take care of it, and then she went to a private room with Diana for acupuncture.

Steven went back to his room to make a call.

He knew the principal of Gaffa Design University. Companies like his would recruit some outstanding designers every year.

It was a pity that he failed to recruit the best designer two years ago. Otherwise, his clothing business would have been the best in Capitole long ago.

"Hello? Is this Mr. Wilson? I'm Steven Gellar. I want my wife to study at your university. She's an excellent designer."

Mr. Wilson said, "Mr. Gellar, I didn't expect you to be married. Can I get a file on your wife?"

Steven prepared Diana's personal information and designs early the morning. He returned to the message page and sent the file to the principal.

The principal didn't say anything. He clicked on the file to take a look. When he saw the name Diana Bishop, he was stunned. "Does this big shot still need to come to my university for further studies?" he wondered.

"Mr. Gellar, you are joking, right? Two years ago, your wife, Diana, was a doctor of philosophy, the highest degree for designers in our school. She's not only a professional fashion designer, but also a capable jewelry designer. In the field of art, she's had three major strengths of designers. She's also the most famous animation designer, and animation design is a piece of cake for her. We've been inviting her to be a professor in our school.

"But we never found her. Turns out she married you.

"Mr. Gellar, please stop joking with me. That's just too discouraging. She's the senior designer you were looking for two years ago."

Steven's mind went blank, and his upright body trembled for a moment.

Turned out Diana was the designer he'd been looking for two years.

So he was too narrow-minded last night and misunderstood Diana.

He had never been so shocked before, and he was speechless for a good while.

He was shocked and pleasantly surprised.

"Is it so hard to admit that I am excellent?" Diana's words came to his mind.

He suddenly thought of a question, so he asked in confusion, "Mr. Wilson, why couldn't I get this information on her before?"
