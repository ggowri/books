The one who had been clamoring for a divorce suddenly didn't want it anymore.

Cecil took Diana to a decent grilled meat restaurant. As soon as they entered, they smelled a strong aroma of grilled meat.

Diana chuckled, "After we finish dinner and go out, we'll still carry the scent of grilled meat on us."

Cecil actually enjoyed grilled meat quite a bit. After every car race, he would go to have grilled meat and beer with his good friends. It was their hobby.

"I've eaten here before. It's pretty good. I've booked a private room, and the smell won't be as strong in there."

With his hands in his trouser pockets, Cecil's casual, roguish charm attracted the attention of the girls around them.

"Such a handsome man! He looks like a big star from a TV show," one girl screamed excitedly.

Cecil winked at her, then put his arm around Diana's shoulder, signaling to the girls that he already had a girlfriend.

The girl looked dejected, waved her hand, and sat back down to continue eating.

Diana always found the outside world quite interesting.

These girls were so lovely. When they encountered someone they liked, they could shout it out loud, with kindness in their eyes.

She really should get out more often.

They settled in a private room on the second floor. Cecil poured water for Diana.

Diana picked up the glass and took a graceful sip of water.

She said, "Cecil, if Queenie is indeed behind your situation, she will send people to retaliate against me tonight. If we encounter danger later, you figure out a way to escape. Don't worry about me."

Cecil's movements halted. He looked at her with deep eyes, "Why? Do you think I'm that kind of person?"

Diana replied leisurely, "I'm afraid you'll hold me back."

Cecil's hand trembled. Alright, he overthought.

It turned out he wasn't worthy.

Seeing his stiff expression, Diana couldn't help but chuckle.

"But I have a second plan..."

Diana shared her plan with him.

Then, Cecil's hand trembled even more.

He thought, "Are there really women that malicious in this world?"

Cecil had, in fact, underestimated Queenie's malice.

They enjoyed their grilled meat without any incidents.

However, when they came out, a sudden fragrance filled the air.

Before Diana could warn Cecil, he collapsed weakly on the ground.

Diana asked, "Cecil, how do you feel?"

Cecil looked at her weakly. She struggled a bit but still couldn't get up. "Diana, my legs are weak."

At that moment, four men wearing silicone masks emerged from the opposite private room.

One of the men approached Cecil and kicked him.

"You little brat, you escaped before, but you won't get away tonight." The man laughed arrogantly.

Cecil asked angrily, "Did Queenie send you?"

The man shook his head. "I don't know her. Someone paid a lot of money for me to kill both of you. Now, come with me."

Cecil looked at their masks and was slightly shocked. They were from the Chaos League.

He looked at Diana. "Diana, they are from the Chaos League. You have to run."
