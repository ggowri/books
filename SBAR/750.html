Diana could afford to wait three years.

When they left, Diana walked in front and Alexander walked behind her.

Alexander looked at her thin back and said with a frown, "Diana, you should eat more. You are too thin."

Wearing a down jacket, Diana still looked thin though.

"Hiss..." It hurt Diana a lot the moment she stepped back.

Steven and his group happened to come out of the room.

From Steven's perspective, Alexander and Diana were hugging each other.

Steven and Owen were at a loss for words.
