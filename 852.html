Osborne emerged from the dungeon, cradling Kendra and Yulissa in his arms.

The scenes of God of Star wielding Destruction Orbs and Disintegration Rays continued to flash in his mind, leaving him deeply shaken.

With God of Star's strength, if he unleashed Destruction Orbs, he could easily annihilate an entire city! Anyone within the area covered by the orbs would be doomed, and even I would be helpless against them! Even the highest-ranking Class S Lesser Gods would likely fall.

This is what divinity means? And how many gods like God of Star are there? He once mentioned that during the Age of Gods, he was just an ordinary god. There must be many more like him!

And how many Dark Deities like Mosley are out there?"

Osborne shuddered, shaking his head.

What mattered was to continue improving his strength, await his Honor Quest Ceremony, defeat everyone there, and pass Sebastian's test.

Then, he would set out to find his parents.

"Osborne, you found them?"

Nathan and the others gathered around, their faces filled with concern as they looked at the unconscious Kendra and Yulissa.

"Don't worry. They're fine—just poisoned. They'll wake up soon.

The Dampston branch bloodline of the Augustine family had already moved from Mount Seven's Third Estate to the First Estate at the summit. This move was requested by Marce's branch, given that Osborne was now the primary heir of the Augustine family.

With his elevated status, Marce's branch bloodline couldn't afford to offend them.

"Take good care of them and let me know if anything happens."

Queenie nodded firmly.

"Now, I need to retreat and focus on improving my strength.

He was heading to a secure location for his seclusion—the Augustine family's dungeon.

It was the perfect place for uninterrupted training.

If Osborne had any questions, he could always consult the God of Star. What seemed like a complex issue for Osborne might be resolved with just a few words from God of Star.

Despite his severe injuries, God of Star nodded in acknowledgment as Osborne entered.

Finding a quiet corner, Osborne began his rigorous training regimen.

Over the next two weeks, he aimed to master Destruction Orbs and Disintegration Rays, elevate Thunder Realm, Spatial Domain, and Spirit Realm to Level Two, and, most crucially, perfect Explosive Force.

With these achievements, he would stand a slim chance against the formidable Isa.

"Let's get started!"

Osborne wasted no time and swiftly entered a deep state of training.

As he plunged into his training, news of his Honor Quest Ceremony began to spread rapidly around the globe, sending shockwaves throughout the world.
