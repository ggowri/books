Outside the gates of the Augustine Estate, tens of thousands of the Aberrants' Human Slaves and clones stood in eerie silence.

They didn't even attempt to surround the Augustine Estate.

They seemed utterly unconcerned about any potential escape routes. It was as if they were simply waiting, playing a psychological game.

Greyson murmured, "Are they trying to mess with our heads?

"Leaving us a way out, making us think we could still run? It's like they want us to crack before the fight even starts."

Wesson frowned, sensing the same unease but knowing it was more complicated than that.

He scanned the other 3 directions, his instincts telling him that there were more lurking in the shadows.

"Send the word out. Get everyone ready for battle!" Wesson commanded.

The Aberrants might not have launched an attack yet, but they couldn't afford to let their guard down. With both sides numbering nearly 50 thousand, all Class A and above, the destruction of battle would be devastating.

A fight would lead to catastrophic losses in no time.

His gaze fell upon a distinct group in the distance.

Nearly five thousand members clad in blood-red combat suits stood there, their expressions grim. Each held a uniquely designed energy gun.

They were not just any fighters—they were Class AAA.

The Blood Demon members!

The front line consisted of one hundred elite Blood Demon Vanguard members, their crimson uniforms adorned with golden and silver threads, each embroidered with a sharp dagger.

Though they, too, were Class AAA, their aura was undeniably stronger than the rest. Even Wesson felt a shiver of fear in their presence.

They were equipped with the finest armor, each wielding a divine artifact alongside a more extravagant energy gun.

Those were super energy guns, hastily delivered from the Weapons Research Institute just the night before.

Wesson felt a flicker of relief.

"Thank goodness we still got them!" Wesson thought.

However, he couldn't shake the feeling that even their strength might not be enough to hold off the Aberrants.

Just then, the Aberrants' forces stirred.

The massive army, nearly 50 thousand strong, suddenly saluted in unison.

"All hail the King!"

"All hail the Master!"

The booming shouts sent a chill through those at the Augustine Estate.

At that moment, a figure emerged from the throng—Gerardo.

He strode forward, and every clone and Human Slave gazed at him with fervent admiration and zeal.

It was as if they would gladly lay down their lives at his command; even the Class S clones wore the same expressions.

Gerardo approached the gate with a subtle smile and raised his gaze to meet Wesson and the others.

"Well, well, if it isn't Wesson. Where are Sebastian and Osborne? You're not worthy of a conversation with me."

Wesson's face darkened, silent.

Gerardo arched an eyebrow, feigning curiosity.

"What's the matter? Can't they come out to play? I hear Sebastian's in pretty bad shape. Did his injuries worsen? And Osborne... he's not still missing, is he?"

With each word, Wesson's expression grew grimmer.

Gerardo noticed the look on Wesson's face and knew his assumptions were right.

A flicker of surprise crossed his features.

He frowned, "Sebastian's not here? I expected that. After all, I was the one who defeated him. And now he's in a coma after detonating that divine artifact to save Osborne. That makes perfect sense.

But Osborne, too?

Did whoever rescued him not bother to bring him back?

Hmph! I actually worried for a moment and even went through the trouble of merging with a second divine corpse!

Turns out, there was nothing to worry about! But hey, the merger went well enough; my power's even stronger now.

With both Sebastian and Osborne out of the picture, I won't waste any more time here.

No one can stop me!"

With that thought, Gerardo fixed a cold, penetrating gaze on Wesson and the leaders of the other major factions standing beside him, their faces betraying a shared intent to fight.

Gerardo declared, "You all are going to die today! This is the price you pay for helping Sebastian against me!

"You will all die!

"Not a single one of you can escape!"

Gerardo raised his hand high.

"All Hyenas and Human Slaves, heed my command! Charge forward!

"Annihilate them!"

In an instant, the Aberrants' army surged forward like a tidal wave.

"Kill!"

"Kill!"

"Kill!"
