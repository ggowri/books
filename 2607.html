At this moment, Clara and Leroy were extremely excited and thrilled.

The Dragon Elixir not only enhanced their potential but also fully unleashed it.

Before taking the Dragon Elixir, their potential, even at Class S, was considered prodigy level but hadn't reached supreme. Now, their potential has definitely broken through to Class SS and is even advancing toward Class SSS.

Now, they are the most supreme prodigies.

Osborne led the two out of the hotel.

At this moment, two Royal Family guards, one male and one female, were already waiting outside the door. Their expressions were cold, and their attitude was unfriendly.

After all, in the Glorious Divine Kingdom Duel, Osborne and the other two defeated numerous princes and princesses to become joint champions, causing the Royal Family to lose face.

As Royal Family guards, they had always been arrogant and demanded respect from everyone they encountered.

However, this time, they found people around them constantly criticizing them.

People's conversations all revolved around yesterday's Glorious Divine Kingdom Duel and how Osborne, Clara, and Leroy fiercely defeated the princes and princesses.

Those people wore mocking and sarcastic expressions, which deeply humiliated the guards, so they naturally showed no kindness toward Osborne and the other two.

Osborne didn't care at all.

"Lead the way!" he said calmly.

Today, everyone who participated in the Glorious Divine Kingdom Duel can enter the Royal Treasury to exchange their Kingdom Duel Scorepoints for anything they desire.

The other contestants in the Empire Hotel were also ready and, led by a group of guards, entered the Palace through the side door.

Except for Osborne, everyone else was stepping into the Glorious Divine Kingdom's Palace for the first time. They all appeared extremely curious, looking around.

Only Osborne, gazing at the familiar surroundings, revealed deep disgust in his eyes.

Because this place carried too many painful memories for him.

In this cold Palace, he endured countless pain and anger. He felt no attachment to this place, only endless hatred.

If he could, he would destroy this place right now.

Of course, this was just his wish.

Not long after, the guards led them to the depths of the Palace.

At this moment, Aiden appeared.

His gaze swept over everyone and finally rested on Osborne. In just an instant, Osborne caught the cold killing intent in his eyes.

Lost in thought, Osborne's gaze shifted to the distance.

In the distance stood a massive rock, its surface covered with intricate patterns that emitted dazzling light and intense energy fluctuations.

Osborne knew this was the entrance to the Royal Treasury.

This massive rock was called Skywarden Stone.

Skywarden Stone was a Supreme Sacred Artifact, linked to the Palace's formation system. Only with the Skywarden Token could one control the Skywarden Stone to move, revealing the passage to the treasury.

Otherwise, no one could enter the Royal Treasury. Even if several warriors at the Supreme Proto-Imperial Empyrean Sacredness level joined forces, they could hardly shake the Skywarden Stone, let alone break into the treasury.

There were only two Skywarden Tokens.

One was in Frederick's hand, and the other was in Callista's hand.

Aiden was the person Callista trusted the most.

This is the Skywarden Token.

"Oh my god, 1.896 billion Sacred Artifacts?"

"Superior Sacred Artifacts actually number 3.6 million?"

"Legendary Sacred Artifacts amount to 280,000?"

"Supreme Sacred Artifacts exceed 10,000?"

Even though most of those present were from First-rate Powers or were prodigies, they couldn't hide their shock.

Anyone daring to steal these Sacred Artifacts is courting death!
