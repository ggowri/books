In the blink of an eye, Osborne activated his interdimensional travel and closed the six-mile gap like it was nothing.

He reappeared right behind the sixty Class S clones, precisely where he had planned to be.

As he materialized, all sixty pairs of eyes turned toward him. They were Class S clones, after all—sensitive to even the faintest fluctuations in space.

Osborne's arrival hadn't gone unnoticed; he was instantly locked onto by every clone in the vicinity.

Their reflexes were lightning-fast. Recognizing him as an enemy, several clones launched their attacks without hesitation.

But this, too, was part of Osborne's strategy.

His focus remained on his true targets: the eight Class S Vampire Kings.

The blood coursing through the veins of these Vampire Kings surged like a raging river—they were not like ordinary folk.

Their blood was potent, the source of their power, enabling them to manipulate their enemies' blood as if it were a weapon.

The Vampire Clan was notorious for this ability, earning them the name Blood Clan.

And Osborne? He was the progenitor of the Blood Clan, a level above these Vampire Kings in a hierarchy that was crucial to their kind.

The higher-ranking vampires could easily dictate the fate of the lower-class.

Now, Osborne aimed to take them down.

"Kill him!"

The command rippled through the ranks of the Class S clones, and their response was immediate; they surged to attack. Osborne's presence had been barely a blip on their radar, as he only emanated the energy of a Class AAA.

Thus, just the nearest Titan Kings went for him.

This was also part of the plan.

He had anticipated that only the closest Titan Kings would react.

Their movements were clumsy compared to other foes—the Tauren Kings and Wolf Kings packed a serious punch, while the Medusa Queen had the ability to turn others to stone and confined his agility.

Ignoring the Titan Kings' attacks, Osborne unleashed his martial art aura and pointed directly at the eight Vampire Kings.

"Die!" Osborne called out with fierce determination.

In an instant, his target shifted from physical flesh to the very blood coursing through his enemies.

A powerful jolt rippled through the eight Vampire Kings as they sensed their blood losing control, ramping up in speed as though sent into a frenzy.

A pressure that felt like it was rooted deep within their souls bore down upon them, paralyzing their bodies.

"Explode!" Osborne commanded, his voice piercing the tension.

The blood within the Vampire Kings detonated with explosive force, tearing through their bodies as though they were nothing more than balloons losing air.

Gouts of blood erupted.

The Vampire Kings' faces changed expressions of horror and disbelief. They couldn’t move.

A deafening explosion followed as the Vampire Kings' corpses ruptured from the inside out.

Not even Osborne's Light of Redemption could save them now.

With their demise, the circle of the sixty Class S clones shattered, and for the first time, Osborne caught sight of Bryce, who was trapped in the center.

But danger loomed; the attacks from the nearby Titan Kings struck down upon him.

The Titan Kings might not have the sheer brute strength of their counterparts, the Tauren Kings and Wolf Kings, but they were still a terrifying force within their own right. Each Class S Titan King delivered a fierce onslaught against Osborne, their punches echoing like thunder as they crashed into his martial art aura.

A total of six Titan Kings unleashed their fury.

If it hadn't been for the transformation of his martial art aura, it would have crumbled under such ferocious pressure.

But now, standing firm, it held steady against their might.

"Now's my chance!"

Osborne absorbed the Titan Kings' blows before launching himself forward. In the blink of an eye, he slipped through the gap in their circle and appeared right beside Bryce.

The power of his Divine Bone surged in his right leg, and with a swift kick, he sent the Medusa Queen flying, denying her any opportunity to unleash her petrifying gaze.

With no time to waste, Osborne snatched Bryce back.

"Light Realm! Light of Redemption!"

Without hesitation, he enveloped Bryce in the protective glow of his abilities.

Relief washed over him; his plan had finally succeeded!

But would fortune continue to favor him? The answer was shrouded in doubt, for he was now encircled by fifty-one Class S clone.

Trapped within their midst, Osborne knew the stakes.

It was a dangerous game, and if he couldn't break free, death would surely be the price.
