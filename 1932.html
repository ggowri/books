A sense of unease settled in Osborne's heart as he pondered the implications of his situation.

"I really hope that World Tree offshoot takes down the Shadow Terror Fiend!"

Just then, he watched as Primordial's massive form began to shrink rapidly, transforming in the blink of an eye into a petite sapling that radiated a brilliant green light, shimmering like a finely carved gemstone.

In an instant, Primordial dove into Osborne's mind.

A deafening roar filled his head, and Osborne felt Primordial enter his Soul Current Ocean.

The golden hues of his Soul Current Ocean were instantly illuminated by a vibrant green glow.

Surprisingly, he didn't feel any discomfort.

With confidence, he observed Primordial, eager to see how the fusion would unfold.

He noticed that Primordial seemed incredibly excited. Its roots trembled vigorously, and every leaf quivered, casting a green light that made Osborne's Soul Current Ocean ripple gently.

At that moment, an overwhelming sense of pleasure washed over him.

"This feels amazing!"

And this was just the beginning.

Primordial settled in the center of Osborne's Soul Current Ocean, its dense roots plunging deep into the waters, creating massive waves that surged around him.

Yet, Osborne still felt no discomfort.

Instead, he sensed his Soul Current Ocean becoming incredibly solid.

If he were to describe his Soul Current Ocean as a house for his soul, it had once been built from dirt—more robust than the average person's but still limited in strength.

Now, with Primordial's roots anchoring themselves, it felt as if steel reinforcements had been added, transforming his earthen dwelling into a concrete fortress.

The solidity had increased by dozens, if not hundreds, of times!

Osborne was thrilled.

"Is this one of the perks of merging with Primordial? With this, I won't have to worry about soul attacks anymore! Even a Supernal Deity King specializing in soul power won't be able to breach my Soul Current Ocean or harm my soul! My Soul Current Ocean is as solid as a rock!"

His spirits soared, but then he felt something strange.

After Primordial had established its roots, he noticed his soul power being drawn into Primordial's trunk at an alarming rate.

In mere moments, his Soul Current Ocean was completely drained of its soul power!

Shock coursed through him.

"Primordial swallowed all my soul power?"

Just as he was about to react, Primordial's voice echoed in his mind, "Don't panic! This is part of the fusion process, and it's beneficial for you! I won't harm your Soul Current Ocean or your soul. Just trust me!"

Hesitant but willing to give it a chance, Osborne paused.

To his astonishment, he soon noticed that after being absorbed into Primordial's trunk, his soul power reemerged from its leaves.

But this time, it was purer and more potent than before!

Primordial had become like a massive soul power purifier, enhancing the purity of his soul power through its absorption and even increasing the quantity slightly.

At the same time, Osborne sensed that Primordial's trunk seemed to grow a bit larger.

"Is this a win-win situation?" he mused, feeling the changes in his Soul Current Ocean.

Suddenly, it dawned on him why Primordial was eager to merge with him—his soul power appeared to be something special.

For Primordial, this World Tree offshoot, the effects of absorbing Osborne's soul power were nothing short of extraordinary. It was growing at a visible pace, almost second by second, becoming stronger with each moment.

Yet, Osborne wasn't losing out either. He was reaping benefits from Primordial's growth.

His soul power, which had already glimmered with a soft golden hue, transformed into a richer gold with a hint of green after being purified by Primordial's essence.

He could feel the strength surging within him automatically.

This enhancement continued with every bit of soul power Primordial absorbed.

"Has the fusion been completed?" Osborne murmured, a mix of hope and curiosity in his voice.

Primordial replied, its voice steady, "Not yet! I've only just begun to emerge.

"My current strength is still too weak to complete the full fusion. I need your soul power to continue growing for a while longer!

"However, I can offer you more benefits right now! For instance, I can use my World Force to cleanse your Divine Body, pushing it beyond its limits and granting you even greater power!"

Osborne's heart raced at the prospect.

"Breaking through the limits of my Divine Body? Does that mean I could reach Level Eleven?"

His breath caught in his throat.

Primordial's tone remained calm. "Level Eleven Divine Body? No! The highest level is Ten! Level Ten is already the pinnacle. Breaking that barrier means... Infinite Sacred Body!

"In this vast cosmos, only beings with an Infinite Sacred Body can traverse freely! Otherwise, there's a significant danger. Without it, being struck by the various energy flows in the cosmos could be catastrophic.

"Rest assured, I will help you achieve the Infinite Sacred Body!

"And yours will be unique, infused with a touch of World Force! If you later merge with that fragment of the World Tree's root, you will possess the complete World Force!

"At that point, you'll truly be formidable!

"With a single punch, you could shatter a planet—or even a star! The entire cosmos will tremble at your might!"

Osborne stood there, dumbfounded. "Infinite Sacred Body?"

He had never even heard of such a thing before. He always believed that reaching Level Ten was the peak of Celestara, thinking only by leaving Celestara could one break through that limit.

Now, Primordial was telling him that surpassing Level Ten meant attaining the Infinite Sacred Body!

"An Infinite Sacred Body infused with a touch of World Force?

"Show me what you've got!" Osborne said, determination in his voice.

"As you wish!" Primordial responded, and in an instant, its massive trunk began to shake once more.

Leaves, vibrant and green, detached from its branches, transforming into streams of energy that swiftly merged into Osborne's Divine Body.

In an instant, Osborne felt his Divine Body stiffen, and an overwhelming surge of power began to wash over him, flooding his very being.

"Ah!" he couldn't help but cry out in agony, biting down hard on his lip as his body trembled from the intensity of the experience.
